From what I understand, there are two main problems with Conquest being worldwide:

1. Most ancestor characters are based in Ransei, and as such any that aren't would be limited in the scope of what they can do and who they interact with. I don't see anything that would stop ancestors hailing from other regions or having land there, but the focus would likely be centered on Ransei, seeing as it's supposed to be the key to ultimate power and all.

A. Not only is a conflict on multiple fronts more confusing, but we appear to only have two or three ancestors who so much as plan to operate outside of Ransei. Short of inventing half-a-dozen new characters, I can't see how a worldwide conflict would be sustainable.

So my vote is to have most of the action focus on Ransei, but with nothing to stop characters from hailing from other regions or occasionally leaving Ransei for short periods.

Well, finally got my act together and made myself a profile for Ancestor!Dune.

Ancestor!Dune

  • Name: Sabaku
  • Age: 26
  • Appearance: Sabaku looks much like Dune, except Sabaku is also sporting a short, well kept beard. They even share the same tanned skin, as well as brown hair and brown eyes. They are almost identical in height. In battle, Sabaku wears a breastplate, open faced helmet, and greaves, along with his signature bladed gauntlets. Out of combat, he wears loose cloth clothing, much like that of Middle Eastern cultures.
  • Kingdom: Born and raised in Terrera, Sabaku is now it's proud Warlord.
  • Personality: Sabaku is very honorable and somewhat hard headed. He takes his job as protector of Terrera and it's people very seriously, and will do whatever is necessary to protect the Terreran border. He believes that a warrior's word is his honor, and never goes back on a vow, but also never expects other to go back on theirs. On the battlefield, Sabaku creates a plan for each individual fight, and prefers MacGyvering to extensive preplanning.
  • History: Sabaku made a name for himself protecting the mines and quarries of Terrera from bandits and wild Pokémon. Soon after he was inducted as a true warrior of Terrera, the old Warlord sought to begin a war against all of Terrera's neighbors, simultaneously. Sabaku pleaded with his old master to call off his plans of crusade, but his words fell on deaf ears. The Terreran Army, having no plans on being crushed by their enemies' combined might, staged a coup and removed the mad lord from power. Sabaku was unanimously called upon to take his place.
  • Perfect Link: Drillbur line
  • Warrior Skill: Fortify
  • Weapon of Choice: A pair of bladed gauntlets, fashioned to look similar to Drillbur claws. After transforming, they become much larger and resemble Excadrill claws.
