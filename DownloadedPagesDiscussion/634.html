I think I have a good setup for Dune's team as he does his first Gym Challenge.

Laharl the Tyrunt & Flonne the Amaura: These two would be Dune's dual starters. That may sound weird, but bare with me. The two of them will have such conflicting personalities (being revived fossils) that they will essentially be like traded Pokémon in-game; They'll ignore orders, fight amongst themselves, angst, etc, even if they should just be walking all over anything they fight at the beginning.

Zeus the Stunfisk: Remember that Dream World Stunfisk that Dune caught waaaaaay back at the end of Unova-2? Yeah, Dune's eventually going to unbox him. Anyone who remembers what he was like will likely see how bad of an idea that is.

Candace the Shiny Zubat: Based off of an In-Game Pokémon of the same name, species, and coloring, Candace is a commander at heart. She's essentially a Bisharp in a Zubat's body. She'll likely be the one to get Dune's crew in order.

Eos the Mawile: A familiar face, Selene's Dream World friend would join somewhere around the third, maybe fourth Gym. He's a huge scaredy cat, and really doesn't like ghosts after being tormented so much in the Spooky Mansion.

Joyeuse the Honedge: A Honedge that Dune finds following him for a time. He'll eventually be caught, much to Eos's chagrin. As opposed to hot-blooded valor, he prefers caution unless danger is immediately present.

Odysseus the Clawncher: This guy will be caught post-arc, since Dune isn't one to fish normally. He's a very laid-back kinda guy with wit and tact, as well as a thing for coming up with off the wall plans, much like his namesake.
