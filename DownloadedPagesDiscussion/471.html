Name: Shokaki (Sho), 小火狐 (hopefully) meaning little fire fox. Shokakis name uses the kanji sho (小) meaning small; the kanji "ka"(火), meaning fire; and the ki from kitsune (狐).
Age: 33
Appearance: Sho is tall with bright green eyes and black hair. Well-built, but not muscular, Sho looks like he just entered his twenties, rather than looking his actual mid-thirties.
Sho wears a shin length, Vulpix-brown with Vulpix- orange trim, sleeveless trench coat-like outer garment, over bright red and orange upper-garments (Theyre of the more form fitting variety that dont have excess sleeves or cuffs like kimono have. I have no clue what they are called btw). Shos pants have the same pattern and coloration as his upper-garments, and are also of the more form-fitting variety. (i.e. Like the pants ninja wear. Again, no clue as to what theyre called.) Sho wears a brown cord around his waist to keep his "coat" from flaring open. Sho carries a well used walking stick and a backpack with merchandise and other things in it. Think of Magoichis first outfit, but without any armor.
Occupation: Merchant
Kingdom: Comes from the Fire Nation, Ignis, but now travels the Ransei region.
Personality: Outgoing and polite, Shokaki likes meeting new people. While not one to back down, Sho knows his limits and knows when its better not to engage an enemy.
Background: Born in Ignis, Sho was fascinated of being a merchant. Sho studied hard to be a merchant studying economics, history, law, the arts, and swordsmanship. Sho now travels Rensai to forge business relationships in hopes of starting some trade routes.
Weapons/Abilities: Sho has a concealed sword in his walking stick and has enough swordsmanship skills to protect himself from bandits, but he knows better than to tangle with proper swordsmen.
Warrior Skill:
  • Burning Heart Restores a little health to all nearby allies.
  • Blazing Heart Burning Heart upgrade. Heals all health of all allies and cures status afflictions.
Burning Heart is renamed Sweet Song. Blazing Heart is a renamed Soft Light.

Transformation: Sho's transformation is meant to show that Sho is becoming a warrior as a well as merchant. The cord around Sho's waist becomes a black leather belt. Sho gains small Ninetale tail-like furs along on his belt. He also armors up, gaining a red and gold breast plate under is coat, shoulder and shin guards that are yellow with orange highlights. His outer and inner garments swap shading with his inner garments becoming darker, and his outer garments becoming brighter. His walking stick/ concealed sword becomes straight, smooth, and highly polished.

Again, think Magoichis first outfit for the overall appearance (with the collar folded down), with Motochikas shoulder armor (sans spikes) for the appearance of the shoulder and shin guards.
Specialty: Fire and Fighting
Perfect Link: Vulpix and Ninetales

Sho's Pokemon

Name: Kasaiko
Species: Female Ninetales
Personality: Serious, but not excessively so, Kasaiko was Shos first pokemon. Kasaiko cares for Hinoto like a mother. Kasaiko is also very caring to other living creatures human or pokemon. Kasiko helps Sho by carry packs of merchandise on her back.

Name: Hinoto Hino
Species: Male Vulpix
Personality: The youngest of Shos pokemon, Hinoto was found hurt by Sho and company why traveling. After nursing the young fox back to health, Sho decided to adopt the Vulpix, dubbing him "Hinoto". Hinoto is playful and curious as most young children are, and his personality has yet to truly develop. Hinoto gets babysat by the rest of Shos pokemon.

Name: Mariko
Species: Female Torchic
Personality: Upbeat and loving action, Mariko cant wait till she can evolve and take a bigger take part in Shos adventures. Mariko also loves making witty comments about situations she finds herself in.

Name: Kaito
Species: Male Meinfoo
Personality: The only sane mon of Shos party, Kaito is very practical. Kaito is the voice of reason among Shos team (<If anymon would actually listen to me!>), but ends up getting dragged into any misadventures anyway.
