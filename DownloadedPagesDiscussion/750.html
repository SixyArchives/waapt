Regarding the earlier Neo-Galactic stuff: Thanks for the feedback and suggestions. At the moment, I think I'm going to go with a mixture of options 2 and 3 - leave the senior members of Neo-Galactic to divvy up responsibility amongst themselves, then have a leader seize power once I get back from my break.

Speaking of a leader, I had an idea for a character who could potentially fill the role, or at least show up even if he doesn't end up sitting in the captain's chair. Still, I might as well post a bio for him anyway.

Neo-Galactic Grunt Profile

Name: Callisto (real name unknown)

Age: Mid-thirties (presumably)

Appearance: Callisto is tall and broad-shouldered, with a mop of messy blonde hair, sharp blue eyes, and a genial smile that never seems to disappear regardless of the situation he finds himself in. His attire varies from day to day, going from a lab coat to a Hawaiian shirt to nothing at all with little to no pattern.

Biography: Living proof that genius and madness are indeed two sides of the same coin, it's difficult to tell if Callisto's brilliance is because or in spite of his instability. An exceptional physicist and tactical mastermind, with physical and mental skills that may well surpass even Titan's, his talents are offset by his numerous eccentricities - hence why he has never risen above his current rank. He is easily distracted, physically unable to keep himself from vocalising every single thought which passes through his head, and frequently suffers from delusional episodes which can easily become violent. However, on the few occasions where he is able to maintain his focus long enough to become an actual threat, his insanity is matched only by his cunning.

Very little is known of Callisto's past before he came to work with Neo-Galactic. The issue is not helped by Callisto himself making up a completely different backstory every time he's asked about it.
