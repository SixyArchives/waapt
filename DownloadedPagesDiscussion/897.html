So, here comes an idea for a Dream World media-arc I had:

Shovel Knight Mini-Arc

Shovel Knight is a very simple and silly game, so I think adaptating it to Pokémon wouldn't be too hard, besides, it would probably be funny to have everybody in Pokémon form running around wearing armor and digging stuff up and generally getting involved in silly shenanigans.

Plot

Long ago, the lands were untamed, and roamed by legendary adventurers! Of all heroes, none shone brighter than Shovel Knight (A Marowak with a shovel) and Shield Knight (I'm still deciding what she'll be).

But their travels together ended at the Tower of Fate; when a cursed amulet wrought a terrible magic. When Shovel Knight awoke, the Tower was sealed, and Shield Knight was gone.

His spirit broken, a grieving Shovel Knight went into a life of solitude. But without champions, the land was seized by a vile power: The Enchantress (A Gothitelle) and her Order of No Quarter!

Now, the Tower is unsealed, and devastation looms. A new adventure is about to begin…

Yes I copy-pasted this from the game, but really, there's not much else to say

How it'll work

I figured that it would be basically the P Cs helping Shovel Knight in his quest and pretty much follow the game's story, however depending of how many people get interested, I might have the group split up a bit in order for things to not get too cluttered, I suppose I could always create some extra boss fights or just silly situations to have one of the groups wander into.

I also thought that in regards to the game's relics, the characters could either be assigned one relic each (there's 10 of them) or just swap them around as necessary for maximum efficiency.

I'll try my best to make sure everybody can contribute to the plot without making anybody feel like being left in the sidelines.

Also, just for silliness, if possible those interested in taking part in it could possibly try to have their characters fit into the naming theme of "[something] Knight" (like the ones above, Shovel Knight and Shield Knight). It's not required, but it could be fun to do, Duke would likely be something like "Aura Knight"
