Female

Description: Kimmi keeps her long, thick black hair nestled under her large pointy witch's hat. She's quite short for her age. Her eyes are a magenta color. She usually wears a typical witch's outfit, her main colors being pink and blue in the thematics.

She has no ties to any organizations.

She hails from Jubilife City in Sinnoh.

[Background]

Before Kimmi was born, her father walked out on the family, leaving her mother to take care of Kimmi herself. Kimmi's always had an interest in the unknown, and one day, upon wandering into the woods, she had an encounter with Jirachi. She made a wish; one that nobody is sure of, but Kimmi's life changed from that day. Since then, she's had these strange powers that she's never really understood herself. She adopted the personality of a witch, and set off on a journey.

Among her powers is the ability to speak Monese.
