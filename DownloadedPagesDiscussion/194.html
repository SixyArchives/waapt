  • This arc is open to any and all benched Pokémon in the RP. There main role will be to assist in documenting all of the Pokémon that live on Pokémon Island (Which now consist of species from Kanto, Johto and Hoenn).
    • In keeping with the Pokémon Snap theme, this shall be done by taking photos of Pokémon. To that end, each Pokemon will be supplied with a Digital camera (With the necessary modifications required to operate it in event of lack of hands/limbs, etc.) to take pictures with. as an incentive, the Pokémon that takes the best picture of will receive a prize of some sort.

  • The arc will take place across Pokémon Island, with at least one significant event happening in each area (Those being the Beach, Tunnel, Volcano, River, Cave, Valley and the mythical Rainbow Cloud).
