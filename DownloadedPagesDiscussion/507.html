12666 PippingFool5th Jul 2013 06:03:24 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three
Earlier today I was discussing small details regarding the now starting Conquest arc. These details were just about small plot details, time-frames for certain events and character quirks between Izumi and Umbra. During these talks, I got an idea for an event within Conquest arc.

Why not, before the Conquest Grand Finale, we have our own Ember Island Players?

Now for those who don't know what I'm referencing, Ember Island Players is an Episode of Avatar: The Last Airbender that takes place right before the finale in which Team Avatar watch a play version of their journey. The play takes enormous liberties with the events, and turns each character into a highly flanderized version of the characters, as well as getting some events entirely wrong. The comedy in the episode is how TA react and riff the liberties taken with their story. It also provides the viewer with a needed break before the finale.

And that is what I suggest we do in Conquest. We hold our own version of EIP and have our ancestors riff on the inaccuracies and liberties taken with their persons.

So far, I have come up with a few play!versions of my characters.
  • Izumi is a nagging and whiny housefrau instead of a cold and sarcastic Action Girl
  • Ryuku is a Too Dumb to Live yokel. As opposed to while not quite sharp, an insane and dangerous Psychopathic Manchild
  • Umbra and Izumi are depicted as secret lovers instead of mortal enemies.

I just thought it would be fun for us to have a bit of comedy before the finale showdown in conquest and MS Ting the story in the form of a play would be a blast. So, what do you say?
