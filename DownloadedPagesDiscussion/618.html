15443 MrMasterMrDoom16th Oct 2013 03:33:01 PM Get RP Mod
All right...

It's kind of really soon after I decided to leave, so I'm not positive you guys would be very enthusiastic about me coming back.

If I'd be welcome back, though...

I've given a couple of things a bit of thought. I think I know why I haven't been having as much fun as usual with this lately...

First of all, I kind of disabled Zeal way too early. His insanity thing was supposed to happen more gradually, but I thought it would happen more quickly after his battle with Verax. Aside from Youta, who barely got any screentime, Zeal's my favorite character to write for. So his insanity is not the best for me. I was sort of thinking that Red Eyes would start to have some influence even when Zeal is in control, causing Zeal to become a Green-Eyed Monster. (And, yeah, that's referring to the evil from envy trope, not the fact that he has green eyes and is being controlled by a monster.)

Secondly, I've been kind of stressing out over the 8 scientists. The main problem, I think, is that I introduced all of them at once. They were all going to be important to my Iulianus-related plans for Orre, but I haven't spent enough time with them individually to pull any of those plans off yet. So, I need to give each of the scientists more interactions individually, which I've kind of made into an impossible task. I can't really have them interact with other Cipher members, as their whole codename and mask system is something that only Iulianus uses, and they have different codenames outside of that group. And I can't really have them interact with the J-Team, either, since that would lead to conflict pretty instantly, and they'd still be more like "Scientist A" than individual characters. So I can only really have them interact with each other or Rickard Gavin. And the latter idea is so ridiculous I'm not even going to consider it anywhere close to useable.

Speaking of Rickard Gavin, there's the baking contest. I'd wanted to have Zeal be a part of that, but in his insanity, I don't think he'd be particularly interested. It's kind of funny... the baking contest was a spontaneous decision back in Holon, and I made it vital to a few of my Orre plans when I wrote them. Still, I'd be fine with just Lanie participating. But I'm away far too often to really have the baking contest work. Maybe it could have been TS'd? Or shortened? Maybe I could have put the baking contest on the second forum, too... I suppose there are alternatives there.

Lastly, the thing that I think has been bugging me most...

I wanted to get Lanie more character development over the course of Orre, since she was somewhat flat and static through Unova-2 and Holon. But I'd only managed to give her maybe one or two lines over the course of the arc so far. I don't really know how to solve that problem, though. The same goes for Zeal and Lanie's mons. They're all pretty flat and static, too, yet I haven't been able to do anything with them.

All this, combined with a bit of Real Life stress, is probably what caused me to decide to leave so abruptly. I'm still not exactly sure that I want to come back, though... I just... I just don't know... I really just don't know...
"Even if everything has been predestined, will you not oppose it?"
