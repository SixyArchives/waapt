So I had an idea for something that would happen during Almia, specifically in Hia Valley and Haruba Desert.

The idea is, that since two of the extra missions in Shadows of Almia involve Dialga and Palkia creating rifts in time and space, in Hia Valley and Haruba desert specifically, there would be lingering aftereffects of this that appear every so often. They'd just so happen to occur when the group's there.

In Hia Valley, things and people would be see around the place outside of the time that they should be in. Objects would appear just like they did however long ago, people would act like they're still in their own time. Might have some of the ancestor characters cameo?

In Haruba Desert, things like Fishermen fishing in lava, Water Pokemon swimming on land/in the air, land Pokemon having trouble moving on the ground/walking on water, etc. Maybe a Gyarados living in Lava or something. Might feel a bit like the earlier days of the RP.

Both of these locations have the two remaining Emotion Feeder machines from Illusion, so I figure it'd be good to give the areas something else to make them seem less barren.

A lot of people already expressed that they'd be fine with this in-chat, but I just wanted to make sure, since it was kinda hard to follow.
