Only the brightest in her gym.) in Celadon City returned to Kalos, yes, but she started looking for something... new. Something to help her stay elegant forever. She found the Illusion Project, and never looked back. Now she's looking for a way to stay 29 25 forever, and she's hoping the Illusion Project will help with this.

  • Mons: Note, she has more than are seen here, as it is her personal mission to gather every mon she deems "Elegant", but these are her main 6.

    • Nickname: Nettle
    • Species: Lilligant
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality: She is Mathilde's starter, and has stuck with her through thick and thin. Nettle loves nothing more than to sit in the sun, soak up the rays, and SOLARBEAM EVERYTHING BLARGH. She is the slightly unhinged member of her team, specializing in battle, and... information extraction.

    • Nickname: Rose
    • Species: Gardevoir
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality: Rose the Gardevoir is usually seen walking with Mathilde on the Lumiose City streets. She is a positively sweet mon, slow to anger, and quick to forgive. But if you hurt Mathilde, you'll see that this Rose has thorns.

    • Nickname: Narcissus
    • Species: Liepard
    • Gender: Male
    • Personality: Can you say ATTENTION WHORE? I hope so. But don't say it around Narcissus, you'd just inflate his already enormous Ego. Has a strange infatuation with Snapdragon (and all dragons, but especially Snapdragon), that is only surpassed by his love for himself.

    • Nickname: Snapdragon
    • Species: Altaria
    • Gender: Male
    • Personality: Snapdragon is a simple mon. He loves to sing, he loves to preen his feathers, he loves to BURN HIS ENEMIES TO THE GROUND. But mostly, he loves gardening and interior decorating! :3

    • Nickname: Water Lily
    • Species: Milotic
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality: Water Lily is a the Team Mom. She watches over all of her "Babies" with tender loving care. And Arceus help you, if you dare touch anyone of them. She mostly uses Support moves in battle, but will switch to powerhouse moves, if her teammates have been hurt.

    • Nickname: Violet
    • Species: Froslass
    • Gender: Female
