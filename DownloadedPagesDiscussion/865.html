 21619 Darker Shining, Fri, 11th Jul '14 1:48:15 PM from Norway Get RP Mod Relationship Status: Shipping fictional characters

So, for whoever wants to write for Linnéa and Ray...

Um, I guess I should give you guys some more information... First the basics, I guess.

Linnéa is a girl with long red hair and brown eyes, and Ray is her second personality, and thus looks just like her, except he keeps his hair in a ponytail and obviously wears different clothes to try to hide the female figure as much as possible.

Personalities

Linnéa is a kind girl, but isn't very good at standing up for herself or dealing with confrontations. Those things are Ray's job.

Ray started out as Linnéa's imaginary friend, who helped her cope with her stepmother's abuse. He promised to be strong, so Linnéa wouldn't have to be. Essentially being a defense mechanism of sorts, Ray is very protective of Linnéa. He's much more serious than Linnéa, and doesn't trust a lot of people. He is, however, a fairly good judge of character.

Towards other people, if they are on the side of good, he won't be hostile towards them, although he won't be all that friendly to them either. However, if Linnéa interacts with them, it's usually a sign that he trusts them, at least enough to know they won't harm Linnéa. When it comes to bad guys, he will usually avoid them to keep Linnéa safe.

Unlike Linnéa, Ray is aware of what he actually is, although he doesn't like being reminded of it. He wants Linnéa to stay as she is, and not grow any stronger as a person, since he fears that if she were to change, he would no longer be needed, and thus would cease to exist. He often wonders about the nature of his existance, wondering if Linnéa's pain is really the only reason he exists.

As you can probably guess from this, Ray doesn't smile a lot. Linnéa often worries about Ray, since he often seems so sad.

Linnéa and Ray's two Meowstic obviously know the truth, but play along since they don't want to upset either personality. If Linnéa and Ray being the same person, or at the very least their resemblance to each other is pointed out by another character, the two will insist that Linnéa and Ray are separate people.

The Meowstic also use their psychic powers to quickly change Linnéa/Ray's clothes when the two switch which personality is currently active.

Oh, and Linnéa is also a bit of a Sweet Tooth, although probably not to the same extent as some of the other characters in the RP.

When is which personality active?

Depends on the situation. Ray is more likely to be the active personality in situations that require some sort of physical activity, as part of wanting Linnéa to stay as she is. (of course, Ray wanting to keep Linnéa the way she is and training to become stronger does mean that Linnéa also has gained a lot of physical strength, even if she might not be fully aware of it)

If Linnéa is in any sort of danger, Ray will immedietly switch in in order to protect her. (and fight off/threaten whatever/whoever was threatening her)

So...

Any questions so far?
