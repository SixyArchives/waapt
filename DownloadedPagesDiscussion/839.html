Region of Origin: Hoenn

Occupation: Trainer, derr

Age: 23

Appearance: SHOOP◊

Bio: A quiet woman, who once resided in Sootopolis. Riley was never really outgoing, but overall friendly, bonding strongly with those that she does befriend, such as her Pokemon. And then the Kyogre and Groudon event happened. Traumatized by both the destruction (do not tell me two gods wrestling left the immediate city around it unharmed) and fear, she locked herself further, and essentially cut ties with everyone she knew, leaving Hoenn and her family. She now seeks only to become more powerful, more unbeatable, so she attempts to hide her emotions at all times. Gwen, however, is trying her damn best to recover the previous woman, but is it all for naught?

Pokemon:

  • Gwen (Female-Gardevoir): A kind, motherly, passionate who's prone to snark at times. (WATCH AS I FUCK THIS ONE UP). Riley's starter, Gwen has been trying for ages to help her trainer . Ability is Synchronize

  • Eris (Female-Klefki): A prankster, as both her name and ability would show. She's more of a "practical jokester" most of the time, however, and she knows well enough to not play against fears for her own amusement. Besides that, she's childish and sweet in demeanor, being the youngest of Riley's team. Ability is prankster

  • Heckle (Male-Hydreigon) A normally polite and gentlemanly Dragon. However, when he evolved from Zwelious, his other head didn't quite fade away, instead acting as a split personality. Said personality is that of a violent, badmouthing asshole. Ability is Levitate.

  • Clyde (Male-Gengar): Also a jokester, but on a different level then Loki. In fact, he commonly takes things too far, and doesn't realize this until it's too late. Even then, he's a pretty friendly guy- when you're on his good side. Also a massive snarker. Ability is Levitate.

  • Twitch (Porygon-Z, identifies as Porygon-Z) A spastic, glitchy mess of a Porygon, its capture was more of a "spur of the moment", what with it trying to Burn-Freeze-Eletrocute Riley. Spastic, broken, and somewhat violent, Twitch is powerful, but perhaps dangerous. Ability is Adaptability.

  • Grunt (Male-Aggron) A 'Gron of few words, and an aggressive one at that. He definitely loves the battle, and isn't afraid to show it. He typically considers more peaceful, docile mons as "weak", and usually won't even spare them words. Though he IS willing to befriends those who are able to prove themselves... Ability is Rock Head.
