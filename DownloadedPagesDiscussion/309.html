In the chat, I brought up an idea that might make Luke more accepting of the RP, hopefully without making anyone else want to leave due to "uncommon interests".

Perhaps we should start making more prominent "slice of life" plots concurrent to the more major stuff. I specifically say "more prominent" because, from what I've seen in the 4410 pages of the RP I've read, all previous attempts at "normal" Trainer stuff have been either drowned out by or abandoned in favour of the "saving the world" type stuff.

In fact, PMD-R really could have used it. Before you accuse me of bashing on the arc, hear me out. Perhaps you remember, me and a few others went back to the OU a couple times to do "normal" stuff. And they got abandoned because, well, absolutely nothing else was happening there. Perhaps people wouldn't be bashing on the arc itself if the normal, OU stuff hadn't been virtually forgotten.

Back to the main point.

The RP could really use some normal stuff to dilute the, in Luke's choice of wording, "epic" stuff. It would really help avoid future Arc Fatigue and the like, as well as avoiding conflicts about what the RP consists of - the two things that drove Luke away.

And in case you're wondering, no, Kanjoh doesn't count. Kanjoh is a Breather Arc. In fact, perhaps taking the "concurrent slice of life" idea could help avoid the need for Breather Arcs in the future.

If anyone has any concerns, suggestions, etc., please give them.
