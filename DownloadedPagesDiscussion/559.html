- As opposed to straight conversion between humans and mons, the voodoo that goes into making the bombs work is really part of a larger set of conversion voodoo that we see with the armbands... With the potential to force mode lock. With some specific conversions easier to create than others. - As such, there's 4 main easy conversions, and 2-3 that are hard for plot purposes:

Easy-ish

  • H->P
  • P->H
  • H->Locked!P
  • P->Locked!H

Not Easy

  • Locked!P -> H
  • Locked!H -> P

Along with the potential of:
  • P -> P (We see with Wabbajack)
  • P -> Locked!P and presumably its inverse if we decide to canonize that gag reveal of Eon.

- The bombs that were utilized by the 7JD are H -> LP at site of impact, and kick up H -> P fallout. Explaining how the transformative effects of visiting the -Bverse were temporary, but the societal nuking was not. - As such, cooking up the counter to these bombs would presumably take more than a couple betas and false leads.

Thoughts? And are there any objections / hesitations to running under this set of rules?
