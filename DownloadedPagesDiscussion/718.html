Guess Who...?
Hello everyone, so it's taken a while, but after a couple days of discussion, we've come to the conclusion that in the interests of allowing Rt AU to be resolved faster, that the original plan of "sequenced visits" should be broken into "concurrent events", with Rt AU's postgame picking up whenever they've all finished.

Unova

  • A: Propaganda campaign to stir unrest (in-progress, can basically be time dialated)
  • B: In-universe contact between the various factions of the Resistance. Mezzery, Del, Kirk, anyone else.
  • C: Team effort to wrestle control of Subway (or at least enough of it to be useful)
  • D: Twin precision strikes against Bahahkun (Del + Kirk) and Cassandra (Mezzery), potentially kept from being a protracted dramafest by a certain duo counter Rules Lawyering to finally get active.
  • E: Terms of surrender after some complaining from the defeated side that basically amount to "look, we have our own lives and you have yours, go home already" with reluctant dragonmons being routed to Opelucid where troublemakers can be wrestled into submission by Drayden(gon).
  • F: Character arcs and gags until anti-mon bomb deployed. Things would mutate into PMD-B were it not for them.

Kanto

  • A: Tagg/Tracer arrive as parties in Vermillion meet each other
  • B: Complete meetup, start rallying resistance
  • C: Nab plans at Schrodinger's Location, don't necessarily know what it is right away
  • D: Continue logistics, prepping for uprising
  • E: Uprising
  • F: Endgame and starting to train focus on Johto for a couple missions when 7JD showdown interrupts

Hoenn (Note that level of detail is due to quite a bit of intertwining of plots)

  • A: Stuff before "Courtney Gears":
    • Benpost teased in walkabout
    • "Opening up" Speck/Stacatta post
    • Pick one of "Gino/Jackie recruit E4" and "Run into Viral again"
  • B: "Courtney Gears / Death To Squishies"
    • Make the actual ear worm, distribute
    • 2nd Benpost
    • Gino does other option from A not chosen
    • Hoenn players stop CG antics
  • C: Vallok gets MAD:
    • Zurin becomes a mech
    • Numidium's Emergence
    • Rematch between Viral + Gino interrupted by above
  • D: Climax
    • "Prepare to die"
    • Zurin: "Vallok, I didn't sign up for this"
    • Vallok ragequits, grunts more or less give up and stop attempting to futilely throw their weight around
  • E: Endgame
    • Cuteness + gags, as in Unova's endgame
    • Anti-mon bomb somewhere down the line

Orre

  • A: The Orran resistance getting into communication with the other regions.
  • B: The liberation of Phenac City and Gateon Port.

Would this course of action seem acceptable to everyone? Do note that if you want involvement but don't already have anything planned, the details of the above can be tweaked to include you.

EDIT: Updated Orre sequence. Note that there are some PMD-B details that will either need to be worked in to sequence or otherwise handwaved into not happening thanks to cosmic Butterfly Effect.
