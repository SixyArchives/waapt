I'm with Straw. No spoilerblock. For the following reasons:

  • Ruby and Sapphire are over 10 years old. If you're participating in a Pokemon RP and have never bothered to find out what the story is, sorry, but that's your own damn fault.
  • The events of Gen III have been important to the RP since almost the beginning, with Gamer being the canonical protagonist of Emerald.
  • The people who are trying to go in completely blind are vastly in the minority, especially on remakes. It's unreasonable to demand that people not talk about new games just because you personally don't want to hear about them. The onus is on you to avoid that conversation, not on everyone else to not have the conversation.
  • There's a difference between information that Nintendo officially releases, and actual spoilers. Anything that is put on the official site, or is officially released to the media should be fair game. Just don't talk about potential leaks.
