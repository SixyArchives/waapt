Hi, thought this RP looked interesting, so I made a character to join, if you'll have me.

Player

  • Name: Alyssa
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 16
  • Personality: Shy and introverted, but very friendly on the rare occasion she opens up to people.
  • Appearance:
    • Blue-green eyes
    • Tanned skin
    • Incredibly long (down to knees, no exaggeration) light brown hair which fades to black at the bottom.
    • Wears a white sleeveless shirt and denim shorts. Does not wear shoes.
  • Backstory:
    • At age 3, she barely escaped a hotel fire which ended up killing both her parents. She ran away from the hotel and out of whatever city she was in at the time, and came across a swamp. Surprisingly, the local Pokemon took her in and raised her. Over time, she bonded with a Totodile named Rain, to the point where they were practically inseparable. At age 15, she set back out into soceity with Rain - now a Feraligatr - to attempt to become a Pokemon Trainer, specializing in Water-types. Despite always being with water, however, she retains a paralyzing fear of fire, given to her by the flames which stained the end of her hair black with soot, a permanent record of her trauma.
  • Other details:
    • Can speak Pokemon language - speaking it, not just understanding it. She is actually much more fluent in Pokespeak than in English, which is part of the reason she's so shy: she is actuallly slightly scared of speaking, because it's so hard for her compared to most people her age.
    • She does not know her last name; she had only caught on to her own first name by the time the hotel trauma happened.

Pokemon

  • Rain
    • Species: Feraligatr
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality: Kind, but very protective of her "sister" Alyssa
    • Backstory: As stated above, Rain met Alyssa as a Totodile. Specifically, they met three years after Alyssa was adopted by the Pokemon of whatever swamp she ended up in. She herself taught Alyssa how to speak in Pokemon language, in turn learning the little English that Alyssa knew. She was actually the one to convince Alyssa to go out and be a Trainer, volunteering herself as Alyssa's starter; secretly, she envied one of her actual brothers who had been chosen as an "official" (as in, given to the trainer by a Pokemon Professor) Starter Pokemon.
  • Sapphire
    • Species: Vaporeon
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality: Vain and boastful about her "beauty"
    • Backstory: Given as an egg by an Eevee fanatic, Alyssa accepted the offer because, frankly, she was absolutely terrible at catching Pokemon the normal way. She ended up becoming very spoiled, having taken advantage of Alyssa's kindness and generosity to get what she wanted. It took Rain to end this selfish behavior, but she still retains her attitude.
  • 'Wonder
    • Species: Magikarp
    • Gender: Male
    • Personality: Very headstrong
    • Backstory: Wonder was caught just recently, as Alyssa's first successful "normal" capture. He was found as a literal fish out of water, flopping on the shore of the Lake of Rage, looking for challengers to attempt to defeat in order to evolve. When Alyssa tried to help him return to water so he could breathe, Wonder flopped out of the way, and decided to Bounce up and slap Sapphire in the face with his tail. Needless to say, Sapphire got mad, and attacked back. After he somehow KO'd the Vaporeon, Alyssa took the option of trying to catch him, and as stated before, she succeeded.
