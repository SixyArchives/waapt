Lewis Kathrada
  • Male, 18
    *Appearance Neat and wholesome. Trim hair and conservative clothes with a tie. [Indian] in ethnicity.
    *Ties - Church of Helix
    *Home region Sinnoh
    *Backstory Born and raised in Veilstone City, and a dedicated parishioner of Cathedral of the Fresh Princess since the age of six. Lewis has just begun his two-year mission to help spread the word of Lord Helix through the tried-and-true method of door-to-door soliciting. Church elders have decided that the Ranger regions are in need of some explanatory pamphlets, and chose Lewis for his strong faith, keenness for raising Pokemon, and blind obedience. He spent a few years training abroad before coming home for his eighteenth birthday and then being sent to Fiore. While he admits to not fully understanding his faith, he is determined to serve it well and live according to its bizarre, sometimes contradictory principles. In the tradition of Helix followers, his goal is to eventually become possessed by the holy spirit of the Mob and lurch his way to the Pokemon League championship, evangelizing along the way, never giving up until the voices inevitably prove too much for his mortal frame. The prospect does not scare him in the slightest!

Arkady, male Cherrim
*Ability Flower Gift
*Bio Lewis' starter. He met Arkady on a field trip on his eleventh birthday which is also the number of days it took Lord Helix to rise from His fossil - and so believes theirs to be a fortuitous friendship. Arkady is happily atheist, thank you, but plays along so as not to disappoint his trainer. Lately, though, hes been having repetitive nightmares about voices mumbling in his head...

Thou-Shalt-Riot-When-Democracy-Is-Upon-Us, female Aromatisse
*Ability Healer
*Bio Also known as "Riot" (or "Riot Control" to Arkady), she was a gift from Lewis' childhood Kalosian pen pal. She is actually a militant adherent of democracy and worships the Dome behind his back. More to come when I figure out the rest of her personality.

EEEEEEEEER, male Sharpedo
*Ability Rough Skin
*Bio Or "ER" as he prefers. Issued to Lewis by the Church as his obligatory water transport and named by parish mob rule as is custom, he desperately wants to go to the Pokemon League. He has an all-consuming obsession and devotion to AIIIIIIRRR, the patroness of Lewis' church, and thinks by helping him reach the championship, he'll gain the attention of Air Jordan.

Dasher - female Ponyta
*Ability Run Away SEEEE CHAMELEON
*Bio Lewis uses her to travel, since he can't afford a bike and Fly is prohibited by Church law. He named her after Dashbat, who he feels didn't get enough attention in the canonical texts. Dasher has actually never won a battle in her life, as she runs from every wild encounter and tries to flee trainer battles. But mang can she run.

Altar, male Altaria
*Ability Natural Cure
*Bio Readily adapted to Helixism, but has it all screwed up in his head. He's got some kind of early-onset Poke-dementia, and thinks he's done everything and knows everything. At least the annoying babbling is offset by his generosity. Manasseh's terrified of him because he resembles the monsters of her nightmares, clouds.

Manasseh, female Durant
*Ability Hustle
*Bio She was sent on a mission by the queen of her colony and accidentally dug halfway around the world, just in time to run into Lewis. While she wants to go home, she doesn't have the best sense of direction or sense in general. The team decided it's best if she stays - there's less of a chance of her ruining the foundations of random buildings.
