Lewis has just begun his two-year mission to help spread the word of Lord Helix through the tried-and-true method of door-to-door soliciting. Church elders have decided that the Ranger regions are in need of some explanatory pamphlets, and chose Lewis for his strong faith, keenness for raising Pokemon, and blind obedience. He spent a few years training abroad before coming home for his eighteenth birthday and then being sent to Fiore. While he admits to not fully understanding his faith, he is determined to serve it well and live according to its bizarre, sometimes contradictory principles. In the tradition of Helix followers, his goal is to eventually become possessed by the holy spirit of the Mob and lurch his way to the Pokemon League championship, evangelizing along the way, never giving up until the voices inevitably prove too much for his mortal frame. The prospect does not scare him in the slightest!

Arkady, male Cherrim
*Ability – Flower Gift

EEEEEEEEER, male Sharpedo
*Ability – Rough Skin
