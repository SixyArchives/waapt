Alright, here we go.

Umbra's Hoenn-2/Wrapup plans!

This is it, folks. The final countdown.

I am hoping to be able to do all or at least most of these plans. However I am willing to save a few of the more minor ones for post-retirement oneshot material.

I am trying to make this a comprehensive list for myself and others, and will be updating it as I remember stuff. Friendly reminders of things to put on here are much appreciated so long as there aren't too many.

Anyhoo...

THE PLANS

  • The subplot leading to Odin's ultimate fate. This is THE big loose end I need to wrap up before I go.

  • Participating in whatever the main plot of Hoenn-2 is.

  • Beedrilite, Sableite, and Metagrossite get.

  • Chrome's Mega subplot.

  • Waka discovers how to Mega Evolve.

  • Seish and Lexi's duel.

  • Something to help Seishirou and Fuuma be badass again... But also more silly stuff with them.

  • If Herbert's still around, a Mindscape visit.

  • Certain mons meet their ancestors.

  • Arika explains everything that's happened to her to her parents, hopefully with the help of other J-Teamers.

  • A little something I call Werehuman Madness.

  • Lina and Ammy meet up with Kaguya again.

  • Robin gets some mons.

  • Introducing Bartelby.

  • Ammy meets Ho-Oh and tells him what really happened to his mother. (May need Luke or Bitter's help with this.)

  • Umbra and Lina learn the full truth behind their immortality.

  • Styx, Duolis, and Umbra learn who that spirit who helped them REALLY was.

  • Misc. things in the new Hoenn, including cameos from Legendaries from other regions.

  • Some misc. loose ends that need to be wrapped up before the retirement post, including ones with Chrome, Ilya, Umbra-B, Other!Umbra, Cyrus, and several 'verses' versions of Arika.

  • Depending on timing my final April Fools, giving a certain guest-star trickster deity some closure.

After all that...

  • The Retirement Post, with endings for as many of my characters as possible. (Certain parts will need to be spagged with other people, including Crow, Bitters, Pippi, Tracer, Herbert, Mezzo, and possibly Crewe and/or Moon.)

  • A special farewell/retrospective post in Discussion, posted around the same time.

Post-retirement extras

All these will be posted in a thread on the second forum to reinforce that the Retirement Post is pretty much THE END for me in the main thread (aside from cameos).

  • Umbra/Lina's little Mega Stone subplot.

  • A little thing with Styx and Xavier.

  • A subplot with Sari and Yomi.

  • Prism's Broken Pedestal moment

  • Anything else that comes to mind when I get the urge to re-visit my characters.

I will be focusing on these plans more than general interaction, but not entirely. Additionally help with certain plans (like Odin) is very much appreciated.

Additionally, two more things:

  • This year's New Years post from me will be relatively subdued, due to me wanting to save most of that stuff for the retirement post. The stuff that will be in it is to show that certain characters are alive and well and set up for their appearances in said retirement post.

  • However, I AM doing something special for my third anniversary. It will probably make some of you very happy.

That's all for now. May update more later.

(Also, how are we stretching Halloween this year? Because I'm confirmed busy on the day itself.)
