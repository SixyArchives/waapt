Ok, here we go.

Valencio Rodrigues

Age: 19 Height/Weight: 6'1, 167lb Hometown: Sunyshore City, Sinnoh Appearance: Black hair, messily sweeped back, with the sides and back cut short. Wears a striped black and purple muscle shirt and black skinny jeans, with grey converses. He has two tattoos. One is a large smiley face with a line crossed through it on his shoulder. The other is of a large stylized sun on his other shoulder. Symbols of the Donquixote and sun pirates Personality/Backstory: Val is a calm, level-headed young man, that loves the idea of being cool. He started his journey in Sinnoh and proved to be a skilled battler. That wasn't enough for Val, however. Though he loves battling, his true goal is to catch each Pokemon in existance. To that end he had caught over 500 of them for the Pokedex, the majority of them staying in his brothers sanctuary . At one point in his travels he went to Johto to catch some new mons and battle the leaders. He found himself in deep trouble when he was forced into an illegal underground death match organization. For a year he was forced to battle with a Quilava he befriended, before escaping and turning the head of the organization in. A year after that traumatic event, he decided to press on with his journey and headed to Kalos where he is now.

Pokemon

Since Val has so many, he changes them out quite a bit, but these are the three core members.

Name: Fuji Species: Torterra Gender:Male Pers./Backstory: Val's first Pokemon, he had been with Prof. Rowan for 50 long years as a Turtwig. Something about Val caused him to finally leave and go on a journey. Very old fashioned and sometimes crotchety, he does his best to take care of Val.

Name: Jet Star Species: Staraptor Gender: Male Pers/Backstory: The first Pokemon Val caught on his own. Always cheery and optimistic, Jet can brighten the mood in any situation. Is a strong battler, but dislikes fighting. Helps Val search for Pokemon from the air, and Fly from town to town.

Name: Corona Species:Typhlosion Gender:Female Pers./Backstory: The Quilava that Val was forced to use in the death matches, which evolved. Was left badly scarred on her torso from the fights, and has a scar on her left eye. Originally very cold and abrasive she warmed up to Val and began trusting him. She still has terrible nightmares about what she went through, and was left with a bad temper. Getting her too heated will cause her to go on a rampage and activate her Blaze. Has a large Smily face across her back with a line through it, identical to Val's. This was actually a brand that her previous trainer had her marked with in the fights. She deeply hated it, so Val modified it to look like it does. He also got a matching tattoo to show her how much he cared about her.

Well, that seems about it. Sorry if I've made some mistakes, kinda rushed it. Any thoughts?
