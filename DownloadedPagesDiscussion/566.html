Cipher Member Bio:

Here be my possible addition to the growing roster of evil-doers.

Name: "Cid"

Age: 29, but looks easily in his early forties due to self-induced stress.

Appearance: Standard Ciphertist outfit, with somewhat frazzled hair. He has obvious signs of sleep deprivation and work-related stress. He constantly wears a heavily tinted visor over his eyes.

Affiliations: Cipher, possibly ex-Galactic

Origin: Possibly Hoenn

Bio: The mysterious scientist who goes by "Cid" has been working with Cipher ever since the recovery operation immediately after Greevil's arrest. He has a personal interest in reviving the old XD project, though not many of his counterparts feel the same, seeing it as a waste of personnel and resources after XD-001 was purified, against all odds. He seeks to prove them wrong, and has personally attempted to recreate coding and schematics that were either destroyed by Team Extra attacks or taken by police. Despite his single-mindedness and obsession, his seniority still entitles him to the rank of Commander... though even Peons tend to crack jokes at the "crazy guys who sniffs from his own beakers".

Pokemon:

Name: Hector

Gender: Male

Species: Magmortar

Bio: Cid's only Pokemon, Hector is probably the only reason his trainer hasn't driven himself into the ground, bringing him food and drink during his more manic work nights. Cid has refused to use him as a test subject for his Shadowification processes, though whether this is due to personal feelings or convenience (Shadows tend to be far less caring of their owners), is unknown.
