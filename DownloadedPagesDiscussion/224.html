Non-RCable characters

The following characters are not available for RC'ing, period. I have offscreen plans for them.

RP!Silent, Maekrite, Inka, Baluarkos and their family, Lileep, Idrano and his mons.

Overall Guide for RC'ing Silent's characters

First of all, there are two keys to correctly handling my characters.

  • No excess silliness. Some silliness is OK, as is some randomness, but since I base my character development on predefined levels and directions of depth, I prefer my characters to progress in their intended direction more steadily. If you want to try something that you think is too silly, please ask me or someone who has already RC'd them. As for randomness, go for it, just remember to keep it in the mundane context of the arc.
    • The middle part of the OI is more or less a good guide of the level of silliness and randomness I like for my characters.
  • Silent's mons are not one-track mind creatures. While they might be mostly occupied with one thing, there is always some ghostly motivation surrounding them. They also tend to approach problems with a mind open to weird or novel strategies, even if they don't have time or opportunity to eventually to use them. This is more patent in the more experienced mons (Dintel and, to some degree, Ichtacya).

With that out of the picture, let's check some characters:

Characters intended for RC'ing by specific players

(Note that this does not mean they can not be RC'd by other characters)

Ichtacya / Leafeon♀ / Every or Wolf

Ichtacya is essentially a visitor in Silent's team. She is well-versed in living on the road, off-the-grid as a wild mon, and prefers to spend time with one or two friends rather than in large groups, unless they are Eeveelutions. Her reaction to most threats is to warn once, then charge in with sequences of attack combos where possible. Her better performing combination is to Energy Ball a projectile and use it as cover to build up a good angle for an X-Scissor or an Iron Tail. She is also quite clingy, more than Algira sometimes, and loves to be taken out for a walk. She does not like to talk or divulge much of her life, but will gladly talk at length of places she has visited or things she has nommed.

One core component of Ichtacya's personality is that she desires for her life to be more varied, not necessarily more exciting or thrilling.

Algira / Masquerain♀ / Tagg

Currently in a relationship with Helios, the two of them spending time in the Johto Safari Zone getting to know the bugs in the area and the different types of honey. Algira's personality is more or less well know, not much to comment on there. Initially prudish on the exterior, she's evolved to be more sincere but at the same time a bit unreadable. She likes attention but does not like to be the center of it.

Algira also tends to exaggerate some things. Algira speaks in flash patterns sometimes, but uses them more to express emotion. She expresses confusion or misunderstanding by "hmph"ing, hovering clockwise, or both. When it comes to attacks, she uses her flight capabilities for evasion as a backup to charge up attacks, since with her small size she can not exert much strength easily. When required she can try and use moves like Water Pulse and Silver Wind to barricade herself against projectiles.

Python / Ekans♂ / Tangent

Currently in PEFE Goldenrod with Silent. A very young mon that looks more fierce than he actually is, his motivations are mostly food and screentime. He does not like to be too much time in the open. Tends to hiss and rattle at everything (hiss is more friendly than rattle, though) and to wrap himself around wrists and arms. His Ability is Shed Skin. He sometimes speaks in (pseudo-)Python code which he uses mostly to express his (lack of) happiness and related stuff.

Because Silent is pretty open to the wild as a Trainer, Python still has some wildish behaviours. He will sometimes try to nom Pokémon smaller than himself if he is not sure they are "friends". He will also follow some people around in silence and staring intently, a la HG/SS.

The Bird Family / various species / Rex or Every

Copahue the Fearow♂, Harriet the Honchkrow♀ and Shahinne the Starly♀. The three are living in a birdhouse atop a PEFE building in Goldenrod.

Copahue is a practical and sincere mon, but his very limited movepool and past as an HM slave have left dents in his capacity for initiative and expressiveness. He is a very good fisher, something he's been trying to teach to his family, and also has a relatively good sense of direction. His reaction to most threats is to stay away and circle them waiting for a first move

Harriet is a relatively individualist mon - she escaped her life as a flock member to go with Luke because she got fed of that lifestyle. Tends to keep things to herself a bit and is currently undergoing some stress because of a Murkrow murder in the city. Still, she cares for Shahinne deeply, and although her feelings for Copahue are true, her hear might be split over for Bill (the bird) or Treeo. She's as physical as Copahue is but has more Special tricks as well - though she will hardly last enough to use most of them.

As a couple, Copahue and Harriet are a more or less solid item and despite the history behind their relationship they do not feel any sort of shame for being together. Take into consideration that a big component of why they got together was that Copahue was not enough caretaker to keep Shahinne out of danger. Copahue and Harriet like to spend time alone in Faith's rooftop "cooing"; however they carry out most of their other activities, such as tidying the nest, fishing or collecting berries, separately.

As for Shahinne... there is not much to be said that hasn't been said already. A chick with some street smarts and a limited degree of hyper awareness, she is also really individualist and at some point quite self-centered. She believes that she deserves people's attention and caring because of her prettiness, not out of malice but because of her high degree of self-awareness. As such, detests being hurt or feeling pain, which leads her to reject most forms of training; her reaction to most threats is to back away, observe for a moment and then annoy the threat with pecking to make it leave. Her only TM move is Secret Power. Shahinne is the character for whom the highest degree of silliness will be tolerated so long as it does not break her established character.

Characters open for RC'ing

Dintel / Sandshrew♂

To understand this mon you only need one trope: Never Grew Up. Dintel still lives in an almost eternal happyland. He also shows quite a bit of initiative, in particular when he is left to his own devices, being Silent's Second First Catch. However he will patently growl his disregard and discomfort for water, Pokémon to take away his stuff, or claw hygiene.

Rook / Human♀

Rook is a Trainer from elsewhere ( a non-canon region perhaps ) who is in Johto looking for some interesting Pokémon to catch. She is self-sufficient and aims high in her life, but is also not too expressive and tends to disregard, or at least ignore on first hearing, the advice that is given to her. If you have seen the third saga of Robotech you can get a small glimpse of her character from her namesake.

Rook's Pokémon are an offscreen Starter♀; Geratte, a combat-experienced Golduck♀ who can not stop making questions and trying to understand what is going on around her; and Adua, a Medicham♀ who is calm and observant, enjoying the small thins of life, but also seems to lack in such things as a sense of time or direction. The team is focused on catching a Johtoan mon of interest, which might be a Teddiursa, a Houndour (inb4 they are in Kanto) or a Skarmory. As part of a trend this mon to be caught will also be female.
