On Magic Mirror

Now that the current plan does allow me to start it fairly Soon (tm) , I'm once again looking for participants. For those of you who don't know/forgot what it is, a reminder:

Basically, Umbra, Ammy, and hopefully a few others find a magic mirror stowed away in PEFE, and they enter it to discover it's a portal to a pocket universe of sorts called Makbaliv. Umbra gets kidnapped by a    dark, maelovent force   , and Ammy must team up with whoever goes with her and a band of mons known as the Seven to save him, traveling across Makbaliv's subworlds and finding more about its history in the process.

Anyway, each subworld is an Affectionate Parody / Whole Plot Reference to a Disney movie. We have to clear them to progress and get closer to saving Umbra as well as finding out more about Makbaliv's history and the nature of the    dark, maelovent force.    So... superficially like a Kingdom Hearts game, only with less Square Enix and more Pokemon.
