Okay, so here's my char:

  • Full Name: Whackie H. Pancakington.
  • Alias: Pancake, the WACKY thief.
  • Personality and characteristics: Pancake is, simply put, the quintessential Ur Example of a Dastardly Whiplash. He's tall, lanky, bald, old, wears a top hat, a monocle, a bowtie, and a tuxedo, likes drinking tea, and has a white handlebar moustache which is perfect to twirl. He's a villain, which means he won't actively be part of the group, but might follow it around, try to steal Pokmon occasionally, and get his butt handed to him. He's more of a Plucky Comic Relief character than a villain, so don't take him too seriously. Please note that he doesn't resemble me IRL at all.
  • Pokmon:
    • Kricketot: Pancake's main mon. He rarely battles (I might even have him only know Growl and Bide a la D/P), but is nonetheless always out of his Pokball, aiding Pancake in his evil schemes. He thinks his trainer is a little spacey, and shows it quite well despite his Pokmon Speak problem.
    • Farfetch'd: A mon only kept around for "emergency rations" (Pancake says that only gentlemen get to eat a delicatessen such as roasted Farfetch'd). He's brave, but weak. He has a bad habit of popping out of his Pokball whenever he senses a battle, which annoys Pancake a lot.
    • Dugtrio: Caught as a Diglett. He's what Pancake dubs an "utility Pokmon". He does all the dirty work: He is used for battles and for making quick escapes using Dig Or Sandstorm. Despite being strong and fearsome, he's a complete and utter coward.
