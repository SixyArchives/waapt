5622 Luke92414th Apr 2012 03:06:31 PM Get RP Mod
Alright, since the Pokmon Mystery Dungeon arc is probably going to start sometime this week, I thought it would be a good time to write a primer for those still unclear on how the whole thing will work. Hopefully, this should be able to answer any questions you may have with the arc as a whole.

Grab your Treasure Sacks and Exploration Team badges, 'cause here we go...

Pokmon Mystery Dungeon Returns

What is this all about? Haven't we done a PMD arc before?

We have, which is partly why we're doing this arc in the first place, as the first PMD arc took place over a matter of days at the beginning of the RP and could hardly be considered an arc at all by modern WAAPT standards.

To put it more simply; we're doing it properly this time.

How is this arc different from other arcs?

Well, for a start, this arc will take place in the world from the games Pokmon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Darkness and The Sky. For those who haven't played these games, the main draw of this world is that consists of only Pokmon, who live in small communities, and for the most part, no humans.

Including you.

That's right, for this arc, you'll be mode-locked into your Pokform without the use of the Human -> Pokmon armband, as will everyone else, gaining all the benefits and drawbacks of your Pokmon form.

Of course, although your body may have changed, you are still a trainer. You're very welcome to bring along as many, or as little, Pokmon on your team as you'd like to the Pokmon World. However, keep in mind that there won't be any access to the PC storage system while in this world, so make sure you have the Pokmon you want with you before you leave.

So...how are we going to get there?

The arc will presumably begin with everyone gathering at the Entralink in Unova, where a portal network to many different worlds has been set up, presumably by one of the legendary Pokmon. The exact reason why we decide to leave for the Pokmon world yet, but several ideas have been thrown around, ranging from "The J-Team leaves for a while so that everyone doesn't grow too dependent on them" too "Our more genre-savvy mons decide that we didn't do the PMD arc properly first time around and convince us to do it again properly."

What do we actually DO in this arc?

Soon after arriving, Tagg decides to go off and climb Destiny Tower, which you might know as the hardest Dungeon in PMD-Sky, to collect a rare artifact called the Space Time Orb. However, due to an accident, the orb is shattered, scattering it's pieces all over the world.

The goal of this arc is to find the pieces of the orb and reassemble it. Although the actual number of shards of the orb hasn't been decided upon yet, collecting them will be very similar in manner to collecting the Lake Trio's Tears in the recent Infinity Keystone arc.

I wasn't in Infinity Keystone. Care to elaborate?

Huh. I thought everyone was in that arc. Shows what I know...

How you find the shards of the orb is entirely up to you. You can find them by exploring Mystery Dungeons, earn them as rewards from helping Pokmon in need, or even fight another Pokmon who might have stolen them to get them back.

Of course, if you can't come up with any interesting scenarios of your own, then that's perfectly fine. You can just as easily tag along with somebody else and help them out as they try to find some shards.

What's this I've been hearing about "PMD-A" and "PMD-B?"

The shards of the Space Time orb have the unusual effect of being able to open portals to other universes. A few of us will be making use of this function to travel from "PMD-A", the world the PMD games take place in, to "PMD-B"...which is actually a "Bad Future" of the Alternative Universe from the AU arc, in which humanity was almost entirely converted into Pokmon by an army of Dragon Pokmon.

Basically, as well as exploring a Pokmon World long left untouched by humanity, this arc will serve as foreshadowing for the "Return to AU" arc that will be happening at some point down the line.

Do I have to take part in "PMD-B"?

No, you don't have to take part in PMD-B if you don't feel like it. And even if you do, participatns will be able to move back and forth between both universes easily.

When does this arc start?

That still hasn't been decided yet. At the time of this post, the start date is currently set at "some time this week." This should be decided on soon, hopefully.

Okay, that's pretty much everything. If there's still anything that needs to be decided on, post it here to let us know.
