16717 rmctagg0920th Nov 2013 11:05:13 PM from Brooklyn, NY Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I won't say I'm in love
The Wanderer

To All Current and Prospective Players

So there's been something that's been kinda bugging me for a while now. Namely, that it seems that we tend to have new P Cs just get magically plopped into the world like in an RPG of your choice, knowing little to nothing about it. (For instance, I'm pretty sure some of the higher profile events like Mt. Pyre nearly erupting and Twilit Sinnoh should be common knowledge to just about everyone, even if they're not the kind to watch the news that often.)

So basically what I'm trying to say is that we ought to strive for better integration in the future, like for example coming up with some background suggestions new player characters can use. For instance, a PC knowing Monese off the bat wouldn't seem as special snowflakey if, say, they happened to be among those humans and mons that got shunted to the PMD verses by the power of the Shards during PMD-R like a couple of our current PCs already did. (Before anyone else says it, yes, I'm aware that has some potential for abuse, not least of which having so many people do it that it becomes kinda ridiculous, but I'd imagine we'd be heavily watching over anyone who'd decide to go that potential route.) Another example could be having to move because some Armortomb managed to destroy your residence in Sinnoh during the IK arc. (And yes I realize that we can run into the problem of people doing stuff that almost certainly couldn't have happened, but like I said we can talk to them about it if they want to go into any sort of detail.)

On our (meaning current player) end I'd suggest that we could possibly work on making a basic outline of events that PCs would likely have at least a "this was a thing that happened" knowledge level about, it doesn't need to be in super detail. (It could also vary by region.)
