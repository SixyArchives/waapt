So, here's my Conquest character, because I just had to make another girl:

  • Name: Miyu Nagayoshi (Mi means "beautiful", Yu means "gentleness, superiority", Naga means "eternal and Yoshi means "good")
  • Age: 22-ish
  • Appearance: Long straight black hair with sides locks that go to shoulder level, frequently wears a purple and black kimono with yellow and white floral patterns all over it, frequently carries around this◊ fan and purple sandals.
  • Personality:
    • Miyu is elegant, graceful, mature, humble and quite competent at what she does, she's also quite a casual person, though still very respectful towards others, she's also a bit quick witted and sometimes makes jokes at others expenses, though most of the time she doesn't mean any harm and would apologize almost immediatelly if she ended up offending someone. She tends to play everything as if it was her little game, however if things get serious so does her and she can be downright cruel when in a serious fight.
    • However, she's not violent by nature and actually wishes for a more peaceful world, as such she has a strong dislike for war and those who cause them, even though she may take part on them herself, she knows that it's needed in case she wants to reach her objective. Miyu is also quite loyal to Ichigo, being his second-in-command, and would do anything he told her to, however if someone or something catches her eye and would be against what was asked to her, she frequently tries to employ Loophole Abuse and Exact Words to get what she wants while also technically following all orders.
    • In the rare occasion of something or someone really ticking Miyu off, she gets much more agressive in the way she does things, however she's more passive-agressive than anything, she can quite rude and become much less subtle in the way she says things, but managing to annoy her that much is quite a feat in itself, as she's quite difficult to anger.
  • Good Points: She's quite the pleasant person and devoted to those she cares about, while also being a very strong person herself, she's also very good with Pokémon.
  • Bad Points: She can be a bit cryptic at times and it isn't unusual for her to act by herself and ending up complicating things.
  • Likes: Tea, social interaction, Pokémon, peace.
  • Dislikes: War, rude people.
  • Weapon of Choice: A Naginata.
  • Warrior Skill: Love and Honor.
  • Perfect Link: Growlithe Line.
  • Backstory: In planning.

Her Pokémon would be Ancestor!Grant, Ancestor!Duke, Ancestor!Riku and Ancestor!Seth, those I still need to think about.
