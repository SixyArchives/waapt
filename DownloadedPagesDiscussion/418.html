 Let it be known that, whether he does get this message or not, I mean every single word I'm saying right now.

The next section is not meant to push blame away from myself. That kinda defeats the purpose of the above apologies. I am trying to prevent it from happening to any of us in the future. It's probably a futile attempt, but I'd rather go down trying to pull us together than pushing each other away.

A lot of the drama is caused by burnout. Burnout caused by Arc Fatigue. We all know the reception previous excessively-long arcs have gotten, and this one leaves all others in the dust.

I know Tangent is probably thinking something along the lines of "we need to take a minute to calm down to an operable level." As it stands, we've got minor ideas, but no reasons to go there or to stay there once we do get there.
