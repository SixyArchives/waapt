Okay! Sorry this took a while, Tracer, but here it is, a guide to Team Iris. The descriptions are just the official bios from the game, so substitute terminology where appropriate (Vigilante force = Rescue Team, etc.). Also, these weren't out yet when this whole thing started, so if you haven't noticed some of these character traits before...that's why.

  • Krom◊ — Lucario
    • The prince of Iris and descendant of the Hero King. Although royalty, he serves as captain of the vigilante force and personally strives to protect his country. He may seem unsociable at first glance, but bursts into enthusiasm whenever anything interesting happens. Breaks the most things during army practice.
  • Liz◊ — Riolu
    • Krom's sister, princess of the Holy Kingdom of Iris and a member of Krom's vigilante force. Despite being royalty, she acts in a completely natural way, mingling with anyone regardless of their social class. Has the mentality of a child and often acts spoilt. She hops around more than anyone in the army.
  • Fredrick◊ — Empoleon
    • A knight serving Castle Iris and deputy commander of Krom's vigilante force. Calling him extremely serious would be an understatement. He loves poking his nose into other people's businesses, but gets his priorities wrong more often than not. Likes building fires more than anyone in the army.
  • Soiree◊ — Ponyta
    • A female knight who serves Krom's vigilante force. She has a straightforward personality, acting exactly as she thinks. Because she's cooler than a lot of men, she has many female fans within Iris and has trouble dealing with the sheer mountain of fan letters addressed to her. The worst cook in the army.
  • Viole◊ — Sneasel
    • A noble from some country. Though he's always joking, he's actually a sharp and able man who rarely divulges his true intentions. He's apparently in search of a wife and, as such, asks out any and everyone in his path. He's at the mirror more than anyone else in the army.
  • Sol◊ — Deerling
    • A knight who serves Krom's vigilante force. Kind and helpful, he's one of the few vigilante members with an ounce of common sense, yet is still kind of a scatterbrain. His hair is a permanent mess, as if he's just woken up. The biggest glutton in the army.
  • Vake◊ — Axew
    • A fighter who serves Krom's vigilante force. He's mischievous individual who wears his heart on his sleeve. For some reason or other, he's always brimming with confidence. He considers Krom his rival and is always trying to compete with him. Misplaces things more often than anyone in the army.
  • Miriel◊ — Misdreavus
    • A member of Krom's vigilante force. She's extremely methodical and a bona fide perfectionist, getting irritated when things aren't properly organised. Vigorously curious, she thoroughly researches everything that piques her interest. The person with the most personal belongings in the army.
  • Sumia◊ — Altaria
    • A member of Krom's vigilante force. Silly, foolish and totally clumsy, even she considers herself a dunce. However she is able to bond with wounded animals and possesses a hidden strength that most people lack. She falls for absolutely no reason more than anyone in the army.
  • Callum◊ — Graveller
    • An armoured knight who serves Krom's vigilante force. He wears a blank expression on his face and rarely speaks. His presence is sorely lacking and isn't noticed even when he's standing right in front of people. Not that it bothers him much... He's the tallest person at the table.
  • Donny◊ — Tyrogue
    • A common villager from Iris. He's a typical young man who's good at ploughing fields. Has a heavy accent and a peculiar way of speaking. Because he's never stepped outside his village, he's interested in many things. He gets wavy hair more easily than others in the army.
  • Ronku◊ — Scyther
    • A swordsman from the Kingdom of Felia whose skill with the sword is respected by even King Basilio. He's the silent and unsociable type, but gets flustered around females, making a red-faced getaway when they get near. He's the best vegetable peeler in the army.
  • Velvet◊ — Nidoqueen
    • A beast warrior tied to no particular nation. Although she's the last surviving member of the rabbit Taguel, she doesn't particularly care about preserving her race. A strong and cool woman with plenty of common sense. She's the fastest eater in the army.
  • Anna◊ — Charmeleon
    • A mysterious travelling merchant. She has many sisters who look identical to her, so many in fact that even Anna has lost count. As one would expect from a merchant, she's friendly and polite. She loves gold and wealthy customers and did I say gold? Has the most savings among her comrades.

I can also do the NPCs if you'd like.
