Straw's Invisible Shiny Bulbasaur Arc post

So for Kanjoh 2, I've been planning on having an miniarc in Kanto involving the Invisible Shiny Bulbasaur Glitch.(Also known as ——-)

Basically, it involves a Shiny Bulbasaur that was involved in an accident with Silph Co.'s experimental infinite storage PC system. The system glitched up, causing the machine running it to malfunction. The Bulbasaur is unable to escape, and is trapped, glitching up his body. As time passes, he begins to fade out, until the only part of him visible is his sparkle. After leaving a trail of glitched terrain, Pokemon, and humans, he runs away through several places to somewhere unknown. He does this to avoid affecting anyone else. The Pokemon and humans that are affected contract temporary blindness, being unable to see anything but the ISB during that period.

The arc would be the persons involved in it tracking down the ISB, and trying to cure him, or at least help him in some way. I might get the ZZAZZ glitch involved too, but only for the Explosion Bulbasaurs.

Please not that this arc will not affect anyone else's plans for Kanto. It will mostly be something that happens in the background of anyone else's plans. There may be NP Cs who comment on it though. There is no world ending threat.

I've posted some testing for this miniarc here.
