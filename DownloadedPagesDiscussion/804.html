So, my idea that I had for the Machinima was something centered around a genre of gaming that is near and dear to pretty much all of us in the group. Since this group is Pokemon based, what would be more fitting and diverse than an RPG-based Machinima?

The Setting

So, RPG City takes place in a city occupied by RPG characters and mascots. The city is divided into six main districts, (with one Central Square unifying the whole town into a common ground) representing the six factions.

There's J-Town, where the JRP Gs (or Js) reside.

There's the West Side, where the Western RP Gs (or Wests) can be found.

There's MM Otown (Get it? Like Motown? No? Okay....), where MMORP Gs (or Emmos [NOT Emos]) run the district.

Rogueland is the turf of the roguelike dungeon crawlers (Rogues).

Then, there's Miscella Village, where tactical RP Gs (like Advance Wars), Mon battlers (like Pokemon), and other non-standard genres of RPG reside. Residents of Miscella Village are called Miskies.

And then...There are the Outskirts. Spinoffs that aren't really RP Gs (Pokemon Snap, for example), and RPG spinoffs of franchises that aren't really RP Gs (Legend of the Seven Stars) get sent here. The Outskirts surround the entire city. The only way to get from the Outskirts to the Central Plaza is through the Tunnels, as Outskirters are forbidden entrance into any of the roads that connect the six districts.

Characters

Pretty much any character from any RPG can qualify as a citizen in RPG City. Sky's the limit!

(And I'm sure it would be a huge pain in the Froslass to find character models for some characters, but I have confidence in the Internet that someone out there has character models for just about any character ever.)
