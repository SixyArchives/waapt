Anyways, visualization help on PEF Exp, at least as I see it:

  • modules hang from 2D rails (meaning they can be moved forwards, backwards, left, or right, instead of linearly)
  • rails are both suspended from cables bound to the superstructure, and found on the bottom of existing modules
    • "elevator" rails exist, essentially rails connected to a crane so they and a module can be quickly moved in 3D space to be docked elsewhere
    • this allows 3D clusters of related modules to be assembled
  • high-speed rail trams, bridges, and passenger elevators also exist, for carrying peoplemons quickly between clusters (the module rail system probably averages only 10-mph, so while moving rooms to get places in fun and dramatic, it's not good if you are in a rush)
