I've gotten better at doing less serious, more social character interactions during the "Sage Saga" and interacting with Dopants, IRP. With this in mind, here we go!

Trainer

  • Name: Neil Mercury
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 19
  • Origin: Phenac City, Orre
  • Associations: Works alongside the ONBS.
  • Biography: Neil is a hopeful musical artist. He wants to start a rock band with his team, and tour the world. So far, he's been pretty successful within Orre, and has just started his first tour outside of his home region. He is still looking for a drummer that will stay in the line up. However, after viewing some of the wanton cruelty of Cipher, he also decided that he wants to use his dream to help combat their efforts. Thusly, he turned to the ONBS. He wants to be able to help them spread knowledge of just how cruel Cipher is, to the regions outside Orre, using his popularity to spread the message, mainly by holding charity concerts and the like.
  • Trivia: The band's name is Orren Story.
  • Monese: He is able to speak Monese. This is because every winter, throughout his entire childhood, he would spend his days with a flock of Chatot. He learned music theory from them, and in the process, they taught him Monese. This did take 5 winter's worth of time, though. Furthermore, he feels uncomfortable speaking Monese with Pokemon he doesn't know or doesn't intend to get to know.
