So some of you might've noticed that I haven't been posting much recently. And by not posting much, I mean I haven't been posting at all. My apologies to anyone who had plans with me, but you can consider me out of action until further notice.

There's a number of reasons why I haven't been able to bring myself to post. I'm not insisting that something has to be done about them, and I doubt I'd magically return even they did all go away, but I figure I ought to at least give an explanation for my inactivity. The speed's a major obstacle, for a start. Just to clarify, this isn't related to the earlier discussion of plot pacing in any way - I'm referring to the way that the RP is now consistently averaging twenty pages per day if not more. Posting is starting to feel like it has all the significance of gobbing into a river. Following the plot threads I want to keep track of amidst the sea of posts is nigh-impossible. That, coupled with the fact that my characters aren't relevant to anything anymore, means that I'm barely even feeling the urge to give them some kind of conclusion. I just want their arcs to be over and done with, and even that I can't find the motivation to do.

Some of you might've also noticed that I'm still doing stuff on the second forum. That's stress relief more than anything. Being able to post at my own pace with a separate thread for each plot is practically the only way I can still RP. Sorry for picking such a bad time for a quasi-retirement announcement, especially given recent events, but from here on out I'm probably going to be solely doing wrap-up stuff - assuming, of course, that I can find the energy to do stuff at all.
