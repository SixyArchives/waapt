2548 Luke92419th Jun 2011 12:54:27 PM Get RP Mod
I dunno really. Although I do agree that you could use something to do right about now. The whole GC hasn't really been friendly to newer players.

Anyway guys, I came up with what could potentially be an interesting idea, if a bit of a tricky one.

How about, at some point after the AU arc, we do an arc where we explore a new region? a-bit, one that we've made up ourselves?

Although, the twist would be that this region is populated entirely by Pokmon. It would be sort of like Pokmon Mystery Dungeon except without the actual Mystery Dungeons and rescue/Exploration teams. Oh, and the whole "Turning into Pokmon" thing.

I just think it would be kind of interesting to visit a place like that, if only to see what kind of towns that Pokmon would build. For example, I can easily see a town of Flying type Pokmon being made up of very tall buildings with entrances on the roofs as well as the ground so that bird Pokmon could enter without having to land on the ground.

Another example I could think of would be a Water Type settlement. While the vast majority of it would be underwater, there would be a district built under a glass dome or something that could accommodate Pokmon that can't breath underwater. Of course, it would be criss-crossed with canals and glass tubes of water for the more aquatic species to access.

Even though making up an entire region from scratch might be rather hard, the sheer creative possibilities help to make up for it. Plus, it would be a group effort, so we wouldn't have to rely on just one of us to think everything up for us.

So, is this a good idea? We don't have to do this, by any means, I just think it would make an interesting change of pace.
