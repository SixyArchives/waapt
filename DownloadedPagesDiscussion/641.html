Something From The Chat

Alright, due to a discussion in the chat, I'm officially putting up my plans for Galvani's "Pseudo-Armband". This was something that I had originally planned for the long term, but after the matter of the limited amount of armbands coupled with their necessity for Interspecies Romance was brought up in the chat, I've decided to release my plans early.

Galvani, ever since her first transformation via Armband, has been thinking of a way to replicate the transformation effect using SCIENCE! instead of having to play "who's got the armband?" all the time. Also, because of her feelings after feeling the rush of hormones for the first time, but that's neither here nor there.

Anyway, Galvani's first, prototype "armband" is that metal bracelet she's been carrying around for a while. Right now, it doesn't work. However, I had planned after a few months of development, to have it work. It would actually work more similarly to Crewe's transformation rather than that of the armbands it was originally based on.

  • It can only be used once a wearer is in physical contact with another Pokemon/human that is "compatible". What that actually means is up in the air as of right now. Did I mention I hadn't planned to release this for another few months?
  • The bracelet "bio-locks" after it's first use. Say, Galvani can only use it while touching Dune. Dune can't use it while touching Galvani, and Galvani can't use it while touching, say, Umbra.
  • The effect is indefinite until reversed. However, if the linked person is unavailable, the bracelet is effectively dead, trapping the wearing in his/her current form without the use of an armband of the proper orientation or Legendary transformation powers (I.E. you are Crewe, who really doesn't need it).

This process could easily be refined, with Galvani constantly working away the little bugs like the bio-locking, if the RP would allow it. It could also be replicated, though no doubt the brand new models would have their own glitches because, remember, this is Galvani we're talking about.

This would allow for those wishing to involve characters into transformation hijinks without having to unearth more 3k year+ armbands that are supposed to be countable on two hands. Also, the glitches allow for other sorts of hijinks, in a manner of speaking.

Reflecting from this

Silent raises a good point. Even if it's a far looser restriction than with the armbands, mass-production was never my intention.

In-universe, the bracelets are distinctly SCIENCE!, or, to put it in Genius: The Transgression terms, Wonders. They have No Plans, No Prototype, No Backup where each is unique and must be made from scratch. No reverse engineering, because any attempts will find that there's no way that Galvani's setup should have done what it did.

On a meta level, I believe that, while they should be more available than an armband, there should be either a petition, similar to the process for requesting an Attribute, or, as Silent suggested in chat, "something based on speficic (shared?) character development goals is both easier and funnier / more interesting to do".

Possibly, someone (just as an example, Keys) begins showing shared character development between Kitsune and Ren. He could post here on the Discussion thread his intentions to use the two together a lot. If they make noticeable headway in terms of a relationship, he could ask here on the Discussion thread if their current development is warrant for a bracelet. If accepted, the two would continue to build the relationship as the plans for the bracelet (getting Galvani properly inspired, etc.) are being drawn up.

Once the relationship has reached a peak, we revisit and, with any luck, approve of the bracelet. Galvani goes on a bender, a bracelet is made, and the two give a big kiss.
