So, I made a Cipher Character.

Bio

Name: "Cecilia"

Rank: Technically Peon, but don't mention it to her. It's a quick way to get a stiletto to the face.

Age: 28

Affiliation: Cipher, Science Department.

Origin: Possibly from Holon.

Bio: Cecilia was born and raised in Holon, graduating with honors from Coulomb University with a degree on Biotechnology. She traveled to Orre with mostly good intentions, but got mixed up in the wrong crowd, and got drafted into Cipher shortly after. She doesn't much care for Cipher's goals, and even speaks out against Shadowing Pokémon, but is quickly silenced. She's brilliant, but her views have led her to not advance very far in the society. This is about the only thing that can anger her, as she's usually a very chill person. She's in charge of developing other forms of artificial enhancement of Pokémon besides Shadowing, but she hasn't gotten very far. Whether she's deliberately sabotaging her work or not is a matter of much debate among her higher ups.

Pokémon:

Name:    Peeta   

Gender: Male

Species: Audino

Bio: Cecilia's first mon, upon induction into Cipher, it helped her in her research, healing mons that were wounded in the process of testing. After her first major infraction, Peeta was taken from her and    Shadowed   . Cecilia has since kept her mouth shut most of the time, and now researches ways to reverse the Shadowing process more covertly.

Name: Katniss

Species: Remoraid

Gender: Female

Bio: Katniss was caught at around the same time Peeta was. She had a crush on a Mantine named Gale, but after being taken, she was frequently chased by Peeta. She eventually decided she had feelings for him too. Unfortunately, it was after Peeta was Shadowed. Now, Peeta attacks Katniss every time he sees her. Katniss mostly helps keep Cecilia cheered up these days, lacking any appendages to help her with her research.
