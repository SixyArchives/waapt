21828 Umbramatic24th Jul 2014 06:58:26 PM from WAAPT or PEFE Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Hoping Senpai notices me
Admit it, you didn't see the avvy at first
This is gonna look kinda bad after what Zeal posted, but here goes.

You know that scene in one of the Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy books where they show the vastness of the universe and then a microscopic dot on a microscopic dot saying "You Are Here?"

That's how I usually feel on the Internet.

I rarely feel like I'm contributing anything in most online discussions. My posts usually get passed over. And wiki edits I make are usually overwritten or deleted. In short, I feel insignificant, and I usually leave most communities out of shame for being such a noob.

The only exception is usually when I write a story, and that's part of why I write stories a lot. This led to me joining PEFE, where I felt I got some attention.

Then from there I joined WAAPT, and from there things changed.

Sure, I still feel my characters and posts are ignored a bit too much sometimes. But as for the community itself I genuinely feel for the most part like I'm among a circle of friends, and that's amazing to me. This RP's community is one of the only ones where I truly feel like I belong, and I really appreciate that.

Then there's the RP itself. It's a thoroughly enjoyable, awesome, wacky, stupid-in-a-good-way ride, and I have immense fun doing crazy shit with my characters and following the antics of everyone else's. I actually have very few true regrets about my R Ping (though there are several things I do wish I did a bit differently), all because it's thoroughly for fun, and I love that.

But all good things come to an end, and that is why I have decided Hoenn-2 will be my last full arc in this RP.

I know, I know, most of you are wondering "What?! Why?!" I assure you, it has nothing to do with my problems with this RP or its community or anyone in it. (I actually don't have any real big problems with the RP itself.) I made this decision with a heavy heart, and I will dearly miss the RP, its community, and working with my characters.

The reasons for this decision are as follows:

  • It'll be a nice way to bookend my experience: I started with Hoenn and will end with Hoenn-2.

  • I want to grow and expand as a writer; I've got plenty of non-WAAPT story ideas, both fanfic and non-fanfic, and I want to be able to focus on those.

  • With school and work pressures increasing I'd rather leave the RP on my own terms than be forced out by RL.

  • As Tracer said when he announced his retirement (and this is part of why I was so burnt out earlier this year), I've told most of the stories I wanted to with this RP- there are only a few left.

I was initially holding off on retiring due to certain dangling subplots; however, I now have workarounds to show the conclusions to those, and they'll be shown in my retirement post along with everything else.

But don't worry, I won't be gone completely! I'll still be around to:

  • Occasionally say hi in the chats and Discussion, and show you guys things I'm working on.

  • Post one-off posts in the main thread and the second forum, as well as wrapping up dangling subplots in the latter. Not to mention occasional cameos on request!

  • Watch cool shit in Watch-This.

  • Help newbies in Signups.

  • Be 100% PM-or-I Mable as always.

  • Be first in line to get a backup copy or archive of WAAPT once it all ends.

Additionally, I'll be making nods to WAAPT in my future stories, so look out for those!

Also, there's a caveat to the "end after Hoenn-2" thing. Most of you probably remember I got hit by SAD hard earlier this year, and I still have mental scars from it to this day. Hoenn-2's happening around the same time of year, and if it happens again I'll take it easy for the arc (and possibly avoid the chat) and save most of my wrap-up for months where I'll have the energy and emotional stability to do it.

Regardless, I intend to have my retirement post up by the middle of next year, even if certain plans have to be saved for post-retirement oneshot material.

Also, I do NOT want this post to start a stream of other retirement announcements. I thought long and hard about this decision, and I did not decide lightly.

Thank you all for listening. It's been great R Ping with you all.

(Stay tuned for a post detailing what I'm doing with my NP Cs.)
