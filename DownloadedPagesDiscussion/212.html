Well, there are a couple of issues with balance that could get problematic at some point...

This arc was more or less designed to favour Souls, which while not a problem by itself does call attention to the numbers. It did present an incentive for people to (want to) make their characters Souls, which adds to their number in the wake of some players of other Attributes leaving, without much of an explanation beyond "it'll help me move in the twilit". And it is an Attribute-focused arc.

I'm more interested in an evening out process than in the Attributes being actually even, so it's no like I want Soul Attributes to be removed, I simply want the option to be available.

Things like: promoting arcs focused (or not) on certain Attributes, depending on whether we want people to make their characters hearts/souls/minds just because or not, and not leaving people who don't have Attributes at an obvious disadvantage; also defining their powers more clearly, for example, Hearts are supposed to be more emphatic, but whatever usable power they would get from that, which would essentially be The Force, is taken by Souls getting heightened Aura sensitivity; and Minds understanding Kyurem is a bit... situational.

As for Discussion usage, nothing to comment really. I mean, I usually fastforward through most offtopic post, which only causes a problem when I realize I have to reel back to find where did the subject change again. And I'm happy and envious <_< that people can travel to other countries...
