Basically Madoka, but with less freaky paper mache monsters.

The WAAPT version of this will have the NPC's and such have a single Pokemon, as well as reconfiguring some of the monsters into more Pokemon-esque forms. I'll even mix up some game mechanics while I'm at it (Sif with Intimidate wooooo).
