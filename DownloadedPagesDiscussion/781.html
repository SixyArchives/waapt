Evening.
So, there has been a degree of tension lately between people who are struggling with the Kalos arc and people who are enjoying.

Let's make a compromise. People who are struggling with Kalos, avoid defaulting to complaints during slow days. Having concerns is fine, but filibusters waste everybody's time and spoil the mood. In that vein, don't complain repeatedly or without any point. Instead work on coming up with something to make the arc more interesting for you, whatever that happens to entail (obviously within the realm of sense*). Meanwhile, to be fair, people who are enjoying Kalos, avoid shooting down any ideas based solely on not fitting your perception of the arc, in order to facilitate the development of an arc that people will enjoy more.

*Sense does not necessarily refer to everything being perfectly logical and realistic. This is Pokemon, this is WAAPT.

tl;dr: "Don't complain about arcs, do feel free to take initiative on creating plot-of-the-days when things feel too slow for you. In addition, don't complain about people who dislike the arcs, try and help them find what to do, or keep silent."
