    • Vierr learned of his affinity for aura at a very young age; it's kinda hard not to notice tiny black or purple sparks breaking stuff when you get angry. But no matter how hard he tries, he can only manage tiny bits, most of which occur accidentally. Having gotten frustrated with his failures and remotely breaking a potted plant out of frustration, he gave up and set out from home to go down to Kanto so as to finally be able to become a trainer.
