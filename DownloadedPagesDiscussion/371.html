So... Being somewhat out of the loop on matters... There was a few things that I wished to clarify regarding RtAU...

Atmosphere on a Per-Region Basis

PLEASE KEEP IN MIND WHEN PLANNING YOUR RTAU ACTIVITIES! IF THERE IS A CONFLICT, PLEASE BRING IT UP SOONER RATHER THAN LATER!

  • Kanto / Johto - Dead Serious (or at least what passes for serious in this inherently anarchic and goofy RP. :P) These regions are currently planned on seeing every single nasty trick in the book the 7 jerks have come up with for their antics. If you run in guns blazing here, odds are you will break things more than you will make them better.
  • Sinnoh - Action Romp. Place is kinda depopulated anyways. Locals may or may not be undermotivated for fighting given the still-fragile nature of Sinnoh's biosphere.
  • Hoenn - Comedic Relief. 1/3 of all Hoennites become Draconic. This is the region where every attempt to apply the same conquering shtick as in other regions falls hilariously flat and never gets more than a couple hours' worth of meaningful traction. (They still nominally "conquer", just never are able to meaningfully assert force.)
  • Unova - Schizophrenic. Things vary heavily by region. There are two main camps here. One is intended to be a camp lead primarily by Hydreigon going out of their way attempting to be Villains With Good Publicity and failing in amusing manners. The other camp can be played as serious (or not) as desired.

  • Any town with Legendaries within consistent spitting distance of it (e.x. Ecruteak, Icirrus) is likely well out of the ability of all but the suicidal to attempt to conquer thanks to centuries' worth of clauses built around the protection of settlements that in all likelihood arose at least in part as shrine complexes.

7JD Military Organization

Does Crewe + Straw's organization represent a unified force? Or are they closer to leaders of a "theater" of troops. If anyone else wishes to play a NPC with ties to this group, how should integration be pursued?

Contemporary AU!Unova (read: What's Going on Now)
