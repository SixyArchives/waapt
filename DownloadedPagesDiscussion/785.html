New character bio! I've been working on this all day! Any thoughts?

Name: Jaxon Armstrong

Age: 18

Gender: Male

Description: Jaxon is 6 feet tall, and has a thin build. His eyes are a deep brown and lie behind a pair of thick glasses. His hair is a similar brown to his eyes, and he's covered in freckles. He is rather pale, and typically dresses semi-formally.

Organizational Ties: He's a card-carrying member of the New Sun Associates Group, an online scientific community contributed to by a select few youth. It's more of a hobby than anything of pressing importance.

Place of Origin: Cherrygrove City, Johto.

Backstory: Jaxon is what ome could call a child prodigy. He always winds up at the top of his class, and almost seems bored with his studies sometimes. He taught himself C++ at age 10. His IQ registers at 166. But he is not without his flaws. Jaxon's personality is often very dry and serious. He hates crowds and public speaking, and is a bit of a neat freak, almost to an obsessive point. Among his social anxieties and borderline OCD, Jaxon also has intense claustrophobia as a result of a childhood incident with a cave-in. Jaxon studied under Professor Elm as an intern for three years, and was inspired to try to become a Professor himself. So, he set out in the world to explore and learn. His goal is to catch, observe, and study every type of Pokemon.

Mons

Ito (Feraligatr): Ito is Jaxon's very first Pokemon, and his faithful scientific assistant. He is named after famous Japanese scientist Keisuke Ito. Ito will do whatever it takes to help Jaxon in his endeavors, and bears a similar scientific curiosity to his Trainer.

Ability: Torrent

A.A. (Corsola) : Named after Albert Abraham Michelson, the famous scientist who calculated the speed of light, A.A. is a close companion of Jaxon. She is very useful in times of panic or stress, as she always knows how to calm her Trainer down. She is a very gentle, caring individual, and knows how to brighten anyone's day.

Ability: Natural Cure

Shellshine (Shiny East Sea Shellos) : Shellshine is the only Shiny Pokemon Jaxon has encountered. Jaxon tends to be very protective and overbearing of Shellshine, and frequently finds himself studying her in attempts to solve the mystery of Shiny Pokemon. Shellshine is very restless and hates being intruded upon.

Ability: Storm Drain

Plato (Inkay): Inkay is a fellow intellectual, but unlike Jaxon, Plato is more of a philosophical, subjective thinker than Jaxon's scientific, objective realm of thought. Plato is prone to using big words, and is fond of poetry.

Ability: Contrary

Keio (Krookodile): Keio is Jaxon's battling pride and joy- a real powerhouse through and through. She loves to fight, and she'll challenge anyone who gets in her way.

Ability: Moxie

Crick (Mightyena): Named after molecular biologist Francis Crick, Crick is a fun-loving, energetic optimist Pokemon. He is a loyal team member, and although he may seem sweet and nice, when Jaxon says the word, Crick can become your worst nightmare.

Ability: Intimidate
