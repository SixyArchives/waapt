I'm reminded that I did write a bio for Lucanus a few dozen pages back or so, but I can't be bothered to dig it up and it was missing a bunch of information, so I'll just post a new one here.

Ancestor!Herbert

  • Name: Sir Lucanus Cain
  • Age: Early twenties
  • Appearance: Lucanus is very much an Identical Ancestor. He sports the same black hair, grey eyes and high cheekbones as Herbert, and stood side to side the only difference between the two would be a couple of inches in height. In battle he wears a suit of yellow-tinged knight's plate armour, whilst off the field he prefers a doublet and breeches. He generally looks ridiculous, is what I'm trying to say.
  • Kingdom: His actual homeland is unknown, though given his rather European mannerisms he may well be a native of Kalos. In Ransei, he serves as the new Warlord of Pugilis.
  • Personality: Lucanus is an idealist to the core. He believes wholeheartedly in ideas such as chivalry and honour, and the very idea of fighting dirty is difficult for him to comprehend. Youthful, naive and from a noble upbringing, he has almost no idea how to lead an army or fight a war, and is more interested in holding tourneys and catching the eye of noble ladies. Thankfully, his Alakazam, Revan, helps to keep him grounded and deal with some of the more logistical aspects of being a Warlord.
  • History: An exceptionally gifted Auric (though one with more potential talent than actual skill), Lucanus quickly rose to become leader of an exploratory force to conquer new lands in the king's name. His small army established themselves Pugilis but had neither the lands nor the forces to fight a war - however, the former Warlord of Pugilis agreed to concede his position to Lucanus, in exchange for Lucanus vowing to destroy the Ghost Lord. To Lucanus, this is merely another noble quest for bards to sing about hundreds of years later.
  • Perfect Link: Abra line
  • Warrior Skill: Resolution
  • Weapon of Choice: A relatively recently-forged bastard sword with absolutely no unusual properties, Auric or otherwise.

Let me know if I'm missing anything or if something seems out of order.
