6972 BadWolf212nd Aug 2012 12:14:23 PM Get RP Mod
The Watcher on the Wall
Hmm...I'm not sure I agree with what is and is not required on that sheet. I'd prefer:

  • Name (required)
  • Age (required, approximate is good enough)
  • Gender (required)
  • Appearance (required)
  • Home Region (required)
  • Team (required: species, gender, nickname (if applicable), starter; optional: other background information)
  • Basic character personality and history (required)

It should be obvious why we need name, age, gender, and appearance. These are major factors for interacting with people. People will react differently to a six foot six, 30 year old strongman than they will to a four foot nine, eleven year old girl.

Home Region is just a personal interest. Also, it's one of the things that gets kept track of on the UND, so having it early is helpful.

This being a Pokemon RP, of course we have to know what Pokemon new players intend to have ahead of time. However, giving a background story to each of them in the sign-up sheet is overkill. That's what the RP itself is for. But since a person's starter will often say a lot about them, that should be required information.

Finally, the character background and personality are to give an idea about the direction the player is planning to go with their character. Again, it should be obvious why this is important.

Any quirks in characterization should also be mentioned during the sign-up process, but there's no reason to put them in the sign-up sheet. For example, if a new player wants one of their 'mons to be a Delta, they would have to bring it up during the (for lack of a better term) "interview process".
