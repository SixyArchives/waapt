So, Illusion character time!

  • Name: Isuka (Real Name Unknown)
  • Age: Either late teens or early twenties
  • Gender: Female
  • Appearance: Long, flowing black hair, green eyes, tanned skin, a little bit short and has a slender body. Her attire changes depending on her mood, when she's not wearing a silver suit of course.
  • Organization: Illusion
  • Region of Origin: Sinnoh
  • Backstory: Isuka is a girl who loves to live in a intense way, often putting herself at risk just for the thrill of it. That comes from living a mostly boring life in Solaceon Town where she thought she wasn't living to her full potential, eventually deciding to embark in her own journey for the thrill of it. She hates being left in the dark and is a bit of a fashionista, despite being essentially a stuntwoman, she's a firm believer in Kicking Ass in All Her Finery. She's also very affable and treats everything in a very casual way as well as being a bit of a party animal. She has a deep adoration for Steel types and respects anyone who trains them, especially the Scyther line, which she seems to love to the point of squeeing, despite Scyther not even being Steel type.

  • Nickname: Intel
  • Species: Metagross
  • Gender: Identifies as male
  • Ability: Clear Body
  • Bio: Isuka's starter and Hyper Competent Sidekick. They're rarely seen apart and it's not unusual for Isuka to be riding on him. Intel mostly makes sure his trainer doesn't forget anything important and acts as an information database in case she needs help with something she doesn't quite understand. He's very protective of her and usually doesn't make any effort to interact with others mostly due to looking down upon them and just doesn't consider others worth of his time. If you do manage to befriend him, he's actually quite helpful and affable, not afraid of making jokes at others expense.

  • Nickname: Baccarat
  • Species: Chandelure
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Infiltrator
  • Bio: Baccarat is very short-tempered and easily offended, being quick to start fights. At first glance he acts calm and has a very Faux Affably Evil atittude, but once he blows up he's very quick to start setting everything on fire. He's arrogant, but not above asking for help in a bad situation and often makes a fool out of himself by overeacting over slight problems or just being easy to pick on, not that you should.

  • Nickname: Durandal
  • Species: Aegislash
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Stance Change
  • Bio: More often than not, if Intel isn't with Isuka, then Durandal is. He's very devoted to his trainer and is not afraid of putting himself in harm's way to protect her. He doesn't take it lightly to others insulting his mistress, but for the most part he behaves well, though he can get very passive-aggressive when provoked. Outside of that, he's very sociable, having a noble personality and not being afraid of calling Isuka out when she's acting particularly rude.
