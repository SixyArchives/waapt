Okay.

We're pretty much scattered throughout two worlds right now. They are refered to as PMD A and PMD B. PMD A is the game universe, while PMD B is the future of an AU where everyone is a Pokemon. In PMD A, there are some of us hanging around Treasure Town, others are doing Kirby or Fire Emblem miniarcs. In PMD B, people are exploring the world or participating in a Portal 2 miniarc. To get to the PMD, you either find a Space time shard in the bormal world, or accidently end up there from the entralink. You end up as your Pokeform, and end up able to understand pokespeak.
