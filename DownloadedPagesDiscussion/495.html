12353 PippingFool25th Jun 2013 05:25:19 AM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

At Ruler of Ignis

Power play in Ignis is... complicated. Technically Ryouku (Ancestor!Vyvyan) is the ruler while Izmui (Ancestor!Fool) is a consort. The reality is is that Ryoku is too wrapped up in his own ambitions and plannings to actually rule and his wife picks up the slack.

Since everyone else is doing those neat Bios. May as well do 2.

Ancestor!Fool

  • Name: Izumi Tomoe (Native name Keiko)
  • Age: 26
  • Appearance: She is medium-tall in height, has long curling that falls down to her waist that she adorns with Hibiscus-esque flowers, shells and Chatot feathers and wears sandals and long, flowing blue kimonos with water and sea based patterns on them. She has rather soft and rounded features and sharp teal eyes that watch everything. She also wears an ancient looking pendant that she claims was passed down to her from her mother. Her Rank 2 form, on the other hand, is based off traditional Maori clothing with a Hydriegon theme to them and she is covered in traditional Polynesian tattoos.
  • Place of Origin: An atoll not far from the Hoenn region. Known to the inhabitants as "Wairuata Moku 'aina" (Shadow/Spirit Island)
  • Kingdom: Wife of the Warlord of Ignis.
  • Personality: Izumi is a quiet and reserved individual who doesn't normally talk to others because she is of the belief that they're not worth the time it takes to talk to them. She has a love/hate relationship with her husband who she constantly belittles, berates and snarks back to constantly. yet when the chips are down, she will vehemently defend her partner to the bitter end, even if she sees him as a unfit ruler + blithering idiot. Her relative aloofness and misanthropy leaves her with few friends, but her chambermaids are deathly loyal to her and she respects them back. The only thing she willing tells people she loves is the atoll she comes from, which is represented via the necklace that was given to her by her mother.
  • History: The daughter of an Island Chief on a atoll southeast of the Hoenn Region. Izumi was captured when the Island was hostilely taken over by Ignis merchants looking to set up a trade post on the Hoenn Trade Route. From here she was sold to be a concubine to the heir to the Ignis throne due to her exotic nature. From her position as a concubine, she was able to use her wits, charm and manipulative ability to become the consort of the Ignis throne. Her capture from her beloved atoll left her bitter and angry towards others which is why she prefers spending her time hunting alone or playing with her pet Simipour than she does interacting with other people. She wears an old heirloom that was passed down for generations and was given to her by her mother. Due to the fact that it reminds her of both her homeland and her mother she wears it constantly, never taking it off.
  • Perfect Link: (Seemingly) Panpour line, Deino line
  • Warrior Skill: Grace (Stage 1)/Cold Eyes (Stage 2)
  • Weapon of Choice: Composite Bow

Ancestor!Vyvyan

  • Name: Ryuku Atakashi
  • Age: 28
  • Appearance: Ryuku is a tall, lean man. He is not overly muscular, but he isn't completely slim. He has jet black hair that sticks up at four random points on his head (correlating to the four fins of Rayquaza). He wears little armor, gauntlets and shoulder pads being of his choice, a brown cape, a short light sandy coloured tank top and low hanging carnelian harem pants. Stage 2 is straight up Samurai garb with a Rayquaza theme. He also has a cane adorned with a Jade Rayquaza that he believes has magic powers.
  • Kingdom: Ignis Warlord.
  • Personality: Ryuku is rambunctious, spoiled and vain. The sole child of the last warlord of Ignis, he was spoiled beyond belief. He's a bit obsessive over Rayquaza and devotes his time to the study of how to Link with the beast. Despite seeming to notice her, he cares deeply for his wife and loves her more than anything in Ransei. 'Cept Rayquaza.
  • History:When he inherited the throne, he initially expanded on his fathers ideas and plans for both continental expansion, especially a planned invasion of the long term rival kingdom of Spectra and an expanded trade empire. After a year or so, he became infatuated with the power of legendary Pokemon, especially that of Rayquaza, and so he secluded himself to his studies. It was during this time that his control on the country started slip, and Ignis start going into a state of anarchy until Izumi stepped in and started bringing back order to the country with a repressive iron fist. He's fond of games, and often takes breaks from his studies and plans to play games of Hanafuda and Go.
  • Perfect Link: Torkaol line.
  • Warrior Skill: Bewilder (Stage 1)/Desire(Stage 2)
  • Weapon of Choice: Cane kind, Tekpi/Sai
