Return of the Brilliant Thief

Alright, since I'm gonna have some time to phonetype this out, I might as well keep you guys abreast to the most recent draft of RotBT. The beginning, at least. And no spoilery bits.

My current plan is to start off with hints as to some planned misdeeds as early as Petalburg, though nothing that would normally grab people's attention. Just some announcements of a new, much more detailed model of the SS Anne being revealed at the Nautical Museum in Slateport. By the time the group reaches Rustboro, however, rumors will begin to spread that the reveal has been delayed. These rumors get stronger and more believable as the group gets closer to Slateport itself, but (partial because Reality Ensues is fun and partly to keep people from rushing to Slateport and thus breaking up the group) the Museum curator will outright deny anyone coming to ask questions or offer help, mostly in an effort to cover his own ass. So don't try and jump any guns. By the time that the group reaches Slateport, though, he'll finally cave and admit that the model of the SS Anne has indeed been stolen, officially kicking off the plot and you guys get to try and piece the clues together to find out where the culprit went with the quite large model, how they perpetrated the crime, who they are, and why they stole that one particular item.

But, as I mentioned before, you will be timed at an interval of my discretion. Don't expect to even have half of the questions from a crime scene answered before another occurs one town ahead. However, I do encourage resourceful thinking and clever planning, and will reward it thusly. Even if it turns out you were completely wrong, I'll still throw you a hint nevertheless, so brainstorm away, people! If you manage to pick up the pattern and get ahead of the thief, you can even stop it before or during the actual theft, earning yourselves a very grateful assistant NPC in your hunt.

Also, as a last bit, because of how intensive this plot is likely to be for me, Dune will NOT be doing a Gym Run, and Duniree is going to be a bit Out of Focus.
Go.
