 23410 Corvus Atrox, Sun, 26th Oct '14 9:41:24 AM from a Jolly Caucus Race Get RP Mod Relationship Status: Don't hug me; I'm scared
Okay, I thought of this over a year ago, but it seems to have changed a lot and since I'm probably going to do it in Hoenn-2 I'll try and put it all in one place.

The Hundred Thousand Year Door Arc

What is it?

A miniarc based on the events of the game Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door.

Why 'Hundred Thousand'?

It fits better with the bits of the RP timeline I'm trying to link it with.

When is it taking place?

During the Hoenn-2 arc, at least chronologically. Depending on my availability, it may extend past that for actually writing it.

Basic Primer?

Sometime after getting to Hoenn, Maggie's now-Emboar, Squealer, will wander over to Slateport and find a Magical Map. Sometime after this, Squealer will suddenly disappear, the only clues to find him are the Magical Map, the Hundred Thousand Year Door, and the Crystal Stars it apparently leads to, about half of which are in The Dream World.

Dream World?

Yeah. Some of the plots would be quite distruptive to the area they're in and I think this is the best solution which keeps everyone in the vicinity of Hoenn, but hopefully doesn't derail things for the most part. That, and the Dreamstalk helps with the getting humans there bit and maybe the focusing more on it in general.

You mentioned Slateport a lot?

Yeah. Ideally, Slateport will be taking the place of Rogueport in this, which would mean for the purposes of the plot, it would need to have the Hundred Thousand Year Door, what it leads to, and probably an explorable sewer system underneath it. Other than that and a few other buildings needed for plot purposes, though, people can react to or ignore things at their leisure. (Save the sky around Slateport and possibly Hoenn as a whole growing dark at some point, but I'll try and make sure that doesn't derail anything.)

What about the Crystal Stars? Are they going to end up as another super-powerful artifact?

No. For reasons which will be explained in-universe, the Crystal Stars will only be able to use their powers in specific circumstances, which shouldn't make much more than glorified door keys in the J-Team's hands.

How closely will this be following the game's plot?

There'll be some changes to make it fit better and some just because, otherwise, it's up to you. If everyone wants it to, it'll flow almost exactly like the game, but if people do something to get to the goal that you couldn't do in the game, that's fine too, though it may affect things down the road.

Are you going to need any help with running this?

Possibly. School's not going to make it easy, but running it on my own shouldn't be a problem if it doesn't matter when it finishes. It would certainly be appreciated, though.

Can I offer suggestions for how to handle certain things?

If you want to, sure.

What if no one else wants to participate?

Well, a fair amount of stuff I wanted to do is intertwined with this, so I'd probably do it on my own and Fanfic Mode some highlights, but I'd much rather do it with others.

You're not giving much details for a game that's been out for 10 years already.

Yeah, just want to make sure for the people who haven't and I'd like some of the ways I integrated it to be a surprise. (Though I can explain via PM if there's concern.)

Anyway, that probably had far too many subpoints, but I hope that covered everything.

Thoughts? Concerns?
