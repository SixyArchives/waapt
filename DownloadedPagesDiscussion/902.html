She initially got her piercings in an attempt to change her classmates' perception of her. It didn't work, but it turned out she really likes them.
Pokemon: None.

Wait, what?

"But Wolf, " you're asking me right now (don't deny it), "this is a Pokemon RP! How can she be part of a Pokemon RP without any Pokemon?"

"Well, " I respond, "she doesn't have any Pokemon, yet."

You see, Danni is a brand new player character that was created to take over a concept that was initially planned for Katie. The initial idea was that during my absence, while Wolf was living through BioShock Infinite, Katie was also doing a game-inspired arc. That game? Pokemon Red. I liked the idea so much that I dropped it as what she did while away and was going to save it for a later story with her.

Earlier today, I realized two things. First, that if I did this with Katie, she'd have to essentially reboot her entire team, which I've already done with Wolf. Second, that I haven't started LeafGreen yet. If I created a new character, I could do an in-RP Let's Play of Pokemon LeafGreen. Her design comes from an appearance idea I was kicking around for Serena in my X file. I have no idea where I plucked the name Danni from, so don't ask because there isn't a satisfying answer.

Not only could she do LeafGreen, but SoulSilver, Alpha Sapphire (which is what I'm currently leaning toward), and all the other main games up through X (and beyond, if I'm slow). Some of the stories will obviously need to be changed a bit, as some of the events in them have already transpired in the RP, but I will worry about that when I get past the point where the story is more substantive than "Fight Team Rocket, fight Team Rocket again, meet Lugia."

This idea was born of a desire to get back to basics. Since she will not be interacting with the J-Team at all until at least Hoenn, Danni will not speak or understand any monese, she will not encounter any magical artifacts (that aren't part of the plot, anyway), she won't be spirited off to alternate universes or subplots inspired by non-Pokemon video games, and she won't know her Pokeform (or even know that that's a thing). Even though she won't be interacting with the Team, I would like her to have a connection to them. Specifically, I think it would be cool if she was the first-cousin of another player character. If you're willing for Danni to be part of your family (whether your character knows she exists or not) let me know!

Because of the style of this adventure, a lot of it will, by necessity, be done solo. It will probably be slow going, since I have to play the games, and take copious notes so that Danni's experience in the RP is as close to mine in the game as possible. And although I'm hoping to keep a buffer between my progress in the game and Danni's, there may be points where we just don't see her for a while because I haven't been able to play recently. However, I will need your help (yes, you) for certain aspects. Like Gym battles! And plot battles! Actually, it would be almost entirely Gym and plot battles. Since the goal is to be as game-accurate as possible, the attacks and outcomes of these battles would be scripted, but dialogue and characterization are open. I would like you guys (one at a time, obviously) to write and edit these battles with me as spaghetti-posts.

And I think that's just about everything. Special thanks to Crewe for coming up with Danni's last name, as I was completely stumped. And thanks to everyone else who gave me ideas as well.

For reading through all that, here's a special treat. In addition to the pic of Danni, I have also created pictures of Wolf, Katie, and Faith. These are their new (and in Faith's case, first) canonical appearances. The clothes can be taken as Broad Strokes due to limited options (Wolf's coat is missing the yellow accents, and Faith's clothing and jewellery in general is not quite right), but they're otherwise correct. Hope you like them!
