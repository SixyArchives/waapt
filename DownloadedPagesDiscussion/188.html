Those of us who want to dedicate to working the RP should not have to feel like we are chasing other people out, that's a true thing. We still have the basic issue that people who want to discuss RP stuff either have to ask and wait an eternity because their question sinks in a sea of overused memes, references to YOUR FACE and KH fanfic smut in the worst case, or barge their way in, force an OT and see as the rest of the people simply get out.

Redirecting natter to a third chatroom seems to be the best idea. If we want to develop an idea, we should post in Discussion, then use the planning chatroom (whose dynamics certainly help a lot, it's not something Discussion can provide), then bring in results.
