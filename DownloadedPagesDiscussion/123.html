 3059 Bad Wolf 21, Fri, 1st Jul '11 10:07:34 PM Get RP Mod
Hello!
I think they'd retain the memories but not in the sense of "This happened to me. These are my memories."

Here's how I imagine this working. Picture two parallel lines. We're currently travelling along the top line. When the Dialga/Celebi incident occurs, we drop down to the bottom line from our current position. This means that we do not experience the beginning of the bottom line, but since it theoretically happened, we have "memories" of it. When we meet our Constants (again, cribbing the term from Lost) it causes us to recall our real memories from the other timeline. So we'd remember our "past" in the AU, but just as a story, not as experiences. When we jump back up to the top line though, we would have memories of the time we were on the bottom line because it actually happened to us (although because of time shenanigans, we would jump back to a point near where the incident actually happened).

Minds of Crystal aren't affected by jumping the timeline, so we'll have both sets of memories right off the bat.
