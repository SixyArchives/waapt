As for the regions, I have:

  • The Sevii Islands are split into the Northern, Central, and Southern Isles (Knot/Boon/Kin, Floe/Chrono, and Fortune/Quest, respectively).
  • Kanto is Eastern Kanjoh. Johto is Western Kanjoh.
  • Hoenn is split into Mainland Hoenn, and the Hoenn Archipelago.
  • Sinnoh is split into Eastern and Western Sinnoh (with the line being Mt. Coronet, as always), as well as Northern Sinnoh, which encompasses Celestic Town and Snowpoint City (and everything in between). The Resort Area is part of Eastern Sinnoh.
  • Unova is split along its rivers to become Western, Central, and Eastern Unova.
  • Orre is still Orre.
  • Fiore and Oblivia are one region.
  • Almia is its own region.
  • Ransei is split into its 17 countries (one for each type). Note that you might not want to look at a map of Ransei if you're avoiding Conquest spoilers.
  • The Orange Archipelago consists of the North Archipelago, and Southern Archipelago. The border between the two is the line of Deserted-Pummelo-Kumquat-Butwal-Ascorbia Islands. The border belongs to the Northern section.
