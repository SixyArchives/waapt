All the characters? In one place?! Talk about chaotic...

This isn't related to anything, but seeing as I would rather like it if the Sabot Warriors (Cobalion, Virizion and Terrakion) played a bigger role in a future arc (Assuming we return to Unova at one point), I thought about how I would characterise them. And here's what I came up with.

  • Cobalion: The serious one. He acts like a true gentleman both in an out of combat (I.e: referring to Virizion as "My lady"). He also takes everything very seriously and can be considered rather uptight. He tends to clash with Terrakion over this occasionally, thinking that Terrakion should take things more seriously. They do get on rather well though.

  • Virizion: The lady of the group. Virizion is very elegant, which is reflected in her graceful battling style. She doesn't take well to fighting dirty and would never fight that way herself, claiming that "It's no way for a lady to fight." Also, as previously stated, she sees herself as Keldeo's surrogate mother and cares for him very deeply. To the point where she can be a bit overprotective.

  • Terrakion: The funny one. Unlike the other two members of the group, Terrakion is a lot less uptight. He tends to be very loud and constantly cheerful, so it's hard not to get along with him. His battle style is a lot more rough then the other two, preferring to simply take his foes out with brute force and avoiding what he calls "fancy swordplay" altogether. As mentioned previously, he tends to clash with Cobalion occasionally, thinking that it would do him some good to loosen up a bit.

And then there's Keldeo, but you know about him already.
