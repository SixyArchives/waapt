So, considering that we now seem to be planning for the Kalos arc proper - or beginning to, at any rate - I figured I ought to bring up some of my plans for future arcs, just to make sure they're not treading on anybody's toes. A lot of them are vague at this point, so I figure that getting them out in the open might help solidify them somewhat.

It shouldn't come as any reals surprise that most of them concern villains. Specifically, Titan.

Titan's always been in a position where his agenda is kind of nebulous. Sure, he's got an ultimate goal in mind - destroy the world and recreate it in his own image like Cyrus wanted to, since he's got no imagination - but he's never really had a concrete way of going about it. However, now that Cipher is on the verge of collapse, he's in a position to start poking through the ruins and put together his own organisation out of the resources and disassociated thugs that remain.

The result, assuming that people are okay with the idea, will be Neo-Galactic. Unlike Saturn's reformed Galactic, they'd still quite decidedly be an evil team and wouldn't be above using terrorism and other such methods to get their way, but since Titan is the one in charge, they wouldn't be anywhere near Cipher levels of bad. A good portion of their members will be ex-Cipher, though - including some villains we've already seen during Orre that I can't mention for spoiler reasons. Ideally, they'd be fairly major, arc-wide antagonists, but if that idea falls flat then I'm fine with having Titan continue pursuing his goals with a much smaller collection of personnel.

The reason I bring this up now that discussions about legendaries are going on is because, really, is there any better way of destroying the world than using the Destruction Pokemon itself to do it? Attempting to capture and harness Yvetal would make for an ideal arc-wide evil plan for Titan and Neo-Galactic to pursue, since he'd actually have a credible method of achieving his goals in mind.

Problem is, it's not only cliche, but something that can't really be done during Kalos itself, since it doesn't really fit with the whole Lighter and Softer theme. Even if Neo-Galactic do end up being a thing, they're not going to really rear their head in Kalos. Even if they're not a thing, Titan will have an inside operative within Team Flare (whatever they may be doing) but he'll generally be fairly nebulous and offscreen for most of the next couple of arcs. My main Kalos antagonist is going to be a far more comically-oriented Malamar with a massive ego complex.

As for Titan's ultimate fate... my current plans require him to be a villain for at least one more arc, but after that it's all pretty much entirely up in the air. Though I'll admit that I have been steering him in the right direction for a Heel-Face Turn, so I'll just see how things go.

So, I figured I might as well get this all written out now, just so that it's out in the open. Apologies if it's too long or too incoherent, but at this point the only thing keeping me awake is copious amounts of caffeine. I might make some edits in the morning when I'm feeling more coherent. In the meantime, if you have any thoughts, comments, or concerns, let me know.
