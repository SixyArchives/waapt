As much as it pains me to say this, but alas, I have life to consider over the next 10-12 months. As such, I am just putting it out there that my last full arc in the main RP as a player, barring IRL catastrophe - personal or otherwise, will either be RtAU or Unova-2 depending on whether one, both, or none of the two seem like they will be finishable by next May.

Now, to answer a few questions:

Whoa, Whoa, Whoa, You're REALLY Leaving, Tracer?

Yes. I will be "leaving" in the sense that I won't be active in the main RP after one of the above two arcs. A cameo for a couple of days if RL permits, but RtAU or Unova-2 will be the last wild ride that I enjoy in the RP in full. I do intend to continue to make use of the testing thread for oneshots and the like at my convenience. Folks expecting a daily posting habit (in BBCode, no less) will be sorely disappointed.

Wait, But WHY?

Okay, to start with, the drama in the chats since around October was not the main impetus for this. Even if it did help me to take a step back from the RP and evaluate its position relative to the rest of my life... Rather, the main reason why I will be leaving by the time my Junior year ends is more meta.

As much as I've enjoyed my time here as a WAAPTer and being able to interact with your characters and sculpt our wonderful (if slightly insane) rendition of the Pokeverse... I need to grow up eventually. I need to find an internship/job, complete my upper-division courses, start considering whether or not I truly want to be 'forever alone'... You know, starting to do all those unglamorous-sounding but ultimately necessary and rewarding things that we're all going to have to do at some point or another as legal adults.
