Name: Fillet (his real name is Eric though) Gender: Male Region: Azalea Town, Johto Age: ??? (I had the idea of leaving it mysterious as a joke and having cryptic references that make him seem younger or older than everyone) Occupation: Trainer, but hes also had experience being a delivery guy for Silph Co., and several dozen miscellaneous odd jobs.

Appearance: About 57, very skinny and scrawny but with a little bit of muscle (mostly because theres no fat to cover it up), dark Caucasian skin, dark brown hair that almost looks black, brown eyes, bags under his eyes, crooked smile, slouches. Usually wears graphic t-shirts, trademark red jacket, and jeans and sometimes a backwards hat. So yeah, really dorky looking.

Personality: Usually out of it, kind of a cloudcuckoolander. Although not in the sense that hes random and crazy, but because he doesnt pay attention to a lot of things and has weird logic. Usually pretty soft-spoken and chill and a little awkward, but it doesnt take much to get him to become overdramatic, usually when really happy or really angry, waving his arms around and yelling and generally being obnoxious. Very self-depreciatory. Though his exterior doesnt show it hes very into philosophy. He also has a dry, sometimes inappropriate sense of humor.

Backstory: Fillet completed his journey a year or two ago and it was pretty normal compared to most other peoples. Had brief encounters with a few legendaries but that was more chance than anything so really nothing special happened. He conquered the Kanto and Johto Pokmon leagues and has competed in many conferences since then, and his job as a delivery boy sent him to Hoenn, Sinnoh, and Unova, where he caught Pokmon there. (Hes beaten 3 gyms in Hoenn, 6 in Sinnoh, and 6 in Unova but finishing their leagues isnt a priority to him.) Overall, a pretty average life. Trainer Class: Normal trainer, but he has a lot of weird Pokmon so maybe supernerd? Idk.

Pokemon: Boots the Aipom (Male) Nature: Impish Met: Azalea Town, Johto Ball: Poke Fillets first ever Pokmon. Hes a troll and kind of a jerk but is still a good friend (depending on your definition of good). Holds himself on a pedestal compared to the other members of the team. He never evolved and despite being the first Pokmon Fillet ever owned hes not that strong either, mostly relying on shenanigans to win battles. (Usually he doesn't even fight though, just hangs around.)

Wraith the Gengar (Male) Nature: Naughty Met: Pokmon Tower, Kanto (Ghastly) Ball: Great Wraith is a jerk and a troll and likes to scare and mess with people, but really hes a nice guy deep down when you get to know him, and hes one of the strongest and smartest Pokmon on the team. New Pokmon tend to hate him and older ones really like him. Hes a total bro, pretty much.

Latula the Flygon (Female) Nature: Adamant Met: Route 111 (Hoenn) Ball: Great Captured as a mischevious Trapinch, Latula likes to do things her own way and don't take nothing from nobody! She's stubborn and loud and boisterous and acts like a gamer girl coolkid. Fillet uses her for flying around a lot. She's friendly with all the other Pokemon but gets on their nerves quickly. Nevertheless she's a skilled fighter.

Slim Shady (Slim) the Scrafty (Male) Nature: Bold Met: Route 4 (Unova) Ball: Great Slim was captured on Route 4 and resented it ever sense. He has a gigantic ego and believes himself to be the strongest, smartest, and most handsome Pokemon on the team. He's street smart but very book dumb. He's also very quick to break down under pressure and start crying. He's very loud and insults everybody, and tends to get Fillet in trouble because he likes stealing things and likes to try and beat up innocent people. Thug life yo

Yo-yo the Gliscor (Male) Nature: Quirky Met: Route 206 (Sinnoh) Ball: Ultra Yo-yo (real name withheld because he hates it) was caught on Route 206. Fillet set his stuff down to sleep there overnight and upon waking realized his bag was missing. A Gligar had found it and was eating his food, and Fillet chased him for half the day until Gligar released one of his other Pokemon who beat him into submission. Fillet then captured him. He's treated as a butt monkey, usually, and is very immature and afraid of screwing up. Likes to hug people. He's also a big eater and extreme omnivore, some traits inherited from his buddy Taft the Snorlax. He's a fairly skilled battler, just immature and klutzy.

Rapunzel the Tangrowth (Female) Nature: Bashful Met: Route 21 (Kanto) Ball: Ultra Caught as a Tangela on Route 21 while Fillet journeyed in Kanto. Her first battle was against Blaine, who she had a type disadvantage to, and another Pokemon on the Kanto team constantly made fun of her for being ugly, which was not great for her self-esteem. She is a total nerd and likes anime and other dorky stuff and constantly geeks out over it. She is, however, good friends with Yo-yo and some other "misfits" on Fillet's team. One of Fillet's top battlers (at least top 5), even with her flaws. She's a quick thinker and is good at making up strategies in the heat of battle.

Like 30 other Pokemon kept in the box, too.
