So, since Conquest is coming up, and we've already had an incident with Warrior Transformations, I figured I'd clear up some things...

Warrior transformations are when a Warrior or warlord undergoes a change in armor/outfit. This occurs after a strong bond with their perfect link happens. This usually involves a power increase. This is a permanent change, much like evolution.

What they are not are mid-battle powerups one can use to get an advantage. Those are Warrior Skills. Transformations should not be used like this. And they certainly shouldn't be used without justification, since random warrior 12 suddenly transforming makes no sense without an established stong bond.
