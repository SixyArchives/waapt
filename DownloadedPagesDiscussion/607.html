So, I came up with some other Shadow attacks, since the existing ones are mostly physical oriented. Though, there are a few physical ones.

Shadow Scythe

The User coats its arms/wings or tail in a shadowy aura, and slashes the opponent with it.
  • BP: 90
  • Accuracy: 90
  • Basically a more fitting move for Pokemon like Gallade and Skarmory.

Shadow Wind

The user summons a vicious wind of shadow aura to attack the foe. Hits all opponents. Doubles in power when Shadow Sky is in effect.
  • BP: 65
  • Accuracy: 100

Shadow Sonic

The user uses its voice to make a horrible-sounding noise that may lower Sp. Def. Negated by Soundproof.
  • BP: 70
  • Accuracy: 100

Shadow Rage

The user uses a portion of their HP to increase its Attack two levels, and increase Speed by one level, while harshly lowering its Defense.

Shadow Bullet

The user shoots forward and strikes the foe with an shadowy tackle. This attack always strikes first.
  • BP: 40
  • Accuracy: 100

Shadow Infection

The user bites down into the foe with shadow aura coated teeth to inflict Confusion and lower Defense.
  • BP: 25
  • Accuracy: 85
