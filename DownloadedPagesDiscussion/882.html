Switching gears to a personal matter

Okay, after some internal deliberation, I'm going to jump on Verax and Dune's bandwagon and launch a Renscape side-plotline.

Back Story and other info

As some of you might remember, Ren and Jacob did a mindscape swap a few months ago, during which, Jacob defeated - but not destroyed - Ren's negative aspect, Horror. Due to Horror being defeated - and how everyone thinks him dead - two new aspects were "hired" to take over Horror's job; A female aspect that looks like Rena (i.e. Fem!Ren) dressed like this lovely lady◊ and a male aspect based on Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde. The reason for two hires that differ from the normal them of Ren's mindscape is to one, divide the responsibilities and power associated with horror to make the weaker; and two, and make theme harder to corrupt. However, with the addition of these two aspects, Ren's mindscape expands to include new areas and safeguards based on ideas and themes from White Knight Chronicles. (Don't worry if you haven't played any of the games, I'm not bringing in any of the stories from them. Just some Locales, items, and plot points)

Plot idea

Since Horror was only mostly dead (and still hasn't given up being the dominant aspect of Ren's mind), he decides to use the new Aspects hire to replace him - Sorceress and Jekyll - to get what he wants. Fortunately, six safeguards were created when Sorceress and Jekyll where hired, the six Knight Arks. The Arks allow for characters to transform into 20 to 30 foot (7 to 10 meters) tall Incorupti Knights to defeat Horror and his dark legions.

Info on the Knights

Each of the Arks will bind themselves to one human, mon, or aspect at a time, making the bonded entity a "Pact Maker". As a Pact Maker can then transform at will into the Knight of the Ark they are bonded to. Five of the Knights have set appearances and abilities, although a Knight's weapon might adjust its form a bit based on the Pact Maker. The sixth Knight, the Arc Knight's (or Avatar Knight) form and abilities are fully based upon the Pact Maker (There are some base abilities that remain constant). While knights are exceedingly powerful, they put a constant strain on the user's stamina and mind. Overuse of a Knight can lead to permanent injury, insanity, or even death.

Info on Pact Makers

Any character can only be a Pact Maker to any one Knight at a time. While it is possible for multiple Pact Makers to be bonded to a single Knight, it is not advisable for more than one Pact Maker to invoke the same Knight at the same time. Being a Pact Maker is generally a permanent thing, so under most circumstances if a character becomes a Pact Maker you are stuck with the Knight you bonded with. However, a Pact Maker can change if the current Pact Maker of an Ark dies, Leaves Ren's Mindscape, or Willingly relinquishes the title of Pact Maker. Becoming a Pact Maker is difficult as each Ark is protected by a their own "security system", which any potential Pact Maker must overcome to be "deemed worthy of a pact".

Also, to prevent any sort of power creep or abuse of the Arks, the Arks can not be carried out of Ren's mind

Conclusion

So with six Arks to collect and a definite Big Bad at the end, This can last a few mind dives.

Any thoughts and/ or constructive criticisms?
