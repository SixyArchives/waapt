Depends on what you mean about working them into scenes. There are a couple ways to handle that IMO...

  • Work on having multiple characters be part of the same conversation

  • Try and get parallel conversations going with your mons

  • Try and have your mons approach people instead of just your P Cs

  • Try making a longpost with two of your mons talking to each other on occasion.

  • Work on making subplots for your mons
