 16612 Luke 9924, Sun, 17th Nov '13 2:58:08 PM from England Get RP Mod
Lemme' tell you a story, kids.

Now this old man joined in back on page a hundred and somethin' all the way back in February of 2011. I was young and naive. Everything was new and wonderful. I went along with all of it like the epic adventure it was.

For months I rode this train. Meeting new people, seeing the world, doing the craziest stuff. But as time went on - rounds about July-August 2011 or so, I began to grow jaded. I was seeing things I done didn't agree with. To me, it was my way or the highway, so I told the fine folks to cut that stuff out, and...well, they were reasonably offended. How dare this guy think he could stop us from doing things just because he don't like it? Disgraceful.

But it kept happening. It was at it's worst around Christmas 2011 when lack of motivation to write combined with a disagreement on the direction of the current arc came to a head. Words were said. Dogs barked. People cried. It was awful. I never really recovered.

2012 came, and I left for the first time for the early months of the year. I jumped back in around the time of the second PMD arc, but - through nobody's fault, I might add - it went on for such a long time that I was like "That does it, I'm outta' here", and I hopped off this WAAPT train. For good this time, I said to myself.

This is where things get hazy. I think I got back on near last Christmas, only to leave again. I came back for about a week in January, only to get cold feet at being tied down and jumped ship again. Then in about...March or April of this year, I came back for a bit longer, only to get angry that I wasn't friends with everyone (Again, pretty unreasonable of me), and bailed out once more.

I've been wandering alone since then, trying to forget all the wrongs I wronged, the people I hurt. I apologised once or twice, but the dark times never left the back o' my head. I knew I would have to face them eventually. Which is what brought me back here.

I'm just a tired old man trying to redeem himself. I dunno' how or why, but I swear I'm gonna' make things right in the end.

(TL;DR: I did some bad stuff in the past and I'm trying to make up for it. And no, I don't know why I wrote this like some cowboy. ._.)
