I just got an idea for a possible Illusion Project admin, or at least member.

Claude Orllof

A stern, hard-faced man, Orllof is a Kalosian native who grew up watching the region's growing tourism and cosmopolitanism with dread rather than awe. He sees the growing service industry and urban lifestyles as downright sinful, and sees every Cafe as one would see a brothel. He hates and fears the excesses of technology, even as he uses them himself to maintain his congenitally defected heart.

Orllof demands perfect obedience from his inferiors, a group in which he includes most people and virtually all Pokemon.

His team consists of a male Delphox, a Escavalier, and a Starmie. Orllof has deemed them unfit of receiving nicknames.

  • Delphox: A quiet Pokemon, he is loyal to Orllof... up to a point. Even when directly commanded so, the Delphox will never injure a child.

  • Escavalier: Orllof's personal bodyguard, the Escavalier is efficient, calculating, and surprisingly erudite when his trainer isn't around.

  • Starmie: The Starmie is the one moral tether that keeps Orllof from flying off the handle. It is also the only Pokemon that Orllof deems fit to speak with him, which on it's own is an achievement.
