22450 Crewe27th Aug 2014 02:48:34 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
Li'l Punk
Okay, so I want to do another contest.

Keys suggested doing one of the canon contests in Hoenn, but personally I'd rather do one a bit sooner than that, unless everyone wants to wait for that. We can naturally do that one too later.

Anyways, learning from past experiences with contests, I am going to be kind of strict about running this thing. It's really frustrating to have contests go on for days and days because someone wasn't prepared or we get three last-minute additions. If at all possible, I'd like the contest to take a day or two.

To that end:

  • Participants will be limited to 3-5 entries. If you miss this post and don't get in, sorry. If someone drops out, you can join. But I'm serious about this limit.
  • Write your appeal ahead of time. "Ahead of time" does not mean "five minutes before it's your turn". You want to be done with it by the time your character is going on. If you don't get in, but still want to participate if someone drops out, write an appeal. If you don't get in, you can use it for the next contest, but if you don't have an appeal ready for your turn, you're not going. Again, sorry, but it's incredibly frustrating to wait on somebody writing an appeal for their turn when they've had ages to prepare.

Those are pretty much the main rules. Also, if you don't want to write an appeal/don't sign up in time to get a spot, but still want to be involved, you can play a judge for the contest. A judge can either be a regular character of yours (that maybe gets press-ganged into service because of a judges strike or something) or an NPC just for the contest.

Also note that for judges, they do actually decide the winners of the appeals round, so be prepared to do something besides sitting there. Of course, if your character got pressed into service and has no idea how to judge, it is reasonable for them to have no idea how to properly judge. :P

So... aside from all that, we need to decide on a time/place for this thing. Preferably a weekend, to accommodate timezones and impending school.
Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
