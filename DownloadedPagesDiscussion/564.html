Your Trainer's name: Cebu

Age: 18

Gender: Female

Brief description of his/her appearance: A kimono girl, Cebu dresses elegantly at all times. Her blond hair is ruffed in a manner similar to that of Ho-oh's frill. Her kimono is also heavily based on Ho-oh, mostly white, with a red back and multicolored sleeves. A large golden ribbon, with a bow based on Ho-oh's tail, holds the kimono together. Her sandals are black and wooden, and traditionally styled. Her crimson eyes have a dark ring of eyeliner, allowing them to pop against her lily-white skin.

Any ties to an organization(i.e Pokemon Rangers, Team Rocket, International Police, etc): Kimono Girls

Region of origin: Johto, Ecruteak City

Backstory: A young member of the kimono girl order, Cebu has decided to leave her training behind to travel the world, searching for her true purpose in life. With her pokemon in hand, she wanders around, befriending and learning from those she meets, like Querulous Quagsire.

Pokemon:

Kata, a female Scizor. One of her oldest pokemon, Kata has been Cebu's right hand since she was a small girl. Deeply loyal to her mistress, Kata gets very unsettled when the two are apart.

Biko, a female Golem. The first pokemon Cebu ever met as a baby, Biko has been like a mother to Cebu as she has grown up, and that role has not truly changed. Extremely protective of her surrogate daughter, Biko will sometimes make rash decisions if she is worried about her.

Fema, a female Ferligatr. Given to her by Professor Elm on her 10th birthday, Fema is a powerful, if somewhat reckless fighter. There has been many a time that Fema's uncommonly strong jaws have solved a problem for her trainer.

Bono, an Ambipom. The newest (though not by much) member of Cebu's team, Bono as hatched from an egg Cebu received when she became a Kimono girl. Expected to be an Eevee, Cebu was surprised when it hatched into a bouncy Aipom. Though Bono has since evolved into a powerful Ambipom, Cebu still treats Bono like her little baby, doting on him.

Here we go.
