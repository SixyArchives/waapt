In-game, the Magnagates are portals used to access Mystery Dungeons that can't be reached by normal means, and are opened via special cards invented by an Espeon and an Umbreon, with a Keldeo later in the story being shown to have been able to create these cards as well.

There's also the V-Waves, a special weather phenomenon (sp?) that is different every day and powers up one of the Pokémon types, which was also absent in the first two PMD games.

There's also Frisms, special icy stone things that one can use to record their voices, by speaking into it, and then defrosting the layer of ice on top when you want to hear the recording. They are used as a minor plot point at several points in Gates To Infinity.

And then there's the Voice of Life, which... should I go on? I'm not too sure if anyone who hasn't played the game might plan on playing it, and so I'm not sure how much detail I should go into?
