Just thought I might talk about the plot I'm planning to do over the Kalos Arc.

If you'll remember, back at the end of the Orre Arc, Cebu received a letter from Kalos informing her of her father's passing. Not long after that, on a stopover trip to Johto, Kata was nearly killed protecting an egg during what appeared to be a freak rock slide that took out a side of Mt. Mortar.

Little does Cebu know that this rock slide was orchestrated in order to disadvantage her in the days to come.

For you see, Cebu's true identity is Celeste Berceaux, one of the Twin Scions of the Berceaux clan of Dendemille. The Berceauxs are legendary throughout Kalos, and the rest of the world, for their proficiency in commanding pokemon wordlessly, be it through gesture or sound. (one particularly famous ancestor could choreograph a whole battle with a single blink) The Patriarch of the family in particular is famous for the Noble Roar, the ability to influence the emotions and actions of others with his voice. The latest Patriarch, Guillome Berceaux died recently, and so the family needs to find someone new to lead it, and so a battle for succession is to begin between Guillome’s two daughters, Celeste and Narissa.

However, Celeste left 9 years ago, taken by her mother for reasons unknown. Her mother is gone, but Cebu remains, and she has come to take her inheritance.

In order to be acknowledged for the battle for succession, though, she must first be acknowledged by the side houses. This means gathering the Sacred Instruments of the various houses, the first being the Trumpet that belonged to her uncle Baldr.

However, her sister Narissa has become a true monster, and will stop at nothing to secure power for herself, at any cost…
