Alright, since I've got some more things about this worked out now, might as well make a proper D!Post for it, even if there are some things I still need to get worked out. I've got a good while to get some of that worked out though.

Anyway...

The Hundred Thousand Year Door

What is it?

Pretty much a subplot based on Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door, though with a few changes hopefully people will enjoy.

When/Where will it be taking place?

Sometime next year hopefully. Orre wouldn't really work well for it and it would probably get in the way of some of my Orre and Kalos plans, not to mention possibly those of others who may want to participate.

It'll probably be in the next arc we have in Hoenn or Sinnoh that people who are interested can spare characters for because both regions have nice places to stick TYD locations, though I am still stumped on a good place for Keelhaul Key...

Of course, if everyone's fine with it, we could always just go to other regions during the arc as well and we're going to need to end up leaving the region proper for the X-Naut Fortress anyway, so eh.

Basic Plot Summary

The plot starts off in [Rogueport Equivalent]* Either Slateport or Canalave, depending on where we're doing this, where Sakura and team find a strange figure with a treasure chest. Sakura manages to open the chest and inside finds a map which is supposed to help lead to a treasure deep beneath [Rogueport Equivalent]. Sometime after finding this map, though, she is kidnapped by a group of Beldum-line mons with questionable motives known as the X-Nauts who seek the treasure as well. They are unable to get ahold of the map, however, and it ends up in the hands of whoever ends up participating in the arc. Said group eventually runs into a duo of Foongus-line archaeologists and with them learns of an old Alakagross City located underneath the main city containing a large door behind which the fabled treasure lies. To reach this treasure, however, they need seven keys known as the Crystal Stars that the Magical Map leads them to in order to rescue Sakura, stop the X-Nauts, and find out what the treasure really is.

Possible problems

A couple of plot points for this arc somewhat require me to devote certain locations to it, for example, a giant Hydreigon terrorizing Mauville and Vendanturf and having turned New Mauville into its fortress, if that ends up taking place in Hoenn as well as probably making an entirely new location for Glitzville and pretty much the existence of an Alakagross city under [Rogueport equivalent] and the establishing of a Pokemon Underworld in it (that isn't necessarily Murkrow) as well. Hopefully the changes I make are fine enough with everyone, though I'd like to get said problems out of the way while this is still in planning and will try and make as few changes that people would find questionable permanently, so hopefully we can work through that.

So... that's what I've got so far.

Thoughts? Concerns? Suggestions?
