So, since I hadn't really made a D!Post about it yet, I figured I might as well do so now.

You may have heard mention of a "Fantasy mini-arc" set to happen after Kalos? Well, here's the current outline for the plot, I guess:

While suffering from writer's block, having difficulty figuring out what should happen next in her fantasy version of the J-Team show reboot, DS comes across a bunch of Unown while wandering through one of the ruins, and hearing of her problem, the Unown decide to try to help her find some inspiration.

This leads to the Unown trapping DS, along with several of her friends, getting transported into the fictional world in DS' notebook. However, for the purpose of keeping everyone "in-character", the Unown end up sealing the other's memories inside a crystal ball (while leaving them believing themselves to be the characters DS based off of them), and in order to leave the fictional world, those memories must first be retrieved.

DS, the other Minds of Crystal, and possibly Pokémon without a character based on them within the story keep their memories, and must figure out how to get out.

Further complicating matters is DS finding herself coming to the conclusion that her friends might be happier if they all stay in her fictional world...
