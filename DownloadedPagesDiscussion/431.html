Age: 18

Physical Description: She's somewhat short, has messy long pale pink hair and electric blue eyes. She looks almost unhealthily thin at times, but her lively personality usually makes this ignorable. Organization Ties: None.

Home Region/Town: Lilycove Hoenn

Personality: Shy and quiet usually, but fun loving and adventurous. All her odd quirks aside, her most often seen disposition is that of a quiet, happily content young woman who just loves life as a whole.

That would all change one day when a Rocket Grunt attacked her parents. Her father's Abra teleported her to the safety of a nearby rooftop. She watched as the Rocket mercilessly beat her parents into a critical condition. The following day at the hospital a mysterious woman approached her, told her something shocking and handed her a Bronzor. From that day onward Tesse has traveled the regions, looking to bring justice upon the viscous Rocket.
