The moral choices are very ambiguous, so you can't tell what result they'll have down the line.

They can also affect the basic plot. One of the big choices in Episode 1 (it's an episodic game, and only the first one is out right now) is deciding who needs your help more out of two choices that you have information about. A character's life hangs in the balance depending on where you go, but there's no real way to know that when you choose, and the other option might seem much more urgent.
