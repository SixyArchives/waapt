(Note to those who just joined recently: Move along, there's nothing to see here. Just some old guy who doesn't know what he's doing with himself.)

I always seem to end up back here somehow. I've sworn of this place so many times for good, yet I always get drawn back by my own morbid curiosity. "Just peek at the logs, check the thread. See how everyone's doing." I tell myself. "It won't hurt."

It always ends up hurting.

Mainly because it seems that whenever anything to do with me is mentioned, it's always accompanied by "Let's not talk about [Major Argument I caused]" or "There was one person [Hint: It's me] who caused a who bunch of arguments about [X Trivial thing]".

It's like knives to my heart.

"But why do you care about what we think?" You ask. "It's not like YOU care."

Well, put it this way: When you put nearly two years and countless hours into something, and the only thing to show for it being leaving with your tail between your legs, a whole bunch of trivial arguments, everyone disliking you, and the fear that the same thing might happen again whenever you try to forge connections with any other groups, well...It doesn't leave you very happy, that's for sure.

I dread this place whenever I think about it. About everyone shouting at me for not liking the way thing were, at trying to get my point across, at being the one who gets into arguments with everyone. I hate this reputation.

It shouldn't be this way.

I know I've apologised in the past. But they were merely band-aids to cover up this festering wound (I hope you weren't eating lunch when you read this. Sorry for the mental image). As someone once said "Sorry doesn't fix things."

And he's right. I still feel like a pariah. I still feel like I'm incapable of being sociable, and that opening up to others about the things I like will only end up with me getting hurt.

Well I've had enough. I don't know how, I don't know what it'll take, but I'm going to fix this. It's time to unshackle myself from all the fear and doubt and prove to everyone that I am a nice, fun guy to be around.

It's time for redemption.
