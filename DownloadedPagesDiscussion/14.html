Chase is now a strong, confident Unfeazent, and is even said to be the second-in-command of DS' Unova team.

As for Ribbons, she started out as a Shrinking Violet who didn't speak much. As she learned how to use her psychic powers, she started to become more outgoing, not whispering all the time when she spoke.

Once she evolved, she lost the Shrinking Violet aspect and took on a bit more of a Cheerful Child personality.

And then of course, there's Artemis.

Started out as a little jerk who kept making fun of Chase at every oppurtunity. However, she did seem to have a soft spot for Ribbons, as she did take an attack from a Scolipede that was meant for Ribbons.

Her development into Jerk with a Heart of Gold began when Chase rescued her from said Scolipede, which was when she finally started showing him some respect and referring to him by his name. So yeah.
