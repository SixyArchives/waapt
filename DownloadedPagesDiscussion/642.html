Bracelets

After much deliberation in chat, including Silent, who helped me iron out the complications greatly, we have a new idea.

  • There will be two types of bracelets. The first shall be called Galvanic bracelets, in honor of there inventor. They will be the true, lasting bracelets that go beyond arcs. As such, they will be bio-locked to an individual, glitchy, and possibly very inconvenient to use. Like Galvani's first, which will require that she be touching Dune for it to activate. These are the ones that the aforementioned process will be used on.

  • The second type, however, are the non-Galvanic bracelets. These are on demand, actually pretty reliable, and made for a purpose. These are for when an armband isn't available, but the situation does not warrant a full-blown Galvanic bracelet. They only last until a specific purpose is fulfilled, and then break or become inert. Do note, however, that Galvani (and her proteges, if she ever gets any) have their limits, and so might deny requests if they're tired of being asked for them or the majority of players are against their use in a certain situation.
