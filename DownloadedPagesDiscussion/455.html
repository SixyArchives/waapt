Name: Ishi, sometimes known as 'The Shiftry'.

Age: 18

Appearance: Tall, black hair and green eyes. Scar on his left cheek. Wears an unassumin' brown robe and matchin' pants. On his head is a conical straw hat with a leaf/twig ornament. Wears guards on his hands and forearms, concealed under his sleeves.

Occupation': Spy/Ninja

Kingdom: Spectra, under the servitude of the Ghost King. Originally from Greenleaf.

Personality: Friendly to those he likes but generally mysterious and enigmatic. He isn't above deceivin' others as his job entails him to do so.

Background: Once a farmer's son his village was ransacked by Lord Umbra. Swearin' vengeance he traveled to Viperia and mastered the art of ninjutsu. Years later he would present himself to Lord Umbra and offered him the head of an insubordinate commander. Impressed Umbra hired him as own of his premier spies and assassins. Ishi remains loyal to Umbra for now until he can find a way to defeat the Ghost King for good and exact his revenge.

Weapons/Abilities: Kusarigama (Chain Scythe which he keeps concealed in his robe). He also has several Ninja 'gimmick' weapons such as smoke bombs, kunais and shurikens. Havin' mastered ninjutsu he is an adept of stealth and silence as well as heightened senses. He also appears to have some 'mystical' qualities as well, claimin' he can 'talk to the wind'.

Warrior Skill: Ninjutsu (duh!)

Transformation: His robe is removed and tied around his waist revealin' a scar covered chest and bandage wrapped around his midsection. His hat is removed as well and hangs off his back. On his face is a brown mask with a pointed nose and white fur trim along the edges, not unlike a Shiftry. Grasped in his left hand is his Kusarigama with the chain wrapped around his arm. In his right hand a set of three large kunai, arranged to look like a fan.

Specialty:Grass-Types

Perfect Link: Seedot Line

Pokemon

Name: Dismas

Species: Nuzleaf

Personality: Cunnin', much like his master, and playful at heart. Dismas is thief with a fascination with precious gems. He's not very big on battlin' and prefers to deceive opponents in order to make quick getaways than to actually fight them head on.

Name: Garb (Ancestor!Poncho)

Species: Breloom'''

Personality: Once a hard workin' farmer's 'mon, Garb found a new love in combat when Ishi was trainin' in Viperia. An honourable and humble warrior he takes his victories with a grain of salt and his defeats as a learnin' experience.

Name: Spade (Ancestor!Ace)

Species: Crobat

Personality: Ishi's trainin' partner Spade is somethin' of a mentor. The Voice of Reason, Spade always has some words of wisdom to offer. He is very protective of Ishi and sees him as a younger sibling of sorts.

Name: 'Servant' (Really he's Vladimir)

Species: Ghastly

Personality: One of the many guards that patrol the corridors of Umbra's fortress. He isn't really one of Ishi's 'mons but more like a friend who hangs around with him. Ishi couldn't think up a name for him so he just calls him 'Servant'.
