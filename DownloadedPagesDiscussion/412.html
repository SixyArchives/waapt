Tracer's R Ping Situation Until At Least This May

... It's kinda bleak. As you might have noticed, my availability to RP (or do anything not related to school, for that matter) has more or less gone off a cliff dive since school started up almost a month ago. And pretty much the only potentially light spot that I can see in it is during Spring Break.

To give you an idea of what I'm facing this spring, this is my schedule...

For one class. Out of 4.

So... Right now, I essentially have 3 options for how to progress:

  • A: Quit. Or otherwise go on indefinite hiatus. And have all of my running plot strands get resolved offscreen / through Word of God / via oneshots.
  • B: Keep on chugging when I can, and keep falling further and further behind the pace of the RP until at least May, where I *might* be able to catch up. With no guarantees.
  • C: Ask the rest of the playerbase for help in running plot strands until things clear up on my end. And acknowledge that while things may play out differently than how I originally wanted them to, they'll at least happen in some form and not bottleneck the broader narratives of this RP.

I really don't want to have to resign myself to one of the first two options, but I realize that I can't compel you to help me. Indeed, some of you are possibly in situations that may be comparable, or god forbid, worse than my own. But if you can provide any help, I would be extremely grateful for it.

  • Shadowing the rest of the group to wherever they may go narrative-wise. At least up until Unova-2.
    • A confidential pairing that will show up around the time of the former.

Rt AU

  • Hijinks with Cassandra and peons
  • Hijinks with Desert Resort mercenaries
    • Getting Psyche involved in AU!Sinnoh through political intrigue

    • The Weavile among the stranded goons at PEFE turning out to become the missing factor in Rt AU's divergence from PMD-B.
  • Viginettes with Tangent for Ben
  • Following AU!Vektor
  • Fighting the rest of the good fight of Rt AU
  • Guardianshipping with Silent

PMD-B

  • Continuing the story of RP!Tagg + RP!Tracer's misadventures and unseen boss fight on the way back home for last Halloween
  • Othersaga

If there's anything that you see that you would like to help out with, please let me know. I actually have the rough direction / chronology, and in some cases, actual scenes pre-made for some of these strands. And I am more than ready to provide them to you if you wish to take up the mantle.

Once again, thank you for humoring my wall of text, and whatever your decision is regarding my request, I respect it and thank you for being such a fun and supportive community to write stories with.

Backstory: A budding young Psychic, Nico spent most of his early life in the small, arid town of Lentimas. After receiving a Sigilyph egg for his eighth birthday, Nico decided to go on a Pokemon journey to prove his worth.
