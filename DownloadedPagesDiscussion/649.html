I'll just post the pre-written part, the part I wrote when I was still lucid, now and then leave to clear my head.

Basically, for those wondering, at a certain point in the game, Team Flare awakens the box legendary. However, here in WAAPT, we can't say one or the other got awakened... so my opinion is, we say they both were awakened. And the Fairy-type gets awakened along with Xerneas.

Now, there's a blatant question: How did the Fairies exist prior to this in the games? My answer: Because it was Kalos-centered. Team Flare has been implied to have been trying to awaken the legendaries for at least a while before it happens in-game. Perhaps disturbing Xerneas's "slumber" revealed the Fairy-type throughout its home region. The moment Xerneas awakens fully, the full-blown Cosmic Retcon happens.

What does this mean for other universes? Well, Tangent suggested that it mean nothing; perhaps in RtAU, it can be made a plot point that reawakening Xerneas can help defeat those pesky Dragons. And in other universes, it could happen offscreen. For all we know, perhaps in other universes, Xerneas awakens on his own without interference accelerating the process. It still happens somehow.

Now, there's another problem. We've just answered who, what, where, why, and how, but we haven't answered when. So let's handle that now: When? Well, if I recall correctly, the majority of the playerbase wants the Kalos arc to occur soon, if not immediately, after the events of the games. The event which awakens the Kalos legendaries occurs at the final stretch of the game, so perhaps the event and associated Retcon should occur when we're gearing up to go to Kalos, or even better, trigger aforementioned "gearing up".

As for the issue of Valerie, the trainer that has to have made the Fairy-type league-recognized... well, we haven't exactly been doing much relevant to any leagues recently, have we? We've kind of been busy being on the lookout for Cipher, so perhaps it's not that a secret was kept, but rather that we just didn't notice. Until Cipher's gone, they're kinda a bigger deal than something happening half a continent and a full ocean away. And either way, it's entirely possible, and in fact almost definitely true, that Valerie was a very, very new Gym Leader as of the games' plot. Which is another reason we should hold off on the retcon until around the postgame.

And now on to more personal ideas tying to this.

If we go ahead with that, would anyone object to Ivy's Klefki, Locke, being the first Fairy-type to be very obviously a new type? Sure, there are other mons who are retconned into having the new type, but they'll be confused as to what happened. Perhaps Ivy's Klefki could be the mon to make people realize that, hey, something changed and there's a new type. Of course, this can only really happen if we do agree on the "retcon just before we go to Kalos" timeframe, so give your opinions on that before you respond to this.

Okay, there. Now I'm out for what's likely to be the rest of the night.
