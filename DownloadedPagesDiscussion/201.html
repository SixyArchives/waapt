On-Topic Stuff

"Finale" Plot Discussion

The branches we have established are as follows:

  • 1)Confront Charon and try stop the Caldera
  • 2)Confront the Teams, proceed to arrests, quell infighting, etc
  • 3)Protect the populace from the incoming disaster
  • 4)Whatever Tracer and Luke and Silent can plan

What we want is:

  • 1)To not have a damage sponge final boss.
  • 2)To not have simply Tagg, Fool, and DS finish the plot while everyone else spectates.
  • 3)To not have everyone doing the same thing.
  • 4)To avoid a war.

Actions in consideration:

  • 1)Raid against Ozone to free the mini-trio.
  • 2)Stopping/Stalling the Eruption.
    • 2a)Groundhog's Day loop involving the Eruption.
  • 3)Holding off the Anniversary until a later time and claiming that we were on time.
  • 4)Stopping whatever stuff Neo Team Aqua and Neo Team Magma are doing so they can join us against Ozone.
  • 5)Both the natural and mini Weather Trios band together to stop the calamities. Neither group is sacrificed.

Things to take into account:

  • Ozone does not necessarily have to be evil.
  • Protecting the populace means keeping the peace, be it from uprisings of the common folk, attacks from any of the teams, or the natural disaster of the eruption.
  • Whatever the Neo Team Aqua and Neo Team Magma groups are doing does not have to involve battling.
  • Branches 1 and 2 are preferred to take place concurrently.
  • As the Caldera eruption may involve the surrounding areas or even across all of Hoenn, remember to mind what NP Cs you may control.
  • Charon may or may not be a threat.
