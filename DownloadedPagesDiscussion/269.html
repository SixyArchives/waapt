Silent: It can't really be helped. I don't want to make up regions for the Conquest arc, which limits us to what is in the games. I've included all the regions that are listed on Bulbapedia (including the Orange Islands), so that's what there is.

Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh are unquestionably part of real-world Japan. There's just no getting around that. We put Almia directly south of Sinnoh, so it's there, too. Fiore is probably in a similar situation.

Unova, and Orre are New York/New Jersey, and Arizona, respectively. Oblivia is likely Hawaii.

Neither the Orange Islands, nor Ransei exist in the real world. The Orange Islands are vaguely near our Japan analogue, but aren't necessarily part of it. Ransei's character distribution is roughly analogous to the real Japan during the Sengoku period (as I discovered last night, when talking to Pika), but seeing as it's one island shaped like Arceus, and not several islands shaped like Japan, it could theoretically be anywhere.
