New character time! I feel like this one might have some fun interactions with other PCs once Kalos starts.

Human

  • Name: Aline Bellamy
  • Age: 8
  • Gender: Female
  • Appearance: Picture this as an 8-year old.◊
  • Ties to a Organization: None
  • Region of Origin: Lumiose, Kalos
  • Backstory: Aline is a mute girl who comes from a rich family in Lumiose, despite her disability, she lives a pretty much normal life with no problems whatsoever, instead of communicating through speech, she does so by writing on some sheets of paper or signs that she carries around with her as well as many erasable markers of many colors and she also communicates with her Pokémon by tapping the markers on a surface in certain patterns that they can easily identify as well as body language. She's an avid fan of the J-Team TV show and as such spends a lot of time making drawings about them as well as hoping to meet up with them at some point and in fact wants to start her own journey so that she can go through as many adventures as the team.

On a small note, one thing I thought for Aline is that she'd use a different color for her messages as if to represent the entonation of what she's saying:

Red -> Anger

Blue -> Neutral

Teal -> Cheerfulness

Gray -> Depression

Bold -> Raising her voice/yelling

Purple -> Reassurance/Confidence

Cinnamon -> Discomfort

Green -> Awkward/Embarrassment

   Evil -> Beyond angry   

Pokémon

  • Species: Spoink
  • Nickname: Spring
  • Gender: Female
  • Ability: Thick Fat
  • Personality/Bio: Aline's starter, Spring likes to bounce all over the place and see what people are doing. She has a relatively short temper and will not tolerate anyone trying to badmouth her or Aline and will do everything she can to punish anyone who does, despite that, she's usually very easy-going and friendly as long as her buttons are not pressed.

  • Species: Noibat
  • Nickname: Divoom
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Telepathy
  • Personality/Bio: A gift from a friend, Divoom is more relaxed than Spring and prefers to rest on Aline's head and make her company than to meet other peoplemons, he does share Spring's curiosity though and will often make use of his impressive hearing to listen on others conversations that interest him. Due to being always close to Aline, he acts as her voice when there are other Pokémon nearby that cannot read her messages and even provide further comments from her by using his Telepathy ability to communicate with her.
