Anyway, was talking to some other players as well as to Shimaspawn about the character page split, and we have a couple of issues.

First, while we could take the obvious choice of splitting alphabetically... Who do we alphabetize on? The Trainers? But the Pokémon are their own characters! The Pokémon? But that'll make teams and relations a pain to sort out! Of course we could also split by trainers instead of alphabetically. Pikaninja also suggests split by time period, by which I assume he means player join date. There are various ways to go around it, none is even nearly optimal, and none is really correctable without page cutting if we mess up something.

Second, the characters page has too much redundant information, and at this time it is more a character treaty or Bestiary than, well, a Character Sheet. It really needs a trim, or better a liposuction, and needs to make better use of data sources. Do we really need to eg.: inform people that Nadia has flight, is blue, attacks with her claws or can serve as a mount? She is a Salamence! And we have at least two pages detailing everything that Salamences can do! What the character sheet should detail are things that pertain to the character, not the species.

In view of that I'm asking for opinions about how to split and how to liposuction the page, ideally both without losing too much information. I still suggest we launch WAAPTWiki ^.^
