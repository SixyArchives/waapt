3578 PippingFool12th Jul 2011 09:40:20 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three
I've had an Idea for Fool in the AU Arc for a long time now. I may as well post it.

AU!Fool works for Artemis and Scolipede by funding their schemes and supplying them with new recuits, how she does this is through her Chessmastery. In this timeline, not only does she live in Undella, she controls it and uses to aid her schemes.

She puts on a presents it as a "Resistance Town" and will take in those who've been suppressed elsewhere with the promise that they'll be safe here. However, AU!Fool will always be watching them, monitering the people and Pokemon who seem to be showing promise and could be useful for her schemes. The ones that show promise are taken into her villa and are taken to the "Midori Room" (the room where AU!Fool keeps her sanguinary personal guards all dressed in smashing green garb) where they are basically given the option of "Join us, or die" and she has no qualms of watching her personal guards rip others to shreds in front of her eyes as long as they don't get blood on her dress. The one's who choose "Join us" are personally trained to be effective to benefit Artemis and Scolipede and her own personal goals. She's pretty much an Evil Overlord with Class.

People who are permanent residents pretend that nothing is wrong, after all, it's better to be alive and pay her a safety ransom then be "dealt with".

AU!Fool's downfall is that she is pretty much nothing without her personal guards, her own team (Especially Napoleon) or the backup support from Artemis and Scolipede. If she gets cut off or gets on the bad side of either of these sources then she's done for. So AU!Fool spends most of her time trying to keep them all happy.

Yeah, AU!Fool is Darker and Edgier by far with mixed elements of Watership Down, Half-Life, Animal Farm and 1984. Around the end of the arc,Karma will roll around and bite her.
