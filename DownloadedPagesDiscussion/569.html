It's come to my attention that quite a few people are bringing Conquest characters back as Ghostmons. And bringing back dead people/mons back as ghosts in general.

Now, I'm not gonna name names to avoid people guessing certain twists coming up for multiple people, but some people have been planning for people or mons to come back for months, even over a year in one case I know about, and I don't want the impact to be deadened by a whole bunch of "spur of the moment" resurrections. Or worse, that said spur-of-the moment resurrections cause a moratorium to be placed on new ones, effectively making secret plans in the works for months undoable.

So... I'd like a solution to this, but in the meantime please don't be rash about Ghostmons. It may have consequences for people who've been planning a while.

Plus, we don't want to make death too cheap.

So here's my Cipher character's profile:

Bio

Name: "Tokarev"

Rank: Admin

Age: Somewhere in his late 30s

Affiliation: Cipher, Himself

Origin: Unknown

Appearance: Black hair, with cold blue eyes. Tokarev tends to wear suits when not on duty, though when he is on mission he either wears an updated version of his old mercenary outfit or simply wears a bulletproof vest over his suit.

Bio: Tokarev was born in an unknown region and spent some time in its military before leaving and becoming a mercenary, working for the highest bidder. Some of his actions included the murder of undesirables, acts of terrorism, and one instance where he managed to gather a team to interfere with international communications by tapping the [Pacific analogue] comm wire. This gained him the attention of Cipher, which had him join at first as a Peon, which was when he met a certain trainer in the Under, before rocketing through the ranks to Admin with his cadre of former mercenary squadmates.

Tokarev is an incredibly ruthless individual, being willing to kill anyone if ordered to and/or paid enough. This, more than his skill as a trainer or as a gun wielding former mercenary, makes him extremely dangerous. Tokarev's view on problems can be best described as "A paycheck is harder to get rid of than a body", and is the kind of person who will indeed "Just Shoot Him". He mainly stays with Cipher because they pay very well, but would leave them in a heartbeat if anyone offered a better deal. (Of course no one ever does, so he sticks around.) For this reason he also takes particular relish in eliminating any evidence of Cipher's actions to make his own getaway if said better deal comes up much easier. He views mons mainly as tools (Though like any tool, he prefers to keep them in good condition), and tends to favor those with high Attack and Speed. All in all, he's mainly out for himself and will eliminate anyone who stands in his way.

Pokémon

Name: Lev

Gender: Male

Species: Fearow

Bio: Tokarev's right hand mon, a being just as cold and ruthless as he. Lev normally spends his time in his ball or perched near his trainer, but is trained to be able to kill on command.
