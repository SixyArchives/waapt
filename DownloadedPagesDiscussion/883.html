All right...

I am officially announcing my retirement from WAAPT. This has been coming for a while now, as I'm sure you've all been able to tell by my quitting and coming back becoming far more frequent and constant. I apologize for that. I'm really, really sorry.

In spite of this, I'll still be in the chats and such and I'll still be following the RP. I don't hate WAAPT, I just don't have any motivation to write anything for it anymore. Since I haven't done much of anything IRP lately anyway, it'll probably be as if nothing changed at all in that respect.

I would still like to see my characters' subplots and the like wrapped up, so all of the characters on this list are up for grabs. Just PM me about them if you'd be willing to play them. I'll probably end up writing several essays on anyone that people would want to play, though, so be prepared for that if you ask. Of course, no one has to take these characters. I don't think that'd be a very common worry at all, but still...
