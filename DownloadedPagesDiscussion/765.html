I feel that there are definitely benefits to limiting how edgy we are I Cly. It's hard for me to explain it without sounding confrontational because I'm used to a very professional principle of RP where mixing IC and OOC is never okay; I'll give it a shot though.

I feel like it's important that we don't go too far with projecting our sexual morality on the RP, not because anyone is wrong or anything asinine like that but because sex and sexuality are extremely edgy subjects for some people, even beyond violence.

There will come a time when society as a whole is open minded enough that what we do here doesn't even raise a brow for most people. I feel fairly safe saying homosexuality for instance has reached that point or at least close enough where we shouldn't have to worry about it. Things like transexuality and polyamory have not quite reached that point however... Both of those things still experience a terrifying amount of prejudice in real life; those groups are not remotely free of prejudice and hatred which just goes to show not only that they deserve to be fought for but that society sadly hasn't caught up with our progressive mindsets. It'd also be a disservice to all the people who suffer as a result of being trans or polyamorous to say otherwise.

Now by jumping the gun and using the RP as a bit of a playground to experiment with these sorts of 'edgy' things (Edgy means overly dark or overly progressive, in this case the latter.) we do kind of heavily shove some people who are still struggling with their feelings about this stuff out of the RP.

And that's not... ideal. I've seen some things, man. I've been on the other end of this conversation being told the same thing and boy did it suck, but in hindsight a little restraint would've stopped a lot of emotional discomfort and probably a couple of fights / ruined friendships in the process.

So... There's my feelings on the matter.
