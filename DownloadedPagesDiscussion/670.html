Character Dump Time!

Name: Crowley Szandor note Since I made a big song-and-dance about it, Crowley's name is derived from the names of famous occultists. His first name comes from "wickedest man in Britain" Aleister Crowley, and Szandor (pronounced "shandoar") was the adopted middle name of Satanic Bible author Anton Lavey. This name is an Artifact Title: I came up with it for the villain character I was originally planning, then came up with the idea of having the "villain" actually be a pretentious artiste-type in a stupid costume, then dropped the villain entirely and ended up with a harmless Hipster bearing a villain-tinged Awesome McCoolname. Yeah... my thought processes are weird...

Age: 27

Gender: Male

Appearance: Highly variable. Crowley enjoys dressing up in bizarre costumes, due to his personal belief that your physical appearance can be art. If you stripped off all the coloured sequins and day-glo hair dye, he'd be a guy of average build with shoulder-length black hair and a wiry goatee.

Ties to any organisations?: If you mean villainous teams, no. If you mean organisations period, he's a respected member of the International Artist's Guild.

Region of origin: Suburban Cyllage City, Kalos

Background: Crowley Szandor was born into a dull middle-class family in the suburbs of Cyllage. His "unusual" surname is of Hungarian origin; Crowley is quite proud of this for some reason, and has a tendency to insert Uralic cultural symbols into his artwork.

From a very young age, Crowley displayed extensive creativity. The young Crowley didn't know when to stop being creative, however, and thus, anything that stayed near him for too long became incredibly beautiful, a Mind Screw, or sometimes both. His grades in most subjects suffered as a result, since science teachers don't appreciate you turning your jotter into a sketchbook or taking five pages to create a surreal representation of atomic bonding, but he was the undisputed top of the class in Art and a promising student in Music. On the back of glowing recommendations from those two teachers (and those two teachers alone), Crowley received his higher education at a specialised art college. This was a mixed blessing; it perfected his skills with pencils and brushes, but it also helped foster the latent pretentiousness and cynical views on life that had been festering beneath his skin for a while. After graduating, Crowley went on to become a professional

As of right now, Crowley is respected and reviled in equal measure by his colleagues in the International Artist's Guild. He's earned quite a lot of money by auctioning his work and signing temporary deals with corporations, so he's free to do just about anything he wants. What he wants to do today is go on a journey with his Pokmon; to that end, he's headed off to the Kalos docks to meet other Trainers and prove he's better than them go on a journey with them. His eventual goal is to prove that Pokmon battling, with its varied styles and visual appeal, is a form of art (a view Siebold would probably appreciate).

Crowley's Team. Kalos mons included, since he's a Kalos native and won't appear until the arc begins.

Species: Roserade

Name: Goya

Gender: Female

Ability: Natural Cure

Description: Take everything potentially obnoxious about Crowley himself, add on a severe case of vanity-induced depression a la the kind suffered by supermodels, and you have a grossly underwhelming approximation of Goya's personality. Crowley can't speak Monese, so he sees her as a kindred artistic spirit and thinks her constant frowning is an emo-type thing. His other Pokmon can't stand her, and resent her position as Crowley's go-to first Pokmon.

Species: Ninjask

Name: Dali

Gender: Male

Ability; Speed Boost

Description: Dali has a rather cold and dispassionate view of life, dividing everything into two categories: stuff you can fight, and stuff you should ignore. This doesn't tread into Blood Knight territory, and his battling style is actually as coldly methodical as his personality, but the thought processes are definitely there. When he talks at all, he's noted for his clipped, monotone speech.

Species: Sylveon

Name: Rembrandt

Gender: Male

Ability: Cute Charm

Description: The Straight Man. Rembrandt is optimistic, but not nave; confident, but not egotistical; deadpan, but not rude. He serves as a mediator between the other Pokmon in Crowley's party, which, given the amount of friction produced by Crowley's Ragtag Bunch of Misfits, is no easy task. Oh, and one other thing: ''he's not gay.'' He's loved many a vixen, though none have ever loved him back... which is also something he's tetchy about. Basically, just don't talk about love or sexuality around him and you'll be fine.

Species: Blastoise note starter mon

Name: Della Francesca (often called DF)

Gender: Female

Ability: Torrent

Description: The cutesy pink mass of ribbons is male, the giant turtle with cannons on its shoulders is female. Yeah, the universe sometimes works like that. Anyway, DF is a prime example of the upbeat belligerent (for an example of the angry belligerent, see Svavel). She'll willingly leap into any battle, and have fun doing so; by extension, she has a rather stunted sense of danger, and would take on world-destroying legendaries as willingly as silly Pidgeys. When not fighting physically, she tends to fight verbally with Goya.

Species: Honedge note Yes, I went there. Sue me.

Name: Nevinson

Gender: Male

Ability: No Guard

Description: Nevinson is Crowley's newest Pokmon, mostly because he never intended to catch him. Instead, the Honedge took an interest in the flamboyant artist-trainer, and followed him around incessantly until Crowley finally relented and officially caught him. He combines the traditional Honedge virtues of chivalry and honour with a somewhat jilted view of the world and life, making him kind of creepy, but polite enough that you can forgive him. He will probably become a member of the Council of Swords That Wield Themselves at some point.
