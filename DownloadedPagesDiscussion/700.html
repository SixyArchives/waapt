So, I know I've mentioned it in the chat a few times, but I finally have a draft of a second PC that I'd like to introduce in Kalos.

Cheryl LeFae

A native Courmarine City, Cheryl is an eighteen year old girl who just seems to have two left feet. And two left hands. And two left eyes. Bottom line, she's very clumsy. She has a habit of dropping things, tripping over rocks that don't exist, and running into doors. That's not to say that she's unlucky - it's just that her coordination leaves a lot to be desired.

She's medium height with a slight build. Her hair is a dull black and her eyes are a washed-out hazel. Her clothes are rather loose on her out of preference.

She is also a second cousin to Ren.

And now her team:

Doug (Male Diglett)

Cheryl's starter, Doug was a native to Route 13 until Cheryl's father caught him for her. He uses his natural speed to his advantage, and has been known to catch items that would have otherwise shattered after Cheryl dropped them. He likes showing off his reaction speed, and sometimes jumps to conclusions in his haste.

Flicker (Female Chinchou)

Cheryl caught Flicker when she accidentally dropped a fishing rod off of her mother's boat. When they got the fishing rod back, Flicker was caught on the end of the hook, which she's never been able to live down. She's a snarker at heart, but is quite bashful around strangers.

Roland (Male Heracross)

Roland is a case of karma throwing Cheryl a bone - After falling down a tree she had climbed to reach a tasty-looking fruit, she had knocked the poor Heracross off on the way down and landed on him. Needless to say, this would have infuriated the Bug-Type had Cheryl not offered him the fruit she had found as recompense. Roland is Cheryl's strongest Pokemon, even though he's a big softy beneath that carapace of his. He has a love of woodwind instruments.
