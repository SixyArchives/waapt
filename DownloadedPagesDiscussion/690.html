Okay, heres a custom move Im gonna pitch at you guys, because bored and I think this is kinda cool.

Name: Presdigitation

Type: Ghost - Status

The user switches place with another Pokemon in its trainers party. That Pokemon is treated as if the move Protect has been used on it, but can otherwise not use another move this turn. Abilities and items of the Pokémon switched in do not trigger until the turn after the pseudo-Protect is lifted. The Pokémon switched in can not use Protect, Detect or a switchout move in the turn after switching in. It shares the same successive-failure chance Protect does. If it is used in battles that observe the 4-move restriction, it takes a move slot. Whoever switches in gets a "slow start" (isn't able to chain moves or immediately dodge once the protect drops, etc) If there are no team members left to switch to, the move fails.
