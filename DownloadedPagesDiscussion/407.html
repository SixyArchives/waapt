AN IMPORTANT NOTE ABOUT MAGIC MIRROR

Got any mons in mind for future captures? You can get them in Makbaliv~

There is no special rule saying you can't take any mons with you out of the mirror. As long as it's not a named/important character, it's possible to recruit miscellaneous mons in a fashion similar to PMD mons and take them with you. Even nameless evil minions can be recruited! (Want a mon from Captain Hook's crew? Go right ahead.)

An interesting benefit to getting mons this way is that they're just as likely to have Hidden Abilities as normal Abilities. So if you recruited a Rufflet from Neverland 's Indian camp, it could have Defiant.

Here's that list (Consider it the official reveal of what worlds/movies we're doing~) :

Neverland: All sorts of mons all across the island. Indian Camp has a large concentration of Rufflet and Vullaby line members, Mermaid Lagoon has Clamperl line members, and Hook's crew is Water and Dark mons (Including Fossils!)

Wonderland: Pretty much anything, but mostly Grass and Bug and woodland mons. Note more abberant mons (such as glasses-birds) cannot be recruited.

Pride Lands: Any mons analogous to RL savannah animals, but especially Shinx and Poochyena line members.

Clock City: Mostly Pikachu line members, but anything else not out of place in a big city can be found here.

Surf Island: Any mons you'd expect to find on or around a tropical island.

Agrabah: Any kind of desert-dwelling mon.

Strange Symphony: Pretty much anything, though there's a pod of Wailmer and Wailord at one point, and the very end has a large concentration of Dragon mons.

Atlantis: Mostly Water and fossil mons.

Thorn Kingdom: Woodland mons, and Dark-type minions.

Feel free to ask me more about any of these.
