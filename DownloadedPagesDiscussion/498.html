On the subject of the morality of murder. If my time with the RP serves me correctly, murder and killing sprees serves three main purposes.

  • Desperation: It's the only sensible course of action. When either every other form of negotiation has failed or there is a serious time limit and the foe is a considerable threat.

  • Atmosphere: It's used to show a Crapsack World setting or to establish morally bankrupt character. I might just be thinking terms of the AU arc, but the setting was a war zone, where moral ambiguity is king.

  • Rage: Bit of Coloumn A, Bit of Coloumn B. Has triggers.

Murder for murders sake, even taking into note Blue and Orange Morality or murder due to the fact that they belong to a certain syndication, is generally frowned upon within the RP. And, again, any possible murder that would happen in RP would have to be discussed beforehand and be okayed by other players
