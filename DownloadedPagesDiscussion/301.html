How about this:

Being able to talk to plushies is a two-fold skill.

1. He has to have talked to them while in his Pokeform.

OR

2. He has to have a plushie that he's previously talked to present.

So, for example, if he's in his Pokeform, and Plushie A talks to him, he will always be able to understand Plushie A. Now, if he's human, and talking to Plushie A, and Plushie B is around and decides to start talking, he will be able to talk to Plushie B. Since this has happened once, he'll always be able to talk to Plushie B after this.

Basically, a plushie that he's had direct contact with can act as a conduit for other plushies.
