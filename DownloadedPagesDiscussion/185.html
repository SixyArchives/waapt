Should the Chat be split? Or should we stick with the "OT" Model?

Right now, we currently have an "On Topic" set-up. In which at certain points, an appointed moderator will change the topic to a single one for us all to discuss for a certain amount of time. During these periods, the topic may not be changed, and you must not discuss off-topic matters (under penalty of being kicked from the chat).

An alternative arrangement, however, is to have two chatrooms: One devoted to the RP, and one for off-topic. This way, those that want to discuss the RP can do so at their leisure without fear of disrupting the people who would prefer to discuss over matters or who otherwise can't contribute.
