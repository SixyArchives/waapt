Okay, since it's felt that I should explain what's going on exactly in the keystone arc, I'm doing that right now.

Anyway, so within the keystone arc, the Obscura wizard within that keystone that RP!Tagg's been carrying since the GC breaks loose (After feeding off RP!Tagg's dark feelings since before the AU.) and decides to take over the region again with an army of Spiritomb. To assist in this, he uses high level illusionary wards to seal Sinnoh off from the rest of the world and reduce the majority of the human and Pokemon population to spirits. Said spirits would pretty much consider everything normal except for the fact that electronics don't work, there's a twilight haze around everything, and the obvious of large amounts of Spiritomb.

Everyone, that is, except for humans and Pokemon who happen to be Souls, who also have a AOE around them that makes them graduate non-Souls they have a bond with from shadedom. Our main objective for the arc besides defeating the villain will be to de-twilight (for lack of a better term) Sinnoh, piece by piece, centered around the lakes.
