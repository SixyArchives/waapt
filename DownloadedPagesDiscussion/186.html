4635 SilentReverence17th Jan 2012 05:51:58 AM from 3 tiles right 1 tile up Get RP Mod
adopting kitteh
Have been writing this since yesterday, more or less...

I'm ambivalent towards either choice (Split or OT-Mode) myself, and can understand where both sides are coming from with reasons to not vote A besides yes vote B. For the Speeqers it will be an annoying extra burden to split, and even after enough experience is acquired I can foresee a lot of hair pulling at the interface and connection effects, but at the same time the OT model is not working as cleanly as it should, and it is barely having the desired effect of, well, prioritizing OT conversations.

The thing is, we have not been enforcing the "OT" part of the OT model as we should. OT discussion should take priority over randomness, and that does not only applies to being in subject but also to actually subject selection - many times it has happened already when we enter randomness; then some other players are too afraid to propose their ideas because they'll still be swallowed by the randomness, or, even if they're picked up, other players will complain start leaving while there's OT discussion, giving a very strong impression that OT mode is unenjoyable.

Of course, this and the whole thread reflection issue are something a third chatroom gets rid of, but at a larger cost. There is a concern, justified in the same above mechanics, that if we split into three chatrooms the RP one will "wither and die" because everyone will want to spend their time at offtopic, and juggling chatrooms is already hard enough for Speequers. As a result, plans discussed there would "bounce" between Chatroom and Discussion much more. While to that I would retort that you don't have to actually be always on all the chatrooms and that if people are willingly ignoring RP building discussion for randomness we can not always accommodate for them (the more with RL/RP delays), that also builds the exact same impression that RP building mode is unenjoyable.

In the end, I'm gonna be OK with whatever method you choose, so long as the ontopic chatroom, whichever it is, serves the RP first. I ask that both to those who are going with OT model and those who are willing/designated to implement it: please make sure that OT Mode is OT Mode, not "OT Mode if we feel like it".

Recently, we also discussed a worldbuilding element borrowed and tweaked from a fic that was most definitely not mine. It involves a civilization of Precursors, and will have applications for the end of the Hoenn arc and (likely) beyond. After a few discussion sessions, here's the current state of the idea:

A civilization of Alakazam and a civilization of Metagross arose long before human civilization (since they're two of the smartest non-legends, I figured they'd fill the sapient "niche" first), and for a while they cooperated with each other but always remained wary. Then, something started a war between the two. The war escalated until one of the civs tried to activate the Mt. Pyre Caldera. The Weather Trio then sacrificed themselves to stop it. Outraged at the deaths of their fellows, the other legendaries wiped the civilizations from the face of Pokearth, and they were reduced to an animalistic existence until humans showed up.

Any tweaks and other feedback are appreciated. In particular, we're still trying to figure out how their societies worked as well as future applications to the RP's plot (including a possible universe).
