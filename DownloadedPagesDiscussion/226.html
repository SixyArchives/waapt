So, with PMD fast approaching, I thought it would be a good idea to list out the planned subarcs, so people without one planned know what their options are.

Mine is a Generator Rex-themed plot, which doesn't really require knowledge of the show.

Basically, two villains and their henchmen (a Carnivine named Van Kleiss and his Lucario henchman Biowulf and a Zoroark named Black Knight and her Alakazam henchman Cesar) are both searching for the shards for their own nefarious purposes.

There are also two dungeons that house the villains: Desolate Abyss, Van Kleiss's home, which is an old, decrepit, really creepy forest with a broken down castle in the center, and Divine Monolith, Black Knight's base, which is a salt flat with a massive hollow rock filled with hallways and rooms and stuff in the center.
