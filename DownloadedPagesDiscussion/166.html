First of all, nice to see you back Jane! Will enjoy short cameo from you!

Also, regarding chatroom shenanigans.

Now I don't think I could say anything that other have said better, but I will put in my two cents. The chatroom is good for plot planning once the plot is in motion. The best example of this is the AU arc. Now, despite what you might think about that AU Arc and it's Repercussions on the RP, one cannot argue that the AU Arc was well thought out and the chatroom did help to that, planning of certain events during the AU Arc (How Pokepan was going to play out, Napoleon's Rebellion and his demise etc.) were planned in advance, but this was well after we discussed who were the villains, what the chartcters were, what their personalities were, overall plot and whatnot.

In other words: First of all you need a good foundation/ Otherwise it's risky from the start

Now let's compare that to the Orange Islands Arc.

Most of that arc was planned in the Chatroom itself and, let's face it, it wasn't well though out with hotchpotches of plot scattered here and there that was hastily strung together to make a hap-hazard story. People were getting involved in arcs they didn't really want to get involved in simply because there was nothing else to do. A sign of poor planning.

But I digress, That's not the real issue.

The issue bringing up plots in the Chatroom. Plots with potential that get shot down simply by one person saying "I don't like [X]/this makes me do [X] and I don't want to do [X] so I don't want [this plot] to happen". This will reside in one side try to reason with the other, the other side sticking with their P.O.V which leads to unwanted conflict, childish debates and then to outright flamewars that keep people up much longer than they should be up for the sheer purpose of arguing (Hence, why I've seen expends in time stay up which should not happen because sleep is godsend people).

And, let's be frank, it makes EVERYONE feel like shit afterwards (and I normally like being the "michealjacksoneatspopcorn.jpg" gal)

So all probable major plots should be discussed in the Discussion Thread from now on in. It was our friend during the planning of the AU arc, it will continue to be our friend in future arcs. I suggest if anyone sees another post a probable plot that is most likely to become major, would they ask them to post it here before a bitchfest rises again in the RP. Remember, this is supposed to be FUN, not a chore or a job.

I also feel that doing this will help us... work better together. For the last month it's been nothing but continual debating tennis between two or more parties. It's stopped people from posting as they would; it's stopped people from being able to create plots. Hell, these stupid arguments have stopped me from being able to wrap up some of the AU stuff that I really wanted to do and wrap up ages ago before my next Cadets Bivouac (Which is next weekend in fact).

So yeah... Rant over, post major plot (If you want to discuss plots you intend doing just by yourself, I wouldn't object to anyone doing that in the chatroom. Just no plots you intend for a majority of the group to participate in) and please don't snarl and growl at the other users now because of all this built up tension. Next post I will post my plot.

Also Jane. Feel free to nitpick my syntax in a graceful and intelligent manor as you DID promise me some grammar lessons .
