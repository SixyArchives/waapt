Hmm... I have several plans that I should probably get organized, fleshed out, and that I will probably need help with if there now must be time limits. Plus, some of my ideas had been kind of set up with the assumption that we were going to carry out our planned story arcs in the order of Kanjoh + RTAU —> Holon + Conquest —> Unova 2 —> ?

Remaining Kanjoh plans

- Have DS earn her remaining Johto and Kanto badges.

- Have Candida evolve in the Ice Path after a battle with a Piloswine, after Eli had prevented her from evolving a few times earlier.

- Resolve Eli's little sub-plot and have his would-be-self return.

- Have DS and Lillith become friends.

- Have Eli evolve, and in the process do a Split Personality Merge with his would-be-self.

- Have Sunburst evolve at some point after Eli has evolved.

- Possibly have the Sawk egg hatch at the very end of the arc, leading into my plans for Conquest somewhat. I'll get to that after...

RtAU

- Have AU!DS' destiny change thanks to the intervention of AU!Wolf and AU!Alastor after receiving the message from AU!Wolf's future self.

- Have AU!DS and AU!Cynthia have the small population of Sinnoh seek shelter in the underground, while they and possibly a few others stay to defend the region.

- Have AU!Goggles, AU!Chase, AU!Tulip and AU!Artemis return to Unova for the Resistance's reunion, and end up defend Unova.

- And when it's over, have AU!DS return the Lustrous Orb to Mt. Coronet, and AU!Cynthia revoke AU!DS' trainer's liscence. AU!Alastor may choose to ascend to a higher plane of existance as he feels he has fulfilled his role in this world.

Holon/Conquest

- I have absolutely no plans and no intention to participate in the Holon arc, however, I may have DS research the history of Ransei, which catches her new Sawk's interest.

- For my Conquest plans, Ancestor!DS will have become the warlord of wherever she is the warlord of. Ancestor!Veronica will be concerned about her safety and request to join her army, only for Ancestor!DS to turn her down, as she does not want her friend risking her life on the battlefield.

- Ancestor!Veronica links with a Zebstrika, and with the ability to link with Pokemon proving that she has what qualifies her to be a warrior, she decides to join Ancestor!DS' army in disguise.

- Ancestor!Sierra will be involved somehow, exact details I'll probably go over with Wolf.

- Ancestor!Lillith might also be there, and often includes an order in her army's missions to have any female warriors prettier than her assassinated, but said order is always ignored.

- Ancestor!DS discovers Ancestor!Veronica's true identity after a while, and Ancestor!DS allows her to stay in her army.

Unova 2

- DS and some of her Pokemon, including the Sawk that has grown a little older by now, head back to Unova to explore the new places and stuff.

- DS encounters a Blitzle that has been staying at Floccessy Ranch, and after staying there a few days, slowly has the Blitzle warm up to her.

- The first time the Blitzle allows DS to pet her, DS has a vision revealing some of the Blitzle's history with Melinda.

- The Blitzle allows DS to catch her, after challenging her to a battle for it, of course.

- DS encounters a Zorua named Mirage in Virbank City, whose current trainer has had difficulty finding someone to adopt her due to her apparent inability to use her illusion powers.

- DS adopts Mirage.

- DS takes a trip to Pokestar Studios, where Mirage discovers her love of movies, and I introduce Heather and another actress character.

- DS may or may not attempt to get the new badges introduced in B/W 2.

- Possible sub-plot involving Dream World Pokemon having started travelling to the real world by large numbers.

- DS at some point travels to Black City and discovers Melinda's secret, and helps the Pokemon imprisoned in her basement escape.

- Melinda kidnaps DS to prevent her secret from getting out, but DS' Blitzle (probably a Zebstrika by then) manages to escape along with her Dewott friend, who was among the Pokemon DS rescued.

- Lillith may join in on rescuing DS, and adopts the Dewott.

- Mirage (who is most likely a Zoroark by then) may finally use her illusion powers for the first time by using them to distract Melinda long enough to allow DS to be rescued.

Orre (may as well include what I've got for this one too)

- Something involving Jayna getting screentime.

- Something with Lillith, possibly involving saving some of the Pokemon that Melinda had sold to Cipher. DS and Lillith may end up obtaining a shadow Pokemon each.

- Showing that Orre is more than just Cipher and Shadow Pokemon, seeing as that is part of Lillith's character.

That's what I've got so far, although I may have forgotten to include a few things.

9579 TracerBullet8th Jan 2013 05:40:20 PM from A Dark and Rainy Alley Get RP Mod
Guess Who...?

An Important, If Somewhat Abrupt Announcement

I have a matter that is a bit hard for me to share... And one that I never saw myself having to be mindful of around 6 months ago...

Over the last half week, I have been feeling incredibly drained as a RPer, and as if I was increasingly divorced from real control over how to run my own characters in a struggle just to keep up with other ongoing activities. In the end, I can't really fault anyone beyond myself for this. They're my characters, and I should have noticed something was amiss earlier. As such, I have decided that the best thing for me to do to maintain my sanity while not wholly shutting myself off from the outside world would be to enter a low-activity state and try and focus on things that I REALLY don't want to have to juggle when RtAU enters full swing.

As such, starting tomorrow, I intend to enter a "2 posts a day, max" state for the next week, perhaps sooner if I feel refreshed before then, and perhaps until I return back to school if I can't muster my energy back. I will make one post after getting up in the morning, and one before going to sleep at night. With a "wildcard" slot used for pre-planning/writing, character testing, or making a third post if I feel up to it.

During this low activity state, my characters should be considered on autopilot. If you need to get something done, assume that they will help you... Within reason. This is NOT your chance to rob RP!Tracer blind, take his mons for yourself, get him in trouble with the law, or otherwise do anything that would be a potential headache for me. This is your license to have him act as a transport / lodging agent if he already knows you. If you want / need to put words into the mouths of one of my characters for whatever reason please ask before you do anything.

The topics of my 2/3 posts a day will depend on whatever I feel like, though will most likely consist of some combination of:

  • Katpost
  • Finally getting Tracer's body back in Almer
  • Continuing Othersaga
  • Reaction to day's events with main group + cutesy asides
  • RtAU forshadowing
  • One Shots

In THAT order of likelihood. If there is something that you wanted to do with me that involves rapid exchange of action or dialogue, I would kindly request that you spaghetti the event with me over IM / Google Doc. It's less intimidating than an open-ended chain of 20 shortposts, and has more room for editing and refinement. I will also be withdrawing a bit from Creative, but will still be very much alive and lurking Social.

I deeply apologize if I've concerned you or thrown a wrench in your plans with this announcement, and thank you for your understanding.

9582 BadWolf218th Jan 2013 07:49:51 PM Get RP Mod
The Watcher on the Wall
Hmm...

If I'm not mistaken, the arcs that take place in another universe/time period are RTAU and Conquest. So the end of Kanjoh, PEFE, Holon, Orre, and Unova 2 all take place in the OU's Present (with a capital P)

Let's assume that, with no major holidays coming up, we can possibly finish Kanjoh by the end of January. Note that any times I give during this post are estimates, not hard dates to be done stuff.

Kanjoh
  • Since there's really not anything in Cianwood except the Gym, I think it's possible to be done in Cianwood by Sunday night. Because it's just the Gym, we can stay in Olivine, and only head across the water when we actually want to challenge the Gym.
  • If we go that way, all that's left in Olivine is the Gym, too, so I think it's possible to get through both that and Mahogany (another very small town, although it has the Lake of Rage) within a week to a week and a half. That would bring us to sometime between the 21st and 24th.
  • If we can hit that mark, we can spend the rest of the month in Blackthorn and the surrounding area.
  • This means that the Elite 4 would be done in early February, which would wrap up Kanjoh nicely.
  • Again, these are just estimates. There is flex room, here.

PEFE
  • PEFE is in a mostly undisclosed location, but I've always personally assumed that it's stationed in Kanjoh.
  • Since we're already in Kanjoh, this would be the next logical step. I don't know exactly what the plan is here, but I figure that if we're done the Johto League in early February, this can take roughly the rest of the month. PEFE, despite all the spacial wonkiness that occurs around it, is still only one building complex.

Holon
  • I have no idea where Holon is. But let's assume that it's in Pokepan. I usually default to that, unless there's evidence to the contrary.
  • I also don't know what people want to do, here.
  • My thinking is that we can give the months of March and April fully over to Holon.

Orre
  • Orre = Arizona. Therefore, it is between Pokepan, and Unova, and is a logical next stop.
  • It seems like we need more time here than in the previous arcs. Let's say three months. So May, June, and July will be spent in Orre.

Unova 2
  • It's much later than I would like (almost a year after release), but if it makes everyone's lives easier, Unova 2 can be the last arc before Gen VI.
  • There are still five months left in the year at this point. That is a lot of time. So if some of the previous arcs run a little long, we still have room for Unova 2. Consequently, if we finish it early, we don't have to hang around.

Gen VI
  • I would like to start this as close as possible to launch, but I understand if that ends up being in December. I would like to be in the new Region before Christmas, if at all possible. We can deal with how we'll handle the plot later, but if you're one of the people who can't get the game before Christmas, please try to understand that while we'll wait for you, we can't wait forever.

IMPORTANT!!!

I propose a moratorium on Gen VI until we are there to do the arc. That means no Gen VI Pokemon, no characters from the game (including Gym Leaders, Elite 4, Professors, etc.), no references to the plot, and no player characters from the region.

The only exceptions to the above would be allusions to the Box Legendaries, a la Reshiram and Zekrom back when we were starting Gen V (although holding off on too much information until close to going would be preferable), and if we get wind of any characters from previous games showing up. So if, for example, we find out that Looker is going to be in X and Y, we can hear that he's going to be there.

RTAU and Conquest
  • These are completely divorced from the Present Timeline, so can be done as we please. RTAU first, then Conquest. We have a lot of time to get through both of them, although perhaps Conquest shouldn't start until around Orre, to make getting through the earlier stuff easier. That's around when we have a lot more time.

So yeah...there it is...
