Armbands Discussion

I'll start this by saying it is completely possible I have misconstrued what someone may have said in this entire kerfuffle, so if I did, please tell me.

In the chat today there was a talk about the current state of the armbands in the RP, specifically whether the system of how they are distributed and owned is one we wish to keep.

Currently there are about 8(?) armbands in the possession of about five players/characters, and they are more or less owned by these characters, with the spread of the armbands seen as a loan in which the loaner expects to have the armband returned. Some people think that this system is restrictive and possibly unfair, disallowing for many players, new and old alike, to obtain them for their own purposes and freely move about with them.

One suggested solution was that the concept of ownership of the armbands cease to exist as it does, that the giving of armbands become not lending them but just passing them on, so it can be passed on again, and again. No one owns them, they're passed on as soon as you aren't using them and someone else wants to. This could let the armbands be spread about more effectively than if a small group had constant possession of them and expected them to be returned, potentially undoing any of the armbands' progress in spreading to new hands. How this would affect the armbands of Ammy, for example, or Skye and Diane, who use them for different purposes, is undecided, although possibly it would affect them less.

In the cases of armbands which don't see constant use, it shouldn't be too much of a problem to have them move about. Although, if there is a problem with this, please bring it up and talk about it.

To clarify the above: Armbands lose absolute ownership, they move around freely as demand changes, how this applies to Ammy/Skye and Diane(?) unclear.

A concern is whether or not this is making people think armbands are a necessity and he/she absolutely must get them for his/her characters. This seems like a small concern to me, but if anyone thinks otherwise, please say so.

Anything else that I missed, which could very easily be a lot? Thoughts, particularly from those who do own armbands and were not present in the Chat discussion?
