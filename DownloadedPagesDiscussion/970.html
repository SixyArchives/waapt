Let me just say three things

  • 1) We are not saying that you can't have characters who are ambiguous or have a white heritage. We just want more people to think about it when creating characters.

  • 2) Researching stuff for creating minority characters isn't that big of a barrier as you might think. Tagg has linked the wonderful Writing With Color blog that you can all have a peek at. I have also generally found Googling "____ Culture" or "____ History" are good jumping points. From personal experience, I have found some great websites such as this one by googling "Romani Culture" and having an afternoon read on a rainy day. It's been simmering in some of our minds (Tagg, Every, Myself etc.) for a while now. We just decided to bring it to the forefront as of recent.
