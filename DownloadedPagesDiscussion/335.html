OK, figured I might as well post some of my future plans here.

In order of when they'll probably happen...

Magic Mirror Subplot

Sometime after PEFE Exploitation starts, I plan on Umbra and Ammy discovering a Magic Mirror(tm) that leads them to a pocket dimension known as The Land of Makbaliv. There, they are assaulted by an EEEEEEEEEVIL force, and Umbra disappears. Ammy, naturally, goes on a quest to find him, and is joined by a group of mons known as the Seven in an epic plot to stop the ancient evil.

As you may have guessed, this is a tribute. However, I am going out of the box and taking a risk and trying something that ISN'T a video game reference.

It's actually gonna be an Affectionate Parody of various Disney movies. o3o

"Are you CRAZY, Umbra?!" you're likely saying right now. "This is too kiddy for this RP! We might as well do arcs based on Barney and Power Rangers! And you know very well Disney is evil/a fossil of old times/inferior to Grimmified tales!"

But that is exactly the perception I greatly dislike, and hope to subvert with this tribute. (I especially hate how trendy Grimmification has gotten lately; the trope page I just linked to seems to mostly be in favor of it, even. :/ So, this is also meant to be an antidote to that.)

But anyway, enough of me ranting. So far, my only tributes I've got planned are Snow White (Hence "The Seven") and Aladdin (Because Jafarbok is too cheesy a pun to resist. o3o) I need to know two things:

1. Which other movies to affectionately parody. Anything in the Animated Canon is fair game (Not Pixar, sadly), but I'm more likely to pick it if it's at least decent (Or if I have a soft spot/nostalgia for it, like Atlantis the Lost Empire or Dinosaur.)

A. Who wants to do this with me. I'd like someone to tag along or at least play some NP Cs so I'm not doing this by myself.
