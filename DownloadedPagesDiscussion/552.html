Me? I'd say I'm one of the oldest members, having been here from page 2 of Sinnoh through page 11, 294 and counting of Holonquest, and hopefully beyond. When I joined up I thought, "Oh hey, this forum game is pretty much an RP without the commitment", since I'd retired from RPing beforehand due to starting college the year before and finding it too hard to continue. Of course, it would appear fate had something different in mind two going on three years later, since that little forum game I thought would be just a minor time waster is now a full RP in its own right.

Are there things I've regretted doing or wished I could've done better? Oh, most certainly. There are times when I've wanted to give everyone a high five, and others when I've wanted to introduce my palm to the backs of nearly everyone else's head. It certainly hasn't been the easiest road, and I recognize that others could consider all we've created to be a massive pile of crap, even the stuff we've made following our growing pains at the very beginning of the RP. With that in mind, I realize that this RP isn't exactly everyone's cup of tea, especially considering our habit of breaking the unofficial rules of the fandom with gleeful abandon.

But me? When I look at the world we've all created, utterly batshit insane as it is, I'm filled with pride. I plan to be in WAAPT for as long as I'm able, no matter what life may throw at me in the future, and I hope we'll be able to get through at least two more Gens.
