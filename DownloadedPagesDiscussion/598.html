14926 Verax28th Sep 2013 04:13:03 PM Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I'd need a PowerPoint presentation
Leader of Mechonis

Orre Arc Plot Direction Idea

Since the Orre arc has no real clear plot direction as of now, just a lot of individual plots in the same region with one threat, I had the idea of giving it some actual structure. Because the Cipher Bases will be raided, the sequence of these raids, if set, would give an overall sense of plot to the arc instead of the characters just handling their own thing, which they will anyways, but still.

THE SUGGESTED SEQUENCE, AS FOLLOWS;

1. Shadow Pokémon Lab and/or The Under

This is so that the characters learn who got what, where their Pokémon went and various other expository things so that the ball gets rolling. Note: This obviously not going to stop production, just distribution. They won't be producing many more after it.

NOTE: As Silent said in the chat: Hitting Distribution, which is the *business* backbone of Cipher, first means we can no longer rely on distribution and travel patterns to locate Cipher members - they'll become harder to pinpoint. Second, it puts them in the defensive, meaning both they will be more aggressive when found and we'll be exposing our teammates and civilians to more immediate danger. Once we hit Distribution, we will no longer be able to pursue Cipher with the usual method. There will probably a lull in organization until they are "solid" enough again that we can hit them without hitting water.

Take this into account!

2. R&D & Resources

After the first raid, and the initial momentum, the group splits off and handles their own shpeal. They follow their own story, since a lot of the issues are stemming from these two, it's possible most of the characters go on to focus on these groups. Along the way, these two are eventually taken care of, but it's likely, that unlike the first and last, there is not an entire group effort to put these ones down.

3. Operations

As the only one left, and the one a lot of people seem to rely on being hit last, Operations remains until the end, and the assualt on the Operations HQ may be the big climactic event of Orre.

More notes

If anyone has any thoughts or objections on the above, please speak up.

As well, I feel there should be a general schedule of when these things happen. Such as we put when each HQ is assaulted at around either Early, Mid, or Late X-Month. Thoughts on that?

Also just because a department is targeted doen't equal their Admin being defeated, and in fact Admin battles are not listed here, and probably will not be, as this does not pretain to specific battles, just a general sequence
