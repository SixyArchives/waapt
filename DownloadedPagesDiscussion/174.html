4347 SilentReverence20th Dec 2011 10:13:40 PM from 3 tiles right 1 tile up Get RP Mod
adopting kitteh
Well, uhm... I don't know of a sweetcoated way of saying this, but...

The last days in the chatroom have sucked. Hard. And the issues born there have serious enough. Heck, we could have scared a new member away from the RP people.

So, in the interest of avoiding the heavier chatroom drama in the roleplay chatroom, another one has been created for us to vent and squeeze Azurrill stress balls and hug each other and provide virtual emotional support. Won't post the details here, us members can go into the normal chatroom and ask anyway. Those logs won't be public at the moment, due to the expected sensibility of the conversations, until Tangent and I figure out how to make something usable happen.

On another issue, and precisely as a result of the discussion originated in the chatroom, there will be some changes in the RP that I will now announce and that are more or less an emergency consensus reached after a pretty heated point.

Base of the issue is, Luke wanted to get rid of some characters, Ret Gone them not only from the RP but also from meta, which could have caused quite some issues from a TV Tropes perspective. Since the premise was to avoid those characters from having any more representation in the RP, some measures will be taken. Also, taking advantage from this situation, some measures to sort out the Characters page somewhat will be taken.

  1. First of all, those characters will not be mentioned by Luke anylonger, and should not be mentioned to Luke's character or, if possible, in any context that requires his interaction. From his internal, in-character perspective they should be Put on a Bus leaning towards Ret Gone.
  2. Mentioning the characters is still acceptable in other contexts, in particular in those that are unavoidable (eg.: mentioning them when talking about an event they were part of). Just try to exercise common sense and not cause unnecessary tension by ignoring the above constraints.
  3. The characters were removed from the Characters Page. Instead, they will be moved to a new page We Are All Pokemon Trainers Retired.
  4. Taking advantage of the creation of that new page, retired characters will be moved to that page. That includes, at the moment: A, Jane, Luke's mons of issue, some of Silent's mons and... whatever else I'm forgetting. That page will be linked to from the normal Characters page.
  5. Removing references Luke's mons of issue from the Main (Roleplay) page should not be accepted, at risk of entering edit wars. We authors are supposed to not have more weight than any other troper on those pages, and even if the characters are retgoned, even that would fit a trope that goes on the page.
  6. Instructions on who the mons of issue are and what is their (meta) situation will be added to the UND, so that, for example, we can warn new players about stuff to avoid.

Now, before anyone complains that this situation is being given too much operational weight, two things to consider: the original plan was much heavier than this and was intended to originate changes even to the meta layer of the RP - it was essentially "these characters never existed, period"; second, at this point we already lost one player to discussions, apparently, so let's avoid losing another if we can reach middle grounds.

Also, on that very issue, I repeat what I have stated before — we seem to be, socially speaking, not ready to have a chatroom. Ironically, now that means we have two. Oh well, if it helps...

Changes to the Characters Page and the creation of the new page, plus edition of the UND, will be carried out tomorrow (Wed).
