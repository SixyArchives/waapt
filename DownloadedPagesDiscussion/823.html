So, Blitz and I have been talking, and I told him of how some of the other songs in the Kagerou Project series would be cool to do for a plot.

Then we kind of got talking, and he said 'Would it be separate mini plots, or would it all be a sub-arc?'

So...I guess I'm here to ask if I could do a sub-arc by any chance?

Here's how we thought it would work. Every once in awhile, the characters involved in the arc would find themselves in the other universe that spawned from Celebi's time loop, and would have to try and escape back to their own universe. We thought that since it wasn't continuously in the other world, it wouldn't be too big of a distraction from the main arc, and that it would be cool to let the newer people try it out.

Blitz pointed out that since there's been a decent amount of other timelines, we should really ask before we try anything else, and make sure one more time line wouldn't be too big of a deal.

Another thing is, that in the song series, each of the main characters have an eye power, which lets them do different things. One girl can become invisible, One boy reads minds, another girl attracts people's attention, that sort of thing.

I was thinking that every time a character leaves the other world, they would gain a power, and they would have to learn how to use it, as the characters in the original series had to learn to control it as well.

To make sure the abilities aren't over powered, I suggested that once they finally escape the world for good, they wouldn't be able to use their powers again, and they would go back to normal, while Blitz suggested that they could only use their powers in the other universe.

All of this stuff is mostly just ideas, and the arc as a whole isn't completely planned out yet, so we both decided to come here and talk it out with the more experienced people. :)

I hope you give the newer people a chance to do something like this, as well as help us along the way. :D

If you want to listen to the songs, then here is a playlist of all of them (Some songs are more about the characters than plot, but I think we could use them anyways, and kind of change the ideas around a bit): https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXNX3lPZVdpcOe1nqidHVUF5sDzLUXHhc
