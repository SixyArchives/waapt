Ok, here's my two cents on the matter.

One: I agree that the racial diversity of characters in the RP is near Golden Oreo status, and I think this is a potential issue for new players. There is certainly a way to increase racial diversity, but as it is a subject that could prove... problematic, we need to tread lightly.

Two: I think that putting it as a separate line on the Signup sheet is possibly problematic, as like Wolf said, most of the characters correspond in ethnicity to their controllers, and (at least to my knowledge) most of the R Pers are white. This is not to say that people can't play characters of different ethnicities, but the tendency is to write what you know. And we know our own ethnicities.

I do agree that it needs to be touched on, but I think forcing people to decide on an ethnicity up front may lead people to think that Race relations are more important in a relatively politic free Pokemon RP than they are. As Wolf pointed out, race is only pointed out when relevant to the topic at hand, which is rare.

I feel like leaving ethnicity in the appearance section leaves enough of an impact to get people thinking about it, as opposed to having it be a line on the sheet itself.
