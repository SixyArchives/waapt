Name: Zrone Dyspav

Personality: Zrone is a semi-paranoid man of intellect, with little skill at battling, science, or manipluating. He is very goal and schedule oreiented, but does enjoy setting time aside for personal pleasure, or enjoying hobbies such as gaming, logic puzzles, or tinkering. He's a man who understands his limits and when he decides to do something it gets done. Despite his lack of talent at any particular area, he has unnatural tenacity and is an excellent strategist, scared to confront his enemies, he is keen to set up events so that their own actions assist Zrone's immediate goals, whatever they may be, and in the event they fall apart due to some trick of fate or luck, tends to have an escape route or three prepared. Anything regarding the furthering of humanity to be independent from Pokémon is of interest to him. He's also fond of electricity, magnetisim, and the mechanics of light, and spends time modifying where ever he hides out with puzzles that encorperate these to entertain himself.

Well, the thing about bracelets is this: my logic was to create something that would allow people interested in long-term plans involving Pokémon-human transformations to have consistent, consolidated access to it for that one particular character while still providing interesting "conflicts" in the form of glitches.

My reasoning behind "consistent" and "consolidated" is that armbands are extremely important items. In any circumstance, they are never given lightly, and always gravitate to certain characters by default. If someone wants to try and progress a story involving one, they need to ask and then receive one IRP. This can be difficult if those characters with one available are occupied.

Also, as it has been stated ad nauseum, armbands are Artifacts with a capital A. They are rare, limited, and arcane. Bracelets are not restricted in the same manner, but the rather extreme application process as well as glitch-induced complications are also in place so as not to "cheapen" the value of a transformative trinket.
