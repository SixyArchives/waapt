Since I'm on the subject, thinking about this subject made me sort of come up with a backstory of sorts in my mind, even if it's only based on the limited information we have, and the assumption that one or more Fairy type legendaries will be introduced.

Perhaps a group of Fairy type legendaries (the type may have been known as the Magic type back then) decided that they didn't trust regular Pokémon and the humans those Pokémon lived with to use the powers of the Magic type responsibly (sp?), and thus convinced the other legendaries to have it sealed away, thus giving the Magic/Fairy typed legendaries exclusive access to it.

However, due to some sort of disaster, possibly related to something in Kalos' backstory, but again, this is only based on what little we know so far, the legendaries decided to restore the Magic type to the Kalos region, but everyone started calling it the Fairy type since they had forgotten what the type was called after it had been sealed away for many years, and none of the legendaries bothered to correct them.

So, possibly the legendaries have since then been slowly restoring the eighteenth type in certain areas (and by that I mean regions that'll be introduced post Gen VI) and been observing the results until the Fairy typed legendaries are convinced to allow the type to be restored to the entire world.

Or something like that. Again, this is only based on what little information we have, and assumptions that there might be a Fairy legendary.
