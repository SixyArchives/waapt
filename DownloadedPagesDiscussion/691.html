And now for something completely different

OK, there was some discussion in the logs of the Attributes of the new Legendaries, and we now have two possible configurations.

  • Xerneas = Mind, Yveltal = Soul, Zygarde = Heart

  • Xerneas = Soul, Yveltal = Heart, Zygarde = Mind

Arguments for former: Third Legend always gets Mind and it's not fair to mortal Minds. Plus, Zygarde is a meditator, which fits Heart.

Arguments for latter: Fits personalities established by chartesting far better. Plus, souls are associated with life, and death often is portrayed with a compelling voice.

I'm personally fine with either, but supporting latter, since it allows Yveltal to possibly be a (very deep-down) Jerk with a Heart of Gold.
