14524 0blivionmobile1st Sep 2013 07:44:54 AM from Oooooooooooooooooooooklahoma~! Get RP Mod
You Wouldn't Like Me When I'm Bored
I tend to agree with Umbra's second point. Fall and winter are very "high stress" seasons due to either school, holidays, or both. The weather also doesn't usually help when people are crabby after getting snowed in.

In general, if you ever get the inkling that one of your ideas might be taken less than openly, I recommend writing it down somewhere private, getting a good night's sleep, then reading it over again in the morning. Preferable over breakfast, which unless your running really late for something, is possibly the least stressful part of the morning.

On the first subject, I think, yes, time should be balanced between overrepresented and underrepresented legendaries, but we should also avoid over saturating the RP in the process. Maybe have a Legendary we see a lot (Ho-oh, one of the Creation Trio) send us off to find a Legendary they haven't seen in a while or ever (Ho-oh is concerned about Lugia's health, the Swords of Justice want to meet Genesect to gauge his intentions now that he's free, Groudon is concerned that a Heatran is about to cause a volcanic eruption, etc.)
