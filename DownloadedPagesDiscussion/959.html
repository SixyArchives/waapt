 23971 Crewe, Mon, 24th Nov '14 7:04:11 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
Li'l Punk
It's a TV show. It's kinda like Lord of the Flies with teens and a character focus, if the kids from Lord of the Flies eventually got their shit together and also accidentally started a war with natives. With some political intrigue thrown in as a side plot.

Basic plot: 97 years after nuclear apocalypse, the remnants of humanity live on a space station called the Ark, and 100 teenage delinquents (and one stowaway) are sent down to Earth to see if it's survivable, the idea being that if they all die horrible deaths, it's not a big deal because they're criminals (all criminals over 18 are executed, so there aren't any adults around to send). On the ground it follows the power struggles and struggle for survival among the 100 (mostly between Clarke, the main character, and her handful of friends and Bellamy, the stowaway, and his charisma/minions/fear of execution). It also follows what goes on among the political elite of the Ark, including Clarke's mom and some other politicians, and the decisions they make to try to save humanity.

And it's full of strong female and non-white characters (of the core 7 characters of the teens, only two are white guys, and of the core 3 of the adults, one is a white guy).

Fair warning, though, it's not for the faint of heart. This show moves along at light speed and takes no prisoners. And it gets intense. These characters are in a seriously messed-up situation and have to make some messed-up decisions to survive, and then they have to deal with the consequences. And it is so good.

The first season is on Netflix, and the second is currently airing.

EVERYONE SHOULD WATCH IT BECAUSE IT IS SO GOOD, YOU GUYS. Admittedly the first episode is the weakest and the first few have some eye-roll-worthy moments, but it grows into itself pretty quickly. I was totally hooked by the third or fourth episode.
