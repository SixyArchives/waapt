12036 BadWolf2114th Jun 2013 07:40:02 AM Get RP Mod
The Watcher on the Wall
No, I agree. At least say "Hey guys, I want to do a Gen VI cameo."

I find there to be a bit of a difference between using a new Pokemon as an NPC, and a player character (even an alternate one) being related to a new Pokemon in some form. Lillith having a Fennekin Pokeform works because it introduces the idea of Gen VI Pokeforms, instead of being locked into choosing one of the current 649, and she's probably not going to be spending all that much time as a Pokemon between now and the Kalos arc, anyway. Likewise, Forte having a Sylveon aspect is fine because it makes less sense if he doesn't have a Sylveon aspect.

The point of the moratorium on info from Gen VI is, in my mind, so that we can maintain a sense of wonder going into the Kalos arc, instead of getting there and being like "Oh, yeah, I know Pokemon that are a bunch of these species already." Cameos exist to give little teases and tastes of the idea that there's someplace else where there are Pokemon we've never even heard of. I personally think it defeats the purpose for the new Pokemon to show up in a normal RP context. It...I dunno, it takes away the specialness of them being new.

That's my opinion, at least. I'm not going to say "You can't do it" because I don't have the right to do that. But I think from now on, a little heads-up about Gen VI cameos would be appreciated, just so that their use can be justified instead of making people feel like there are others with more privileges than themselves when it comes to decision-making about these things.
