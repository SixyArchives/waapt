So... uh... after talking to some other PEFEers and RPers*
a pretty limited sample though, which is one of the reasons I bring it up here
, I've seen a growing feeling that the GC arc is, with all, taking too long. Statistically, this single arc has so far taken more than 1/5th the total posts of this thread, and it has been running for more than a month; perhaps more than any other individual arc. The Recap.WeAreAllPokemonTrainers page is also an indication — the GC arc alone is split in three entries.

Now, I get that part of it is the consequences of a Tournament Arc structure, and how some battles have been delayed somewhat because of timezones and real life; even contrasting that this time of the year could, in my experience, be the easier to carry plots around. I guess the entrance of extra characters, PEFEers included, makes the overall dynamics more complicated, as does the fact that for an occasion like this too many boxed characters are brought in for a ride.

That said, I think I can quote "END IT ALREADY DAMN IT" (and whatever the trope is for that, apparently Arc Fatigue) from enough people that I should ask, in representation of other members, if given the situation that the GC continues for still much longer IRL, stronger measures are considered as to finish the remaining battles in a (relatively) clean and quick manner. It is true there are few battles remaining; it is also true however that those will likely be the most well-thought ones, both in real life and in terms of roleplay.

There is a conversation log that I will link to accordingly; there are also two base strategies that have been posted to be offered, one by me, as the most extreme measures that could be taken if it comes to the worst and this drags for too long; however, I would like to first sample opinions and see a guesstimate of how longer will the arc take IRL, the particular dynamics of the RL!characters's posting habits considered. Surely we can avoid the worst scenarios.

Also I'd like to ask for the opinion of how long would next arcs be taking in comparison to this one. I guess one of the first things we'll hear here is "No more Tournament Arcs please"... Considering that what comes next is a MST arc and a alternate universe arc, both of which might change plotting dynamics strongly.

Not that this RP has too much of a tight plot planning anyway; I have the understanding it's not exactly the ideal.
