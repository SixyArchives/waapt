Alright, so CHARACTER PROFILE UP IN YO GRILL:

Name: James "Jim" Hudson

Gender: Male

Region of Origin: Unova

Age: 17

Appearance: Tall and thin. Brown, slightly shaggy hair with similarly brown eyes.

Personality: Jim is decidedly quieter and more awkward than either his sister or cousin, and has difficulty getting a word in edgewise when in a conversation with either of them. He is ever so slightly touchy about the perceived girliness of his two favored types.

Backstory: Jim is a normal teenage boy from Virbank City. Due to his adoration of his aunt (Joseph's mum), Jim decided to pursue a similar type specialty to her. As such, every one of his Pokemon has either a Normal or a Fairy typing. Similarly to Joseph, he is massively knowledgeable about Pokemon biology, care and battling (though his knowledge came from lots and lots of studying, rather than a job as a breeder).

Pokemon:

  • Elizabeth the Cinccino (Female): Elizabeth is dutiful and intelligent, and serves as something of a snarky butler.

  • Percival the Clefable (Male): Percival (or Percy for short) considers himself a knight, and is every inch as brave and combat capable as the title implies. He has difficulty getting it through his fluffy, cuddly exterior, though.

  • Marigold the Flabebe (Female): Caught in Kalos, Marigold is a spacy Cloudcuckoolander. Imagine a tiny, flowery Bjork, and you've got Marigold.

  • Sourball the Swirlix (Male): Caught in Kalos, Sourball is belligerent and rude. He is convinced that he is the sole genius in a world of idiots, and though he actually has a very good intellect, he exaggerates it. Sourball curses how goofy his species looks.

  • Jane the Ralts (Female): Caught in Kalos, Jane is a Ladette to the core. As she grows, she'll become less tomboyish and more of a foul-mouthed sorehead. She can be pleasant willingly though, unlike Sourball.

  • Annie the Chansey (Female): Typical nurse. She is very patient with her team, but has a hidden dark side. When she evolves, she'll be surprisingly adept with special attacks.

  • Laura the Zangoose (female): A hatchling. She ends up seeing Wolvie as her big brother. As she grows, she's a combination of the childlike enthusiasm of Wolvie and the snark of Newt.
