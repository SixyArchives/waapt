Name: Juliet Staley

Age: 23

Gender: Female

Position: Illusion Specialist

Appearance: In contrast to her superior, Juliet appears much sunnier. She has masses of wheat blond hair, and her face (almost permanently in an expression of cheer) is freckled. She is quite short (about five foot two), even shorter than Kyrie.

Personality: Juliet is, in a word, delightful. She is genuinely charming and friendly to everyone, always around for a cheerful conversation or an apropos pop culture reference.

Around Alys, however, she becomes...different. Whatever cheerful sun with cool shades serving as her heart seems to become eclipsed. She becomes morose, bitterly snarky, but nonetheless serving at the order of her boss. Juliet is terrified of Alys, but the spidery Admin seems to like Juliet a great deal. It's unknown whether this is platonic, romantic, or gastronomical.

Juliet has friends all over the Illusion project, but she would say that her closest friend is a newcomer working under the crazy redhead woman; a blonde, just like her. The two really are tight friends, but Alys is almost everywhere in the facility...and she's not happy that her favorite Specialist is getting too buddy-buddy with someone else.

Pokemon

Blobette the Female Goodra: In contrast to the Big Fun archetype of Goodra, Blobette is a stone-faced bodyguard to Juliet. Quite fond of her trainer, she rarely speaks.

Alvy the Male Whimsicott: Snarks a bit (and something of a Pungeon Master), but is mostly goodhearted. Juliet dotes on him.

Annie the Female Azumarill: Sweet natured and gentle, despite her HUGE POWER. Juliet dotes on her like her Whimsicott. (She's a fan of Annie Hall.)
