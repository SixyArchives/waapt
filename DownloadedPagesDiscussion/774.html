Alright, now. CHARACTER PROFILE UP IN YO GRILL

Name: Mimi "Mimmy" Cloutier

Gender: Female

Region of Origin: Kalos (Laverre City)

Age: 11

Appearance: Mimmy, while (very recently) turned eleven, looks younger than she is. She is short, with pale skin, lively blue eyes, and curly, girlish green hair. Mimmy, rather like Fortis, enjoys wearing girly clothes.

Personality: Mimmy is a contradiction. She is young, girlish, and stereotypically cutesy, and usually acts the part. However, beneath that exterior lies intelligence, cunning and determination unexpected of a girl of her age. Despite this, she is still a preteen girl, and is occasionally prone to bouts of immaturity or tantrums.

Mimmy is a coordinator, and moved up to the senior circuit after dominating the juniors. Unlike some of her other competitors, she takes a very active role in creating and choreographing her appeals.

She has an older sister in Noelle. Mimmy half adores her and half manipulates her.

Mons

Snowdrop the White Florges: Mimmy adores the Flabebe line, so Snowdrop is often one of the first mons she turns to. In terms of personality, Snowdrop is quiet and antisocial, capable of being kind and nasty in equal measures.

Freyr and Freyja (shiny) the Male and Female Sylveon: This couple is adorable. They are almost always seen with each other, they seem to be of one mind, they respect each other deeply...and they fight crime. :3 (They will have run ins with Bonnie and Clyde)
