I going to resist making the obvious reply to the above in favor of laying down what will be happening during the Mammor Battle.

As I said before in an earlier post, The Battle portion of this will be split into two phases. However, this is slightly misleading due to events in the battle. So there are no nasty surprises, here's a run down:

Phase 0 - Ren Calls for Aide

Ren is going to be contacted by Mammor and be challenged to what is undoubtedly a trap at [a unpopulated/ abandoned rocky area/quarry or city district] in Fiore. So instead of walking into a scenario that he has no chance of surviving alone, Ren will contact the J-Team to bring with him to help him - which is what Mammor wants - and try to bring more forces than NG and Mammor can handle - which Ren does.

Phase 1 - Battle Phase

This is Exactly What It Says on the Tin - the J-Team Members vs NG and Ren vs Mammor. However, to help with the amount of RL!people that I'd like to see involved be so large, I'd like to offer those that where not on the list to consider to RP a NG member rather that one of the others can battle (Although any PC can join if they have an in-universe to know about the battle). Said NG members can be one-off characters or be used as a framework as a new NG if NG ever makes it back into the limelight again. With that in mind, I'd like to request not making the characters NG Admins (unless they already are one - Io and Allen are currently the only NG admins atm); as well as remembering that any NG member was hand chosen by Mammor to be a part of this attack - so make them competent to a fair degree, but I'm open to arguments on both those requests. Other than that, feel free to make the NG member however.

Of course, if it comes to it, please be prepared to write your own vs NG battle. As for the Battle themselves, Please limit to no more than 4v4 battles, as we don't want to drag out this event unnecessarily. Also not that while there WILL be competent battlers, there will ALSO be lots of rank-and-file NG Grunts for "one vs a group battles."

Transition Phase - Somebody Set Up Us the Bomb

After Ren snags Mammor's Shadow mon, Mammor will be a sore loser and detonate LOTS of incendiary bombs made with white phosphorus and thermite/ napalm - meaning one is more likely to die from severe burns than from being thrown by kinetic energy or shrapnel (the latter is almost nonexistent in incendiary bombs).

This is where you all need to be clever. Mammor is, to a degree, Dangerously Genre Savvy and will have taken measure to prevent the bombs being found/disabled and LOL!Teleport-Or-Protect-To-Esape! So be clever and think of a way to negate the flames!

Phase two - Humiliation Conga 2.0

This Phase starts when Ren drops Mammor of the edge of [an elevated area] where Ren and Mammor had their battle onto [a sightly lower area] near [a character who has yet to be determined]. Said character will take a turn at humiliating Mammor in a non-lethal and not-permanently debilitating fashion. This will continue until every character has taken a turn.

Please give any feedback you feel this needs as well as availability.
