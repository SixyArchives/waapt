^x2 No one said that a deranged time pony necessarily would have been able to create an airtight prison.

But anywho, summon magic typically scales with respect to what's being summoned. e.g. Summoning a group of 4-5 normal Pikachu will take less time, effort/skill, and mana than attempting to summon a Kaiju Pikachu (assuming that such a summon would even be possible). In both cases, the summon will disappear off to wherever it came from shortly after its arrival (e.g. a turn in battle).

Were legendary summons possible in the AU arc, they'd be three things: Not available at the start (with good reason), insanely costly to prepare and use (again, with good reason), and ultimately wouldn't change the predicament that the legendaries captured by Dialga were in (having a whopping turn of freedom before being returned to whatever prison ye olde deranged time pony set up for them).
