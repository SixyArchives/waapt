Well, considering one of the characters seems to be based around the idea of Steel types resisting Ghost moves, it's kind of hard not to notice the change.

Of course, the Fridge Brillance page on the YMMV page for X and Y offers up a mundane explaination, in that dark types and ghost types may simply just have gotten smarter over the years and figured out how to get around the Steel types' defenses.

The one thing I find a little strange is Electric types being immune to paralysis.
