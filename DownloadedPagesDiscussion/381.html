All you need to know about Conquest's plot:

Everyone is fighting to unite all 17 Nations in order to acquire the Pokemon of Legend, which turns out to be Arceus. Nobunaga allows the player to do this in order to attempt to destroy Arceus, whose legend has blinded people with greed and caused them to mistreat their Pokemon. Arceus decides to leave Ransei for the time being to respect Nobunaga's wishes and to spur the PC to work towards training harder to become a better partner for it. Nobunaga reforms.

Aside from that, you could just look up info on the Nations and Warlords on Bulbapedia: http://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ransei, http://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Warlord
