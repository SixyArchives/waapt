OK, after talking a bit with Tangent I have a proposal to make. Hopefully it will help us get stuff done without too much rushing.

Basically, since Kanjoh-2 has gone on absurdly long already yet some people still aren't done, I say we might as well keep it going for at least a while as a "mega-arc," but have Rt AU going on as well, as well as starting PEF Exp soon.

It would help us get our plans done quicker, (eg. it's good for stuff I'm planning with Tracer) while providing a low-key sandbox for people who don't want to participate in other arcs, as well as newbies.

However... There's one thing that I'm worried about, and that's that it will encourage some players to be "hermits."

Keeping a mega-arc like that going is good for people who life gets in the way for a lot, (eg. Tangent, Silent, etc.) But I'm worried some players will use it as an excuse to isolate themselves from most of the others, which can already be kind of a problem.

One potential solution is to keep said mega-arc going only until the next more easily jump-in-able arc (most likely Unova-2) rather than making it a permanent thing. Though would like alternative solutions if anyone can think of them.
