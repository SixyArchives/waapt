Touhou Arc

So I was giving the arc some thought and had a few ideas of how to adjust it to fit the WAAPT verse:

I thought that maybe Gensokyo exists because Arceus was doing some experiments on whether or not humans should have powers of their own and if they should, what said powers would be, things got a bit out of control as he ended up making humans a bit too strong for the balance of the world, so he sealed Gensokyo away with it's residents who now live in their own little paradise. I think maybe Gensokyo, if the barrier was weak or something of that kind, could be accessed from the Entralink, but since it's a blocked area 99% of the time, nobody would know of that, with the exception of a certain Reality Warper that managed to move in and out of Gensokyo as she so desires.

Changes that would be made:

- All youkai are going to be humans, they'll keep their powers, but otherwise are just normal girls possibly with a longer lifespan than other humans, maybe even being of The Ageless type.

- The god of the Hakurei Shrine, which is unknown in the Touhou series proper and which is supposedly the god above all others would be Arceus. This means that Reimu Hakurei is Arceus's Shrine Maiden.

- The other two gods of the series, Suwako Moriya (controls land and water) and Kanako Yasaka (controls plant and wind) aren't going to be gods here, instead being two other shrine maidens along with Sanae. The legendary they're going to worship are going to be Groudon (or maybe Landorus?) and Kyogre, which is fitting since in the series proper there were two gods in the Moriya Shrine.

- The "Gensokyo is the place where myths and legends nobody believes in anymore" thing is going to be dropped entirely.

- The normal humans that live in the Human Village are all outsiders or descended from them, probably as a result of being spirited away by Yukari

However all of this make the Imperishable Night plot completely impossible to happen, so instead I'm going with the Emboidment of the Scarlet Devil plot:

The Scarlet Mist

A thick Scarlet Mist started to spread around Gensokyo and it's so think that is actually blocking sunlight! Noticing that, the shrine maiden Reimu Hakurei starts to investigate the source of this strange mist, eventually meeting with the J-Team members (after the little Info Dump prologue) and gets their help, eventually finding the source of the mist: the Scarlet Devil Mansion.

Changes from the original plot:

- Remilia, the Big Bad who was a vampire in the original game, here is just a normal girl, her reason for blocking the sun? She hates light. Yes, Remilia is incredibly petty even in the series she hails from.

- The reason the J-Team is likely to get involved in this incident is because the mist is strong enough to leak outside the Hakurei Barrier and actually start spreading to the WAAPT world if it's not dealt with. This actually happens in the Touhou series, but the mist was stopped soon after it started to show up in the outside world, the same is likely to happen here.

- There will be a few Bonus Bosses thrown in before the characters actually get to the mansion and a few more after the "post-game", as well as the game's actual Bonus Boss.
