11166 Herbert40k12th May 2013 12:17:12 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
Chords of Steel
Alright, so at the moment I'm having a bit of a dry spell with regards to ideas for future arcs, so I figure I'll make one big infodump post with all my ideas and leave them open for feedback. If you have any problems, suggestions, or points about how they intersect with other people's ideas, let me know.

Conquest

The main spot where I'm running out of ideas is for Conquest. I'm having trouble nailing down a personality for Ancestor!Herbert. I've already decided that he's part of an invading power, coming as part of a more medieval Europe-inspired force in order to conquer Ransei for his king. This is, there's two ways I could go about it.

The first is to have him be an evil, conquering bastard, who wants to take Ransei for himself and betray his king at some point. Problem is, "evil conquering bastard" is kind of Ransei!Umbra's territory. The second plan would be to have him as an idealistic knight with glorified views on warfare and the like, who grows more cynical as he witnesses more and more of the war (specifically, Ransei!Umbra's invasion). The problem with that idea is that I'm already doing the "staunch traditionalist whose views are challenged" thing with Herbert.

The other thing I'm undecided about is the development of his Aura abilities. I've already established that he was a powerful Auric (possibly a Soul of Silver?) and as a result of this his descendants Titan and Herbert are both Aura-sensitive, but I'm not sure whether he should begin Conquest as a powerful Auric or learn his abilities along the way. Again, I don't want him to be "Herbert, but in the past" but having him start off with powers doesn't leave much room for development. And I still haven't decided how I want to tie his development as an Auric into Herbert's, if at all.

...On a somewhat related note, I'd love to use Ancestral Awakening (a la Homestuck) as a power at some point. And I'm fine with Conquest opening with Okamiquest, so long as it doesn't constrict the development of other plots.

Edit: Also, I seem to have misplaced the link to the spreadsheet detailing whose ancestor comes from where and what their perfect link is.

Holonquest

My plan for Holon at the moment is just for Herbert to tag along and see what happens. And if we do go with the "shady Holon scientists" thing, then he's got a good reason to tag along in the first place - after all, he'd love to help hunt down evil scientists. I thought of him maybe having visions flashing back to ancient Ransei, or maybe the radiation dampening his Aura abilities somewhat and him investigating the source, but both of those just seemed too trite so I pretty much dropped them. I suppose he'll just find out about his ancestor the same way he found out about the Ghost Lord. :P

Orre

At the moment, I don't have any real plans for Orre, and if I do run any villains it'll probably just be Titan and Deimos again as opposed to Cipher, though a Titan/Cipher alliance could work. As for Duke being Shadowed - personally, I love the idea, if only because it gives Herbert a chance to be Lyuri's source of tough love rather than the other way around. I've no plans to Shadow any of Herbert's Pokemon, though. And the admins suggested so far sound pretty cool.
