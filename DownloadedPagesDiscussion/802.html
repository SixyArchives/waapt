Sorry this is coming out of the blue, but I'd like to start a motion to have a Fairy type Hidden Power (as well as allowing for hidden Powers of any future types to exist in the future - after any spoiler bans and other similar restrictions are lifted, of course) in the RP.

I know in-game that a Fairy-Type Hidden power doesn't exist, but to me, that seems to be more out of the dev team not finding a way to make it a Fairy type Hidden Power work in the coding. And since we have had precedence altering the mechanics of certain moves IRP because of the setting - Attract and Bonemerang come to mind - I feel that we can justify the inclusion of a fairy type Hidden Power.

In regards to how it would work IRP, I propose that only the mon who have not already been shown to have a Gen II-V Hidden Power type can have a Gen VI (and later) Hidden Power type. (i.e. Christine and Rio have been shown to have Water and Electric type hidden powers, respectively, so they can't ever have any other HP type. But mon like Maxilar and Bahamut, who have yet to use HP or otherwise have their HP analyzed, could have HP-Fairy or another eventual type - so long as it's never shown, discussed, or alluded to until it becomes eligible IRP.)

I get why people want Fairy-type Hidden Power, but at the same time, I think adding a new Hidden Power type is a headache, even in-universe.
