Is it okay if I pitch my own idea for an Illusion Admin? God, I feel like I'm throwing myself in at the deep end here... then again, I usually feel like that.

Name: Clarice Voynich

Age: Unknown, though she appears to be a youngish adult

Gender: Female

Appearance: Average build, surprisingly short. Has dull brownish-orange hair, which she wears short, and unnatural-looking deep purple eyes. Wears a standard issue Illusion Project uniform.

Affilation: Illusion Project Administrator

Region of Origin: Unknown

Personality: Voynich is, in a word, deranged. She flips seemingly at random between a soft-spoken Affably Evil personality and an Axe Crazy maniac prone to screaming at people. The central element in both personalities is a single-minded devotion to the Illusion Project's goals, based on her belief that humans are evil monsters who need to be replaced as the dominant species. Why she thinks this is unknown - indeed, much of Voynich's life pre-Illusion Project is shrouded in mystery, and the woman herself has a tendency to tell outlandish and conflicting tales about her past life. In battle, Voynich is a Psychic monotyper.

Mons

Species: Gardevoir (with Gardevoirite stone)

Gender: Female

Ability: Synchronise

Personality: Gardevoir is unswervingly loyal to Voynitch, but it's clear to any observer that this obedience is based on fear of her unstable Trainer. As such, she will carry out any order, no matter how destructive it may be, but always with fear and regret in her eyes.

Species: Espeon

Gender: Male

Ability: Synchronise

Personality: Were it not for Espeon, Voynich simply would not function as an Illusion Admin. Sure, she has the charisma and the insanity necessary to lead a Villainous Team, but he's the one who keeps a level head and has the big, complex ideas. But don't go assuming Voynich has any respect for her Right Hand Nekomata; to her, he is just another tool, like all but one other personmon.

Species: Metagross

Gender: Mentally male

Ability: Clear Body

Personality: Strong and silent. Voynich's Metagross lets his Metal Claws do the talking for him. And talk they do; even for the Psychic type's resident pseudo-legendary, this guy is very strong indeed, serving as Voynich's ace in combat.

Species: Meowstic

Name: Flood

Gender: Female

Ability: Keen Eye

Personality: The fact that she actually nicknamed her is the least unusual behaviour Voynich displays towards Flood. The little Meowstic seems to be the only being the sadistic Admin actually cares for; she is treated like a beloved household pet, and Voynich will only use her in combat if absolutely necessary. So why does Flood elicit this response? Surprise, surprise, nobody knows.

Illusion Project Admin Bio

Name: Shard (real name unknown)

Gender: Female

Affiliation: Illusion Project Admin/Member

Appearance: Difficult to tell, since her usual attire of a gas mask and an ice blue set of padded body armour serves to conceal most of her features. What is immediately obvious is her long black hair, short frame and slender build.

Personality and Backstory: The best word to sum up Shard with would be "cold", referring to both her demeanour and the type of Pokemon she trains. She does whatever she believes is necessary to achieve her goals without a trace of hesitation or regret, though she appears to have a fondness for Pokemon and can often be found using her free time to raise her team. A woman of few words, she prefers to let Frost, her Jynx, do the talking wherever possible.

Shard rarely talks about her herself, her past, or her motivations for working with the Illusion Project, though judging by the amount of money and resources that she is able to supply the organisation with she apparently comes from quite wealthy origins.

Shard's Pokemon

  • Name: Frost
  • Species: Jynx
  • Gender: Female
  • Personality: Shard's closest teammate, Frost mainly acts in a support role or as an intermediary towards others when her trainer is feeling particularly antisocial. She is far more talkative and personable than her trainer, but some of Shard's ruthlessness has clearly rubbed off on her.

  • Name: Stalactite
  • Species: Cryogonal
  • Gender: Neuter
  • Personality: Stalactite is one of Shard's two more battle-oriented Pokemon. It is vicious and aggressive, lashing out at those around it at the smallest provacation. Oh, and it is quite definitively an "it", and will react violently if you try to pigeonhole it with gender pronouns.

  • Name: Stalagmite
  • Species: Abomasnow
  • Gender: Female
  • Personality: The second and more powerful member of Shard's battling pair, Stalagmite is both strong and abnormally large for her species, standing at just over nine feet tall. She is verbose, goal-oriented, and loves crushing intellectual inferiors beneath her feet whilst listening to their screams.
