to the overall idea. Adding in the optional part, it gives us a good justification to play a storming the castle scenario against Team Ozone, thus diverging from having just one target to spam attacks on. Also it helps justify th e other Teams getting involved in the whole issue.

So, on finale preparations, there are some concerns that need to be addressed so that we can arrange for the finale events in an adequate manner that allow for as many of us as we want to to play a significant role.

  • What is Team Ozone's resources? What amount of personnel do they have and how well equipped they are? also I realize them having Zubats makes sense...
  • Where is Team Ozone's HQ located? How approachable it is?
    • Someone suggested an airbase. Considering the precedent of airborne megaship / carriers in the anime, I'd guess TO could have amassed enough power for a S.H.I.E.L.D.-esque airbase.
    • Otherwise (or along with it!) there are a couple of semideserted anim islands near Sootopolis and Pacifidlog that we could use.
  • Common sense says Charon won't be operating from any of the known TA/TM/TO/T[whatever] bases, and that he probably can control his best weather machines remotely. Where will he first be confronted?
  • Are we handling the issue on our own or can we count with eg.: E4 or Law Enforcement?
  • Since eventually the stability of Mt. Pyre is gonna be at risk, perhaps some of us could play scenarios either evacuating the nearby settlements, or taking down TA/TM efforts to control nearby areas.

EDIT: Quick mental rundown of the scenario, or what I know from it, suggests we split in three logistical groups (thus also avoiding the Sponge Boss problem):

  • The "stop Charon / caldera" group, mostly concerned with avoiding catastrophe at the core or with handling Charon. I'd guesstimate at least DS and Tagg will be here. This scenario favors more the classical boss battle, plus puzzle bosses, and it will also be the one to directly involve the Legendaries.
  • The "stop the teams" / "storm TO's place" group, mostly concerned from halting the Teams from causing yet more chaos (since they'll peobably be both interfighting and infighting). I'd guesstimate Umbra and Rex will probably like this scenario so that they get more or less a Baptism By Fire (I still feel we owe Rex his BBF from the OI, since we've never got to Shenanigan the Delta Battle). It allows for more variable development, and it is not as much limited by location, timezones or plot minutia as the above one can get to be if we delay.
  • The "protect the public" group, mostly concerned with stuff like evacuating Mt. Pyre and nearby settlements, keeping civil order in nearby cities as the news go out, fetching or recovering supplies and tools we might need, and in the end probably performing arrests on the evildoers not named Charon. I'd guesstimate probably Luke will be interested in this kind of scenario. This kind of scenario favour less classical boss battles, if any, and is more tied by dependence on the effects of both the above groups than by
any timezone stuff.
