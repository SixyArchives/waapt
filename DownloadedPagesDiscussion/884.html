A Potential Slow-Plot idea

Since I'm on the slow-plot path, I asked Bitter for her help in doing a raid based on the Doctor Who episode A Good Man Goes to War. Obviously due to the change from DW to WAAPT, several plot points will change - especially during the second half. While this idea was made with players who are Whovians in mind, anyone can feel free to join in as any part - though non-Whovians may feel more comfortable a part of The Cavalry. However, I recommend both Whovians and non-Whovians alike should review the recap page in the link above to ensure you have a general idea what will happen.

A Basic Outline

The plot kicks off when one of Ren's mon and Sarah being capture by an Illusion Grunt. Of course, this hits Ren's Berserk Button about 50 times more than any one person should press it, so Ren calls in some help from other J-Team members to storm the [Illusion facility of unknown function and importance as of the posting] where Sarah and Conifer are being held.

Character Roles

Though while Bitter and I have tentatively discussed which WAAPT PCs should be analogues to DW characters; these ideas are not set in stone, and can easily be changed.

  • The Doctor's role in the story will be shared by a few PCs.

  • Amy Williams Pond's role in the story might be best done by Sarah and/or Sheila.

  • Rory Williams Pond's parts might be best done by Ren.

  • Dorium's role in the story might be best done by Dezi and Wuffles and/or Gamma.

  • Madame Vastra's role in the story might be best done by Pent.

  • Strax's and Jenny's roles in the story might be best done by Dune.

  • The Cavalry's (consisting of an army of lizard people from the dawn of time, an intergalactic SWAT team, a pirate captain and his son, Space Spitfires covering the skies, and those mentioned above) role is going to be filled by other various J-Team members and their mon.

  • Madame Kovarian's role in the story might be best done by Echo.

  • Colonel Manton's role in the story might be best done by Mathilde or a throw away NPC.

Other notes

If you are interested, be sure to contact Bitter or myself (TVT PM is the best way to contact me) so we can figure out which character(s) will do what.

Thoughts?
