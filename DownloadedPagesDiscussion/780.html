Alrighty then! I had an idea!

The Flying University for Adventuring Minds

At first glance, it appears to be a very large airship in the form of a flying pyramid. It is in fact much bigger on the inside. This is the Flying University for Adventuring Minds, also known as Adventure University, or AU.

Here, alongside more standard college fare, the curriculum offers such courses as "Anti-Hero 101", "Adventuring Archaeology", acting classes with a focus on using skills to maintain a secret identity, classes in the "Strange Sciences" (PC for Mad Science) and many others.

Attending are a variety of students - heroes in training, heroes seeking to further either their education or their skills, and just normal students.

Fun fact: The J-Team are something of celebrities among the students, as they are, after all, the world's foremost group of heroes at this point in time.

Anyways, now to the why of this.

I plan on this being a reoccurring location - so we can have, at least, one "School" arc/plot thing.

Once I introduce the school, it's open to all - you can write your own teachers, student, staff, whatever.

I will be writing, of course, the head person, but otherwise - go nuts!

Thoughts? I really hope people will use this as a setting for tons of awesome sub-plots, maybe even eventually a full arc. Who knows. Anyways, yeah.

Name: Simon Treves

Gender: Male

Appearance: Average height, with a build that seems to be all points and angles. Clothing-wise, Simon wears a red-and-black plaid jacket, denim jeans, and fur-lined hiking boots, a style which has been known to invite derisive comparisons to a lumberjack.

Ties: Student at the Flying University for Adventuring Minds, working for his honours in Pokémon Journeying.

Region of Origin: Lumiose City, Kalos

Personality: Simon is the epitome of a smug, self-satisfied Hipster... most of the time. Generally, he acts abrupt and condescending, with little time for others and seemingly endless time for himself. However, he can soften up if you earn his respect (not that that's an easy task), and also has a Super OCD streak that can be exploited by anyone cunning enough to do so.
