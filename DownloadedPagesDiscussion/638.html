Name: Roxie Dione

Age: 18

Appearance: Somewhat tall and toned athletic build. Brown hair and brown eyes. Wears a blue track jacket and blue sweat pants.

Background: A native of Kalos who moved to Unova with her father after her parents separated. She adored her little step-brother Alex and loved to catch and trade pokemon with him. She decided to move back home and take the Kalos Gym challenge with a team that is predominantly made up of Unova 'mons. Secretly she's a huge fan of the J-Team and is tailing them in hopes of joining their prestigious ranks (which, admittedly, are not prestigious at all. The J-Team will pretty much take anyone). Roxie wants to prove herself as a trainer and win over the admiration of the J-Team's members.

Personality: Deadpan but generally nice. She can be snarky at times but deep down cares about other people's feelings. She likes to crack jokes usually at anyones expense (including her own).
