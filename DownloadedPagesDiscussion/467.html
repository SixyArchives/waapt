I feel it important to post information about Ren's cane collection. To prevent Ren from becoming a cheap knockoff of The Penguin, I am making myself a personal rule that I will not create any more gimmick canes for Ren beyond what I listed here in the future. Although, if Ren receives a Chekhov's Gun in the guise of a gimmick cane for plot advancement - I would not object to that - provided it won't fire too often. For future reference, here is a listing of Ren's canes:

  • Ren's Standard Cane: Exactly what it says on the tin. A standard collapsible cane that can purchased anywhere. It has a Black shaft and a gray comfort grip handle.
  • Ren's Mp3 Player Cane: Again, Exactly what it says on the tin. The way this cane works is the outer casing of a mp3 player was taken off. Then the inner hardware was wired to small speakers. The speakers and hardware were installed into a hollow cane handle. This cane is all black. It also has a headphone jack on the top of the handle.
  • Ren's Electric Cane: This is Ren's final line of self-defense and primary form of intimidation. This cane is essentially a battery operated, high powered cattle prod in a custom casing. The shaft is black metal, while the silver-like handle has a transparent, non-conductive coating so Ren doesn't hurt himself.
  • Ren's novelty canes (multiple). Again, Exactly what it says on the tin. These are non-gimmick canes with interesting handles and/or patterns. Doctor House would be jealous.
