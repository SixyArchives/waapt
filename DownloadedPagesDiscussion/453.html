Cipher Villain- Matter Hat

Name: Matter Hat

Age:?

Occupation: Deranged Criminal Mastermind/Former Pokefutures Researcher (Will be working as a High Ranking Cipher under and admin once Orre rolls around).

Appearance': Tall, thin man in a black and white spiral patterned suit with matching top hat, cane and bow tie. On his hands he wears white gloves. Neatly combed blonde hair and pale blue eyes and a 'Phantom of the Opera' style mask on the left side of his face.

Background: Not much is known about him except that he once worked for Pokefutures and was fired due to an obsession with a certain 'mirror'. He and Des have faced-off in numerous occasions. Des always pullin' off some Eleventh Hour Victory and foilin' the his plans. Hat considers Des to be a Worthy Opponent.

Personality: Two words to describe Matter Hat: Insane and charismatic. He absolutely loves to screw around with people and enjoys every minute of it. If he can't win, then he'll have a good time tryin'. Also a sucker for theatrics and flashy entrances, he tries to turn everythin' into a stage production. Hat's ultimate goal is to wreak havoc and cause destruction, but is very patient about it. It may seem like he's playin' it by ear, but there will almost always be some ulterior goal in mind.

Equipment/Abilities: Matter Hat is a technological genius with a background in Quantum Physics. He has a variety of gadgets that allow him to do incredible and sometimes near impossible things. Somewhere on his person is a portal generator that allows him to generate portals anywhere within a certain area i.e. about a 15 foot radius about him. These portals can vary in size, only last a few seconds and require his full concentration to maintain. If his concentration is broken they will disappear. He is currently workin' on a way to make them more efficient. His hat contains a pocket dimension and is therefore bigger on the inside while his shoes contain gravity manipulators allowing him to levitate and seemingly walk on air. The last of his personal affects is his black and white striped cane that has a rapier within it. Both his cane and his gloves have integrated remote controls that are linked to his nervous system allowing him to control all of his devices with a mere thought. He also has some larger devices that have either blown up, overheated or just plain malfunctioned. Matter Hat is also psychically linked to his Metagross, Catastrophia.

Pokemon: Matter Hat tends to stick to Psychic and Ghost types. Much like him his 'mons appear to be insane by different degrees. All of them are ruthless and/or cold.

Name: Catastrophia

Species: Metagross, identifies herself as a female.

Appearance: Your average Metagross only with a big pink bow on her head.

Personality: Catastrophia mind is Psychically linked to Matter Hat's. When he became insane she did as well. She loves to smash things, there's nothin' that satisfies her more than the crunch of somethin' under her massive claw. She wasn't always so violent....

Name: Grieve

Species: Male Dusknoir

Appearance: Your average Dusknoir

Personality: Hat's other right-hand 'mon, Grieve is maniacal and blood thirsty. He likes to cause pain and will go to great lengths to inflict it upon others.

Name: Tiresias

Species: Male Xatu

Appearance: Average Xatu though his eyes are completely blank.

Personality: In the pursuit of total enlightenment Tiresias blinded himself and purged all emotions from his mind. Runnin' on pure logic alone Tiresias doesn't really care whether his 'Master' accomplishes his goals or not. To him it's all irrelevant in the long run. However he is loyal to Matter Hat only because he finds his intelligence admirable.

Role in the Plot

Matter Hat will be working for Cipher once Orre arc rolls around. He'll offer his knowledge and technology to an admin in exchange for their resources, facilities and manpower. Hat likely won't care for Cipher's own goals, instead lookin' to further progress his own plans.

If he works under Odin (with Umbra's permission) he'll assist him in the hunt for the 'metahumans', takin' a special interest in Umbra and his Obscura abilities.
