So since Herbert opened the option for new characters to join Neo-Galatic. I've been toying with the idea of creating a charcter to join Neo-Galatic. After a while of pondering I decided to bit the bullet and go with it.

Trainer Name: Anka Motyl.

Appearance: Short brown hair, brown eyes and pale skin. Will mostly be wearing the Neo-Glatic uniform but otherwise wear t-shirts and 3/4 jeans.

Bio: Poke-Polish in heritage and running on a streak of unfortunate events in life. Anka is socially awkward, shy, always picks the wrong phrases of expressions and gets muddled and stressed about the simplest things. Despite this, she tries her hardest and when she focuses on things, she tends to get them done on time. Her reason for joining Neo-Galactic is simple, she has just been fired from her regular job and really needs the money. She knows what she's doing is morally wrong but because the pay is good and keeps her well fed and a roof over her head, she's not complaining about it. She has 16 Pokemon, all of which are Vivillon.

Pokemon: Although Anka has 16 Vivillon. I have only got the personalities for 6 as of yet. Which will be the basis of her team so far.

Clermeil the Ocean Vivillon (M): Clermeil is the Team Leader and the Only Sane Man out of the group. Clermeil is level headed and grounded in reality and tries to keep Anka in good spirits despite her awful luck. He often has to put up with the trollish antics of Nyami, the terrible temper of Vash and the attitude of Ran. But he manages.

Vash the Elegant Vivillon (M): Vash has a Hair-Trigger Temper about nearly everything and flies off the handle at the drop of a hat. Because of this, he often rushes into dangerous situations without thinking and often gets into trouble. He easily irritable nature makes him an easy target for Nyami's trolling.

Nyami the Archipelago Vivillion (M): Nyami is a Trickster and a Troll. He often acts at the expense of others, especially Vash. Despite this, he is probably the wisest Vivillon under Anka's handle.

Rán the Monsoon Vivillon (F): Ran is a snarky, sassy Tsundere. While not as ill-tempered as Vash, she often has to have the last opinion on everything and finds entertainment in belittling others. She is also the strongest Vivillon under Anka's command.

Fuya (Female Polar), Hima (Female Icy Snow) and Snowshoe (Male Tundra): These three are fraternal triplets and often act together as a unit. Despite this, there are noticeable differences in the siblings. Like for example, Hima is the most dominant out of the three and Fuya is the more reserved one. While Snoeshoe is a bit of a joker.

Other Vivillons without personalities yet: Germany (Female Continental), Halia (Female Marine), Gooseberry (Male Garden), Changi (Female Jungle), Eros (Male Meadow), Solana (Female Modern), Apache (Male High Plains), Karraur (Female Savannah) and Yhi (Female Sun)
