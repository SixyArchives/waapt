Name: Alys Boucher

Gender: Female

Affiliation: Illusion Admin

Personality and Backstory: Alys is, in a word, creepy. Her general demeanor gives you the impression that she could, at any moment, turn into a giant spider and suck out your juices. She acts like a pleasant, quirky woman to all that she meets (well, for a given value of pleasant), but make no mistake: she can be very unpleasant and occasionally violent (though not like Kyrie).

Alys hails from Laverre City, and was born into a well-to-do family. She signed with Illusion partially so that she can achieve their goals, and partially so that she can make people squirm.

Mons

Ariadne the Female Galvantula: Alys tries relentlessly to have her serve as a spidery Right-Hand Cat to sic on her enemies, but Ariadne is content to act like, well, a normal cat. She's a capable battler, though.

Sparkles the Male Espeon: Contrasting the serious, cerebral nature of many Espeon, Sparkles' head is firmly in the clouds. He doesn't engage in the creepy poetic ramblings of, say, Marigold, but he's...out there.

Female Talonflame: This mon is more like her trainer. She is enigmatic, speaks in half-truths, and executes whatever is asked of her quickly, no matter what it is. She claims that she has a name, but refuses to tell it to even Alys.
