OK, so now that Crater Dreams is done and I've gotten the first few chapters of the WAAPT spinoff-prequel-thing planned out in its WIP plot outline, I think it's about time I give you guys a few more details on the latter.

First off, the title of the damn thing is...

-drumroll-

Heroes After All.

Now, it'd be mean if I just gave you that, so have a few more ridiculously vauge details on what's currently planned:

  • The fic is set at some point before the RP's equivalent of the events of RBY.

  • The fic features a proto-J-Team.

  • Said proto-J-Team features predecessors of Lucius, Vierr, Tagg, Psyche, PERFECTSIS, and some of their mons. (All with permission from their original authors, of course.)

  • Two actual WAAPT characters of mine (as in, the exact same peoplemons) will have a prominent role. At least two others will have minor roles.

  • At least one canon character will make an appearance, though naturally they'll be younger.

  • Mega Evolution factors into the plot... Probably. There's still a few kinks to work out.

  • One of the villains is a character I was gonna use for WAAPT but decided to use for this fic instead.

  • There are exactly two protagonists, one human and one mon. They don't meet until midway through the plot.

  • A part of this fic's plot involves... "Reconstructing" something. Said something has to do with another something that happened in the RP.

And that's all for now. You see, I'm being so ridiculously vauge because there are a couple aspects of this fic I don't want kneejerk reactions to before anyone reads it, and as usual I'm ridiculously paranoid about giving them away. There are a few people in the RP who I've told why, as well as one relatively prominent Pokefic author outside the RP (I'll happily take guesses as to that last one). And besides, I genuinely feel those aspects are best left as surprises. :P

So... Any thoughts? Or even better, WM Gs? I'd love to see people's guesses! :3
