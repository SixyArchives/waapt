Kalos Plans

Okay, cosidering Zeal may or may not be showing up, I have two routes I may take-

Canon Time Line!

This is the one where Regina is saved, in this case, the following happens.

  • Jacob, after discovering Fairy Types existence, attempts to impress what he thinks is analogous to "PEFE Admins" by studying them, possibly with Hikari

  • Considering he's as much of a battler as he is a scientist, he will eventually decide to take the Gym Challenge with a Fairy only team, with Regina being a part of it since post-purification she'll gain Fairy type.

  • Jacob gets a Mega-Wristgaurd, obtaining only Gardevoirite as a gift from the Champion

Character wise:

  • Wraith does helpful stuff, getting more screentime and hopefully being involved in side plots

  • Jacob shows the less jerk-y side of himself after his various yet-to-be revealed successes in Orre

  • All Jacob's older mons get screen time

  • Regina recovers from being a Shadow mon, even though she is purified

  • Sylva starts growing up, her personality being very open and warm and loving, while also being relaxed

  • Verax tries becoming part of the group, tailing people and generally making a lot of mistakes, slowly learning other ways to be happy than other people's suffering, learning to tone down the violence as the first solution, and showing the more personable side of him

A Series of Unfortunate Events

This is what happens should Jacob fail during Orre. It will contain spoilered things so if you still want to be at least somewhar surprised, skip over them.

  • In his final confrontation, Jacob does not have the means to save Regina, as a result not only does Titan remain her handler, Edge is killed off for real Jacob becoming permanently devestated on a deep level, though he will recover

  • Discovering the Fairy type, Jacob decides to look into them more as a possible form of getting further power to save Regina

  • His personality does not change from what we've seen, and in fact will act nicer and more lightly to people as a way of masking how he feels

  • Sylva still grows up, though more shy, quiet, and independent than originally intended due to her mother being gone

  • Jacob obtains a Mega-Wristgaurd and Tyranitarite, using them to hunt down Regina, likely obtaining her eventually

  • Verax is killed before Orre by Jacob in a fit of despairing rage.

  • Other than this, the two routes are the same.

So then!

Those are the two possibilities. I'm not asking which of those you like more, since that is independent of whether or not they'll happen, but thoughts on them in general? They are subject to change, but changes are unlikely and probably will be small.
