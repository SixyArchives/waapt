Guess what guys? It's CHARACTER DUMP TIIIIME!

Introducing a new family- The de Niles.

The de Niles are an academic family hailing from Kalos. Specifically from around the Ambrette/Cyllage area. The family has a history of both producing a lot of Adventurers and training Rock, Ground, Ghost and Fire types (With the odd Steel Pokemon thrown in for good nature). They're not the most social of people, but they tend to be good nature and are talkative to those who they are comfortable around. Most of the de Niles also have a hobby outside of their academic field of choice and Pokemon training.

Cleo de Nile

Bio: Cleo is the 18 year old daughter and youngest child of the de Nile family. She is a competitive trainer and is often in it to win it. She is sort of the black sheep of the family as she has no real interest in perusing a future in some sort of science or academic field, which puts her at odds with her family. She's also quite spoiled, hot-headded and bratty, with a Hair-Trigger Temper about nearly everything. This leaves her with not many human friends and preferring the company of her Pokemon. Despite this, she has a good relation with the eldest child of the de Nile family, Lawrence, who she cares for deeply. She loves rollerskating

Appearance: Cleo is short and slim. Her skin is a Mediterranean olive color with black, scruffy hair cut into a bob, and emerald green eyes with light freckles. She wears an orange, red and black exotic cap, orange Midriff Halter top, orange Jean Hot Pants, OTK Camo Socks and brown laced boots. Her bag of choice is a deep red colour.

Pokemon:
  • Montu the Blaziken M: Like trainer like mon. Montu is as every bit stubborn, hot-headded and ill-tempered as his trainer. He is a ferocious battler, and can tear apart entire teams id he has the opportunity, but like most Blazikens he is a frail as a wet wafer. His knees are also quite sensitive due to one-too-many Hi-Jump-Kicks to a wall.
  • Pakhet the Delphox F: Cleo's official starter and one of her most used mons. Pakhet is a rather Modest individual who sees her victories and strengths as the works of others rather than her own. However, she is not a doormat, and has a rather lively and energetic (if humble) personality. Pakhet can also be a bit of a trickster, teleporting mons (mostly Montu) to various places and taking people's stuff with her Magician ability. Has a sort of Slap-Slap-Kiss relationship with Montu.
  • Sokar the Talonflame M: Sokar is a very paternal and joyous mon. He's the father figure of the group and is very caring towards his fellow team-mates. Despite his often jolly nature, he can be quite scary if provoked, especially if one threatens his teammates. He often urges Cleo to make more human friends.

Cleo also has a Houndoom (Anubis), Pyroar (Maahes) and Charizard (Sobek) but their exact personalities are still being decided/being difficult to come up with.

Lawrence de Nile

Bio: Lawrence de Nile is the eldest child (at the age of 26) in the de Nile family. Following the family tradition, he became an archeologist and works mainly in the [ PokeNorth Afric/Middle East ] area of the world. Lawrence is the polar opposite of his younger sister, as he is calm, laid back and highly generous/selfless. Despite this, Lawrence adores his younger sister and spoils her greatly. Lawrence is a man who can derive enjoyment from the most simple of tasks and is often content with his life. He is a man who believes that actions speak louder than words, as such he isn't the most talkative of people; but he can and does have long-winded conversations with friends about various things. Despite not being an avid trainer like his little sister, he does has a very powerful and bulky sandstorm team that is quite formidable. He is both a Motorcycle and airplane enthusiast.

Appearance: Lawrence is much taller than his sister, towering around the 6'1 mark. He has darker skin than his little sister (due to long periods in the desert) with Peridot eyes. Lawrence has short, scruffy black hair (like his sisters) that is covered by his shoulder-length Keffiyeh with his Go-goggles placed over his Agal. Lawrence's clothes are a light, dusty grey Adventurer Outfit with tall boots and a creme and red knee-length scarf (a longer and recoloured version of Morty's scarf)

Mons:

On hand:

  • Ur the Camerupt: Ur is a very brunt, honest and quiet mon. He doesn't really talk much except for "Yes, sir" and "No, sir". Loyal to only Lawrence, he performs any of his trainers wishes without any sort of fuss or hesitation. Ur, like Montu for his little sister, is Lawrence's signature mon.
  • Istanbul and Constantinople the Garchomp and Flygon respectively: These two mons are the two sides to a coin, cream to coffee, like shadows . In other words, they have that weird twin synergy thingy without actually being twins. They finish each-others sentences and are always found together, never found apart. They constantly go off to explore their new surroundings and make up wild and imaginative adventures while doing so.
  • Memphis the Tyrantum: Memphis is a very vain and self-cantered mon, constantly calling himself "The King" and believing himself to be the best mon on Lawrence's team bar none. Despite his self-inflated ego, he's not the brightest bulb of the bunch and often falls victim to Istanbul and Constantinople pranks. Memphis speaks with a very pronounced southern drawl.
  • Eridu the Tyranitar: Eridu is a well educated Tyranitar, often spending his time digging through books alongside his trainer. Like his trainer, he is well versed in the knowledge of the historical world and often enjoys discovering things like broken pottery and middens as they reveal fragments of life in the Ancient World. His curiosity and thirst for knowledge doesn't always do him good though, as that one time that he opened a kings tomb while accompanying Lawrence on a dig that had him cursed with bad luck.
  • Hieraconpolis the Skarmory: Hieraconpolis is a bird of the arts, enjoying such high-brow entertainment such as opera, theatre, art and classical music. He himself wishes greatly that he could be an opera singer, and trains his voice every day to achieve this goal. Don't tell him that humans can't understand Monese, it just makes him upset. His pretentious attitude grates on Memphis, and the two often squabble about it,

Retired:
  • Kish the Gorlurk: Kish comes from the Menhir Trail, and is often quite remorseful yet nostalgic about the past. Kish was Lawrence's starter, and even though he's retired, has a special bond with his trainer. Kish likes finding out the human nature of history, as it makes him feel nostalgic about his own past. Mentioning Kalosian history or the Kalos royal family turns him silent, as such the subject is often avoided.
  • Asyut the Lucario: Asyut is a crypic mon, who never answers any question asked of him directly. Asyut has very faint markings on his face that seem to symbolise something, though he never says what. Asyut since his retirement from battling normally helps Lawrence decipher hieroglyphs
  • Barcino the Marowak: Barcino hails from the Glittering Cave, and all the oddities that come with it. He's a hippie character, often talking in non sequitur ramblings about "Love", "Peace" and "Freedom" and indulging in cave moss (which Lawrence also enjoys doing on occasion)
  • Syracuse the Barbaracle: Syracuse was the Montu of Lawrence's team. Brash, stubborn, prone to violence and fighting and rather devastating to those who don't expect him. Though, after one too many Close-Combat's, his body started to weaken and Lawrence pulled him out of his team and retired him. Syracuse has taken up the mantle of a Grumpy Old Mon sitting on a Porch and waving his cane and pesky young kids and ranting about his victories.

(Just a note, I imagine that Cleo and Lawrence would be half the Poke-equivilent of Egyptian or Arab. Not important, just a note.)
