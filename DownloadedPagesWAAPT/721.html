 18011 Luke 924, Sun, 27th Mar '11 3:50:23 AM Get RP Mod
-Me and my Pokemon are asleep. The long day having taken it's toll on us.

However, a certain Legendary horse isn't. Not wanting to wake us, Keldeo I tries to sneak past us as quietly as his hooves will allow.

Unfortunately for him, the sound of his hooves is not quiet enough to keep him from waking Lake up and noticing what he's doing.-

Lake: -Quietly- <Hey, what are you doing?>

Keldeo I: -Stops, looking back at Lake as he puts his cloak on.- <I'm going to see my son.>

Lake: <What, now?>

Keldeo I: <It will be dawn soon. I understand that my son likes to get up earlier then the other warriors. As far as they know, I perished in the First Battle of Truth and ideals. And I intend to keep it that way.>

Lake: <Why?>

Keldeo I: <Because I'm not entirely sure how they would react to me. It could cause trouble, plus I prefer staying undercover.>

Lake: <...Okay. You have your reasons.>

Keldeo I: <Thank you for understanding.>

-Keldeo I goes to leave.-

Lake: <Oh, and Keldeo I?>

Keldeo I: <...Yes?>

Lake: <Good luck.>

Keldeo I: -Smiles- <Thank you, Lake.>

-Lake smiles back. Keldeo I turns, then gallops off.-

Moor of Icirrus

-It doesn't take long for the Legendary to reach the moor. The Pokémon knew the Unova region like the back of his hoof, allowing him to navigate Unova even without the help of the route network.

Knowing that his son would probably be awake by now, Keldeo I trots through the marshy grounds, keeping an eye out for his son.-

Keldeo I: <Knowing Keldeo, he'll most likely be up by now. He never did like sleeping in...>

-Keldeo I pauses briefly to reminisce.-

Keldeo I: -Sighs- <Why couldn't the ancient Unovans have been more like those trainers? If only there were more humans like them...Then maybe the world would be a better place, both for people and Pokémon...>

-Finally, Keldeo I reaches a clearing. There's nothing here, apart form one rather large dead tree in the middle.-

Keldeo I: <...This place...> -Trots over to the tree in the middle.- <This...This was our home...We slept under this tree, and took shelter here whenever the weather was bad...>

-Keldeo I pauses to think again, remembering all the good times he and his family had here. But he is jolted out of his thoughts when he hears the sounds of hooves nearby.

Thinking that it might be one of the warriors, Keldeo I panics, them quickly hides in the nearby trees to avoid being seen.

When the sounds of hooves stops, Keldeo I peeks out from behind the tree to see...-

Keldeo I: <...Keldeo?>

-Indeed, it's Keldeo. The little horse legendary has stopped in front of the tree.-

Keldeo: <...Mom? Dad? It's me, Keldeo...>

Keldeo I: <...Oh...>

Keldeo: <I'm not sure if you're here...But this is where we used to live, so I thought this is where I would find you...>

-Keldeo gazes up at the sky.-

Keldeo: <...Are you happy where you are now? 'Cause...I'm happy...>

-Keldeo smiles to himself.-

Keldeo: <Yeah...I've got Miss Virizion and Misters Terrakion and Cobalion...They're taking care of me...And their's Mister Luke and his friends...They're not like other trainers. They really care about their Pokémon...Plus they're fighting M' for us...>

-Keldeo I watches in silence, shocked by all of this.-

Keldeo: <...I...I'd just thought I'd tell you all of this while we're here. We may have to leave soon...>

-Keldeo looks at the tree again, slightly teary eyes. Having heard enough, Keldeo I, with tears of happiness gathering in his eyes, silent walks over to his son, unseen.-

Keldeo: <Do you miss me Mom and Dad?>

-Keldeo I stops nearby. Struggling to hold himself together.-

Keldeo I: <...Yes. I missed you...>

-Shocked, Keldeo turns around, then almost jumps at the sight of his farther.-

Keldeo: <...Dad?>

-Tears of happiness streaming down his face, the young colt rushes over to his father and gives him a friendly nuzzle, which Keldeo I eagerly returns.-

Keldeo: -Sniff- <Dad...I...I though you we're gone...>

Keldeo I: <I was gone son...-Sniff- but it's okay...I'm back now.>

Keldeo: -Sniff- <...I love you Dad...>

Keldeo I: -Sniff- <...I love you Keldeo...>

-The two stay like this a while, both happy to be together again.-
