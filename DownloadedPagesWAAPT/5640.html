Route 43 - Johto

It had certainly been a while since they had last been here, and it was certainly nice to visit it under more...pleasant circumstances. Indeed, the last time they had traversed Route 43 towards the Lake of Rage, they had been in the middle of foiling Team Rocket's operations in the local area. In fact, they had narrowly avoided being shaken down for cash at a nearby guardhouse by Team Rocket Grunts.

But thanks to their efforts, Team Rocket had been disbanded long ago, and the Johto region was saved. Which was why the young man and his Feraligatr were free to roam of the Route without being mugged by criminals.

Unfortunately, the wild Pokémon hadn't seem to have gotten the memo.

"ARGH! Get away, you silly Pidgeotto!"

"PIDGEOOOOOOOOO!!!"

"Ow ow ow! Lake!"

Being too busy pecking the intruder on the head to notice, the angry bird Pokémon had no chance at avoiding the Ice Beam attack fired straight at her, the ultra-cold beam of energy striking her and knocking her of his head and onto the ground, literally knocked out cold.

The threat taken care of, the young man reached up and feels his head for bumps, instead finding locks of his hair frozen solid. With a sigh, he gives his partner a slightly annoyed look.

"You need to work on your aim."

The Feraligatr frowns, rolling his eyes.

Lake the Feraligatr♂ — <Well, I'm sorry. I was too busy protecting your head from an angry Pidgeotto to worry about potentially messing up your hair.> Shakes his head. <And it's not as if it could have been made that much worse.>

His trainer shoots him a glare.

"What's that supposed to mean?"

Lake — <Your hair's always a mess! You never comb it, and you shower, what, once a week?> Shakes his head. <No wonder your mom always complained.>

Slightly annoyed at this comment although knowing that it was made in jest, the young man gives the Feraligatr a punch on the arm playfully. However, it only served to hurt him more than Lake.

"Ow. Well, you're the one who can spit water from his mouth, so you're more than welcome to provide a shower if you feel my hair is messy."

Lake pauses, then grins maliciously.

Lake — <Oh? I can, can I?>

Only just realizing the ramifications of his words, the trainer jerks back out of Water Gun range.

"Don't even think about it!"

The Feraligatr smirks, letting out a hearty chuckle.

Lake — <Only kidding. Gosh, Luke, you're so gullible...>

The trainer, known as Luke, apparently Like you hadn't figured it out already, gives Lake a disapproving look, then turns around and continues heading north towards the lake.

"Honestly, I wonder why I keep you around sometimes."

Still chuckling, Lake drops to all fours and follows, and wide grin still on his face.

Lake — Playfully. <Because we both know you can't live without me.~>

"I would if I could."
