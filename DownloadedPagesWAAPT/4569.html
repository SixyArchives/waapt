The young woman in front of the city hall concludes her speech, smiling warmly at the crowd before her.

"Thank you all for your kind attention. Please, do not hesitate to approach me, my colleagues, or my Pokemon. And remember, every little bit you do protects the homes of our dear Water Pokemon, and gives these poor creatures a new, larger home to live in."

She descends from the stairs and mingles among the crowd, shaking hands and speaking softly but passionately. Her Pokemon, a female Buizel, female Sealeo, male Corphish, and a runty male Marshtomp. Several children come up and pet them, giggling, The woman turns to them, bending down.

"These are my Pokemon, Bluebird, Discovery, Bounty, and my very first Pokemon Argo. They're my very best friends in the whole world."

A little girl scratches Argo's fins, smiling.

"My big brother has a Mudkip! When I get older I want one too!"

"And my cousin from Sinnoh has a Buizel named Jet!" a boy adds, cuddling with Bluebird.

The young woman smiles. "That's great! They're excellent Pokemon. But, you know, they're in trouble if we don't help them and give them a bigger habitat to live in!"

"I'm gonna tell my mommy and daddy to support Team Aqua!" another boy says, punching the air with one fist while scratching Discovery under the chin with the other. The Sealeo slaps the ground with her tail happily.

"Well I'm gonna join Team Aqua! Well, when I'm older, " a girl one-ups him, feeding Bounty a Pokeblock.

"That's wonderful! On the behalf of Water Pokemon everywhere, my Pokemon and I thank you."

The children wave to her and run off as their parents call, and the young woman waves back before returning to speaking with the crowd. The group slowly disperses, and the young woman brings her Pokemon towards the center of the town, waving to a few fellow Team Aqua members she passes.

"Great speech, Effie!"

"Yeah, you knocked 'em dead, Eff!"

"There's a fight starting in the park, Effie. You might want to check that out."

Effie smiles and graciously accepts the compliments, heading towards the park.
