Mahogany Town Pokémon Center

Stratus: <Sure you know how to use this thing?>

Lake: <I've seen Luke use this thing enough time to know what I'm doing. Now let's see, hit start, choose "Internet Explorer"...>

Anyone entering the Pokémon Center would most probably be shocked to see a Dragonite watching a Feraligatr over his shoulder as he tried to access the PC. But that being said, there was nothing to say that Pokémon couldn't use the PC that anyone was free to use.

<Alright, now I just need to Google a map of the world.>

Stratus: <Wait, you can use Google to search for pictures?>

Lake: <...You didn't know that?>

Stratus: <I've never used one of these things before, This is all new to me!>

Lake: <Oh. Well now you know.>

Taking a moment to peck out "Map of the world" on the keyboard (which wasn't designed with Feraligatr hands in mind), Lake was soon able to bring up a rather comprehensive image of the entire Pokémon world.

<Alright then! Now where should we go?>

Stratus: <Hmm...>

Stratus leaned in close, rubbing his chin in thought as he gazed over all the places available to them.

<Well, where have we been already?>

Lake: <Let's see...Johto, Kanto, Sinnoh, Unova, Oblivia, Fiore, Almia, the Orange Islands, The Sevii Islands...>

The Dragonite blinked, somewhat shocked.

Stratus: <Wow, that's pretty much everywhere.>

Lake: <Well, I've only been to one or two places very briefly, like Almia and the Sevii, so there's that.>

Stratus: <Oh yeah, we only went to the Sevii for a little while in-between the Orange Islands and Hoenn, didn't we? As well as that time in the other universe. But we don't talk about that.>

Lake: <See? It didn't really count, so I guess we could still head to either those places if we wanted.>

Stratus: <...Hmm.>

The Dragonite paused to mull this over.

<There's not really that much in the Sevii, is there? Only a few small towns on each island. Bet none of them would have a fancy hotel or anything where we could relax by the pool all day, sipping those fancy drinks with the little umbrellas, you know?>

Lake: <Yeah, but that's the point! It's the untamed wilderness! Who knows what kinda' things we could find?>

Stratus: <...Do you not remember Seven Island? Do you really wanna' go there and deal with the angry Tyranitar and Skarmory and that Cubone Tribe? Or the Unown in those supposed ruins?>

Lake: <UNOWN?!>

The Feraligatr recoiled in shock, recollection of the past two traumatic times he had encountered the Unown flashing before his eyes.

<...Scratch that, we're not going there.>
