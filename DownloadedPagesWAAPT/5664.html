Aloise looked surprised as she suddenly emerged from the Roar, the Luxray in front of her giving a low growl at the sight of her. It seemed almost disappointed somehow...

'...Oh, I see, ' Rex mused to himself. 'You're scouting my team with Rivalry to boost Luxray's attack whenever a male Pokemon is sent out. In that case, using Rampage at the moment would likely make things worse. Luxray is much faster, and with a boosted Fire Fang that could mean trouble...'

"Waiting for me to make a move? Then I'll oblige. Luxray, Thunderbolt!"

Getting back into the match, Rex shook his head. "Aloise, use Protect!"

Hastily bringing up a shield to defend herself, the Rapidash watched as arcs of electricity crackled around her, thankfully not hitting Rex or any of the machinery.

"...Now that's strange, " Volkner mused. "I would've thought Thunderbolt to big a bit bigger than that after two Nasty Plots..." His eyes widened slightly in understanding. "Ah, now I see, " he chuckled. "That flailing your Scizor was doing...it was Struggle Bug. It reduced my Special Attack gain slightly."

Luxray: 2x Agility, 1.5x Nasty Plot, 1x Rivalry ATK Boost

"Not a bad attempt, " Volkner offered with a smile, "But that's only a minor setback. Luxray's strength lies in its Attack stat, after all. Now, Luxray, let's try another Roar."

With a resounding snarl from Luxray, Aloise found herself sucked back into her ball, another Pokemon taking her place automatically due to the effects of Roar. At the sight of a new male opponent, Luxray's attack shot up once more, filling its body with more strength.
