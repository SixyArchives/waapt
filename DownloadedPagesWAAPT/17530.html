Granite Cave

As they walk through the corridor, the three are met by a different Sableye, one covered in some tattered rags adorned with various gemstones.

Turquoise: <You three have caught him?>

Amethyst: <Yep! Easy as diamond pie~>

Turquoise: <I see. I'll go inform the queen, then depose of him as quickly as possible since she will not be in her good graces when she catches a glimpse of him.>

Garnet: <Understood.>

Turquoise walks away into the large room. A few minutes later, she comes back.

Turquoise: <You are free to go. But remember, make it snappy.>

The three nod as they scuttle as quickly as possible to the Queen's chamber. When they arrive at the Quuen's chamber, a quick shudder goes down their back.

Amethyst: <Hiqueenwecaughtyourghostguyhesyoursnowwerejustgon- naleavebye>

The three chuck Umbra on the ground and make mad dash out of the den.

Umbra may notice that the Den itself is packed to the brim with thousands of varieties of gemstones. In the centre, a throne of sorts has been crudely crafted from gems with blue-green tones to them. And sitting on that throne is the colony Queen.

The queen is noticeably larger than the other Sableye of the cave. She is wearing a sort of tattered cape, again with gemstones adorning it. In her right hand she holds a spear of sorts with a carved diamond head with the rod studded with aquatic toned gems (in particular, Kyanite). On her neck is a beautifully carved necklace with sapphires, emeralds, rubies and topazes leading into the large, strangely coloured stone pendant in the centre that glows with power.

The queen stands up from her throne, and walks towards Umbra, a nasty scowl adorning her face. As she gets closer to him, Umbra may notice darker patterns on her body similar to tattoos.

As she comes face to face with Umbra she stares at him for a moment, throws down her spear, and violently grabs his face by the cheeks with her claws.

Kyanite: <Nox...~>

She digs her thumbclaws further into his cheeks.

Kyanite: <IT'S BEEN SIX.>

<HUNDRED.>

<YEEEEAAARS!!>

She shrills so loudly that debris falls from the cave ceiling.
