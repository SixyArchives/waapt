Skye: Which is kinda why we're here. She wanted to snagproof my ball the same way.

Eskay: I see...

-He looks at Diane, then back at Skye-

Eskay: ...You've always been okay on your own, right?

Skye: Yeah...? I mean, I spend a lot of time with Diane, but I don't feel the... need to...

-She looks at Eskay, realizing what he was implying-

Skye: ...Oh...

Eskay: -nods- You're the person I'm closest to. Not the Pokemon I'm closest to. Other than Caroline — and even then, it's difficult to decide — you've been more of a "sister" than a Pokemon. It took me until I could understand you to realize that.

-He looks at Diane-

Eskay: ...She keeps your Pokeball safe, right?

Diane: ...I'm guessing you want me to do the same with hers?

Eskay: If you would, yeah. For you two, it's more of a mutual agreement than it is for us. However...

-He pulls out Skye's Pokeball, and holds down the button.-

Eskay: ...I'd rather you seal the bond personally.

-The front of the Pokeball glows for a bit, then fades. The Pokeball opens, defunct.-

Eskay: As of right now... you're free. You two decide what to do now.

Skye: ...Eskay—

Eskay: I literally can't change my decision, and I wouldn't even if I could. You're free. You might as well have been for the past half a year. Even longer, really. I almost feel like I've been holding you back all these years.

-Skye stares silently at Eskay... before pulling him into a great, big hug-

Skye: ...Thank you...

Eskay: It's really the least I could do.

-The hug breaks-

Eskay: -smiles- Now go. Don't be dragged down by me more than you have already.

Skye: ...All right. See you around, Eskay.
