Agate Pokémon Center

Sakura woke up early and is currently sitting with Daisuke while looking up some things in the internet.

"<Say, why did you make me evolve into this form?>" asked Daisuke

"Well, I've read that Gallades have a good sense of honor and also protect the weak from harm, it seemed like something you'd try to do, besides the fact that I think you look better as a Gallade." answered Sakura, giggling

"<Hmm... I understand now. But what sort of honor code are we talking about?>"

"I figured it would be something like the chivalric code, in which you have to protect the weak from harm and also protect all your fellow teammates, , be loyal and generous, always tell the truth, never refuse a challenge from an equal, be disciplined, among other things."

"<And those are?>"

"Well, here it says that you have to obey those place in authority and 'always respect the honor of women', which I don't quite understand." she explained, looking at some website.

"<Hmmm...>"

"There's also Bushido, whose main virtues are courage, righteousness, benevolence, respect, honesty, honor and loyalty, it's not too different really." she added, then pausing for a moment "You plan on following these codes?"

"<It doesn't seem too hard, I think I can do that with no problem.>" answered Daisuke, confident.

"If you say so... What's your objective with this though?"

Daisuke crosses his arms and sighs "<I just want to be able to help and be useful. I've sensed a lot of emotions over time, but couldn't quite make out what they meant, but what I know is that a lot of those feelings were also making me feel rather depressed at times, so I figured that some mons out there just needed some help, but I couldn't really do much for them.>"

"So you want to be everyone's knight in shining armor just to make both you and them feel better?"

"<I guess, I don't quite understand the term. But in any case I just figured that following an honor code could be a good way to start, it does look like something that could help me be more pleasant for others to talk with and make things easier for me.>"

Sakura smile "Guess you'll have quite the honorable Gallade then, you changed a lot upon evolution again, but well... Let's see if this change was for the better."

Daisuke just gives some sort of smirk as Sakura got up and started heading to the kitchen

"Anyway, mind helping me make something to eat?" asked Sakura

"<Is that an order?>"

"Oh boy, you already began. No, it's a request." said Sakura, giggling

Daisuke then lets out a chuckle "<Surely.>"

Daisuke then follows Sakura inside the kitchen.
