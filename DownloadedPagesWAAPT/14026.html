A forest in Ransei, a few months after the end of the Conquest arc

-Youta is talking to a mysterious-looking person. Cherish and several other Magnemite-line mons are nearby.-

Youta: Yuuto.

-The man looks at Youta.-

Yuuto: You truly intend to leave everything behind...?

-Youta nods.-

Youta: I do.

Yuuto: I see... Then this is farewell, my friend.

Youta: ...What do you mean by that?

Yuuto: You...

Youta: Nothing is stopping you from following me.

-He approaches and hugs Cherish.-

Youta: Though, this will be my final goodbye to them, without a doubt.

-He faces the Magnemite-line mons and raises his arms.-

Youta: After all that you have done for me, I do not wish to part so quickly. However, this is the only chance I have... You are dismissed.

-The large group of Magnemite-line mons does nothing.-

Youta: ...I am sorry...

-The mons slowly float away, one by one, until only Cherish remains. Youta looks into Cherish's eye for a moment, then nods. Cherish quickly floats to him.-

Youta: ...Sorry. I could never have left you behind.

-Yuuto clears his throat. Youta turns to face him.-

Yuuto: Now?

-Youta is silent.-

Youta: ............Yes... now.

Yuuto: ...Very well.

-The two walk through the forest for a while, Cherish floating beside them. After hours of this, they reach a clearing.-

Youta: This is it. Have you made up your mind? Will you join me?

Yuuto: ...I will not.

-Youta faces away from Yuuto.-

Youta: If you are to remain behind... I hereby grant you full power over Violight. Your reign may continue.

-Yuuto does not reply.-

Youta: Do you accept this?

Yuuto: ............I never said anything about remaining behind.

Youta: ...What are you—

-He is interrupted by a sword through his back.-

Yuuto: Have I...?! No... what have I...?

Youta: ......I s...see... Then... this... is farewell... m... frie.......

-Youta's body falls to the ground. Yuuto collapses, pale and shaking.-
