Or I just don't go to new places often. How long ago did you know this guy, anyway?

Right. Ghost types.

Fortunately, the lives of Amhlaidh and my compatriot, a Weavile he later renamed Kori, were saved by a young native man. As it turns out, this man would soon take power in Spectra. Because he saved us, Amhlaidh swore his life to him, and worked as a bodyguard of sorts. In his service, we fought in a great war... The Ghost Lord wanted something, I never cared what, and to aid him, my Amhlaidh fought by his side. It was an exciting time.>

I see...
