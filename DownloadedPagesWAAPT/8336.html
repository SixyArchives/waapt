<AND I AM NOT PUFF THE MAGIC DRAGON!>

<I! AM! A!>

<D-don't kill him! He...didn't hurt me at all, really...>

Stratus: <...B-but...It's...IT'S THE PRINCIPAL OF THE THING! HE ATTACKED YOU, MY SON!>

<HE REFUSED TO TAKE ME SERIOUSLY! JUST LIKE EVERYONE ELSE!>

Lake: <But...but, I take you seriously!>

He turned back to Lake, scowling at him.

Stratus: <NO YOU DON'T! NOBODY DOES!>
