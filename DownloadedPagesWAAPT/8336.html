<WELL, LET ME TELL YOU! ALL MY LIFE, I HAVE HAD TO PUT UP WITH ALL SORTS OF PEOPLEMONS NEVER EVER TAKING ME SERIOUSLY, NO MATTER HOW MUCH I TRY OTHERWISE! NOT MY SIBLINGS, NOT MY FARTHER, NOT MY MATE, NOT EVEN MY OWN SON!>

<AND YOU KNOW WHAT?! I'VE HAD IT! BECAUSE, I AM NOT A YELLOW BARNEY!>

<AND I AM NOT PUFF THE MAGIC DRAGON!>

...Which burst into emerald flames, as he prepared to use Dragon Claw.

<I! AM! A!>

Lake: <STRATUS!!!>

Stratus immediately halted, turning around to see the now awake Lake staring at him, distraught.

<D-don't kill him! He...didn't hurt me at all, really...>

He didn't say anything in response, simply staring at Lake with a raised Dragon Claw, ready to deliver the killing strike.

Stratus: <...B-but...It's...IT'S THE PRINCIPAL OF THE THING! HE ATTACKED YOU, MY SON!>

He turned back to Des, once again glaring at him.

<HE REFUSED TO TAKE ME SERIOUSLY! JUST LIKE EVERYONE ELSE!>

Lake: <But...but, I take you seriously!>

He turned back to Lake, scowling at him.

Stratus: <NO YOU DON'T! NOBODY DOES!>

Lake: <...I'm...I'm...>

<...Sorry...Dad.>
