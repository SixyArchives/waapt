Navel Mountaintop

(Continued from Here in Time Shenanigans)

-Danny is showing me a signpost with some designs of what seems to be a bobsled-

Danny — so you have to get your Pokémon to work from that — points behind us, where our mons are holding the ice pillars — into this.

— ...I hope the specs aren't too strict... so we are using three mons each, right?

Danny — Yes, and there you have my team. I assume you are going to switch that Masquerain of yours?

— Yeah...

But...

-I return to the others, noticing that the Machoke and the Nidoqueen seem to be having a heated discussion. Algira perches on my head-

Algira — <I'm not gonna be of much help, right?>

Ichtacya — <We heard from here...> — tilts head — <and Algira is too [tired].>

— I know... I just have to decide who to bring in.

-I mumble for a moment, evaluating my choices, which are drastically reduced to two-

Damn... why didn't I go competitive and caught like over nine thousand Pokémon like Tracer and the others?

But... no. I've always keep only a couple of Pokémon close to me, for a reason... and now it is the time to try it.

— Algira? Do you think Dintel can help [me]?

Algira — <...What?> — buzzes and peeks out from the top of my head.

-Ichtacya and Copahue look with interest-

Algira — <Well... he's... Dintel. If you have to follow those weird instructions, I doubt he's gonna be of help, honestly...>

-the rest of my team stare down, none of them willing to exactly come out to defend Dintel-

Algira — <Oh... I guess I have done it... Well, Trainer asked... dunno why because I guess he knew the answer... but... but...>

-looks around, watches Copahue for a moment-

Algira — <I'm not going to be a good mate to Helios if all I can do is complain that others won't do what I say...> — she looks up at the Sun past the clouds — <I need to say useful thins first!>

-Algira buzzes red for a moment-

Algira — <I LOVE HELIOS!!!!>

-The Nidoqueen and Machoke go jaw down and stare at us; the Scyther can barely care, walking around to keep himself warm-

Ichtacya -blushes furiously — <Wait what? What brought this?>

— Er... Algira?

Algira — <Well I do! And I would be able to spend a lot more time with him if you weren't so weak and so focused on [isolating] yourself!> — spins around — <I mean, look at the others!>

-Ichtacya bares her fangs-

Ichtacya — <Hey! I'm the Team Leader, and you don't come talk like that to my walk-along!>

-Algira buzzes a sort of laugh; not a demeaning one though-

Algira — <You can complain all you want so long as you back it up! I want to see you fighting! Earn the spot!>

— ...What?

Algira -to Ichtacya — <Your Razor Leaf was nice, you what we are going to need here is to [cut] something very specific; you'll have to use your fangs and claws or Trainer is not going to make it!>

Ichtacya -narrows her eyes- — <Well I can! Who the heck do you think I am!>

-suddenly, Algira leaps from my head and perches on Copahue-

Algira — <And you...> — smooches him — <I want you to show little [bird] how well you can Peck.>

Copahue — <But-er... I'm not that good... I never learnt Drill Peck... I don't know if...>

-Algira wingslaps Copahue. Softly-

Algira — <Just shut up about what you can't do and do it for once, please? Look, I can not help anylonger, and is obvious that Trainer is not going to bring his best mon out...> — the others stare at me quizzically — <so we need you to Carve this the right way while Dintel breaks the ice off!>

-Copahue blinks-

Copahue — <So you are bringing the Sandshrew, Trainer?>

-My hand, that was grasping the Moon Ball, moves to Dintel's Ball-

— Fine... I'm trusting you on this one, Algira.

Algira — <You better do. You win this. And if you [excuse] me, it's cold and I'm tired. I'd rather have a warm dream...>

-Algira pecks her Ball and recalls herself; in her place I send out Dintel-

— Dintel, look, I want you to break that Ice pillar... — I have to pick him up before he goes "wheeeeee~" at it — ...following Copahue's instructions. Can you do it?

-Dintel looks at me and blinks, then Copahue steps forwards-

Copahue — <Think of it as a [gift] for little bird...>

-Dintel raises a paw-

Dintel — <I can has break stuff very well! Just tell me where do I hit it~> — leaps on top of Copahue.

-My team nods in agreement and we walk to the ice pillars, where I give Danny a sign that I'm ready. The Nidoqueen still eyes me suspiciously-
