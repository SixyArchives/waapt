It became one when you went through at night.

With hundreds of Zubat hanging from the ceiling.

And a fire-type that generated her own light.

Fleeing from the horde of bats on their heels, Ponyta shot through the exit, thankfully not too far across from the entrance and dove to the side of the cave. Moments later, the Zubat pooled out of the cave and into the night sky, no doubt they were flying off to inspire some billionaire child with parental issues.

Dismounting from Ponyta and falling to his knees, Rex gave several gasps, trying to calm himself down from the recent scare. Ponyta, for her part, simply kneeled and kept a stoic look, though he had no doubt she was just as frightened as he'd been. Composing himself, he sat at her side, giving her mane a gentle and appreciated rub. He reached into the pouch on his waist, producing a few Oran Berries for the steed, which she chewed gratefully.

"Heh...no offense...but I think I'll walk next time..."
