Lumiose City

Aline and friends are in Eve's house, specifically her room. Aline was holding Nazrin with her, while Eve was holding a Cleffa and Lara had a Tyrogue by her side.

"So, this is my pet. Her name is Satella." said Eve, smiling cheerfully as she hugged the Cleffa, who nuzzled her quite affectionatelly.

"Satella? That's a pretty weird name." said Lara, looking a bit weirded out, then turning to Aline "Right, Aline?"

Aline looks from Lara to Eve, proceeding to nod in response.

"The name of your Pichu is Nazrin, you can't really say much." said Eve, looking mildly irritated.

"<Hey! My name's awesome!>" said Nazrin, puffing her cheeks at Eve.

"Well, Aline can't really say anything." said Lara, wincing at the faux pas.

A look of realization comes across Eve's face "Oh, right. Sorry Aline, that was insensitive..." she then looked at Lara "What's the name of your Pokémon anyway?"

"I just call him Mac!" said Lara, smiling proudly.

"You mean the computer?" asked Eve, prompting Aline to snicker.

"<What's a computer?>" asked Mac.

"No, you doofus! Who names their Pokémon after a computer?!" asked Lara, facepalming.

Eve just rolled her eyes at Lara and looked down to Satella, who was still nuzzling her.

"Anyway, you two want to see something fun? Satella here is a box of surprises! You never know what she may be able to do next!" said Eve, smiling

"Really?" asked Lara, Aline also tilted her head at Eve

"<Oh please don't tell me->" said Nazrin, filled with dread.

"Okay, Satella, use Metronome!"

The Cleffa perked up at the mention of the move and looked at everyone else, she began waggling her fingers back and forth. Then she opened her mouth and started firing a thick Smokescreen across the whole room, worse yet, the room was completely sealed off.

"What the-" said Lara, before beginning to cough so much that she couldn't really speak properly

"Satella!" said Eve, coughing

"<Cleffa?>"

"<Could've been worse.>" said Nazrin, also coughing

"<How does she have so much smoke inside her?!>" said Mac

The girls rushed out of the room, opening the door to let the smoke out, once most of it was gone and they could breathe safely, both Aline and Lara gave Eve annoyed looks.

"Y'know, I don't want anything to do with that thing if those are the surprises she has ready for us." said Lara, with Aline nodding in agreement.

"Sorry..." said Eve, sighing

"<Cleffa!>" said Satella, flailing her stubby arms happily.

Aline snickered at the Cleffa, amused with how oblivious she was to the problem she just caused.
