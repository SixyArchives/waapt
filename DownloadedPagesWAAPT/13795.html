"Dragonite? This is your new team member. His name is Rex and he's... Well, it's time to train and I need to see what he can do." Valorie explained.

<Hi! My name is Dragonite and this is my mom.> the giant dragon pokemon replied light-heartedly.She didn't know the meaning of the words Tyrantrum was using.

<What—...What's wrong with you?> Rex asked, skeptically narrowing his eyes. <Are you a child?> he proceeded to ask in disbelief. How could someone who acted like that be so big?!

<I... guess so!> Dragonite replied hesitantly. <Why..?> she proceeded to ask, raising a hand to her cheek in a concerned manner.

"...Y'know, if I didn't know any better I'd say you were compensating for something." Shu's ghostly voice spoke up. Valorie furrowed her brows for a long moment as her teenage brain tried to process what Shu was implying.

"...Shut up!" Valorie snapped after the moment passed, earning herself a chuckle from the yamask hiding within her presence. Valorie glared away at an unseen foe before disregarding the teasing and turning her attention back to the training at hand.

"Dragonite, this is a strength exercise! Try and overpower Rex with superpower! Rex, give it the best you've got!" Valorie called out.

Dragonite's body began to glow with a blue aura and stretched her wing; her wings expanded and filled out to catch wind as she suddenly launched off the ground, closing the short distance between the two in the blink of an eye—given that it was an incredibly short distance—and grabbing Tyrantrum with both arms. Tyrantrum immediately applied his terrifyingly powerful jaws to one of Dragonite's arms, only to find that even as he crunched down on her flesh and bone it didn't even slow her down.

Dragonite winced as pain shot through her arm and she yanked Rex off the ground, raising the kingly pokemon above her head.

<FFFFFFUUUUUUUUUUU—!!!> Rex roared out at the top of his lungs, easily heard throughout the entire town. His cries of fury were cut off however by an equally loud bang as Dragonite slammed Rex down into the sand with a terrifying amount of strength, creating a short-lived spray of sand high into the air and leaving a large crater. where in Rex's body laid.

The tyrant pokemon twitched erratically. <This is so... wrooong...> he cough-groaned out.
