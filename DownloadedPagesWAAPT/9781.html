Bravely Fashionaaabluh

Driftveil City, PWT

A few days following Yancy's departure, the duo in Driftveil were still exploring the city, getting know the various intricacies of the place. Rex, for his part, had been rather eager to meet the regional leader, Clay, for a chance to discuss a particular matter. However, his Gym had been closed earlier, so he'd decided to travel to the second most likely place the man would be.

And, along the way, he'd picked up another companion.

This was, of course, was the young male he'd met in Johto a while back: Owen Baker. For some reason, the 10-year-old was convinced that he was a ninja and even named his Pokemon in some foreign language to match the fact. Either way, the young male seemed to look up to him after their meeting, tagging along with him on the trip.

Rex didn't mind; he had proved to be rather compelling company.

Owen: So, people come from all over the world to battle here?

Rex: Seems that way. Trainers, Gym Leaders, and Champions. But the Elite Four remain in their home regions, as far as I know. I guess it's just in case there's some kind of threat to their region while the Gym Leaders and Champions are away.

Owen: Huh. So, Wattson Xiānshēng would be here, too?

Rex: Xi-...? Well...if it's the Wattson I'm thinking of, then yes.

The young blonde seemed pleased by the answer, his red headband ribbons bobbing to and fro behind him as he looked around for any possible sign of the Electric-type Gym Leader.

Rex: You seem pretty eager to meet him.

Owen: Mhm~ Wattson Xiānshēng is a hero of mine! I hope I get a chance to talk to him.

???: You should check inside.

Owen: Oh?

Both boys turned their attention in the direction of the voice, a burly man riding a motorcycle riding over. The wind began to blow suddenly, causing his purple hair to flutter dramatically in the wind. Were this an action movie, surely some kind of 80's rock track would be playing over his arrival.

Motorcyclist: The Gym Leaders that are here register for the tournaments inside. You can check the roster to see who is here, though at this time of year, it wouldn't exactly be uncommon to find only Unovan Leaders registered.

Rex: Oh, that's helpful. Thanks, mister... umm...

Motorcyclist: I'm a heartbreaker... My name... Charles.

Silence.

Owen: Why the dramatic pause?

Charles: Here, I want you to have this.

Ignoring the boy's question, the motorcycle driving man rode forward, placing a Rocky Helmet into Owen's hands.

Charles: I'm a heartbreaker... My name... Charles. Giving exposition and bestowing trainers with random items fits a bad boy like me.

With that said, the man rode off on his motorcycle, leaving two confused youths to stare after him blankly.
