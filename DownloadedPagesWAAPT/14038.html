350927 ChaoticMarin13th Feb 2014 03:39:06 AM from Nintendoland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: 700 wives and 300 concubines
Chiptune DJ

Route 12

Blink. Blink. Valorie had woken up standing again, this time not even in a city. She quietly cursed her luck. At this rate she'd be falling asleep during battles with how little sleep she'd gotten! She began checking over herself, seeing as she wasn't in a safe place and wasn't entirely sure what she'd done while asleep. She was, oddly enough, dressed. She also had her satchel, which as always contained some food and water in the event she couldn't find her way back to town quickly. As eerie a thought as it was she was grateful she didn't have to worry about being stranded without food or her pokemon. With a sigh she began to back up, only to bump into something even shorter than she was conveniently placed behind her.

Valorie shrugged this off and sat down on what felt like a bolder, only for it to rise up underneath her and begin shaking itself back and fourth.

<Get OOOOOOFF!!> a geodude yelled, startling Valorie. The shortcake just sort of looked around frantically.

"Stop! Aah!—Sorry!" Valorie replied in an alarmed tone before haphazardly diving off and stumbling several feet away before regaining her balance.

<Hmph... Stupid girl.> the grumpy geodude muttered as it began relocating to somewhere less likely to be kicked or used as a seat.

Valorie's cheeks puffed up a little in irritation and she shook a fist vaguely in the general direction of the voice in the darkness. "Try taking it as a compliment next time!" she called out backhandedly. Geez, what a jerk..!

Valorie began digging through her satchel. It was the dead of what was only technically 'morning' and pitch black as a result. She needed light, but a part of her just... did not want to see Charizard right about now.

She released Eelektross instead, because she was entirely reliable!

"Hey Eelektross, can I have some light..?" she asked. The super eel began to glow with a powerful electricity, staring quietly at her. What Valorie couldn't tell, because highly inhuman physiology, was that Eelektross was giving her the Kubrick Stare.

<...>

Valorie obliviously produced a light sandwich and began helping herself to breakfast. "Thanks. Charizard and I are going through some tough times right now and I just..." she started, shaking her head helplessly.

<...>

Valorie did notice something odd in that Eelektross was not replying. She met the other's gaze and frowned, somehow a tad uncomfortable with that stare full of intent. Could that be..? Nah! She probably just wasn't a talkative pokemon.
