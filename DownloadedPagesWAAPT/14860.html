Route 17, near Anistar City [1]

The interpol agents had finally caught up to the man in the suit. The first thing they noticed reports missed was the girl. Quickly Kelly connected the dots with her husband; whatever the image of the girl that traveled alongside him had a connection with the murders.

Kelly: "Drop any weapons and withdraw any pokemon you have outside! This is your only warning!"

Johnson: "We know you have a Cofagrigus in your possession, and we are properly equipped to stop you by force if needed. You are surrounded!"

The man simply smiled, while Cofagrigus slowly revealed itself from the illusion that surrounded them. However that man still wore an expression of indifference.

Johnson: "Not amused yet, you sick freak?"

Kelly: "I don't understand, how can he hide a Cofagrigus in plain sight?"

Johnson turned to face his wife. "It's Night Shade... he must be constantly sending out attacks to fake people out and catch them by surprise."

The husband and wife team sent out their own Pokemon, Alakazam and Absol to put down the threat once and for all. Even then, the Man only let out a small chuckle.

That Man: "You should leave us alone. You have no chance to make it out alive."

Johnson: "I'd rather die than let someone like you roaming free! Alakazam, use Psychic!"

Alakazam began sending waves of PSI straight towards Cofagrigus, softening him for Kelly's Absol to use Night Slash. However as Absol neared Cofagrigus, the ghostly woman stood in front of the attack.

Kelly: "What the-?!"

Johnson: "How didn't we get this sooner? That's not just an illusion, that's a-"

Just as they reached the realization, their bodies began feeling tired, their vision blurred. Soon enough the whole area began to ripple as if it was a reflection in a pond.

Kelly: "Where's back-up?"

Johnson didn't answer to his wife. He felt as if his fighting spirit was being sucked out of a straw, when he saw flames appear out of the corner of his eyes.

Johnson: "Alakazam, grab Kelly and teleport out of here, now!!

Alakaza: <But sir->

Johnson: "THAT'S AN ORDER!! GET OUT!!!"

Kelly: "I'm not leaving you here!"

The pokemon quickly ignored the rest of the battle and grabbed his trainer's wife, her eyes where tearing up as she tried pushing Alakazam off.

That Man: Do you believe you have any other choice?

A very large, humanoid shadow appeared behind Alakazam, slamming a fist against him. The shadow turned to face Kelly with a single, red eye. Johnson tried to react and help his wife, but his body gave in under his own weight. He didn't give up, and began crawling towards her.

Absol attacked the shadow with another Night Slash, making it reveal itself as a Dusknoir as it writhed in pain.

Absol: <Kelly what are your orders?>

Absol saw as Kelly had a vacant stare, her face contorted with horror as she saw her husband lay motionless. Alakazam wasn't responding either. Absol was able to fight, but he was distracted by what her trainer was going through.

That Man: You are the ones that came closest to put up resistance. But you are still quite out-matched.

The ghost girl's glamour faded, revealing her identity as a Mismagius, as well as her comrades Chandelure and Dusknoir. Absol was surrounded by the flames and slowly began to tire.

Absol: <You consumed the support team's souls... using the illusions to keep us busy.>

Kelly picked up her husband's radio and desperately tried to send out a last message as she ran towards Anistar. Absol provided cover, fiercely attacking those that came close.

Kelly: "Locke, they're a whole team, they got us. We lost everyone. I'm sorry I couldn't do more. Tell Maria... we-"

The last thing to be heard from the radio was a short struggle, and eventually static.
