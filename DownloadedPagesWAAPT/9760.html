“You just never learn do you?” he asked, with a chuckle and a gentle smile, he then noticed her clothing and was at the very least surprised by it “I thought you hates this kind of clothing, why are you wearing it?” he asked again, now intrigued.

“It... It’s just that the paper said that we should try to be creative with the clothing, so I asked Sakura to buy this.” lied Lyuri, looking away and noticing that Duke was holding something “What’s that?”

“It said that? Oh, then I messed up.” said Duke, scratching his head with his free hand, before raising what seemed to be a small bag “This? It’s for you, I thought I’d give you something just in case you were still a bit down.” he answered, handing her the bag.

“O-Oh, thanks.” she said, taking the bag and seeing what was inside, she saw the Master Ball and a big box of chocolate, her eyes widened and her mouth was wide open as she saw the presents “T-That’s… Thanks Duke.”

“Hope you don’t mind that I helped myself at some of your chocolates.” said Duke, chuckling a bit before starting to head to the exit “So, to the festival?” he asked, gesturing at the entrance door, a sweet smile in his face.

“R-Right! Let’s go already!” said Lyuri, going ahead of him.

Duke then followed, but just as he got outside he glanced at the inside through the transparent doors and saw Sakura come down as well, wearing a blue and purple yukata like Lyuri’s, as well as holding a fan, she also noticed him and stopped on her tracks, just smiling and waving at him, what was she going to do...? Duke didn’t even have time to think that as Lyuri suddenly called him out and he had to follow her to the festival. The two of them could end up coming across Herbert, Jacob, Sam and Tagg.
