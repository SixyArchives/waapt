Eventually, the effort of putting one foot in front of the other becomes too much, and he keels over.-

Herbert: Urgh... why can't I just go home...?

-After kneeling on all fours for a while, trying to track the passage of time and failing, he eventually becomes aware of two voices speaking nearby.-

??? 1: <Well well, what do we have here?>

??? A: <Fresh meat, by the looks of things.>

??? 1: <Meat? You wouldn't get more than a morsel or two off of this skinny little runt.>

-Herbert looks up to see a pair of Scrafty standing over him, leering.-

Scrafty 1: <Oh look, it's moving. Perhaps it's not quite dead after all.>

Scrafty A: <Well, we can always remedy that situation. Don't want it trying to crawl away before we finish our meal, do we?>

-Herbert groans, barely cogniscient of the present danger through the haze of his exhaustion, and raises his sword with a weak, trembling arm.-

Herbert: Stay back... I'm warning you, I'm armed...

Scrafty 1: <Aww, would you look at that! How cute! It's got a stick.>

-The Scrafty kicks the sword out of Herbert's hand and delivers a hard blow to the side of his head, causing him to collapse face-first onto the ground.-

Scrafty 1: <Do you really think you can fight back, runt? Go on, then. Show us!>

-The Scrafty starts to kick Herbert viciously in the ribcage, each blow punctuated with a groan of pain and an occasional sharp crack. After a period of time which is again indeterminable to the exhausted and battered Herbert, the Scrafty is pulled away by his companion.-

Scrafty A: <Stop playing with your food! If you're going to finish it off, then just get it over with. I'm getting hungry.>

Scrafty 1: <Urgh, fine. You're no fun.>

-Stepping away from Herbert for a moment, the Scrafty reenters his blurred field of view a few seconds later armed with a fist-sized rock, and he suddenly realises just how real the danger facing him is.-

Herbert: NO!

-He lashes out at the Scrafty with an open-palm strike, hand glowing slightly, and it is knocked back several paces by the blow. Unfortunately, it remains conscious, now rather battered and severely pissed-off.-

Scrafty 1: <Oh, that is it. You're finished now.>

Scrafty A: <When we're done with you, there won't be anything left for the Mandibuzz.>

-Herbert sighs, fatigued from the effort required to pull off a Force Palm, and closes his eyes in preparation for the finishing blow. Only it never comes.-

Scrafty 1: <Hey, who the hell are you?>

Scrafty A: <You can't just come marching over here and - URK!>

-There is the sound of a scuffle, several punches being thrown, and two bodies hitting the ground. A pair of firm hands pick Herbert up off the ground, and the last thing he hears before he loses consciousness is a grizzled voice.-
