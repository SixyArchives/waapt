Past (Olivine City: Pokemon Center)

Rex: A...trade?

Young Boy: Yeah, a trade! You know: two trainers exchange Pokemon! It's easy~

He hugged the Farfetch'd next to Rex happily, the duck-like Pokemon letting out a happy coo at the affections.

Young Boy: I've always heard about how cool Farfetch'd are, so I wanted to catch one myself! But...I'm scared of all the Miltank on the route where they are! So, if you traded yours to me, it'd be a really big help. What do you say? I have a really good Pokemon that I'll trade you for it!

Rex: ...

Looking over to the happy duck and the boy, Rex felt a small nagging sensation in the back of his head. He didn't want to trade any of his Pokemon! He caught them fair and square through battles and training and all other kinds of things!

But looking at the expression of joy on Farfetch'd's face, he felt his desire to say no waver. This boy did seem to care a great deal about the species...and Farfetch'd did seem to take a shine to him almost immediately.

...Would Farfetch'd be [happier] if he went with him?

Rex: I...I...

Future (Lilycove City: Contest Hall)

Girl: Rex? ...Are you alright?

Blinking out of his reverie, Rex's eyes focused on her face, hesitation clouding his features.

Rex: I'm fine...

He looked over at Dahlia, the plant Pokemon finally meeting his gaze. She looked at him not with anger, but confusion. She seemed...uncertain about the possibility. To be honest, any unwillingness he felt was probably greater than hers. There was no way that he wanted to lose any of his teammates...but at the same time, wouldn't it be better for Dahlia were she able to be with someone who could better meet her needs? He wasn't even sure about the direction to take his own life, stumbling between battles, researching, and being...whatever a member of the J-Team was.

Dahlia had never cared for Gym Matches. She'd found his attention to her during his researching phase flattering, but being able to become a famous Contest star was greater than any attention that he could give. And as far as the J-Team, he doubted that she'd enjoy "saving the world" from disasters on a frequent basis.

So, there was only one thing to do.

Rex: ...I accept.
