363217 FullMoon23rd Mar 2014 12:07:16 PM from Kalos Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Squeeeeeeeeeeeee!
The most unique type
Thanks to Herbert for spagging this with me

Lumiose City, Hotel Richissime

-Herbert is sitting on his bed in his hotel room, rather anxiously swinging his legs to and fro.-

Herbert: ...Are you sure he's going to show up? I mean, it's not like he knows I'm back yet or anything...

Bishop: <Do bear in mind that Duke is an Auric, and quite a capable one at that. I suspect he will find his way to you soon enough.>

-As if on cue, there's some knocking on Herbert's door. Herbert jumps slightly, then relaxes and looks over at the source of the noise.-

Herbert: Come on in.

-Duke then comes in with his usual smile on his face, though he also seemed rather worried-

Duke: Hey Herbert... Are you doing all right?

-Herbert gives a small nod.-

Herbert: Yeah, I'm fine. Or at least I'm doing better than I was, at any rate. Taking a couple of weeks off really helped. Kind of ironic that I had to take a vacation from a vacation, huh?

-He chuckles nervously, then scratches the back of his neck.-

Herbert: So, uh... there's some stuff - scratch that, there's a lot of stuff I ought to be apologising for, isn't there?

Duke: There's some, yes. Though it's not like I came here just to demand an apology. I was just worried since part of the reason you went into depression was me.

-Duke was noticeably more serious now and he also looked as if there was something he wanted to say, but that he wanted to leave for later.-

Herbert: No, you weren't responsible for anything... it was all my fault, really. I'm the one who said all the stupid Tauroscrap that got me into trouble in the first place. Again, I'm sorry, I just... well, I can try to justify it all I like, but by now my apologies have probably become kind of meaningless anyway.

Duke: To be honest, I was already expecting you to react like that from the instant I saw you that day. That's why I was so nervous about telling what happened, well... That and the fact that it was rather embarrassing. I knew you'd react like that from the start.

-He then gives a small sigh and looks down slightly-

Duke: And I guess that's the main issue here... Right?

Herbert: ...Yeah. Yeah, I suppose it is... I mean, Bishop told me what the two of you talked about whilst I was away.

Bishop: <My apologies for any breach of privacy, but I felt that it would help clear the air between you somewhat. Hopefully I was not mistaken.>

-Herbert glances away from Duke, shifting somewhat uncomfortably.-

Herbert: Well, it... the whole incident showed me that I haven't been a particularly good friend. I'd like to say I tried, but I'll admit that I started to think that everything that went on in Orre made up for all the times I screwed people over... I guess somewhere along the line I forgot that trust is supposed to be mutual. And there's no apologising for that, really.

-Duke then leans against the wall and lets out another sigh as he folds his arms-

Duke: Right now... I'll admit that you're hardly the first person that I think of when I'm looking for advice. Usually I'd go for Lyuri or Sakura, maybe Dune and Deziree or Arika and Chrome since they've helped Lyuri and myself with our issues before. But you... There was always that fear that you might make things worse due to how blunt you are. It took me that conversation with Bishop to realize that maybe I didn't trust you as much as I should...

-Herbert continues to avoid Duke's gaze, though he gives a quiet and somewhat nervous chuckle.-

Herbert: ...Can't say I blame you, really. I guess that makes two of us, then.

Duke: Believe me, I want to be able to trust you to help me when I need, or at least help me with more minor things that don't have anything to do with brainwashing. But unlike your own trust issues, mine come just from how you don't seem to be able to keep some thoughts for yourself. I know that you not only dislike Lyuri, but also the entire concept behind our relationship, but... Well, it would be nice if you could leave those facts aside and give me some support once in a while when I have a similar problem.

-He then unfolds his arms and gives Herbert a hint of a smile-

Duke: By the way... You don't need to act so nervous like that, it's not like I want to hurt you for what happened or anything, nor am I cutting off our friendship. I just thought that maybe this could help us deal with any remaining doubts we have about each other.

Herbert: I know... I'm not nervous, I just can't help but feel rather awkward. After all, the whole subject matter kind of makes awkwardness unavoidable.

-He looks back up at Duke.-

Herbert: ...I guess one of the reasons I'm so blunt is because I hate the idea of people hiding things from me. That's why I was so torn up back when I first found out you were a Lucario, and then there was the whole Shadowing thing... suddenly finding out that there was so much that you were keeping from me was painful to say the least, and even now I still can't stop myself from wondering if there's anything more... but I'll be the first to admit that my excuses don't justify anything I said or did. So... how about we come to a compromise? You can stop feeling like there's stuff you have to stop me from finding out about so long as I make an effort to be less of a jerk - or just try to be a less vocal jerk, if nothing else. Deal?

-Duke then lets out a smile-

Duke: Deal. Admittedly, I don't have much to hide from you, even the whole Lucario thing wasn't me being afraid of you finding out and more like Lyuri being afraid of the reaction that would come from it, if the circumstances were different I wouldn't have any problem to reveal it. After all, I'm probably not the only one that feels this way regarding how you act sometimes. Still, I guess it'll be easier for me to keep my end of the bargain that it'll be for you.

-Herbert frowns.-

Herbert: I'm supposed to be the one who makes smartass comments at the expense of others. Unless you want a role reversal or something.

-Duke chuckles a bit-

Duke: I guess that's what I get from hanging with you and Lyuri so often.

-Duke just smiles and thinks for a bit before remembering something-

Duke: By the way, have you watched the karaoke video? I believe Hikari came here to deliver a CD.

Herbert: Oh yeah, Bishop's still keeping hold of that for me. I should probably get around to watching it at some point... might even make a start now, in fact.

Duke: All right then, I think you'll enjoy it. The lyrics in the song Sakura and Lyuri sang together were pretty amusing.

-He gets significantly sadder-

Duke: Also... Lyuri was worried sick about you. She was the one who told me about your situation after she talked to you herself. She was pretty much begging me to come here talk to you and sort things out since she was feeling really bad for everything and was afraid of making things worse by trying to talk to you again. That conversation probably got into the second CD as well. Of course, upon learning about how depressed you were I didn't need anyone to ask me to come here.

-He then gives a sigh-

Duke: I know you still hate her due to what happened in Orre, but... Try to restore some faith in her, please. She does care about you, in her own way, and she does recognize that she can get a bit out of control when angry. Like you, she wants to improve, but it doesn't happen so quickly.

-Herbert glances away again.-

Herbert: ...I probably said some things I didn't mean last time around. I wasn't really thinking straight.

-Duke grimaces a little-

Duke: She told me you had threatened to hang yourself with your own scarf. Needlessly to say... That gave me quite the scare.

-An almost ashamed expression passes across Herbert's face, and his gaze drops back down to his feet.-

Herbert: ...I wish she hadn't seen me like that. Not that it's a new experience for her...

Duke: It's all right. Lyuri is not unfamiliar to that kind of thought, she probably can relate to some extent. She was just scared that you would end up dying for real because of her, just near-death was bad enough for her to deal with.

-Herbert falls silent, focusing intently on the bedroom floor.-

Duke: I should probably go tell her that you're fine now and that she doesn't need to worry. Don't worry, she won't be down for long, just really relieved. Though... I do have to say that ever since what happened in Orre, I never expected to tell you to not worry about her like this. I guess maybe you two will be able to sort things between yourselves eventually...

-Duke seemed to have an almost hopeful smile on his face. Herbert's expression is nowhere near as optimistic, but eventually he looks back up at Duke.-

Herbert: ...I ran into Allen the other day.

-Duke's expression becomes a lot more serious at that point-

Duke: You did...?

Herbert: Yeah... I'm not sure what he was up to, but he was hanging around one of the coffee houses in town with Aline. He teleported away before I could get my hands on him, though...

-Duke raises an eyebrow-

Duke: He was with Aline...?

Herbert: Well, I wouldn't have said he was if he wasn't, would I?

-He folds his arms.-

Herbert: By the way, you never told me that you told Aline about what happened in Orre. Or that your version skipped over some of the important bits. Namely the ones about who was or wasn't responsible for their own actions.

Duke: Oh, that... I'm guessing she told you about it... Are you upset about it?

Herbert: More annoyed than anything. I get that you don't want to tell a kid about all the gory details, but that doesn't give you an excuse to try and whitewash the whole affair.

Duke: Sorry about that. I just didn't want her to get any wrong ideas and I wasn't sure if she would understand the complicated situation Lyuri and I were in, as well as telling her just how much you suffered could potentially scare her.

Herbert: Well, she knows the whole story now. Least I could do after trying to decapitate someone in front of her was explain things. She... took it pretty well, actually. I don't think I managed to scare her at all. She just kept going on about how "heroic" I supposedly am. Kids these days have pretty low standards when it comes to role models...

-Duke lets out a chuckle-

Duke: I don't think she has low standards, even with how difficult you can be sometimes, it's clear that you're willing to sacrifice a lot to save someone you care about. While your personality isn't very heroic for the most part, your determination and resolve are something to be admired. And, well...

-He then gives a small, if somewhat playful smile-

Duke: Since you saved me, so you're kind of a hero to me as well.

-Herbert goes an exceptionally vivid shade of red and starts fanning himself with his scarf.-

Herbert: I, uh... well, I don't think I'm... erm, anyway, was that everything?

Duke: Yeah. I think thats all I have to say

-He then gets a bit more serious-

Duke: Though... Can I make a request?

Herbert: Uh... sure. I'm not really in a position to say no at the moment, after all.

Duke: I know that you have problems with secrets, considering your experiences, but... Don't go jumping to conclusions just because someone isn't telling you everything. Sometimes it could be just a small thing that they want to keep hidden to make themselves look better, maybe it would be a bit embarrassing to reveal the whole thing... Not everyone has dark secrets hidden. Don't get me wrong, I'm not trying to make my end of the deal easier, I just want you to... Have more faith in me and the others as well. I'll keep being honest around you as much as I can, but maybe sometimes I can end up being a bit vague, so please don't go getting any wrong ideas.

-Herbert starts to look rather uncomfortable again.-

Herbert: ...I can try.

-Duke then decides to sit on the bed next to Herbert and pats him on the head a bit-

Duke: I'm not expecting you to change so much in the span of a few days, especially considering all the trauma you've gone through. But... If you start having doubts about one of our friends, you know you can talk to me about it. Even if you have any doubts about myself you can confront me over them, after all, you're an Auric, you can tell if I'm telling a lie or not. I want to help you get over your problems, because I really don't want to see you depressed over them anymore.

-Herbert manages a faint smile.-

Herbert: ...Alright. Thanks for everything, Duke.

Bishop: <And you have my thanks as well for endeavouring to avoid any further misunderstandings.>

-Duke then playfully ruffles Herbert's hair a bit as he stands up. He then just heads towards the door-

Duke: See you later!

-He waves goodbye then leaves-
