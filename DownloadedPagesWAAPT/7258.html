Bluescale Swamp

Most of the group is asleep still.

But one of them isn't, for a certain Druddigon has gotten up early and made her way to the town's market, in hopes of making a purchase, if somewhat nervously. She really didn't want the others to know what she was up to.

Making one last check to be sure that nobody else in her group was around, Teala cautiously approached a certain Plushie stall that they had visited a week or two prior.

Steve the Feraligatr♂: <Oh, hello! I haven't seen you for a while.>

Teala: <Yeah, hi...>

Steve: <What's wrong? You look a bit nervous...>

Teala gulps, shaking her head.

Teala: <Nothing, I'm fine. I'm...I'm here to make a purchase.>

Steve: <Oh? Anything in particular you're looking for?>

She nods, then points a claw at a rather soft and pudgy looking Nidorino plushie, before quickly pulling out some of the local currency and setting it down on the counter.

Teala: <I think that's enough, yes?>

Pausing to do a quick count of the money, and finding that it was just enough, the Feraligatr proceeded to scoop it up and deposit it in the nearby safe.

Steve: <Would you like a bag? My friend Carle would be happy to provide you one.>

A familiar Caterpie pokes his head out from among the plushies, giving Steve an annoyed look.

Carle the Caterpie♂: <I'm a master of my craft, and yet you use me for the simple act of bag weaving. Truly I am wasted on you.>

Steve: <Oh, shush.>

Teala cocks her head at the two.

Teala: <Er...I'm good, thanks.>

Steve: <Okay, then...>

Steve hands the Nidorino over to Teala, which the Dragon carefully accepts.

<Enjoy, and...well, be careful when you cuddle him. Rough Skin and all.>

Teala silently nods, before giving her plushie a longing look. It wasn't quite the same as him...but it would do for now, at least.
