National Park

A Heat Wave or two from an Arcanine later, Sakura is standing next to the Audino while the Beedrill either fainted or retreated.

"Good job, Grant, if I didn't have you with me I would be in some real trouble." said Sakura, petting Grant.

"<Hey, it's not like it took that much effort.>" said Grant, grinning

"Still made all the difference."

Hearing that the Audino perks up and tugs at Sakura's sleeve.

"<You... You can understand me? Are you a trainer?>"

Sakura smiled at the injured Audino, carefully petting it on the head "Yes, for both."

The Audino goes wide-eyed and proceeds to suddenly cling to Sakura

"<Please let me be one of your Pokémon! Life in the wild is so mean and I'm hungry and tired and my body hurts a lot and... And...! Just please help me!>" it begged, sounding as if it was about to burst into tears.

Sakura was caught off-guard by the request "Just what did this Audino go through...? I'm not having much of a choice here..." she grimaces.

"<Ooh, you got to her weakspot.>" said Grant, looking at Sakura with a knowing smile.

Sakura sighs and gives Grant a small smile before looking back at the Audino still clinging to her "All right then, I'll keep you safe, you poor thing." she pets it.

The Audino perks up "<Really?! Thank you thank you thank you thank you thank you THANK YOU>" it hugged her tightly, yelling the last bit quite loudly

"<Geez.>" said Grant, grimacing.

"Ow, my ears..." thought Sakura, wincing and proceeding to take a Heal Ball from her bag "There, now just stay still..."

Sakura touches the Audino with the Heal Ball and letting go of it, sucking it inside the ball. It wobbles three times before making a click as expected, Sakura then picks up the Heal Ball and sends out the Audino, who now looks perfectly healthy.

The Audino blinks "<Wow... I'm feeling so good right now!>" it said, smiling warmly

"Good to hear~" said Sakura, smiling "Say, are you male or female?"

"<I'm female~>" she answered in a very soft-spoken tone.

"<Well, do you have a name?>" asked Grant

She shakes her head "<Does Audino count?>" she tilted her head

"Well... What about Valentine? Sounds good enough for you?" asked Sakura, smiling

The Audino pondered about it for a bit before smiling at Sakura "<Sounds nice! Thank you, Ms.Trainer! I promise I'll do anything you ask me to!>" she cheerfully stated.

Sakura giggles "Well, good to see I made you happy, and healthy."

"<Now she just needs to meet the others.>" said Grant, smiling

Valentine began to hesitate a bit upon the mention of other Pokémon "<I hope they're nice...>" she thought.
