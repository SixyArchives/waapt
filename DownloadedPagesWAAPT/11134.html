"<It's his birthday tomorrow? Huh, thanks for telling me.>"

"<Yeah! We even got that armband thingy for him!>" said Conan

"Yes, Grant likes to spend some time as a human and..." Sakura then paused, and then took a look at Seth "... Hmmmm..."

"<... Tell me you aren't->" said Seth

"Could you put this armband on?"

"<No.>"

"Please? I want to see how you look as a human!"

"<No thanks.>"

"It's not nice to ask me for something when you can't even do something for me you know~" said Sakura with an almost taunting smile

"<...>"

"..."

Seth sighs and takes the armband "<Fine...>" he said as he put it on.

Upon wearing it, Seth became a human about 1.76 tall that looked like he was at the range of 18~21 years old. He had short, spiky green hair and wore a jacket with a similar tone of green as the one he usually has, a yellow t-shirt under it and dark green pants with some small yellow details on it, he was also wearing a pair of white and green sneakers. His eyes were also gold colored.

"How do you feel?" asked Sakura, curiously

"Light." he answered

"Huh? Wh- Oh. Anyway... Pretty nice, you look a bit shorter than before, but you're still taller than me. Quite young too... Too young to be a parent" said Sakura.

Seth gives her a pretty unamused look "Really now?"

Sakura then starts giggling "Anyway, you look pretty good as a human, big bro." she said, playfully

"<You look kinda cool!>" said Conan

"Thanks I guess, can I take it off now?" asked Seth

"How about spending the day like that while I go looking for something to give Grant? I'm sure Regina would like to see you like that." suggested Sakura

"Oh come on."

"I'll hear you out, just not right now, please?" said Sakura, with a look that pretty much begged him to do so.

"... Fine, just because it's you. I can understand that you're busy." he answered, with a sigh.

"Thanks! But in case you meet up with Grant, just pretends that you never saw him, I don't want him to find out about the armband yet."

"Can do."

"Great! Let's go Conan!" said Sakura, running out

"<'Kay!>" said Conan, following.

Seth then looks at them going and takes a better look at himself, he didn't really care about turning into a human, he just prefered to stay in the form he was already used to, though at least one day couldn't hurt.

He then tried to stretch himself "At least I feel a bit more nimble like this." he said, with a small smile forming on his face.
