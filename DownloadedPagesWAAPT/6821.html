Meanwhile, in another universe similar to our own...

On a sunny Friday morning in Aspertia City, a familiar young man and his not so familiar Mareep are taking a walk to the local park to stretch their legs, and in the latter's case, find some yummy grass to nom.

That said, the young man did want to see if he should get some Pokémon Battles in against any of the local trainers, if only to make his Mareep stronger for what lay ahead.

For this young man in particular was planning on starting his Pokémon journey in a few months time.

That said, the whole situation for him was rather awkward because, by the time he would be leaving, he would be a ripe twenty-one years of age. Which was astonishing considering that most trainers left home at around the age of fifteen in Unova. Heck, in faraway places such as Kanto and Hoenn, trainers could start their journey at as young of ten years of age!

So for him, it was a very late start. But as the saying went "Better late than never", so perhaps it wouldn't be as big a deal as he thought.

A tugging on his trouser leg interrupted his line of thinking as he looked down to see his Mareep companion giving him a concerned look.

Neon the Mareep♂: <Why so quiet? You haven't said a word since we left home.>

The human frowned awkwardly at his friend's statement, only just realizing that he had been rather quiet for the past few minutes.

Luke(?): "Sorry, I was just thinking."

The Mareep tilts his head quizzically.

Neon: <About what?>

Luke(?): "Our upcoming journey, mainly."

The two continue to walk, passing several houses in a row as they make their way to the end of the street, where the Aspertia park awaited them.

"You looking forward to it, Neon?"

The Mareep nods, grinning happily as he swings his tail excitedly from side to side.

Neon: <Baah! How could I not be excited at the chance to get our names up in lights? The path of a trainer is one lined with fame and admiration!>

Luke frowns at this.

Luke(?): "Not for everyone. Not all trainers can be as famous as Red, or Ethan. Or that mysterious guy with the Zekrom that was everywhere in the news recently."

He wondered what had become of that enigmatic man. There had been an entire media circus surrounding him ever since he had thwarted Team Plasma's plans over a year ago. Recently, however, he had disappeared to Arceus' knows where. It was almost as if he had vanished off the face of the Earth...

"Considering what happened to him, I'm not sure I want to be famous..."

Neon: <But why would you not? What could be more wonderful than being shined on by the spotlight of eternity? Our very names being spoken of in glorious legends of our successes! Oh, how we could shine, Luke! Like two of the brightest stars in the night sky! Our radiance forever lighting the way for people and Pokémon the world over!>

Luke couldn't help but chuckle. Neon couldn't help but be a little overenthusiastic when it came to the prospect of fame.

Luke(?): "It does sound wonderful the way you put it, Neon. But...there's a lot to be said for living the quiet life, too."

The Mareep simply smirks as the two reach the gates of the park.

Neon: <If you enjoy the quiet life so much, then why are you becoming a trainer?>

Luke pauses, knowing that his partner had backed him into a corner.

Luke(?): "Well, because being famous could be fun, I guess..."

With that, Neon smiles proudly, his tail bulb glowing to show his happy mood.

Neon: <I knew you would agree with me. Now, why don't we go and show off my dazzling coat of wool?>

Luke(?): "Only if we can get some battles in, too. Maybe now that you know Thunderwave, we'll be able to beat that kid with the Riolu that thinks he's so great."

Neon nods in agreement as the two enter the park.

Neon: <As long as we don't get my wool dirty...>
