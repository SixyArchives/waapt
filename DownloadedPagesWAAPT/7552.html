Lake: <Why's that?>

Lake: <Oh...well, lucky that I'm not like that then...>

Before he could call for help though, the wild Pokémon quickly covered Stratus mouth with a claw. Sucessfully restrained, the wild Pokémon lent down to speak to him, revealing to the Dragonite that he had been coaught by a huge Feraligatr that looked like it had seen more than it's fair share of battles, if all the battle scars covering it's body were anything to go by.
