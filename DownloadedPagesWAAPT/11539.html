288453 Herbert40k4th Sep 2013 04:44:21 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
Chords of Steel

Illusio - Around 600 years ago

-Two old Pugilian Warriors are sat in a rather dim room in some seldom-visited wing of the castle, talking, eating and drinking to while the night away. Just as they do every night. A Toxicroak and a Scrafty are accompanying them.-

Takuma: ...And in all these months, not once have we been called upon to help with the war! I served as the Warlord of Pugilis for forty years, and apparently my reward is to drink myself to death in a foreign backwater because those alliance bastards think they're too high and mighty to associate with common-born scum like me. Bloody Mandibuzz, that's all they are. It's just politics to them.

Brandon: If you say so, sir.

-Takuma takes another swig of his tankard - filled with water thanks to the drinking ban - before giving his drinking buddy a hard look.-

Takuma: It's all Lucanus' fault, you know. He's the one that started all of this and got us into this mess.

Brandon: Oh? How do you figure that?

Takuma: Things only started going to hell once the Ghost Lord took Pugilis, right? Can't blame the kid for that, I guess, but then he went and started working with the bastard! Thanks to him, those Spectran bastards have a puppet state smack bang in the middle of Ransei. I should never have chosen him as Warlord, kid's only ever been a headache since day one...

-He takes another sip of his drink with a melancholy gaze into the middle distance.-

Takuma: It seemed like a good idea at the time, you know? I was getting old, knew I had to hand over the reins eventually. Then you lot arrived on your ship from Kalos... usually I'd never have dreamed of giving power to a foreigner, of all people, but when I saw Lucanus my gut told me the kid would be a born leader. My gut's never lied to me before, so I put him on the throne, and look at where we are now. Ruined, that's where.

Brandon: It is indeed a lamentable state of affairs, sir. Your drink appears to be rather drained - would you like me to get you a refill?

Takuma: That'd be great.

-Brandon takes Takuma's tankard and quietly sweeps out of the room. He returns several minutes later not with two ale mugs filled with water, but instead with a tray carrying a pair of wine bottles and two flute glasses.-

Takuma: Hang on, what's all this about?

Brandon: Well, sir, I am aware of how the alcohol ban here in Illusio has been affecting you. So I took it upon myself to acquire some... special beverages for us to enjoy.

Takuma: Brandon, you brilliant son of a bitch, you know me too well.

Brandon: It was nothing, sir.

-He sets down the tray and carefully pours out two glasses of wine, one of which he offers to Takuma. The grizzled old Warlord smiles.-

Takuma: You're a good kid, you know that, Brandon? Not like that two-timing backstabbing yellow-bellied scumbag Lucanus. Perhaps some of you Kalosians can be decent folks after all.

-Brandon gives a small smile in return.-

Brandon: Thank you, sir. I'm flattered.

Takuma: Pleasure's all mine, lad. Now then, bottoms up!

-Brandon's smile grows wider as he takes a sip of his drink, watching his companion over the brim of his glass. By the time Takuma tastes the poison in his wine, it is far too late. Just as the Scrafty becomes aware of the situation around him, the Toxicroak has already delivered a sharp jab to his throat, and he slumps to the floor besides Takuma's chair. Brandon drains the last of his wine before looking at the two bodies in front of him and giving a small smile.-

Brandon: Well, I do believe a toast is in order.

Sumac: <Finally! Man, I'm pretty sure poison's not supposed to take that long to work. I mean, the poison I'm named after, sumac? That kills you so quickly you don't even know you're dead. In fact... oh my Arceus, we could've both been killed by sumac right now, and we wouldn't even->

Brandon: Quiet, Sumac. Our patience is a virtue that has paid off. Now that Aiura has finally taken off that damned diadem, we have no reason to fear retribution. For all she knows, the old fool died peacefully in his sleep. Not that it matters, since we will be long gone by the time this scene is discovered.

Sumac: <What about the Scrafty, though? Are we going to say that he fell down some stairs and landed in a pile of ketchup or smething?>

Brandon: Oh, that thing? If you feel that disposal is really that important, just dump its body in the sea first chance you get. Nobody's going to investigate. I doubt they'll even notice that it's gone missing in the first place.

-He gives a cold, quiet chuckle.-

Brandon: Heh. Even with the alcohol impairing his judgement, it still amuses me to think that such a famously paranoid man allowed himself to fall for one of the oldest tricks in the book. No doubt his heirs would be devastated, if they were still alive. I can only imagine their-

Sumac: <Um, boss? We might have a minor problem.>

-The Toxicroak gestures to the Scrafty lying on the floor, which upon closer inspection is revealed to be not dead but instead merely lying very still and making faint gurgling noises.-

Brandon: Really? I'm disappointed in you, Sumac. Make sure that it's a clean cut next time around. Quick and painless, remember?

Sumac: <Hey, I've gotten used to using my claws to open jars of jam. And I'm pretty sure jam jars don't feel pain. They make the same kind of mess when you rip them open, though...>

Brandon: Not to worry. It is a problem that can be quickly remedied.

-He pulls a dagger from his belt and kneels in front of the Scrafty, but pauses for a moment when he hears it speak.-

Scrafty: <...Y-you...>

Brandon: Still capable of speech, are you? Well, if you've got anything to say, you'd better get it off your chest quickly.

Scrafty: <W-why...?>

Brandon: Why? Because Takuma denied me the power that was rightfully mine. When our expedition landed in Pugilis and we made contact with the local leaders, who did Takuma decide to nominate as his protege? Not the humble, hardworking servant boy who had earnt his knighthood through fifteen years of service and loyalty to the crown. No, he chose the rich, arrogant, idiotic Lucanus, whose only claim to knighthood is his parents' fortune and nothing more. But rest assured, once this war is over, Pugilis will be mine. Sadly, you won't live long enough to see that.

-With a flick of the knife, he finishes the Scrafty and rises to his feet.-

Brandon: Now all that remains is to ensure that any potential competitors find themselves unable to make a bid for power. But where to begin...?

Sumac: <I dunno. That Poderick kid, maybe?>

Brandon: Lucanus' former squire? I do believe the boy has taken up permanent residence here in Illusio. And he is how old, again? Fifteen summers? I doubt he will prove to be a threat. No, I believe we should commence with the other members of the Pugilian court. Now that Takuma is dead and Lucanus exiled, that only leaves one potential canditate.

-He nods to Sumac.-

Brandon: Now, I do believe we should be on our way. With any luck we shall bump into our next target whilst we are travelling.

Sumac: <Alright, road trip!>

-The two head off.-
