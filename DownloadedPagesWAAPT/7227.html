180664 PippingFool2nd Aug 2012 05:39:25 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

Efrafa

Laurel's ears rapid twitch from place to place attempting to absorb all the sounds he can hear in the ditch. There is an infernal hissing/snarling/grunting/growling/chewing sound coming from the end of the ditch, as well as the scratching of claws on the dirt and the small yelps and squeals from some unfortunate creature dragged down here that Laurel couldn't identify at this time.

In no time at all, they reach the near-end of the ditch.

Silverweed: <Wait here>

The Nidorino leaves his accompanists sides to to dive down into the last stretch of the ditch to talk to that... thing that is currently feeding down there. Laurel overhears the conversation between the two.

Silverweed: <Sir I->

Woundwort: <Why are you here Oswlafa?>

Silverweed: <S-sir, we've found this hlessi on patrol. He said he want to... join us.>

Woundwort: <Join us... How strange...>

Silverweed: <I believe he's worthy sir.>

Woundwort: <I will be the judge of that...>

The next thing Laurel saw was a skirmish Silverweed scampering out of the hole. Followed by him.

Monstrous size, twice of that of any Nidoking he's seen before, with the bodyspikes and head horn to match.

Hide littered with the claw wounds of rivals he has mercilessly disposed of.

One dead eye.

So the rumors of the mystical General Woundwort were not exaggerated. He really was a demon of a Nidoking.

Woundwort: <Hlessi, pray do tell, who are you.>

Laurel: <My name is Laurel sir>

Woundwort: <Laurel?> -grunts- <What is you're story?>

Laurel tells Woundwort the same story he told Acorn and Silverweed above ground, and Woundwort carefully calculates it. He, like Acorn before him, can very well believe that this Nido was an Owsla in his previous warren, and that he managed to survive on his own without being captured or eaten by an elil of some kinds.

He reminded him of himself when he was a Nidorino.

Woundwort: <Very well, Laurel. You shall not only join Efrafa, I'd see it for you to become a member of the Owslafa. For we have... lost a few members in the last few weeks from a careless miscalculation. You're times will be the dusk and dawn silflays and the evening patrols. If you wish to have some of the flayrah, you may help yourself to it. If you wish for a mate, heh-heh, you can have any Nidorina you please, unless she's been marked by another Oswlafa. Those are you're "perks" if you will.>

Laurel twitched an ear ever so slightly. He found rather concerning that the females of this warren have no freedom what-so-ever in the face of the (all male) Owslafa. It made his drive to free at least some of the Nido's stronger.

Woundwort: <I shall have it organized for another Nido, Campion, to show you how we do things around here.]]

Laurel: <I understand sir>

Woundwort: <You shall be identified... by you're mark.>

Before Laurel could question what the mark was, he felt a wave of pain envelop his right-hind leg. For Acorn had just slashed and bit two huge, gaping incisions in his leg. He was then dismissed from Woundwort's quarters and was lead towards the Owslafa quarters. Fresh blood dripping behind.
