Somewhere southeast of Goldenrod - Swampy Region

Although seemingly deserted, the swamp was a cacophony of noise. Bug Pokémon buzzed, a distant Politoad croaked, and bubbles...blooped?

It seemed as if things were half a world away from new Pokémon, new-types and the fantastic phenomena known as Mega Evolution. As if untouched for nearly a decade and a half.

But some peoplemons liked it that way.

Through the turgid water, several worrying large shapes swam along, the only thing to give their presence away being a few large, vibrant red crests jutting out of the water's surface, gliding along without a sound.

The alpha predators were on the hunt. And their prey would never know they were coming. Not until it was too late.

They soon found their prey - a Golduck who was floating lazily in the water. It would put up a fight, but it would be worth the effort.

Stopping just outside of the dozing bird, a few blue heads stuck out of the water so they could quietly converse.

Wild Feraligatr #1: <Here's the attack plan: I'll move in, grab the target by the spine, and attempt a clean break. While it's struggling, you all move in, grab a limb and tear 'em off. Remember: you eat what you keep. Got it?">

Wild Feraligatr #4: <We know. You've knocked that through our heads more than enough times already.>

Wild Feraligatr #1: <Can the lip or we'll eat you ne- >

Wild Feraligatr #2: <Hold up! There's something else in the water over there.>

The crocodillians could all feel it through the water, something else was moving through the water - right towards their intended prey, at that.

Wild Feraligatr #3: <Hey! Who is that? He's got some nerve going after our target...>

Wild Feraligatr #1: <I think that's...Oh no.>

In an instant, the water in front of the Golduck erupted. But what emerged was not a Feraligatr. Almost the opposite in fact.

While it was Draconian, it wasn't really much of a traditional example. This cream-scaled Pokémon possessed a big round nose, a small horn on it's head flanked by two antennae, a pair of green wings that were seemingly far too small to pull the creature skyward's, two stubby arms ending in three small claws, a pair of large hips the bore two flat clawed feet, and a long thick tail.

Even so, this non-threatening Pokémon still startled the Golduck awake.

Wild Golduck: <QUACK!>

Stratus the Dragonite (Lv. 89): <Hello there! Me and my family are just about to have a huge dinner, and we're inviting everyone in the swamp, would you like to->

The Dragon blinked, noticing that his quarry had long since fled.

<...Oh. Perhaps I came on too strong?>

There were several large splashes as the pack of Feraligatr emerged from the water nearby, all bearing annoyed looks pointed straight at the Dragonite.

Wild Feraligatr: <DAD!!!>

With a heavy sigh, the Dragonite turned to his apparent offspring.

Stratus: <I know, I know! Next time I won't sneak up on 'em!>

The lead Feraligatr sighed, burying his face in his hands.

One of his sibling had a lot to answer for.
