Psyche looks up. "I wonder if Mom would be proud of me..."

Aiura smiles. "I'm sure she'd be extremely proud of the lovely young woman you've become. Speaking of which, happy birthday, dear. It's traditional to give young women a book when they reach adulthood, so I figure I'd continue the tradition."

Psyche sighs. "It's not 'Psychic Humilty for Dummies' is it? I have 3 already."

Aiura nods. "Any lady worth her salt needs to know how to hold her own in combat. It was my coming of age gift."

And Now For Something Completely Different

Slateport (Around a month ago)

After the events in Mauville a month ago, the Tiny Trio has decided to take a leisurely tour around the region to recover from either the sudden regain of old memories or the shock of the reveal. Currently, they're back in Slateport enjoying the beach rays.

Vyvyan emerges from the change room with a pair of green and black boardshorts matching his usual hoodie attire. Though, by the fact he's clutching his upper arms, red cheeks and his constant, shaded glancing between various beachgoers indicates his discomfort/unease with being so... exposed in public.

Vyvyan: WHY did I get them to talk me into this? Oh yeah, because wearing a hoodie to the beach would be too conspicuous.

Why the beach anyway? Why not somewhere where wearing layers is commonplace...

Santiago: HEY! VYV! Over here!

Vyvyan turns to his right to see Sandy relaxing on a metallic Beach lounger and waving to him. Santiago himself has a pair of red [Hawaiian] print shorts, dorky shuttershades, a tanning mirror and a glass of Piña Colada placed on a small side table of said lounger.

Vyvyan shuffles towards his friend. Still embarrassed at his current attire.

Vyvyan: Hey Sandy... Didn't know you were so fond of... beaches... and stuff...

Santiago: Vyvyan. While I may not be able to swim anymore. I'm from a small, coastal fishing/tourist town - beaches hold nostalgic value for me.

Plus there is all of this wonderful sand for miles around! I can't say no to sand.

Santiago notices Vyvyan's uneasy body language and raises an eyebrow.

Santiago: Hola. Worried about being exposed as an almighty sky god there, Vyv?

Vyvyan: Not just that...

Vyv lowers his right arm while he strokes his upper right shyly.

Vyvyan: You know me... I'm.. self-conscious... Always have been... I've always thought myself to be... too thin?

Santiago chuckles as he grabs his drink.

Santiago: Aw, you really shouldn't be so hard on yourself. -Winks- You're cute, yannow.

Vyvyan goes scarlet.

Vyvyan: H-HEY!

Santiago: -Sips Pina Colada daintily- o3o.

He gestures to the beach lounger beside here.

Santiago: Just teasin' ya Vyv. C'mon! We're here to relax, so relax. Being high strung leads to an early death, they say~

Vyvyan: We're immortal.

Santiago: An early stasis then~

Vyvyan: *Sigh...*

Vyvyan slowly reclines in the lounger. Inhales. and sighs.

Vyvyan: -From the corner of his eye.- You got a steel lounger?

Santiago: Nah mate, Steel would melt after too long.

Sandy taps on his lounger.

Santiago: This is pure tungsten. Sturdy, and not liable to melt after prolonged napping periods~

Vyvyan raises his eyebrow at the tanning mirror.

Vyvyan: What's with the tanning mirror? Seems like an odd choice? For you...?

Santiago: Oh, I'm not using it to tan. I'm using it because it intensifies the sun's rays and that feels awesome.

Sandy brings up the mirror.

Santiago: Aaah~ The feeling of burning skin cells~

Sandy looks back to Vyvyan with a o3o face.

Santiago: Want a drink? The little drink shack down the way makes killer Piña Coladas, and their Daiquiris and Bellinis aren't bad either.

Vyvyan: Maybe later..

The two begin to relax, before a realisation hits them.

Vyvyan: Have you noticed..?

Santiago: The quietness? Yeah.

Both: -Look around- Where's Levi?
