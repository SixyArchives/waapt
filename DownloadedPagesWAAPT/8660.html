Ria: <Oh, um...>

Dihrao: <Come on, it'll be fine.>

Dihrao walks off into the woods. Ria hesitates, then ultimately decides to follow.

Dihrao: <You know, I've never really had a chance to come up here to Sinnoh until now.>

Ria: <Neither have I...>

Dihrao: <Oh? Well, it's not really much, but that's another thing we have in common.>

Ria: <I, uh... I guess we do...>

Dihrao: <...Is something wrong?>

Dihrao: <If you say so.>

They keep walking, Ria trying to stop thinking about Dihrao

Ria: <~Come on, Ria, he's just a friend! Stop thinking about him that way...~>

Dihrao looks up at a branch.

Dihrao: <Heh, what do you know, natural mistletoe's on this... I guess you know what that means...>
