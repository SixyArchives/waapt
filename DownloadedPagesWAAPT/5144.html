Near the W.R.I.

Silent walks towards the bridge, carrying a backpack and the egg. He reads the message that Rex has just sent and chuckles, then reads Wolf's message as well.

Hat!Lileep: <...What is what?> — waves tentacles trying to reach the Pokégear, can't.

Sol: <...Hey! You finally spoke loudly!>

Lileep would stick eyr tongue out to Sol if ey could. Ey limits eyrself to waving tentacles around, ignoring the land-dweller.

"Heh... Pierce, Rex's Eevee, seems to be having some fun. Here Maekrite, read this..."

...

...

Silent looks around.

"...Maekrite?"

Algira flies nearby.

Algira♀: <Oh! Pincushion flew ahead to clear the path.>

"Hmm...."

Ahead on the road

Maekrite is swinging his horn around in the tall grass, scaring some wild Pokémon away. Most of them Oddish and Wingull. He fires some Poison Stings to a nearby rock, scaring some Zigzagoon away.

Suddenly, something taps Maekrite's shoulder.

???: <Hey you haymaker!>

Maekrite: <...Oh?> — turns around, is Slashed right in the face — <...Aaaagh!>

Maekrite hops back, shakes his head and turns his attention towards the opponent. Hissing and bristling, a Linoone stands before him, claws extended.

Evolved!Zigzagoon: <I'm here for vengeance, justice and the Zigzagoon way!>

Maekrite blinks and then turns his head to the side.

Maekrite: <...You are only asking for you to get caught or fainted.>

Evolved!Zigzagoon: <I'll teach you humility.>

The Linoone darts at Maekrite, Honing Claws, until a short distance where he digs his claws in the ground and Mud Slaps at the Nidorino. However, Maekrite simply swipes the ground with his horn, releasing some pebbles and dirt as well, partially blocking the attack. After flickering his ears for a while, the Nidorino steps to the side just as a Pin Missile barrage passes through.

Without missing a beat, Maekrite then leaps back and rolls on the air just as the Linoone is leaping in with a Headbutt which seems to fall just a bit too short; as the wild Pokémon breaks and tries to reorient himself for a Water Pulse, Maekrite completes his roll, his horn slashing the Linoone's shoulder. Maekrite lands and lunges forwards, landing a Double Kick on his opponent.

Evolved!Zigzagoon: <...Aaaagh! Ooooooffff!> — is sent sprawling on the dirt.

Maekrite: <...You better go home. You might have evolved, but it seems you still need to get used to that body.>

Evolved!Zigzagoon: <...Y.You....> — coughs.

Maekrite Leers at the Pokémon and waits until the wild has risen up. Then, the two reach a tacit agreement and both turn around, heading their own ways.
