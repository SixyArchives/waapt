-rummages through her backpack, plucks out her notebook and flips through the pages- -clears her throat once she finds the page-

Before the Continents had been sent into place by the Great Regigigas, there were once two dragons. Reshiram, a holy angel of light to bless the day, and Zekrom, a holy angel of darkness to bless the night. They had assigned and selected many things to their domain, of which they would bless with Yang or Yin. Reshiram would bless the flight, the grass, and the fire with Yang, while Zekrom would bless the shadows, the spirits, and the metals with Yin. The world was at peace for a time, with one side distinct over the other, and neither of them interfering with each others works.

But then a conflict arose. Both Reshiram and Zekrom had chosen to bless the Dragon with their side of the duality. Reshiram wished to bless Dragon with flight and fire, while Zekrom wished to bless it with metals and shadows. Their conflict did not end, did not slow, did not quiet, until eventually a blow was made. And then, the pokemon blessed by Yang and the pokemon blessed by Yin came to the defense of their angels and fought against them in war.

Then, one day, the other legends of Unova had had enough. They did not participate in the battles, and they didn't want their homeland to be ruined. And so, with all their powers pooled together, they sealed both dragons away, to where I don't know. But within that place, where only the two dragons inhabited, a miracle took place. Peace was restored to Unova through the absence of the conflict, but the peace between the dragons was achieved through the replacement of conflict.

And friendship was the replacement.
