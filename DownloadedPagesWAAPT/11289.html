He was adored and honored by all his subjects, from the military to the elites to his own people. Now, the Shogun inherited Ignis after a long and bloody civil war with his own brothers. To avoid such a waste of human life, he chose to have one child, later in his life, who would be his only heir to the throne.

Gaston: And that was Lord Ryuku.

Chikiko: Yes.

Now Lord Ryuku, on the other hand, was not revered like his father was. Ryuku was seen as lazy by the military, slovenly and brutish by the elite and spoiled and selfish by the people. At this point, the country was on the tipping point of a coup or rebellion from etheir the military or by the people. The straw that broke the Numel's back was when he decided to make a foreign slave, one who couldn't even speak much Ranseian at the time, his wife.

Gaston: Lady Izumi...

Chikiko: Mmmhmm.

This single event had the people in an outrage. They took up arms, a decided that they were going to overthrow lord Ryuku in a rebellion. However, what they were not expecting was the cold, caculating and ruthless nature of the foreign maiden herself. Izumi, using her smarts, skills and her ferocious personailty, singlehandly took reigns of the military and quashed the rebels with relative ease. Driving away anyone else from comitting another act of "trechary", she had the ringleaders executed. On further measures, she set up her own secret elite forces.
