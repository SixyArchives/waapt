Wurmple: <The poachers have guns!>

GoE!Blitz: ~Guns? Oh dear, that would complicate things...~

-Blitz lands, and puts a paw to his chin as he thought to himself ''{What would Mew do?}

~Hmmm... Well, an army would help. But we wouldn't want anymon getting shot if we can avoid that... Why don't you show me where they're camped at first?~

Taillow: <Not me, I don't want to die!>

Wurmple: <They're this way!> -he starts crawling east-

-The substitute Mew nods and starts to follow on foot before tripping over "her" own awkwardly shaped feet. Blitz quickly decides there's a reason Mew float around, even if the flight is still a little wobbly-

Nearby Clearing

-some goons in faded Team Rocket uniforms can be seen guarding a pile of cages-

-the electrified cages are full of Mightyhena, Swellow, Dustox, even a few Ralts-

-the goons' arms are full of shotguns, bazookas, and rocket launchers-

GoE!Blitz: ~Mhmm... I see. We'll just have to make sure they can't use those, won't we?~

-Blitz gets as close to the edge of the clearing as he can while remaining hidden and concentrates on turning the goons into shapes without hands to use the human weaponry with-

Mook #1: Ugh, I feel... funny... Wait what?!> -is a Voltorb-

GoE!Blitz: ~Not going to happen today. Now, where's your boss? I think we need to have a little talk about the rules~

GoE!Blitz: ~Well congratulations then!~
