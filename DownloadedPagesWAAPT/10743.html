268551 luke92424th Jun 2013 02:47:39 PM Get RP Mod
Luke: "Well apart from the false start, is she alright?"

Typing a few commands into the computer, Dusty brought up the reborn Shieldon's vitals.

Dusty: <Let's see. She's breathing steadily, brain activity is normal, body temperature is at optimal levels.>

The Flygon smiled, buzzing his wings happily.

<She's in perfect health. Unconscious, but she'll be awake in a few minutes.>

Luke: "Can we see her?"

Dusty: <Well of course! Since you're her trainer, you'd probably be the best person to welcome her back to the land of the living!>

With that, the Flygon tapped a few more buttons, the box-like machine behind the glass dissembling and retracting back into the floor, finally revealing the newly restored Pokémon.

Curled up in the middle of the room was a small, reptilian Pokémon, her tough hide a sandy tan in colour. Her most distinctive feature was her dark-grey face, which was in the shape of a shield and several inches thick, looking hard enough as to be almost impenetrable.

Although it had only been dozing for a few minutes, it already seemed as if she was beginning to stir.

Luke: "Well...let's go and meet our new team member."

It felt like she had been asleep forever.

Of course, she had no idea that while it wasn't the case, she had been "sleeping" in a sense, for eons and eons. But at any rate, her restful state was making it rather hard to wake up fully at that point.

<"Is she okay?">

"She's breathing properly. Must be a heavy sleeper."

<"Oh, wait! She's coming to!">

Who was that talking? It didn't sound like anyone in her herd. Were a bunch of predators watching her sleep? Was a mob of Sharpteeth ready to eat her for breakfast?

Now determined to avoid danger, the Shieldon finally opened her eyes, ready to run in case her fears were confirmed. But instead of finding predators, it was something she had never expected.

Woolly: <Oh! She's awake!>

Dusty: <Ssh! You'll startle her!>

She found herself in a large white box surrounded by various creatures. Four of them seemed to be other Pokémon of some sort. But one of them was unlike anything she had ever seen before. It was tall, lanky, was wearing odd colored bits of various fabric, and had an ugly squashed face!

It was freaking her out. But then, everything was freaking her out at that point.

Shieldon: <Who...what...>

Climbing to her feet, she stood up stiffly, glancing around nervously, looking for something - anything familiar. Plants, trees, her herd, the sky.

But none of these were to be found. Just the same white box that surrounded her on all sides.

<What's going on?! W-where's my h-herd!>

She glowered at the group, trying to appearing intimidating, but the odd quiver of her body gave away that her confidence was just smoke and mirrors.

Luke: "C-calm down. We don't want to hurt you..."

Shieldon: <How do I know that? Why would you take me to this...this place if you didn't want to eat me?>

Nervously, she began to edge backwards away from the group.

Woolly: <We don't want to eat you! I just had some popcorn!>

Comet: <Woolly!>

Woolly: <Oh, and I'm not even a carnivore!>

This did little to reassure the Shieldon, who was still edging away slowly.

Shieldon: <I...I don't know who o-or what you are, b-but I won't let you eat m-me!>

Feeling something pressing up against her back legs, she turned around and found to her horror that she was literally backed against a wall, leaving her nowhere to run.

Treeo: <Please, we're friendly! We just want to help you.>

She turned back, giving Treeo a look that displayed a mixture of fear and defiance.

Shieldon: <I don't care what you want! I'm not going to let you eat me!>

Straining herself, the little dinosaur flashed as a dark grey dome materialized around her, shielding her from the perceived threat.

The Wild Shieldon used Protect!

<Now where's my herd?!>

Comet: <Well, look at that. She knows Protect already.>

Treeo: <Saves Luke having to break out the TM at least.>

Luke: "Look..."

Luke bent down, hoping to not look as large or threatening to the frightened Shieldon.

"I know you're frightened. This most be a very strange and scary experience for you. Especially since you've never seen a human like me before."

Human? Was that what this strange, somewhat ugly thing was?

"I'm...not really sure how to sugarcoat what's happened to you. So I'm just going to be blunt. You don't deserve to be lied to, after all. The thing is...you've been dead for a very very long time..."

She couldn't believe what she was hearing. Dead? How could she have died? She certainly didn't remember dying. Just curling up to go to sleep with her herd under a peaceful night sky. Then the next thing she knew, she had woken up surrounding by potential predators in a great big box.

Shieldon: <W...What are you talking a-about? D-dead P-Pokémon...can't come back to l-life! N-not without t-turning into Ghosts!>

Even in prehistoric times, Ghost Pokémon were a thing. All those souls had to go somewhere, after all.

Treeo: <Actually, we used some really really advanced...>

Treeo paused, not sure how to describe technology to a dinosaur.

<...Stuff, to restore your body from what was left of you.>

Dusty: <Yay for the power of science!>

The Flygon did a small fist-pump so not to be inappropriate.

Shieldon: <But...If you found me...then did you find what was left of my herd, too? Can...can you bring them back?>

That was when a horrible silence fell upon the group. There was no easy way to tell her what had happened to her herd. Or anyone else she might have known in her own time.

Luke: "We...only found what was left of you. There wasn't any sign of any other Shieldon or Bastidon where we dug you up..."

The Shieldon shivered, her entire life seemingly crashing down around her at that very moment.

Shieldon: <...No...>

Luke: "I'm sorry..."

The Shieldon sniffled, a few tears leaking from her eyes and rolling down her face. It was if someone had entirely uprooted her sense of reality and shattered it completely. Her herd was gone, her life was gone. Everything was gone.

Wrecked with grief, she dropped her Protect shield and buried her head in the floor, sobbing uncontrollably. In a vain attempt to make her feel better, Luke patted her on the back gently.

"That's okay. Let it all out, little one."

Woolly, Comet and Treeo exchanged sad looks. Even Dusty had lost his happy demeanor.

Dusty: <It's the one thing no amount of Science or tech can fix. Grief...>
