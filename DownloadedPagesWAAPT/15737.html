Illusion HQ

Constantin is currently in his living quarters, sitting up in his bed lacking attire of most sorts outside of some undergarments and bandages strapped across his chest. By his drooping eyes, pale complexion and laboured breathing (with the occasional cough), it is evident that Constantin has fallen rather ill recently.

His old Medicham - Kahina - is currently crouched over a small pot in the corner of the room with a few embers simmering under it provided by Octavius, the portly Emboar currently resting opposite of Kahina. As she adds a few remaining ingredients, she pours some of the contents of the pot into a bowl and places it on her trainers bedside table.

Kahina: This should help you recover Master Perilloux. It is most effective when hot so it is best if you consume it quickly

Constantin eyes the soup before staring at the wall again, placing a clenched fist towards his mouth to stifle an oncoming cough.

Kahina: Constantin-

Constantin: They were here, right where I wanted them to be. A blood brawl where I could fight in the conditions that I thrive in. Yet by this Arcues-forsaken illness of mine I was refused my chance when they were all lined up like pigs lining up to the slaughterhous-

Kahina slams the ladle on the pot.

Kahina: CONSTANTIN. You are in no shape to fight in your condition, especially knowing your tendacies! You really should not fight like you do at all! Look at you! It's been weeks since you fought with that man and your health is still suffering for it! Because the fact you have little to no control over how much energy you exert and the injuries and blood loss you sustain from such battles is a deadly combination. And that's only with a fraction of your full capabilities! Why do you punish yourself so? Your body cannot handle the amount of pressure you push it through!

Constantin stares at Kahina with those intense, brown eyes of his. He licks his teeth before answering.

Constantin: I will not show any weakness. Especially not to that damned nègre. I will not let a frail body from getting in the way. Men like me with such destructive tendacies don't tend to live long anyway.

Constantin drinks down the entire soup and turns over, his back facing Kahina.

Constantin: ...Just ask at whatever pit became my father'a grave the reason why

Kahina becomes speechless and thoughtless. She picks up the empty bowl and heads outside the room, where Shaka is looking out the window.

Shaka: <Hey old girl, feelin' alright? Is old fancypants actually drinking that soup now?>

Kahina is just standing there, looking up at the full moon.

Shaka: <Kahina?>

Kahina shakes her head before replying to Shaka.

Kahina: <Just had a flashback to happier, and yet painful, memories.>

Shaka: <Ah, those kind. Those memories become harder on when you know the outcome.>

Kahina: <Yes...>

Kahina places the bowl on a nearby ledge and let's out a deep sigh. A few tears start streaming down her face.

Kahina: <...Despite everything we've done, everything we tried. We couldn't stop that vicious cycle of hate that clenches that family line...>

Shaka: <Huh?>

Kahina: <Constantin may loathe the old man with the fury of a thousand fiery suns, nor was he even his biological son. But Constantin has become the very same destructive, spiteful person the old Comte was. Repeating the cycle that he went through, and so on... This never ending cycle of dispare...>

Shaka remains speechless for a moment , before he leans his arm on the ledge and rests his head on his palm.

Shaka: <It was a toxic environment. We tried all we could, but we couldn't do much when Brutus snuffed it>

Kahina: <If only he went through...>

Shaka: <Oh?>

Kahina: <...I'll tell you that in the near future...>

Shaka: <Ah...> (looking around) <Well, it's a nice night. And you look like you need something to take your mind off a few things. Would you like to join me for a walk?>

Kahina: <That would be lovely, my son>

Shaka: <Then let's go>

Shaka carefully links his right arm with Kahina's left and begins guiding her down the desolate halls of the base during the soundless night.
