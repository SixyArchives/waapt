Minccino’s POV

As I touched the stone, images and feelings flashed before me, and for what felt like an eternity, even if it was likely just a matter of seconds, it all came back to me. My memories were no longer buried under the cardboard boxes that hid them away. The dark stain that wouldn’t come out from the back of mind was finally gone.

Yes, I remembered…

I was just a wild Minccino, living with my family…

Sometimes, I would pick up items that absent-minded trainers had left behind in the tall grass, and I found it fun to clean them up…

I never really worried about anything… until the lady with the Chandelure showed up and took me away from my family…

I was locked in a cage, in a room full of other Pokémon in cages…

The Chandelure was cruel to us, particularly this one Dewott who kept getting into arguments with him…

However, the lady made sure that all of us were kept healthy, if only so her customers would be satisfied with our quality…

Eventually, I was sold to one of her regular customers, along with a few other Pokémon… including a certain Seviper…

We were carried off at night, and sent off in a plane…

And then… these people… Cipher…

…

Such horrible, disgusting things… I’m not even sure what happened to me… All I know is that suddenly, everything seemed so… filthy… there was this spot and I tried to clean it out, and cleaning it out was the only thing that mattered… I had to get it out, I had to… I barely noticed as purple sparks flew off my paws on the spot I tried to clean, and my attempt to clean it was interrupted when someone pulled me out of… whatever that device was, saying something about me being “finished” and to “get me out before I damaged it”…

…

… I don’t know how long I was in the cage after that… I was just focusing on cleaning the bars of my cage… They were dirty; I didn’t want to be in such a dirty cage, it had to be cleaned…

… Eventually, those two girls came… one blonde, one with dark hair…

… And now, the blonde girl… no, DS… Thanks to her, I’m free… and, as I look around at the sunlight shining down on this place, I can finally see how beautiful everything is, the things I weren’t able to notice before…

… DS… Thank you!
