171661 Luke92426th Jun 2012 05:51:00 AM Get RP Mod

Sauna Hold - PMD-B

A certain reptilian pair of Pokémon have gotten up early to investigate their surroundings more throughly. Although it's still early in the morning, a of the town's residents are already and awake and going about their business, if somewhat less lively than normally due to the low amount of sunlight.

While Lake was watching in interest, Stratus wasn't impressed at all, choosing to instead focus his attention on a particularly interesting metal panel on the floor, something which didn't slip the Feraligatr's attention.

Lake: <What's wrong?>

Stratus glances up at him, scowling a little.

Stratus: <I still can't believe after everything those awful dragons did, they actually let them build a town here! It's like inviting a Pokémon that collapsed your cave to come and live with you in the next one you claim!>

The irate Dragonite crosses his arms in disgust.

<It's stupid, that's what it is...>

Lake frowns at Stratus' annoyed demeanor, before taking an awkward glance around to make sure none of the local residents had heard what he had to say. Thankfully, the few that were up either weren't paying attention, or out of earshot.

Lake: <Perhaps you should watch what you say. I don't think any of the Pokémon here would be happy to hear that. Besides, it wasn't these dragons that wrecked everything.>

The Dragonite responds by kicking an imaginary pebble in disgust.

Stratus: <Well, who else am I supposed to be annoyed at? The jerk Dragons that did this are probably long dead by now!>

Lake: <Again, watch what you say, and is it really fair to hold their descendants responsible for what their Great-great-great...Er, their forefathers' did six-hundred years ago? You yourself were upset that they were doing the same thing to you back at The Verde.>

Stratus: <...I know. I'm just...I'm so annoyed! Those Dragons did a terrible thing, and they need to be punished for it!>

He clenches his claws, assuming an aggressive stance.

<I want...nay, DEMAND RETRIBUTION!>

It's at this point that some of the locals notice the rather angry looking Dragonite and his Feraligatr friend, making things rather uncomfortable for them.

Lake: <Er...don't mind him, he's just upset that someone...er, stole something from his horde.>

Lake wasn't sure if any Dragon Pokémon that wasn't out of one of those stories his mother used to tell him actually horded treasure, but it seemed to do the trick in drawing attention away from them, as the dragons that had taken notice were now going back to whatever it was they were doing.

Breathing a sigh of relief, the Feraligatr turned back to his still rather annoyed Dragonite companion.

<Let's go and find somewhere quieter, where you can complain without bringing the wrath of a bunch of dragons down on us.>

Stratus: <Grr...fine.>

With that, the two head off to find somewhere less populated to discuss their issues.
