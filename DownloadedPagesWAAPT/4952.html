"Look, dear, you're just seeing it wrong! You just have to say—"

"Minerva, you and I both know I am not the one she will come to for advice. But if she does, I will give my opinion honestly. I will not lie for you."

"Now, now, I am not asking you to lie! I am simply trying to make sure that your honest answer is the correct answer."

"You dare to look out that window and tell me this? Get out. As you so love to say, you are not welcome here."

"My dear, it is because of this window that I know you will see it my way! But if you insist, I will leave you be for now. Think on what I have said."

When there is no reply, the sound of footsteps is heard, and the door opens, revealing Minerva. Behind her can be seen what looks like an exact copy of Crewe's uncle's house in Ecruteak from the AU. At the far end of the room, a large window takes up most of the wall. Behind it, six Pokemon frolic in a yard surrounded by trees: a Furret, Quilava, Pidgeotto, Flaaffy, Sudowoodo, and Drowzee. Sitting on a windowseat, looking out at them, sits a girl identical to Crewe, the only aspect of her same age, wearing jeans, riding boots, a flannel shirt, and an empty Pokeball belt.

Minerva looks disdainfully at the interlopers.

"So. You've come back. I thought I told you you were unwelcome."
