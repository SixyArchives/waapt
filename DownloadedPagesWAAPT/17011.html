Curiosity Intensifies!

Goldenrod, Clavis Estate

-Ren has been spending the day down loading various episodes and specials of the J-Team TV Show to his tablet and setting up one of the large hall that open out to the gardens for the meeting. The room has a large group of various chairs and sofas on one side of the hall facing a second group of five chairs. Between the two groups of chairs sits a single chair. on the wall opposite of the doors that exit to the gardens is a large projector screen and a digital projector hooked up to Ren's tablet. The screen can be clearly seen by anyone in the room. As ren sits down on one of the sofas, Ren is talking to Doug at the Ranch.-

Ren: Did everyone get there safely Doug?

Doug: They sure did Ren. But why send all but Kitsune, Rio, Gamma, and that Klefki - Keys, I think - to the ranch for only a day? I mean, you are at the Goldenrod estate right now, there's plenty of room for your entire team there.

Ren: I know it's odd Doug, but there is a very good reason for it.

Doug: Okay then, once I take care of the last of this work, I'll call Sara and She'll have Karma pick up me and Shade, then the four of us will head over there. But I still think I should know why me and Sara are being called to Goldenrod.

Ren: *sigh* I have something to tell everyone, although my father already knows about it - but I think it would be better to tell everyone all at once, rather than have to do this multiple times.

Doug: -confused, but trying to understand- Okay... It's apparent something is up with you, and you've kept it quiet, but your father knows about it, and now you want your family to know.

Ren: Family and my closest co-workers, but yes Doug, that is the gist of what is happening.

Doug: Okay then Ren, I understand. Me and Sara will be there in a bit.

Ren: In a bit then, Doug.

-Doug nods as he hangs up. Ren hangs up on his end as well as he slumps in the sofa. Kitsune, Rio, Gamma, and Keys were watching. Keys, while acting more subdued than normal, is apparently in higher spirits than the other four in the room. Kitsune walks over and nuzzles Ren's face.-

Kitsune: <Remember, no matter how this turns out we're with you till the end.>

Rio: <You can count on it.>

Gamma: <Affirmative, Master Ren.>

Keys: <Normally, I would say something possibly tactless now, but I think I'll go with "It's Showtime!">

Ren: -smiling at the members of his team- Thanks guys. That means a lot to me.

-Ren breathes deeply and stands and starts heading to his room- And for once, Keys is right.

-Ren waxes lyrically in a light-hearted tone as he leaves the room-

Ren: Seal my fate tonight -
I hate to have to cut the fun short
but the joke's wearing thin
''Let the audience in ... Let the opera begin!

-When Ren returns downstairs, he is wearing the outfit he started his travels in.◊-
