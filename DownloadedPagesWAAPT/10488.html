-Whirling around on the Garchomp-

<Eon: We're supposed to be afraid of gods? You're talking to a god yourself!>

<Sinnoh Grunt!Garchomp: "M-MEEP!">

-And hastily retreats-

<Grunt!Salamence: "... We are at least dropping the bomb first, yes?">

-Eon mutters something-

<Eon: "I suppose we must. I utterly loathe being bound by these inane laws.">

-Several of the more seasoned grunts give an infamous salute-

<Seasoned!Grunts: "Heil Qoriik!">

-Those grunts from this troubled land as well as the ones who have simply not been under Eon's wings until this point give the saluting grunts a series of confused and horrifed looks as an object drops from the airship-

Back to the Original Vantage Point

-The group of flying-mons looks on as a bright, dome-like flash can be seen filling the sky in the direction of Canalave-

<All: "ACK!">
