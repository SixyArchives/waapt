Ren: Coming.

-Ren opens the door-

Borked!Kitsune:: Hello, Benjamin!

-Ren freaks and stumbles backward landing on the bed. Kitsune walks in and closes the door behind her.-

Borked!Kitsune: -pleasantly, to Ren- You can relax, I don't plan on jumping you ... today.

-Ren calms down a bit, and sits up - with his hands in lap. Kistsune sits next to him and looks him in the eyes. She suddenly hugs Ren. Ren promptly freaks out-

Ren: -struggling- You just said you weren't going to jump me!

Borked!Kitsune: -emotionally- Please let me stay this for a while... I've only got till this evening.

Ren: -calmer- You mean this isn't permanent?

Borked!Kitsune: -softly, shaking her head on his shoulder- No, I have to return the armband to Pentigan this evening. -Looks at Ren with a mixture of happiness and sadness- I thought I'd never get to interact with you like this.

-Gamma tactfully disables the audio input of Ren's tablet as to give the two some privacy. Ren is shocked by Kitsune's attitude-

Ren: -awkwardly- Y-you've always wanted to do this?

Borked!Kitsune: -releasing Ren and looking straight into Ren's face- Yes, I have. I wanted to hug you, talk to you, and... -blushing- and lots more. -Wipes her face- Why am I crying?

-Ren blushes awkwardly at Kistune's words and takes her hand-

Ren: -soothingly- You've wanted this for a long time right?

-Kitsune nods-

Ren: -continuing- And you've have to return the armband tonight, correct?

-Kitsune nods again, wiping her tears with a free hand-

Ren: Then you're crying because you're happy your wish came true, but it's ending soon. And that would make anyone cry.

-they both sit in silence for a moments as Ren thinks. Suddenly Ren starts putting the pieces together-

Ren: -to Kitsune- You love me, don't you?

Borked!Kitsune: -smiling- You always figure it out in the end don't you?

Ren: -to Kitsune- I don't know how I feel about you Kitsune...

-Kitsune hangs her head-

Ren: -continuing-... but I know how to find out.

-Ren stands and picks up an envelope on the desk. Kitsune raises her head looking at the envelope-

Ren: -smiling- Sara forwarded me an invitation to a party I have to go to. Will you be my date?

-Kitsune Jumps up beaming-

Borked!Kitsune: Yes! I'd love to...

-Kitsune feels her tail brush her leg. She reaches up to feel her elongated, fox-like ears. Looking in the mirror Kitsune noticed her irises were ruby red. Her face falling turning to Ren-

Ren: -smiling- It's a masquerade. And you can go as you are. Sara has my costume ready for me when we get to the office. One of her Abras will be here shortly to pick me up. But who says I can bring someone I met a the League?

-an Abra suddenly teleports in to the room. It nods to Ren and offer one of its hands. It looks at Kistsune for a moment and offers her its other hand-

Ren: Well, are you coming?

Borked!Kitsune: -taking the abra's free hand and kissing Ren on the cheek- I wouldn't miss this for the world.

-Ren turns as red as his ascot as the abra chuckles then teleports-
