Butwal Market

Sticky: -a familiar Liepard plush has been hot-glued to a nearby bench by some prankster- Shenannigans. Not that you care about what happens to me.

Rufus: -backs away, paling-

Sticky: -sighs in an imaginary manner- You aren't my business either. But since we've run into each other again, may as well take a little friendly advice.

Rufus: <He... he sent you here!>

Sticky: I am in no way affiliated with that cat, beyond having been glued to him for a couple days. Which was neither the first nor last thing I've been glued to. And you were glued to me for just as long.

Sticky: Coincidence. I'm a plushie. I have no motive abilities of my own.

Rufus: <He- he brought you here! He's here now!>

Rufus: <You- you...>

Sticky: Fine, tell you what. If he DOES pull anything on you, I'll personally pay him back for you.

Rufus: <You expect me to beleive that? How can I trust you?!>

Sticky: A shopping cart is about to run over your tail.

-Rufus glances backwards, and narrowly dodges an inattentive soccer mom-

Rufus: <...maybe...>
