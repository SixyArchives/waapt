-types-

Firuz — "Boss? You there at vacation?"

Firuz — "I see... I was really hoping the environment there would help it. Uhm... Anyway."

Silent — "You were preparing something to send?"

Firuz — "Yeah... actually I'm preparing to return to the fort..."

Silent — "Tell me it's good news."

Firuz — "It's good news."

Silent — "Oh?"

Firuz — "Some days ago Mawile found some seeds apparently not native to the area, thriving in a secluded place with a particular soil composition. It was somewhat salty despite it being far from the sea."

Silent — "What do you make from it?"

Firuz — "It's probable that the Tropii brought them around in their migration. The guys at Goldenrod finished running some analysis for me. As it turns out, the seeds show relatively little genetic variance and they have particularly regular traits..."

Silent — "Well, it could be from a farm or something. Perhaps the Tropii went through one during their migration."

Firuz — "Hardly... or at least, not what we understand for a farm... see, these seeds show no signs of human manipulation. Heck, they don't even show the minimal traits of pollution associated to the OI coasts."

Silent — "That wouldn't make much sense unless it's a Pokémon-managed farm..."

Firuz — "Would that even be possible?"

Silent — "..."

Firuz — "Oh, right... the Gold Berry farm your Nidos had back at the lab..."

Silent — "Is anything of what you have definitive?"

Firuz — "Not yet. I expect answers tomorrow. If we get the expected results, it means we could trace Basket's flock by tracing the soil attributes of the different islands."

Silent — "That's gonna be good news. Hey, my battery's dying. Why don't you upload that report yourself?"

Firuz — "Sure?"

Silent — "I don't see why not. Tangent's busy in his own matters, but surely once you have ready results he'll be happy to follow them."

Firuz — "Gee, thanks boss. Oh, but before that..."

Silent — "Returning to the fort?"

Firuz — "Yeah... but see, there was this news three weeks ago... remember the Houndoom?"

Silent — "Yep"

Firuz — "Well this report from Ecruteak came shortly after about a band of Nidos-"

Silent — "My battery is pretty much gone, Firuz. Let's talk later."

Firuz — "But-"
