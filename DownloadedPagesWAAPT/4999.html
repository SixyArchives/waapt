Ruins of the Alph, several months ago

Rex and Co. gathered around the tiny sheep, taking in her new appearance with some surprised murmurs. After a moment, Rex decided to let the Pokemon converse among themselves, stepping back to call Prof. Elm about the Pidgeotto.

Rascal: <Hey! Ohm isn't woolly anymore! Good thing we didn't decide to name her Fluffy after all!>

Gaea: <Yes, thank Arceus for that...>

Anya: <How are you feeling, Ohm?>

Ohm: <Umm...I think I'm okay. I feel fine, anyway.>

She took a step towards the Scyther to prove her point...only to fall flat on her face, letting out a pitiful bleat.

Ohm: <Owie...>

Krystal snickered at her plight, pawing at newly exposed flesh tauntingly.

Krystal: <Poor little Whimsicott; it seems that gravity isn't your friend either~>

Rascal: <Hey! Leave her alone! She's just not used to walking on hindlegs yet!>

Krystal turned to the Flareon, no doubt preparing to make him the new target of her insults when she sensed someone moving behind her. Turning around, she found Ohm on her feet again, giving her a glare.

Ohm: <...>

Krystal: <Hmm? Have something to say?>

Ohm: <...>

Ohm's face clouded over, a grim expression plastered on her normally kind features. Krystal seemed unworried, though Rascal took a fearful step back, noting something very different about the sheep.

Ohm: <You...>

Krystal: <...Eh?>

Ohm: <   I DON'T LIKE YOU!   >

With an angry bleat, Ohm's cheeks crackled with energy, a blast of electricity flying at the Zorua. Rex's group stared in surprise, the fox Pokemon taking the hit dead on and twitching violently. Then, she collapsed to the ground in a smoky heap, coughing weakly.

Rex: Oh no! Krystal!

Rushing over to his Pokemon, Rex kneeled down and checked on the Zorua. Apparently, the light of Krystal's Thundershock caught his attention.

Rex: Are you okay?

Krystal: <Hah...you think that hurt? Ugh, my spleen...>

Satisfied that she way still in decent condition, Rex sprayed her with a bit of Super Potion from his bag, giving her an Oran Berry for good measure. Then, he recalled her into her ball, placing it on his belt. Then he turned to Ohm, the Pokemon looking extremely sheepish at the moment.

Rex:    Ohm...   

Ohm: <...baah... >

Rex: That was a great shot!

Ohm: <...eh? o_o>

Rex: It's so great that you can use electricity now! I was so worried, but all it took was some training for you to evolve!

Ohm: <Baaah~>

Rex: But still, hitting your teammates is bad, okay? Be sure to apologize to Krystal when she finishes resting.

Ohm: <I promise~>

Rex smiled, petting the Flaaffy on the head. A sudden tingle went up his arm, the appendage locking in place rather suddenly.

Rex: Urk...Static seems to be working better now too...

He pulled away from the sheep, fishing through his bag for a Cheri Berry. Meanwhile, the other three Pokemon turned to Ohm, looking at the changed sheep curiously. For her part, she simply smiled sweetly, her eyes dark and full of malice.

Ohm: <   Vengeance will be swift~   >
