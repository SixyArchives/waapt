Guess Who...?

Almer, PMD-B (Meeting Spot)

-Within the meeting place, our group is currently staring across at the Emperor of Hoenn and a few of his retainers-

<Coulomb: "Are you sure this is gonna work Tagg?">

<Tagg: "Come on Coulomb, we've got this.">

-Thinking-

<Archeops(?): "Alright... Keep the heat up... But be formal..." >

-Preening self-

<Archeops(?): "... Well... I must say... You have peculiar taste in a location for a summit... But we Ancients are civilized enough to humor more... Peculiar requests in the name of hospitality...">

<Tagg: "It beats Angela, I'll tell you that.">

-Thinking-

<Muddy: "Are they going to say something over there or what?">

-As if reading the Swampert's mind-

<Emperor!Mence: "It reminds me of the castle back in Ever Grande, but to business, shall we?">

<Archeops(?): "So... As you know, the Empire of the Ancients is an empire of the more creatively-inclined... Our empire's prosperity is driven in no small part by artisans, who in turn trade off their wares for those that our lands cannot acquire as reliably...">

<Emperor!Mence: "And as you know, the Empire of Hoenn has had...poor relations with the rest of the world at large.">

<Bohr: "That's kind of an understatement.">

<Archeops(?): "Hmm hmm... Indeed... It would appear that we both have something that we desire from each other... This would be a rather straightforward matter, really...

... Were your Remnant a bit more... Stable...">

<Emperor!Mence: "Oh yes, the Bay has proven most troublesome lately, though my agents have been able to help get things on the right track.">

<Archeops(?): "Oh? What makes you so certain of success? And what could you possibly offer as a barometer of efficacy? The Empire of the Ancients has no interest in resource-laden places lacking order... Whether imposed locally or in a more... Direct manner...">

<Tagg: "Why am I reminded of the other day when that gnarly crack appeared on the Adamant Orb?">

-Giving a sly smile-

<Emperor!Mence: "Call it a Miracle of Peace if you will. The point is, Hoenn stands more united than ever before.">

<Archeops(?): "Hrm... I will admit... Your confidence is strong... But surely you understand the value of verification, yes? My Empire is wont to place such sweeping trust in an Remnant dogged by past ages, and I'm sure we can both reasonably conclude that the outside world would concur, yes?">

-Appearing deep in thought-

<Emperor!Mence: "Hmm... That is true, words alone are not enough to sway you.">

-Drops a scroll-

<Emperor!Mence: "Perhaps this will.">

-Takes scroll... Looks at it and sees a veritable wall of signatures, matching up with each of Hoenn-B's constituencies-

-Thinking-

<Archeops(?): "Okay, that's impressive. How'd this guy manage such a sudden turnaround? Even if it's just for the here and now..." >

<Tagg: "Wow...">

<Cobalt: "There's more to this, I'm sure.">

<Archeops(?): "Hmm... It would appear that you seem to have had some success lately... Though I'm not exactly sold... Mons are fickle... Especially ones of less... Attuned stock... You've demonstrated that you've rallied your Remnant together... But will your hodgepodge of a remnant still be of one mind in 10 moons? In 50? In 100? Whenever the next poor rainy season is?

And most importantly... Will it change the fact that your Remnant still remains isolated from the rest of a hostile world?">

<Emperor!Mence: "The world's... Misplaced distrust of us due to believing we are little more than a continuation of the World Eater's machinations on this planet has been a major thorn in our side for far too long. We would much like to change that and end up on better footing with the world. Especially if the Bay acts up again, even with four stable powers rising up from the morass.">

<Archeops(?): "... What if I told you that my Empire could offer your Remnant a possible reprieve?">

-Doing the Salamence equivalent of an Eyebrow raise-

<Emperor!Mence: "I'm listening.">

<Archeops(?): "Some of my servants abroad happened to stumble upon a document in a small hamlet... I don't anticipate on you being able to read ancient script, so I had a transcription of it made.">

-And pushes a paper across the table-

<Tagg: "Hopefully this takes.">

<Emperor!Mence: "I'm actually quite well versed in the ancient script, it was mandatory learning in my studies, but-">

-Reads the paper-

<Emperor!Mence: "This, this is?">

-Eyes narrow-

<Emperor!Mence: "What's the catch?">

<Archeops(?): "I have the original in a secure place... Which I'm sure would be vastly more credible as evidence than an easily manufacturable collection of ink, yes?

However, I have no interest in parting with such a potent tool... Especially not without a few assurances of a long-term stability and amicability between our two realms...">

<Emperor!Mence: "Fair enough, name your terms for reconcilation.">

<Archeops(?): "I have three demands...">

-A dull boom goes off in the background-

<Archeops(?): "Beyond the obvious of the immediate cessation of any ongoing hostilities between our two realms...

The first is access for my realm's ships to all ports in Hoenn, not merely Mossdeep as is customary in your dealings with outsiders.

The second is for the right of my empire to maintain on-the-ground peacekeepers in your realm. I realize that the proposition must make your empire uneasy given the history of my realm, but were I to seriously entertain thoughts of forcible incorporation of your lands in the future, I would not be entertaining methods that would involve such a wide dispersal of monpower... I have been informed that our military may need to revise its recruiting policies to make the endeavor viable, but I am certain that the benefits of trade will offset those hurdles...

The third, and final condition, is for you to use your newfound basis for improving your stance in this world on behalf of a client community of ours... The aforementioned small hamlet in which the document was discovered...

Mount Vern of Johto.">

-Shifting a bit-

<Archeops(?): "I'm certain those are reasonable terms, yes?">

<Emperor!Mence: "You drive a hard bargain, but these are fair terms. I accept.">

<Tagg: "Yes!">

-Pushing a pair of papers over-

<Archeops(?): "Simply sign here on these two copies for verification... And I will turn over the key for a new era for your realm.">

<Emperor!Mence: "Very well.">

-And signs-

<Emperor!Mence: "May this begin a new era of peace and prosperity.">

<Archeops(?): "I look forward to it...">

-And slides over a certain My Little Blitzle poster-

-Taking up the poster-

<Emperor!Mence: "As do I.">

<Tagg: "All's well that ends well, I suppose.">
