Ilex Forest

Even though it was the middle of the day, the thick leafy of the canopy of the Ilex Forest blocked out so much of the sun's rays that the perpetual shade made it feel like the middle of the night.

But this did not deter Lake and Stratus, who were busy scouting the forest for any large rocks they could take a sample from.

Stratus: <Rocks, rocks, gotta' find some rocks~>

Lake meanwhile, paused to take in the atmosphere of the forest, taking a moment to look around, noticing such things as a Caterpie crawling through the leaves on the floor, and a Hoothoot dozing the branches of a nearby tree.

Although he was a bit disappointed that he and Stratus hadn't ended up going to Hoenn, this wasn't so bad...

<Ooh, I found a rock, Lake!>

Following Stratus' voice, the Feraligatr soon found him standing in front of a large rock covered in moss.

Lake: <Ooh, this looks perfect!>

Pulling out a small hammer and chisel that Dusty had supplied him, he knelt down in front of the rock and started to try and chip away a small sample.

As he was working on that, though, Stratus noticed something shift from behind the rock, which soon reveled itself to be a Parasect. A rather irate looking Parasect.

...At least, Stratus guessed that it was irate. It was hard to tell with those blank, almost zombie like eyes.

Stratus: <Uh...Lake?>

The Feraligatr paused, having not noticed the Bug Pokémon yet.

Lake: <What's u->

Wild Parasect: <LEAVE OUR ROCK ALONE!>

Lake: <Eh?>

Lake turned to face the source of the voice, only to come face-to-face with the Parasect, which exhaled a cloud of strange spores at him.

<What the- URK!>

He briefly froze...then fell over backwards, collapsing in a heap...a snoring heap.

The Wild Parasect used Spore!
Lake fell asleep!

With Lake out of action, the Parasect turned to the Dragonite...who was now shivering in fright.

Stratus: <EEP! Stop! We don't want any trouble...>
