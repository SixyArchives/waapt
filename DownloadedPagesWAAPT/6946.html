-We all start getting shunted into different testing chambers-

Module A

A room divided by glass panes into four sections. One section contains a cube with just enough bulk to be appreciable; another contains a large red button. The third section contains the exit door, wired to the button. The entrance section contains three push buttons located by each divider, and a panel with orange lights on it. Each section except the entrance contains a similar panel with blue lights.

PEFeDOS: Before we go entrusting you with the testing apparatus, we must ensure your weak biological brains can handle the advanced cognitive gauntlet these tests represent. Use the buttons to activate the portal.

Module B

A long hallway divided into thinner halls by a glass divider; all flight-capable Pokémon are bunched in the left lane, the rest distributed among the remainder. Various team-building exercises are placed along the course, such as signs only visible to one side containing riddles to be punched into keypads on the other side, or exchange turnsiles to swap cubes between the sides. The whole length is intruded on by vines and stained by mildew.

PEFeDOS: Such a mess. What use are all these plants? You can't put plants through these tests. We tried!

They also stick around for far longer than they are needed...

Module C

A tall chamber, watched over by a laser sentry; the exit is at the top of the tall shaft, while a small alcove about the height of a Ursarang is cut into the wall at "ground" level. All surfaces are portalable below the halfway height of the shaft, including the alcove.

PEFeDOS: Some of your number have done these tests before... no explanation needed, you may pick up where you left off.
