Banette!Sol: What happened to me? Well let's start from the top.

Remember when we met in Castelia when we thought we were alone? Well evidently, we weren't. The trainer I battled from earlier that evening followed me to the point where we met and he overheard our conversation. Thereby, learning my name and that I was in Unova. Obviously, he reported this to Unovan authorities and they were scouting the country trying to find me. I was leading them on a Wild Goose Chase and I didn't even know it.

Then they got angrier, I overheard a duo talking about some files and journals they found in Napoleon's study. Revealings that Napoleon had watchful eyes over rookeries, breeding grounds for the Unovan starters and the Zorua farms. His journal claimed that If he saw potential rise from anywhere and direct influence or interference wasn't possible, he would manipulate them from a distance and sculpt them into a form that would suit his needs. In other words, he basically said "Yeah, I created and manipulated Lanius. Whatchya gonna do about it?". More scandalous, he wrote "We" instead of "I" to make it look like I was involved in all of this, even though I had no idea what was going on

Then I was studying Sigilyph in the Desert Resort. Believing that they communicate like Unown do in other parts of the world. I used a radio transmitter to see if it was true, my signal was tapped that day and a lone agent was sent out to "fetch" me. I made a dash for it, leaving the country as quick as I could. On the way back I had to spend a whole month in Mt. Silver surviving on what, Opal my Larvitar-now-Tyranitar (Bless her heart) could get for me and I made it back to Hoenn safe.

I thought that now I was away from it all, I would still conduct studies in Hoenn, I would live with my family before returning to Dewford and spending the rest of my days there and I would work the rest of my life to make others happy. Evidently it was not over, Unova sent agents to all over Pokepan just to find me, to hunt me down and bring me back there at all costs. Dead or Alive, preferably alive so they could have their revenge fresh

My first study I was to conduct was on the Zigzagoon population on Route 123. I flew there with only Stella and Cuddlekins, my most loyal and powerful out of all my mons and started to wind down and just enjoy my time studying the little creatures... Until a pair of Unovan agents appeared to ruin my day.

I tried to fight them off, and I held my ground for quite a while too. However, their power was overwhelming and they beat me, knocking out Stella and Cuddlekins before brushing them out of the way. Then they knocked me out and dragged me all the way to Unova where I was to be trialed.

Of course, the trail had only one outcome as it was obviously rigged for the Unovan's wanted outcome... and...

-Banette!Sol turns to face the sun-

I was hanged at dawn the following morning. My parents payed a hefty amount of money just so the could bury me here. My ghost manifested itself in the form of a Banette, fitting that I should take the form of a puppet Ghost

-Sol turns back to Tagg-

And that's the story of my death. I guess in the end, Napoleon truly had the last laugh... He ruined everyone...
