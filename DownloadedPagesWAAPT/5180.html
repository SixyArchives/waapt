Super Trainer 2 Hugh

Jubilife City, Trainer School (Future)

Entering the building, Rex immediately looked around at the sights. It was smaller than Violet City's school, as he recalled, but still fairly big. To his immediate north was a woman seated near a chalkboard, several key terms for the day written out neatly. It seemed the current topic of their class was Status Effects and how to treat them, something Rex noted in the back of his mind.

A few children were seated at a desk, reading up on the course material. Upon noticing Rex enter, they look in his direction curiously, wondering if he was here for a battle. Trainers were usually allowed and encouraged to visit these schools in order to allow the younger students to get practice. Rex smiled at them and continued on, not wanting to disrupt their studying further.

His current destination was a large, blue mat, a white Pokeball symbol imprinted in the center. Clearly, this was the designated post for battling other opponents. And it seemed that someone was already there...though, Rex was unsure as to whether he was a student or not. He wore a pair of black pants, the side pockets bulging slightly from whatever they contained, and set of beige shoes. Similarly to Rex, he had a blue winter jacket, which hung over a red dress shirt. Around his neck was a scarf, though the manner in which he had it tied reminded him of a cravat. Similarly, he wore a red beret atop his head, a blue Pokeball symbol on its right. Rex briefly wondered if he was from this region before he spoke up.

"You're here for a practice match, right?" he asked, Rex somewhat surprised by the normal Sinnoh accent.

"Erm...yeah."

"Cool. You don't mind if I'm your opponent, do you? Some of the kids are a bit shy about battling, so I thought I'd help break the ice."

Rex looked over at the children, the group having set down their books and turned to his direction, peering at him intently. "That's fine with me."

"Great! One on one, then!"

With a grin, the boy tossed a Pokeball to the floor, releasing a peppy Clefairy. Nodding, Rex tossed his own ball, releasing Minty onto the mat.
