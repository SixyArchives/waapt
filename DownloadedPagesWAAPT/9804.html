The Drifblim claps with it's tassels <Ooh! I don't know that one! How do you pla-> But it was cut off by a Thunder Punch between the 'eyes'.

The Drifblim floats back over and chuckles <Hehehehe, that was fun, but I've got a new game. it's called    DIE YOU DUMMY!!!   > it shouts, hitting Eneru with an Ominous Wind.

The Drifblim tries to move but seems to be stuck in place.

"Paralyzed, good job Eneru! And, go!" Sam said, tossing a ball at the balloon ghost.

<   I'll swallow your soul! I'll swallow your-!   > It cries as it's drawn into the ultraball, shaking once, twice, and clicking.

Sam sighs "We need to be careful with that one."
