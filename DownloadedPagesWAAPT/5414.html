Route 126 - Underwater

A lone Lanturn swims through the clear waters, looking for something to eat for lunch. A Remoraid or two would probably do the trick, and for thanks to his well developed luminescent lure, finding a school would be a breeze.

The fish begins to flash a pattern with his light, hoping to lure in a curious school of fish Pokémon, getting ready to stun a few with a Thunderwave before gobbling them up.

He was very surprised, then, we he received a reply.

???: Hello? Is somemon there?

Lanturn: Blinks. Er...hi?

???: Oh, hai! Who are you?

Lanturn: Me? Oh, I'm just a Lanturn. What about you? Are you a Lanturn? A Chinchou?

???: Er...neither, actually.

Lanturn: Oh...are you a Lumineon then? Or...a Manaphy?

???: No! I'm definitely not a Manaphy! How about I come over and show you?

Lanturn: That depends. Are you a predator?

???: Nah, I'm a herbivore. You're safe.

Lanturn: Oh, well come on over then!

???: 'Kay, be right over...

The Lanturn floats there and waits, eventually seeing another Pokémon approaching. He raises a fin and waves, hoping to get the stranger's attention. Noticing this, the other Pokémon waves a flipper and approaches...to find that it's not any sort of Pokémon that he was expecting to see at all.

Lanturn: Stares. <...You're a->

Woolly: <An Ampharos? Yep.> Smiles. <Surprised?>

Lanturn: <Well, yes...>

The fish Pokémon swims around the newcomer, examining her curiously. From the looks of it, this Ampharos didn't look that different from the few others he had seen. Apart from the fact that A; She was breathing underwater, and B; she was wearing an odd silvery scarf of some kind.

<If you don't mind me asking, Miss, how are you breathing?> Tilts head. <Don't you air-breathers normally need to hitch a ride with a Pokémon that can use Dive?>

The Ampharos shakes her head, then runs a flipper along her scarf.

Woolly: <Not when you have a [Bubble Scarf.]> Pauses, wondering why her text was colored. <Er...As long as I'm wearing this, I can breath underwater.>

The Lanturn peers closely at the scarf, blowing a few bubbles in surprise.

Lanturn: <That's pretty nifty. Where did you get it?>

Woolly: <Ah, these water Pokémon that live in the Orange Islands gave it to me.> Looks curious. <You guys here in Hoenn ever heard of them?>

Lanturn: Thinks. <Hmm...there's supposed to be a Reef in the Orange Island where Lugia lives. That the one you mean?>

Woolly: Nods. <That's it, yeah. Hmm...Is there another around here?>

Lanturn: Smiles. <You bet there is. And it puts that tiny one in the Orange Islands to shame. Would you like directions to it? Or...to the nearest island, at least? You seem a bit lost.>

Woolly can't help but chuckle at that last comment.

Woolly: <I'm not lost, silly. I'm exploring with a few of my friends.> Remembers something. <But I wouldn't mind asking...Have you seen a Dragonite anywhere around here recently?>

Lanturn: Shakes his head. <Nope, sorry.>

Woolly: Frowns, her ears drooping. <Darn. Oh well, thanks anyway...>
