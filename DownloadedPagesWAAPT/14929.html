Snowbelle Pokémon Center

Sakura, Lyuri, Duke and Hikari had just finished watching Frozen alongside a few of their Pokémon, namely Recette, Teishi, Ferran and Seth.

"I like how things were handled." said Hikari, stuffing herself with popcorn.

"<That movie was so cute~>" said Recette, happily flapping her tentacles.

"I wonder how bad an eternal winter would be." said Sakura, looking rather pensive.

Lyuri gestures to the windows with a deadpan expression "Welcome to Snowbelle City, where it snows 24 hours a day, seven days each week, every friggin' second."

A good portion of the room sweatdrops.

"<You really walked into that one.>" said Seth.

"Though the winter one doesn't seem to be as bad as the one in the film." said Duke

"Or the one in Westeros." Hikari pointed out, giggling.

In the meantime Teishi just looked completely bewildered as he was sitting on the floor with his legs crossed.

"<Eeeh, are there humans that can manipulate ice?>" he asked as he tilted his head.

"<It's just fiction mi amigo, I don't think such a thing exists.>" said Ferran, amused at the question as he ate some popcorn.

"<I wouldn't doubt it.>" said Seth, shrugging "<There are some weird things out there.>"

Lyuri chuckled "There's always a special snowflake somewhere."

"Not cool, Lyuri." Hikari giggled.

Seth lets out a rather loud groan "<Please don't start with the ice puns.>"

"I wonder if it's possible to make a living out of selling ice." said Sakura, trying to change the topic.

"I guess if you're persuasive enough you can get people to buy anything." said Lyuri.

"<Capitalism ho!>" said Recette.

"<I think you'd be on a whole new level of stupidity to be tricked into buying ice.>" said Seth

"Well, I guess there's such a thing as ice sculpting." said Duke

"<Wouldn't it melt eventually?>" asked Teishi

"If you store it in a fridge or something, I guess it can last for a while." said Duke

"Assuming it's even going to be used more than once. People only get those things so others can stare at it and see how pretty it is, people want to look fancy even if it's costing them far more than it's worth." said Lyuri.

"<Capitalism, ho!>" said Recette.

"<Los seres humanos son extraños>" said Ferran, shrugging.

"I like how the topic went from eternal winter to superpowered humans to ice puns and then to capitalism." said Hikari.
