Over the Course of a Pained Recollection - Hastily Recounted So as to Dredge Up as Few Other Memories as Possible

-There is a young Axew, moving about a forest in search of food, proudly venturing into places that most others would pass up due to being too difficult-

-The young Axew is now challenging a trainer with a hazy figure sent out before it... The young Axew seems confident, eager of what is presumed to be an exciting future-

-The young Axew is now standing in the approach to a laboratory. The surroundings seem drastically different and somewhat intimidating. But all in all, it is still an exciting occasion-

-The young Axew is now in a cage, with other ill, frightened-looking mons beside him. The excitement and novelty of the earlier events having evaporated and been replaced with a sense of strong foreboding and fear-

-The young Axew is now being injected with a foreign liquid via a syringe, screeching in pain as the needle pierces his hide-

-The young Axew is now in a place... Groggy, confined, and yet exposed to the sky... Which appears in his vision aflame with myriad colors-

-The above events cycle a few times, the young Axew's claws and tusks taking on a progressively peculiar texture and slightly grayish hue. At the same time, the young Axew becomes increasingly sickly-

-The young Axew is lying in a cage with tubes dripping fluid attached to them. Too weak to do anything but lie on the cage floor and feebly cry for his parents... The gods... For anyone to help him-

-The young Axew is now freed from his cage and in a field... Healthy once again... Though with changed claws and tusks. One that is seemingly too perfect. A flash of light replaces the field with that of the visage of a large, snarling creature in a cage...

The young Axew attempts to flee, but finds no avenue of escape... Just walls and the cage... Which draws nearer along with the snarling beast... And nearer...

And is replaced by a sudden plunging into darkness. The only light coming from a hole above...-

-The young Axew's claws are caked with dirt. Their owner curled up in a pit of his own making, whimpering. Hoping that the angry noises from above don't follow him into his hole. To be left to lie in the unhealthy-smelling scents of the pit in peace-

-The young Axew is swallowed up by a red light...-
