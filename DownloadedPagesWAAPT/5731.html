The Skorupi and the Politoed. By Lenore

Once upon a time there was a Politoed who lived by a pond where he kept his fighter jet that could turn into a MOTORCYCLE. Not far away from his pond, there was a Skorupi who lived in a cloud and owned an eletric gitaur that could summon marshmellow rain, but it wasn't as cool as the fighter jet/motorcycle.

One day, the Skorupi went over to the Politoed and told him "Polietoed? Can you ferry me across the river? For if I tried to swim I would drown, And That's Terrible.

The Politoed was questioning the motives of the Skorupi "How do I know that you won't sting me?" the Skorupi replyed "But If I sting you, I would drown!". The Politoed thought that that train of logic was unarguable, so he agreed.

So the Politoed was ferrying across the river, when suddenly BAM! A GIANT SPACE KRAKKEN BURST OUT! "Oh noes! Where did Space Krakken come from!?" exclaimed the Skorupi! "This calls for for battle!" exclaimed the Politoed!

And then, the Skorupi summoned his gitaur that could make marshmellow rain. He used The Power of Rock to WEAKEN the GIANT SPACE KRAKKEN. "ARGHJHDFS" said the Space Krakken! "Now it ends!" said Politoed and he flew his fighter jet into the Karkken! The world was safe form evil!

"HORRAY! WE SAVED THE WORLD!" exclaimed the Skorupi. "THE WORLD IS SAFE" said the Politoed. And then they "YEAH!" Shotted to Pluto where they had a dinner and Hot Chocolate. They also met some space pirates who captained a ball of yarn along the way, and they all became friends. THE END~
