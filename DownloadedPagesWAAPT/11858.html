The Gestalt Forms

Of all of the forms, these are the most common, and are the general staple of the Hagane no Tsume. They represent, as their name suggests, the combination of the two base forms. The list of the possibilities is extensive, and can be found separate this scroll. However, even among these many forms are special exceptions. These are forms that require a certain impetus to truly understand. These are Gestalt Forms that are infused by a particular mindset. Of these, I have personally experienced three, which I shall list here. However, let it be known that these forms involve psychological constructs that are difficult to put into words. Expect a lot of confusing and possibly contradictory metaphors.

Jaeger Form: This form is a variant of the Offensive Form - Basic and Speed Form. Its attacks are furious and damaging, easily circumventing guards with grabs, throws, and holds and crushing armor with adrenaline-fueled arm and fist blows. However, this form is very reckless, and while difficult to stop, leaves many openings that will no doubt cause the user great pain after the adrenaline high is gone. This form is best represented by the Krookodile. To reach this form, you must feel enraged at someone who sees themselves as entirely above you. I entered this form after confronting a high-born twat from Kalos who insulted my dear wife and sought to destroy my home because I refused to give him supplies afterward.

Dune: How am I ever going to try and emulate that?
