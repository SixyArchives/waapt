-Alice follows behind him.-

Alice: Well, the journey is over now. Though perhaps we should consider travelling by air for the trip back, when that occasion arises.

Herbert: Right...

-He straightens himself up, and looks around.-

Herbert: ...Hard to believe that the last time I was here was less than a year ago. I'd say it's good to be back, but... well, it really isn't. Still as stiflingly hot and oppressive as I remember. At least I can keep my Pokemon recalled whilst I'm here.

-He waits by the docks whilst Alice heads off in search of a Pokemon Center. Somewhere in the crowds amongst the docks, a figure unnoticeable behind his SEP field watches them go.-

Titan: ...So they have arrived. I suppose this was inevitable. Time to begin preparations for-

-He notices the Locket around Herbert's neck, and almost drops his SEP field in surprise.-

Titan: Wait, is that... it is having the described effects, but surely there is no way he could have obtained...

-He pauses, decides that muttering to himself is pointless, and melts back into the crowd. As he does, he pulls out a modified Pokegear.-

Titan: Alexiel, this is Titan. A situation has emerged which I may require your assistance to deal with. I will provide further details once I have returned to the lab.
