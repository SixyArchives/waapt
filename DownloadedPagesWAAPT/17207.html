1:19 to Cannon Fire

Unite: <Run! RUN!!!>

The human and Braviary both vacated their space just as it was bombarded by various projectile attacks. Luke ran and Unite flew as they dashed towards the back of the factory.

Luke: Did you see anything that looked like a way to the cannon?!

Unite: <I did se- AH!>

He barrel rolled to avoid an Energy Ball flung at him from a-far.

<I saw an elevator at the back! It must lead down to it!>

Luke: For both our sakes I hope you're right. But heck, anywhere would be better than here right now! WHOA!

Luke suddenly tripped as something grabbed hold of his ankle. He looked back to see that an Crawdunt composed of orange gel had pinned him by gripping his leg with one of his thick claws.

WHY DOES THIS KEEP HAPPENING?!

Unite: <Gah! You're such a liability sometimes!>

Luke: THAT'S A REALLY THOUGHTFUL THING TO SAY IN A SITUATION LIKE THIS!

The gel Crawdunt produced some angry lobster noises as it raised it's free pincer, looking as if it was going to end Luke's life with a Crabhammer.

AAAAAHHHHHHH!!!

Unite: <Ack! HANG ON!>

Already accosted by a variety of false Pokémon, the Braviary opened his wings...

<BACK OFF!>

And flapped them so hard it produced a gust of wind strong enough to blow them away.

Unite used Whirlwind!

With that dealt with, he dashed towards the Crawdunt, once more turning his wings metallic as the large lobster brought it's pincer down on Luke.

Luke: AAAAAHHHH!!!

There was a loud clang as Unite halted the blow with one wing, followed by a swift slash as he sliced it clean off. His other wing now unimpeded, he delivered a second slice to the other pincer, cutting it loose.

The grip on his leg loosening as it was separated from the body, Luke got up and pelted towards the nearby elevator.

GO GO GO!!!

Unite: <How did you humans ever become the most successful species on the planet?>

Luke glared at the bird as he ran.

Luke: Gosh, I'm sorry! I forgot to blow that Crawdunt up with my Hyper Beam attack, or knock him out with my Wild Charge! OH, WAIT. I CAN'T DO ANY OF THOSE THINGS.

Dodging and weaving between all sorts of attacks, the two managed to reach the elevator in question, both of them barreling in as Luke began to hammer the door close button.

Close! CLOSE!!!

Unite: <GET LOST, SLIME BALLS!>

The Braviary pecked at a False Blastoise that tried to force their way in before the doors closed shut and the elevator began to move down, the usual elevator musik piping in from a small speaker in the corner as Luke and Unite took a moment to recover.

Unite: <I can't take you anywhere...>

Luke: Oh, shut up.
