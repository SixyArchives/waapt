"W-Wow..." said Sakura, recalling Lotus "We have two wounded Pokémon going against a Mega evolved Lucario, can we really win this?" she asked, looking nervous

"Hey, it's still two-on-one!" said Lyuri, looking confident.

"Teishi, Acrobatics!" said Sakura

"Flash Cannon!" said Korrina.

Lucario then tries firing a light beam at Teishi, but he quickly dodges it.

"Waterfall!" said Sakura

"Karate Chop!" said Lyuri

They try attacking again, but this time all that Lucario had to do was jump out of they way as Teishi ended up colliding with Ferran when dashing while surrounded in water and instead of hitting the Lucario, all he did was knock Ferran out.

"Oh no." said Lyuri, facepalming hard as she recalls Ferran

Teishi just nods slowly.

"Finish this with Close Combat!" said Korrina

"Lick again!" said Sakura.

"<Ugh...>" grunted the Lucario

"Now, Acrobatics!" commanded Sakura

Teishi nods more confidently now and runs at the Lucario and begins hitting him as much as he could, however, the Lucario manages to parry one of his attack even though he was severely weakened off by them.

"Close Combat!" said Korrina

Lucario then starts beating Teishi up by punching him repeatedly, finishing it by punching him against the wall, thanks to changing to a Flying type he managed to just barely avoid fainting. He still manages to stand up, even though it hurt to do even that.
