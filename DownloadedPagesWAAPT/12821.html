A forest, Central Kalos - A while back

-Kirk's journey southwards has taken him away from Kalos' urban centres to a heavily wooded area, the name of which he doesn't know and wouldn't bother to remember even if he did. He has been travelling for a couple of weeks and is now starting to look noticeably dishevelled, his fur growing long and mottled and his stomach growling with hunger.-

Kirk: I need to find something to eat... but I don't care how desperate I am, I'm not going to stoop to scavenging through bins for scraps. Surviving on full meals provided by humans was humiliating enough. No, I'm going hunting. There's got to be something worth eating in this miserable forest.

-He ducks down beneath the treeline and starts concentrating, trying to detect any Aura trails passing through the area. His focus is soon interrupted by the rustling of two Pokemon coming up behind him, however.-

Charizard: <Eerugh, this one reeks of human.>

Haxorus: <Another rez, no doubt. What are you doing so far away from your master, mutt?>

-Kirk tenses, then stands up and turns to face the two dragons.-

Kirk: <For your information, I don't have a trainer anymore. I'm out hunting, just like you two are. So quit bothering me and get out of my way.>

Charizard: <Hah! The mutt considers himself the equal of true dovah if he thinks that he can order us around like that.>

Haxorus: <I'll bet that the rez has never even hunted for himself before.>

-Kirk bristles, clenching his fists.-

Kirk: <You want to see for yourself? Just you wait. I'll show you that I can take care of myself.>

Charizard: <I can't wait to see you try, whelp.>

Haxorus: <Go scurry off and demonstrate your killer instincts, then. We'll be waiting right here.>

-Still visibly indignant, Kirk strides away from the two dragons and uses his Aura Sense to scan the area for potential prey, and is rewarded when he detects a Pidgeotto nesting in a nearby tree. He starts sneaking over, trying to remain concealed in the undergrowth.-

Kirk: Alright... alright, I can do this... it's no different from a battle, I just need to make sure I don't hold back...

-He blasts an Aura Sphere into the treeline, and the Pidgeotto takes off haphazardly with a terrified squawk as the attack makes contact. Kirk attempts to follow up with a Stone Edge, but his quarry easily manages to evade the attack.-

Kirk: <Damnit! No, I am not letting you get away from me that easily! Come back here, you little nuisance!>

-He sprays another Stone Edge into the air, the Pidgeotto again evading thanks to its superior altitude. Kirk curses under his breath and starts charging up a sure-hit Aura Sphere... only for a dark shadow to swoop out of the sky and clamp the Pidgeotto in its jaws.-

Kirk: <Huh?>

Charizard: <Too slow, runt.>

-The Charizard flies back down in front of Kirk, still tightly keeping hold of the Pidgeotto, then lays it down on the ground and starts tearing into it. Kirk glowers indignantly.-

Kirk: <...Wait, you aren't allowed to do that! You stole my kill! That's not fair!>

Charizard: <Life in the wild isn't fair, runt. Besides, do you honestly expect us to believe that you had the guts to finish that lokraan?>

Haxorus: <The rez is too used to having his meals served up on a silver platter. Well, here in the real world, the rules are simple - no kill, no food.>

Kirk: <Oh yeah? And what's to stop me just taking my fair share, huh?>

-The Charizard gives a guffawing bark of laughter.-

Charizard: <You want to give it a try? Go on then. If you cannot even manage to kill a simple lokraan, I doubt you will stand much of a chance against two dovah.>

-Kirk, snarling, launches an Aura Sphere at the Charizard, only for it to be caught in midair by a retaliatory Focus Blast. He conjures an Aura staff in his paw, but is caught by the Haxorus slamming into his side with a Superpower and crumples to the floor. As he tries to recover, the Haxorus lifts him by his neck and slams him into a nearby tree.-

Kirk: <Urk...>

Haxorus: <Hrmph. I thought Lucario were supposed to be strong for those of non-draconic blood, but this one is nothing more than a mangy, pathetic mongrel debasing himself for the humans he serves. How long do you think he'll last?>

Charizard: <I give the rez a week. There's only so long he can survive on human scraps before he starts to succumb to starvation.>

-The Haxorus releases its grip on Kirk's neck, leaving him to fall to the floor, and saunters off with the Charizard. Kirk lies where he fell for a few moments, wheezing weakly, before curling up into a ball and trying to ignore the pains in his stomach.-

Kirk: ...I came to the wild to get away from assholes like those two, not to try and compete with them... is this really what life in the wild is like? Urgh, I'm starving... I guess I'll see if I can scavenge some Berries from the bushes tomorrow, then. Always hated the taste of those things, but somehow Herbert managed to make them into a decent Poffin mix... I guess that's one thing the kid did right...

-He groans, then closes his eyes and tries to get himself off to sleep.-
