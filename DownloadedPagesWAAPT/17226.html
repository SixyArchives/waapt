1:36 to Cannon Fire

Izzy: <Okay, we have one for  if under tenuous reasoning - and one against. Lake, you're the tie-breaker.>

Lake: <Uh...>

The Feraligatr frowned nervously, fearful that Izzy wouldn't approve of his reasoning either.

<You know what I'm like, Izzy. I just can't ignore someone asking for help. It goes against every fiber of my being.>

The Heliolisk looked back at Lake disapprovingly.

Izzy: <But like I said, what if she's loyal to Torque and lures us into a trap?>

Lake: <What if she's not? I wouldn't be able to live with myself if I didn't do anything.>

He shook his head a sad look on his face.

<Sorry, Izzy. That's just who I am. I'd rather take a chance and risk getting betrayed then leave someone in trouble behind.>

Izzy gazed at her friend for a few more minutes before closing her eyes and letting out a brief sigh.

Izzy: <There's nothing I can do to change ya' mind, is there?>

Lake responded with a shy smirk.

Lake: <You're dead right. I am too trusting.>

Izzy nodded.

Izzy: <Alright fine, she's coming with us. But if she does turn on us...>

Lake: <Then I take full responsibility. Deal?>

Izzy: <Deal.>

Stratus: <Deal!>

Lake: <And...break!>

The verdict settled, the team of Pokémon turned back to the Goodra who had been watching nervously from the sideline.

<We've decided - we'll help you get out of he-MMPH!>

The Goodra suddenly wrapped Lake in a slimy hug.

Goodra: <Thank you so much! You three are so nice to help out a poor little Pokémon like me! I won't ever forget this!>

Lake: <Mmph mmmph!>

Goodra: <Pardon?>

Lake pulled his head out from the hug - his face now covered in slime - inhaling heavily.

Lake: <You're welcome, miss...?>

He gave her an expectant look as the Goodra realized she was fishing for her name.

Goodra: <Oh, Gelatine.>

Lake: <Gelatine, okay. I hope I'm not being rude by asking, but why all the slime?>

The Goodra glanced down Mareepishly.

Gelatine the Goodra: <We Goodra naturally secret slime to keep ourselves moist. But when we get nervous, we start producing it uncontrollably.>

Izzy: <Is that why this kitchen is slicker than an ice rink?>

Gelatine: <These past few days have been petrifying.>
