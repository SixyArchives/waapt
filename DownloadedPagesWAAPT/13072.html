Goldenrod City: City Streets

The streets of Goldenrod are fairly active this day, though not primarily due to the hordes of trainers lining up to test their mettle against the infamous Gym Leader's Miltank. Rather, people were about, shopping at the Department Store and Flower Shop for all manner of holiday presents, the city abuzz with the holiday spirit.

Rex: People sure seem to get more active this time of year, huh?

For his part, Rex didn't seem to be partaking in the festivities, never having been raised to follow after Yuletide traditions. Not that he condemned others for doing so, mind you. He was more open-minded than that.

Rex: I wonder what Kim and Owen are doing for the holidays.

Thinking on his two companions, the now seventeen-year-old folded his arms in thought, musing on the status of his friends. Ever since their trip to Unova, he hadn't seen much of the young genin that had followed him around since their meeting in Johto. And it had been longer still since he'd seen Kim around.

Rex: Maybe I should call her.

Thinking on these things, Rex began to make his way back to his home, his hands in his pockets as he plodded through the crowds.
