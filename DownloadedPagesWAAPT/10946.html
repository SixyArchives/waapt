CEO Misaki

Avia Castle, Main Garden

Masamune and Kagetsuna are seated upon a few random rocks dotted around the garden as a maid is carefully scrubbing away at Masamune's pick dotted coat. Not too far away, Magoichi has been coolly occupying another maid (with pigtails, mind you) making casual advances on the woman. At that moment, a sliding panel is knocked down.

Iroha-hime: "Hey pops! There's a message for you!"

Masamune: "Oi girl! Look at the mess you made of my coat!"

Iroha-hime: "Well SORRY for trying to make a nice gesture! I think it looks nice!"

Masamune: "Oh, is that what it is? I think your 'nice gesture' leaves a lot to be desired!"

The two, gritting their teeth, make large strides towards each other (Iroha tossing aside the message) until they eventually butt heads, obviously displeased at one another. Kagetsuna, without missing a beat, casually catches the sheet of parchment and begins to examine its contents.

Iroha-hime: "You don't seem too happy there, DAD. That temper needs some working on. Maybe not enough calcium?"

Masamune: "I'll have you know my intake is fine, IROHA. I think that the problem lies in the family."

Iroha-hime: "Yeah, it must definitely be your fault."

Masamune: "What're you talking about? I'm completely calm. Maybe it's the fault of a little girl who thinks a bit too highly of herself."

Both: "Grrr...."

Kagetsuna: "Lord Masamune, while I don't often enjoy interrupting your infamous daily clashes, I do believe this is of some importance. It appears someone is interested in an alliance."

The two separate, slightly surprised.

Masamune: "Hoh?"

Kagetsuna: "By observation of the messenger Pokémon, there's a high chance this was from Spectra. Their forces aren't very impressive...on the surface. Much of their kingdom's strength lies in their abilities to-"

Masamune: "I don't care about all that, Kojuro!"

Kagetsuna: "..."

Masamune: "Is this person...strong?"

Kagetsuna: -sighs- "I would have to argue he is by knowledge of fact his kingdom still exists."

Masamune cracks a dare-devilish grin...

Masamune: "Alright then. We'll do batt-"

...and is swiftly pushed away by Iroha.

Iroha-hime: "Darn it, Dad! Not everything is done through warfare! Mr. Kojuro, could you draft a response to agree to a political meeting?"

Masamune: "Hey! I'm about to have a bit of fun he-"

Iroha-hime: "Learn to show some restraint! You always did around mom!"

Masamune: "............Tch. Fine. We'll go with your plan for now, but if this goes sour, I wanna be the first to know where the next battlefield is."

Kagetsuna: "Understood, Lord Masamune."

An hour later, Avia, Sky port

Iroha-hime: "Alright, have a safe trip."

Iroha releases a Staraptor, a scroll of parchment strapped to its foot, and the messenger Drifloon lightly bound to its neck.
