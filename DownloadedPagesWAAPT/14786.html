-The Meowstic lands after sending out the barrage of leaves, then braces herself to take Teacher's strike; still, the boosted effect of the attack sends her rolling a short distance, and she seems fairly distraught from the effects the Obscuric energies have over her body-

Ceiling Meowstic: <Ouch… How pitiful...>

-She stands up and cleans herself up.-

Olympia: "This battle is getting hard; time to strike decisively and give a clear signal of out faith, Meowstic..."

Ceiling Meowstic: <Do you think I would not know of your delusions of grandeur? Your mind is so simple, you ambition to little, and born of petty need. Let's examine if this lack of self esteem bugs you!>

-And with a psychic hold coalesces the energies flowing around into a small disc, from which with a swing of her paw she fires a whip of Bug-type energy at Teacher-

-Teacher is struck by the Bug-type energy and looks barely conscious-

Teacher: <...>

<You think your words can affect me like that? How little you truly know me. You are insignificant before me!>

She is thrown to the ground and has to get up panting, looking like she's gone through a rough patch. Still, her demeanour seems to not have changed and after a stern look at the Malamar the Meowstic just resumes the task of cleaning her fur-

-Olympia shares a look with the Meowstic, who does not seem too eager to continue the battle; with this, the Gym Leader decides to attempt a last attack-

Olympia: "Okay, let's try a final shot, something for this battle to be remembered by... give him your Charge Beam."

Ceiling Meowstic: -shakes herself, shrugs- <It's fine by me… if you don't get it. Don't you?>

-She summons electric energy that she maintains only barely isolated from her body, her fur standing on end; then she opens her ears to create a small arc between them and from there channels the energy in a bright beam towards the Malamar-

Ceiling Meowstic: <You win against an opponent naturally disadvantaged, and instead of living happily in that and securing the good things you have, you boast about riches you don't have. I'll leave here for you a sample of the shocks life has in reserve for you.>

-Teacher notices the Beam coming at him and manages to dodge barely-

Teacher: <Hm. I pity you. A creature who acts superior as if she doesn't know how beneath you she truly is.>

-He tries striking the Meowstic with another Night Slash-

-The Charge Beam has missed by a tentacle and change, and the Meowstic is left without much if any chance to brace herself in her condition; she takes the Night Slash without much defense and is sent spiraling to the ground, stopping a couple of meters away. While she doesn't go swirly eyes, several seconds of struggle to get back up pass before the Meowstic simply lets out an annoyed hiss-

Ceiling Meowstic: <Fine, if there's no use from challenging your bias then, > — curls to rest — <shall be it that a Pokémon more aware of the hardships of life comes to give you a spanking.>

-The Meowstic yawns boredly, simply lies there taking a quick nap. Olympia places a hand on her waist and after waiting for a moment recalls the Meowstic-

Olympia: "Very well then, this is how the tides of fate have grown, the doubts in your team to be washed away." — approaches — "You have shown to be prepared for one future, and this marks your chosen path."

-And produces the Psychic Badge to give to Crow-

Teacher: ...Hmph...

Crow: "Oh, thanks."

-He takes the badge, recalls Teacher, then heads off-
