Lake: <Zzz...>

Lake is sleeping peacefully, now having recovered from the mind-blowing revelation that Zack had actually been nice to someone else. Mumbling to himself in his sleep, the Feraligatr turned over onto his side. It was still early in the morning, so he wouldn't be waking up for a while...

Stratus: <HEY! Wake up!>

Lake: <Wha-?!>

Or not.

Blinking wearily in the early morning light, Lake gazed up to find Stratus standing over him, an expectant look on his face.

<Wassamatter, Dad?>

Stratus: <We're going on vacation, right? Everyone always gets up early when they go on vacation!>

Lake: <They do?>

This was the first Lake was hearing of this, and he wasn't sure he liked it.

Stratus: <Of course! It's like a tradition or something!>

Lake: <...Can I have five more minutes, at least?>

Stratus: <Alright...but your non-stop flight on Air Stratus to the Hoenn Region leaves in half-an-hour. Make sure you're ready to go by then!>

With that, the Dragonite left the still groggy Lake alone for the time being. Still rather tired, Lake laid back down, closing his eyes as he tried to eke these last few minutes of all the rest he could. Hopefully he wouldn't be tried for the rest of the day.

He always had the overwhelming urge to eat peoplemons whenever he was grumpy.
