TAU

Ancient Spectra, Behind the Castle

Tau stands at the base of the castle's wall.

"Hmmm. Hard nut to crack."

She cracks her knuckles, then gives a vulpine grin.

"But that's what makes it fun!"

She leaps up, drawing climbing spikes as she does so.

"Hup!"

Quickly scaling the wall, she leaps over the battlements, crouching on the catwalk.

Silently, she looks left, then right.

Hearing voices, she rolls into the shadows.

Guard 1: "So, I hear that there's going to be war soon."

Guard A: "Yeah, Who said? Ronnie? Cuz you know you can't trust anything that drunkard says."

Guard 1: "Mmmm. Yeah, maybe so."

Flying out of the shadows, Tau knocks them out.

"Night night, boys."

She jumps down from the wall, landing in a crouch.

"So far, so good."

She dashes forward, and presses herself against the wall of the inner building.

Scouting around, she sees a large amount of guards in all directions.

"Tsk. Distraction time."

Reaching for her belt, she removes two spheres.

"Fire away!"

Lighting the fuses, she tosses the bombs far away.

*BOOM*

Guard 2: "What the hell was that?"

Guard B: "I dunno. Let's go check it out."

All of the guards move towards the sound of the explosion.

"Alright. And HUP!"

Again, Tau leaps, swarming up the side of the wall until she reaches a window.

"Lightly, lightly."

She extracts a diamond tipped stylus and a suction cup from her belt.

Carefully, she applies the suction cup to the window, then traces a large circle around it.

The circle comes free of the window. Gripping it by the suction cup, she carefully places it inside the room.

"And we're golden. Now just to find the throne room."

She steps into the room.

"Hmm. This has been surprisingly easy."

Checking ahead of her, she through the halls, until she nears the throne room.

"Ah, here we are."

Extracting a vial from her belt, she whips it around the corner at the guards standing there.

Guard 3: "What the...?"

The vial begins to emit gas, and the two guards pass out.

"Success!"

The gas quickly disperses, and Tau strolls on over to the door...

Only to find herself hanging upside down from the ceiling.

"Huh?"

Her ankle is caught in a snare, and she now hangs from the ceiling directly outside the throne room.

"Damnit. And things were going so well."

Suddenly, steel wires come flying out of hidden compartments in the wall, firmly wrapping around her and restraining her from cutting herself free.

"Damn damn, double damn."

She sighs.

"Oh well, guess I'll just wait for the boss man to come on out."
