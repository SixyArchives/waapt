Marvin: "..."

"What's wrong?"

"Ah. Yeah. That."

"Look, I may not be the nicest of people. In fact I can be downright murderous. But one thing I know. Believe me when I say that we've tried every method to keep the harm they bring away from our families. Hell, they killed my grandfather. Believe me, if we knew of another way, we'd use it to the fullest. But we don't, we can't, and so we have to do things the bloody way. I won't blame you if you drop out, and can't stomach this. It's no easy thing, taking another's life. But at this point, it's something that has to be done. I regret every single person I kill. Marvin: "Still... I... I just don't know."

"Good. That means you're still human, and not a monster."
