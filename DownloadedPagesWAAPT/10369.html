"I wouldn't, but then again, have I really helped much? I mean... I've tried and tried but nothing happens when it comes to Herbert's problems, if anything I made them worse."

"Oh, come on... Herbert would give trouble even to the world's best therapist. It's hardly something for you to blame yourself for, it's hard to understand him at times." said Grant as he gave Sakura a pat on the head "Besides, you've done nothing to get people to dislike you, sure, trying to help all the time may be a bit annoying for some, but it's not the only thing you do, you're always the most pleasant you can be and I'm sure the others think highly of you for that, like Rawst."

"I... I suppose, but it still is painful when-"

"You know, you're acting similar to him now, your self-esteem is damaged and now you think you're only a burden to your friends, not only that but you're already a very humble person, you're being a bit hypocrite right now, girl." said Grant, in an almost teasing way.

Sakura then stops to think for a bit and gives a small smile as she does so "I am, aren't I? Maybe I should follow my own advice, for once."

"Yeah. By the way, you've been stirring that thing for almost a hour now" said Grant, with a grin.

"Eeep!" Sakura then quickly turned the fire off, she then started to giggle "Oh well, maybe someone here likes burnt poffins?" she said, giggling a bit more, though it wasn't in her usual tone.

"Maybe a fire type would." said Grant, with a shrug, he then noticed Sakura eyeing him and quickly caught on to it "I don't, though." he added, with a chuckle.

"Right... I'm almost finishing, tell everyone to wait a bit more." said Sakura, giving a small smile.

Grant nodded and went back to the lobby as Sakura went back to making the poffins, however, he expression quickly became a saddened one.

"That still doesn't explain what I should do with myself though..." she said, under her breath as she continued her work.
