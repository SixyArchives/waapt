Pyrite Town

Duke stops due to the paralysis, but still manages some chuckles "<You talk as if you're giving me a choice. I don't want to cooperate with you, can you even get that? Why would I want to cooperate with someone so worthless?>"

Pyrite Pokémon Center

Grant and Lyuri are sitting on the latter's bed.

"That was really stupid you know." said Grant, looking rather upset.

"I think you're like, the tenth person who told me that." said Lyuri, sighing.

"I know you wanted to rescue him, but you shouldn't do something that crazy without a real plan, did you really think you could do everything alone?" asked Grant, still looking rather stern

"I guess I wouldn't know if I could do that if I didn't try." said Lyuri, sighing again and looking down.

Grant remains silent for a while "... Are you hurt?" he asked, now giving her a concerned look

Lyuri then gives him a weird look "Huh? Well... No, not really... Well, outside of Herbert's attempt to strangle me to death, it still kind of hurts a bit." she said, running her hand through her neck.

"Well, that's good. That you're relatively unhurt I mean." said Grant, giving a small smile "Is there something on your mind you'd want to tell me?"

Lyuri then looks down again "... You... You think Duke will forgive me for what happened? I only made things worse for him..."

"Hey, you went through hell for him, sure, you shouldn't have and it did more harm than good, but that shows just how much you care about him. You know him, he wouldn't just leave you like that." said Grant, giving her a few pats on the shoulder.

"I guess... I just hope... I just hope he doesn't change much with this experience."

"Other than feeling guilty about everything, he probably won't change much, in fact you just need to give him some support and he'll be healed in no time."

"... Yeah. Thanks." said Lyuri, giving a small smile.

"Just promise me you'll never pull that stunt again, things could've been worse if you had been fighting Sakura instead of Herbert." said Grant, wincing at the thought

Lyuri shudders "Ugh... Don't even tell me that... I still need to make things up with Herbert though, I doubt I'll manage it, but I can try."

Grant just pats Lyuri again and they remain silent for a while.

"... Hey Grant..." said Lyuri, grabbing Grant's attention "... What would you do if you were in my position? What if Sakura was kidnapped or something?"

Grant stops to ponder that for a few minutes "... Well, obviously I'd try my best to save her, how far I'd go for it though... I guess I wouldn't know until it actually happened, I don't want to make me look better than I actually am by saying something that might as well be a lie." he said, with a smile.

Lyuri gives a small smile to Grant "All right... Anyway, I don't want any of this to happen again, so I assure you I won't do anything like this again."

"Good." said Grant, satisfied "... Did I scare you?" he asked, with a smirk

"Nooooo. I just had an Arcanine in human form slam the door open while I was sleeping and looking as if he wanted to murder me, what could be startling about that?" she said, giving him an unamused look.

"In my defense, I was angry at first that you did something like that, but I wasn't going to help you if I at least didn't keep myself calm." said Grant, he then starts messing Lyuri's already messy bed hair even more "Still, I must say that seeing you jump like that was simply fantastic!" he joked

Lyuri didn't care much about what she was doing, in fact she even chuckled a bit with it "Expect a return later, you can bet I'm going to give you a return for that." she said, with a smirk.

"Gonna keep my guard up then. By the way, I know I'm lazy, but I don't spend two days in a row sleeping. Go eat something before people start thinking you're dead."

"Fine, fine... You talk as if you're my dad or something." joked Lyuri, getting up

Lyuri then heads outside the room, Grant then gives a relieved sigh and gets up as well and gets ready to leave.

"... Well, at least she's feeling well enough to joke with me, I just hope Herbert knows what he's doing with Duke." he said, talking to himself as he left the room.
