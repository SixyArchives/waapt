PEFE - Goldenrod Branch - Lab 4B

Lake: <One question before we start, Dusty.>

Dusty the Flygon♂: <What would that be?>

Lake: <Why all the Eevee?>

The last thing Lake and Stratus had expected was to find Dusty's lab housing a small litter of Eevee kits. The little fox Pokémon we're currently scampering around wagging their tails, occasionally stopping to examine something or to paw at the Feraligatr or Dragonite, perhaps asking for food from them.

Dusty: <Ah, I'm studying them as part of an experiment. The same experiment that I request your assistance with, actually.>

Stratus: <Whoa, whoa, whoa! You're not trying to mutate them, are you?>

The Flygon buzzed in alarm.

Dusty: <No way! I wouldn't do anything like that! Although discovering a new Eevee evoloution would be a pleasant surprise.>

Lake and Stratus exchanged a worried glance, but kept quiet.

<No, I'm actually studying Eevee and their evolution into their Leafeon and Glaceon forms. The focus of my research is on whether it would be possible for an Eevee to take on these forms in the Johto region.>

Tilting his head, Lake gave Dusty a curious look.

Lake: <Wait, they can't evolve like that around here?>

Dusty: <It appears not. You would think that they would be able to evolve in the Ilex Forest of the Ice Path, for example, but it doesn't seem to work. So I want to find out why. And that's where you come in.>

Stratus nodded, standing to attention.

Stratus: <What are our orders, sir?>

Dusty: <I need you two to go to the Ilex Forest and the Ice Path and collect some rock samples for me, so I can compare them to similar samples taken from the various places that Eevee can take on the forms of Leafeon and Glaceon.>

Lake: <Wait...>

Lake gave Dusty a worried look.

<You want to go to the Ice Path?>

Dusty: <Yes...is that a problem?>

The Feraligatr paused briefly, looking a little awkward.

Lake: <It's kinda' cold over there...truth be told, me and Stratus might not be able to handle it.>

The Dragonite nodded in agreement, Dusty responding by crossing his arms.

Dusty: <I know that us reptilian Pokémon aren't well equipped to handle extremely cold temperatures, but I do think you're both rather well equipped for the job. You both can preform the Bubble Shield technique, can't you? And hasn't it been well proven to protect against harsh climates? Actually...>

The Flygon pulled out a notepad and pen, and quickly jotted down a note to run some experiments on Bubble Shields when possible.

Lake: <Oh, right! Forgot about that actually.>

The Feraligatr nods.

<Alright then, we'll get your rocks for you, won't we Dad?>

The Dragonite nodded in agreement, giving Dusty a warm smile.

Stratus: <You can count on us for the job! We won't let you down!>

Dusty: <Oh, fantastic.>

Dusty buzzed his wings contently.

<I knew I could count on the pair of you. I'll just grab the necessary equipment and you can be off. Wait...>

The Flygon blinked, glancing around worriedly.

<Where's subject Beta?>

Lake: <...Subject Beta?>

All of a sudden, Lake's bag twitched, then an Eevee kit suddenly poked it's head out.

Subject Beta: <Mister Science Dragon! This inside of this guy's bag is huge!>

Dusty stared at the Eevee for a few seconds, then turned his attention to Lake, tilting his head in curiosity.

Dusty: <You wouldn't mind leaving your bag with me to study while you're gone, would you?>

Feeling uncomfortable, Lake gripped the strap of his bag tightly.

Lake: <I think I'll hold onto it for the time being, thanks.>
