Tower of Mastery - Shalour City

Valorie Ryder

Korrina had asked Valorie to meet her back at the Tower of Mastery and so she wasted little time, not particularly caring for camping out here for a while. She didn't want to lose Diane somehow!

Within the tower Korrina wanted to know why she wasn't taking the pokemon league challenge, asserting that it was a ton of wasted potential.

"Well... I'm trying to learn more about why pokemon are so close to us." Valorie explained, entirely avoiding admitting to her weakness. There were only four people in the entire world that felt comfortable discussing those around.

"I guess I'm kind of studying them, y'know? So... I'm here to learn about mega evolution." she stated in a matter-o-fact tone, jumping straight to the point.

Korrina nodded. "...So you're like a little pokemon professor in the making!" she exclaimed in understanding, snapping her fingers as it all clicked into place.

"...Not that little..." Valorie protested lamely, though she quickly moved on. She didn't want to pick a fight over this. "I just want to learn how to mega evolve. I went and bought this stone in Lumiose. Can you teach me?" she asked, hopeful in tone.

Korrina's eyes widened slightly in surprise and she examined the stone. "...That looks like Charizardite!" she replied. "See? You're already halfway there. The other thing you need is a keystone, and you need to have a strong bond with your pokemon. I don't think that last part should be much of a problem based on our battle earlier." she explained. "...And as a matter of fact, my grandfather over there has some!" she proceeded to explain.

Gurkinn peered at Valorie for a long moment, his stare almost piercing as if he were looking into her soul. Valorie smiled awkwardly back at him, trying her best to make a good impression.

"...Hmm... Do you think this trainer is worthy of utilizing mega evolution, Korrina?" he asked, peering at the gym leader.

"Mmhm! She really rocked my skates back at the gym; her pokemon looked like they really cared about her too." Korrina replied with a note of confidence that immediately improved Valorie's mood.

"Very well. Master Mega Evolution and one of these keystones shall be yours. Take your pick and work with my grand daughter; I'm too old for the physical part of this training." the old man explained and Valorie beamed. Looking into the box she spotted a ring, a necklace... a necklace?! It was a beautiful, ornate silver necklace with the keystone garnishing the center. Around it were several amethysts twinkling in the low light. Valorie's stomach dropped and she knew for certain which one she wanted right at that moment. She would've bought that necklace herself with or without the keystone.

"Um... this one?" she asked, holding it up. Gurkinn declined to look over, merely nodding with a smile on his face.

"Let's head upstairs. We can give this a try on the balcony; you do have a charizard, right..?" Korina instructed.

"Of course..!" Valorie responded and the two began their climb. Silence followed as Valorie slipped the necklace on. It was cold against her skin. She peered at Korrina for a long moment, waiting for her to say something; it never came however.

"Uhm... I really like your hair, and your dress." Valorie offered offhandedly. Korrina turned her head to look back at Val, hesitating a moment before she responded as she tried to figure out the nature of the offhanded comment.

"Thanks..! Maybe we can put you in some roller skates and a helmet sometime and see how you fare on the rink!" Korrina responded light-heartedly, subtly teasing even. Valorie shrugged.
