Resistance HQ

Unite: <Where's that light switch? Darn it, I always have this problem...>

-Unite fumbles about in the dark for the switch. Woolly briefly considers lighting her tail to help out, but the Braviary manages to find the switch first.-

Unite: <There we go!>

-Now that they can see properly, Woolly and the rest of the team take a few seconds to take in Unite's room.

The space is lit by a single florescent bulb hanging bare from the ceiling, only helping to add to the cramped feel. Tucked in one of the corners of the room is a large nest made out of straw. Obviously Unite's bed (Woolly couldn't help but notice that there was a rather familiar looking Braviary plushie sitting in it).

Stuck to the far wall, however, was a large map of Unova, almost completely covered by arrows, crosses and other various shapes, linked to all sorts of scraps of paper arranged carefully around it. Some appeared to be pages from books, others were clippings from newspapers.

Woolly couldn't help but take a closer look at the wall, only to discover that everything on there was based around a single subject.-

Woolly: <Unite...What is this?>

Unite: <That, Woolly, is all my research on the whereabouts of the Tao Dragons.>

Flare: <Hang on, before you start going on about those Dragons, would you mind telling us just what on Earth is going on here?!>

Harriet: <That would be helpful, Unite. We're all very confused.>

Unite: <Alright. You might wanna' sit yourselves down, though. This is gonna' take a while.>

-The Pokémon proceed to make themselves comfortable. Woolly plops herself down in Unite's nest. Treeo sticks to the wall, Harriet perches on a shelf, while Flare and Wulfric just sit cross-legged on the floor.

Unite then proceeds to explain everything. How the universe they were in was different to their own. How Artemis and Scolipede had risen up and taken over the region and how nobody had been able to stop them. How he had been one of the first to join the Resistance along with Chase, and how, since then, they had constantly fought against the Liepard and Scolipede's forces in hope that they would one day drive them out.

He went on, however, to say that as of late, things hadn't been going so well at all. The Resistance numbers had been dwindling, while the forces of the enemy had only been growing larger. The Resistance have in Pinwheel Forest had been almost constantly under attack, and it probably wouldn't be long until it eventually fell. And it probably wouldn't be long before the two would begin to exert their dominance elsewhere.

However, Unite had a plan. A plan involving finding the legendary Tao Dragons of Unova.

The Braviary was convinced that with Reshiram or Zekrom on their side the Resistance would be able to easy fight back against the combined forces of Artemis and Scolipede. After all, they may have been powerful, but they didn't have the power of a legend on their side.-

Woolly: <Have you had any luck in finding them?>

Unite: -Sighs.- <Not yet. But I don't have much to go on. The Dragons haven't been seen since the Succession wars thousands of years ago. I do have this, however...>

-Unite reaches into his tail-feathers and pulls out his round white stone. Woolly and the others gazed at it, curiously.-

Treeo: <What's that?>

Unite: <It's a stone.>

Flare: <...Duh! We didn't need you to tell us that!>

Woolly: <Is it special, or something?>

-Woolly gingerly feels the stone with he hand. To her surprise, the stone is perfectly smooth. Almost like marble.-

Unite: <I dunno. I found it buried in the sand in the Desert Resort. Since perfectly round stones like this aren't something you find every day, I decided to keep it.

Still, every now and then, I can't help but get this feeling that this stone is special in someway. It's kinda weird...>

-Unite shrugs, then places it on a shelf under the map of Unova.-

Unite: <Oh well....>
