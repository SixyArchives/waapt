Isolde's Cave

-Crewe starts taking steps towards the Froslass, whose expression begins to turn desperate-

Isolde: <No no, you cant leave. I cant lose you. I cant lose them! Not again! I wont allow it!>

-Crewe slows down a little, but keeps moving towards the entrance to the cave. She pulls her slingshot out of its holster at her hip, fumbling for a stone in the pouch on her other side-

Look, Im sorry about your friends but I cant stay here forever. I have a life to get back to.

Isolde: <My life ended with theirs! My Amhlaidh, my dear Amhlaidh there is nothing left of him anymore, nothing I can find to keep him near me Nothing but you! You will not leave me here alone! I will make you stay!>

-she points one of her arms at Crewe, and a blast of cold air hits her left arm and shoulder, freezing them over-

-Crewe lets out a shout of surprise, stumbling backwards. She looks down at her arm, eyes wide with the beginning of panic as she realizes she can no longer use the slingshot-

No no, I wont let you keep me here!

Please work please work

-she holds up her remaining hand and desperately focuses on it. A rather pitiful orb of black energy appears in her palm. She makes to throw it at Isolde, but before it can leave her hand, it explodes with a pop, sending her staggering backwards a few steps-

Eurgh Come on, this is not the time for this to not work!

Isolde: <That was a neat trick I have better ones, though. See!>

-she waves one arm, and a Shadow Ball forms in front of her and fires at Crewe. She leaps sideways, landing on her non-frozen arm-

-she struggles to regain her feet as Isolde drifts closer, raising her arms again-

Isolde: <You cant get away, Crewe. I wont let you.>

You cant keep me here!

Isolde: <Yes. Yes, I can.>

-she fires another Ice Beam. Crewe leaps to the side again, and it hits her right leg, freezing it over. She stumbles, but manages to keep her feet-

Isolde: <Come now it wont be that bad a little cold, and then itll be like your asleep. Well be together forever I wont lose my Amhlaidh ever again>

-she readies another Ice Beam-

No no! Stay away!

-she lashes out wildly with her right arm, fling out a bolt of Obscura just as Isolde releases her Ice Beam. Rather than exploding or flying towards Isolde, however, it hovers in the air in front of Crewe. The Ice Beam hits it and flies through however, the attack that comes through the other end is significantly smaller. It punches into her right shoulder and sends her staggering back a step with a grunt, but does no more than that and forms a slight frost coating over her coat-

What

Isolde: <What was that?!>

I dont

-Isolde draws herself up, eyes flashing-

Isolde: <It doesnt matter! Your little trick wont let you escape!>

-she spreads her arms. The wind starts to pick up, and snowflakes begin to form-

-Crewes breathing comes faster and harder. She takes a half step back with her unfrozen leg. Eyes lighting up with desperation, she lifts her free arm and starts focusing Obscura into it. As she focuses, her eyes begin to glow slightly, and black waves of energy travel down her arm. She begins to feel distinctly lightheaded, but pushes it back and continues focusing. Obscura travels down her frozen arm and leg, slowly causing the ice to crack. Dark energy begins to collect at her palms-

-Isolde unleashes her Blizzard and Crewe desperately shoves her palms out as if trying to push it back. The Obscura in her palms spreads from her hands, arching up over her head and around the sides, forming a sort of dome. As the dome extends, it stretches thin, leaving cracks and holes, but nevertheless makes a mostly solid shield around her. The wind and hail from the attack batter against the dome, each hit draining more and more energy from Crewe. It comes in through the existing holes and tears a few new ones. Crewe, feeling her barrier begin to wear down, and in pain from the damage shes taken from the Blizzard and Ice Beams, starts to panic-

-she lashes out with her hands, and the dome explodes outwards, dispersing the Blizzard and barreling into Isolde herself-

-Isolde, caught off guard, is thrown into the side of the cave-

Leave

-Crewe stands there panting, her panic changing to fury-

Me

-she focuses hard, drawing on all of her remaining energy. Slowly, painfully, Obscura builds up around her hand, forming itself into claws-

Alone!

-she charges Isolde, slashing her Obscuric claws across the Froslass from the joint of one arm to the opposite hip. Isoldes eyes widen, and she meets Crewes eyes for a moment, before sighing. Her form begins to break apart-

Isolde: <Oh>

-Crewe is shocked out of her fury and steps back, the Obscura fading from her hand-

W-waitno

Isolde: <Oh>

No, no, nonononono, no

Isolde: <My Amhlaidh I can almost see him>

No, stop! Isolde!

Isolde: <Hes here I can see him again my Amhlaidh, Ive missed you so much>

Isolde!

-the Froslass sighs, her form fading and wisps seeming to break off-

Isolde: <Amhlaidh>

-with another sigh, the Froslass closes her eyes and fades completely-

-Crewe stumbles backwards, bumping into the other side of the cave. Hardly able to breath, she fumbles around the floor of the cave until she feels her moon stone. Her fingers close around it in a tight fist and she staggers out of the cave. She only makes it a few steps before collapsing to her knees, pitching forward, and falling unconscious-
Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
