Route 110

May and her Pokemon wandered across the vast expanse of Route 110, having successfully registered for contests. Unfortunately, the Beginner's Hall was in another location, hence May's current journey.

Taylor: *chirp* <I'm going to peck something for my contest appeal! People find pecking adorable, right?>

May: *holds out her arm for Taylor to roost on, smiling happily at him*

Taylor: *chirps and lands, pecking at her glove*

As the group continued on, May thought back over the past few days. Apparently, her brother Brendan had gone to challenge the Battle Frontier, explaining his sudden absence. Similarly, some strange flying, flaming figure had been spotted in Lavaridge, chasing away any Team Aqua members who got too close.

May: Probably those J-Team guys. They're so weird... *shakes her head* Well, at least I'm away from them for now, so nothing unusual should happen.

As May's group continued on, she suddenly became aware of a sign in front of her. It was simply an arrow pointing north, the words "Trick House" printed underneath in giant, bold red letters.

May: Trick House? ...Oh, that's a TM, isn't it? We could probably make use of that. C'mon, everyone. Maybe we can use that technique for the upcoming gym.

They headed onwards to the small, yellow house in front of them, the glint of a camera lens shining in the window.

Trick House: Console Room

Keeping his eyes on the latest entrant of his domicile, a small, balding man laughed gleefully, his eyes lighting up behind his glasses.

Trick Master: Yet another eager youth dares to challenge the Trick House!

He rubbed his hands together in excitement, standing up from his chair and heading over to a blank wall. He pressed a sequence of numbers into a keypad on the side, causing the wall to open up. Inside were three costumes: one a colorful green suit and hat covered in question marks, a matching golden cane resting underneath it; another was a strange white and red mask, almost like a puppet's face; the last was an entirely black robe that left hardly any indentifying features visible, complete with a gas mask for the face.

Trick Master: Hmm...now what should I wear today?
