The Streets of Fallarbor

Effie walks down a side road, having released her Pokemon. Argo has his arm in a sling and a stormy expression on his face. Discovery keeps coughing, but otherwise keeps quiet. Bluebird, unable to walk well, rides on Effie's shoulder. Bounty, the only one uninjured, scuttles between Argo and Discovery, making concerned noises and waving his claws around.

Crewe, meanwhile, wanders down the same street. Spotting them, and not really having anyway to avoid them at the moment, she sighs and walks over. Noticing Argo's angry look too late, her ears go back and she looks nervous.

<Uh... hi, guys...> she says awkwardly.

<Crewe, > Argo says shortly, stopping. The rest of the team (and Effie) come to a halt as well.

"Oh, hi Sneasel! Where's your armband?" Effie asks.

Crewe glances down at her arm reflexively, but otherwise ignores her.

<Um... What happened, Argo?> she asks, trying not to stare at the sling.

<We battled some Magma grunt, > he snaps. <And lost.>

<They totally cheated!> Bluebird breaks in from her perch on Effie's shoulder. <They were trying to actually hurt us!>

<Grrr.... >:(!> Bounty waves his claws around.

<Oh. Uh...> Crewe glances around the street for something to change the subject, and can't find anything. <Would... Battling me make you feel better? I'm pretty sure you can beat me pretty easily.>

Argo snorts, and looks very much like he wishes he could cross his arms. <Yeah, right. Like any of us can battle right now. Well, except Bounty.>

<I'll battle Bounty!> Crewe says, looking down at the Corphish. <It'll be more of an even battle, right?>

<Battle? :D?> Bounty hops up and down, waving his claws around.

"Are you okay, Bounty?" Effie asks, looking down at him with concern. Ignoring her, Crewe and Bounty square up across from each other.

BOUNTY is challenged by CREWE

<Okay, um...> Crewe runs towards him, awkwardly extending one paw to Scratch.

Bounty tilts his head, then fires a Bubble at her.

Crewe is knocked on her back and puts a hand to her head, feeling dizzy.

<... Ergh...>

<Okay? D:?> Bounty starts to scuttle over to her, but Argo stops him with his good hand.

<Wait, Bounty. I think you won.>

<Won? :D!>

Crewe staggers to her feet, still dizzy. <Owww...>

"Huh. Well done, Bounty! Hm... you know, this Sneasel keeps following us... I wonder if it wants to join the team...?" Effie's face lights up. "Of course! And that's why she battled Bounty!"

She fumbles around in her bag and pulls out a dusty Pokeball. Crewe, who's not seeing straight, takes a moment to process this.

<... Huh? Buh... Oh, no...!> her voice trails off as Effie tosses the Pokeball at her.
