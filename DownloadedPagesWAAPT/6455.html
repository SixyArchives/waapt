Mt. Moon.

A Spearow is flying back an fourth over the top of Mt. Moon, looking high and low over every point and crevice trying to find a suitable escape trek through the pass. While it's true that the mountain is much too steep and narrow for most humans to climb, maybe there is a path for a smaller creature to walk on. A Nido perhaps?

That's what Skree was looking for and it seems now, after landing on a rather smooth and levelled part of the mountain, he found it.

Skree: <Aye, Skree be flyin' o'er mounteen an' Skree thinks 'e foun' path. Skree weel repor' back 'azel-Rah 'bout 'is findin's>

The Spearow heads down to the Route 3 side of Mt. Moon. Where once again, Hazel-Rah is talking with Laurel alone. Only this time, it is before Laurel has to do his very important mission. Skree lands beside Hazel to give him his report.

Hazel-Rah: <Skree! You are back from your search! Did you find any suitable escape paths over the mountain?>

Skree: <Aye, Skree thinks 'e 'as found a path through th' mounteen suitable fer yer tastes. 'owever, Skree needs yer approval behfore we be runnin' young mudders up throgh th' pass.>

Laurel: <Thanks for your help Skree. You may retire for the remainder of the day.>

The Spearow nods and flutters over to the soft patches of dirt, where Blackberry and Buckthorn are currently out for silflay. Hazel and Laurel go back to their conversation before Laurel has to start his mission.

Hazel-Rah: <...He is said to be nothing short of a demon Laurel, yet I feel no fear from you. Is that so Laurel?>

Laurel: <...If I am to die on this mission, at least I have died for a noble cause. For a noble leader. Besides, I am certain that what this Chief Nido has in brawn, I may have in brains.>

Hazel-Rah: <It is that this point that I must bid you farewell Laurel...>

Laurel: <...Yessir>

The larger Nidorino nods and starts heading down the field, but not before saying a few words of warning.

Laurel: <...But when I do go back, you better be ready to climb over Hraan-Zorn-Hrair. For the foe will be furious, and powerful...>

And with that, he runs down the hill. Slowly but surely getting smaller and smaller until he is out of Hazel's sight. The wise leader of the colony looks over the hill and whispers a few words of hope, before joining his other comrades in silflay.

Hraan-Zorn-Hrair: "Mountain of Many/1000 Deaths"
