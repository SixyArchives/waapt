-Several white marble ionic fluted columns lined the path to the building, though only 2 of them remained standing-

-The fountain in the front of the building and middle of the courtyard had long run dry, and was cracked and chipped in several places-

-The mansion itself was in obvious disrepair, there were many holes in its roof, there were large tears in its exterior, the third floor terrace was in complete and utter disarray-
