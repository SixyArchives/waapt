Hamada leads Teala to a large rock and uses his horn to swipe it, then he moves aside and motions Teala to give it a try.

Teala: <Uhm... well, > — turns to Maekrite, who nods — <I guess I can.>

Teala roars and rushes at the giant rock. An instant later she collides and instead of the large rock there's just several small ones and pebbles thrown around.

"<Eeeeep!>" Varios Nidorino flatten their ears and retreat to safety — that is, behind either Hamada or Maekrite. The lead Nidorino turns around and frowns at them. "<I am disappoint.>

Pierce: <Can we try again?>

Hamada: <Sure we can. Come on overgrown kits, get ready!>

The Nidorinos start assembling themselves into groups again, this time Pierce and Teala paired with more experienced males. Maekrite was paired with a young rookie named Noki so he could teach him some strategies to hold your own ground after being Horn Attack'd.

The training continued for about an hour, with the two guests becoming more and more proficent in the tactics.

Pierce -darts at a Nidorino: <...This is fun! Oooof!>

This more experienced Nidorino knew to move himself to both stand the attack and avoid poisoning Pierce, which allowed the Jolteon to continue his practice for a good while.

Pierce: <...Again! Again!> — runs around to take position.

Teala -graples with her partner for a moment, leers at Pierce: <He never tires out...>

Akela -raises his horn to force Teala to release him: <He is a kit at heart, and has the body to follow his desires.>

Teala nods and steps back for another Tackle. She was enjoying the training, among other things because she seemed to be pretty good at it. She barely had the time to reflect that this was not like the training she had back at the Dragonspiral Tower, as Hamada was insistent in keeping his pupils in motion.

Teala prepared herself for another charge when she noticed something purpleish in her field of vision. "<.....Oh?>" — she swipes the object off her muzzle. "<A leaf... it's purple...>" She sniffed the leaf — it gave her a very threatening and unpleasant feeling that made her drop it at once.

Teala: <...What the...> — she looks around.

Pierce stops too to look around as Teala does. All around the training Nidos, there are small leaves falling, tainted in a lethal purple. Pierce and Teala look up to see the tree Maekrite had thurst his horn into some hours ago is now losing all its leaves.

Teala looks at the big hole in the tree, noticing the deeper part of the trunk is tainted purple as well, then she turns to Maekrite and observes him for a moment. The other Nidorinos didn't seem to care, it was natural for them.

Hamada: <Come on move! Move! A Nidorino never stays an easy target in combat!> — prods Pierce, prompting him to rush a tackle.
