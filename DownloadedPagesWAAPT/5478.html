Mt. Pyre - One Day ago...

A very, VERY frightened Dragonite flies out of the entrance to Charon's Base, the volcano below shuddering violently as it moves ever closing to erupting.

Stratus — <EEEEEEEEEP!!! What do I do? WHAT DO I DO?!>

??? — <STRATUS!>

Hearing a familiar voice, Stratus glances up to see a certain legendary descend and land next to him, looking rather relieved to see that he's okay.

Stratus — <RAYQUAZA! Charon! He...he did something, and now the whole volcano's going to blow up!>

Rayquaza — Nods. <I know...And unless we don't do something soon...>

Stratus — <...All of Poképan will be gone...>

The two stare at each other briefly, before Rayquaza turns away, letting out a sigh.

Rayquaza — <I think we both no what has to be done...>

Stratus — <What? No! You...you can't!>

The Dragonite flaps his wings and flies around to Rayquaza's front, hovering at his eye level.

<There has to be some other way! Surely?!>

As if to answer his question, part of the mountain nearby bursts open, releasing a small stream of scorching hot magma, giving the two dragons a shock.

<Yikes!>

Rayquaza — <...I'm sorry...>

The legendary lowers his head, a sad look crossing his face.

<This is the only way...>

Stratus begins to quiver, his eyes filling with tears.

Stratus — <But you can't...I...> Sniffs. <I don't want you to die...>

He raises his arm and wipes his eyes, on the verge of breaking down completely.

<...You're my friend, Rayquaza...>

Rayquaza stares at Stratus glumly, his eyes beginning to water in sadness.

Rayquaza — <...And you're my friend too, Stratus...>

He raises a claw, patting the crying Dragonite on the shoulder.

<...Thanks for being friends with me. You've...you've made my last few days in this life really memorable...>

Rayquaza sniffs, a few more tears leaking from his eyes.

<And even though you'll probably be long gone when I'm reborn anew...I'll always remember you...>

Stratus — Sniffs. <...And I'll always remember you, too...>

The two say nothing for the next few moments, simply trying to make the precious time they have left just a little bit longer...That is, until the volcano lurches violently again, moving every closer to erupting and snapping the two out of their stupor.

Rayquaza — <Okay, quick! Stratus! You have to get out of here!>

Stratus — Blinks. <But...where do I go?!>

Rayquaza — Shakes his head. <Just go! Find your trainer if you can! Just do what it takes to get the both of you as far away from Poképan as possible! You're a Dragonite, remember?!>

Stratus — <...>

Rayquaza — <Just...do whatever it takes, Stratus! You...you have to get out...you have to survive!

...Promise me that...okay?>

Stratus nods silently, tears streaming down his face.

Stratus — <...I promise...>

The Dragonite turns to fly away towards Sootopolis, hopefully to find his trainer and the others so that they can escape in time, then stops and gives one Rayquaza one last look.

<...Goodbye.>

And with that, he flaps his wings and flies into the air, before speeding off at Sootopolis at a blinding speed, Rayquaza watching him go until the Dragon Pokémon is nothing more than a dot on the horizon.

Rayquaza — <<...Farewell, Stratus.>

The legendary Pokémon lets a few more tears leak from his eyes, then quickly flies off to assist his brethren in calming the volcano down.

"It's not good, the PC Storage system is down..."

I shake my head, turning towards my Pokémon, who are sitting nearby with me in the now deserted Pokémon Center, the vast majority of the residents of Sootopolis having already fled by Boat or Flying Pokémon.

I shake my head glumly.

"It doesn't look like it..."

Lake stands up, a desperate look on his face.

Lake — <Surely there has to be something, anything we can do?> Glances at the others. <Can't one of us go into the Dream World and find Guardé? He could teleport us somewhere safe...>

Comet shakes her head.

Comet <That won't work. Guardé's still awake due to...> Grimaces. <Urk, timezones. All my hate...>

Lake frowns, looking increasingly desperate.

Lake — <No, there's got to be another way. It...it can't end here...not like this.>

The Feraligatr collapses, his eyes beginning to water.

<...I...I don't even get to say goodbye to...>

It's at this point that he completely breaks down, burying his head in the floor tiles.

<...Nadia...> Sobs. <I'm sorry! I...> Sobs. <...I couldn't save you...>

Trying to keep a level head despite the situation, Treeo goes over to Lake, patting him gently on the back.

Treeo — <It's okay, let it all out...>

Woolly, also about to lose he significant other, sniffles too, raising a flipper to wipe a few tears from her eyes.

Woolly — <Unite...It's been so long since we were last together...> Sniffs. <I...I wish I could've spent more time with him...>

The Ampharos lowers her head, a few more tears leaking from her eyes. Treeo glances at her, then lowers his head himself, also looking glum.

Treeo — <...I hope Harriet's okay. At least...at least she, Copahue and Shahinne might have a chance to run. If anyone deserves to survive this, it's them.>

The Sceptile shuts his eyes tight, blinking a few tears away. Nearby, Comet sighs, exhaling a cloud of smoke.

Comet: — <...I guess we all have somemon. Even me...> Glances at the others. <I...I didn't really have any friends before I met you guys. Well, I did, but...> Frowns. <...Anyway, I may not have a mate or a crush like you guys, but...at least I'm not alone...>

She pauses, rubbing the back of her neck with her claw.

<...And I'm thankful for that. Really...>

Woolly and Treeo gaze at Comet for a few seconds, then give her a smile, even Lake pauses from crying for a few moments to flash her a small grin.

Treeo — Sighs. <You're right, Comet. We may be about to die...but at least we're all together, no?>

Lake — Stops crying, sniffling. <Apart from Stratus...> Sniffs. <I just hope he's okay, wherever he is...>

Lake pauses, not noticing something knock on the door nearby.

??? — <Hello? Anymon there?>

Lake — Sniffles. <It's almost as if...he's right outside right now...>

The Feraligatr pauses, only just now having taken in the voice at the door.

<...Is that?>

Stratus — <Guys! There you are! I've been looking all over for- WHOA!>
