Luke: "Alright, to sum things up-"

Luke glanced down at the list he had made as Izzy stuffed her face with various berries Luke had given her from his bag.

"You're a species of Pokémon called a Helioptile. You're a native of a Kalos reigon, you're a duel Electric and Normal type, you know the moves Quick Attack, Mud Slap, Charge and...something called Parabolic Charge?"

Izzy paused to nod at Luke, various juices staining her face.

Izzy: <Oh yeah, that's a fun one.>

Luke: "Right. And you say that eventually evolve into something called a "Heliolisk?""

She nodded again, wiping some juice off her mouth with the back of her hand.

Izzy: <Heck if I know how, though! It feels like I've tried florping everything! Leveling up, leveling up near other Electric type Pokémon, leveling up during the day, leveling up during the night, leveling up in the forest, leveling up in the sea...>

Luke gave her a blank look.

<...Don't worry, I can swim. Point is, I haven't figured it out yet.>

Luke: "Alright then. Now, do you mind if I ask you a potentially sensitive question?"

Now full up, Izzy leaned back on the bench, resting her head against her hands.

Izzy: <Fire away, big boy.>

Luke: "Okaaaaay. If you're a Kalos native, why are you here in Goldenrod?"

Glancing up at the sky absent-minded, Izzy sighed.

Izzy: <I used to live in Kalos, somewhere to the south. But then I was caught by a trainer.>

Luke: "Oh?"

Izzy: <But, we didn't see eye-to-eye on most things. He eventually decided to come here to Johto, but when we arrived here in Goldenrod, we had a...disagreement.>

Luke: "...Oh."

Izzy: <You can figure out the rest. He kicked me out, I wandered around until I found my buddy Lake. Life was good, but then he got taken away, and...well, I've spent the past thirteen years in Johto.>

Lake - who was sitting next to the lizard - glanced down at her.

Lake: <Did you stay in Goldenrod?>

She shook her head.

Izzy: <Naaaah, I traveled quite a bit. Saw all the sights. Olivine Harbor, Tin Tower, the Lake of Rage. Not the Ice Path, though. Too cold.>

Lake: <I'll say.>

Izzy: <Goldenrod was kinda' my home, though. So I always gravitated back there after a while.>

Luke: "Didn't any other trainers try to catch you?"

Izzy met this question with a dismissive wave of her hand.

Izzy: <They tried. But I was always too quick for 'em. Even if living in the wild was hard, it was still preferable to being with a trainer.>

Luke: "...Oh."

Lake: <Oh...>
