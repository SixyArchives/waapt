Lumiose City - Fool's Apartment

Fool is currently fast asleep, snoozing away under the covers of the bed with Orcus snuggled up next to her and Lars curled up at end of the bed. Essentially dead to the world around them.

Including the hunched figure in the windowsill. Who is watching her wordlessly while toying with the brooch in his fingers.

He contemplates moving from his spot to get closer to her, possibly to caress her while she sleeps, but decides against the action with a sigh.

Constantin: Oh Solana... When will you learn?

He turns puts away the brooch. He then pulls out an old and faded looking longcoat (that at one point probably was navy blue with gold trimmings/designs, but is now more of a Cobalt with Tenne trimmings/designs) and slips it on. Before he takes his leave, he glances at Fool one more time.

Constantin: You'll come around... and then on that glorious day, you will be mine.
But for now, I'll bide my time. Farewell, mon piaf, I'll see you sometime in the near future.

Constantin blows a kiss towards Fool before slipping out of the window. He free falls for a bit, before levitating gently to the ground where his Gogoat is waiting for him. He looks back up at the window, which shuts gently at his command.

Bolivar: Are you done sir?

Constantin: Indeed

Constantin mounts his Gogoat and grabs his horns.

Constantin: Go

And with that, Bolivar bounds away with his trainer. Away from the little apartment and the city in general.
