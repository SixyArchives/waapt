CLASH! SCHWING!

The two swordsmen danced around each other. Their blades clashed sending sparks into the air. The hooded man stabbed forward only for Des to parry it away with one sword and slash with the other. The man rolled out of the way, then charged forward, bringing his sword down. Des blocked the blow by putting his swords into an 'X'. Weapons locked they pushed against one another.

Testing each other's strength, seeing who would let up first. Muscling through, Des knocked the hooded man back and swung upward. The man leaned back to avoid the incoming blade. It's tip met the skin above his brow and a long gash was made on his forehead. At the same time his hood was knocked back revealing this enigma's appearance.

Des went wide eyed for a bit but his face settled into his trademark smirk.

"So it is you."

Aside from messy brown hair and dark brown (almost black) eyes the man before him was the spitting image of Des. Like looking into a mirror provided that said mirror changed the colour of your hair and eyes and gave you a large, bleeding wound on your forehead.

"Black hoodie, ill-defined motives makin' it evident that you're playin' this by ear, and an accent thicker than caramel on chocolate. I suspected it was you New-"

"No." Des was cutoff. "Do not call me that. In the vein of Nega Counterparts my designation will be an anagram of your name. Call me...Sed."

"It took you a whole ellipsis to think up of 'Sed'?
