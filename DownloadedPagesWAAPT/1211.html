"In hindsight, maybe the Johto Safari Zone wasn't the best place to try that move for the first time."

I glance at DS.

"Hmm...Honey? I guess that could work DS. Give it a try."

As I begin to search in my bag to see if I have any honeypots, Woolly sits down against a nearby tree, tired out from all of the searching. As she relaxes against the trunk, the Ampharos catches a glimpse of bright green in the corner of her eye.

Curious, she turns to have a look, only to find a green, lizard Pokémon os some sort clinging to the branch next to her, staring back at her with large yellow eyes. Woolly blinks, then nearly gasps in shock when she realizes that she's staring at the very Pokémon they were looking for.

Wild Treecko Lv. 7: <Hello.>

The wood gecko Pokémon smiles warmly at Woolly.

<I heard you were looking for a Treecko?>

Surprised, Woolly nods.

Woolly: <Yeah, my trainer wants to capture one to add to his team.>

She points a flipper over at me, still rummaging through my bag for some honey. The Treecko blinks at this, surprised.

Treecko: <...Trainer?> He tilts his head. <What's a trainer?>

Woolly smiles.

Woolly: <A trainer is the name of a human that captures and...well, trains Pokémon to make them stronger.>

The Treecko blinks at Woolly, and interested look on his face.

Treecko: <Stronger? How so?>

Woolly: <Well...when my trainer first caught me, I was just a Mareep who couldn't even use electricity.> Grins at the thought. <But my trainer let me fight and get stronger, and now...now I'm a really powerful Ampharos!>

The Treecko gives Woolly a smirk.

Treecko: <I can tell.>

Woolly: <And not only that, but we get to travel all over the world, too!>

Treecko: <...Really?>

Woolly: <Yep. We're actually from Johto. We're here in Oblivia for a visit.>

Treecko: <...Oh, I see...>

The Grass Pokémon stares at Woolly's trainer, several thoughts racing through his mind. Sure, he was happy here in the forest with his friends and family. But...if what this Ampharos said was true, if he went with this trainer, not only would he get the chance to go out and see the world...but he could get stronger too. Perhaps maybe enough to become a Sceptile one day...

<...It does sound interesting...>

Woolly nods.

Woolly: <Why don't you come, then? My trainer really wants a Treecko. I bet he would take really good care of you.>

The Treecko rubs his chin, pondering the decision.

<That, and...you seem really nice.> Woolly blushes, swinging her tail slowly in happiness. <...I'd quite like to get to know you a bit better.>

The Lizard Pokémon glances at Woolly, smiling awkwardly.

Treecko: <Thanks...>

The two smile at each other for a second. Then to Woolly's surprise, the Treecko hops onto her head, sticking there with his adhesive feet.

Woolly: <Oh! What are you doing?>

Treecko: <I've decided...I'm going to come along with you.>

Woolly: <Really?>

The Treecko smiles.

Treecko: <Yes. But...I'm not going to make it easy for your trainer.>

Woolly: <Eh?>

Woolly looks confused, then slaps her head gently in realization.

<Oh. You want to battle. Of course...>

The Treecko tilts his head at her, slightly confused.

Treecko: <That is how this works, isn't it?>

Woolly: <Yeah, it is. It is...>
