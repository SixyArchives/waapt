136372 Luke92410th Feb 2012 07:20:53 PM Get RP Mod

Hoenn Skies

The Two Dragons soar skillfully in the clear skies above the thick cloud layer, Stratus following Rayquaza as he leads him towards Mt. Pyre.

Stratus: <How do you know where it is through all this cloud?>

Rayquaza: Grins. <I've lived in Hoenn since it's creation! So I know the whole region like the back of my claw! So I can instinctively tell where we are even if I can't see the Earth below!>

Stratus: <Oh, that's cool!>

Stratus pauses for a second, a certain topic beginning to weigh on his mind.

Stratus: <Hey...Rayquaza?>

Rayquaza: <Yes?>

Stratus: Awkwardly. <Um...that supervolcano you mentioned...has it every erupted before?>

Rayquaza: <...> Looks away. <Once. Almost...>

Stratus: <...Almost?>

Rayquaza nods.

Rayquaza: <It happened a long, long time ago. Many thousands of years, before humans rose as a civilization, even.>

Stratus: <Wow...really?>

Rayquaza: Nods. <Not many know this, but back then...there were two races, one of Alakazam and one of Metagross, who both possessed advanced technology. More advanced than humans, in some cases.

However, the two races eventually grew distrustful of each other, and it wasn't long before they broke into an all out war. The two sides fought long and hard, causing an untold amount of devastation to the Hoenn Region, before eventually coming to a standstill, neither side able to better the other.

However, both sides had one final plan to destroy the other. Using their advanced technology, they would attempt to erupt Mt. Pyre, hoping to destroy their foes in the ensuring devastation. But they failed to realize that the resulting eruption would be strong enough to destroy all of Poképan!>

Stratus' eyes go wide, a cold chill running down his spine at this revelation.

Stratus: <So not only would they have destroyed Hoenn, but Kanto, Johto, and Sinnoh as well?>

Rayquaza nods gravely.

Rayquaza: <Indeed. Unfortunately, one side I forget which, for some reason. Well, it was a long time ago, actually went as far as to invoke an eruption of the mountain.

But...before it could erupt and scour Poképan of life, Groudon, Kyogre, and myself intervened, using out power to halt the volcano...but at the cost of our own lives in the process.>

Stratus: <...You...Died?!>

Rayquaza: Frowns. <...Yes. But! A legendary can never truly die, and all three of us were eventually reborn a-new.>

Stratus: <Oh...well, that's a relief.> Tilts head. <But...what happened to the two civilizations?>

Rayquaza: <Well, naturally, our fellow legends were not pleased at the extreme devastation they had almost inadvertently cause. So to ensure that something like this would hopefully never happen again, they wiped the Earth of their advanced technology, leaving no almost not trace that it had ever existed.>

Rayquaza pauses, chuckling to himself.

<It's funny...by doing that, the legendaries changed the course of history. No doubt that the human race would never had become as prominent had those two civilizations not been wiped out...>

Stratus: <Yeah, I bet it would have, too...> Frowns. <So...should I be worried about Mt. Pyre? What if somebody tries to set it off again?>

Rayquaza: <I sincerely doubt that. Only a fool would attempt something so potentially devastating. Hopefully, whoever is responsible for this weather has more sense than that...>
