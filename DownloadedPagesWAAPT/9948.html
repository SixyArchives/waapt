-Two men dressed in white and grey uniforms are walking through a large, rocky cavern, deep in conversation. A certain Kadabra is observing them from a distance, occasionally twirling his fork idly.-

Deimos: I figured you'd be more surprised by the fact that your son's using this Aura stuff or whatever it's called.

Titan: Correct. Which reminds me, have your Pokemon been restored to full capacity yet?

Deimos: Grunt, Runt and the Bisharp are all back in action. Brute still hasn't regrown his claws after that fucker's Gallade cut 'em off. But don't worry, I've left him with the techies. They're making some... improvements.

-A wry smile spreads across Titan's mouth for a second, before disappearing just as quickly.-

Titan: Excellent. My Pokemon are strong enough to survive such an encounter, I have no doubts about their wellbeing. Which leads me onto my second question. Did Omega recognise the Metagross from last time?

Deimos: Couldn't understand 'em, obviously. But judging by how enthusiastically they went at each other, I'd say they had some history.

Titan: Then my suspicions are confirmed.

-Titan stops for a second.-

Titan: Pallad... it's been a long time since I last beat that "unbeatable" Metagross.

Deimos: Wait, you know that monster?

Titan: I had my suspicions since your encounter with it in Black City, but this confirms them. I have fought Pallad before. Omega was able to defeat it. It was the secret weapon of the Sinnoh champion, Ivy, about twenty years ago.

Deimos: Well, it evidently ain't hers any more.

Titan: Indeed it is not. Ivy passed away five years ago. A Toxicroak attack in the Johto Safari Zone, if I remember correctly. However, she was survived by her son... and her daughter.

-Deimos gives a wide, malicious grin.-

Deimos: Ah, so that's the little bitch we've kept coming up against. That would explain why she pisses herself every time I sic the Terrible Trio on her.

Titan: That is a weakness we can use to our advantage, yes. Though I am more concerned about Pallad at the moment. You say Omega lost the fight?

Deimos: Yeah, but those little fucks haven't heard of fighting fair. It was three Pokemon up against her, one of which was that damned Metagross, and all of them looked pretty powerful.

Titan: Omega's pride will not allow her to be beaten so easily. Undoubtedly she is already preparing for her next encounter with Pallad. Perhaps I should take her to Quest Island, where she will face a challenge befitting her skills for once.

Deimos: Why are you so concerned about her? She's just an animal. I don't know why you gave her a name in the first place.

-Titan is silent for a moment.-

Titan: We will discuss this matter later. In the meantime, you were able to observe more members of the group and their abilities?

Deimos: Of course. For a start, there was this posh fuck in a suit who tried to...

-Deimos and Titan continue to walk deeper into the cavern, their conversation echoing back down the cave until it eventually fades. The Kadabra remains in place for a few more moments, before teleporting away in silence.-
