Iron Island Cave

As we went deeper through the cave, I was reminded of two things. One was the cave in Hoenn I had to more or less make my home for a month while Cobalt completed his evolution into a Metagross and fused minds with that Metang hanger-on of his. The second was the cave where I first met Steel, the Granite Cave, and Muddy and I saved him from being a Mawile's breakfast. Tropius had gotten released earlier, since it was warmer inside the cave than outside, though he was a bit annoyed about the fact that this cave lacked sunlight.

Around us were the many wisps of different species of Pokemon, including that bane of cave spelunkers from my home region of Kanto to all the way up here in Sinnoh, Golbat, with even a few Crobat amongst them. For once, I was glad that I was invisible to these wisps, because fighting all of these Golbat would be just plain annoying, causing me to snicker to myself about Uxie's statement about no longer being in the spotlight. Some Sableye seemed to have noticed us, probably due to their typing, but luckily they weren't interested in a fight or allied with the Spiritomb, judging by their behavior. As I looked over at them, I thought about when I was turned into a Mudkip around this time last year, and how I assisted in the defeat of a horde of their species, evolving into a Marshtomp. Ever since then, I may have felt a bit closer to Pokemon than my own species before, but I feel even closer to them now.
