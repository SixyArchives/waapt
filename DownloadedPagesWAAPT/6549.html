Flopside, Gummi Manufacturing Plant Manufacturing Plant

The manufacturing floor is considerably more advanced than the Fallarbor Gummi plant; overhead tracks carry hanging machines past assembly line stations, which are in turn supplied by conveyors from other assembly stations, unloading docks, and the odd pneumatic tube.

This factory seems to be focused on assembling electronic mixing machines; pipemaking machines feed into stations that assemble electronic valves, which are linked to arrays of vacuum tubes linked to what appear to be punchcard readers.

A close inspection shows that many of the actual machines in the factory have been repaired with heterogeneous materials; many supports appear to have been cannibalized for other purposes and replaced with wooden beams, and many chains have been replaced with ropes.
