Valorie Ryder had spent the day doing work, hard work against her doctor's orders. Rest just wasn't in her nature! That being said... she was /exhausted/; nothing a little makeup and a bit of moxie couldn't fix!

Valorie couldn't wait until her arms had healed enough for them to cover them with makeup too. She hated feeling ugly every time someone stopped to stare, and to be fair she couldn't blame them! Her arms were covered in very visible gashs, bruised held together with stitches. To say they looked like they hurt was an understatement.

...And to be fair, she had pain medicine to mitigate that, but her arms weren't what were important. What was important were the things betwixt her fingers, three badges twinkling in the sunlight.

Valorie examined them proudly before filing them away into a badge case which in turn fell into her satchel. She'd challenge the other gyms soon enough, but for now she was tired from a very poor night's sleep and a day full of traveling and battling.

The very short girl took the liberty of treating herself to a cold blueberry smoothie, easing herself down onto a public bench and letting out a sigh of relief as her thighs ceased their complaints.
