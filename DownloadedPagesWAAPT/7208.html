Brine the Feraligatr♂: <Good morning, ladies and gentlemons!>

Brine: <We'd also like to thank all of you for making this years festival so wonderful! Your conjoined efforts have truly made this one of the best yet!>

Zephyr: <But it's still far from over, as our guest oh honor if about to arrive!>

Brine: <And with that comes the start of our most prestigious event; the annual Bluescale Battle Tournament!>

The crowd gives a collective "Ooh", as the mention of a Tournament gets a certain few Pokemon listening.

Crowd

Lake: <Ooh, a tournament?>

Luke: <As in a battling tournament? Sounds like fun!>

Stratus: <Yeah, this is something I want in on!>

On Stage

Zephyr: <Of course, it wouldn't be a tournament without a prize for the winner, and this year, we're offering...>

The Salamence reaches into her hammer-space and pulls out...a rather worn yellow scarf, much to the confusion of the crowd.

Random Croconaw: <...Huh? It's just a crummy old scarf!>

Random Mudkip: <LAME!>

Random Salamence: <What kind of farce are you trying to pull here?!>

Zephyr: <Oh? But this isn't just any old scarf, right Brine?>

Brine: <Nope, this is a very special scarf...in fact, it's the very scarf that was worn by our one of our first elder's; Lake himself!>

The very mention of the scarf beloning to Lake cause the entire crowd to gasp in surprise.

Crowd

Luke: <Wait, what?!>

Lake - our Lake - stares, in shock that they were offering none other than his alternate counterpart's scarf as the prize for winning the tournament.

Lake: <I...I'm...I'm surprised it hasn't rotted away by now.>

Stratus: <Gee, that sure is some strong silk Dintel weaved it from...>

On Stage

Zephyr: <Indeed, the champion of this tournament will win this priceless relic to wear with pride!>

Brine: <Obviously a prize worth of a true hero! Now, with that out of the way...>

The two pause to glance skyward as a formation of Salamence rider's soar overheard.

<It seems that our guest has arrived!>

And with that, everymon turns upwards to witness the form of a HUGE silver bird soar overhead, flapping it's great wings as it gracefully flies down and lands on-stage next to the Elder Feraligatr and Salamence, much to everyone's amazement.

Crowd

Lake: <Oh wow...>

Stratus: <WHOA! Pleo's gotten HUGE!>

Luke: <No kidding, he's even bigger than our house back in New Bark!>

On-stage

The Lugia in question gives the gather crowed of Pokemon warm smile.

Pleo: <Greetings, Pokémon of Bluescale. It's a pleasure to be here once again!>

The collective crowd cheer, or in most causes, roar loudly.

<I'm so happy to see the little town I saw the conception of so long ago is still going strong after all these years, standing as a triumph born from the unity of two species of Pokémon coming together to live and work in harmony with each other. Granted, there have been some...bumps along the road...>

He turns to give Zephyr a disapproving look.

Zephyr: <Hey! Why should those Poison Rabbits get to boss us around? It should be us telling them what to do!>

This prompts her compatriot to face-palm.

Brine: <Let it go, Zephyr...>

With a sigh, he turns back to gaze at the crowd.

Pleo: <Anyway, I look froward to seeing the many events you've planned for this year's festival, so...how about we get things started?>
