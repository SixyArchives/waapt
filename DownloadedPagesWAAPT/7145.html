Even if it seemed far-fetched, Luke and Stratus knew that Lake wasn't one to make such bold, claim without being to back it up.

Even so, they were still slightly apprehensive, but we're willing to at least take a look anyway. Besides, it's not like they had anywhere else to go.

Luke: <Alright, ready to go?>

Stratus nods in agreement, while Lake frowns, having been spending the past half-an-hour or so trying to charge his new 3DS so he could give it a try at some point.

There was one major snag, however.

Lake: <Aren't these things supposed to come with a charger? Because I don't think mine did.>

Luke: <Oh, a charger?>

Luke frowns.

<Shoot. 3DS XL's don't come with chargers.>

Stratus: <Huh? Then...how are you supposed to charge it?>

Luke: <Either you use a charger you've already got, or buy one separately. I didn't think it would be a problem, since Lake could just use mine, but...>

He waves a hand at their vague surroundings.

<...I didn't think I would be giving it to you 600 years after humanity was wiped out.>

Lake sighs, an annoyed look crossing his face.

Lake: <Shoot, I'll just have to wait until we get back home then.>

Placing the console in his bag for now, Lake pulls himself up.

<Well, I'm ready if you->

He stops, having had a thought.

<Do you think we have time for me to show you something before we go?>

Luke: <What do you have in mind?>

Sauna Hold - a little while later...

Having found a pool of steamy water to themselves, the three Pokémon are taking a little while to relax in a warm bath.

Luke: <Ahh...here's a better idea, let's stay here and relax instead...>

Lake: <Honestly? I'm tempted to agree with you...>

Lake sighs blissfully as he sinks into the water.

<But we really should get going...>

A certain Dragonite pops up from under the surface.

Stratus: <Hmm...five more minutes?>
