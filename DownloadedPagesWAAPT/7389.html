Efrafa

The sweep at the foot of Mt. Moon was covered in a wild and dark rain on this very day. During these seldom dangerous days, Woundwort takes it up on himself to personally train the new recruits about fighting in unruly weather. Leaving the slightly jumpy but ferocious, loyal and savage Toadflax in charge of the warren for the time being.

For Laurel, it couldn't have been a better time for an escape.

At Siflay, when Woundwort was taking the kits away from the rest of the warren, Laurel slipped off to talk to Skree. He told Skree that it was today in which he will make his escape with the 11 does and Blackavar and told him that the others should be ready to run over the mountain pass. The Fearow understood this, and flew back to the party waiting at the clifface of Mt Moon. After Siflay, he rounded up all the does and told them that it was today was the day they will escape. The does nodded, and ran to their positions in the warren.

Now all he had to do was get Blackavar, and then they'll run for it.

He calmly walked out to the ditch, the mouth to Woundwort's dwelling and the place where Blackavar was being kept prisoner. He was currently beind guarded by a soul known as Thistle, a young and energetic buck but not excatly the strongest of the young scouts. Laurel approached Thistle and told him calmly:

Laurel: <I was asked on the orders of Toadflax that I am to watch over the prisoner now>

Thistle: <May I ask permission why? My round is not up yet.>

Laurel: <Look over and you'll see.>

Thistle turned to the opening of the warren, where he witnessed Nildro-Hain and her 10 partners in crime dash out of the entrance. Before he could raise an alarm or persue the does, Laurel tackled him into Woundwort's warren and bit down on his leg. Effectivly rendering him useless for persuit.

Laurel: <Blackavar! Run! Meet up with Nildro-Hain and her friends! You wanted to be free! Here's your chance!>

Blackavar stared in shock at Thistle for a moment, before dashing off in the direction Nidro-hain went. Laurel smacked Thistle once more, rendering him unconcious before following Blackavar and eventually, joining up with Nildro-Hain's party.

So far, going smoothly. Yet, Laurel knew it was only time until the General himself persued him.

Less than Three

Efrafa

Laurel was running. Running for the does, running for Blackavar, running for his own life. He knew that time was precious and that, for highest chance of survival, they needed to run fast.

After running for some time or so, the group of 13 met up with Speedwell in the long grass. At first they were afraid of Blackberry, wondering if he was one of the younger lackeys out on patrol. Laurel quickly reminded them that Blackberry and the other Nidos that they'll meet at the intervals are from his warren. Wasting no more time, they start running again.

They run for quite a while, meeting Sandelford nido after Sandelford nido. It was only around ni-Frith, that they made it too Hazel-rah and Skree at the cliff face. Hazel-rah was not slow in making his remark to the newcomers.

Hazel-rah: <My new friends! Your freedom is only a mountainside away! However, it is highly trechorous and lethal so heed my words closely! Skree, the Fearow here, knows a safe route over the mountain face. He will fly ahead outlining the safe path! None of you are to deviate from it! Alright?>

The Nidorinas and Blackavar nod. They didn't want to take any chances anyway.

Hazel-rah: <Skree! Show us the way!>

Skree: <Yah! Skree vill fly ovar mounatin! Show nido safe way! Follow Skree!>

Skree started flying over the mountainside, outlining the safe route across the cliff face. The Nidos alligned themselves as one Sandelford followed by one Efrafan, with Hazel at the front and Laurel at the back.

Just when Blackavar, the last Efrafan, started climbing. A thunderous roar was heard not far away.

Woundwort: <LAUREL!!>

Laurel knew that facing the General was inevitable, but hopefully, he'll gain the higher ground before doing so.
