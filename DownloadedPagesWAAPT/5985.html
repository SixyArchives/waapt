Shahinne: <Dad? When do we have to go?>

Roc: <Right now, Shahinne...> — leers around — <Precisely now. Go on, fly.>

Girafarig: <Oh, well, thanks for the afternoon. Have a nice day!>

The two birds fly past the center of the city and then head to the east towards the PEFE storage building in the city. They fly slowly and steadily to take advantage of the sea breeze, taking small turns every once in a while to look at some particular buildings.

Shahinne: <Dad?> — waits for a prompt — <How is Endeavor?>

Roc: <It's... complicated...> — shakes head — <Your other parents should have told you...>
