Heading to Ilex Forest

A certain male Flareon is trying to strike conversation with Ichtacya. When she politely declines telling him even the time of the day, the Flareon darts forwards engulfed in flames, then steps in Ichtacya's path.

Ichtacya -stares menacingly: <...Let me pass, please?>

Flareon♂: <Come on, we could talk about things. I have a nice place with food.>

Ichtacya -smirks: <I have a Trainer if you haven't noticed. Back off.> — walks aside to get past.

Flareon♂: <A trainer with two Eeveelutions... it must be awesome, right?>

The Flareon sees Ichtacya stop for a very brief moment before continuing. «Gotcha» he congratulates himself, then calmly walks behind the Leafeon.

Flareon♂: <There are other Eeveelutions back at my place, and we have nice neighbors in the city... you could come visit sometime.>

Ichtacya: <No thanks.>

The Leafeon rushes to Silent's side before the Flareon can try anything else, so the fire Pokémon turns in Sol's direction and goes catch up with her.

Flareon♂: <So... You look a bit tired...>

Sol: <More like bored...> — rolls her eyes, and tail.

Sol looks at the Flareon for a moment, without any clear expression. Of course, the Flareon is not intimidated by that.

Flareon♂: <Why don't we go under a tree and talk about what crosses your mind... at length?>

He moves a bit closer, prompting Sol's jewel to shine. She raises a paw, exposing her claws, and hisses, fur standing on end.

Sol: <Give. me. an. excuse.> — charges some Psychic power in a threatening manner.

Flareon♂ -blinks, tilts head innocently: <Well, your fur looks very well maintained...>

Sol bares her teeth at the Flareon, hissing what would be almost an expletive, then rushes to catch up with Silent too.

The Flareon sits on the road for a moment, thinking about what has just happened.

Then he smiles at himself with his cutest Eeveelution face and chases ahead.
