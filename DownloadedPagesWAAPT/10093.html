Jubilife City, Apartment Building - Earlier Today

-Herbert and Alice are standing outside the door to one of the apartments, looking rather awkwardly at each other.-

Herbert: ...Well, this is going to be awkward. I haven't seen my mother in over a year, and truth be told I suspect she's happy with that arrangement.

Alice: I know. But we were in the region, and it's Mother's Day today. You should at least say hello.

Herbert: Yeah, but we're not going to see your mother, are we?

-Alice's expression darkens.-

Alice: You know perfectly well why I am not visiting my mother. At least you are on speaking terms with your remaining parent.

Herbert: I know, I know! I was only trying to lighten the mood.

Alice: Well, don't.

Herbert: Alright. Fine. Here goes nothing, I guess.

-He sighs, then rings the doorbell. After a moment, a surprisingly young-looking woman opens the door and frowns at Herbert.-

Herbert's Mother: Oh. Hello, Lucius. I wasn't expecting to see you today.

Herbert: Well, me and Alice came back to Sinnoh for her birthday yesterday, and we figured we ought to come back here for Mother's Day before we left. So... happy Mother's Day, I suppose.

Herbert's Mother: Thank you.

-There is an awkward pause.-

Herbert: So, um, where is...

Herbert's Mother: Your half-sister and stepfather have gone out for the day. We'll be having dinner together later.

Herbert: Right.

-There is another awkward pause.-

Herbert: ...Is it okay if we come in?

Herbert's Mother: Not at the moment, I'm afraid. I've only just gotten the place cleaned, and I don't have any refreshments in the apartment.

Herbert: Oh, okay. In that case... I guess we'll be going, then.

Herbert's Mother: I guess you will.

Herbert: Bye, Mom.

-She closes the door on her son. Herbert sighs, then turns back to Alice.-

Herbert: Well, that went about as well as could be expected. Let's get back to enjoying ourselves, shall we?

Mistralton City, Airport

-Herbert and Alice have returned from Sinnoh, and are quietly sitting on a bench together, watching the planes fly overhead.-

Herbert: I guess this is goodbye, then. For the time being, at least.

Alice: I guess. Before you go, though, there's one last thing I wanted to do.

Herbert: Oh?

Alice: Follow me.

-She leads Herbert through the city and into the Pokemon Center, where she shoves Arsenic's Pokeball into his hands.-

Alice: Keep hold of that. Give me Maul's Pokeball for a second, please.

Herbert: Huh? Why?

Alice: You'll see.

-Herbert hands Alice Maul's Pokeball with a rather confused expression on his face. She nods, turns around, and starts typing into the Center PC before casually tossing Maul's ball over her shoulder. The Kadabra emerges, and only has time to blink before he is enveloped in a glowing ball of white light. After a moment, he becomes visible again, standing much taller and clutching two forks instead of one.-

Congratulations! Maul evolved into Alakazam!

Alice: You know, you probably should have done that four years ago.

Herbert: Oh, I see! Thanks, Alice! So, how do you feel, Maul?

Maul: <...>

Herbert: Is this evolution interesting? Do you prefer it? Do you have any new abilities? Do you feel any different?

Maul: <...>

Herbert: ...Well, glad to see that evolution doesn't change some things. In any case, thanks again, Alice. You can have Arsenic back now.

-He hands her Arsenic's Pokeball, and she nods.-

Alice: It was nothing. I was surprised you hadn't done it already.

Herbert: Yeah, I probably should have done. So... this is goodbye, then?

Alice: Yes. Goodbye.

-The two pull each other into a brief hug.-

Alice: I'll be keeping an eye on you, of course. I'm staying in Unova for the forseeable future.

Herbert: As am I. Again, Happy Birthday. Thanks for... well, everything.

Alice: Again, it's nothing. Anyway, don't let me detain you.

-She pulls away, and gives him a small smile. Herbert returns it, before walking out of the Pokemon Center and in the direction of the rest of the J-Team, Maul following behind.-
