Celestial Tower

The person climbing the stairs soon shows itself, being none other than Allen.

"Hello again sister, it's been a long time." said Allen, bowing to the girl with his left arm in front of his chest "What is my beautiful sister doing in such a depressing place?" he asked, with a small smile.

"Allen!" said Rillianne, rushing to hug her brother, who hugged her back with a look of satisfaction "How are things going with your new job?" she asked, with a happy smile

"It's going well, Hikari is a admirable and fascinating boss." answered Allen, breaking the hug and smiling at his sister "You didn't respond to my answer though, is there something wrong?" he asked, with a look of minor concern.

"I was just paying my respects to... You know." said Rillianne, with a gloomy smile

"Ah, but that had nothing to do with you, why are you getting so sorrowful for?" asked Allen, keeping a gentle smile.

"As much as it doesn't have anything to do with me, it's still someone I know and also someone important for you, it's normal for me to be here, isn't it?" asked Rillianne, starting to get rather teary-eyed

"I suppose, you're right, it was foolish of me to not think of that." responded Allen, before wiping a tear that was about to fall from Rillianne's eye "It's nothing for you to be crying about though." he added, still keeping a docile smile

"Sorry, but when I think of how much this affected you, I can't help myself." she said, trying to force herself to smile "You don't need to be so formal with me around, just to let you know."

"That's right, I don't... But it's already my nature to be like this." said Allen, looking at the ground briefly "Since we're here, are you up to ring the bell? I bet the spirits will appreciate it." he said, in a more casual tone.

Rillianne smiles and nods, the siblings then both went to the bell and started ringing it, a somewhat somber smile on their faces, as the bell rang, they both went to the borders of the tower and looked at the ground from up there and at the distance for a few seconds.

"Unova is a really beautiful place, you must really like living here." commented Rillianne.

"That I do... It's one of the few good things that have happened since that time." said Allen, keeping a smile, before turning to Rillianne with a serene expression "We should go now, I still need to talk to Hikari about something." he suggested.

"Oh, let's go then, I still have to tell you about my new crush don't I?" commented Rillianne, with a somewhat embarrassed smile.

Allen stopped to think for a bit, already knowing who it was and his current situation, he wasn't sure if he should tell her about him right there or play dumb, his smile faded for a few seconds until he made his decision "Hm? That's something I'd like to hear about." he said, smiling again "I can't simply break all of her hopes like this again, I can't bring myself to do it, I'm sure her friends can do this for me, I'm sure Hikari probably already knows, so... I'm counting on her."

The siblings then started to climb down the stairs, ready to go back to Mistralton.
