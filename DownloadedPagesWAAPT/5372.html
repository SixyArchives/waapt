Route 209

As Rex and Lina made their way across the route, they happened to notice several females looking around, moving bushes aside and checking under trees as though searching for something.

"I wonder what that's about," Rex murmured, looking to his companion for an answer. She seemed just as confused as him, and he wondered if they had missed something important.

"...There one is!" came a squeal, Rex turning his head just in time to notice a glimpse of pink dart into a bush with a yelp of "Snubbull~". The girls squealed and followed after it, leaving behind the duo of trainers and their mons.

"Oh, that's right. There was mention of a swarm of Snubbull earlier today..." Rex chuckled and smiled, shaking his head. "Anyway, it's late. We should probably set up camp for the night."

Removing a set of foldable tents from his bag, Rex gave one to Lina before crawling into the other with his mons. It wasn't long before he was asleep, his partners falling asleep not long after.
