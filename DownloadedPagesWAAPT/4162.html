Cave

"That would be an incredibly angry Crystal Onix who is intent on killing us. Lake stayed behind to deal with him while we escaped." -Glances back into the depths of the cave.- "I just hope that it doesn't cost him..."

Caverns

-Twenty Seconds.

Twenty seconds until the Onix was to try and obliterate him with Solar Beam.

Lake's mind went into overdrive, trying his best to find a way to avoid or whether the attack in the short time he had left. Could he dodge the beam? Could he take cover behind a rock or in the Onix's pool? Would his Protect shield be enough to stop the beam? Or would it be worth spending some of his remaining HP on a Bubble Shield to take the hit for him?

Time was running out. He had to make a decision now, or he would be done for.

The Onix was glowing incredibly bright now, although it was almost painful for Lake to look at, the way the sunlight scattered through the Pokémon reminded Lake of their prior conversation about light scattering through a...

Something clicked in Lake's brain, and in a moment, he knew just what to do.

And not a second too soon, as the Onix was nearly finished charging.-

Crystal Onix: <BEFORE YOU DIE, FERALIGATR I WILL CONCEDE THIS: YOU WERE A WORTHY OPPONENT.>

Lake: -Smiles.- <Well, I do try.>

-Spotting a rather large growth of crystals nearby, Lake begins to sprint towards it.-

Crystal Onix: <I JUST HOPE THAT YOUR DEATH ISN'T TOO PAINFUL!>

Crystal Onix used Solarbeam!

-The Onix aims at Lake then fires a beam of intense solar energy out of his mouth, the beam hitting the floor and scorching the rock as the Onix tracks the Feraligatr with the beam, intending to catch him in it.

Lake was closing the gap with the crystals, but the Onix was tracking him faster than he could move. And the crystals were still a fair distance away.

He would have to take a chance.

The Feraligatr jumped, soaring through the air and landing behind the crystal formation, putting them between him and the Solar Beam just as the Onix sweeper it towards him.-

Crystal Onix: <THOSE CRYSTALS WON'T SAVE YOU FROM-> -Eyes widen.- <NO! NO! NOOOOOOO!!!!!>

-Like light through a prism, the crystals scattered the Onix's Solarbeam into thousands of smaller ones, the light reflecting through the crystals in every direction. Although scattered, the beams of Solar energy were still potent enough to do serious damage. Especially to a dual Rock/Ground type like the Onix.-

Crystal Onix: <AAAAAAAAARRRRRRRRRGGGGGHHHHHHH!!!>

It's Super Effective!

-The Onix roars in pain, thrashing about as several beams of light strike him for Super effective damage, draining his reserves of HP.

After a few seconds, the last of the Solarbeam's light dies away. With one last roar, the Crystal Onix collapses, slamming into the ground and shaking the cavern, but thankfully not causing the cave or the surrounding tunnels to collapses.

Not hearing anything, Lake cautiously pokes his head out from behind the crystals, finding the Onix lying there on the ground, not dead, but defeated for the time being.-

The wild Crystal Onix fainted!

-Thankful that the battle was over, the Feraligatr comes out from behind the crystals, taking a few moments to catch his breath.-

Lake: <Power of Crystal, eh?> -Smirks.- <Well, I'll know better to mess with them after this. That's for sure.>
