Maekrite stands back up and offers his berry, a not very rare but still valuable Shuca Berry. He walks up to the Nidorina and sits, while she stares at him for a moment. Then she closes up and with her muzzle grabs the berry; when Maekrite tries to push a little bit closer for a kiss and cuddles, he is met with a Scratch to the face.

After the Nidorino falls back to the ground though, the Nidorina seems to soften a little. She giggles and then grabs her mate by the neck, pulling him to the burrow. He jerks up and walks alo-
