Hamada points to Iggy and Teala to keep moving. "<Past the next hill to the south there is a safe ground where we can talk.>" Looks at Teala. "<And if you are friendly you can sleep there.>"

Somewhere deep into the Colony, some minutes ago

Maekrite has rushed into a clearing; once he reaches there he puts down a berry he was carrying; he turns his eyes to watch as a single Nidorina is tidying up the entrance of her burrow.

Maekrite: "Rawr♥?"

The Nidorina turns back and leers at him harshly, his ears instantly flattened; she then dashes at him and paces around him, checking him up.

Crerah: "...Rawr~"

The two Nidos get closer to each other for a moment, but then the female steps back and gets elusive; Maekrite knows he'll have to perform quite a gesture if he wants to earn her back. He playfully falls to the grass and rolls around, offering his ear for noms; of course, this has no effect and the female simply hisses at him disapprovingly.

Maekrite stands back up and offers his berry, a not very rare but still valuable Shuca Berry. He walks up to the Nidorina and sits, while she stares at him for a moment. Then she closes up and with her muzzle grabs the berry; when Maekrite tries to push a little bit closer for a kiss and cuddles, he is met with a Scratch to the face.

After the Nidorino falls back to the ground though, the Nidorina seems to soften a little. She giggles and then grabs her mate by the neck, pulling him to the burrow. He jerks up and walks alo-

Pierce used Call for Family!

The two Nidos's ears flicker for a moment and Maekrite's body stiffens, he turns to the northeast to check the direction of the call, which he has recognized as a call geared for him (or for Silent). Then he realizes what he has done and turns back to his mate, who is sitting on the grass giving him a deep glare, which despite the angry bloodlust red, still lets a little spark of confidence. That he would choose to stay, or perhaps that he would come back if he goes away.
