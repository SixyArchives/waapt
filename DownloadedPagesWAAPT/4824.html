- One of the ice sculptures depicts a young pregnant woman staring disappointedly at a man who can barely been seen watching her from the corner of his eye while his arm is around another woman -

- The wall behind said ice sculpture has questions such as: "If he really loved her, why'd he choose the other?" "Can external influences really tear apart love that easily?" "Would he have continued seeing her behind his fiancè's back if I hadn't come along?" -
