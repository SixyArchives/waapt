Route 202, Sinnoh (Future)

Rex and his Pokemon made their way up the small path of Route 202, a few Sentret scattering to get out of their way. A lone Zigzagoon approached Rascal, but the Flareon shooed it away, recalling the last time they ran into the mischievous creatures.

Pierce took the time to look around at all of the creatures on the route, watching a small herd of Bidoof roam through the grass with dopey grins on their face. He wondered if his papa would let him chase them, but decided that probably wouldn't happen.

A Growlithe circled under Claudia curiously, the Castform cooing in happiness as she watched it. <I like sunlight too!> she sang, beginning a mild Sunny Day in their direction. Her body shifted to acclimate better to the change, surprising the little dog Pokemon. It pawed at her playfully, the forecaster giggling as she floated out of reach.

Minty, meanwhile, was having a bit less fun than the others. The poor bug was snuggled against Rex's coat, the cool winter air unpleasant to his sensitive body. While the heat from Ponyta did help somewhat, the idea of being so close to fire wasn't exactly appealing either. <A-are we at the next city yet?>

Reaching up for his head, Rex removed his cap, letting his thick, bushy hair spring free. Scooping up the little Swadloon in one hand, he placed him into the hat, adjusting it so that only his face was uncovered. "Better?" <Y-yeah! Thanks!> He snuggled happily inside of the hat, resting peacefully on Rex's lap.

The final member of their group, Nico, took the chance to climb atop Rex's head. With a mischievous grin, he grabbed the boy's goggles and placed them on his own head, chittering happily at his lately acquisition. Rex simply shook his head, not bothered at all by the "theft". "I think those look better on me, " he chuckled, patting the Aipom's gently.

Ponyta continued along the path, the road to the next city coming into view. Rex smiled, pleased that they had been making good time today. As they continued towards the path, Rex noted something out of the corner of his eye.

Turning his head, he noticed a Shinx playfully poking at a little Kricketot, the latter making an almost musical noise of protest before ducking into some bushes. "Hmmm..." Thoughts of Eclair, Kim, Star, Diesel, and Every floated into Rex's mind, and he briefly wondered how each of them were doing. "Hopefully, they haven't gotten into too much trouble lately..."

Unsure of what her trainer meant, Ponyta simply continued onward, the paved roads of the city not too far now.
