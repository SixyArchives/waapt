Petalburg Gym

Norman: So you think you're ready this time?

-Eskay nods-

Eskay: As ready as I can get. I won't be making the same mistake I made last time.

Norman: Good, that means I don't have to try and bait it out of you this time. Shall we start?

Eskay: Ready when you are.

Take 2! Champion's Son Eskay vs. Gym Leader Norman!

Eskay sent out Lamarr!
Norman sent out Exploud!

Eskay: (So it is different this time. Very well. No need to worry about either of its possible abilities, that won't be much of a problem.) Lamarr, Swords Dance!

Lamarr: <Wheeeee!>

-Lamarr does a dance. With swords.-

Norman: Hammer Arm.

Exploud: <YOU GO SMASHY NOW.>

-Exploud swings an arm at Lamarr, who takes severe damage but manages to survive.-

Eskay: (Just a reminder that this isn't going to be easy. He's got something planned.) Superpower!

-Lamarr's eyes glow, and she hooks her scythes on the Exploud, flinging it with incredible force at the nearest wall.-

Exploud: <WAAAAAAAARGH>

Exploud fainted!

Norman: Not bad. Slaking.

-He sends out Slaking, who eyes Eskay and Lamarr.-

Slaking: <...Your trainer looks... different.>

Lamarr: <Oh, yeah! She's a he now.>

Slaking: <...Humans are so strange.>

Norman: Earthquake.

Eskay: -smirk- Protect.

-Lamarr quickly shields herself with a barrier, which somehow nullifies the shaking produced by Slaking's attack.-

Norman: ...Hm. If it works, it works, I suppose.

Slaking is loafing around!

Eskay: Trust me, back when I had a Slaking, I hated this strategy as much as you do, but it's effective regardless. Lamarr, Swords Dance again.

-Lamarr dances around the Slaking, who simply sighs and rolls his eyes.-

Norman: Hammer Arm.

Eskay: Protect.

-Once again, the Slaking's attack is for naught.-

Eskay: Superpower.

-Maybe it was simply too powerful. Maybe the Slaking figured trying to keep the pattern going another turn was pointless. Maybe it's Maybelline. Point is, the attack knocks it out.-

Norman: ...It's evident that a Slaking is no longer a challenge to you. Very well.

Norman sent out Kangaskhan!

Eskay: Huh. Been a while since I've...

-He trails off as he notices Norman raising a Key Stone.-

Eskay: ...Oh, don't tell me...

-As the Key Stone glows, the Kangaskhan kid in its mother's pouch raises its own Key Stone, growing and leaping out.-

Kangaskhan Kid: <I can fight too!>

Kangaskhan Mother: <Yes, yes, sweetie, yes you can.>

Norman: Retaliate.

Kangaskhan Kid: <Look, ma, I can beat up this weird fossily thingy!>

Kangaskhan Mother: <Yes, yes, sweetie, yes you can.>

-Eskay just sort of stares blankly as the Kangaskhan Kid punches Lamarr square in the face, knocking her out.-

Eskay: ...That's actually legal?

Norman: Mega Evolution does strange things for some Pokemon. For Kangaskhan, it gives it an ability that lets its child join in on the fight.

Eskay: I can see that, but that's legal?

Norman: ...Yes.

Eskay: ...Remind me to petition for Rotom-Z, then.

Norman: Rotom what now?

Eskay: Rotom possessing a preferably-willing Porygon-Z. It's Electric/Normal, so you'd probably like it.

Norman: Interesting... But that's irrelevant. We have a battle to do now.

Eskay: Er, right, yeah. Spectrum!

Eskay sent out Spectrum!

Spectrum: <Ooh, is this a Double Battle now?>

Eskay: Surprisingly, no. They're together. Just one. Somehow. Point is, we need to do as much as we can to them before they can do much to us. Superpower!

Spectrum: <I AM SUPERBUUUUUG!>

-She lifts up the Kangaskhan Kid and throws her at her mother.-

Eskay: Geez, talk about a guilt trip...

Kangaskhan Kid: <Owwie!>

Norman: Counter.

-Eskay's eyes go wide-

Kangaskhan Kid: <You know what, miss Superbug? We can do that too!>

Kangaskhan Mother: <Yes, yes, sweetie, yes we can.>

-The Kangaskhan Kid grabs Spectrum by the tail and swings her around a few times, before throwing her at the Kangaskhan Mother, who whips her around and throws her at the ground.-

Spectrum fainted!

Eskay: ...That's LEGAL?

-Norman nods. Eskay stares blankly, before snapping back to his senses.-

Eskay: Okay, okay, we can figure this out. We still have a chance. I... hm. Holly!

Eskay sent out Holly!

Holly: <~...Her kid is standing. Out of her pouch. Why is her kid standing out of her pouch?~>

Eskay: ~Apparently that's what happens in a Kangaskhan's Mega Evolution.~

Holly: <~...Is that legal?~>

Holly: <~This is so weird...~>

Mega Kangaskhan used Rock Slide!
It hit 2 times!
Holly flinched!

Norman: If it works, it works.

Eskay: Right, right...

Norman: So make it work again. Another Rock Slide!

-The Kangaskhan duo slam on the ground, dropping another pile of rocks on Holly.-

Holly: <Hrk—!>

Holly fainted!
