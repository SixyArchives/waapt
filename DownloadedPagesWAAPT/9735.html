"I got the solution for your problem!" said Sakura, showing the poster.

"... A festival?" asked Lyuri, not getting it.

"Yes! A festival is a perfect place for you to-"

"Oh no!" said Lyuri, now getting what Sakura is planning, and getting very embarrassed as well and, as such, looking away from Sakura.

The silence reigns in the room for a few seconds as Sakura's smile slowly disappeared from her face.

"Listen... I talked to Duke today, and he has the same opinion as you in the regards of that kind of relationship..." she said, worried about how Lyuri was going to react.

Lyuri just stares at the corner for a few seconds before sighing "... And you still want me to invite him to that festival?"

"... You're conflicted, he's oblivous, but when the moment comes, one of you will have to take action, I think we're going to put your feelings to test, sis." Sakura then gives Lyuri a small smile and hands her the poster.

Lyuri stares at the poster, leaving Sakura just in a big suspense.

Should I do this? I don't think we can work this out...

On the other hand... If he says "no", I at least won't have to wonder anymore...

But... Am I ready for a "no"? Or a "yes" for that matter?

And... He's a Pokémon... Am I all right with this?

I never felt so lost before...

Lyuri then glanced at Sakura for a second before looking back at the poster.

But I have someone who's willing to guide me... I've been helping Sakura with every problem she had until now, I think... Maybe... No, it IS time for me to take her advice this time...

Lyuri then looks at Sakura, giving a nod and a small smile "All right, let's see how this ends up, the sooner we find out how this ends, the better."
