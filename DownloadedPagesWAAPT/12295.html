Verax: -Is in his quarters-

-He looks around cautiously before pulling out a transciever-

"Oh, do come in. My apologies for any delays, but I am dealing with a rather heavy workload at present."

Verax: ".... Is he reporting to Titan... I wonder-"

"It's fine. I just got here. I did end up getting a chance to talk to Verax, though."

Verax: ".... Wait. That voice..."

"You did? Excellent. I take it he is coping with his recent injury relatively well, then. Typical Operations grunt. Too bloody-minded to let anything stand in the way of his recreational thuggery. But I digress. Tell me what you found out."

Verax: -Scoffs- "Oh I am so going to get that guy back for this..."

"He actually did get his new leg in, yeah. He said he thinks Operations is currently the best off, though I admittedly forgot to ask how exactly it gets access to all the things it does. I asked him about him and Paula and he viciously denied any sort of relationship, so it sounds like they're just close friends, if nothing else. And he actually seemed a bit shaken up at the murder."

Verax: "Wait... he's reporting on me...? But that voice... where have I..."

"His overconfidence will be the death of him. Quite literally, I suspect. And if he denied the possibility of an emotional attachment between him and Paula in such a vehement manner, then that may well lend credence to the theory. It matters little, in the end. And he was fazed by the murder in R&D? Perhaps the reason Verax is so confident in the status of Operations is due to them receiving supplies from the aforementioned department... a plausible possibility, yes, but not one worth acting on without hard evidence. In any case, is that all you gleaned from the conversation?"

"I'm... not entirely sure. He did say the person's name was Wound when I said what I knew and that he was the only person he had faith in that worked in R&D."

"Noted. I would be inclined to suggest that R&D is by and large the most competent of our four departments - with the unfortunate exception of its Administrator - but I suppose that is another point where I and the esteemed Commander must disagree. And I am in no position to complain about my current posting, given the amount of freedom it affords me."

"Right. Well, besides that, the only real thing that came of the conversation was me mentioning I thought we were getting along fairly well and him responding 'Lucky You.' So... not much else of note."

Verax: "Is he spying on all the other departments? Through Albert? But dammit where have I heard that voice before?"

"...I see your ability to detect sarcasm has evidently not improved, then. In any case, consider yourself commended for maintaining the standards I have come to expect. Are we concluded for the night, or is there more you wished to discuss?"

Verax: ".... You know I never knew that old man could be so snarky."

"Actually, there is something, though it's admittedly the result of idle thought on the way back from Operations."

"As always, do feel free to proceed with whatever your line of thought may be."

Verax: "You know I can almost hear those eyebrow raises."

"Well... I was kind of thinking of the names of some of the people here. You... Deimos... You've been calling Crewe Europa... I'm... kinda convinced there's a connection here. Would you mind telling me what it is?"

Beat

Verax: "Really? Really?"

"...You are genuinely unsure."

"I could hear that facepalm."

"I suppose that ignorance is a weed that must be rooted out wherever it appears... in any case, Titan, Deimos and Europa are all names derived from another source. Names of places, specifically. Are you beginning to see the connection?"

"Places... where, exactly?"

".................... Wow."

"...Places within this Solar System. If that was not immediately obvious already."

"Er... I only really know the names of the eight planets."

Verax: "Oh my goodness I could turn this into a sitcom. I'd be famous. I mean really this is comedic gold if all their conversations run like this. I might just keep it to hear more about Albert's life."

"...Moons. Titan, Deimos and Europa are all names of moons, which themselves are largely derived from the names of Greco-Roman mythological figures or characters from Shakspearian plays. Using the names of the actual planets would be highly unoriginal. Not to mention that they are all taken."

Verax: "DAMN RIGHT that's unoriginal!"

"...Ohhhhh. Thanks for clearing that up."

"You are very welcome. Now are we concluded, or did you have more to ask? Hopefully any future queries will be of a less banal nature."

"Well, this might sound kind of bad considering I didn't know what the names meant until a bit ago, but considering the others have them..."

"...You are requesting that I assign you a similar moniker?"

"Yeah..."

Verax: "Albert you are a strange strange man. But damn where have I heard you before...?"

"...Very well then, I suppose that is a request I can grant. I would ask for your opinion regarding preference, but given your general ignorance on the subject matter, doing so would likely be a pointless exercise. From now on, your new alias is Triton. Would you rather I use this form of address during all our interactions in future, as I do with Deimos, or revert back to your real name for private conversations, as I do with Europa?"

".... Real name?"

"Oooh~."

"But I'll leave that up to you. My team at least is probably going to still be calling me Crow."
