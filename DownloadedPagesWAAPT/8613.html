- Everyone is getting ready to go to Sinnoh.-

Somewhere, AU

Thanks to Crow and Tracer for spagettiing this over a Google Doc with me.

-In what appears to be a throne room, two Hydreigon are conversing, with several other Dragons looking on.-

AU!Alduin: Anyway, Quostiid?

Cassandra: >_< >_< >_< Is that REALLY necessary, my lord? And what is it?

Alduin: Well... Since you're clan leader of certain sectors of Unova, I thought it would be in your interest if you got to meet one of the recently appointed generals.

Cassandra: ...Generals? Who on earth-?

Alduin: Bahahkun! Come greet your superiors!

-A Haxorus enters and walks toward the two Hydreigon-

Alduin: Quostiid? Meet Bahahkun. Bahahkun... Well, you know the drill.

Bahahkun: Yeah, yeah.

-Giving an unimpressed leer-

Cassandra: ...I was under the impression that the selection of generals was to be done collectively... Who is this axehead, and why on earth should she be given any position other than that of a foot soldier?

Alduin: Now, now, there... Bahahkun may be blunt, but she's got a fine repository of tactical knowledge!

-To Bahahkun, in a less-than-inviting tone-

Cassandra: ... I’m waiting...

Bahahkun: For what?

-With a touch of hostility-

Cassandra: For an explanation of yourself! If you expect me to be impressed with any random dragon that brings herself to this place, you are sorely mistaken! What is your background? Why on earth were you even considered to be a general?

Bahahkun: Explanation? The only explanation I need are my blades!

Cassandra: ...Lord Alduin, please tell me that there’s something more tangible for why she’s being considered than a stubborn attitude...

Alduin: She may not look impressive at first glance, but she’s moved her way up the ranks quite quickly, considering her origins as an arena fighter for humans... She’s practically a prodigy!

Bahahkun: Yeah! My last boss never knew what hit him!

Cassandra: ...'Arena fighter'... You’re joking...

Alduin: We need Dragons from all walks of life in our ranks, Quostiid. And again, her knowledge of battle tactics and statistics is extraordinary!

Cassandra: But the Arena is nothing like a proper battle!

-Leering at Bahahkun-

This isn’t some sparring match where you can rely on a human to give you instructions to mindlessly follow! In the battles we will wage, it is you that will need to carry your own fate!

But I’ll give you the benefit of the doubt. If you were incapable of thinking for yourself, you would not be here... So tell me, arena fighter. You hopefully are not completely pathetic at combat... Let’s hear about your tactics as an administrator... How do you lead your troops into battle? And how do you control that which you have rightfully taken with your claws?

-In the background-

Fuuma: I swear, she’s in love with her own voice...

Seishirou: Rambling exposition is her specialty...

Bahahkun: Lead them? That’s easy. I beat them up if they don’t get the job done. And if anything slips out of my control, I beat it up too!

Cassandra: ...

-To Alduin-

Cassandra: ...She will have oversight, yes? I wouldn’t entrust her with a squadron, Let alone an army! Just what on earth would you need a dragon like her in the capacity of a general for?

Alduin: She will be overseen by us, yes... But we need more forceful and assertive commanders!

Cassandra: Forceful and assertive? We have no shortage of that, Lord Alduin! Just look at Fuuma and Seishirou! Or whatever they style themselves in that frog speak we have to use for these meetings... They’re the epitome of assertiveness! What we need at this stage is long-term planning, and I honestly can’t see the likes of her being remotely helpful with that matter!

Alduin: Long-term planning is for AFTER we conquer! What we need to focus on is forcing humanity into submission!

Cassandra: ... I will admit that that is a more immediate concern... But still, the utter intangibility of... Well... Everything we have post-conquest barring a couple matters is still deeply concerning to me...

Alduin: Well then! I’m glad to welcome you to our ranks, Bahahkun! Now, why don’t you and Quostiid go for a stroll, work out some of those differences of yours?

-Reluctantly-

Cassandra: Rrgh... Welcome aboard...

-And heads out of the meeting place-

Bahahkun: Heh. Glad to be on board.

-Bahahkun goes with Cassandra-

Alduin: -turning to the others after the two females are out of earshot- So... The Haxorus seem adequate for our purposes?

-Most of the others nod in assent-

Seishirou: She seems less obstructionist...

Fuuma: Not to mention less annoying in general.

Vallok: I can’t wait to be rid of ‘Oh, that’s too impractical!’ Now I can get my Biobliterator prototype finished the way it was meant to be! Free from her idiocy!

Lawrence: Yes, it would appear that a bit of that matter has been resolved... A small, relatively superficial one, anyways...

Alduin: ...

-All three heads grin evilly-
