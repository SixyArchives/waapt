291185 Herbert40k16th Sep 2013 01:46:24 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
Chords of Steel

Holon City Pokemon Centre - Last night

-There is silence in the darkened lobby for a moment as Bishop processes Kirk's statement. Eventually, Bishop manages to form his thoughts into a sentence.-

Bishop: <...What do you mean?>

Kirk: <Didn't I just tell you? I'm done with this place. I'm sick of sitting around on my ass doing nothing when we're free to as we please now. I'm sick of the assholes that you guys insist on hanging around with getting on my case constantly. I'm going to head off on my own. Do what I want for once.>

Bishop: <And... where do you plan on going, exactly?>

-Kirk shrugs.-

Kirk: <I dunno. Anywhere but here.>

-There is another period of dead silence.-

Bishop: <...You don't have to do this, you know.>

Kirk: <I know I don't have to. But I don't have to stick around here any more either. Now that our trainer's gone, there's nothing tying us down. We're free to do whatever we want.>

Bishop: <But what about when Herbert comes->

Kirk: <Herbert won't be coming back, Bish. He's dead. I know you don't want to accept that, but it's time to get over it and move on. Otherwise you're just going to stay stuck in the same old rut.>

-He takes another step towards the door, only to be interrupted by Bishop again.-

Bishop: <What about us?>

Kirk: <...Look, we've had our ups and downs as a team. But I know that if I asked you guys, you'd all rather hang around here and desperately cling to the past rather than taking the chance to move on. I'm heading off on my own.>

Bishop: <So you decided to leave in the middle of the night without saying goodbye to any of us.>

-Despite his flat tone, Bishop still manages to sound vaguely accusatory. Kirk shifts uncomfortably.-

Kirk: <Well... yeah.>

Bishop: <What about Eowyn, Kirk? How do you think she is going to react to this?>

Kirk: <...That's different. Things aren't going to work out for us.>

Bishop: <But you want them to work out. And isn't this all about what you want?>

Kirk: <...>

-Kirk is silent for a moment, then suddenly shifts into a battle-ready stance.-

Kirk: <See, this is why I didn't tell any of you! I knew that if you found out, you'd try to stop me!>

Bishop: <Kirk, it was never my intention to->

Kirk: <Then what do you call this, then?>

-Bishop sighs.-

Bishop: <...Where you go next is your choice to make, Kirk. I do not agree with your decision, but I respect your right to it. As you said, you are a free Pokemon. I am merely pointing out the multitude of reasons why I believe this to be a very bad idea.>

Kirk: <Well then, don't. Okay?>

Bishop: <...Very well. May I at least know what prompted this decision?>

Kirk: <Huh? What do you mean? I already told you why I'm doing this.>

Bishop: <Kirk, I have been your companion long enough to know that whilst you may have been harbouring these sentiments for a while now, there must have been some form of catalyst that prompted you to act upon them. What was it?>

Kirk: <...Ask the Mightyena. He'll tell you.>

-With a scowl that clearly indicates that he has no desire to elaborate, Kirk turns and strides out of the Centre. Bishop watches him go.-

Bishop: <...Goodbye then, Kirk.>

-Kirk doesn't reply, and continues walking until Bishop has lost sight of him in the dark streets. The Scyther sighs, then looks back up the stairs with a defeated expression.-

Bishop: Now what am I going to tell the others...?
