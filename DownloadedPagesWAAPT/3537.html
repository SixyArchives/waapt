PEFE HQ, two days ago

-I'm finishing pack up some things, while my Pokémon are wandering around... well, most of them-

Dintel (chases Shahinne around) — <Wheeeee~!>

-Shahinne makes a sudden turn, but miscalculates and crashes against the nightstand as she tries to swoop away. Dintel reaches there to find Shahinne pretty much stuck in-

Dintel (sits idly) — <That's gonna leave a dent...>

-he waves around while Shahinne tries to free herself.-

-Meanwhile Algira helps me packing. She's very insistent that we finish up 'soon and depart to Vermillion City. She doesn't want to hear my complains that I have to make a stop at Viridian City first-

— I got all those permissions finally signed, Algira. I don't want to waste this opportunity.

Algira — <Well we've got some new friends already...> — points to a certain mon nervously sitting at the other side of the room, examining our Pokéballs and some other things. — <Do we really have to take that many new peoplemons?>

-Besides my bed, a newly-revived Lileep rubs his tentacles against the bed, as if trying to feel the textures of the bedsheets. He seems even more zoned out on his tasks than what Dintel usualy does, but that's understandable-

Lileep — <Oh so soft, and so green... it's like the fern of the water... why is there that much of this here...? And where are the 'gators, the turtles, the otters who owned the seas?>

-I let Algira rest on my hand-

— Actually... yes. I'm tired of something and want to try something new. — stares at her — Seriously what's with you? They's not gonna eat you or something...

Algira (buzzes) — <It's not that, hmph!>

-She flies to the nightstand and tries to help Shahinne get unstuck, while Dintel looks at the spectacle clapping-

-Suddenly, Algira flashes some red and purple colors-

Algira — <Hey... about that, how come we haven't seen Maekrite in two weeks already?>

-Dintel and Algira stare at me, and I remain there feigning not having listened to them while I pack a thermos and some items. Shahinne can't look at me- she's still stuck at the nightstand. At my bed, there's a Leafeon snoring, but she lends an ear to the conversation. Lileep seems lost on musings...-

— Look, team, Maekrite is gonna be a bit busy, so in the meantime... — sighs — Algira is going to be the team leader.

-Dintel looks a bit let down, but the other team members (sans Lileep) pretty much "yay!" in approval, with Algira blushing orange colours-

I hope not to regret this...

Lileep (looks around, waving tentacles) — <And so much air... I wonder how large are the valleys on the lands outside of Queen Kyogre's domains... yes I shall help reclaim them for Queen Kyogre...>

Six hours later

-I pick Maekrite's Moon Ball, attach it to my belt and go to the PEFE Airdock with Shahinne and Algira following-

Shahinne — <Hey! Look at that big thing!!!> — stares at the PEFE airship — <Is that a ziplin?>

Algira — <Er, I think it is a carrier, little miss.>

Shahinne (chirps) — <Carrier?>

— It means it carries people and Pokémon and stuff, Shahinne.

-Shahinne blinks and seems to think for a moment. Then, while we are packing our stuff...-

Shahinne — <Oh! I get it, Trainer is a carrier too!>

-I have to refrain from facepalming-

This will be a fun trip. Yes, fun, fun and more fun. If we have to face an enemy, please let it be just sunburn...

— Now guys, er, girls... we have to go to Viridian first...

Algira — <Awww...>

— ...I have to pick up Fearow from there...

-I say hi to the pilot-

Juanito the PEFE Airplane Pilot — Afternoon, Silent. The other interns are almost ready loading the new exploration machines.

— Afternoon, Juanito. Good to see you working here now. And don't worry, I'm not in a hurry...

-Ten minutes later, the plane departs.-
