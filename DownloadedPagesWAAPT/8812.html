220297 ParadoxicalStone9th Jan 2013 03:21:06 AM from The Dreaming Get RP Mod
The Penitent
Outside Tracer's House, in the alley

The Penitent woke early, with the sunrise. He was used to it by now - it came with sleeping in alleys - and after last night's encounter he couldn't afford to be recognized. Crashing in a Pokemon Center would have been too risky; no, he'd have to spend the night on the streets. Adjusting his cowl, he tugged it down, keeping his face well concealed, then smoothed out the creases in his cloak and stretched his aching back muscles. He missed the softness of a warm bed, but there was nothing to be done.

"Maybe I should get myself a mask," he muttered. Those two soccer moms had identified him way too easily, and he'd almost been arrested. A mask would solve that problem - and also make him more noticeable than ever. He shook himself, dispelling the idea. "Yeah, right," snorted the Penitent. "As if the black cloak isn't dramatic enough." He knew that the cloak was unusual, but wasn't willing to give up on it just yet - it allowed him to conceal a whole lot of useful items, kept his face (mostly) in shadow, and of course it was warm. And warmth was always welcome when sleeping rough.
