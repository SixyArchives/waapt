Route 35 - Johto

Phyco — <OUCH!>

"Hold still! It won't hurt so much if you don't move."

The Kingdra grudgingly holds still, wincing a bit as his trainer pulls a poison barb from his scales, applying some Antidote on the wound to neutralize the toxin.

Earlier on, they had tried to do some training against some of the wild Nidoran that lived on the route, only for them to easily fell Phyco with a barrage of Poison Stings to pop his bubble, leaving him unable to move as they Scratched and Pecked his HP away, leaving Phyco rather humiliated, and a few Nidoran kits several levels higher than they had previously been.

In an attempt to cheer him up, Luke had released Phyco into the Route's pond so that he could at least soak for a little while while he treated him. Lake was also in the pond, taking the opportunity to relax by swimming around for a bit.

Lake — Swims up to Phyco, looking concerned. <How are you feeling?>

Phyco — Snorts. <Annoyed. I should have won against those Nidoran...>

Lake — Frowns. <They did sorta' have an advantage. They can move on land and you...can't.>

Phyco — Shakes his head. <That should have meant nothing. A Dragon like me should've been able to won, no matter what odds are stacked against- OUCH!>

The Kingdra winces as his trainer pulls another poison barb from his scales.

"I said hold still!"

The Kingdra grumbles angrily, then tries to remain still as Luke pulls the last barb from his skin, applying some antidote to neutralize the last of the poison. Job done, Luke gently pats the Kingdra's scales.

"There you go. Feeling better?"

Phyco shoots the human a glare...then nods.

"Alright. We'll stay here for a bit longer so you can have a swim, then we'll see if we can find something easier for you to fight. Like a Ditto or something."

Phyco — Rolls his eyes. <Oh yes, find me a weaker target. That'll really make me feel better about this.>

Lake — <Hey, we all have to start from somewhere. Even I struggled against Pidgey and Rattata at one point.>

Phyco — To Lake. <Yes, but were you a Feraligatr at the time?>

Lake — <No...>

Phyco — <Then this simply doesn't compare.>

The Kingdra swims away in a huff, leaving Luke and Lake to give each other concerned looks.

"This is going to take a while..."

Lake — <Oh, joy.>

Route 35, off the trails

A group of Nidoran kits gather between some bushes, slightly tired but fairly unscratched. They are all concerting and commenting on the events of the day.

"<Yay! We beat a dragon! Rawr!>" claims one, high-five-(two?)-ing her adventure partner.

"<That was silly, >" snorted another one, sitting besides a tree, "<you should have been more careful and waited for the adults.>"

"<Yeah, what if the blue thing nomed you?>"

The Nidoran♀ giggles while her partner Nidoran♂ gives the others a "oh please" look, then steps ahead and confronts the Nidoran by the tree.

"<Come on, what are you, a rooli or your marli?>" — assorted giggles. "<Besides, lookie what I can has do now!>"

The Nidoran♂ steps back a moment then lowers his horn and aims it at the Nidoran by the tree, who simply rolls his eyes. The first Nidoran then turns his body a little, flattens ears and charges at the tree with a Horn Attack.

"<...Ouch!>" bounces back and falls flat to the ground.

"<Great, just because you learned a new attack you think you are a master at it...>" the sitting Nidoran snorts again as he runs his paw on the bark, confirming nothing more than a little damage.

The Nidoran female growls. "<Just shut up. You didn't even fight the thing...>" — she readies her claws. "<Besides, that you just learn Toxic Spikes doesn't make you the best at everything.>"

"<Didn't need to.>" the Nidoran walks ahead of the tree, sitting in between it and the fallen Nidoran kit, who was rubbing his flank. "<Using a new attack requires training and discipline, >" he announces proudly.

Some of the other Nidorans comment things like "<There we go...>" and "<Cue the flashbacks...>", but the Nidoran on the focus simply leers at them.

"<For you to know, I heard this from Maekrite yesterday.>"

This gets the attention of some of the other kits, who come closer and sit around.

"<You saw him? Issn't he like, really big?>"

The Nidoran shakes his head. "<He's barely a bit larger than Hamada. I saw him when he was helping the Jolteon with his training.>"

"<Oh! The Jolteon! I wonder if we can go visit him?>" speaks a random Nidoran♀.

"<I wouldn't know. They train on the southern meadows and the parents won't let us go there.>"

"<Oh...>" the Nidoran♀ grimaces for a moment, then flickers her ears. "<Then it is time for us to learn [stealth]!>"

Assorted giggles, hurras and "<Cool! Yay!>"s, after which the Nidorans gather into a troop and rush towards the south.

The Nidoran♂ simply rolls his eyes again, decides that at least by following his troop partners will let him keep them under check. He sighs, picks some grass up to chew on the road, and follows.

Unknown to him and the others, a Nidorino has been watching from behind another tree. He looks around, checking that none of the kits have fallen behind.

"«Well at least Rize is safe with the group...»" comments the Nidorino, proud of his son, though still worried as he sniffs around trying to find traces of something. "«But I wonder where did Vinollo go...? He was right here... Ugh... Émar is going to kick me out of the burrow again...»
