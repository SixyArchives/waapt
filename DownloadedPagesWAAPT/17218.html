Ding!

The elevator doors slid open, Izzy emerging first as she gasped for breath loudly, followed by Lake and an embarrassed looking Stratus.

Stratus: <Izzy, I'm sorry!>

Izzy: <Why couldn't ya' hold it in?>

Stratus: <It was one of those ones that just slips out!>

Lake frowned, slightly disgusted.

Lake: <I can't believe we're having a conversation like this.>

He shook his head, then looked around to see where they had ended up. The three were now in a rather large hall that seemed to serve as the airship's central hub. The walls to either side of them were home to doors every few meters that lead to different rooms, all labelled helpfully.

Problem was, there was an awful lot of them. Which meant a lot of places for this Torque Pokémon to be hiding.

<Jeez, we've got an awful lot of ground to cover!>

Placing her hands on her hips, Izzy nodded in agreement.

Izzy: <This Torque creep could be hiding anywhere. I bet if we're to find this guy in any short length of time, we're gonna' have to start thinking like a villain.>

Lake: <Oh, I can have a go at that. Give me a moment...>

Izzy glanced up at the crocodile as he shut his eyes, placing his hands to his head and rubbing it to help him think.

<I'm a big bad reptile, and I wanna' take over the world, blow up a city, and maybe eat some babies... AHAH! I've got it!>

He raised a pointed finger, smirking triumphantly.

<If some fools were brave enough to come on board my airship looking to defeat me, I'd wait for them...>

He then pointed up a staircase leading upwards to a large door marked Throne room.

<Atop my magnificent throne!>

Izzy: <Bingo! Now why didn't I think of that?>

Lake: <Come, my minions! Let us go vanquish this fool of a Tyrantrum so we can rule in his place!>

Blinking in surprise at Lake's sudden shift in tone, Izzy and Stratus gave him a worried look.

Stratus: <Son? Are you okay?>

Izzy: <You're doing this fake villain thingy a little too well if ya' ask me. Maybe you sho->

Lake: <SILENCE, LIZARD! Now get up there befo->

Izzy quickly gave the Feraligatr a small electrical shock. Not strong enough to do any damage, but enough to hurt.

<OW!>

Lake recoiled, wincing in pain before blinking a few times as he came to his senses.

<Whoa...I'm sorry, I don't know what came over me just there! I even frightened myself!>

The Heliolisk patted her friend gently.

Izzy: <Relax, partner. You're a good guy, through and through. Y'know how we can cement that even further?>

Lake: <How?>

Izzy opened one of her hands and punched the other with it.

<By going up there and kicking some scaly behind!>

1:42 to Cannon Fire

Feeling better, Lake nodded in agreement.

Lake: <Yeah. Let's go and show that Torq->

He stopped as he noticed that Stratus was sniffing the air.

<Dad? What's up?>

Stratus: <...Food.>

Lake: <Food?>

The Dragonite nodded as he turned his attention to one of the doors on the left wall marked Dining Room and Kitchen. He raised a claw and pointed to it excitedly.

Stratus: <In there!>

Impatient to get to the butt kicking, Izzy glared at the Dragonite.

Izzy: <We don't have time for a lunch break! We've gotta' stop him before->

But it was too late, the Dragonite hovered into the air and shot towards the door, which opened automatically to let him through.

Stratus: <DON'T WORRY, FOOD! STRATUS IS HERE TO SAVE YOU!>

Lake and Izzy stared after the dragon, dumbfounded.

Izzy: <...Why did we bring him along?>

Lake sighed.

Lake: <Because he's my Dad. And when he's focused, he's nearly as strong as I am. Also->

He raised a finger.

<He's the only one strong enough to fly while carrying me.>

Izzy: <Oh yeah.>

Dining Room  1:41 to Cannon Fire

Stratus rocketed through the doors, finding himself in a rather fancy looking dining room, dominated by a huge table in the middle surrounded by chairs, with a regal throne at the far end. The room was brightly lit by large, thick windows that made up the far wall, through which could be seen a fantastic view of the Goldenrod bay.

No food, though.

Sniffing with his large nose, the dragon glanced to the right, spotting on the wall behind the far end of the table was another door that the scent was coming from.

The kitchen!

Grinning from internal ear to ear, the dragon dashed over to the doors which slid open to reveal that there was indeed a kitchen behind them.

Stratus: <HERE I->

However, when he stepped onto the kitchen floor, to his surprise, it was cold and...slimy. Also, slippery.

<COOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOME!!!>

The Dragonite slid along the floor at a high speed, whirling his arms in fright as he tried desperately to balance, until...

WHAM!

He collided with a large metal fridge so hard that he left a Dragonite shaped indentation in the doorway. Slowly, he peeled himself backwards and off onto the floor, landing with a slimy splat.

<...Ow.>

This accident had apparently attracted some attention, as the dazed dragon suddenly heard the sound of pots and pans clanking as if being dropped in a panic.

???: <AH! I'm sooooooooo sorry! I still haven't gotten to cleaning the floors yet and- Huh?>

Stratus rubbed his head as the sound of wet footsteps came towards him. Then all of a sudden, a purple head on a long neck came into view looking down on him.

He was surprised. This was a Pokémon, in his many travels, that Stratus had never encountered before. It's body was an almost purply-pink with a purple belly, broken up by the occasional large green spot. It's tail was thick with a curl at the very end. Two legs with large his  very similar to his own - sat on each side of it's body, with a pair of even lengthened arms sitting not far above them.

At a top of a lengthy neck sat it's round head, which was home two green eyes that stared down at him, two four antenna of sorts  two tiny nubs in front, with another two large ones that grew backwards a fair length, almost like a human with pig-tails.

Domesticated Goodra: <You're not one of Torque's lackeys. Who are you? Where did you come from?>

Stratus: <...Uh...>

Sitting up from his slimy puddle on the floor, Stratus rubbed his chin trying to think of a good answer. He wasn't sure where this Pokémon's affiliations lied...or even what sort of Pokémon she was altogether, so he needed to come up with something convincing.

But...for some strange reason, he was lost staring at this strange Pokémon. He felt positively enamoured by the purple creature. The way her body seemed to jiggle as she moved, those long curly things on top of her head, the droplets of slime that fell off her body occasionally.

...Okay, that last part of her wasn't so nice, but everything else made up for it.

Goodra: <...Hello? Are you still there?>

The slime dragon waved a paw in front of Stratus' face, the Dragonite having been doing nothing but staring at her in silence. Stratus blinked, the realised he was supposed to be telling her what he was doing here.

Stratus: <Food inspector!>

The Goodra blinked.

Goodra: <...Is that a thing?>

Stratus nodded vigorously as he stood up.

Stratus: <Yep, yep! I'm here to make sure your food's up to standard!>

Turning around, he pulled the dented fridge door open, peering inside to look at the contents.

<You wouldn't believe how many peoplemons these days travel around without having food up to par. I mean, look at this! All this meat but no vegetables to go with them? By Rayquaza, you don't even have any bread!>

He turned back, giving the Goodra an incredulous look.

<What kind of madmon doesn't even have bread in their kitchen?!>

Goodra: <Er...>

The Goodra quivered nervously, her whole body jiggling.

<There are only two Pokémon  aside from me  on this ship and they both have specialized diets. One is carnivorous and only eats meat, the other doesn't even eat conventional food. He eats scraps of metal ins->

Lake: <Dad, are you in here?>

The two turned to see a familiar Feraligatr enter the room and step on the slimy floor, losing his balance.

<Whoa! WhoawhoaWHOAWHOAWHOOOOOA!!!>

He ran in place for a few seconds, trying desperately to regain his footing before flipping over and landing on his front with a loud splat just as Izzy entered after him.

Izzy: <Stratus! We really don't have time for->

Once again, she stepped in the slime and slid forward...

<Thii IIISSSSSSSSSSSSSSS!!!>

...And careened head-first...

SPLAT!

Into the Goodra.

Goodra: <Ah! I'm sorry, are you alright?>

Izzy responded, but it was muffled by the Goodra's thick stomach.

Izzy: <Mmph...>

Stratus: <She's fine. I think.>
