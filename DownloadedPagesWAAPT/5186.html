Oreburgh Mine (Future)

Rex wandered down the stone path of the mine, watching as several Rock and Fighting-Types assisted the miners in their duties. It seemed that cooperation with Pokemon was important even here; no doubt the Gym Leader was the one who instituted that. It also seemed that trainers were allowed into the mines as well, as the miner near the entrance informed him. Perhaps the Pokemon were instituted to help when the Leader was busy?

He made his way past a few nearby trainers who were inspecting a pile of boulders.

"It's true! If you inspect this wall and look under this boulder, a [Shiny Invisible Bulbasaur] will appear! I heard it from my friend!"

"How can you know it's shiny if it's invisible?"

"Shut up!"

Shaking his head, Rex continued on deeper into the mine. After several moments, he came upon a group of miners, one of them standing out slightly more than the rest. For starters, he was wearing gray with a red hard hat as opposed to the green with a yellow hard hat that the others were wearing. Secondly, he had a Cranidos, a Pokemon that none of the others seemed to possess.

"Now, watch closely, " he said, motioning to a rock near him. His Cranidos wandered over to it, giving a growl as it drove its noggin into it for a fierce headbutt. "By using [Rock Smash], boulders that bar your way are destroyed. However, you should be careful not to cause landslides when using it. It's all a matter of judgment on your part. ...But you all are new. You'll learn eventually." He nodded to them, giving a friendly smile. "In any case, that is all. You can meet back with Mason in the front room."

The group dispersed, passing by Rex as they murmured amongst themselves. Apparently, they had all just started their jobs; if the Gym Leader had to leave in order to teach them, no doubt he was the boss of the mine.

The man in the red hat watched them go, noticing Rex standing off to the side. The two made eye contact, and the head miner approached, his Cranidos following not too far behind.

"A new challenger, right?" he asked, flashing a small grin.

"Yeah..." Rex agreed, feeling a bit nervous. He hadn't really had much interaction with Gym Leaders after Viridian City...

"Well, I suppose I have time for one more battle tonight. I'll meet you there."

He nodded to the boy and stepped past him, Cranidos sniffing at Rex's clothes before following afterwards. Taking a deep breath to calm himself down, he turned on his heel and followed afterwards, preparing himself for the battle to come. If he could do it this time, if he could win those eight badges, then just maybe he could find himself to be on everyone else's level...

"The Bulbasaur should be under this pile of boulders!"

"That's an Onix, you fool!"
