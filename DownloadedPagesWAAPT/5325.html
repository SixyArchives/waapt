My point is, can you SEE why I fled my home universe? It may be selfish and wrong of me... But I just can't take it anymore! ... I just want to be able to live my life as a normal person. No paparazzi, no creepy stalkers, no journalists hounding me the moment I set foot out into a public space... Just me, my mons, and the people that I care about..."

-Pausing noticeably-

<Zekrom: "... Oh... I think I understand... But...

Besides... How is it fair that you should have to leave almost everything that you care about behind just to get some peace?">

-Quietly-

Tracer(?): "... It's not fair... But it's all that I can think of at this point..."
