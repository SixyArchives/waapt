Illusion HQ, last night

-the two Terries, uneasily, make their way into Alys' office-

-it is...strange. Do you know that strange old lady that lives on your street that stuffs her dead cats? Alys' office looks something like her front room. The wall is painted a light rose, but everything else looks decrepit. There are cobwebs in every corner, and the lighting is terrible-

-it smells very strongly of cigarette smoke and strong tea. Terry coughs, while Terri simply looks ill at ease-

-suddenly, a Galvantula drops from the ceiling!-

Galvantula: <Hi! :3>

Terries: GAAH!

-Terry clings to Terri in fear. The redhead, despite herself, clings back-

???: Oh...don't tell me that my Galvantula scared you two...

-the two turn their heads to the voice. A high-backed swivel chair is facing the window, and it slowly turns to reveal a woman. The woman is 27, but looks about a decade older. Thin, ink-black hair hangs about the side of her face. Her eyes are navy blue, and very deep. This woman is very thin, to the point where one could worry about her health. Her lips, reddened with lipstick, spread into a smirk-

Alys: I see that two...little Mothim fluttered into my abode. Make yourself at home. Have a seat!

-she speaks in a syrupy-thick Kalosian accent. Her tone is that of welcoming, but this was clearly not a woman meant to sound welcoming. The Terries sit. Alys takes a drag of a cigarette, using a holder-

Terri: -thinking to herself- Who uses a holder anymore?

-she smiles, again-

Alys: Now, then. My dear Specialist Juliet informed me of a...transgression. Terri?

-both flinch. Alys clarifies-

The female. You had an...Ability Capsule in your possession, is that correct?

Terri: -nodding- Y-yes, Admin Boucher.

-Alys laughs, quietly, to herself-

Please, call me Alys. But tell me, Terri...where is that Capsule now?

Terri: -her professional demeanor begins to fade into sheepishness and fear- I...don't know. I misplaced it.

-in a swift motion, Alys takes the cigarette out of the holder and puts it in an ashtray. Terri jumps-

Alys: ...misplaced? What a childish mistake.

-she grins. Terry stares, wide-eyed, and Terri begins to think:-

Oh shit. She's going to eat me.

Alys: Well, proper actions shall be taken to make sure that never happens again. Like this one.

-idly, she takes a wax pear from a bowl on her desk and fiddles with it in her hands-

Do you see the man seated next to you, Terri?

Terri: Y-yes...

Alys: He is new. It is your job to stay with him, and make sure he doesn't make the mistakes that you have made.

-acting as a punctuation mark, Alys takes a large bite from the wax pear. The Terries stare in fascinated horror as she chews and swallows-

Is that understood?

Terri: Y-y-yes!

Alys: Good. Now, run along, little Mothim.

-the two quickly stand and get out of the office. The Galvantula from earlier climbs up Alys' leg and into her hand-

Alys: Why, hello there, Ariadne...

-she idly pets the Galvantula, who threefaces-

I am a pretty flower child. Gaze upon me and despair.
