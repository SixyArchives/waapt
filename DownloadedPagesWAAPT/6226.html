The Blue Bayou - PMD-B

After finding out that a friend of his was going to end up destroying humanity, Lake did what probably most Pokémon would do in his situation - attempt to drown his sorrows with drink.

The thing was...Lake wasn't an alcoholic.

So instead, he settled for downing several mugs of Sitrus juice while leaning against the bar of the local Inn, possibly with Gino and Skye.

Of course, they're depression hadn't gone unnoticed by the Innkeeper, who was also a Feraligatr.

Innkeeper: <Are you guys alright? You seem awfully depressed about something.>

Lake nodded in response, without looking up from his Sitrus Juice.

Lake: <Yeah. We've just had a rough day, that's all.>

Innkeeper: <Oh boy. What happened?>

Lake frowned, unsure how to tell him that a friend of theirs was going to commit a despicable act at a future point in time.

Lake: <...It's complicated. You wouldn't understand.>

The Innkeeper gives Lake a concerned look, then nods slowly.

Innkeeper: <If you say so. Let me know if you need a refill, okay?>

And with that, he heads off to serve yet another mug of Leppa Cider to a drunken Salamence, who was now telling his drinking buddies just how much he loved them.

Still, at least he wasn't smashing furniture anymore.

Lake pauses to take another gulp of Sitrus juice, before turning to look at his two companions.

Lake: <So...any ideas? 'Cause I've got nothing...>
