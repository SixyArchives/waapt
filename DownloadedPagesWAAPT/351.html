Hmm...

Well, see, you're from Johto, right? Well, think of it this way; Hearts of Gold are connected to Ho-Oh. Ho-Oh is connected to rainbows, color, the sky, and fire. One thing that they all have in common is aggressiveness, compassion, and being extroverted. Those, according to Unovan Mythology, are connected to the White Yang.

Likewise, Souls of Silver are connected to Lugia. Lugia is connected to cold, monochromes, oceans, and moving water. One thing they all have in common is passiveness, an analytical approach, and introversion. Those, according to Unovan Mythology, are connected to the Black Yin.

The Mind of Crystal is connected to... um... Suicune! Suicune is connected to still water, the North Wind, speed, and the fact that it's almost impossible to encounter. One thing those things all have in common is rarity, being apathetic, and basically being disconnected. Those, according to Unovan Mythology, are connected to the Wuji, or Void.

These things are all supposed to be together in order to form substance. If you approach something with Wuji, for instance, you'd probably not do it because you don't really care about it all that much, but assist because someone asked you to. If you approach something with Yang, you assist because you want to protect something specific from getting hurt. And with Yin, you assist because you have an obligation to do it for the better of the collective.

Um... I don't think this is really as distilled as I'd like it to be, but basically, Legendaries are connected to fundamentals are connected to people and vice versa. Do you understand that?
