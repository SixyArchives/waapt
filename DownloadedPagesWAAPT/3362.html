-I swing the cake left and right in front of Maekrite's eyes-

— It's cool... go and use it, why do you want me? You have clearance to ask for the reports...

Timbú — Yeah but, eh, I'm not really familiar with the new management, and things seem to be a bit crazy over there...

-rubs arms nervously, and watches as I pin down Maekrite and show him the cake-

-Maekrite's ears pick up-

— ...Oh! Must be Basket!

Timbú — Ba-Basket...?

— Right... — hand him the cake, trading it for the report — can you keep that one safe? I'll go check this stuff and call later to see what is going on there...

-Down the street and at the first and second floor, the squad of Ursaring, Machops, Haunter, Golduck and Pinsir help a couple Nidos hold the ground-

"Over there!"

"We got you surrounded, duck!"

"Damn it, shoot those things out of the sky!"

"Stop meeting the pavement, eel! Stop meeting the pavement, eel! Stop meeting the pavement, eel! Stop meeting the pavement, eel! ..."

"Oh please, those attacks revitalize me!"

"Eat thorns!"

"Ack! Haunter, look out~!"

-Maekrite and a couple other Pokémon including Aipom and Teddiursa have accompanied me to the higher floors by using the elevator shaft. We all carry some equipment as we can, with mostly the Teddiursa helping-

-I release Magneton, the only of my old Pokémon whom I have allowed to accompany me, and he blasts a door to the rooftop open-

Terio (ordering the other Nidos and Teddiursas around) — <Start clearing some room and leave the packages there!>

— Magneton, we are going to need a temporary power source until we can patch to the building's power trunk.

-I produce special equipment and wires to start hooking up to small antennas and heat-based long-range pointers around the rooftop, with the two more experienced Teddiursa helping-

"...The red or the green?"

"...I guess this green one goes with that red one..."

"Who's back holding the floor?"

-I stare at Maekrite, who nods and takes the three Aipom to the staircase-

Terio — <Can you explain again what you are trying to do?>

-I hook up all the wires to a special machine, where Magneton stands for a short while transferring some energy-

— Well, with what we gathered from PEFE we have determined that the reason the enemy seems to sneak and attack invisibly is because of those Zoroark, or homba as you call them... — points to the overall direction of the Gym — like the one who was running the Gym, but these ones can hide a lot of other Pokémon packed together in a "Illusion".

Teddiursa — <Yeah, it's like you see things that are not there... but Mom said that if one stood close enough one could still get their stench...>

Terio (tilts head) — <I see... and since they were flying, we didn't get them. But other Pokémon couldn't, either.>

— And that is where this equipment comes in. This building is the higher one in the city... unfortunately. — takes a random look at the Radio Tower.

Terio — <But we still can't see them from here if they are invisible... so, those things do... what?>

-Magneton does a thumb up (well, "magnet up") and I start turning on the devices-

— They broadcast at weird signal that we built as a filter at PEFE... long story short, this signal bounces on the energy trails left by Illusion, so techniques such as Detect and Miracle Eye can "see" the trails. But it also has a special function...

-The Teddiursa start pointing the devices to the streets as instructed-

— ...that I hope works soon enough. — to Terio — Go to the edge of the building and launch the prearranged signal when I give the notice.
