"You're just bored aren't you?" asked member A

"Yes, and I'm guessing you two are too?" asked Isuka

Both members stay silent.

"Good, so, I'll look ahead and you two seem if you can find something of use here. With any luck a ghost will jump at us"

Isuka sighed then started to move on to another area of the factory, while the other two stay behind.

"I don't think a ghost would be something exciting." said member A, rather pensive.

"With any luck she'll get lost. This way she won't be able to rope us into her antics." said member 1, looking annoyed

"But hey, at least it's better than staying at the HQ, wish there was something else to do here though."

Suddenly the two members hear a ringtone coming from behind one of the large cases around the place.

"... Oh for Arceus's sake!"

Shingeko then comes out from behind the case along with two Greninjas, Teishi and Sub-Zero, and holding a Pokégear which was the source of the sound.

"Not now!" said Shingeko, answering the call.

-Beat-

"Did you know the number I was calling was just one number away from yours? Me neither." said Hikari, through the communicator.

Shingeko was left completely speechless by that while Teishi and Sub-Zero seemed more amused than anything.

"Pfft, is that the hero people have been talking about?" mocked member A

"And here I was hoping for a ghost." said member 1, genuinely disappointed.

"Oh really? Because I think I'm much scarier." said Shingeko, taking what seemed to be a grenade from his belt.

"Whoa what?!" said both of them, taking steps back.

"Bombs away!"
