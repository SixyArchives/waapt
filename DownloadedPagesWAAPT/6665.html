Lavender Beach - PMD-B

A closer look at the coastal village had revealed that it was home to mostly members of the Cubone and Machop line, as well as whole lot of ghost types. There were a few other kinds of Pokémon too, but they were vastly outnumbered by the aforementioned species.

However, what came as a shock to Lake and Stratus was that some of the Pokémon living there didn't seem to be wearing any scarves...

Stratus: <Are some of those guys...ferals?>

Lake: <I don't know, they're not wearing scarves. Around here, that can't be taken to mean much else.>

Stratus: <Then why are they living in a town like this?>

Lake: <...I think it's the Cubone.>

Stratus: <Huh?>

Lake points to a group of Cubone off in the distance.

Lake: <Can't you see? They're got painted markings, which means...>

Stratus: <Oh! It's a Cubone tribe! Wait...>

Stratus blinks.

<...Are they part of Kat's tribe?>

Lake: <Could be. Depends if it's lasted this long. But I wouldn't see any reason why it wouldn't have...>

Cubone had a habit for being tenacious. Lake suspected that it would take more than the a draconic coup to get rid of a tribe. Especially considering Marowak like Iron Marrow.
