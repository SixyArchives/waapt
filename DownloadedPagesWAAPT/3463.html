So maybe a couple of days of happy rest, forgetting about all these things going on, and let them handle it...

But on the other hand... that lunatic has to be stopped. I have already seen Luke and Tagg, I know how they operate, and if push comes to shove they surely can take out Cyrus for good, but... peer pressure might prevent them from doing so. Magneton — <You better it's not, hmph.> — screeches.

-I pat Magneton on the main head and then we continue our way back-

But in this universe Shahinne doesn't even exist, and if we don't fix this, then she won't ever again.
