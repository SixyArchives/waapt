The Seal: This is you. You are of the People of the Land, and this is the Land of the People.

Zeta: What...?

The Seal: What is here is all that you may be. No more, no less.

| With that, the seal swirls once more, and forms a new pattern of delicate intricacy. Zeta questions it further, but gets no response.

| Left with more questions than answers, Zeta examines again the three floating objects.

| As she focuses on each object individually, it changes form.

| The spear becomes a sword, a shield, and a map.

| The veil becomes a scale, a gavel, and a key.

| The torch becomes a scroll, a bridge, and a home.

| Still more puzzled, Zeta begins to worry. She cannot remember how she arrived here, where here is, or how to leave. As her fear rises, the path leading east swells, claiming more and more of the desert around it. Finally, as it appears to occupy the entire quadrant, the world around her shatters. She falls into the shadows that lie at the edges of the world.
