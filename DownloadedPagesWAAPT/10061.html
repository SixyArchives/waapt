Shadow Wastes - Five years ago...

It was a dark and desolate land. The sky was completely blocked by black rolling clouds, the ground was ashen and dead.

There were no Pokémon, not even any plants. The only living creatures for miles around were a familliar looking Charmeleon, and a wise Green Dragonite, who had both arrived via portal just a few moments prior.

Charmeleon!Comet: <Jeez, what happened here?>

Lutalo the Shiny Dragonite: <What you see here, young one, is a land permanently scarred by the nightmarish creature known as Darkrai.>

Comet: <Oh, Darkrai. Yeah, we all know who he is.>

Lutalo: <Heh. I'm pleased that you're so well aware young one. It will surely help in the trail ahead.>

Comet turned to the Dragonite, looking rather confident.

Comet: <Hey, I already got through the Trial of Strength and the Trial of Knowledge. I bet this Trial of Courage you've got me on will be easy as pie!>

Lutalo couldn't help but chuckle at Comet's spunk.

Latalo: <Oh, Kindle! It's hard not to be cheered up by your confidence! I do have to warn you, though. You should never assume a battle is won even if victory appears certain. Complacency can be the downfall of even the strongest of Pokémon.>

Taking a glance at her surroundings, Comet nodded half-heartedly.

Comet: <I'll try and keep that in mind. So, what do you need me to do?>

Lutalo: <Oh, it's quite simple. All you have to do, is go into the wastes...>

The Dragonite smiled cheerfully.

<And face your fear!>

Not quite understanding, Comet replied with a confused look.

Comet: <...Eh?>

Lutalo: <That's all you have to do! Face your fear, and you'll be accepted into the Dream Guard! Easy, right?>

Comet: <It would be if I knew what you were talking about. What is "My Fear?">

Lutalo: <Oh, I would tell you...>

He smiled sneakily.

<But that would be telling! In all seriousness, though, it isn't that hard to find. Trust me, you'll know it when you see it.>

That still didn't really help. But given just how quirky Lutalo was, that was probably all the information she was going to get from him.

Comet: <Alright, sir. I'll go and take care of my fear. Should be back in a little while, alright?>

The Dragonite nodded to the Charmeleon.

Lutalo: <Best of luck then, Kindle.>

With that, the Charmeleon began to make her way into the foreboding land ahead.
