<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Uh... Eagerly commend you for your bravery?">

<Patrolling!Mienfoo #2: "Dibs on his neck feathers!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "W-Wait! Hold on! I-I'm just here to deliver a message!">

<Patrolling!Vanillish: "A likely sto-!">

<Patrolling!Mienshao: "... No wait, let him continue... Though you have some gall for presuming that you're welcome here...">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Er... Ah... It's on behalf of the Opal Roar? F-For the leaders of the dragonbloods here?">

<Patrolling!Vanillite: "Wait, what's this about an roar? All the Druddigon are busy wintering right now!">

<Patrolling!Mienfoo #1: "Psst, wait, is that the pizzeria that makes those deliveries?">

<Patrolling!Mienfoo #2: "No, you nimrod! It's those door to door salemen!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Those are both... Inaccurate..."> >_< >_< >_<

<Patrolling!Mienshao: "Mmph. You may proceed. How on earth your lot ever developed a tie with the shrine guards, I'll never know...">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "The Opal Roar predates your shrine! It's me that should be asking the questions of association here!"> >.< >.< >.<

A Chamber Within the Tower, Out of Earshot of a Certain Legendary Duo - A Short Time Later

-The Hydreigon is meeting with 3 of 7 elders of the guard base at Dragonspiral Tower who could probably use real names by now... Of the obvious species-

<Dragonite: "Mmph... I thought that we already made it known how we felt about clan dealings... We don't care what you do, and we expect the same from your end.">

<Druddigon #2: "What do they want to badger us about this time? I thought that we already made it clear that service to our lords is our foremost priority as a den!">

<Druddigon #1: "And yes, we asked our lords about aligning with the clan. We've been over this already, they said that they didn't have enough knowledge of its workings to make a decision!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Um... Yes... About that... Erm... You are aware that a pressing concern that has come up is the matter of what to do about the issue of the Vow, right?">

<Druddigon #2: "Do you think we'd know? When was the last time we even- Actually wait, when was the last time we sent someone to a clan meeting?"> ._.

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Well... To bring you up to speed...">

One Explanation Later

<Druddigon #1: "... What?">

<Dragonite: "You can't honestly expect the gods to stand for that! Those hatchetheads are courting their destruction!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Actually... Not so much... The gods have specifically decreed that the Vow dictates conduct between a mon and humans... And... With no humans...">

<Druddigon #2: "So we should inform our lords immediately-!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "What? No! No! The idea is perfectly sound, it's just the implementation that's lacking?">

<Dragonite: "Removing humans from existence is a perfectly sound idea? How on earth would that even work without mass slaughter?">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Don't ask me, I just know that the only casualties of whatever is supposed to happen are the bodies of humans... Not necessarily their lives...">

<Druddigon #1: "But Icirrus is a tributary! If anything befell it, it would be our duty to protect it!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "So... Surely its within your power to deal with your tributary as you see fit, yes...?">

<Dragonite: "... Wait, you want us to dip our claws into this matter?">

<Druddigon #2: "You're out of your mind! What possible benefits could we possibly have for-?!">

-And a commotion comes from downstairs-

<Druddigon #1: "What the-?">

-And the form of a Ruin Maniac being pursued by a group of irate tower guards down a bridge towards the forest can be seen-

Ruin Maniac: "Ack! Come on, that wasn't even a really big piece!"

<Irate!Druddigon #1: "This is a shrine! Not a gift shop!">

<Irate!Druddigon #2: "Give the rock back, you vandal!">

-From the vantage point-

<Dragonite + Druddigon #1 & #2: >.< >

<Druddigon #1: "I never thought that there would actually get worse after that devil cat and insect were deposed..."> >_>

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Well, that's one thing that could change... As would be the issue of imbecile humans pulling your numbers abruptly from their duties with their slave balls... And your lot is already highly respected in that settlement, no? Why, were its residents not humans, perhaps you would be better able to communicate what it is that you desire of-">

<Druddigon #2: "Okay, so there are some benefits, we get it...">

<Dragonite: "We would still prefer not to dip our claws into this matter. Our duty is first and foremost service to our lords. And it is our duty to ensure that as elders...">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "But I just-">

<Druddigon #1: "Perhaps if the guards were more knowledgeable of this development, they would have an opinion on the matter...">

-Thinking-

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Aha! We could work with that!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "So... We could inform them of the matter, then?">

-Looking amongst each other-

<Druddigon #1: "... I don't see why not...">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "I see. I will relay the message to the others of the Opal Roar.">

-And bows before flying off-

<Dragonite: "... So... Should we tell our lords about this matter?">

<Druddigon #1: "... Wintering doesn't end in earnest until the 3rd moon. It's not as if they could coax our guards out before then if they tried... We'll inform our lords of the matter before then...

In the meantime... Perhaps it would serve as a diversion for the guards' fancy? They do tend to get rather restless during warmer spells in the winter.">

<Druddigon #2: "... I'm not sure if this is a good idea... But... Hey, it is two months we have to work with... And giving the clan what they want for a while should get them off our back as a den...

Right?">
