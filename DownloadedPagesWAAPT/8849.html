<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Well... To bring you up to speed...">

One Explanation Later

<Druddigon #1: "... What?">

<Dragonite: "You can't honestly expect the gods to stand for that! Those hatchetheads are courting their destruction!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Actually... Not so much... The gods have specifically decreed that the Vow dictates conduct between a mon and humans... And... With no humans...">

<Druddigon #2: "So we should inform our lords immediately-!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "What? No! No! The idea is perfectly sound, it's just the implementation that's lacking?">

<Dragonite: "Removing humans from existence is a perfectly sound idea? How on earth would that even work without mass slaughter?">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "Don't ask me, I just know that the only casualties of whatever is supposed to happen are the bodies of humans... Not necessarily their lives...">

<Druddigon #1: "But Icirrus is a tributary! If anything befell it, it would be our duty to protect it!">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "So... Surely its within your power to deal with your tributary as you see fit, yes...?">

<Dragonite: "... Wait, you want us to dip our claws into this matter?">

<Druddigon #2: "You're out of your mind! What possible benefits could we possibly have for-?!">

-And a commotion comes from downstairs-

<Druddigon #1: "What the-?">

-And the form of a Ruin Maniac being pursued by a group of irate tower guards down a bridge towards the forest can be seen-

<Irate!Druddigon #1: "This is a shrine! Not a gift shop!">

<Irate!Druddigon #2: "Give the rock back, you vandal!">

And your lot is already highly respected in that settlement, no? Why, were its residents not humans, perhaps you would be better able to communicate what it is that you desire of-">

<Druddigon #2: "Okay, so there are some benefits, we get it...">

<Dragonite: "We would still prefer not to dip our claws into this matter. Our duty is first and foremost service to our lords. And it is our duty to ensure that as elders...">

<Messenger!Hydreigon: "But I just-">

<Druddigon #1: "Perhaps if the guards were more knowledgeable of this development, they would have an opinion on the matter...">

-Thinking-

<Dragonite: "... So... Should we tell our lords about this matter?">

<Druddigon #1: "... Wintering doesn't end in earnest until the 3rd moon. It's not as if they could coax our guards out before then if they tried... We'll inform our lords of the matter before then...

In the meantime... Perhaps it would serve as a diversion for the guards' fancy? They do tend to get rather restless during warmer spells in the winter.">

<Druddigon #2: "... I'm not sure if this is a good idea... But... Hey, it is two months we have to work with... And giving the clan what they want for a while should get them off our back as a den...
