-Maekrite smiles-

Shahinne — <Oh I have a dad and an aunt and a Trainer...> — turns to Maekrite — <Teammate? What does that mean?>

-Shahinne hops her way behind Maekrite and I decide to step in and pick her up-

Maekrite — <Thanks, u Frithrah. And, I know you said you didn't like all this reverence, but... for us Nidorans you mean too much.>

-steps back, and the two of us stay side to side-

— Whatever you did, Maekrite, well done.

Maekrite — <Thanks...>

[AU] Bell Tower

-Maekrite steps forward and bows; I feel urged to do the same-

????!Maekrite — <I think I do, u Frithrah. For generations, our Colony has felt the world slipping to an end as we longed for that light that brought us happiness...>

-I move near Maekrite and try to explain what I have come to understand-

— ...We didn't know that the Legendaries were in such great distress in this world, Great Ho-Oh, until our presences from that other timeline came to us... And now... I see that Maekrite has...

-Maekrite turns to me, then back to Ho-Oh-

????!Maekrite — <But now You are free, and I have been compelled by a higher voice to come seeking you... come seeking our Morning Sun.>

— ...Morning Sun?

-Ho-Oh leans down to us, His shadow cast on Maekrite-

Ho-Oh — <Yes, you have come to us for this power, as once an ancestor of yours did... when the City didn't exist and even that forest were young...>

-Ho-Oh spreads his wings wide, and summons the power of the Sun, even in this late time when the star is already gone past the horizon-

Ho-Oh — <I shall impart upon you the knowledge of your ancestor! The power of the Sun, unfettered and unfiltered! Use it well!>

-from the skies to the west a bright spark of yellow light emerges, turning the young night back into day; then, a shaft of light emerges from it and descends upon Maekrite-

— ...This...!

-I have to cover my eyes, but I can distinguish Maekrite's body patterns changing ever so slightly as the sunlight covers him. Maekrite's body changes, his spots becoming darker and more well defined, but that's only what human senses can perceive, as there are more changes being brought upon him as he is infused by the divine power-

—Maekrite has become Maekrite☼.EX
