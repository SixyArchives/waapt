-Maekrite turns to me, then back to Ho-Oh-

-Ho-Oh leans down to us, His shadow cast on Maekrite-

Ho-Oh — <I shall impart upon you the knowledge of your ancestor! The power of the Sun, unfettered and unfiltered! Use it well!>
