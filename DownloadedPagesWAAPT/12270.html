Somewhere

A door opened into girl's bathroom. Music from outside temporarily filled the room before the door was quickly closed. A girl wearing a mask rubbed her temples.

"Ugh, what a party..."

She removed her mask and ran a hand through her brown hair while looking into the mirror. She popped a stick of gum in her mouth to remove the smell of beer off her breath, then splashed some water on her face. A chilly breeze from an open window made her shiver.

"Who was the frigger that left that window open?"

She walked over and began to close it. Before it shut completely a piece of paper flew in. Curiously she picked it up and looked at it.

" I trust it with you? The fuck?"

Without a second thought she crumbled it up and threw it in the garbage. As she turned to look in the mirror again she yelped and jumped back a little.

There was something wrong with her reflection.

One eye, normally brown, was now a bright glowing green.
