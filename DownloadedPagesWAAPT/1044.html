A Forum Game is a place where you play unorganized games where anyone can join in and they're not locked out of any continuity, because there is no continuity. Games like "The Two Word Story" or "Vandalize the Above Troper's Page" are Forum Games. An RP is a place where regular posters can participate in a storyline that has continuity and established rules, like "We Are At a Teen Sleepover" or "You are in the Industry of Supervillainy". We're more of an RP than a Forum Game.
