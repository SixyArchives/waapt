Space Tunnels - PMD-B

The rather large group of Pokémon has spent the past few hours working their way down a seemingly endless Space Tunnel. Having seen no reason to stay in what was formally Celadon City, they've decided to venture through the tunnels in an attempt to explore parts unknown.

That said, not everymon is so happy about this.

Stratus: <How much further is it? My feet are starting to hurt!>

Lake can't give the Dragonite anything other than a shrug in response.

Lake: <I wish I knew, Stratus. I really do. This isn't much fun for me either.>

In all honestly, Lake was beginning to get sick of caves. It seemed that ever since he had started hopping between these two universe, he had seen nothing but caves, caves, and yet more caves.

Why could there have been more water? That would have been nice. For him and Stratus, at least.

Perhaps once this was all over, he could spend some time in the Orange Islands on a little vacation of sorts. Preferably with Nadia. That would be wonderful...
