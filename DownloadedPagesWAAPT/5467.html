Mauville Underground, Four Minutes Ago

Two Weezing let out a smokescreen then a Roselia fires some Razor Leaves into it, hoping to knock out more LEOs.

????'s Operative: "Come on come on! Can't you detach the machines yet?"

????'s Operative -comes back from another room: "We can't! They're charged and operational! And there are two Tango Eights coming in here by another corridor holding us back!"

A noise is heard coming from the corridor, prompting the first splinter operative to command the second one to return to the engines room. Then the first operative reequips some night vision glasses and special equipment and detects a figure trying to sneak into the corridors.

????'s Operative -to the Roselia and a Manectric: "Spot on! Left side, two clicks! Go go go!"

The two Pokémon enter the smokescreen charging their elemental powers; after about a full minute, some attacks can be heard.

????'s Operative: "...Uh? ...Ice Beam? None of our mons..."

He is interrupted by the Roselia being knocked back by an Ice Beam, kneeling and fainting. Before the operative can move for a recall, Manectric is tossed back from the corridor as well, a large cut going from his shoulder to the flank.

????'s Another Operative: "...What the- Damn. You and you! Go go go!" — signals to an Electrode and Carvanha.

The two Pokémon enter the smokescreen carefully. Some seconds later, more battle sounds can be heard, and a Pokémon is harshly pressed against the walls. Seeing something, the operative ducks to avoid a barrage of Poison Stings, and when he wakes back up he manages to see the faint image of Carvanha lying on the ground, fainted. Electrode can be heard yelping in pain as well beyond the smoke curtain.

Another shadow appears walking through the corridor and after a moment Maekrite reveals himself, equipped with a military armor, an Eviolite and Pankaja's tracker-earring. Three more Pokémon rush into the corridor only to be soundly defeated, while without Maekrite noticing, an Electrodes is sent into the corridor as well for a Selfdestruct.

A large explosion covers the corridor and the grunt lets out a "Yes!" only to notice the Nidorino walking out of the smoke; the Pokémon fires a Thunderbolt at the grunt, knocking him unconscious to the ground.

The corridor almost cleared, Maekrite turns tail and runs back at the group, only to notice Casval and Wally there. Well, mostly the apprentice, who comes out from nowhere and runs at him.

Maekrite: <...Eeeeee~> — hops back — <Y-you sneaked upon me!>

Silent appears there as well checking the radio and the underground schematics.

"...Casval? Wally? Good to see you are fine." — holds off the radio — "Did you meet much opposition?"
