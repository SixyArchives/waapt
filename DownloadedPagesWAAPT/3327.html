-The breeders turn to me, I turn to Taisho-

Breeder #1 — <Sounds fine. The other branch will move troops across Rt.35 and Rt.32 to force the enemy to react, then engage in a debilitation combat, starting Fu-Inlé.>

Breeder #4 (to the messengers) — <All of you will be on watch for when Frithvaorah and his friends make presence too, and support them in their attack. Make sure he tells you where will that big... thing, be attacking, so that we don't risk getting in the way.>

Mayonaka — <You all have your assignments. Now move, for the Colony!>

-I pet her behind the ears-
