PEFE Library

Lifealope walks into the expansive library holding a pot of goulash for Satine in his jaw. He trots around the bookcases and winding hallways looking for his ghostly girlfriend.

Lifealope: {I am worried for Satine. Ever since she's found those Ranseian scrolls, all she's been practically (un)living here since. Though, I know how much this means to her, so I'll be supportive any way I can}

Lifealope finally finds Satine floating over a rather large table, in which she is reading through various scrolls, books and tomes that she pushes aside once read. Lifealope puts the pot on a side table and goes up next to Satine.

Satine: {No no no! I've been at this for months, and I have found little to no records of this war in Ransei, or of Ignisean politics during this time! I'm starting to loose hope in this cause...}

Lifealope: -Pats Satine on the back- {Don't get so worked up about this dear, it's not good for your health. Let me make a suggestion, why don't we both take a break and lets enjoy some vegetarian goulash together. Does that sound nice Satine?}

Satine sighs and looks over to her boyfriend.

Satine: {...A break does sound lovely Lifealope.}

Lifealope: {Then it's settled.}

Lifealope pours the soup into two separate bowls and starts eating it slowly. While the two enjoy their lunch in the library. A large tome falls off the desk and lands on the floor with a loud *ker-thunk*.

Lifealope: {What was that?}

Satine: {Oh, one of the tomes that I thought looked promising. It was located alongside the records of Ransei, but I couldn't read it}

Lifealope: -Swallows- {You? Couldn't read it? This is the first time I've seen you not being able to decipher a source Satine.}

Satine: {...Well, I never learned the Kalosian language...}

Lifealope tilts his head.

Lifealope: Kalosian? I could help you there.

Satine: -Perks up- {You can?}

Lifealope: {Yes... My cousin lives in Kalos, remember? I visited him not to long ago.}

Lifealope totters over to the book and reads the title to Satine.

{Lifealope}: {Ahem! The title reads "An outsiders view on the inner workings of Ignis and the Great Ranseian War: 1413-14(writing illegible)", Gaston de Callone.}

Satine, hearing this, picks up Lifealope and gives him a big, bone crushing hug.

Satine: {EUREKA! THIS IS EXACTLY THE KIND OF SOURCE I'M LOOKING FOR! OH LIFEY, YOU'RE THE BEST BOYFRIEND A GHOST COULD EVER HAVE~!}

Lifealope: {... No worries...o //0 //o}

After the hug, Satine places Lifealope down, gets out a pen and paper and asks Lifealope a question.

Satine: {...Ahem. Well Lifey... can you read it too me?}

Lifealope: {...Of course Satine.}

Lifealope opens the first page of the tome, takes a deep breath and starts reading.

Lifealope: { My story begins in the beginning of the 15th century...}

600 years ago.

A young man, dressed in the Kalosian fashion of a shirt, doublet, hose, overgown and chaperon, is writing on a piece of parchment as his gallery sails towards the port of the Ranseian nation at Ignis. By his side lies a Raticate with deep red fur who is snoozing in the midday sun.

Gaston: I was a young man of 18 when I decided to take on this perilous journey to the east. My father, a lord back in the old country, had decided he wanted to open up trade routes to the nation of Ransei for the exotic goods that the nation could provide, such as silks, teas and various rare Pokemon not often seen in Kalos. It just so happened to be, whether through pure luck or coincidence, that the Ranseian nation of Ignis was interested in trading with the West for the goods we could provide. A departure from other Ranseian nations who seem to have adopted a policy of Isolationism.

The Raticate by his side turns over and emits a loud groaning sound. Gaston stops his writing and looks over to the rat Pokemon, scratching his belly.

Gaston: My journey to the east was made alone. The only companions I had on this journey were my ship crew, loyal to my family down to the last bone, and Charlemagne, my dearest friend. He is as loyal as any companion, and I will be deeply saddened when we eventually separate, as Raticate don't exactly have the longest lifespans.

Gatson is interrupted in his writings by a large sailor.

Ship's Captian: Sir, we are about to dock in the Ignis port.

Gatson: We are? How time must fly when you are recording events. Charlemagne, prepare to land.

The Raticate makes a squeaking sound of delight. He seems happy to finally be going back to land soon.

Gaston: -Resuming writings- I wonder what the people of Ignis are like? I've heard many a tale back home that Ignis was ruled by an insane tyrant. One who threatened death on all those that spoke out to him or questioned him, and who has a wife that is cold as the ice of winter. I chalk up these rumors to be nothing more that Kantonese Whispers, but even the silliest of rumors have some sort of grain of truth in them. Anyway, I will find out later, as the esteemed guest of the Ignisean warlord, Ryuku Atakashi.

Captian: We've docked sir.

Gatson: Very well. Charlemagne, come!

The Raticate quickly jumps off the seat he was lounging on and joins his masters side.

Gaston: Let's see if we can reason with this "Tyrant of Ignis".

Little did I know that things were about to get... complicated.
