164713 MindGamer5th Jun 2012 07:26:38 PM from In your mind. Obviously. Get RP Mod
Help... My hand's stuck.
OU, Hoenn, Mossdeep City

With a crunching sound of one foot over the other across the sand, the now moist shoes of a trainer kicked a few times to finally get to shore. The cheerful cries of the trainer's Pokemon sung out over the waves as the young man in question spat out a mouthful of sea water. Evidently, Surf was not a good way to get around when the only Mon that could use it for you was the size of a basketball.

Spe, however, didn't mind. Rolling around in the moist sand with a wide smile on it's face, the little Ice/Water type was perfectly content where he was, not noticing it's trainer's plight. Pushing back his pitch black hair from his hazel eyes, the trainer began to struggle to his feet, wiping the coarse and drying sand across his now soaked and faded coat. With a sigh, the young man picked off a small ball from his belt, enlarged it, and released his second Pokemon... A little bird, burning with excitement and already hopping around gazing in wonder at the world. Tori, a little Torchic with eyes burning bright, was about to join the white-and-blue Spheal already out in running about and digging in the sand, until her trainer coughed out the last of the seawater from his lungs, and finally spoke.

"Tori... I could use some drying off, if you please."

With a carefully controlled blazing stream, the little Fire-type began to warm up her trainer's body and dry out his soaked form, the trainer letting out a relieved sigh as the water appeared to not enter his numerous packs. With one quick motion, as Tori's aide finally ceased, the trainer gave the little bird and seal a smile in thanks on a job well done, before releasing his two other pokeballs.

From one, the silent, composed form of a spider lept forth, quickly getting a grasp of the nearby area and keeping track of it's surroundings, lest losing one of it's teammates. Rak typically needed to keep Spe from wandering off, as the little Spinarak had gotten used to doing. From the other ball, the little pink form of a smiling Happiny landed in it's trainer's arms, crying a happy shout at the nice sea breeze on it's face, and waving at it's fellow mons. Nina had the least experience of anyone in the team, but certainly the most enthusiasm, in the end. With squinted eyes, the little pink blob looked up at it's trainer once more.

Standing tall, the young trainer breathed out a sigh. Everyone was alright. The team had made it this far... And for Ken, he felt the journey had been far too long coming. Holding Nina in one arm, he opened up one of his satchels to take out a black bandana, and gently letting Nina down into Rak's care for a minute, wrapped the bandana around his head with a small, controlled smile. He'd made it all the way to Mossdeep from Lavaridge, and was exceptionally proud of that fact. The sea breeze once again blowing in his hair, the young man felt ready to take on the world... That was, until a glint appeared in the corner of his eyes, a little shard Spe and Tori had found poking out of a hidden little hole.

Hopefully this was enough. I know it's not the standard format, but I wanted to make it decent in at least giving an image of Ken.
