| His fist bursts into flames, using Fire-Punch. He leaps forward, and punches the door down.

*BANG*

| Suddenly, he is greeted by an array of moves flying at him.

<Del: WHOAH!>

| Thinking fast, he uses Shadow-Sneak, melting into the many shadows.

<Humon!Quagsire: Did we get it?>

<Humon!Pidove: OH NO! IT'S THE DRAGONS! THEY'VE FOUND US! RUN! RUN!>

| Immediately, a panic breaks out among the assembled Pokemon.

| Finally, an enraged voice cuts right through the chaos.

<???:    SHUT IT! ALL OF YOU!   

| They all quite down, and step aside, revealing a Spiritomb.

<???: Now, what's all this about the Dragons having found us?>

| His voice is aged, and cantankerous.

<Resident!Litwick: Elder Taep! Sire! There was an incursion. An unknown Pokemon has entered the House.>

<Elder Taep: I see. And this led you all to assume that these dreaded Dragons have found us? For shame. I thought you all were better beings than that.>

<Elder Taep: Furthermore, unless my old eyes are deceiving me, your supposed "Dragon" is sitting over there, in the shadows.>

| Grinning wide enough to almost split his face in half, Del steps out into the dim light of the House.

<Del: Del knocked, but no one answered!>

<Skully: Yeah! Real rude of you guys.>

| This garners a few stares, but most are too worried about imminent attack to pay it much heed.

| Taep, meanwhile, seems to take it all in stride.

<Elder Taep: I apologize. We've been on edge ever since the bombing. We had to take in all of these refugees, you see.>

<Skully: Well, that is understandable enough. May we stay with you all for a while?>

<Elder Taep: I don't see why not.>

| A furious Banette speaks up.

<Gilt: Hey! We don't know him! He could be a spy for those Dragons.>

| The crowd gives a murmur, agreeing with him.

<Elder Taep: Fool! Hospitality has always been a vital tradition, in these times more than any other. We took in these refugees, didn't we?>

| This quiets down the crowd, the majority of which are said refugees.

<Gilt: This isn't over.>

<Elder Taep: Oh yes, it is. Now get out of my sight.>

| He turns back to Del.

<Elder Taep: I apologize for Gilt's behavior. He's been jockeying for power for a while, and he's seen your presence as an opportunity to make a gain. In any case, welcome to the House on the Rock, colloquially known as the Strange House.>

<Del: Thank you, sir!>

| They wander off deeper into the house, as the crowd disperses.
