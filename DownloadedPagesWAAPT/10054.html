Route 14

Lyuri had Alden teleport her and Duke there and just called him back to his Pokéball, the two then lie in the grass one one right next to the other in the clearing of a forest in the route, trying to be alone with each other.

"Watching the starry night sky, it's quite inspiring actually." said Duke, looking at the sky with a look of minor admiration.

"Heh, yeah, it is." said Lyuri, smiling as she also looked at the sky.

"... You know, I think everybody will hate me after they find out that I'm a Lucario." commented Duke, with a small chuckle.

"If you think that way, why don't you reveal it sooner?" asked Lyuri, now starting to get rather concerned

"Because I don't want others to look at you, or me for that matter, weirdly just yet, I want us to be able to enjoy this time we have together until it becomes clear for the others that I'm not human." answered Duke, now looking at Lyuri with a small smile.

"Figures it was because of me, I really don't deserve you, even more when you're willing to have some start hating you just to make me happy, I'm really glad I got to meet you." said Lyuri, with a sincere smile.

"I'm willing to do many things that will harm me to keep you like that, Lyuri." said Duke, still smiling at her.

Lyuri then starts resting her head on Duke's chest as she kept looking at the stars "So... Are you enjoying this?"

"Sure am."

"Hmm... I'm thinking of maybe putting a dress for next time, have a bit of variety." said Lyuri, with a few chuckles.

"Well, I liked that dress you were using at the festival." commented Duke, smiling.

"You did? Then I guess I'll use it next time."

"Sure, what should I wear then?"

"Eh, your normal clothes are good enough, you look rather cool on them, so I like it." Lyuri then started giggling a bit.

"All right then... By the way, I think this is the first time I heard you giggling, it's quite surprising actually."

"Is it? Good to know..." Lyuri then gives a small yawn "Looking at the sky like that is really relaxing, don't you think?"

"It is, sure is a great way to spend the rest of the night after the fun chat we had with each other while deciding what to do and our... Moment."

"Yeah, that's true, funny, we were thinking of how to have a date together, but it was while we were brainstorming that we started said date, this is a good ending to it at least."

The two then get silent for a while as they kept looking at the stars and seeing if they could see any constellations on it.

"My boyfriend is a Pokémon, but he's probably the best boyfriend I'll ever have, even if him going back to Lucario form may make things awkward, I'm gonna try my hardest to deal with it, because it's definitely more than worth it to endure some flak thrown at me if it means I can stay with someone like him" thought Lyuri, as she soon fell asleep on Duke.

Duke then noticed that Lyuri had fallen asleep and just smiled at it "I guess we're either staying here, or I have to carry her all the way to the nearby Pokémon Center, I'm fine with either really, it's been a while since I've slept outdoors, sure is nice to do so every once in a while, even more if I can stay with her by my side." he thought.

Not too long after it, Duke also fell asleep and the couple remained in the forest, sleeping peacefully and looking forward to the next day.
