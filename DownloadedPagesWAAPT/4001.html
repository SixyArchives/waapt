— Yes, Ichtacya. And that's the first of his [three] tricks, you already saw the second. Surely you are going to learn to use your attacks in different ways, too.

-Ichtacya smiles, rests her head on my lap and purrs for a moment-

— So we are going to take these Gym challenges, they are pretty [novel] in their format, you see... and on the road you are all going to learn to battle in different effective ways.

Ichtacya — <Do you think one day we can do something that awesome? Help save the world too?>

For the most part we have been mostly spectators of the world. When the tides of great changes come, one man and his team can not do much to either resist or promote such changes.'

-We look at the world around us. It is all peaceful with people minding their own business. Not even the Pokémon I have in my team can figure that they were [perhaps] suffering very different fates a little more than one month ago.-

Ichtacya — <I mean... That would be an impressive [tale] to tell back at home...>

I was there before. To be honest, I would rather not ask my team to follow me to something like that... then again... -thinks of Pokéteco and to the then non-existant Shahinne- Perhaps it doesn't matter if I should or not... I guess it is like that for the other guys too...

At least I know who I *won't* have to ask... I'm gonna compensate all your sacrifices, Maekrite. I am going to try hard. Can't be that bad, I have to learn to swim sometime...
