Herbert: ...Alice, I know that being all aloof and uptight is kind of your thing, but seriously, are you okay? You've been really cagey ever since we finished dealing with Cipher.

-Alice subconsciously raises her hand to her still-bandaged shoulder.-

Alice: I am perfectly fine, Lucius. I have simply seen no point in seeking out human company, considering how my last attempt to bond with a member with a member of your group ended.

Herbert: Wait... did something happen between you and Lanie?

-Alice gives a small nod.-

Herbert: ...I'll understand if you don't want to talk about it, but... just remember, I'm always here if you ever need somebody to listen.

Alice: ...Suffice it to say that she elected to place her trust in Cipher rather than those that I thought she considered to be friends.

Herbert: ...Oh.

-He falls silent for several moments, unsure of what to say, before putting his arm around Alice.-

Herbert: Listen... I know how it feels to be betrayed by someone you considered to be a friend. It hurts. You just want to curl up into a ball and try to block out the entire world in case it tries to hurt you like that again. But... eventually, you have to move on. To learn how to trust people again. I'm not saying that you have to forgive, and it's impossible to forget, but what's the point of going through life alone?

-Alice is silent for a long moment, still looking away, before eventually turning back to Herbert.-

Alice: ...I'm not alone, though. I have you.

Herbert: Still, it wouldn't hurt to go out and talk to other people. Trust me, you have no idea how much it helps.

Alice: I... I'll consider it, then.

-Herbert gives a small smile.-

Herbert: Good. Glad to hear it.

-He turns his gaze over to the waterfront, letting go of Alice as he does.-

Herbert: So you keep saying. But we made a pretty good team during the four years that we travelled together for.

Herbert: Hmph. Technicalities.

-There is a long moment of silence before Alice speaks up again.-

Alice: Out of curiosity, is there any reason you brought me to Virbank in particular?

Alice: ...

Herbert: ...
