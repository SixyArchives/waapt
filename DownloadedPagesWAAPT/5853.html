A Suburb of Veilstone, a couple years ago

Eee! There's Ashley! I found here! And a guy!

Um, that's not Hunter...

Of course not, we're too far back for that. But we have one of the two, and that means one thing: it's Narration time!

A younger Ashley is standing by a tree, watching a Nidorino Ion and a Pidgeotto Cole sparring. She has her book open to the page on the Nidoran line, and is scribbling furiously. A boy, about her age, carrying his own book with a Golbat perched on his shoulder.

"Hi!" he says cheerfully as he comes nearer.

"Bat, " his Golbat adds, alighting from the boy's shoulder and gliding over to Cole and Ion, who stop to say hi.

"Oh, hi, " Ashley says, looking up from scribbling notes. "Who are you?"

"Jonathan, but call me Jon. Three syllables is way too long for a name." He offers a hand, which Ashley shakes after shifting her book to one arm. "So what brings you out here?"

Ashley chuckles and gestures towards her house, mostly hidden by trees. "I live here; Ashley Gatlocke, pleasure to meet you."

Jon laughs. "Wow, I can't believe I missed that! Sure makes me look like an idiot. So, are those your Pokemon?"

Ashley nods, smiling proudly. "You bet. The Pidgeotto's Cole and the Nidorino's Ion. What's your Golbat's name?"

"Zack, " Jon says, smiling fondly as he watches the Golbat perch on Ion's back while the Nidorino runs in circles. "So, that's an interesting book you have there."

"Oh, you think?" Ashley glances down at her book, which has flipped to a page depicting an Oddish when it was moved. "It was a present from my dad; I'm trying to take notes on every Pokemon and plant in here."

"Really? Can I see?" Jon eagerly holds out a hand, and Ashley places the book in it. The boy studies the page closely, a confused look slowly clouding his face.

"Smells... pretty? These are your notes on Oddish?"

"Well, yeah. They smell really nice, " Ashley says, also looking confused. "Is there something wrong?"

"These don't sound very... scientific, " Jon says after a moment of thought.

Ashley shrugs. "It's not like they're supposed to be, right?" she asks, holding out a hand for her book back. Jon reluctantly hands it over. "After all, I'm onl doing it for myself."

"But what if you find out something new? Don't you want people to see?" he asks.

"If I find something new, obviously someone else will discover it, " Ashley says, turning the page back to Nidorino.

Jon blinks, looking dumbfounded. "But that's not how research works, " he protests.

"Don't you think you're overreacting? Anyway, I gotta go. I have to help out my mom at the pet shop in a little bit. It was nice meeting you." Ashley waves as she walks off, whistling for Cole and Ion to join her. Zack flies back to Jon and lands lightly on his shoulder.

The boy shakes his head and walks off, muttering.

Okay, Clicker, bring us forward.
