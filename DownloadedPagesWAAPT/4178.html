 104427 Crewe, Sat, 22nd Oct '11 4:31:47 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
Crewe receives the text on the way out of the station.

"Oh. I guess it doesn't matter that we left." She shrugs and continues following Hecate out of the station.

Once outside, they're immediately greeted by Masque teleporting in front of them.

<Mace has found him, > he says simply. <Come.>

He teleports Crewe and Hecate to a tree outside the city, where Mace stands besides a Liepard.

"Razor?" Crewe asks, looking at the Liepard.

<Yeah. Haven't heard that name for a while.> Razor looks somewhat awkward. <Uh, sorry for running away, I guess.>

"Oh, it's no problem. So, what've you been up to?"

Mace clears his throat before Razor can respond. <Crewe, I'd like... to ask you something.>

Crewe raised on eyebrow. "Mace, if you're asking if Razor can come with us, it's up to him."

Mace shakes his head urgently. <No, I don't mean that at all. I'd like to ask... I want... I want to stay here with Razor.> He looks away, not meeting Crewe's eyes.

Crewe stares at him, then at Razor, who looks at the ground, then at Masque, who's face is completely impassive.

"If... If that's what you want, Mace... Of course you can stay here... if you want..." She looks down at her hands.

<Oh! Thank you! Thank you so much!> Mace immediately brightens. <Tell Sammy good-bye for me!> He pauses, then adds, <I'll miss you.>

"I'll miss you too, Mace." Crewe attempts a smile. "Have fun."

Mace smiles and waves, then he and Razor turn and walk away. Crewe pulls out his Pokeball from her bag, drops it on the ground, and crushes it under her foot. She looks at Masque.

"Take us back, " she says calmly. The Alakazam studies her for a moment, then nods and a moment later the three are standing back on the island. Crewe turns and walks away from them, leaving Masque and Hecate to return to their teams.
