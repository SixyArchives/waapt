Holon City

Grant and Allen are at a cafe together, chatting.

"So you guys at PEFE were robbed I see..." said Grant, rather concerned.

"Yes, I was caught off-guard."

Grant stops to think for a bit, before deciding to drop the subject "Anyway, you seem to know Sakura from even before meeting Hikari, I don't quite remember seeing you anywhere though."

"It's been a while I guess, but Sakura caught me treating my Pokémon badly. I was rather frustated at the time and I took it all on my Pokémon, she's a Pokémon Ranger, so she did her job and I lost my trainer card thanks to her." he explained, with a sigh.

"Oh, now I remember..." said Grant, not really liking where that conversation was going "So... You have a grudge against her?"

Allen shakes his head "Grudge? No, I was being a fool and she snapped me out of it, I'm quite thankful for that, I just can't help but remember it when I see her though and I prefer to forget it."

Grant then looked rather relieved "Well, that's nice."

Allen started to ponder something, before looking at Grant curiously "So... It's kinda odd to be talking with a Were-Arcanine so... What about your relationship with that girl? How close are you two?"

"Were-Arcanine? You're really going to call me that? Well... Whatever." said Grant, shrugging and chuckling "Anyway... One might say we're like father and daughter, in fact somebody actually mistook me for her father like this."

"I see... But what about her as a trainer?"

"She was more like my owner than my trainer if you get what I mean. I spent most of my life as her pet. Not that I'm complaining though..."

Years Ago

A child Sakura and Grant, still a Growlithe, arrived at the Floaroma Meadow and were just walking around it. Sakura was eating some cookies her mother had made for her as she walked

"Hmmm..." said Sakura, thinking of what to do, she then has an idea and takes one of the cookies and shows it to Grant with one hand while lowering her other hand "Sit!"

Grant then does as said, looking quite eager.

Sakura then lowers her free hand even more "Lie down!"

Again Grant does as ordered.

Sakura then spins her finger horizontally "Roll!"

Once again Grant does as ordered.

"Good boy~" said Sakura, sitting down on the grass and petting Grant with her free hand before showing him the cookie again "You want it?"

Grant nods excitedly.

"Then come here and get it!" said Sakura, waving the cookie

He nods again and glomps Sakura, who catches him while giggling and gives him the cookie to eat "Good boy~ So cute~" she said petting him as he eats.

Grant then nuzzles Sakura and she spends most of her day ordering him to do tricks to her or just playing tag with him the best she could.

Holon City

"I... See." said Allen, rather unsure of what else to say.

"She was a pretty good kid, I played around with her and her sister all day and even got cookies out of the whole ordeal." said Grant, jokingly.

"But how did that develop into a father-and-daughter relationship?" asked Allen, trying to change the subject as soon as possible.

"She lost her parents a few years ago in an accident, her sister went missing too, you can tell that she was devastated by it. Seeing the usually cheerful and upbeat girl be reduced to a sobbing wreck hurt me more than anything else, I tried to comfort her but it wasn't until Hikari appeared that she calmed down, ever since that day I try to keep her smiling and safe, it's the least I can do for her after she took care of me for such a long time." he explained "Besides, someone has to keep that girl out of trouble." he added, with a playful shrug.

Allen gave a single chuckle at the last bit "It must've been terrible for her to have to go through that kind of thing, especially since she must've been quite young when it happened."

"Yeah. I'm glad she hasn't changed much though, she's still the girl that has been with me since I was born and just that is enough to make me feel happy, that and being able to relax." he then gives a rather curious look to Allen "What about your family?"

That question was seemingly enough to break Allen's composure as he looked rather hurt just by it, he gave a sigh and soon he regained his composure "My parents are divorced, my sister lives with my mother in Jubilife City and I live in Black City with my dad, my step-mother died some time ago and I don't have any step-brothers or sisters." he explained, trying to keep a straight face, but it was clear that he was also rather sad.

"Oh... Sorry about that... At least you have your father, right?" said Grant, trying to cheer him up.

"Yeah... My dad." said Allen, looking down and not even trying to keep a straight face anymore, he remained that way for a few seconds before suddenly his eyes widened "A-Ah... I can't stay like this, I must keep my emotions under control, I must regain my composure..."

Allen then gets up from the chair he was sitting on and looks at Grant "I'm sorry, but Ms.Minami must be waiting for me, it was a pleasure to talk with you, Mr.Grant."

He then bows and starts walking as quickly as he could, leaving a confused Grant behind.

"Even considering everything that happened to her... It's difficult to tell if she's lucky or unlucky, either way, I guess I'm jealous after all..."

He then starts heading back to PEFE, though he was actually hoping that it would take a while before he arrived there.
