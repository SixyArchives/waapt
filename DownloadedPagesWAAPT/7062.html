Mossdeep

''As Ken traversed the depths of Varm, getting in touch with his inner Feeling... abilities, time moved on outside for his crew. And as a little green spider pushed his tiny pink charge up the slope and out of the water onto Mossdeep's grassy earth, Rak gave a low sigh. It had been a while since the Spinarak had saw his trainer vanish in a blinding flash... But neither he nor his companions had any idea where Ken had gone. Or how they could follow.

Still. It hadn't been that long. Silently pushing Nina the Happiny back up into their little hollowed out home, Rak really only had to wonder where Tori and Spe had gone off again. The two were always getting into trouble, it seemed... And that trouble usually ended with more problems for everyone else. With a quick application of Spider Web to reform his makeshift nest, he gently nestled the softly sleeping Nina down for the night, stars twinkling above.''

Mossdeep Space Center

At the same time, inside the air ducts of the Space Center, a little white/blue ball and a red chick roll and crawl respectively through the darkness.

Tori: <Spe... Why are we doing this again? Rak's going to be really mad at us for leaving Nina behind on the beach.>

Spe: <Heeheehee! Come on, Tori, we're just exploring! You know that round... thingy... What was it called again? It looks like a ball, but you said it wasn't a ball.>

Tori gives a small sigh, her breath lighting up the darkness with a bit of flame.

Tori: <A weather balloon. It's not a ball, it's a balloooooon. We saw them at the Weather Research station! I meant why are we crawling through the air duct, at night, when there's a perfectly good front door?>

With a little laugh that echoed through the air duct, Spe just grinned and kept rolling.

Spe: <Silly! They don't let you in after 2 AM! ...Or borrow their stuff without permission.>

Tori: <Spe, I love exploring with you, but why are you so eager to look at this Weather Balloon? What could we possibly use it for?>

Spe: <To fly, of course! Fly wherever we wanted!>

The thought stopped the little Torchic in her tracks, her eyes wide. The thought of flying had come to her before, but she'd never thought she'd get to do it. To explore... Everywhere in the world. Even maybe find Ken!

Tori: <Alright, I'm in. But you have to convince Rak to come with us. He'd likely be worried about us falling from the sky or something like that.>

Spe: <Heehee! Deal!>
