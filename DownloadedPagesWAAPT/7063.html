176561 MindGamer12th Jul 2012 05:30:26 PM from In your mind. Obviously. Get RP Mod
Help... My hand's stuck.

Mossdeep Coast

The last thing Rak expected as he looked out of their little cave at dawn was Tori and Spe returning. Not because of the rather large -for their size- weather balloon they were pulling with their teeth, but that they were returning at all without needing to be dragged back. As he popped the last Pecha berry into Nina's cooing mouth, Rak scurried out to meet the others.

Rak: <...Greetings. What have you two found... And where?>

Tori gave a light, almost embaressed laugh, looking to the ground and flapping her little wings nervously, before pushing Spe closer. Spe just looked at the Spinarak with a smile.

Spe: <Hey, Raki! We found a Weather Ball! ...Weather Balloon, I mean. We're gonna go exploring everywhere with this!>

Tori piped up at this point, passionate little embers in her eyes as she spoke

Tori: <We're going to FLY, Rak! And you can't stop us, so come with us!>

As soon as the words left Tori's mouth, she immediately flinched, and lowered her head into her down as Rak gazed over her with a focused glare. An intimidating glare that had frozen the little Torchic girl many times before.

Rak: <And... How do you plan to make it fly with us? Keeping Nina safe?>

Nina, upon hearing her name, wiggled about in her nest with a delightful little smile, crying out in joy.

Nina: Niiiii!

Tori: <Uhm... W-well... You could make a basket for us! With your spider web!>

...A pause. Spe's grin stayed ever present, as he rolled side to side viewing the world upside down. Nina quieted down, but still enjoyed Spe's show. Tori fell silent as she waited on how Rak would respond... And the Spinarak in question... thought for a moment.

Rak: <...If I made this... Our first priority would be finding Ken... Right?>

Spe: <Well, yeah! We'd just be having fun along the way! And it's not like we can spend all our time sitting around here waiting for him to find us, right? That would be boring!>

Rak: <...Alright. Both of you, watch over Nina... And give me an hour. I want to make sure this is stable.>

A wave of relief washed over Tori as she sat down, breathing out before smiling up at Rak, a silent "thank you" transferred to him. Rak barely registered it as he began to spit silk, attaching it to the balloon and forming the core structure.

An hour later, on the dot, it was ready as promised.

Nina was loaded into a special little holder for her, letting her swing her legs safely, while there was more than enough room for Rak, Spe, and Tori to move freely. Their gathered supplies loaded up, Tori began to breath fire into the balloon's folds carefully, and within a few moments, they were airborn...

And immediately cast into the ocean winds, sending them away from Hoenn despite their wishes and out to sea. None of them seemed to have a problem with this, as long as they were free to fly wherever the wind took them.

And right then, the wind was taking them to Sinnoh.
