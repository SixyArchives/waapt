The Forms

The Forms are the basis of the Hagane no Tsume. They not only represent the position and condition of the body, but also of the mind. They bridge the gap between the mental and the physical, reducing the lag of thinking to action to pure instinct and motion. To start, there are two basic Forms, that shall be represented by the colors Red and Blue.

Offensive Form - Base: The most basic of the attack forms, this position is made for attack. Slow or swift, this form can be best represented by the Excadrill. It's primary focus is beating down an opponent either by crushing their defenses or circumventing them - however, it is not specialized in either, and someone who knows their style better will be able to predict the rather telegraphed moves of this Form.

Defensive Form - Base: Counter to the Offensive Form - Base, this is the basis of all Forms relating to the prevention of harm - be it by guarding or dodging, to yourself or others. This form can be best represented by the Gliscor. As one could guess, the primary focus is defense at all costs. However, this form leaves little room for counterattack, and is quite vulnerable to multiple opponents.

All Forms are derived from these two. Whether they specialize in one aspect of them, or combine the two, mastering the Basic Forms will lead to mastering others.
