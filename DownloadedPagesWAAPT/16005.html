 400102 Crewe, Wed, 23rd Jul '14 1:56:18 PM Get RP Mod
Sneasel-y

Hia Valley

-Crewe runs for a little while, eventually coming to stop and looking around, ears pricked-

I could’ve sworn I heard…

???: Faolan!

There!

-she shifts to human form and darts off in the direction of the voice, hardly noticing the cold despite lacking a jacket-

-through the blizzard, two teenaged boys wearing heavy cloaks come into view, trudging against the snow with a Weavile and Snover behind them. The younger, blonde-haired boy has an Eevee curled up in his arms. He looks up at the older one-

???: Faolan! We’re lost!

I know that name… can it be… ?

Faolan: We’re not lost, Halim, the lake is… just around the next corner.

It must be!

Halim: But Faolan, Mor is cold!

Mor: <I-I’m okay, Halim… j-just a little… ch-chilly… a-choo!>

-the Snover pokes at the Eevee, concerned-

Blaithin: <Are you sure, Mor?>

-Faolan stops and turns to face him, then removes his cloak and hands it to Halim-

Faolan: Here.

Halim: But—

Faolan: Just take it. Mother would never forgive me if I let the little mite freeze.

Halim: Thank you, Faolan…

Faolan: Just keep moving.

-they keep walking, the Weavile padding up and wrapping an arm around Faolan-

Rioghan: <Not much further, Fae, and we can get out of this blasted wind…>

-rubbing his arms, he gives her a small smile-

Faolan: Thanks, Rioghan.

I… I should…

-Crewe takes a deep breath and steps up to the boys. Rioghan jumps and hisses, letting go of Faolan and extending her claws menacingly-

Rioghan: <Stay back!>

Faolan: Who—who are you?!

Halim: You’re dressed so weird! Are you… real?

… No, I’m not real. You can call me Helen. I’m… I’ve been sent to you to tell you something.

Rioghan: <Faolan, I don’t trust it…>

You can trust me, Rioghan.

-they are all taken aback-

Rioghan: <!!!>

Faolan: How do you know her name?!

Rioghan: <How do you know what I just said?>

I told you… I’m not real.

-she takes a deep breath-

Halim, Faolan… the sons of Amhlaidh.

Halim: This is really weird, Faolan…

Faolan: Shhh, Halim. What do you want… whatever you are?

There’s something you need to know. Your father… is never coming back. I’m afraid he’s gone.

Faolan: F-father’s… how do you know this?!

-Crewe smiles sadly-

I’m afraid I can’t tell you that. But it’s very important that you understand this, both of you.

-she pauses and looks slowly from Faolan to Halim-

Your father loved you. More than anything, he wished that he could come home and be your father again. But he was trapped, very far away, and he couldn’t get away. He died there. But not for a day did he ever stop loving you. Whatever else he did, or was… whatever you think of him now, remember that.

Faolan: How do we know you’re telling us the truth, huh?

You don’t. But I am.

-she looks at Halim-

He made you a sword, Halim. He was going to give it to you when he came home.

-she suddenly stops-

Wait… Chad! I really really need your help right now! Get me the wooden sword in Jon’s house! The one that says Halim on it!

-she places her hands behind her back, and one blip inaudible above the wind later, Jon’s sword is in her grasp. She brings it out from behind her back-

Since he’s not coming back… that task falls to me, now. Here you go, Halim. Your father’s last gift to you.

-Halim stares at the sword with wide eyes, then hesitantly steps forward and takes it from her, shifting Mor to one arm, where she snuggles more securely against his body-

Halim: Is it… real… ?

As real as you are.

-she steps aside and gestures at the blizzard-

Keep walking. You’ll reach your destination soon enough.

Rioghan: <Come on, Fae…>

Mor: <W-when I’m a G-Glaceon, this won’t be a p-problem…>

Blaithin: <I thought you wanted to invent a Steel type evolution, Mor.>

Mor: <M-maybe I’ll do b-both!>

Faolan: … Let’s go, Halim.

-he gives Crewe a long look, then keeps walking. Rioghan quickly slings her arm around him again-

-Halim stares at Crewe for a while longer, then abruptly runs up and hugs her. Crewe freezes for a moment, shocked, then hugs him back-

Halim: You feel real…

I’m not. I promise. But this meeting was.

-she gently unentangles herself from him and steps back-

Good bye, Halim.

Halim: Good bye, Helen… Thank you.

You’re very, very welcome.

Halim: Come on, Mor, let’s get you out of the cold…

-he runs off after Faolan-

Blaithin: <You okay, Mor?>

Mor: <Y-you just w-wait till I ev-v-volve, then n-nobody’s g-gonna have to ask if I’m o-okay or not…>

-Crewe watches them go, then turns and walks a short distance away before shifting to Sneasel form-

-bwaCRASH!-

Straw’s shoulder

-there is once again a Sneasel here-
