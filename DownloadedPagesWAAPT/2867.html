This week's interesting news for all researchers!

    • Also in examination, the discovery of a new move dubbed "Bubble Shield" by Researcher Luke, studies might make it to publication level after the Conference ends.
  • Swarms of Poochyena spreads across southern Hoenn. Remember children: Dark Is Not Evil. Be gentle with the Pokémon and remember that the Vow takes priority.
    • Our lawyers advise we recommend to only offer treats if in company of a nearby adult or Trainer.
  • Dr. Tagg has already published the article on the Roggenrola line.
    • The Cinccino, Machop and Marill article are still in the works; Nidoran article to grow by "another 20%, sorry no image yet", Dr. Silent says.
  • Bad news for all young and/or nostalgic researchers: the next entry of the Giga Gollet Man Myths saga has been discontinued. Rioters have already started congregating at the Celadon City game development district. Film At Eleven.
  • Increased activity of Murkrow murders in Celadon City and its surroundings. Trainers are recommended to accompany themselves by Electric Pokémon.
  • **PEFE-Sponsored Link** Have you seen this Glameow? If you have fed it with our approved berries, it might be yours....

Featured News: Charting the Uncharted

Report by Intern Ahlān Firuz from [UNDISCLOSED LOCATION].

«At first I thought that I should have known better than "volunteer" to look for random Tropius herds migrating in the wilderness, but now I'm thankful for the experience.»

«It's been two incredibly crazy days. After I departed from Olivine City to the west and managed to bypass the rock walls with my Lairon, all that followed was an endless maze of green. No wifi, no clean water, no visible settlements, an incredible amount of Grass-type Pokémon that seemed to not respect the Vow. And all that I had to guide myself was the sight of the Embedded Tower by southwest and the egregious mass of clouds by the north.»

«The Victreebell around are a pain. They tend to attack and then let us go, as if expecting that we'll just make a mistake and die out there, alone... Fortunately Solrock has saved us by lifting us to the tops of the trees, where we can rest for the night. There are some incredible sights around here... and lack of them two. I have barely seen a bug Pokémon. Not even a Weedle. The place is a moving mass of Weepinbell, Cacturne, Nuzleaf, Oddish and their evolutions. The mountains to the north however seem to be undisturbed except for a random Houndoom pack that seems to be migrating towards Johto, so here I leave the notice for Wild Pokémon Services.»

«After two days I was about to give up. Dr. Silent had requested someone to help locate the parents of Headmaster Tangent's partner, Basket, and I ended taking it. Being riled up by the video of Red's defeat made me feel kinda invincible, and I thought this would be something easy, just go around and take pictures, but what I found was pretty much a nightmare.»

«However I held my feet, and when I was finally able to find a safe spot to contact our Goldenrod research wing, I met only good news. I even got to meet Researcher Luke via chatroom, and he and the other researchers made me realize that despite my situation and my fears, I was actually living my dream. I was charting the uncharted. Boldly going where no PEFE researcher has gone before. And bringing interesting, and perhaps world-changing, news back to the civilized world. Bonus track? The tales are true — Articuno appeared out of nowhere and gave me a free lift across the sea to the Cianwood Safari Zone villa, which, lo and behold, also belongs to Researcher Luke. Heh... If I am lucky, I actually might be able to find a Tropius herd around here. I have more reasons and strength to continue from now on.»

«But Now I Must Go. My team needs healing and I need to resupply. I have already submitted the log of my conversation with the PEFE researchers and a couple of documents to request extra research material and a stronger team. I specifically asked for a Skarmory, I hope that can happen. Also, it goes without saying but three to five that Dr. Silent sends me a Nido.»

«Basket, stand proud. You SHALL meet your parents. Those wild Pokémon are not gonna stop me. Who the heck do they think I am!!!!»

«—Out, Firuz.»
