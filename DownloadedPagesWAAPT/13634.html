The aggressor this time was not a Sneasel, however, but a Woobat, the bat-like creature valiantly stamping the boy's face with its snout with its wings wrapped around his head. Kim struggled to pry it loose, wincing slightly at the surprisingly tight grip that the small Pokémon had managed to lock Rex in. Fortunately, one last good heave caused it to come loose, the Woobat giving a little squeak of surprise before fluttering off.

Woobat: <Eep! I'm out of here~>

The two blinked in stunned silence as the tiny bat flew off, both a bit surprised by the little encounter they'd just had. Then, both began to laugh, the Riolu holding herself steady against the trainer's head as he held himself against a mirror wall, both remarking at the sheer absurdity of what had just occurred.

Rex: Wow, that was... wow...

Riolu!Kim: <Y-yeah...>

After both calmed down, Kim looked Rex over for any signs of injury before nodding, leaping from his shoulder and onto the ground.

Riolu!Kim: <I'm sorry about that. I really should've used my aura senses to see if any Pokémon were nearby before trying that.>

Rex: It probably would've helped if you'd let me get an umbrella first, too.

As the light rainstorm within the cavern began to die down, Rex looked down at the ground of the cavern, looking at the little gift he'd gotten from Kim.

Rex: You really made this yourself?

Riolu!Kim: <Yep! It was Aqua's idea, though.>

Lifting up the object from the ground, Rex couldn't help but smile at his gift: a homemade bracelet made from Luminous Moss.

Rex: So, I guess this is where you ran off to in Ambrette.

Riolu!Kim: <Mhm.>

Rex: And I'm guessing you learned Rain Dance from a TM, right?

Riolu!Kim: <Yeah. Using those things is pretty weird...>

She rubbed her forehead with a paw, still recalling the surge of data into her mind that taught her the move.

Riolu!Kim: <But it was worth it. Your gift looks pretty great doesn't it? And it even glows in the dark when exposed to water! ...Just don't light it around any Woobat, okay?>

They both giggled a bit at that, Rex slipping the bracelet onto his right wrist with a smile.

Riolu!Kim: <Do you... like it?>

Rex: How could I not? It's wonderful.

Grinning, the wolf resisted the urge to hop in place, a bit of light beginning to radiate around her.

Riolu!Kim: <You mean it?>

The light seemed to shine warmly, as if radiating with the girl's very emotions.

Riolu!Kim: <I mean, I thought it'd be okay, but I wanted to make sure...>

The light shone brightly for a brief flash before fading out, leaving the two of them left in the dimness of the cave, what few Woobat remained also departing at the mirror walls' reflections.

Lucario!Kim: <I'm just glad you like it, Rex. You're my best friend and... I'm glad we can enjoy being around each other.>
