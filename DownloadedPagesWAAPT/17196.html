The Bridge

They did not go unnoticed, as Sinclair was picking up several heat signatures approaching quickly.

Sinclair: <Hostiles approaching, sir.>

The Metagross turned to look at Torque, who was pursuing a book of dinosaur Pokémon. Complete with large full-colour pictures.

Torque: <Honey, you could prey on me any da- !!!>

Having noticed that Sinclair was looking at him, he quickly tossed the large book behind him, which hit the floor with a large smack.

<HOSTILES? Jet fighters? Missiles? Airships?>

Sinclair: <They're giving off too small a heat reading, sir. They can't be anything but Pokémon or humans.>

Torque blinked in surprise. He had expected the military to show up and try to shoot him out of the sky by now, but all that had come after him was a few Pokémon?

Torque: <Is this all Johto has to defend itself? A handful of Pokémon? I've got to say, I'm almost disappointed.>

Sinclair: <Shall I shoot them down, sir?>

The Tyrantrum shook his head.

Torque: <Not worth the missiles. Let them come. If worst comes to the worst, I can deal with them personally.>

Sinclair paused for a few moments, wondering if he should raise his objections, before turning back to the helm.

Sinclair: <If you say so, sir.>
