Goldenrod Skies  1:50 until Cannon Fire

Stratus  carrying Lake and Izzy  and Unite  carrying Luke  soared up and towards the immense airship, on their mission to if not take it out, then to at least disable it's Wave Motion Cannon.

Luke: I would have preferred to do this with a full team of six, but I guess sometimes you have to make do with what you've got.

He paused.

Not that what I've got is inadequate.

The Braviary rolled his eyes.

Unite: <Suuure it's not.>

The human turned to the rest of his team, who were flying alongside.

Luke: Alright, you know the plan. If something goes wrong, use that Escape Rope I gave you to get out and get as far away as Goldenrod as you can, got it?

Izzy and Lake nodded.

Izzy: <It won't come to that! This Torque's gonna' get the pounding he deserves!>

Lake: <But if there's an emergency, then sure! We'll do just that! You guys be careful, okay?>

Stratus: <Yeah! I don't want you guys getting hurt!>

Unite: <We'll be fine, Stratus! I'll keep Luke safe, you can count on it!>

Luke gazed down at the bird.

Luke: I can look after myself, you know.

Unite: <With those thin arms and legs? I bet you'd snap like a twig if someone looked at you wrong!>

Luke: Whatever. Stay safe, guys!

With that, the two groups split, Unite and Luke heading down towards the airship's hanger, and Lake, Izzy and Stratus flying up the top to try and reach the bridge.

The Bridge

They did not go unnoticed, as Sinclair was picking up several heat signatures approaching quickly.

Sinclair: <Hostiles approaching, sir.>

The Metagross turned to look at Torque, who was pursuing a book of dinosaur Pokémon. Complete with large full-colour pictures.

Torque: <Honey, you could prey on me any da- !!!>

Having noticed that Sinclair was looking at him, he quickly tossed the large book behind him, which hit the floor with a large smack.

<HOSTILES? Jet fighters? Missiles? Airships?>

Sinclair: <They're giving off too small a heat reading, sir. They can't be anything but Pokémon or humans.>

Torque blinked in surprise. He had expected the military to show up and try to shoot him out of the sky by now, but all that had come after him was a few Pokémon?

Torque: <Is this all Johto has to defend itself? A handful of Pokémon? I've got to say, I'm almost disappointed.>

Sinclair: <Shall I shoot them down, sir?>

The Tyrantrum shook his head.

Torque: <Not worth the missiles. Let them come. If worst comes to the worst, I can deal with them personally.>

Sinclair paused for a few moments, wondering if he should raise his objections, before turning back to the helm.

Sinclair: <If you say so, sir.>
