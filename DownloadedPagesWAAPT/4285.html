Mandarin S, Park

...Fightin? But Ichtacya went that way... oh hraka hraka hraka...

-I'm rushing to the fountain on the other end of the park, following Copahue and Shahinne who are flying there. There's the sound of cracking thunder and some smoke coming from that direction, and then an explosion and the sound of something cracking and breaking. Then, as Copahue clamps Shahinne to prevent her from running into danger first, I can hear Ichtacya and Maekrite's voices-

Ichtacya — <*gasp* ...Ah... You leave me no choice... I have to finish this...>

-There a large vapor curtain covering the battlefield. Maekrite appears at a side of the fountain and fires a Thunderbolt at the water again, evaporating more and causing Ichtacya, who was drawing near, to scream. Then Maekrite is surprisingly hit by an Iron Tail that causes to flinch. As they move off each other, I realize Ichtacya has taken too much damage, her body showcasing several small cuts that can only be from a poison sting barrage. Maekrite has a large cut on his face, and several scratches and bite marks on his flank-

Maekrite -moves to a side — <Look, you fought a good fight. You can rest a bit, or rest forever.> — assumes Army-style battle position.

Ichtacya -casts a Swords Dance upon herself — <I can't do that...>

Maekrite — <If you refuse to listen...>

-Both Pokémon glare and prepare to lunge at each other again-

Copahue — <Oh, no! We have to go help!>

Shahinne — <Ichtacya! Mister teammate! No!>

-Before I can produce their Pokéballs, Ichtacya and Maekrite rush at each other, with the Leafeon releasing an Energy Ball and jumping ahead of it to charge with a max-attack X-Scissor, and her tail glowing with Steel power as well. However, Maekrite, realizing how Ichtacya intends to cover her attack, blasts the Energy Ball with an Ice Beam and then brakes to let himself be slammed by the X-Scissor, that pierces his left flank-

Shahinne — <No! Teammates no!>

-I pick up Ichtacya's ball and aim it at her, but the recall beam misses as in that very moment, Maekrite, taking advantage of the mutual momentum, clamps Ichtacya's leg and turns around with all his strength. Before Ichtacya can react, she is swung in the air and tossed against the fountain's statue-

Copahue — <...Ack! Oh no, Ichtacya!>

-Ichtacya crashes against the statue, leaving a large dent, and falls to the ground, twitching in pain as she has hit the statue with her spine. She falls into the water-

-Maekrite starts charging a Thunderbolt, so I decide to fetch the Moon Ball and recall him. However, before that happens...-

Copahue — <Wait! Shahinne! Don't go in there!>

-Copahue chases after Shahinne, who has freed herself from the Fearow's grip to fly towards the two Pokemon. To Copahue's initial horror and then surprise, Shahinne lands peacefully in front of Maekrite and chirps to him-

Shahinne — <...Why are teammates fighting? Mister teammate, why are you attacking Ichtacya?>

-Maekrite looks at Shahinne surprised, flattening his ears, and relaxes himself. Then he looks around to see Copahue and me approaching-

Ichtacya — <...No, Shahinne! get out of there!> — barely manages to stand up, bares her fangs.

Shahinne — <But you are all fighting!>

Maekrite — <...Shahinne...>

-Shahinne suddely leaps to Maekrite's face and hugs him as tightly as she can with her small body-

Shahinne — <I missed you!>

-Ichtacya and Copahue stand up, surprised, and Maekrite tries to lick Shahinne, who starts nagging him and pecking him softly-

Shahinne — <Haven't seen you since the big, beautiful bird~>

Maekrite — <...I'm sorry...>

-I come along the two surprised Pokémon, and pick Ichtacya up. They of course ask what is going on-

— Well... Ichtacya? Copahue? Meet Maekrite. The team leader.

Ichtacya — <...What?>

Copahue — <...This... this one is the Pokémon you refused to let out?>

— It's complicated. Now... all of you, come here...

-I get a couple of potions and apply them to Ichtacya, who is having difficulty moving. Maekrite simply stands there, avoiding my gaze while letting Shahinne chatter about a lot of inconsequential stuff like Pidgeys offering her a nut bag that she feels happy talking about anyway-

— What about you, Maekrite? Where the heck have you been?

-Maekrite drops his head, then steps back when I try to apply a potion to him, apparently scared-

Maekrite — <I... I was...>

Ichtacya — <I thought he was the Delta the Wurmples talked about... when I saw him shining with a strange glow, I thought something was wrong...>

— ...I can explain that later...
