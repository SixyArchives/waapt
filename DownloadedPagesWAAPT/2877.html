Hello!
I couldn't care less about all the stupid things the anime does for two reasons (well, one reason with two parts). I was introduced to the anime first. I didn't play a Pokemon game for almost a year after it started, and then only a little bit of a friend's copy of Blue. After that, I didn't play another one until I got SoulSilver last October.

Also, it was my favourite show as a kid. I was six when it started, and it was a big part of my life for quite a few years. The nostalgia is wonderful. Not only that, but I started watching it again from the beginning, and as hokey as it is, I still think it holds up.

Also also, naming the characters after the games may originate with the games, but the games dropped it in Gen III. This is a decision I agree with. Another thing that bothers me about it is the weird divergence from the games (Koga, Lt. Surge, and Sabrina being evil, for example).

I'm not saying you're wrong for liking the manga, or even that I don't want any references to it in the RP. I'm just saying that I don't like it, and for that reason, I'm not comfortable with writing elements of it into what I'm doing.
