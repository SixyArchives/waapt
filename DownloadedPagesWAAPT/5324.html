"It's okay, Mr. Deikun. I don't think he meant any harm...I hope."

Blackbelt: "Or do you want me to teach your POKéMON DYNAMICPUNCH?"

Blackbelt: "This move can only be learned once. Is that okay?"

"Umm...okay..."

Blackbelt: "What? You do? You're a good person! Which POKéMON should I teach?"

"Err...you can teach it to my Kecleon..."

Wally cowers behind Casval. After a bit more shouting, the Blackbelt pulls out a TM, places it on the Kecleon's head, and voilà!

Kecleon learned DYNAMICPUNCH!

Blackbelt: "I want you to win at the MOSSDEEP GYM with that move!"

"Umm...I don't want to challenge the gym...sorry"

Blackbelt: "NOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO!!!!!!!!!"
