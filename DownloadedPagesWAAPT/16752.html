Maray's Room

"Good morning, sleepy buns!" Marie yawned as she got up, and rubbed her eyes, frowning as she gazed upon an interesting sight.

Ray was grinning at her as she stood in front of the stove in a frilly black apron and fuzzy slippers, one hand on the frying pan, laden with sizzling eggs, while the other hand was waving at her with a spatula.

Marie raised an eyebrow."Are you.." "Making breakfast? Yep~!" Ray took a little bit of pancake batter out of a bowl and touched Marie's nose with it, giggling."Boop!" Marie couldn't help it, she just started laughing.

"Ooh, waffles are ready!" Ray walked in front of the waffle machine and pressed the release button. After a few seconds, she frowned, and pressed it again..and again. Suddenly, they saw a spark flash, and the machine caught on fire.

Ray stepped back, eyes wide."WOAH!" Marie jumped up and immediately hit Balthazar to wake him up, and grabbed the fire extinguisher from the wall and starts to shoot.

"<Whu..whut...wait, ooh, tasty fire!>" Batlhazar jumps up, eyes wide, and flares his wrists, sucking up all the fire that wasn't extinguished.

Maray both took deep breaths, with Ray looking at Marie apologetically."Umm...sorry?" Marie raised an eyebrow at her, and ruffled her hair, sighing.

There was a knocking at the cabin door, ''tum-tum-tum, -te-tum-tum-tum, '' which Balthazar went to open. The second the handle turned, Locke burst through, eyes wide.

"Guys guys I heardthealarmiseveryonealright?" Marie held up a hand, and Locke took a deep breath, looked around, and sighed."Late again, huh?" Marie nodded, grinning.

Locke chuckled, and said "Oh, and Jason's got a bit of messed up arm, it got run through by Kusaru. I patched it up a bit, but he just wants to relax right now so best not to go into his room right now."

Ray's face turns pale, and she immediately tries to run off when Marie firmly grabs her by the shoulders and sits her down."Didn't you hear? He doesn't wanna be disturbed, and these are all matters that can be handled after breakfast, which ''I'm gonna make, thank you very much." Ray pouted, and Marie grinned after a second, accepting a kiss on the cheek from the other half of Maray.

Marie then puts on an apron, and turns to both Locke and Balthy and motions them to sit, which they do with eagerness, and gets to work.
