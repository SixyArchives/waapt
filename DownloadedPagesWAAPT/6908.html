Spice Solarium - PMD-B

The group eventually come across at, what first glance, appears to be a huge indoor garden. The large grassy expanse - lit from the sunlight shining down through the remains of the dome above - was home to almost every variety of flower, plant and tree you could think of.

A wonderful mix of fragrances wafted through the air as various Bug Pokémon flitted from flower to flower, spreading pollen between them as they took some sips of the sweet nectar whithin, all set to the quite trickle of a small river of water that snaked through the town to a large pond at the lower end of the settlement.

It was a green town in every sense of the word. Fittingly, so was the population.

A variety of Grass-type Pokémon were going about their business. However, compared to the franticness of the Pokémon who made their home at Dynamo Station, the atmosphere in general was much more laid back and relaxing. Quite a few of the townspeoplemons were simply lying on the grass basking in the sun, chatting with friends, or tending to the greenery that made up the landscape.

Even the architecture had been styled to blend in with the landscape. Like the vast majority of the towns scattered around the PEFE, the buildings were made up of re-purposed modules. However, they had all been decorated with various sorts of plants, such as creeping vines or flowers, so that the stark whiteness of the panels they were built from couldn't mar the natural beauty of the surrounding greenery.

To say that the group was impressed would be an understatement.

Pepper: <Look at it! It's like a Grass-type paradise!>

Lake: <I agree, this is just the sort of place you would want to spend a Summer day just lying in the sun doing nothing at all.>

Stratus: <Ooh...look at all those berries and fruits!>

The Dragonite points a claw to a large farm of all sorts of berries, fruits and vegetables growing in neat rows.

<Just looking at them is making me hungry...>

While Stratus salivated at the thought of getting to chow down on a whole rainbow variety of fruits, a few of the others didn't seem nearly as enthusiastic.

Lardna: <Meh, it looks like a total snooze-fest to me.>

Andre: <Yeah, there's nothing to fight!>

Eir: <And there's no fear here at all! What am I supposed to feed on?>

The Duskull pauses to think, realizing something.

<Ooh, but that means they would never see it coming if I tried to coax some out of them...>

Eir's scheming prompts a certain Salamence to let out a low growl.

Nadia: <If you try anything funny, I'll bite you. Got that?>

The Duskull gives Nadia a disappointed look.

Eir: <Aww, but I've gotta' eat, you know!>
