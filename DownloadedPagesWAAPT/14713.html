Faith: So, you've still been having trouble getting Damian to Mega Evolve?

Sighing, the brunette boy nodded, looking down at his shoes morosely.

Rex: Yeah, I don't get it. I've been trying to get closer to him and even bought him all kinds of books to read and have been battling with him more. I even used the sundial to power up the Mega Stones and nothing! I don't know what I'm doing wrong...

Faith was silent, taking in his words before turning to him.

Faith: You don't? It seems quite clear to me. What you're asking of Damian is a lifetime bond. Something forged through trust. You can't just buy a few presents and expect to create emotional unity. This isn't a video game, you know.

She waited for him to lift his head to meet her gaze before continuing.

Faith: And for that matter, what of your other Pokémon? Have you been attempting to bond with them as much as Damian during this time? Or have you let them sit on the wayside?

Rex: I, um...

Nodding as she got the expected answer, Faith placed a hand on her son shoulder.

Faith: I understand you mean well, but you're going about things the wrong way.
