Somewhere near Azelea Town - Several Hours later

The sun has long since set, the dark landscape now lit only by the light of the moon and the stars, the silent night broken only by the occasional Hoothoot or Noctowl hooting in the trees as they silently hunt for prey.

The darkness, however, was broken by the bright light of Woolly's tail bulb as she worked away across the plains to the Azelea Town observatory. It had taken a few hours for her to get there, but her journey would soon be over, as she spotted the large dome-shaped building on the top of a nearby hill, it's huge telescope pointed skywards as it scanned the cosmos.

Woolly smiled to herself. Soon, she'd be able to speak to the space Ampharos again.

However, she couldn't help but feel sad that Yarn wouldn't be here to see them. Even if he did make them up, they had bonded over his tales of extraterrestrial Ampharos, and she still wanted to show him that he was right about them.

Or that he had made a lucky guess.

Arceus, this was confusing.

Trying not to think about it too much, Woolly makes her way up to the observatory doors. Seeing no sign of anything like a doorbell, she knocks on the door a few times, hoping that whoever was working inside that night would be able to hear her.

Woolly: <Okay Woolly, just act cute and they'll let you in. Easy.>

She couldn't help but feel nervous though. She was so close, that failing would be devastating...
