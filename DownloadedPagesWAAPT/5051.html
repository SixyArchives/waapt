Goldenrod City: Rex's House

The key slid into the lock, the tumblers turning with a click as the wooden door opened invitingly. Faith stepped into the house, noting with some dissatisfaction that she had indeed left the heat on. A certain other person was similarly upset about the news, appearing in front of her almost immediately after she stepped inside.

Yuki: <Where have you been!? I'm practically melting here!>

Faith smiled awkwardly, raising her hands placatingly to try to calm the irate ghost.

Faith: Sorry, sorry. There was an incident that resulted in me landing halfway across the world.

The Pokemon stared at her blankly, as if demanding an explanation.

Faith: Well, you see...

~~~

Yuki: <...Oh.>

The two were seated in the living room at the moment, Faith having had the decency to make the temperature more pleasant for the Ice-type before they had begun to speak. At the moment Yuki seemed rather upset by the news of everyone's adventure.

Faith: Are you alright, Yuki?

The ghost frowned and folded her arms, a petulant frown appearing on her features.

Yuki: <Heck no! Why didn't Rex call me for help? I could've manipulated that obscura! I could've helped get people back into their bodies!>

Her left eye twitched in irritation, the temperature in the room dropping considerably, and not from the A/C.

Yuki: <   I COULD'VE GONE TREASURE HUNTING IN THAT CAVE!   >

Faith sweatdropped, the bead turning into ice and smashing on the floor.

Faith: Ahaha...perhaps I should let Rex know you want to get out...

Not waiting for a response, Faith excused herself and went for her Pokegear, sending a text to her son. As she keyed in her message, she wondered to herself how he always seemed to get himself mixed up with such an odd group of characters...

New Bark Town: Prof. Elm's Lab - Rex's Farm

Dahlia: <   WHAT?   >

Minty: <E-eep...>

The flower Pokemon loomed over the little bug ominously, a terrifying glare on her visage.

Dahlia: <   THERE ARE CONTEST HALLS IN HOENN? AND NO ONE INVITED ME TO PERFORM?   >

Minty: W-well...

She picked up the tiny Pokemon, Minty letting out a whimper of fear as she began to shake him for emphasis.

Dahlia: <   EXPLAIN YOURSELF!   >

The Swadloon stammered weakly as he was shook, trying to ward off the urge to vomit or scream...or both.

Minty: <Oh Arceus please don't kill me it was Felix's fault we were with him I promiiiiiise!>

Almost as if summoned by his very name, the researching assistant arrived on the site moments later. He smiled at the two kindly, failing to note Minty's look of warning.

Felix: Good morning, you two. I trust you're having a good day?

Dahlia: <...>

Dropping Minty to the ground, the Bellossom turned to the human, a dark gleam in her eyes. The Swadloon, realizing good fortune when it made itself apparent, scurried off faster than he ever had in his life.

Felix: Eh...is something wrong Dahlia?

The Bellossom said nothing, moving ever closer to the boy. Her right hand glowed with energy, signifying the beginning of a Drain Punch. It was then that Felix recognizing the look on her face, recalling it from his time in Dewford Cave with Ohm and Jemma.

It was a look that promised pain. And lots of it.

Felix: ...Oh crap.

He turned to flee, but sadly, it was a sunny day.

And Dahlia had Chlorophyll.

~~~

Dragging himself back into the lab weakly, Felix crawled over to table he'd left his PokeNav on, groping dizzily for it until he knocked it to the floor. He began to type in a message weakly, hoping he didn't pass out before it was done.

Felix: I'd better get in touch with Rex and let him know that Dahlia could use some time away from here. Ow, my pancreas...
