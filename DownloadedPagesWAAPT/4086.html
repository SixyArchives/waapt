Johto Safari Zone - Rocky Beach area

-A certain Gallade is riding on a certain Meganium's back as they make their way across the rough terrain of the Rocky Beach Area.-

Pepper: <Are you sure, you're alright, sweetie?>

Guardé: <I'm fine, Pepper. A good night sleep was all we needed to get better.>

Pepper: <Didn't the nurse say not to overexert yourself, though? All this Teleportung you've been doing must have been rather hard on you...>

Guardé: <Hey, the nurse said that I wasn't allowed to battle for the next few days. She didn't say anything about teleporting. Besides, we really need to get Pleo back to the islands sooner rather than later.>

Pepper: <Why's that?>

Guardé: <Well, we weren't actually supposed to stay in the Orange Islands with him as part of our agreement to keep him safe. The Reef Pokémon probably wouldn't be very happy to find out that we've taken him all the way to Johto...>

Pepper: <...Oh. Well, I can see why you're so anxious to get him back.>

Guardé: <Yeah. Oh! Here we are...>

-Pepper stops in front of a wooden door set into the side of a cliff. Guardé hops of the Meganium's back, then knocks on the door.

Nothing happens.-

Guardé: <...Perhaps they're not->

-POP!-

Guardé: <WHAAA!>

-Guardé is caught by surprise by Simon burrowing out of the ground in front of him.-

Simon: <'Ello, friends!>

Pepper: <Oh! Hello Simon...Are Lewis and Pleo around?>

Simon: <Sure, I'll just get him for you.> -Turns around.- <LEWIS, COME 'ERE! AND BRING PLEO!>

Lewis: <Alright, alright! We're coming. Pleo? Guardé's here.>

Pleo: <Aww...Can't I stay a bit longer? I'm nearly done building the Reef!>

Lewis: <Tell you what, if you can get Luke's Laptop, I'll e-mail the files to you, okay?>

Pleo: <Okay...>

-A minute later, Lewis comes to the door, along with Pleo.-

Pleo: <Hi, Guardé!>

Guardé: <Greetings, Pleo! Did you have a good time with Simon and Lewis?>

Pleo: <I sure did! I was worried about you guys, though. Tagg didn't hurt you guys, did he?>

Guardé: <...No, we're all fine, don't worry.>

Pleo: <Oh, okay!>

Guardé: <So, we should probably be off then, yes?>

Pleo: <Yeah, King Nautilus would be really mad if he found out I was here.>

Guardé: <And we'd rather not have that.>

Lewis: <Aww, by then, Pleo.>

Pleo: <Bye guys!>

Simon: <You'll come back and see us, right?>

Pleo: <Uh, I'll have to ask King Nautilus, but I'll try!>

Simon: <And if that doesn't work...You remember the password to our server, right?>

Pleo: -Nods.- <Yep.>

Simon: <Awesome.>

Pepper: <Bye then, Pleo! Come back and visit if you can, okay sweetie?>

Pleo: <I will, Pepper!> -To Guardé.- <Okay, I'm ready to go!>

Guardé: <Alright, and...AWAY!>

-Guardé and Pleo disappear in a flash of light...And reappear again about 10 meters away.-

Lewis: <Huh?>

Simon: <...What.>

Pepper: -Sweatdrops.-

Guardé: <Oh...Still haven't gotten the hang of teleporting yet, it seems.>

Pleo: <Try again?>

Guardé: <Okay...AWAY! Again.>

-The two disappear again.-

Simon: <...He is a really strange Gallade.>

Lewis: <Yeah.>

<"Hey!">
