Oreburgh Gym (Future)

Rex traversed over the stone floor of the gym, somewhat bothered by how creepy the stone corridors seemed at night. At the very least, there were no Gym Trainers to fool around with at this hour, so he could get straight to business.

"Welcome!" the man in the red hat greeted cheerfully. "As you are aware, this is the Oreburgh Pokémon Gym! I'm Roark, the Gym Leader! I'm but one Trainer who decided to walk proudly with Rock-type Pokémon!" Finally, a name to go with the face...though, his name was somewhat odd. Most Gym Leaders had names, whether real or just used for their appearances, that related to the element of Pokemon they used. Hmm...perhaps the pun would come to him later.

"As the Gym Leader, I need to see your potential as a trainer," Roark continued, Rex simply staying silent as he continued his speech. "And, I'll need to see the toughness of the Pokémon that battle with you! It'll be a three on three match. You are allowed to switch out your team, while I am not. Should you win, you will be awarded a Coal Badge and the signature TM of my Gym. Do you accept the terms?"

Steeling himself, Rex nodded, a determined look blazing in his eyes. He wouldn't fail. This time he'd be the winner. All eight Sinnoh Badges would be his, and everyone would see that he wasn't a joke. "I accept."

"Alright!" Roark grinned, gripping a Pokeball in his hand. "Then let the battle commence!"
