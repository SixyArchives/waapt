Lyuri was walking around the streets when she suddenly felt someone poke her on the back

"Hi!"

She turned around to see that it was Akane, smiling happily.

"Uh... What do you want?" asked Lyuri, raising an eyebrow at the girl.

"You match the description of a girl whose boyfriend is looking around for. Are you her?" she asked

"My boyfriend?" Lyuri sighed "Figured he would be worried about me... I guess I just spaced out for too long."

"Did you two have a fight or something?" asked Akane

"Personal matters." said Lyuri, clearly trying to avoid elaborating, she looks away slightly "I'm fine now."

Akane's expression became more serious, but also curious "No, you aren't. You're trying to hide it, but I can see that something is still troubling you."

"And why do you care?" she gave her a cold look

"You look like the kind of girl who keeps bottling up her feelings until you reach a breaking point and start lashing at others wildly. I'm just trying to prevent an accident from happening." she playfully said.

"Keep trying and soon you'll be the victim of the accident."

"Well, that's what I'm aiming for." she smiled

Lyuri tilted her head at the girl, giving her an odd look and then doing a dismissive gestured with her hand "You're just weird. Thanks for telling me about your boyfriend, now just go mind your own business."

She then walked away, leaving Akane behind. The girl tugged on the collar of her shirt, sighing as she watched Lyuri go, unsure of what to do next.

"Some people just don't want to admit they need help. I'm just a stranger for her right now, but maybe I can help her somehow..." thought Akane, with an optimistic smile.
