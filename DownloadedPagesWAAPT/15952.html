Vien Forest

A slight breeze sweeps through the forest blowing the dead and dying leaves to the forest floor. On this particular day, however, the various leaves and forest debris are crushed beneath the massive hoof of a Tauros, a Tauros with a rather imposing looking humanoid figure on his back.

The figure is draped in long, black robes. With the back midsection torn and cut to resemble the claws of Yveltal with red trimming down the side. The front, though, is trimmed with white instead of read. On the figures' head, a black wide brimmed leather hat fits snugly and on the face, a full faced beige leather plague doctor's mask with empty glass eyes with black stitching holding it together. The hands covered on fancy kid gloves and in his left hand, a wooden stave with a Sphalerite◊ orb fixed on the top.

The figure is certainly a menacing one. One might say this traveller looks like death itself. Not helping his case is the Absol following who's red eyes almost seem to glow with a sickly aura.

Absol: <Here we are in the beautiful past~ Here today - built to last~ Oh ho ho~>

The Absol leaps in front of the Tauros which causes the beast to rear up, almost causing his rider to fall.

???: Woah! Woah! Nandi! Calm down boy!

The figure's voice is both very soft and very gentle. Despite its gentle tone, it's quite apparent that the cloaked figure is in fact, male. With a few calming words and gentle strokes of the mane, the bull calms down.

Afterwards, the man sends what feels to be a disappointed glare at the Absol. Though it's hard to tell behind that mask.

???: Siva, must you be so callous as upset Nandi in such a fashion. You knew he tends to get easily spooked by such sudden events...

The man strokes the mane of his Tauros again, who seems calmed by it.

Absol Siva: <A thousand apologies, O patient one~ I was just wondering what you were really plan on accomplishing here. Change the course of the river all you want, it shall always flow into the sea~>

???: ...Our purpose isn't always clear cut as it seems, Siva. Maybe we're not here to change any fate... Who knows

The man gestures upwards.

???: The gods works in mysterious ways, my dear Siva.

Siva: <Hmm~ Sometimes I feel you put too many eggs in the gods baskets, Salvador. But that could just be a by-product of being a clergyman~ Agape is said to be the greatest of the loves, after all~>

??? Salvador: Yveltal be praised, I shall find out my purpose in this time. For now, we should head to a township and rest easy. A soul that wanders tirelessly becomes uneasy...

Siva: <Oh~ Pueltown should be this way~ Follow me sir~>

The Absol leaps and bounds through the forest, kicking up a whirlwind of leaves as he does so. The man on his Tauros, however, moves slowly. Taking the time to savour every tree and every leaf in the forest with great admiration.
