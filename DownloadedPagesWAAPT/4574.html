Slateport City: Inn

Felix rolled over with a yawn, stretching his limbs and sitting up in bed. He was surprised that he'd woken up so early; usually, it'd be another two hours before he got out of bed. Feeling far too awake to return to bed, he rose and headed for the shower, returning several minutes later and re-clothing himself. Then, he gathered Rex's Pokeballs and his other belongings, exiting the room and heading for May's. He tapped gently on her door, calling softly so as not to awaken any other guests.

Felix: May? May?

Surprisingly, there was no answer.

Felix: May? Are you in there?

His question was answered by a sudden snore and some mumbling, both having a distinct feminine sound.

Felix: ...Is she still asleep?

It was odd that he'd wake up before May... Shrugging to himself, Felix left the inn, leaving a note under May's door explaining that he had stepped out for the moment.

Slateport City

Felix walked about aimlessly, not really paying attention to much that was going on. Overhead, a few Wingull flew, the aspiring researcher flicking his gaze over to them for a moment before looking back at the city in front of him.

Felix: Hmmm...I wonder what I should do today?

???: Hey, you...

Felix: ...?

Turning around, Felix noticed a strange man, dressed in all black beckoning towards him.

???: Come here...I have you might want to see.

Felix: Uhh...

???: Don't be shy.

Without waiting for Felix to respond, he grabbed hold of his arm and dragged him into a nearby building, the boy yelping in surprise.
