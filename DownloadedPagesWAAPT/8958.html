-sniffs them-

You smell like... Friends! Hi there! I’m Illya!

Dreyer: Oh? Yay! Being friends is always a good thing! ^_^ ^_^

J!Unown: This one apologizes for its abscence, was attending to other things... Oh! Hello to the Kyurem!

-Staring-

Natrix: ... What is wrong with you? How on earth can you be so calm around that thing?!

Gaia (?): ...You know this Kyurem?

Ilya: Mister floaty letter told me stories! I love stories!

Gelato: Huh? What did he tell you?

Ilya: He told me a cool story about giant robots with drills that run on awesome!

J!Unown: Don't thank this one... This one learned it from the Zorua! ^;

Tagg (?): Giant robots with drills...now where have I heard that one before?

-Giving the reptilian equivalent of a sweatdrop-

Natrix: Ah... Great to hear...

Nimbus: Hey, if it keeps us from becoming a TV dinner here, I’m all for it!

Dahak: ... What’s wrong with Super Robot shows?

Ilya: Do you know any good stories, funny mons? Wait, wait! I can think of one! Hrrrrrrrmmmm...

-Appears to do the Kyurem equivalent of scrunching her face in concentration -

J!Unown: This one says the Kyurem can take her time.

Ilya: Oooh! Oooooh! I got one! Daddy told me a story a while back about a DNA Splicer!

Tracer(?): A... What?

Ilya: Yeah! Daddy and the big black and white dragons out west used to be one bigger dragon! But if I get too close to the little black and white dragons with this weird pointy rock daddy calls a DNA Splicer, he said I could fuse with one of them! And he said that's bad!

J!Unown: ...This one says that's an... Enlightening tale...

Tracer(?)’s Group: O_O;

Teala(?): I didn’t remember that in the lore I was told!> O_O;

Dahak: Me either.

Roc (?): -Sleep bubble pops- What?

Tagg (?): Nice to see you were paying attention Roc. >_>

Savarin: Wait, did you just say that you had a ''dad?'' So you’re ''not*'' the one that they talk about in those stories of Kyurem? Then where’s-?

-And suddenly the cave shudders with mighty footsteps as a most definitely not fun sized Kyurem enters the area-

Kyurem: -Gives us all a look- ...

Tagg (?)’s Group: ...Oh crap.

Tracer(?): That’s- O_O;

Elh: Um... He’s hospitable too, hopefully? O.O;

-Starting to look rather ill-

Nimbus: D-Dragons aren’t supposed to die, dragons aren- ur-urk!

-And begins to retch, clamping claws over snout to try and keep himself from vomiting out of fear-

Illya: Oh, hi daddy! I was just talking to some friends!

J!Unown: This one and its compatriots did not mean to offend the big Kyurem.

Tagg(?): Yeah, let’s go with that...

Ilya: You won't eat these guys, will you, Daddy?

Kyurem: I haven’t eaten a human in ages...

Ilya: See? My daddy is nice!

Natrix: Gee, way to reassure all of us NON-humans here... >_>

Tracer(?): “Ah... Glad to hear? Also, I think I’m having trouble moving...

Dahak: That would be the cold... I’m surprised that you all managed to weather it for so long...

Tagg(?): Why remind me of that now? >_>

Kyurem: Or mons either...Say, you wouldn’t happen to have any scarves would you?

Ilya: Oh! Daddy has a ginormous scarf collection!

J!Unown: This one would give a scarf... If this one weren't a Unown.

Gelato: Scarves? Oh! We’ve got plenty of those!

Natrix: Yeah? So let’s GIVE him one and GET OUT OF HERE!>

-Whimpering in rather visible discomfort, muffled by some regurgitated food at the back of his mouth-

Nimbus: Pl-Please! B-Before he asks for us to stay for dinner!>

Savarin: What are you DOIN’? You NEED those!

Gelato: Huh?

Dreyer: Why’s that important?

Elh: They’re what signals to others that you’re not of feral stock. And that you have no interest in taking a bite out of another mon.

Dreyer: Well that’s important! Just think of all the Vanillite that get mistaken for Castelliacones each year! D: D:

All: ...

Tracer(?): Do Castelliacones even exist in this universe?

Ilya: You're silly, ice cream mon.

Simo: We got spares, didn’t we?

Savarin: We did? I mean, that’s right! We did! We’re so impressed by your hospitality, ya know, what with the stories of you bein’ a monster and all, that we want to make you into a member of Team Multicast!

Teala(?): DREA Mcast... >_>

Savarin: Whatever! And we’ve got just the colors for ya!

-Which prompts a certain Druddigon to hastily fish through the backpack and pull out an orange and a blue scarf with white swirls on them-

Teala(?): Um... Our apologies if they’re a little small... Lord?

Kyurem: Hmm... They appear to be quite vivid...

And the design takes me back quite a bit...

-And reminisces of days past with Ilya as the party slips on-

J!Unown: Goodbye to the Ilya.

Ilya: Bye, Mr. floaty letter!

Nimbus: HUUURGH!

Elh: Ew!
