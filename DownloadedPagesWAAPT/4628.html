Pummelo Island: Pokemon Center

Rex watched Silent's interview continue, a little smile on his face. He was happy for his friend, though a small inward part of him reminded him that yet another one of the group had become a Champion, something he had yet to do for himself. He grimaced, swallowing those ugly feelings and instead turned to his mother, trying to think of something lighter.

Rex: So, umm, mom...how can you speak to Pokemon again?

Faith grasped ahold of one of Rex's arms, rising to her feet and gently urging him to do the same.

Faith: I think I'd like to meet your friend, Rex. Won't you introduce me?

The brunette groaned, getting up as well.

Rex: You're never going to tell me, are you?
