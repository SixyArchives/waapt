Pi has wandered here, in order to be far away from major population centers.

He flips open the case.

Inside, a pair of metal gloves and a boots are nestled in a custom foam packaging.

"Time to make sure these still move well."

He takes them out. As they are pulled out of their container, the pieces unfold a sort of armature, connecting all of them to a small central unit in the back.

He puts it on, and starts moving around, testing his mobility.

He throws a few kicks and punches, checking the looseness of the joints.

"Alright, mobility is good. Let's see how the motor's doing."

He reaches behind him, and flicks a switch on the centerpiece.

Suddenly, a series of servo-motors whir to life.

He listens to the noise they make, then switches the device off.

"Good, this thing still functions."

He takes it off, folds it up, and places it carefully back in the case.

"Well, good to know."

He picks up the case, and wanders off.
