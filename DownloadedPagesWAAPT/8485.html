Guess Who...?

Past - PEFE HQ - Following This

Based off of a script Umbra and I came up with. If you're looking at this, Umbra. I hope my tweaks and additions maintained what you were hoping to communicate through your characters' actions and responses.

<Audrey: "What do you mean? I just turned it on! There's a power switch here! It took all of like, a second!">

-Cocking eyebrow-

<Eowyn: "You sounded like you were trying to get something working right...">

<Audrey: "... I just don't like these machines much, that's all.">

<Eowyn: "Then why do you spend so much time with them when training is an option?">

<Audrey: "Because I like doing other things in life beyond training too? Is there something wrong with that?">

<Eowyn: "No... Just... I don't get the appeal of the little plastic boxes. What fun is there in beating some virtual baddie up over the real thing?">

-In the background, a certain Lucario and Porygon-Z look at each other warily-

-Unimpressed-

<Audrey: "Mmph. Play a game with an actual story and get back to me on that.">

<Eowyn: "Show me a game with a story that makes sense and get back to me on that.">

-Pausing before setting a PFP certain Lawyer Friendly Cameo Device down-

<Audrey: "... I'm sorry... WHAT did you just say?">

<Eowyn: "I mean come on. They're all about dead dads that become the vessels for big honking monsters so the kid can collect the weird plot devices of destiny and destroy him or something like that.">

-In the background-

<Xion: "Philistine.">

<Roxas: "Oh shush, it's bad enough that one mon is getting worked up over this!">

-Facepalming-

<Audrey: "Please tell me that you're seriously not making blanket statements based off of a single game... That's just terminally stupid.

And I liked that game!" >_> >

<Eowyn: "No, duh. That was just the weirdest, stupidest thing out of a bunch of weird, stupid things.">

<Audrey: "   ...   ">

<Xion: "Don't fight...">

<Roxas: "Yeah, this is kinda dumb...">

-Rather obviously restraining herself-

<Audrey: "Rrgh... No... I'm not coming to blows over someone's idiocy, I'm just going to pretend that I didn't hear this and move on. Episode 9 beckons.">

-And starts to head off, visibly smoldering-

<Eowyn: "Hmph. Like she could even take time to fight over playing.those plastic things all day.">

-Stopping dead in her tracks-

<Audrey: "... Care to    REPEAT    that?">

<Eowyn: "No really, I haven't seen you fight once. It's kinda sad.">

-Appearing to be broody-

<Audrey: "Tell me, Eowyn... Do you really *want* to see me fight right now? I'm rather irritated...

And you know what they say about upset mons...">

-Giving a smirking leer that many mons would find most unsettling-

<Audrey: "   There's no telling what they'll do...   ">

<Eowyn: "Oh, really?">

Eowyn Used Swords Dance!

<Eowyn: "   I'm not in the best mood either, if you haven't gathered...   ">

<Audrey: "Oh? Good to hear... Lemme FIX that!">

-And forms a large blue sphere with her paws and casts it directly into Eowyn-

<Eowyn: "Urk... Let me... Return the favor!">

-And lunges forth with a flurry of punches and kicks. Which are dodged blow for blow by Audrey in a seemingly meditative state-

-Opening eyes, scowling-

<Audrey: "Oh, PLEASE. Is thinking with your fists all you DO?">

-And quickly becomes a blur, putting distance away from herself and Eowyn-

<Eowyn: "Oh no... Unlike what you say, I'm no idiot!">

-And catches up with a jab that cuts Audrey off, though due to technique and relative strength, is shrugged off-

<Audrey: "Gack! Rrr! Really?! You could have fooled me!">

-Vaults self into air over Eowyn and tries lobbing another Aura Sphere, which cuts a swath of destruction as it homes in on its target-

<Eowyn: "Hrk!">

-And hurls her into a wall-

<Eowyn: "Grr... You aren't doing bad in the idiot category yourself!">

-And slams the ground, emitting a strong localized tremor as soon as Audrey lands, which catches her off her feet and forces her into a roll-

<Audrey: "ARGH!">

-Picking self back up with noticeable exertion, likely at less than half health-

<Audrey: "Oh, you know what?! FINE! Have it YOUR WAY!">

-Does not use an attack, but seemingly just glowers, in concentration-

<Eowyn: "Oh, so we're showing off now, huh?">

-And Eowyn Swords Dances again-

-Seemingly just standing at rest, exiting focus-

<Audrey: *snort* "Well?! What are you waiting for?! Come at me!">

-Aside-

-Ducking a loose and dangling ceiling light-

<Xion: "Roxas... A little crowd control?">

<Roxas: "Might as well...">

<Eowyn: "Grr... How about I send something your way?!">

-And uses Earthquake again-

-Which prompts Audrey to seeming splits into three, Left + Right!Audrey vaulting over the ground while Middle!Audrey is wiped from existence-

-Giving a bit of a malicious smirk as their paws begin to glow blue-

<Both Audreys: "Nice try...

But this is game over, Eowyn-!">

-And the two are cut off by an Earthquake-

<Audrey: "ACK!">

<Eowyn: "Gah!">

-Which erases the last Double Team clone and forces both of the two Lucario to their knees hanging on via a sliver of consciousness-

<Audrey: "Rrgh... I call hax!">

<Eowyn: "Rrr... You calling me a cheater?!">

<Audrey: "Of COURSE I am! There's no WAY you could have used Earthquake from all the way over-">

-And with a turn of her head, notices a certain observing Lucario holding a mockup keyblade forced into the floor of the module-

-Yanking keyblade out of the rubble of an impact crater in the floor and slinging over shoulder-

<Roxas: "Now, play nice from now on, 'k?">

<Audrey: "There... Oh, so THAT explains it... Seriously, why don't you mind your own affairs, cosplay boy?">

<Eowyn: "Yeah, butt out!">

<Roxas: "Well, I couldn't just let you beat each other to a pulp and wreck the whole module...">

<Audrey: "Are you seriously implying that she wasn't about to get blown across the room by my finisher there? And what do you mean the mo-">

-And notices that the surrounding module is now more or less a warzone-

<Audrey: "... Oh. That's totally gonna help me make my case for getting something to upgrade from that ratty old Phat for Christmas..." ._.>

<Eowyn: "...We kinda did a number on the place..." ._.>

<Audrey: "Hey, I wasn't the one using Earthquake willy-nilly!" >_> >

<Eowyn: "Well, I wasn't firing Aura Spheres like hadowhatsits!">

<Audrey: "IT'S 'HADOKEN', YOU DOLT!">

-Rolling his eyes-

<Roxas: *sigh* "Figure it out on your own, you two... Come on, Xion.">

-Reluctantly following-

<Xion: "See you... And good luck with cleanup...">

-To Audrey-

<Eowyn: "Blugh... Well you two were really helpful... Look, whatever, let's just clean this up...">

<Audrey: "Rrgh... Fine. Though for the record...">

-Getting up in Eowyn's face, with a harsh leer-

<Audrey: "Don't you EVER insinuate that I can't fight again! Otherwise, your cosplayer friend might not be around to bail you out next time...

Got it?">

-Snarling in response-

<Eowyn: "Fine. I didn't even say that! I said you were too lazy to fight!">

-And the two begin to clean up the remains of the module... In the process, one of the two comes across the PFP unnamed device-

<Audrey: "Blugh, figures it wouldn't-">

-And sees that it is still working, if with non-trivial scratches, scuffs, and the occasional crack-

<Audrey: :O "I can't believe it, that piece of junk actually survived!">

<Audrey: "   ...   

... I'm sticking with home consoles for my Last Legend fix from now on." >_> >
