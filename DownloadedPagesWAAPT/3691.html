[OT] Kumquat Island Plaza

-I walk freely around the other contestants, Ichtacya by my side. She marverls herself at smelling the different things around and watching how some other Trainers feed their mons.-

Ichtacya (looks around) — <Oh... er, Trainer?>

— You can call me Silent, Ichtacya.

Ichtacya (rushes to me looking nervous) — <Yes, er... but why are we leaving the plaza, Trainer?>

-I make a gesture for Ichtacya to look at the other side of the street where there is a store-

— I want to ask for something special here. Don't worry, we'll be back in time for the next match. — looks at the people congregated at the stand — Hmmm... or perhaps not.

-Ichtacya tries to not feel too uncomfortable, and both of us blend with the multitude trying to go in and out the store, some of them carrying out small boxes they have bought-

Ichtacya (tries to look around) — <Oh! These are beautiful! People look happy!> — goes from person to person — <I wonder what are those?>

-smiles as I see Ichtacya try to talk with a random Duduo-

-I stand at the end of the queue waiting to make it to the inside and watch at my clock insistently-

Well, at least she can have fun walking around people and talking to one mon or two. I'll give this ten minutes, then I return to the plaza.

[AU] ???? Island

-After having made into the main city of the island via one of the ports, I take advantage of a marathon going on around the beaches to walk around the streets and canals, and from there disappear into some of the backalleys, carrying only a Moon Ball, a wallet and a bag-

-After reaching a somewhat secluded place, I release Maekrite-

Maekrite☼EX (sniffs and paces around) — <Nifty narrow passages. Like the smell around here. Is that...> — sniffs, flickers ears — <sweet water?>

— Fountain water, probably. Are you sure this is the place the other Nidos talked to you about?

Maekrite — <Well, I hope it is. What do we do, ask a random Pidgey?>

-frown, produce a map from the island-

— No... we should try to find our way around and check those landmarks soon. I wish I at least knew what they are.

Maekrite — <You know their approximate location and perhaps they are important or notable...> — watches a couple of Pidgey fly above, then waits for a moment and turns to me — <how do you know about those anyway?>

— The Silent from the other timeline was doing research about this island. He didn't have that much to work on, but it should still help us.

At least until we take another ship to Hoenn. Wonder if anyone is expecting that one...

-looking around at the maze of streets-

— Oh well, we should get going. Which way?

Maekrite (sniffs around) — <That way. Less mons.>

-we walk towards the north, coming across a couple of stores and empty houses, with apparently no one looking around.-

Perhaps it's because of the marathon. Either way, at least Maekrite can walk with me here without being all worried. Let's see... if this point corresponds to the tower I can see over there, we are going the right way.
