 90191 Luke 924, Mon, 12th Sep '11 3:06:08 PM Get RP Mod

Underwater - Somewhere near Tarroco Island

-A school of various water Pokémon swim in a tight formation, keeping a firm eye out for any potential threat. The many water types, mostly Lanturn, a few Lapras, and the odd Qwillfish, Huntail, and quite a few Remoraid, are doing their best to keep their escort safe.

Said escort in question is a silver-white Pokémon that resembled a cross between a bird and a Dragon. It's most distinctive feature being a large set of wings tipped with finger like feathers, a set of Navy blue ridges running down it's back, two similar spikes on it's eyes, and a rather long neck and tail.

Yes, the Pokémon was a Lugia. A-bit, a very small one. A hatching, in fact. Well, he had hatched a few months back, but he was still young by Legendary standards.

The Lugia has wanted to go for a swim, so he had left his home along with his carer, a rather nice female Lapras, to go out for a swim. But not without having a patrol of guard Pokémon to ensure that he was kept safe. Something he was never really happy about. A feeling his Lapras carer easily picked up on.-

Lapras: <Are you alright, little one?>

Lugia: <...Oh? Um...Yeah, I'm okay.> -Looks away, absentmindedly.-

Lapras: <Aww, are you not enjoying your swim?>

Lugia: <No, it's not that.> -Shakes his head.- <It's fine, Ms Lapras. Don't worry.>

Lapras: -Looks slightly concerned.- <Hmm...If you insist, dear.>

-The Lugia was lying, though. He wasn't entirely happy. Partly because he didn't really like not being able to go out for a swim without having all these Pokémon fussing over him...But mainly because he missed his father, another Lugia like himself (and the actual Lugia from all the legends).

Even though the nice Lapras that ruled over the reef where he lived had said he would be back soon, he still missed him a lot. He hadn't spent that much time him so far. He was there when he hatched, and spent the first two months with his life with him, teaching how to swim and walk on land, and the basics of flying (But he still couldn't fly properly yet).

But then, he had told him that he was going away for a long time, and that while he was gone, he was leaving him the care of this nice Lapras that was the king of a whole Coral Reef of Pokémon.

Still...It wasn't that he didn't like his temporary carers...It was just that he missed his dad.-

Lapras: <Do you want to go back now, sweetie?>

Lugia: <Um, just a bit longer?>

Lapras: <Okay. A few more minutes, then.>

Guard!Lanturn: <Urgh, really? I'd better get payed overtime for this.>

Guard!Crawdunt: <Hey!> -Pinches the Lanturn.- <That's our god's son we're defending here! And if he wants to swim a bit longer, he'll swim a bit longer! Understood?!>

Guard!Lanturn: <Okay! Okay...Ow...>

-Nearby, another Water type Pokémon watched, trying his best to keep out of sight, least he be spotted before he was ready.

This Pokémon was much more familiar though. It had blue scales, a distinctive yellow Cheveron shape on his stomach, a large yellow jaw ringed with sharp fangs, bulky legs and arms, a thick tail, and striking red ridges on his head, back, and tail.

The Feraligatr watched the group closely, paying special attention to their escort. His target. If he could get that Lugia, they're plans would all fall into place.

...Then maybe he could be with her again.

The Crocodillian Pokémon clasped a hand around the well worn Pokéball hanging around his neck, a reminder of happier times long by. Times that he could possibly return to if he managed to do this.

It was now or never.

Silently, the Feraligatr began to swim towards the patrol...-
