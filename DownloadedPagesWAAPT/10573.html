Challenger's Cave, Secluded Cavern

-Herbert is lying in the cave next to Alice, a thoughtful expression on his face as he idly observes the Pokemon running, flying and swimming around the cave. Kirk, Bishop, Maul, Wrex and Elan are with them, similarly idle.-

Alice: ...So, why haven't you left yet?

Herbert: Huh?

Alice: With the rifts closed, Deimos arrested and Titan fled, you've got no reason to stay here. I expected you to be all too eager to leave this place. Go back to Sinnoh, or perhaps head to Orre in search of Titan. But you haven't. So why are you still here?

Herbert: Um, well...

Alice: It's because of them, isn't it?

Herbert: Well... yeah. I mean, they're my friends, after all. Hell, they're almost like a second family to me. A big, screwed-up family whose members sometimes can't get along and always have issues to deal with, but family nonetheless. I've always needed friends to rely on, like you, and ever since we stopped travelling together I-

Alice: Don't you think this is rather sudden, though?

Herbert: What do you mean?

Alice: Well, you have only known this J-Team for two and a half months, and already you are declaring them family.

Herbert: Yeah, well... a lot has happened in those three months. I've starred in a movie, been mauled by a Girafarig, hit on a girl who turned out to be a guy, actually turned into a girl, learnt how to use Aura, gotten myself tangled up in a love triangle, killed a Bisharp, failed to beat the Unova League... and I get the feeling things are only going to get weirder from here. There's some things that are impossible to share with people without becoming friends, and I've experienced a laundry list of them over the last two and a half months.

Alice: ...I see. So you are staying with them?

Herbert: Yes. Wherever they go, I go.

-There is a long silence.-

Herbert: ...You could come with us, you know.

Alice: I'm sorry?

Herbert: I know you prefer to work on your own and everything, but you can't do this on your own. Nobody could. And the J-Team could always use new members.

Alice: Well, I'm not interested. They may be a most intriguing group of individuals, with some highly unusual members and abilities amongst their ranks. And they may well be some of the strongest trainers I've ever seen. But I don't need friends.

Herbert: ...You never were very good at lying, Alice.

-Herbert gives a sigh, and closes his eyes.-

Herbert: I used to think I didn't need anyone but you, as well. And we were happy together, for a while. I improved over the four years we were travelling together, however slowly. I went from an antisocial, aggressive loner who woke up every night screaming his lungs out to someone who could actually talk to people. And travelling with the J-Team... it kind of reminds me of all the adventures we had together.

-He opens his eyes, looks over at her, and smiles.-

Herbert: I miss you.

Alice: ...Very well. I will consider your offer.

Herbert: Thank you.

Red!Herbert: So, are we going to beat him bloody like he deserves?

Yellow!Herbert: Do not underestimate the Aspect, friend. Words are the one weapon the Aspect has left, and they bite Herbert deeper than any sword. All we can do now is depend on his confidence... and his friends.
