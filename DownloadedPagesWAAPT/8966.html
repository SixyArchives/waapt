Walking down a hallway, Jack was surprised when he was ambushed from behind by an interesting couch. Which was then nowhere to be seen when he took a second look.

PEFE, room with doors

Stepping through the wrong door, Jack found himself being flung about out of one door and into another.

Jack: "Someone stop this crazy thing!"

PEFE, yet somewhere else

It was a maze, as far as he could tell. Which meant he'd have to investigate further. Except he didn't want to, and so called out Runt and got them both teleported out of it.

Only to discover, once they were free, that things looked bigger than they had before Jack had gone in.

Much bigger. Or so it appeared, with the truth soon being revealed as Jack and Runt being much smaller than they had been when Jack first entered the maze.
