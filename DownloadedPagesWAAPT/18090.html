Grodus: <You certainly don't sound fine.>

Squealer: <...>

<I guess I'm conflicted about some parts of this.>

Grodus: <...What's that supposed to mean?>

Squealer: <...>

Sorry, TEC...

<I... heard that in order to reestablish the Alakagross Society, human society needed to be destroyed.>

Grodus: <...>

<Who did you hear this from?>

Squealer: <I... forget.>

Grodus: <Hm...>

<Well, it's true that we considered this necessary, but perhaps it might not be.>

Squealer: <...What do you mean?>

Grodus: <Well, you would be the one who has the power to rebuild the society, so ultimately, it's up to you.>

Squealer: <...It is?>

Grodus: <Indeed. While it is true human society at large would likely be unwilling to recognize a society of Pokemon better than theirs, perhaps you could use your power to convince them.>

Squealer: <Oh? I could do that?>

Grodus: <Certainly, by whatever means you wish. Friendship... Intimidation... Making examples of some->

Squealer: <...Not that.>

Grodus: <Again, it would be up to you.>

<Which is why it is instrumental you understand what the X-Naut regime's goals would be so you can use this power in the best way possible.>

Squealer: <Hm, fine.>

<But still, you're going to be fighting against Maggie, something might...>

Grodus: <If you are concerned about that, then I can make sure nothing happens.>

Squealer: <You can?>

Grodus: <Certainly.>

-He waves his staff and a display appears before him, which is broadcast to all affiliated with th e X-Nauts, somehow-

Grodus: <Attention all X-Nauts. I have good news.>

<We have convinced the Emboar to use the power that sleeps behind the Hundred Thousand Year Door to rebuild the Alakagross Society.>

<In addition, we have also learned that the seal keeping the door shut this whole time is weakening. Regardless of whether we gather the Stars in that time, we will be going through the door and claiming the power that lies within it for ourselves.>

<You are also to prevent Shirley, Thwap, Goodfellow, and anyone associated with them from getting the other half of the Crystal Stars.>

<Though, I must make something clear: As of now, they are to be dealt with without serious harm. If any of them are to be harmed, it will be by the Emboar's hand.>

<Is that understood?>

<Then get back to work!>

-He waves the staff and the transmission ends-

Grodus: <Was that sufficient?>

Squealer: <...Yeah, thanks.>

Grodus: <Then, is there anything else you wished to discuss?>

-Squealer shakes his head-

Squealer: <No, uh, Sir Grodus.>

<I'll go then.>

Hopefully TEC isn't too put off by the sudden turnaround.

-He starts to leave, but turns around, does the X-Salute, then proceeds to leave again-

-Once the door is closed, Grodus starts speaking to himself-

Grodus: <My, he certainly is easily swayed.>

<If I weren't fully committed to this goal, I might feel guilty for doing this.>
