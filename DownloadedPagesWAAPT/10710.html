<WHY DO PEOPLEMONS KEEP SNEAKING UP ON ME?!>

Dragonite: <Whoa, relax! I didn't mean to startle you!>

Stratus: <...Oh.>

<Come on! I'll introduce you to everyone! Starting with me. My name's Nacre. What about you?>

Stratus: <It's Stratus! But if you'll just let me finish->

Stratus: <Yeah, crazy. But I really need to tell yo->

Stratus: <What have I gotten myself into?!>
