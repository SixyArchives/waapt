141804 Luke9243rd Mar 2012 08:26:18 AM Get RP Mod

Route 37 - Johto

Lake — <Come on, admit it.>

Phyco — Narrows his eyes. <Admit what?>

Lake — Smirks. <That you didn't find Ecruteak ugly?>

The Kingdra rolls his eyes, turning away from Lake dismissively.

Phyco — <Sorry, but human architecture will never be able to match the beauty of a nature, no matter how hard they try otherwise.>

Lake says nothing, simply letting out a small sigh. It was as if seemingly nothing could hit Phyco's seemingly insurmountable standards.

"Can't you at least admit that it was beautiful in it's own way?" Luke added, pausing momentarily from harvesting some Apricorns from a nearby tree. "Sure, it may not be beautiful in the same sense as...say, a coral reef, but it can still stand out by it's own merits, no?"

Phyco responds by blowing some bubbles of discontent.

Phyco — <Sorry, but I just can't get exited about a bunch of wooden boxes, even if they are nicely decorated.>

Luke says nothing, simply going back to trying to pick some Blue Apricorns from the tree.

"Well, if that's how you feel, I won't try to change it."

Phyco — Nods. <Thank you. Now, if you don't mind, I have a question of my own.>

Lake lets out a yawn, lying down on the grass as he glances at the Kingdra.

Lake — <Before you ask: No. I'm still not going to teach you Bubble Shield.>

Phyco — Glares at Lake. <That wasn't the question. And for the record, I still don't see what harm it would do if you taught it to me. Unless you enjoy it when my bubble is burst by anything prodding it too hard?>

Lake — <Well...It is kinda' funny...> Chuckles lightly.

Phyco shuts his eyes, shaking his head.

Phyco — <Whatever, we're going off on a tangent here.> Glances at Luke. <The main thing I want to ask is...well, what are we doing exactly?>

Luke blinks at Phyco as he manages to dislodge a Blue Apricorn from the tree, bending down to place it in his Apricorn Case.

"We're making our way to Cherrygrove City to meet with Silent, remember?"

Phyco — Nods. <Yes, I remember that. But what do you plan to do after that?>

"Well, I'm planning on catching some more Pokémon. It isn't really safe travelling with only two Pokémon with only two types between them. That, and I really need something that can fly or Teleport."

Phyco — <And what do you plan on doing once you have more party members?>

"...Er..."

Luke pauses, unsure how to answer that question. Thankfully, Lake has something to add.

Lake — <We plan to meet up with the rest of our friends at some point. Most likely once they've cleared up that mess in Sinnoh.>

Phyco — <Oh?>

The Kingdra swims over to Lake, but keeps his distance somewhat. He had a habit of popping his bubble for "The LO Lz" as people up here on the surface apparently like to say. With such terrible use of their own language, Phyco wondered how they had ever become the dominant species on this planet, but that was another matter entirely.

<And what do you and your friends do together?>

Lake — <Well...we travel around the world...>

Phyco — <And why do you do that? Are you explorers?>

Lake — Shrugs. <Some of us are, I guess? Gene for example.>

Phyco blinks, looking somewhat lost.

Phyco — <Who?>

Lake — Looks awkward. <Oh, you haven't met him yet, have you? Gene's a Sandslash.>

Phyco — <A Sandwhat?>

"He's like Dintel, but bigger and sharper, and slightly more mature."

Phyco — <...I see. These friends of yours then, you're explorers?>

"...Sort of? We're actually all Pokémon Trainers, but that seems to take a bit of a back-seat to what we end up doing most of the time."

Phyco — <And what is that?>

Luke looks somewhat awkward at this.

"Er...usually, we end up fighting against something or another to save the local region, or the world or the universe, at one point from danger, often with the help of the local legendary Pokémon..."

Phyco pauses, looking somewhat bemused by his trainer's statement.

Phyco — <...I find it rather hard to believe that is what you actually do, but I'll take your word for it for the time being.>

Lake — <It's okay. We get that a lot.>

Phyco — Nods. <Well, then let me ask you two: do you enjoy doing it? And is it what you want to do?>

The human and Feraligatr glancing at each other, a somewhat doubtful look on their faces.

"Er..."

Lake — <I would if we weren't in so much danger all the time...>

"I'll admit it can get somewhat...frustrating to have to deal with the things we go through, sometimes."

Lake — <It does happen an awful lot.>

Phyco gives the two a slightly concerned look.

Phyco — <You both seem to have some doubts about this...perhaps a rethink is in order here?>

"...Perhaps..."

Lake — <Yeah...>
