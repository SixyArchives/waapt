And so, I begin my studies. I will do what it takes to become a member of the J-Team. I will uncover the truth, and when I do, oh, that glorious day when I do, who knows what doors will be opened?

I will frequently be updating my findings and studies. Make of them what you will. My investigation begins, brothers and sisters.

~Jaxon
