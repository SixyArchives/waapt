Oh... oh, right. The beach.

Well, when I was younger my father was always ill. I had to stay around and do the man's work because my mother was too busy with the house work, and my dad couldn't do much with rheumatism and cancer ailing him. So most of my childhood was work and wrestling.

Then one day my dad felt well enough to take me away from tending the Torchic and walked with me to a beach way far north. There was this secret path on the island no one had heard of or walked through, and it was kinda overgrown, so I was always worried about him and wanted to go back because I didn't want him to get hurt. But eventually we made it to the beach that he wanted to show me.

It was completely empty, but the sand was pure white and there were a lot of water-type pokemon swimming in the ocean, or flying up above in the Wingull's case, or sunbathing with some Marill. Then some Wingull flew down and my dad let me play with them. I couldn't stay there for long though, because my dad started coughing up a storm and we had to go back.

After he died about two years into my journey or so, my mother asked me to come home and she took me to the same beach he did, telling me that this was where my dad took her on their first date. A couple of Wingull flew down to us and I played with them a little. Now every time I go near the ocean I try to find a Wingull or two, just to remind myself that my dad's out there somewhere, but in a better place...

But I don't see any right now.
