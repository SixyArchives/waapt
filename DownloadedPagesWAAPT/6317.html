It seemed that Skye wasn't going to wake up for a while. The mind-reading must have really tired her out or something. In the meantime, Lake decided to take a look around the restored Ruins of Alph until she was awake.

It seemed as if the rebuilt ruins had been turned into a settlement of sorts, similar to that of Bluescale, if a bit smaller. Several chambers seemed to have been converted into housing or businesses such as shops by the local Pokémon, which consisted of members of the Mareep flocks, Smeargle, Natu, Xatu, and all sorts of Psychic Pokémon that Lake assumed had been drawn here by the strong Psychic presence of the Unown in the area.

Speaking of which, there were quite a few Unown around. Even if the ruins had been restored, they obviously still made their home here. That said, it was quite odd to see them mingling with other species of Pokémon. With a few exceptions (T, for example), most Unown kept to themselves.

Coming to a stop outside a particularly large chamber, Lake recognized it as home to one of the four puzzles needed that he and Luke had solved when they were exploring the ruins on their journey.

Feeling slightly curious, the Feraligatr pokes his head inside the door to look inside, just to see if anything had changed.
