Hearthome, sometime after the armband incident

- Chrome is sitting by himself when Xavier approaches-

Xavier: You OK, buddy?

Chrome: -sighs- To be honest? No.

Xavier: Well, my date with Styx last night went well, at least... but what's bothering you is her brother, isn't it?

Chrome: I'm worried. He's a nice guy, and I'm worried what we have to tell him will break his spirit...

Xavier: Well, he's High Preist of a Legendary, so...

Chrome: Don't talk to me about legendaries.

Xavier: You know, you should really stop that grudge...

Chrome: YOU HAVE NO IDEA WHAT THEY'VE DONE TO ME, ALAKAZAM!

- pause. -

Chrome: I'm sorry... I've had a long, rough life...

Xavier: If you don't mind me asking... can you show me? So I can understand?

Chrome: ...Alright.

- They link psychically, and Chrome starts beaming memories to Xavier-

Thousands of years ago

- Four Beldum are flying through the charred remains of a massive, but very unhumanlike city-

Beldum 1: Hurry! Before they see...

Beldum 2: Eeek!

-They all see Lugia passing overhead. -

Beldum 3: He's seen us! Hide!

- They fly onward, with Lugia following close behind. They reach a labyrinth of hidden buildings and hide in it. -

Beldum 4: Maybe we're safe here...

- Lugia pokes his head around a corner, but does not see them. -

Beldum 1: We're doomed...

- Suddenly, Lugia hears a loud noise. He flies off. A scream is heard shortly afterwards, but he does not return.-

Beldum 2: T-That was close...

- Suddenly, Darkrai appears. -

Darkrai: Well, what do we have here?

- the Beldums cower -

Darkrai: Hmph. It's not good sport, disposing of kids, but someone has to do it... -Charges a Dark Pulse-

???: Not on your life, Darkrai.

- Skymin leaps in front of the Beldums. -

Skymin: Touch them, and you'll see why everyone fears my Air Slash.

Darkrai: I thought we were on the same side?

Skymin: Not for things like this. They're children.

Darkrai: Fine. -floats off-

Skymin: -sighs and turns to the Beldums- I'd ask you guys if you were alright, but... this isn't alright.

Beldum 3: Why are the gods doing this, miss Shaymin? Why are they angry?

Skymin: I didn't want to do this. None of us did. But some of your people - as well as the Alakazam - tried messing with forces they shouldn't have. And we had no other choice. But I don't want you kids to get hurt.

Beldum 4: Thank you for saving us, Miss Shaymin.

Skymin: -smiles- It's no problem. You guys stay safe, okay?

- she flies off-

A few hundred years later

- Two Metang - fusions of the Beldum from earlier - are watching tribal humans and their Pokemon from afar. -

Metang 1: They look like they have potential...

Metang 2: What are you talking about? They have no claws, no elemental powers, nothing. I would be surprised if they lasted a century in this world...

About 1000 years ago

-the two Metang have fused into a Metagross, and are talking with a man -

Metagross: The Tree of Beginning? You're going THERE and leaving me behind? Why?

Aaron: Metagross, you're one of my closest friends, aside from Lucario. I don't want you getting hurt.

Metagross: Are you sure you know what you're doing?

-Aaron nods-

Meatgross: ...Fine. But be careful.

- Aaron walks out of the room. -

Aaron: Perhaps I should have told him the truth...

A few days later

- Skymin flies in through a castle window into the room where the Metagross is staying. -

Metagross: S-Shaymin! What are you doing here?

Skymin: Sir Aaron and Lucario are dead.

Metagross: What?!

Skymin: -hangs her head sadly- I'm sorry.

- The Metagross is speechless... Then grows furious. -

Metagross: <The Mew at the Tree... He could have solved the problem himself! He tricked them!>

Skymin: <Wait, Metagross, you don't understand!>

-The Metagross, unfortunately, is already gone-

A few days later

- Metagrosss arrive at Mew's cave-

Metagross: Mew! You're going to pay!

Mew: Calm down, will ya? I would've been toast if that guy hadn't stepped in.

Skymin: That's no reason for you to let them die!

Mew: I repaid him in full! He's on a reincarnation cycle right now!

Metagross: A what?

Mew: A reincarnation cycle. He'll be reborn in a few hundred years. You might see him then.

Metagross: That's still no excuse to view him as disposable!

- Mew dissapears. Metagross sighs in resignation-

700 years ago

- The Metagross is talking with some other Metagross and some Alakazam-

Protag!Metagross: But why do you need me for this? I don't even like legendaries!

Alakazam: Exactly. The humans won't suspect us.

Protag!Metagross: And why are you using humans? Wouldn't our kind be better?

Other!Metagross: You're forgetting that humans haven't made quite as bad mistakes as we did back in our "heyday"...

Protag!Metagross: I still don't feel right about this...

Other!Metagross: We're freinds here. And you're the only one here with enough experience with humans to take care of that girl.

-Protag!Metagross thinks of the girl and suddenly feels a twang of pity.-

Protag!Metagross: ...Fine. I accept.

619 years ago

- the Metagross is searching for something -

Metagross: She has to be around here somewhere...

-He comes across a young red-headed girl trying to fight off a swarm of ghosts feeding on her-

Girl: Back! -slices with a Shadow Claw-

- The Metagross Meteor mashes the swarm, scaring it off-

Lina: Thank you, mister Metagross! I'm Lina!

Metagross: <You're welcome.>

Lina: Can you be my Metagross and help stop the ghosts?

Metagross: <I don't see why not.>

Lina: Yay! -hugs the Metagross- I'll call you Chrome! :3

About 80 years ago

- Lina and Chrome are standing outside a bar in Unova -

Lina: -slurps her beer- I'm just glad I can drink this in public again...

Chrome: <Lina?>

Lina: Yes, Chrome?

Chrome: <Have I been a good friend to you all these years?>

Lina: -Smirks- That's like asking if rain is wet. -hugs him- You're great, Chrome.

Present

Chrome: That's my story.

Xavier: Wow. You have been through a lot...

Chrome: Things are better now. -sighs- I just worry if that will change when we tell Umbra the truth...
