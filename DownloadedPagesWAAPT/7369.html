Luke's Module

Although it's made of the same plain white panels of the vast majority of other modules in the PEFE, Luke's one has the appearance of being very well lived in, giving it a rather homey feel. Although not terribly large, it was still rather spacious.

Close to the entrance was a blue couch and some chairs surrounding a fluffy, also blue, rug. There were sat across from the wall, upon which was mounted a rather large HD televison, which was hooked up to Wii console, a stack of games - including the latest in the Capumon, Sonic the Sandslash, and Kirby series - sitting next to it.

On the back wall of the module was where a desk with a comfy office chair sitting next to it, presumably where Luke did all of his work. On it sat a rather fancy looking computer...which was currently in pieces.

Luke: "...Why is my computer in pieces?"

Comet: <Er...>

It's at that point that from under the desk pops a Cubone, but instead of wielding a bone club, like other of his kind, he was carrying a rather large metal wrench.

Scrap the Cubone♂: <Where's that motherboard gone?>

He then happens to notice the new arrivals.

<Luke! You're back! How funny, me and Ad were fixing your computer for you!>

Upon the mention of his name, what appears to be a digital hummingbird emerges from the computer screen.

Ad Min: Salutations, sir! You truly are a sight for damaged optical sensors! As are you, Lake and Stratus!

Luke: "...Thank you, Ad. We'll come back to you in a little bit..."

A door on the right wall lead into the kitchen, which housed all the necessary appliances, but was otherwise unremarkable.

There were three doors on the left wall. One leaded to what looked like Luke's bedroom, which was also decorated with a blue carpet, a smaller television, and neat bed with - you guessed it - blue covers. There were also quite a few Pokémon plushies displayed proudly on shelves on the wall.

The other room leaded to what looked like another bedroom, only one that looked like it had been used by several Pokémon. At the moment, it was mostly empty, apart from a nest that was occupied by a snoozing Braviary.

Unite: <Zzz...Woolly...>

The final door lead to a bathroom, which was like most other bathrooms except for a rather large bathtub, which looked like it could fit even a Feraligatr comfortably.

Finally, there was another door on the back wall next to Luke's desk, which Lake seemed curious at the sight of.

Lake: <I don't remember that being there...>

Luke: "Oh, I had that added in recently. We'll take a look through it in a minute."

Lake: <Oh. Still, good to be back after all this time.>
