Deep within the Nidoran Colony

Silent has been hidden in one of the deeper parts of the forest for pretty much the entire day, away from Maekrite, from Tracer and the others, in a place where a sole Nidorino roams around making sure his needed peace is not disturbed.

He feels worried, sad, guilty and depressed because of RL cat something, perhaps a fear of losing Inka as she has grown old.

Fortunately Inka is still there though. She's half-curled in Silent's arms while the Researcher rests against a tree, letting herself be petted and returning some affection because she knows her former Trainer is worried.

Not a word has been said in a couple of hours.

"Inka?" Silent finally asks.

"<Ah?>" The Nidorina looks up to her Trainer whose sight is fixated on the horizon.

"Do you remember Milena?" Silent asks absentmindedly.

Inka's ears flicker for a moment, she senses her Trainer feels tired, fed up of something, but she can't realize exactly of what. After a moment of hesitation and of examining her own mind in search of memories of adventures, she shakes her body a bit.

"<Do you?>"

Silent sighs. "I do... I just wonder... for how long I will."

In normal circumstances Inka would stand up, let out a growl and teach her trainer and set his mind straight, the same way she has taught her kits when they were worried about their lives. This time she simply lets out a hiss and digs her claws in Silent's arms; she knows the message will get through.

"<Will you care?>"

Silent takes a moment to parse the question, the signification of the future tense, then answers with a blunt, half-hissed yes. For an answer, Inka simply smiles and curls herself into a spiky ball.

"<Then there's your answer, >" she states before delivering herself to sleep, to ease her Trainer's own mood.
