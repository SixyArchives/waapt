Summerland Pokémon Center

Sakura is currently in her room with Teishi drinking tea with him, she was still in the Shingeko costume, except without the helmet.

"You know, Teishi, meeting you ended changing my whole life." said Sakura, smiling

"<H-Hm?>" he seemed both confused and curious.

"It was you and your team that inspired me to do this, alongside Hikari, and you guys also trained me. I never felt so in peace before, so... Thank you for everything."

"<Oh, uh...>" Teishi looked quite embarrassed "<Well, meeting you changed everything for me too. Like... I'm a lot taller and cooler now, and you also reunited me with dad, I'm also a lot stronger and have a lot of friends because you took care of me and let me recruit others in the first place. I think I owe you more than you owe me, Sakura.>"

"We both helped each other in very life-changing ways, I think we're even."

"<Still, you did so much for me... That's why I can't allow myself to disappoint you, that's... A good part of the reason I want to become a master ninja now.>"

Sakura giggles "You're a pretty cute kid, you know?"

"<I... I am?>" he asked, he seemed to be pretty much shrinking in embarrasment.

"Yep, in fact I think your cheerful nature was one of the things that helped me recover from all the trauma I went through in Orre." said Sakura, then giving Teishi a hug.

Teishi hugs back "<Um... Good to know I helped then!>" he said, though in reality he was in a bit of a loss.

Sakura breaks the hug "So, master ninja, how are things going with your father?"

Teishi rubs the back of his head "<... Dad showed me, and all of us for that matter, just how much more practice we need.>"

Sakura giggles "Basically he beat all of you guys up without breaking a sweat."

"<Come on! Don't put it like that! It's embarrassing...>"

Sakura just giggles again and lets the topic rest.

"<... So, why are you in costume? It's still morning.>"

"Oh, I just feel good wearing this, it makes me so happy." Sakura gives a warm smile "Besides, it's pretty comfortable, Hikari sure knows how to make clothes."

Teishi gives Sakura a cheerful look "<I guess we're all really enjoying this whole hero business.>"

"Yes, it's been very refreshing and enjoyable to help others and making the place safer, so I sure am going to enjoy this happiness while it lasts!"

Teishi chuckles, but then starts thinking more about it and looks a bit saddened "<While it lasts, huh...>" he thought

Sakura raises an eyebrow "Is something wrong?" she asked in a concerned tone.

Teishi snaps back and shakes his head "<I'm fine, it's nothing too big, really!>" he then drinks some tea while looking cheerful.

Sakura tilts his head slightly "... Well, all right then..."

She then goes back to drinking tea herself.
