"You know, forget that, we shouldn't let that bother us, human or not, it's just a change of looks. You never stopped being a Pokémon, you're my boyfriend now and that's not going to change, we'll just continue as we usually do."

"Yep. Just don't expect me to give you a hug or kiss you anytime soon while you're in this form, the former because it's potentially dangerous thanks to your badly positioned spike and the latter because... That would be just too weird, even for me."

Meanwhile, a certain Deerling was hiding from the couple just to see what they were talking about.

"We both know you're there." said both of them, still looking up and eating some popcorn, rather casually.

"<Eep!>" said the Deerling, embarrassed.
