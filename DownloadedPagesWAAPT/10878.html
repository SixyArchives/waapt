Lyuri then looked up as well "You know, I would never expect to be dating someone who is shorter than me, I prefer taller guys." she said in a playful tone

"We both know you're there." said both of them, still looking up and eating some popcorn, rather casually.

"<Eep!>" said the Deerling, embarrassed.
