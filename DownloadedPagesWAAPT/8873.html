UTINNI!

Somewhere North of Kanto/Johto – Now

With only rumors and speculation of the Alakazam and Metagross civilization to go on – or as it been referred to a few times, the “Alakagross” – Jack was forced to search for solid signs of them the old fashion way. The best he had was reports of where any remaining ruins ‘’might’’ have been, and even that was questionable, especially given how many people over the years had no doubt done searches of their own with nothing to show for it.

The closest reports he could find pointed to “somewhere” north of the joint Kanto/Johto region. “Somewhere” being a very vague term that did little to narrow things down. Even attempts by Runt to try to use his psychic powers to try to hone in on where they might have been had been for naught, though that had admittedly been a long shot anyway.

So instead, Jack decided to focus on areas where the ruins would likely be most difficult for him to find. Most of this travel had been on riding on top of Groot’s head. While he ‘’could’’ have chosen to have Runt teleport him around, that would have been unwieldy. Out here, Runt wouldn’t be able to teleport anywhere without having a real sense of his surroundings. Unlike most civilized areas, neither he nor his Abra had much idea as to the surroundings.

Occasionally he had taken Runt out of his pokeball to see if the Abra had any new leads, but it was to no avail, and the pokemon had been content to spend most his time catching up on his sleep.

As they traveled along, Jack could hear the sound of howling. He couldn’t make out the exact species, but guessed it was probably something like a Houndoom or a Mightyena. Despite this, and despite the wild terrain they were traversing, Jack wasn’t feeling too worried. Jack wasn’t looking to pick any fights with any of the wildlife out here, and the wildlife was usually content not to pick a pointless fight with a man riding around on the head of a giant, rocky snake-like creature in turn.
