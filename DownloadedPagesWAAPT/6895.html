White Wafers - PMD-A

Sometime later, the party of three find themselves trekking across the snowy expanse of White Wafers, a seemingly endless frosty plain, dotted with a few frozen trees, with a what appeared to be completely snowed over volcanoes (or were they geysers?) looming in the far distance, all lying under a deep pink sky.

Comet: <You know...all these places we've been to so far have been a real source of eye-candy.>

She pauses briefly, a frown crossing her face.

<Kinda' hard to appreciate it when we're fighting feral Pokémon while preforming complex acrobatic feats, though.>

Treeo nods in agreement, before turning back to gaze at the beautiful landscape spread out before them.

Treeo: <It's a shame, it's all so beautiful. As if we've wandered into an abstract painting.>

The Sceptile takes a deep breath, breathing a happy sigh.

<We should really find the time to just stop and look around more often. It seems a shame that we're getting to visit such fantastic places and not taking a few moments to appreciate them.>

Phyco also takes a few moments to observe their surroundings, his expression remaining neutral as he does so.

Phyco: <It's still no match for the marvel that is the ocean...>

He pauses, looking away from Comet and Treeo briefly.

<But I'll admit...it's beautiful in his own way.>

Turning back to the two, he nods his head forwards, urging them onwards.

<Come on, if we hang around too long, my bubble might freeze.>

Comet: <Okay, okay. We're coming...>

And thus, they continues onwards further into the frozen plains, Treeo shading his eyes with a claw as he scans the horizon.

Treeo: <Hmm...no sign of Mezzo or Every yet...>

He turns to look at the Treecko riding on his back.

<Can you see them anywhere, Nazz?>
