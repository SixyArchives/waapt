Goldenrod City

Maekrite returns to the PEFE building located... somewhere there. He suddenly starts complaining about being purple, not pink, but then decides Manly Nidorino Wear Pink. After drinking something, he goes see Silent who is preparing for a trip downtown tomorrow. Shahinne, Lileep, Algira and Copahue are there as well.

Copahue — <Hello there....> — waves a wing to Maekrite.

'Maekrite — <Hey all... hey Lileep... what is going on?>

Shahinne -peeks out from under Copahue's wing — <Travel tomorrow! Yippie!~>

Lileep -anchored to the desk — <That air-dweller is so happy it annoys me.>

Algira -buzzes joy — <You'll get used to it.>

Silent turns to check his Pokémon. "It's not a travel, it's just a trip downtown to buy stuff. How are your parents doing, Maekrite?"

Maekrite -sighs — <They're fine... Mom nagged me a lot.>

"That's good." Silent chuckles, then observes Ichtacya arrive as well. "It means she cares about you. Anyway, I am buying the last things I need to set up Inka's new burrow, I just hope she likes it."

Lileep — <Wait, why are you organizing the Pokémon's burrow again?> — waves tentacles inquisitively — <Can't that Pokémon organize it on its own>

Algira — <It's a good gesture, Lileep...> — the Masquerain flies around to come to rest on Silent's head — <also an excuse for being away for so long...>

Ichtacya — <Wait what? Who's been away long?>

Algira and Copahue point to Silent.

Maekrite -drops ears — <Ugh.... I'll have to tidy up an entire forest...>

"So, we should have this ready tomorrow, and we'll take the time to go walk around, so that you can enjoy the city. Then," Silent checks his mail and some of the PEFE BBS reports, "we are probably going to Headquarters..."

Algira — <Wait what? Why can't we go see the others?>

Silent turns around and takes the Masquerain off his head.

"Well I don't know the others but it is very likely that if we go there, you'll only be able to see the others for a day or two. They're always moving too fast."

Ichtacya -drops ears — <Awww, that's a shame...>

Silent's Pokémon start organizing.

Shahinne -chirps angrily — <I want to show Dad my Trophy~!>

Ichtacya — <I wanted to see Icicle. And Rascal. Aqua too! Oh and perhaps get to meet Sunny...> — looks at her paw and starts enumerating. — <...Am I missing anymon?>

Algira — <B-but I want to go spend time with Helios!>

Copahue — <...wonder if Harriet is bored? Or knowing my luck, that Sceptile must be entertaining her...> — ruffles plumage nervously.

"Guys calm down, calm down..."

Lileep — <So you want to go around and see friends?>

Maekrite -tilts head — <I'd love to see Teala, actually...>

Algira — <HELIOS!!!> — peck Silent.

Shahinne — <DAD!!!>

Ichtacya — <RASCAL!!!> — pulls Silent's shoes.

Copahue — <...Harriet...>

...

...

...

Silent's mons look around.

Ichtacya — <Uhm... I feel like somemon is missing...>

Algira -flutters around — <Shouldn't Dintel be here crying for "Vlad"?>

Maekrite — <What,> — walks in — <wasn't he walking around the building or something?>

"Hm, I thought he was sleeping?" Silent goes retrieve Dintel's ball, he tosses it to the ground and it opens, revealing nothing. "...Weird. I haven't seen him."

Ichtacya — <Rrrr.... he was pretty sleepy... but haven't seen him ever since...>

After Silent fetches Dintel's ball again to leave it on the desk, Shahinne flies and sits besides the Pokéball, observing it curiously.

"I haven't seen Dintel leave, perhaps it would be better if we ask the others... Algira, Maekrite, could you ask the mons around, please?"

Algira spins nervously, then goes to sit on Maekrite's head.

Algira — <We'll go, yes, but this'll be short. Uhm, Copahue, you comin'?> — waits for the Fearow to look at her — <There's this fun Xatu you should meet...>

Copahue — <Uhm, is this a good time?> — points with wing at Shahinne and Dintel's ball. — <I can catch up with Shahinne later if you want.>

Maekrite — <If we're lucky you won't have to. Let's go...> — turns tail and walks away, taking Algira with him.

Silent picks some things up.

"I'm gonna go ask the Interns. Perhaps that shrew is just chewing wires somewhere..." Silent notices Shahinne pecking Dintel's ball. "Come on, Shahinne, leave that ball alone."

Shahinne -pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks — <Mean mon!> — pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks.

Copahue -walks in — <No, lil'bird...> — brushes Shahinne's head with his wing — <Don't say that... Dintel is just... well...> — beat — <quirky. Or something.>

Shahinne -narrows eyes — <Mean mon.> — continues pecking.

Copahue warns Shahinne that he'll be waiting outside, then he leaves. Silent recalls Ichtacya and Lileep and decides to go ask the interns as well.

Shahinne stays alone on the desk, glaring at Dintel's Pokéball and examining from every angle.

After a while, she starts pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking pecking it again.

Shahinne -pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks, then glares — <Mean mon!> — pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks pecks — <Mean mon took Dintel away!>
