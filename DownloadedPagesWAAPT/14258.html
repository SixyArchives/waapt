Stalagmite: <Quite obviously not. I would've hope you'd be intelligent enough to realise that.>

-Shrugging off the damage done by the Dark Pulse, she swings at Greninja with a Razor Leaf, knocking him off the countertop and to the floor.-

Shard: Excellent work, Stalagmite.

Leonard: As if I'd let myself be so easily bested by some common mugger! This isn't nearly over yet!

Leonard: Oof!

Stalagmite: <It seems that your reaction times haven't been honed to the degree that mine have. Mental conditioning is important, you know.>

Shard: Not letting you do that again. Don't want to risk battle with professional trainer. Would rather end this as quickly as possible.

-As Leonard attempts to heave himself to his feet, Shard delivers a sharp kick to his ribs and sends him sprawling to the floor again.-

Shard: Simply require Key Stone for own purposes. Would say this is nothing personal, but would be lying. Find men like you dispicable. Embody everything wrong with Kalos. Believe that inherited fortunes and obsolete titles somehow place you above others. Lost in your own imaginary world that exists only to serve your lust for wealth and pleasure. Eagerly await the day you will be brought down to the level of those you sneer at. Until then, forced to tolerate parasites like you.

-She pins Leonard to the ground with a foot and starts gathering a blade of Obscura around her hand as Stalagmite steps forward.-

Shard: Still... I'm sorry it had to reach this point.

-She stabs the Obscura blade through Leonard's shoulder at the same time as Stalagmite stomps down on his other arm with a dry crunch, causing him to cry out in pain. Shard takes advantage of his helplessness to rid him of his Mega Ring in one swift motion.-

Shard: There. This should do. Should also ensure you are unable to use anything similar for a while.

-She recalls Stalagmite and turns away as Leonard weakly props himself up into a sitting position.-

Leonard: I'll... ack... I'll have you put away for this, you-

Shard: Unlikely. Don't intend to be caught, and doubt a second confrontation would end well for you. Will be long gone before you pick yourself up anyway.

-With that, she strides away, the shadows congealing around her until they conceal her from view completely. Leonard gives a weak groan and cradles his shattered arm.-
