Bravely Fashionaaabluh
The day had passed without much incident. After a visit to Berry Fields to plant a few berries and pick some in return, the group had passed through the Connecting Cave, finding themselves moving past horde of sleeping Zubat.

Upon exiting the cave, the scent of the sea wafted over everyone, the sight of the far-off city spurring everyone to move with a bit more haste. As they did, the sight of a Mienfoo and a Seviper warding off a few Zangoose became noticeable to Rex, the boy wondering briefly just how Tanith was doing at her new home. Hopefully, she was enjoying it.

Ambrette Town was their next destination, the sight of a Fossil Lab nearby making Rex tingle with anticipation. But he couldn't go and visit there just yet... Ulysses would definitely complain about missing the food festival. And Amanita was definitely getting restless about her Skrelp.

And so they continued on, Rex making a mental note to return once they'd concluded their business. Perhaps he'd bring Aloise for quicker travel.

Cyllage City: Beach

Sometime later, Rex found himself alone on the beach, looking out at the sea in deep thought. The twinkling ruby lights of the Staryu surfing across the water reminded him greatly of Olivine, thoughts of home swirling around his head. He hoped his mother was doing well, his departure having been sudden and unplanned. He really had wanted to stay home and visit a while longer, but it seemed that would have to wait.

Riolu!Kim: <You okay?>

Rex: Hm?

Turning his head to the side, the teen became aware of a tiny wolf tottering towards him, an inquisitive expression on her face.

Rex: You're a Riolu again.

Riolu!Kim: <Well, we don't exactly have working phones here. I had to find you based on your aura.>

Rex: Oh. I, um... I'm sorry.

Moving to his side, the transformed girl plopped down into his lap, much to the male's confusion.

Riolu!Kim: <You don't mind, do you? I don't want to get sand in my fur...>

Offering her a little shrug of his shoulders, Rex leaned backwards and placed his palms on the ground, allowing her to get comfortable without his limbs obstructing her movement. Looking a mixed of pleased and somewhat shy, the Riolu rested her head back against his stomach, folding her legs and looking out at the ocean with him.

No words were exchanged between the two as they sat there in the light of the moon, their eyes looking out at the Staryu that shimmered like the most precious of jewels among the waves.

Riolu!Kim: <The first night we met was just like this, too.>

Smiling a bit at that, Rex nodded, memories of a lost girl from Almia stumbling around in a daze until he'd offered a helping hand.

Rex: I remember.
