Sirocco had nodded off a while earlier, the boy walking on his own after temporarily parting from Melody and Ulysses after a trip to Berry Fields. Given the time, they'd likely be back soon.

Putting his hands into his pockets, Rex hummed a little tune to himself, looking around the small town curiously for any sign of Kim. He hadn't seen her since earlier in the day, and he couldn't help but wonder what she'd gotten up t-

???: <There you are!>

A furry blur of blue suddenly latched onto him, the brunette giving a yelp as a familiar shape made its way up his body and clung to his chest.

Riolu!Kim: <You have to help me! They're driving me crazy!>

Ever since their time in Sinnoh's obscura, it seemed that Kim's own aura had been tainted by the energy that had poured over the land during that time, causing her to transform without the need for the armband.

Rex: Wh-who is?

Riolu!Kim: <The townspeople! I just wanted to get some fresh air and they're always asking me if I'm okay and if I need anything!>

Rex: Aw, come on. They're just being friendly...

Riolu!Kim: <I just want to meditate in peace!>

Rex: Why not just stay in the hotel room, then?

Riolu!Kim: <Because fresh air is bett- why are we having this conversation in public? Just don't let those people know that you've seen me!>

Soon enough, a pair of women approached Rex, looking a bit confused as they noticed him.

Rex: Umm... may I help you?

Woman 1: Well, we noticed that female friend of yours earlier who seemed to be in a rather foul mood.

Woman 2: We were going to see if she wanted a tour of the town to cheer up, but she ended up running away from us.

Rex: Well, I haven't seen Kim for most of the day... but I'll be sure to let her know about your consideration.

Woman 1: On the subject, is there anything we can do for you.

Rex: No, thank you; I'm fine. I was just heading back to the hotel.

Woman 2: Oh... Well, be sure to let us know if you need anything.

The second woman turned her gaze to the furry Pokémon clinging to the boy's clothes, Kim smiling awkwardly despite her transformed state.

Riolu!Kim: <D-don't mind me! I'm just a cute Riolu who lost her way~>

Rex: ...

The two women looked at Rex for a moment before nodding politely, making their way back towards town. Grinning a bit at his friend, Rex carried the human turned Riolu in an arm, making his way towards the hotel.

Rex: You know, you can't pull that off nearly as well as Aqua.

Riolu!Kim: <Oh shut up.>
