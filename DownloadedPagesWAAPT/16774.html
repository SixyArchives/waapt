Kalos, Airport, some time ago

A panting and weary Agent Smith steps up to the check in counter, the men having been apprehended and carted off.

Beat

"Yep, I checked, you aren't.." she stops when she sees Smith's face, growing increasingly redder, his veins popping out; "..Is everything alright?"

"No", he spat out."Everything is most certainly not alright. I will not, cannot, be stopped here. I've been beaten up, mobbed, stolen from, humiliated, had to face a frickin' Legendary over and over and over again and I just had to face off a ground of frickin' POKE TERRORISTS. I have not gone through all that just to be stopped," he slammed his fist on the table, causing the woman to jump back,"''because my name isn't on the DAMN CHECKLIST!!!"

The woman blanched as she checked it again, and said "Wh-wh-wait, did you say Gerald Smith? Yes, right here, seat A3, sorry."

Smith sighed, and after throwing a glare to the place behind him, went forward.

Pueltown Airport, Night

Agent Smith was waiting around, while a jeep pulled up, it's window scrolls down, to reveal Locke, grinning."Hey, Sensei."

Smith smiles with relief as he gets into the shotgun seat, while Locke hurriedly gets out and puts Smith's luggage in the back, before hopping back in the front seat.

They start driving, and Locke turns to Smith and asks, concerned "How are you? I hope it wasn't too troubling, Sensei.." Smith scoffed."Hah! Nothing I couldn't handle, though I profess, I was close to my limit. But.." he pulls out a velvet sack, tied around his neck,"..I hope this was worth it."

Locke smiled as he took the sack, saying "I've done worse for you before, y'know, but really, thank you Sensei." He pulls one out, to admire it, but Smith says sharply "Both hands on the wheel, Locke!"

Locke sticks both of his hands back on as quickly as he could, and he smiles."Well, sir, I'm glad you brought them, I managed to build a Mega Scabbard with it's own sword, so this'll fit great."

Smith raised an eyebrow, impressed."Hmm, was that in my honor, by any chance?" Locke grinned."Mayyybe. Oh, great, we're here."

They pulled up next to the airship, and Smith whistled."Impressive, Locke. This all your base?"

Locke shook his head."Not really, I share it with the other members of the J-Team." Smith looked at him, eyebrows raised."'Other members of the J-Team?'" Locke smiled."Yep, they're a very nice bunch, I hope you get to meet them tomorrow, before you head off. But for now, there's a warm bed with a shower waiting for you, if you stay the night."

Smith sighed in audible happiness, but then stopped himself when he said."Wait, I have to check the security measures first."

Locke patted his back and said "I got that handled, just rest, you've done a great deal." He paused, and then gave him a hug, saying "Thanks, Sensei." Smith smiled, and ruffled his hair."See you tomorrow kiddo, and it's no problem."

He sighs in relief as he goes into his room, coincidentally next to Maray's, where Marie is currently cuddling Ray, both asleep.
