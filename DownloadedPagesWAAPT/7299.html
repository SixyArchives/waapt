Treeo: <Are you sure about that though? We did end up someplace we really weren't meant to be.>

Comet: <They can't throw us in prison for this, it was an accident! All we need is to be given a chance to explain, and we'll be free mons again, simple as that!>

Comet narrows her eyes at Phyco.

Comet: <Yeah, yeah. No need to get technical...Oh?>

The three turn their attention to a police officer, who has just entered a the room with his Arcanine companion.

Officer: "Can you handle this for me, Smudge?>

Smudge the Arcanine♂: <My master would like me to tell you that we found where you came from using their strange glowy boxes that the humans here like to stare at for hours on end.>

Treeo: <Er...they're called "computers".>

Smudge: <Wow, really? I never knew that...>

Phyco: <Ah-hem.>

Smudge: <Oh! Sorry. Anyway, they said that they found that you come from this "Pee ee eff ee" place, but when they tried to find your master, they told us he was away.>

Comet blinks in surprise, tilting her head.

Comet: <Luke's gone?>

Treeo: <Did they say where?>

They receive the Arcanine equivalent of a shrug in response.

Smudge: <Dunno'. But! They did say that they were going to have you sent back over to this "Pee ee eff ee" place, and that they would talk to your master about this once he returns.>

Smudge nods as the police officer punches in the override code into a nearby control panel, which opens the cell doors.

Smudge: <Yup, you're all free to head back home to play with your owner again! I wish you many long days of fetching sticks and barking at those funnily dressed people who try to break into houses every morning!>

Comet: <Um...>

Phyco: <Clearly you know nothing about Kingdra.>

Treeo: <And I wish the same for you, Arcanine.>

<Now let's get home. Everymon's probably worried sick about us. I just hope my little sapling is there, too...>
