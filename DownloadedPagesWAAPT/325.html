Page 12: Leavbom, the Explosion Plant pokemon.

Leavbom breed by spreading spores through explosion. As such trainers are advised by the PWS to avoid marked forests during breeding season to prevent accidental deaths. After the spores come to rest, Leavbom grow like plants, detaching themselves from the nursery plant after reaching maturity. A Leavbom captured by a trainer often loses the ability to produce spores, this helps keep the spread of Leavboms limited to Leavbom Forest. It is said that people in ancient times plucked immature Leavbom from the nursery plant to use as explosive devices.
