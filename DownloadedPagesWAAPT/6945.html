 173620 Luke 924, Tue, 3rd Jul '12 6:59:27 AM Get RP Mod

White Wafers - PMD-A

Treeo: <So, you're telling us that Leaf hat gives you the ability to use Grass Type Moves?>

Comet gives Treeo a nod in confirmation, prompting a certain Kingdra to gaze at her skeptically.

Phyco: <That sounds completely ridiculous. How can a hat give you new moves?>

The Charizard narrows her eyes, annoyed at Phyco's skepticism.

Comet: <How about a demonstration, then?>

Phyco: <Be my guest, but I'm not expecting much.>

Clenching her claws in annoyance, the Charizard shakes her head vigorously, launching a flurry of leaves that spiral around her...

Comet used Leaf Tornado!

Causing a few to nick Phyco - Or rather, his Bubble Shield - knocking him around a little, much to his shock and surprise.

Phyco: <AAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHH!!!>

Having witnessed Comet preform a move that she shouldn't have been able to use under ordinary circumstances, Treeo stares, his jaw hanging open in shock.

Treeo: <...How...how did you do->

Phyco: <HEY! ARE YOU TRYING TO POP MY BUBBLE?>

Treeo blinks, having been interrupted by Phyco forcing himself in Comet's face, the Charizard responding by giving him a harsh look.

Comet: <You said you wanted a demonstration, and I needed a target, so...>

The Kingdra snorts in annoyance, releasing a stream of bubbles from his snout, to which Comet rolls her eyes.

<Oh, chill out! I knew your Bubble wouldn't pop from a small Leaf Tornado. It's not the end of the world.>

Comet's words do little to reassure Phyco, who remains fixated on Comet with a sharp glare for a few seconds, before turning around and floating off, muttering something under his breath in annoyance. Treeo watches him go, then turns to face Comet, still shocked by what he has seen her do.

Treeo: <I can't believe it, you actually used Leaf Tornado!>

The Charizard nods, smiling somewhat proudly.

Comet: <I know, it's weird. But at the same time, it's really awesome! Being able to use Grass type moves is really handy for a Fire Type like me. For obvious reasons.>

She smirked at the thought of using her new-found power over Grass Type moves to be easily beat those pesky Water, Rock and Ground types that Fire Pokémon like herself always dreaded dealing with. Having a similar thought, Treeo grins as well.

Treeo: <Heh, I guess it would help you against all the Pokémon you're weak too. But...>

He pauses, frowning.

<Something tells me that it wouldn't be legal in a tournament battle. League officials aren't so keen on Pokémon that can use moves they wouldn't be able to otherwise.>

Comet: <...Oh.>

The Charizard frowns, her fantasies of asserting her dominance over her weaknesses having been snatched away by the harsh truth of reality. Noticing this, Treeo frowns.

Treeo: <But there's nothing to keep you from using them outside of official battles. In fact, this is going to be really useful for tracking down the last few parts of the Starcutter.>

Perking up at this, she nods.

Comet: <Hey, you're right! Which leads me to wonder, if my hat lets me use Grass moves...>

She shifts her glance up to Treeo's Firey Crown.

<Will yours let you use Fire moves?>

Treeo blinks in surprise, finding the thought of a Grass type such as himself using Fire moves to be a foreign idea.

Treeo: <Oh! Well...do you think I could?>

Comet: <Yeah, I don't see any reason why you couldn't!>

Treeo: <Oh...>

The Sceptile fiddles nervously with his claws as he gives his Firey Crown a worried glance. He wasn't sure if he could be trusted with power over Fire. What if he accidentally burned himself? Or Comet? Or anymon else, for that matter? What if he started a fire and burned down Treasure Town?

And what if he enjoyed it and became a Pyromanic?

He let out a sigh, burying his head in his claws. It was true what they said; with great power really did come great responsibility.

Comet: <So...are you going to try it, at least?>

Treeo takes his head out of his claws, glancing at Comet, who seemed rather worried at the reaction she was getting out of him.

Treeo: <I...I could give it a go, I guess. But how do I know how what moves I can use?>

He cocks his head, a confused expression crossing his face.

<And how do I know what moves I can even use?>

Comet shakes her head, just as clueless in this situation as Treeo was.

Comet: <Er...tell you what, just try using a Flamethrower.>

Treeo: <Flamethrower?>

Comet: <Yeah, it's pretty simple. All you have to do is take a real deep breath, then breath out. Normally, your Pyro sack does the rest, but I'm guessing that since you don't have one, then that hat of yours will just work it's magic instead.>

Treeo: <Ah...wel, if it's that simple, then I'll give it a go, but...>

He beckons at her with his claws to move back.

<Can you make some room? I don't want to accidentally burn you.>

Comet smirks, chuckling to herself.

Comet: <Silly, it takes a lot to burn a Charizard! But fair enough.>

She takes a few paces back, leaving Treeo with some breathing room. Literally, in this case.

<Alright, give it your best shot!>

Treeo nods, although somewhat nervously. Steeling himself, he takes a deep breath, wondering if this was really going to work or not, then exhales...releasing a plume of billowing flames, much to his shock.

Treeo used Flamethrower!

Treeo: <WAAAAHHHH!!!>

The Sceptile instinctively jumps back, letting out a frightened hiss as Comet gasps in amazement.

Comet: <WHOA!!! Did you see that?! That was an actual Flamethrower!>

Treeo blinks, still surprised that he had actually managed to produce fire.

Treeo: <Did...did I really do that?>

Comet: <YEP! It was awesome! A lot of Fire Pokémon would be proud to produce a flame like that! Granted, you kinda' had a magic hat to help you out, but it was still really cool!>

Feeling a little bit calmer, Treeo tilts his head at Comet.

Treeo: <...You really think so?>

Comet: <Yeah! Come on, doing it again!>

Treeo: <Er...alright then...>

With another deep breath, Treeo once again exhales, producing another stream of flames, this one slightly more vigorous than the last, much to Comet's delight.

Comet: <BEAUTIFUL! Again!>

Treeo once again inhales, then exhales another powerful fiery plume, his Charizard friend clapping in delight.

<Wow! You're a natural at this, Treeo! I beginning to think you were born to be a Fire Pokémon but ended up as a Treecko instead!>

Treeo: <...You really think so?>

Comet: <That's what it seems like to me! Now, have you got another one in you?>

Treeo nods, but pauses, panting a little.

Treeo: <Maybe in a second, I'm all out of breath...>

Even with a magic hat, fire-breathing was sure took it out of you.
