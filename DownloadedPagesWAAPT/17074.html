Deziree: Wow, you weren't kidding!

Dune: Huh. I wonder what these monuments are for...

-Dune goes over and attempts to read the stone slab.-

Dune: I don't recognize this language...

???: <That is because it is not any language still on this earth.>

-Dune, Deziree, Candace and Big Dom turn to the source of the new voice. Matching almost perfectly with the breaking dawn is a Chandelure, floating toward the island from across the water.-

Chandelure: <Though I am flattered that you would come here to pay respect, as I once did.>

Deziree: ... Wait a second, what's a Fire-Type ghost doing all the way out here?

-The Chandelure turns to look at the young woman.-

Chandelure: <For much the same reason said Ghost is not surprised that you can understand him, despite the Tongue having long become a lost art in this day and age.>

Dune: You were expecting us.

-The Chandelure floats over the monument.-

Chandelure: <Or someone like you. I knew that, one day, someone would arrive, not searching for a way to communicate with the Eon Pokemon, but for the grave that has since fallen into the sea.>

-Dune and Deziree look at each other before turning back to the Ghost.-

Dune: ... You're the one who was buried here?

Chandelure: <Indeed I am.>

Deziree: You made Sir Thomas's silver helm?

-The Chandelure is given pause for a moment.-

Chandelure: <I do not know this "Sir Thomas" by name, but if you are referring to a silver helm associated with me, which you must, then yes. I created the helm.>

Dune: What does it do? Someone desecrated a tomb looking for it, specifically.

Chandelure: <...>

-The Chandelure floats down near the waterside.-

Chandelure: <This means that someone knows just what it can do.>

Dune: Which is...?

Chandelure: <The helmet is just one part of the entire set. One helm, two matching pauldrons, one breastplate, two gauntlets. Tell me, have you ever heard of the Golden Armor?>
