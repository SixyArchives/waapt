An Entry From Schrodinger's Date

It's been a few days since the bomb went off. And... Here I am... Stuck as a two-foot tall bird that went extinct like 150 million years ago. Due to the urging from Tangent, we've all been starting to keep logs about life without the bodies that we took for granted...

All and all, you could say that it's been fairly inconvenient... There's many things that I can't do on my own any more... I can't open my module door, I have to jump just to be able to reach the buttons on the telvision. Not that we get any news from the outside now. Our main communications array was downed when everything happened. And somehow, I think that my already atrocious handwriting somehow got worse.

From the Day After Schrodinger's Date

A PEFEgon finally tracked down what's left of the bomb. Apparently, for all of the lightshow it put up, it's non-explosive... So we have the shell left over from it. The comm techs finally got an auxillary array working, and the news is not promising. All of the main frequencies from Kanto, Johto, and Unova appear to be down. We made contact with Hoenn and Sinnoh, but they seem about as clueless as us. ... Could that annoying Altaria and servile Druddigon have been telling the truth?

But, how's a revolt supposed to be organized "For Dragons" anyways? There's so many ways to define it... There's set to be a pair of excursionary teams to go out tomorrow. One to Sinnoh, another to Hoenn. I'll be off on the former along with some of the guys. Some of the others are headed along with the other group. Hopefully we'll get to the bottom of this soon.

A Couple Days After That

Sinnoh seems so much colder than I last remembered it... Perhaps it's because of this body... Perhaps it's because of the time of year... I can't help but suspect that it might be recent events that are weighing me down.

Apparently that uprising is going on somehow. Details from the outside world are scarce. But deeply, deeply troubling. How can home be going through this again? And on the heels of a bitter episode that we're still licking our wounds from?

Worst of all, the J-Team is out at least one member. DS died earlier this week, her Espeon killed her.

An Entry Shortly After The Last One

Things just seem to be getting worse and worse for us... Sinnoh is apparently next on the chopping block for these lizards. We saw mon bombs go off and an invasion force land on the beaches earlier today. Sinnoh's resettlement program was still in its earliest phases, so there was hardly anyone to stand up to them. Worst of all, these guys seem to be recruiting locals... And they seem to be listening.

Also, the matter of how these guys define "Dragon" seems to be cleared up a bit. I and a couple others had to fight off a Charizard and a Sceptile that are apparently crack troops earlier today. We got away by the skin of our teeth.

That Druddigon Karst that joined us from back around the time of Scolipede and Artemis' overthrow seems to have been troubled lately... I hope he's alright.

A Few Entries After That One

So... We're more or less here... Waiting. Holed up in Oreburgh waiting for something to happen. We saw Hydra Marrow again... Most of us were glad to see her again... Nibbler was... Well, let's just say that he didn't take it so well.

Oreburgh's become a lot more busy. People and mons (not that there's much difference any more) fleeing other towns have come here, since it's the toughest and best-defended spot in all of Sinnoh. We saw a few of those lizards patrolling the ridges, but some of them seem rather reluctant to do anything. Apparently they still rely on this place for rations since prey's still fairly scarce.

We've found out a bit more about our foes... Their leader's apparently called 'Qoriik'. They have their numbers bolstered from a number of sources, since there's not too many locals here for them to recruit... I didn't even know that Celestia's peons did mercenary work on the side. I can't imagine that she'd be happy if she ever found out about this though...

Also, Karst has gone missing. We've tried to find him, but have had no luck.

A Bit After That Entrywise

I don't think that things could possibly get any worse from this point. We got chased along with the Sinnoh Tribe through a space tunnel back to HQ. We've got hostiles prowling the corridors here now. They took over the main medical ward, and have apparently destroyed pretty much everything they can get their claws on for the labs.

We've talked a plan over with the Sinnoh Tribe members and their allies who made it here... We're going to attempt to kill the space tunnel that leads here. It's risky... But it might be our only hope at keeping this place intact enough to do anything meaningful for reverse engineering that bomb.

Also, we found out why Karst went missing a while back. He apparently wound up joining these guys. We found him badly shot up by a turret in a hallway earlier today. I don't know why he would do this though. He seemed to get along so well with everyone else. And I'm not sure if we'll be able to find out. The medics say that there's a good chance he might not be alive by the next sunset.
