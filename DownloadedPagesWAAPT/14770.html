Route 18

So, after failing a hundred times at my first training, I ended sleeping in the wild with Teishi and his friends. From what I could remember I was sleeping alone and leaning against a tree, but when I woke up I saw Teishi leaning against me, Ryu lying on my lap, Strider and Sub-Zero leaning against opposite sides of the tree and Shadow on one of it's branches, all sleeping. I have no idea what happened while I was out.

Today I started doing a lot better on keeping my balance on that rope. I was actually doing pretty good on my first attempt of the day while Strider and Sub-Zero were sparring to pass the time, what surprised me was that both started evolving at the same time and the bright flash broke my focus and I fell down, Teishi got distracted as well so he didn't catch me, fortunately, for me at least, I landed right on top of him

"<Ow!>" said Teishi, on the floor.

"S-Sorry Teishi, I lost focus and..." said Sakura, getting off Teishi

By the time I got up again, Strider and Sub-Zero were already Greninjas, Teishi didn't even seem to care about me falling on top of him, he just got up and went to congratulate his friends alongside Ryu.

"<Awesome! Now we're all Greninjas!>" said Teishi.

"<Yeah!>" said Sub-Zero, fist pumping

"<Aren't there some other members who aren't Greninja yet?>" asked Ryu

Teishi shrugs "<That's true, but I was talking about who's currently here.>" he then looks back at Strider and Sub-Zero "<But that doesn't matter, you are finally Greninjas too!>"

They proceeded to high five after that. It's kinda nice to see them like that. I wonder why Shadow didn't join them though, I guess he just doesn't quite fit well into the Squad yet.

Today somebody got a bit too excited with his new skills

Sakura was almost at the end of the little balance exercise when suddenly a Water Shuriken flies by and cuts the rope and makes her plummet down, fortunately Teishi was ready to catch her this time.

"<Oops. My bad.>"

The source of the voice turned out to be Sub-Zero, who had a very sheepish look on his face. The only answer to what he did was Ryu slapping him lightly on the back of his head.

We got a new rope after that one.

Finally, after 234 tries (Strider kept count) I managed to get to the other side!

"<I must say girl, you're really persistent.>" said Shadow, looking fairly impressed.

"It didn't even feel like it was that many tries, but well, I'm glad it's done!" said Sakura, sighing in relief.

"<Yeah. Now you just gotta do it again, but running!>" said Shadow

"... WHA-"

Then the branch I was on cracked and I fell all the way down with nothing to catch me or cushion my fall. Yeah... My back is hurting quite a bit right now.

We probably should've test to see if the branch would be able to withstand my weight before doing this.

Sakura was leaning against a tree and just relaxing a bit while the Squad as doing their own thing, be it sparring or just doing miscelaneous training that she was just watching, Teishi then approaches Sakura.

"<Hey Sakura, are you sure you're ok?>" he asked, looking a bit worried

"I'm fine, Teishi. I'm sturdier that you might think, my back is just hurting a bit, that's all." said Sakura, peacefully sighing.

"<Oh, okay... It's just that I feel bad that I didn't catch you when that branch broke...>"

"You couldn't have known it was going to do that, so it's fine."

"<Yeah, but I gotta have good reflexes! React to any situation as fast as I can even if it's something that I'm not expecting!>"

"Reflexes huh... Can't say I have the best of them either, that's going to take some work. Anyway, you're just as much in training as I am, Teishi; well, less so, but you're still learning, maybe next time you'll be more alert to this kind of outcome." Sakura then smiles at Teishi

"<So... You're not upset?>"

"Never was."

Teishi then gives a determined look "<Okay, I'll make sure to be more alert next time!>"

"If it means I'm going to get less hurt, then I won't complain." Sakura giggles.

Teishi chuckles and then proceed to hug Sakura carefully so as to not hurt her before breaking it seconds later

"<I'm sure you'll become a great ninja soon too! And then we can become the coolest team ever!>" his tone had a lot of confidence in it.

"Sure! But you should probably catch up with your Squad's training, right?" Sakura then points with her head towards what the others were doing

"<Yeah! I'm going to start training too!>"

Teishi then goes off to join the others, while Sakura just sits there watching.

I might be going crazy, but sometime after I fell asleep I started to hear some faint voices. From what I could hear, they were Strider and Shadow, but I couldn't make out what they were saying to each other. I'm guessing everybody else was sleeping too since it was otherwise quiet, I would've checked what was going on, but I was so tired I couldn't even open my eyes anymore. I wonder what's going on...
