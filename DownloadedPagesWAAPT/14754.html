Kalos Route 18

-Samantha is sitting by herself, scritching Harbinger behind the ears, when a voice starts speaking from somewhere behind her.-

Herbert: You know, I always seem to find you sitting on your own like this. It wouldn't hurt to start talking to people your own age every once in a while, you know.

-Samantha turns her head-

Samantha: Oh, I do. I guess you just don't see me.

-she sighs a bit-

Herbert: Something wrong?

-she shakes her head-

Nothing. Not really. Anything with you?

Herbert: Nope. Nothing. And this is the first time I've been able to say that for a long, long time.

-He glances down at the ground.-

Herbert: ...Though I do have some bad news.

-Samantha's face darkens slightly-

Samantha: Go on...

Herbert: Well, I've thought about it for a while, and... I think I'm going to leave the J-Team.

-Samantha stares-

...you are?

Herbert: Yeah. I think I am.

-she stares even more-

...what prompted this?

Herbert: A lot of things, honestly. I mean, I've done my best to try and rebuild some of the bridges I've burnt, and I don't think I'd be leaving any loose ends behind me. My father's still out there, as is Illusion, and I think I'd be better working alone than with the J-Team. It's not that I don't still have friends on the team, but I've caused enough problems for the team as a whole. It's time for me to start proving that I can fight by myself, that I don't need other people to keep me alive and sane.

Samantha: And you intend to do that...by leaving.

Herbert: That I do. I have a plan in mind. It's crazy and probably pretty dangerous. Exactly the sort of thing you'd approve of, in other words. And I'll have people and mons I trust to help make it work.

Samantha: ...would you care to tell me them?

Herbert: That'd ruin the surprise, I'm afraid. Though you don't need to worry yourself, I'll be in good hands. I'll have my team with me, as well as a certain other studious redhead of [Poke-Irish] descent.

Samantha: Ah. Alice? You've mentioned her a couple times.

Herbert: That's her. I've yet to get her on board, but I'm sure a quick phone call will be all it takes. She's a sucker for this kind of thing.

Samantha: As long as you're safe. Although…

-she sighs-

As you said earlier, you have friends on the J-Team. That fellow that's actually a Lucario,    Hudson   , and, well…

-she looks down at her feet, and Herbert places a hand on her shoulder-

Herbert: Hey, no need to get down about it. Think of this as a chance to make new friends. You're a great person, after all, and I'm sure that after a while the others will start to realise that.

Samantha: But...but, well...I think you're the only one on the team that thinks that. And what if they don't think so?

-Herbert looks genuinely confused for a moment.-

Herbert: Why wouldn't they? You're smart, you're funny - even if you don't mean to be at times - and you're pretty… not to mention that most of the team are pretty sociable. I'm sure that you'd become firm friends if you just took the time to hang out with some of them.

-Samantha raises an eyebrow and smirks despite herself-

Samantha: What was that part? Right between "funny" and how the team's sociable? I didn't catch it.

-Herbert goes slightly red.-

Herbert: I... I said you're pretty. It's true, after all…

-Samantha's smirking gives way to a massively flattered smile-

Thank you, Herbie...

-Herbert gives a small smile, then rather tentatively places a gloved hand on Samantha's cheek and leans in to give her a quick kiss. Samantha leans in and places her lips over his, kissing for likely longer than Herbert intended. It lasts for about ten seconds before Herbert pulls away, now a shade of red that could be used to hail ships.-

Herbert: ...You know, that almost made me change my mind about leaving.

Samantha: Not completely? Maybe I ought to practice.

-she chuckles slightly before sighing-

I'll miss you terribly, you know. And I won't be alone.

Herbert: Same. Just don't lock yourself indoors and spend the next few months staring longingly out at the sea on my behalf, okay?

Samantha: I'll do my best. Should we...keep in touch, or do you not want to be bothered when you're doing what you have to do?

Herbert: I'd like to keep in touch, but... honestly, I doubt I'll be able to. I doubt they've got anything close to a reliable internet connection where I'm going…

Samantha: And you don't wanna say where that is?

Herbert: I don't know where that is yet myself.

Samantha: Oh. Well...you're going now, then?

Herbert: Not right this moment, but pretty soon. I just wanted to let you know ahead of time... y'know, in case you wanted to make these last few hours special or something.

-Samantha thinks-

That might be a good idea, actually...it'll make us closer. Maybe we'll even start sleeping in the same room again when you come back.

-Herbert rather abashedly looks at the ground.-

Herbert: Maybe... I don't know if several months on my own will make me any better when it comes to physical contact, though.

Samantha: -head tilt, smirk-

You sure about that?

Herbert: ...I guess we'll just have to wait and see.

Samantha: I guess we will.

-looks down at her feet again-

Herbert: So... I suppose this is goodbye, then.

Samantha: I suppose it is. Before you go, though...I ought to tell you. Herbert Cain, I...love you.

-she blushes furiously and averts her gaze. Herbert gives a small smirk.-

Herbert: It's Lucius Cain. That, or Herbert. Not both.

Samantha: It just sounds odd if I don't use your middle names, and I forgot the order. >_<

-Herbert giggles. It's a very odd noise.-

Herbert: Honestly, I forget the order myself sometimes.

Samantha: Well...anyway, there's that. Figured I ought to say it before you left.

Herbert: I, uh... well, I'm glad you did. You know, I don't think I've ever heard anyone say that to me before. I mean, sure, I've had my fair share of crushes and girls who like me and even a girlfriend once but I don't think anyone's just come out and told me "I love you" and now I'm wondering if I'm a terrible person for leaving someone like that behind...

-He seems to be getting more and more flustered as he continues.-

Samantha: Lucius. You're dancing around the issue. Do you feel the same? As much as I do?

Herbert: Erm...

-He starts to glance around at anything but Samantha.-

Herbert: ...I don't know. That's the truth of it, I'm afraid. I've never been in love before, I don't know what it feels like, and I don't know if I'm ready to commit to something like that yet…

Samantha: ...well, I love you, Lucius. I don't really care if you feel the same right now. I want you to, but I can wait til you're ready.

-Herbert smiles - a warm, genuine smile that seems almost out-of-place on a face like his.-

Herbert: ...Thanks, Samantha. That means a lot to me.

-He pulls Samantha into a hug. An incredibly brief and awkward hug, but a hug nonetheless.-

-Samantha hugs back-

Now...can I go? I mean, I know you have to leave, but that's not gonna stop me from needing to have a depressed period.

Herbert: ...I can stay here with you for a while, if you want. Like I said, I'm not going anywhere anytime soon.

Samantha: But...only you just...said goodbye…

Herbert: Goodbyes aren't forever, you know. We can say goodbye as many times as we like, but it won't mean anything until there's an ocean between us.

Samantha: But soon there will be one.

Herbert: I know. But until then... there's just us.

-He runs his hand through Samantha's hair and smiles gently.-

-she smiles back-

I guess there is.

-After a few hours spent enjoying the last of his time in Kalos with Samantha, Herbert finally says his goodbyes to her and wanders off to find a secluded spot beneath a tree. Once there, he pulls out the Holo Caster that Hikari gave him.-

Herbert: Okay, let's see if I can figure out how to work this thing...

-He fiddles with it for a while, and after a few moments a hologram of Alice's upper body appears with a Petilil in her arms.-

Herbert: Ah, there we are! Hey there, Alice. Long time no see. I didn't know you had a Petilil.

Alice: Your friend Joseph gave her Egg to me. I named her Elderberry.

Petilil Elderberry: <A most bootylicious greeting to you.>

-Herbert blinks.-

Herbert: ...Did she just-

Alice: I believe she meant to say "bountiful". In any case, might I ask why you have elected to resume contact with me now after such a long period of absence?

Herbert: Well, I had an idea, and I was wondering if I could count on your help in order to make it work.

-Alice raises an eyebrow.-

Alice: ...I know what your ideas are like. Dare I ask what you have in mind?

Herbert: Well, I was thinking something kinda like this...

One explanation later...

Herbert: So, what do you think?

-Alice blinks twice.-

Alice: ...That's insane. You're insane.

Herbert: But you're going to go along with it anyway, because you're just as crazy as I am.

Alice: You know me too well. Anyway, have you given any thought as to how you will finance this endeavour?

Herbert: I'm rich, remember? Money's no object. What we really need to worry about is resources. Sure, together your genius and my stunning good looks will go pretty far, but there's no way the two of us are going to be able to put some of this stuff together alone.

Alice: Are you suggesting we enlist some outside assistance?

Herbert: Maybe. Just so long as we can do it discreetly. Anyway, shall we meet up and discuss this in person?

Alice: I think we shall. I believe that it's going to be a pleasure working with you, Lucius.

-The line goes dead and the hologram disappears. Herbert gives a small smile, before sending out Kirk, Bishop and Maul.-

Herbert: Shall we be off then, guys?

Bishop: <We should probably begin as soon as possible.>

Kirk: <Can't see any reason to hang around here.>

Maul: ~Time to begin this new chapter alone.~

Herbert: ...I'm just going to take that as a third "yes". Take us away, Maul.

-With a blip, the boy and his mons disappear.-
