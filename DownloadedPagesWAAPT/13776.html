Corona: <Please. Call me Victoria.>

-Crewe shakes her head-

I'll never forget what I did to you all… and you especially, Cor—Victoria.

-Corona sighs-

Corona: <Crewe, no one wants you to torment yourself over this. If we didn't care about you, we wouldn't have waited until you asked us to leave.>

But—

Corona: <Ask Electivire. She'll tell you.>

-Crewe knees buckle and Corona quickly grabs her to stop her from falling-

Corona: <Crewe! What's wrong?>

E-Electivire… they're gone… they're all gone…

-Corona goes quiet for a moment-

Corona: -softly, half to herself- <Oh, Crewe… what have you done…?>

-Crewe breaks away and starts running down the beach. Corona drops to all fours and runs after her-

Corona: <No, Crewe, stop!>

-she quickly overtakes the half-stumbling, half-running girl and stands up, dropping her paws on her shoulders-

Corona: <Crewe. Don't run away from me like this.>

-she grips her shoulders tightly and takes a deep breath-

Corona: <Tell me. Can you accept that I've moved on and found a new Trainer?>

-Crewe nods, mutely but urgently-

Corona: <Then please. You need to move on, too. We've found our own lives. Find your own.>

I'm so sorry.

Corona: <Don't apologize to me. You've already apologized too many times.>

I treated you like a—I didn't—

Corona: <You loved Ashes too much and couldn't let her go. I know. Why do you think I let you go on acting like she was still around to battle for you?>

-she bends down to Crewe's height-

Corona: <I loved you, Crewe. We loved you.>

You left me.

Corona: <We did. But we still loved you. We needed our own lives. You gave that to us. You realized how you were treating us, and you made amends the best way you knew how. How many Trainers do that?>

How many need to?

Corona: <I would say plenty. But does it matter? Don't kill yourself over your old mistakes, Crewe. Move on. Like we all did.>

I don't think I can. I've tried, Victoria. I can't.

Corona: <Try again. If you feel you owe me anything, Crewe, then promise me this. Try to move on.>

I… I promise I'll try.

Corona: <That's all I ask.>

-she glances up at the sun-

Corona: <… I should be getting back to Billy.>

-Crewe wipes an arm over her eyes-

Bye, Victoria.

Corona: <… We had some good times, Crewe. It wasn't all bad. It wasn't mostly bad. You do us all a disservice by only remembering us by the bad.>

I'm sorry.

-Corona sighs-

Corona: <… Good bye, Crewe.>

Bye.

-Corona gives her a long look, then pats her shoulder, drops to all fours, and starts running towards Cyllage. Crewe watches her go and continues walking down the beach away from town, hands shoved in her pockets-
