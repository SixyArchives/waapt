Sunyshore, a room

Hunter has moved to the room he and Ashley are renting, and is going through his backpack for junk to throw out. A pile of candy wrappers, crumpled pieces of paper, and broken T Ms are sitting on one side of his bed, but he's currently distracted by something else he's pulled out.

He sits cross-legged on the bed with a silver and yellow jacket folded on his lap. On top is a wooden case, and inside a matched set of first place medals for the Jubilife Trainer's School's junior Pokeringer competition. Set inside the lid of the case is a picture of Cesar, then a Staravia, with one medal around his neck, and a grinning Hunter with the other, arm around his Pokemon. Hunter takes out the medals and opens a small compartment in the bottom, taking out a silver pin with the initials JTS engraved in them, surrounded by yellow circles. He rests it on the jacket, half smiling to himself.

His Pokemon watch him from various positions of boredom and laziness around the room. Cesar, perched on top of an armchair, looks on fondly. Serge, laying on his back in front of Hunter, paws at the air.

<He's getting all nostalgic,> the Luxio complains. <Someone snap him out of it.>

<I would, but spraying him might get that picture wet, and then he'd have my head,> Trixie says from her spot lounging on the armchair below Cesar.

<We wouldn't want that, now would we, darling?> Lockspur asks, brushing the junk into the trash can with one flower. <Well, you wouldn't.>

<You want to say that again, pretty boy?> Trixie demands. She is stopped from leaping to her feet by Cesar putting a claw on her shoulder.

<Relax, you two. Let Hunter do what he wants,> the Staraptor snaps, pushing Trixie back into the seat and letting go.

<Whatever,> the Golduck mutters.

<Of course, my dear Cesar,> Lockspur says, giving him a mocking bow.

<Uggggh can I please shock him out of it? Like, accidentally?> Serge asks, rolling to him feet.

<No.>

<You're no fun, Cesar,> Serge growls, sparking a little but flopping back down.
