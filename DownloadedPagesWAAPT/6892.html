Phyco: <That Elgyem had better appreciate our efforts, especially since we're risking life and limb to put hos ship back together while he just sits there staring at his computer all day.>

<Give the little guy a break. He's from another planet! I bet he took one look outside after he crashed, and was so frightened of this crazy alien landscape that he can't even go near the door without getting the heebie-jeebies.>

Dynamo Station - PEFE (PMD-B)

Have you ever tried to sleep with a constant noise in the background? It's not easy, is it?

Lake was having that problem. He just simply couldn't fall asleep with the constant whirring of the generator a short distance from the lodge they had decided to spend the night in.

But while Stratus didn't seem to be having any issues sleeping with the endless droning of the power generator in the background, Lake wasn't faring nearly as well. He just couldn't relax and fall asleep unless it was completely quiet.

Clenching his eyes shut tightly, the Feraligatr tried covering his ears, but that did little to dull the noise. Feeling rather hopeless at the thought that he wouldn't be getting any sleep that night, he sighed quietly, yet sadly to himself. If only this lodge had been built with sound-proofing...

...Wait, that was it!

Pulling himself up, the Lake too a deep breath, then blew a large bubble, continuing to puff air into it so that it was large enough to envelop himself.

Lake used Bubble Shield!

That seemed to fix the problem. Although it wasn't completely sound-proof (because a shield that cut off your sense of hearing completely was far from useful), it did render the whining of the generators quiet enough so that it wasn't so invasive.

Letting out a loud yawn, Lake laid down on the floor of his bubble, which was pleasantly cozy, and curled himself up...

Lake used Rest!
Lake fell asleep!

Magolor: <My scans have pinpointed that the Starcutter's Emblem is located some here, in a region called "White Wafers.">

Comet: <...Oh, Muk.>

Phyco: <Well this is going to be fun...>

Magolor: <Oh, don't like the cold, eh?>

Treeo shakes his head vigorously.

Treeo: <Us Grass types can't cope with chilly weather at all! we need warmth and sunlight to survive. Take that away, we start to wither, we stop producing our own food, and then...>

He gulps.

<...We die.>

Comet frowns sadly, shuddering at the mental image of Treeo turning a sickly brown and whithering away.

Comet: <Well, even though I'm a cold-blooded, I'm also a Fire type. As long as I keep my inner fire lit, then I should be okay. It's everymon else I'm worried about.>

Phyco, meanwhile, is gazing down at the floor. As much as he hated to admit it, a regal Dragon Pokémon such as himself would be sunk at the prospect of braving icy conditions...

Phyco: <I'm a Dragon, and like much of my kind...I'm ashamed to admit that Ice is one of the few things we cannot deal with.>

He looks back up, preforming the Kingdra equivalent of a shrug.

<Granted, my Water typing prevents it from being Super Effective, but it's still an issue, especially considering this...>

Comet: <Long story short; Ice messes all of us up badly.>
