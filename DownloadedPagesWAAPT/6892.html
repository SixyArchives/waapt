Dynamo Station - PEFE (PMD-B)

Have you ever tried to sleep with a constant noise in the background? It's not easy, is it?

Lake was having that problem. He just simply couldn't fall asleep with the constant whirring of the generator a short distance from the lodge they had decided to spend the night in.

But while Stratus didn't seem to be having any issues sleeping with the endless droning of the power generator in the background, Lake wasn't faring nearly as well. He just couldn't relax and fall asleep unless it was completely quiet.

Clenching his eyes shut tightly, the Feraligatr tried covering his ears, but that did little to dull the noise. Feeling rather hopeless at the thought that he wouldn't be getting any sleep that night, he sighed quietly, yet sadly to himself. If only this lodge had been built with sound-proofing...

...Wait, that was it!

Pulling himself up, the Lake too a deep breath, then blew a large bubble, continuing to puff air into it so that it was large enough to envelop himself.

Lake used Bubble Shield!

That seemed to fix the problem. Although it wasn't completely sound-proof (because a shield that cut off your sense of hearing completely was far from useful), it did render the whining of the generators quiet enough so that it wasn't so invasive.

Letting out a loud yawn, Lake laid down on the floor of his bubble, which was pleasantly cozy, and curled himself up...

Lake used Rest!
Lake fell asleep!
