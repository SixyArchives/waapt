Castelia City, Night Time, Unknow Date

It was dark and cold. The snow fell slowly upon the streets of the city, illuminated by the big full moon up in the sky. Bundled up the people walked. Some quickly and quietly, anxious to get to home and warmth. Some swaddled along having already found warmth against one another and enjoying the beautiful snowfall. A few people would point out a dark shadow leaping from roof top to roof top, but make almost nothing of it.

A women, a single mother was making her way home. Her arms were full of groceries and she was in a rush to get home and cook for her only child. Suddenly a hand shot out from an alley and dragged her in. She tried to scream out for help but the hand had clamped her mouth shut. All she could do was hopelessly try to squirm loose from her captors grip praying that someone would hear her muffled screams.

"My my, aren't you a pretty one?"

The assailant took out a switchblade and retracted it, waving the knife in from of her face while tightening his grip on her.

"They say that you can tell a lot about a woman by the contents of her purse. However I don't see a purse on you so it looks like I'm gonna have to-"

He was cut off by a low growl coming from the darkened end of the alley. There was a blur of motion and something bit down on his hand causing him to yelp out and cry in pain as he was dragged into the darkness. The women fell to the ground and began crawling back to the street. Her eyes widened in fear as she heard the continued screens of pain from the would be thief. The thief's body was flung out of the alley, past the women and onto the street. He moaned in pain as he lay there, his hand now sporting a nasty bite-like wound and his clothing tore-up.

Slowly the women looked back into the alley. She thought she say a small tongue of flame before a chunk of the darkness leapt up and disappeared over the rooftops.
