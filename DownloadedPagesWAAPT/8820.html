UTINNI!
There was little to go on. Even with Runt's help, and even visiting every library and museum he knew of in Kanto, Jack found minimal information on this ancient civilization that Michael had spoken of.

Jack: "There is something that man wasn't telling us."

After having arrived at the first library and discovering next to nothing there, Jack had quickly taken to writing down what Michael had told on a notepad to make sure he didn't forget what had already been told to him. As he and his abra continued to search, however, they discovered that what Michael had told them was about as much as he was going to find anywhere.

About the only information he was able to find that he didn't know already were rumors about potential ruins from whatever was left over from the supposed civilization. At the very least, he couldn't say Michael had just made it all up. There was some basis for the stories about this precursor civilization.

Runt: [Telepathically] "Like he's been there before?"

Jack: "Or he knows someone who's done research. Except why wouldn't they publish it?"

Runt: [Telepathically] "There's a secret about it they don't want anyone to know?"

Jack: "Which means this is starting to look like something worth investigating."

Runt: [Telepathically] "Somehow, I knew you were going to say that."

Jack smirked - the Abra knew him well enough at this point to expect that Jack wasn't going to just let the rumors sit pretty, not if there were leads for him to go off of. Especially if no one else was covering this and there might be something about it all that people needed to know.

Jack could have gone to libraries in other regions, but if not a single one of the locations he had visited in Kanto had yielded anything, he had no reason to suspect traveling to another region would yield better results. Someone would have said something if there was any real hope of that.

No, his best source would be the original source. He'd go back to Michael, see if he couldn't get anything else out of the man, and probably ask him about those other topics while he was at it.

Then he'd take a good look at following up on those ruins he'd read about.
