Celestial Tower

-It's now been a year since the Elgyem and the Lampent have met. Lampent has been trying to impress a couple of other Lampent with her tricks, but has so far failed to do so. Very rarely does she let it get to her though, satisfied knowing that the Elgyem will like her no matter what.

Of course, that doesn't mean her mother isn't concerned about it.-

Mama Chandelure: Lampent?

Lampent: -currently attempting to keep a small ball of flame in between her pole-hands- Yeah mom?

Mama Chandelure: How many friends do you have?

Lampent: Do we really have to talk about this now? I mean, I'm kind of busy practicing here.

Mama Chandelure: Lampent, I'm serious. I want to know how many friends you have.

Lampent: Augh, fine. -snuffs out the ball of flame- I've got like... I dunno, 4 friends?

Mama Chandelure: What are their names?

Lampent: Well, two of them are Lampent, one of them is one of the Lampent's little sisters, and that Elgyem I met a year ago.

Mama Chandelure: You've known that Elgyem for a while now, haven't you?

Lampent: Yup! He's probably the best friend I have in the entire tower!

Mama Chandelure: And you don't have any more friends than that?

Lampent: No. Not a lot of people seem to like me.

Mama Chandelure: -sighs- That's not a good thing, Lampent.

Lampent: Oh come on! We've talked about this before, and I've already told you; I don't mind!

Mama Chandelure: Well I mind! When I was your age I knew what was happening with every Lampent in the tower! Now why can't you do the same?

Lampent: Because I don't want to have friends who gossip on and on about what a Lampent did with a Golett five years ago, or where the next party is!

Mama Chandelure: You never go to parties!

Lampent: I hate parties! Parties bore me!

Mama Chandelure: Are you sure you don't want even one more friend?

Lampent: You know what mom? I'd rather my only friend be that one little Elgyem than have a billion friends whose names I don't even remember half the time!

Mama Chandelure: Fine, but when the Elgyem gets caught by some random trainer, don't come crying to me about how you lost your "best friend!"

-with that, the Chandelure flickers off and goes into stasis. The Lampent growls and, in a fit of rage, her head bauble lights on fire. As she tries to snuff out the fire on her head, she doesn't notice the little Elgyem hiding behind a different Tombstone, staring up at her and her mother, and worrying...-

Elgyem: (thinking) What if I do get caught? ...
