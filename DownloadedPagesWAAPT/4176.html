After a brief, silent train ride, Crewe and Hecate disembark in Anville Town. Hecate moves purposefully towards the forest, and Crewe follows her, not looking around and trying not to remember all that happened last time she was in Anville.

Entering the forest, she moved a little closer to Hecate, thinking with a kind of wry amusement how the Zoroark was probably the only way for her to remain safe in these woods.

After a short while, the trees started to darken. Crewe thought she was imagining it and was telling her mind to quit playing tricks on her when Hecate stopped and turned, staring at a gap between two trees. Slowly, Zoroark began to enter the clearing, most of them skinny and covered in battle scars. As they came, they muttered to themselves.

<It's the chief!>

<Why does she have a human with her?>

<What's she doing here?>

<What does she want?>

Hecate stands beside Crewe, looking around at the approaching Pokemon with her chin held high. Suddenly, a young Zoroark, fitter than most of the others, breaks out and charges towards Crewe, claws charged in Obscura. Several others follow, snarling. Hecate whirls on them and with several snarls, slashes, and a few Focus Blasts, pushes them back.

<Enough!> she snarls. <No one is to hurt this human, or they will be punished severely. I am not here to rejoin the pack, or to take power from whoever has stepped into my place. I want only to see my family. Bring them to me.>

A few of the older Zoroarks exchange mutters, then turn and depart. Crewe waits anxiously, sticking close to Hecate.
