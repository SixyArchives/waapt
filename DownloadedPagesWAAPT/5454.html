Rayquaza: <Yes. While it is mainly used by humans nowadays as a final resting place for departed Pokémon, Mt. Pyre's summit is where the Red and Blue Orbs, which summon my counterparts Groudon and Kyogre respectively, are kept under guard.

Also, the mountain itself is actually a supervolcano. One of the biggest in the world, in fact. It's been dormant for many, many moons. But if it were to go off...>

<...It's wouldn't be pleasant for anymon. That's all I shall say.>
