Laurel: <Here I am sir! Now begone with you!>

Laurel attempts to pust a few boulders onto Woundwort, but they are easily dodged by the beheamoth, who starts climbing the mountain himself.

Woundwort: <I trusted you Laurel! Now it will be of my utmost pleasure to TEAR YOU TO PIECES!!>

Laurel had to think fast. He noticed one loose boulder delicatley balancing itself on the edge, one not big enough to push the brute off the mountain, but enough to stun the brute for a sizeble amount of time. Laurel positions himself behind the rock and starts pushing it until...

A flash of lightning suddenly occurs, blinding the general and scattering his croonies. Skree takes this chance opprotunity to stike at Woundwort, causing him to lose balance and wobble slightly. The final straw comes from Laurel pushing the boulder square on the General's chest, sending the brute crashing down the mountain side.

Laurel pants. That encounter was close, too close. While this would keep the General out of action for some period of time. He knew it wouldn't keep him down forever, and when he is ready to persue, he will be furious.

Laurel: <WE CAN'T WASTE ANY MORE TIME! HURRY!>

Skree caws in agreement and flies ahead in the storm. The others take heed to Laurels words and continue their perilous journey across the mountain. At least the don't have to worry about the Efrafans for the time being.
