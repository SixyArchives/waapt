West of Meteor Falls

Copahue and Harriet are still perched on a large tree, with the Shahinnes playing underneath under they watch. The two larger birds hush at each other quick recaps about their recent lives. Copahue is senseful enough to not press the issue when Harriet more or less sidesteps goig further after any mention of the Sceptile.

Harriet — <...but that was not the weirdest thing, you know? Because after wandering in there for a while, a Beedrill came up...>

The Shahinnes are more or less playing, more or less trying to get the fake Shahinne off that hole. A short but healthy skirmish ensues where the fake Shahinnes are one by one winglslapped or pecked out of their fleeting existence...

Copahue — <...turns out she was still nagging him when we woke up. You'd think a Pokémon whose son wins a tournament would be more proud...>

As the sun goes away to the Sea of Kyogre, Copahue and Harriet start getting closer, sharing the moment. They take a moment to check up on a Karate Trainer who is packing his stuff to go sleep.

Copahue — <...thought it was unfair, yes, but... well, Silent is so unlike my previous Trainer...>

Harriet — <Yeah, most Trainers are not even remotely alike. And so are us Pokémon... See, you wouldn't believe Tracer has this nasty Honchkrow...>

After some time, when they are getting more comfortable, Shahinne flies up the tree and starts circling them.

Shahinne — <Adoptive mom! Adoptive dad! [Help]!>

Copahue and Harriet — <...!>



Harriet and Copahue blush momentarily, then they cough and make room for Shahinne to perch besides them.

Harriet -tilts head — <Oh, little Shahinne... what is happening?>

Shahinne — <That mean Shahinne...> — sobs — <she has the nesting spot and...> — more sobs — <doesn't want to leave it!> — points to the lower branchs, still sobbing — <She's hiding there!>

Copahue — <Oh...>

Harriet — <Wait, what?> — blinks — <How does that work?>

Copahue — <She's practised Double Team a lot, you see...>

Harriet nods and then moves aside as Copahue nuzzles Shahinne.

Copahue — <Ssshhh, there... come, we are going to get you that nesting spot.> — turns to Harriet — <You coming?>

Harriet — <Erm... sure! I want to see this...>



The two birds hop to the lower branches of the tree following Shahinne to a wide branch where there is a large hole. As the larger birds approach, they see a Starly all puffed up inside the hole.

Air!Shahinne — <You! I want my hole!> — chirps.

Hole!Shahinne — <No wai!> — narrows eyes at Shahinne — <We got this place for me!>

Air!Shahinne — <Yeah but you sneaked in before! We are all angry at you!>

Hole!Shahinne -growls — <LIAR!>

Air!Shahinne -hops around, menacingly — <UGLY!>

Hole!Shahinne — <COWARD!>

Copahue blinks, then stomps on the branch, Harriet observing the situation amused.

Copahue — <That's it! Calm down you two!> — looks at the surprised Shahinne in the hole — <You two can share that hole...>

Air!Shahinne -peeks in from behind Copahue's talon — <Give it to me!>

Hole!Shahinne — <...I don't want to!>

The Shahinne in the hole tries to get deeper inside it, prompting the Shahinne in the air to shriek. As she flutters around for a moment, Copahue walks carefully closer to the hole, and probes it with his beak.

Harriet — <Errr... well, this is so weird...> — ruffles plumage — <If you want I can try and get her out... my beak should be able to grab her more easily...>

Copahue -turns to her, blinks — <Er... well, it is my responsibility, but, yes, thanks...> — moves aside — <I welcome your help...>

Air!Shahinne -slaps the air angrily — <Get her out!>

Harriet walks in and carefully forces her way into the hole, reaching the Shahinne and clamping her carefully with her beak.

Hole!Shahinne — <[...OUCH!!!!]> — pecks Harriet's beak back.

Harriet — <...Eeeeeh?> — hops back in surprise.

Copahue — <What? WHAT????> — surrounds Harriet with a wing.

Harriet — <She...> — tilts head, surprised — <she pecked me...>

Copahue — <B-but that means...>

Copahue and Harriet look down at the hole where Shahinne is hiding, and then look back at Copahue's talon where the other Shahinne, the one who called them in, is looking up at them quizzically.

..

...

...

Air!Shahinne — <Aww bummer...> — chirps and disappears in a puff of smoke.

Copahue and Harriet — <Wh-- ◯v◯`>

Hole!Shahinne (Real) -still in her hole — <This one is mine... I want to sleep in it!>

Copahue and Harriet stare at each other for a moment, and then they try to get more comfortable around Shahinne's nest. The Starly is rearranging herself to sleep.

Harriet — <That was... strange.>

Copahue — <You tell me... I deal with this every day yet she surprises me...>

The two birds look inside the hole...

Shahinne — <...zZz...>

...then they look at each other and smile.

The Sun starts setting by the west.
