Kiloude City, Interpol HQ

There stood the International Police HQ. It's white walls gleamed with radiance, and it's brick walls bespoke resolution, and reliability. A Smeargle, working on the paintjob, stood back with pride.

It was huge, powerful, and imposing on the outside, the epitome of order.

To contrast, chaos raged on the inside.

There were the desk-jobbers, working furiously with mountains of paperwork on their desks. There were operators, so, so many, listening to clients. There were operatives training in the battle rooms, covered in white tiles that could be removed, and from underneath would arises showers, and landscapes, at but the command of someone at the control panel. There were some in the rec rooms, doing things best left to their own, there were those working out cases in the Clue Room, filled with maps of the countries, reference books, and devices.There was the underlying presence of activity.

There were two, however, who seemed calm and stoic. A man and a Gengar, playing a card game at a table at which none but them sat.

Captain Gregory Banks sat opposite the Gengar, his full hand of cards held in front of him. He was wearing a crisp blue suit, his tie dangling, open and untied. The Gengar looked at him and put down a card. It had 4000 attack, all of which would go directly to Gregory's life points.

With an ungainly ease and laze, Banks flipped up a card. It would block all the health from reaching him, and would instead send it back towards the Gengar.

The Gengar sniggered, as he flipped up a card of his own, one that would cancel the effect of Banks'. He sat, back, his permanent smile seeming to widen, triumphant.

Banks stroked the end of his graying mustache. He looked up at the Gengar and smiled.With a deep and clear voice, he said:"Well Jacques," and flipped up a card that would destroy the Gengar's" that's game."

The Gengar shrugged, and shook Bank's proffered hand. A nervous young man rushed up, and said in a quiet voice to Banks "Sir, Agent Argent wishes to speak to you."

Banks took the call and nodded and the man rushed away."Agent Argent, what can I do for you? You want me to allow you to go to Fiore? Funny enough, I was just about to tel you that. Maria'll be there as well, so hurry to the airport quick."

"No need to worry sir.."
