Umbra’s Pokestar Story!

Pokestar, past

-Umbra is looking around Pokestar when he’s approached by a talent scout-

Talent Scout: Hey... You’re part of the J-Team, right?

Umbra: Um, yes... Why?

Talent Scout: And you look the part... Perfect! I want you to play the lead role in this movie my boss is filming about the infamous Ghost Lord of Ransei!

Umbra: -Completely flabbergasted- But- But I-

Talent Scout: Excellent, you’re thrilled! Come with me!

-He takes Umbra’s arm and pulls him along-

Set of “The Ghost Lord”

-Umbra is all dressed up for his role and on the set-

Umbra: -Looking at his outfit- This isn’t how I dressed...

Director: OK, you remember those lines I gave you?

Umbra: Yes...

Director: OK, then... Action!

Umbra: -pounds his fists on a table and glares at two other actors- You insolent *fools*! Your blundering could have cost us this entire campaign! You better have a good explanation for yourselves if you want to keep your heads!

Director: -to the producer- Told you this guy was good.

Producer: Wow, it’s almost like he’s done this before...

Set of “The Ghost Lord”, later

Director: OK, the next scene involves your wife Duolis... We’re bringing her in now.

Umbra: Duolis?! :D

-A big-breasted, gray-eyed brunette smoking a cigarette is brought in-

“Duolis”: Hey there handsome. -cough-

Umbra: ...

You are not Duolis. >:|

“Duolis”: I can be whoever you want me to be, baby.

Umbra: YOU ARE NOT DUOLIS

Director: Can we just start filming, now, please? >_<

Set of “The Ghost Lord”, even later

Director: Aaaaand that should wrap up the necessary filming!

Umbra: But... What about the final battle?

Director: Oh, our SFX Zoroark Andy is taking care of that.

-At this point, the aforementioned Zoroark walks in... Chatting with an Arcanine that has bleached fur and red-orange stripes painted on it-

Umbra: O_O

AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA- AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH- HHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH

-Flees, pushing aside humans, mons, and equipment until he hides in a corner-

Director: Dude, are you still in character? ._.

Umbra: -Hysterically- GET THAT DEMON AWAY FROM ME

Arcanine: <Why is that human scared? Does he need cuddles?>

Andy: <I don’t think he’d appreciate your cuddles, Finley...>

Finley: <Awww...>

Umbra: -whimpers-

Lina: Pfft. Please. Husbands back then never did that. Why I...

-Goes into a long, angry rant-

Ammy’s Pokestar Story!

Pokestar, past

-Ammy is looking at some props, unaware that a man is inspecting her in turn. -

Man: Hmmm... Looks about right... -Approaches her- Excuse me, miss?

Human!Ammy: Hmmm?

Human!Ammy: Oh, sure! ^_^

-She follows him off-

Set of “Burn”

-The director and producer are inspecting Ammy-

Director: Hmmm... Physically, you look right for the part... And you audition well...

Producer: But your voice... It’s too soft. Can you do any other voices, other than your natural one?

Human!Ammy:    Like this?   

Director: Less terrifying.

Human!Ammy: -Deep voice- Like this?

Producer: A little more feminine.

Human!Ammy: -Doing what would be a dead-on impression of Sigourney Weaver in our world- Like this?

Director: Perfect! You’ve got the part!

Human!Ammy: Yay~

Set of “Burn”, later

-Ammy is dressed in a space marine outfit, toting a gun, and talking to the director-

Director: So, in this scene you’re unloading some lead into these space bugs. -Holds up some concept art-

Human!Ammy: Awww, they’re cute!

Director: Wha? Uh, they’re definitely not cute...

Human!Ammy: Why not, mister?

Director: Uh... A girl like you has a boyfriend, right?

Human!Ammy: Yup! He’s really nice! ^_^

Director: Imagine these things ate your boyfriend. On your wedding day.

Human!Ammy: ...

-Cocks her gun-

   LET ME AT THEM   

Director: That’s the spirit! Now come on, let’s get filming.

Set of “Burn, ” even later

Director: Now here is where you rip the alien queen’s spine out.

Human!Ammy: Okay! -does so-

Director: Ok, great! Now do that when we’re actually fil-

Human!Ammy: -swinging the spine around- Huwah! Huwah! Spine-fu!
