Ecruteak City - Outside

Eelektross seemed to stare at Diane intently. Valorie peered thoughtfully at Eelektross from behind Diane, just sort of peeking out from behind the taller girl.

"If you release me, I will haunt you." Shu's ghostly voice echoed in turn from behind her in an extremely deadpan tone. The ghost pokemon seemed to slide up onto Valorie's shoulder before moving to float off to the side. Valorie turned to face him.

"Listen—Uh... Back in my day we really didn't -care- about this sort of thing." the ghostly echo of a voice remarked, rather unable to summon passion. "But back in my day talking to girls like this was a scandal, so whatever. You know if talking to Pokemon could give you all the answers you're looking for I would've given them to you already. I have psychic powers, and even I don't have a clue how some things work." Shu began saying, working up to a speech.

"Like... the bond between a human and her Pokemon. I've been both and I still don't really understand that. It's... Well, that's not true actually. When you caught me, I felt something; it was a unique feeling, an incomprehensible force filling my ethereal body. I guess the best way I could describe it is that it made me feel strong... and warm." Shu mused, beginning to ramble a bit. Valorie's expression was priceless, a look of mild bewilderment on her face. Shu noticed her and snapped to attention.

"The point is that maybe you're not looking at this hard enough. Maybe Pokemon are a lot more like humans than you imagined; that just means you need to make it your goal to understand them even better, and you can't do that without your Pokemon." Shu finished, leaving Valorie in a wide eyed state as she just sort of absorbed what he'd told her... the important part anyway.

Lyuri and Duke are searching around the island, arriving at the clearing.

"Darkrai! You there?" yelled Lyuri.

Lyuri hadn't noticed that there was a shadow right in front of her from which Darkrai suddenly emerged from. She almost bumped into him when he showed up.

"GAH!"

With the scare, she takes a few steps back while looking shocked. Duke started to snicker behind her back.

"Hello, Lyuri, Duke. We haven't seen each other in quite some time." said Darkrai, completely stoic.

"H-Hey. Please don't try to give me another heart attack." said Lyuri, sighing.

"It's good to see you again, Darkrai." said Duke, smiling.

The legendary seemed midly amused with Lyuri's request, though he was also rather curious "Has something keeping you busy these last two months? You usually don't take too long before visiting again." he asked

"Well... That's exactly what I came to tell you about." said Lyuri, sighing "Say, because we're friends and all I must be your favorite human, right?"

"That sounds rather awkward." thought Duke.

"I... Wouldn't quite put it like that, but I get what you're saying. Yes, you're correct." answered Darkrai

"So... You won't really like this..."

Lyuri then starts telling Darkrai everything that happened with her in Orre and the whole blackmail ordeal with Allen, Duke just listens while looking slightly concerned with how that conversation was going to end.

"... And that's it. Basically, I was forced to work to an organization who wants to turn all Pokémon into weapons against my will to save my boyfriend." concluded Lyuri, looking down and rubbing the back of her neck.

Darkrai blinks a few times to the whole thing and stays silent for a minute or two before commenting.

"... I will say that I'm not entirely surprised, knowing how reckless you tend to be and acting more than thinking..." said Darkrai, giving a small pause

"That I can't disagree with." said Duke, scratching his head while looking away from Lyuri.

Lyuri sighs and leaves her arms hanging "Now you two are going to gang on me."

"... Still, I can understand why you did that, even if did backfire and had a heavy cost to you. I hope you at least won't be so impulsive next time you're put in another difficult situation."

Lyuri flips her hair while having a rather distant expression "It wasn't that difficult of a situation. I just acted like an idiot. And yeah, I'll think thrice before pulling another stunt like that."

"If she doesn't, hopefully I'll be there to stop her if she tries." said Duke, with a playful smile.

"Very well then. It's good to see that you two are still getting along fine after everything." said Darkrai.

"Yeah, I'm glad for that as well." said Lyuri, smiling at Duke "Good to see you're not upset with me for messing up that badly. Though I suppose you'd be really upset if I did it again."

"Correct." said Darkrai, bluntly.

Duke grimaces "I think everyone would be."

"Right. Of course. Anyway... Let's drop this subject and talk about something else." Lyuri then shrugs "I guess we have a lot of catching up to do." she then smiles
