Guildmaster's office

PGTC: Here is the application of a traveling merchant who wishes to join, guildmaster.

-the PGTC gives the Guildmaster the forms-

His name is Shokaki, and he's from Ignis. His business model is quit good; he gathers all the materials for his wares so he can sell them at better prices for better profit. He seems a bit goofy, but he appears to be a competent merchant as well as a nice guy. He also appears to be a bit of an idealist, as he wants to forge trade routes throughout Ransei. Despite his quirks, I personally believe he will be an excellent asset to the guild as he could travel to other kingdoms on our behalf and engage in trade negotiations in the future. I say this because if he does indeed gather all his own materials, he'd be in a better position to travel than the merchants we have in our guild, who are primarily based here in Terrea.

Because the Guildmaster is probably one of the people who reports directly to Sabuku, or to Sabuku's Minister "Who-Deals-With-Things-Like-Trade-And-Finance" (I have no clue what the proper tile would be for such a minister), I will refrain from naming or R Ping him unless given permission to. In fact, because of Sho's station, Sho is unlikely to talk directly to any kingdom's Warlord barring extreme circumstances. As such, all the kingdoms probably need a Minister of Trade (for the lack of a better title) of some sort for Shokaki to interact with unless the warlord themselves needs Sho. Otherwise I might accidentally affect the functions of other players' kingdoms and no one wants that.
