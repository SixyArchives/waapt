Team Gelato climb another stairway, emerging into a rather large valley on the side of the mountain.

<Mmm...>

Pausing to briefly wonder why Mystery Dungeons had all these items lying around inside of them (some of the potential answers seeming rather...grim), he does a quick count in his head.

Lake: <Eight, I think. Which means that our client must be around here somewhere.>

The Dragonite takes a deep breath.

<HEY, TYROGUE! WE'VE COME TO RESCUE YOU!!!>

Well, that was one way of doing it, Lake guessed.

For a few seconds, the valley remains quiet. Then, a small figure pokes his head out of a small crack in the wall several feet away. On closer inspection, it appeared to be a rather nervous Tyrouge.

Tyrogue: <...Who are you?>

Stratus: <We're Team Gelato!>

Tyrogue: <...Gelato?>

Lake: <We're an Exploration Team! Your friend Natu sent us to find you!>

Tyrogue: <Oh...that's great!>

The Tyrogue glances skywards, seemingly nervous about coming out of the crack in the wall, something that doesn't go unnoticed by Stratus.

Stratus: <What's wrong? You looked frightened...>

The fighting Pokémon shivers.

Tyrogue <I...I've been hiding...>

Lake: <Hiding? From what?>

Tyrogue: <From...>

The Pokémon's eyes suddenly go wide.

<DUCK!!!>

Stratus: <Huh?>

<SKREEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE!!!>

Lake: <WHOA! Everybody down!>

Lake grabs Stratus and pulls him to the ground as something huge swoops down and makes a grab at them with it's legs. Thankfully, they managed to duck just in time to avoid being carried off. Screeching angrily, the mysterious assailant soars up, out of sight.

Stratus: <...WHAT WAS THAT?!>
