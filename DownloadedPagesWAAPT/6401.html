160024 PippingFool19th May 2012 07:01:33 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

Mt Moon, Route 3 entrance. (Part 1)

The crisp spring air blows gently over the crevasses of Mt. Moon, weaving and dancing through the cracks on this late afternoon. The mountain is a soothing kind silent, with only the rolling of small rocks and the rustling of leaves and the occasional twitter of one or more birds as they settle down into their nests.

This peacefulness did not last forever though, for the silence was broken by a large snarl and the small grunts and yips of a party of Nidorino bucks staggering out of the entrance of the cave. Letting the sun shine on their fur for, what felt like to them, a lifetime.

These bucks have been travelling as hlessi* for a long time now. When they entered the cave, they didn't know how much of a dangerous labyrinth it could be for a wild Nido. The mountain cave was fierce, and it's inhabitants took just as kindly to intruders as Purrlions take to water. While their eyesight was much more adapted to the darkness, the twists and turns that lead them more often than not to dead ends and the gross lack of food (The only food being cave moss and small bugs) tired and weakened the party quickly. Had it not been for the small Spearow they befriended in the grass patches of Route 3, they would have perished in that cave.

The bucks slowly trek towards a small grassy patch by the base of the cave, eating clumps of flay here and there, clawing the soft earth between their paws or tending to some of the cuts and wounds they acquired why in the cave. One of the smaller Nidos, Buckthorn, lies down to let the Spearow they helped in Route 3 off their back.

Buckthorn: -Speaking Hedgecrow- <Here, we arrived at other side of the Hraan-Zorn-Hrair*, thank you for your help. You can leave if you wish.>

The Spearow hops off Buckthorn's back, he hops around before scratching at a part of the earth to dig up an earthworm. After he eats his prize, he answers the young buck.

Skree: <Leave? An' go where? Flocks' bin eatin' by 'em poisenfangs or bin caught by 'em hoo-mans. 'ere, 'ave food. 'ere, 'ave protection. Skree will stay 'ere, with poisen'orns.>

And the Spearow continues on with his merry business. Buckthorn does the closest thing a Nido can do to a shrug and lies down on a patch of soft earth, licking his wounds.

  • Hlessi - Nomad
  • Flay - Food
  • Hraan-Zorn-Hrair - lit: Mountain-Death-Many, or "Mountain of Many Deaths"
All about big hair, big shoes and big attitude!
