130590 Luke92419th Jan 2012 10:20:57 AM Get RP Mod

Cove Lily Motel

Checking us into the hotel wasn't easy, mainly because I could use my real name, or any of my credit cards, so not only did I have to pay up-front, I had to wear a disguise, and make sure that none of the staff saw me without it, too.

So not only was it difficult, but annoying, too.

Qualms aside, all of us were still asleep in our room at that point. Lake was cured around me protectively on the bed with me while Stratus slept close by leaning up against the wall, mumbling quietly in his sleep about wonderful room service was.

Treeo, meanwhile was snoozing by the window, having positioned himself there so that the morning sun would shine on his scales, essentially providing him with breakfast without having to be awake for it.

The last of my current team, Woolly and Comet, were asleep on the room's sofa. The Ampharos was curled up comfortably, resting her head on her flippers while her tail dangled of the side of her seat, the bulb at the tip occasionally flashing weakly.

Comet, although slightly less comfortable due to having to make sure she was in a position where her tail couldn't set fire to anything, was scrunched up in a tight ball at the other end of the peice of furniture, cradling our Pokémon egg gently in her claws, her body heat warming the egg as it had been these past few weeks since Christmas, when we had received it as a gift.

Having gotten it such a long time ago, and not having had any indication that it was close to hatching, we had all been wondering when the Pokémon inside would eventually emerge.

But we wouldn't have much longer to wait.

The egg shakes slightly, a crack appearing on the top of it shell. Shaking more, the crack grows bigger as the baby Pokémon inside begins to work it's way free. Such vigorous effort, however, is enough to stir Comet from her sleep.

Comet: <Zzz...Urgh, please don't see me... ...Uh?>

The Charmeleon opens her eyes, raising a claw to shield her eyes from the morning sun. She takes a second to get her bearings, then notices that the egg in her lap is now cracked in several places.

Her first instinct upon seeing this is to panic. Had she accidentally cracked it somehow in her sleep? What would everyone else think? Oh Arceus, she couldn't afford to mess up, not after last time.

To Comet's relief, however, he fears were allayed when she witnessed the egg shake of it's own accord, the cracks spreading even further across the shell.

Comet: <Oh! Everyone! Wake up!>

Hearing her friend shout, Woolly wearily raises her head, the Ampharos eyes only half-open. Still tired, she lets out a yawn.

Woolly: <Comet? What's the matter?>

The Charmeleon points at the shaking egg.

Comet: <It's hatching!>
