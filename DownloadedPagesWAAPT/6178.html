Treasure Town - PMD-A

Lake has expected that the kind of items that would be sold at a shop in this world would be different...but he hadn't expected to find a what appeared to be some sort of jaw-guard for sale.

Lake: <Excuse me, but what's this?>

He indicates the piece of amour to one of the owner of the stall, a Flygon.

Flygon: <Ah, that, my reptillian friend is a Hydro Jaw, a rather sought after item by Feraligatr such as yourself.>

Lake: <I see...what does it do?>

Flygon: <Well, by simply wearing it over your lower jaw, it will convert any electric attack that you are hit by into HP! Marvelous, no?>

There was a short pause as Lake gave the Flygon a skeptical look.

Lake: <...You're joking, right?>

At Lake's remark, the Flygon's eyes go wide, the insect-like dragon letting out an annoyed chitter.

Flygon: <Absolutely not! I would never even think of selling an item that didn't do work as intended!>

Lake: <Whoa! Sorry, sorry. It's just, it heals damage from any electric move? It almost sounds too good to be true.>

Flygon: <But it is true, good sir! And it can be yours for the price of 5000 Poké!>

Lake: <Oh, right...>

Lake frowned. He didn't have any of this "Poké", so buying anything was out of the question. For the moment...

<This might sound a bit weird, but if I wanted to earn some Poké, where would a Pokémon like me go?>

Flygon: <Oh, are you new in town?>

The Feraligatr nods quickly.

Lake: <Yeah, that's it! I'm new around here and totally not clueless about how things work in this society at all! Honest!>

Real smooth, Lake. If Feraligatr were capable of blushing, his cheeks would have been a rosy red. The Flygon shopkeeper, meanwhile, was beginning to suspect that this Feraligatr had spent a bit too much time at the bottom of a river or something.

Flygon: <...Anyway, there are various ways you could earn Poké but the two easiest are to get a job helping somemon at a stall...>

Lake: <Right...>

Flygon: <...Or find some like-minded Pokémon and form an exploration team at the guild.>

Lake: <Okay, got it.

...

...What's an exploration team?>

The Flygon stares blankly at Lake, the Feraligatr replying with an uncomfortable look.

<Yeah, I came from really far away to get here.>
