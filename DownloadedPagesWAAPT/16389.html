"We died. That's fine. That's a part of life. You need to move on, please. This? This is just a past version of us, I know it's hard to accept, but we have no future. You do. Don't spend it in the past, please."

Locke looks at his father properly now, and he's crying."I just don't want to let you guys go..I miss you so much..."

Drake pats his shoulder."That's why it's so hard." Locke moves forward into a fierce hug, that Drake recuperates, patting his son's head, as the snow twinkles down on them, like a cleanser, a blanket, the landscape of the heaven that now, might get some of it's denizens back.
