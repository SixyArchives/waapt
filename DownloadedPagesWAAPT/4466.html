 111648 Crewe, Sat, 12th Nov '11 8:33:06 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
Crewe, having left time a little while earlier, is doing what she always does when she's bored or upset: training.

And, having promised Dana a long time ago that the Rattata would never have to battle if she didn't want to, and being frankly too terrified of the Natu to let Kelly out of her ball, and the rest of her team who knows where, she's left with a single Pokemon to train.

This Pokemon has finished beating up several Seedots, and is frankly getting bored and more aggressive the more she battles.

"Okay, we need something else to fight. A non-Grass type. This is too easy for you." Crewe consults her Pokedex, slightly annoyed that she never thought to download Hoenn Pokemon on it and as such it is of pretty much no use to her.

Nova responds by setting her fires blazing and looking around for something.

Suddenly, while Crewe is still glaring at her Pokedex, the Quilava disappears for a moment, and hisses and squeals come from a small patch of grass. Crewe runs over to find Nova standing over a small, pinned Pokemon. The Pokedex beeps and registers it, flashing the name "Zigzagoon". After a moment, it beeps again and a star flashes by the image of the Zigzagoon.

"Huh. A shiny. Nova, stand down."

After a moment, the Quilava steps off, her flames dying down a little. She crouches down and watches the Zigzagoon, head tilted. Crewe reaches out one hand towards the cowering Pokemon, who backs away.

"Shh, shh, it's okay. I'm Crewe.''

<I'I'm A-Amberjack...> the Zigzagoon murmurs, ears flattened. She eyes the Pokedex. <I liked the shiny. Don't hurt me.>

"It's okay, I won't hurt you, Amberjack. That's a nice name." Crewe smiles as Amberjack's eyes flash with surprise. "Yes, I can understand you. You can trust me."

Amberjack takes a step forward, sniffing at Crewe's outstretched hand, her ears slowly coming forward.

<You're weird, human. But I'm weird too.>

Crewe carefully picks up the Zigzagoon, scratching her under the chin.

"You're extra special, Amberjack. Not weird. We're both special."

Amberjack smiles and purrs. <I like you, special human.>

"I like you too, Amberjack."

<C-can I... c-come...> She shrinks into herself a little, mumbling, and looks down as if embarrassed.

Crewe pauses, deciphering this, then her eyes light up. "Would you like to come with me?"

Amberjack nods a little, still looking down.

"Sure, Amberjack. Of course you can come with me."

The little Zigzagoon's eyes light up and she purrs again. Crewe smiles and pulls out a Pokeball, tapping her head with it. A few seconds later, she's walking through the grass with three young Pokemon (and a psychotic Natu in a Pokeball).
