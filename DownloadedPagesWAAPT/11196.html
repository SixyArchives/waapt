Terrera is under no obligation to appease the demands of Pugilis. Let me count the ways:

One: Our open border agreement specifically states that Terrera must avoid diplomatic relations with Spectra. Even if we were to consider your collaboration a breach of trust, we would not be able to acquiesce to your demands without nullifying the basis for them simultaneously.

Two: The previous Warlord Lucanus Cain may now have returned to Pugilis, but until the people have accepted him as their Warlord once again, he will only be considered the commander of an occupying force.

In summary: Go suck a Geodude.

Sincerely, Sabaku of Terrera

-Sabaku sets down his pen, looking equal parts proud and apprehensive. He heads of toward the Gligar dens, and afterwards sets out to rendezvous with his men at the border.-
