Kalos League Pokémon Center

Upstairs, Lyuri, Duke, Aline, and Hikari are on the former two's room, sitting on a table. Lyuri was playing solitaire, Duke was reading a big book Hikari lent him, Aline was drawing and Hikari was making adjustments to her water balloon bazooka.

They are all completely silent and the only sounds are of Aline's pencil scribbling on the paper, Hikari's tinkering and the occasional page flip from Duke.

About five minutes later

As Hikari is tinkering, she accidentally pulls the trigger on the bazooka and it was still loaded, ending up firing right at Duke. Somehow the book managed to escape unharmed, but Duke was completely drenched.

Aline starts laughing, but of course no noise comes from her, Lyuri starts snickering while covering her mouth with her hand. Hikari just gives a sheepish smile as she puts her hand on her head.

Duke seemed mildly annoyed, then got up from his seat to dry himself.

A few minutes later:

Lyuri decided to make a card castle. She was doing it pretty well and was about it to finish.

Then Duke gave a rather strong sneeze that made the castle lose it's balance and collapse.

Lyuri glares at Duke, who just decides to cover his face with the book. Aline and Hikari both snicker.

Later:

Lyuri went back to solitaire, Duke was still reading, Aline had finished her drawing and Hikari was about to finish her work, she then took a leave to go to the bathroom.

Aline and Lyuri glance at each other, then at the bazooka. Curious, Aline picks the bazooka up.

Unfortunately it wasn't exactly stable yet and upon doing so it completely fell apart.

Aline jaw dropped while Lyuri just blinked, Duke snickered a bit at the whole thing.

Hikari soon comes back and see all her work gone to waste while Aline was still holding some of the pieces.

Aline looks at Hikari sheepishly while she slowly slides down her seat so as to go out of sight.

Hikari blinks for a few seconds before giving a big sigh.

A few moments later:

Hikari was just sitting with one elbow on the table, looking bored. Duke was still reading. Lyuri was looking up as if waiting for something to happen while tilting her head back and forth. Aline was now painting and had a few small jars of colored paint on the table.

Hikari decided to start what she was doing earlier from scratch.

Lyuri was so bored she proceeded to headdesk so hard that it made the table shakes and cause the paint jars to fall, the paints ends up all falling onto Aline's dress, who quickly gets up with a now rainbow skirt and legs.

Hikari winces at the situation, while Duke just blinks.

Lyuri raises her head and looks at the mess she made, she blinks for a moment.

"... You know what, how about we do our stuff at our rooms so there's nobody around to ruin everything?" asked Lyuri

Hikari and Aline nods and the former picks up her things and leaves, while the latter goes to take a shower. Duke and Lyuri obviously stay.

Lyuri then proceeds to put both of her arms crossed on the table and lie her head on them as if she was going to sleep.

Of course she forgot that the table was currently drenched in paint and soon got up to see her arms dripping with paint.

She groans and heads to the bathroom. Duke just stays there, reading without a care in the world.
