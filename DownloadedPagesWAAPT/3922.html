(Switching to para-RP for this post.)

Rex yawned a little, sitting down on the beach sand wearily. His gaze flickered over to the nearby dancers, marveling at their complex movements. Some of the islanders looked his way, but most were too enraptured in their dance to pay him any mind.

'I'd probably be more interested in what they were doing if I wasn't so tired...' he thought to himself, rubbing his eyes, a yawn stuck in the back of his throat. 'Maybe tomorrow...'

He curled his legs up to his chest, drooping over sleepily as fatigue began to take its hold.

Naturally, he was surprised when he found himself suddenly jerked to his feet, being dragged in the direction of the dancers.

"H-hey!" he yelped. "What gives!?"

"This is no time for sleeping, young one, " said the person moving him, a woman from all indications. He couldn't tell due to her face paint and slightly masculine build. "You must join in the dance."

"What? No! I-I don't know how to dance!"

"Come come, " the woman continued, dragging poor Rex over to the dancers. "This is the Festival of Flames. What good is a flame that sits still?"

"But but..."

The woman gave him a gentle shove, Rex finding himself in the middle of about half a dozen people around his age. He felt his cheeks flush when some of them looked his way, some of the girls smiling encouragingly, while others giggled a little at his awkwardness.

"Oh, what the heck..." He sighed and began trying to imitate some of the male dancers, hoping to Arceus he didn't make a fool of himself.
