Shalour City

A flurry of half-processed images and sensations rushed through Valorie's head. She was leaving home. It was dark and she immediately set foot through the snow towards the Iccirus City Gym. For some reason her satchel wasn't with her. The cold brushed past her thighs and suddenly she was on route 10. The cold was rapidly intensifying. She was reliving her dream-death at the hands of Lina; the cold was already eating at her, her limbs going numb and her voice failed her as she tried to call for help.

Charizard was nowhere to be found.

Suddenly Valorie jolted to a new reality. She was outside on a dimly lit street, heart racing and head pounding. She took a moment to look around her, frightened by the prospect of not knowing where she was. A long moment's analysis revealed that she was entirely lucid and that she... had an odd craving for seafood.

Huh. She'd been dreaming; judging by her location she'd also been sleep walking, in Sparkers' coat too! Valorie took a deep breath and sighed to calm herself. She quickly took the moment to confirm that she was still in Shalour City before tugging the coat more tightly around herself and proceeding on to Route 12. Her rest was ruined as far as she was concerned, but at least she could do some training...

She needed company anyway after that horrible dream she'd had.
