Sitting off to the side, Rex is a bit silent, not really sure how to reacquaint himself with everyone after so long. While everyone was still the same, more or less, it felt like there was a bit of a gulf between them. It was likely just silly on his part, but for now he'd simply mull on his feelings. His team was currently resting in the Pokéballs from their early training and walkabout, the boy absently reaching for his Pokédex without anything further to do at the moment.

As he did, however, his Pokégear began to beep, the boy blinking and checking the device to see just whom he'd gotten a message from.

Rex,

Hey, are you alright? I know you don't really celebrate this time of year, but I thought I'd at least wish you a happy holiday. I hope you're doing well and, well... I kind of miss you. At the moment, though, I'm a bit tied up in Ranger training in Sinnoh. Not having a styler to use anymore really makes things a lot more difficult, but that's how it goes, I guess.

I hear that you've been up to some research projects, too. Is there anything I can help with? I know I'm far away, but I could always send Aqua or one of my other Pokémon whenever you need it.

Anyway, just wanted to check in on you. With any luck, we can hang out again sometime soon.

Stay safe, okay?

- Kim

Smiling a little as he read, Rex couldn't help but feel a little guilty about not calling the girl sooner. And yet, she didn't seem to be upset at all. She really was a good friend.
