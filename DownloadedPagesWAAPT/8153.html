Conquistador: <Hey, watch where you're going!> -he regains his balance and looks up to see a ruffled and annoyed looking Pidgeot, with a Scyther and Arbok flanking her-

Conquistador: <Whatever I want! And you can't stop me!>

Pidgeot: <Stop you? Now, I'm a reasonable 'mon, I'm not against a young'un getting up to some mischief, so long as he'll keep the peace with those that need it... But I'd say you need to be taken down a peg, runt.>

Scyther: -hisses- <Let me do it for you, ma'am. This one don't strike me right.>

Arbok: <You got the last one, it's my turn!>

Pidgeot: <Peace, friends, you'll all have your chance. Now, then, runt, why don't you scurry along before I let my friends here show you your place?>

Conquistador: -snorts, smoke trailing from his nostrils- <I don't have to listen to you! I can beat you up and show you your place!>

Scyther: -hisses louder and brandishes his scythes- <Please, ma'am, let me—>

Pidgeot: <I said peace, boy. Now, little Ponyta, as I see it, there's a lesson to be learned here. What might that lesson be, d'you think?>

Arbok: <That some runts deserve to be beat senseless?>

Pidgeot: <Let the boy answer!>

Conquistador: <I think the lesson is that stinky birds should stop bothering me!>

Pidgeot: -titters- <Oh, dearie me. Do you think he got the answer right, my friends?>

Scyther: -hisses-

Arbok: <No, ma'am!>

Pidgeot: <It would seem my friends are rather better at guessing, runt. Would you like me to give you the correct answer?>

Conquistador: -stamps his hoof and snorts- <Just leave me alone!>

Pidgeot: <Now now, there's a learning opportunity here! Let me tell you what you should learn here.> -she preens herself- <Some mons are just better than others. Better fighters, more friendly, more humble, smarter... you understand, no?>

-Conquistador starts backing up, ears pinned-

Pidgeot: <And some mons are worthless. That's you, dear.>

Conquistador: <I'm the best mon ever!>

Pidgeot: -sighs- <I see. I suppose some mons need to be taught what worthless wastes of space they are the hard way. Allow my friends to show you.>

-she spreads her wings and the Scyther and Arbok advances on Conquistador, hissing. The Ponyta colt snorts a few wisps of smoke and leaps forward in a tackle.
