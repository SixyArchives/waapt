156557 Luke9244th May 2012 08:33:52 AM Get RP Mod

Cave of Lore

Lore!Salamence: <You want to know about our founder?>

The Salamence nods, then retreats further back into the cave, where the sounds of a pile of items being dug through can be heard.

<Well, Bluescale was actually founded by two Pokémon - a Feraligatr and a Salamence, who both fought against the Dragon's during the Draconic Uprising several hundred years ago. Although they were unable to succeed in preventing the human race from being destroyed, they eventually managed to drive the Draconic oppressors from the land of Johto.

Having succeeded in saving the land, the two came to the swamp that would eventually become Bluescale and settled down with the Feraligatr's family. Eventually, Salamence from all over the land, who were being discriminated against simply for being dragons, even though the weren't aligned with the invaders at all, came to the swamp to seek refuge.

Unable to turn them away, the Feraligatr and Salamence welcomed them with open arms to liv alongside them peacefully. As word spread of their kindness, more and more Salamence came to live here in peace, and eventually there were just as many of them as there were Feraligatr.

Working together, the Feraligatr and Salamence, under the guidance of the two who had fought the dragons, built the town of Bluescale, which took it's name from the azure scales both their species shared.

And it was here in Bluescale that the two lived the rest of their days. Ah, here it is.>

The Salamence returns, carrying a rather old painting of a very familiar looking Feraligatr wearing a yellow scarf, and a certain female Salamence.

<This is one of the few surviving portraits we have of them. Oh!>

The Salamence pauses, looking slightly embarrassed.

I've just realized, I never answered your question. The names of our founders...are Lake and Nadia.>
