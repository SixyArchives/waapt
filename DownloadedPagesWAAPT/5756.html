"What? Suddenly chicken?" Ashley asks, teasing.

"Um, you realize you only have like, two types, right? And I can beat all of them?" Hunter raises an eyebrow.

"Actually, I have six types. Cole, Clockwise, and Domino are all Normal/Flying. But I also have Bug, Poison, Ground, and Fire."

"Yeah, whatever, six types. But, um, you have four Flying types. Serge counters all four of them," Hunter says, gesturing towards the posturing Luxio. "And Trixie and Martin can both easily counter the other two."

<Whoa! That 'duck can not counter me,> James says, setting his fists on fire.

<I already kicked your butt, nose boy. Want me to do it again?> Trixie crosses her arms, narrowing her eyes.

<You two. I said truce,> Cesar snaps.

"Okay, so you have some type advantages," Ashley says. "Your point?"

"Well, that's not it. Like I said, I've spent weeks training against other trainers. All my Pokemon are pretty strong. How often do you train?"

"Um... whenever a trainer sees me and demands a battle," Ashley says, still not quite getting it.

"And how often does that happen?"

"Well, we spend a lot of time in the wilderness, so... a couple times a month? Plus angry wild Pokemon," Ashley says after a moment of thought.

<Actually, we run away from wild Pokemon mostly,> Ion corrects.

<Dude, he can't understand you,> James says, rolling his eyes.

<Oh, right.>

"Okay, and considering Clockwise was only recently caught, none of your Pokemon spend a lot of time training..." Hunter says, motioning with his hands for Ashley to track this thought process.

"What's your point, Hunter?" she asks, tapping her foot.

Hunter sighs. "Let's try it this way. Who's your strongest Pokemon?"

"Hm... Cole or James."

<No offense dude, but it's totally me. You're kind of a wimp.>

<You want to say that again, James?>

"Okay... what are their strongest moves?"

"Well, James has Slash, and Cole has Air Slash."

"Okay, so look at my Pokemon. Cesar knows Brave Bird, which the strongest move Staraptor can learn. Trixie knows Hydro Pump. Are you getting it?"

Ashley sighs. "Hunter, you're leading up to something. Just spit it out."

"Sand."

"Whatever."

"Okay, wise guy, let's just see."

"Suit yourself."

"Cole!"

"Serge."

"Now!"

Serge lets off a Spark just as Cole crashes into him, the Charge increasing its power. Cole's attack knocks the Luxio back and he hisses in pain, digging his claws into the sand.

The two separate, both shaking their heads.

"Well, Swagger's pretty risky," Hunter says, shrugging. "Serge, Leer and Spark again."

"Quick Attack. Finish it!"

<With pleasure,> Cole hisses, shaking his head again and mantling his wings before charging. Serge waits for him, leering, then crashes into his underbelly while coated in electricity.

"Again!" Hunter calls.

"Don't let him escape!" Hunter yells as the Pidgeot starts trying to hop away. Serge Bites down on his wing, pinning him with one paw.
