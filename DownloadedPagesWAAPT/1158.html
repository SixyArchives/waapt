...The Sky Fortress is a weapon. Many, many years ago, like a thousand or more, an evil man designed the ultimate weapon; a huge, flying fortress with tons of surprisingly modern weapons attached to it, such as the massive plasma cannon that completely vaporized Dolce Island. And to go with it, he forged a suit of Golden Armor. The armor let you control the Sky Fortress, mind-control Pokemon, and to top it all off granted immortality. Now, no one is sure how it was all stopped, but the man was defeated, the Sky Fortress was sealed at the bottom of a lake with awakening the legendary birds being the only way to unseal it, and the Armor was stashed away within it as well. The Pokemon Pinchers, hired by the Societea, managed to awaken the birds, at which point the Societea entered the Sky Fortress, donned the Golden Armor, and tried to make Oblivia bow to their will, destroying Dolce Island as a demonstration. Three rangers and a brilliantly smart girl were able to invade the Fortress, defeat the Societea, then defeat a spurned Pincher admin who wanted revenge for being used as a pawn, and then used Ho-oh to prevent the Fortress from making a tsunami.

Whew...

3 members of the Societea are in jail, the other is gone and won't be coming back, the Pincher admin is... only Arceus knows where, and I mean that literally, the Sky Fortress floats inertly on the surface of the ocean, and the Golden Armor is locked up at the Ranger Union HQ.
