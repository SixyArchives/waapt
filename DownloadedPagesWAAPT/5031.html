Terrakion: <Argh! If they don't hurry up soon they're gonna' be late!>

Virizion: <Oh, please. When have those three ever been late for anything?>

Cobalion: <Lady Virizion is right, Sir Terrakion. It is unlike them to be late for such an important event as this.>

Terrakion: <Well, I guess so...>

Keldeo I: <So...how does everymon think this year went, looking back on things?>

Keldeo: <Well, I think it was awesome. We got to make friends with Luke and the others, AND we got to kill icky glitch Pokémon!>

The Colt Pokémon sticks his tongue out at the thought of 'M.

<He was nasty!>

Terrakion: <I agree with you on that one, little guy.>

Keldeo I: <Aren't you forgetting something, son?>

Keldeo: <Huh? Oh, um...>

Keldeo thinks for a second, scratching tapping ground with his hoof in thought.

<Oh, yeah! You came back, Dad!>

Terrakion: <WAHAHAH! I can't believe you forgot something as important as your daddy comin' home! Aww!>

Terrakion hugs Keldeo affectionately, the young horse sweatdropping in embarrassment.

Keldeo: <Hey! Somemon get him off me!>

Keldeo I: <Okay, okay! That's enough, Terrakion!>

Terrakion: <Aww, okay.>

Terrakion releases Keldeo from his grip, but not before playfully rubbing his head.

Keldeo: <Aww, jeez! I just got this right!>

As Keldeo tries to fix the damage, Virizion gazes up at the sky longingly, an act Cobalion doesn't fail to notice.

Cobalion: <What's troubling you, my lady?>

Virizion: <...It's just...it's been so long since we've seen everyone. I'm beginning to miss them.>

Cobalion: <Well, I'm sure we can take efforts to correct that. Perhaps we could arrange a meeting the next time they visit our fair land?>

Keldeo I: <I'm not throwing another party...>

Keldeo: <Hey, that was my idea! And it didn't go that badly...>

Virizion looks at Cobalion, a smile crossing her face.

Virizion: <...I'd like that a lot.>

Suddenly, three loud roars sound from far away.

Terrakion: <Hey, they're comin'! Quick, before we miss it!>

The Five Pokémon gaze up at the sky to witness a Black and a White shape fly past at almost supersonic speed, leaving a trail of electricity and fire in their wake, eliciting a cheer from the sabots. Until...

Keldeo I: <Wait, where's Kyurem?!>

Another roar sounds, and a grey shape soars past, leaving a trail of ice...which promptly freezes over the moor, leaving the Sabots buried on snow. With a sigh, Cobalion shakes the snow off his head.

Cobalion: <With any luck, Kyurem should find his true form this year...>

The others nod in agreement.

Keldeo thinks for a second, scratching tapping ground with his hoof in thought.
