[AU] Inka's Place, yesterday morning

"Dad! Dad~!"

"Eeeeep~!"

"Burrow! I have to burrow!"

-Baluarkos stands near a tree, holding his position and waving the cloud of dust that has arisen, as he shields nine small kits that have run to his side for cover. After they cling to his ankle and knees for dear life, crying, he carefully curls his tail around himself and clenches a bit to offer them protection. And on top of Baluarkos's head, a sole Wooper is also trying to hide behind the Nido's ears-

Wooper — <No please don't kill the messenger! I is a good woopah!>

Baluarkos — <Sshhhh! Don't worry! Don't worry!>

-Before him, a couple of meters away, Inka can be seen clawing the remains of a tree into oblivion, slowly calming herself down as the dust also subsides-

Inka — <If they even dare to think to go...!>

-The bravest kit peeks from behind Baluarkos's leg to watch the destruction before him. Where there was a row of trees and some nifty grass to nom there is enough pierced bark and moved around rocks and pebbles that it looks like a Nidoqueen would have gone on a rampage, which is pretty much what happened-

Inka — <I warned them! I told Mayonaka to warn them...!> — dissolves a rock into dust in her paw.

Brave Kit — <...! Eeeep~! No! Mom no!> — jumps down back with the group, the nine kittens hugging each other as Baluarkos wraps them.

-Inka moves her ears around and then turns her head slowly-

Baluarkos — looks around — <Good thing we didn't hear that Silent had been ACTUALLY hurt...> — to his mate — <Inka...? Inka, you are fine already?>

-The Nidoqueen breathes hard, then lets out a couple of tears before rubbing her face and turning back to the group-

Inka — rubs arms — <I... I am sorry...>

-the kittens remain clung to Baluarkos strong enough that he can afford walking forwards a couple of steps, however he has to stop still a good distance from Inka because the kittens notice and start crying-

"No Mom no!" they cry.

-In response, Baluarkos gives a very angry look at his mate-

Baluarkos — <You scared them all. They are too young for this! Look around!> — tilts head to notice a berry tree torn down — <Our place...>

Inka — <I did not want this... I am sorry, really!> — sighs — <I have been stressed and tired... and when I heard those news it was as if my mind went on a loop, showing me once and again what his death would be like...>

-Inka soundly sits in the ground, abated, and avoid looking back at the group. After a couple of minutes of calming down, Inka starts waging her tail. The Wooper takes his chance to jump down to the group of kits too for hugs and he is welcomed by them-

Random Nidoran♀ kit — <Why Mom why?>

Random Nidoran♂ kit — <We didn't do anything bad...>

Random Nidoran♀ kit — <I don't want to lose Mom again!>

-The kits start trembling and sobbing again-

-And that has an effect on Baluarkos too; he doesn't realize when he soundly stomps on the ground and bares his fangs to his wife, addressing her in a menacing tone-

Baluarkos — <Is that you don't think what you are DOING???> — swings his arm around, randomly knocking yet another tree — <We brought them here so that they WOULDN'T BE SCARED!>

Inka — <Don't...>

Baluarkos — < AND LOOK AT WHAT YOU ARE DOING!!! WITHOUT ANY CONSIDERATION!!! HAVE YOU FORGOTTEN HOW TO BE A MOTHER????>

Inka — <...> — obviously flinches.

-now the kits start crying even louder and hugging their adoptive father for comfort, too-

-Luckily for the kits, with Inka and Baluarkos tired, the effect of the cries trigger the release of soothing hormones in the royal evolutions; Baluarkos softly starts petting the kits (and the Wooper) as he tries to hush them and calm them down-

Baluarkos — <I'm sorry I'm sorry....>

-Inka starts calming down as well-

Inka — <I am sorry, too...> — hiccups — <This... this is not gonna happen anylonger. Shhhh....>

-Inka stands up and turns around, to see the remains of the berry tree that she had planted as a welcome for her adoptive children just a couple of days ago. It had been laborious, noisy, restless days ever since, as the couple were not used to tend to this many kits and were not that used to childraising any longer, not even with the help of Inka's memories from the other timeline, as those were more apt for a Nidorino couple than for handling by larger, stronger Royal Evolutions-

Inka — <You kits will forgive your mom, please?> — picks up a couple of random, fallen Mint Berries — <Mom promises to not be this misbehaved again.>

-Baluarkos stares at her, inquiring with a look what will happen if worse news come around; Inka shakes her head and stares at the ground for a bit-

Random Kit — <It was me who ate the carrot! Please don't punish my siblings!>

Random Kit — <We didn't want to do anything bad! We promise!>

-Inka walks in a couple of steps, and then sits in the ground as peacefully as she can manage-

Inka — <This is not about the carrot, understand? Mom and Dad have had a terrible day... we are a bit tired and feel threatened too sometimes...> — offers a berry to the group — <But this is no excuse for Mom and Dad to be unnice to you.>

-The kits look back at their adoptive mother, still to scared to get close to her again, and cling to Baluarkos more painfully (for him) this time. Baluarkos has to resist with all his might the urge to cry to the heavens as the kits reach for his knee-

-However, as the kits cry, the messenger Wooper curiously pops out from the group and carefully takes the berry from Inka's paw-

Wooper — <This is... a rare berry~> — licks it — <It is bitter~>

-Some of the kits turn their ears to their visitor as he turns to them and, with a smile, goes to the center of the group too carrying the berry-

Wooper — <Here, try it~> — offers a piece of it to one of the kits.

-The kits, after smelling the berry, start forgetting the Royal Evolutions and the destruction around them and let their instinct to examine the berry take over, mumbling and screeching and joyfully poking and pushing each other as they try to get a piece of the rare berry-

-Inka raises her sight to Baluarkos, who wraps the kits (and the Wooper) tighter with his tail, covering them fully as he leans forward and nuzzles his mate. Inka doesn't return the gesture in the beginning, but after a second try she allows herself to shed some tears and embrace her husband-

Inka — <I hope nothing bad is going to ever happen again...>

Baluarkos — <We are going to be fine, promise. Look... you need to learn to trust in other peoplemons to protect him, right?>

-Inka nods-

Inka — <I would still feel safer if we left the humans a warning. I guess they are going to be more insistent on coming here to look, and I don't want the kits to run any danger...>

-Baluarkos looks at a patch of ground where a weird red spike can be seem barely emerging from the ground, and grins-

Baluarkos — <You let your mate take care of that...>

Inka — <But->

-Baluarkos presses his muzzle against Inka's to shut her up, and they nuzzle a bit more as the kits start casually fighting over the pieces of the berry-

Baluarkos — <You let me do this, and you spend the day with the kits, right?>

-Inka smiles a bit, then faces her mate and as the two of them close their eyes, willingly unaware of the world around them, they let their instinct to take over as she starts to happily lick Baluarkos's muzzle, and from there they start licking each other in more places...-

-Up the hill, the two Nidorina observe-

Breeder #2 — <What is she doing?>

Breeder #1 — <It's something humans do, I think it's called "make-up mating".>

Breeder #2 — <...And they do it in front of their kits too?>

Breeder #1 — <I think they do, yeah...>

-they tilt their heads and blink at unison-

Breeder #1 — <...>

Breeder #2 — <...> — scratches head — <Looks like an interesting trick...>

Breeder #1 — <Yeah...> — flickers ears — <Hey! Let's try it on Rize and Vinollo~>

Breeder #2 — <Yay~! They're not gonna escape this time~>

-they scout away-

-meanwhile, still under the Nidoran cuddlepile-

Wooper Messenger — <Can I get out please? ...Some help?>
