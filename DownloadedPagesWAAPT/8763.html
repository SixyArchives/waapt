Draconic Council HQ, some time after Cassandra meets Bahahkun

- Cassandra enters and approaches Alduin-

Cassandra: Lord Alduin... You summoned me? Please tell me that this is about dealing with that imbecile strongclaw that was presented before the council earlier...

AU!Alduin: Ah, yes, Quostiid... I have some very important news for you...

Cassandra: ... 'Cassandra', please, my lord... >_< >_< >_<

I don't understand why my clan *insists* on mons in leadership positions on carrying such mouthfuls for names... >_< >_< >_<

Alduin: Cassandra, yes... Anyway, I wish to inform you of a... Err... How do I put this?

Cassandra: ... Has something come up? We could always postpone the invasions! I was of the opinion that they needed more planning, myself! ._. ._. ._.

Alduin: No, I'm talking more a... Re-positioning.

Cassandra: ... Re-positioning? Of what? I mean, Qoriik or whatever he's styling himself now seems haughty, yes... But the legitimacy he provides our cause is well worth tolerating the annoyances he causes!

Seishirou: I don't like Lord Alduin's prospects, brother...

Fuuma: Oh, he'll be fine. This is entertaining.

Vallok: Bah, spit it OUT already! Were I leader already, I'd have handled this ages ago!

Alduin: Not him, dear...

Cassandra: ... I... I'm not sure if I follow... Whom are you talking about, then? Seishirou and Fuuma are both competent, if shortsighted... Vallok... Uh... Well, he devised our airship and weapon designs, so let's give him that...

Vallok: Oh, I cannot WAIT for that crone and her nagging to be gone...

Cassandra: ... What are you talking about?

Seishirou: Am I the only one who is annoyed by how she always forgets Paarthurnax, as tenuous as his position is?

Fuuma: Hardly.

Alduin: I mean you, Cassandra. You're being demoted.

Cassandra: I-I- WHAT?! But WHY?! And for who?!

Lord Alduin, I understand that I have a bit of a tenuous position as clan leader, but-

I have actual battle experience! My superior was one of the most respected generals under Scolipede and Liepard! I still have connections to most of the former reserves! It's what the Unova campaign is reliant on in some quarters!

Alduin: Yes, but Bahahkun has shown outstanding ambition, prowess, and knowledge of battling tactics. I am sure she shall be able to fill your clawprints adequately.

Cassandra: And-

...

... ...

... ... ...

Fuu: Here come the fireworks...

Cassandra:    YOU'RE FILLING MY POSITION WITH A   

-Background coffeemaker-

   -ING ARENA FIGHTER?!   

Alduin: Young ladymon, we need the council to represent all walks of dragon life.... And she helps with species representation! Three Hydreigon is nice in some ways, yes, but not quite feasible...

Cassandra:    IT'S PROPORTIONAL REPRESENTATION! OUR KIND MAKES UP MORE THAN HALF OF ALL DRAGONS IN UNOVA! IT'S BEEN THAT WAY FOR ALMOST A DECADE!   

   AND WHAT ON EARTH COULD THAT IMBECILE HATCHET-HEAD POSSIBLY PROVIDE FOR YOU?! SHE'S DUMB MUSCLE! I'D SOONER PROMOTE A WOODEN POST TO A LEADERSHIP POSITION BEFORE A MON OF HER STOCK!   

Alduin: It's about more than just Unova... And she's active on the battlefield! Something I've only.really seen Odyol and Fusgol doing...

Cassandra:    THAT'S BECAUSE I'VE BEEN BUSY HERE TRYING TO CORRECT THEIR INANE OVERSIGHTS!   

-struggling to compose self-

Lord Alduin, do you remember the initial proposal for what we were to bombard the cities with?

Seishirou: Inane? :/

Cassandra: INCENDIARIES. The INITIAL plan of this council was to attempt to turn every human settlement it ran across into an ASH HEAP!

We're ALREADY treading tenuous ground with the gods here! I watched as my superior was forced to concede defeat lest he throw away his subordinates' lives simply for being on the WRONG SIDE as the gods!

Alduin: Yes, but we quickly realized that wouldn't work with said gods breathing down our necks, and we adapted! It's what we always do, Cassandra.

Cassandra: But our present plans do nothing to account for LONG-TERM perpetuation of our empire! There's no planning beyond what I have set out as a matter to be brought up at the next meeting! No planning for infrastructure, none for political organization, the only thing we have at the moment is mass evictions, that quite frankly, am baffled that we're humoring that as a preventative measure for handling dissent!

Humans are apathetic creatures! Why give them more grievances to rally around! Let them, their culture, and their knowledge of the past die off in a few generations! Don't attempt to force it in a matter of a few years!

Alduin: The long-term is long term for a reason, Cassandra! And humans are also easily made to submit! And besides, they'll have greviances and pathetic attempts to preserve old knowledge no matter what we do!

Cassandra: That's if they deign it pertinent to! Look at humans, why they've all but forgotten how to fashion slave balls without those massive factories of theirs! If you wean them and make them complacent, their knowledge will all but dissapate on their own!

Alduin: I doubt they will be complacent about us trying to cleanse them from this planet... Forceful ruling is necessary in times like this!

Cassandra: You say that you want our kind to assume the head of an empire expanding beyond a patchwork of dens, do you not? The plans that we have at the moment are fit only to wage a campaign of extermination! And you and I BOTH know that will end in nothing but disaster! We cannot afford to wield the cruelty needed for such a campaign, and it would destroy what we hope to gain, so why are we still planning in that mindset?!

Lord Alduin, do you want my bare, naked opinion on our odds of success with this strategy? Without modifications?

Alduin: Humans have kept us in slave balls, forced us to fight, caused untold numbers of atrocities... If anything, we're being merciful compared to them! And what is it?!

Cassandra: I see an empire collapsing under its own weight by over-extension thanks to a lack of allies, a suffocating number of enemies, and overly thin ranks. Without a single prize greater than the dens we possess now that lies not in ruins. In 60 moons, IF that.

Alduin: ...Look, Cassandra. Continual paranoia gets you nowhere. We will succeed in wiping out humanity and assuming our rightful place, and your catastrophizing just slows us down!

Cassandra: You- You- FINE! Go off and march your clansclaws into oblivion! I for one refuse to have any part in this charade! And I am certain the rest of the Opal Roar will concur!

Seishirou: ...Lord Alduin? A suggestion?

Alduin: ...Yes, Odyol?

Seishirou: Perhaps... Move her into a position more applicable to her... Interests?

Alduin: Hmm... Could work... Perhaps...

You shall be repositioned in Unova, somehow... Is that an acceptable comprise?

Cassandra: Repositioned HOW? To be pushed around by that arena fighter? PLEASE... -rolls eyes-

Alduin: You will have control over your own forces at least. And have a degree of autonomy.

Cassandra: ... Go on...

One explanation later

Cassandra: ... And the arena fighter will be responsible for her own monpower?

Alduin: Indeed. Ideally with some assistance from you, though...

Cassandra: Mmph. If any in the Opal Roar are idiotic enough to court their own destruction, the fool is welcome to their strength. My clan needs not such mons contaminating the gene pool.

Alduin: ...If you insist~

Cassandra: So be it. Though for the record, it will ultimately be the Hydreigon that establishes the rule of dragons over all Unova. And not the adventurism of that hatchet-face!

-flies off-

Alduin: -once Cassandra’s out of earshot- Now that she's taken care of...

Sitrus mimosas, on me.

Other Jerk Dragons: -Raucous cheering-
