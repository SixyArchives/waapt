 16506 Crewe, Thu, 24th Mar '11 6:23:31 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
All right, here goes nothing. In case you can't figure it out, Roy is the name I chose for my rival in Platinum. And I really hope Twinleaf Town is the right town, I always get it and New Bark mixed up.

- Twinleaf Town, Two Years ago. Crewe has returned home for the first time in a year after defeating the Elite Four and Cynthia. She sneaks in, hoping the house will be deserted so she can catch a few hours of sleep on the couch and be out before her mom wakes up. No such luck; her mom is washing ditches with one eye on the TV, which is playing some news about a man named Cyrus. She looks up as the door opens and her eyes widen as Crewe walks in. Crewe adopts a sheepish smile -

Oh… hi, Mom.

Mom: Crewe? You came back?

Yeah. I, uh, just beat the Elite Four and my team’s exhausted. We were hoping we could sleep here tonight.

Mom: Elite Four? Your team?

-smiles weakly- Yeah.

Mom: –deadpan- So you took the Gym Challenge after all.

Yeah. I mean… Roy was doing it, and I thought it would be a good way to get out, and see the region, and meet new people. And I wasn’t alone, I had my team with me. Would you like to meet them?

-lets out my team. It’s the same as it is, except Golem and Electivire haven’t evolved yet and there is a Houndoom with us-

Mom: –suddenly angry- Get those animals out of my house!

-Mom picks up a broom and waves it angrily. I step in front of my team, alarmed-

They aren’t animals! They’re my friends!

Mom: You know how I feel about those… creatures! Getting a Piplup I could deal with. But an entire team of six? Evolving Piplup? And there are two Dark types on your team! And a… a Houndoom! You know I won’t stand those beings of the devil, and Arceus help me if I’ll let you stay within a foot of it!

-I spread my arms out in front of Houndoom-

Mom! You can’t take my team away from me! They’ve been my family for a year! Weavile and Houndoom, too! I won’t just give them up!

Mom: If you want to stay in this house, you will!

-tears begin to blur my vision-

Fine! I won’t stay here, then! I’ll find somewhere else to live! I’ll go all the way to Johto!

Mom: Crewe! You will stay right here in Twinleaf where you belong, and you will give up those animals! I’ll have to talk to Rowan, he gives you a Pokemon and all of sudden you just ignore everything I’ve been telling you your entire life! And that Roy! I knew he was a bad egg right from the start!

No! I’m not giving up a single member of my team! It’s not Rowan’s fault, and it’s not Roy’s! I’m a Pokemon Trainer, and a good one, and I have been for a year, and you won’t change that!

-teary-eyed, I run out of the house, my team following. Not stopping till I reach Route 201. Suddenly, I realize Houndoom isn’t with us. Nervously recalling everyone but Empoleon, I creep back into town. I cry out as I see a dark heap lying in the middle of the road and run to it. Houndoom is lying there, not breathing, blood flowing from a wound on the side of his head. A large rock lies nearby. Sobbing, Empoleon and I carry his body back to Route 201 and drag him to Lake Verity. I release Graveler, who helps me dig a hole, and we bury him.-

-the next morning, I arrive in Johto-

Ugh. Someone remind me never to copy and paste from Word. I had to redo the bolds and italics. What do you guys think? Sufficiently badly written enough not to make it all that sad?
