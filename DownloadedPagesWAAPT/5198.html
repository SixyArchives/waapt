Route 3, Efrafa colony

   General    Woundwort: <Oh what is the matter this time?>

The huge Nidoking turns around from his flay to answer Thethuthinnang.

Woundwort was huge even by Nidoking standards, his father was a happy-go-luck buck who constantly stole from a farmers vegetable patch. One day, the farmer shot his father and decided to dig up the doe and her litter. The doe told her young to run, but alas, none made it with the exception of Woundwort and herself, and after a few days, was taken away by a Fearow to eat.

Woundwort was lucky to survive, for he had been found by a man not long after, who took him in and cared for him as he started to grow, but he grew violent. After attacking the human's Persain (and losing an eye to that pfeffa), he was put in a much studier hut, but he was still able to chew through the wire and run away.

He became a hlessi for 5 months, evolving from a Nidoran to a Nidorino in the meantime, he grew vicious, cold and generally hating to all elil, man and foxmon in particular. One moon he stumbled upon the warren simply known as "Efrafa", who he promptly decided to take over.

He killed the Cheif Nido and a Rival in a fight for that position. After both of their demises, no-one would dare stand up to him. He was ruthless like the world shaped him to be. A few more months, after becoming the Cheif Nido of Efrafa and establishing new rules, he found a Moonstone and evolved into a Nidoking, putting his foot further down as the sole ruler of Efrafa.

As of late, quite a few young Nidos have began to question his rule, but all seemed to tremble in fear in his mighty prescene. Thethuthinnang's bravery amused him, so he let her talk.

Thethuthinnang: <Sir, I have come to talk to you about something... I have been discussing with the other does about this... Will you allow a small handful of us to leave the warren?>

   General    Woundwort: <LEAVE THE WARREN! NONSENCE! NO NIDO IS TO EVER LEAVE EFRAFA!>

Thethuthinnang: <Sir! We won't move that far from Efrafa! We will still live within the Efrafan range!>

   General    Woundwort: <THIS IS COMPLETELY OUT OF THE RULES! YOU'RE NOT LEAVING EFRAFA!>

Thethuthinnang: <But Sir! We're too crowded! Most of us can't even produce litters->

   General    Woundwort: <ENOUGH! THIS TOPIC IS TO NEVER BE BROUGHT UP AGAIN, NO NIDO IS TO LEAVE EFRAFA UNDER MY WATCH! NO NIDO AT ALL!>

Thethuthinnang: <Yes sir...>

Brokenharted, Thethuthinnang leaves Woundwort's Den and heads back to her own. Still thinking about what a new, fresh warren not ruled by a tryant would be like... Surely it would be better than this.

Thethuthinnang cruls up into a ball and drifts off to sleep, dreaming about a paradise warren that she's always wanted.
