Lumiose City

Lyuri and Duke were out of the Prism Tower and back into the city, walking along the streets in the rain, Lyuri putting her hood on. The rain had started to get stronger.

"Maybe we should use our rollerskates to get to the hotel faster." suggested Duke

"Nah, it's fine. I don't mind a bit of rain getting on me. Let's just walk." said Lyuri, smiling "Rain can be just as romantic as it can be depressing."

Duke seemed slightly worried "But we might get a cold this way..."

Lyuri then holds on to Duke's arm "Worth it."

Duke smiles "All right then. I guess it really will be worth it."

The couple then starts walking through the streets of Lumiose, Lyuri still holding Duke's arm tightly as they went.

"I guess we're the Lucario couple." said Lyuri, out of nowhere, coupled with a few chuckles

"Maybe it could be our gimmick." joked Duke, smiling.

They keep walking, hearing only the sounds of the drops of rain falling on the ground as they keep themselves silent. By this point both of them were completely soaked.

"Thank you for this lovely day. I had fun, ate some great food and now I feel lighter after getting those things out of my system. Thank you for sticking around for me." said Lyuri.

"I had fun too. And, well... It's thanks to you that I'm able to enjoy myself like this so easily, so I have to thank you as well. Thank you for being a part of my life." said Duke, blushing a bit

"... That was kinda cheesy, you should improve your romantic speeches a bit." joked Lyuri, a genuinely happy smile on her face.

Duke smiles "I'll try."

Lyuri then lets go of Duke's arm and then stops, looking up and getting her face hit by some rain, her hood fell out and leaving her hair hanging.

"You know, getting caught in rain isn't really as annoying as people make it seem. I feel great, despite my clothes being completely soaked and that I'm probably getting a cold later. It's not too different from taking a bath." said Lyuri, then looking back at Duke, fixing her hair as best as she could so it would be straight again and not get caught in her eyes.

"I guess it's annoying for the consequences that come with it later on." said Duke.

"Well, I'll just let the future happen. Right now I only care about this very moment."

They then resume walking back to the hotel and eventually Duke noticed a solitary umbrella on a nearby bench, with no one else in sight

"Seems like someone forgot their umbrella. Say, my rain princess, do you want to lower your chances of getting a cold by sharing an umbrella with me?" asked Duke, with a smirk

Lyuri smiles "Sure. I already had quite the bath."

Duke then picks up the umbrella and opens it, staying under it with Lyuri, they were holding hands, which proved to be inconvenient as Duke ended up tripping and ended up falling on a puddle and dragging Lyuri with him. They both get up, wincing

"Great, now I have mud all over my hair." said Lyuri as she ran her hand through her hair

"I'm sorry. I don't know how this happened." said Duke, embarrassed.

"Nah, it's fine. I'm going to take a hot bath after this anyway. Let's just keep going, I think we must be close now." said Lyuri, smiling sincerely.

"A-All right..."

Duke then picked the umbrella up again and the couple headed back to the hotel together, upon getting at the entrance, however, Lyuri suddenly stopped.

"Is something wrong?" asked Duke, going back to her.

Upon getting close, Lyuri pulled him down by the collar of his shirt a bit to get him at around her level and then gave him a long kiss in the mouth that lasted for a minute before Lyuri ended it

"I just thought we should give this date a fitting end before we call it a day." said Lyuri, smiling

Duke gives a wide smile in response "Of course, I should've imagined." he chuckles a bit "So, let's head inside now. I guess we both need a bath and dry ourselves."

Lyuri nods and then the couple heads back into the hotel as the rain kept falling behind them.
