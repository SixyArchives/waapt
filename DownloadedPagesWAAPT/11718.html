Gateon Port

Rillianne finally arrives at Orre, she walks out of the ship that brought her with a yellow and black suitcase, upon arriving, she started looking around, before two figures approached her.

"Hey! Rin!"

She then looked at the source of the voice and saw two boys, almost identical physically except for the hair of one of them being longer than the other. They both had red hair, black eyes and had the same height, both looking around 17.

"There you are! I thought I was going to have to find you two myself!" said Rillianne, sighing in relief.

"H-Hey Rin." said the other boy, the one with a long hair, quite sheepishly as he waved at her.

"Hey Neon." she responded, waving back at him "Still the same meek boy as ever huh? Still letting your hair grow I see."

"Yeah, nobody changed ever since we moved here, how're things going with your brother anyway?"

"He seems to be doing well, what about you, Leon? Getting into much trouble recently?" she teased

"Hey! Our birthday is coming, can't you at least go easier on me for now?" said Leon, pretending to look upset.

"E-Everything is ready for the party, w-we're just waiting for the day to come." said Neon.

"Oh, good, that means I don't have to help with the preparations." said Rillianne, chuckling

"You're so lazy. Anyway, there's one thing I need you to do for me, Rin. You probably won't like it at first, but I bet you won't regret it later." said Leon, with a grin

"Oh boy, what do you want this time?" she asked, already with a feeling of dread.

"Well, let's talk about this when we arrive at home, come on!" said Leon

"Y-Yeah, l-le-let's go." said Neon.

Rillianne shrugs "All right then."

The two boys then start guiding Rilliane through the area, the girl was looking around rather curiously due to it being the first time she ever went to Orre, but at least since she was between friends she would feel more at ease in that region than she would normally.
