Citidark Isle

-Odin is staring at some images of the J-Team, particularly Lina and Chrome, and reminiscing...-

A house in Orre, about 20 years ago

-Once, I was a pampered child. My father treated me well and always provided me with new toys.-

- A man approaches a small boy with wild blonde hair, one red eye, and one blue eye playing with some action figures-

Man: Hey Max! Bought you a new toy on the way home from work.

Max: Awesome! Thanks, Daddy!

-He takes the action figure his father hands him and inspects it-

Wow, it looks powerful! Can it beat my other toys?

-He presses a button and watches as a projectile fist knocks over another action figure-

It can! So cool!

-He continues playing-

A few months later

-He also taught me the joys of science at every opportunity.-

- Max is sitting at the kitchen table with his father and mother, his father having gotten some baking soda and vinegar-

Max’s Dad: Now Max, I’m going to demonstrate the power of SCIENCE!

- He pours the two into a bottle together, and Max watches in amazement as they react-

Max: Wow! That’s amazing! I wanna know more science!

-My mother... Was never as approving.-

-Meanwhile, his mother leers at him-

Max’s Mom: Those eyes... I can’t get over those eyes...

About a year later

-The tension... Eventually reached a head.-

- Max is hiding behind the stairs as his parents argue; his mom is carrying some bags-

Max’s Mom: I can’t believe you would join a group like Cipher! With all they’ve done to this region?!

Max’s Dad: Honey, they pay well, it could help us... And their advancements in my field could change the world for the better!

Max’s Mom: You can keep your science! You can keep our freak of a son! I’m leaving!

-She storms out the door. Max quickly tears up and runs away, sobbing. -

A few weeks later

-My father grew increasingly paranoid, Always worried something was out to get us.-

- Max’s father is trying to drag a protesting Max through the door-

Max: I don’t wanna go to work with you! I wanna see my friends at school!

Max’s Dad: I’m sorry, Max, but you’re safer with me! I can’t have anyone hurting you or taking you away!

Max: O-Okay...

-He follows his dad out-

Cipher Lab, four years later

-Eventually, my father began experimenting on a strange woman. I was fascinated.

-Max walks in to find his father reading a computer readout... and a woman punctured with needles and strapped to various devices-

Woman: ...

Max: Woah...

-reaches out to touch her-

Why is the lady strapped into a machine?

Max’s Dad: She’s an experiment, don’t touch her.

- Max stops reaching and merely stares-

A Few Weeks Later

-Then... It came.-

- Max is watching his dad work when an alarm sounds throughout the building.-

PA: Attention. A Metagross, Steelix, and Drapion have stormed the building. Come to the defense or evacuate immediately.

Max’s Dad: ...Max? Hide.

Max: O-OK...

-He hides in a cabinet, but peeks outside-

-He sees a Metagross storm into the room-

Max’s Dad: Stop right there! As Cipher's head scientist, I can't let you take away our most valuable experiment!

-To Max, the Metagross is completely silent in response.-

-His father, on the other hand, clutches his head and screams-

-And screams and screams until he collapses on the ground, unmoving-

A few minutes later

- After he is sure the Metagross is gone, Max rushes out of the cabinet and over to his fahter’s body and desprately begins pushing at it-

Max: Dad... Wake up...

-Tears stream down his face as his father continues to lay there-

Please... Wake up...

Two years later

- For the next few years, I was pretty much raised by Cipher. The others... Did not treat me well.-

- Max is pushed to the ground by two older Cipher Peons-

Max: Aaah!

Peon 1: Peh, what a weakling.

Peon A: -eating a sandwich- He has nice lunches, though.

Peon 1: Well, smell ya later, chump.

-They leave, as Max curls up and cries.-

A few months later

-But then, I got my own Pokemon. And things started changing.-

-The Peons approach Max once again, who has his back turned to them-

Peon A: Hey kid! Cough up your lunch or-

-Two Magneton suddenly flank Max, who turns around with a slightly unsettling grin-

Max: Or what?

Peon A: Ack! That’s strong against my Pigeotto!

Peon 1: And my Buizel!

-The Magneton spark-

Max: I suggest you run now, before my new toys decide to teach you a lesson.

-The Peons do so as Max continues to grin-

Two years later

-I quickly began to climb up the ranks of Cipher. Before long...-

-Max is shaking Ardos’ hand-

Ardos: Congratulations on making Admin, Maxwell. I trust you will continue to serve Cipher admirably.

Max: Please...

...Call me Odin.

A few weeks later

-But I pined for the one thing I always wanted.

-Odin is staring at some files on Lina and Chrome-

'Odin: The Crimson Lady...

-He clenches his fist-

Mark my words... I will have my revenge on you, and the thing that killed my father.

-He smirks-

I just have to take my time.

Now

Odin: Yes... I will have my revenge.

-He turns to an alcove where Iulianus’ armor rests and gives an impossibly wide Slasher Smile-

And now I have the perfect means to get it!

-His giggling echoes throughout the base. -
