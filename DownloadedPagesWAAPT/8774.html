 219350 Luke 924, Sun, 6th Jan '13 9:00:01 AM Get RP Mod
Lake quickly got to his feet, standing near Stratus, who was still trying to frighten the Parasect away with his ignited wrist, but to little effect.

Lake: <...What happened?>

Stratus: <I tried to frighten this guy away, but he just called for back-up! I did manage to put up a Safeguard so that they can't put us to sleep or poison us or anything like that...>

He frowns gravely.

<But I think that just means they'll try and shred us with their claws instead.>

Lake: <...Great.>

Stratus: <So...what do we do?>

Lake took a moment to scope out the situation. They were surrounded on all sides by the Parasect, who were keeping their distance from Stratus' Fire Punch, but were still poised to attack at a moment's notice. They probably weren't be able to just run for it.

Lake: <Can you fly us out of here?>

He was met with a shake of the head from Stratus in response.

Stratus: <No good! The treeline is too thick and there isn't enough room for me to move around in here without crashing into something!>

Darn, scratch another escape option, and the Parasect were starting to get more aggressive, a few of them were making slashing motions at the air with their claws as a few more hissed nosily.

<I don't want to pile on the pressure but could you hurry it up? They're starting to get antsy!>

Lake: <...I think I have something.>

Stratus: <You do?>

Lake: <Yeah. I'm gonna' count to three, and then I'm going to use Earthquake. Jump to avoid it, and then we'll make a run for it, okay?>

The Dragonite gave Lake a nervous nod.

<Okay then. One...two...>

Lake slowly raised his leg as Stratus flexed his legs...

<THREE!>

...Then sprang into the air as Lake brought his foot down hard onto the ground, the floor below them shaking and splitting from the force of the impact.

Lake used Earthquake!

Caught off-guard, the Parasect all hissed in-pain as they were knocked off their feet and buffeted by the ground as it ruptured from the localized seismic activity, giving Lake and Stratus the small window of opportunity they needed.

<RUN FOR IT!>

Dropping to all fours, Lake galloped away into the undergrowth of the forest as fast as his legs could carry him ad Stratus followed closely behind, the Dragonite looking back over his shoulder to see if any Parasect were chasing them. Thankfully, they were all far too dazed from the attack to attempt a pursuit...

A few minutes later...

...and it wasn't long until Lake and Stratus stopped by a small pond in the middle of the forest, gasping for breath.

Lake: <...That went badly...>
