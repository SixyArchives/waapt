Denshi: <Never seen that before! It really looks like an ice cream.>

Wulfric: Ain't gonna work this time, buddy. Avalanche!

The hail stroke it. While Ice-type do not really care about it, this Vanillish seemed to absorb the hail, regained the HP lost via burn.

Eridanus: And once again... I am left with only one Pokemon to finish. That leaves... Forge.

Forge: <Well, that's third Gym I partake in. Guess I need to prove my skills, or something.>

Eridanus: It's the last finish, so it'll be straightforward... Thunderbolt!

Wulfric: Counter the jolt of electricity with Ice Beam!

As Vanillish took in a breath and releases ray of cold energy, Forge released jolt of electricity... Both attacks partially got through each other, dealing damage.

The hail stroke, the burn damage got negated by Ice Body.

Eridanus: One more time. Thunderboltttt!

Wulfric: ...I guess this is where the fun ends. Ice Beam for the last move!
