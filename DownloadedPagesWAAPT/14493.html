While Kim's free schedule meant that she was able to battle alongside him now, the fact of the matter was that he had overlooked several issues he had learned in his rather dogged and hasty pursuit of the girl.

Most immediately was the factor of the second Mega Stone: the Heracronite. Her stipulation for their rematch was that he would need to activate it in order to convince her to give him a second chance to retrieve the bracelet she'd stolen. Currently, that was proving to be difficult, but Damian's progress was coming along admirably.

But what he hadn't factored into the equation was the information he'd learned from the Tower of Mastery: only one Mega Stone could be used within the Mega Ring at any given time. He would need to decide between Kim or Damian for usage in the battle.

He had promised the former that she could help, but the Heracross' bond would likely be weakened if only strengthened for such a cheap reason...

But the was also another problem. He was unaware of just what Pokémon she would use for their encounter. He could assume that it was her Houndoom or Delphox, but what if she had a hidden ringer? A secret member she intended to use against him?

As pressingly was another fact: both Kim and Damian possessed a weakness to at least one Pokémon that she owned. Additionally, that Pokémon was capable of Mega Evolution.

And from there, one final issue presented itself as he thought back on their last battle, remembering how easily she'd shut down the Lucario's mental abilities. Kim's aura blindness had not been properly tested.

Rex: Ugh... this is far too much of a handful...
