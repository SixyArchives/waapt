Suddenly the silence is broken by a Noblewoman's Laugh, which causes Constantin to jump and go immediately on the defensive - bringing out his bagh nakh constructs and taking up a stance.

???: Oh ma petit prince, you're back! I thought we would never have a chance of finally be alone together~

A figure with long, black hair in Regal Ringlets walks out into the smatterings of moonlight filtering through the roof

Maria von Boehm: No need to be on the defensive, schön. Though I can understand why you'd would be so jumpy, even if you really shouldn't be even considering such indulgences - knowing what a sickly lad you are~

Constantin: Wait, how-

He is cut off by a sudden hug, Maria snuggling in to his clothes.

Maria: Mhmm~ You're just as huggable as I always thought you were~ Less boney than perceived.

Constantin snarls at Maria

Constantin: Give me a reason why I shouldn't attempt to rip out your throat.

Maria pouts at Constantin

Maria: No need to be so aggro, love~ But you should know that I will always be fiercely loyal to you Constantin~

A group of Illusionite stragglers start congregating in the faint moonlight, muttering and glancing at eachother as look towards Maria and Constantin.

Maria: We all are loyal to you~

Cons looks around, slightly amused and slightly bemused.

Constantin: So you all... Swear allegiance... To me? Am I hallucinating? What a dream and nightmare come true...

A murmur of agreement flows over the crowd.

Straggler #3: You're ruthlessly efficient. That's what I admire about you, sir

Straggler #5: We know you won't kowtow to their bogus bullshit, sir.

Constantin: ...

Const pushes himself off the wa and stands in the light.

Constantin: ... I see what you are asking of me.

Cons pulls out a small badge, and holds it up into the moonlight.

Constantin: ...Then I propose from that the ashes of the old titans, we rebuild and rise again like Ho-oh from the pyres. From this point on, we shall band together under the fires of a new dawn and work towards to goal of a brilliant and beautiful new world - one without the thieves, warmongers and deadwood that pollute and destroy it. Should it mean that we have to wipe the slate completely clean, so be it. A brave new world calls for us, and it calls to be one free of those who wish to exploit or destroy it's natural beauty - Like those violent thugs who call themselves the J-Team who justify their crimes as vigilante justice and peacekeeping. As I know some of you know all to well...

The is a deep growl from the collective.

Constantin: But alas, we are small, and we have nothing in the way of funding like the previous organisations had. We can work fast, but it will still take time before we can carry out any sort of world changing plans that we push. But from this day, we will work towards a glorious new world!

The reflection of the Flare badge gleams as a cheer sweeps through the posse.

Constantin: We refomed on this very night, as such, we begin working towards our goals if a beautiful word now. We shall start by finding a temporary spot to set up shop and begin building our organisation. So follow me.
