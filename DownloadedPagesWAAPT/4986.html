Goldenrod City Streets, One Hour Ago

(As Last Seen Here)

From the portal to the    Netherworld   , the footsteps of the Swampert sound stronger and harsher, until the figure finally crosses the gate and enters our realm. Firmly held on her arms, a Sandshrew looks around and gleefully growls in recognition of the place he finds himself back at.

Swampert♀ -walks into the alley: <Ugh... there, we are back. I'm very sure this is the place.> — looks around — <Ugh... it smells like [freedom]. Filthy place... I can't believe this took so long...>

Dintel, once let down on the ground, stands up, sniffs and turns back to the water Pokémon.

Dintel: <We're home! See! That is where I was~> — points to the local PEFE facilities — <...You can has stay?>

Swampert♀: <The heck I don't! Can't you UNDERSTAND????> — flails and swings tail around, knocking a nearby trashcan. — <LEAVE. US. ALONE!!!!>

Dintel -tilts head: <B-But... we had fun~>

The Swampert sighs, for one moment, just for one moment, she considers the idea of Surfing the Sandshrew, but then she shudders to the thought of now having to lawfully keep the little digger into her Overlord's realm for an [eternity].

Dintel: <We can play more. We collected a lot of gems with my friends...>

Swampert♀: <Look, little bugger, you [traded] gems with them. Do you have any idea how many generations is it going to take us to remove that freedom from their mindsets again?> — sees Dintel say "no" with his head — <Tangrowth will surely call the DSNee lawyer-forms and then... all that process again and then we-> — throws arms and head in disgust. — <Ugh... As if you could understand...>

Noticing his Swampert "host" disgusted, Dintel stands up and goes offer her a hug. The Swampert retreats a couple of steps, of course, but Dintel is quick to get to her and cling to her leg.

Dintel: <But we had so much fun. I'm gonna miss you...> — hugs.

Swampert sighs and rolls her eyes. She looks down and feels motherly towards the darned thing for a nanosecond; but that feeling is quickly superseded by her desire to just return to her place and throw up over a couple of slaves.

Swampert♀: <I said leave us alone...!>

Carefully she retreats into the portal to the Netherworld, taking care to keep her hugges leg out of it, lest drag the darned Sandshrew back into her Overlord's realm. Then she starts shaking her leg to get rid of the Sandshrew. First softly, to no effect, then after a minute faster and stronger, letting out some growls in the meantime; finally, fed up of the situation, she forcibly grabs Dintel off herself and holds him up at arms length.

Dintel: <...Pleeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeease????> — puts up a puppy face.

Swampert♀: <Rrrrrrrr......>

After a moment of hesitation, Swampert screams right into Dintel's face:

Swampert♀: <   NO!   >

...and tosses the Sandshrew to the ground. The Pokémon simply lands sitting in the ground, and turns around to look back at the Swampert entering back into the portal, confused.

Dintel: <...Will you write to me?>

Swampert♀: <NO! Look, live a looooong life and keep AWAY from us!> — fades away.

Dintel simply stays sitting on the ground, tilting his head and tail as he watches the portal, still open. For a moment he wonders if he has been a bad [guest] to the Swampert, who has provided a lot of entertainment for the Sandshrew and the other chained Pokémon. Then, trying to remember the manners he learnt from his family, Silent, and the online videos by Darach, Dintel decides that perhaps the Swampert and their peoplemons are too busy to keep receiving guests.

Some heated discussion can be heard coming in from the other side of the portal.

???? #1: <...Are you serious? We are instructed to go into every possible world and [dominate] them...>

???? #2: <Well admittedly the creatures here are incredibly annoying.>

???? #1: <Does that even matter? We have our orders, and the [lawsuits] are ready...>

Swampert♀: <I don't care! Look, you would think like me if you spend two weeks with that thing!>

???? #1: <He is kinda cute....>

Swampert♀: <Did you see how easily did he ruin an entire shipment of slaves with ideas about sharing and fun? And heck, not even this verse's dead ones will take him!!!!> — some rumbling can be heard.

???? #2: <It can't have been that bad...>

A loud growl and some meeping can be heard. Could it get through, the smell of two creatures's bowels failing from sheer fear could also reach this universe.

Swampert♀: <TWO. WEEKS.>

???? #2: <Uh... er..... eeeeeep~>

Swampert♀: <Now close the dam gate forever! I don't [   care   ] what our Overlord says!>

As Dintel hears this he stands up and walks to the portal, but he suddenly stops as he hears he is being addressed.

Swampert♀: <Now, darned thing, you don't come back...!>

Suddenly a berry flies from the portal, and hits Dintel's head...

Swampert♀: <And take your damn berry!>

...The berry continues to roll on the ground. Dintel goes grab his berry, sits on the ground a bit confused and then turns to the gate. Which slowly closes and evaporates, fading out of existence in this verse,    forever   .

Dintel stays there for a good while, doing and probably thinking nothing.

The Zoroark's Clearing

The two Zoroark begin circling each other again, the watching pack silent as they watch. Rosaleen suddenly rushes in with a Night Slash. Hecate easily side-steps, then grabs the younger Zoroark by the shoulder and flings her to the ground. Crouching over her, she stares into her eyes for a moment. Rosaleen starts feeling dizzy, and Hecate slowly gets to her feet, grinning.

The younger Zoroark stumbles to her feet and shakes her head to clear it, then tries to rush at Hecate again with a Night Slash. But while her claws come out, they aren't sheathed with Obscura. Startled, Rosaleen takes several steps backwards, staring at them in confusion.

<Ah, my dear Rosaleen... There is an advantage to age and training, it seems. Tell me, dear, have you ever heard of Imprison?>

While Rosaleen still looks dumbfounded, some hushed muttering spreads through the crowd. Darwin nods appreciatively, smiling. Even Corbin bears an expression of grudging admiration.

<Well, it would seem you are ignorant of its effects. So allow me to enlighten you.>

Hecate begins circling Rosaleen. She moves around four times, coming closer with each rotation. Every turn, she uses Nasty Plot.

<Imprison is a bit of a funny move, and rather rare. If the subject of the move knows any of the same attacks as the user, they are unable to use them. It's temporary, of course, but I believe it will last long enough for my purpose.> She reaches Rosaleen and looks down at her. <Now, Rosaleen, we are both Zoroark... How much do you know of my past?>

<Y-you—> the younger Zoroark chokes out, trembling as she stares up at Hecate, who looms over her.

The older Zoroark smiles almost benignly at her.

<That's right. You, my dear, are helpless.>

Eyes wild, Rosaleen swings her claws at Hecate. They graze her muzzle, drawing a few drops of blood.

Snarling, she kicks her over and plants one back paw on her chest. A large Focus Blast, powered up by Nasty Plot, grows between her two front paws. Rosaleen starts whimpering out a few sniveling pleas, but a few seconds later and a large blast of light later, she is silenced and the battle is over.

The Zoroark pack fall silent, then a small, high voice pipes up.

<Yay! Mommy wins!>

The voice draws the attention of both Hecate and Darwin. From between the legs of two startled Zoroark, a runty female Zorua pokes her head out. It's soon followed by an identical one. Darwin takes a step towards them, but Hecate is faster. In a black-and-red blur, she's gathered the two Zorua in her arms, and buried her face in their fur, doing her best not to start crying in front of the pack.

As they did so long ago, when Hecate last set foot in the clearing, the pack silently parts to leave an aisle. A small group of runty Zoroark and Zorua do their best to look stately walking between the silent, watching rows of Zoroark, when Hecate breaks into a run towards them, they lose it completely, and soon they're huddled around her, a few sobbing, a few laughing, several just snuggling silently.

Hecate runs her hands over their heads, holding the young twins tightly to herself, murmuring their names over and over again.

<Vonda, Jetta, Swift, Douglas, Lamya, Garnet, Senka...>

Wiping her eyes, she gathers her children closer to her and drops to one knee to be closer to them.

<Mother's come home...>

And slowly, around them, led by Darwin, the Zoroark pack begins to cheer and gather around the reunited family, and Corbin carries a disheveled, unconscious Rosaleen away.
