Ken breathed a short sigh, and took a look back at his team before silently nodding. Rak was the only one who was aware of the situation enough to nod back seriously. Looking back to the front, Ken reached out his fingertips to take hold of the shard. "I'll never see the world if I don't explore it with a smile..." The dark haired young man said to himself, repeating the words of wisdom his mother eventually gave him.

"Let's see what th-" Ken's comment was cut short by a blinding fast light enveloping his body, leaving his mons stunned and left behind as the trainer vanished into the Multiverse.
