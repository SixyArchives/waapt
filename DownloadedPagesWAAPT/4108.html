Guardé: <Eurgh, I didn't think we would be back here again so soon.>

-For the second time in three days, Guardé and Chord have been confined to a hospital bed to recover from another thrashing. While the two rest in bed, me and the other Pokémon are hanging around, trying to figure out what to do next.-

Guardé: <I'm sorry, comrades. I should have done a better job guarding those awful Delta Pokémon. This wouldn't have happened otherwise.>

Stratus: -Shakes his head.- <I just feel like this is my fault, you know?>

Harriet: <It not though, Stratus. If anything, it's that swindling Emolga's fault.>

Woolly: -Tilts her head.- <What does Swindle have to do with this?>

Harriet: <Think about it: Swindle steals Tagg's Cynical side. Tagg goes mad and tries to kill us, injuring Guardé, Chord and half of Tracer's Pokémon, leaving us weakened for the Delta mons to wipe the floor with us and steal Pleo. Honestly, if we blame anymon, it's that thieving Emolga's fault.>

Treeo: <She is indirectly responsible, yes. But she was only trying to make things better, apparently.>

Chord: <Well a fat lot of good she's done...>

Woolly: -Sighs.- <We've really messed up badly this time, guys. We were supposed to be keeping Pleo save from those Delta Pokémon for the Reef Pokémon. Once they find out about this, they're not going to be happy.>

Harriet: <CAW!> -Ruffles her feathers.- <I totally forgot about the Reef Pokémon! If they somehow get word of this, they'll probably throw us in a murky underwater jail cell for Arceus knows how long.>

-The Honchkrow shudders at the thought.-

Stratus: <Oh mon, we've screwed up really badly this time...>

Woolly: <There has to be something we can do! We can still try to find him, can't we?>

"Yeah, we're not just going to give up just yet. We won't let those Delta Pokémon get away with this. I don't care if we have to search the entire Orange Islands to find them, we're going to get Pleo back."

-As I say this, I stroke Lake gently, who has been lying on one of the beds, saying nothing.-

"Right, Lake?"

-Lake glances at me for a brief second, then goes back to staring into the middle distance, a sad look on his face.-

"..."

Harriet: <Lake's taking this really badly.>

Woolly: <Well he promised so many times that he wouldn't let those Delta Pokémon get him. And now that they have him, he must feel awful.>

Treeo:<Poor thing...>
