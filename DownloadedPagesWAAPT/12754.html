Buggle slips the ring on Bridget's middle finger. A radiant white glow envelops the area and the two weather legends cover their eyes. As the light dies down, the result of the first experimental gadget is revealed...

To have shrunken Bridget down to a comical size.

Depthcharge: Eahh... >_>

Buggle: -Ring clangs off Buggle, who doesn't seem to notice Bridget's ire- <O-kay!> -Pulls out a checklist that most certainly wasn't there before.- <First Gadget: Ring. Effect: Shrinkage. Result: Colossal Failure.>

Chords of Steel

Phenac Pokemon Center, Herbert's Room

-Herbert groggily starts to stir awake, quite unsure as to how he ended up back in bed in the first place. He can just about make out the blurry figures of a Scizor and a Bisharp in front of him.-

Herbert: Urgh... how did I get back here again?

Falchion: <You staggered in at some ridiculous hour in the morning, yelled something about cookie dough, and then collapsed into bed.>

Herbert: ...Ask a stupid question, I suppose.

-He sits himself up.-

Bishop: <So, would you mind explaining exactly what happened last night? We were rather concerned for you, you know. And it would appear that you went out of your way to do something you are almost certainly going to regret.>

Herbert: ...Really? Huh... well, I think I can remember most of it. I went out with Duke, some of the others showed up as well, I had a load of ice cream and... things start to get blurry after that. Me and Crewe were bickering and... oh yeah, now I remember. We kind of challenged each other to see who could become a PEFE researcher first.

-Bishop gives a stunned blink.-

Bishop: <...Would I be safe in assuming that said challenge was the result of your rivalry with Crewe combined with a state of mental fatigue?>

Herbert: Bingo.

Bishop: <Ah, I see. So you are not actually going through with this endeavour.>

Herbert: ...Actually, now that I think about it, I might just give it a shot.

Bishop: <...What.>

Falchion: <That's a very good question, actually. Seriously, what.>

Herbert: ...What's with the looks? The tests don't sound difficult, and it's not like I'm planning on making a career out of it. I figure it's just something I can breeze through, then rub my new title in Crewe's face and forget about it within the week.

-Bishop struggles to find the correct words.-

Bishop: <...Yes, but... I was under the impression that you hated PEFE. And scientists in general, really. I will say that I have not had brilliant experiences with either, but->

Herbert: I changed my mind, okay? Why does everyone consider that to be such a big deal? Aside from the whole "terminally negligent about global safety" thing, they don't seem all that bad. And hey, in the event that I'm wrong and they do turn out to be evil, then I'm in an ideal position to put chemical solvents in the water supply or something.

Bishop: <...Please tell me that your last statement was a joke.>

Herbert: ...Sure, if it makes you feel any better. Anyway, I take it this is the thing that you were expecting me to regret in the morning?

Bishop: <...Erm, no. I was referring to something else entirely with that statement.>

Herbert: Well then, what were you referring to?

-In response, Falchion pulls open Herbert's laptop and starts playing the recently-uploaded video of Crewe kissing him.-

Herbert: ...Okay, what the hell was in that ice cream?!

Herbert's Mindscape

-Purple!Herbert is currently all aflutter, much to the chagrin of Herbert's other aspects.-

Purple!Herbert: Who would have thought that a simple kiss would be enough to prompt such a rush of emotion? When her lips met ours, I thought perhaps we were merely in an idle dream, but no, the feeling was all too real... and yet after such a display of affection, she returned to her usual scorn. Oh, to be able to understand the mysteries of your heart, my sweet Gracidea...

Grey!Herbert: Stop calling her that! Can't you get your head out of that "tru wuv" bubble for just one minute and recognise her as the feral animal she is?

Purple!Herbert: I pity you, Grey, for your stubbornness blinds you to her wild, untamed beauty. If only there was some method of description that would do it justice...

Grey!Herbert: I swear, if you start writing sappy poetry, I am going to-

Purple!Herbert: Yes! Poetry! Perfect! What better way is there to express the desires of our heart than the literacy medium? Thank you for this flash of inspiration, Grey! I shall craft a masterpiece that will make even the most jaded of cynics weep with emotion!

-He dashes into the Gym. Blue!Herbert sighs.-

Blue!Herbert: Why do you insist on encouraging him...?

Yellow!Herbert: ...Someone should probably inform Purple that we are preparing for combat today. Speaking of which, I should probably locate Red as well. Usually he would be training in the Dojo in anticipation of the upcoming battle, but today he is nowhere to be found...
