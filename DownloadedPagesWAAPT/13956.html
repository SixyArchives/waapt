348895 ChaoticMarin8th Feb 2014 06:02:43 AM from Nintendoland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: 700 wives and 300 concubines
Chiptune DJ

Shalour City Hotel

A cleaning maid began going through and cleaning a secific room by chance. She noticed the little pillow fortress on one of the beds and began to correct them, only to notice there was something underneath...

...A tiny girl curled into a ball was snoozing peacefully there.

The cleaning maid yelped, startled by the sight as if she'd stumbled upon a murder. Valorie's eyes jolted open and she looked around, equally startled. Noticing she was being watched she promptly squirmed free of the remaining pillows in a panic and nose dived directly into the floor with an audible "Ouch..!"

Scrambling to her feet she totally just bolts out of that room, stopping only long enough to realize... hey! She wasn't a dratini anymore! That was awesome!

Stumbling clumsily out of the hotel, Valorie finally took some time to regain her composure and /think/ about what was going o. Well clearly she'd turned back somehow...! She examined herself. She was fine, except for the large chocolate stain on her pretty shirt. She looked like a slob! She needed a bath pronto and worse yet, she was in /public/!

Suddenly Valorie felt a funny feeling throughout her body as it started to deform again. "No... no... noooo!" she quietly raged at the heavens as her legs were forced together and she fell to her knees, body slowly shifting into the form of a dratini's over the course of half a minute or so.

<Why?!> Valorie cried out, enraged. What kind of sick joke was this?! Was Arceus itself taunting her with her normal body just long enough to steal it from her again? Why did she need to suffer like this?! Who was doing this to her?! Why was this happening?!

Valorie totally just... begins throwing a temper tantrum in front of the hotel, rage-crying and thrashing about in defiance of... gravity, I guess.

"Hey, who's that pokemon..?" a boy's voice asked from nearby, snapping Valorie out of her tantrum as she noticed a... familiar look in his eyes.

Sniffling, her blood ran cold. It was a youngster and she could see the speculation in his eyes. He was thinking of catching her! But of course he didn't recognize what she was... Dratini were all but extinct, as were their evolutions!

"I dunno! It looks kind of like a snake." a nearby lass remarked thoughtfully. Valorie looked around. There were trainers all over the place and she'd just drawn plenty of attention with her tantrum.

Valorie just sort of... began floating away, feigning a calm pace as she prayed none of them recognized the opportunity they were being faced with. Lucky nobody happened to have a pokedex on them, huh!

Before she could get all that far however a man blurted out. "Hey that's a dratini, a dragon type pokemon!"

Valorie felt the gaze of a dozen trainers weighing down on her and began to shake with fear. It only took a moment before the chaos began. "It's really rare!" someone chimed in. "I want it!" "It's mine!" "Get off!" various trainers began yelling out as they suddenly rushed Valorie.

<No no no no no NO NO NO!!!>

The tiny dratini squealed and began doing her best to flee, only for a pokeball to whack her on the head.

...For a moment her world felt as if it were sucked into a single point, her form shrinking down as it was drawn within the ball. Screaming, she attacked the ball with her best slam attack, thrashing about until she broke free.

Bursting forth she resumed running. <That's not even how catching pokemon works!> she remarked redundantly, only for a flamethrower to nearly deep fry her face. Emitting a high pitch squeak she continued leading the kids and their pokemon on a chase throughout town which steadily gained size until it resembled an angry mob with pokeballs instead of torches.
