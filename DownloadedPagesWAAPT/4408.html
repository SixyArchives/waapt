paratrainzordpost part #2/3

(Related post part #1/3)

Mandarin South, Pokécenter Lobby, Earlier Today

After some deliberation with Algira and Copahue on how soon to return to the city of Trovitopolis, Silent decides to fetch Naga from her first, successful medical examination to trade back Shahinne from Rex. Silent decides to let Naga say goodbye to Algira and Lileep while he goes to the recovery area to fetch Ichtacya as well.

However, what he gets is not good news.

"So wait, my Leafeon has to stay here at least until the end of the [day]?"

Nurse Brisa nods and proceeds to show Silent some charts with medical data showcasing Ichtacya's evolution.

"She's already stable, Mr. Silent, but she's tiring too fast even with the sedative. That is going to impact not only her recovery time, but also the time it will take her to get back to full capacity."

"I didn't expect this..." Silent examines some of the cheets, focusing on the protein absorbption data. "She [has] pretty good physical condition, considering my other Pokémon."

"It might be the different pressure at sea level. In any case, I'm holding her on observation for the rest of the day."

"Thanks, Nurse Brisa." Silent nods to the doctor and returns to the Pokécenter Lobby.

After a couple of minutes, Silent decides to send a message to Rex.

From: Silentran♂
To: GRTrainer-Rex

Message: Hope Shahinne is having a good time. Naga's initial exams pretty good. You'll love to hear she seems to have a good ??-spread. She seemed hesitant to accept her new form at first. I recommend that you spend a lot of time with her in the water so that you build up a sense of what she can do and she can build up trust in your instructions.

Will be sending Naga back to fetch Shahinne by the end of the day.

After pressing 'Send', Silent reminded himself about something again. "Should definitively change that screenname..."

Silent walks away to get himself a meal. He notices Maekrite has walked back from wherever he was last night, and is drying himself under the sun. The two share a couple of uneasy looks and a moment of petting, before Silent grabs some documents and goes back to the inside of the building.

Over the Sea of Kyogre

The PEFE blimp heads to the PEFE building at [REDACTED], with the interns sleeping for the moment. Unknown to them or to the blimp's crew, three [Pidgeys] have got in as stowaways. They are at the moment hopping around the blimp, looking for a certain Sinnohan blackbird.

<Coo! Where is she supposed to be, ah? Coo!> inquires one of the Pidgeys, pecking her teammate. The other Pidgey responds with a sound wingslap, prompting the girl to continue. <Because I don't see her human anywhere.>

<Can you please shut your beak?> chirps the third Pidgey, jumping down from a box. <We've been looking for barely an hour. The human is supposed to work at this thing called PEFE, so here we are.>

<Yeah! Let's look around. We shall have that bird!>

The three Pidgeys concert for a moment.

<And when we do...    :3   >

The blimp flies north, slowly leaving the Orange Islands behind without the Pidgeys even realizing.



Back at Mandarin South, Pokécenter, Today

Silent walks to the Nurse's treatment room, still undecided about what does he have to do next. He takes the opportunity to peek into the second recovery room that is almost empty save for two or three Pokémon. There a young boy is talking to the nurse as they examine a certain Nidorino's injuries, the most notorious the one across the belly.

The kid seems happy to receive the good news that, as far as Silent knows, there is no serious or permanent damage. Even the scar will disappear in time. Silent can not help but feel uneasy that because of that scar, that marks his well-trained Nidorino's victory, the female has actually chosen this male over his.

"Then again, " realizes Silent thinking back at Maekrite feeling so uneasy outside, "he did not want this either."

One Hour Later

After noticing that the Nurse has left the treatment room, Silent decides to go talk to her. Summoning the strength to do is pretty difficult and after letting out a couple of random words about how to treat the Nidorino or how he is worried for his safety, the Nurse is about to dismiss him since she is too busy.

"But you don't understand..." Silent says, not exactly sure about how to go on. "I mean, I-"

"Would you please save it for tomorrow?"

At that moment a knock is heard on the door.

"One moment please? Private meeting!" answers the Nurse.

"I am talking about the injuries that Nidorino suffered. I think I know..."

"We know how to treat those injuries, Dr. Silent. We got copies of your early medical reports." The Nurse shrugs and adds. "Not that I ever thought I'd have to use them. There's barely four Nidorino living in this island..."

Some pushes are heard and, after a stronger knock, the door opens, as it was apparently inadequately closed. The nurse, not seeing anything at first, peeks in to see a grown-up Nidorino with dark-yellow markings walk into the office, head and ears down.

"...What? That is a..."

As Maekrite sits down on the ground and looks away, ashamed, Silent realizes what Maekrite is trying to do. He stands up to the nurse.

"What I have been trying to say is that I know who caused those injuries." He poins to Maekrite. "That Nidorino is mine."

The Nurse stays there for a moment, observing the specimen curiously, and then sits to pick up her phone.

Maekrite growls something, prompting Silent to lean in closer.

<Sorry for causing you trouble.>

"Sorry for not being able to solve them." Silent answers, petting the Nidorino behind the ears.

Some minutes later, a couple of police officers enter the room. Genuinely scared, Maekrite rubs Silent's leg.

Butwal Island, Some Days Ago In The Future

Future!Shahinne is curiously observing how "Rosary", Tangent's newest catch, is introduced to her new team. Shahinne brushes against Copahue's wing and coos a couple of times.

<When do I get my [picture]? I liked it a lot...> the little bird asks, prompting Copahue to nuzzle her.

Future!Silent walks in and fetches his Pokégear from his pack. He proceeds to turn it off and disable its internal battery.

<Why did you do that?> inquires Copahue, only mostly undersanding that Silent is disabling a sort of phone.

"Because we need to stay clear from time paradoxes, Copahue." Silent grabs a couple of Pokéballs and equips them in his belt. "I want to make sure we can return cleanly to Mandarin Island for Celebi to send out our past selves before Maekrite's ban commences. I don't want intromissions."

Copahue nods and then returns to his nuzzling of Shahinne, to keep her distracted from annoying her or Tangent's teammates.

Ichtacya rests outside, trying to recover from the tiredness she's been feeling the last three days (time travel included).



Back at the Mandarin S Pokécenter

Silent is explaining (most of) the situation to the police officers in what regards to the little infight his team had and how Maekrite ran away to end up meeting another Nidorino. The officers show some discomfort at the idea that it would be one of Dr. Silent's Nidos that would end up doing something like this, but fortunately for Silent the Nurse speaks up, backed by SCIENCE.

"There's simply no other Nidorino around here that could have done it, " she explains showing some basic medical folders (to keep patient confidentiality). "The other four Nidorino registered in this island are not capable of inflicting wounds with the swiftness and severity that 'Tony' suffered."

"I understand that the Nidorino who attacked went there with intention to maim?" inquired one of the officers, taking a quick glance at Maekrite.

"Usually these skirmishes are intended to quickly and briefly establish dominance, as when there's only a couple of contestants the females tend to stop them." The Nurse answers. "This one simply got out of hand. You have the victim statement that says that the Nidorina intervened?"

At those words, Maekrite's ears flickered, but he refused to show more direct attention on the matter.

"Besides..." added the Nurse. "Consider it this way. Why would Dr. Silent here protect a random, possibly dangerous Nidorino by putting up his one on the line?"

The two officers stared at each other for a moment and retreated for a brief talk, while the Nurse compiled some data. She handed Silent a small note saying that she would take care of Silent's Pokémon while he was, most likely, going to be headed to the police station for a statement. Silent didn't express much comfort at the idea though.

After some deliberation, the two officers came in.

"Well... assuming it is your Nidorino, Dr. Silent, we'd still need to collect some hard evidence beyond your statement..."

"Not saying your word in these matters is untrustworthy, I hope you understand, " adds the other officer.

Silent nods, then pets Maekrite for a moment. "What would you need?"

The two officers stare at each other.

"Well... we'd need a profile and a lineup with the victims and witness. Although I'm sure a Nidorino like... that, would be easy to identify."

"Yeah, what with the yellow bands, Dr. Silent, if we may ask?" inquired the other officer, leaning in to examine Maekrite closer.

Silent waves a hand, keeping the officer a safe distance away, as Maekrite gives a half-shame, half-hatred growl. "I would prefer to explain this at a private meeting. It's a matter of public safety."

One of the officers shrugs. "If you say so..." he states, then closing his notebook and turning to address the nurse. "We are going to need access to the early reports and pictures of the wounds to compare."

"I will provide that if and once both parties here" points to me and to where the Recovery room is "agree to the profiling. You know our island's policy on invoking fantastic racism."

"Fine then. Ma'am." The officers turn to Silent. "Dr. Silent? You and your Nidorino have to accompany us."

"Is he being charged?" inquires the Nurse amused, rationalizing the consequences of the battle.

"Dr. Silent is being brought in as a [witness]. For the time being. Doctor? We'd feel safer if you recalled and sealed your Pokémon, no offense."

Maekrite drops his ears and presses harder against Silent's body. Silent picks him up and wraps him in his arms carefully.

"I'm recalling him after he has some herb noms to relax him, yes, but I'm invoking the aforementioned public safety concerns and denying the petition to seal his Pokéball."

One of the officers opens the door of the office and Silent is led out of the office.

After some minutes, the Nurse compiles a report and goes to talk with some of the other patients to check them. When she goes to check Ichtacya, she sees she has some visitors — Eeveelutions as well, interestingly — and decides to let her be for the moment. She doesn't need extra stress.

Thus Silent's Pokémon stay behind. Algira is taking care of Lileep and Naga at the pool, where they'll spend [most] of the day. Ichtacya and Dintel are on recovery. Copahue is resting on the lobby taking care of most of Silent's stuff. Shahinne is with Rex.

Mandarin S Police Station, Some Hours Later

After having some talks with the police officers and compiling a statement on the issue, a Psychic Pokémon is brought in who has compiled some chatter from the streets about recent incidents about the wild population helping PEFE hunt some Delta Pokémon. With that information and the barebone reports on the latest incidents (such as Pleo's kidnapping and the Moon Almost Going Colony Drop), Silent is allowed participation into the case to help clear out any pending issue. Silent is allowed to send [Dr. Tangent] a brief message explaining the issue.

"Can't comment much more at the moment, but yes, some Pokémon went in and requested assistance from the wild population, " explains Silent checking the first printout of the psychic's compiled statement. "One of my own Pokémon is at Recovery at the moment precisely because of a misunderstanding caused by these news spreading."

"I take it your Pokémon was hidden precisely to avoid scrambling the search and causing misunderstanding, " commented one of the officers.

"Sure, let's go with that, " answered Silent. "In the end it turned out these Pokémon weren't [even] here."

"But we do have a report, " says one of the officers bringing up a report from Trovitopolis "that there was a case of a weird Skitty on a city square."

After some hours, the Nidorino's owner's father is brought in as well to discuss the situation as well as the damages, both to the Pokémon and in property.

Back at the Mandarin Pokécenter

Ichtacya receives visitors, these being Aqua and Blossom. She is a bit worn out from the sedatives, so she does not notice them at the moment. Dintel, meanwhile, is sleeping curled and taped to his bed to prevent further chewing accidents.
