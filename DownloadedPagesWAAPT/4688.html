Route 111: Mountain

Aqua giggled as the TM implanted new information directly into her head, burbling happily as Rex pulled the disc away.

Rex: Alright, that move should help you on Ranger duty too, in case you and Kim need to camp out at night. I'd better teach it to some of my Pokemon as well. Anyway, let's make a secret base for those Sandshrew.

Aqua: <Yay!>

Moving closer to the mountain, Rex traced his fingers over the rough stone. He tapped at a few parts here and there, checking to see just how thick the stone was. In one particular region, however, a hollow noise greeted him. With a nod, he turned to Aqua and stepped back.

Rex: Try to hit it right here, okay?

The Eeveelution yipped in understanding, turning her gaze to the stone in front of her. She focused on the power the TM imbued her with, her body glowing a soft green. Her tail swung back and forth like a pendulum, Aqua's eyes shut in concentration. Rex, Jemma, and the Sandshrew were quiet as she focused, not a sound coming from among them.

Then, as if spurred to life by some alien force, the hollow stone eroded away, leaving a gaping hole in the wall. Arid air seeped out, washing over the group and sending Rex into a coughing fit. Jemma and the Sandshrew, however, found it delightful, heading into the cave with excited cheers.

Rex: Urgh...good job, Aqua.

Aqua: <Hehe~>

With that, the two made their way into the cave as well, planning to survey the new area before they left the Sandshrew to their new habitat.
