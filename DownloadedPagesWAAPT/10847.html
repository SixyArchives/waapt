Left Patrolling!Meinshao: <They're actually nice enough... When they're not being aggravating and stuck up.>

-Kirk growls in annoyance.-

Kirk: <No, you idiots, I meant the dragons who are currently in charge of the region. Have you been paying any attention to the world outside your little bubble?>

Left Patrolling!Meinshao: <Mmph. Well gee, excuse us for focusing on our duties!>

Right Patrolling!Meinshao: <And why would we want to live out THERE anyways?>

Kirk: <I don't know, perhaps because there's mons out there actually trying to make a better world for us, instead of sitting on their backsides and guarding a pile of useless trinkets!>
