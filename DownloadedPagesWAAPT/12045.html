301112 ButNotLeast17th Oct 2013 08:32:28 PM from Your Worst Nightmares Get RP Mod
YOU ARE TEARING ME APART, LISA!
Martin sat at a lonely table, raising his hand to call the waitress over.

"I'll be with you in a second." The waitress said.

Martin sat quietly, waiting. He looked out the window, clutching his lucky charm, thinking over this whole J-Team thing.

He chuckled.

"What have I gotten myself into?" He wondered, staring of into space.

He recalled Pi's words.

"We're the heroes of this world!"

Heroes...Could this whole thing be for real? Did Pi really see him as a hero, just like that? Pi had said something about how they wander the earth. Not much different than what Martin's been doing the past couple of years. He remembered the day before he set out on his worldwide journey. It was his fifteenth birthday. Greg had come back to town.

Olivine City, 15 months ago

Martin and Greg sat along the shore. The birthday festivities were over, and now they were just taking the time to relax and watch the sun go down.

Martin cleared his throat. "Hey, Greg?"

Greg looked up. "Yeah?"

"Can I tell you something?"

"Sure. What?"

"I...I'm thinking of leaving."

"What do you mean?"

"I mean, Johto is a big place, but I feel like...I feel like I want to see more, you know?"

Greg smiled. "Yeah. I know what you mean. So, you plan on taking a trip?"

"Yeah. Exactly."

"Then you'll want this." He produced something from his pocket. It was closed in his fist. "Here."

He opened his hand up- it was the small good luck charm that Martin had to this day.

"A necklace?" Martin said curiously, taking it out of Greg's palm. "What do I need a necklace for?"

"It's for luck." Greg pulled a similar necklace from his pocket, this one with a red crystal. He out it on around his neck. "See, some cultures believe that certain types of jewels and precious metals are connected to certain legendary Pokémon. Mine is made with ruby- which represents Groudon. Because Groudon is connected to the ground, and the earth, it's supposed to bring me good luck, you know, being a geologist. Yours is sapphire- connected to Kyogre. As a water-type Pokémon trainer, it'll help you on your journey, and it'll give you safe travels on the seas. It might sound dumb, but..." Greg tilted back the brim of his Stetson. "I think it might make a real difference for you, little brother."

Outskirt Stand, Present

"What can I get you, hun?" The waitress asked.

"Oh, um, can I please have a sandwich?"

"Sure thing! Want anything to drink?"

"Water's fine, thanks."

"Alright, then. Be just a bit!"

Maybe this is what Greg was talking about. Maybe this is the reason he left home in the first place.

It could very well be that this would be Martin's chance to prove to the world, and to himself, just what he could do.

At any rate, Martin wasn't going to waste an opportunity handed to him on a silver plate.

Nor would he pass up a sandwich handed to him in a basket.

Mmm.
