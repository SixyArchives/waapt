[OT] Route 32 - Two weeks later...

-Once more, it's a beautiful starry night, and my Pokémon team are gathered in the coastal meadows gazing up at the endless expanse of stars above them. Even though it's been two weeks since they returned to their own world, they are still no less happy to be home again, at last. More so now that they had recently returned to Johto.-

Unite: <Your brother's really nice. If a bit overprotective.>

Woolly: <He's always been a bit worried about me ever since our parents died. But he took really good care of me. I'm glad I got to see him again.>

Unite: <And I'm glad I got to meet him. Hmm. I supposed that's one good think about that whole Alternate timeline thing. We got to meet so many new Pokémon, didn't we?>

Woolly: <Oh, yeah! Cinder, Bullet, Dusty, and all of Tracer's other Pokémon. I wonder if we'll ever be able to see to them again?>

Unite: <I'm sure we'll be able to work something out. And if not...There's always the dream world.>

Woolly: <Ooh, of course! How could I forget...>

-Aside.-

Harriet: <Hey...Treeo?>

Treeo: <Hmm?>

Harriet: <Well, I was wondering...If you knew what was going to happen to you before you joined this team, would you have still let yourself get caught?>

Treeo: <Huh. Why do you ask?>

Harriet: -A bit awkwardly.- <Oh, no particular reason...I'm just curious.>

Treeo: -Rubs his chin thoughtfully.- <Well, had I know that I would have gone through this whole alternate universe thing as well as spent a few months in a PC box then I might have had second thoughts.

...But, since I would know just how awesome all of you guys are...> -Gazes at Harriet, smiling at her.- <...I would have done it anyway.>

Harriet: -Looks bashful.- <Aww, Treeo. You're so sweet.>

Treeo: <I do try. Wait...What's Chord doing?>

-The two look over at a nearby Ariados, who appears to be carving a small log of wood into something.-

Harriet: <That's strange. Maybe that's something all Ariados do when they just evolve?>

Treeo: <It might be, but I wouldn't know. I haven't spent much time around Ariados.>

Harriet: <Same here...Hey, Treeo?>

Treeo: <Yeah?>

Harriet: <I just wanted to say...thanks for being there with me throughout all this. You know I only just joined this team recently, and thanks to you, despite all that madness...you were always there for me.>

Treeo: <Well, I felt the same. Even though I was caught a few months back, I hadn't spent a meaningful amount of time on the team until Luke brought me back in Kanto. So I was still technically a newbie, too. It was overwhelming catching up on all the stuff that had happened and going to that other universe.

But having somemon else with me that knew what I was going through...Helped make it all bearable. So...I guess I should say thanks to you too.>

Harriet: <...Aww, Treeo.>

-Harriet nuzzles against Treeo lovingly, causing the Sceptile to blush heavily.-

Treeo: <...! ~ ♥♥♥>

-Woolly and Unite briefly watch as Treeo turns as red as a Tamato Berry.-

Unite: <Heh. If I knew any better, I'd say that those two...

...Nah, you don't think?>

Woolly: <No, Harriet's in love with Bill, isn't she?>

Unite: <That's what I thought. Oh well.>

-The two turn their attention back to the beautiful night sky.-

Unite: <...It's amazing, isn't it?>

Woolly: <It sure is. You know, when I was just a Mareep, Yarn told me that all the stars in the sky were actually other Mareep, Flaaffy and Ampharos signaling with their lights at us from all across the universe.>

Unite: <Really?>

Woolly: <Yep. I remember staying up late one night and shining my own light back at them. Just to let them know I was getting their signals.> -Rubs the back of her head.- <Of course, I later learned that they were stars and not others of our species. But...I always thought it was a lovely idea.>

Unite: <...It is, you know.> -Looks at the stars for a few seconds.- <But who's to say that some of those lights up there aren't others like you? There's nothing that proves that at least some of them aren't.>

Woolly: <...Yeah, they could just be...>

Unite: <So...Why don't you say hello? I bet any Mareep, Flaaffy or Ampharos that are up there would really like to get a reply. And even of there aren't, it's worth a shot, right?>

Woolly: -Smiles.- <Of course!> -Stands up.- <Okay, here we go!>

-Woolly turns her tail bulb on, shining it's bright light up at the sky.-

Unite: <Oh, hey! Why don't we go one better and send a message? Since you guys can talk with those tail bulbs and all.>

Woolly: <Great idea! But...What should I say?>

Unite: <Ooh...I dunno. Just tell them about us. I'm sure they'd like to know about what goes on here on our planet.>

Woolly: -Nods.- <Here goes, then...

Hello, space Mareep! I hope you get this message. Assuming you're out there.

My name's Woolly, and...I just wanted to let you know that everything's fine down here on Earth. Sure, we had some trouble a few weeks ago. But we managed to fix it, with a bit of help from all our friends.

Did you have the same problem? Were you all sent to an alternate timeline too? I supposed you must have been, seeing as we ended up with a new universe and all. But it's okay now. Everything's okay...

Anyway, I'm okay. I've got all these wonderful friends on my team. Lake, Harriet, Stratus, Treeo. And Unite...He's really special. He's this Braviary, and he loves me so much.

Do you have Braviary up there on your planets? I hope you do, they're wonderful Pokémon, if Unite is anything to go bye.

Anyway, I'm feeling tired, so I should be going to sleep now. But whenever I'm awake at night from now on, I'll keep an eye on the stars, so I you do send a reply, I won't miss it.

That's all for now, then. Maybe I'll send another message soon. We'll see...>

Unite: <Wow. That was a long message.> -Yawns.- <Oh man, I'm tired...Come on, let's got to sleep.>

-Unite lies down on the ground, Woolly joining him and wrapping her arms around him lovingly.-

Unite: <Goodnight, Woolly.>

Woolly: <Goodnight, Unite.>

-Soon after, the two are asleep and the millions of lights in the sky.

And as for whether Woolly's message was received or not? Well, that's another story...-
