Duke seemed taken aback "M-Marriage huh. Don't you think you're still a bit too young for that?"

"Hey, you're younger than me." she turned to him, smiling.

"Well... In human years, I guess... Though as far as my ID goes I'm 19." he smiled back.

"Yeah, you did a nice job cheating the system." she then begins petting Duke on the back.

Duke let out a chuckle and started running his hand through Lyuri's hair "I guess there's not much more for us to do now."

They stay silent for a bit, until Lyuri begins taking off Duke's coat.

"Uh... Lyuri? What are you doing?"

"Man I can't understand how you manage to wear a coat every single day, you don't feel heat or anything?" said Lyuri as she fully took off his coat.

"I-I just feel more comfortable in full clothing, you know that."

"Come on, Mr.Charisma, I think people would be happier if you showed more of your body." she laughed.

"Charisma?" he raised an eyebrow.

"You never noticed how you earn people's respect so easily? I thought you were improving. You don't even talk to them much and yet Blitz and especially Jason look up to you quite a bit."

"I... I guess." said Duke, looking unsure of what to think of it.

Lyuri then stopped and started staring at Duke "You know, I never really tried something."

"What?" he tilted his head

"I never tried to tickle you!" she said, smiling mischievously.

She then begins to tickle Duke on his sides and he reacts by laughing really loud, it was the first time she had ever heard Duke raise his voice, especially to that extent. For someone without context, they would probably think he was being tortured. Even more so considering how Duke was flailing like crazy and it didn't take long for him to fall down onto the sand. Lyuri eventually had to get on top of him to pin him down.

"Lyuri...! Stop! Please!" said Duke, between his laughing fits.

"Looks like I found your one weakness!" said Lyuri, grinning.

"If you guys are going to go wild, I think it would be better if you two got a room so you could do it without clothes."

-Record scratch-

Lyuri stops cold as she realizes the rather suggestive position they were on and looks back to see that the source of the voice was Hikari, who was walking around the beach with a metal detector.

"Well..." said Duke, catching his breath "That's awkward."
