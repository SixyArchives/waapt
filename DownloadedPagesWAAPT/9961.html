 249015 luke 924, Wed, 1st May '13 7:10:14 PM Get RP Mod
Comet: <Hmm...>

While the rest of the group was talking among themselves and not paying much attention to her as she prepared the food, Comet began to set to work on dinner.

The truth was, though? She hadn't had time to restock her food supply upon arriving in Mistralton earlier that day. But that wasn't an issue, oh no.

Even if she didn't want to be there, as a Dream World Pokémon, Comet was right at home. And here, there was almost nothing she could not do.

Tapping into the chaotic nature of the very world around them, the Charizard, with a little effort, suddenly conjured up a bowl of assorted berries out of nowhere. But then she realized something.

If she didn't want to draw attention to herself, then she couldn't create something that she wouldn't be able to otherwise. And everyone knew that she didn't have nearly as many berries as she was about to serve.

Grumbling to herself, the Charizard discarded the berries, bowl and all disappearing into a puff of Dream Mist as she tried to think of something that she could create with supplies on-hand, or from Mistralton.

A few seconds of thinking later, and she had the perfect idea.

Manipulating the Dream World once again, this time, Comet created a rather large pizza - with thirteen slices, of course. However, she deliberately made it frozen, so that she could then proceed to cook it with a quick blast of her Flamethrower attack.

Presentation was the key, here. If it hadn't look like she had cooked it, everyone would get suspicious.

With all her bases covered, the Charizard pulled out a rather large plate, slapped the pizza down on it, then turned around...to find Stratus standing right behind her.

Comet: <AAAAAHHHH!!!>

Stratus: <Is that Pizza?>

Comet: <...Huh? Oh!>

Relief washed over Comet. Either Stratus was too distracted by the food, or he simply hadn't noticed her conjure it out of nowhere.

<Yeah, it is! Hey everyone! Come and grab a slice!>

Stratus: <Ooh, don't mind if I do!>

The Dragonite grabbed himself a slice...then went for another one, only to be slapped by Comet.

<OW! What gives?!>

Comet: <There's only enough here for one slice each! You'll just have to make do with that, I'm afraid.>

Stratus: <Aww, are you kidding?! Once slice is hardly even a snack...>

<Thank you, Comet.>
