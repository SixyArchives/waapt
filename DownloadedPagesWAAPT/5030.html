[AU] New Bark Town

A familiar Meganuim and Typhlosion are sitting on the shore, gazing out upon the river that leads up to Route 26 and the rest of Kanto. The two cuddle each other, glad to have this interment moment together.

Cinder: <I don't know about you, Basil...but I'm glad this year's almost over.>

The Meganium tilts her head.

Basil: <Is it because of all the awful things that happened?>

Cinder nods.

Cinder: <I'm just glad that it's all behind us. I don't ever want to go through anything like that again.>

Basil: <I'll admit, you've probably seen things that no peoplemons should ever have to see. But...can really say everything has been bad?>

Cinder: <What do you mean?>

Basil: <Well some good came out of this yeah, didn't it? Even though we went through such awwful things...we did get to meet Lake and his friends from that other universe, and it lead to use meeting the Lake from this universe...All those things wouldn't have happened otherwise.>

Cinder: <...I guess so...But...you did leave one important thing out there...>

Basil: <I did?>

Cinder: <Yeah...>

Cinder smiles warmly at Basil.

<...we would have never got together.>

Basil: <...Oh...>

Basil looks away, blushing in embarrassment.

<How could I forget about that? I'm so silly...>

The Meganium chuckles. Cinder grins, then wraps his arms around her again.

Cinder: <...I love you...you silly little flower.>

Basil smiles back, hugging him with her vines.

Basil: <And I love you too, my warm little stoat...>

Both of them nuzzle each other lovingly, glad that, despite rather harsh circumstances, that they were both together.
