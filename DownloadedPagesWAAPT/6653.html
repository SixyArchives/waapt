The Next Entry

<Lardna:

Ran into a group of Volcaronan mercenaries today, and set what I think might be a record in fastest transition from "bellicose confidence" to "bowel-voiding terror" (On a tangent, are we really that frightening? Trading our testimony of their having accosted us in return for their cooperation.

Apparently, the Hydreigon haven't seen so much as oppressors as nuisances ever since they took over by most locals. If incredibly intimidating ones. The foremost complaints that arise seem to stem from violations of normalcy. e.g. Taking 5 minutes to have to text something, ham-fisted propaganda on the television, and air raids from the nominally allied Haxorus and their cohorts. So the key to giving the resistance a foothold here is getting the locals sufficiently annoyed with their new rulers? Not sure if I like the implications of that... Even if it would explain a lot.

In other news, the weather's getting more and more intolerable as we head steadily further north. Raptor seems to have noticed, she's made a bit of a habit of nesting right beside me.
