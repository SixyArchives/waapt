The Reef - East Edge

-Various kinds of Water Pokémon are patrolling the east edge of the reef, keeping a vigilant watch for any potential threats to the Aquatic Pokémon that live there.-

Captain!Lanturn: <Report!>

Guard!Octillery: <No threats detected, sir. A few Sharpedo did swim a little too close to the Reef for comfort earlier, but we managed to drive them away without any violence.>

Captain!Lanturn: -Nods.- <Well done. Now, have you received the latest orders from the Royal Guard?>

Guard!Kingler: <Yes sir. Any and all Feraligatr spotted swimming in our waters are to be apprehended on sight, then imprisoned until ready to be interrogated.>

Captain!Lanturn: <That's correct.>

Guard!Octillery: <Have the patrols had any luck at locating the Prince, yet?>

Guard!Kingler: <No. Not yet, at least. They've pretty much searched every nook and cranny around The Reef, so they're going to branch the search out into further waters.>

Guard!Octillery: <Hmm. Did they try searching around Shamouti Island? He may have gone there to seek shelter with the Legendary birds.>

Captain!Lanturn: <Whoa! Don't you remember the last time we sent a patrol into those waters? Those mons were lucky to make it back alive!>

Guard!Kingler: <Yeah, those birds are fiercely territorial. So even if The Prince did swim over that way, we wouldn't be able to go and check. Not without risking our hides.>

Captain!Lanturn: -Sighs- <I hope he turns up soon. It won't be that long until his father returns. And if he finds that he disappeared when we were supposed to be looking after him...> -Shudders.- <The whole Orange Islands could be doomed.>

Guard!Octillery: <Hey. I'm sure we'll be able to find him before then, we've got best mons in the reef searching for->

???: <SIR!>

-A rather shocked looking Mantine swims over to the Lanturn.-

Captain!Lanturn: <What's the matter?>

Gatherer!Mantine: <It's the Prince, sir! We've found him!>

Captain!Lanturn: <What?! Where is he!>

Gatherer!Mantine: <Me and my gathering party spotted him swimming down the Valley of Clamperl half an hour ago. He was swimming towards the Reef with a bunch of Diving Pokémon and a human!>

Captain!Lanturn: <Oh? Did you manage to see what type of Pokémon they were?>

Mantine: <Er...Let's see. There was a Dragonite, Ampharos, Ariados, Sceptile, Honchkrow, Gallade and...A Feraligatr.>

Guard!Kingler: <Whoa!>

Guard!Octillery: <A Feraligatr?!>

Captain!Lanturn: <Hmm. Was it the same Feraligatr that attempted to kidnap the Prince?>

Gatherer!Mantine: <We're not sure, sir. But he did look rather similar to the eyewitness reports.>

-The Lanturn narrows his eyes upon hearing this.-

Captain!Lanturn: <Hmm. If it is the same Feraligatr though, what would posses him to return here with the Prince? Does he not realize that he's now the most wanted Pokémon in the entire Sea of Kyogre?>

Guard!Octillery: <Maybe he brought him back to organize a hostage exchange?>

Captain!Lanturn: <Perhaps. But whatever the reason...We're going to make him regret ever showing his face here again, as well as taking back the Prince at the same time.> -To the Octillery- <Okay, I want every mon available to set up an ambush at the Sandy flats just south from here. Not only will they have to cross it to reach the Reef, but they'll be nowhere for them to hide from our Rescue party.>

Guard!Octillery: <Okay, sir! I'll get all my mons sent down there right away!>

Captain!Lanturn: <Very good. Also...Have them station some Qwillfish sentries there, too.>

-The Octillery and Kingler are visibly shocked at this.-

Guard!Octillery: <Qwillfish?>

Guard!Kingler: <But sir, we only use them to easily dispatch of Divers. And that's only when we really want them gone. Do you really want to drown these Pokémon?>

Captain!Lanturn: -Shakes his head.- <I'd rather not drown anymon if I had the choice. But...If that Feraligatr was able to fight off a Royal escort by himself, imagine what an entire group of mons like that could do to us? It's not ideal, but give the choice between them and the Reef's safety...>

Guard!Octillery: <...It's okay, sir. We understand.>

Guard!Kingler: <Very well, then. We'll mobilize and head to the Sandy flats ASAP.>

Captain!Lanturn: <Excellent.>

-The Laturn turns and gazes in the direction of the Sandy flats, the almost featureless expanse easily visible from their position.-

Captain!Lanturn: <Today, we'll get our Prince back. One way or another...>

Underwater - The Sandy Flats

-We've been swimming for quite a while now. Having just passed through an underwater valley filled with dozens of Clamperl (And the odd Gorebyss and Huntail), we now find ourselves in a flat sandy expanse, it's only features being the odd rock or piece of seaweed.-

Woolly: -Awestruck.- <Wow...Isn't this amazing? It's like a completely different world down here...>

Harriet: -Looks around nervously.- <...Yeah, real amazing...> -Shivers.-

-Treeo, Woolly and Stratus can't help but take note of Harriet's discomfort.-

Treeo: <Harriet? What's the matter?>

Woolly: <Are you alright? You've been nervous even since we came down here.>

Stratus: <Yeah. It's really worrying...>

Harriet: <...Well...Um...I may not have mentioned this before, but...I'm rather afraid of tight spaces like this.>

Treeo: <Oh. So you're claustrophobic, then?>

Harriet: -Nods.- <Yeah. And the constant threat of drowning isn't really helping things.> -Ruffles her feathers.- <I really shouldn't have come...>

"Yeah, you don't look so good, Harriet. Are you sure you don't want me to recall you?"

Stratus: <Eep! Same here...>

"Wow. That does sound scary. But you can't stay out, You're scared half to death!"

Pleo: -Swims over, looking a bit worried.- <Is there anything I can do to help, Harriet?> -Tilts his head, worriedly.-

Harriet: <Um...I don't know...>

Guardé: <Perhaps I could put her to sleep using Hypnosis? At least, until we've arrived at somewhere where there's air.>

Woolly: <You do?>

Stratus: <What is it?>

Treeo: -Smiles.- <Let me show you.>

-The Sceptile places an arm around Harriet, then leans against her, nuzzling her lovingly.-

Treeo: <...Does this help?>

Harriet: <...> -Blushes heavily.- <...Yeah...It does.>

-Harriet relaxes. She's still slightly on edge, but not as much as she was a few moments prior.-

Pleo: -Tilts his head.- <Lake? Are you sure that they're sure they're not in love?>

Lake: <To be honest, little guy? I don't really know.>

Stratus: <How sweet!>

Woolly: <Aww!>

Guardé: <So...Are you in love, then? Would you like me to get our bard to serenade you with a romantic ballad?>

Chord: -Faceplams.- <Can I push him out? Please?>

-Harriet turns to me and nods.-

Pleo: <Okay! Come on, everyone! The Reef's right over->

<"FREEZE!">

-Suddenly, what seems like hundreds of Aquatic Pokémon of all kinds pop out of nowhere. Octillery, Krabby, Kingler, Staryu, Starmie, Corpish and Crawdunt emerge from the sand, Chinchou, Lanturn, Qwillfish, Remoraid, and Mantine swim out from gaps inbetween nearby rocks, and Squirtle, Wartortle, Blastoise and Lapras dive down from the surface to surround us, all of them looking ready to attack at any second.
