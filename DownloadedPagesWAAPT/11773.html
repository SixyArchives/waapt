Gateon Port - Elsewhere

Sakura was walking around when suddenly she hears running and turns around to see that it was Grant running towards her.

"Sakura! There you are!" said Grant, panting a bit and barely managing to keep himself standing "M-Man... Humans tire quickly..." he said, chuckling a bit as he panted.

"Grant? What happened?" asked Sakura, getting immediatelly concerned.

"Well, what do you think? You completely vanished last night! I've been looking around for you all day thinking that maybe you'd been kidnapped or something!" explained Grant, sounding almost insulted with the question.

"O-Oh... I'm sorry, it's just that-"

"Nevermind that, I'm just glad to see that you're safe." said Grant, catching his breath and looking at Sakura with a relieved smile.

"A-All right, but why are you so worried about me all of sudden? It's not the first time I've stayed away for an entire day." asked Sakura, puzzled

"Well, there seems to be a lot of stuff going on around this region, so I started to get worried something could've happened to you." said Grant, scratching his head a bit, then taking a better look at Sakura "... Did something happen to you? You look as if you haven't slept for days."

"Um... Well... Nevermind." said Sakura, she then walks to Grant and hugs him "Thanks for the concern though."

"You really shouldn't be thanking me for something like that." he said, hugging her back.

"Could you... Could you make me a favor, Grant?" asked Sakura

"Yes?"

"Never take that armband off and please, don't get involved with dangerous stuff while I'm not around, I don't want to lose you after what happened with Duke, please?" asked Sakura, with a fairly weak and tired tone

"Sure, if that'll make you feel better. As long as you don't make me worry again." he answered, hugging her a bit tighter.

"Deal." said Sakura, giggling a bit "You know, even as a human, you're still rather... Soft."

Sakura then ends up falling asleep while being held by Grant, who only looks at her, rather surprised.

"... She must be really tired, she fell asleep so quickly, but wait... Now I have to carry her around. And I just promised that I wouldn't take the armband off too..." thought Grant, giving a frustated sigh and looking at Sakura with a hint of annoyance in his eyes "... You little trickster, did you do that on purpose?" he thought, before sighing again and smiling.

Grant then starts carrying a sleeping Sakura back to the Pokémon Center.
