Although, that was probably invalidated by the fact that he had brought some muffins to eat while out flying.

...Well, Several muffins. But hey, He hadn't had breakfast yet!

As the Dragonite paused, hovering in mid-air for a few minutes to nom another cake (chocolate chip!), he was unaware of the fact that he was being followed by something very big, very green, and VERY long. The immense Pokémon slowly snuck up behind the Dragon Pokémon, then tapped his shoulder to get his attention.

Stratus, thinking that a bird was pecking him gently, turns around to see what's up...and recoils in shock at the sight of a huge emerald dragon Pokémon hovering there behind him.

Stratus: <EEP!!!>

In a panic, the Dragonite drops his muffins...only for the Pokémon to quickly snap them up in his huge jaws, licking his lips in pleasure as he does so.

Rayquaza: <Mmm! Tasty. Makes a change from rainwater, at least...>
