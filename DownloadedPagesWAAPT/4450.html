 111250 Crewe, Sat, 12th Nov '11 1:30:43 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
Crewe, wandering around on her own with Weavile, Nova, and Dana, finds herself under a few trees, idly looking around.

She's about to say something to her Pokemon when a weight suddenly drops under her shoulder, and a beak starts violently pecking her head.

<DIE, HUMAN!>

"OW! What the—" Crewe flails her arms at the back of her head, trying to knock off whatever is perched there.

With a hiss, Weavile slashes at the whatever-it-is and knocks it to the ground. She crouches over it, pinning it with one paw, snarling. Nova looks on, her ears pinned back and her front paw up.

Weavile is standing over a struggling Natu, who alternately pecks at the paw holding her to the ground and lets out loud shrieks.

<UNHAND ME! I WILL KILL YOU ALL!>

<Shut up, > Weavile snarls, whacking the Natu on the head with her free paw.

"What the hell is going on?" Crewe asks, dropping down on one knee by the Natu. "Who are you?"

<I am Kelly! NOW DIE!> the Natu teleports back onto Crewe's shoulder, but is pounced on by Dana. The two small Pokemon go tumbling to the ground, and after a brief scuffle Dana pins her, bristling.

<Um, Crewe? We may have to do something about this, > Weavile comments, crouching near Dana and eyeing the Natu.

"Yeah, probably. Hold on a second."

Crewe rummages around in her bag, then pulls out an Ultra Ball and taps Kelly on the head with it. The Natu is sucked into it, and after shaking a few times is caught.

<WHAT? Are you serious?> Weavile whacks Crewe on the back of her head with one paw.

"Ow! Hey! I did something about it! C'mon, let's go back to the others."

She clips the ball onto her belt and heads back towards the group. Nova and Dana pad along by her heels, and Weavile trails behind, muttering about catching menaces.
