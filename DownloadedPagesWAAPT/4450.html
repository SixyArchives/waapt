"OW! What the—" Crewe flails her arms at the back of her head, trying to knock off whatever is perched there.

With a hiss, Weavile slashes at the whatever-it-is and knocks it to the ground. She crouches over it, pinning it with one paw, snarling. Nova looks on, her ears pinned back and her front paw up.

<UNHAND ME! I WILL KILL YOU ALL!>

<Shut up,> Weavile snarls, whacking the Natu on the head with her free paw.

"What the hell is going on?" Crewe asks, dropping down on one knee by the Natu. "Who are you?"

<I am Kelly! NOW DIE!> the Natu teleports back onto Crewe's shoulder, but is pounced on by Dana. The two small Pokemon go tumbling to the ground, and after a brief scuffle Dana pins her, bristling.

"Yeah, probably. Hold on a second."

<WHAT? Are you serious?> Weavile whacks Crewe on the back of her head with one paw.
