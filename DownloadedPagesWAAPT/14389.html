Clockwise circles overhead, Silver Wind trailing from his wings like a cape, the scales hovering in midair, then flying up into the sky above the arena to spell out, in lovely calligraphy, "A King's Favor". The scales blow away as if a soft breeze dispersed them and fall in a gentle spiral to form a circle around Crewe. A silver light flashes in the center of Clockwise's path and silver shards fly out, then back around to encircle his head like a crown. He lands behind her. She curtsies to the crowd, then raises her arms. Clockwise opens his wings and leaps into the air-

-as he flies, he starts to use Silver Wind, the light green scales flowing from his wings like a cape and flying out behind him. They stay in the air, glowing faintly with a pink aura and marking his path. He shoots straight up in the air, then hovers briefly and starts slowly circling back towards the ground, his circles getting wider and wider as he gets lower, until he's flying directly over the crowd-

-he starts circling inwards and upwards, with long, smooth movements, moving counter to the previous spiral. As he reaches the top, he stops using Silver Wind and hovers just above the highest point of the path, flinging his wings out to send the last shimmering scales flying out over the crowd-

-Crewe lowers her arms and he starts circling in the opposite direction of the outwards Silver Wind spiral, this time trailing an Ominous Wind trail behind him. He repeats the pattern of wide circles followed by smaller circles leading him up to the top. He once again flings his wings open at the highest point of his path, ghost Obscura billowing out behind him and slowly dissipating-

-Clockwise pauses at the top and a bright sphere appears in front of him. It expands, then implodes in on itself and suddenly explodes outwards in many silver shards. The shards stop in place, surrounded by the same pink aura, and slowly move into a circle, orbiting Clockwise. They circle him faster and faster, then break their circle and expand to form a shimmering sphere around him-

-by this point, the center of the spirals is barely visible between the spirals of green and purple. Clockwise folds his wings and dives down into the center, the sphere keeping up with him. He starts flying about within, barely visible as a brief flash of tan and silver. Every so often, there is a bright flash and shining silvery particles burst out from the center and stop just on the surface of the spiral, hovering in place. Once the entire surface is peppered with silver, Clockwise emerges once more at the top-

-Clockwise's eyes glow and the spirals start moving, unwinding from around the center trail flying about as he moves them into new shapes. When he's done, the Silver Wind forms the stem and leaves of a huge purple rose. The silver shards dot the stem as thorns and make dew drops on the leaves and petals. The entire thing is surrounded by a pale pink aura-

-the rose hovers above Crewe's head for a moment, then Clockwise's eyes glow brighter and it begins to shrink-

-the rose shrinks and compacts until it is the size of an average garden rose. With one last pulse of pink psychic energy coming off the rose, the aura fades and the flower falls. Crewe deftly snatches it out of the air and waves it to the crowd, beaming-

-she turns to the judges and curtsies again, offering the rose. As Clockwise descends from the sky above the arena, he levitates the rose to the judges' table. He lands beside Crewe as turns to the crowd again and waves, curtsying one last time. Clockwise sweeps a wing out and bows beside her before they leave the arena-
