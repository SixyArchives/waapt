The most unique type

A House in Kalos

An eight years old girl is lying on the living room of her house, watching television while many sheets of paper were lying around; she was drawing on one of them while at the same time watching the TV. A Spoink was bouncing next to her and a Noibat was currently lying on her hat and watching what she drawing. Her mother and father were watching her minding her own business while quietly discussing so that she wouldn't hear.

"You think she'll be able to go on a journey of her own someday?" asked her father

"She seems to get along well with Pokémon and she can still comunicate with others even without a voice of her own, I'd say she'll be fine." answered her mother.

"I don't know... If she becomes unable to write for some reason she might get in trouble and have no way to get help..."

"Oh please, body communications exists. Don't worry that much, still... Maybe she could travel with a group?"

"Like that J-Team she watches on TV?"

"Yeah, like that. Doesn't need to be such a big group though, she probably could go on a journey with her friends. Travelling alone isn't as good as travelling with friends after all"

"Point. I'm not sure if her friends even have Pokémon yet, but I guess it's only a matter of time, she can wait until then."

Without they noticing, the Noibat turns in their direction and rolls his eyes

"<You know, if they're going to whisper to each other, at least they should do it where I can't hear them.>" he thought, not feeling minding the conversation.

After a while, the girl turns the TV off and walks to her parents with a few sheets of paper in hands.

"What is it, dear?" asked her mother

She then shows a sheet with "Can I go outside?" written on it while she kept smiling.

"What do you want to do outside?" asked her father in turn

She then flips the paper, where it was written "Divoom and Spring must be hungry" and she then switches to another paper where it was written "So I want to buy some Galettes for me and them!"

"Oh, it's okay, feel free to go." said her mother

She then flips the paper "But I don't have money."

Her father then hands her some money, to which she replies by smiling and switching to another paper with "Thanks papa!" written on it. She then takes the money and some more sheets of paper, picks up her rollerskating helmet that she had left on the sofa and recalls the Spoink before heading outside, the Noibat still staying on top of her hat. Once she was already gone, her parents turned to face each other

"She sure knows us pretty well if she pre-wrote all of those messages." said her mother, smiling

"She even guessed I was the one going to give her the money." said her father, impressed.
