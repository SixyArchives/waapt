Unovan-B Tripartate Demilitarized Zone - What Was Once Nimbasa

-Were one to look at the demilitarized zone from a distance towards the desert, the first thing that one could notice would be an abundance of fortifications built down a region vaguely resembling a 'Y'. Along its periphery, palisades and walls are erected, many plastered with one of 3 of Schrodinger's insignias. With sharpened pieces of glass, metal, thorns and the like on the tops to dissuade mons from vaulting over. Watchtowers abound, and mons seem to be constantly scanning the skies for unwelcome intruders. While others seem to be fixated on the ground to detect intruders from below. Set further back from this region are three highly regimented-looking settlements. Which locals would recognize to be the garrisons of three nations.

The region inside the 'Y' is about as inviting. Semi-obscured traps are seemingly omnipresent, designed to ensure a trip to a medic for one who would be unfortunate or otherwise foolhardy enough to trip one. At the center of the 'Y' lies a twisted, vaguely wheel-like mess of metal, with strange cabins dangling from parts of it

Were one to pay attention to the mons from the three garrisons, one would notice a rough pattern. Most of the mons from the northern two garrisons appear to be acclimated for a temparate existence, a mix of locals and of mons more frequently found in other parts of Unova. The westernmost garrison containing many mons hailing from the western regions of Unova. The easternmost garrison containing mons from its respective direction. The southernmost garrison is rather peculiar, its makeup seemingly almost entirely composed of desert mons... And the occasional Surskit line member-
