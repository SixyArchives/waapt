UTINNI!

PEFE

Jack didn't know the details, but he knew the Interesting Couch had to be alive somehow.

Or haunted.

Or both.

It was impossible to tell which, or even prove it, since it never seemed to do anything as long as it was being looked at. But as soon as his back was turned, Jack would feel the interesting couch - and he was certain it was the Interesting Couch - smacking him from behind. He would then turn around, try kicking or punching the couch and getting no response, and then the process would repeat.

Jack would try to get sneaky, but the Interesting Couch was sneakier. This contest to try to out-sneak one another - a competition the couch was clearly winning by the appearance that it wasn't actually doing anything - moved across the PEFE, until finally the couch seemed to vanish.

Jack: "Huh...weird."

And then he turned around and outright tripped over the Interesting Couch, which he was certain hadn't been there just a moment ago.

Jack: "GYAAAAAHHH!"

And as he fell over, Jack managed to fall into what he guessed was some kind of mirror in the split-second he had before he lost consciousness.

Enchanted Forest

As he woke up, Jack was surprised to discover that he had fallen into a forest of some kind. And he was being watched.

He was soon approached by a Pachirisu that seemed like it was trying to tell him something. Naturally, he called upon Runt to translate.

Curious Pachirisu: (Via Runt) "Hello!"

Jack: "Uh...hi."

Happy Pachirisu: (Via Runt) "Are you here with the others?"

Jack: "Others?"

Happy Pachirisu: (Via Runt) "The others who went that direction."

The squirrel pokemon pointed to a path. If he had to guess, he suspected that other people from the PEFE had found their way into whatever this place was.

Jack: "Uh...thanks."

Until he knew what was going on, Jack decided he would try to meet up with whoever else had already gone on ahead. As he moved, the little critters followed behind him, running along the ground as they tried to keep up.
