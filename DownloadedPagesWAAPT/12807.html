"Tell ya what, kid...There's a branch in Kalos that's opening up soon. If you can get there in time for opening in around a month, and perform for the opening day? I'll make sure ya get all three of those for free."

"Really?" Martin seemed suspicious. "What's the catch? I mean, can a cashier really just give clearance like that?"

Somewhere in Orre

A young girl is walking through the streets, looking rather dejected and sullen. This girl's name is Anka. And she traveled all the way to Orre to take up a job. However, not everthing is a peachy as it seems.

Anka: I got fired today.

She sighs and continues to walk down the street. Still pondering to herself about her current predicament.

Anka: I guess it was along time coming. Hours were getting tight and they were cutting back on employees. Luck dictates that I would be the first to get cut. I mean, the pay was terrible, but it was still pay. Now I'm kinda worried what to do about the rent... and the food.

Anka stops and groans to herself.

Anka: I'm going to have ice-cream for dinner for the fourth night in a row. Man I would kill for some real food..

Anka shrugs and continues on with her march.

Anka: Well. I guess it can't get worse than this...

While walking down the road, Anka notices a flyer plastered onto one of the alley walls.

Anka: Oh?

Anka takes a good look at the flyer and reads the following

Join Team Neo-Galactic and help create a new world by destroying this one!

All applicants welcome

Anka: Huh... Maybe my luck isn't so bad after all. I mean, it isn't moral work. But money is money so...

Anka peels off the flyer and follows the directions to the recruitment place.
