"<Hey, Valentine!>" said Teishi, cheerfully

"<GAH!> yelled the Audino as the surprise made her fall on her rear "<D-Don't scare me like that, Teishi!>" she nervously said.

"<S-Sorry, I didn't mean to scare you.>" said Teishi, helping her back up.

"<It's okay.>" she then accepted his help to make her stand up again "<Thanks. Do you want something?>"

"<I was just wondering, what can you do? I never really saw a Pokémon of your species before, at least I don't think I did.>" said Teishi.

"<Oh, um... I guess I can heal mons and... Um... Can you crouch a little bit, you're kinda tall.>"

"<Oh, okay.>"

Teishi then crouched a bit so he wouldn't tower over Valentine as much. Valentine then took the opportunity to place her stethoscope-like ears on his chest, right over where his heart would be.

"<Um... What are you doing?>" Teishi asked, confused

"<You're very happy and full of energy, you're also feeling bored and confused.>" she then removed the extensions of her ears from his chest.

"<Huh? Yeah, I guess that's right. Did you figure that out by->"

Valetine nodded, with a sweet smile on her face "<I can tell how you're feeling by listening to your heartbeat.>"

"<Wow, that's really nice! Sakura can read feelings as well, but it's more like she senses them. You literally listen to someone's heart, that's really cool!>" Teishi was looking quite excited at this point.

"<Y-You really think so...?>" Valentine started to look away from Teishi, though a warm smile slowly starts showing up on her expression

"<Yeah! It's pretty nice!>" Teishi then paused for a bit and tilted his head "<Is something wrong with you?>"

Valentine's eyes go wide as she realized just what she was doing "<Ah! Nothing!>" she said as she flailed her arms for a couple of seconds, before regaining her composure "<I, uh... I'm just flattered. Thanks.>"

Teishi chuckled at her reaction "<You're welcome!>" he cheerfully said

Valentine was growing increasinly nervous as she looked at Teishi "<So, um... There's really nothing else I can do; and you're bored already so I guess you probably should go look for something more fun to do?>"

Teishi then got rather pensive "<Hm... Well... I don't think I'm really going to find anything. But you're looking pretty nervous so I'm just gonna let you relax a bit.>" he stands up "<See ya, Valentine!>"

"<Bye...>"

Teishi then hops away, looking for something to do, leaving Valentine standing in place, confused.

"<... What just happened to me...?>" she said to herself, looking quite perplexed.
