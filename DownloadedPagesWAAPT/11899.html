Yakshan Dungeons - Around 600 years ago

-A deposed Pugilian Warrior is pacing up and down the length of his cell, muttering to himself, as the Toxicroak by his side makes another scratch in the wall to mark the passing of yet another day.-

Brandon: Mark my words, one day I shall have my revenge... upon Lucanus, upon Ayame, upon Fukurou, upon all those who saw fit to wrong me... one day...

Yakshan Dungeons - Around 580 years ago

-The scratches now completely cover one of cell walls and half of another.-

Brandon: One day... one day...

Yakshan Dungeons - Around 570 years ago

-One day, the prisoner fails to rise in the morning. The guards enter his cell in a futile attempt to rouse him, turning their backs to his Toxicroak for just a second. Unfortunately for them, a second is all the Toxicroak needs.-

A ship - Around 570 years ago

-A Yakshan trading ship sets off on a course for distant lands, unaware of a stowaway hiding in the cargo hold.-

Sumac: <Boy, these guys sure do pack a lot of luggage when they're going on holiday, don't they? I wonder where we're going anyway. Are we going to a dessert? I like desserts! They're tasty! Are we there yet?>

Orre - Around 570 years ago

-The ship eventually arrives at its destination, and the Toxicroak scuttles off before he can be noticed. He soon finds himself a mate, and eventually a small community of Croagunk is thriving in the marshier areas of the region.-

Pyrite Town - Around 10 years ago

-A rather thuggish-looking teenage boy is stood in a rather squalid-looking building, surrounded by empty cages and talking to a thickset man.-

Man: You want some Pokemon, huh? Well, you're shit out of luck. We've sold most of 'em, and aren't due another delivery till next week. There's only three left.

-He gestures to three caged Croagunk, to whom the boy gives a leery grin.-

Man: You want 'em?

Deimos: ...Yeah. Yeah, they'll do.

Pugilis - Around 600 years ago

-A spymaster is making her way back to her old homeland, a Machoke and a Banette following by her side.-

Ayame: What did Brandon even do to you guys, anyway? I couldn't find you for weeks!

Thor: <Grunt.>

Libitina: <Ugh, I don't want to talk about it... I don't know how his Toxicroak of his managed to tie up a ghost, but I think it was because he was too stupid to realise that he couldn't.>

Ayame: Ah well, at least you're safe now. And hey, we're coming home again! Man, it's been ages since I was last back here. Hopefully things have been going okay...

-She approaches the border, only to find the checkpoints abandoned, devoid of either Spectran or Pugilian troops.-

Ayame: Hmm... it looks like the mean purple people are gone, but what happened to everyone else?

Libitina: <I guess they got the hell out of dodge whilst they had the chance.>

-As she continues walking through the kingdom, she finds most of it in a state of abandonment and disrepair. Only a few people are out in the streets, and none of them make any move to acknowledge her. Rather disconcerted, she heads up to the castle and pushes open the doors.-

Ayame: Hello? Hellooooooo? Anybody home?

-After a moment, a pair of fairly if not perfectly generic Pugilian Warriors step forward to greet her.-

Warrior 1: Wait, is that... Ayame! Finally, it's good to see a friendly face around here for once.

Ayame: Uh, do I know you?

Warrior 1: Probably not. But I saw you around the castle once or twice before things started going downhill. How have things been going on your end? Hopefully you've had better luck than we have.

Ayame: Well, kinda. After we got invaded by those Spectran meanieheads I went to Illusio and then to Yaksha and did some spying for Aiura and Fukurou and I talked to them a bunch and I met this nice guy called Sabaku but I also ran into Lucanus and he was really mean then I helped fight this crazy ice guy before getting attacked by Brandon but he got arrested so things turned out okay!

-The first Warrior blinks.-

Ayame: Anyway, where is everyone?

Warrior A: Gone, mostly. The Spectrans cleared out a few days after the Ghost Lord was defeated. Guess they figured they'd cut their losses before an angry mob drove them out of town. Once they'd left, a lot of the kingdom's inhabitants followed suit. Went to old allies like Illusio, Terrera or Yaksha. Not much point in sticking around here, considering the state of things.

Ayame: What is the state of things, though?

Warrior 1: We've got no food, no infrastructure, and maybe a dozen Warriors left to keep the peace. No Warlord either, because no-one wants to take the job. We're lucky that none of our neighbours are particularly belligerent, because at the moment we're sitting Duckletts. Not that there's anything of value worth taking around here, though.

Ayame: Hm...

-Ayame thinks for a moment, before apparently coming to some sort of conclusion.-

Ayame: Well then, let's fix all that!

Warrior A: Huh?

Ayame: You heard me! Let's stop sitting around here feeling sorry for ourselves and start cleaning things up instead! It's only a matter of time before the next lot of meanies come along and start trying to boss us around again. But we're not going to let them, because we're Pugilians and Pugilians are fighters! So come on, let's get to work!

-She marches off into the streets, the two Warriors glancing at each other as they watch her go.-

Warrior 1: ...Well, she's better than nothing, I suppose.

-They follow behind her.-

Pugilis - Around 599 years ago

-Ayame is lounging around on her throne in the castle, having switched her regular black ninja outfit for a more regal looking red one. Libitina is still floating by her side, as is Thor, who has now evolved into a Machamp.-

Ayame: Hey, Libi, do you know what day it is?

Libitina: <Wednesday. And don't call me that.>

Ayame: Nope! Well, it is Wednesday, but it's also the one year anniversary of me becoming a Warlord!

Libitina: <...Good for you. Do you want us to throw a party or something?>

Ayame: Aw, come on, you've gotta be just a little excited! I mean, look at all the cool stuff we've done since we took power! We've fixed Pugilis! People are well-fed and working and happy again!

Libitina: <Only because the kingdom's now a Yakshan vassal state. Without their aid and supplies we wouldn't have lasted a month, and you can't so much as blow your nose without asking my son for permission first. Not that you're complaining, I suppose. Technically, you're not even a Warlord at all, just a Junior Warlord.>

Ayame: Ugh, you're no fun.

-She pulls a map out of her robes and starts looking over it.-

Ayame: Should we go invade somewhere to celebrate? Fukurou already took care of Cragspur and Viperia, but we could go teach Spectra a lesson! Or Nixtorm, they used to be mean as well!

-Libitina sighs.-

Pugilis - Around 560 years ago

-Eventually, Ayame's reign comes to an end as old age and illness succeed where Brandon failed. She is succeeded by her eldest son, and the Arakaki dynasty continues to rule Pugilis - under the watchful eye of the Yakshan Empire, of course. Thor continues to accompany the family, whilst Libitina floats off in search of greener pastures.-

Sinnoh - Around 150 years ago

-A ship from what is now the united region of Ransei arrives on Sinn shores, carrying a cargo of passengers with it. One of them - a young man would identify as a Pugilian if such labels still existed - looks over the coasts of his new home, and smiles.-

Hearthome City - Around 25 years ago

-A couple are stood hand-in-hand at the front of aisle in the Foreign Building, smiling, a vicar stood between them and friends and relatives watching the ceremony from the pews.-

Vicar: Do you, Miss Arakaki, take this man to be your lawfully wedded husband?

Miss Arakaki: I do.

Vicar: And do you, Mr Morgan, take this woman to be your lawfully wedded wife?

Mr Morgan: I do.

-The vicar smiles.-

Vicar: I now pronounce you man and wife. You may kiss the bride.

-The couple embrace as the friends and relatives watch in quiet celebration.-

Sunyshore City - Around 15 years ago

-A young girl with long blonde hair who bears little resemblance to her distant ancestor is leaving the city, her father holding her hand. When they reach the entrance to Route 222, he lets go and gives her a small smile.-

Mr Morgan: Well, this is it. Time to begin your Pokemon journey. Have you decided what starter you want yet?

Girl: Not really. I think I'll choose a Piplup.

Mr Morgan: Alright. Just remember to take good care of it. Now then... good luck. I won't keep you waiting.
