Maekrite: <...So?>

Silent: "Faith hung up on me, Luke is in Johto, still don't know anything of Rex but at least Pierce seems to be registered in Johto."

Maekrite -drops ears: <Ah good... I have to admit I was getting worried...>

Teala -walks past: <..."Pierce"... is that the mon he is tutoring?>

Silent: "Hang on, I am going to call Casval..."

PEFE HQ

Silent has been trying to find Casval's number, without much success.

"Hmmm... perhaps calling this kid Wally..." — checks Pokégear — "I am sure I had this number..."

Far from there, Tracer and team have arrived to an office to deliver the bags with documents. Ichtacya and Dintel appear too, carrying Silent's bags.

Audrey -sets her bag on the ground: <I can't believe we can not do much more than this...>

Dintel -effortlessly carries his bag: <I find this fun! But is so slow...>

Teala stands behind, grumbling, stares at Dintel more or less surprised.

Ichtacya -to anyone who listens: <...Why can't we Bite those legal guys and scare them away, again?>

Audrey: <To solve this strange matter, Tracer and Silent must fight in the same terms those laywers do.> — shrugs — <It is fortunate that we get to pick our weapons, even if it means giving them the grant of picking the battlefield.>

Ichtacya -finishes dragging her bag and casts it away, shows surprise: <So it is like a Pokémon battle, but with humans?>

Zoltan -tosses a bag as well: <How would that even work? Can humans shoot fireballs?>

Lardna: <Some do... there are even humans who can use the same abilities a Lucario can.>

Zoltan "whoa"s in amazement and looks at Audrey, who nods.

Teala: <Well we could go in then.... ugh...>

The Pokémon continue chatting for a while as they observe some human operators carrying, measuring and tagging the packages. Then as they walk away from the room, Audrey notices something.

Audrey -leans to Teala: <...Uhm... did I say something bad to you?>

Teala -blinks: <N-no... it is not that... it's, uhm... today I've felt empty...>

Audrey: <As have I. Look, this is not exactly an arena where we can go in.>

Teala: <I-I know, but...> — lowers her head, trying to avoid Audrey's gaze — <...Why couldn't I have helped with it? I... I am supposed to try finding things to protect Tracer from...>

Audrey -shrugs: <Well, this is not it. For any of us, except perhaps the bookworm psychic ones. But look at it this way...> — pats Teala carefully — <If we don't assist Tracer and Silent with this, they'll fall sick from it.>

Teala stares back for a moment, then Audrey makes coughing and tiredness gestures.

Teala: <I... guess you are right...> — clenches fist — <But I still feel it is not enough...>

Audrey raises an eyebrow, noticing something new in Teala's expression.

Audrey: «Well she is not questioning herself this time...» — to Teala — <Well it is a start. From all of us. See, even Dintel helped...>

The two Pokémon look ahead at the ever-cheerful Sandshrew for a moment; the Pokémon pretty much seems to not have noticed they all have just been through a heck of a day and is having a talk with Lardna about something with Cubones and trials; the Emboar does not seem to be liking the conversation though, and insistently rubs her beard.

Teala: <Well someone is having fun.>

Audrey: <And for some of us it is learning from a new experience as well.> — she points at Ichtacya, who is trying to strike conversation with another mon offscreen.

Audrey: <I'm sure we all feel pressured by this, and we all feel insufficient...> — leans closer — <even him.>

Teala -rears up, blinks: <H-... uhm...?> — leans in again — <W...who do you... did you read...?>

Audrey -shakes her head: <I won't delve deeper... but I know you have interpreted his concerns right. Probably he suffers more than us in this matter...>

Audrey sees Teala blush for a moment (somehow), then the Lucario returns her attention to the front of the group who are already meeting with Algira, Helios, Tracer and Silent. Audrey and Teala are quick to notice that Maekrite is at the corner talking to Tagg's Staraptor, Roc.

Audrey: <Oh, that's right... Silent is raising his chick, right?>

Ichtacya -comes in: <Well, yes, but now she is in charge of Copahue and Harriet...>

Audrey: <Hmmm... something in me wants to scream "just as planned" for some reason...>
