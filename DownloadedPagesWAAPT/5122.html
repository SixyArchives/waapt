Goldenrod City, that morning

Copahue the Fearow♂: <...zZz... ...zZz... ...zZz...>

Harriet the Honchkrow♀: <...zZz... ...zZz... ...zZz...>

The sun has risen to greet the two lovebirds who are sleeping inside the birdhouse atop the PEFE storage building. They continue sleeping cuddled against each other, perched on a large beam.


However, their calm is about to be broken as usual...

Copahue: <...zZz... ...zZz... ...zZz... ...zzOUCH OUCH OUCH OUCH uh?>

The Fearow raises his talon instinctively, already used to having it pecked by their adopted daughter to the point of bleeding pretty much every day.

However, to his surprise, there's no one down there. No little Starly looking up at him and calling for food on the beam.

Copahue: <Oh thanks Arceus...> — breathes alleviated at the thought of finally not having to wake up with bruised talons.



Only then it dawns upon him.

Copahue -blnks: <...! Harriet!> — pecks the Honchkrow — <Wake up! Harriet!>

Harriet: <...zZz.... today is your turn to go for food... zZz...>

Copahue: <Shahinne. is. not. here!> — holds the other bird by her wings.

That is enough to get the Honchkrow freshly up, eyes open wide.

Harriet: <...What? But- where? She was sleeping here in the night!>

Copahue: <...It seems she's not here!>

The two birds start looking around their birdhouse incessantly, moving some straw and food around, as well as almost turning the entire place upside down. However, except for a couple of shed feathers from an attempt of teaching her Feather Dance to no avail, there's no trace of the little bird.

Harriet: <Oh no no no... we have to go look for her!> — kicks Copahue — <Come on! Move!>

Copahue: <...I'm on it!>

Both birds fly out of their den and alternate between perching on top of the structure to look around and flying in circles above the nearby buildings, letting out calling trills. However, there is no answer...
