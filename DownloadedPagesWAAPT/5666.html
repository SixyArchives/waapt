Ashley and Cole land soon after Hunter, and follows him through the crowded streets of Jubilife to an apartment building. Leaving Cole (much to his displeasure) at the door to wait for Domino and the Noctowl, Ashley follows Hunter and Cesar inside. They troop up a few flights of stairs, then stop outside a door on the third floor. Hunter lets out his team, then knocks on the door. He grins at the peephole a second, then there is the sound of several locks being undone.

A moment later, the door opens a crack. Hunter leans over to open it more when a flash of blue hurtles out and knocks him over. Ashley, letting out an involuntary "Ah!", takes a step forward to help, but is stopped by Cesar, who opens his wing and shakes his head. It quickly becomes clear, however, that the Golduck on top of Hunter is not attacking him, and the boy is laughing.

"Hey, Trix! How've you been? C'mon, I gotta get up!" He says, scratching the Pokemon where her head meets her neck. The Golduck gets off of him and Hunter gets to his feet, just as a Kricketune and a girl at least a foot taller—and likely several years older—than him comes to the door.

"Hunter. Nice to see you've finally decided to pick them up, " the girl says sternly, but she's smiling. Hunter laughs and fixes his hat.

"Sorry, Joana, I was a little busy. How were they?" He moves over to the door and pats the Kricketune, who chirps happily, then moves to the side so the two humans can talk.

"Well-behaved, as always. Lauren's a great help around the house." The girl smiles at the Kricketune, who chirps again, then walks over to stand beside Cesar, who gives her a nod.

"Anyway, Hunter, who's your friend?" The girl turns to Ashley.

"I've been traveling with her for a few days, " Hunter says. "Ashley, this is Joana. She's an old friend of mine."

Ashley smiles and offers a hand, which Joana shakes warmly. "Nice to meet you. I'm Ashley."

"Right back at you. You've been putting up with this pest, eh? Not an easy feat. Good luck to you."

"Hey! If you're gonna insult me to my face, I'm leaving." Hunter puts on an over-exaggerated pout, which causes Joana to playfully swing at him, which he dodges.

"Fine, then, scat, you little mongrel. Mr. Brown'll have my hide if your Pokemon mess up the hall, anyway." Joana gives Cesar a stern look, who gives her an offended one.

"Oh, all right. Bye, Joana. Thanks so much for taking care of them!" Hunter gives her a hug, which she warmly reciprocates.

"G'bye, Hunter. I'll see you around. Call your mom, she's worried about you."

Hunter makes a face, then steps away.

"C'mon, Ash, let's get going. Mr. Brown's a real jerk to people that don't live here." He starts walking off with his Pokemon, leaving Ashley and Joana alone in the hallway.

"All ready nicknaming you? He's taken quite a shine to you, Ashley, " Joana comments, brushing her hands off on her jeans. "Now, he's not that great at getting along with people, but you stick by him. That kid means a lot to me, and he likes you. Who knows, maybe you'll learn to love him." She shrugs. "Oh, and he wasn't kidding. The super around here won't take kindly to you loitering in here. You'd better get going."

"All right. It was nice meeting you, Joana, " Ashley says, backing away from the door.

"Likewise. G'bye." Joana gives her a friendly wave, then steps back inside and closes the door.

Ashley starts down the hallway, hurrying down the stairs to catch up with Hunter.
