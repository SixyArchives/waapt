Route 32

Although it was cold, that did little to deter the Mareep flocks of Route 32 from munching on a meal of fresh grass, a meal a certain Ampharos was taking part in.

Not so much her boyfriend, though, who was just content to watch.

Unite: <So, Woolly...just what ended up happening after you left your brother that night?>

The vaguely draconic sheep Pokémon pulled up some cud to chew on as she began to relay the story to the Braviary.

Woolly: <Well I went off to find this observatory after this nice Ampharos that drove the Magnet Train had taken us somewhere with fresh grass. At first I was scared because I was by myself and Yarn hadn't wanted to come with me...>

She raised a flipper.

<BUT! Then I got to this house where this Scientist and his pair of Zubats - who were really cute by the way - and they gave me something to eat and let me look at their equipment, and guess what? I found a transmission from actual Space Ampharos!>

Unite: <...What.>

Woolly: <Ah, but what happened next is even better, because then...>

A few minutes later...

<...And then they dropped me off back here, right next to Yarn! You should have seen the look on his face, it was priceless...>

Unite didn't need to see it, for he was wearing the same expression of utter disbelief. Or at least...as close to a bird could get to replicating the expression of a mammal.

Unite: <...> O_O

Woolly: <...Are you okay, Unite? Because Yarn was doing this too...>

She frowns, a little perturbed.

<It's creepy how similarly you're acting here...>

The Braviary tried to raise a wing, struggling to find the words.

Unite: <...>

His volcabulary failing him, he simply gave up, shaking his head as he walked way to try and clear his head, leaving a thoroughly confused Woolly.

Woolly: <...Jeez. You'd think after all the Legendaries and alternative universes we'd seen, aliens wouldn't be much of a big deal.>

With a shrug, she lowered her head to eat another mouthful of grass.
