Castelia

As Duke was walking along the streets of Castleia in the middle of the rain, he happened to find Lyuri in the entrance of a building, probably to shelter herself from the rain as well, he decided to go talk to her.

"Hey, Lyuri, locked in there by the rain?" asked Duke, with a smile.

"Yeah, didn't know you were a fan of Zack though." said Lyuri, looking at the Floatzel umbrella and giving Duke a playful look.

"It's-"

"Sakura's, I know."

"So, are you going to stay here?"

"I kinda want to go back to the Pokémon Center, but all this rain is really taking my motivation away." Lyuri then sighed.

"Well, we can share this umbrella, I'm also heading there." Duke suggested.

Lyuri then thought for a bit and nodded, they then started to head to the Pokémon Center together, as they walker, however, some of the rain drops were still falling on Lyuri and annoying her quite a bit.

"This umbrella is too small!" she complained, looking quite annoyed.

"... You know, I'm considering taking the umbrella away so you can cool your head a bit..." said Duke, with a playful smile as he instead got closer to Lyuri so that they both would fit under the umbrella. "... Then again, I don't want you to catch a cold or something."

Lyuri lets out a chuckle as she was better shielded by the umbrella, though being close to Duke like that made her uncomfortable for some reason, they didn't talk much to each other as they headed to the Pokémon Center and Lyuri was feeling pretty tired already, the raindrops hitting the ground just making her feel more sleepy, to the point that she decided to rest her head on Duke's shoulder for a bit.

"So... How is Riku doing?" Duke asked, trying to keep the girl awake and being rather amused by what she was doing.

Lyuri quickly removed her head from Duke's shower and shook her head slightly before giving a small yawn. "He's fine, what happened yesterday made him a bit afraid of the desert, but he's doing fine, he just needs to relax a bit." she said, feeling somewhat embarrassed.

"That's good, I have to talk to him later." said Duke, in response "I think you also need some rest, it's clear that they aren't here, yet you went searching for them anyway?"

"Yeah, who knows? They might show up..." said Lyuri before holding to one of Duke's arms and staying close to him "At least Riku's back, it was bad enough to have him get lost."

"Don't worry, we'll find the others soon, they're my responsability as much as they're yours you know?" said Duke.

"Yeah, I know that, at least you're still around..."

Duke just smiles as he leads the way to the Pokémon Center, though by taking a quick look at Lyuri he saw that she seemed a bit conflicted, he didn't know what to think of it, but it was probably better to not ask about it, instead, he just kept guiding Lyuri around the city.
