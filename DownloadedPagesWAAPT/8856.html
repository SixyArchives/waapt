Oak's Lab

"Wait... What happened then?"

<Iggy: "... Well...">

Thanks to Tagg for helping to make this next part.

12 Years Ago

-Leading a younger Tagg to the starter desk-

Prof. Oak: "So today you'll be beginning your first steps as a trainer, but first you'll need to get your starter."

-And releases each of the three Pokemon sitting inside their Pokeballs-

-Gestures at the first-

Prof. Oak: "The Grass/Poison type Bulbasaur."

-Gestures at the second-

Prof. Oak: "The Fire type Charmander."

-Gestures at the last-

Prof. Oak: "And the Water type Squirtle."

-Thinking, looking at the starters-

Young!Tagg: "Why do I have to go through the intro? I already know what they are."

-Staring at the comparatively massive form of Young!Tagg and shrinking back nervously-

<???!Charmander: "M-Meep! Pl-Please don't eat me! I don't t-taste good!">

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Eh? Relax! This human's here to pick one of us to travel with him! What good's a partner if he's in your tummy?"> :P

-Annoyed-

<Bulbasaur: "So quit being such a baby already.">

<???!Charmander: "I-I'm not a baby! Y-You two are j-just insane!">

<Bulbasaur: "Sure...">

Young!Tagg: "That Charmander looks kinda scaredy to me."

-Breathing a sigh of relief-

<???!Charmander: "Oh... Oh thank goodness, he d-doesn't want me...">

-Waving-

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Heh! If you're lookin' for a level head, you can't go wrong here!">

-Waving his vines-

<Bulbasaur: "Hey, pick me! Two types are better than one!">

-Looking between the two mons-

Young!Tagg: "This is kinda hard..."

-Getting closer to Tagg and starts looking directly into his eyes-

<Bulbasaur: "Pick me! Pick me!">

-Looks at Bulbasaur for a while before breaking eye contact-

"I think I'll choose...Bulbasaur."

-In a bit of a disappointed tone-

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Aww... But still, good luck out there!">

<???!Charmander: "W-Watch your back, you d-don't know wh-what he'll do to you!">

-Getting picked up, hearing the Charmander-

<Bulbasaur: "I'll... Keep that in mind.">

-Thinking-

<Bulbasaur: "He'll probably get a trainer as scaredy as he is.">

-To Past!Iggy-

<Bulbasaur: "Yeah, thanks.">

Back in the Present

"But... How does that add-?"

<Iggy: "Don't you remember the day that you picked me, Tracer?">

"... Hmm?"

Not too Long After the Earlier Past Scene

-A certain trainer is reviewing his choices of starters-

Prof. Oak: "-The Fire type Charmander... And the Water type Squirtle.

"... Eh? Only two choices? I thought that you also gave out Bulbasaur, though..."

Professor Oak: "Ah... I make it a point to give out starters in groups of three... As it would happen, someone already chose the Bulbasaur of this group not too long ago."

"Hrm... Well... At least it narrows things down somewhat..."

-Hyperventilating-

<???!Charmander: "Oh no, oh no...">

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Relax! Haven't you ever wanted to see what was outside of that lab?">

-In a whining tone-

<???!Charmander: "B-But th-there's m-monsters out th-there!">

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "So? Just duck into your shell when they come around!">

-Exasperated-

<???!Charmander: "I d-don't have a sh-shell!">

"Hey there, little guy."

-And flinches after the hand of a young trainer rests on its head-

<???!Charmander: "Eep!">

"You're a cute little bugger, you know that?"

Professor Oak: "Erm... You might not want to-"

-Flailing-

<???!Charmander: "Lemmegolemmegoleemego!">

-And scratches at the trainer's arm-

"OW! GEEZ! "

-And hastily flees behind a table leg-

Professor Oak: "You should be more aware of your Pokemon's emotional state... If they're nervous, you should try and coax them to you rather than approach them directly like that..."

"... I thought that this was going to be more straightfor- Huh?"

-And notices a Squirtle drawing near looking at the scratches-

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Are you alright? I mean, he's not a bad Pokemon... He's just a little more paranoid than he really ought to be...">

"Oh... Uh... Hi? Uh... Wait, is this one nervous too?"

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Nervous? Ha! I've been waiting for this!">

-And spins around in shell a bit before popping back out-

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Really, I'm probably not the 'cool' mon or all... But if you pick me, I'll look out after you...

... Uh... Or at least I'll try to..."> ^_^;

-From behind table leg-

<???!Chramander: "I-Is he gone yet?">

"I think I'll take the Squirtle..."

-And picks him up-

<The Squirtle that Would Become Known as Iggy: "Oop! Whoa!">

"Heh... I dunno why, but he seems like a real trooper."

Back to the Present Day

"That's... Surprisingly plausible..." ._.

<Iggy: "... I always knew something felt similar about Tagg... I just never could place it before... Wonder whatever happened to that Charmander, though...">
