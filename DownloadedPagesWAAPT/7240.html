Lake: <How are you feeling?>

Nadia: <...Hmm.>

To be honest? Nadia couldn't help but feel a little nervous. It wasn't that she didn't have the confidence in herself to win her upcoming match, but she didn't feel confident enough to see it as a sure-fire victory. That, and there was the nerves of having reached the final to contend with as well.

<To be honest? I'm a little bit nervous. Just a little.>

Lake: <Huh, really?>

Lake gives her a reassuring smile.

<I don't see what you've got to be worried about. You've made it this far, haven't you?>

She nods.

Nadia: <I know, but I could still mess up.>

Lake: <You're not gonna' mess up. In fact, I'm confident that you are going to win this. Do you wanna' know why?>

Nadia: <Go on...>

Nadia: <And remembering that is supposed to make me feel better how?>

She had to admit, there was a wisdom to Lake's words.

Nadia: <Well...>
