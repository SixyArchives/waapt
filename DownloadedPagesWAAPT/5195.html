Kanto, Route 3

In the grass at the base of Mt. Moon, two Nidorinos emerge from a warren hole. They scout the area, looking around for any elil, in particular humans. These Nidorino seem to be a tad larger than most Nidorino, with huge gaping scars on their flank to tell them apart. This indicates that these are sentry Nidorinos for this colony.

After some time of Scouting, the Nidorinos head back to the warren hole they came from before emrging once again. This time, with a group of Nidorans, Nidorinas, smaller Nidorinos and two more sentry Nidorinos. The sentries form a ring arounf the Nidos who have come up for their mid-day silflay, for here, silflay times are decided by the Owsla and not by the Nidos themselves.

A young Nidorina sees fresher grass just outside the ring that the sentries have set up for silflay. As she attempts to go over and eat it, a sentry Nidorino snarls at her and bats her back into the ring.

Nidorino Sentry: <BACK INTO THE RING! NO NIDO IS TO PASS THE AREAS ALLOCATED TO US BY THE OSWLAFA FOR SILFLAY!>

Injured by the batting, the Nidorina hobbles back into the silflay ring, mutturing something about "zorn".

Nidorino Sentry: <Tharn doe. She should learn her place in the colony.>

After a half an hour of silflay, the Nidorino Sentries shoo the other Nidos back into the hole. While the flank sentries take up the position and continue to scout the area, the front Nidos go down into the warren, pass the bleeding "example" in the main hall, shoo the others into their dens and report back to their Cheif Nido.

If one was to look at this Warren from a distance, this odd Nido behaviour was happening all over the place, alibet at different times. The massive Sentry Nidos scouting all over the place, asking any intruders what they were doing on "Efrafan" territory, that if they pass again, that they will be forced to see their fearsome Cheif, who would ask them many questions.

Yet back in the warren, a very... unusual (well at least for this warren) thing is taking place. A large Nidorina has emergerd from her den and is walking down the warren, to the Cheif Nido's den. Upon reaching the entrance to the den, she is greeted by two high officials of the "Owslafa".

Vervain: <Doe, what do you think you are doing here?>

Thethuthinnang: <I wish to speak to General Woundwort. Is that not a decent and civil request?>

The two Owslafa officials scoff at Thethuthinnang, who seems unfazed by their taunting.

Chervil: <Very well doe, but the Cheif doesn't like those who disturb him with such trivial matters that your ilk tend to bother him with>

The two move aside, as Thethuthinnang makes her way down to General Woundwort's den.
