441323 ChaoticMarin23rd Jan 2015 05:50:35 PM from Nintendoland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: 700 wives and 300 concubines
Chiptune DJ

Dewford Gym - Some time ago

Two large pokemon stood across from eachother, arms bared aggressively as they each attempted to intimidate the other into making a mistake. Hariyama was the first to move, waddling in close and faking Dragonite out with a powerful clap of it's massive hands. With a quick followup order, Hariyama then proceeded to grasp Dragonite's belly and begin, with quite a bit of effort, lifting the titanic pokemon off her feet.

Valorie turned her head slightly at the scene, a tad disconcerted by the act. "...Dragonite, fly up!" Valorie ordered. Hariyama couldn't throw Dragonite if she was in the air. The titanic pokemon flailed a bit, her wings stretching and discharging several powerful gusts of winds into the ground as she attempted to lift Hariyama. Before she could generate enough lift however Hariyama flipped her weight over and body slammed Dragonite into the ground with a crash that rocked the very foundation of the gym.

Valorie winced, quickly backpedaling. Plan B, plan B... Dragonite forced herself back to her feet, now sufficiently irritable and slightly worse for wear. "Dragon Rush Dragonite, make it happen!" Valorie ordered.

"You can take that hit, Hariyama. Grapple and retaliate with ice punch!" Brawly replied. Valorie's brow furrowed as she watched Dragonite crash into Hariyama, causing the two of them two of them to slide half a dozen meters across the battlefield. As they stopped one of Hariyama's hands pulled back and an icy energy enveloped it. The arm thrust pokemon promptly struck Dragonite hard whilst grappling her with his free hand, preventing Dragonite from moving out of striking range or evading.

Judging by the way Dragonite winced Valorie knew this wasn't a good position for her to be in. Perhaps a sufficiently violent jolt would pave the way to freedom?

<L-Let go!> Dragonite protested snappily, about as angry as her gentle demeanor allowed.

"Dragonite, use Dragon Rush again!" Valorie called out, quietly mumbling something to herself as she tunnel visioned in on the battle. Dragonite violently threw her weight into Hariyama at an angle, trying to rip herself free. It was no use however, as Hariyama's giant hands proved perfectly capable of keeping a grip upon her. Her efforts were punished with another open palmed icy strike in response. Tears came to Dragonite's eyes and she shuddered in anticipation of having to endure another numbing attack like that.

Frustrated, Valorie threw balled both her hands and called out to grab Dragonite's attention. "Don't give up now Dragonite. I know you're strong enough to break that hold! Use Superpower!" the shortcake demanded, turning the intensity up a notch or two." Dragonite's gaze snapped to Valorie's and she reached deep inside of herself, drawing out a hidden auric power to enhance her natural strength. She hunkered down and grappled with Hariyama in turn, attempting to wrench the other off of her.

"Don't let her move an inch Hariyama. Superpower!" Brawly replied. Aura radiated from both the pokemon as they attempted to overpower the other, a growling noise escaping their throats as they struggled, shoved and grappled eachother. Valorie watched with tension for a moment before a startling realization occured to her. Dragonite was having trouble making any headway! She quickly stole a glance at Brawly. Observing the tension on his features, she quickly concluded Hariyama wasn't holding back. They were at a tie.

Valorie frowned. How was such a... relatively small pokemon outputting so much power? At that moment an apiphany struck her, her eyes widening with realization. That was right! Dragonite needed to use her size to their advantage. As the two titanic pokemon continued to struggle with earth shattering power behind their arms Valorie's smirk grew confident. It attracted Brawly's attention, but he was unable to make sense of it before the command was given.

"Dragonite, lift!" the shortcake encouraged. "Like Hariyama did to you before!" she explained, watching eagerly to see the results of her efforts.

Dragonite shifted her weight back, throwing Hariyama off-balance just long enough for her to take initiative and lift the other off his feet. With a cry of defiance, Dragonite shifted Hariyama over her and violently smashed Hariyama into the ground with terrifying force. Such force was it that at the last moment Valorie realized the sudden need to duck and promptly raised her arms to shield her face and neck as the impact shattered the floor, sending debris flying in seemingly random directions. Brawly too was forced to draw back slightly, wincing a bit at the collateral damage.

As the dust and debris cleared, leaving Valorie only with mild scratches upon her forearms thankfully, Dragonite was revealed standing upon of collapsed Hariyama breathing heavily. After a moment the wounded and tired titanic pokemon looked over her shoulder at Valorie, seeking approval.

<Did I do good, Mom..?> the giant pokemon asked, out of breath. Valorie, unable to understand the question, merely answered the other's gaze with an approving nod and a smile. It wasn't ideal, perhaps, but Dragonite had pulled through and that's what mattered.

Hariyama groaned from his resting place.

Meanwhile Brawly quickly took inventory of the situation. As he finished his gaze turned back up at the victor and her loyal pokemon. He beamed. "Well! I've fought dragonite before but I've never seen something like that~" he remarked good naturedly, running a hand through his hair. "In the past I've used Hariyama to force Dragonite to rely less on their brute strength and see how trainers roll with the punches, but this was something else." he went on, chuckling a bit before moving to select a new pokemon. "I'm confident that if you wield that strength intelligently it'll take you to great places, which is why I have a favor to ask." Brawly finished, eliciting a bit of confusion from Valorie.

"What's that..?" the shortcake asked, puzzled by the detour in the middle of a gym challenge.

"I'd like to see one of your other pokemon battle. One last round, one on one, just to see if you're as versatile as you are skilled." Brawly requested, shifting to toss his new pokeball. From it emerged a Pangoro, who just sort of looked up at Dragonite and did a double take at Valorie, debating their sizes.
