Lake: <Oh?>

Saunahold!Dragonair: <Dragon Pokémon from all over the dome come here to relax in our spa. There's nothing like a warm bath to sooth your body and soul after a long day of work.>

<Hmm. You two look a little worn out. Would you like to take a dip?>

Lake frowns slightly, looking a little awkward.

Lake: <It's a nice offer, but I'm not a Dragon...>

The Dragonair simply chuckles in response.

Saunahold!Dragonair: <You're close enough! If anymon asks, just tell 'em you're a River Dragon!>

Lake: <Oh...>

<Okay, will do!>

Stratus: <Thanks a lot!>

<Oooooh...This is nice...>

Lake: <I know!>

The Feraligatr sighs with content, sitting back against the edge of the pool.

I've only just gotten in, and I feel as if all my worries are washing away...>
