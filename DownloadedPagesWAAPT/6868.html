Sauna Hold - PMD-B

Looking for a quiet place to talk in private, Lake and Stratus come across what appear to be some shallow dips in the PEFE dome, perhaps caused by a failed breeching attempt long ago, that appeared to be filled with steaming water.

Naturally, the two find this quite a curious sight.

Stratus: <What's all this?>

???: <Why, this is where Sauna Hold takes it's name...>

The two glance over to see that they've attracted the attention of a nearby Dragonair.

Lake: <Oh?>

The Dragon Pokémon nods, pointing with his tail at the steamy pools.

Saunahold!Dragonair: <Dragon Pokémon from all over the dome come here to relax in our spa. There's nothing like a warm bath to sooth your body and soul after a long day of work.>

He tilts his head inquisitively at the two.

<Hmm. You two look a little worn out. Would you like to take a dip?>

Lake frowns slightly, looking a little awkward.

Lake: <It's a nice offer, but I'm not a Dragon...>

The Dragonair simply chuckles in response.

Saunahold!Dragonair: <You're close enough! If anymon asks, just tell 'em you're a River Dragon!>

Lake: <Oh...>

The Feraligatr smiles, giving the Dragonair a nod.

<Okay, will do!>

Stratus: <Thanks a lot!>

After picking a quiet pool, they climb in, the warm water instantly beginning to relax the two Pokémon.

<Oooooh...This is nice...>

Lake: <I know!>

The Feraligatr sighs with content, sitting back against the edge of the pool.

I've only just gotten in, and I feel as if all my worries are washing away...>

After a few minutes, which included Nadia coming to relax in the waters with him (which he was obviously very happy about), Lake decides to pick up the lingering conversation with Stratus about the so called "jerk dragons."

Lake: <So now that we're a bit more relaxed, can't you see why it's a silly idea to seek retribution against these dragons? I know that their ancestors wrecked the place, which you're more than a little annoyed about.

But as us Feraligatr would put it "Don't eat the hatchlings of a 'Gator that steals your territory.">

Unsure at this proverb's meaning, Stratus gives Lake a confused frown.

Stratus: <What does that mean?>

Lake: <It means that you shouldn't punish peoplemons by proxy. Especially for events that happened several hundred years ago. Granted, it's not a usual situation for most short lived Pokémon as ourselves.>

He gives a small shrug.

<But then, there's nothing "usual" about any of this, really.>

Stratus nods, staring down at the water.

Stratus: <I get your point, it's just...those Dragons...>

He let's out a sigh, leaning back in the water.

<They've done so many terrible things here, and just making about it makes me so angry.>

He scowls, clenching one of his claws tightly.

<I wanted to take it out on them somehow, and when I found out that they had let them build a town of all things on the facility they had a claw in wrecking...gah, it really annoyed me...>

Un-clenching his fist, Stratus sits back up, the antennae on his head drooping sadly.

<But you're right. I really shouldn't be taking it out on these dragons. Because then, how would I be any different from all of the Pokémon that mistrusted me and Nadia back in Kanto? I was the Totodile, but now I'm the jerk Feraligatr, to continue your analogy.

...Wait...>

He pauses, going through what Lake said again.

<...Okay, that doesn't make sense, seeing as an eaten Totodile can't really evolve...But you get my point, right?>

Lake nods as he sidles over to Nadia to cuddle with her, wanting to enjoy this relaxing time with her.

Lake: <Yeah, even if you get blamed by proxy, that doesn't make it okay to do the same thing to others. Because then it just becomes a chain of victimization and blaming.>
