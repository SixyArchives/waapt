179801 Luke92429th Jul 2012 08:11:49 AM Get RP Mod

Bluescale Swamp

A certain pair of Feraligatr have awoken early, Lake wanting to make good on his promise on teaching Luke how to use Bubble Shield.

Lake: <I've forgotten just how many times I've done this now. It feels like I've taught all the Water type Pokémon in our group this move.>

Luke nods as he pulls a bunch of Sitrus Berries out of Lake's bag, storing them in his hammerspace pocket. If he was going to be spending his HP practicing this move, he was going to want to refill them easily.

Luke: <Look at it this way: once you've taught me how to do this, I can show other peoplemons how to do it, like a Move Tutor!>

He pauses, an odd thought have occurred to him.

<...Is this how Move Tutors learn how to teach Pokémon moves? By turning into Pokémon and learning how tp preform them themselves?>

Lake: <Er...>

Lake simply blinks, a blank look crossing his face.

<That sound a bit far-fetched if you ask me. Anyway, are you ready to do this?>

Luke replies with a somewhat nervous nod.

Luke: <It really doesn't hurt that much...does it?>

Lake gives him a shrug.

Lake: <I dunno. I guess I'm just used to getting hurt since I battle a lot, so it doesn't bother me. You haven't been in many battles, so I guess you're not used to it yet.>

Luke: <Oh...>

Lake: <Let's put it this way: It'll hurt less than taking some attacks. Does that help?>

Luke: <Well...I'd rather take less pain than more pain.>

Lake: <Yeah, that's a good way of putting it. Alright, now the first thing you need to do is spar some water into your mouth and slosh it around a bit.>

Luke: <Oh, like when I rinse my mouth out when brushing my teeth?>

Lake nods an affirmative.

Lake: <Yeah, just like that!>

Luke: <Okay then...>

Luke shuts his jaws, then fills it with water from his internal water bladder, beginning to slosh it around throughly for the next few seconds.

Lake: <Okay, next...ooh, what's the best way to put this...have you ever blown a bubble with bubblegum?>

Surprisingly, Luke shakes his head.

<Oh, what? You've never been able to do that?>

The Feraligatr gives him an annoyed look, as if to say "Hey, it's hard!"

<Alright, alright! I'm just saying that it seems kinda' similar to that. Anyway, you're gonna' need a lot of air for this, so I suggest that you take a few deep breaths. In and out, in and out, you know, like those deep breathing exercises.>

Luke nods, then opens his jaws (trying not to dribble the now soapy water in his mouth everywhere), and takes a few deep lung-fulls of breath.

<Okay, obviously the hard part is next. What you've gotta do is blow as hard as you can. Like...like blowing up a balloon. You've done that, surely?>

Rather tellingly, Luke looks away awkwardly.

<Seriously? Maybe that's what we should be practicing here.>

He looks back, shooting Lake an annoyed glare.

<Eh, I guess we could just consider this a life lesson. It'll help you even when you're a human again! Anyway, enough joking around, give it a go. Take another deep breath, but this time, puff as hard as you can.>

Steeling himself, Luke does as instructed and blows as hard as he can, exhaling a rather large bubble out of his mouth.

<That's it, keep going! When you think it's large enough, hop inside of it.>

Luke nods, then adds a few more puffs of large air, making the bubble large enough to fit inside of comfortably, before spitting it off his mouth and jumping into it. That done, he takes a few moments to catch his breath, panting heavily.

Luke: <By Ho-oh...that was...rough...>

Lake: <It is exhausting, but hey! You made a pretty nice bubble there! Congrats!>

Luke: <Phew...thanks...>

Dropping to his feet, the Feraligatr lies down on the floor.

<I'm just...going to rest...for a few minutes now...Whew...>
