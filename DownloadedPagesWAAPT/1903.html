Oh. Well, a Spirit Pokemon is a species of pokemon that is considered to be tied to your individual spirit. And it's not really all that broad— it's considered to be a specific member of a specific species of pokemon of a specific gender of a specific state in evolution. The only instance of a spirit pokemon that I've found so far was my mother and June, her Camerupt.

When a person finds their spirit pokemon, it's sort of like the pokemon embodies everything the person stands for. June's like a strong mountain, a foundation on which to build something, but she can also move and change whenever necessary. June also knows exactly how my mother feels about something at any given moment, sort of like a window into her deepest thoughts.

It doesn't go both ways though. A Spirit Pokemon is kinda like a shell, meant to be a door or an object rather than an ally or a friend. So I don't know what goes through Gallade's head, but he knows exactly what goes through mine.

When I was younger I thought it was really creepy.
