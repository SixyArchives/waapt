Route 38 - Johto

After Phyco's rather...humiliating defeat yesterday, Luke and Lake had quickly taken him to the Ecruteak Pokémon Center, where head made a full recovery from his Marsh Blob inflicted injuries.

His ego, however, wouldn't heal so easily. Especially considering that his humiliation had been immortalized by the "Doodlebug", as Phyco had overheard somemon say (Although Luke and Lake insisted that her name was Rosary. not that he really cared).

Anyway, the next morning, the trio had checked out of the local Pokémon Center and headed off to the west of the city, in hopes of finding a new addition to the team, as well as hopefully leveling Phyco up a bit.

However, the Kingdra was refusing to battle at all.

Phyco — <What's the point? I'm only going to end up being humiliated. Again.>

Wild Farfetch'd (Lv.16) — <Hey, don't worry buddy! I'll go easy on ya'!>

Phyco glares at the duck Pokémon, causing the bird to jump in surprise and ruffle his feathers in shock.

<QWAH! Or perhaps not! Whatever works for you...>

The Kingdra continues glaring at the bird, then shakes his head and tursn around, floating back to his companions with a upset look on his face.

Phyco — <Sorry, but I'm just not doing it.>

"But how will you ever get stronger if you don't fight?"

The Kingdra shakes his head in response.

Phyco — <I don't want to fight anymore. Especially if it means getting beaten that badly all the time.>

Wild Farfetch'd (Lv.16) — <Er, hello?>

Lake — Frowns. <You can't give up after just two battles that didn't go so well!>

Phyco — <Why not? How do you know that it won't happen again, even if I do get better?>

Wild Farfetch'd (Lv.16) <We're still fighting, you know!>

Lake — <You can't win every battle, Phyco. Sometimes you do end up getting humiliated, but that comes with the role of being a trainer's Pokémon.>

Phyco — Snorts. <Well, if battling means sparing myself from humiliation, then I don't want to do it anymore.>

Wild Farfetch'd (Lv.16) <...Does that mean I can leave?>

Luke lets out a sigh as Lake rolls his eyes, frustrated at the Kingdra's behavior.

Lake — <So you're just going to give up, then?>

Phyco — Raises his snout. <I see no shame in that. I have much better things to do than battle, anyway.>

Lake tries to come up with a response, but fails.

Lake — <Fine. Be that way.>

Wild Farfetch'd (Lv.16) — <Do I still get exp. if he forfeits?>
