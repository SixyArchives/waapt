[AU] Inka's Place, the Morning after Inka's Rage

-The sound of several Nidoran kits waking up and starting fighting to see who comes out of the burrow first can be heard-

"Dibs on the Mint Berry!"

"Wheeeee~!"

"Paps is going to take us to see the train today!"

-Baluarkos, the so-called (adoptive) Paps, is just returning from a stroll when nine small Nidoran emerge from the burrow and go for his knees-

Baluarkos — <Oh my...>

-The Nidoran circle him and greet him reciting their dreams from last night and how they shared the berry that survived yesterday's fury. Then, as soon as Baluarkos tries to step back, they turn to cuddlepile him-

-From the burrow, a last figure can be seen emerging; bipedal and blue in shape with strange and funny whiskers. He peeks out from the burrow and examines the surroundings-

Wooper Messenger — <I did it! I survived! :3>

-The Wooper walks to the grass and sits to yawn and looks around, letting himself take on some sunlight, accounting the events of the last day-

-Inka appears from behind some bushes, dusting the ground with some leaves and feathers-

Inka — <Oh, I see you are awake...> — leans curiously — <Had a good night of sleep?>

Wooper — <Ah yes...> — blushes — <I couldn't resist when the kits asked me to stay...>

Inka — nods — <They were quite insistent yes. I... er, I hope we have not caused you any trouble.>

-The Wooper simply shifts around and looks at the kits making short work of Baluarkos-

Wooper — <It was warm and we had food. I'm not complaining... and they tell interesting stories.>

-After a short silence, Inka continues her cleaning routine while Baluarkos manages to bring the kits to order-

Baluarkos — <Now, you have to understand, Dad has to go to the human town to leave a notice.>

Random kit — <Awwww...> — leaps around — <Can we go? Pretty please?>

Other Random Kit — <We promise to behave orderly!>

Baluarkos — frowns — <Not today, kits. You are going to stay with your mother and behave until the teachers come in to take you to the grassland.>

-The nine faces turn to see Inka, who tilts her head and swings the random leaves she is carrying-

"Are you coming home early, Dad?"

"Can you bring us berries?"

-While the kits assault Baluarkos with more questions and he tries to get them off himself, Pokéteco comes flying from the north and perches on Inka's head-

Inka — <Morning there. Didn't expect you to come today.>

Pokéteco — <I just fell like checking around...> — sees the random destruction and the cut down trees — <Perhaps I missed something...>

Inka — <Not much...> — sighs — <I can explain to you later.> — turns to the kits — <Now all of you leave Dad and come here! We have to go drink water~!>

"Wheeeee~!"

-All the kits turn around and start surrounding Inka, playing around. Meanwhile, Baluarkos moves to a random place where a small red spike can be emerging from the ground...-

Baluarkos — <...About time I found an use for this!>

-...and starts unearthing what looks like the hide or carapace of a Pokemon, of a red colouration and with several ridges and spikes, the entire structure with some muscles and tendons, not to mention a couple of vertebrae, still visibly attacked to its inside-

"...Wow!"

"It's huge!"

"Dad! Dad! What is it?"

Inka — <You still keep that thing?> — gives a reprimanding look — <And couldn't you lift a dust cloud to prevent the kits from seeing it?>

-assorted blinks-

Baluarkos — <They were already trying to unearth it yesterday, so I guess they already suspected about it. Now...> — swings tail — <I'll go leave the warning to Goldenrod and hopefully I'll be back by Ni Frith.>

Inka — <Take care...> — sends an air "kiss" (a lick) to her mate.

-Baluarkos starts walking away carrying the carapace

"Let's go!"

"Dad! Wait"

-The kits start following Baluarkos, but Inka is fast to halt them-

Inka — growls — <Ahem...>

-the kits turn around and look up at her serious, slightly reprimanding face-

"...Eeeeep~!"

"Mom look angry..."

"...Err.... Breakfast first?"

"...Rawr? ^_^"

-As the kits orderly return to the burrow to get ready, Pokéteco ruffles his plumage and glides from Inka's head to the random structure of rocks and sticks serving as a sundial-

Pokéteco — <You are feeling much better now, I sense. You just... look happier.>

Inka — grins — <Yes. I think I can overcome my fear now...> — lends an ear to the playful kits — <...with enough distraction. Everything will be better...>

Things won't go that bad anymore...
