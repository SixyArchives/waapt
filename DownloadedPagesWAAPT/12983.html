Gateon Port

-Herbert is pacing up and down on the waterfront, occasionally checking his Pokegear as he does. Eventually, he gives a sigh and turns to Bishop.-

Herbert: ...Well, I got two replies. DS and Lilith both say that Miki and Samael would be interested in coming to another meeting. Other than that, nothing. Not even a "wait, you're leaving Orre?" I think it's safe to say that we should probably just slip away quietly before it gets dark.

Bishop: <...I see. I can understand the desire to leave discreetly, but I was hoping to say goodbye to some of the new acquaintances I made over the course of our meetings. Miki and Samael included.>

Herbert: You'll get to see them again soon, Bishop, don't worry. It's not like we're leaving for good. If all goes well we'll be back with the team within a week. Hell, you could even host a meeting after we get back. Integrate it into the Christmas celebrations or something.

Bishop: <I suppose...>

-Herbert nods, pauses for a second, then hugs Bishop as tightly as you can feasibly hug a six-foot-tall metallic insect.-

Herbert: Look... you don't have to come with me if you don't want to. You can stay at Challenger's Cave, just stay here, or do whatever you like. I'm not going to force you into anything. That's what made Kirk leave, after all...

-Bishop looks surprised for a moment, then returns the hug, giving a small smile.-

Bishop: <...Herbert, do you really think I would have stuck with you for this long if I did not enjoy your company? Of course I will accompany you. Just inform Miki and Samael that I will arrange for a meeting at a later date.>

Herbert: Sure thing. And... thanks for this, by the way. Thanks for everything, Bishop.

-He pulls away and starts typing out another text on his X-Transceiver.-

To: DS, Lilith

Great! Tell them that Bishop's really glad to hear from them again and that he'll set up another support group meeting once we get back.

From: Herbert

-Once the message is sent, he pulls a Pokeball out of his bag and sends out Maul.-

Herbert: You know the drill. Maul, can you take us to Challenger's Cave?

Maul: ~In about three minutes or so, sure.~

Herbert: ...Right.

-He sighs, and turns back towards the sea.-

Herbert: So... this is it, I guess. I'm leaving Orre. Hopefully for good this time around.

...I should be happy. Happy now that Cipher's gone, now that my friends are safe, now that we don't have to spend our every waking moment fearing for our lives. And I can't deny that I've had some good times here. Not many, granted, but stuff like signing up for PEFE with Crewe... that was fun. But... things haven't gone back to normal. Not really. We can try to pretend otherwise, but we'll only be fooling ourselves.

Sure, we took down Cipher. But did we really win, in the end? Even if they're gone, the trauma they inflicted - both on us and on all the Pokemon they Shadowed - will stay with us. And so many of its members escaped without ever answering for their crimes. Tokarev, Verax, Odin, Titan... Allen. What's stopping them from coming back and making hell for us again - or even worse, simply disappearing, never facing the retribution that they justly deserve?

...I guess the most I can do is promise that I'll make sure they receive it. I've changed over the last couple of months, after all. Been desensitised, almost. I don't know if I'll ever be able to go back to a normal life... but if I can't, then I'll dedicate myself to making sure that each and every one of those bastards spends their final moments screaming.

-He sighs again.-

Herbert: ...No doubt this is what Tagg was thinking to himself before he snapped. I guess when you look into an abyss for long enough, it starts looking back into you...

-He turns to Bishop.-

Herbert: ...You ready to go, Bishop?

Bishop: <Indeed I am.>

Herbert: Good. Let's get the hell out of this place and never come back to it. Leave all the bad memories behind. Maul?

-Maul gives a nod, and blips Herbert and Bishop away.-
