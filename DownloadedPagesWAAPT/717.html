A funeral. The girl stands with her head bowed and a woman behind her, both wearing black. No one else is there but a small boy that looks rather uncomfortable to be in formal clothes.

The girl and her mother yelling at each other. A Piplup is cowering behind the couch, watching with wide eyes.

Night. The girl and the Piplup are sitting against an outside wall, the girl biting her lip and looking around nervously. A door slams and they sprint off.

Day. The girl, now surrounded by a Prinplup, Crobat, and Graveler walk down a sunny path. Suddenly a Houndour jumps out and runs at them, tail wagging and tongue out. The girl shouts and huddles against Prinplup. Crobat and Graveler chase the Houndour off.

Night again. The girl and her three Pokemon huddle around a small fire. A pained howl pierces the night and they run to see what happened. The Houndour from earlier lays on the ground with slash marks on its side. The girl gently picks him up and brings him to the nearest Pokemon Center.

Snowpoint Gym. The Houndour—now evolved—faces off against an Abomasnow. It knocks it down with one attack. The battle ends, and the girl runs to the Houndoom and hugs him, celebrating.
