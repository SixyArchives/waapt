Some Swamp near Azalea Town - Johto

On a sunny but cold November day, a familiar Alligator and Dragon Pokémon pair are sunnimg themselves on a rock by the edge of the swamp water, trying to catch as much sunlight as they can, the former Pokémon in particular seeming somewhat down, for some reason...

Lake: <...Hey.>

Lake
the Feraligatr
Brave Nature | Somewhat Stubborn.

The other Pokémon, a Dragonite, turns his attention to his companion.

Stratus: <What's up?>

Stratus
the Dragonite
Naive Nature | Loves to eat.

Lake sighs.

Lake: <You remember those days when me and my mate used to live in the same universe?>

Downcast, he turns onto his side, gazing down sadly at the floor.

<Arceus, how I miss those...>

In response, Stratus frowns, sad at seeing his offspring - as was revealed the last time we saw these two - in such a sorry state.

Stratus: <Missing your girlfriend, huh?>

Lake nods without looking back at him.

Lake: <More than anything.>

The Dragonite shifts over to pat Lake's scales gently.

Stratus: <Perhaps it's time we went looking for her?>

Without moving, Lake rolls his eyes to gaze up at the Dragonite, a frown crossing his jaws.

Lake: <Um, how? Universe hopping 'ain't easy. There's, like...an Infinite amount of them.>

Stratus merely shrugs in response.

Stratus: <Who's to say that she didn't come back to our world at some point? I mean...>

He waves a paw, gesturing at their swampy surroundings.

<We've been here for a long time.>

Lake pauses to consider this for a few moments.

Lake: <...Perhaps we have been here for long enough. After all...>

He pulls himself up off the rock, sitting up.

<We got to spend a lot of time with Mom and the others, which I've wanted to do since, well, most of my life. And I bet everymon else is wondering where we've gone. So...perhaps now wouldn't be such a bad time to leave. Well, that and...>

He stops, shivering slightly.

<I wouldn't mind heading someplace warmer for winter.>

The Dragonite also sits up, nodding in agreement.

Stratus: <Me neither. Oh! Not only that, but...>

Stratus gives Lake an excited look.

<This would be the perfect chance for me and you to do some father-son bonding, eh?>

In response, Lake says nothing for a moment, simply giving the Dragonite a glance.

Lake: <Uh...>

He looks away, somewhat unsure, leaving Stratus confused.

Stratus: <What? What's wrong?>

The Feraligatr looks back, still somewhat awkward.

Lake: <Sorry, but...I...still don't really see you as my Dad yet, Stratus.>

Stratus: <...Oh.>

The Dragonite raises a paw to rub the back of his head, looking a bit uncomfortable. Lake, quick to notice this and thinking that he might have hurt the Dragon's feelings, panics just a bit.

Lake: <Not that I won't ever think of you that way. Perhaps...perhaps I just need a bit more time? And...it's not like we're not still...>

Lake glances down at his feet.

<You know...friends.>

Stratus, in response, shuts his eyes, giving Lake a nod.

Stratus: <Yeah, yeah. I know.>

Opening them again, he flaps his wings, hopping to his feet.

<Well, I suppose we'd better go and tell you're Mom that we're going.>

Although somewhat concerned that he may have hurt his feelings, Lake nods as he pulls himself up, tightening the long yellow scarf around his neck.

Lake: <Perhaps we could ask if she wants to come along?>

To this, Stratus reacts with a look of abject horror.

Stratus: <Are you crazy?! You know what she's like!>

Lake: <I know! But we'd be there to make sure she doesn't eat...everyone.>

The Dragonite crosses his arms, giving Lake a disapproving look.

Stratus: <What, have you forgotten that time that those Nidos from the colony came over to ask her to stop hunting so close to their grounds, and she ended up trying to eat them? And the both of us were only just barely able to pin her down long enough for them to get away?>

Lake pauses, the events in question replaying themselves in his head, also remembering the stern talking to they had received from some of the Nidos in question.

Lake: <...On second thought, I don't think I will.>
