Luke: "Alright, Izzy. I want you to hit this target-"

The human gestured at a rather large tree branch that he had embedded in the ground, since he didn't really have anything better to use.

"With your moves as I call each of them out."

About ten feet away, the Heloiptile nodded, crouching down on all fours and assuming an aggressive stance.

Izzy: <Pfft, amateur stuff!>

Luke: "Right then. Quick Attack!"

Almost invisibly fast, the little lizard charged and rammed the branch hard.

"Mud slap!"

Backpedaling a few feet, she spun around and slapped the floor with her tail, throwing up a few clods of dirt that spatted against the branch.

"Charge!"

Rearing up back onto two legs, Izzy shut her eyes as the frills on the side of her head fanned out, gathering solar power from the sun which she internally converted into electricity.

"Parabolic Charge!"

Crackling with electricity, she shot a yellow ball of electrical energy that struck the branch, scorching some of the wood black. To Luke's surprised, the projectile then arced back to it's source, the Helioptile allowing herself to absorb it.

"...You still need to tell me what that does."

Izzy: <I shoot it at somemon, it zaps them and takes some of their energy, then it comes back and gives it to me. What's hard to understand about that?>
