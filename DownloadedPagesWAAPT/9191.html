"<Uh... What are you trying to get at?>" he asked, not really understanding why she came out with that all of sudden.

"It's just that... Do you think I should just mind my own business and not meddle into other's affairs, even if I just want to help?" she asked, still looking at the ground.

"<Well... I wouldn't get involved unless somehow I had to, but... Just be yourself.>" he said, not really feeling very comfortable with having that kind of conversation.

"But that's the issue... When he talked to me like that, I wasn't happy with the way he talked to me, so rude.... But yet I held the feelings back and just tried to be pleasant."

"<I'm sure nobody would mind if you called him out on that, it wouldn't be you being mean, just being assertive that you don't let just anyone talk to you like that.>"

"I don't know... I don't want to change and having my friends turning away from me because of it."

"<... We wouldn't.>"

"I know you wouldn't, you're not my friend, you're family." she then looked at Seth with a small smile "At least, we spend so much time together that we might as well be family now."

"<Heh... I guess I can't deny that.>" he said, giving a small smile as well "<I never expected things go like this when I got captured...>" he blurted, talking out loud

"Hm...?"

"<U-Uh... Nevermind.>" he said, looking away from Sakura.

The silence then came back as Sakura could only wonder what Seth meant by that, she was expecting him to say something about the matter, but since he didn't, she just let the topic rest and just looked forward with a more positive expression on her face.

"<... If you change or not, I don't care, and if anyone cares about something as small as that and turns away from you, it's because they were never your friends to begin with.>" said Seth all of sudden, still looking away from Sakura.

Sakura just looked at him with surprise for a few seconds and, seeing that he didn't react anymore after that, she just got closer to him and gave him a hug from the side

"I'll keep that in mind, thanks."

She then let him go and just looked at the sky, looking far happier now, while Seth just stood there, without doing anything.
