"So, you are saying that we can try and go to Mirage Island but even if we manage to get in, it is not there?"

"So, time there does not flow normally?"

Celebi: <Oh it does, it flows exactly at a speed of one second per second, just like the rest of the universe. But the direction of time shifts constantly and thus it falls out of sync with the perception of this universe.>

Celebi: <You humans think of time in one, two dimensions at most. But for Mirage Island, it is like a rollercoaster. It can go left and right, up and down, then for extra madness turn, brake and go back, rotate, spin and even do a barrel roll. For all you know, your friend is fine, but his time normality has him at the day of Cerulean's foundation or something... and "tomorrow" he will be hearing reports of the first stable human colony in Pokémars, if he could.>

"Ugh... if that is happening, how did his message get through? How do we even reach the island?"

Celebi: <There are many ways. His message got through because plot convenience the time direction in the island realigned with the one in this universe. Since the location of time was already different due to all the shifts, the message he sent yesterday was in the present in your future.>

"...I do not really understand."

Celebi: <It's OK. I still like that you care for my house.>

"So what do we do, go to the future to when you received the message to access the island from there? Uhm... then?"

Celebi: <No, silly!> —slaps Silent. — <The island already left that timeframe and went in another direction.>

"But it was there when the signal-"

Celebi -sighs: <No. It is no longer in that present anymore, because it has crossed this universe's past at some point again and altered its own future time velocity.>

"Allright... you say we have to somehow sync with the time direction and location of the Mirage Island, right? Can we change that direction and location from here?"

Celebi: <Yes, provided you have either enough power, or enough time directions to perceive in this universe.> — narrows eyes — <You did what I said, yes?> — Silent nods — <Someone entered the island thanks to Legendary power.
