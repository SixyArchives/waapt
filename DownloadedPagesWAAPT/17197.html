Unite & Luke

Hanger Bay - 1:48 Until Cannon Fire

The Braviary glided quietly into the hanger, setting down onto the metallic floor softly. Luke quickly hopped off as they took a look around. Surprisingly, there was nobody else there. No workers, no security, nothing.

Unite: <This is getting freaky. Not only has nobody tried to shoot us down, but there's nobody in here either?>

Luke: Don't complain, just makes it easier for us.

Luke began to head forward, Unite following close behind.

Unite: <Hey, I'm not complain. It just doesn't sit well with me. There's been no effort to stop us whatsoever.>

Luke nodded as they passed by what looked like a small drop-ship. He mused to himself that it was perhaps the airship owner's personal craft or something like that.

Luke: Maybe they don't know we're here?

Unite shook his head.

Unite: <Nah, they must have a radar or something that would have picked us up. Even if we're small targets, we're still targets.>

They then came across stacks upon stacks of wooden crates of all shapes and sizes.

Luke: We found the cargo hold. Wonder what all of it is?

Unite: <Weapons? Food? I dunno', we don't have time to worry about it!>

Luke: You're right, you're right. I'm sorry. Just curious. Let's get a move on.

He pointed further ahead.

I can see an elevator over there. I bet it goes to the upper decks.

Unite cocked his head at the trainer as they headed towards it.

Unite: <The cannon's further ahead, though.>

Luke: Right. We go up, then we go down again.

The bird nodded as the two stepped onto the elevator platform. Luke pressing the button to send them up.

Unite: <Ooooh, I get it now.>
