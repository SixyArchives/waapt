426484 PippingFool5th Nov 2014 05:45:29 AM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

[???]

Constantin is relaxing in what seems to be some sort of temporary lounge room, browsing through a Kalosian paper with a smirk on his face. Meanwhile at the billiards table, Maria von Boehm is chalking a pool cue and setting up the table for a game of Snooker.

Maria: Heh, you look like you've come across something lively ragdoll. Normally you're not so chipper~

Constantin: Nothing but the greatest modern tragedy my dear. The blight that was the Cain family imploded in on itself in a fantastic display of bloody red fireworks! A painting done in crimson! Reminds me of my actions a decade ago actually, it makes me feel nostalgic.

Maria sits on the table, leaning on the pool cue.

Maria: Oh?

Constantin: An abused pup of an old money family finally bites back at the abusive hand that raised them using the innate powers that they were forbidden from showing. They also take out the enabler to further sweeten the vengeance. No fault of the enabler, mind you m'dear, but the neglect was still there and those scars - the ones you don't see - they run deep.

Maria starts analysing the billiards table and begins aligning her cue.

Maria: Aah. You sayin' that this bout of patricide was not the different from your own experiences?

Constantin chuckles as he folds up the paper and places it on the table.

Constantin: For all her high and mighty attitude presenting herself as being above the cold and calculating sycophancy she associated with me, we were never really that much different from each other. Hence why we could never get along.

Constantin makes his way to the pool table and picks up a cue, chalking the tip in preparation for the game.

Constantin: But this is where the wheat gets separated from the chaff, Maria. The idiot got herself caught and now has a bleak future ahead of her. Since she offed her moronic brother, she will probably be lucky to end up with a punishment as humane as the guillotine if Ibrahiem's Al-Gala little firespat wishes to involve herself in the case behind closed doors. Aliments of the heart are not easily cured, and Plata o Plomo is that particular [Arab's] bread and butter.

Maria: You really think the de Nile brat has it in her?

Constantin: If she's anything like the Don her Granduncle is rumoured to be, then she would have no qualms in getting her hands a sullied for revenge. Admirable, considering the spinelessness of the Kalosian family she comes from. You don't mind me breaking formation, do you?

Maria: You're the boss, ragdoll.

Constantin aligns his cue to the cue ball and calculates the best way to break the formation of the red balls at the table's end.

Constantin: Regardless, that's one decrepit family removed from the gene pool. And a lot less work for me come in due time. Though I will say this, I would like to somehow encounter little Lucy before her trial - if only so I can pour brine on the wounds.

Constantin takes a deep breath as he exhale, striking the ball and breaking formation at the end.

Maria: Heh. You always like the last laugh, dontcha ragdoll?

Constantin: It keeps me going. Lets leave it at that.

Maria lines up her cue and strikes the cue ball in the direction of a lone red ball, nearly pocketing it.

Maria: Drat. Missed it by that much.

Constantin: You were always terrible at this game.

Maria: Shut the fuck up you skeleton someone happened to throw a leather bag over.

Constantin positions his cue behind his back, striking the cue ball and pocketing the red ball that Maria failed to score. He sends a smarmy smirk Maria's way, and she pouts in response.

Maria: Hmph. You enjoy making me look like a fool, dontcha?

Constantin: It's the only reason I keep you around.

Maria: On yer bike

Constantin fails to pocket another red, and the turn swaps over to Maria who starts calculating her next move.

Maria: Oh by the way. Did you hear about that snuff film circulating 'round the onion routing, Tor client side of the internet?

Constantin: Since I only really use the internet to purchase goods from Silk Road, not really.

Maria: Two green-leafed psychopaths filmed themselves torturin' two snot-nosed brats to death in lieu of that other snuff film that circulated around last year. Probably in another act of revenge like the Cain case.

Constantin: Idiots. If you're going to torture someone, don't paste it online for everyone to see - and then hunt you down like bloodhounds.

Maria pockets a red and does a small victory jump.

Constantin: Finally sunk one.

Maria: Stuff it, ragdoll.

Maria moves on to the next ball.

Maria: As I way saying, green leafed psychopaths. Neither skill nor finesse was displayed in that video, they just wanted to look big and bad in front of the entire world.

Constantin: If they miraculously don't get caught, they'll learn. Regardless, I highly doubt we would be hearing these amateurs again.

Maria: Indeed.

Maria leans on her cue again.

Maria: I'm parched, I'll go get the fuel. Anything in particular?

Constantin: Brandy would be appreciated mon amie

Maria: Brandy. Got it. I'll fix up a rum on the rocks for meself. Back in a tiffy

Maria walks off to go fix up the drinks, while Constantin walks over to the paper and opens it up again. Working on the samurai sudoku puzzle on page 11.
