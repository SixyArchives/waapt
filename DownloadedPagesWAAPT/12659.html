Crewe's Mindscape — House one

-Crewe opens the door to one house. Inside is a single room, with one wall covered in TV screens, with a counter sticking out below it. Sticky notes cover the walls and screens haphazardly, and the place seems to be a mess, papers with notes and drawings scribbled on them lying all over. Sitting in a swivel chair is a girl that looks like a ten or eleven year old Crewe, in a bright green shirt and jeans, an empty Pokeball belt around her waist. Playing on the TVs are various scenes of Crewe training or battling Pokemon, changing every few seconds. While there are some of generic fighting, there are also ones that Crewe winces at the sight of, such as firing stones from her slingshot while yelling at a Salamence, or yelling at an angry Zoroark in the middle of a battle.
