429721 Luke992419th Nov 2014 03:02:55 PM from England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Is that a kind of food?

Somewhere over the ocean...

The calm of the open sea was momentarily broken by a vast shape zooming overhead. An immense airship, coated stern to bow in armor plating that resembled hardened obsidian cut through the sky, en route to civilization.

Airship Bridge

At the top of the airship just next to the ship's helm paced an enormous Dinosaur Pokémon. His brick-red scales were craggy, almost as if made from solid rock, and just as hard. His massive head was home to a set of jaws that made even a Feraligatr or Krookodile's seem merely entry level. His lower jaw in-particular was covered by a thick coat of downy feathers, almost like a beard of some sort.

These same feathers formed a large ruff around his neck. And adding even more to his rough yet regal appearance, a bronze crown-like structure sat on his head, which housed his two rather small but piercing eyes.

This was not the sort of Pokémon you would want to meet in a dark ally. Especially not now, as he was more than a little irritated.

Torque the Tyrantrum: <How much further is it?>

A short distance ahead at the airships helm, it's pilot – a large metallic blue Pokémon with four large legs and a saucer shaped body paused briefly to deliver a response in a distinctly robotic tone.

Sinclair the Metagross: <With the engines at full burn, we will make landfall in approximately six hours minimum.>

The Tyrantrum grumbled irritably. He was not a patient Pokémon.

Torque: <Is there nothing you can do to make this thing go faster?>

Sinclair: <Unfeasible. While I can increase the fuel consumption to boost the engines even further, we would not have enough fuel to return to base in the event of emergency or mission failure.>

Torque scowled. As much as he hated the idea of failure, the Metagross had a point. Best to run away with your tail between your legs than to have your foe tear it off completely.

Torque: <Fine, fine. Guess I'll just have to be patient.>

The immense dinosaur turned around, stomping loudly over to his throne at the back of the room.

<But I sure won't enjoy it!>

Sinclair: <I never expected you would, sir.>

Sticking his tail through a hole in the back, the Tyrantrum took a seat on his cushy chair. The image gave one the impression of an emperor surveying his kingdom. Which was precisely the point.

Grumbling, the Tyrantrum fiddled with his claws, a somewhat nervous feeling developing in his gut. Not that he would ever admit it.

Torque: <Do you think they'll love me, Sinclair?>

The Metagross responded with a curt nod, not even bothering to look away from the control panels and screens laid out in front of him.

Sinclair: <I predict your approval rating with the populace of Johto to be around ninety percent.>

Torque: <Good, good. Of course, it would be better if it was a firm one-hundred percent...>

He waved a claw dismissively.

<But that's why we brought the cannon with us.>

Sinclair: <Yes. But I feel inclined to warn you, sir, that most peoplemons have a negative reaction to having a large gun pointed at them.>

Torque: <I know that, Sinclair. But sometimes, the threat of force is necessary to get what you want out of life. If gaining the approval of a region so that they can crown me emperor besets having to wave a gun in their face...>

He nodded to himself.

<Then so be it.>

Sinclair: <Understood, sir.>

Torque: <Ideally, we don't even want to fire it if possible.>

A brief moment of silence followed as Torque gazed up at the ceiling at nothing in-particular, thoughts of mass devastation dancing through his head.

<But I'm not gonna' lie, I wouldn't mind if we got to atomize something with it. Is there anything on the way we could just stop and shoot at? No...?>

He looked back at the Metagross, the robotic Pokémon remaining silent.

<...I thought so.>

Sinclair: <Even with all of the assets at our disposal, have you considered the option that the populace of Johto won't let you simply take charge, sir?>

Leaning back in his chair, the Tyrantrum sighed contently.

Torque: <Of course I have, Sinclair. But the thing is? It's not even a question of “Who will let me.” Oh no...>

A wicked smirk crossed his jaws.

<The real question is: “Who will stop me?”>
