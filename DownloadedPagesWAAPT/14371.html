"Well... Why don't we spend the day together then? I have nothing to do as well."

Aline seemed stunned by the suggestion for a moment, before frantically writing on her sign again

"Really?!"

Duke only nods in answer, finding some comfort in the girl's excitement. He then gets up from his seat and looks around for a bit

"Is there anything you want to do?"
