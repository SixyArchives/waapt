Camphrier Town

Perched atop a bench with her legs folded beneath her, Kim took a cleansing breath, her eyes closed as she rested her palms atop her lap, letting the gentle town breeze wash over her. The town truly was a nice place. It had a pleasant atmosphere akin to Floarama's and was small enough that she could truly get some meditation done if left in peace.

Resident: Are you sure there's nothing I can do for you? We just want you to enjoy your stay, after all.

If being the operative word.

The people of this town truly weren't used to dealing with so many people at once and were doing their best to try to placate their every whim. Every one except being left alone, it would seem.

Exhaling through her nose, Kim opened one eye, hoping the man wouldn't be startled by the iris changing from a dark brown to a now glowing amber.

Kim: It's fine, really. I promise I'll let you know if I need something.

Resident: If you're certain...

Kim: I am. I promise.

Looking a bit uncertain, he nodded and began to walk away, Kim's eyelids sliding shut. Despite that, she could still tell he was watching her. Perhaps he had noticed after all. Or perhaps he was still hoping that she'd need his assistance with something. Either way, so long as he let her go about her business, she had no problem with him doing as he pleased.

Kim: Now then...

With another exhale, Kim began to focus her mind, letting her senses adapt to the aura around her...

Route 7 (Rivière Walk)

Returning from the nearby Berry Fields, Rex hummed lightly to himself, feeling more than a little pleased at how his trip was going. He'd been able to reunite with a number of all of his old friends for the first time in ages and he was able to do so in a new region. It was just like old times... but not so close that he had to worry about some kind of unholy invasion.

Rex: Enjoying your berries Sirocco?

The Fletchling in question was perched atop its trainer's shoulder, nipping hungrily at a few Nanab Berries that they'd acquired from the fields. It was a pleasant coincidence that some trainer's crop was being attacked by Spewpa and Combee. Sirocco needed the training.

And he'd even managed to capture the two bugs and transfer them to Prof. Sycamore's Laboratory. Apparently, the evolution for those Pokémon came in a number of magnificent patterns. With any luck, those two would provide some he hadn't seen before.

Stepping back onto the main path, Rex stretched his arms skyward, the little bird on his shoulder flapping into the air as its treat hung from its beak, taking roost once more as his stretching died down.
