Psyche's Mindscape

Psyche has ascended to Nyx, trying to find her way to Lina. Upon the way, she meets a figure that looks suspiciously like Lucius... Erebos, Aspect of Death Endings.

"...In order to continue on your quest... First you must pass my ordeal..."

He shows Psyche a vision of herself as a 20's something woman. She and Noelle are happy in their home, watching a little one crawling around. Psyche is happy... for a while.

The scene shifts. Psyche cradles Noelle in her arms. Noelle isn't breathing. Their child cries, "Mama, wake up!"

Psyche shakes her head. "Child... She's gone..."

The scene shifts again. Psyche is practically catatonic, unable to move except through psychic impulses. The child has grown up, and gone away, unable to watch both of her parents die before her eyes.

She shakes her head. "This isn't real..."

Erebos whispers. "This could be your future. This is what will happen if you fail... If you do nothing..."

The scene shifts again. Noelle and Psyche are quite happy. Their child has grown up to be a fine Psychic, not as powerful as her mother, but strong in her own right. She still has to go to therapy once a week, to make sure she doesn't hurt anyone through her power, but she is at least stable... for now.

Psyche crosses her arms. "...And if I succeed?"

Erebos shakes his head. "I cannot tell you. It's knowledge lies beyond my existence. If you destroy Lina, you will never be the same again. What will you do?"

Psyche looks up with steely determination. "I will battle. I accept the potential of my defeat, and I wish to do battle anyway. If I do nothing I will still have the risk to hurt those I love."

Erebos nods. "Do you resign yourself to the fate you have chosen?"

Psyche grits her teeth. "I do."

Erebos smiles. "Then go with peace, my child, I grant blessing onto you."

Psyche climbs the stairs into Nyx proper and stares down Lina. "FOUL ASPECT, I STRIKE AT THEE."

Lina cackles. She bends down."I'd like to see you try."

She swarms the area with Satyrs, trying to kill Psyche before she can make it to her. Psyche slashes through the Satyrs viciously, as they vanish.

The battle lasts hours. Lina striking Psyche with lightning, clouding her mind with rage, creating creature after creature to strike down.

Psyche parries Lina's every move with deft strikes, disciplined tactics, and careful maneuvers. At last, she manages to get to a high point. She leaps after Lina and plunges the spear deep into Lina's heart. The Aspect screams. "NO, THIS IS INCONCEIVABLE! I AM A GOD, YOU CANNOT KILL ME!"

She shrinks to a normal size and disappears into stars. Psyche smiles. "...Finally, I can be at peace." She drops the spear exhausted.

A final figure takes the spear. "You have done well..." Psyche looks up to see a figure covered in light, unable to be made out. Heliod, Aspect of The Sun Enlightenment grimaces. "Too well." He stabs Psyche with the spear. As Psyche bleeds out, vanishing into stars, the figure leans in to reveal... Grohl's face.

"...You sick... bastard..."

Grohl: Good night, sweet princess. o3o

The world vanishes from view.
