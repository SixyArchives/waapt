-The Honchkrow pulls Pleo back, setting him down gently on the floor.-

Pleo: <Will you catch me again if I fall?>

Harriet: -Nods.- <Sure! Why wouldn't I?>

Pleo: <...Okay then!>

-Pleo climbs back up onto the balcony, then gets ready to try again, thankful for Harriet's assistance.-
