Ambrette Town Beach

Valorie stood barefoot in the sand on the small, rather humble townside beach. She had abandoned all of her normal clothes in favor of a simple leotard bathing suit for the occasion. Where did she even keep her pokeballs..? Woman's secret.

Looking around her gaze scaled the steep cliff walls leading back up to the town. This seemed like as good a place as any to train!

Haxorus and Charizard were called forth. "Alright guys, it's time for a warmup!" Valorie instructed without a hint of irony. The two pokemon turned their heads to Valorie in acknowledgement and she smiled at them both. "Charizard, I know Haxorus isn't going to hold back so you shouldn't either! I want you to see what you can do without my help." she continued, placing one balled fist on her hip and using the other to try and move some of her long, flowing blonde hair out of her eyes.

The exceedingly short trainer waited a moment to throw either pokemon hoping to get a pre-emptive strike in off before raising the hand she'd been using to shift her hair out of her eyes and calling out. "Go!"

Charizard immediately shifted her body weight, snorting dismissively. <Think you can take me?> she asked, taunting the dragon pokemon. Haxorus' lowered her body down, stalking around Charizard in a half circle. <Maybe. You /have/ put on a lot of weight.> Haxorus taunted right back in a rather amused tone. Charizard pulled her head back and emitted a roar into the sky, spewing a flamethrower into the air blindly before whipping her head back down to try and catch Haxorus in the blast. Haxorus used protect, a powerful shield materializing to block the attack.

Charizard's mouth flashes with a bright light as she responded to this by launching a fire blast attack. The flame seemed to crash into the very same shield and explode on contact, failing to damage it's target. <Come on, try fighting like a man.> Haxorus goaded, still stalking around Charizard as if she were prey to be hunted. Charizard snorted dismissively and her eyes began to shine with a blue aura, her tail following suit and flaring up much larger than it had been before. A blu'ish purple'ish shockwave escaped from her mouth, ripping through Haxorus' weakened shield and pummeling the axe-headed pokemon with an intense energy that send Haxorus' flaming body careening into the nearby rocky wall with an extremely audible crash.

The severe attack didn't seem to have been enough however, as Haxorus abruptly lunged from the impact site to throw herself bodily through the air, head twisting back and swinging down hard as if to cleave Charizard in two. It was Guillotine! Valorie's eyes widened and she watched with pleasure as Charizard quickly shifted positions, outspeeding Haxorus' desperate attempt and punishing her with a mega punch to the abdomen.

The raptor-dragon pokemon crashed into the sand, this time not getting up. Valorie emitted a sigh of relief and moved over to the two. "Charizard... you did good. I was almost expecting her to take you off guard. Haxorus... I know what you were trying to do, but that just doesn't work against Charizard. You should've gone right for a rock slide attack when you got the chance. Guillotine is... too easy to dodge." she lectured as she crouched down to begin examining Haxorus' wounds.
