An incredibly short girl struggled her way through the snow. It was hard when you were so short that it rose up to your knees! Her rain boots were at least waterproof, doing a fairly good job at keeping her dry.

She had switched into a lavender dress-coat for the cold day, and save for her thighs it was keeping her quite snug and warm. This was a sacrifice she was willing to make in the name of fashion. Her beanie was ever present on her head and her scarf was pulled up, just barely not covering her mouth. She'd put on mittens for the occasion.

Geez, who froze the distortion world over?

Valorie didn't know why but the snow filled her with a deep sense of dread. It was seemingly supernatural, as she'd never had such feelings against snow before! She'd grown up around snow in Iccirus City, spent half her life in it even! Yet now as she stood there alone a sense of melancholy and panic settled over her.

She didn't want to be here anymore.
