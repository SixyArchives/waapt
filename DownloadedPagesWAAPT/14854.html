Glavo: <That's certainly true.>

Zeal: ...It's... I was... how do I put this...?

-He takes a deep breath.-

Zeal: I don't think I ever really made it clear. Back in Holon, I mean.

Glavo: <Hm?>

Zeal: I was... really happy to have you back after your disappearance. Happier than I think I'd felt in a long time.

Glavo: <...It's only natural. You didn't know where... no, you didn't even know if I was still alive, did you?>

Zeal: Of course I knew you were. You left of your own accord. It wasn't anything like it was with Fajro. But still...

Glavo: <...I'm sorry. Even after searching on my own, my lead got us nowhere. That... Silvio, his name was...? He was lying about everything.>

Zeal: Yeah, he was. And that only makes me want to meet him again even more...

-Glavo lies on her back.-

Glavo: <How so?>

Zeal: I don't understand him. And it makes me curious. What would motivate him to research my past, to flawlessly fake a photograph of Fajro? And more importantly... What does he have to gain from this?

-Glavo grins.-

Glavo: <...You really haven't changed, Zach.>

Zeal: ...Zach? You should kn—

Glavo: <I know you're Zeal. But you're still Zachary Motte. You're still the same as you were back then. Zach changed. But you, you didn't.>

Zeal: So... I'm what Zach used to be? That's what you're saying?

Glavo: <Yeah. Ever since you joined the J-Team, you've been a lot less solemn. You're just like you were when we first met, when you and Chase were just starting off.>

Zeal: There's that name again... Chase Espen... The name he went to great lengths to hide from me, all for some reason he never so much as hinted at...

-Glavo closes her eyes.-

Glavo: <...Hey, Zeal. There's something this reminded me of... I'm not sure I ever told you, but...>

Zeal: ...I think I may have known to begin with.

-Glavo opens her eyes.-

Glavo: <Back when you found me in Cianwood City, I...>

Zeal: You had no memories of anything before that, right?

Glavo: <Yeah... I'm not exactly sure why I tried to hide that.>

Zeal: ...You and I are a lot alike. I mean, you just gave me two examples. There's the whole memory thing, for one. But that's unimportant. Once... no, not just once... All the time, we're keeping secrets from each other. And I know that neither one of us knows why.

-He lies on his back and looks up at the sky.-

Zeal: Heh. Maybe I'm just desperate to find parallels. But still...

-Glavo looks at Zeal for a moment, then returns to quietly gazing at the sky. Zeal shuts his eyes.-

Zeal: Still...
