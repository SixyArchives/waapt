Ivy — How are you going to get to talk to the colony leaders anyway? You don't expect to send in that little bird...

— No, I don't. I just need to recognize them at a safe distance... don't worry, I know how to move around them.

Ivy — I'd be careful because at this time of the day they all kinda gather together and start running from place to place in clusters. Perhaps you'll have to get all dirty and spend the day chasing them :3.

— Let's hope not... Also, I just remembered while exploring the islands that it is this research center that provided the Bellsprout and Nidoran introduced to the east islands.

Ivy -smiles slyly- — More or less, yes. You sent us three breeding couples once. A shame one of the males died...

— Can't win them all. What [happened] to the female? Do you know?

Ivy — Well... — thinks for a moment — She was taken away to Ascorbia, then to Navel. Actually, she's residing there with the Gym Leader.

-smirks in realization-

Ivy -notices, but says nothing — They have got along with the polulation here well, and have grown to a pretty stable number. I think they and the Snubbull made friends with the Seel to go explore the nearby islands.

— ...Seel come around this side of the islands too?

Ivy — Very few of them, yes. They cross the area around Pinkan Island checking for different food to eat before migrating, or that's the impression I get.

-after a couple of minutes-

— Oh... before I forget, you mention that the Nidos here [organize] themselves in clusters?

Ivy — Yeah.

— I guess the Snubbull do, too.

Ivy — Well, if five-a-pack can be called a cluster. — narrows eyes at me — We have a higher and more volatile population of Nidoran here.

— But you have done a good work so far. How many specimens do you keep here?

Ivy -puts a finger on her chin, then tilts her head the other way, and thinks for a moment — Considering the relatively small surface of this island... I'd say about three to four hundred. — crosses arms and glances at me — And you? How big is your... colony?

— About five thousand specimens.

Ivy — M-hm... impressive. Can't imagine trying to run a census on that many...

-I notice a fork on the road ahead-

— Yeah, they start getting angry at me when I count to about three thousand.

Ivy — Those must be fun times. — points to a fork on the road — Down that road you'll find the Snubbull living with some Weedle. Be careful with the silk traps.

— Thanks. I will report back to the house as soon as I'm done.

Ivy — Sure, suit yourself. I'll go to the Bellsprout with my assistants.

-walks away-

-I stay at the fork, waiting for the mons around to go away-

Shahinne — <...Aren't we going to go exploring?>

-I pet Shahinne's head, she chirps happily-

— Yes, we are, but first...

-After checking that there's no human or mon around, I pick the Moon Ball and send out Maekrite-

Shahinne — <Yay!>

Maekrite — <Hello there, chick.>

Shahinne — <♪~>

Maekrite -smiles, shakes ears, then looks up- — <Ugh, I was getting bored... Is this the place?>

— Yes, Maekrite. The colony lives north from here. Be careful, the researcher here told me they gather in big clusters.

Maekrite — <I'll be able to find my way in easily.>

— Try and convince them to get the Seel to explore the nearby islands as well.

Maekrite — <I'll try... how do I do to get back to where you are when I have explained to them?>

— Hmmm... you could try sneak into the bling, but I don't want to risk leaving you behind... Try and get close enough to call Shahinne outside, that'll serve as a good excuse.

-Maekrite nods, then looks around and walks out of the trail into the forest, while I head down the road to meet the Snubbull-
