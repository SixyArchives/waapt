-RedEyes!Zeal teleports away as Jacob slashes at his waist, but he's wounded and bleeding when he reappears.-

RedEyes!Zeal: "...Do you want me to tell you a little story...?"

Jacob: -Tries standing up but stumbles to one knee, his left thigh still bleeding- "...."

-He's breathing heavily but staring intently at Red Eyes-

RedEyes!Zeal: "There used to be many more aspects than just us."

"As for who I was originally... by now I don't really know."

"I was created when Zachary Motte's Emptiness aspect..." -The sword in his left hand turns white.-

"Consumed his Rage aspect." -The sword in his right hand turns gray.-

"And when you combine emptiness and rage..."

Jacob: "When your soul is voided and you pour rage into it... you do nothing but create a vessel filled with hate..."

RedEyes!Zeal: "That's one way of putting it."

-His wound heals, and he resumes attacking Jacob.-

RedEyes!Zeal: "And that monster... consumed all the other aspects, one by one... and eventually... that beast became me."

Jacob: -Stands up and readies himself but the yellow blade is knocked out of his hand and sinks beneath the lake and he staggers backwards, falling back on one knee again-

"...."

-He manages to catch both of Red Eyes' swords on the white edge of his sword- "What is a man... then? Are you human? Because you are a collection of several assimilated aspects of a personality? Because you're more than just a fragment? Is that what you think?! Is that why you call yourself Zachary Motte, you think yourself complete enough to be called truly human?"

-He grits his teeth as he tries to prevent Red Eyes from overpowering him-

RedEyes!Zeal: "No. I'm not foolish enough to consider myself human. I'm a monster, and that's all I've ever been. But..."

-Jumps back and charges at Jacob once again.-

RedEyes!Zeal: "You yourself said... "No one man can understand what's best for the whole"... Correct, Jacob Iridis...?"

Jacob: -Rears back, winds up, and meets Red Eyes' charge with a heavy slash- "Yes I just said that! Your point is...?"

-A small crack appears in the middle of Jacob's sword-

RedEyes!Zeal: "I am not one man."

-He pushes forward with more force.-

"I am far more than that!"

Jacob: "You...!" -The force of RedEyes!Zeal's advance breaks through Jacob's guard, creating a large gash in his right shoulder, and sent skidding backwards when topples over on to the lake's surface-

-The sword cracked even further, and the water beginning to turn red-

"You are nothing but a shadow trying to play god, and I-" -He pushes himself to his feet-

"I am the one about to blot out that shade forever!" -He takes the sword from his right hand-

"You know what's great about me, Red Eyes? I'm ambidextirous. BOTH HANDS!"

RedEyes!Zeal: "Ngh..."

-He vanishes.-

Jacob: "Run all you want! You already know I'm the one going to come out of this victoriously! You are a blight, you are nothing but a shade of my friend. And I will.

End.

You."

-RedEyes!Zeal appears over the water once again, a wall made up of swords of various colors behind him.-

Jacob: -Eyes go wide- "............."

-Then his face hardens and he readies his sword- "The future does not belong to you!"

RedEyes!Zeal: "Maybe not... But it doesn't belong to you, either."

-The blades go flying at Jacob one-by-one in quick succession.-

Jacob: -He swings his sword- "You-"

-And again, knocking a sword off course- "Will-"

-He reacts slowly this time, a sword impaling itself in his side- "NEVER-"

-He deflects another blade, but his own sword cracks further- "WIN!"

-With one last act of defiance he throws his own sword, shattering it and again blocking the incoming projectile-

-He looks at the incoming rain of blades with indifference as they approach-

-In a flash, he's left almost like a pincushion, laying on the surface of the lake-
