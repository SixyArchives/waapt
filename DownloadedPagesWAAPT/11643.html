 291075 Full Moon, Sun, 15th Sep '13 6:44:01 PM from Kalos Get RP Mod
Instant Awesome, Just Add Ninja

Lacunosa Town

Lyuri and Duke somewhere around in the city, sitting on a bench, it was drizzling in the city, but neither of them seemed to care, Lyuri's bag was open and Duke saw her sketchbook in it

"<Say, Lyuri?>"

"Yeah?"

"<You mind if I take a look on your sketchbook?>"

Lyuri hesistates a bit before answering "Well... No, but the water drops would ruin the drawing."

Duke raises an eyebrow, but doesn't think much of her hesitation "<Oh, right. I can look at it later.>"

They then just stay in the same place for a while, but then the rain started to grow and made Lyuri get up and take an umbrella out of her bag, accidentally dropping her sketchbook in the process, Duke picked it up while Lyuri opened the umbrella which Duke quickly got under.

"Well, let's head to the Pokémon Center and call it a night then." said Lyuri, with a small smile.

Duke nods and follows Lyuri under the umbrella, he looks at the sketchbook curiously and decides to open it, he saw many scribbles that were probably done by a little kid on the first few pages, but as he ran through the pages he saw the art getting better and better, many of the drawing he had already seen before, but then he saw a new one that was Lyuri in a rather cute looking outfit and carrying a staff, it had no color so it made it harder for him to guess what was the staff's material; he found the drawing rather amusing, but as he ran through the pages he saw more and more of the same kind of drawing and at some point he started seeing some drawings of Sakura as well.

"<What's with these drawing with you and Sakura dressing in cute outfits?>" asked Duke, quite curious.

Lyuri then goes a bit red "It's... Just something that came into mind at some point."

"<You did a lot of drawing of it though and I never saw you draw on more than one page.>"

"... Well, it's just something from a couple of dreams I've had since I was a little kid. I practiced my drawing a lot before attempting to draw what I saw in them." she explained, still a bit red.

"<Dreams?>"

Lyuri gets even more red "Yeah... When I was a kid I used to fantasize that I was a magician that could make use of a lot of magic to beat villains."

Duke chuckles a bit "<So, your dream was to be a magical girl?>"

"W-Well... Yeah, I still have some dreams about it every now and then and when I'm bored I can sometimes daydream about it." Lyuri then looks at the night sky "It's silly, but I've always wanted to be able to use magic and that stuff, you know, be a magician. I know that it's fantasy, but it's just something that I wished it was real sometimes."

Duke lets out a chuckle "<You're such a child at heart.>"

Lyuri then looks at Duke and sighs "It's embarrassing, I know."

"<No, it's not something bad. I guess real life can be a bit hard sometimes, so living inside your fantasies can be a good way to find some peace after a bad day. These are some really good drawings too, you must have a lot of creativity to come up with those outfits.>" said Duke, smiling.

"Well... Thanks, I guess." said Lyuri, now smiling as well.

Duke then looks at some other pages and sees that the last pages are lot of drawings of himself in human form, though the first couple had him in both Pokémon and Human form.

"<That's... A lot of drawing of me.>" he said.

"Well... I guess I've been inspired to draw you lately." said Lyuri, blushing a small bit again "The first few are just some random drawings I made of you, the rest are about how I fantasize you in my personal Dream World, you might see that there's no differences whatsoever and well, I guess you were just perfect as you were."

Duke smiles "<So... How is this Dream World of yours?>"

"Well, I always picture us as a happy little married couple, me being a magician and you being a martial arts fighter that is an expert in Aura. I hope you don't feel offended for me always thinking of you as a human in these."

"<Not at all. So, I'm the warrior and you the sorcerer?>"

"Pretty much, I thought it would work out well, you're like that in real life as well anyway." said Lyuri, chuckling a bit

"<How about Sakura?>"

"I picture she would have the ability to make plants grow and would have a little flower shop. Oh, and she would also go around making flowers grow around places to make them prettier and also taking care of sick people with medicinal herbs."

"<Seems like the kind of thing she'd do if she had those powers. What would we do then?>"

"Well... Um... I always think of us as a couple of heroes who around helping with any threats that may come while living together in a house somewhere and having fun when not doing anything important." said Sakura, now getting really embarrassed "I know, it's stupid."

"<It's nothing to be ashamed of>" said Duke, a bit amused with her embarrassment.

Lyuri cheers up a bit "Maybe... It's just fantasy after all."

"<Well... I might not be able to give you or Sakura powers, but I'll try my best to make so that we can be a "happy little married couple" in real life too, it's certainly something I'd love to have happen, even if there might be a lot of complications in the way.>" said Duke, smiling.

Lyuri blinks, then smiles and pulls Duke closer to her and hugs him from the side "Human or Pokémon, I love you, don't forget about that, all right?"

Duke smiles "<Wouldn't dare to.>"

They then arrive at the Pokémon Center, Duke stores the sketchbook in Lyuri's bag again and she closes her umbrella.

"Do you want to go back to your Pokéball or...?"

"<I think I'll asleep outside of it, I find the sound of rain quite relaxing so I'll have a better night of sleep.>"

"Right, I'm going to ask for an extra matress or something for you to sleep on then since there's only one bed."

A few minutes later, Lyuri put a second mattress on the ground of her room and was getting ready to sleep herself.

"Try not to turn around too much on the bed or else you're going to make a hole on it." said Lyuri.

"<I'll try. By the way, this will sound ridiculous but...>" said Duke with quite the sheepish look in his face

"Yeah?"

"<If you have another of those dreams, can you tell me what happens in them?>" he asked, chuckling a bit at his own silliness.

Lyuri smiles "I'll try." she said.

Duke nods and they both lie down on their beds and just stood there hearing the sound of the rain falling, which was quite soothing and making them more and more sleepy each second that passed by.

"Good night." they said to each other.

They both then fell into a peaceful sleep.
