 107802 Crewe, Tue, 1st Nov '11 1:48:53 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
After a few minutes of waiting, Crewe spots the other Trainer walking towards her and goes to meet her. The two walk into the empty arena together, and after a quick word with the janitor working there secure permission for a battle. They take their places at opposite ends of the arena and release their two Pokemon.

Xatu and Skywalker eye each other, Xatu looking rather unperturbed, while Skywalker opens and closes his wings nervously.

"Let's go! Skywalker, in the air!" Crewe yells, and the Salamence takes off like a rocket, straight upwards.

"Signal Beam, Xatu! Go!" The other Trainer shouts her own command. Her Pokemon turns upwards towards Skywalker and fires its attack. It singes Skywalker's wings as he levels out and circles high above the battlefield, and he winces but continues flying.

"Time to shatter the battle! Earthquake!" Crewe stressed the word 'shatter', praying Skywalker remembers their training sessions.

The Salamence twitches his tail, then folds his wings and plummets back towards the ground, looking for all the world like he did during his suicidal Earthquake attack on Leaf's Pikachu back at the GC.

The Xatu's trainer gives Crewe a weird look before turning to her Pokemon.

"Get off the ground, Xatu!"

The other Pokemon opens its wings and gets into the air, Skywalker coming ever closer to the ground.

Just as the other Trainer is about to give the command to attack, Crewe yells, "NOW!"

Skywalker's wings flare open and he shoots off sideways across the arena, pulling up dangerously close to the ground. He extends his claws and sends up massive shards of rock in a Stone Edge behind him. He zig-zags rapidly across the arena, flinging up bits of the ground in his path. His attack is spread all across the arena, giving Xatu no room to evade, and his speed and erratic flying pattern prevents him from presenting a clear target for an attack.

"Now drive him down! Don't let him escape!"

Skywalker zooms upwards again, flipping over in midair so he's facing the Xatu and lets out a huge Dragon Pulse, sending it right over the Xatu's head. The other Pokemon instinctively ducks to avoid it, running right into the Stone Edge.

"Get above him!"

Skywalker levels out again, moving over towards the Xatu without letting up on the Dragon Pulse.

"Finish him now! Shadow Claw!"

He dives down, still firing his Dragon Pulse, the Xatu floundering between it and the Stone Edge and trying to get off an attack. Skywalker comes at him with his claws wreathed in Ghost-type Obscura, and slashes at him, sending him careening to the ground.

Crewe shouts triumphantly and punches the air. Skywalker slowly circles down and lands heavily, sniffing the fallen Xatu, his eyes wide. The other trainer recalls her Pokemon and Skywalker turns slowly towards Crewe, then lets out a joyful roar and launches himself into the air, sending out a celebratory Dragon Pulse and lighting up the sky.
