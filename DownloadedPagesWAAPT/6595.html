Help... My hand's stuck.
Somewhere in the multiverse...

-is doing here...

The half-sentence drifted out into nothingness, as within the instant he touched the shard, Ken... Ceased to be himself. His form was quickly reduced into one he couldn't see... Or truly understand. His eyes ceased to be, yet he could see a swirling mass of colours all around him. His ears ceased to be, yet he could hear a soothing melody from nowhere and everywhere at once. His touch ceased to be, yet he could feel a warmth deep within his being, and all around him at once.

The world seemed to hang in place around him. One moment blended into the next, with no sense of time whatsoever in this place. A nexus of energy, life, and stimuli.

And yet... He wasn't there to wait. As the initial shock began to wear off, and the soothing world around ceased to dazzle the trainer beyond his ability to think, a thought came to his mind.

...Where am I?

His thoughts echoed through the space, even as Ken realized he didn't have a mouth to voice his question.

His question wasn't answered by a single, descriptive voice, however... More like a presence that merely made itself... It's will... known.

You are the next to journey on.

Wha-...?! Who... Who are you?

Questions. Questions of being, questions of circumstance. As you have given them to me, I shall give them to you... As I have to the others before.

Others... Then... What questions are they?

The first... You are faced with a chance. A chance to take what you and others desire is before you. Your response...?

The question was vague... Ken's mind began to swim as it found itself questioning exactly how it would respond. To take it for himself, to leave it where it was, to share it, or to give it to those closest... Choices appeared in his mind, and before the trainer had realized what happened, a choice was selected. Ken could hear his voice respond on it's own.

I would give it to those closest to me.

Self-sacrifice... You are asked to overcome a difficult ordeal, that you may not succeed at. Your response...?

Again, the answer seemed to be extracted from Ken's mind before he could grasp the question in his consciousness.

I would act as best I could, and hope for the best.

Headstrong... You are given a choice of 4 different attributes to gain. Enough wealth to live as you wish, the knowledge to act on what you desire, the power to protect that which you want, or the love of whomever you hope. Your first selection...?

The power to protect that which I want.

That answer, Ken was able to choose for himself before the voice seemed to choose for him. And it made sense... The power to protect those around him sounded better than being loved by them.

A desire for enough strength... The last question. What is your greatest weakness.

This question seemed... Different from the others. As if it had a different meaning than the others. The answer came out almost instantly anyway, as before.

My indecision about what path I follow.

Indecision... Very well.

As the last question was answered, the world began to turn once more. As if time was beginning to move again. Ken's mind resurfaced in this instance, overcoming the blissful warmth and speaking up yet again.

...ey.... Hey.... Hey..! ...You! I answered your que...ons, now answer ... own!

Something was shifting. Ken's own form. His voice, his thoughts... Whatever they were, they were beginning to fade in and out. The presence spoke anyway.

You are at the crossroads of all things. I am the one who began... Not the original... But the first. You will be next to cross. Desiring to be strong, to care for others at your own detriment, but without the wisdom to know when that is best, or the resolution to know where... Yet. You are the Feeling.

Ken's form now began to take another shape, though he still could not see just what, yet. He still had one question, though.

Then... Why... Are you do..g this...?

I grow stronger here. Guiding those who cross to the forms they are... From my own being. Soon I will be allowed to leave... But not yet. You must go now... Ken.

As his name was spoken, the entire world before him, the lights, the sounds, the warmth, the presence... Vanished and was replaced with an empty black. No feeling, no sight, no sound. Nothing but a void for a few fleeting moments. But as quickly as it had come, the void flashed away yet again...

And lying face-down atop a field of grass, a little Ralts with a grey scarf around it's neck opened it's eyes, and took it's first gasp of air in a new world.

Give me a mind, I will play with it. A character, I will roleplay it. Not so different.
