Dream World

Comet: <Hey!>

Treeo: <Hi...>

Comet: <How goes the part hunt?>

Treeo shakes his head, letting out a sigh.

Treeo: <Well...Mezzo and Every's group may just be the worst I have ever worked with.>

Comet frowns.

Comet: <That bad, huh?>

Treeo nods, as he walks over to a rather large rock to sun himself on.

Treeo: <How else do you describe a bunch of Pokémon that stop in the middle of an underwater cavern filled with feral Pokémon to have a shipping discussion?>

Comet stares, blinking in disbelief.

Comet: <...You're kidding me, right?>

Treeo: <Oh, I wish I was making this up...>

Treeo lowers his head, a hint of annoyance and frustration crossing his face.

<At the rate we're going, we're not going to find that part of that spaceship or whatever for a long while.>

Comet: <Oh, joy.>

Treeo glances back up, tilting his head at Comet.

Treeo: <Let me guess, you're not having much fun either?>

Comet nods in response.

Comet: <Yeah, it's boring.>

Treeo: <Boring?>

Comet: <Well, there's nothing much to do, and the only Pokémon to talk to are Fortis, Salamence, Cerebus and Murumasa.>

Treeo: <Oh? Are they not the best conversation partners?>

Comet: <No. Fortis and Salamence are sweet, but they're both naive. Cerebus only wants to talk about fire, and how she wants to set everything on fire. Believe me, I like fire as much as the next Charizard, but I know better than to think that everything would be better if you added fire to it.>

Treeo: <And Muramasa?>

Comet shrugs.

Comet: <I don't know what's up with her. She barely ever speaks, and when she does, it's only one word at a time. I can't tell if she's shy, antisocial, or never learned to say more than one word at a time.>

Treeo: <...Huh.>

Comet nods, crossing her arms.

Comet: <So It seems we're both having it rough right now, eh?>

Treeo: <Tell me about it. Of course, it's worse for me since we could die from drowning, or being eaten by a feral Pokémon.>

Comet: <I won't argue with you on that one, but...>

She frowns sadly.

<...I wish there was more I could do to help...>

Treeo: <Oh? But you are helping...>

Comet: <I am?>

Treeo nods, smiling slyly.

Treeo: <Just being able to vent to you here is going a long way to keep me sane!>

Finding this funny, Comet smirks, chuckling to herself.

Comet: <Heh, well if I can keep you from going mad and killing everymon there...>

Treeo: <HEY! I would never do that, even if they are annoying!

...Well...maybe if ticked me off just enough...>
