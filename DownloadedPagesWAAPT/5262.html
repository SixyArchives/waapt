Route 206, Sinnoh (Future)

A herd of several Ponyta grazed around the grasslands, roaming freely without fear of capture or being bothered by trainers. A few chewed at a few choice bushes, while others butt heads in playful battle. The adults seemed content with watching the foals horse around, occasionally stepping in to prevent them from hurting themselves. Observing this all from a small distance away, a trainer and his own Ponyta stepped a bit closer, mumbling quietly to themselves.

"So, you want some alone time with your family?" Rex asked, removing himself from Aloise's back. "Are you nervous about how they'll react to you having a trainer?"

<Not necessarily, > she murmured, grinding a hoof absently against the ground. <It's just...I would appreciate being able to speak with my herd as a member, not a Trainer's Pokemon.>

"...Makes sense, " Rex agreed after a moment, nodding his head. He gave the Ponyta a pat on her flank, smiling warmly at her. "Take all the time that you need. We'll be around if you need us."

<Thank you, > she whinnied softly, nuzzling his side gently. He grinned, wrapping his arms around her neck in a gentle hug before pulling away, the Ponyta cautiously making her way to the group before them. After a moment, she paused and turned back to him.

<...I've heard there is a cave on this route housing exotic species of Pokemon. Perhaps you could spend your time there?>

Rex smiled and nodded. "Sure thing. Thanks for the info."

With that, Aloise nodded and broke into a mild sprint, approaching her herd with a loud whinny to alert them. Almost immediately she was swarmed by several of the foals, the group looking pleased to recognize her return.

"Hmm...guess she's more popular than I thought, " Rex mumbled, wondering just who Aloise was to command such respect. Shrugging and going about his business, he made a mental note to ask her later, releasing the other five Pokemon he had on hand.
