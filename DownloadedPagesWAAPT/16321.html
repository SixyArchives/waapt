Storytime!

Once, there was a Pancham. Let's call her Natalie. "Nat" to her friends, if she had more than just a Furfrou as a friend. The Furfrou was a good friend, but he was a bit of a downer.

"It's a nice day out, " said Natalie, laying in the sun. "It'll rain tomorrow. I can tell, " said the Furfrou, sticking his head in a pond to wash off Fletchinder droppings.

"I just beat up a Bunnelby that was giving me trouble!" said Natalie, dancing in happiness at her triumph. "Just be ready for a bigger enemy that will hurt you, " said the Furfrou, before a Fletchinder relieved itself on his head.

"This flower is really pretty!" said Natalie, smelling it. "Eventually, everything will die, " said the Furfrou. "I will die, you will die, the world will die, the sun will die, the universe will die, and that flower will die." Then a Fletchinder shat on his head.

Eventually, Natalie got tired of her friend's downer attitude, and looked for new friends. After a while, she found a Scraggy, who was very friendly to her. This Scraggy wasn't like the Furfrou. Instead of being morose and pessimistic, this Scraggy was positive, cocky, and very rarely covered in Fletchinder droppings. Natalie and the Scraggy fought together, played together, laid in the flowers together.

One day, after a fight against a bunch of Skiddo, the Scraggy turned into a Scrafty! What's more, Natalie turned into a Pangoro!

The Scraggy celebrated this fact by running forward and performing a flying jump kick to a Furfrou. Natalie recognized this Furfrou as her friend, and immediately felt a flood of guilt. By getting with a bad influence, Natalie had forgotten her first friend!

After a moment to consider this, she decided to go off with this Scrafty, eventually deciding to mate. She left the Furfrou laying on the grass, ignored, beaten, and waiting for the impending Fletchinder to fly over his head.
