Peeko(?)'s Boat

A certain Feraligatr and Salamence, who got up a little while agao, are taking a little time to talk before they reach Johto.

Lake: <I think you're gonna' love Bluescale. It's the perfect place for both Feraligatr and Salamence, for...obvious reasons.>

Nadia: <Hmm...If you say so.>

Lake gives Nadia a confused look.

Lake: <Why so apprehensive?>

She gives him the Salamence equivalent of a shrug in return.

Nadia: <I don't know, it just seems a bit far-fetched? I can't really see you founding an entire town...>

She frowns.

<Or me, for that matter.>

Giving her a sorry look, Lake pats the Salamence's scales gently.

Lake: <I'll admit, when I first found out that me and you founded that place, I couldn't quite believe it myself. I make a good team leader, sure. But that's only five other Pokémon. But a whole community? That's pushing it just a little...

But I'll admit, when I found out that all this is the future of that other universe - and not our own, like I first assumed - I was both relieved...but a little disappointed.>

Nadia tilts her head quizzically.

Nadia: <Why?>

Lake: <Because it meant that Bluescale wasn't our work at all. It was the other me and you that did it. I was a little upset to find that something I thought me and you had done was all the work of somemon else, even if that somemon else is nearly the same Pokémon as you. Alternate universes are weird like that.>

Nadia: <Tell me about it. So, you're unhappy that the other Lake and Nadia did it and not us?>

Lake nods somewhat glumly.

Lake: <We've sure got our work cut out for us trying to live up to them...>

Again, Nadia gives the Salamence equivalent of a shrug.

Nadia: <So why do we have to live up to what they did? It's not a competition! Just because they went and founded a town doesn't mean we have to as well. Besides, you have a whole laundry list of things to be proud of! You beat the Johto League, finished off what was left of Team Rocket, and there's also the little matter of, you know, being Ho-oh's chosen one? And that's just the start!>

Lake blinks, a little taken aback.

Lake: <...I...I guess I do have a lot to be proud of, don't I?>

Nadia nods affirmatively.

Nadia: <So who cares if it wasn't you and me specifically that built that place? You've done more than enough to be happy about! It's not like you really need this too, do you?>

Lake: <...Yeah, you're right. In the past few years, me - and you - have accomplished more than most Pokémon in their entire lifetimes...Wow, we're really lucky, aren't we?>

Nadia: <Eh...perhaps lucky isn't the right word. More like we always happen to be in the wrong place at the right time.>

Lake can't help but chuckle at this.

Lake: <Heh. That's a real good way of putting it...>

Elsewhere...

A certain other Feraligatr has decided to talk to a certain other Salamence, having not really had the time to say hello to him properly.

Luke: <Hey, so...you're name's Toothless, right?>

The Salamence nods.

Toothless: <Yeah, that's my name.>

Luke chuckles at this.

Luke: <Heh. It's funny, you have the same name as this little Salamence plushie that belongs to Nadia.>

Toothless says nothing, having realized that Luke hasn't made the connection between him and the aforementioned plushie.

Toothless: <Oh, well...there's a reason for that.>

Luke: <There is?>

Luke gives him a quizzical look.

<What do you mean? Are you named after him?>

He pauses to think for a few seconds, then nods.

Toothless: <Well...let me be honest with you, Luke. You know that plushie you mentioned?>

Luke: <...Yes...>

The Salamence pauses, taking a breath.

Toothless: <I...am that plushie. Or rather, I was.>

The Feraligatr blinks, staring blankly.

Luke: <...Huh?>

Toothless: <Listen, you know that world with only Pokémon? The one you visited in the past with Lake?>

Luke: <Oh, yes! I was there a few days ago, actually.>

Toothless: <...How?>

Luke: <Long story, but go on.>

Toothless: <Okay...well, Nadia and some of the others spent a bit of time there before we ended up in this world with Lake and Stratus, and during that time we formed an Exploration Team - Team Uncharted.>

Luke: <Oh...fitting name.>

Toothless: <Yeah, it was Gene's idea. Anyway, we did the usual Exploration Team stuff, until this one mission in which we had to catch an outlaw. We were travelling through one of those strange "Mystery Dungeons" they have in that world when we came to this large room where we were ambushed by a large group of Feral Pokémon.>

Luke gives the Salamence a worried look.

Luke: <Ouch...we're you all okay?>

Toothless nods affirmatively.

Toothless: <Of course. We're all made of stern stuff. But anyway, in the confusion, one of the Feral mons sliced open Gene's bag - which I was being carried in at the time - and I sipped out, along with that evil Potion...>

He snarls as he recalls how suffering that awful thing had put him through.

<Anyway, what happened next was a bit of a blur, but in the confusion, somemon stood on this weird tile that filled the room with odd smoke, and the next thing I knew...I wasn't a plushie anymore.>

Luke says nothing, simply staring with a look of pure shock on his face.

Luke: <...

... ...

... ... ...What.>
