A youthful, blue haired girl wakes up in her mansion in Undella Town, Unova. Being the daughter of a wealthy painter and researcher hailing from Dewford Town back in Hoenn, she was able to get practically anything she ever wanted. After training in the Hoenn, Kanto and Johto she decided to see what Unova was like and explored the region. Using her start-up money her parents gave her, she was able to buy a villa in Undella Town and everything seemed like Sunshine and Oranges.

Until Artemis and Scolipede took over the damn region, that’s where things went wrong. But this girl, Sol, was smart enough to know that an allegiance with these two forces would pay off in the end, and besides, her most trusted Pokemon and right-hand mon, Napoleon the Emboar, said it would benefit both of them greatly. So she agreed to fund their projects all over Unova and agree on setting up some projects in Undella town itself. Napoleon, who’s charisma was unbound, became their propagandist yet still works from Undella Town

She picked a few select Pokemon from her journeys to Kanto and Johto to become her personal bodyguards. She trained these Pokemon to be ruthless assassins and to murder on whim if she pleased. With these Pokemon, so took over Undella Town and turned it into a secret base for Artemis and Scolipede’s forces. By posing it as a refuge town to those who have been oppressed by Artemis and Scolipede’s rule, she was able to see many trainers and Pokemon a like that showed potential and could be used to further the duo’s scheme. The citizens of the city know this very well, but don’t say anything about it. After all, it is better to pay a hefty ransom then be personally “Dealt with” by her sanguinary bodyguards.
