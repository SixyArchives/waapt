Nidoran #1 — <Marli ma!>

Lapis -delighted — <See, kits? Mom is done talking with your uncles already. Now, who wants grass?>

Nidoran #2 — <Wheeeeee~!> — prances around — <Nomtime! :3>

Nidoran #3 -hesitantly — <I can has a berry?>

Lapis — <Berries are for tomorrow, now...>

Lapis — <Now, kits, say good bye to uncles!> — waves a paw.

Nidoran kits — <Oh! Good bye to uncles! \ô ô ô/>

Maekrite's sight focuses on the family, and he feels a bit pained.

Maekrite — «Uncle Maekrite...»

Lapis -smiles, turns to the Nidorino — <Are we set to go, émar?>

Lapis's Mate -nods — <I have a new burrow all ready, from the northern side of the creek this time...>
