Lapis (Nidorina) -flickers ears, turns to the side — <Aaand that's my call, so I have to leave you here...> — smooches Maekrite and one of the other siblings — <Care, Maekrite. You too, Acute.> — grins to another Nidorina — <Don't need to say anything, eh, Lenia?>

The aforementioned Nidorina giggles.

Acute (Nidorino) -nods — <Relax, I'm only going to explore Rt.37...>

Lenia, Maekrite and Acute watch the female to go a nearby ledge. Maekrite tilts his head when he sees a nearby thicket moving and three small Nidoran appearing from it.

Nidoran #1 — <Marli ma!>

Lapis -delighted — <See, kits? Mom is done talking with your uncles already. Now, who wants grass?>

Nidoran #2 — <Wheeeeee~!> — prances around — <Nomtime! :3>

Nidoran #3 -hesitantly — <I can has a berry?>

Lapis — <Berries are for tomorrow, now...>

The Nidorina starts gathering and licking her kits, who resist playfully. A Nidorino emerges from the thicket too and shares a knowing look and some nudges with Lapis, who then finishes gathering her family and turns to where Maekrite is.

Lapis — <Now, kits, say good bye to uncles!> — waves a paw.

Nidoran kits — <Oh! Good bye to uncles! \\ô ô ô/>

Maekrite's sight focuses on the family, and he feels a bit pained.

Maekrite — «Uncle Maekrite...»

He, Acute and Lenia wave their paws to the kits, who playfully wave paws back even faster. Lenia can't help but giggle.

Lapis -smiles, turns to the Nidorino — <Are we set to go, émar?>

Lapis's Mate -nods — <I have a new burrow all ready, from the northern side of the creek this time...>

After the Nidorin@ couple share a hug and turn tails, the kits following, Maekrite and his siblings nuzzle for goodbyes.

Acute — <Yeah, this is my signal too... need to sleep well now that I can...>

Lenia — <I guess we are all leaving...> — turns to Maekrite — <Take care, rusati éan✦
"old brother"
.>

Maekrite is staring down at the direction Lapis left, eyes a bit closed. He suddenly feels his shoulder rubbed by a horn.

Acute -lends an ear — <Rusati éan?>

Maekrite — <I'm OK...> — steps away and hesitantly stares back for a moment — <I think I'm gonna say goodbye to our parents...>

Maekrite bows to Lenia and Acute; after the two Nidorin@ bow back to him, he leaves walking away slowly. His siblings don't think too much about it and they say their goodbyes as well to depart to their places.
