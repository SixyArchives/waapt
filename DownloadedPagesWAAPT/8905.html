UTINNI!

Somewhere north of Kanto/Johto

While Runt was off retrieving Groot, Jack took the time to change out of his wet clothes and into something dryer. The good news was that his bag - designed with these kind of unexpected circumstances in mind - had worked as intended. He hadn't lasted as long as he had by doing things like going out into the wild and not being prepared for situations like finding himself taking an unexpected dip.

But that still left his old clothing as wet as ever. Figuring he had time to kill while he waited for Runt, Jack hung the clothes from the branch of a nearby tree. The lake certainly had plenty of them to spare. But as he was about to lay his old wet shirt over a branch, something flew out of the tree and nearly clocked him on the head.

Wild Attacking Gligar: GLI!

Jack: "Oh $#...!"

The man ducked under the flying pokemon's attack.

Jack: "I don't suppose I could convince you to leave peacfully, could I?"

The Gligar answered by leaping into the air and gliding at Jack for a second attempted attack.

Jack: "I'll take that as a no!"

Knowing how less than ideal his situation was, Jack went for what - sadly - was his best option. On the other hand, he also knew that if there was one thing Penny loved more than trying to make him miserable, it was fighting other pokemon.

Jack: "Guess what, Penny. It's fighting time!"

For the second time in the past hour, Jack called his Beedrill out of her pokeball and into the fight. While the pokemon emerged in a burst of light, he proceeded to activate a small technical device that would give him a more detailed readout of the fight than he could discern with his own eyes - the closest thing he had to a genuine pokedex.

As the Beedrill emerged and hovered close to the ground, she and her opponent focused on each other and locked eyes. And while it wasn't at all apparent to Jack, there was a subtle spark that was set off the moment the two pokemon had a chance to read each other's body language and what was being said through their eyes.

Angry Wild Gligar: <"Hello there.">

The Gligar rushed forwards at Penny with a Quick Attack, but one that clearly wasn't meant to hit when Penny dodged it by inches.

Penny (Who Rarely Speaks): <"Hello yourself.">

Jack was surprised by the easy with which Penny had gotten out of the way, but wasn't about to question his good fortune.

Jack: "Uh...good move! Keep up with Agility till you find an opening, then hit him with Fury Attack!"

The Beedrill spread her wings and increased the speed of her buzzing. The Gligar swung around and flew back in Penny's direction but took a hard left at the last moment, causing Penny to pursue. After a few yards the Gligar twisted around and let Penny catch up with him. The Beedrill launched into her attack just as Jack had ordered, but to his surprised, all the swings missed entirely despite how close they were. And he could almost swear she was missing on purpose.

Penny (Who Rarely Speaks): <"I sense a lot of anger in you.">

Angry Wild Gligar: <"You're one to talk.">

Jack (Who has no idea what's going on): "Penny?"

Penny jabbed at the Gligar with one of her stingers, but the Gligar easily blocked it, and the two pokemon used the strike to back away from one another.

Jack: "Ok, let's try that again."

The Gligar and Beedrill flew at one another, and Penny began stabbing her drills in the Gligar's direction, each one easily being blocked, almost as if it was some kind of rehearsed routine. Their movements mere feet above the ground only added to the effect, and left Jack with a confused look on his face.

Angry Wild Gligar: <"Does this angry Beedrill have a name?">

Penny (Who's said more in the past few minutes than she usually says in a month.) <"The idiot over there who calls himself my 'trainer' refers to me as 'Penny'". And what do you go by?>

Angry Wild Gliger: <"I go by many names...">

A solid but just as deflected attack once again separated the two, leaving them standing several feet apart. The Gliger nearly said something else, but was disrupted by a rumbling from the ground.

Angry Wild Gligar & Penny: <?>

The rumbling was, as Jack would have hoped for, caused by Groot, who burst out of the ground into the sky right underneath where the Gligar had been 'standing', sending him flying off into the distance.

Penny: [Is Speechless]

Penny: [But in a shocked way.]

Penny: [Not her normal angry, brooding way.]

Penny: [Because she's normally speechless anyway.]

Penny: [But not like this.]

The Beedrill just watched, dumbfounded, as the pokemon she had been 'fighting' flew out of sight faster than she could possibly follow. But as she was left in a state of shock, Jack was left in a state of relief to know that Groot was back.

Runt: (TP) Looks who's back!

Runt was there two, currently standing on top of the Onix's head and grabbing onto the "fin" that stuck up out of the center.

Jack: "I'm glad to see that!"

Runt: (TP) We miss anything?

Jack: "You could say that. But the important thing is I didn't die while you were gone."
