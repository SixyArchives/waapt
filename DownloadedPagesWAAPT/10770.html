Castelia City, Ren's Penthouse

-Ren gets up early and makes himself tea. Ren sits at the table his tablet out and speaks to Gamma-

Gamma: Affirmative Master.

Ren: Good what can you tell me about it?

-Gamma brings up a satellite image of circular landmass-

Gamma: The Holon Region is a peninsula located North of Kanto and is connected to the mainland by a narrow landbridge. The Holon Region also has a small archipelago associated with it. The "strange storms" Lady Creselia mentioned mostly likely means the storms of radiation and magnetic energies caused by the the equipment in the Holon Research Tower in Holon City, Holon.

Ren: Holon Research Tower? Whats that?

-Gamma diplays an image of the tower-

Gamma: It is a facility specifically constructed in order to manipulate the unique magnetic properties of the area to produce electromagnetic energy that hoped to aid scientists to track Mew in Mirage Forest. Through a process called "Deltification", the energy storms produced by the tower cause the local Pokémon to mutate into abnormal types inconsistent with their genes elsewhere.

Ren: Wait I've heard of those Pokémon what were they called again?

Gamma: The affected Pokémon came to be known as δ Species, or the more common term Delta Pokémon. Delta Pokémon can have a type added to their normal type or be of an entirely different type, with the Steel-type prominent among fully evolved Pokémon.

Ren: Is Deltaification only limited to the local Pokémon?

Gamma: Negative Master. Any Pokémon can be Deltified. Pokémon who has been exposed long enough to the radiation of the storms Deltify. A Pokémon can also be Deltified during the process of hatching from an egg or during evolution, due to the rapid change of the body's make-up. And finally, a Pokémon can be Deltified with stones called Delta Crystals. Delta Crystals also have the property of reversing the Deltification process.

Ren: Anything else I should know?

Gamma: I am unable to give a proper response Master. There are to many variables at this moment. However, I will alert you as soon as I have analyzed the situation further.
