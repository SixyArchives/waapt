Slateport City: Battle Tent

May and Felix wander in, mingling with a small crowd of people. Before long, a scientist steps forward, clearing his throat and beginning to speak.

Scientist: Welcome to Slateport's Battle Tent. Our purpose is to enlighten you on the various facilities hosted by the Battle Frontier. For the moment, we are offering insight on the nature of the Battle Factory. The Battle Factory is a facility that features using rental Pokemon in place of a trainer's standard team. This is done for two reasons:

1. To test a trainer's capability at handling Pokemon of different strength levels. For example, a strong Pokemon in the hands of a mediocre trainer might still end up losing to a weaker Pokemon in the hands of a great trainer.

2. To gather research data on Pokemon battle potential when being used by various trainers.

Simply put, to win a Battle Factory challenge or apply for a job in the facility, one must have a sufficient amount of Knowledge. Battle Factories are a very ground-breaking in Pokemon research, prompting Johto and Sinnoh to make a joint-facility shared between their regions.

The Battle Factory is headed by a Factory Head, the current Hoenn Head being a man named Noland. As his title suggests, he leads the research of the Factory by challenging trainers. Much like others who take part in the Frontier Challenge, he uses a team of rental Pokemon himself. Trainers who defeat Noland will earn the Silver Knowledge Symbol the first time and the Gold Knowledge Symbol the second. It is proof of that trainer's understanding of the Pokemon that they used.
