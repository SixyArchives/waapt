An Apartment Building, Gateon City

-Kyrie sits on her bed in her small apartment. It looks something like the aftermath of a hurricane in the middle of a music store. Tons of sheet music, both original and arrangements, are strewn all throughout the room. Kyrie stands up, picks a sheet at random, and walks across the room before picking the next page from the middle of a pile as though she knew exactly where it was. She returns to the bed with a pencil to work on it before her X-Transceiver rings-

Arceus, right when I sit down...

-she answers-

Hello?

-on the other end is a blond, middle aged married couple. The husband is wearing glasses and looks rather unassuming. His wife, on the other hand, looks much stronger and willful. It gives the impression that she's taking part in a conversation with her accountant by her side. When she speaks, she has a very faint Scandinavian accent-

Kyrie's Mom: Hi, Valkyrie!

-Kyrie smiles in return-

Hi, Mom! Hi, Dad! How are you?

Kyrie's Dad: Good, good! And you? Being careful in Orre?

Yeah, yeah. Don't worry about me.

Kyrie's Mom: You really shouldn't, you know! She's named Valkyrie for a reason!

Kyrie's Dad: I still don't think that was a good idea, Greta...

Kyrie's Mom: If I had my way, she wouldn't have Elaine as a middle name, either.

Kyrie's Dad: You try arguing with my mother. Anyway, we just called to give you some good news!

-Kyrie places both hands to her head in mock surprise-

Don't tell me you're having another baby!

Kyrie's MomGreta: You were good enough for us, don't worry. We did get something else, though!

-she holds up a baby Purrloin to the phone-

Say hello to Felicia, Valkyrie!

Felicia: <Purr~>

-she waves a paw at the camera, and Kyrie more-or-less melts-

Oh, isn't she just precious? Hi there, Felicia!

-she waves back-

Kyrie's Dad: Yep, she's a cutie! She's been hanging around outside our house, and we've decided to take her in!

Greta: She'll become a fine Liepard someday!

-she places Felicia on the ground, and she skitters off-

So anyway, what have you been up to? Still got Ophelia with you? Practicing your violin?

-Kyrie nods-

Yes on both counts! She's asleep in the corner, though. Emilie's right next to me, though!

-Emilie plays a chord in greeting-

And yeah, I've been practicing a lot! I've been writing a lot of music on my downtime, though!

Kyrie's Dad: Great! I miss being able to hear it whenever I wanted...

-Kyrie smiles happily-

Greta: Right, then! Just calling to check in on you! Bye!

Bye Mom! Bye Dad! Take good care of Felicia!

-blip-

-Kyrie blinks-

Man, they really have no idea...
