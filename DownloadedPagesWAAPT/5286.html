Tracer(?): "... Simo, what's going-">

<AU!Nimbus: "... As I've said already, I was captured... It was during the siege...">

AU - Dragonspiral Tower - Sometime During a Six-Month Long Siege by Scolemis Forces

-A small number of sundry guards from the tower are being held in a clearing, bound, and visibly drained from their wounds in battle-

-Unseen-

<???: "Grr... You think that your threats faze me? I am a servant of the gods! You can take you dishonorable offer and stuff-">

-The speech is cut off by the sounds of a scuffle, being replaced by silence-

-The sound causes some of the guards in the clearing to flinch, and visibly intimidates some of the others-

<AU!Nimbus (Dragonair): "(*shudder*) Eep!">

<Captured!Meinfoo: "Oh gods... I think I'm gonna be sick...">

-Whimpering, barely audible-

<Captured!Druddigon: "Th-This is h-how it ends? B-But I barely st-started, d-doesn't th-that m-mean an-anything?" >

-Abruptly materializing thanks to a Zoroark's illusion-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Mmph... Pathetic...">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Indeed... It's amazing how just the right strike can show you how feeble these miserable fools can be.>

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Hmm... Who to question next...? You lot should consider yourself fortunate. Were that oversized pig of a Samurott still here, you would in all likelihood be subjected to a far more... Direct procedure...">

-Scanning the group of guards, a few visibly shrinking back from its gaze-

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Let's see here... Hmm... How about that blue serpent there?">

<AU!Nimbus: "M-Me? AAAH!">

-And is dragged off by the two into the illusion-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "So. Tell me a little bit about yourself Dragonair... Do you feel lucky?">

<AU!Nimbus: "I-I'm n-not t-telling y-you a-anything!">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Oh look, he thinks he's brave... How... Cute... Let's fix that...">

-And holds up a Leaf Blade to AU!Nimbus' throat-

<AU!Nimbus: "Erk!">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Personally, I'd prefer to run this along your underbelly first before doing this... But hey, we're in a rush...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "What is it that gives you your bravery? Your numbers? That so-called "Blue Death" of your compatriots who a Hydreigon incinerated today?">

<Unseen!Zoroark: "Uh... Technically that wasn't confirmed...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Whatever. I saw his position! There is no way that he could have survived that!">

AU - Ranger Station Near Dragonspiral Tower

<AU!Nimbus: "That Ninetales and that Leavanny were sniped later that week by that Meinfoo... Not that it made anything better...">

AU - Dragonspiral Tower - Sometime During a Six-Month Long Siege by Scolemis Forces

-To AU!Nimbus-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Or perhaps its those false gods of yours? Those worthless black and white rocks that you couldn't be troubled to hand over peaceably to our lords...">

<AU!Nimbus: "Th-Those a-are th-the v-vessels of Z-Zekrom a-and R-Reshiram! A-And i-if th-they w-were he- EEK!>

-Has Leaf Blade physically pressed up against throat, enough to just break his hide and cause a small trickle of blood to dribble out-

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "If I so much as move this, your life is over... So choose your next words carefully...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "So... What will it be, worm?">

-Visibly terrified, but still making an attempt to be brave-

<AU!Nimbus: "I-I'm a-a p-patrol l-leader! I-I'd r-rather d-die th-than b-betray m-my l-lords in s-such a f-fashion! A-And I h-have f-faith th-that th-the o-others h-here w-would d-do l-likewise! Th-They'd n-never j-join th-the l-likes of y-you!">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Heh heh... Smooth, sna-">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Let him go for a moment.">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "WHAT?">

-Bracing for the killing blow-

<AU!Nimbus: "W-Wait, wh-what?">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "You heard me!">

-And has the Leaf Blade moved away from his throat-

-Panting heavily-

<AU!Nimbus: "Wh-What? Wh-Why d-didn't y-you d-devils f-finish m-me off?">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "You said that you'd rather die than betray those pathetic stones of your compatriots, did you not?">

-To the Leavanny-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "The Druddigon.">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Huh? But w- OH! I think I see where you're going with this... Which one though?">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "I'm sure that you already know the answer to that one...">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Heh heh heh... Thought so... Just checking!">

-The Leavanny departs the illusion, and the sound of a dragon's yelp can be heard, the Leavanny dragging back the Captured Druddigon from earlier in this post-

-Visibly quivering-

<Captured!Druddigon: "Pl-Please d-don't...">

<Unseen!Zoroark: "Er... Wait, what's the point of this again?">

-To AU!Nimbus-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "So here's a chance for you to own up to those words... We will be deciding the fates of everyone else here ranked lower than you...">

<Captured!Druddigon: "B-But... Th-That's every- EEP!">

-And has a Leaf Blade held up against its throat-

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Shut it!">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Hmm... Well that makes things convenient for us, does it not? We will be defaulting to your assumption that everyone here would have been choosing as you did, and would have preferred death...">

-Starting to cry-

<Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) N-No... N-Not l-like th-this...">

<Unseen!Zoroark: "Er... Isn't that a little mu-">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Quiet! And remember who's your superior here!">

<Unseen!Zoroark: "Sorry! My bad!">

<AU!Nimbus: "B-But that's- And-">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "If you decide to remain intransigent after we're through with these pathetic worms, you shall join them!">

-To the Leavanny-

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Dispose of this wretch!">

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Gladly... Let's have a little fun with this though...">

-Pulls Leaf Blade away from throat and back into air, aiming to decapitate its unlucky victim in a fell swoop through momentum-

<Captured!Druddigon: "AAH!">

<AU!Nimbus: "NO! Pl-Please! Don't!">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Hold your blade!">

-Stopping the Leaf Blade just a couple inches from the Druddigon's neck-

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "Oh come on! Am I going to get to kill something or not?!>

-Eyes closed, shivering-

<Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) (*whimper*)">

-Feeling utterly sick-

<AU!Nimbus: "I'll join...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "A wise choice... For you, anyways.">

-To the Druddigon-

<Scolemis!Leavanny: "What about you, O great servant of the gods? You feel like living for another day?">

-Nodding, utterly miserable-

<Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) Y-Yes...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "Good then, let's check up on your pathetic comrades...">

-Dragging the two out of the illusion, before a group of disbelieving and fairly demoralized group of local guards-

-Avoiding eye contact with the other guards-

<Captured!Druddigon: "Nngh... (*sniffle*)">

<AU!Nimbus: "I'm sorry... I'm so sorry...">

<Scolemis!Ninetales: "So... You've heard these two... Now which of you will be making the sensible choice along with them?">

AU - Ranger Station Near Dragonspiral Tower

-To Nimbus, bitterly-

<AU!Nimbus: "Not a single one of us who were captured had the courage to defy them after that... I undermined the bravery of a bunch of rookies to stand up and not dishonor their shifts... When they needed it most... Is that what you wanted to hear?!">

-Quietly-

<Nimbus: "N-No...">
