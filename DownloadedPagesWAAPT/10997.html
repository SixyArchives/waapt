"<Was it always this big?>" asked Gex.

"<Hey!>" said the kids.

"<Oh come on Zack, you told you didn't want one just to steal from them?>" asked Duke, looking slightly annoyed.

"<Not really, no, I just want to see if those two can take it back from me.>" said Zack, with a sly smile.

"<Oh yeah? Then I'm going to show you!>" said Gex.

"<Wow, you two are really hopeless.>" said Zack, tauntingly

"<Come on Zack, just give it back to them already.>" said Duke, already getting tired of that and giving a sigh.

"<Sure.>" said Zack, with a shrug.

"<Aaaaw...>" said Riku, disappointed

"<Oh come on!>" said Gex, upset.

Critical Hit!

"<Hey! Wait!>" said Riku, following behind.

Zack then finally starts getting up, with one paw rubbing his stomach "<O-Ow... No fair, I wasn't ready yet.>" he said, visibly hurt.
