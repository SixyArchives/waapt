Chords of Steel

Pugilis - Around 600 years ago

-After leaving Herbert alone for a few minutes, Revan reenters the room, carrying Herbert's Aura sword with him.-

Revan: ~This was lying nearby when we found you. We assumed it was yours, but... the design on it is like nothing I've ever seen. It's clearly ancient, predating any civilisation I know of, yet there's not a dent or trace of rust on it anywhere. Either you take very good care of this thing, or it's a bit more than a sharpened piece of metal.~

Herbert: Wait... doesn't Lucanus own a similar sword?

Revan: ~No, of course he doesn't. Why would you think he does?~

Herbert: Well, that sword's been passed down through my family for about six hundred years. I thought Lucanus would have been the original owner?

Revan: ~Nope. Though he'd kill - wait, no, what am I saying, he'd never kill - he'd do almost anything to get his hands on a blade like this. Don't worry, though, he's too naive to consider stealing it.~

Herbert: Erm... good to know, I guess. But if Lucanus never owned it, then where did this sword come from?

Revan: ~Beats me. It doesn't matter at the moment, anyway. The longer we sit here wondering about it, the longer we keep Lucanus waiting.~

-He heads out of the room and up towards Lucanus' chambers, Herbert following behind him. Revan stops him just before they enter, however.-

Revan: ~A word of warning before we go in, though - you are under no circumstances to inform Lucanus that you are his descendant. Aside from the whole "disruption of casuality" thing, Lucanus is not the brightest of individuals, and the concept of time travel - not to mention the idea that he might one day sire an heir - might be a little too much for him. Herbert is your preferred alias for some reason, correct?~

Herbert: Erm, yes.

Revan: ~Then that shall be your name whilst you're here in Ransei. Anyway, go on through. Your great-great-great-great et cetera grandfather is waiting for you.~

-Herbert steps through the door, and is greeted by a man who looks almost exactly like him sitting at a desk.-

Lucanus: Ah, greetings! Do come in. My apologies, but you'll have to stand, I'm afraid there's currently something of a dearth of chairs in this room.

-He narrows his eyes.-

Lucanus: I must say, you look awfully familiar... as does your Aura...

Herbert: Um... I can assure you we've never met, my lord. My name is Herbert. I'm a traveller.

Lucanus: From what Takuma told me, you are no ordinary traveller. Not every common wanderer can use Force Palm, after all!

Herbert: Wait, did he see that?

Lucanus: Of course he did! Hence why I have taken something of an interest in you. As you have no doubt already heard, I am an Auric myself. Though the population of Aurics in Pugilis is relatively high when compared to the other kingdoms, it is still rare to meet an individual with the same abilities. Come, let us take this discussion to the courtyard. Perhaps I can show you a couple of new tricks.

Herbert: Erm... I'd be honoured, my lord. But first, could I ask you a quick question?

Lucanus: Of course, my boy. Ask away.

Herbert: Have you ever met a gold-and-silver Bisharp who can use Obscura powers and would have some sort of history with you that causes him to relentlessly pursue your descendants?

-Lucanus furrows his brow.-

Lucanus: ...No, I don't believe I have. Why?

Herbert: No reason.

Lucanus: I see. Well then, let us head down to the courtyard.

-Lucanus stands up and starts heading outside, Herbert following behind him.-
