<"Oh speaking of which, we were there earlier.">

<Stratus: "... Point taken.">

-And hastily flies off after us-

<Everyone Other Than Stratus: "WHOA!">

KLANG!

???: "OW. RUN, FOR GOODNESS' SAKE! COME ON, COME ON!">

-And we run onto a large space connected by a catwalk-

<"... Shouldn't she be trying har-">

-And panels connecting the space begin to abruptly close up, with us in it, and turrets begin to move into place-

???: "AAAH! WHAT'S GOING ON IN THERE?!"

Turrets: "TARGET ACQ-"

<Stratus: "MEEP!">

-And erects a Barrier-

< CAW! "QUICK! SMASH THEM!">

-And we hastily use attacks to topple the turrets, including Vine Whip from Pepper... And unfortunately for me and Lardna, Surf from Lake-

-As the turrets flail uselessly and spray bullets-

-Still holding up the Barrier-

<Stratus: "... Is it over?">

-Standing in front of everyone else with a Protect-

<Lake: "It's o- Wait, we're trapped!">

???: "CAN YOU GET OUT?! WE HAVE TO GET YOU OUT OF THERE! I HEARD GUNFIRE! A BIT LATE FOR THIS, BUT LOOK OUT FOR GUNFIRE. PROBABLY DOESN'T HELP OUT AT THIS POINT, BUT I HAVE AT LEAST TRIED."

-Nervously glancing around at surroundings-

<Stratus: "H-How are we going to get out of here?!">

-Scanning surroundings-

<"There's gotta be something, there's gotta be somethi-">

-And notices a portalable wall in with us-

<"OH!">

<Lardna: "I'm on it!">

-And casts a portal at the wall, which doesn't open up due to the lack of a second-

<"Quick, look for something outside!">

<Pepper: "Walls, walls- OH! There!">

-And squeezes off another portal at a wall above a catwalk through a gap in the closed panels, allowing us to flee through it-

-Upon making it out, we are greeted by an increasingly familiar voice-

???: "YOU'RE OKAY! GREAT, COME ON! THIS WAY!"

-While running on circuitous catwalks once again-

<"Could this possibly get any worse?!">

???: "TURRETS!"

-And we turn and notice several turrets coming to life at point-blank range-

Turret: "ACTIVATED."

<"Oh, fu-">

-And they fire just after Pepper hastily throws up Protect as we run-

-An elevator comes into view in the distance-

???: "GO! GO GO GO! THERE'S THE EXIT! WE'RE ALMOST OUT OF HERE!"

<"Okay, now things can't possibly get any-">

-And the catwalk abruptly shudders and we begin to notice debris falling from the ceiling-

???: "SHE'S BRINGING THE WHOLE PLACE DOWN, HURRY!"

<>_< "Me and my big mouth...">

???: " GET IN THE LIFT! GET IN THE LIFT! "

<Stratus: "ACK! MY SPLEEN!">

<Lardna: "I'M PRETTY SURE YOU CAN REPLACE ONE OF THOSE! NOW SUCK THAT GUT IN!">
