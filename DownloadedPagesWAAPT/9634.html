"Yeah, and Lyuri doesn't like the thought of Human X Pokémon too, it's funny really." said Sakura, giggling as she looked further on the website "There's also you and someone named Grace in here, though it's not as frequent as you and Lyuri."

"Grace, huh..." said Duke, stopping to think a bit.

"<Well, look at that, you're attracting maidens and you don't even notice.>" joked Seth, chuckling at Duke's expense.

Sakura giggles at Seth's joke "To be fair, some boys would kill to look like you." said Sakura, half-joking

"I just brought this upon myself, didn't I?" asked Duke, with a shrug, but also rather amused by the thoughts coming to mind "I wonder what Lyuri would think of all that?" he wondered.

"Well, look at that, Lyuri x Darkrai." said Sakura.

The trio stops walking and look at each other in silence for a few seconds, before bursting into laughter at the thought.

"Oh, this is just gold!" said Sakura, still laughing "Like I said, we all know what we would do and who we want, so if something like this appears, just have fun seeing and reading what other people think would happen, why get mad at some people's imagination?" she commented, now giggling a bit

"<Yeah yeah, I get the point now, still, that's so stupid.>" said Seth, chuckling.

"Let's not tell Lyuri about this, ever, ok?" requested Duke, also chuckling

"All right." answered both Sakura and Seth
