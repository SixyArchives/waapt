-He starts getting to his feet.-

Kirk: <Hey, get back here, you little punk! I'm not just going to let some human->

-A second rollerskating trainer follows behind the first, knocking Kirk to the ground again as they breeze past.-

Kirk: <Urgh..>

-This time, he elects to simply lie in the street until he becomes aware of a boy of about eleven or twelve standing over him with a Crustle by his side.-

Boy: Oooooh, a Lucario! Hi there, mister Lucario! Are you okay?

-He stands up and brushes himself off huffily, then notices that the boy is still standing next to him eagerly.-

Kirk: <...What are you staring at?>

-He facepalms.-

Kirk: <And to think I'd almost gotten used to humans understanding Monese...>

-He looks back at the boy and shakes his head. The boy looks rather disappointed.-

Boy: Aww. Well, I'm gonna go find some other really rare Pokemon and make them really mega strong! Bye then, mister Lucario!

-The boy dashes off, but his Crustle stays behind with Kirk.-

Kirk: <...He yours, then?>

Crustle: <Indeed. He's a nice enough kid when you get to know him.>

Kirk: <Yeah... you know, that's what I would've said about mine a while ago.>

-An awkward silence falls between the two.-

Crustle: <...You ran away, then?>

Kirk: <A few days ago. He went missing a couple of months back. Nobody's seen him since... I don't know what's happened to him, but I doubt it's anything good. So I left.>

Crustle: <Oh... sorry to hear that. Sounds like it must've been difficult... you don't miss the old team or anything, though?>

Kirk: <I... I do, yeah, but->

-He is interrupted by the young boy calling for his Crustle.-

Boy: C'mon, Shelby! We've got Pokemon to catch!

Crustle Shelby: <Coming!>

-Shelby turns to leave, but glances back at Kirk before she does.-

Shelby: <Listen... I'm not quite sure why you don't just go back to your old team, but if you're looking for a place to go, then I've heard about this place down south. Apparently it's a gathering spot for Pokemon that've been abandoned, abused, or just plain ran away like you did... almost like a village, of sorts.>

Kirk: <...Really? Huh. I'll go check it out. You haven't been there yourself?>

Shelby: <Nah, and I'm not planning to visit. I'm happy where I am. Anyway, I really should be going now... I never did get your name, did I?>

Kirk: <...Kirk. It's Kirk. Yeah, I know it's stupid, but I didn't choose it.>

Shelby: <Well, I think it sounds cool. Anyway, bye, Kirk. See you later sometime.>

-She turns and scuttles after her trainer.-

Kirk: <...Yeah. Maybe.>

-He thinks for a moment.-

Kirk: South, she said... I guess a village for runaway Pokemon sounds like a good place for me. But... would I be able to settle down, even there? I suppose I'll find out when I arrive...
