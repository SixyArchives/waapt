Teala: <...Boss? Oh r-right, you lived there as a guard...>

Maekrite walks away, reminiscing...

Maekrite: <Those were good times... get up at sunrise every day, run back and forth across Rt.36, getting into a scuffle with the city mons every once in a time...>

Teala tilts her head and follows after the Nidorino, listening, wondering if his life as a guard was so enjoyable in contrast to her own, or if there is something else she does not know.
