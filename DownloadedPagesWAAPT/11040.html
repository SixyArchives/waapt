I was a little nervous, 'cause, y'know, sometimes some not-very-friendly things live in caves in the mountains. But this Froslass came out, and she told me I reminded her of someone, and she asked me to come in so she could tell me a story, because she hadn't had anyone to tell it to in a long time. She looked so sad, and she hadn't chased me off... so I agreed.

-she pauses and looks down at her feet-

I was there for days. She told me about how 600 years ago, she was partners with a man named Amhlaidh and a Weavile named Kori, and that I looked a lot like Amhlaidh. Every time I tried to leave, she begged me not to go, because no one ever came to talk to her or hear her stories. Eventually, she started just staying between me and the entrance of the cave all the time. I couldn't leave.

-she hugs her knees to her chest-

At one point, she implanted a copy of her memories into my head, so I could relive them, too. And then at anotherI ended up showing her my Sneasel form, because I wanted to hunt. That's when she told me I must be Kori's descendant, and when I explained about my dad, she said he must be Amhlaidh's. That's when things got... bad. She'd decided that she couldn't bear to be separated from Amhlaidh anymore, so she had to keep me there with her forever. I decided I couldn't stay in that cave any longer, and I had to get out. There... was a fight.

Things were going pretty badly, since she stole my pendant. I used the Obscura shield without even realizing it. It saved my life. I finally managed to knock her up against a wall with some Obscura, and then I... I...
