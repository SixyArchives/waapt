"<Woah!>" said Riku, quite impressed as he soon looked back at the couple.

"<Heh, nice, now dodge this!>" said Hayato.

Hayato then rushed at Blade to try and hit him a few times with his wings, beaks and talons, but he was stopped by Rei who made a green energy barrier in front of him.

"<He doesn't need to, silly~ He has me around~>" said Rei, with a soft tone and a smile.

"Aren't them the sweetest and most effective couple around?" asked Hikari, playfully as everything seemed to go the way she wanted.

"Don't be so confidant, we just started." said Lyuri, having a hard time thinking of what to do "Riku! Swords Dance!"

"<'Kay!>"

Riku then started to make the same movements as Blade did with his claws, to boost his own power, while in the meantime, Blade made good use of Rei's defense to polish his armor, taking out any excess it had on it to make himself less heavy and more agile, he then took advantage of the close quarters and swiftly slashed Hayato with Night Slash just as Rei's barrier went down, making the Staraptor stagger quite a bit and back off. Riku wasn't sure of what to do now, but he then remembered that Hayato was still airborn

"<I can't fail Lyuri right now, there's a lot of people watching us... I guess it's time for me to surprise her!>" thought Riku, now quite pumped.

Riku then stomped the ground, making a very strong Earthquake, Hayato was unaffected by it, but it also took absolutely everyone by surprise, even Lyuri, Hayato and Hikari themselves.

"What?! Since when- Nevermind!" said Lyuri, quite impressed.

"Woah!" said Hikari, blinking in response to what just happened

Blade was, of course, more damaged by it than Rei was, promptly making her switch item with him, she took his Life Orb while she gave him some Leftovers.

"<It's my turn now, rest a bit!>" said Rei, quickly recovering from the attack.

"<All right, do your best.>" said Blade, struggling quite a bit.

Rei then struck Hayato with a strong kick while he was too busy surprised with what Riku just did, however, Rei was nowhere near as strong as Blade was, so Hayato managed to take the hit quite well.

"<Ugh, I don't like to hit ladies like this, but you aren't giving me a choice here.>" said Hayato, getting angry.

Hayato then tried to ram Rei while flying, his body surrounded by a blue aura as he did so, however, Rei managed to evade it, only for Riku to take that opportunity and rush at her while trying to use X-Scissor on her, only to be intercepted by another psychic blade from Blade, who was now a little better.

"<Thanks sweetie!>" said Rei, rather cheerfully.

"<Unless you two want to lose, don't forget about me.>" said Blade, still quite stoic.

"<If you want us to notice you so much, so be it!>" said Hayato.

Hayato tried to strike Blade with another Close Combat, but he managed to dodge every single attack he did, Rei was about to strike Hayato from behind, but Riku instead did that to her with a X-Scissor, with a clean hit.

"<Ooow! Don't interrupt me!>" said Rei, very annoyed now.

Rei then hit Riku with a Return, the knockback throwing him on Hayato and stopping his attacks, and also knocking Riku out.

"Damn!" said Lyuri, now extremely nervous as she recalled Riku.

Hayato then got up again and tried to ram Rei with another Brave Bird immediatelly after recovering, this time he was successful and Rei was knocked back quite a bit, Blade responded with another Psycho Cut, that Hayato quickly dodged, before going to help Rei out, she got up again, but was stumbling around as well.

"<Here sweetie, I think I'm done.>" said Rei, giving Blade his Life Orb back before falling to the ground again, fainted.

"Oh boy, I almost can't contain myself..." said Hikari, recalling Rei.

Blade then focused on Hayato now, giving him a glare.

"<Oh boy, he's pissed.>" said Hayato, not looking very forward for the next attack.

"Don't lose focus, Blade! Just strike him down fast!" said Hikari

"Wait for a chance to counter, Hayato!" said Lyuri

Blade then tried to slash Hayato swiftly again, but Hayato narrowly dodged the attack, he then finally managed to do what he wanted since the beginning, Hayato then hit Blade many times, using Close Combat, which combined with all the damage he had took previously from Riku's Earthquake, not even the healing that Rei had offered to him was enough, he collapsed on the floor, uncounscious.

"Yes!" said Lyuri, jumping for joy

"Aaaaw! I lost..." said Hikari, with a sigh.

"Amazing! This was the first battle of our tournament, and it was already a very close one! Lyuri narrowly manages to best Hikari in this battle!" said the announcer, in pure awe.

Lyuri then sighs and looks at Hikari "We need to talk. Now." said Lyuri, recalling Hayato.

"Fine, fine..." said Hikari, rather grumpy now.
