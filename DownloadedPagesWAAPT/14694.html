367350 Herbert40k14th Apr 2014 02:40:12 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
Chords of Steel

Anistar City, Minerva's House - Last night

-Herbert glances over at Blackquill.-

Herbert: You can thank me when I'm not in the middle of a fight!

Titan: A fight you insisted on creating, I hasten to add. Do make this quick, I have little patience for-

Herbert: I thought I told you to stop talking!

-He sends out Bishop whilst Titan sends out Iota.-

Iota: <Hi hi hiya! New people!> :3

Bishop: <...It would appear that Titan's team has gone through some lineup changes since we last faced it.>

-He charges forward with an X-Scissor whilst Iota swings a Hammer Arm in his direction. Meanwhile, Herbert attempts to strike Titan with a Force Palm, only for him to Detect out of the way.-

Titan: Your anger makes you predictable. Control it. Don't give your opponent any tells. If they sense weakness, they will capitalise on it.

-He Detects out of the way of another Force Palm from Herbert.-

Titan: Your attacks are repetitive and easy to predict. In addition, your relentless offence leaves you wide open to a retaliatory strike. Focus. There is no point in making an attack if you are not certain that it will connect.

Herbert: Grngh... just stand still!

Titan: ...As you wish.

-He catches Herbert's third Force Palm in his own hand and kicks the boy's legs out from beneath him, sending him crashing to the floor.-

Herbert: Urgh... can't be bothered to fight fair, can you?

Titan: Those who would seek to kill you will not engage you on a level playing field, I see no reason to allow you to become complacent. If you are to have any hope of defeating an older and more experienced opponent, then you will have to rely on ingenuity rather than brute force.

-Herbert surges to his feet, this time summoning an Aura staff and swinging at Titan's head. Titan ducks beneath the blow and blasts Herbert against the opposite wall with an Aura Sphere. At the same time, Bishop is fainted by an Earthquake from Iota.-

Bishop: <I'm sorry, I failed to... guh...>

Titan: Inefficient use of your energy. Summon constructs at the beginning of a fight or not at all. Doing so at any other point is simply needlessly exhausting yourself.

-Herbert remains on the ground, collapsed.-

Titan: ...Well? Get up. You cannot hope to win by simply lying prone and relying upon your opponent's mercy.

Herbert: Nrngh...

-He attempts to heave himself to his feet, but fails, collapsing to the ground once again. Titan gives a quiet sigh.-

Titan: ...And once again it is proven that enthusiasm and good intentions are no match for martial prowess. Whilst certainly impressive for one so young, your powers require a significant degree of improvement before they can be used effectively. I regret that I could not train you myself... in any case, I do believe the fight is mine. Farewell, Lucius. I hope our next meeting can be less confrontational.

-He sends out Sidious and blips away.-

Herbert: You... you bastard...

-He glances up at any others still present.-

Herbert: ...Thanks for the help, guys. Really appreciate it.

Anistar City - Today

-Herbert is sitting near the sundial, looking out over the sea with Bishop by his side.-

Herbert: ...You know, Bish, I think I've finally figured out what my problem is.

Bishop: <Hmm?>

Herbert: I'm weak.

-Bishop sighs.-

Bishop: <You are not weak, and I won't allow you to delude yourself into thinking that you are. You just have an unfortunate habit of picking fights with those stronger than you.>

Herbert: Either way, I still need to get stronger. No matter how hard I try, I never seem to make an actual difference. Allen, Zrone, Titan... all of them got away, and they're all going to carry on making people's lives miserable, and there isn't a damn thing I can do about it...

-He sighs.-

Herbert: ...I have to get stronger. I need to find a way to toughen myself up. Prove that I can save Kirk, that I can help people. Otherwise I'm just going to get in the way of people that actually know what they're doing...
