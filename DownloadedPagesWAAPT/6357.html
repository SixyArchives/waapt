Treasure Town - PMD A

Since it seemed that he and the others were probably going to spend a little while in Treasure Town, Lake had decided to try and find somewhere to accommodate them.

Unfortunately, he had spent all of the Poké he had found in beach Cave back in Bluescale, so he couldn't afford to stay at the local Inn.

So, he had decided to make like the Oblivia's Suicune and build a Bubble Fort for him and the others.

...Well, less of a bubble fort and more of a bubble house, really. He was going to function over form.

Stratus was more than willing to help, mostly by assisting Lake by joining bubbles onto other bubbles, then putting them in place.

Stratus: <I've always wanted to do this~>

The Dragonite flies up to stick a bubble onto the roof as Lake finishes blowing another one.

Lake: <I really need to teach some more Pokémon this move. If only to help with the workload.>

Stratus: <Ooh, perhaps I could learn it? Dragonite can learn a few more water moves.>

Lake shrugs.

Lake: <Perhaps. If you can make Dive Bubbles, it's not much of a stretch to go to Bubble Shields. It's the same thing, you've just gotta' put more into them.>

Stratus pauses to pounder for a moment.

Stratus: <Perhaps if I put some magic into it...>

Lake tilts his head as Stratus lathers his mouth up, then blows what appears to be a green Dive Bubble.

Lake: <Ooh...>

He then proceeds to puff more air into it, the emerald bubble getting larger and larger, larger even than what they had made of the house so far.

<...Okay, Stratus? You can stop now...>

Stratus, however, glances at Lake and shakes his head.

Stratus: <I CAWN'T STWOP!>

The Feraligatr looks on worriedly as Stratus' bubble gets even larger, until it finally bursts...

KABOOOOOOOOM!!!

...or rather, explodes.

Thankfully, Lake had thrown up a Protect to shield himself from the blast. Stratus, however, had been thrown backwards, right into a tree.

Lake: <Stratus? Are you alright?>

The Dragonite nods weakly.

Stratus: <I think I used...too much magic...>

He then falls off the tree trunk, landing face-first on the floor.

<Ouch.>

Lake shakes his head, then goes to help the dragon up.
