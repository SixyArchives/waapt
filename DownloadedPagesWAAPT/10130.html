Herbert's Mindscape

-Purple!Herbert is wandering through the streets of Eterna City, seemingly not noticing or caring about the darkness enveloping the area or the blurred shapes darting around in the shadows. He starts to meander out of town and down the road to an old, abandoned manor house, talking to himself constantly.-

Purple!Herbert: Oh, woe is me! Fate has seen fit to inflict upon us that most cruel of human curses - a choice. Our heart still longs for fair Carol, and every moment we are seperated is an eternity for my tortured and lonely spirit. Her warm, tender hands, her soft and gentle voice... the thought of being with her once again, whether as friends or as lovers, is enough to console us through even the darkest of hours.

-He sighs, and makes a dramatic gesture, even though there is no-one around to see it.-

Purple!Herbert: And yet an old fire has been rekindled in my heart. Sarah, you play a tune upon our heartstrings, and we cannot help but be enraptured by your enchanting music. Your sharp wit, your stunning beauty, your eternal support - is it any surprise that we have fallen for you in Carol's absence? But you have your Psyche, and we have our Carol... it is a relationship doomed to failure, and yet one I still desperately desire. Oh, my heart is split in twain with the agony of conflict! Such choices were not made to be - um, where am I?

-He looks around in confusion, to find that his aimless walk has taken him just outside of the run-down manor, in an overgrown garden filled with dark, enroaching vines and shadowy undergrowth.-

Purple!Herbert: Oh dear. I really shouldn't be out here alone at night, should I?

???: No. You shouldn't.

-A dark tendril bursts out of the shadows and wraps itself around Purple!Herbert's neck, pinning him to the wall of the manor. The tentacle is followed by an indistinct figure, who steps out just far enough for his features to remain concealed by shadow.-

Purple!Herbert: Hrk!

???: Let me guess. Lost yourself in one of your melodramatic sollioquies there, Purple? How very predictable. Neither of them care about you, you know. It's all just an act. Sarah loves to tug at your heartstrings, toy with your emotions... to her, it's just one big game. And you're letting yourself be played like a fool. Pathetic.

Purple!Herbert: What are you doing here? You... you should be...

-The shadowy figure gives a harsh, derisive laugh.-

???: I should be locked up in the manor? Trust me, Lucius can't keep me caged forever. Every inkling of self-doubt, every moment of insecurity and weakness... each of them makes me stronger. Each of them makes my voice louder. He is giving me the tools to engineer his own destruction, and there's nothing you can do about it.

Purple!Herbert: We're... we're going to stop you, you know...

???: With what? The "power of wuv"?

-He gives another snarl of laughter.-

???: Do you even understand who I am? What I am? What I'm capable of? No, you don't, do you? You're just some teenage boy's overactive libido. Whereas I wield powers that our dear Lucius can only ever dream of. You can't hold me back for ever. Soon, I'll be the one in-

-There is a flash of blue light, and suddenly Purple!Herbert falls to the ground as the dark tendril holding him in place is severed. The dark figure spins around in surprise to see Yellow!Herbert standing with sword drawn, looking disheveled and exhausted but with a defiant expression on his face.-

Yellow!Herbert: Leave.

???: Yellow? I should have expected you'd show up now, of all times. Shouldn't you be busy moping or something?

Yellow!Herbert: I'm giving you one warning. Back into the manor. Now.

???: You think you're in a position to threaten me? You're just as pathetic as your little purple friend over there. What kind of powers do you have? A glowing blue sword? Frodo called, I think he wants it back. The parlour tricks you can do with Aura are nothing compared to my skill. I might be unable to kill aspects, but I can make you wish that I could. When I have dominion over this place you won't even have a mouth to plead wi-

-Yellow!Herbert blasts the dark figure with a ball of glowing yellow Aura, and he goes flying back through the heavy manor doors with a thud. Yellow!Herbert sighs, then strides up to the doors and bars them using his sword.-

Yellow!Herbert: I did warn you.

-He walks over to Purple!Herbert, and with a gauntleted hand pulls him up to his feet. Purple!Herbert spends a minute brushing off and adjusting his tuxedo, before sighing.-

Purple!Herbert: It's no use. This tuxedo is quite simply ruined. I'll have to find a-

Yellow!Herbert: Don't you think we have more important things to worry about than your tuxedo, friend?

Purple!Herbert: What? Oh, yes! Of course. That fellow. A most ghastly business, I am aware, but there is really not much I can do. This kind of thing is your jurisdiction, is it not?

Yellow!Herbert: It is. But he's growing stronger. I can still defeat him, but if something were to push Herbert over the edge... I can't guarantee that I would be able to protect us, friend.

Purple!Herbert: So what do you want me to do?

-Yellow!Herbert smiles.-

Yellow!Herbert: What you do best, friend.

Purple!Herbert: You simply wish for me to continue with my quest for love? Why, I am insulted by the suggestion that you think I would have stopped! Even when my heart is torn asunder by indecision, even when our noble ship is assailed by the rough seas of conflict, I will ensure that love's message is delivered. For of all the things worth fighting for in this world, there are none so beautiful, so incomprehensible, so magnificent, so pivotal to human nature as love.

-Purple!Herbert turns dramactically and walks away, singing softly and tunelessly under his breath.-

Purple!Herbert:
