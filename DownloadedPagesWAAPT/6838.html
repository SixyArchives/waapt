Starcutter - PMD-A

A rather irate looking Phyco floats through the automatic doors of Treasure Room A, where Comet and Treeo had been waiting for him expectantly.

Comet: <Any sign of them?>

The Kingdra shakes his head, snorting angrily.

Phyco: <I swear, those Pokémon are the most useless creatures I have ever had the misfortune of working with!>

Treeo and Comet frown at this.

Comet: <So that's a "no" then?>

Phyco leers at Comet, seemingly not in the mood to be messed around with.

Phyco: <What do you think?! But if you must know, Magolor say he hasn't seen Mezzo or Every's group in days.>

He let's out a bubbly sigh, a frustrated look on his face.

<It's a miracle that they managed to get even two parts of this ship back together without any of our help.>

Treeo: <What did they even go off back to Treasure Town for, anyway?>

Comet shrugs.

Comet: <I don't know. They ran off before I could ask them.>

Treeo: <Oh...>

He rubs his chin thoughtfully.

<What could they be doing there that would take several days?>

Phyco snorts angrily.

Phyco: <Nothing that could possibly be more important than, oh, I don't know, putting this spaceship back together?!>

Comet: <...To be fair, he has a point.>

Phyco: <At least somemon here has some sense. Now, I don't know about you two...>

He turns around, floating over to the door to leave.

<...But I'm going to inquire with Magolor about where we should be looking for the next part of this spaceship. If that lazy cat and snakebee aren't going to get their act in gear and work on getting this ship back together, then I will!>

And with that, he floats through the open door, presumably heading off to the Starcutter's control room, leaving Treeo and Comet to give each other worried looks.

Treeo: <...What do we do? Do we wait for the others to get back? Or-?>

Comet sighs, a lick of smoke escaping from her maw as she pulls herself up.

Comet: <There's no way he can do this on his own. Even if I am kinda' miffed at Every and Mezzo's priorities...we haven't been doing much either.>

Treeo: <...You're right.>

The Sceptile jumps to his feet, a determined expression on his face.

<We can't just sit here and do nothing. We made a promise to get this ship back in shape, and that's what we're going to do...Even if we have to do it by ourselves.>

He glances at Comet, who gives him a wide grin in return.

Comet: <Well that's lucky, because I just love a good challenge...>

Bladeshore - PMD-B

After a harrowing journey through the space-tunnels, Lake and Stratus have arrived with the others at a place that was...hauntingly familiar.

Indeed, as soon as Stratus had caught sight of the massive, ruined dome that was once the PEFE, he couldn't stop staring at it with a look of pure horror on his face.

Stratus: <...What...happened?>

Lake too was concerned. Although he hadn't spent much time living there like the rest of his team, it still had a sentimental place in his heart, and to see it a mere shadow of it's former self was a little upsetting for him.

Lake: <Whatever it was, it must have been really serious to do that amount of damage to this place...>

Stratus: <...Was...was it the Dragons? Did they do this?>

The Feraligatr gives Stratus an unsure look.

Lake: <I'm not sure, but given that they were behind everything else we've seen so far, it wouldn't be much of stretch.>

Stratus: <...>

The Dragonite hangs his head silently.

<...It's not fair...this...this home, Lake...They wrecked my home...>

Lake frowned at his partner's apparent sadness. Until now, everything the dragons had done in the past hadn't been aimed at them. But by wrecking their home...the dragons had just made things personal.

Trying to cheer him up, Lake pats the Dragonite on the back.

Lake: <It's okay. It might not be as bad as it looks.>

Stratus glances back up, blinking at Lake.

Stratus: <...How can it not be that bad?! Just look Lake!>

He waves a paw at the ruined structure.

<Most of the roof is gone! And that's just the outside! Who's to say what those dragons did once they got in?! How do we know if there's even anything left?!>

Lake: <Well...why don't we go and take a look? It's where everymon else is off to.>

The Dragonite frowns...then after a few seconds, gives Lake a reluctant nod.

Stratus: <...Okay, but I'm expecting the worse...>

With that, the two head off to catch up with everymon else.
