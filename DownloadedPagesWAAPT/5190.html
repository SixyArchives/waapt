"Larvitar, kick up a Sandstorm!"

Obediently, the little reptile spread its arms wide, letting out a loud cry as its eyes shone a brilliant red. Streams of sand suddenly began to whip about the Gym, Rex shielding his eyes with his goggles. Roark, for his part, seemed completely unbothered by the sand, despite the lack of protection his glasses might've offered. Claudia, likewise, seemed impressed by the display, though she seemed almost disappointed not to have discovered another weather-based transformation.

"Claudia, counter it with Rain Dance!" Rex hollered over the swirling sands. Immediately, the little puffball sprung to action, whirling about with a little giggle. Beckoned by some otherworldly force, drops of rain began to fall from the ceiling of the gym, dissipating the flow of sand that had been kicked up. Larvitar seemed greatly displeased by this, not only due to the cancellation of its natural element, but due to the sudden introduction of water. It whined and growled at Claudia, the latter yawning sleepily as she shifted into her Rainy Form.

<So tired...> she mumbled.

"Hmmm...not bad. You countered my initial measure, " Roark murmured, looking the Castform over thoughtfully. "Your Pokemon seems tired, though... Are you sure you'd rather not postpone this match until the morning?"

"Well...she always gets like that when she makes it rain. She's kind of [Quirky] that way."
