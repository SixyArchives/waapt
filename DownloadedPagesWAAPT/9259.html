<???: "Huh? Who's there? Are you alright?">

<Familiar!Ledian: "Who on earth is-?">

-And the group watches the form of Sentret paw its way out of some human belongings-

<All: O_O;>

<Rhyhorn: "Uhm... Guys... I think April shrank...">

<Humon!Sentret: "Shrank? What are you talking about? Wait... How are you talk-">

-And notices brown fur on two much stubbier arms-

<Humon!Sentret: "Ack! What is this-?! How is this-?!>

<Druddigon: "Calm down! Calm down! We'll find some way to-!">

<???: *whistle* "Those feather dusters weren't kidding about that thing being helpful keeping these jerks in line.">

-And the group turns and sees a pair of Druddigon of Dragonspiral Tower stock lurking nearby, gawking-

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "Shouldn't we be the least bit concerned about this?">

<Guard!Druddigon #2 "Nah, what's effective's effective.">

<Druddigon: "Uh... Why are you out so early? Isn't it cold? And the only time we ever go into the human settlement is-">

<Guard!Druddigon #2: "Hrm... I see someone's a bit out of the loop.">

<Familiar!Ledian: "Wait a minute... You did this? But... How?">

-The two cast blank stares at each other-

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "... We'll get back to you on that one.">

<Guard!Druddigon #2: "As for you, take your annoyance with you and return to your den. We're putting a curfew up until we can figure out how to isolate some mons that we have...

Grievances to address...">

<Druddigon: "Ulp.">

<Rhyhorn: "Eh? Grievances? Why not just smack 'em around! That'll show 'em!">

-Giving a bit of an unamused look-

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "That was the plan, yes...">

-Noticing a rather nervous-looking Druddigon among her party-

<Humon!Sentret: "Uhm... What's going on?">

<Rhyhorn: "Wait, so are these like your friends from that tower place that you got taken from or something?">

<Druddigon: !!! >

-Casting a bit of a suspicious glance-

<Guard!Druddigon #2: "... 'Taken'? Surely you knew what you were getting into with the Vow and all...">

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "And who would've stopped you anyways? Especially now. If this whole 'human' thing wasn't working out for you, we do take our own back...">

<Druddigon: "Uhm...">

<Rhyhorn: "Eh? But didn't you say that you left with some jerk Poke- OW!">

-And is belted by a familiar Ledian-

<Familiar!Ledian: "Can it, would you?!">

-Which prompts a realization... And harsh glares from the two guards-

<Guard!Druddigon #2: "Oh... You're one of them...">

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "You truly forgot everything that you learned when you switched, didn't you?">

<Druddigon: "I-I can leave if you don't want ARGH!">

-And crumples up under a forceful Dragon Claw-

<Guard!Druddigon #2: "Oh no... You've got it all wrong... You're exactly the sort of mon we were looking for...">

<Rhyhorn: "Hey! You can't just thrash our friend like that!">

-And charges the two-

<Rhyhorn: "Eat this!">

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "Oh please.">

-And is swatted aside into a light pole-

<Rhyhorn: @_@ "O-Ouch...">

-Turning to the sprawled-out counterpart-

<Guard!Druddigon #1: "As for you... Eternity awaits, traitor... And it will be our lords who decide how to cast you into it!">
