Shounen Brave

Route 4 - Kalos

The young girl is currently there, painting the fountain present on the route in a canvas, having a palette in one hand and a brush on the other as well as a beret on her head, which currently has a sleeping Noibat on top of it. She also had a camera hanging on her neck and there were a couple of signs with markers on top of them and another canvas on the ground as well as a big bag.

As she finishes the painting, suddenly a Furfrou comes by and spots her. Finding it weird to be seeing one of those there, she soon noticed that it's fur was trimmed in what she knew as the La Reine style. It must've gotten lost from it's trainer somehow. The Furfrou walked to her, standing right in front of her, but doing nothing.

She smiled, put her palette and brush on the floor while being careful to not mess anything up and started to pet it, who seemed to really like, making her smile even wider. She stopped petting it for a bit to pick a sign and some markers and started to write a message that read "Can you read?" in it, to which the Furfrou just tilted it's head in confusion.

Sighing, she glanced at her Spoink, who was currently playing with some Flabebes that lived around and she also could hear her Noibat snoring, none of which she wanted to disturb. In that situation, she simply put the marker and signs back in place and went back to petting the Furfrou, who started licking her face, which she was enjoying even though it was a bit icky.

"Oh, Sacha! There you are!"

A teenage girl rushes towards her and the Furfrou, looking very worried.

"Don't go running away like that! You caused me no end of worry!" said the teenager, looking upset and then looking at the girl "Sorry if she gave you any trouble

She shook her head with a smile.

Relieved, the teenager smiled "My name is Natalie. Yours?"

The little girl then picked the markers and the signs and wrote another message with "My name is Aline" written in one and then switching to the other where it read "The cute Noibat on my head is Divoom and the cute Spoink is Spring!

Natalie raised an eyebrow at Aline, but soon understood what was going on.

"Nice to meet you, Aline! You're such a cutie." said Natalie, giggling

Aline smiled and started to write again "You have a cute Furfrou." she then went to another sign "My mom works in the Friseur Furfrou, " and she switches again "so I know a lot about their haircuts!"

"Oh really? Well, if you want to see Sacha again, I go there every Saturday with her. I'm sure she'd love seeing you again too!" said Natalie

Sacha then let out a happy bark, which promptly woke Divoom up, who started to look around, startled. Feeling him moving on her head, Aline started to pet him so he could calm down.

Natalie grimaced "Sorry about that."

Aline shook her head slightly and upon feeling that Divoom was calmer, she wrote another message "I'll be there every Saturday then!"

"Okay then! I need to go now, see you next Saturday!"

Natalie waved and walked away, with Sacha following alongside her. Being left there, Aline got a bit saddened until she got an idea. She grabbed her extra canvas and ran towards Natalie and Sasha, stopping right in front of her. She then wiped out one of the sign's messages and wrote another one "Can I paint a picture of you two?"

Natalie was a bit surprised by the sudden request, but she still smiled "Sure! If it makes you happy!"

Aline jumped for joy a bit and then went to get all of her equipment, she got Natalie and Sacha to pose right in front of the Lumiose gate, the former lowering herself to be at the latter's height and having an arm around her while the Furfrou nuzzled her. Aline smiled as she started to paint. As she finished the picture, Divoom had gone back to sleep. She then showed it to Natalie

"Wow! You're a great artist!"

Aline blushed a bit and scratched the back of her neck, but then wrote another message for Natalie "You want it?"

"Really? Can I?"

Aline nodded, but before handing the canvas to Natalie she took a photo of the painting with her camera. That made Natalie raise an eyebrow

"Why did you take a picture of it?" she asked

Aline then started to write messages again "I want to keep everything recorded, , "I want to see everything about the world!", "I want these experiences recorded forever!", she then erases one of the messages to write another "So that I'll never forget them!"

Natalie then smiles from ear to ear "That's a great dream! I wish you best of luck with it!"

Aline then writes one last message "Thank you!"

She then hands the painting to Natalie and she waves again as she and Sacha walk back to Lumiose. After that, Aline saw that Spring was still playing and decided to join them. After a bit of playing, Aline put the painting of the fountain, the palette, brush and most of her signs save one in the bag, carefully organizing them so that it wouldn't make a mess.

Spring then made the bag levitate with telekinesis and she and Aline started to go back to Lumiose as well, with Divoom sleeping on Aline's head all the way.
