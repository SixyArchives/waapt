Ad Min used Tri Attack!

Stratus' Bubble Shield burst!

<...Urk...>

A satisfied look in his eyes, the Porygon-Z turned to look at the shocked Lake and Quin.

Ad Min: I believe - as they say on the Internet - that I just "Pwned" Stratus' Bubble.
