Seth then easily guesses that Sylva had made a mess out of her face while eating and attempts to clean it, only then he notices Sakura and Grant looking at him.

"<... What's with the goofy smiles?>" asked Seth

"Nothing, just admiration." answered Sakura

"<Huh...?>"

"We're both just admiring how well you take care of this girl, considering that the thought of being a father never once came into your mind before." said Grant

"<If I have to take care of her, then I might as well do it right.>"

"It's not just that, but you like to have her around don't you? If you were doing that just because you were unfortunate enough to take care of her, then I bet you wouldn't be so happy right now." said Sakura.

"<Well, I...>"

"Don't even try to say otherwise. Ever since you adopted Sylva I could sense that you became a lot more happy in the inside, you still act pretty much the same way as always, but I know you truly feel, Seth." interrupted Sakura.

"<... Yeah, I'm happy having to raise her, but being her dad still doesn't feel right to me. Sure, she calls me "daddy" all the time, but I mostly feel like I'm playing pretend than really being her father.>" said Seth, looking at Sylva with an expression of doubt

"If you're playing pretend, you're very convincing. You've changed a bit since the Unova League, Seth, but I think your problem is less that you don't feel like you are her father, but instead it's your pride." said Grant, followed by a sigh.

Seth then looks at Grant with a thoughtful expression

"You can't really let yourself being seen doing silly things or being soft with someone can you? Is it because you've come to like Sylva so much that you can't resist but become a lot more soft than you'd be comfortable with? Because you're in no way being a bad parent for her." said Sakura.

Seth stays in silence.

"There's nothing wrong with being a bit more sensitive sometimes, you're a firm, strong and brave Pokémon and wants people to think of you as such, there's nothing wrong with that, in fact I like you this way, but you shouldn't be worried about acting soft in front of others just because of your image, it's not going to ruin it, in fact, it may even improve because you'd be showing that even though you're the strong, reliable and determined Pokémon you are, you're still able to show that you're a kind-hearted mon that loves his adopted daughter. I personally think that's a better image to have." said Sakura.

"You don't see me acting all tough all the time do you? And you know I have my fair bit of strenght as well." said Grant.

"<Right... I guess it's not impossible for me to be both at once.>" said Seth, with a small smile.

"I understand that you don't want anyone thinking that you're weak, but at least with us and your proper family, don't be afraid of going soft on us." said Sakura, with a reassuring smile.

"<Understood... Well then, I must admit that I really like this girl here even if taking care of a child is a bit difficult sometimes, she at least listens to me and doesn't cause too many problems.>"

"She must have a lot of respect for you." said Grant.

"<Maybe, I think that getting together with Regina and raising Sylva was the second best thing that happened to me so far.>"

"Care to tell us the absolute best thing?" asked Sakura

"<Being caught by you, of course. After all, if it wasn't for you I probably wouldn't be a Scizor and wouldn't have met Regina and started all of this, besides, you're probably the best trainer I could've hoped for and probably is far better than I deserve.>"

Sakura was stunned after hearing that little confession while Grant was also rather impressed by hearing all of that coming from him out of all mons, Sakura then gets up from her seat and walks over to Seth and hugs him.

"I love you too." she said, with a big smile on her face.

"Already going soft on us huh?" said Grant, with a teasing smile.

Seth would hug back, but Sylva's presence would make it hard to do so, instead he just gives a smile of satisfaction "<I'm just stating the truth here, nothing more, nothing less.>"

"You know... I always wanted to have a big brother, someone like you was exactly what I wanted in a big brother. I'm not complaining about Lyuri, of course, she always protected me from anyone who tried to cause me harm and was always there to give me support, but I thought that having both a big brother and sister would be very nice, maybe it was because I was a very meek child, but it's no matter. I guess that with the time we used to spend together before, I started to picture you as the big brother I wanted so much; so thanks, what you said is really important to me." said Sakura, breaking off the hug and giving Seth one of her happiest grins ever.

"<... I guess you're part of the family then, as if we weren't weird enough, Sylva now has a human aunt.>" joked Seth.

Sakura giggles and goes back to her seat while Grant lets out some faint chuckles

"Now that everyone is shining with happiness, can we go back to eating now?" asked Grant, trying to change the subject.

"<Yeah, I'm still a bit hungry.>" said Seth.

"Well, then let's resume our dinner!" said Sakura, clapping her hands "A lot happened since that accident, most of it was spent on a wild chase for the only family I had left, but I was wrong to assume that, even if we haven't interacted much, Straw is my cousin and I'm happy with that, I have Rawst and Lyuri has Duke, and I guess that while I was chasing after Lyuri, I got a big brother out of the whole ordeal and I couldn't be happier for it." she thought

The three of them then go back to eating and chatting about other things while they just enjoyed the moment.
