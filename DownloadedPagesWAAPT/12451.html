Solomon stood calmly by the fountain in the center of Phenac city, admiring the beautiful flow of water that served as impressive decoration. It brought him back to being her in his earlier years.

"Ahh, anyway, time for lunch methinks, " he chimed, turning back to Sammy and Mento, and taking out the Luxury Ball that held his Torterra and released him, smiling as he saw the Pokemon appear before him, with his tongue out, ready to eat. "I've got some poffins, chaps!"

<Oooh, Pooooffins! Goody!>

Solomon reached into his bag, and brought out three different poffins for each of his companions.

"Sammy, my son, I know how you like your spicy food, so here's a spicy poffin just for you! Mento, I have your very special sweet poffin, and Twif, don't think I forgot about your sour poffin! Eat up, you three!" Solomon spoke gleefully, as the three all began to devour their little snacks.

Sammy ate his poffin in pretty much 3 seconds flat, whilst Mento took little pecks around her poffin for a short while. After both of them had finished, they looked at Twif, who appeared to have eaten his poffin entirely in one go, and Mento couldn't stop herself from collapsing on the floor and rolling around laughing at the amount of food covered around his large mouth.

Solomon left his troopers to it, and instead marveled at the fountain again, putting his bag back upon his shoulder once again.

"I wonder what adventure we're going to have today."
