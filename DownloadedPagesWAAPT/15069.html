???: Seriously, where am I? What is this? Why on earth did that blasted magician-

???: Send me here, of all places!?

Following his totally graceful stumble out of the forest, an Unfezant and Talonflame flutter ouch with much more grace.

Unfezant: <It appears that the young master is not all that well acquainted to the wonders of the wilderness.>

Talonflame: <Indubitably. A life in the city would do that to a person though.>

The Unfezant walks over and starts picking the twigs and web out of his master's jet black hair.

Unfezant: <And like always you have all the grace and elegance of a Spinda, young master>

The young man ignores his Unfezant's preening while he takes off his left glove and rolls up his sleeve, revealing that his left forearm is entirely robotic. The youth examines his forearm while the Unfezant finishes off his preening.

Talonflame: <At least you look somewhat presentable, young master>

???: ...Thankfully, nothing appears to be broken or damaged. Though, there is only one way to test this hypothesis...

The man eyes a small boulder in the distance and points his index and middle finger at the formation.

???: Pew~!

On cue the tips of his index and middle finger open as two laser beams are fired at the rock, blasting it away to smithereens.

???: -Grinning down at his arm- Yep! Everything's in ship shape~

Talonflame: <...Splendid>

Unfezant: <...Just try not to draw any sort of unwanted attention to yourself.>

???: -Rolling down his sleeve and putting his glove back on- Now, onward to civilization!

The young man starts walking off in a northwards direction, he keeps on walking until he notices that his Pokemon aren't following him.

???: Archimedes? Hipparchus? Why aren't you-

The two birds point their wings towards Ringtown, which is to the south.

???: ...Oh.

The two birds fly towards their trainer and land on either side of him.

Unfezant Archimedes: <You are quite the paradox, young master.>

Talonflame Hipparchus: <You are both a genius and a fool at the same time.>

???: Anyway, onward to civilization!

The young man starts making his way towards Ringtown with his two Birdmons in tow.
