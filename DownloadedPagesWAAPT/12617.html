 315402 Luke 9924, Thu, 21st Nov '13 2:37:23 PM from England Get RP Mod
Once more, unto the breech...

PEFE HQ - Luke's Module

"...Zzz..."

Here's our hero. Still asleep because he was up all night playing videogames with his Feraligatr. He would have slept in until 2PM had the doorbell buzzed him out of his sleep.

"Uh... AH! Give me a second!"

Jumping out of bed, ripping off his pyjama's and pulling on a pair of pants and trousers, he rushed out of his bedroom and dashed over to the door.

Luke the Human: "I'mcomingI'mcomingI'mcomingI'mcoming!"

He hurriedly punched the button next to the door, which unlocked and quickly slid open, revealing the visitor. Oddly enough in this case, it was a rather tall draconic Pokémon, with a cream belly and bright orange scales. He stood rather tall. Tall enough that Luke had to crane his head off just to glance at his face.

Stratus the Dragonite: <Well, hello!>

Luke blinked. Partly because he was still sleepy. Partly because he was in disbelieve.

Luke: "...Where've you been?!"

Expecting this question, the Dragon scratched the back of his head with a claw a little awkwardly.

Stratus: <A-heh. Long story. Mind if I come in?>

Luke shifted out of the way so that the Dragon could come in, pushing the button to shut the door behind him.

Luke: "Yeah, just let me get something to wear. It's bloody freezing!

A quick trip to his room to pull on a t-shirt, a hoody and some socks, he was back in the front room. Perdictably, Stratus had already raided the kitchen and was now scarfing down a tube of Burungels.

"I was gonna' eat those! Alright, you've got some explaining to do mister Dragon! I give you some money so you can go over to Unova to buy yourself some fancy-pants burger, and you go and disappear for months! And not only that, but I've gotten pages and pages worth of bills frpm pretty much every fast food joint in Icirrus City!"

Stratus: <Ah, well you see...>

The Dragonite briefly paused to pour the last few crisps in the tube into his mouth, chewing on them a few times before swallowing them completely.

<Kinda' a long story. I went and got my burger, but then this Hydreigon just...swooped down and stole it!>

Luke: "Okay..."

Stratus: <I wasn't just about to let him take my food, so I chased him all across Unova until we got to the Pokémon League, where we got into a fight! But then the police came and threatened to take us away, but this one guy - whose name I forget, bailed me out! So they only got that Hydreigon and I was free to go.>

Sighing, Luke raised a hand and massaged his temples, somewhat annoyed.

Luke: "That explains that one stern letter and the huge bill from the Unova Police I got. What happened after that?"

Stratus: <Alright, so I hung out at the league for a while, but then I went to that tower just outside Icirrus to look around. But there was this Druddigon that thought I worked there, and before you know it I ended up actually working there!>

Luke: "Wait, you got a job at Dragonspiral?"

Stratus nodded affirmatively.

Stratus: <Yep! A whole bunch of Dragonite like me work there! But you should have seen them. They had barely anything to eat! Just a bunch of thin old Basculin and weak alcohol.>

A perplexed expression crossing his face, Luke blinked.

Luke: "Dragonite get drunk?"

Stratus: <So of course I had to do something about it! So I became their chief food gatherer!>

He paused for a few seconds.

<...And by "food gatherer" I mean "Fast food caterer.">

Now a little despondent, Luke stared down at the ground.

Luke: "And that explains the fast food bills."

Stratus: <So that's where I've been for the past few months.>

Gazing back up at the dragon, Luke crossed his arms in annoyance.

Luke: "So why are you back here all of a sudden, then? And don't you know how worried sick everyone's been? Some of us thought you were dead!"

Stratus: <Really?! Who?!>

Luke: "Jello, for a start. And Crowley. Although he was more amused than upset. And we both know how paranoid Jello is."

Stratus: <Oh.>

Luke: "And what about your own son? How could you just leave him like that?"

Stratus recoiled in shock! His antennae standing on end!

Stratus: <I would never abandon Lake like that! And...it's not as even as he would be that bothered about it! He's strong, he's got a mate, it's not like he really needs me!>

Luke: "It's still not cool to just have your Dad disappear on you, though! It's borderline neglect!"

Stratus: <No! No it wasn't! I swear! And...>

Glancing down, he twiddled with his claws awkwardly.

<I'm back here because I kinda'...got fired.>

Luke: "Fired? How?"

Taking a deep breath, the Dragonite bellowed a loud sigh.

Stratus: <I may have gotten an entire shift of Dragons ill with some expired fish I kept lying around for too long. It...it wasn't pretty.>

Imagining a whole bunch of Dragons puking their guts out, Luke grimaced in disgust.

Luke: "I can imagine so."
