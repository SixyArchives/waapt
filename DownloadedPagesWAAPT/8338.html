Lake used Bubble Shield!

Stratus: <HEY! What did I do now?>

Chuckling to himself, Lake took another deep breath, then blew another large bubble before hopping inside.

Lake used Bubble Shield!
Lake protected himself with a Bubble!

Not sure where his son was going with this, Stratus blinked at him in confusion.

<What are you up to?>

Lake: <Just having a bit of fun! Come on!>

He gestured for his Dad to follow him over to the edge of the dock, where the sea lay about ten feet below them.

Stratus: <Are we going for a swim?>

The Feraligatr shook his head.

Lake: <Quite the opposite! Watch...>

Stratus watch as Lake stepped back, then lept over the edge of the docks, landing in the water below with a loud splash, frightening a family of Wingull who had been swimming nearby. However, instead of sinking beneath the waves, the bubbled Lake popped back up, floating on the surface like a cork in a glass of water.

<Ha! I knew this would work!>

Glancing back up at his father, the Feraligatr waved, beckoning him down.

<You want in on this, Dad?>

The Dragonite grinned, amused.

Stratus: <Do I ever!>

Following his lead and taking a few steps back, the Dragonite sprinted to the edge of the dock and lept into the air, tucking his legs into a ball as he fell towards the sea below.

<CANNONBALL!!!>

Lake: <...Uh-oh.>

SPLASH!

Stratus hit the water with such force that it was able to propel Lake and his bubble out of the water and through the air...

<WHOOOOOOOOAAAA!!!>

SPLASH!

...And into the water, only for him to once again bob to the surface, dazed. Much to Stratus' concern.

Stratus: <LAKE! You okay?>

The dazed Feraligar gave the Dragonite a thumbs up, signaling that he was fine.

Lake: <It's okay! I'm good...>
