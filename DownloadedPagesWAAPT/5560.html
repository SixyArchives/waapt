Challenger: "That Light Screen is gone... Calm down, Triaca, slide away and Water Pulse!"

Bartemo -walks up to Aamu: <That was nicely done. Where did you learn to shift your aim like that?>

Aamu -stares at nowhere: <Back at home... used to do this for a living.>

Bartemo: <Home... do you miss it?>
