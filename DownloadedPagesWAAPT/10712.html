Icirrus Pizza - Some time later...

A rather bad storm had rolled into Icirrus that night, pelting the town with rain, which only served to turn the town into a even larger marshland than it already was.

Of course, the citizens were more than used to it by now. Most of them were so used to being rained on, that they didn't wear coats, even when it was pouring down!

The same couldn't be said for Robert, the owner of Icirrus Pizza (Home to the Divine Special) and his faithful Heatmor, who were almost ready to close down for the night.

Robert: "Almost done cleaning up back there?"

Heatmor: "Morr!"

Robert: "Atta-boy!"

All of a sudden, the door slammed open, giving Robert a start.

"Sorry, we're clos-"

He stopped as he turned to face his potential customer...only to find a large orange Dragon standing in the doorway, which he soon recognized as one of the dragons that lived in the moat of the old tower just north of town.

But what was one doing here?

"Uh...can I help you, sir?"

Hearing his owner sounding rather shaken, Robert's Heatmor poked his head out of the kitchen and watched as a Dragonite strode up to the counter...and chucked a bunch of Pokédollars down on the counter.

Heatmor: <What the Muk.>

Robert blinked at the pile of money lying in front of him. There was more than enough here for twenty pizzas!

It was then that he put two and two together.

Robert: "...What would you like on your pizzas, sir?"
