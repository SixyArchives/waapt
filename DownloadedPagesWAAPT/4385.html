Treeo: <Aww...> -Strokes Harriet's feathers gently.- <You had every right to feel frustrated, though. We've had a rough couple of weeks, haven't we?>

Harriet: <Sometimes I wish I could get those two to give it a rest already.>

Treeo: <...I don't see why you couldn't.>

Harriet: <Huh?>

Treeo: <Well, you'd have to be a bit more polite than that, but they would probably listen to you. And aren't Honchkrow supposed to be born leaders?>
