Jacob's Mindscape - Castle - Grand Hall - Entrance Chamber

Jacob: "Well... this is... impressive."

-The hall is long and massive, with many doors lining it's walls and a massive gold staircase at it's visible end, a blue carpet lines the main path and the crimson walls are only barely lit by torches-

-Looking from side to side- "... Is this... what is all this? Is there no one else here? What is this place...?"

???: "You should know that better than anyone else. Though I suppose you also should not. This place which is on the edge of the real and not, wake and dreams, consciousness, lucidity, and the strange. This place whose walls you yourself built up, that's chambers you made to seal away that which you hate about yourself. This is you. Your castle. This is where woe is nourished, hope is fostered, where experience is chronicled and function maintained. This is Despair's Sanctuary, your fortress."

Jacob: -Tenses and turns around to see the two giant grand entrance doors closing, and someone who looks like himself, but dressed in more blue and wearing glasses- "... And who are you?"

???: "An excellent question. I am many things that you would appreciate. But for simplicity's sake, call me Mind."

Jacob: "Mind... can you tell me more? Despair's Sanctuary? Where is this place? How did I get here?"

Mind: "This place... I suppose it'd be best to describe it with your own experience. You know that worlds exist within the mind. You fought your friend to the death in one such world. This is yours. As for how you got here... well I brought you. You could really use some help."

Jacob: ".... So a Mindscape. This is... my own. So you... you're my mind. Or rather a personification of my qualities that fit under that category, then? And if this is mine..."

"..."

".... I don't know what to think of it. Is it usual for a mindscape to be this... big?"

Mind: "Probably not. However I don't know. You yourself know precious little on the matter. Also before you ask, no. The aspects that reside here cannot control you. Zeal is unique in that. It is rare for one aspect of a personality to completely overwhelm the others, and the changes become physical. However it is not unusual for one of us to have a greater influence. Secondly you are correct in ascertaining the nature of my existence."

Jacob: "Well that's good to know... Hey you said you brought me here? Why?"

Mind: "Well..."

-Sighs and paces- "You are aware that in Orre you underwent traumatizing circumstances. They were not nearly as bad as some people's circumstances, no in fact you were off better than most. But your situation did break you, as much if not more than the others because of who you are. Losing Regina, among the other events was equivalent to being kidnapped, brainwashed, shadowed, what have you to your psyche. It was in this chaos that your apathy surged. You are the opposite of an empath. When others feel, you do not. You understand the emotions you should be experiencing, and act as if you are feeling, but rarely do you actually feel. This inhibits many functions of-"

Jacob: "Okay wow now I know how people feel when I'm explaining stuff to them. Can you just get on with it?"

Mind: -Sobs a little on the inside-

-On the outside he just adjusts his glasses and clears his throat- "Okay. Long story short your mind suffered heavy damage and is critically fractured. Because you're not under stress right now the implications of this are negligible. However the slightest amount of stress could cause your whole mind to collapse."

Jacob: "That sounds bad."

Mind: "It's very very bad."

Jacob: "So how do we fix it?"

Mind: "Under normal circumstances it would fix itself on this vacation. Relaxation and the company of others would help you. However..." -The two are blipped away-

Jacob's Mindscape - Grand Hall 2F

-However the door itself is wrapped in gray wrappings, and a black shimmer dances along its surface-

"Most of your mind is weak and inaccessible, the force responsible for this is not allowing those who reside here any way to fix it. Outside intervention is needed."

Jacob: "Wait why can't you do anything?"

Mind: "Because, we cannot actively influence your psyche. It works the other way around. However parts of your mind are cut off and thus the energy within them is inaccessible to us to help restore it. A mind cannot change a mind, however you can change yourself, as well as others.
