UTINNI!

Somewhere North of Kanto/Johto – Now

It was times like this that Jack wished he had a water-type pokemon. Once he had found out where Groot had gone and recovered the Onix, he would have to return to the lake and investigate the statue he had found more thoroughly. That would be a task which would be much more difficult to accomplish on his own.

‘’Jack’’: “This really isn’t my normal kind of thing, is it?”

It was one of the drawbacks of not making pokemon collecting part of his regular routine. He didn’t have an answer to every situation he could just call upon at a moments notice. But he was a photojournalist, not a trainer, so it was something he dealt with.

Jack: “Runt, I need you.”

The man merely needed to tap the Abra’s pokeball to call out his friend, and even that was little more than a formality.

Runt: (Telepathically) You look terrible.

Jack: "I look worse than I feel."

Runt: ("TP) That saying much?''

Jack: "Not really, no. But I can feel sorry for myself later. We've got two things to do right now."

Runt: (TP) Dry yourself off and...?

Jack: "Ok, three things. The other two being finding where Groot when to..."

Runt: (TP) Groot? What happened to Groot?

There was a clear 'sound' of worry from the Abra at word something had happened to his friend.

Jack: "We were ambushed, he had to go underground to avoid falling in a river."

The man pointed up towards the waterfall.

Runt: (TP) Right! I'm on it! You stay right here!

Jack: "Wait...!"

But before Jack could say anything, the little psychic pokemon had vanished, leaving Jack alone with his wet gear and only a beedrill that was as much a danger to him as anything else. If not more so.

Jack: "I really hope he hurries up."
