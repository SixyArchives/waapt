Strormchaser, Today

-Ren and company are on the Stormchaser. Ren, noticeably or not so noticeably, has a new addition to his outfit - a fire-red bracelet on his right arm.-

Aqua Resort, Past - specifically, two days after Dezi gives Kitsune the Bracelet

-Kitsune sighs as the bracelet slides off her right foreleg once again.-

Kitsune: <Galvani apparently doesn't have this problem because her Bracelet is on her antenna...> -_-

Aqua Resort, Past, a few days after the previous scene

-Kitsune is looking at the bracelet on the ground in front of her with an intense, questioning look as a fire extinguisher, a tennis racket, a cooler of ice water, a baloney sandwich on rye (hold the mayo), a pen knife, and a battered and bruised Dirus and Monochrome sit on the ground behind her.-

Kitsune: <Seriously, what does it take to activate this thing?! Galvani mentioned bracelets needing external sources of energy to activate. Her bracelet runs off of contact with Dune - and considering I've tried say various phrases while touching Ren's forehead as he sleeps - so I doubt that activate this bracelet... Hmmmmm...>

-to Dirus and Monochrome- <Okay you two, that's enough of a break! Let's get back to work!>

-Dirus and Monochrome groan, gripe, and curse and Kitsune forces them to help her find out what activates the bracelet.-

Aqua Resort, Past, a day after the previous scene

-Dirus and Monochrome are lying unconscious on the ground amongst an iron bar, a fur coat, various types of jewelry, a frying pan, a steering wheel, and empty can of various soft drinks as Kitsune is on her last nerve with the bracelet on the ground in front of her.-

Kitsune: <Okay bracelet, I've tried everything that I can think of to charge you. You were made by Dezi, I know more than likely you can hear me, so give me a hint on how to activate you!>

-The bracelet, of course, being a truly inanimate object, just sit on the ground. The lack of response, however, causes Kitsune to snap.-

Kitsune: <AAAARRRRRGGGGUUHHBIBBLE!    BURN! Burn you evil little thing!   >

-Kitsune, in her anger, unconsciously activates her ability and Fire Blasts the bracelet before immediately turning to drag Dirus and Monochrome away to get treatment.-

-Leaving in her anger, Kitsune fails to notice that instead the bracelet being scorched black and incinerating the ground around it, the bracelet absorbed the Drought-boosted flames in an almost black hole-like manner. As the last embers are absorbed, the button on the bracelet lights up as the bracelet grows warm.-

-A few hours later, Ren and a few of his mon come by at Kitsune's behest to clean up after her. Ren see the bracelet on the ground and gives a small sigh as he remembers Kitsune's statement, "<I don't think I can look at that bracelet for a couple of days without    burning    down the resort!>". Ren picks up the slightly cool bracelet and puts it on his right wrist, then helps clean up the stuff Kitsune left without the bracelet ever falling off his wrist.-

Aqua Resort, a few days ago

-Kitsune, after composing herself finally approaches Ren to ask for the bracelet back so she can try again. When she finds Ren, he is wearing the bracelet while flailing his arms trying to get a very energetic Flo to stay still long enough so he can brush her as Rio watches the scene amusedly.-

Kitsune: <... ... ... Ren, how are you keeping that thing on your arm like that?>

Ren: -still trying to deal with Flo- Oh, you mean the Bracelet? I'm not. See.

-Ren slides the bracelet of his arm to reveal noting to keep the bracelet secured.-

Ren: I just assumed that Dezi made it so it won't slide off accidentally when it's being worn.

Kitsune: <Guess again.>

Ren: Huh?

Kitsune: <Here, put it on me.>

Ren: Sure.

-Ren walks over and slides the bracelet onto Kitsune's fore leg after she presents it. With the bracelet on her leg, Kitsune walks a few step before the bracelet slides of of it's own accord.-

Kitsune: <See? Now let Rio try.>

-Rio picks up the bracelet and slides it up his left arm, only to have the bracelet slide off as soon as his arms return to his sides.-

Ren: That's... odd.

Kitsune: <I... I don't think so... Put it on again, Sweetie.>

-Ren picks up the bracelet once again and puts it on his right arm.-

Kitsune: <Swing your arm as hard as you can.>

Ren: -unsure if he likes where this is going- Okay.

-Ren jerks and swings his right arm with fast movement, and change directions frequently. Ren's, Rio's, and Kitsune's eyes grow steadily wider as all the while as Ren swings his arm around, the Bracelet rattles and bounces around on Ren's wrist, but it never slides off. Ren stops and slowly removes the bracelet from his wrist without resistance. Ren gulps as he puts it back on his wrist and then lets his arm hang loosely at his side, the bracelet still on his wrist.-

Ren: -nervously- Does this mean the bracelet is mine?!

-After several moments of silence, Rio speaks up-

Rio: <Kitsune, What did you ask Dezi to do?>

Kitsune: <I asked her to make something to help Re— Oh. Oh Arceus. Really?>

Ren and Rio: What?>

Kitsune: <I... I asked her to make something to help Ren so he didn't do anything rash trying to solve the armband problem...>

Ren: -slightly thunderstruck- So Dezi made me a bracelet?!

Kitsune: <I suppose I shouldn't be surprised that Dezi fulfilled my request to the letter while under the influence of ice cream...>

Stormchaser, Now

-Ren looks at his bracelet.-

Ren: *sigh* At least we know how to activate it now.
