Oh yes! I've heard of that hymn. It's meant to recreate the scene that happened back when this world was just being formed, the trumpets represent Groudon, the flutes represent Kyogre, and the bells represent Rayquaza. The rising notes are meant to symbolize the rising of the land and sea, respectively, until they clash and begin to fight, but the bells come in before there can be a climax, reflecting Rayquaza's usual interference in the quarrles between Groudon and Kyogre.
