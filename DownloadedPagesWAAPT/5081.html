Closing 2011!Megapost, part Ⅱ

(Continued from here)

Former Mirage Tower, Hoenn, morning of Jan 1

Several Pokémon have pooled their efforts into the reconstruction of (something similar to) the [Mirage Tower]. Silent has set up his laptop's camera and left it recording part of the process for posterity. Walking the Egg he got from Fool around, Silent is careful to keep his distance from the tower and the Pokémon there to avoid interfering in the natural process, and is also careful to keep away from the laptop, under the care of [Algira the Masquerain♀], so that he can not be recorded speaking to Pokémon (or showing understanding of their language).

The two Eeveelutions at his side walk observing the labour of the wild Pokémon.

Sol the Espeon♀ of Luke -looks around: <If you ask me, it is a very beautiful thing what they are doing here.>

Ichtacya the Leafeon♀: <I'm not used to see this many mons working together.> — rolls eyes — <Even when for Lugia's son's kidnapping, we had to do a lot of convincing...>

Sol -peeks in, interested: <...The kidnapping? So it is true that Lugia has a son... How did that happen?>

Ichtacya: <Oh, it was a horrible thing... some Delta Pokémon were trying to blackmail Lugia into making the world better for them.> — waves a paw — <One would think they'd simply turn around, as if the Moon falling and the Zombie apocalypse around the same time weren't enough indication.>

Sol: <But Zombies are not too hard to beat, you know?> — waves tail proudly. — <You can just use a Revive or cast Wish on them...>

Ichtacya: <Well not all of us have such kind of techniques...> — half-growls.

Silent meanwhile observes as Dintel and his sister are assisting a group of Sandshrew and Vibrava in setting small sand walls and blocks, solidified by some nearby Bug Pokémon using their silk, as containers and barriers to assist the construction against the strong winds.

The egg shakes a bit, causing the three to notice.

"...The sandstorm is growing worse, girls. Do you want us to go back to the sandcave? You two can cuddle the egg."

Ichtacya: <Eeeeeeeee~ yes please :3>

Both Pokémon nod at unison, at least Ichtacya knowing full well how uncomfortable and damaging a sandstorm can be for a Pokémon not well adapted. Sol seems to have or at least be growing an understanding too, as every once in a while she stops to try and lick her paws clean and try to remove some dust of her ear fluffs.

Watching Silent walk with the egg, Sol approaches.

Sol: <Uhm... you do know what the egg will hatch into, yes?>

"I have no idea, Sol..." Silent answers as he covers the egg from the sandstorm.

Sol: <Errr... that's weird, I saw that was a blatant lie. And why was it linked to something else anyway? You are a researcher... have seen that kind of eggs several times...>

"I have my suspicions, Sol, but... If you don't mind, I prefer to keep some level of surprise."

Sol -swings her tail, trying to understand: <...Keep surprise?>

"See, sometimes it is not that bad to not know everything." Silent thinks back to his old years, the memories strong enough to be temporarily eclipsing Sol's ability to read the more shallow thoughts of the human. "They say happiness lies in the journey to the truth. That kinda requires a journey to be available."

Sol: <...Wouldn't have expected to hear something like this from you.>

"I've been there... wished I knew less."

Sol senses Silent thinking back to Giovanni for some reason, and sensing the accompanying mixture of envy and disgust, decides to not press the issue further. She falls back to where Ichtacya is also walking, and for some minutes the two Eeveelutions dedicate themselves to stop periodically and lick each other's fur clean of sand.

Sol: <...Well, at least it is not the friggin techniques of homing rocks that Trainers of Battle Tower-level of experience [tend] to abuse in battle.> — shivers — <Ugh...>

"Let's go." Silent walks ahead of both Pokémon.

Ichtacya -tries to remember: <...Homing rocks? I don't think I've seen anything like that...>

Sol -squints at Ichtacya: <...You have. You don't remember because you- oh wait, it is better if I just show you...>

Sol walks closer and focuses her mental powers on Ichtacya, her jewel glowing green; the effect of their shared evolutionary memory gives Sol an easier access into Ichtacya's mind, channeling the dormant memories of the Leafeon to allow her to flash back to the battle she had with Gaea, Rex's Golem, for Maekrite's birthday party party party party~...

~Some Months Ago~

Gaea turns to Ichtacya, waving a stubby arm. <Hello; it is a joy to meet you as well, > she announced.

Rex then instructs Gaea to go at Ichtacya, but not too much. Ichtacya's senses are momentarily invaded by the memory of the growing pain in her spine due to injuries suffered in combat at the time, and her mind only clears in time to see Gaea curling and shooting small rocks that fall everywhere around the battlefield... except at Ichtacya.

Memory!Ichtacya -rears up and looks around: <...What is going on?>

Ichtacya: <Whoaaa! That was a trip!> — blinks a couple of times, realizing she is back in the desert, turns to Sol. — <But what did those do? I didn't notice much of what happened later due to being recalled and having a lot of pain.>

Sol: <Well, when an opponent appears, those rocks spring to life, propelled probably by a psychic link to the caster's aural memory of the pain opponents have caused...> — blinks and shrugs as she sees Ichtacya frown at her — <Hey, I'm mostly guessing here. The Safari Zone does not have that many research books, you know?>

Ichtacya: <Fine, sorry... So...?> — waves a paw and :3s.

Sol: <Well, digging in your memory and that of your Trainer, I can sense even his team leader had some problems with it. The rocks had laid dormant longer than needed, perhaps because Rex's mons did not sense Silent's as threatening. Which, when you take into consideration they skipped Dintel, might make sense. Thing is...>

Maekrite is sent out to battle Laplace, the Nidorino leering to the Lapras. As the marine Pokémon charged power and shot a Water Pulse, the memory was suddenly filled for a brief moment with a weird sensation of contestation, as if she or something close to her had been challenged or called out. The rocks spread across the field suddenly levitated themselves off the ground, aimed at the poison Pokémon.

Memory!Rex: "Huh...that was kind of delayed. Almost like they forgot they were there."

Targeted by so many attacks, the Nidorino surprisingly had no choice but to stand still, easing the job of aiming for the rocks. Corageously he let them crash against him, but that had the effect of partially blocking the path of the Water Pulse also aimed at the Pokémon. Still, with that damage, Maekrite was forced to roll to the side to avoid staying a sitting Ducklett for longer.

Ichtacya yips as her recollection of memories ends. It is surprising for her, since she was not directly there, to have a so clear and "first-person" view of those events.

Ichtacya: <Those things are mean! He could not avoid them...>

Sol: <They usually can not be avoided, > — tilts head — <as they are a surprise for entry and have no aura or motion of their own, much like spikes.>

Ichtacya: <So it is like a Grass Knot, only you can not ever see it coming?>

Sol: <More or less...> — nods — <They are inherently riskier that way. I saw a news [report] about a Charizard in a match having a wing clipped by a sudden burst of the flying rocks in a Battle Tower tournament, the kind of stuff where some Trainers have their Pokémon just throw all their artillery at each other.>

Ichtacya: <...Artillery?>

Sol -turns to her somewhat annoyed, although not at her: <Exactly what you are thinking.>

Ichtacya gulps, not knowing what to answer. The weird idea of being stuck in a sandstorm of Stealth Rocks almost caused her to vomit. But the worst part was something else. For a single moment an imaginative memory of [Shahinne] being crippled or crushed by a surprise attack of Stealth Rocks crosses her mind, and the Leafeon has to make the effort of physically shake that thought off her head. The Psychic type also winced, feeling the psychic whiplash from the Leafeon's enormous care for the little Starly in Silent's team.

Both Pokémon, tails down and ears flattened from the conversation, decided to drop it for the moment and simply followed Silent around, as the human headed to a nearby oasis where other Pokémon were gathering to take a rest.

Dintel and his sister remained at the construction site, lying on top of some rocks, talking about things. After Sandslash narrates more or less what was life like after their parents left to the north and she inherited their old nest, Dintel starts drawing some figures in the sand and tries to tell a story; however, it takes some adequate prompting from his sister for Dintel to actually go somewhere with his narration, as he tellsa summary about his life after he left the Mirage Tower and until he was caught by Silent.

Meanwhile, Algira has fallen asleep on top of Silent's backpack. Sol observes the strange patterns the Pokémon seem to emit in her sleep, and then turns back to the group, to notice Silent talking about something to Maekrite as the Nidorino motions to the lands to the west.

Sol: «Weird... why was the human's voice tagged [golden] in the Eevee's memory as well?» — turns to examine the group — «Wonder if Luke would allow me to stay with these peoplemons to research this matter further... it is [admittedly] very intriguing.» — realizes what she has thought and shivers from surprise — «...And it is randomly happening to me too!»

Mandarin Island S, some days ago

An opulent family is celebrating the holidays with cake, wine, drinks for the kids and all kinds of berries for the two Pokémon in the house. In particular for the one still in recovery.

A young boy enters a room in the second floor holding a tray. There are a couple of food bowls, a water bowl and a carrot. The boy walks in and moves to the balcony, where a Nidorino is lying on a low makeshift bed, his chest and part of his left flank bandaged. The Pokémon watches the nearby forest and flickers his ears in apparently random directions.

Boy: "There you are. Still don't want to come with us to eat?"

The Nidorino doesn't respond although he does give the human enough deference to turn his ear at him. The boy answers with a smile as he leans in closer to examine his pet.

Boy: "You don't. Well, I brought you some food."

As the kid leaves the tray on the bed, two blood red eyes pop out from nowhere to look at him. The kid yells as a Nidorina appears from behind the bed to growl at him, in a not menacing but still unwelcoming manner.

Boy: "Fine, fine! I'm leaving..." Points at the Nidorino. "But you eat all your food, you have rehabilitation treatment tomorrow."

The boy smiles at the female Pokémon, who lunges at him rushing him to leave. As soon as the human has closed the door behind him, the Nidorina honks happily and turns around to notice the Nidorino has got on his feet and is trying to reach the tray.

Maria: <Again with that, love?>

Tony: <I... I can feed myself.>

Maria -swipes the Nidorino's paw, forcing him to wait as she drags the tray: <We agreed that I would take care of you.>

The Nidorino sighs and goes lie on the carpet as the Nidorina picks the carrot and feeds it to him patiently; he takes begrudgingly it and grumbling something unintelligible as he observes how much fun she seems to have.

Maria: <...What was that?>

Tony -drops ears: <...Sorry...> — turns to the female — <I just want to be healed. To run and hop around in the forest again.>

Maria: <Awww...> — goes hug the Nidorino — <I want you to be healthy too, you know?> — playfully noms his ear — <So you are taking it slow. I don't want to lose the bravest Nidorino around.>

She nuzzles him for a moment and then lets him continue his meal, staring at him intently. The Nidorino goes for the water bowl last, and slurps soundly and steadily until there is just some water left in the bowl. He proudly offers the bowl to the Nidorina, who grabs it and drinks the rest of the water.

After a moment of nuzzling, the Nidorino turns to the Nidorina and asks with some hesitation.

Tony: <Say it again, please?>

Maria: <....Uh? What?>

Tony -tilts head: <What you say when you nom my ear?>

Maria: <Silly. I'm proud of you~> — holds the male closer, careful to not bruise the bandaged flank — <You saw that [evil mon], the police humans said he destroyed a plaza in a fight... You survived him...> — shrugs — <They threw him out, Trainer said. And there were so many weird things happening around afterwards...>

Tony: <But I had to protect you from them...> — snorts, trying to scratch his ear — <and I lost.>

The Nidorina sits on her hind legs and watcher at her boyfriend curiously; a comment comes to her head and escapes it before she can really give it much thought.

Maria: <Come on, you were doing pretty fine until he slashed your chest.>

The Nidorino winces from the comment and tries to curl a bit to hide his wound as he uses to do; the female notices though and with a sly smile she forces the male to uncurl and embrace her. Not that he would offer much (willing) resistance.

Maria: <And I'm sure that is going to leave a pretty scar...>

The Nidos rest on each other for some minutes, only giving soft growls every once in a while. At some point the male raises his head and answers with some confidence.

Tony: <...Yeah! In three sessions they're going to remove those bandages and we'll be the envy of all the other Pokémon here!>

Maria -chuckles: <That's the spirit. Now come~>

Before the Nidorino can do anything, the Nidorina drags him (carefully) back to the makeshift bed. Once he gets comfortable there, the couple stare at each other lovingly for a moment.

Maria: <Silly. I'm going for food.>

The Nidorino nods and looks as the Nidorina goes to the nighstand on the room to open the door from there; with that done, she lets out a promising growl and then disappears into the red-carpeted corridor... not before dragging in the family's new priceless ming vase closer to the door.

Just in case~

Mounts Near Lavaridge, earlier during Jan 1

The fireworks explode all around the Hoenn sky. Orange, violet and green blasts of sound and powder filling the skies. The more the better for two aerial figures battling in the sky against this backdrop that could so easily pass for a warzone one in different circumstances...

???? #1: <.....Ack! That one almost gets me. Take this, invader!> — flamethrowers his opponent.

???? #2: <...Take my advice!> — blocks the flamethrower with one if his blue paws, then responds with a Swift — <Be more decisive!>

Twenty starts fly above and the Salamence above tries to fly past them to circle and once again try to evict this foreign Salamence from his mountain. Decisive, he wants. The local Salamence flies up a short distance and turns towards the intruder, hearing the energy stars among the loud blasts of powder turning to chase behind him as well. As the first dragon dives, imbuing his claws in draconic energy, the intruder charges up as well: with a Headbutt to the neck, he meets the charge of the first dragon while getting his flank pierced by the defender's Dragon Claw.

Both Pokémon, still in close quarters, some fireworks explode a very short distance from them, temporarily blinding both Pokémon with a green light. It is at that moment that, while both dragons spiral out of control biting and clawing each other in a blue and red ball of violence, the Swift stars whirl around above them impacting both dragons's faces and wings due to the angular velocity of their fall and flailing...

With the world spinning wildly around them, both dragons continue to try biting and clawing each other, their roars eclipsed by the explosions in the sky. After one blast of purple and orange that to the dragons smells like the spicy berries of the land below, the local manages to perceive the lack of lights and explosions to his right — which means the ground is in that direction. Roaring and swirling to reorient himself, the Salamence manages to land on the intruder consecutive slashes.

Local Salamence♂ used Fury Cutter! It hurt a little!

Local Salamence♂ used Fury Cutter! The Intruder Salamence's hide was scratched!

Local Salamence♂ used Fury Cutter! The Intruder Salamence's arm bleeds!

The intruder is forced to curl and Headbutt to make away, then realizes that the direction he has pulled to is the one gravity is pulling towards as well and extends his wings to try and recover flight capability. As he turns around, he sees a large rotating stream of orange headed in his direction and quickly summons Flying type energy to propel himself upwards, off the course of the Fire Blast.

Intruder Salamence♂ used Fly!

Quickly and unexpectedly having his dominion of the higher "ground" overtaken, the local Salamence charges his natural power and flies towards his opponent in a spiral, trying to build a good angle of attack. Finally managing to pinpoint his opponent's position thanks to the lights the fireworks provide, the local dragon covers himself in a shining blue aura and charges upwards with a roar and a Dragon Rush; the intruder dives from his Fly attack and both dragons collide in mid-air, the severity of the impact and the released elemental energy causing a small blueish explosion and a shockwave that sends both Pokémon spiraling down again.

The local dragon's senses are overtaken by the explosion and the pain, and it takes him a moment to notice that the sound of the fireworks has started to faint, realizing with this that they are both pummeling to certain death. He opens his eyes and tries to overcome the pain to stretch his wings; with great difficulty he manages to reorient himself to see the intruder pummeling a short distance away as well, his face bleeding as he tries to recover himself as well.

The two dragons let themselves fall a short a small distance until they hear a buzz and turn their heads to avoid one, two projectiles that fly past them.

Local Salamence: «...The missiles of light! We are pummelling towards the human settlement!»

Summoning all his (pseudo)legendary strength, the Salamence curls to facilitate a swirl, and then extends his wings again to brake easier; he turns to his opponent, ready to Dragon Claw him, only to notice one second too late that the intruder has also recovered and, with his tail imbued in draconic energy, also swirls to land a powerful whiplash at the local; the impact's elemental energy is disruptive enough on the local's nervous system to send him spiraling again without control, and without an immediate chance of recovery, and the dragon falls to the darkness of the hot springs near to the town.

Foreign Salamence♂ used Dragon Tail!

Local Salamence was launched out of battle!

The foreign Salamence glides and circles around until he can stabilize his flight and then, carefully avoiding the human projectiles, flies upwards a short distance again. Looking for a moment at the explosions around him, he summarizes his situation in his mind with only one word: "fun".

«Well, had to find something to do. I'd take this so-called "[Poké Ringer]" competition to show the birds around here how to be awesome, but... I'd need a Trainer to get into it. Or...» — smirks as he catches an updrift to observe the roads to the east — «I could just go and crash the place. Why to stop the fun of the new year?»

Content with the results of this adventurous day, the Salamence stretches his neck and lets out an egregious growl as the sky continues to be saturated with celebrational explosions and colors around him.

Foreign Salamence: <These skies and mountains are mine now! Whooo hoooo~!> — casually lets out an Hyper Beam to the skies.

Goldenrod City, earlier during Jan 1

Copahue and Harriet are waiting on top of a building, in a more or less secluded position, waiting for Shahinne to come up with them. From that place they observe the festivities taking place across the city as some city officers walk across the shorewall, giving the countless launchers for the fireworks a last-minute check.

Harriet: <....Oh?>

Suddenly Shahinne flies very fast at them as behind her there is a ruckus in the plaza. Trilling, she clings to her adoptive mom's belly plumage and starts crying soundly.

Shahinne: <Mom! Mom!>

Harriet: <....Ooooof!> — surprised — <Shhhh... there there, what happened?>

Shahinne digs herself further in her mom's plumage and speaks from there, only a little fluff of tailfeather visible, at the very same moment that some hurras and celebrations can be heard from the plaza below.

Shahinne: <[-The other birds are mean to me! And I did nothing bad to them!> — cries more, sneezes in one of Harriet's feathers — <They said I am a [mean] bird that bullies other birds when I grow up! ...And I don't even know what that means!!!> — chirps.

The two birds try to interpret that as they try to calm the Starly down and speak to her for some moment, retreating nearer to the wall of the building to protect her from the loud fireworks... After some cuddling, convincing and a couple of requests for Shahinne to repeat the information, Copahue and Harriet reach a conclusion.

Copahue: <...A Staraptor? Here?>

Harriet: <Oh dear... Must be to the north in the tall buildings...>

Shahinne: <I'm not mean! >.< > — claws her mom's talon, nervously.

The two larger birds nuzzle their adoptive daughter, wishing that for once the loud fireworks stop. After some waiting time seeing the situation not improve, Copahue instructs Harriet to have Shahinne hold tight, and the three birds fly across the streets, looking for the less populated route back to the PEFE building...

Down in the City, at about the same time

At a random window below them a Hoothoot has just perched on a heap of clean and warm clothes to watch the fireworks. The Pokémon looks at the streets below him and decides to wait until the next series of fireworks to fly to a nearby tree where his friends are probably watching the celebrations as well.

Suddenly, a purple tail appears and swings eerily behind the bird, and before the Pokémon can react, the heap of clothes moves and the Hoothoot's leg is clamped by... something. The bird's back is slashed by purple claws as well and then dragged to the bottom of the heap with barely a screech. Only a couple of feathers pulled by the sudden movement glide for a moment and deposit themselves gravefully on top of the heap, as only testimony of what has just happened.

Some moments after, the swinging tail appears again and a soft growl can be heard. A Purrloin climbs up to the heap and, after licking her claws for a moment, watches outside through the window, enjoying the experience. Some minutes later, a bit tired, she arches, stretches, and starts clawing and scratching the heap of clothes.

Purrloin♀: <Free meals :3> — curls and purrs contently.

A human enters to the room, screeches and walks right up to the heap with the Purrloin on top.

Girl: "...Mina! What did you doooooo! I need those clothes! Tomorrow!!!!!"

For an answer the Purrloin uncurls and curls again, purring.

Girl: "...What am I gonna do with you!" She angrily points at the cat and tries to remove her from the heap, without much success. "I should give you a bath! So that you see how terrible is what you did!"

The Purrloin turns on her back and swings her paws playfully; seeing no positive answer from her human servant, she tries purring, which is responded by a buff, a glare, and angry footstomps.

Mina the Purrloin♀: <Oh well...> — licks a paw — <I hope I didn't have to resort to this so early...>

The Purrloin plays on the heap a bit more and then turns to the human, ears pointed to the sides, playfully looking at her.

Mina: <:3~>

Girl: "Oh no I know that trick." Thumps.

Mina: <:3?>

Girl: "..."

Mina: <:3?> — swings her tail.
