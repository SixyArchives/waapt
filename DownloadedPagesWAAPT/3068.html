-Lake watches as I serve my team some food. Taking note of the Pokémon on my team.

The first Pokémon that immediately stands out to Lake is the Typhlosion. Apparently named Cinder. So this was the starter Luke had chosen instead of him? To be honest, Lake couldn't help but feel a little jealous.

Interestingly, Cinder had mentioned that he could cook. And judging by the smell wafting from whatever Luke was handing out (Sausages? It was definitely meat of some sort), Lake was inclined to believe him.

The next Pokémon that stood out to Lake was a Flygon. Lake found it odd at first that Luke had one, but then remember seeing a bunch of Trapinch in the Safari Zone one time. It wasn't easy to put two and two together, there.

Lake then took note of an Umbreon, who went by the name of Luna, who was eating her lunch in the shade of a nearby tree. For some reason, Lake couldn't help but be reminded of Sol at the sight of her.

Actually, Lake was begining to suspect that Luna WAS Sol, but in a different form.

This whole new timeline thing was obviously going to take some getting used to, Lake concluded.-
