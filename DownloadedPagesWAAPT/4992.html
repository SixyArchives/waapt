Ecruteak City, several months prior to the day

Current Team (Past!Rex): Rascal the Flareon (♂), Gaea the Graveler (♀), Ohm the Mareep (♀), Krystal the Zorua (♀), and Anya the Scyther (♀)

Wandering the streets with his team, Rex grumbled softly to himself, a metaphorical dark cloud billowing over his head.

Rex: Ugh...

His Pokemon (sans Krystal) looked amongst themselves confused. They'd won the badge, so why was their trainer not happy?

Rascal: <Maybe he misses Fang and Breezy?>

Ohm: <Maybe he's sick?>

Gaea: <How curious...>

Noticing his Pokemon huddling up behind him, Rex turned a curious gaze to them. As a result, he ended up missing the brown shape speeding towards him until it was too late.

???: <Pidge!>

Rex: Huh? WHOA!

Falling backwards in surprise, Rex barely avoided the talons of the startled Pidgeotto, the bird Pokemon gazing at him curiously. After a moment, it decided to roost on the boy's shoulder, spreading its wings carefully. Underneath its right wing was a small bag, the strap resting at the base of the back of the wing. Rascal eyed it with some suspicion, but Rex simply smiled, patting the bird on the head.

Rex: Hey...I know you! You're the messenger Pidgey from Prof. Elm's Lab! ...Well, a Pidgeotto now.

The boy reached into his bag, fishing out an Oran Berry and giving it to the bird.

Rex: Look at you, all grown up now~

Carrier Pidgeotto: <Pidge~>

Taking the berry in its beak, the bird tossed it to the ground and followed after it, giving it several pecks before it was finally gone. Then it turned back to Rex, spreading its wings again.

Carrier Pidgeotto: <Pidge!>

After a couple of moments, the Pidgeotto fluttered into the air, flying in circles around the boy's head. Rex frowned, unsure as to what the bird was trying to convey. Then, as if a lightbulb had switched on in his head, he realized.

Rex: Oh...you've never been out this far, have you?

Landing on a nearby bench, the Pidgeotto lowered its head in shame.

Carrier Pidgeotto: <Pidge...>

Smiling at the disheartened bird, Rex gave it another pat, his own Pokemon simply sitting back and letting him handle the situation.

Rex: Don't worry about it. I'm just getting used to seeing Johto for the first time too. I'll help you look for where you're trying to go.

With a warble of joy, the Pidgeotto flapped its wings, resting on Rex's shoulder again with a happy look (for a bird).

Carrier Pidgeotto: <Pidge!>

Opening the bag, Rex slid out the top sheet of the paperwork, hoping that it contained some kind of clue as to the Pidgeotto's destination.

Rex: Let's see here..."To my dear friend. I do hope this data proves useful; I feel that there is very much that the Symbol Pokemon have to teach us should we choose to..." Oh! Symbol Pokemon. He must mean the Unown.

He placed the paper back in the bag, nodding his head in understanding.

Rex: You must be headed to the Ruins of the Alph, then. Fortunately, you're not too far from it. Just follow us and we'll lead you there.

He turned to his team with a smile.

Rex: Right guys?

The general consensus was four yeses and one "Whatever".

Ruins of the Alph, several months ago

Rex exited the rest-house, looking around at the area in front of him. In truth, he'd never been here either, though it was within walking distance of Goldenrod. He'd simply been too enraptured with the idea of playing his childhood games with Rascal to even consider hanging around some dusty old ruins.

Now, though, it was a different story entirely...for him anyway.

Rascal: <Ugh...this place looks lame.>

Krystal: <For once, I agree with the mutt.>

Rex began to make his way in the direction of a small group of people that was gathered near a cave entrance. The place had become a bit more popular in recent years, notably after the lead researcher received a Nobel Prize for his contribution to Prof. Elm's research. His "Unown Dex", as it had been called, not only helped decipher several of the messages within the Ruins, but also gave way to research into different forms of Pokemon.

As Rex made his way closer, he overheard a few trainers talking amongst themselves.

Trainer A: So, what's up with these Unown anyway?

Trainer B: I dunno. They don't seem that great to me. All they learn is Hidden Power.

Trainer C: But they have their own radio channel! Don't you think they're kind of cool at least?

Trainer A: Oh, you heard that channel too? I thought it was an alien broadcast or something at first.

Ohm: <Oh, Pokemon on the radio? I know a Flaaffy that picked up DJ Ben's morning show with his Signal Beam once!>

Picking out a man in a white, lab-coat ("Why do all of these guys wear white anyway? It's so dirty in this place."), he made his way over to him, waiting for him to finish speaking with an elderly woman before chiming in.

Rex: Excuse me, sir? I was wondering if I might be able to speak to the man in charge. I believe I intercepted a package meant for him from Professor Elm.

Ruins of the Alph, several months ago

Following the other man's lead, Rex ended up in a small building to the east of the main cave. It seemed like more of a shack for hanging out in someone's spare time rather than an actual research facility. Still, the building served its purpose well, and Rex had to applaud them for getting work done in such conditions.

As Rascal and Krystal continued to gripe about how boring the ruins were (Gaea eventually snapping at them to hush), the man from before lead Rex to a side room, knocking on the door gently.

Researcher: Excuse me, Calvin. There is someone here to see you.

The knob turned after a moment, and a young, clean shaven man opened the door. Rex seemed surprised to note that he only seemed to be in his thirties; he'd been expecting someone much older. Calvin swept his gaze over the young boy in front of him, noticing the package the Pidgeotto on his shoulder was carrying.

Calvin: Ah, I see. Thank you.

As the other man left, Calvin's gaze turned to Rex, a smile of gratitude on his face.

Calvin: You must be with Radek?

At Rex's confused look, he chuckled.

Calvin: Excuse me. Radek is Prof. Elm.

Rex: Oh. Yes, sir, I am.

Calvin: I see. I was afraid something had happened when I didn't get that paperwork sooner.

Rex: Well, I ended up coming across this Pidgeotto in Ecruteak. It seems Prof. Elm sent him to deliver something to you, but he got lost along the way.

Pidgeotto: <Pidge~>

Calvin chuckled at that, allowing the Pidgeotto to fly over and rest on his shoulder.

Calvin: Well, in any case, I appreciate you getting him to me. Perhaps I can give you something for your troubles...ah, I know!

He reached into his pocket, pulling out a grayish stone, placing the object in Rex's palms.

Calvin: A trainer such as yourself might have use for something like this.

Rex: Oh...thank you, sir!

Rex got a Moon Stone!

Ruins of the Alph, several months ago

Calvin: In the meantime, I suggest that you go ahead and view the ruins. Normally, we would refrain from letting so many people enter at this time in order to prevent startling the Unown and potentially destroying any property, but I trust someone in Radek's tutelage knows better than to do anything destructive in a place such as this.

Rex: Yes, sir.

Calvin: Good! Then feel free to look around. In the meantime, I'll be sure to let Radek know his Pidgeotto arrived.

Bowing politely, Rex exited the building, preparing to begin his tour of the place. Calvin, meanwhile, returned to his office. After setting aside some bird feed for the Pidgeotto, he pulled the papers from his bag, looking them over attentively.

Calvin: Now let's see here... "Collaboration with Prof. Rowan has yielded new results in the Solaceon Ruins. Of equal help has been the Champion of Sinnoh, Cynthia, with her excavation of what seems to be called the 'Mystri Stage'..."
