<Archeops: "Hmm... Good to see that the mons at the royal treasury have been making themselves useful-">

-The sound of a door being forced open and some figures rushing in can be heard-

-The Archeops whirls around and sees a Venusaur, an Omastar, and a Kabutops standing in the doorway-

<Archeops: "... This had better be important... For your sake...">

<Omastar: "Trust us...">

SHING!

<Kabutops: "You wouldn't know the beginning of it... My liege...">

<Venusaur: " NOW! ">

-And the former attempts Hydro Pump, while the Kabutops lunges at the Archeops, and the Venusaur attempts PoisonPowder-

<Archeops: (*sigh*) >

-And flies up, dodging the attacks after a hasty Agility-

-Hmm... Perhaps it's time to launch a new conquest? The western empire certainly isn't ready for that... Their gods slumber deeply, yes... But they are still a little too unified to risk stirring... Perhaps in a few years when the lot of it looks like that bay region. A collection of squabbling towns lead by useless rulers who could likely be swayed every bit by rewards as threats... Were they of suitable stock... Why, most of them are barely fit to call themselves peasants! Let alone be servants of the ancients!-

-And smashes down onto the floor with Earthquake, insta-fainting two of the three-

<Omastar: X_X "Oog... M-Mommy? I think that m-my shell broke...">

<Kabutops: @_@ "A-Argh...">

-With HP mostly depleted-

<Venusaur: "U-U-Ugh... We're not done yet!">

-And attempts to latch onto the Archeops' neck with Vine Whip-

<Archeops: "Oh please...">

-And Acrobatics the Venusaur into a wall-

<Venusaur: "O-Ow...">

-And collapses-

-The lands to the east are a bit of a non-starter... We have taken the island and its port in the southwest corner... Yes, it is diffuse... The entire center ceded to a collection of ferals and bandits... And those imbecile livestocks' capital would likely fall with just a short thrust... But their gods are very much awake... And stand in the way of any march eastward...-

-The forms of an Aerodactyl, a Lileep, and a Croconaw rush into the room-

<Lileep: "My liege! Are you okay?!">

<Croconaw: "Yeah! We heard a scuffle!">

-Leering at the Croconaw-

<Archeops: "And just what do you suppose that you're doing here?">

<Croconaw: "M-MEEP! Th-The captain said that y-you needed e-extra security... A-And I-I'm a-about to e-evolve, I sw-swear!">

<Aerodactyl: "He was really helpful today, my liege... Also, what is this, the third attempt on your life this month?">

<Archeops: "Fourth. That still doesn't explain why you are in the ranks of my personal guards in place of a true ancient! One who isn't fully evolved at that!">

<Croconaw: "O-Okay! I-I'll l-lea-">

<Archeops: "But since you are here, you might as well make yourself useful...">

-Motions at the three would-be assassins-

-And claiming the center for ourselves doesn't appear to be a strong possibility... The very mention of its residents is enough to make some of this empire's soldiers quake in fear... There's some sort of history there. Some legendary demon apparently terrorized their kind thousands of moons ago...-

-And the three drag the attempted assassins before the Archeops-

<Lileep: "(*sigh*) Knew I should have stuck with marine service.">

<Kabutops: "Wh-What are you going to d-do with us?">

<Archeops: "That depends... If you tell me who sent you, I might decide not to make the lot of you into the centerpiece for next week's banquet...">

-At the mention of this, the Omastar weakly retreats into its shell, and the Kabutops and Venusaur both begin to tremble-

<Venusaur: "B-B-But I'm n-no good f-for e-eating! M-My b-body and pl-plant are full of p-poisons!">

<Archeops: "Oh, I'm sure that I'll find a use for you at the table... Perhaps as a garnish...">

<Venusaur: "A- G-Gar-Ur-Ur-Urk...">

-Eying the quivering plant-toad and utterly petrified-looking vampire horseshoe crab-

<Archeops: "... And I see that you have not eaten recently. You're a wise lot... I'm not fond of annoyances, and you don't need to give me any further ones...">

-But no matter. What the warrior cannot take in battle, the merchant can... Given enough time. Why, the diplomatic bureau has informed me that they have begun to move puppets on whom we can depend into positions of power in that realm of livestock... To think that the mons of this world are so ignorant as to not have one of their number know how to craft items specifically tailored for their own kinds!-

<Archeops: "Now... Who sent you?">

<Kabutops: "The C-Carracosta H-House! Th-They sent us!">

<Archeops: "Anyone in particular? That's rather vague...">

-From within its shell-

<Omastar: "On-One of their me-messengers g-gave us the d-debriefing!">

<Venusaur: "H-He said th-that La-Lady H-Hawksbill s-sent for it! Th-That's all we kn-know! H-Honest!">

<Archeops: "Very well... GUARDS! Take them away!">

<Aerodactyl: "Uh... Have you considered my proposal of the heraldry for your successor?">

<Archeops: "What on earth is this empire supposed to do with a seal with lance-toting Liepard?!">

<Aerodactyl: "It's symbolic! The Liepard stand for cunning and guile! Just like how this empire started when it was founded so many moons ago!">

-Rolling eyes-

<Archeops: "... I'll keep it in mind.">

-And the three are led trembling out of the room by the guards-

-There's apparently some sort of exploratory party that's due to report back from canvassing the seas to the south for some legendary island that holds this world's knowledge of how to produce gummis... With such an island under our control, the world would more or less be ours to force to kneel before their rightful masters... So I suppose that my boredom here shall soon be addressed by their findings...-

<Archeops: "Hmph. Now... Where was I...?">

-And returns to his dust bath-

-And if not, there's always reassigning them to that malarial swamp outpost that we squabble over with the lands to the west, to the east, and with the very sea itself. Reassignment always seems to be a decent motivator... And it's more practical than death or incarceration anyways-
