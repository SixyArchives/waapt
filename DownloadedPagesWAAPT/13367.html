Sycamore Pokemon Lab

Cebu walks into the lab, and gently knocks.

"Professor Sycamore?"

"Zzzzzz..." Cebu peers over to see the prone form of Professor Sycamore, snoring with a scientific journal on his face. Cebu can't help but crack a smile at the rather silly scene in front of her. A rather grinchy idea comes to her mind, and she sucks in her breath.

"Oh, Professor Sycamore...    Wake Up.   "

Sycamore's eyes shoot open, and he jumps into the air with a yelp, landing on the edge of his desk. The sudden increase the weight not only cracks the desk, but causes it to flip, sending Sycamore onto the floor, his head hitting the chair and knocking it onto him as well.

When it's all over, the whole desk is in shambles, papers cover the floor, and Sycamore is moaning weakly from beneath his office supplies. Cebu puts a hand to her mouth in shock and embarrassment.

"Oh my- Professor, are you alright?!" Frantically, she starts pulling the desk and chair off of him, but the professor merely starts chuckling.

"Haha, no problem, no problem at all. I'd be lying if I said that hasn't happened to me before. Now, what can I do for you?"

Cebu sighs in relief as she helps the professor up. Hi, Professor You may not remember me, but-

No, no, I never forget a face. Youre Youre Did you serve me coffee in Anistar the other week? Oh, I must have forgotten to give you your tip! Let me see here He starts rifling through his pockets and pulling out loose change.

Ah, no, Professor. My name is Im Celeste Berceaux. At the name, Sycamores eyes light up, and he runs to a filing cabinet entitled Missed Appointments. Passing the 6 drawers labeled My Fault,  he arrives at the one on the bottom, labeled Not My Fault,  pulling out a file and opening it.

Ah, Celeste Berceaux. You were supposed to come pick up your starter, oh, about 9 years ago. Phoo, even Ive only been that late well, lets not count useless numbers, huh? I take it youre here for it now?

Cebu nods obediently. Yessir, Im quite excited.

Sycamore sighs. Thought you might be Unfortunately, Im fresh out! A kid came in here earlier and picked up my last one.

Cebu frowns. Oh When are you planning to get more in?

Well, I could pick up some from Kiloude right now, but Ive got to clean up Say, if I give you information on how to pick one up, would you mind terribly going yourself?

Cebu nods. I can do that. Besides, I havent been to Kiloude in ages- Id love to see how it is nowadays.

Sycamore grins widely. Cest bon! He reaches into his pocket and pulls out 3 index cards. These are registry codes for the Friend Safari. Show them to the clerk and shell let you into the habitat. Come back later, show me what you picked!

Cebu smiles as she starts walking out. Sure thing! See you soon!
