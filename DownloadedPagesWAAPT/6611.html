Flipside, PEFE-B

Merle is joined in the air by quite a few other flying-types; Pidgey, Spearow, Tailow, Starly, Pidove, and Tropius are all well-represented.

"As the Murkrow flies" would seem to be the guiding philosophy for this town, in fact. Like previously seen towns, it is cobbled together from modules; they are rather spaced out in this instance, though, arranged in a pattern that takes more advantage of three dimensions- though care seems to be taken to avoid blocking any module too much from the sun streaming in the roof of the dome.

Traversal by foot seems to be more of an afterthought; apart from a few sections, bridges between modules are sparse, and circuitous paths are often required to travel between seemingly adjacent modules.

Several modules possess notable antennas, and larger antennas can be seen outside the facility proper, mounted on the dome.
