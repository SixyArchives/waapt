Sandelford

It was mayhem in Sandelford. Hazel-Rah ordered that all runs into the Warren were to be sealed up in order to stall the incoming Efrafans and that all those who were unable to fight were to hid themselves in concealed warrens and disguised runs. If they were going to be killed, they would fight tooth and nail until their demise.

Suddenly, Hazel-rah's brother, Fiver, started shaking and shivering and jabbering on about this and that. Hazel quickly turned around to see what was up with his brother.

Fiver: <Oh it's cold... so cold... Freezing, stop! O zorn!> -Starts shaking on the ground-

Hazel-Rah: <Fiver!>

Hazel starts nudging Fiver, who is still madly babbling on.

Hazel-Rah: <Speak to me Fiver!>

Fiver: <There's a dragon loose on the hills...>

Hazel-rah thought long and hard about what Fiver had said. "A dragon loose on the hills"? The only dragon he knew of was the dragon of that trainer camping on the base of the hill...

Hazel-rah then got an idea. He rushed over to Dandilion, who was surprised about his rahs abruptness

Dandilion: <Hazel-rah? What->

Hazel Rah: <Quick, we must find a way to get to the bottom of the hill to find the dragon. Is there a run we haven't blocked yet?>

Dandilion: <...There's the secret run that runs out behind the hill. What is your plan sir.>

Hazel Rah: <I shall tell you on the way>

Losing no more time, Hazel and Dandilion rushed out of the secret run and start bounding down the hill, towards the area where the girl set up camp.

Just as the Efrafans were scrounging up the hill.

Hazel Rah: <Quick, we must find a way to get to the bottom of the hill to find the dragon. Is there a run we haven't blocked yet?>
