With a loud screech, a Gel Scyther darted forward and swung a blade at Luke. But before he could be bisected, Unite blocked it with a hastily raised Protect.

Unite used Protect!

Unite: <Escape Rope! USE THE ESCAPE ROPE!>

After scooping up Ad Min's dormant parts, Luke reached into his bag and pulled out the length or rope, holding it up triumphantly.

Luke: SAYANORA, SUCKE- AH!

He flinched as the Scyther swung it's other blade at him, dropping the rope in surprise, allowing the insect to slash at it and nearly bisect the rope in two before it had even dropped to the floor, rendering it useless.

AAAHHH! NO!

Unite: <YOU IDIOT!>

Looking around quickly for any other means of escape, Luke spotted a door that lead out to the catwalk overlooking the deployed cannon.

Luke: QUICK! Out there!

The two bolted for the door, narrowly avoiding attacks from the false Pokémon as Luke slammed the open button, the metal door sliding open and allowing them out.

Unite: <Go! GO! GO!'>

They both rushed out onto the catwalk, only to see that there was no other way out...except downwards.

Far below the cannon hanging beneath the airship with the vast waters of the Goldenrod Bay. If they were to jump down through the hole, they would be able to pull up and fly off with a bit of effort.

Unite: <Have you got a plan! They're coming!>

Luke looked to see that the false Pokémon were squeezing through the doorway to catch up with them. With another loud screech, the Scyther rushed forward at an almost invisible speed, slamming into Unite.

The False Scyther used Quick Attack!

Unite: <BWARK!>

Unite squawked in pain, but as the Scyther attempted to bisect him with a well placed slash, he blocked it with a metallic wing.

Unite used Steel Wing!

Luke: We're gonna' have to jump!

Unite: <What? ARE YOU NUTS?!>

The Scyther swung at him again, but with a flap of the wings, he just narrowly dodged it.

Luke: There isn't enough time for me to get on your back!

Nervously, the trainer climbed onto the railings.

There's not enough time for me to get on your back! You'll have to grab on and carry me in mid-air!

Unite: <But what if I can't catch up wi-AAAH!!!>

He quickly raised a Protect shield as a false Manectric discharged a Thunderbolt at him.

<OKAY! Let's just do it together! On three!>

Dropping the shield, the Braviary shot over the railing and dived.

<THREE!>

Even though his primal instincts were telling him not to step over the edge, Luke gulped, outstretched his arms, and fell forward, plummeting after Unite.

Goldenrod Bay

The two plummeted out of the bottom of the airship, the vast  and hard  ocean below racing towards them.

Luke: I IMMEDIATELY REGRET THIS IDEA! AAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHH!!!

Unite: <HOLD ON!>

A few meters ahead of him, Unite stretched his wings, angled up to loop over and gain some height, before tucking his wings back in and diving towards Luke as they fell closer and closer to the water below.

Luke: HURRY!

Unite: <I'M TRYING!>

The bird was closing the gap, closer, closer...the bird extended his talons and reached for the human's back, trying to grab on as the ocean rushed up to meet them.

Luke: AAAHHH!!! THIS IS WHERE IT ALL ENDS!

Unite: <I WON'T LET YOU DIE!>

The bird clasped his talons Luke's shoulders and began to flap with all of his might.

<NOT TODAY!>

Luke: AAAAAAHHHHHHH!!!!!

Luke covered his eyes, unable to bear looking as they hit the ocean and both became bags of shatter bones. Unite strained as hard as he can, flapping his wings to slow their falling speed down, further and further...

Unite: <GAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHH!!!>

Luckily, Luke was thin and didn't weigh much. That, and Braviary were strong enough to fly while carrying a car in their talons. Or was that just something the Pokédex had exaggerated on?

Either way, Unite flapped as they fell slower...and slower...until they stopped. Just above the surface of the calm ocean. Realizing that they hadn't been smashed to pieces, Luke uncovered his eyes to find that they were both hovering safely. Although Unite was justifiably warn out.

Unite: <...See? What did I tell you...phew...>
