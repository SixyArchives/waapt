After what seemed like months, a certain rather ragged Braviary finally staggered into his trainer's module, glad to had made it back home after having to travel all the way from Unova back to the middle of the Sea Of Kyogre.

Unite: <Mukking finally...>

But before he could settle in his nest for a well deserved rest (After letting Kazoo know he was okay. She had probably been worried sick about him!), Unite was suddenly set upon by a rather frantic blob of gel.

Jello: <YOU'RE ALIVE!>

Unite: <SQUAWK!!!>

The Braviary hops backwards in shock, bristling his feathers in a fear response.

Jello: <Oh Arceus, I thought the murderer got you!>

He blinks, tilting his head curiously.

Unite: <...Murderer?>

Jello nod frantically, giving the room a glance as if to check if nobody else was listening in.

Jello: <Yes! There's a murderer on the loose! He's already killed Lake, Stratus, Woolly, Treeo, Comet and Phyco! And I thought he had gotten you until you got back just now!>

Skeptically, Unite narrows his eyes at the blob, not quite believing what he was saying.

Unite: <Seriously?>

Jello: <How else would you be able to explain why they've just vanished without a trace?>

Unite: <Well, I could think of some other ideas...>

It sounded to him like perhaps they had been having trouble with those odd crystal thingys that had teleported that Gabite here from her world, and then the pair of them to Unova.

<How can you be sure they're dead, anyways? Did you ever find the bodies?>

The Reuniclus blinks, then shakes his head.

Jello: <No...I mean, have you seen the size of this place? The murderer could have hidden them anywhere! He may have even buried them all in the foundations so we'd never find them!>

Deciding at this point that Jello was just being his usual paranoid self, the Braviary rolled his eyes.

Unite: <Suuuuuuure he did. Listen, I'd love to chat some more, but I'm tired, and it's been months since I last slept in a decent nest, so I'm gonna' go and catch some Z's for a little bit.>

And with that, he flaps his mighty wings and takes off to find his nest, leaving Jello to call after him.

Jello: <HEY! At least lock the door so that he can't kill you in your sleep!>
