The bug charged at Sophia and sent a Bug Buzz her way, disorienting her with the annoying high pitched sound and letting the bug ram her few feet back.

Sophia: <Oh, that thing's gonna pay!>

Sophia then braced herself for the second charge of the Pokemon, an arrogant glare on it.

Nathan: "What are you doing? Get out of the way!"

Sophia didn't listen, she had another plan. She waited until the bug flew close enough, and focused all her leftover energy and struck the dragonfly straight in the face, severely disorienting it, it's flying halting as it struggled to regain it's senses. Sophia took this opportunity to quickly hop onto it's back. The pokemon didn't take this well and started flapping it's body wings, but Sophia's got a firm grip on it.

Nathan: "What are you doing?"

Sophia: <Quick, throw a ball!>

Nathan: What? Why?

Sophia: <You needed a Pokemon? Capture this thing, quickly!> She said as she was flailed around on the pokemon's back.

Nathan hastily pulled outt a Poke ball and threw if at the bug. The ball successfully collided with it and sucked it in, dropping Sophia on the ground to stare at the ball's shaking.

Nathan: "Why is the ball shaking so much?"

The ball violently shook around for several seconds before a click sounded, marking the capture of the pokemon.

Nathan: "Did... did I capture it?"

Sophia: <I think so...> She said before exhaustion fainted her. Nathan recalled her and headed back to Mistralton.

He noticed the same crew as earlier and paid a visit.

Nathan: "You didn't warn me of the giant dragonflies, Nico."
