It made sense. He had a package that he knew just needed to be delivered to somemon. Who else would have such strong feelings for such a simple job?

...Nah, that didn't seem right. "Mail-mon" just didn't seem like the sort of job a Feraligatr such as himself would take. Thanks to their bulk, as well as their agility in water, they were more suited to professions that required brute strength or working in aquatic environments.

But that left the question...why did he want to deliver this package so badly?

Was it for a friend? A family member?

It seemed more likely, but for now, there wasn't really any way he could find out. With a sigh, he placed the box back in his personal hammerspace, then got up to head over to everymon else.
