Kalos League, Two days before Rens E4 challenge

-Ren and co are walking towards the League's Pokemon Center and are promptly spotted by Silent; who walks in in smug fashion and casually blocks Ren's way into it by standing right in front of the door.-

Silent: Hello there... looks who's here.

Ren: -taking this in stride- Hello, Silent. I'm assuming you needed something?

Silent: Well, I was thinking, since you have been doing pretty fine so far, that my team needs some extra training. -shrugs- And you just happen to be around to ask.

Ren: -smiles genuinely- Is that a challenge, Silent?

Silent: Sure, a challenge it is. And since Im so much of a gentleman I'll let you choose your Pokémon first. -readies a couple of Pokéballs-

Ren: -stepping into a more open area- You're too kind.

-Ren takes out a Pokéball.-

Battle! PEFE Researcher Silent vs. Veteran Coordinator Ren!

Ren: Wendi, its time for your grand come back and show off your new look!

-As Wendi emerges from her ball and takes her position, a bronze headband with a dark teal, white, slate, and ice-blue stone set in it is gleams as the sky darkens and hail begins to fall.-

-Ren, holding his Mega Cane vertically with his left hand, presses his right palm to his Key Stone. As the key stone begins to gleam, Ren raises his cane aloft.-

Wendi's Abomasite is reacting to Ren's Mega Cane!

-As the crystal refracts and reflects the light from the Key Stone, Wendi is surrounded by a spherical cocoon of dark grey energy. After a brief moment, the cocoon shatters.-

-Emerging from the sphere, the sprouts on Wendi's back have grown into great shafts of ice. Wendi's fur has become longer and shaggy over her entire body, particularly around her head. Green fur, or the remnants of the sprouts that grew from her back, surround the base of the large icicles on her back, while white fur covers the of her back. In addition to the shafts of ice on her back, Wendi's body has also grown in size. Wendi, due to her massively increased weight and size is now on all fours, supported by her feet - which have gotten smaller - and her forearms have also grown in size, shape, and strength.-

Wendi has Mega Evolved into Mega Abomasnow!

-Silent takes a look at Wendi and her evolution, then steps back to make more room for the battle and sends out a Pokéball, which opens to reveal the feline figure of Sol.-

Silent: Okay then, let's see those tactics that brought you so far. Sol, you ready?

Sol: -examines Wendi for a moment, then licks her paws: <I think I can do it.>

-Silent then sends out Python and Shahinne to sit nearby and watch the battle, which they do by sitting on a nearby bench-

Python: <Hiss?> -looks as both Silent and Ren are around and battling-

-Keys lets himself out of his ball and joins Python and Shaninne.-

Keys: <This will be fun.> >:D

-Keys brings out some Deluxe Poképuffs from somewhere.-

Keys: <These are from Ren. So who you rooting for?>

Python: -slithers closer to the puffs, rattles: <Uhm. extra one if I / for Ren?>

Shahinne: <Im rooting for Sol obviously!> — puffs her chest — <I like those tricks she does~>

-Keys grins at Pythons response and expecting nothing less from Shahinne.-

Keys: -to Python- <You can have as many as you like~>

-Ren glances at Keys, then roles his eyes.-

Ren: He just had to come to me didn't he?

-Meanwhile Silent takes a quick look at his Pokégear for stats and notices, letting it show for a moment that the device has a Mega Card Reader attached. He then speaks to Sol-

Silent: This is our start, it seems. Sol, go ahead with a Swift, let's see if she can move.

-Sol takes it to the air with a high leap and once there she unleashes from her head jewel an arc spread of stars. Of the normal Swift kind, not "lil'stars".-

-Indeed, Wendi is almost immobile due to her increased girth, as the the star-shaped bolts strike her easily from above, causing moderate damage.-

Wendi: <I dont suppose you'd mind standing still?>

Sol: -lands, gets to the ground in hunting position- <I would, but still, feel free to attack~>

Ren: Status moves are out of the question sooooo...

-out loud- Wendi, use Blizzard!

-Wendi inhales deeply, and then exhales, blowing out ice particles as the hail storm in creases the size and strength of the attack, adding its ice particles and chilling wind to Wendi's blizzard.-

-Sol attempts to endure the hail, which by itself already causes the usual slipperiness and sticky paws problems to her; she is distracted just a moment and is hit by the blizzard, having to curl into a small ball and face away from the attack to withstand it.-

Python: -slithers closer to the puffs, attempts to drag one away- <Three coins to the big snow thing. :3>

Keys: >:D

Shahinne: -puffs her chest feathers harder because of the sudden cold-
