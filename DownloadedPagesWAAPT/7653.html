Psyche's Dream

Psyche is in Black City. She's got a stand up, running a Three Card Monty scam. ~You sir, would you like to try your hand at a game of luck? No? Maybe you, ma'am?~ A small crowd is gathered around, most of them broke. She reels in a sucker and sets up. ~See the red queen? Try and find her. Here we go!~ She starts slow, letting him win a couple of games, then when he drops a hundred, she smirks. He thinks for a little while, then flips a card... the 10 of hearts. ~Oh, dear. Not the queen I'm afraid. Care to try again?~ He shakes his head and walks away, not getting into more than he can chew.

She sighs and packs up a couple of hours later. ~Can't keep doing this. I'm running out of suckers to con. Not without active thought projection... And I refuse to do that.~

Selene shakes her head. "You can do better. You don't have to con people like this."

Psyche sighs. ~I know. But no one will hire me... I'm underage and crippled to boot.~

"You can be a trainer..."

~You told me you wouldn't be subservient.~

"I don't have to be subservient to follow orders. We can act as a TEAM, not just master/servant."

Psyche shakes her head again. ~No... Besides, you know I can't keep this up. My leg brace locked up in Icirrus... My hearing aid died in Opelucid. I ran out of Pokeballs. Twice. And I don't have a steady income. I just get lucky and find stuff...~

Selene slumps. "I don't like you doing this. Why can't you go back to Nimbasa? Isn't the city council in charge of taking care of you legally?"

~Technically. But really all they do is adjust my leg brace, tweak my hearing aid and my glasses prescription, and you know, make sure I'm not dead. Nothing important.~

"Yes, but they can place you in a foster home."

~Ick, I'd rather go back to the forest. I don't want to be in Nimbasa any more than humanly possible.~

"Fine..."
