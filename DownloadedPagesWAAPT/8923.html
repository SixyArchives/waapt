We haven't met up in ages, and there's important things that need talking about, so I'm asking that we all head over to [Shrodingers Port City in Kanjoh] ASAP. I'll explain more once everyone has gotten there. If you're wondering how you're even going to get there, don't worry, I have...connections.

While technically that's kinda true, you've likely been accosted by members of our batshit insane fanbase, and it's also quite likely that our enemies will target you as well due to your association via the show or otherwise. Since obviously I find the concept of your blood on my hands to be about as pleasant as getting Dark Pulsed in the back a second time, it would be in your best interests to make your way over.

P.S. Bring Honey :3

That was big - clearly, Jack had stumbled upon something far bigger than he would have ever suspected. Going back to the ruins would have to wait - he was certain they'd still be there and waiting for him when he wanted to return.
