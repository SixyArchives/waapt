Oh, well the truth is, I suck at descriptions too. So I know how that feels. :P

I can help with the other stuff, though.

Habitats: Younger morphs tend to live in brackish water environments and rivers. Feraligatr are the same, but may swim out to see occasionally to hunt, but generally stay close to land.

Locomotion: All morphs are capable of standing and walking upright, but drop to all fours when an extra burst of speed is needed. Croconaw and Feraligatr doing so more often due to their larger sizes making it harder to move on land. In water, all morphs tuck their feet to their sides and propel themselves with their forearms and tail. They are much faster at swimming than moving around on land.

Courting rituals: Um...I haven't actually asked Lake about this yet. o_o;;. I would do it now, but he's not here at the moment. Will have to remember to ask the next time I see him.

Strategies and abilities: Some Totodile posses the ability to use the a weaker variation of the move Glare, which only works half the time due to not being proficient with the move. Others instinctively know where to bite an injured opponent in order to inflict more damage.

Some Croconaw have developed a special technique to hold their prey with their jaws in a way that renders them unable to escape. A nastier varient of this move is capable of rendering the foe incapable of acting entirely.

Others can use a technique that can mostly be summed up as a Water Type Sonicboom, due to it's effect of bypassing the target's weaknesses and resistances.

Similar to the above Totodile example, Feraligatr know best where to attack an injured foe to inflict more damage. Others can use thier itimidating appearance to their advantage to essentially gain an effect similar to Intimidate.

A subspecies of Feraligatr that has gained the electric typing by being exposed to electromagnetic waves in the Holon region (Dubbed "δ Feraligatr" by researchers), are able to generate a sort of "Battle Aura" that strengthens allied δ Pokémon.

Perhaps the most distinctive of these techniques is one in which a Feraligatr is able to to cloak it's fangs in water when biting a target. The swirling waters only adding to the sheer damage caused by the immense force of Feraligatr's jaw strength and sharp teeth.

Another distinctive move is essentially a variation of the move "Substitute." However, rather than producing a set of body armour or a plush likeness to take damage for them, the user instead blows a bubble of water around itself (or another target, essentially trapping them), at the cost of some of the users life energy (Aura?) to serve as a shield against attacks.

Although effective at absorbing damage, these bubbles are rather vulnerable to Grass and Electric type attacks (As well as sharp objects or extremities), and will easily burst if they are used against it, rendering this move rather situational (Note: This move has only just recently been discovered and is still under investigation by the PEFE).

And that's pretty much everything. Hope this helps.
