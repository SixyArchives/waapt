Cyllage City

Aline was walking around the city in her lonesome self until she spots a Golett. She heard that there were some wild ones outside of the city and thought it was just a wild one who wandered into the city. She taps her head a bit so as to make Divoom come out from under her hat.

"What?" asked Divoom, telepathically

"Can you speak with him? I wonder if he's lost." requested Aline, pointing at the Golett with curiosity

Divoom doesn't seem very thrilled by the idea "Fine, fine..."

Aline then approaches the Golett, who looks at her with a the typical lack of expression you'd expect from a Golett.

"<Hey, are you lost? Wild Pokémon shouldn't be wandering around cities.>" said Divoom.

The Golett shakes his head

Divoom looks a bit more curious, his interested piqued "<Do you have a trainer?>"

The Golett nods and points towards a certain pink haired girl having tea with a green Scizor out in the open.

"I didn't know Sakura had a Golett!" said Aline, to Divoom, looking shocked as if it was a mind blowing revelation.

The Noibat rolls his eyes and then looks back at Phantom "<Say, are you mute or something?>"

Phantom nods

"... He's mute. Really?" thought Divoom, momentarily forgetting about Aline listening in.

"What? Really?!" responded Aline, shocked, but then giving a smile "Well, it's good to have someone like me around, even if it's a Pokémon."

Divoom narrows his eye for a moment before focusing on Phantom again

"<My trainer is mute too, maybe you can communicate with her?>" asked Divoom

Hearing those news seems to have made Phantom perk up somewhat, at least as far as Aline could tell. He then starts making some gestures with his hands that just leaves Aline and Divoom puzzled, until it dawns on the latter.

"<... Woah. I didn't know there were Pokémon out there that knew sign language.>" said Divoom, blinking in surprise.

"What's up?" asked Aline, curiously

"He's doing sign language, you know, that gesture language." answered Divoom

"Ooooh! But I don't know how to read that..." said Aline, loosening her shoulders in disappointment

"<Sorry, but my trainer communicates through writing instead of gestures.>" explained Divoom, to Phantom

Phantom looks down in disappointment for a moment, but then looks back at them and nods. He then waves at them and starts walking off.

"Hey, couldn't you have made this an entirely mental conversation?"" asked Aline

"I can't talk with more than one person at the same time like this and switching back and forth would be really annoying."

Aline sighs in exasperation "You're so lazy..." she then looks at Phantom going and looks rather sad "... I wonder how many people know sign language."

"Not many."

Divoom then breaks the mental link with Aline and hides back under her hat. Aline just looks at Phantom for a few seconds before turning around and walk away herself.
