Driftveil City

From a quiet and unassuming building to the north of town, a young brunette trainer emerged with a yawn, his amber eyes narrowing slightly as they adjusted to the afternoon light. Grass crunched under his black and white sneakers as he began to move back in the direction of the emerald covered city, his shoulders shrugging as they adjusted his shirt neck.

Rex: They're rather like Team Galactic, aren't they? Trying to recover from past stigma.

Apparently, the group was trying to make amends for their past kidnappings, returning the Pokémon they'd stolen to the trainers that they'd taken them from. However, in some instances, the original trainer could not be found. In such a case, if the Pokémon was eager to leave, a new trainer would be found for it based on a "first come, first served" basis. Hardly a perfect way to make up for their previous transgressions, but it was all they could think to do for now.

Lifting his hands, the boy looked over the "gift" he'd received from the group: a nondescript Pokémon egg. Apparently, it had been nabbed by a Plasma Grunt sometime back, though it hadn't hatched in all the time since it had been with the group. He wasn't quite sure what species was contained within, and Anthea, the woman who had given it to him, didn't seem to be any more aware than he was.

Rex: Hmmm...

???: Find what you were looking for?

Snapping out of his reverie, the 16-year-old looked up at the top of the building he was in front of, a young boy in a ninja costume grinning down at him cheerfully. At his side were two Pokémon, a Marshtomp that was currently gazing longingly at the nearby shore of the light tower in the distance. The second was a Dewott, the otter Pokémon giving a happy noise as it sighted Rex, leaping for him with a happy cry. It landed on his head and nuzzled against its trainer affectionately, making certain that its scalchops weren't rubbing uncomfortably against the boy's flesh.

Rex: Come on, Caspian. It's only been a few hours since I left the hotel.

He looked up at the Dewott with a fond smile before turning his gaze back to the young boy, giving a nod in his direction.

Rex: And, yeah, I did, Owen. Thanks for waiting for me.

There was a sudden gale of wind, Rex shielding his eyes from the heavy winds before opening his eyes. As he did, he was surprised to find that the young blonde ninja was now directly in front of him, Marshtomp included.

Owen: No problem! Caspian seemed like he wanted to know where you were, so I decided to go find you.

Caspian: <You were gone for hours!>

Looking a little sheepish, the male adjusted his hold on the egg so that it was against his chest in one arm, reaching for his belt with another. Plucking one of his remaining Pokéballs from his belt, he released the Pokémon inside, petting her head with a free hand and handing the egg to her with a smile.

Rex: Sorry, sorry. How about I make it up to you all by buying dinner tonight?

Owen: Alright!

Caspian: <Food!>

Lian: -holding the egg- <Generic exclamation!>
