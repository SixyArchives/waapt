"Forgive me if I'm rude, but you should've moved on from that little arguement in the beach, it's unfortunate that Duke was already taken, but you can't let another failure at romance slow you down." said Allen, giving a slightly annoyed sigh.

"Whatever..."

"It's habit, forgive me." he responded, with a small smile

Rillianne then sighs again, this time it was a concerned one "What has dad been doing to you all this time..." she muttered as Allen put the tray on a nearby table.

The siblings then started to eat together, they didn't say a word to each other, mostly because of the rather heavy atmosphere and the fact that Rillianne looked rather lost in thought and seeing that, Allen decided to leave her be for now.
