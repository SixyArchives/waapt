Everything is so AWESOME.

Fall City, Marie and Ray's home

Ray and her Smeargle were painting, both had a canvas each in front of them. The TV screen was open, a gaming console on, a Gothitelle.Ray blew her hair out of her face, stepped back a bit, and asked."Well, Pablo? How does it look?"

Pablo, her Smeargle, looked at her painting. It was an elegantly colored portrait of a circus, white a clown balanced on the nose of a Donphan, an Ambipom and two acrobats flying through the air, and a little child right in the center, beaming after being shot out of a cannon.

Pablo shakes his head."<There's a little too much going on at once for my liking, mon cherie.>" Ray got the clue from the shaking, and dabbed some point on to Pablo's face."Well foo to you too, Pablo!"

Pablo moved away, indignant, and picked up his tail."<Gah! Two can play at that game, Madame Palmer!>" The Gothitelle from the couch lazily called out to them:"<Oh please, don't the house dirty again, we just cleaned it.>"

"Bring it on." Ray stepped forward, her brush extended threateningly, when the door bell rang. She rushed forward to the door, hoping that it was Marie, and slowly opened it."Yess?"

Suddenly, a Lucario and a Swampert burst through the door, landing right on top of her."<We missed you, Ray!>"

"Geo, Axel!" Ray beamed."You fellas are lookin' healthy as ever!" Suddenly, a Hawlucha burst through the door as well, yelling"<MOSH PIT!>" and landing on to the heap of people, along with a Froslass, who happily floated on top of everyone. Ray was still smiling, but she looked like she was really straining now.

A voice came through the door, a male voice."Come on, guys, that's enough, you're going to squish her into the ground." The 'Mon get off of Ray, who takes a deep breath, as Locke and Marie peek through the doorway.

"Locke!" Ray rushes towards him and hugs him, moving a little to the side when Locke winces, a she touches his left shoulder."Sorry" she says", and hugs him even tighter,"How have you been?! When did you get here?" "I've been good, Ray, just got here from Kalos today." Ray then moves away from Marie huffing."You never told me he was coming."

Marie looked indignant."I didn't know either!" Locke nervously scratches his head."I..um..I wanted to surprise you guys."

Ray looked at him and beamed."And you did! C'mon, U was just about to make lunch." Marie peered into the house, where Pablo still stood frozen, and said:"Doesn't look like it to me."

"Oh hush up" Ray said as she ushers everyone in, including Balthazar who was bringing in Locke's heavier bags, along with Mirror.

Marie says quietly to Ray"I'm sorry to dump this on you, honey, but I gotta get to Summerland for some work later on. Just letting you know, 'kay?"

Ray pouted."You're always going on missions." Marie kissed the top of her head."Don't worry, I'll be back soon. Locke'll be asleep by then, so don't worry him, 'kay?"

"Kay" Ray said, as she smiled and hugged Marie. Unbeknownst to them, Locke had heard every word.
