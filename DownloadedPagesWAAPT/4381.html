"Nova, can I talk to you for a moment?"

<O-of course, Trainer, > Nova stammers, padding over towards her.

Crewe leads her over to a fallen log beside the tent and sits down. Nova sits down in front of her, trying to look attentive.

Crewe takes a deep breath, then begins. "Nova, are you unhappy? About anything besides being left behind in the islands with Dana?"

Nova looks alarmed. <No! No, I'm not unhappy! Not at all! I-I mean... I'm very happy with you, Trainer.> Her ears flicker and she rubs one paw against her other leg.

Crewe frowns. "Are you sure?"

Nova nods urgently. <Yes, Trainer, I'm very sure.>

"Well... All right, then. I'll see you later, Nova."

Nova looks up at Masque with an alarmed look on her face.

<So... If I say I'm unhappy... She'll release me?>

The Alakazam looks at her gravely. <It is how your mother, along with many of my teammates, and other Pokemon, were released. She believes she is right, but if you would like to stay on the team, do your best to be happy.>

Nova shrinks back. <I don't wanna leave the team.>

Masque's face softens. <If you want to stay, she won't make you go. It is what happened with myself, after all. But if you really are unhappy, child—>
