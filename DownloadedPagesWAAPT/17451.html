436265 PippingFool29th Dec 2014 05:52:57 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

Slateport City

A young, tall man is sitting in the designated smoking area of one of the seaside cafes, reading a book on ruins and archaeological sites of the Hoenn region. In intervals he checks his watch before petting the elderly Lucario curled up around his feet. It seems like he is waiting for something or someone.

Meanwhile, at the Slateport, a cruise ship pulls up and prepares for unloading luggage and passengers from the vessel. Passengers that include a few rowdy individuals.

Cruise Ship - Bar Lounge

A large, dark skinned fellow with wild red and black hair is sitting one the bar table, holding four different types of rum in-between his fingers.

Santiago: LIVE LONG, PARTY HARD!

It seems like the rest of bar inhabitants are just as enthusiastic about partying as Sandy is

Bar Goes: YEAH!

Santiago stand on the bar counter. The fact that the bartenders don't stop him imply that their either don't care, or that he's done it enough that it becomes a Sisyphusian task to get him down.

Santiago: Since we just got in port, I will buy everyone here a parting drink!

Bar Goes: YEAH!

The shorter, blue haired fellow sitting next to him - instead of reminding him about the how much that bill would be - instead is more interested in his friend's ability to hold multiple spirits.

Levi: Ooh~ Hey Sandy?

Santiago: Ee-yeeees?

Levi: How do you that?

Levi points to Santiago's rum bottles.

Santiago: Oh this? It's quite simple really, roll up those sleeves of yours and I'll show you~

Santiago gives Levi a big, hearty grin as Levi rolls up his admittedly comically long sleeves. Though once rolled up, Santiago's brows change to give off a rather... different expression.

Levi: Okay! I'm ready~

Santiago: Uh... Levi... How do I put this..

Santiago puts the bottles down and starts drumming his fingers on his lip, wondering how to approach the delicate question.

Santiago: Well. Er. To hold all four bottle of the life blood. You kinda need, uh, all ten fingers.

Levi: Huh?

Santiago buries his face in both of his hands.

Santiago: Levi. Your right hand. Look.

Levi looks down at his right hand and, to Levi's nonchalant surprise, his ring and pinkie fingers are nothing more than small nubs.

Levi: Huh. Would you look at that?

Levi begins examining his right hand in childish curiosity.

Santiago: How do you not notice something like that!?

Levi: Hey! Cut me some slack! Have you seen my sleeves?

Levi flings his arms forward, causing his sleeves to drag over his hands once again.

Levi: I barely even see them most days!

Levi rolls up his right sleeve again.

Levi: Though... I wonder how..?

Santiago: I would assume it's a sort of glitch in the processing system. I did notice how your right pectoral was missing a few of the tabs.

Levi: (wiggling his fingers) But this is going to make things a lot harder...

Santiago: Ah, you'll learn! Anyway.

Santiago tosses Levi a beer.

Santiago: That's your drink on the house. (Just don't tell Vyv mmkay?)

Levi: Thanks man, you are the best worst friend I could ask for. (Just don't show Vyv the bill and you'll be fine~)

Santiago pales as Levi takes a swig of his drink, a smarmy grin painting his face as he wonders how large that tab is going to be.
All about big hair, big shoes and big attitude!
