 170258 Luke 924, Thu, 21st Jun '12 6:19:55 AM Get RP Mod

Former Tribe Grounds - PMD-B

Lake couldn't sleep. There was far too much going through his mind at that moment. All of them questions on where he was and what was going on.

Everything he had seen so far had suggested that this was the future of his world. But the appearance of a Space Tunnel had thrown everything into question. There wasn't anything to say that the Cubone hadn't found a way to create them somehow. After all, if they could use Dialga's Toothbrush to give a network of caves time traveling properties, who's to say that they couldn't do the same with, say...Palkia's Mouthwash?

...Do legendaries even use mouthwash?

No, that was off topic.

There was also Bluescale to consider. How could it have been founded by himself and Nadia if there wasn't a Nadia in the other universe? From what Lake remembered, she hadn't been born in favor of a Salamence named Kyle that the other Tracer owned. Plus, while he wasn't sure if he himself had an alternative counterpart or not, Cinder and Basil had to have known him from somewhere...

This was all so confusing. It was like trying to put a puzzle together, only somemon had gotten the pieces he needed mixed up with another one that was similar but not quite the same, making finding the relevant pieces much harder than it would have been otherwise.

At any rate, he was going to need more evidence than simply Space Tunnels to know the truth for sure.
