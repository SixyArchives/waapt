His Lordship's Personal Chambers, Ignis

Izumi is getting prepared for sleep, groaning about this and that while she takes all the decor out of her hair. Putting it into a box.

Izumi: Urgh. What a day... The generals and I were not able to agree upon anything in regards about what to do if a Northern Nation goes too far south and threatens the Peaceful Coexistence we have going on here, I wasn't able to catch anything while hunting and the springs were closed for the day... ~Sigh~ At least I didn't get some bullshit proposition from a nation to form an Allegiance, those days are the worst.

At that moment, Ryuku slams open the slide door (???) and gives a huge bear hug to Izumi.

Ryuku: Kiki~! How are you darling~?

Izumi: Put me down, cinderblockhead.

Ryuku: -Snuggling- Nope~ Not quite yet~

Izumi: ... I hate it when you get all mushy.

Ryuku: No you don't. You love it~

After a while of being bearhugged, Ryuku puts Izumi down and starts preparing for bed himself.

Ryuku: So how was your day birdie?

Izumi: Oh mine? Mine was juuusst peachy. Not only did I have to deal with the blithering idiots from your father's old gaurd who wouldn't know how to conduct a successful invasion if a scroll was laid out for them, but all venues for quelling my non-Spectra related rage were either closed or void. All while Fire Lord Ryuku was playing Hanafuda with his handmade Rayquaza plush!

Ryuku: Ol' Stuffy is amazing at Hanafuda, though! You should play him some time!

Izumi flops face down on the bed.

Izumi: I give up...

Ryuku stays silent for a moment, and then lies down beside Izumi. Izumi rolls onto her side, not facing Ryuku.

Ryuku: ...You remember how we met? Right Izumi?

Izumi: ..Yes. It involved a beach, moonlight and lots of blood everywhere. I believed I either kicked you or headbutt you into the water.

Ryuku: -Small chuckle- Yeah. You know what I thought when I met you?

Izumi: Hmm?

Ryuku: I thought that not only were you the most beautiful woman I had met, but you were also the most iron-willed and smartest person I had met. As the hopeless romantic I am you could say that I fell madly in love with you.

Izumi: That feeling wasn't mutual.

Ryuku: You see... Father didn't wantme to marry you. He wanted me to marry a local noble girl, pretty, quiet and demure. One who wouldn't get involved in politics. But I didn't want that...

Izumi turns over to Ryuku and cocks an eyebrow at him.

Ryuku: On my lonesome, I would be a terrible leader. I'm deathly afraid of public speaking, not smart enough to manage my nation or plan attacks and I constantly make bad decisions over and over again. The only thing I'm mildly good at is fighting and even then there are better warlords than me. -Faces Izumi directly- I need someone like you, who is smart and strong and who I can trust with my life, because I'm terrified of ruling on my own... I guess that I'm just trying to say that I need you Kiki...

Izumi: -Similing slightly- You're sweet, you know that love. Still doesn't excuse you from not being present at important meetings -Playfully punches his shoulder-

Ryuku: Oi, I hate those meetings -Playfully punches back-

Izumi: -Smirking- So do I -Jabs in the ribs-

Ryuku: Ah! Two can play at that game! -Starts tickling Izumi-

The two continue their play fighting throughout the night.
