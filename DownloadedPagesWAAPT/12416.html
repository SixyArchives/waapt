Jacob: ".... Hey, Regina..."

Jacob: "No I know it's just... well erm..."

-Looks up- "Well I was just... I was hoping you'd help me understand something since the Ralts line is particularly attune to the emotions of others. W-Whenever we talk like this... or when we win... when I know I can leave things to you and they pay off... whenever we celebrate... I feel this... well it's like this orange warm glowy feel... and it fills up my lungs and chest and it's... actually sort of nice... I don't get it and it's making me nervous. Do you know what it is?"

Regina: -Looks surprised for a few seconds before relaxing into a very warm serene smile- <Well... maybe. Come on, let's go do this then we can talk about it.>

Jacob: "Right!" -Walks on forward, tripping on the hem of his big coat, pushes himself up and keeps walking forward with a look of almost adult-like determination- "As long as I have you, my white queen, no one will ever be able to keep me in check for long!"

-He keeps marching forward, towards the doors of the building-

-He doesn't look, but he can feel the Gardevoir following along right behind him-

-And he can almost feel the grin stuck to her face-
