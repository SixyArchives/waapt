Well, I've had this in mind for a while... Figured I'd might as well just do it, just to give some sort of closure or conclusion, or whatever.

Sinnoh - Resort Area - DS' villa - Unspecified point in time

- DS is standing in front of the bathroom mirror in her villa, trying to put on a contact lense. After a few failed attempts, she manages to place it correctly on her eye, and then tries to do the same with the other eye -

(thinking) ... I wonder if they've noticed my absense by now?

Flashback

- DS explores Olympia's gym, looking around in awe at the space area, before it cuts to DS getting her badge, and again to DS in Snowbelle, building a few snowmons while listening to some familiar music coming from one of the houses nearby, before it again cuts to DS getting her eigth gym badge and placing it in the badge case -

I feel a little bad... just leaving without even saying good-bye...

Present(?)

- DS is now brushing her hair -

Hopefully Tagg and Fool will be able to sort things out... I don't want him to be lonely since I won't be around anymore...

- Sighs -

Another flashback

- DS returns to her villa, taking out all her badge cases, briefly looking over them before putting them inside a drawer. She then takes off her X-Transceiver, along with the bracelet and locket Wolf gave her, pausing a bit before taking off the ring as well, and then puts them in a box and puts them in the drawer along with the badge cases -

I had a lot of adventures with the J-Team...

- DS takes out her notebook, and puts it down on a table -

I made a lot of friends... Caught some new Pokémon... Explored a lot of new places...

- We see DS at Spear Pillar, returning the Lustrous Orb, before leaving -

... It was worth it, wasn't it?

Present(?)

- After finishing brushing her hair, DS is now applying her make-up -

... At the very least, things probably could've ended on a better note.

But... I knew that if I tried to say good-bye in person, then...

- DS finishes applying the last bit of make-up, and looks at her reflection -

... I wouldn't have been strong enough...

Flashback

- DS seems to be typing something up on her X-Transceiver -

I did try to write them a message, but...

- She looks over what she has written -

To: J-Team

From: DS

Hey, everyone. Figured I'd let you guys know that I've returned to Sinnoh, and that I intend to mostly retire from being a Pokémon trainer, and to focus on my writing. I've had a lot of fun travelling with all of you, even if not all our experiences were pleasant ones... Anyways, I have one last thing I'd like to ask of you guys: Stay as far away from me as possible.

Sorry, I guess that may have sounded a bit harsh, but... really, I need some time to myself, to think about things and try to get in better shape. You understand, right?

Oh, and Tagg? Sorry that we didn't get to do that rematch, or to start on those self-defense classes you promises me. I'm not sure if I would've made it through these last months if you weren't looking out for me, so... thank you. I hope things work out for you and the rest of the team.

Also, if you happen to come across Wolf... Tell him I'm sorry.

Everyone, good luck with the fight against evil and all that. I'll keep working on my things, such as the J-Team TV show reboot. I'm not entirely sure how I'll get it on TV yet, but I'm trying to figure something out.

- DS looks over the message, and sadly shakes her head before putting the X-Transceiver back in the drawer -

... I couldn't even bring myself to send it.

Present(?)

- DS checks the time on her Poketch -

I guess I'm back where I started... all alone... but, for now, I'm okay with that.

- DS picks up her notebook -

The J-Team and the fight against evil can go on without me. I won't get involved in it anymore.

- DS starts heading outside -

I know some things were left unresolved, but... I'll try to not let it bother me too much as I have other things requiring my attention now.

- DS takes out a pokeball, sending out Wings. She climbs onto the Crobat, and tells him where she needs to go, and he takes off -

I'm not 100% sure what the future holds, but I know what my goals are, and I will do my best to reach them.

- DS closes her eyes for a moment, thinking back to some of the time she has spent with the J-Team -

... I wish I could truly say I did what was best for me, but I suppose it'll still be a while before I'll know for sure...

- Sighs -

I'm sure there are already rumors going around parts of the region about what may have caused me to separate from the J-Team... People tend to start talking if something happens, I know that all too well.

But, for now, I'll just pretend not to see it. I'm sure Cynthia will help me make sure nothing gets too out of hand.

- DS opens her eyes -

So, I'll focus on myself for now, and hopefully figure out a way to sort things out between me and my friends in the J-Team.

- Smiles -

Yeah... I just have to believe that everything will be okay, and then surely things will turn out all right eventually. It's as simple as that, isn't it?
