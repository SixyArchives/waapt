432012 PippingFool6th Dec 2014 09:15:13 AM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

~*Embedded Tower*~ (Nothing interesting ever happens here yo')

It's another day in the Embedded Tower with the Tiny Trio.

Buggle and Depthcharge were nowhere to be seen at this particular moment. They were probably off somewhere either trying to create substitute Mon -> Human or hatching some hair-brained scheme to become highly successful entrepreneurs of a Toothpick factory in [New Mexico] somewhere. Either way, Bridget is probably regretting the day he allowed himself to play babysitter for those two.

Vyvyan on the other hand, has perched himself on one of the fragmented Rayquaza themed stairs, staring into front of a chintzy thrift shop mirror with it's 99c tag will dangling on the side since nobody could be arsed to remove the damned thing. When he's not being distracted by the gaudy weather trio themed frame, he is examining his current appearance. Pushing back fangs that seem to be of the habit of protracting now and again, and rubbing his eyes when the whites occasionally switch to black.

He rolls up his sleeve and pouts at the armband before sighing, shaking his arm and flicking the armband with the back of his hand.

Vyvyan: Hmm. I wonder if I should find those J-team guys and have them check if this contraption is bust. I seem to be "leeking" as of late.

Vyvyan ponders for a moment.

Vyvyan: Is that even the right turn of phrase? "Leeking"? For slipping back into base form?

Vyvyan would have pondered on this line of thought more - and he would've liked to, I might add - had two identifiable human males not bust through on of the walls like the Kool-Aid man.

Identifiable Human Males: YEAAAH! WE DID IT!

Following the two Identifiable Human Males is a rather dazed and confused Identifiable Male Gardevoir, covered in soot. It seems that he was the immediate test subject for whatever strange experiment those two were conducting.

Bridget: -Leaning against the wall, trying to catch his breath- <You guys...>

One of the fellows is a shorty. Around 5'4 in height with skin the colour of white pearls, he has a mop of scraggly sapphire blue hair that falls down to the back of his neck at the longest. His clothing choice appears to be a longcoat tied loosely around his waist with red chord. The pattern is a strange one, the sleeves and the upper half of the coat are a royal blue shade while from the naval down it flows into an ivory colour. The coat itself his lined with red trimming and has intrinsic Kyogre themed patterns on the chest and the sleeves. The sleeves themselves fan out and are have small white square flaps dangling off the rims. The clothes underneath the longcoat are a bit more basic; a silver necklace set with sapphires and a long black tunic that has a few more of the blue and red trim patterns on the upper half of his coat. Underneath his the right of his striking amber eyes is what appears to be a red tattoo of some kind.

The other identifiable human male is almost a complete opposite of the other. He is large fellow, 6'7 in height with skin the colour of burnt sienna. His hair is a short, wild mane of ruby red with two black streaks, and his eyes - just like Vyvyans and the smaller blue fellow - are a fierce amber colour. He apparel is more basic than his friend and consists of a yellow scarf with strange, ancient looking designs, a [Hawaiian] t-shirt with a strange pattern of the black Groundon markings and Hibiscus flowers, a pair of bog-standard black shorts and a pair of black Havaianas sandals for footwear. Like his compatriot, he also has a blue "tattoo" down his left arm that seems to parallel those of Groundon.

It isn't a mystery who these two are.

Vyvyan: Buggle. Depthcharge.

Buggle: Yo!

Depthcharge: That would be us.

Buggle: What gave it away? I thought we blend right in o3o.

Vyvyan parkours his way down from his mirror, and and shakes his head at the two with a knowing smirk.

Vyvyan: Because, if I could visualise what the deities of the oceans and earth would look like should they be human. Your current forms are exactly what comes to mind..

Vyvyan points a thumb at Buggle.

Vyvyan: Though your clothes are not what I expected. Good job~

Buggle: Yay~ I'm unexpected! Slightly!

Depthcharge folds his arms and pouts.

Depthcharge: But at least I look fancier....

Vyvyan walks over to Bridget and helps him up.

Vyvyan: Erm. Sorry Brig.

Bridget: You owe me. BIG time...

Vyvyan: Will do.

After consoling his long time companion, Vyv looks back over to the now Human mini-Kyogre and mini-Groundon.

Vyvyan: Well since you now have human forms, have you tried thinking of human names? And not something obvious like Hugh Mann, please.

Depthcharge points both of his index fingers at Vyvyan. Though his large sleeves quickly drape back over his hands.

Depthcharge: Glllaaaaad you asked! That's the 64$ question right there.

Buggle: We were getting bored of these corny ass names anyway o3o.

Depthcharge points a sleeve at himself.

Depthcharge: From now on I wish to be addressed as "Levi"~

Buggle then gestures a thumb to his chest with a big smile.

Buggle: Call me Santiago! "Sandy" for short. I like shorts, they're comfy and easy to wear.

Vyvyan: So. Levi and Santiago?

Depthcharge Levi: Yep~

Buggle Santiago: Got it in one! Oh, and we also have something to show you~

Santiago flashes out three tickets he pulled from his back pockets.

Santiago: WE BE GOIN ON A CRUUUUUSIE!

Levi holds his hands in the air, which causes his comically large sleeves to slide down his arms.

Levi: TO HOENN

Both: AWWWW YISSSSSSSS

Vyvyan stops breathing for a moment.

Vyvyan: ...When do we leave?

Levi: Oh. Tomorrow morning~ We've packed.

Vyvyan's Mindscape

Sirens are going off

Announcer: MAYDAY! MAYDAY!

Back to reality

In the blink of an eye Vyvyan grabs the two by the collar.

Vyvyan: AND YOU JUST TELL ME THIS NOW!?

Vyvyan dashes around at the speed of sounds trying to pack a suitcase that was hidden Behind the Black.

Vyvyan: DOYOUKNOWHOWLONGITTAKESFORMETOPACK?IMEANIHAVETOFIGUREOUTCLOTHESANDTOILETRIESANDBACKU- PSANDPLANEXPENDITURESANDINEEDTOASKSIERRAIFSHEWOUL- DLIKETOCOMEALONGANDARRGH.

Vyvyan curls up and starts rocking back and forth. Levi and Santiago stare at Vyvyan, then at each other.

Santiago: Uhh...

Levi: Did we do something wrong? I thought he would like to go on a trip to his home region! D:

Bridget stands up and walks over to the two.

Bridget: <You've known him for two years and only realise now that he has quite severe anxiety? Tsk Tsk>

The Gardevoir goes over to console his trainer while the two hooman legendaries look sheepishly at one another.
