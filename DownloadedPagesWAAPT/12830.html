A forest, Central Kalos - A while back

-Kirk is still wandering, entirely uncertain about whether he has entered a new forest or if he's still stuck in the one he entered last, and is looking much more ragged and emaciated than he did a couple of weeks ago. Currently, he is kicking stones around, muttering to himself and generally sulking as per usual.-

Kirk: ...How long has it been since I last had a cooked meal? I honestly can't remember... ugh, I'm hungry. But I can't turn back now... I have to keep going, I'll find what I'm looking for eventually...

-His musings are interrupted by the sound of something coming through the trees behind him.-

Kirk: <Huh?>

-He decides to duck behind a nearby tree. A few seconds later, a Malamar comes into view on what appears to be a litter carried by a Granbull and a Braixen, taking a sip from a black goblet filled with unidentifiable liquid.-

Malamar: <Hmm... minions, set me down here. I wish to admire the local scenery.>

Granbull: <Yes, sir!>

Malamar: <Now now, we've been over this. "Sir" just isn't quite befitting enough for someone of my status. From now on, I want you to address me as "Supreme Aquarian Overlord" unless instructed otherwise. Understood?>

Granbull: <Yes, si- I mean, yes, Supreme Aquarian Overlord!>

Malamar: <Jolly good!>

-Kirk raises an eyebrow at the Granbull's behaviour, but before he can say anything to himself he is promptly noticed by the Malamar, who hops off the litter with drink still in hand or the Malamar equivalent.-

Malamar: <Ohohohoho, what do we have here? A Lucario, by the looks of things. And a fine specimen at that, though unable to compare with my strength, intelligence and dashing good looks. Then again, what Pokemon could?>

Kirk: <...What?>

Malamar: <So, come to bask in the glory of my mere presence, have you? Or was it simply happy coincidence that led you to what will undoubtedly be the most significant moment of your life?>

Kirk: <Look, you crazy kook, I don't know what you're going on about, but I just want to be left alone.>

Malamar: <Ah, yes, I know your type all too well. A lone wanderer, without cause or companionship to accompany you on your journey. I was such a wanderer myself - before my ascension to greatness, of course.>

-The Malamar claps an appendage around Kirk's shoulders, causing him to recoil and back away, growling.-

Malamar: <You are searching for something, I can tell. Searching desperately for a purpose, something to do with your life, something to accomplish. But you haven't found it yet, have you?>

Kirk: <...None of your damn business.>

Malamar: <So stubborn! One could almost be forgiven for thinking that you find being in the company of a being of such wisdom and benevolence as myself to be something of a drain. But of course, that couldn't possibly be the case, could it?>

Kirk: <What do you want, exactly?>

Malamar: <Why, I came to make you an offer, of course! An offer few lesser beings such as yourself are fortunate enough to receive.>

-Kirk's eyes narrow.-

Kirk: <...What kind of offer?>

Malamar: <I can give you the purpose you so eagerly seek! The chance to serve beneath yours truly, whose skill, martial prowess and intelligence equal - no no no, surpass - that of the legendaries themselves. Your life would be dedicated to a greater cause. No-one has turned down such an opportunity before!>

-He gestures to the Granbull and Braixen, who smile and wave at Kirk. Kirk's eyes narrow further.-

Kirk: <Well, you can consider me the first. There's no way I'm joining your little cult.>

Malamar: <Such insolence! Usually I would have you severely punished for addressing your superior in such a disrespectful tone, but I am in a generous mood today. My offer still stands. After all...>

-The Malamar takes another sip from his chalice, then grins at Kirk as his eyes start glowing and his voice becomes layered.-

Malamar: <~I insist.~>
