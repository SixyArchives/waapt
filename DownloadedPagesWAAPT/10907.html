Gabriel: Damn. Where the hell could that boy have gone. Get some rest, Achilles. You've been using your powers non-stop for the last week.

Gabriel: Oh, I was wondering how long it'd take for you to call back.

Micheal: I got your message this morning. Where is Victor?

Gabriel: ....Not sure. Achilles lost contact with his energy about two weeks ago. Haven't been able to track him since.

Micheal: Bullshit! You've kept track of him for over six years, you expect me to believe you could lose him so easily. M-Maybe he went to a different region or something.

Gabriel: No, I had considered that. Achilles would have, at the very least, felt his energy leaving. This was different. We were close to him, he could feel his energy as clear as day, then(snaps fingers) gone. As if he had vanished into thin air.
