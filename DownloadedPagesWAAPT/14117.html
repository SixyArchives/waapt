Dratini!Valorie floated along the side of a house, attempting to be somewhat discrete. Floating was now reasonably easy. She was starting to get a handle on her new body, not that she was any happier about losing the one she was comfortable in.

It seemed... improper.

After a very long moment's consideration she relented however. If she was going to be forced into the body of a pokemon she might as well take advantage of how others perceived her. Having studied the species, she knew very well that dratini preferred the water. Slipping into Courmarine's waters she emitted a sigh of relief as it soothed her skin and—holy shit!

...She could breathe underwater!

Valorie took a long moment to just sort of float there, absorbing this revelation.

...Water-derived air tasted /weird/!

She surfaced and began idly watching passerby idly. She supposed she could stay here for a while longer.
