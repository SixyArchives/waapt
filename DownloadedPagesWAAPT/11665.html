Allen and his mother are drinking some tea in the living room

"So, how are things going with your father?" asked his mom, with a rather worried look

"The same as always." answered Allen, taking a sip of his tea.

His mother then looks rather disheartened "I see..."

"What about my sister? Where is she?"

"Oh? Rillianne is going to stay in Orre for a while to visit some of her older friends."

Allen totally loses his composure "O-Orre?!"

"Yes, I know it's dangerous and probably a bad idea to let someone like her go there, but I want to have faith on her and that everything will turn out all right for her. Besides, it's going to be for her friend's birthday."

Allen then looks at his cup of tea for a few second and sighs "I hope she gets home safely, I wished to spend some time with her, like the times when we were still children."

His mother smiles "Well, she's not going to stay there for long I don't think. Until then, you're free to stay here as much as you'd like, or you could go after her in Orre."

"Uh... No... I'm afraid of going to that place, it's not really the safest region for one to go to and I'm afraid I might become a target there." he answered, ashamed of himself.

"I understand, but let's not be pessimistic, I'm sure my daughter is coming back safely." his mother then smiles.

"Yes, I guess there is no point in expecting the worst, can I really stay here?"

"If you want you can stay here forever, dear."

Allen gives a small smile "I think forever would be too much time, but I think I'll spend the night here since it's already dark."

His mother smiles again "You're at home, dear."

Allen nods and they spend the rest of the day chatting after all the time without seeing each other.
