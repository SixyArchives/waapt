-The mid-day son shone down on the coast near Azalea Town. Despite the warm weather, there were no humans present at the beach, only a few sand dwelling Pokémon such as Krabby and Shellder. The residents of the beach didn't mind that nothing ever really happened here. tHey enjoyed the peace and quiet.

On this day, however, the peace was about to be shattered. If only temporally.

A lone Krabby spotted it first. Something large was just of the coast. A large dark shape moving through the water. What was visible of it's body, though, was utterly terrifying.

Large red ridges, marine blue scales, and a huge yellow jaw were the beast's most prominent features. Features that the beach Pokemon all too well.

The humans called them Feraligatr.

But the beach Pokémon called them killers.-

Krabby: <IT'S A GATOR! EVERYMON RUN!!!>

-Upon hearing the Krabby's warning, the Beach Pokemon began to attempt to hide amongst their surroundings or flee inland for their lives.

Shellder closed their shells shut tight and buried themselves in the sand, while a lone Shuckle withdrew into it's shell and hid amongst some stones. A few Krabby scuttled under some rocks for protection while a group of Wingull flew alway into the sky, cawing loudly.

Within minutes, the beach was seemingly devoid of life. Just as a certain Ferligatr crawled up onto the shore.-
