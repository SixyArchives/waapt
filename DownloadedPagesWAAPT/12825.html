Angry Tailor
From the notes I've seen on purification; a Shadowed mon can't get stronger with experience, but it can still earn it. However, once purified, the mon in question gains all the experience it's accumulated while Shadowed. In fact, it not unusual for mon to evolve after purification. My guess is that while you where shadowed you gained so much experience in using Obscura your body makes obscura easier, but you haven't adjusted your control for the increase of power.
