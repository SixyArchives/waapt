Fruit Forest - Cookie Country (PMD-A)

On a serious of wooden walkways built into the tree-tops of a leafy forest, a familiar team of a Charizard, a Kingdra and a Sceptile are continuing on their hunt for the Starcutter's Energy Spheres.

Comet: <Y'know, I'm really glad Chord isn't here right now...>

Comet quickly comes to a halt as a feral Spinarak dangles down from a length of web in front of her. Slighly annoyed, she puffs a plume of fire at the spider Pokémon, fainting it instantly.

<I have a gut feeling he wouldn't approve of this.>

Phyco: <Gee, you think?>

Comet ignores the Kingdra as the three come to a gap in the walkway. Oddly enough, the terrain they had been through had been covered in hills and pits that had required jumping over or across to navigate. Something Treeo hadn't failed to notice.

Treeo: <Is it just me, or does it feel like we're in the middle of a giant natural obstacle course?>

Comet: <Yeah, but is that a bad thing? I mean, it's fun, right?>

Treeo: <...I guess so.>

Comet: <Good. Now then...>

The Charizard jumps, flapping her wings in mid-air to give her the extra air-time needed to clear the gap. Treeo is nimble enough to cross the gap with a single long bound.

It's Phyco that takes the more usual approach. The Kingdra, most likely using his latent draconic magic, preforms a short jump. Upon hitting the ground, his bubble bounces into the air and over the gap, landing safely on the other side with the other two members of his party. Not that he seemed to be enjoying it.

Phyco: <Fish Pokémon aren't meant to jump...>

Treeo: <Hey, you've been doing well so far. Even if we did have a close call with those feral Wurmple a little ways back.>

Phyco: <Eurgh...don't remind me.>

Treeo: <Okay...let's get- Oh!>

Comet and Phyco blink.

Comet: <What is it?>

Without a work, Treeo rushes over to a nearby tree and jumps up into it's branches, emerging a few seconds later clutching a golden gear of sorts.

Treeo found an Energy Sphere!

<Hey! Nicely spotted!>

Treeo nods happily.

Treeo: <Yep, that's another one down! At the rate we're going, we'll have the Starcutter fully operational again in no time flat!>

Phyco lowers his head, breathing a bubbly sigh.

Phyco: <I honestly doubt it's going to continue being so easy.>

Comet: <Oh, don't be such a downer all the time! Now, let's keep things moving!>

Treeo nods as he stores the Energy Sphere in his pocket of hammerspace, before the three continue to move further through the leve- sorry, area...

Comet <You know, all this is actually putting in the mood for trying out those "Video Games" the humans have when we get back home.>

Treeo: <Huh. I feel the same way...>
