-Dune reaches the door to the old mansion. It's old and eaten away by Bugs, both the door and the threshold. He shoves against it with his shoulder, and it gives after spitting a few moldy splinters around.-

Dune: I can see why people thought this place was haunted now...

-Old tattered banners litter the walls inside. A carpet of refuse and debris covers the floor.-

Dune: Well... Home sweet home, I guess.

-A Stoutland nudges past Dune to get inside, his nose to the ground.-

Cun Annwn: <Something smells... familiar.>

Dune: Wait. How so?

-Cun sniffs a little more.-

Cun Annwn: <I smell that... chemical-y smell from before.>

-Dune arches an eyebrow, begging the question.-

Cun Annwn: <Remember, in that tomb? The little nook we found? It smells like that.>

Dune: ... Oh. Lead the way, then.

Cun Annwn: <Right.>

-The floor creeks as the two make their way through the old, dilapidated manor, Dune's headlamp being the only light in rooms without windows. They eventually find a stone staircase leading down into the basement.-

Dune: Well... let's just hope the steps aren't wet...

-Against Dune's hopes, they were indeed wet, and, not wanting to die the ignoble death of falling down stairs, Dune takes the steps slowly and one at a time. Cun follows shortly, also careful. The two reach the basement, filled with wine casks. Cun sniffs them a bit.-

Cun Annwn: ... <These are still full.>

Dune: Wait, really? Wow. The stories of Kalosian wine cellars must be true.

-Cun slowly paces the perimeter of the room, until he finds an odd part of the wall.-

Cun Annwn: <There's a breeze coming from here.>

Dune: Which means there must a door there, and if there's a secret door, there must be a secret switch... Look for a candlestick that looks a little off. Or a lever hidden behind a cask...

-The two search high and low, and eventually Dune comes across a familiar sight.-

Dune: Wait a minute... Cun, come over here.

-The Stoutland stops his sniffing and steps over as Dune tries to clean the wall the best he can. On it is a sigil of an Arcanine walking across a desert road. The Arcanine's eye looks... off.-

Dune: I wonder...

-Dune presses the eye, and a small *click!* can be heard. The area previously identified as the secret door swings open, revealing...-

Dune: Woah...

Cun Annwn: <Woah...>

-A 10x10 room filled with display cases. Each of them holding something different. In one, a pair of large, bladed gauntlets can be seen. In another, a magnificent leather hat with a huge tan Pidgeot feather embellishing it. Another holds a gem-studded canine collar, and another holds a Fire Stone. In the last one, farthest from the door, however...-

Dune: Wait... what is this?

-Dune walks over and inspects it closer. Inside the glass case is a steel bracer, and set in that bracer is a luminescent gemstone.-

Dune: Huh... That gem looks familiar... It's like the one that jerk in the silver suit had. Maybe this has something to do with that thing he did with his Gengar...
