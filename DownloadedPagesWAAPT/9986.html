| With the change in the world, the shrine has changed as well. Instead of the ruined remains of a shrine, it has become a dilapidated, old playground.

| Zeta grasps the torch tightly. She has a feeling that letting go of it casually will end disastrously.

| As she looks out into the City with wonder and mild bemusement, an ethereal cloud of light blue mist rises up from the ground.

| She looks at it with mild apprehension. As she observes it, the mist speaks.

Mist: Oh, thank goodness~! You've figured it out.

Zeta: Oh? So those three things are the "keys".

Mist: Yep~! Now I'm allowed to tell you some things.

Zeta: Then, where is this place.

Mist: Nope.

Zeta: Sorry?

Mist: I said some things, not everything.

Zeta: Oh. Right, too much info too soon, huh?

Mist: Yep!

Zeta: Alright, what do the keys do, in general.

Mist: They allow you to exercise some control over what aspect of the Land that you are currently viewing. They also allow you to control how long you stay, and let you leave properly.

Zeta: Alright. Now, I know that the shadows aren't good in general, 'cuz they just don't feel right. But what's the specific risk in letting them take me away?

Mist: Eventually, they won't let you back out on the other side.

Zeta: Oh dear. Yes, that is indeed bad.

Mist: Quite.

Zeta: So, how do I work the keys?

Mist: Well, each one will show you a different aspect. As far as time, as long as you hold onto a key and don't actively will yourself out, you'll stay for as long as you like.

Zeta: I see. And what exactly do the different aspects do, beyond the appearance of the Land?

Mist: Can't say~! Anyways, that's all the answers I can give, for now. Bye!

Zeta: Wait, one last question.

Mist: Yes?

Zeta: Are you the Seal in the Ruins?

Mist: What do you think?

| And with that, the mist dissipates once more.

Zeta: Huh.

Zeta: I need to go back, think about this.

| She concentrates on the sensation of leaving.
