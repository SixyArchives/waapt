<Sorry... :(.>

Sighing, Effie picks him up. "I'm sorry Bounty. I shouldn't yell."

<It's not your fault the higher-ups are all idiots, > Argo assures him, walking over. Comforted, the Corphish snuggles against Effie and blows a few bubbles. Smiling, she tickles him, then turns to the others.

"Well, let's go. It's something of a trip, so if you like you can travel in your Pokeballs."

Discovery immediately recalls herself. Argo, after making sure Bounty's all right and giving Crewe a pay on the shoulder, follows suit. Effie sets the Corphish on the floor and picks up the still-sleeping Bluebird.

"I'm sorry, dear, but you'll have to be recalled as well until your ankle's healed, " she whispers, and then does just that.

Bounty stays put by her ankle.

<Walk! ^_^!>

Setting him on her shoulder, Effie turns to Crewe.

"Well, Vestas?" she asks, raising one eyebrow. Crewe crosses her arms and gives her a stubborn glare.

<I'm sure as hell not going in a Pokeball.>

Picking up her general intention, Effie shrugs.

"Suit yourself. We're walking, come on."
