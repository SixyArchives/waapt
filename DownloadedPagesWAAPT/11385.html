Along AU Kanto Route 9 - After This

-A certain Haxorus is slowly making his way down the periphery of a certain route, to an outside observer, he would seem to be alone, but...-

<???: "Wow! Look at all those mountains!">

<Vektor: "Uh huh... Do you remember what they used to be called?">

<???: "Oh! Oh! I know this one! My mom told me! They're the horns of the earth!">

<Vektor: "Yeah... I... I never knew that this place had something that was so like home...

... Except... It's so quiet. It's like nothing's alive here-"> ._.

<???: "L-Look!">

<Vektor: "Huh?">

-The Haxorus then sees the forms of a party consisting of a Druddigon, a Dragonite, and a Salamence ringing a rather frightened-looking group of rather obviously trained mons and Cubone-

<Trained!Cubone: "Wh-What do you want from us?">

<Frightened!Cubone: "Y-Yeah, w-we're just taking in tr-tribesbones!">

-Pointing at an accompanying Happiny, Rhyhorn, and Tangela-

<HES!Dragonite: *snort* "Explain the others.">

<HES!Salamence: "We know that you rock lizards are harboring julsille, don't mince words with us.">

<???: "Th-They're in trouble! What do we do?">

-They're humans? But... Why would they be?-

<HES!Druddigon: "Allow us to explain why that is such a problem...">

-And bares a claw-

<HES!Druddigon: "You julsille have your dens now... To forsake them is to forsake the safety you are provided there...">

-And pins a Cubone against the ground, arcing a claw up-

<Pinned!Cubone: "E-Eep!">

<HES!Druddigon: "And there's no place for the allies of such abominations either.">

-Those mons... They're- They're.

They're the reason why that bag was—

-The Haxorus shakes its head and barrels towards the Druddigon preparing to finish off its trapped quarry-

<Vektor: "LEAVE THEM ALONE!">

<HES!Druddigon: "Eh? What the-?">

-And is cut off by a tusk embedding itself into the Druddigon just below its arm. Which staggers and collapses limply-

<HES!Salamence: "What the hell was-?!">

<HES!Dragonite: "Oh, so you're hiding behind a pet? Well fine! He can die with you!">

-And attempts a Dragon Pulse on the Haxorus, which strikes the creature head-on-

THUD!

-And crashes to the ground... Slowly picking itself up-

<Vektor: "Nnnn.. Grr...    GRAAAAAWR!   ">

-And roars fiercely and leaps up to its feet, descending upon its assailant and slashing with blind rage at the Dragonite, which quickly collapses under a torrent of blows-

<Vektor: "   I SAID LEAVE THEM ALON-!   ">

-Before being cut off by a searing pain-

<Vektor: "ARGH!">

-And thrown a good distance, the leering form of a Salamence peering into the Haxorus' eyes-

<HES!Salamence: "Look at you... A monster... Fighting for the lives of other monsters that shouldn't exist!">

-And draws up a claw, and begins to bring it down-

<HES!Salamence: "You don't belong in this worl-!">

-And has its swipe hastily parried by a claw slash-

SHING!

<HES!Salamence: "Hnnrk!">

-And goes limp after a desperate tusk chop connects with its neck, a rather shaken and fatigued Haxorus pulling itself up from under its assailant-

<Vektor: *pant pant* "Am... Am I-? Are they-?">

-And sees the form of a certain Axew nervously backpedaling-

<???: "... M-Meep!">

<Vektor: "Huh? What's wrong with yo-?">

-And looks over at a rather pallid group that was just a short moment ago, being harried by three powerful opponents-

<Humon!Rhyhorn: "H-Holy-">

<Formerly Pinned!Cubone: "Who-? Who are-?">

-Scanning the surroundings, beckoning the Haxorus to follow after-

<Trained!Cubone: "We'll introduce each other later! Come on, q-quick! This way!">

<Vektor: "Huh? B-But-">

<Frightened!Cubone: "Hurry up! Before someone finds this!">

-And the group hastily flees into the shrubbery, plus one additional member-

A Mountain Pass South of AU Kanto Route 9

-The group from earlier is walking ahead of a certain Haxorus, conversing in hushed tones about their recent brush with death-

<Trained!Happiny: "Talk about your close shaves...">

<Formerly Pinned!Cubone: "Do... Do you know him at all?">

<Trained!Cubone: "No, he came outta nowhere! Like he was a guardian angel or something!">

<Trained!Tangela: "If he's a guardian angel, I don't think I wanna know what a demon looks like."> ._.

-The Haxorus turns to the form of an Axew, staring blankly at the form of the larger creature-

<Vektor: "Why are you looking at me like that?">

<???: "... You just... Scared me... That's all.">

<Vektor: "... Scared you?

... What did I do?">

-A patrol of 3 Cubone hastily darts in from some rocks serving in as cover from further up the pass, clearly relieved to encounter the group-

<Patrol Leader: "There you are! Are you alright?!">

<Sharp Club: "Yeah, we heard that fiends were spotted around the place where you guys were supposed to be headed off to!">

<Blunt Club: "We came as fast as we could when we heard about it! What happened?">

<Trained!Cubone: "... We had a bit of a close scrape...">

<Frightened!Cubone: "A fiend helped us get away.">

<Blunt Club: "A fie-?">

-And at the sight of the approaching tall axeface from the back... The color rapidly drains from underneath the faces of the patrolling mons-

<Sharp Club: "Aa-Aah...">

<Patrol Leader: "Wh-What are you doing?! R-RUN!">

<Formerly Pinned!Cubone: "That's the fiend who helped us get away in the valley, you numbskulls!">

<Patrol Leader: "... Oh...">

<Blunt Club: "U-Um... Is he alright?"> ._.

<Sharp Club: "Yeah... He looks... Terrible."> ._.

<Vektor: "H-Huh? Wh-Why? What's wr-?">

-And absentmindedly brushes a claw up against his torso, and feels a sticky sensation-

<Vektor: "... This is-">

-And pulls it up to his eyes, seeing that his claws are almost entirely coated in blackening red fluid-

<Patrol Leader: "Um... How is he still walking?"> ._.

<Humon!Rhyhorn: "Because that's not all his."> ._.

<Vektor: "Wh-What? What do you mea-?">

-And happens to glance at a puddle... And finds his reflection's tusks and claws to be stained a deep shade of red, with a formerly purple cloth around his neck having been discolored by an ugly misshapen band-like blotch. The figure's belly is also stained red, not from the still-oozing wounds on its flank, but from having a profusely bleeding creature fall on top of it, the smear moving in roughly the same direction as it had extricated itself-

<Vektor: "O-Oh...">

<Blunt Club: "Come on... You should get yourself cleaned up...">

<Sharp Club: "Yeah... I'm sure the elder will understand if you need a moment before you're ready to talk.">

<Vektor: "Alright...">

-And trudges off, being lead ahead by some wary-looking Cubone deeper into the mountains-

A Stream in the Mountains South of AU Kanto Route 9

-A certain Haxorus is quietly washing himself off in a stream, most of the red fluids caked on different parts of his body melting away easily, leaving behind some more stubborn flecks clinging in nooks and crannies that the Haxorus is attempting to be rid of without much luck-

-... I know that I wanted to help them... But...

... Is this really all that I am? A monster to be pointed at things to destroy them?

What else could those looks possibly belong t-?-

-And cries out after feeling a stinging sensation from his wounds-

<Vektor: ARGH! Wh-What was-?!">

<???: "Sorry! Sorry!">

-And the Haxorus turns and looks down to spot a Happiny with a beaten potion bottle, accompanied by a Marowak guarded closely by a number of others-

<Trained!Happiny: "You looked like you were hurt and we had this, and- Well-">

<Cracked Femur: "My tribe holds a great debt to you... Surely helping you heal your wounds is the least we could do...">

<Vektor: "... I guess...">

<Cracked Femur: "Would you mind if I spent a while talking with you? After all, it is not often that a fiend warrior comes to the noble aid of a-">

-Protesting-

<Vektor: "I'm not noble! A-And a 'warrior' isn't noble either! Warriors attack and conquer, they prey on the weak!">

-Shaking head-

<Vektor: "I... I just couldn't bear watching them die like that...">

<Cracked Femur: "... You speak as one who has seen a great deal of adversity in life...">

-Flinching as more spray is applied to a wound on his flank-

<Vektor: "U-Um... I guess that's not completely inaccurate...">

<Cracked Femur: "... Is it related to why you came to this place?">

<Vektor: "Came to this place? I... I mean... I'm mostly here to try and find my trainer. Some mons gave me something to deliver for a power plant around here.">

<Cracked Femur: "Oh? You know where your trainer is? Where is it that you will go afterwards?">

<Vektor: "Uh... Vermillion, I guess... That's where I heard she was last.">

<Cracked Femur: "... I see... You're aware that it's supposed to have been a large tribesground in the time of humans, right?">

<Vektor: "... Huh?">

<Cracked Femur: "Lore from the south speaks of it as being broader than the valley below, with mountains of glass and stone reaching for the heavens in it.">

-Starting to look rather daunted by the Marowak's words-

<Vektor: "I-I mean, I have an address... It can't be that hard... Right?"> ._.;

<Cracked Femur: "... Perhaps I should not have said that... But... You are wounded, and in need of rest... We cannot offer much given that we have had to seek new grounds in the caves to evade the fiends that hunt for our traveling brethren who seek refuge with us, but...

Surely you could spend a night?">

-A number of faces poke out from over a ridge, watching the Haxorus' form with a mixture of wariness and curiosity-

<Cracked Femur: "And... We would like the chance to be able to thank you a bit more for today...

Whether or not you are 'noble' in your eyes... We are very much in your debt...

And we'd like to have an opportunity to repay you if we can.">

-Pointing at a ruined violet bandanna-

<Cracked Femur: "I'm sure that at the very least, you'd likely need a replacement for that...">

<Vektor: "... I... I mean, I guess I could use the rest...

Oh... And... Do you know who a 'Zapdos Feather' is, by any chance?"> ._.;

-Blinking-

<Cracked Femur: "... We'll talk more in a safer place.">

-Turning to leave-

<Cracked Femur: "And... You said you travelled with a human once, yes? Did your human ever give you a name?">

-Getting up from the stream and following after-

<Vektor: "Yeah... She did...

It was Vektor.">

<Cracked Femur: "I see then, Vektor...

Thank you.">

<Vektor: "H-Huh?!">

<Cracked Femur: "And if you find your human... Tell her that the Umber Tribe thanks her too, for her bond breeding such a brave guardian.">

<Vektor: "... I-I mean- I-">

-Shaking head-

<Vektor: "I'll tell her. I promise.">

-And the Haxorus heads off after the group as the sun wanes, seeking shelter from the night's enemies along with strange and unfamiliar company-
