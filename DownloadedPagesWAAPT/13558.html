Zeal: But there's more. I'd never heard this part before. Maybe you have, but... "Upon his grandfather's death, Youta is said to have gone into a fit of rage, destroying half the castle, gathering every Magnemite-line mon in the kingdom, and vanishing, never to be seen again. In his absence, chaos emerged within Violight, until a friend of Youta's from overseas took the position of Warlord. That friend was said to have delivered Youta's last words: 'I leave you this world, for I claim another.' The meaning of these words is unknown to this day."

Lanie: No, I knew that, too. Where are you going with this?

Lanie: I'm not going to doubt that, since you're usually right when it comes to that. But still...

Zeal: There's no use worrying about it now. It's just a thought, y'know? But, I'd like to look into this a bit more. Into Youta's disappearance, I mean. And maybe into Iulianus' interest in him. If I can find anything about that, anyway...
