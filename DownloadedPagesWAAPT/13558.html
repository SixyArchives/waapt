Lumiose City

-Lanie has won the Cliff Badge offscreen. Zeal is looking at Iulianus' tablet once again.-

Zeal: Hey, Mel.

Lanie: Hm?

Zeal: Just... I can't seem to figure out what was up with Iulianus.

Lanie: Ah?

Zeal: Well... like this, for instance. He's got a music playlist here labeled "Play While Working". It's made up entirely of the Empire Souls soundtrack.

Lanie: That isn't all you were going to say, is it?

Zeal: No, of course not. I'm just a bit curious about it...

Lanie: What else did you find?

Zeal: He's got a folder labeled "Ransei History".

Lanie: What about it?

Zeal: Well, he went into great detail writing about Youta Fukui. He was our ancestor; I thought you'd be interested.

Lanie: Sure. But I'm still not sure where you're going with this...

Zeal: "The Warlord of Violight was killed during Youta's time as second-in-command, but he and his grandfather kept that information hidden, and he secretly took over as Warlord. His grandfather served as Warlord in his time away, and as an advisor when he was home."

Lanie: ...And? That was brought up at least once in my history class.

Zeal: But there's more. I'd never heard this part before. Maybe you have, but... "Upon his grandfather's death, Youta is said to have gone into a fit of rage, destroying half the castle, gathering every Magnemite-line mon in the kingdom, and vanishing, never to be seen again. In his absence, chaos emerged within Violight, until a friend of Youta's from overseas took the position of Warlord. That friend was said to have delivered Youta's last words: 'I leave you this world, for I claim another.' The meaning of these words is unknown to this day."

Lanie: No, I knew that, too. Where are you going with this?

Zeal: I'm just confused, is all. Iulianus never seemed to be the type of guy to be interested in Ransei history.

Lanie: But you said yourself, you don't know what was going on wi—

Zeal: No, not... Er... what I'm most interested in is the fact that this is the only file on this tablet related to Ransei history in any way.

Lanie: Oh...?

Zeal: I'm just kinda curious. Why would he write about Youta, and just Youta, in such detail? I think there's more to this that I'm not seeing.

Lanie: I'm not going to doubt that, since you're usually right when it comes to that. But still...

Zeal: There's no use worrying about it now. It's just a thought, y'know? But, I'd like to look into this a bit more. Into Youta's disappearance, I mean. And maybe into Iulianus' interest in him. If I can find anything about that, anyway...

Lanie: But what about Fajro?

Zeal: I'll still be looking for him, of course! But this tablet... I found it in the desert after Iulianus' death, and all it's raised is more questions. It's pretty fun, actually. And that's why I'm interested. This search is secondary to my search for Fajro, of course. But this is something I intend to complete, as well.

Lanie: I'll help you with that, if you need any. If you're so interested in it, I suppose there might be more to it.

Zeal: Exactly. But I don't think we'll get anywhere with it anytime soon, so don't worry about it too much.

Lanie: Yeah. I know.
