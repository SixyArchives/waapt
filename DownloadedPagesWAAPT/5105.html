Somewhere In Fortree

"Honestly, Stratus...why?"

The Dragonite glances down at the floor, too ashamed to make eye contact, his chin and stomach satined with a mixture of juices from various berries.

"Did you honestly pick now to decide to go off and find something to eat by yourself? We had to look all over Fortree to find you! And now we've missed our chance at entering you in the Pokéringer!"

Nearby, Lake lets out a disappointed sigh.

Lake: <I bet we've missed Nadia's match, too...>

Woolly: <And Salamence and Spathi's! And I really wanted to be there to cheer for them...>

Stratus glances up at everyone, his bottom lip quivering.

Stratus: <...Are you mad at me, guys?>

The rest of the team glance at each other, unsure of what to say, until Treeo steps forward.

Treeo: <We're not angry, per-se. But we are disappointed, Stratus. Couldn't you have told us you were hungry? We could have gotten something for you to eat without you having to go off on your own.>

Stratus looks at the Sceptile, fiddling with his claws awkwardly as he does so.

Stratus: <Well...there's more to it that that. I...I was nervous about the competition, and when I get nervous, I get really really hungry...>

Woolly tilts her head.

Woolly: <Couldn't you have asked Treeo for some berries?>

Stratus shakes his head in response.

Stratus: <I mean really hungry. Like, "Haven't had a meal for a long time" hungry. Berries are nice, but they're more of a snack...>

Comet: <Unless you eat a whole bush worth.>

The Dragonite nods, wiping the berry juice of his chin.

Stratus: <There wasn't much else I could do...>

Comet looks away briefly, rubbing the back of her head.

Comet: <...Maybe there is.>

Stratus gives the Charmeleon a curious look.

Stratus: <Oh? What do you mean?>

Comet: <Well, if you're feeling nervous of just hungry...I could always cook something for you.>

The Dragonite eye's go wide in shock, the rest of us giving Comet interested glances.

"You can cook?"

Comet nods.

Comet: <Just as a hobby. I couldn't make you a fancy free course meal, but if you wanted some soup or something simple, I could whip something up for you quickly.>

Treeo: <Oh...That's interesting Comet. Not to mention a handy skill to have around.>

Comet glances down at the ground, smiling awkwardly.

Comet: <Thanks...>

"Well, what do you think, Stratus?"

The Dragonite doesn't respond, Stratus being too busy staring at Comet with tears of joy in his eyes.

Stratus: <...Thank you...>

Comet: <...I'm just going to assume that you're pleased about that.>
