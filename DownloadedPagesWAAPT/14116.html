Less than Three

Shalour Hotel, earlier today.

Fool is sitting at the hotel room desk, writing something down in a well-worn notepad. Elsewhere in the room, Sonny Jim is having a nap on the bed, his twin tails curled around him.

The setting is rather calm and quiet, the relative silence of the place broken at the sound of knocking on the door.

Fool: -Looking behind her- Come in.

On her calling, the door opens as the figure of Constantin enters the room. Fool beams as he enters the room and pulls out from her desk to properly greet him.

Fool: Ah! Bonjour Constantin! -hugs Constantin-

Constantin: -Gives a solemn smile and hugs back- Salut mademoiselle Solana.

Fool notices that there is something... off about Constantin's tone and body language. After letting go of him she raises an eyebrow and tilts her head.

Fool: Is... something wrong Constantin?

Constantin: Mademoiselle... do you mind if we have a private conversation?

Fool seems a little concerned about what Constantin is saying.

Fool: Sure, just close the door behind you.

The door snaps shut. Hey, being a Psychic does have it's perks.

Fool: ...I know you're more of a coffee guy Constantin, but I'v just finished making a pot of tea. Would you like a cup?

Constantin: While tea is not as good as coffee, at least in my opinion, I would happily accept your offer mon cheri

Fool nods as she sets out the table for tea for two. When she is done, Constantin pours tea for both Fool and himself.

Fool: -Picking up her cup from the saucer- Thanks Constantin.

Constantin: -Grabbing his cup from mid-air- You're welcome mon cheri.

Fool: So... what did you want to talk about Constantin?

Constantin: ...

Solana, I need to discuss matters regarding Monsieur Tagg with you.
