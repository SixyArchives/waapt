Reborn

Al-Gala S.A Headquarters, Lumiose City

A young woman is sitting in a dimly lit room, chair turned towards the window and nose stuck in a copy of the city times. Two henchmen stand by the door and look over to their boss with worried glances.

Henchman #1: A-Are you all right boss?

Henchman #2: You've been staring at those papers for a while now...

The girl places the paper behind her on her desk, but doesn't turn around.

Cleo: Leave

Henchman #1: Are ya sure you want to be a-

Cleo: I said... Leave.

Henchmen #2: ...As you wish madame

The two leave the domain of their boss and close the door quietly behind them. The deafaning silence from before looms back over the room as Cleo turns around and sinks back into her chair.

In the corner of the room, Pakhet and Montu lie in their custom sized Louis XV Pet Pavilion with rather hang-dog expressions. Pakhet in particular appears to poking what appears to be the bottle of fancy, expensive 50-year old whiskey with her nose.

Montu: <We never did give our "Anniversary of being the best drinking buddies ever" gift, did we?>

Pakhet: -Pulling the bottle closer to her and cuddling around it- <Now this is all we really have left of the old fuddy-duddy...>

Montu: <It never shoulda ended... Not like this anyway...>

Pakhet uncurls for a moment and looks up at her trainer.

Pakhet: <She hasn't really done much since the news...>

Montu: <Should we ask her if she is okay?>

Pakhet: <That's probably for the best...>

Pakhet slinks out of her be and slowly walks over to Cleos' desk on all fours, placing her head on the desk when she arrives.

Pakhet: ~Cleo... You haven't really shown anything since you got that paper a few days ago...~

Cleo stares back at her Delphox for a good minute with Dull Eyes of Unhappiness before staring blankly at the door again.

Cleo: Being professional. That is all.

Pakhet: ~You're allowed to cry you know...~

Cleo pushes back her chair and stands up, this time giving her Delphox an intense gaze.

Cleo: Big girls don't cry.

Cleo grabs her cane and walks over towards the door, glaring back at both her Delphox and her Blaziken.

Cleo: Now if you excuse me, I have a meeting to attend to. I'll be back to pick you up later tonight

And with that Cleo flings the door open and jolts outside, slamming the door shut behind her. Montu and Pakhet exchange glances at each other, before Pakhet silently sulks back to the pet bed, curling up once again around the bottle of whiskey.
