 180169 Pipping Fool, Tue, 31st Jul '12 4:47:07 AM from koth_lakeside_event Get RP Mod
Good Guy Germany

Efrafa.

It's a cold, crisp morning. The chilly breeze sweeping across the mountain foot like a lone elk. In this morning breeze, a pair of Nidorino are observed roaming the area looking for anything even slightly out of place. These Nidorino would notice even a pebble in it's incorrect location as they have been scouting these ground since the day they were picked by Woundwort himself to be apart of his council police. Their massive size and gaping scars send the message quite clearly to any intruder, that they will maul any trespasser who gets in their way.

Yet, Laurel was not scared. He had traversed from his Doomed Hometown on foot, facing the qualms of hunger, thirst, tiredness and loss in a search to find a new place to call home, and he succeeded. He's taken up on a perilous and life endangering quest to secure the future of his new warren, and he passed the first boundary of the cruel and unforgiving caverns of Mt. Moon.

He wasn't worried.

Not a twinge of fear pinged in his heart when the Nidorino approached him.

Silverweed: <Hlessi*! Stop where you are!>

Acorn: <Who are you!>

He answered their question, as cool as the air around them.

Laurel: <My name is Laurel. I am but a weary, hlessi looking for a home>

Silverweed: -Leering at Laurel, and growling- <What are you doing here?>

Laurel: <I have came here to humbly ask to join your warren.>

Silverweed and Acorn exchange glances of dumbfoundment and bewilderedness at eachother. Never in their lives had they heard of any Nido asking to join their warren, nor have they heard any of the Owslafa ever mention of such a Nido. Clearly, this one should be questioned.

Acorn: <Where do you come from, Laurel?>

Laurel: <I came from a warren far away from here, alas, that warren was destroyed by the hands of humans. I was once a member of the Owsla* there, so I was able to survive on my own for some time.>

Acorn takes a good look at the Nidorino in front of him. His size and stature was incredibly large for a Nidorino. It would be no doubt that whatever warren he came from, he would have been in the Owsla there, if he were not already their Cheif. he also noticed that his body was littered with numerous scars of different kinds. Scratches, jabs, bite marks, burns, electrocution marks, plant induced rashes etc. Whatever journey he was on, it most certainly was a long one.

Acorn: <... I believe you're telling the truth hlessi.>

Silverweed: <Honestly Acorn? What makes you think->

Acorn: <Look at his body Silverweed. His size and stature is grand, a typical body type of Nidorino's accepted into the Oswla. He is also littered with every kind of beastly scar imaginable. I believe he's telling us nothing short of the truth.>

Silverweed paces as he considers his partners advice. Acorn was infamous for being one of the best observers in the Owslafa. If he detected nothing wrong, then surely, this hlessi wasn't lying.

He still didn't trust him.

Silverweed: <Come Laurel, we'll introduce you to our leader. Though, I don't think he'll be pleased to be disturbed from silflay at this time in morning.>

Silverweed and Acorn lead Laurel away from the dense grass, into a small prairie. As they pass by small pods of Nidorans, Nidorinas and some of the more meek Nidorino's, Laurel can only think about how quickly these sentries were to believe his story. After all, he didn't lie to them, he only omitted certain truths.

He smirked as he was lead down into a ditch, covered in brambles and tree-roots. Where he swore he heard some monster grazing on whatever prey it had in it's clutches.

Totally Trustworthy.
