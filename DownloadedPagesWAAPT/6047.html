Phyco: <Why does it feel like that we've been in a giant circle?>

"Because we have."

Phyco: <...Oh.>

A certain trainer and his Kingdra are sitting outside the Pokémon Center, the human currenttly browsing through his PC boxes on his laptop, searching for a specific Pokémon. Finding this rather boring to watch, the Kingdra turns his attention to a familiar Sceptile, watching as a Charizard lands next to him.

Treeo: <Any luck finding her?>

The Charizard shakes her head in response.

Comet the Charizard: <Nope. I asked that Ampharos that's in charge, and he said he hadn't been seen since she left with her brother to graze by the Magnet Train line.

Treeo frowns, now rather worrying.

Treeo: <You don't think something could have happened to them, could you?>

Comet: <Well, they weren't stolen by any hungry Charizard, that's for certain.>

Treeo: <We really should have tried to stop that while we were there.>

Comet: <I'll have to remember to go back and "convince" them sometime.>

The Sceptile roll his eyes.

Treeo: <Just because you're a Charizard now, it doesn't mean you can solve everything with violence.>

Comet: <Hey, has it failed us yet?>

There's an awkward pause, broken only be Treeo shaking his head.

Treeo: <...No.>

Comet smirks proudly in response.

Comet: <Then I rest my case.>

"Oh! I found him..."

Treeo and Comet turn their attention to their trainer, who has just withdrawn a Pokeball from his PC.

Phyco: <You didn't tell us you had a Ditto...>

"I don't get him out very often. Especially after what happened last time."

Knowing just what he's referring too, Comet and Treeo exchange a frightened glance, which Phyco doesn't fail to notice. Looking rather worried himself, Phyco tilts his head at his trainer.

Phyco: <I dread to ask, but what happened last time?>

"You don't want to know, but I don't intend for it to happen again."

With that, Luke tosses the Pokéball, releasing a Ditto. The pink blob blinks at the sunlight, then glances around, soon spotting his trainer.

Ditto: <Oh hai!>

Before the Ditto can grow several times his size and glomp the human like he had planned, Luke quickly slips on a strange silverly scarf...then leaps into Phyco's Bubble Shield, startling the Kingdra inside, and giving Comet and Treeo a shock.

Comet: <Has he gone insane?!>

Treeo: <I...honestly can't be sure...>

Phyco: <Hey! HEY! Do you think you're doing?!>

"Just borrowing your Bubble Shield for a sec..."

Confused, the Ditto wobbles over and pokes the bubble with a pesudopod, unable to enter it himself.

Ditto: <Aww...>

Phyco: <Wait, how are you breathing in here? And did you wipe your feet first?!>

The human makes a "hush" gesture at his Kingdra before turning his attention to his Ditto.

"Hey, Ditto. I need you to do something for me..."
