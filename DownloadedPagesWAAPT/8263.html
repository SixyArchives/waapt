206573 Luke92419th Nov 2012 05:22:13 AM Get RP Mod

Azalea Town

In an out of the way part of the town, Stratus is keeping an eye on Deluge, still locked away inside her bubble prison. Not that she had been saying much. In fact, the two Pokémon had spent the past few hours reflecting on what had happened with Lake earlier.

Stratus: <...Deluge?>

The Feraligatr, who had been curled up on the floor, glanced up at the Dragonite.

Deluge: <Yeah?>

Stratus: <Do you get the impression that we might not be the best parents?>

Deluge: <Oh, so it's not just me then?>

She gives the Dragonite a frown.

<Yeah...we kinda' suck, don't we?>

With a nod, Stratus sighs, glancing down at the ground.

Stratus: <I guess it's because that we never got to be Lake's family for very long, is it?>

Deluge: <Not really. I only had him for a month, while you've had him for over a year, yet without even knowing he was you're son...>

Stratus: <I know...>

Deluge: <It didn't help that you ran off before I even laid his egg...>

Hearing this, Stratus gives the bubbled Feraligatr a foul look.

Stratus: <You tried. To EAT me.>

Deluge: <No I didn't! I only wanted a little bite to keep me going until breakfast!>

She stops, shaking her head dismissively.

<But that's not the point here. The thing is...well, he's kinda' grown up without us, hasn't he?>

The Dragonite nods.

Stratus: <Yeah. When I met him for the first time, he was already fully grown, so I'd missed all the important things. Like seeing him get a trainer...>

Deluge: <Winning his first battle...>

Stratus: <Seeing him evolve. Both times, at that.>

Deluge: <Seeing him taking his first Stantler down...>

Stratus: <...Yeah, I think we get the idea with that.>

The Dragonite give Deluge a sad look.

<I dunno'...maybe...he doesn't really need us?>

Deluge: <No...that can't be right. If that were true, he wouldn't have gone through all that effort to track us both down now, would he?>

Stratus: <Well, maybe. But I can't help but think...>

Stratus pauses briefly, finding it hard to say the next words.

<That he might be a bit disappointed with what he found.>

Meanwhile...

Lake, feeling a bit better after yesterday's events, as well as knowing that Deluge likely wasn't off eating anyone thanks to Stratus keeping an eye on her, was now just about ready to show Umbra, Lina and Ammy's friend how he could use Bubble Shield. Most likely for her Pokémon, he presumed.

Since he wasn't sure how many times he was going to have to pull this move off, he had taken the precaution of gathering a few Sitrus Berries to restore any HP spent, and was warming himself up with some breathing exercises.

Lake: <In...>

He inhaled deeply...

<...And out...>

Then exhaled.

<In...>

He inhaled again...

<...and out.>

Then exhaled once more, now thoroughly warmed up.

<Okay...who do I need to instruct, then?>
