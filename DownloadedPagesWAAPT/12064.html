Shore Near Gateon Port

-Two Aqua members are overseeing a barge lower a piece of heavy machinery to the shore: a canal-digger-

Effie: Things seem to be proceeding well so far, ma'am.

Ryklys: Naturally.

Effie: And no news of this... Cipher?

Ryklys: Not as of yet...

-she pauses-

You have not dealt with them before, I assume.

Effie: No, ma'am. Mayor Ruhle seemed terrified of them.

Ryklys: There is nothing redeeming to them. They are the lowest of scum.

Pokémon thieves, you can hope for. Some of them are even liberators, after all.

But Cipher?

They seek nothing but to reduce Pokémon to hollow, soulless shells. Mere weapons for their self-centered ambitions.

Effie: -places a hand on the Pokeballs at her belt- ... I see. And they are powerful in this area?

Ryklys: Yes. Keep track of your team at all times. And if you see any Cipher members with Pokémon showing... odd behavior?

Our new higher-ups may discourage it, but you go right ahead and liberate those poor creatures. Read up on Shadow Pokémon if you do.

Effie: ... Yes. Of course. I'll be sure to.

Ryklys: As for our role here...

As a matter of fact, this machine is pretty state-of-the-art. GPS-guided, onboard power plant, it can even time-release Pokéballs for habitat population. Technically, the canal could dig itself without us.

We're here for security. Whether Cipher, Magma, stampedes, or other threats.

Effie: ... Other threats?

Ryklys: -shrugs-

Never assume you have accounted for every factor in a plan. For when you assume...

Effie: Of course. I'll be ready.

Ryklys: Good. The cab should have a bunk, I'll take first watch if you like.

Effie: That's all right, ma'am, I can take the watch. I have some research to do, so I might as well stay up.

Ryklys: Very well. Good-night, Effie. Wake me up at 03:00 hours.

Effie: Yes, ma'am. Good night, ma'am.

-the digger begins its trek inland under the moonlight-

-A certain Haxorus is looking around some cramped accommodations repurposed from its original use as a single-family residence, in spite of the clutter and lack of space... A sense of homeliness can still perhaps be seen to peek through through small decorations put up to preserve a sense of normalcy-

<Vektor: "So, um, Carol...

Have you been... OK..?">

<Carol: "Oh? I mean I wouldn't call everything that I've been through ideal...

But the neighbors have been nice And we've been looking out for each other">

-Waving a set of claws an Electrike's height off the floor-

<Vektor: "I mean... you know, you got...">

<Carol: " Well, yeah. That was one of the less ideal things"> >_>;

-Shaking head-

<Carol: "On the plus side, it's made finding lost things a bit easier.

You notice a lot more when you're close to the ground.">

<Vektor: "I just wish I could do something to help undo that...">

<Carol: "Oh? Well There is one thing we could try">

<Vektor: "Oh?!">

<Carol: *leaps up into Vektor's arms* "Well, it doesn't solve being a foot tall and not having thumbs anymore, but">

<Vektor: "Huh?">

<Carol: "It does at least let me see over the counter again"> ^_^;

<Vektor: "... ￼:)

Heh...">

-Looking around the clutter on top of the counter-

<Vektor: "Um... Amelia mentioned something about cans?">

<Carol: "Eh? I mean... If you really want to help, but you really don't need to feel any rush to-">

-Calling out from a different room-

<Fern: "Hey! Carol? What kind of screws are supposed to go into the door hinges again?">

<Carol: "The cross-headed ones!">

<Fern: "Where do we keep those again?">

-Sweatdropping-

<Carol: "Erm... Think you could give me a moment to help Fern out with this, Vektor?">

<Vektor: "Eh? But... Should I help-?">

<Carol: "In time... Just... Focus on taking things easy a bit first, Vektor... That stuff will still be there for you to help with tomorrow.">

<Vektor: "Erm... Okay...">

-And lets the Electrike back down on the floor before she darts off... Before turning his attention back to the counter-

<Vektor: "... Um... Wait, where exactly are the cans in all of thi-?"> ._.;

-In a teasing tone-

<9-Volt: "Hey... Already doing chores? Thought listening was supposed to be your strong suit...">

<Vektor: "Eh?! N-No! I was just looking at-!">

<9-Volt: "Hmm hmm... Relax... I'm not trying to give you that much of a hard time..."> ^^;

-Looking down uneasily-

<Vektor: "I... still don't get it though... you and Carol and everybody are happy to see me, but... I kept seeing other dragons whose teams wouldn't talk to them anymore..."> ._.

<9-Volt: " I'm not saying that I feel a whole lot for them But If what you went through is anything like what they did I can understand you.">

<Vektor: "What... happened?">

-Doing the equivalent of a a brow furrow and shaking head-

<9-Volt: " They're a bunch of punks But they're understandable Ish... A lot of them joined up in order to try and stick up for their friends

And We were thinking Being the big idiot who's always trying to make things right that you are That They weren't so different from you.

And that you probably had it rougher At least they get to see the mons that they're being jerks for You didn't">

-Easing up a bit expression-wise-

<9-Volt: "Heh Besides, we figured that if it was the last thing that he did That Randy would at least help keep you from completely destroying yourself">

-Mind being jogged by the orange rat's comment-

<Vektor: "Oh! Randy! He did say he'll be trying to get here... I showed him the address...">

-Smiling a bit-

<9-Volt: "Then let's give him something to look for-">

-And spots a passing shadow of a large creature in flight from the window-

<9-Volt: ""> ._.

-Wincing-

<Vektor: "What happens if they see me here..?"> ._.

<9-Volt: " Probably nothing good if that warning was anything to go by"> ._.

<Vektor: "Oh-OK...">

<9-Volt: "Erm How good are you at sleeping standing up?">

<9-Volt: "Right So much for keeping you in the closet.">

<9-Volt: "Erm Well, when a human likes a-  You know what, we're getting off topic Um Let's try the crawl space?">

<Vektor: "OK... I mean... It's not as if I couldn't make it a little bigger if it had to be... Digging's always been strangely relaxing, I'm not sure why...">
