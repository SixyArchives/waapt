Devon Corporation

Felix and Mr. Stone stepped through the revolving door, the temperature dropping slightly due to the central A/C flowing through the building. Minty shivered, unused to such temperatures, and cuddled next to Ohm for warmth. Jemma pawed curiously at the white tiled floors, finding her reflection in them an amusing distraction at the moment.

Mr. Stone: This is Devon Corporation, lad.

He gestured in front of him to the front desk. Behind the counter, a woman in a suit was chatting with a young couple, enlightening them about the various opportunities they could receive at such a prestigious business.

Mr. Stone: As you can see, we are more than willing to discuss matters with any and all kinds of people. Children, adults, no age is too high or low for Devon Corp.

Stone lead Felix down the hallway to the right, Rex's Pokemon following after. Ohm, noting Minty's movement was slower than usual, picked him up, cuddling him against her body.

Ohm: <Sorry I don't have any wool to keep you warm.>

Minty: <It's okay.> *nuzzles*

Mr. Stone stopped in front of a closed door, motioning for Felix to look through the window. Peering in, he could see a team of scientists gathered around a machine with a strange rock inside. The rock was suspended in a green liquid, bits of it chipping away and revealing what appeared to be...forelegs?

Mr. Stone: Our R/D Department. This is one of their crowning achievements: a machine that revitalize fossilized Pokemon. It was developed around the same time as the one in Kanto, although we had no way of knowing they were working on one at the time. *chuckles* Aubrey was a bit disheartened when she found out she hadn't created the first of the machines.

They continued on, heading towards a staircase. Stone waited for Felix and the Pokemon to pass through before letting the door shut, ushering them gently up the stairs.
