Lumiose City - I don't know when

Aline is dressed in a blue and white school uniform, being right outside school with two friends, both girls, all of them carrying report cards.

"Hey, Aline, let me see your report card!" said one of her friends, a blonde wearing glasses.

Aline nods and hands her the report card.

Aline's report card:

History: B

Science: D

Art: S

Citizenship: A

Mathematics: C

French: B

Languages: B

Geography: A

Pokémon Training: B

"... How did you get a S in art? It only goes up to A" said the blonde

"Well, Eve... She kinda did draw a whole comicbook perfectly..." said the other friend, a redhead, peeking at the report card.

"Not the best at science are you?"

Aline rubbed the back of her head, embarrassed.

"Well, you're doing better than me at least. I still can't get the hang of Pokémon training." said Eve.

"I almost got a perfect score at it! I got cheated out of a perfect score!" said the redhead, folding her arms in frustration

"Lara, you got Floette's type wrong, of all things." said Eve

"Are you gonna tell me it doesn't look like a Fairy/Grass type?!" said Lara, still looking pretty miffed.

Aline snickers at the whole discussion before taking her report card back.

"So, who's gonna pick you up today? You said your parents were gonna be busy today." said Eve.

Aline was about to write an answer in her sign, but then she was a figure coming from the sky from the corner of her eye. She waved at the figure, soon showing itself to be Divoom in his evolved form, much to the surprise of her friends. Aline casually got up on the Dragon type, who had some kind of saddle made just so she wouldn't suffer the risk of falling mid-flight.

"Um..."

"Um..."

Aline then waved goodbye to her friends before Divoom took off with her, while her friends just awkwardly waved as she flew in the distance to the other side of the city

"... Do mute people have superpowers or something? I mean, she can't possibly have-" said Lara until she was stopped by being dope slapped by Eve "Ow!"
