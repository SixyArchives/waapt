Spectra, 600 years ago

-while Isolde makes up with the armorer's Skitty and Kori goes off to find some Pokemon, Amhlaidh hunts down the captain of the guard, finding him pacing in the practice yard-

Amhlaidh: Captain Keiji!

-the man stops and turns to him, hurrying over-

Keiji: Amhlaidh. Tell me you have answers. Is the Ghost Lord really dead?

Amhlaidh: Yes. Our lord is dead. Lady Duolis is leaving the region.

Keiji: Good luck to her. So then... are you in charge? The new Warlord of Spectra?

Amhlaidh: -shakes his head- I'm going home.

Keiji: But... Lord Umbra never had any children. At least, none that we know of. Will Spectra go leaderless?

Amhlaidh: No. Spectra needs a leader now more than ever, to rebuild and protect its people from the same enemies that killed its last leader. Lord Umbra never named a successor. I suppose he never though he would need one. Lady Duolis is leaving, and is far too distressed to worry about the fate of this country. I need to go home. That leaves you, Keiji, to take up the mantle as Warlord of Spectra.

-Keiji hesitates, looking like he wants to say something-

Amhlaidh: I can think of no one better. You are a competent warrior, and you lead your men well. And perhaps you have the compassion the country needs. If you can name a better successor, do so, but otherwise, step up and take the throne.

-he remains quiet for a few more moments, then nods-

Keiji: All right, Amhlaidh, I'll do it. For now. Until someone better comes along.

Amhlaidh: Be prepared for a wait, then, old friend.

-he clasps his hand-

Amhlaidh: Good bye.

Keiji: Good bye. I don't suppose I'll ever see you again.

Amhlaidh: I don't suppose you will, either. Lead well.

-he bows, then turns and leaves the man standing there, going to his room and snatching a piece of paper, taking several minutes to write a letter. By the time he's done, Kori and Isolde have made their way back to him. He gathers his things, heads to the stable, and saddles his Rapidash, saying goodbye to the stable staff, then leaves the letter with a messenger boy to be delivered and rides for Dragnor-
