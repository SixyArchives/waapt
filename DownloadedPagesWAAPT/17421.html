Flashback — Valorie Ryder

Where oh where had our shortcake gone? Great question! Valorie had been challenging the gyms in Kalos last we heard of her, going through a series of very weird personal issues at the same time. Yet—She never quite got around to finishing the gym challenge. It was Laverre City where Valorie's challenge had been interrupted prematurely. She recalled it vividly, a beautiful gym that quite frankly suited her fine. It was very cutesy, just as the gym leader's outfit had been. Valorie appreciated all of that, perhaps even envied Valerie's bravery in being so open with her whimsical nature. What she hadn't been able to tolerate however ... was losing.

Yes, Valorie had lost in spite of all her relentless training and vigorous pushing of her limits. Her team of dragons hadn't appreciated Valerie's team of fairies much at all, and her arrogance had ultimately led her to making several mistakes she was punished dearly for. It was all clear as day... Haxorus' poison jab devistating her Sylveon and failing to hurt Valerie's Mawile. Mawile using iron defense to shrug off earthquake and bringing Haxorus to her knees while Valorie watched in horror. How could she have made such stupid mistakes? She had felt so... helpless.

The shortcake remembered being furious—No, more than that. She had felt ashamed, embarassed, frustrated and anxious all at the same time. She remembered her body betraying her as her curse reduced her to the form of a dratini, seemingly punishing her for her bad sportsmanship.

It all became a haze as she fled the gym, emotions distorting judgement and as she vanished into the wilderness. All she'd wanted was to be alone, for everyone to forget what they'd seen... She had laid low, returning to her human form by then ext day, trying desperately to figure out where she had gone wrong. What was she missing? She was good to her pokemon, she trained hard and she never backed down from a challenge? Where was the success? She should at least be able to make it to the elite four! Why then was she struggling so much with mere gym leaders?

It had taken a long time for Valorie to once again recover from her shock, filled with training and rethinking her strategies. Where had this brought her? Well...

Pokemon Trainer's School / Rustboro Gym — Valorie Ryder (Open)

Valorie found herself staring at a computer monitor. She was doing research, studying the information available on Roxanne to better understand her soon-to-be opponent. She'd been waiting some time for this, studying at the pokemon trainer's school to build her confidence back up. Today felt like the day; she was going to rock this gym.

...

Logging off, Valorie got up and wrapped her half-cloak around her. She then began walking to the gym, channeling all of her arrogance to keep her from backing down. She was tired of waiting—Life didn't last forever you know! The doors swung open and the short dragon tamer entered, her cardinal team ready for action.
