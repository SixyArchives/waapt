Goldenrod

Doing what looked like it was supposed to be a crack of the knuckles - but coming off as far less impressive - Runt walked over to the group that Straw and Crewe had formed. Grabbing on and making sure everyone in the group was connected, he concentrated for a moment before blinking out from sight.

Saffron City

And a moment later, they blinked back into existence on the edges of Saffron City. Taking note of where he was, Runt teleported a second time, this time to in front of the local Pokemon Center. He wasn't sure what it was, but there was something about the Pokemon Centers that acted like a magnetic compass if he ever got close enough and focused. Runt let go of the group.

Runt: [Thinking Out Loud] "I'll be back with the others pronto."

And just like that, the Abra teleported away again.

Goldenrod

And once again, Runt found himself within Goldenrod City, and a second teleportation placed him within a few yards of the station where the others were waiting for their turns.

Runt: [Thinking Out Loud] "Is group two ready to go?"
