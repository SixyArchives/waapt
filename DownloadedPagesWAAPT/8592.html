AU!Mauville

The ringing of a school bell wafts out the doors of a school complex, last year's war damage patched up in recent months by tin and drywall.

The kids chatter amongst themselves as they disperse- some to the buses, some to their parents' car, some to an orphanage across the street, some to loitering to meet up with siblings.

One such girl, 9.97-year-old Patricia, eagerly seeks out her sister; a difficult task for some... but fortunately, she's not the only one looking for Katrina. She glances up, and smiles as she sees a Tropius fly in for a landing beside a girl across the yard.

Tamato the Tropius: <Kat!>

Katrina: Hi Tamato! Have you been a good Tropius today?

The Tropius grins mischievously.

<Maaaaaaaybe~>

His 12-year-old Trainer gives him a playful punch on the side.

Katrina: Oh, you're just trying to make me paranoid and you know it!

Her sister reaches her at this point.

Oh, Pat! How was class today?

Patricia: Great! I passed the test!

Katrina: Um... which test? Math? Spelling?

Patricia: Nonono, the important test!

Katrina: Oh, your Trainer's License!

Patricia: Yeah! Eleven days until I can journey!

Katrina: Have you decided where you're getting your starter yet?

???: Pfft, chillax, you know this cool dude has you covered!

An older boy from the high school sneaks up on his younger sisters.

Patricia: Hi Benjamin!!!

Katrina: ...you're getting her her starter?

Benjamin: Oh, you know it! A bro's got to make sure his blood's got a mon with the right 'tude, ya'know? A mon that will keep her safe in a real world....

...but more importantly, a starter that shows her to chill, right? Colonal Sandra went and made you all busy-like, your Trainer's Leave went by way too fast!

Katrina: I got six badges in a year! And Sandra is a Blaziken now. It's been six years since your journey, where you got, what, one badge? And Troopa's still a Wartortle!

Benjamin: Pfft, we kept it real! Had a good time, didn't mess around with stupid classes for "smashing rocks" or "cutting trees", Surfing's all you need.

'sides, weren't all those leaders you fought foxes? Man, you know all that running around for the Man was just the Man trying to keep you in the Rattata race, man.

Katrina simply pats Tamato on the wing, as Patricia scrambles onto his back.

Katrina: Well I have a flier. So I get a say in Pat's starter, or you're walking to the apartment!

Benjamin: ...that's cold, sis. You know my job pays for our digs!

There is a brief awkward silence, neither of them really keen to remember why Ben is now the head of the house.

Patricia: Let's goooo....

Snapping out of their funk, Kat and Ben pile on to Tamato, who takes off, mumbling to himself.

Tamato: <Three humans to carry, Kat better be glad she trained my Strength...>
