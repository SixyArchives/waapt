Okay, so there's two pokemon I came up with: Onax and Oplan.

Onax is the carrier of sleepers, and escorts them to the place where dreams and nigtmares occur. It's natural form is a purple-black shadow, and he takes this form in the real world or in the sleep world when it wears the Awakened Jewel, a gem coloured like the sunrise. He is strongest in this form. His weaker form is like Oplan's natural form, but in a sepia rainbow instead. It also can appear as a man cloaked in shadows with a single opal pendant, but only during cutscenes.

Oplan is the carrier of wakers, and brings them back to the real world at the end of sleep. It's natural form is some sort of cute, floating creature, I have no idea how it would look though, colored like a rainbow. It takes this form in the sleep world, or when carrying the Dormant Jewel, a gem colored like the sunset. When in the real world, it is like Onax, but light grey instead of black. It appears as an elegant lady wearing a shimmering dress and an onyx pendant during cutscenes.

The Awakened and Dormant Jewels practically change each of them into either their real world or sleep world forms respectively. They are also the same jewel, the form is set by taking it to a special place in the centre of the real and dream worlds. Real World = Awakened Jewel, Sleep World = Dormant Jewel.

I guess someone would make a fanfic pairing them together if they were real.
