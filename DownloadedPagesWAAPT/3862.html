Less than Three

AU Unova

-The two trainers walk up the road continuing their conversation, Sol is hiding in the tall grass hoping not to be noticed by either of them-

Bypasser #1: Oi! Did you hear that?

Bypasser #2: It was probably just a Mienshao ducking for cover when it heard us coming, nothing out of the norm. So c'mon, let's get going.

Bypasser #1: Okay then.

-The two move away from the grass, Sol slowly lets the air escape from her lungs and starts breathing again-

Bypasser #2: So what were you going on about?

Bypasser #1: Oh, you know the "Witch of Undella's" mansion?

-Sol freezes once again-

Bypasser #1: The finally unbolted that Steadfast Iron door.

Bypasser #2: They did?

Bypasser #1: Yeah and behind it was a study of kinds. Due to the size and shape of the study (and the fact that it was 100% fireproof), the reckon it most likely wasn't used by the witch herself, but rather by that devil in an Emboar's skin that shared the same home with the witch.

Bypasser #2: The Emboar's study!? What did they find?

Bypasser #1: The motherload my friend. Files of all the people who passed through Undella Town over the two years of hell, training videos, maps of Unova, Sinnoh, Hoenn, Johto and Kanto with extreme accuracy and detail, files of all sorts and kinds and, probably the most important find, the Pig's journal.

Bypasser #2: The Emboar could write?

Bypasser #1: Very eloquently from the notes taken. It's rare to find a Pokemon that can comprehend human language, let alone read and write fluently in it.

Bypasser #2: Any new facts about the old regime coming to light?

-Sol is listening in intently, not making a peep-

Bypasser #1: Plenty. From the files that have been analysed so far, it seems that the witch had a farther reach than expected. Some files mention having eyes watching over some breeding grounds and rookeries used for the Starter Pokemon as well as the Zorua Farms. The Journal refers to it as "We look to see potential rise at anytime and anywhere and reap its benefits. No matter who or what the subject is. If we can't influence directly in to subjects life, than we shall alter the flow of the river in the way that benefits us in the grand scheme of things".

(Thinking) Napoleon had files about the breeding grounds, rookeries and farms? I... I didn't know this... but what's more shocking to me is the way he wrote his journal. He refers to "we" even though he only means himself... It seems that he knew the price of failure and that if he was to fail...

-Sol slides her hands onto her neck-

I was to be dragged down with him.

Bypasser #2: ...Golly...

Bypasser #1: And by the other notes in the journal, as well as the elaborate maps of the other regions, he was going to plan well thought out invasions of Hoenn, Johto & Kanto. While this doesn't come as a surprise, considering what the Bug and Cat sent tho Hoenn, Kanto and Johto...

-The two shudder-

Bypasser #1: The difference was how well thought out these invasions were. Every weakness that was observed in each region was studied upon with rigorous intent. When it came to planning positions of the armies, he improved and refined, then went back to refine each improvement.

Bypasser #2: Imagine, all four of those regions run by one insane Emboar. That's a frightening thought...

Bypasser #1: I still can't believe that the Witch got off Scott free.

Bypasser #2: No-one can. Though sometimes in history, horrible people who commit awful crimes get off scott free.

Bypasser #1: Though if is brave, and stupid, enough to return to Unova... Heh, she ain't gonna get a warm welcome, that's for sure.

Bypasser #2: Yeah. But enough about that, we have some errands to do in Black City so let's hi-tail our way outta here!

-The two run off in the direction of Black City. Sol remains motionless.-
