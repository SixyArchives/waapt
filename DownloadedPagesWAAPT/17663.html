Duke and Lyuri are walking around the city with no clear goal.

"So... It's been a while since we actually did something together." said Lyuri, giving Duke a smile that clearly was trying to imply something.

"I think so, some things that happened really distracted us." said Duke.

"How about we make this a date?" Lyuri then embraced one of Duke's arms and leaned against it as they kept walking.

Duke smiled at the act "Well, I don't see why not." he paused "You can act surprisingly..." he trailed off, unsure of what word to finish his sentence with.

"Girly? Innocent? Soft?" she gave Duke a knowing smile.

"More like fragile. For someone with a fierce atittude like you, seeing you speak in such a soft-spoken tone and act so gentle usually means something managed to go through your defenses."

Lyuri takes a moment to reply, taking a deep breath right before doing so "Yeah. That's true." she smiled once again "Usually if I'm like this it's because something bad happened. But I just feel very comfortable around you. I feel safer just being close to you."

"You're not exactly the kind of person that needs someone to protect you, I'd say."

"It never hurts to have back-up, you know. A tall, muscular, superpowered guy like you works very well as a bodyguard."

"Bodyguard?" he raised an eyebrow, then letting out a small chuckle "I guess I can see it."

"You're clumsy, dense and a little awkward, but yeah." Lyuri then winked at him.

Duke then rubbed the back of his neck while giving some embarrassed chuckles, until suddenly he felt Lyuri pull him down by the collar of his shirt and kissing him in the mouth for about five seconds before breaking it.

"And it's just the way I like it. Otherwise making jokes at your expense wouldn't be as easy and fun." said Lyuri, letting go of his collar.

Duke smiled "Glad I can provide you entertainment."

"In more than one way too." Lyuri gave him a suggestive smile.

"O-Oh, right..." said Duke, looking away from her as he went back to a normal posture.

"Hey, you actually understood what I was refering to. You're improving." she then held his arm again "So, how about we go to the beach?"

"Well, we would need to change to more appropriate clothes."

"We don't really need to. Just a walk on the beach would be good enough. Besides, I'm wearing a bikini under my clothes anyway."

Duke blinked, then gave her a knowing smile "You planned all this from the start didn't you?"

"Of course. Don't you know me?" she said, flipping her hair.

Duke chuckled, he then tried to pull his arm to free it from Lyuri's grip, something she picked on and so let go of it. Duke then embraced Lyuri with his now freed arm and pulled her close to him, something she had no objections to.
