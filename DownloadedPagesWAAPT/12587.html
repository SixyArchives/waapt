314675 Crewe19th Nov 2013 04:01:53 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
Li'l Punk

Phenac Center

-Crewe rolls her eyes-

Yeah, whatever. So, um, anyway, my life story story. Or at least some of it. I'm glad you got your freakout in earlier.

-she takes a deep breath-

So... I was born a Sneasel. Up north in Sinnoh. My parents died at some point very soon after I was born... maybe the same day even, I don't know. Anyway, I ended up alone out in the snow. There's probably no way I would've lived, but... Mew found me. She decided to... help me. And the way she decided to do that was make me human.

My parents—my human parents—were living in Twinleaf Town at the time, and trying to have a kid but couldn't. Mew made me into a human baby that looked like what their kid would be, and left me on their doorstep. My parents found me, and... they never told me. I thought they were my parents all my life.

-she leans backwards-

Anyway, time skip... lot of depressing shit happened, I ran away and went on my Pokemon journey, ended up in Unova and met up with the people that would eventually become the J-Team. Later, we went to Hoenn. Jab—Every's Weavile, he was a Sneasel then—and I were in Meteor Falls. We were both Sneasel at the time for...

-she coughs-

Er, reasons. Anyway, we were trying to find out where all the water went, because Team Magma had been screwing around. All the water disappearing had left some Goldeen stuck, and... a certain Mew decided to help them by turning them into Zubat. Jab and I came upon her, and Tagg's Xatu brought everyone else. They started yelling at her—I was hiding—and she got annoyed and recognized me and tried to use me as an example in her defense... And she told me everything.

-she goes quiet for a few seconds-

What happened after that... I don't like to think about it. It's... it's not something I'm proud of. But eventually Mew came along again. She apparently felt bad and gave me the ability to switch back and forth between Sneasel and human using moonlight. Later on, in Kanto, I was avoiding Crow during the full moon because it was gonna force me to transform and Crow didn't know at that point. We happened to be on Mt. Moon at the time—he, Straw, and I were chasing this glitchy Bulbasaur to stop it from screwing stuff up—and I found this.

-she holds up the Moon Stone pendant-

I figured out how to use it to transform eventually, so I turned it into a necklace so I'd always have it on me. At this point, without it I can't transform at all.

-she sighs-

In fact, that's pretty much why I got Snagged in the first place. That bitchy scientist lady that made me a Poison type in Holon stole my pendant so I was stuck as a Sneasel, so that other lady could Snag me. Luckily for me, that scientist also happened to work for Cipher...

-she shakes her head-

Anyway, yeah. That's the story.
