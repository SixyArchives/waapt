Li'l Punk
Effie is in front of Mauville's town hall, giving a speech. Argo and Discovery stand on either side of her, trying to look adorable and needy.

Suddenly, a soaked teen in a Magma uniform staggers through the crowd and points one shaking finger accusingly at Effie. He gives her a Death Glare from under his hood.

"YOU! YOU DID THIS! And I'm here to make you PAY!" He hurls two Pokeballs at the ground, releasing a Claydol and Torkoal.

Effie takes a step back, but Argo and Discovery step forward across from the two Magma Pokemon.

"I assure you, we had nothing to do with this unusual weather; perhaps it's Kyogre, supporting our cause!" Effie smiles at the crowd, who exchange glances. "But if you insist on settling this in such a barbaric fashion, I shall answer in kind. Now, if you please... You know what to do."

Argo nods once, then let's out a Water Gun aimed at the Torkoal, charging after the attack and using his tails to fire Mud-Slaps as he goes. As the water and mud from the first attacks clear, the Torkoal draws back her head to attack, slightly dazed, but is met with an angry Take Down from Argo. The Magma Pokemon is pushed back despite the not very effective damage, and th Marshtomp pants for a moment before repeating the process.

Discovery eyes the Claydol, and as it prepares to attack, fires a Powder Snow at his eyes. The Claydol shivers, his attack interrupted. The Sealeo takes the chance to scoot closer, then aims an Aurora Beam at the ground, freezing it. Sliding herself down it, she stretches out and fires rapid fire Powder Snows and Water Guns at the Claydol, aiming for the eyes.

Argo, noticing the ice, nods to Discovery. He fires a Water Gun just to the side of the Torkoal, forcing her to the side. Moving faster, he shoots rapid fire Water Guns and Mud-Slaps, just barely grazing the Torkoal's side, while remaining at a safe distance to dodge incoming attacks. As the Torkoal nears the Claydol, who is all but blinded by Discovery's attacks, the Sealeo freezes the ground below her with Aurora Beam. She slides backwards between Discovery and the Claydol, and the Sealeo quickly flips off the ice and out of the way. She and Argo keep the Torkoal on the ice by firing a Water Gun at her from opposite sides.

The Claydol, still not seeing clearly and believing Discovery to be in front of him, fires a powerful Hyper Beam—eliciting gasps from the surrounded crowd as they leap back. The Torkoal let's out a loud cry and, despite a type advantage, slumps to the ground.

Argo leaps up onto the Torkoal's slumped-over body and tackles the Claydol with Take Down. Pinning him and panting slightly, he fires a point-blank Water Gun into his face. The weakened Claydol retaliates with a Psybeam, but Argo doesn't let up and the combined attack of him and Discovery's Aurora Beam finally faint the Claydol.

The two Aqua Pokemon step back, Argo panting but triumphant. The Magma grunt recalls his fallen Pokemon and starts to charge Effie, who watches him calmly with her hands clasped behind her back, but the Marshtomp and Sealeo knock him on his butt with a combined Water Gun. The crowd advances on him and, scowling, he turns and scrambles away. Effie watches him go, then proudly scratches Argo's head fin and Discovery's whiskers.

"Well, that was unfortunate. But our young friend has demonstrated to us the obvious superiority or Water types..."

Effie continues her speech, unfazed. A few helpful bystanders heal Argo and Discovery, who stand proudly on either side of their Trainer.
