Riku, Jordan, Zap, Gex and Pyry are standing near the beach chatting.

"<So Lyuri is a Mawile now...>" said Riku, his expression being one of uncertainty.

"<Until you see those extra jaws though...>" said Pyry, shivering a bit even though it wasn't even that cold and he was an Ice type.

"<Yeah, Lyuri seems like the kind of girl that acts cute just to take you off-guard and then punch your face in.>" said Zap, snickering while having a rather satisfied expression

"<She's really small though. I don't think she can reach the face of many people.>" said Gex, snickering.

"<She's smaller than all of us, it's kinda funny, yeah.>" said Riku as he began to snicker himself.

"<You five seem to be having fun at Lyuri's expense.>"

All five sort of freeze for a moment as Duke approachs them with a smile on his face, seemingly not bothered by what he just heard.

"<Uh...>" said Riku, timidly rubbing the back of his head.

"<Say, Lyuri is a bit dispirited with the change, so maybe you all could hang around with her so she can have some fun?>" asked Duke, looking very calm and also rather expectant.

"<So we have to babysit our own trainer?>" asked Zap, quite amused with the idea.

"<Well, she babysitted us. I mean, she does take care of us, so I guess it's time for us to pay her back."> said Jordan, his eyes being rather distant and pensive.

"<Will she want to hang around with us though...?>" asked Pyry, looking more calm, if still a little nervous.

"<Why not? It's not like she has anything to lose.>" said Duke, then glancing at Riku "<Besides, I'm sure she'd like to spend more time with Riku and you all considering you've done so little together recently.>"

Riku suddenly gets a boost of confidence "<... Yeah! I'm sure we can cheer Lyuri up if we try, we just have to make sure to not overwork her!>"

"<So does that mean we should teach her how to use her Mawile powers first?>" asked Gex, a little lost.

"<We could always teach her how to fight. I mean, it's not like she's a total stranger to fights.>" said Jordan, with a shrug.

"<Yeah, she probably knows what Mawile can and can't do so I'm sure she'll learn quickly.>" said Zap, folding his arms with a smile of certainty.

"<Good to see you're all up for the task. I think she's still sleeping so why don't you five surprise her?>" suggested Duke, looking very pleased.

"<What about you?>" asked Pyry, though he soon gets rather hesitant "<I mean... Why don't you come with us?>"

"<I'm going to hone my skills a bit, Lyuri probably needs some help from someone who isn't me anyway. Don't let her know it was my idea, all right?>" asked Duke with a somewhat mischievous smile.

Everybody looks at each other, then back at Duke

"<Sure thing! We can take care of her while you do your stuff.>" said Riku.

Duke then pats Riku on the head.

"<Knew I could count with you for this, just make sure to keep control of everything, all right?>" said Duke, with a more playful smile.

"<You talk like we're going to push her off a cliff or something.>" said Zap, narrowing his eyes and with an upset expression.

"<Don't worry, I trust all of you. It's just that I know Riku here is going to make sure everything goes all right.>" Duke then pats Riku again

"<Y-Yeah...>" said Riku, looking rather doubtful

"<Anyway, I'll be off now. Good luck with Lyuri!>"

Duke then walks off and waves at the group, who waves back

"<... Well, this will be fun!>" said Gex, excitedly.

"<Come on, let's search for Lyuri. I mean... We totally forgot to ask where she is.>" said Jordan

"<Oh yeah!>" Zap then looked at where Duke went only to see that he was gone "<... No easy way out either.>"

Riku perks up a bit "<Well, we just have to look around. Come on!>"

Riku then starts leading the group somewhere else in the city in search of Lyuri.
