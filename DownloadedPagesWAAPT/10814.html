Chords of Steel

Joulant Center Centre

-Herbert is cautiously opening one eye, expecting to see the visage of Maul hovering by his bedside once again - only to jump back with equal surprise upon seeing Alice instead.-

Herbert: GAH! Dear Arceus, please don't do that again...

Alice: Good morning to you too. I apologise to wake you at such an early hour and in such an unnerving fashion, but I have to inform you that me and my team will be leaving shortly.

Herbert: What? You mean leaving Holon?

Alice: No. We will be staying in Holon. But to be perfectly blunt, staying in a backwater fishing village such as this one and merely cooing at the flashing lights in the sky is a waste of time when we are in such a rich and mostly unexplored region. I am heading north. One settlement in particular has piqued my curiosity.

Herbert: But... what about the group? What about sticking with the J-Team?

Alice: What about it?

Herbert: Well, back in Challenger's Cave you said you'd be travelling with the J-Team for a while!

Alice: I said I might be travelling with them for a while. I will confess, however, that outside of that Iridis fellow there have been few amongst their ranks that interest me. For the most part, I was staying with you, but I believe that you do not want to accompany me on this venture of mine.

Herbert: Of course not! This is... well, it's...

-He pauses for a moment, then sighs.-

Herbert: ...You'll keep in touch at least, won't you?

Alice: As much as I would like to offer you that assurance, the area in which I am venturing to is within the reach of some of Holon's most powerful magnetic storms and likely has little to no human population. Communication will be difficult if not impossible.

Herbert: In that case, then...

-He pulls her into a tight hug. Alice doesn't resist, and in fact returns the gesture.-

Herbert: Stay safe, okay? I don't want anything to happen to you...

Alice: Of course. The same goes for you as well, Lucius.

-The two pull away from each other.-

Alice: Well, I believe I should be departing now. Arsenic is getting restless. I shall see you again in around three months. Farewell, Lucius.

Herbert: Yeah... bye, Alice.

-Alice nods, and walks out of the Centre.-

Herbert: Well, that was unexpect-
