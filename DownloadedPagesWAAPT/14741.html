In the blink of an eye in Phantom's place there was only a little boy that was just a little taller than Aline. Wearing a teal shirt and pants, with the shoulders of the shirt having the same markings as he had as a Golurk and there was also the drawing of a yellow crack on the center of it. Phantom also had teal hair and golden eyes and had a largely inexpressive face.

Aline blinks for a while before signing "You look my age. So that big hulking Golurk is actually just a little kid like me?"
