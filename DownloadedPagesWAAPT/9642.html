Pi's Return: Finale

The Penthouse Suite

- music: http://youtu.be/CD-E-LDc384 -

Pi: Here we go, Hellfire!

- Moving lightly on his feet, Pi dodges Grunt B's knife. With an elegant sweep of his hand, he guides B's outstretched knife into Grunt A's abdomen, causing him to sink to the floor in pain. He turns just before Grunt C hits him, and parries his gladius with his katana. Turning back to B, Pi quickly closes in and clubs him with the hilt of his sword, knocking him unconscious. C closes in and manages to score a minor hit on Pi's arm, but is quickly pressed back by a flurry of strikes. Pressed into a corner, C has nowhere to run. Pi quickly knocks the gladius out of his hands, and then clubs him unconscious as well. -

- While Pi is busy with the Grunts, Hellfire deals with their Pokemon -

- As the Trubbish closes in on her, she counters its Double-Slap with a Double-Kick, followed by Incinerate. The Trubbish faints, but Hellfire has already moved on to the next Pokemon. She dodges the Sandile's Assurance by a hairsbreadth, and swings down on its head using Brick-Break. Having knocked out the Sandile, Hellfire focuses on the Zangoose. Unfortunately, she is unable to dodge the Crush-Claw, taking a significant hit. However, while the Zangoose is within close reach, she uses Brick-Break, quickly followed by Low-Sweep. The combination is too much for the Zangoose, and it faints as well. -

- Garret begins a slow, sarcastic clap -

Garret: Oh, nice. You took out the Grunts. You should feel so proud.

Pi: Well, you're next.

Garret: Much as I'd love to accept your offer, I am afraid I must decline. It's been a pleaser, "Sandman". Goodbye.

- Suddenly, the couch Garret is sitting on drops through a hatch in the floor, which promptly seals itself behind him -

Pi: DAMNIT!

- The earpiece squawks to life again -

The Pilot: You done?

Pi: Yeah, but the leader got away.

The Pilot: Damn. Oh well, you've managed to cripple the entire so-called "Team Thug". Thanks. I'm waiting right outside the building. Come down, I have a reward for you.

Pi: I'll be right there.

- As Pi and Hellfire turn to leave, they hear some whimpering coming from one of the crates in the corner. Prying it open, Pi discovers a young, injured, malnourished Deino -

Pi: Oh! You poor thing! Were they abusing you?

Deino: <Yu-Yu-Yes.>

Pi: Well, you're alright now. I made all the bad guys go away.

Deino: <Re-really?>

Hellfire: <Yeah! They'll never bother you again!>

Pi: Do you have a name?

Deino: <No.>

Pi: Would you like me to give you one?

Deino: <That would be... nice.>

Pi: Then I will name you... Lern.

Lern: <Thank you.>

Pi: Would you like to join my family, Lern?

Lern: <I-I-I'd be s-s-so happy.>

Pi: Then, here's a Promotion Ball that I've been saving. This'll just take a second.

- Pi lightly taps Lern's head with the Promotion Ball, and catches him -

Pi caught Lern!

- Pi and Hellfire exit the building, and meet The Pilot again -

The Pilot: You done good, kid. I could use someone like you. Here, take this.

- The Pilot gives Pi an International Police Consultant Badge -

The Pilot: This'll give you access to certain things that you might need. Keep the earpiece. I'll contact you again when I need you to do a job. You'll be rewarded for each one. And on that note, take this as well.

- The Pilot gives Pi a small sack full of Rare Candy -

The Pilot: Don't eat it all at once.

Pi: WOAH! That is a lot of Rare Candy. How much did this cost?

The Pilot: Not important. Its yours. See you around.

- With that, The Pilot melts into the crowd -

Pi: Well, Hellfire, looks like it's time we went and found Jack and the rest of the gang.

Hellfire: <Might as well.>
