And Now For Something Completely Different

PEFE Habitat Facility, Rainforest enclosure.

An Aipom is hanging upside-down on a tree, eating a berry of some sort, in the simulated rainforest enclosure of the PEFE Habitat Simulation Facility. The Aipom is happily enjoying it's berry when...

???: Forest Density: Above Average, Water Acidity: Low, Solar Radiation: Very Low...

The Aipom looks over to the young lady hanging on the branch next to him, taking down notes of the enclosure. He's rather confused at this act as normally, the researchers aren't this... high up when conducing their protocols.

PEFE!Fool: Oh? you don't mind if I have a bite of that Pecha Berry, do you?

The researcher was talking to him, and he could understand her. Curiouser and curiouser...

Aipom: <Uh sure...> -Offers the Pecha Berry- <Here you go... I guess.>

Fool takes the berry AND EATS IT and takes a bite out of it.

Fool: Fruit Taste: Delicious!

Fool smiles to herself. She liked working here, she met lots of people and Pokemon, got to manage some rather funky habitats and even got the opportunity to travel when new habitats or species were discovered, it really was a fantastic job. Though, if one were to look at her now, they may not even guess she was the ratty-looking forest girl◊ she was only a month ago.

???: Umm... Solana? Are you done yet?

Fool: Hmmm? Oh, yes I am Matthew! I'll be down in a tick.

Matthew◊, her assistant, was a rather quiet, nervous and shy person who often opted to stay in the background rather than be in the spotlight. What he lacked in charisma, he made up for in intelligence and focus and often kept his scatter-brained boss in check.

Fool: -Leaping down from the tree branch above- Hey Matt! How's it going?

Matthew: -Is slightly startled- OH! Um... I'm fine... -Looks around the enclosure- Is everything satisfactory with the Rainforest Enclosure?

Fool: Quite so...

Fool feels that there is something.... Missing from the picture at the moment.

Fool: Hey, where is your Beartic? And Lars for the matter?

Matthew: Oh.. Kuma? I think he's calming down your Druddigon again.

The two start walking out of the enclosure and down the vast hallway.

Fool: Again? I'm guess Lars tried to woo Femke again... and failed.

Matthew: It's strange that your Druddigon was interested in a mate outside of his own specie. You don't see many Druddigons actively pursuing Nidorina's in the wild.

Fool: Well, Lars isn't totally pureblooded himself. Maybe that has something to do with it... Mmm, it's a good thing he has found a good friend in your Beartic Matt.

Matthew: -Smiling- Indeed

The two continue to talk about this and that as they head down the hall.

Seaside Bluff

Melody (+Ulysses), Gillete and Albert are sitting by the seaside, watching the waves roll in and crash against the rocks below.

Gillete: <Wow, it sure has been peaceful for the last month or so. The number of outlaws we've had to deal with has dwindled drastically.>

Albert: <Yeah, but I preferred beatin' up baddies over running un-heroic errands daily...>

Ulysses: :3

Melody: <Oh the young and energetic! With their head stuffed with wool!>

Gil & Al: <Oi!>

Melody: <You should be thankful that we have had a good run so far! We're all quite wealthy and the energy radiating off you two is rather noticable!>

Gil & Al: <Really? :D>

Melody: <Gil moreso~>

Gillete: <YEAH! AREN'T I JUST AWESOME!>

Albert: <Aww....>

Aegis Ruins

Acheron floats by the ruin walls and drifts his ghostly appemdage across the ancient runes in-scripted there. He bubbles out a ghostly sigh when a blue Pawniard approaches him.

Athena: <Hey Acheron...>

Acheron: <Hi...>

The Pawinard sits beside the Frillish, fussing with her claws.

Athena: <I just wanted to say... Sorry for being a huge bitch to you. I just went over my head with all this "Need to find those dragon orbs" quest that hasn't lead either of us anywhere... So...>

She shuffles a bit and kicks a pebble.

Athena: <Can you forgive me?>

Acheron couldn't believe his ears, the girl who got him into this mess in the first place and treated him like dirt the entire time, is apologizing for it? This was too good to be true! He wanted to ask someone to pinch him, but he was afraid that, in this world, it would be super effective.

Acheron: <... I forgive you...>

Athena: <Thank you!>

Athena leaps into his "arms" and hugs him. Earning a frusih blush for the both of them. She quickly let's go and starts muttering to herself, the blush not leaving her face.

Joe: -In the background- <I knew it! I knew it! I knew it~!>
