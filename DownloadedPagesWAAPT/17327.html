-Ray is walking along the route to head to Petalburg while Peppy flies circles around his head-

Marcus: -In front of a wild Zigzagoon- "Alright then, let's show this thing what we've got! Trifka! Howl!"

Zizagoon: <GAH!> -Backs up a bit before Growling-

Marcus: "Hah! Trifka, Tackle!"

Trifka: <Okay!> -Tackles the Zigzagoon-

-Peppy notices Marcus and Trifka-

Peppy: <Whee! We should have a battle, you should battle them!>

Ray: "Oh... Huh... Looks like Marcus got a Pokemon too."

Peppy: <Come on, challenge them!>

-Peppy starts trying to push Ray towards Marcus and Trifka-

Ray: "Whoa! Uh... If you want me to challenge him, I'll ask him when he's done with that Zigzagoon..."

Zigzagoon: <Ow!> -Growls again-

Marcus: "Hah! I don't get why these silly wild Pokémon like using moves like Growl! It's wasting valuable attacking time! Tackle!"

Trifka: -Tackles the Zigzagoon again-

Zigzagoon: <Ooooooww! Okay just remember you made me do this! I've been beaten up by five trainers and an angry Lotad today! I just want to be left alone!> -Tackles Trifka, doing relatively little damage to her-

Marcus: "Tackle!"

Trifka: <Got it!> -Tackles the Zigzagoon again, and it faints-

-Chases her tail for a few moments before jumping up into Marcus' arms and licks his face-

Marcus: "Yay! Great job Trifka! Nothing can stop us when we work together!"

-Seeing that they finished the battle and Peppy is continuing to prod him, Ray heads over-

Ray: "Uh... Nice Battling, Marcus."

Marcus: -Puts down Trifka and turns to Ray- "Oh hey Ray! Thanks, I think I'm doing pretty well, well Trifka is. That was our thriteenth Zigzagoon! And ooooh a Wingull! I didn't know you had one!"

Ray: "Yeah... Mom lent me one of her Pokemon so I could catch my own and... I caught him."

-Peppy is flying circles around Ray's head again, talking about battling-

Ray: "He's... kinda excitable."

Marcus: "Huh. You know that sounds easier than what I did. I just sat out here until Trifka came along and fell asleep next to me! I caught her while she was asleep! Well that's great! Excitable is great. HEY! You know what would be a good idea? A battle! We're both trainers now! Right?"

Peppy: <Yay! Battle! Finally!>

Ray: "Uh, yeah, sure. I think Peppy wanted one, so yeah... I'd be up for that."

Marcus: "Great! First step to being a Fronteir Brain, beating you!"

Ray: "Alright..."

"Peppy, use Water Gun!"

Peppy: <Aw, I can use a better move than that, but okay.>

-He shoots a blast of water at Trifka-

Trifka: -Is hit- <Ow!>

Marcus: "Trifka! Howl!"

Trifka: <BARK.>

Ray: "Uh... Raising Attack is bad. Use Growl!"

Peppy: <Oh, you want me to do that, then, okay.>

-Peppy makes a cute sound to lower Trifka's attack-

Trifka: -Her attack is lowed via cuteness-

Marcus: "Trifka, Tackle!"

Trifka: <Sure thing, come 'ere you little-> -Tries tackling Peppy-

-Peppy is hit by the Tackle, taking decent damage-

Peppy: <Hrgh, you're good! I should do better!>

Ray: "Peppy, u-"

-Peppy sends out a blast of salty water at Trifka-

Ray: "...That works."

Trifka: -Is hit by the Brine and staggers, now almost fainted and whimpers-

Marcus: "Don't give up! Tackle again!"

Trifka: -Rushes at Peppy and Tackles the Wingull again-

-Peppy is struck by the Tackle and drops to low health as well-

Peppy: <I may be at low health, but this is going great!>

Ray: "Uh... Water Gun again!"

-Peppy sends another blast of non-salty water at Trifka-

Trifka: -Dodges the blast of water and charges at Peppy-

-Ray recalls him-

"Thanks, Marcus."
