Forgetting about Stratus for the time being, Deludge pulls herself off the Dragonite and swims over to Lake, who seemed a little shocked himself. Partly because he had actually found his mother, and partly because she seemed ever bigger than he remembered.

Deluge: <...I can't believe it. Is that...is that really you, Lake?>

She blinked in shock as she gazed at him.

<I mean, the last time I saw you, I could still carry you in my jaws. But now...you're all grown up...>

Giving her a smile, Lake swam a bit closer to the the female Feraligatr.

Lake: <I can't believe it. It's been eleven years, and yet...you still remember me?>

She nods, returning his smile with one of her own.

Deludge: <Of course I remember you! I could never forget about my own son...no matter how long he may have been gone for...oh, Lake...>

She stretches her arms and embraces Lake, hugging him close.

<I'm so happy to have you back...>

Even though she was squeezing him just a little too hard, Lake happily returned the hug.

Lake: <I missed you so much, Mom...>

Stratus, meanwhile, couldn't help but smile warmly at the touching display. But...there was one nagging thought that was bothering him. If Deluge had apparently only...engaged in courtship once, then...

<But wait...can I ask you something?>

Deluge blinked, glancing down at Lake.

Deluge: <What is it?>

He looked back up, giving her a curious look.

Lake: <Why were you arguing with my friend Stratus?>

Deluge: <...Friend?>

With a nod, Lake pulled gestured at the Dragonite, who was now looking very awkward for some reason.

Lake: <Yeah. Stratus here is my friend. We met last summer and we've been pretty much inseparable since then. I...brought him here because I was a bit frightened to...you know...meet my family for the first time in years...>

Deluge shifts her gaze to the Dragonite in question, cocking her head at him.

Deluge: <...You never figured it out, did you?>

Lake: <Huh?>

Stratus swims back a short distance, not wanting to be in grabbing range again.

Stratus: <...Uh...>

Deluge: <You did mention me to him, right Lake?>

He nods.

Lake: <Once or twice...>

Deluge glances back at Stratus.

Deluge: <And you never thought he meant...?>

Stratus: <Whoa! I'm sure that there are lots of female Feraligatr named Deluge out there in the world. Who...also have a habit of eating anything that moves...>

Deluge: <...So you both don't know.>

Lake gazes up at his mother, frowning.

Lake: <...Know what? What are you talking about?>

Deludge: <That the pair of you...>

She pauses, steeling herself for what she was about to say.

<...Are father...and son.>

Stratus: <...>

Lake: <...WHAT?!>
