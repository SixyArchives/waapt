I was 8 when I first convinced a family that I was their child for a few nights, just so I could have a hot meal and a bath for once. ...That hurt when the illusion wore off. At 9, I could read a person's mind better than they could read a book. In a conversation, I could have their wifi password, their bank account, and every questionable website they frequented.

I was 15 when I graduated from Nimbasa U. The entire criminal underground was present for my graduation, though of course, they were all hidden by my mons' power. It included almost 1000 people at the time. My diploma was quickly taken from me when they realized I was Psychic. Several professors turned up a few weeks later, completely psychotic, babbling formulas that only the strongest minds on the planet could truly understand. I did not receive my diploma back. So the west wing burned down a few weeks later. I got it in the mail the next day.

I was 16 when I met the J-Team. I had spent the last year meditating in the forest, trying to avoid being killed by everyone who realized they were being manipulated by me. At one point, I implanted the thought in everyone's head that I had been killed by my own people.

At 16 I met the first person in the world who actually understood me, and he was a Noctowl at the time.

The point I wish to make here. I am not clairvoyant. I didn't need to be though to realize that Lucius was going down the same path I was. If I had thought for just a second, he would still be here. And I wonder what that says about me.~
