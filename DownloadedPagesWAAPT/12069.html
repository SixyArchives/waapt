Haxorus: -watches the Dragon Tail, bored, before Dual Chopping the Spiritomb-

Duolis: Oh no...

Gengar: -goes flying, but misses the Spiritomb and Shadow Balls again-

Spiritomb: -tanks the Dual Chop before replying with its own boosted Shadow Ball. -

Dragonite: -is hit by the Gengar's Shadow Ball- <Oww...> -faints-

Haxorus: -grunts from the boosted Shadow Ball- <Grr...>

-The Haxorus tries another Dual Chop, while Famire aims her own Dragon Tail at the Gengar-

-The Spiritomb tanks the hit again and goes for another Dark Pulse; the Gengar hops over the Dragon Tail and Shadow Balls Famire-

-Famire is struck by the Shadow Ball, and collapses-
