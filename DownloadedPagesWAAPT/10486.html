Des stands atop the mountain, looking across the region over to the League where all his friends are to.

Damn I've missed a lot. I can't just show up out of the blue after all that Deimos BS that went down...

Sparkers tilts his head.

<Say, weren't we in Holon? Or was it Hoenn?>

Des doesn't answer. He rolls up the sleeve of his coat to reveal the Fourth Wall Manipulator. A device of unknow origin that could teleport him to pretty much anywhere in the world. Problem was that it seemed to work on it's own accord. Des didn't really know where it came from only that it was suddenly there on his arm. By the looks of things it wasn't doing said arm any favours. The area around the watch-like device was greyed. His veins were popping out and what appeared to wiring was making it's way up his arm.

You. You're the reason why I haven't been around. You're the reason why I've been popping up in random locales. You're the reason I've been havin' these blackouts.

He starts to bash the device against the rock face, hoping in vain to damage it in someway. It's safe to say that he was doing more damaged to himself.

<Des stop! We've tried remember? It won't come off>

Des slumps to his knees giving the Manipulator an intense glare. Nary scratch was on it.

"Just what the hell is this thing..."
