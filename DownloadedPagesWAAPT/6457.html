Spinda's Café - PMD-A

Comet: <You'd think a Typhlosion with a baby Treecko and Mudkip would be easy to find, but no...>

Comet stops to down the last few drops of a rather thick Ale from her mug. After loosing a firey burp, she waves at the Spinda bartender to attract his attention.

<Can I get a refill over here?>

The Spinda gives her a rather worried look.

Spinda: <~But that's your fourth mug, miss! Any more might leave you dangerously intoxicated!~>

The Charizard merely chuckles in response.

Comet: <What are you worried about! I'm not fine, I'm perfectly drunk! Wait...>

Comet blinks, as does Treeo, who looks up from his Oran juice to give her a concerned glance.

Treeo: <Maybe you should quit while you're ahead.>

The Charizard frowns at her companion, grumbling in annoyance. Quit now? She was only just getting started!

Comet: <Fine...>

That said, there was an old saying amongst her kind: A drunk Charizard was a Charizard that burned half your village down. So morning's after were always...awkward.

But at the same time, it summed up the Charizard line pretty well: The never did anything by halves.

<You know what, though? This place isn't half bad. I could used to spending time here.>

Treeo: <I'll admit, it does have a certain charm to it. Although...>

He shifts his gaze to the door of the bathroom, where the sound of some unfortunate Pokémon vomiting rather loudly could be heard.

<...I don't think I'll be bringing Nazz here any time soon.>

Comet: <Maybe when she's older?>

Treeo: <Even then, I'm not so sure...>
