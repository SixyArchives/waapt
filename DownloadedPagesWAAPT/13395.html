Prof. Sycamore: Well, barring anymore unforeseen encounters with undocumented Legendaries, I believe we have all of the research needed for your studies in the Kanjoh regions.

Rex: That's great! Thank you, professor!

Prof. Sycamore: No, I feel I should thank you for being so patient with me for all this time. You've had to wait for quite a bit for this upgrade and I feel that I should apologize for it.

Producing a containment case from a nearby shelf, Sycamore placed the device atop his desk before pressing a button on the side, revealing three Pokéballs containing the Kanto starters.

Rex: You're offering me a starter?

Prof. Sycamore: I thought that it might help in regards to your studies. After all, I'm sure that as a lover of evolution based research, Elm would love to understand the process of Mega Evolution.

Rex: Mega Evolution?

Even Rascal, who had contented himself to laying atop a nearby chair and waiting patiently perked his ears up at the new term.

Prof. Sycamore: Yes, it's a rather interesting new field of study. Some Pokémon have the capacity to evolve further beyond their normal limitations for a short period of time, although this process involves the usage of Mega Stones in conjunction with a Trainer's own Key Stone.

He placed four stones atop the desk, one next to the balls of Bulbasaur and Squirtle, and two next to Charmander's.

Prof. Sycamore: Current research has discovered that Venusaur, Charizard, and Blastoise are capable of becoming Mega Evolved, with Charizard having two forms that it can swap between. While I can't exactly offer you a Key Stone, I'm certain that if you find one on your travels, Mega Evolved Pokémon would be a great boon to your team.

Rex glanced at the stones, then the three Pokéballs on the table before turning his gaze back to Sycamore.

Rex: Well... while I appreciate the offer, I think it'd be best if I didn- awww, who's a cute little Bulbasaur?

Rex: I-I mean... as much as I would like to, I'd rather just study the indigenous Pokémon. Perhaps along the way I'll find some Mega Pokémon to observe, but I'd like to stick to just one focus for now.
