The most unique type

Alistar City

Aline is currently walking around with Scout. She gives him a command with her own version of morse code and he then proceeds to sniff the ground in search of a scent, which he starts tracking with Aline following right behind him. Once they got to some nearby tree, Aline didn't pay attention to a ring of rope on the ground that she stepped into, which then proceeded to wrap itself on her ankle and pull her up and leaving her hanging there while Scout just stared. Divoom obviously fell from her head, but landed on her hat at least.

"<What just happened here?!>" asked Divoom, looking up and seeing Aline's state "<... Oh.>"

Strider then comes out from a bush next to the tree, holding some rope. Teishi then comes out of his hiding among the tree leaves soon followed by Sub-Zero popping out of the ground and Ryu materializing from the tree's shadow.

"<Guess we got you again.>" said Ryu.

"<Good job acting as bait, Ryu! Nice plan, Strider!>" said Teishi, excitedly.

Aline just folds her arms in annoyance, Teishi then gets off the tree and gets under her with his arms extended. Soon a Water Shuriken cuts through the rope holding Aline and she falls on Teishi's arms, who quickly puts her back on the ground. Shadow then reveals himself to be hiding on the roof of a nearby house.

"<You guys really take the ninja thing seriously.>" said Divoom

"<Hey, it's our training! Sort of.>" said Teishi, chuckling

"<I thought we were playing.>" said Scout, tilting his head.

Aline starts snickering "<'I never thought I'd play with ninjas someday, you guys are awesome!'>" Divoom said in her stead.

"<Thanks! You're an awesome girl too!>" said Sub-Zero

"<I wasn't expecting that our training would involve capturing a little girl over and over with different traps, but if anything we know we can fool a dog Pokémon's nose into doing what we want.>" commented Ryu.

"<Maybe you should try this with a willing adult or teenager next time, serial escalation and all that.>" suggested Shadow.

"<That's a plan!>" said Teishi.

"<'Hey! I'm not done yet! I swear you guys won't catch me next time!'>" said Divoom, pointing at Aline with a wing.

"<But we don't have any ideas left, or time to do it. It's getting kinda late.>" said Teishi.

Aline sighs in disappointment, but she soon perks up and gives Teishi a hug. Fortunately she was just small enough that she wouldn't touch his tongue in the process.

"<'All right then, tell me when you want to play next time! I had a lot of fun!'>" said Divoom, once again, for Aline.

Teishi hugs back "<Will do!>"

Aline then breaks up the hug and picks her hat, which Divoom soon hid under again. She then waves goodbye as she runs off with Scout, everyone in the Squad waves back at her as she does so.

"<Senpai is really good with kids!>" announced Sub-Zero, impressed.

"<Well, pretty much all of you are kids yourselves. Or rather, I guess you're teenagers while she's still a little child.>" said Shadow.

"<That doesn't really matter.>" said Ryu, with a shrug.

Teishi gets a bit sheepish "<Heheh... Well guys, let's regroup back with Sakura. We probably should go eat something.>"

"<Sure thing, senpai!>" said all the others, Shadow giving a mock salute in the process.

They then start heading to the Pokémon Center.
