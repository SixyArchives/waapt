Slightly less worrying, but still a potential threat, was the Gallade and the Ariados, the Gallade being slightly more dangerous, as due to her Delta mutation, Holly was part Poison rather than Ground like the vast majority of her species. So if he hit her with a Psychic attack, it would hurt. Badly.

The thing was, though, that it seemed unlikely that the Gallade and the Ariados would be able to fight her in their weakened condition.

Even if she did manage to beat them, though, the noise from the scuffle would likely alert the other Pokémon on the boat to her presence.
