Psyche's dream

Psyche is wandering around Route 13. She sees a lone Slowking staring at the sea.

~Beautiful today, isn't it?~

The Slowking nods lightly. "Always is..."

Psyche nods too. ~Penny for your thoughts.~

"Oh, I was merely pondering life, the universe, everything, nothing, cake, death, water, air, cake, shells, stones, rocks, cake, and cake."

~Cake seems to be a common thought.~

'I like Cake.'

~Indubitably.~

"Oh? Now that's a fancy word for one so young. Where did you learn it?"

~From my Natu here. Say hi, Totem.~

Totem pops out of his pokeball, pecks the Slowking, and returns.

~Or... do that... Sorry. So, what's your name?~

"I have many names. He who listens to the waves, The one with the crown of shell. Some call me Tim. But my given name is Horace."

~Nice to meetcha Horace! I go by Psyche.~

"You GO by Psyche? I sense there's a story behind that name... You don't use your real name?"

~No... And I don't really want to talk about it...~ She sits on the sand.

"That's fine. Question. Why do you thoughtspeak?"

~Long and painful story.~

"Related to your name?"

~Kinda.~

"Then I won't press further."

~Thank you...~

"Do you like Gothorita?" He asks, motioning to her current state of dress.

~Yeah. They're my favorite mons. And I lived with the Gothitelle Tribe in Lostlorn Forest.~

"I know of them. Is it interesting?"

~Not really... I want to learn more than divination. The future is a complex, scary thing for a human. We don't live nearly as long as many mons do, especially the Gothitelle. I think they've lived for about 1000 years apiece...~

"I see... But do you like the society itself?"

~What do you mean?~

"The mons. Interactions. Do you find that the Gothorita are as varied in personalities as Humans are?"

~Oh, indubitably.~

"Do you enjoy the mons?"

~Well, I guess. No more than I would people, I'd think. But mons have less of an aptitude for brutality than humans do...~

"Do you like that?"

~Yes.~

"Then go back to them. Have fun. Enjoy the company of your friends while you can."

~You... you're absolutely right. I'm going back to the forest. I've been from one end of Unova to the other, and now it's time to return home. Thank you.~

"You're very welcome, Miss Psyche."

~Hey, would you like to join me?~

"You don't seem like the Pokemon trainer type."

~I'm not really. I see my mons as friends and siblings more than anything else. Even as a team.~

"That's very wise of you."

She blushes. ~Why thank you... But I'm rather foolish.~

He smiles. "Only a fool would proclaim the opposite. I would be honored to accompany you to the forest."

Psyche holds out a Pokeball.

3...2...1... Click!
Horace has been caught!
