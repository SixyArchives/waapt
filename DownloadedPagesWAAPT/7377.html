3: I'm sure you remember what I said way back when I first recruited you. At the first sign of disloyalty...

He aims his gun at her.

3: ...The last thing you'll live to see... is your sister dying.

AU!Eskay 1: ...No...

Two shots are heard.

One ended up in AU!Alli's left shoulder.

The other in 3's right hand. The hand he shot with.

AU!Eskay A walks out of the shadows.

AU!Eskay A: You really shouldn't leave these things lying around.

3: ...What...

AU!Eskay A: He wasn't lying. We didn't run away. We were forced away. And not by the cops, either... You just shot our sister. Despite your promises.

He aims again.

AU!Eskay A: ...Disloyalty.

One final shot is heard. AU!Eskay A drops the gun and goes over to AU!Alli.

AU!Alli: ...How...

AU!Eskay A: I'll explain later. First, we need to get you to a hospital. And I doubt the one here in Goldenrod will be willing to accept a criminal's sister.

He cuts the ropes binding her hands, pulls out a shard, and taps it.

Disheveled: ...What just-

AU!Eskay 1: I'm going home now. I've been here for one year too long. I hope I never see any of your faces again.
