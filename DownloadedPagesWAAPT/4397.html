<Crewe. What was the last you saw of Masque, Robin, Rampage, Qui-Gon, Izzie, or Damian?>

Crewe frowns and tries to think.

"I, um... I saw Damian sparring with Electiore when I realized Dana and Nova were missing, and Robin was with me when Steven showed up..." She fixes Ian with a sharp look. "Why? What's going on? Ian?! Is something wrong?!"

Ian blinks at her slowly, then flicks his tail. Electivire steps out of the trees, holding six Pokeballs.

<They left these behind, > he says softly. <There's no doubt of what they wanted.>

Crewe bites her lip, blinking quickly. Electivire sets the Pokeballs down and steps forward, slowly extending her arms.
