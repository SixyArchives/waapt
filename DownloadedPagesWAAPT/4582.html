Silent is sprawled on the bed, as is Dintel. Only difference is, Dintel is soundly asleep.

"..."

Algira is half-sleeping perched on the lamp, flashing patterns that if Silent could read he'd notice they say <WHAT DO WE WANT? HELIOS HOENN! - WHEN DO WE WANT IT? NOW!!!>. Maekrite is sleeping at the side of the bed.

"..."

After trying and failing to get asleep, Silent decides to go and take a walk. Maekrite notices and after a long yawn, goes follow him around.

"..."

<...?>

Silent goes to sit on a couch and Maekrite leaps up to him.

Maekrite — <Silent? Why can't *yawn* you sleep?>

"I don't know Maekrite... I am kinda angry, but I don't know at who... perhaps I'm angry with myself..."

Maekrite stares for a moment then nuzzles his Trainer.

"I think Rex is angry with me, too. Like I have not been welcoming enough to him. And for Forte it might be worse..." Scratches the Nidorino's ears. "Rex says he is not angry that I left him behind, but that's happened like, three times already."

Maekrite — <...But you didn't go away. We are still together in the island, right?>

"Yeah, but... that's not the issue." Frowns. "When I read that the rest of the group went to Hoenn, I felt abandoned by them. Not to mention we always were late on the news for everything that the others were doing... we only attended the Halloween Party thanks to time trickery, and when it reaches that level it's hard not to feel like the others are living too fast."

Silent thinks for a moment, then sighs.

"Then I remember that we have been leaving Rex by the sidelines as well and I feel like a hypocrite. He's a newcomer, as was I a couple of months ago; one would think that I would be far more receptive, considering the weird kind of stuff he has been trying to put up with..." Looks at Maekrite. "Sometimes I truly feel like I should just ditch the group and go back home, offscreen."

Maekrite stares back at Silent, narrows his eyes at him and after a moment suddenly bites him.

"Ouch!" Silent yells; he shoves Maekrite. "What was that for?"

Maekrite — <Brooding. We have a battle tomorrow, and if you are going to stay overnight brooding, then we are going to lose.>

"..."

<And you are exaggerating with the whole leaving Rex behind stuff. You talk to him and message on the phone a lot.> Maekrite looks around. <You have been there for when he's needed stuff, like the whole armband thing.>

"But I feel like what I do amounts to nothing, because when I get there the others have already solved the problem with a wave of their hands... like with the whole looking for Sarabi Star's traOUCH!!!" Silent looks down, Maekrite has bitten him again.

Maekrite — <...You better go sleep, now.>

"Yeah... but I can't get these things off my head... what do I do?"

<...Cuddle Shahinne? Copahue says it works wonders.>

"Oh..." chuckles. "I'd have to try... don't want to wake her up."

Silent and Maekrite walk back to their room to sleep.
