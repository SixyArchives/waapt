Anyway.

-The screen blips to a familiar picture of Eclo Canyon.-

Dune: You guys are still digging around there?

Prof. Mesquite: Yep. We found something this time, though.

-She hits a button on her remote, and the image shifts to an excavated cave.-

Prof. Mesquite: Sonic location devices, as well as the movement of the burgeoning Diglett population in the area, shows that there's something here. We found it.

-Again, she presses a button. The image shifts to a wall of stone not like it's surroundings, and definitely hand-carved.-

Prof. Mesquite: But we don't know what it is.

Dune: Hm... I found something like that back in the Holon Ruins. It was an old burial chamber for a group called "the Dustrunners", royal guards of Sabaku apparently. Wait... Can you zoom in on that writing?

-The professor hits a different button, and the image is zoomed.-

Dune: That looks very similar to the writing at the entrance to the burial chamber. It might be a cipher just like the other one was.

Prof. Mesquite: That would certainly explain why it's just gibberish as is.

Dune: When can I go see it?

Prof. Mesquite: The next transport there isn't until this evening. Go ahead, make yourself comfortable. We've received quite a bit of extra funding since you were here last.
