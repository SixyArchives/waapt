267770 luke92422nd Jun 2013 04:40:22 PM Get RP Mod

A whole bunch of introductions later...

Nacre: <And that's everymon that lives in the moat!>

Relieved that the several hour meet and greet was finally over, Stratus was now finally able to tell this Nacre just exactly what was going on when.

<Whoa, look at the sun! It's time for dinner!>

Stratus stopped, realizing that the moment of truth could wait just a teensy bit longer.

Stratus: <Well, what are we waiting for?>

Dragonspiral Tower Moat - Dragonite Caves

Rather than bunking in the tower with the rest of the Dragonspiral Pokémon, the moat Dragonite bunked in a rather large series of caves underneath the water instead. It was here that Stratus and Nacre arrived just as dinner was being served in the dining chamber.

A whole bunch of Dragonair and Dragonite were gathered around a long rock in the middle of the room, helping themselves to various fish Pokémon that had been caught earlier, amoungst other things.

But as Stratus went to grab something to eat, he couldn't help but be a bit concerned by a few factors. Something that Nacre took notice off as she grabbed a Basculin to chow down on.

Nacre: <What's the problem?>

She paused to take a large chunk out of the fish's hide as Stratus frowned.

Stratus: <This is what you call a meal? Seriously?>

Swallowing her mouthful, she gave him a confused look.

Nacre: <What do you mean? This is a pretty good haul, if you ask me.>

Stratus: <You've got to be joking! There's not enough here to feed one Dragonite, let alone however many of you there are?>

Some of the other dragons paused, wondering why this Dragonite nobody seemed to recognize was making such outrageous claims.

<"Who's that Pokémon?">

<"Never seen him before in my life. Pudgy thing, isn't he?">

<"Heh. Make's you wonder if he's been snacking on the Vanillish in the forest.">

Stratus: <And not only is there hardly anything here, but you've got barely anything to choose from! All I see is Red Basculin, and...and...BLUE Basculin! It's an outrage! And I don't mean the move, either!>

Nacre was starting to get ever so slightly embarrassed, that the new guy was causing a scene. At their first dinner together.

...With other peoplemons, of course. Why did she think of it that way? Weird.

Nacre: <Uh, you might want to give it a rest, pal...>

Stratus: <NO WAY! Not when food is at stake!>

Slamming his paws down on the rocky table, Stratus scanned the crowd.

<OKAY! Who's in charge here?>

<"That would be me.">

Everyone glanced down to the far end of the table to find the source of the voice, which was a HUGE, - at least a foot taller than Stratus at least - Anicent looking Dragonite. His dull scales and ragged wings showing his long history of defending the tower for all to see.

Naturally, Stratus was a little intimidated.

Stratus: <EEP! Who's that!>

Nacre: <That's Alto! He's been in charge of all of us for a really long time!>

Stratus: <And how long is long?>

Nacre: <Well just a few weeks ago, we celebrated his two-hundredth and twenty-seventh birthday...>

Stratus: <...Oh.>

Stratus was beginning to regret making a scene in front of a Dragonite who had been around since his great-grandparents were only Dratini. He couldn't help but shake in fright.

Alto: <I don't know who you are, drake, but my clan has been surviving and thriving on the Basculin that live in our waters since I was but a whelp.>

Unimpressed, he crossed his arms as he gazed over at Stratus.

<Yet now all of a sudden, this is unacceptable to you?>

...Yes. Yes it was unacceptable. Even though he was intimidated, Stratus love for food was enough to give him courage to overcome the fear of this old dragon, so he could stand up for what he felt was right.

Stratus: <Of course it's unacceptable! Not only are Basculin too chewy and tasteless...>

The Dragonite picked up one of the Basculin from the table, shaking it to put emphasis on his point.

<They're barely even warm! And this...>

He threw the Basculin to Nacre, who easily caught it (With her mouth) - as he picked up an old goblet filled with liquid.

<This chardonnay is supposed to be served slightly chilled! But this! THIS IS ROOM TEMPERATURE!>

He threw the drink on the floor, spilling liquid onto the rock as the gathered Dragonair and Dragonite went into uproar. Until...

<RAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAWR!!!!!>

Alto used Roar!

Everybody shut up, sitting back down quietly as Stratus and Nacre just stood there, shocked and shaken.

Alto: <Listen here, you disrespectful drake. We spend most of our times serving the Gods in the tower above, so we only have so long to hunt enough food for all of us. We barely even have time to cook any of it, so you'll forgive us if it's not exactly up to whatever high standards you're used to! It's kept us going for long enough, so I don't see any problem here!>

Sitting back down, Alto leaned back in his rather ornate chair.

<I don't know. If you know a way of getting warm food quickly and easily, please - feel free to share it with us!>

Stratus responded with a confident look.

Stratus: <It would be my pleasure...>
