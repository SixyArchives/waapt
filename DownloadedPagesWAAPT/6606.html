What Was Once Lacunosa - PMD-B

-A settlement composed of a fairly well-preserved partial wall, albeit with sections that have been rather obviously patched by materials such as wood and stone forming a semi-enclosed space to the north, with a mix of occasional buildings and remains of human buildings located on and behind it. Strangely, the central area of this settlement appears to be meticulously planned and laid out. The hodgepodge associated with Mill and Opal Towns to the west only visible in the outskirts where younger and less sturdy human structures have all but melted away due to the ravages of time.

Curiously, the denizens of this place appear to keep to a most peculiar rhythm of life, the town as a whole seemingly winding down together in unison for the night. Were a resident of this place back in the era of humans present, he or she would likely recognize these rhythms as being not terribly far removed from their own

It is in the midst of this setting, that a certain Fraxure lurks, catching the occasional glance of suspicion, contempt, and some occasions, fear, from its residents-
