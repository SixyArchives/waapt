AU — Ecruteak

-the vast majority of the Ecruteakan population has gathered in the Bell Tower and reunited with friends, family, and Pokemon. Ranger has formed a sort of council, including a local wild Pidgeot that has a flock currently residing in the tower, a Zangoose in the team of an out-of-towner, as well a few local mons, including a Gastly belonging to a former Gym Trainer, a Charmeleon, and a Geodude-

Drake the Zangoose: <Now, the first thing we gotta do is get all these mons to band together. They gotta rally around something, y'know? Distract 'em from their troubles, give 'em something to look towards.>

Kimmy the Geodude: <Yeah, okay, but what? A mon, a person...?>

Nocturne the Gastly: <Morty?>

Ace the Pidgeot: -taps his claws on the floor- <Morty's the Gymmy, right? He was a human. That's outdated. The humans are over, looks like. You gotta move forward, not look back.>

Smokey the Charmeleon: <Who, then? Ranger's a good mon, no one doubts that, but he's not really the hero type.>

Ranger: <Acknowledge, and agreed. Go on, Drake, you're a clever mon.>

Drake: <Well, I don't know much 'bout the mons here. My team, they look up to me, 'cause I've proven myself to 'em, but these locals don't know me. D'they know Ace, here?>

Ace: <Only the local wilds and maybe some of the mons. Not the people, though, and they're the ones that need looking up, right?>

Kimmy: <... So, what do we do?>

Jerry the Growlithe: <The Officer Jenny and I are willing to step up!>

Drake: <The police? Nah, they need a hero, not official authority, see?>

Jerry: <... The police are heroes...>

Ranger: <Hush, hush! Drake, what do you suggest?>

Drake: <Well, what d'you think 'bout a phrase?>

Ace: <... A phrase?>

Drake: <A slogan, see? Somethin' that'll stick with 'em, to draw 'em together and give 'em some sort of identity.>

Ace: <... I see. But what?>

Nocturne: <I urge you to remember, this slogan will become the words of the group here, and everyone must be able to identify with it!>

Ace: <Howsabout "best of the best"?>

Kimmy: <... My Trainer doesn't like fighting, that sounds like a fighting slogan...>

Smokey: <"Blazing against the Dragons"!>

Jerry: <Oh, I like that one!>

Kimmy: <That's no better. >_<>

Drake: <No, no, 's clear you lot won't come up with somethin' good—>

Jerry: <Hey!>

Drake: <—So here's an idea. We make somethin' up.>

Smokey: <... Isn't that what we were just doing—>

Drake: <No, no, I mean make up some words. String some nonsense together. Make it sound like a code or so. Let 'em all give it their own meaning. It don't have one, so it has all of 'em, see?>

Ace: <... You are a clever bugger.>

Smokey: <I think it's dumb.>

Jerry: <Well, I guess...>

[[cinammon:Kimmy:]] <I like it!>

Ranger: <All righty then, we'll use Drake's idea! Now, we're three and a half minutes late for the planned address, so if you would, Drake, hurry along!>

Drake: <Me? Well, if you say so, Range. See you lot in a bit.>

-Drake ascends the pile of debris to address the gathered mass of refugees-

Drake: <Attention, you lot! M'name's Drake, if you hadn't known. Ranger's been nice enough to let me address you all. So, then, hullo! Welcome to the Bell Tower. For ages, this has been the sacred spot of Lord Ho-Oh. We are certain that he will protect us from the Dragons, and all will be safe here! It is imperative that we band together in these dark and dangerous times. Not all of us come from Ecruteak; not all of us were even born Pokemon. But we are all refugees, we are all a community now, we are all the Bell Tower!>

-there are a few cheers from the gathered mons, though many still seem uneasy-

Drake: <Thank you all. Dasher-2-6!> -he gives a salute-

Ecruteakan!Koffing: <What nonsense is he spouting?>

-he steps back while most of the crowd starts muttering among themselves. Ranger flits forward-

Ranger: <Hush, hush! To business, then. If you would all organize yourselves by former address, we will ensure that everyone has a place to sleep. Yes, even those of you not from Ecruteak! Master Mike, Miss Helen, you are here, I know, and you have extensive knowledge of the surrounding area?>
