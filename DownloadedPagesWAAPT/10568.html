"You've grown soft Des."

She flicks his nose.

"Hey!"

Rose then places her hands on cheeks and cradles his face. She stares up into his eyes.

"I broke out for you. Now we can run away together. It'd be just like old times only without the uniforms. We'd truly be free. Go wherever we want, do whatever we want, take whatever we want. Please, run away with me."

Des gently removes Rose's hands from his face and hold them.

"Y'know I can't so that Rose."

"It's because of these friends of yours, isn't it?"

Des doesn't answer her. She tears away and turns her back to him, crossing her arms defiantly.

"You've changed Des..."

"No..."

"Rose if you run now I'll have no choice but to take you in the next time we meet."
