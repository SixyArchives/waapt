-Dave turns to see a pair of ears belonging to a Clefable peaking out from a nearby barrel. He gives a chuckle.-

Dave: <You can come out, young one. I don't Bite.>

-The scared Fairy slowly pulls her head over the lip of her hiding spot.-

Clefable: <H-h-hello.>

Dave: <Hello to you, too. My name is Dave.>

Clefable: <I-I'm Selene. Have... you seen a Mawile around here?>

Dave: <I have seen plenty in the Mansion. Any one in particular you're looking for?>

-A worried look crosses the Clefable's face.-

Selene: <Y-yes... Do you know a male by... by the name "Eos"?>

-Dave does indeed know Eos. The scaredycat Mawile was one of the few tenants of the Mansion Dave had regularly talked to.-

Dave: <Hm... I know him, but I'm afraid you won't be finding him here anymore.>

-Selene's face shifts to extreme concern.-

Dave: <Do not worry, last I heard he was just fine. He left the Dream World, you see, to look for a friend of his in the Waking World. I can't tell you where he ended up, though.>

-The Clefable's ears drop.-

Selene: <Oh...>

Dave: <But...>

-The ears go back up.-

Dave: <I do know that, wherever he ended up, their are other Mawile. I hope that narrows down your search.>

-Selene nods.-

Selene: <It does, thank you. Now, uh...>

-She drops back into the barrel.-

Selene: <I need to wait until I wake up...>

-Dave laughs.-

Dave: <Very well, young one. Best of luck!>

-He goes off, doing his regular sweep of rooms until his Sunday nap is interrupted, as it always is.-

Dave: <Oh, a Sitrus Berry!>
