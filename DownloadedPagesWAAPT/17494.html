Gem of the Mines Hotel, past

Marie walks into the hotel, yawning a bit.Well, sleeping in the park was more comfortable than I was expecting, so I'm thankful for that...still a bit odd, though.." she looked around, frowning.."Why did I feel like someone was holding my hand in my sleep?

She walks up to the counter with a prepared look, as if she is getting ready for bad news."Um..do you have a spare room yet, please?"

The man looked up, and quickly nodded, giving her a grin."Why yes, ma'am. You're in luck, another young lady, a brunette around your age, I think, just left this morning after she came back. It's room Ruby, second floor. Enjoy!"

Ruby room, past

Marie walks into the room, looking a bit surprised."Well...that was convenient." Her jaw then opened slightly."Preeettyyyy.."

The walls shone cherry red, with light touches of pink and white making the walls look like they were made of ruby. The desk and chairs were made of sequoia, the legs twisting around like the gnarly trunks of a tree giant, splitting off from the main leg to form a bit of a peak that rose in the air, for all four sides. She looked around, puzzled a bit, at the slight touches of metal in the headdress, desk, and chairs."Why would you do that?..I mean, if you're gonna have it metal plated, might as well plate the whole thing.."

She then looked at the light switches, and one of them simply said "Gem Time". She flicked the switch, and watched in awe as the light shone off of the metal, mixing with the colours, and made the whole room like it was made of ruby."...That is an acceptable reason."

She happily bounced on to the bed, lying flat on it, gazing up at the soft pink chandelier."Man, ruby's already my favorite gem, and this is just adding caramel sauce to my chocolate ice cream, mmm mmm mmmm." She then sighed."Ray would've loved this, ruby was her favourite too..." She then inhaled, and then smiled warmly.."Mmm, smells like vanilla with candy sprinkles. Odd, the only person I know that smells like that is..." Her face then clears as her eyes go wide, and she quickly exists the room and speedwalks down to the lobby.

Lobby, past

Marie quickly walks over the the counter and rings the bell, rather hurriedly. The concierge turns to her and goes "Yees?"

"Could I please please please find out who was in my room before me? I think...I think they might be somebody that I know." The concierge picks up his register and looks through it, finally landing on a page that mad him go "Aha, here it is! It says here her name was Ray Palmer. It seems like, after coming back from the park in the morning, as she had apparently spent the night there, judging from the leaves around her person, she packed up and left to go to Rustboro." He then looked up at Marie's crestfallen face."Oh, I'm sorry for bursting your bubble...if it helps, I'm sure that they would've liked to have known you if you had come earlier, you seem like the kind of people that would be friends, really."

She looked up at him, doing her damnest not to break down as she gives him a weak little smile."Y-yeah, I would've liked to have seen her too.. She then slumps off, back to the room, shaking a bit.

It's..it's better this way, right? She walks into the room, and heads straight to her suitcase, beginning to rummage through it..I mean, if I'd seen her than she might've been targeted, right? I can't let anyone from my life as Marie know about my life as Catherine, so it's better that I didn't see her, right? She then pulled out a photo in a frame. It was of her and Ray, at the karaoke, cheerfully beaming at the camera, the brunette being carried princess-style in her arms.

Tearfully, she hugged the photo close, whispering"But Arceus, it would've felt great." She sat there, legs curled up, cradling the photo next to her heart, gently and silently crying.

Rustboro

Hopping off the boat, Ray coughed, looking around, her eyes spotting the Rustboro gym, and she nodded grimly."Time to move on and begin anew, I suppose."
