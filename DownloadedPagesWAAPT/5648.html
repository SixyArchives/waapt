AU Pinwheel Forest

For a certain Leavanny colony, life for the most part hadn't really changed much even with the rise and fall of the Scolperd Empire. They were in a part of the forest that was rarely traveled, and even Scolperd forces had only managed to get some conscripted mons once, through threatening to burn down everything if no compliance was given. So it was on this day that one of the stolen children finally managed to get home, a Swadloon that in the original universe would be known as Crochet the Leavanny.

"Finally, I've made it home.", said Crochet as she shuffled through the undergrowth, "I wonder if the colony's changed all that much since I was there last." Above her, the Leavanny of the advance guard stood watch in the treetops, vigilant in their search for any predators such as Scolipede, or even the occasional Simipour or Simisage, along with some contracted Throh, Sawk, and Gurdurr further away.

After getting taken away from all she knew and getting conscripted in their army, Crochet was trained as a medic, which she was happy about because it meant that she didn't have to hurt anyone or participate in some of the things her more...sociopathic fellow soldiers did. Though some of the injuries she helped treat weren't exactly good dream inducing either. "Well, I made the best of it, at the very least. And at least not all of those mons were bad", Crochet thought as she continued shuffling.

Lately though, Crochet had been having the strangest dreams. Mainly about willingly leaving the colony with some human and being part of a group of similarly young Pokemon, going on silly misadventures in some islands far away. And being with an Audino of all mons, but that part she thought was a little silly, even for her. However, she couldn't help but wonder if those dreams were trying to tell her something...
