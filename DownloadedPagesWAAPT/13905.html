Duke is sitting on a sofa in the lobby, reading a comic when Hikari approaches him, holding a lemonade can

"Hey Duke, want some?" she offered, with a smile that seemed almost devious.

Noticing her smile, Duke took the can and looked at it for some time

"... You probably did some sort of chemical experiment on this and is using me as a lab rat, but I guess you wouldn't try anything dangerous on me, so..."

Duke then starts drinking, only for his eyes to widen as he suddenly spits everything right at Hikari's face, who keeps her eyes closed as her devious smile stays on her face.

"What... What did I just drink?" asked Duke, coughing a little

"Beer! I wanted to see your reaction to drinking some!" said Hikari, taking a small towel from her bag and cleaning her face and then cleaning her glasses with some tissues.

"... This is what some people spend all day drinking?" he asked, looking downright shocked

Hikari nods as she cleans her glasses

"... Sometimes I really can't understand you people." said Duke, sighing

"Do you want some actual lemonade to get the taste out of your mouth?" offered Hikari

"Yes. But I'm getting it myself, thank you."

Duke then gets up and starts heading to the kitchen.
