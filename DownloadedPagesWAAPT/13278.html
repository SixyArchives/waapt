Lumiose, Desjardins Household, Last Night

Roxie sighed to herself as she entered her room and locked the door behind her. As predicted her mother was thrilled to see her again.

She started rummaging through her bags until she pulled out a rather large body pillow. One with Pentigan printed on it. She flopped onto her bed and snuggled it as she drifted off to sleep.

???

Roxie's eyes slowly opened. She sat up and looked around at her surroundings. It appeared she was on a Kalosian hillside. The wind warm and gentle made the grass beneath her dance a slow dance. She was about to lie back down and enjoy the peacefulness when suddenly a giant black crack spread across the sky.

"Huh?"

Tendrils of blackness started seeping from the ground and made their way towards.

"Get back. I ain't afraid of you!"

In truth she was very afraid. As the tendrils descended upon her she curled up and shut her eyes. However to strike came. Instead the tell-tale swing of a sword slashing sounded through the air. She opened her eyes to find the dark visages momentarily bisected before retreating back into the ground. A new dark figure had arrived, his torn and burnt trench coat slowly flapping in the wind. She immediately recognized this person, the big green 'D' on his shoulder being a dead giveaway.

"Youyou're the one from my dreams."

The figure said nothing at first. He used his sleeve the wipe away an ink-like substance off of his blade before sheathing it. He turned his head slightly to look back at the young woman but not so much as to reveal his facial features.

"Although I wear black I represent white."

Roxie blinked at this.

"What."

The figure spoke again.

"Not anymore though. Now it's the one in blue who is white."

Before she ask what exactly that meant the figure crumbled to dust in the wind.

Lumiose, Desjardins Residence

Roxie shot up out of bed, a massive throbbing migraine right behind her right eye.

"Ugh.mornin'. "
