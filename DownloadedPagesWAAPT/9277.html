Blackthorn

Sakura and Grant are in Sakura's room in the Pokémon Center, along with Conan, who was being held by Grant.

"All that training really seems to have paid off." joked Sakura, seeing that Grant had a rather athletic body, she then glances at Conan "But... Who's him?"

Grant gives a few nervous chuckles before answering "... He's my son." he answered, feeling rather awkward.

"... I was out for one month and-" said Sakura, before being interrupted by a quick facepalm by Grant "Oh, not like that..."

"Yeah... I adopted the pup, I just thought he needed some help, he seemed really lost when I met him"

"But-"

"That's why I asked for Straw's armband, if you didn't want to have Conan in the team, I'd take care of him myself."

"... Does this mean you're going to leave me and the others?" she asked, her eyes widening

"Of course not, I won't just leave you, it just means that I'm not giving up on taking care of him, no matter what you think about the matter." said Grant, looking away from Sakura now.

"That's just fine." said Sakura, smiling and feeling rather relieved "I'd be more upset with you if you didn't help him out, but I won't train him, all right? You're his parent, that's one of the things you must do as one." she said.

"<Yes!>" said Conan, waggling his tail happily.

Grant smiles back "All right then, he's my responsibility and mine only, by the way, if you actually need me for battling, just ask, I guess Conan could learn a thing or two from watching me." he said, chuckling a bit.

"I was wondering that, so it's settled! I never thought I'd see you as a parent someday, Grant." she commented, half-joking "So... Can I..."

Sakura was then looking at Conan, Grant was of course quick to catch on and let her hold him in her arms, she happily started to pet him while he waggled his tail and licked her in the face, both looking very happy, Sakura was even giggling a bit.

"Nice to meet you Conan, I'm sure we'll get along just fine." said Sakura, as she petted the Growlithe.
