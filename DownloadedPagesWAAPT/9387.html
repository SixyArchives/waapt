Believe in Myself

Route 11

Gex and Pyry were together in the route while Seth was just watching them from afar as he leaned against a tree and relaxed a bit.

"<Why must I, of all mons, be the one that has to keep an eye on those two?>" he thought, even then, he was still enjoying himself as they hadn't gotten into trouble yet.

Gex was looking pretty restless though, while Pyry looked far calmer, if a bit nervous.

"<Hey Pyry! What should we do?>" asked Gex, clearly looking bored

"<I-I dunno... What do you want to do?>" Pyry asked back, looking around and seeing a few wild mons.

"<How about we fight those other mons? I bet it'll be really fun and cool to beat them all!>" suggested Gex, looking quite confident

"<I-I don't like battling...>"

"<Why not? It's fun!>"

"<Battling hurts, and I don't like being hurt...>

"<You're no fun...>" said Gex, with a sigh.

They then stood in place for a few seconds before Gex's eyes widened as he thought of something.

"<Hey! Do you want to learn this cool thing I can do with a leaf?>" he asked, as he quickly climbed a tree to get a leaf, then got down.

"<W-What cool thing? Does it hurt?>" asked Pyry, somewhat afraid.

"<I pity that Snover once that Treecko evolves.>" thought Seth as he heard everything from afar

"<It's hard to explain, but it's like this:>"

Gex then started to use the leaf as a flute and making a soothing melody with it, it calmed Pyry down a bit and he even seemed to be enjoying it, same thing with Seth, who also started to feel a bit sleepy, Gex then stopped and gave it to Pyry

"<Try to do like I did now!>"

"<O-Oh. U-Um... All right...>"

Pyry then tried to play the leaf like Gex did, but nothing came of it for a good twenty seconds where after the Snover simply gave up.

"<It's too difficult... How do you do that?>" asked Pyry, surprised with Gex's ability.

"<I dunno, I just do it, here, let me show you again, pay attention!>" answered Gex as he took the leaf back from Pyry.

And then for a long time, Gex tried to teach Pyry how to use Grasswhistle, however, he made little progress with that, Seth at least was enjoying it whenever Gex started to use the move again.

"<Who knew that leaf lizard was good with music?>" he thought, giving a small yawn as he was feeling quite drowsy "<''I suppose they'll be like that for a while, might as well get a little rest...>"

As Pyry and Gex resumed their musical training, Seth slowly drifted into a long sleep, fortunately, the two kids never got themselves into trouble since they were too busy with what they were doing, as he fell asleep however, he knew that Duke wouldn't like to know that he just let them do whatever their wanted like that, oh well, he could simply not tell him.
