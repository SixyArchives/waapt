"Saaa, that was tough."

Rex sighed as she dusted off her body. She was glad Usa-chan and her swapped traveling routes. Going along he underground path through Mount Moon was loads easier. She grinned and tapped the screen, pulling up some information. She looked to Zap and offered a thumbs up. Zap sighed at his partner, but he supposed it was fine.

"It seems Usa-chan got the goods to Celadon so we can spend some time at the Nursery."

The hoodie garbed rapscallion perked her ears and turned to face the Hydreigon.

"Chary, come on we're gunna take a break and get a snack."

Sort of hoping she was not mad about the whole tunnel run. They had gotten far and out. From what little the group could understand from uncle Suana and the Idohori triplets was that the underground excavations in Celadon were done and all they needed to do was connect the tunnels, unifying the Generation.

The Idohori triplets promised to meet them in three days to take them to Celadon via the underground routes. Uncle Suana gave Rex what appeared to be some sort of old stone tablet, no bigger than the pokegear, to use in the passage in the Cerulean Cave.

Rex winked at Zap.

"We'll have to do well to avoid the Cerulean Police Department after last year's fiasco..." She lowered her head and groaned. "How was I to know that the house had been burgled before. He shouldn't have such pretty things in there! Shiny rocks..."

Zap: (We should get to the pokemon center first to sign in if we want a place to sleep tonight.)

The Raichu calmly noted. Rex nodded in agreement and made her way toward the pokemon center, the stone safely tucked away in her bag.

Celadon

The doors burst open from the Celadon hotel. Usa and his Nidoran, Nino, bound off, waving to the hotel owner.

"Thanks oba-san! We'll be back soon!"

He chuckled as he smiled with those adorable rabbit-like front teeth of his. Nino chuffed out and bounced after his master. Usa carried a small box and Nino pulled another both with his harness. Weaving in and between the streets and back allies of Celadon the pair arrived at the game corner. Usa grunted as he pushed in he door with his shoulder and stepped in.

it was like stepping into a Fairy Tale. The quite murmurs of the back alley erupted into the ringing and clamor of falling game coins and buzzing prize machines! Nino and Usa's eyes sparkled with glee.

"Ouuaaah! It's so much cooler than Gen-chan described." The bunny-boy squeaked and dashed off to the front desks. "Hellooo!" He glanced around with his squint eyes before someone came to the window. It was a young woman, dolled up to look like a Clefairy.

She bowed her head in welcome.

"Irashaimase young master, can we help you?" She smiled brightly, causing Usa-chan to blush slightly.

The young man reached into his pocket and pulled out a business card, offering it to her. She accepted the card and looked over it, scrutiny coming over ehr features. She looked to usa who chuckled.

"I may look young but I can assure you I am the best at what I do. Company sent me because these sort of settings are best for me. They siad you had Ratrata chewing through the walls here right?"

She hesitated but nodded. "H-hai, at least we believe they are Rattata nests...."

"Say no more, " Usa-chan assured. "This is why I am here! Come on Nino." The boy bowed his head and made his way to the back of the facility into the maintenance hall. When Nino slipped in Usa whistled.

Nino released himself from the harness leads and kicked the door closed. The Nidoran still wore his silk scarf around his neck as wel las the working harness. The room was dark, dimly lit by the large machines that kept the game hall up and active 24/7. The large electronic panels that controlled the majority of the security system were pulled back, as if a secret bookcase was opening. A pair of glowing eyes peered out at the boy.

"Minna~!" He called with a smirk. From the darkness stepped two large pokemon.

The first to step out was a averaged sized Nidorino with a collar. From the collar hung a Poison Barb. After stalked a massive Nidoking wearing a Black Belt around his waist. The Nidoking snarled out and stalked over toward the young man. usa looked behind them to the tunnel.

"That's a good Job Nino." He looked to the Nidoking with a smirk. "Nino the First...." The Nidoking held his head proudly and brushed his thumb across his nose.

Usa was well known in their group. His family raised the Ninoran species from select specimens. The young Nidoran, who currently nuzzled up to his big brother, were fifth Generation Nidoran of Usa's family. Nino the First was owned by his grandfather and now belonged to him. The old poison spined brute was well trained and has worked with the family since he was first caught. His beloved wife, Nina, belonged to Usa's older sister.

"We'll just tunnel a little deeper and place the Divider. After we hook it up, we can go meet up with Rexy-chan."

The brute grunted and turned to head back into the tunnel followed by his grandchildren: Nino the Fifth and Goku.
