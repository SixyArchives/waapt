Sakura was now dressed in a mostly dark blue suit except for a light yellow shuriken drawing in the chest.

"Wow, did you do all of this yourself?" asked Sakura, her voice sounding more like that of Johnny Yong Bosch.

Hikari seemingly freezes for a moment upon hearing Sakura speak "You're a completely different person now... But yeah! "Yup! That's why the visor is red. It also has the same capabilites of my glasses, while the helmet himself has some fun functions as well such as a built-in Aura Jammer and communicator!
