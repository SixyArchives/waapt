Comet — Smirks. <Awesome.>

"Anyway, Treeo? What do you want to do?"

Treeo — Looks a bit awkward. <I...want to go and see how Copahue, Harriet and Shahinne are doing.>

Comet — <Ah. Wanna' see how the lovebirds are doing?>

Treeo — Rubs the back of his neck awkwardly. <...>

"Well, I'm sure all of us want to go and say hi, so sure, Treeo. Hmm..."

"Isn't there anything you want to do, Lake?"

Lake — <...> Shakes his head. <...No. I can't think of anything.>

"...Nothing at all?"

"...Oh." Pauses, looking awkward. "...Well, if you do think of something, will you let us know?"
