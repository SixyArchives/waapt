Rayquaza looks back at Stratus, a sad look still etched on his face.

Rayquaza: <Well, I would attract a lot of attention. I am a legend, you know...>

Stratus: <...Oh.>

Stratus frowns, looking a bit awkward.

<...So, avoid populated areas, then? There's quite afew places where there's no humans if you want to avoid them, like Pokémon Island! There aren't any humans there!>

Rayquaza: <Well...I suppose...>

Stratus: Grins. <Yeah, there are lots of quiet places you could go and have a look around without having to worry about any humans coming after you! Not that I have anything against humans, but some of them can be real mean sometimes...>

Rayquaza: <Yeah, I get you...>

The legendary glances down, fiddling with his claws idly.

<I'm still worried about something, though.>

Stratus: <Oh?>

Rayquaza coils his body nervously.

Rayquaza: <The thing is...I...I want to go down to Earth and make some friends...but...>

Stratus: Tilts his head. <But what?>

Rayquaza: Scratches his scales. <...What if none of the Pokémon down there want to be friends with me? I mean, look at me...>

He gestures at himself.

<I'm kinda' scary, aren't I?>

Stratus: <Uh...No! You're not scary! You're not scary at all!>

The Dragonite grins nervously at the legendary, who narrows his eyes at the dragon in response. Fearing he may have made Rayquaza angry, Stratus begins to shiver, trying to avoid voiding himself, least it fall and end up ruining somebody's day...

<Eep!>

Rayquaza: Frowns. <See what I mean?> Shakes his head. <Nobody from down there would want to be friends with a big frightening legendary like myself...>

Feeling defeated, the dragon curls up, burying his head in his scales.

<It's hopeless...>

Stratus just hovers there sadly, his antennae drooping at the sight of a legendary in such a sorry state. If only there was some way he could help...

...Wait...maybe there was...

Stratus: <Hey, Rayquaza...?>

The Legendary uncurls slightly, staring back at him with eyes filled with sadness, causing a chill to go down Stratus spine. If this went wrong, it probably wouldn't end well for him...

Rayquaza: <...Yes?>

Stratus: <Well, I was wondering...> Fiddles with his claws. <Perhaps, if you wouldn't mind...> Rubs the back of his neck. <...Would you, like to, you know...> Smiles nervously. <...be my friend?>

Rayquaza says nothing, instead simply staring at Stratus in silence, which is more than enough to put the Dragonite on edge.

<I mean, only if you want to! We don't have to be friends, it's just that you seem really lonely and- URK!!!>

Stratus suddenly finds himself receiving a snake hug from a clearly overjoyed Rayquaza, much to his shock and surprise.

Stratus: Constricted. <Is that...a yes?>

Rayquaza: Smiles. <Of course it is!>
