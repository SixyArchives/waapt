Guess Who...?
Okay, so this might be a bit more incoherent than I had hoped, but still. It's something (and potentially one of the only posts I'll be able to put out until M Ts end).

Sometime Around This Time

-2 instances of a Flygon 2 universes apart are busy dozing... And having a most peculiar sequence of events unfold in their dreams-

In Hazy Surroundings In a Desert - The Apparent World Seemingly Consisting of the Sides of an Earthen Pit and Clear Blue Sky Above - Several Years Ago

<Trapinch!Speck: "Zzz... Huh?">

-And senses the vibrations of a passing creature-

<Trapinch!Speck: "Oh! Something's passing by! Wonder if it'll taste any good!">

-Which causes a Marshtomp to tumble into the pit-

<Marshtomp!Thibodaux: "WHOA!">

Call: "Thibodaux!"

<Trapinch!Speck: "Eh? What are you? Well... I guess there's one way to find out!">

-And latches onto the Marshtomp's arm-

<Marshtomp!Thibodaux: "ARGH! Hey!">

<Trapinch!Speck: "... You taste kinda slim- OW!">

-And is raked from above by a slashing sensation-

<Trapinch!Speck: "Un-Ungh... Wh-What hit me?">

-A Grovyle comes into view, rushing to the Marshtomp-

<Grovyle!Willow: "A showstopper. Come on, let's get out of here Thibodaux!">

<Marshtomp!Thibodaux: "Right behind you!">

<Trapinch!Speck: "Wh-What? Who are you? And-">

Call: "Eh? A Trapinch?

... Think we should catch him guys? Or at least I think that's a him..."

-An observing Combusken seems particularly taken with the idea-

<Combusken!Rawk Hawk: "Hey! I'm down with it! I've been looking for some more newbs to raaaaaawk!">

<Grovyle!Willow: "Can't you ever can that stupid speech tic of yours? And we totally need a mon that can't keep its mouth shut in the party!">

-Not fully understanding what's going on, but perking up nonetheless-

<Trapinch!Speck: "Wait, 'rock'? I can break those! Did you want me to show you-">

Call: "Go! Pokeball!"

-And lobs a ball at the Trapinch-

WOBBLE!

WOBBLE!

WOBBLE!

CLICK!

<Grovyle!Willow: "... That was sarcastic, Call!">

A Place That Was At Once The Same and Yet Far, Far Removed - Within 6-8 Months of the Last Scene

-Spluttering, hacking up water after making way out of a flooded pit-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: *hack hack* "U-Ugh... Wh-What's h-happening?! I-It never r-rains this much!">

-And desperately ducks under some rocks for shelter-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: *cough cough* "... Huh?">

-And notices that it is in an abnormally large tunnel-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "Wh-What? When did th-this?">

-And the entrance caves in-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "ACK!">

The Interior of a House - A Few Years Ago

-A Trapinch is curiously eying his surroundings-

<Trapinch!Speck: "... Is there a place to make a pit anywhere?">

<Combusken!Rawk Hawk: "Eh? But if you want a throwdown, just ask for one. I'll be more than happy to send one your way to toughen you up, gah hah hah!">

<Grovyle!Willow: "... You're not making a pit in the house!" >:| >

<Trapinch!Speck: "Huh...? But how am I going to catch food?">

<Grovyle!Willow: "Why on earth would you need to do that? You're a trained mon now!">

<Trapinch!Speck: "... You all don't eat? How can you survive?" :/ >

<Marshtomp!Thibodaux: "No! No! That's not what she meant at all!

... It's just that things work differently under a human... Er... Where to start...? There's so many things to talk about...">

<Trapinch!Speck: (*yawn*) "... Could you explain later? I'm a little tired...">

A Temperate Location - During a Chilly Season - The Surroundings Muddled and Indistinct

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "... I'm so lost... I knew that I shouldn't have run too far from that tunnel! Now I'm never-">

-A loud crash can be heard in the brush-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "Ack!">

<Fleeing Figure: "Quick! Run! They're coming!">

-And a number of indistinct figures burst past, frantically running away from something-

-Calling after the figures-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "H-Hey! Who are you?! What's wron-?">

-And notices that the sky in this scene is filled with smoke-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "Unh! Wh-What's- ACK!">

-And suddenly finds itself propelled into a tree, hemolymph oozing from a crack in his exoskeleton-

-Dazed-

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "Urgh..." @_@>

-While dark, indistinct figures cloud its vision-

<Dark Figure #1: "Eh? What's one of these things doing up here? They're all in that forest to the south!">

<Dark Figure #2: "That's irrelevant...

This army needs a number of things... And monpower's one of them...">

<AU Trapinch!Speck: "M-Monpower?">

<Dark Figure #2: "Take him and drag him off with the others. Perhaps he'll be pliable enough to be more useful alive...">

Some Time in the Past - Hoenn - A Battlefield Staring Down a Salamence

-A Flygon is sliding back, digging feet into field so as to keep balance from an attack that just connected-

<Speck: "ARGH!">

<Drake!Salamence: "You know, I'm not just going to hand you the right to go and lead your friends off to the Champ! Is that the best you've got?">

<Speck: "... Alright... Just stay calm... Yes, you're running on fumes... But so's he. Just stick in there, and maybe we'll pull through...">

Call: "Speck! Just hang in there! If you can land one more hit, I think that'll be enough to win this!"

<Speck: "You're right! It's something that I'll just have to take from you!">

-And lunges at the Salamence with Dragon Claw-

<Drake!Salamence: "Hup!">

-Which is dodged by the Salamence flying up-

-Attempting to pursue-

<Speck: "Hey! Get back here!">

<Drake!Salamence: "Oh, as you wish.">

-And proceeds to knock the Flygon out of the sky into the arena floor by dive bombing him-

<Speck: "... Ow..." @_@>

An Hazy Scene in a Badly Damaged City Block - Poles With Stilled Figures Attached to them Looming in the Background

-A Flygon is fighting to stay awake in front of a ruined structure that a few others might recall, trying to avoid acknowledging the background for too -

<Shadowed Figure: "Hey you!">

<AU!Speck: "Zz- Nngh!">

<Shadowed Figure: "Stay awake!">

<AU!Speck: "... Why do we have to be here? Why on earth can't we guard something that's a little less... Well... Dead.">

<Shadowed Figure: "... Don't ask me, I didn't come up with this stu-">

-And a shrill cry and the sounds of flesh rending can be heard from further out-

<Shadowed Figure: "Crap! We're being attacked!">

-And dashes off-

<AU!Speck: "Hey! Wait up!">

<AU!Speck: "Oh Arceus... This again. Please... Just be over fast... Why can't these mons just stay out of our wa-?">

-Which is cut off by the sensation of being struck by something... Followed by searing pain along the other side of the Flygon's body coupled with the crunch of fractured chitin along with a hoarse cry of pain...

And then everything melts into blackness-

Around 8 Months Ago - A Sealed, Tense Room

-Looking on at the dozing form of a Totodile being cared for by a Salamence-

<Willow: "... So... We're literally waiting around to die... Fantastic...">

<Gauss: "... There's gotta be something that we can do...">

<Thibodaux: "... I really can't think of anything... The fact that we were told to hunker down means that we can't expect anyone to come for us...">

<Speck: "Then... Let's make the most of the time we've got together...">

A Short Time After The Episode 2 Parts Up - In a Haze of a Color Pallete That Would Perhaps be Soothing to Creatures Without Greenish Circulatory Fluid

<AU!Speck: "Ungh... Wh-Where- A-Am I dea-">

-And the haze becomes more distinct... Namely that of a sterile-looking room in a medical wing-

<AU!Speck: "Oh... Oh Arceus... I n-need to get o-out of-! Augh!">

-And finds itself recoiling out of pain when it attempts to move, and begins to scan desperately, hoping to find bindings to attempt to undo...

And instead finds oxygen tubes and IVs attached to different sectors of the body, and casts stained a greenish color different from its carapace... Including one seemingly encompassing the better part of what used to be a left wing-

<AU!Speck: "Wh-When th-they f-find me, th-they're- Th-They're gonna-">

-And hears the sound of footsteps approaching-

<AU!Speck: "A-A-AAH!">

-And frantically attempts to extract itself from its bed... But more or less succeeds only in collapsing off the bed with a pained yelp, and dragging itself along the floor with some of the equipment-

<???: "What the-?">

-And the footsteps draw nearer-

<AU!Speck: (*whimper*) "I don't wanna die I don't wanna die I don't wanna-">

-And the sound of the door opening can be heard-

<Medic!Chansey: "What's going on he- Oh Arceus...">

<Medic!Audino: "Stay on your guard... If he tries anything, go for his injured limbs and then run for it.">

-Trembling and feebly curling up, too weak to face the figure entering-

<AU!Speck: "Pl-Please! I f-fought as h-hard as I c-could! I d-didn't t-try to g-get captured! I r-really d-didn-!">

<Medic!Audino: "Yes, yes, we don't like you either. But it's our job to make sure that you don't die on us.">

<AU!Speck: "... H-Huh? Who a-are-?">

<Medic!Audino: "Some mons who are kinda busy with you and your friends. You're all a real handful, you know that?">

<Medic!Chansey: "I dunno, I'd prefer this kind of handful to the handful that Ninjask represents.">

<Medic!Audino: "Tell me, do you want to kill yourself or something?">

<AU!Speck: "N-No! I d-don't! I-I-">

<Medic!Audino: "Then go back to your bed and don't rip out those tubes like that! One of those is a painkiller!">

<AU!Speck: "Eep! Y-Yes! R-Right aw-ARGH!">

-And flinches in pain while attempting to crawl back-

<Medic!Chansey: "Hang on... Let us help with that...">

-And the two aid the battered Flygon back onto the bed and begin to reconnect the various tubes, one placing a bowl of water beside it-

<AU!Speck: "Wh-What are y-you gonna d-do to me?">

<Medic!Chansey: "Can't really say. Our job ends at making sure that you're still alive at the end of the day.">

<Medic!Audino: "Yeah... I'm sure that the others here will find something to do with you... I'm no buff on international law, but you're probably supposed to be returned to wherever you're from... POW and all.">

-Flushing pale or the equivalent thereof for a creature with an exoskeleton-

<AU!Speck: "Wh-What? Th-They're gonna s-send- O-Oh...">

<Medic!Audino: "Look. We don't have answers, and quite frankly, I'm not sure if you want any yourself while in this condition...">

<Medic!Chansey: "Just try and get some rest...">

-In a stammering, nervous tone of voice-

<AU!Speck: "O-Okay! Okay! Wh-Whatever you w-want!">

-And lies down as the room goes dark once again with an uneasy drift into sleep-

An Unclear Setting - Facing Another Flygon

<???: "... Speck? Are you okay? You seem confused...">

-And begins to draw the near with outstretched arms and wings-

<???: "I'm right here... Tell me what's on your mind...">

AU - PEFE

-A certain Flygon with a crippled left wing is being roused from sleep-

<AU!Speck: "Zzz... Marmalade? What are you-?

... Huh?">

-And looks around to see the trappings of a laboratory-

<AU!Speck: "But... She was just...

... And what did all of the rest of that... Getting caught by a human when young... Those other mons... What did that mean?">

Hoenn - A House in Ever Grande City

-A certain Flygon is abruptly startled awake-

<Speck: "Zzz... Ungh!

... What a strange dream... And... Wait-

... Didn't I meet that last Flygon at the Pokeringer in the Winter?">

-And stares out at the stars briefly-

<Speck: "... Maybe it portends something... (*yawn*)

I... I guess I can talk about it in the morning when everyone's awake...">

-And slips off back to sleep-
