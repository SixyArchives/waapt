Silent goes gather some of his Pokémon.

"Copahue, Algira and Ichtacya? Come here please..."

Shahinne — <...zZz...>

"...Fine, stay there, but I want you to pay attention." Turns to Ichtacya and Algira. "Since you two and Copahue are my team's [fully evolved mons], we have to work on your battle ability."

Silent sits with the duo.

"Most importantly, you are able to learn Hyper Beam or Giga Impact. Now, I don't have the TM discs for that yet, but I'll get them as soon as we return back to the [headquarters]. In the meantime however, there is an important thing that I want you to understand. In particular you, Ichtacya."

The Leafeon sits on the ground and pays attention, eyes open wide, while Algira dedicates herself to hover around Silent. They see Silent produce some documents.

"You see, unlike the teams of Tagg or Luke, we are not particularly strong. If this was a Pokémon videogame, we'd be pretty far from level 100... even Maekrite." Silent spreads around sheets and diagrams of battle strategy. "And I guess one day you guys would like to win a battle against the others."

Algira — <Not to mention the whole [worldending] stuff...>

Ichtacya -grunts — <Yeah. I mean come on, this was a vacation, I wanted to explore... but suddenly it is raining milk and the Moon is falling...>

Maekrite -from the side — <You'll soon get used to it. Though not as many thrown altogether, I hope...>

Ichtacya -blinks — <Yeah what's up with that? I mean now I had to learn the plural of apocalypse!>

"Ahem... anyway, girls? And Copahue. Look, the point is, by sheer experience or power output there's not much we can do, and we're always falling behind. Even our Gym battles have been more or less lacking in that respect."

Ichtacya — <Well... what could we do besides training?>

"...Trying [new] stuff, perhaps."

Algira and Ichtacya eye each other, then take a look at the battle strategy schematics.

Algira — <...I can't read human that well... are those moves?>

Ichtacya — <Yeah, I think. None of them I can recognize though...>

Maekrite stares at Dintel for a moment wondering why is the Pokémon staring at the laptop so intently, then comes to sit in too and examine the documents.

"See, these are moves and abilities not approved for use in Conference battling. They've been tried in places such as Holon, or some non-canon regions."

Algira — <Oh? Are they like TMs?>

"More or less... now, I would normally not try to teach those you those moves, but... there's no reason why we can't. We will be using them to explore and survive, not in official tournaments."

Maekrite — <And you are a researcher anyway. You are supposed to try this stuff, right?>

"...Yeah. Now, [Abilities] will be a different matter in that I must first study if you are compatible with them, but I wanted to know if you girls and Copahue wanted to try it anyways."

Maekrite -drops ears — <Wait what about me?>

"Well, there are a lot of nifty tricks that Nidos can learn that are not regulated in the Big Four conferences or in Unova..."

Algira — <I wouldn't bother trying new moves so long as they are Cute or Beauty~♥>

Ichtacya — <Well... I'd love to try it too. Any new trick is a nice trick!>

"Fine then. First thing we'll do is to try and have you checked for compatibility with the abilities described in these documents."

Assorted "woo-hoo~s".

"Now, for [Ichtacya], we'll be looking to study if your body has another way to refresh energy during battle."

Ichtacya — <Like when I'm synthesizing?>

"More or less like that, but we'll study the actual process. For [Copahue], we'll be studying your maneuverability when flying as a way to get yourself off combat easily."

Copahue — <Wait wh- ugh... I mean, wait what?> — covers Shahinne carefully. — <I thought I was supposed to go and earn experience?>

"Yeah, that's the point, but you'll need the ability to easily escape battles you can't win yet, or get the rest of us to safety. Not to mention, you are taking care of Shahinne..."

<Oh, lil'bird... that's a fair point.>

"And for [Algira], we'll be improving on the capacity and effect of your intimidation patterns. They are beautiful, but somewhat limited in effectiveness in the battlefield."

Algira — <Now that would be impressive. I'm very proud of my patterns!>

Maekrite nudges Silent's leg repeatedly.

Maekrite — <Come on, anything available for me? I'm sure you have something really impressive for me.>

"I have, Maekrite, in fact..." pets the Nidorino behind the ears, "I have five moves and three Abilities you could acquire, becoming the most impressive Nido around. But..."

Maekrite — <...But? :3>

Silent drags himself closer to Maekrite, leaving Ichtacya and Algira to check the documents.

"You need to be fully evolved to handle those powers."

<...Rrrrr.... you mean...> — sees Silent not hesitate — <you... you are serious.>

"Yes. Wouldn't you think I'd do my research when it is related to Nidoran?"

<But... but... I'd have to become a Nidoking...>

"Yeah. And you'd need a mate as well."

<W-what do you mean?>

Algira picks one of the paper sheets and takes it closer to a pond, where she begins trying to read it carefully.

"In the team. These abilities are for Royal Couples to handle."

Maekrite blinks and shakes his head. He grunts and growls. He motions for a moment to lunge at his Trainer, but composes himself.

<I-I'd really have to...>

"...I'm sorry, Maekrite. You are very good, about as good as your mother, it's just..."

<it's... not fair! I don't want us to be like... like that man...>

"Ssshhhh... there there. You know we're good enough..."

Maekrite leans on Silent, hiding his face from the other Pokémon, and lets himself be petted, feeling from angered to worthless. He scratches Silent's leg and bites his own tongue. He feels at the peak of frustration.

-Maekrite learns Frustration!-
