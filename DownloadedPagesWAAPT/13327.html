[Yaien walks the streets of Lumiose City, after having hit a dead end with Professor Sycamore. He was told that all the information ultimately would not help him in his quest. Sadly, this appeared to be the best place he could go for such information. He had heard Sycamore had information that could have aided him, but in the end, it became futile. However, he did get one bit of information that would help him for the moment. The trainer, Heracross and Blaziken beside him, stood amongst one of the ally's that peppered the city, standing in front of a rather broken down building , coated in spray paint and falling apart at the seams. He gives a little sigh and scowls, but presses the door open.]

Heracross: <This place is a dump. What're we doing here? Shouldn't we be out there looking for...anything else?>

Blaziken: <I do not know per say, but I assume we are here to see if anyone could help Yaiwen, I assume, to find The Great Three.>

Heracross: <So, I guess the scientist couldn't help. Stellar. That's like, what, three guys who are supposed to be Geniuses of this not helping us out?>

Blaziken: <They've been trying to help, and that's what's important.>

Heracross; <Eh. I could use a good fight right now. Let's see if there's anyone even here, and get out. This place looks ready to fall apart at seams.>

[The small group walks inside where they find...nothing. The pews are all but rotted out, falling apart and dust coats everything. There are three stained glass windows up at the front. The one on the right is blue, and holds Jirachi. The one on the left is green, and shows Celebi. The one in the middle was pink, and showed Mew.]

Yaiwen: ...Hello? Is there anyone there? [His words echo out and he gives a small sigh] I should have figured. [He takes a step forward to one of the pews and presses his foot against it, applying pressure and making sure it wouldn't fall under his weight. So, with a grunt, he sits down and stares up at the three Stained Glass figures and gave a small sigh. His eyes close, and he bows his head]

Heracross: <Hup, looks like he's going in. He's gonna be out of it for a while.> [Turns his head to see Blaziken following suit, his head bowed and his eyes closed] <Aaaand so are you. Fine, I'll go look around for someone...anyone. Better than just sitting here.>

[Heracross flies up to the ceiling with his wings and lands on one of the rafters, hanging on upside down and staring down at the ceiling, scanning anything and everything. Suddenly, from up on the rafters, two lines of bright, yellow energy appears, followed by a crack just below the eyes.]

???: <Who dares disturb my home!?>

Heracross: <GAH!>

[Heracross spins around quickly and lands on his feet, this time on top of the rafter and turns to see an immense figure, a Golurk, approaching from the shadows, floating just above the rafters. Its 'eyes' turn and narrow down at the insect, clenching its fists tightly]

Golurk: <Who dares intrude on my domain, and wakes me from my slumber!]

Heracross: <Erm...I do, I guess?]

Golurk: <I should punish you for your impudince...but that is not what the three teach, to intentionally harm those who, at the moment, do not deserve it.>

Heracross: <The Three-Oh Arceus, you're one of them, aren't you?>

Golurk: [Looks down to the floor and tilts his head] <Who...is that?>

Heracross: <That's my trainer. He came here looking for information on Mew, but hey guess what! The only person here is you. So he's prayin'.>

Golurk: [Begins to float down to the floor, head still tilted] <Far too many humans have done nothing more than vandalize this place, until people stopped coming and leaving it in the sad state it lays in today...but this human does not harm it, but rather, live under what I have learned, and what the three teach?>

Heracross: <...I...Guess?>

Golurk: <I see...I see...> [He lands in front of him and gives a nod] <He is a good man, this one! A good head on his shoulders and a good heart-I can sense it! If he wishes to find the Great Mother, then-then I shall join him in his travels!>

Heracross: [Floating down and groaning] <Goodie, another one. Surely, this cannot end badly, or annoyingly.>

Golurk: <What was that?>

Heracross: OH, nothing~ Nothing at all. I'm...SURE he'd be happy to have you join us!>

Golurk: <I should hope! HUMAN!>

Yaiwen: GAH! [Falls off the pew and lands on his side] Ow...what the...

Golurk: <Human! It is my wish to travel with you!>

Heracross: <He can't HEAR you. He can't even UNDERSTAND you.>

Golurk: I am well aware! Now, I believe the way for a human to capture me would be...> [He kneels down and presses one of his bulky fingers against the spare pokeball that Yaiwen keeps on his belt, and, seconds later, it pops open and converts into a bright red energy, disappearing into the ball>

Yaiwen: [Stares at the pokeball as it shakes once...twice...three times...and then, with a click, stops moving] Erm...did I just...what the...

Blaziken: <...What just happened?>

Heracross: <My migraines just doubled, that's what happened.>
