<Matryx: "I don't know! And that's why we're checking it out!">

<Lamarr: *munching Pop-Tarts* "Well hey, he hasn't been totally off-kilter... We found these earlier!">

<Matryx: "Wonder how those are made anyways...?">

<Nuzleaf: "You're at the Recipe Vault and you're asking how something is made?">

<Miles: "... Recipe Vault? And who are you?">

<Nuzleaf: "Yes, the Recipe Vault! Professor Banana commissioned its construction so as to ensure that no food of cultural significance would be lost to the ravages of time! And the name's Locke. Chef Locke.">

<Lamarr: "Er... Hi... Locke...">

<Matryx: "Wait... So we could get the recipe for those sweet fruity pocket things we found?">

<Locke: "Oh, definitely! I'm more a fan of crepes myself, but the more cooks, the better!">

<Matryx: "Er... Okay... I think this could actually be useful!">

<Miles: "What do we need to do to be able to have it?">

<Locke: "Well, you could just photocopy the microfiche records... Or keep it where all chefs keep their best recipes!" -taps forehead->

<Lamarr: "... Photocopy?">

<Matryx: "Er... Could we just write it down on paper or something?">

<Locke: "Well, that's basically photocopying, but a bunch slower...">

<Matryx: "Why? How's photocopying work? And what is this recipe anyways?">

<Locke: "Well- *DING!* Oh! My cookies! Come in, I gotta handle these.">

<Matryx: "O... Kay?">

-The group follows the Nuzleaf into a chamber. Its foyer of the chamber resembles a cottage kitchen, illuminated by apparent sunlight from a window plugged into the wall; the aroma of chocolate-chip cookies wafts through as the Nuzleaf removes a tray from the oven-

<Miles: "Hey! Not bad! What are those things anyways?">

<Locke: "These? They're my cookies.">

-A spiral staircase leads to lower levels of the complex-

-Setting down the tray-

<Locke: "Anyways, the recipe archives are down the stairs, on tiny cards. There's machine to look at them or copy them onto paper.">

<Lamarr: "Whoa... What's with the boxes?">

<Locke: "The appliances?">

<Miles: "Appliances?">

<Locke: "These are what chefs cook with! Like the oven, the range, the refrigerator...">

<Matryx: "... They all look so complicated..." ._.>

<Locke: "Food is ART! It is only right that there be complexities! Now, you sought the recipe for Pop-Tarts? Let us go to the pastry files...">

<Lamarr: "Pastry files? That's what all these little card things are? What's this one for anyways?">

<Locke: "That one is for... S'mores Pop-tarts, actually. Which flavor were you looking for?">

<Matryx: "... How many ARE there?" ._.>

<Locke: "Several dozen... maybe a hundred, counting all the discontinued flavors.">

<Matryx: "Uh... Can we get... All of them?">
