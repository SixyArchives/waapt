Sakura's Dream

Sakura found herself in a field surrounded by cherry trees, the wind was blowing and making the cherry blossoms scatter, making for a quite beautiful sight, she then noticed that the clothes she was wearing weren't unfamiliar but definitely different from the ones she wore normally◊, she was also holding a fan with her that was also familiar, it was then that she realized what was going on.

"This is..." she said as she looked around the area to see some wisps floating around the place, giving her the final hint she needed "... The land of the dead, right? It's such a pretty place for something so depressing as death...

Walking around the field, Sakura wasn't sure she wanted to do in there, but there was a slight curiosity of what she could find in there, she walked and walked until she came across a big staircase, and that was one BIG staircase, it seemed to go on forever.

"... This is the bane of all lazy people everywhere."

As she started to climb the stairs, Sakura wasn't really feeling tired as she went up, but it was certainly not the most pleasant of experiences, though as she climbed she could still see some wisps around along with the cherry trees, in a odd way, the wisps just made the place look even more pleasant than it would be with just the trees. After a long enough time she arrived at the top and saw what seemed to be a shrine, a big one at that, it seemed rather old, but it was very well kept, she went inside the shrine to find not a single soul inside, however she did find what seemed to be the kitchen, there, she started to prepare some tea for herself, after doing so she started to drink while looking at a nearby window and seeing a big garden filled with cherry trees, a very pleasant sight indeed.

"You know... I could get used to this, such a pretty place, shame that I couldn't quite find what I wanted..." she said as she took a sip of the mug of tea she was holding.

Virbank Pokémon Center

Sakura then wakes up and gets up from her bed, giving a little yawn as she did so, she went for the room's window and took a look at the city outside.

"Of course... A dream." she said as she closed the window and started to get herself ready for the day "I wonder what happens when we die, do we get to go a place like that one or... Well, I think only time can tell, the only thing I know for certain is that I gotta keep living, that's all that matters for now, though I guess once the day comes, I hope I can be reunited with them in a place like that one, it would be... Enjoyable, maybe death isn't so bad after all..."
