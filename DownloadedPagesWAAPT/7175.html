Stratus: <I think being a 'Gator is going to his head just a little.>

Lake simply shrugs.

Lake: <I wouldn't worry about it. When humans stay in Pokémon form for a while, they begin to act more and more like a Pokémon.>

Stratus: <Oh! But...does that mean he'll lose his humanity if he stays as a Feraligatr for too long?>

Lake: <Nah. Sure, he may still get meat cravings for a little while after he changes back, but they'll go away over time. It's really nothing to worry about.>

Luke: <Hey, it's nothing personal! A mon's gotta' eat, you know!>

Lake: <LUKE! Let him go!>

Lake: <Off. Now!>

Luke: <Aww, but I was hungry!>

Stratus: <Technically->
