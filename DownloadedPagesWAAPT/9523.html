"I lived an average, happy childhood until my ninth birthday. I had gone out and caught a wandering Ralts. I thought it was a bit odd, they usually are quite reclusive, to see one wandering close to the inside of town was strange. It wasn't until I went home that things change for me."

"I walked inside, wanting to show my mom and dad Regina, as I had named the Ralts, and as she had happily accepted being named such, to find our home torn apart. It had been ransacked, anything and everything of value had been taken... including the lives of my parents."

"Shortly after, because I couldn't keep the home, I was orphaned. Then, in an effort to some how survive, I took to unofficial tournaments. Like I shared with the others, I lost a lot. After one particular loss, to... I think it was Steven Stone, actually, who had crushed me, I think he gave me an egg. I don't remember what he said or why I had it, but that's that."

"Eventually, I got better. The egg hatched into the Beldum I named Argentum. I still was going no where. Then, of course, was the point in which I decided that I would use brains to overcome brawn."

"Five years. Five years of winning and saving money. I bought clothes. I bought food. I finally could live on my own and not have to rely on the charity of others. It was around this time I decided I would see every Pokemon in Hoenn. And I set out to do so."

"It was when I got to Mt. Chimney I found them. Two team Rocket goons. One of them was holding the pendant my mother made. When they saw me, in my father's jacket, they instantly turned on me."

"It didn't last a minute. Regina, now a Gardevoir, literally tore one in half. The other I threw into the volcano, along with all their pokeballs. I decided to move on."

"After that... nothing much. I traveled Hoenn. I saw everything. I beat everyone. Then, when I was finished, I decided I would do the same in other regions. And that's how I got to Johto."
