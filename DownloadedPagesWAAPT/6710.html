-Looking around at her surroundings while traversing down the hallway, Hatchet can see the remains of a number of rooms, their debris occasionally spilling out into Hatchet's path. One is missing a door, and has a number of corroded metal implements with more intact plastic handles lying around. Metal boxes lying strewn about, long since moved from their original locations by the walls, with rusted and broken blades and the remains of other utensils lying around in it

Most other rooms are in similar states, if occupied by other debris. One is strewn with plastic containers, having remained fairly intact due to the absence of sunlight to degrade them. Were Hatchet of our world, she would perhaps notice that they are of the sort that would normally hold cleaning agents such as bleach. Another room has the remains of plastic and metal boxes, with some broken-up monitors lying all over the room. A rounded stone block, perhaps a paperweight or a stamp, lies disused in the hallway, with the same sigil that the Salamence showed Hatchet yesterday
