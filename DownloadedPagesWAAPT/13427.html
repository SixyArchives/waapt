Lumiose City

Fool: Of the ones I know, there are the de Niles, de Callones, Cains and Perilloux. All of these families are among the oldest of the old noble blood in Kalos.

  • The de Niles are a scholarly family from Cyllage and are actually quite insular. They prefer their books, Pokemon, fellow academics and studies to the company of others, especially other nobles. Have a reputation of being "philobarbic" for their family heritage extends outside that of Kalos and Europe as a whole.

  • The de Callone are based in Laverre and are the most "people orientated" of the families I know of. They were once considered the "conscience of Kalos" due to both their usually high positions in the royal court and their egalitarian morals. Populist nobles if you will.

  • The Cains in Kalos are a merchant family and put the money in "old Money". Infamous for being quite ruthless businessmen, they run Cain Industries over here. Though, have been faltering in recent times due to competition from Lysandre Labs and their own frugality.

  • The Perilloux are the last family I know of, and I only know of them because a friend I met here was one of them. Considered the quintessential "idiots with money", the family pretty much survived on leasing out their lands to other barons and spending their money on lavish parties and unneeded luxuries until they became bankrupt. Often not considered a "noble family" anymore due to the lack of a heritage estate.
