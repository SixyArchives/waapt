Victory Road Caverns

As Locke stepped into the cave, the world seemed to slow to a stop. Everyone stopped moving, for one brief moment, everything froze, all the sweat on the faces and all the expressions in the room were stuck in time, for one brief moment.

At least, to Locke they did.

There were four people in the dark, stinking cave, and one flashlight, lying on the ground. One poor gentleman was lying propped up against the wall, his bleeding like a fountain. There was a woman, standing next to the poor old man. She had hair like solid fire, and a sexy dress, lips, and opera gloves to match. The man was in a velvet suit, and his singin' parrot, a Chatot, was next to 'im, worried.

There were two men behind them. To Locke, they look like bystanders to the crime. One was short and stocky, not a dwarf, Locke wouldn't call him one. He looked like he'd kill 'im if he did.

The other was havin' his lunch....spewing it out, actually. he was leanin' against a rock spire. He was tall and lanky, with a mop of hair like a raven.

The three that weren't bleeding were turning their heads towards him, all three looked shocked.

He noted every facial feature, every pose, and then finally, let the world continue on in his head.

The skinny guy, bless his soul, stepped in front, as if to act as a shield."Wh-what are you doing here?"

Locke felt glad that he had wore his trenchcoat today. He also whipped out a strawberry lollipop and plopped it in his mouth. He moved it to the side and said, from the corner of his mouth:"Relax, nobody's gonna hurt ya. I'm one of the agents for the International Police that are supposed to be policing the area. I'm here to help."

They relaxed a little. Underneath his calm demeanor, Locke was a bit giddy with excitement. But he kept a straight face, stopping from twitching. He connected with Mirror."Scan the room."

The bruiser man stepped forward. Suspenders and a blue jacket, his was wearin' a hat askew on his hand. Not a man that takes much pride in his job.He said:"Ohh, lllook. Mr Bbbabyface here wants to seeee if heee can be a big boy and solvvve a murder. Well get the heeell outta here and call someone older!" He seemed drunk, and swung at Locke.

In the span of an instance Locke had grabbed the hand and gripped it tight, seemingly crushing the hand. Bruiser gasped a bit, but Locke remained stoic. He did not feel the need for such petty trifles. The dame in the red number and the bloke got up and pulled him back. Now they looked at Locke with a tinge of fear. While meanwhile, Locke was panicking under his mask of stoic:"''Oh Arceus what did I just do I nearly broke the poor guys hand oh Arceus oh Arceus no need to panic no need to panic just get a full scan just proceed as normal"

"Jackson Lorts" He said aloud, his scrambled thoughts brought in order, like soldiers in a military line."Transporter of food and goods to the Kalos League, you seem to have taken a bit too much Unovian booze to drink."

The man stared at him like a dumb fish. The name, read of the badge. The transporter? Coincides with the drinking; the transporters would have the easiest time getting them. His accent was Unovian, a natural guess that he'd drink his come country's alcohol the most. The expression on Jackson's face confirmed all of his thoughts.

He walked over the the bleeding man, and lay a hand upon him. Pulse was still beating. The woman spoke, with a husky voice,"Why hello there, stranger. Come here to help little ole me?" Her eyes were withering green, if she told you to do something with that voice, those lips, and those eyes, then you just couldn't say no.

He remains indifferent, pushing his hormones down from the inside."I'm here to help this unconscious man. You people are going to help me.You're Ivy Francesca, one third of the Chatot Jaybirds, and this is Sonny Mulligan, and Paulie the Chatot, am I correct?" She slowly nods her head. He turns to the other man. He stood still, like a scarecrow blowing in the wind. He then snapped his fingers."Put a vest and a bowtie on you and I remember. Tim Joel, you're a waiter here, am I right?" The man nodded.

Locke then got up and stood in the centre of the cave. He took in the aroma, the smell of perfume, cologne, booze, floor polish, and blood. There was a touch of rain outside, he hoped he could bring it inside. Use it to wash this all away.

After a bit of communication with Mirror, he said:"I presume you're all going to say you just found him like this."

They nodded, like guilty children trying not to get caught. But he didn't know if any of them were guilty. Not yet.

It's not actually time-stopping, it's just my interpretation of Hyper Awareness. Please, tell me what needs to be improved.
