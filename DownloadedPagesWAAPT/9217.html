???: "...reduced to this."

His attention shifts once again to the Grunts.

???: "I must have been asleep a long while, as our king is gone. What of the Seven Sages?"

Char: "Disappointing. Nevertheless, we must continue for the sake of our king. I will gladly accept this appointment if it will help Lord Ghetsis. I do owe much to him."

Team Plasma Grunt 3: "Preparations in the room are complete, sir."
