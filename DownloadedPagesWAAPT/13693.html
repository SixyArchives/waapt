"<Correct. Going back to the previous topic though, you seem to be daydreaming.>"

Lyuri looks aside "Oh, right... I was thinking about my boyfriend, that's all."

The Hawlucha chuckles "<Figures. Romantic problems?>"

"... It's more of a conflict over who he really is." said Lyuri, lowering her head a bit.

"<Found out his mysterious dark secret?>"

Lyuri then frowns "... No, and it doesn't concern you anyway."

The Hawlucha smiles "<All right. I won't bother you about it. But if you're going to daydream, I suggest doing so somewhere you might not end up getting lost or attacked.>"

Lyuri folds her arms "Whatever. I was just exploring."

"<The depths of your own mind I suppose?>"

Lyuri grunts "Shut up."

The Hawlucha chuckles some more "<I like you, chica. You seem quite fierce>"

He then leaps out of the tree branch and lands a few meters ahead of Lyuri.

"<I'm interested in seeing if you, or rather, your Pokémon can back up the act.>" he said, with a challenging smile

"... Sure thing. You're really nosy, but I guess it can't hurt to have you on my team." said Lyuri.

"<Oh, I apologize. I just wanted to know how much information I could get out of you. In any case, prepárate para luchar.>" he said, chuckling.

Route 10

Lyuri sends out Pyry.

"Pyry, help me catch that bird." said Lyuri

"<Uh... All right.>" said Pyry, getting nervous

The snow falling around the area then starts to become even more intense.

"<... Maldita nieve. As if I wasn't freezing to death before.>" said the Hawlucha, looking up at all the snow, then looking at Pyry "<Very well then. We're both at a disadvantage against each other. This will be interesting.>"

"Put it to sleep!" said Lyuri

Pyry nods nervously and then picks up some grass and starts using it to make a small song. The Hawlucha just listens to it, seemingly unaffected.

Pyry used Grasswhistle!

The Hawlucha starts clapping "<Bravo! Do it again!>"

Hawlucha used Encore!

"<U-Um... Ok...>"

Pyry tries to start playing again, but that interrupted when the Hawlucha suddenly dashes at him and slashes at him with his claws/talons

Hawlucha used Aerial Ace!

The Hawlucha quickly retreats after striking Pyry, who staggers for quite a bit before recovering from the slash. The Hawlucha could also feel that the snow was going to get seriously on the way as it was slowly draining his health.

"Pyry! Are you all right?" asked Lyuri, a bit worried.

Pyry nods and weakly starts playing his song again, this one was really slow and sad to an almost pitiful degree.

Pyry used Grasswhistle!

The song actually started to affect the Hawlucha who slowly fell asleep. Pyry kept playing for a while to make sure he was completely asleep.

"Ok! Now Icy Wind!" said Lyuri

Pyry then breaths some icy wind at the Hawlucha, damaging it and also freezing his legs in place. He wakes up after the attack, noticing his condition

"<... Qué astuto.>" he said as he tries to break free.

"I don't know what you just said, but whatever, now you can't escape!" said Lyuri, picking a Premier Ball from her bag.

She then throws the Premier Ball at the helpless Hawlucha.
