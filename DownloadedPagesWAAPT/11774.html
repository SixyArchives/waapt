The Under, Tunnels, The Extra's Hideout

The door slams open, the two sentries flying through.

People immediately scramble to their feet, grabbing weapons and training them on the door.

"   THAT. IS. IT.   "

"I come home, expecting to see my friends, most of whom I haven't seen in, what, eight years?"

A veritable fallen angel of unholy wrath, Pi stalks through the doorway.

The sentries, still on the ground, groan in pain.

"And what do I find?"

The only response is terrified, shocked silence, punctuated by pained noises from the downed sentries, and the rattle of weapons held in trembling hands.

Calmly, coolly, Pi stalks over to the nearest person and lifts him by his shirt.

In a small voice that would be pleasant if not for the murderous rage barely restrained by gates that had long ago became loose, Pi speaks again.

"   Do. you. know. what. I. find?   "

Terrified, the Extras member shakes his head no.

And then the rusty hinges on the gates finally give way.

"   I FIND THAT CIPHER HAS LOCKED DOWN MY TOWN, MY FRIENDS ARE NOWHERE TO BE FOUND, AND THAT OUR ESTABLISHED FALLBACK POINT IS FULL OF COMPLETE STRANGERS, WHO IMMEDIATELY GREET ME WITH WEAPONRY!   "

Pi falls back into the previous tone, quiet and barely controlled.

"So. How do you think that makes me feel?"

Scared beyond words, the man stutters something that only marginally resembles speech.

Infuriated once again, Pi moves to throw the poor man when-

???: "WARDEN!"

Pi freezes.

???: "Warden, drop him."

Pi makes no move to comply.

???: "Pi, buddy, I said drop him. He's done nothing wrong, and you're scaring the crap out of the poor man."

Slowly, Pi lowers the man to the floor, and then turns.

"Lou...?"

"Lou": "Hey buddy. Long time no see. I missed ya."

Speaking is a man of average height, average build, and average face. In fact, the most notable thing about him is his utter and all-encompassing averageness. He had the kind of face that you could instantly forget.

He's dressed in fine clothing, in the standard uniform of an upper-class twit.

"Lou..."

"'Lou": "Heh heh heh."

They grab each other, hugging briefly.

Lou turns to the group.

Extras Guy 1: "What? No way!"

Extras Girl 1: "Well, explains the beatdown."

"So, what happened?"

Lou sighs.

"Lou": "It's a long story, buddy. Tell you in the morning. For now, you need sleep."
