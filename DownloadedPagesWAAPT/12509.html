Phenac Pokémon Center - Early in the morning

Duke was sleeping in Lyuri's room, after she got an extra bed for him to sleep on. He was still in human form and was looking panicked as he slept.

???

Duke finds himself in a seemingly endless open field, the ground was plain and was completely soaked drenched in blood. Just the smell of it was enough to make him nauseous, he notices that he was still in human form and starts walking around the field while trying his best the smell all the blood the least he could.

"What is this place? Why is there so much blood...?" he thought as he wandered.

He kept walking around the field for a long time before he finally found something; and that something was a corpse with blood all over it and it was almost impossible to recognize what it was due to it's condition. That sight, along with all the blood around him was making Duke struggle to keep himself from vomiting.

He then looked around, suddenly more bodies like the one she saw were visible around him, most of them he couldn't identify what it originally was. However, some he managed to barely identify, due to not being able to look at them for more than a few seconds, those were the bodies of an Alakazam, Scyther, Monferno and Bisharp, all of them so horribly disfigured and mangled that he was surprised he even managed to identify those. Duke was horrified and feeling very sick at that point.

"<What are you so horrified for? This is all your work after all.>"

Hearing the voice come from behind, Duke turns around to meet with a Lucario with a very dark colored fur that made him look like some sort of haunting and next to him was another body just like the others, however that one Duke could easily identify not because it was in a better state than the others, but because he saw a very familiar object piercing it, a sword. That was Herbert, that's when Duke finally understood what was going on.

"T-This is... N-No..." said Duke, taking a few step back, barely managing to pay attention to anything else.

"<Isn't it fun? I know you'd have loved to do this to that boy before, a shame you missed your big opportunity, don't you think?>" said the Lucario, grinning cheerfully as he pulled the sword off the corpse.

"I... No... Would I... Would I have done that to... Them all...?" said Duke, took freaked out to pay attention on what the Lucario was doing.

"<Yes, yes you would; and you would've loved doing so too. Quite the killing machine, isn't that right?>" said the Lucario, walks towards Duke and dragging the bloody sword along the ground.

It was then that Duke finally registered the threat and tried to take to his armband off, however, it was stuck to him and didn't come off no matter how much he pulled it.

"<What's the matter? Scared of becoming a Lucario and fighting? I guess that now that you're back to your boring self, you probably should join them in this blood bath.>" said the Lucario, grinning.

In the blink of an eye, Duke barely managed to see the Lucario charge at him with a sword, all he felt was a great pain in the chest and the cold metal that pierced it.

Phenac Pokémon Center - Early in the morning

Duke bolts awake and sees that he's in Lyuri's room, he was panting and looking downright terrified, his first instinct was to rub his chest to confirm that yes, it was all a nightmare. He looked at Lyuri sleeping on the bed next to his and saw her sleeping peacefully, that sight was enough to calm him down a bit, but he soon gets up quietly so as to not wake Lyuri up and quickly gets out of the room, stumbling around the hallway outside for a bit and feeling a very painful headache.
