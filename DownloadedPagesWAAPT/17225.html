430606 PippingFool25th Nov 2014 09:16:33 PM from Sydney, The Vivid City Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Getting away with murder
Less than Three

Cyllage City

The ringing of an alarm clock cracks the silence in the looming, sparse room. A single green eye flicks open, meandering over the shreds of light filtering in through the shutters, to her alarm clock and then drifting back close again.

6:30 AM

Early Morning...

Cleo tosses and turn in her bed. She shoves the quilts and sheets off as she sluggishly sits on the edge of the bed, rubbing her face before finally standing up.

Cleo: Another morning, eh.

Cleo yawns ans stretches, trudging over to her en suite personal bathroom.

Cleo: No point in dawdling... I guess.

After a long hour of shower, and and even longer period of figuring out what clothes to wear while in the end just settling on her business attire that she's found herself dressing in regularly as of late. She makes a slow trod out of her dressing room before slumping down at her vanity, wearily staring at her reflection as she pulls out various forms of makeup from the small draws and shelves around the mirror

Cleo: It's time for make-up

Cleo gives a half-hearted smile.

Cleo: Perfect smile~ Give 'em a good picture, and they'll leave ya alone.

Cleo plays and fiddles with some of her brushes, flicking off the forgotten dusts and dirtying her fingers with congealed gunk before actually beginning the process proper. She applies the make up in a rather robotic fashion: First a bit of concealer to hide the rings under her eyes, then some foundation to even out the skin, a touch of blush to bring out the cheeks, shimmering eye base followed by some purple eye shadow to make her eyes pop and a a combination of a dark coloured lip pencil, lipstick and lipgloss to bring out the colour in her lip.

There seems to be no thought, no motive as to why she would do this, considering she was never a fan of the stuff before, but as her fingers mull over a peculiar stick in a pot of what appears to be some sort of powder mix.

Cleo: He always commented on how I never acted "typical", hmph.

Cleo begins applying the Kohl along her eyes, in a Mesdemet◊ style. As she finishes off the last flick of her Kohl, she spends another minute staring at herself in the mirror.

Cleo: Would he even recognise me now?

Suddenly, a voice from below rings out.

???: Cleo! Breakfast!

Cleo: (Yelling out) Coming maman!

Cleo stares intensely at the mirror, before picking up her hat and making her way downstairs.

Château de Nile, Cyllage

Breakfast at the maison was quiet, as per usual since most of the more vocal birds left the nest. At the head of the table is the patriarch Carter, sitting very sharply in his chair with a cup of the finest saidi tea pressed to his upper lip and curled moustache. His interest, at the moment, seem to be some sort of unfinished paperwork from the previous night about finances.

Sitting next to him is his wife, Mariam. A short yet steadfast woman draped in a blue embroidered kaftan with a matching headscarf pouring herself some tea of her own. However, her fierce amber eyes seem to be more focused on her daughter at the moment than the meal in front of her.

Lawrence is off by the side. Relaxing on his chair with a cup of coffee in one hand and a newspaper in the other. His keffiyeh has been placed to the side, the cloth folds dangling off the edge of the mahogany table. Occasionally, he drops his paper to either dig into the tomato and egg dish that has been served for breakfast, or to observe his sister of who he is worried sick about. Even if he doesn't show it.

And then there is Cleo, sitting at the far side of the table - a noticeable distance from her parent and brother, but still within their range - occasionally sipping her tea while taking nibbles out of her croissant and the egg dish. Lawrence and Mariam exchange glances at each other, before turning their attention to Cleo.

Lawrence: Cleo... sis... Are you all right?

Cleo looks over to them before staring blankly at the wall once more.

Cleo: Fine. Is something a matter Lawrence? Maman?

Miriam: You... have barely touched your breakfast Cléopâtre.

Cleo: Not hungry at the moment.

Mariam: But it's my shakshouka, your favourite. You've always found room for it before...

Cleo looks down at her meal and sighs.

Cleo A first for everything, I guess.

Cleo begin poking one of her eggs

Lawrence: Cleo, you've been like this for a month.

Carter looks up from his papers to share the same worried glance his wife and son are expressing at the moment.

Carter: Cléopâtre, we're your family. You can tell us, we won't judge.

Mariam: We love you, and we just want you to be okay.

Cleo: Work has been tough. That is all.

Cleo finishes off her tea and places her hat on her head.

Cleo: Speaking of, I best be heading off to work. Merci beaucoup maman for the meal.

Cleo begins walking out of the room while the rest of the family glance at each other.

Carter: -Looking away- It's Friday...

Mariam: Uncle doesn't work on Fridays...

Lawrence stands up and walk over to his parents, giving them a comforting pat on the shoulder.

Lawrence: I'll go talk to her, she has always listened to me.

Carter: Thank you son.

Mariam: She's been worrying us ill lately.

Lawrence: I'll try my best.

Lawrence grabs his keffiyeh and hastily begins folding it as he follows his little sister through the halls and corridors of the château.

Château de Nile, Library

Lawrence walks into the Library, where he finds Cleo sitting on a mat, looking up at the vast shelves that tower over her filled to the brim with books, tomes, novellas and manuals accrued over the centuries this family has lived here. Lawrence sighs, and begins approaching his sister.

Lawrence: Hey.

Cleo looks over to him with a sharp and somewhat annoyed glare, though it does soften as he draws closer.

Cleo: Brother.

Lawrence sits beside his sister and looks up at the ceiling in silence, after a while he turns over to his sister and whispers to her.

Lawrence: You miss him dearly, don't you.

Cleo merely exhales and falls back on her brother's chest. Lawrence allows for this, and begins to gently stroke her hair.

Lawrence: Remember those poems that maman used to read to us as kids?

Cleo: Like Rumi?

Lawrence: Heh. Yeah, like Rumi. Do you remember much of his work?

Cleo: A little...

Lawrence: Then recite to me what you remember.

Cleo: Mmm...

If we come to sleep
we are His drowsy ones'
And if we come to wake
we are in His hands"
If we come to weeping
we are His cloud full of raindrops
And if we come to laughing
we are His lightning in that moment
Cleo pauses for a moment, Lawrence takes this time hi draw her closer for a hug.

Lawrence: Go on.

Cleo: If we come to anger and battle
it is the reflection of His wrath
And if we come to peace and pardon
it is the reflection of His love
Who are we in this complicated world?

Cleo and Lawrence glance back up to the ceiling, with not another spoken word between them.
All about big hair, big shoes and big attitude!
