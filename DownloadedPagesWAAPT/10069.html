Approximately 2 months ago, Accumula Town

Gordon, Nathan's father, was sitting out in a bench outside, reading a magazine to pass the time with. The weather was cloudy at best, but it was bright.

Past Gordon sat a man, long raincoat, scarf and hat covering his appearance quiote a lot. Unusual for the time of year.

Gordon didn't pay the man no mind, however, to appear polite.

Raincoat Man: "Do you know if anyone in this town has a Ralts?"

Gordon: "Is that meant for me?"

Raincoat Man: "Indeed. Do you know?"

Gordon: "I'm afraid I do not. Well, besides the one my son has."

Raincoat Man: "Can you describe it?"

Gordon: "Hold on, why do you want to know so much of my son's Pokemon?"

Moment of silence lasted.

Raincoat Man: "It is likely this pokemon is owned by someone else. I wish to know as much as I can before making a judgement on whether or not it is the one I'm looking for."

Gordon: "Who owns it, then?"

Raincoat Man: "A person by the name of Francine. I was tasked to retrieve this pokemon."

Gordon: "I see. Well, the one my son found in the forest and took with him on his journey was very curious, it wanted to flotate and inspect lots of items in our home. The times I had to make Nathan clean his room over her deeds is too much to count with two hands."

Raincoat Man: "That sounds close to the description I was given. Thank you for your cooperation. But before I go, can you tell what direction did your son headd to?"

Gordon: "It was a while ago, so I don't know, I think he might be somewhere in Nimbasa?"

Raincoat Man: "Very well. I will head there and explain him the story. Surely he will understand."

The man took off from the bench and walked away, his destination Lentimas.
