UTINNI!

Somewhere, probably Celadon or Pallet, An Earlier Point In Time

- Human!Chrome is waiting around-

-Jack approached the man, hoping he might finally be able to finish getting information from him.-

Jack: "Hi, Michael. I think there were a couple things yet you were willing to tell me about."

Human!Chrome: Oh! Oh yes... Hmmm... Immortals or weremons first?

-Jack decided he might as well go with the first of the two options.-

Jack: "How about immortals?"

Human!Chrome: Alright...

Human!Chrome: (Continued) It is said that there is at least one human for which the ravages of time do not apply. Rather than aging like the rest of the populace, they were changed... changed to never age past a certain point. As for who changed them...

-he hesitates-

Human!Chrome: (Continued) It's... something of a mystery... But what we do know is that they still walk among us. I'll give you a hint as to finding one... He or she is an infamous dictator from long ago.

Jack: "So they're pretty much what they sound like - they're immortal. You still had these "weremons" to tell me about yet, then?"

Human!Chrome: Hmmm... well, since that last one was rather short and obvious, and you've been so good about coming to see me about it... Alright, I'll give you this one as well.

Human!Chrome: (Continued) "Some of those who walk among us are shapeshifters... humans walking as mons, or mons walking as humans. They don't seem too out of place, for the most part... Oh, sure, the mons who have become human may still look the part in their hair, eyes, and clothing, and the humans who have become mons may have physical traits that echo their human selves, but otherwise, they are indistinguishable from those who are "normal." As to how they do it... well, while a select few have been altered by the Legendaries themselves, others use strange artifacts. It is said there are two types of these artifacts - one that can make humans mons, and another that can make mons human. If you are lucky enough to find the former someday, there is one important thing to remember... When you become a mon, you can forever understand their language."

Jack wasn't sure how to react. It wasn't exactly what he would have guessed, but it still sounded along the lines of what 'Weremons' might have implied. He thought he had heard vague rumors about such transformations before, but never given it much thought. Even now, he was skeptical about what Michael was telling him, even if his information had proven accurate in the past. And unlike the Alakazam and Metagross ruins, it would be much more difficult to investigate the validity of. He'd have to be content with keeping his ears open in the future. He wasn't about to put much stock in the idea of humans becoming pokemon and pokemon becoming humans, but he'd have refrain from dismissing it all together.

Jack: "That should cover all of it, correct?"

Human!Chrome: Yes. It's been nice, talking with you.

Jack: "Likewise. Thank you for the information."

Jack gave a courteous nod to the man, not quite leaving yet in case any last-minute thoughts came to his mind he wanted to say. But if not, he was well-prepared to get on his way and start searching for those ruins...

Human!Chrome: And now I must be off. Hope to see you again.

-walks away, thinking -

Human!Chrome: Heh... Wonder how much he'll find out for himself...
