"Too true. Luxray, Rain Dance!"

A small rainstorm kicked up in the gym, Rex groaning in response.

"I knew it was too good to be true..."

<Suggestions?> Rascal asked, lowering himself to the ground as the Luxray closed in.

"Oh, erm...hit him with Return!"

"Return?" Volkner asked. "So, that would make this your starter, huh?" He chuckled to himself, Luxray offering a small smile of his own. "This really is a great match."

"Yeah..." Rex found himself agreeing. "It is."
