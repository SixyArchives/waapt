Mt Moon Summit

Crewe and Conquistador have finally made it up the mountain; fortunately for Crewe, the Ponyta colt is too exhausted to demand any sort of activity or adventure she's in no condition to provide, and simply finds a grassy spot to graze while his Trainer rests on a nearby rock.

She's tired. The earlier battle, coupled with the long trek up the mountain, has taken its toll on her concentration. She can barely keep herself stabilized; her eyes remain bright red and there is a haze around her hands and feet. She rubs fiercely at her eyes.

Stay awake. Don't slip up.

While she is concentrating on this thought—and at keeping a transformation at bay, she becomes aware of a peculiar sensation coming from off to the right: a feeling of being drawn, as well as a warm feeling deep in her core—not unlike what she feels when she transforms, but yet... different. Dropping her hands from her eyes, Crewe looks to her right to find the source, and notices a Moon Stone in the grass, sparkling in the Liv of the full moon.

Ever so slowly, she gets to her feet and walks over, the pulling sensation getting stronger, and kneels beside it. Crewe reaches out a hand and touches the Stone, making an alarmed sound: the stone, despite lying on the ground on top of a mountain at night, is warm. Crewe feels a shock as she touches it that travels through her hand and up her arm. Her entire outline blurs and she draws away quickly, resting her hands on her knees as she gets her concentration back. She looks up and waves Conquistador, startled at her sudden noise, back to his grass.

Crewe studies the Moon Stone carefully, then lifts it from the grass. Prepared this time, she manages to keep from losing control. The warm feeling in her core increases, and she realizes that despite her original impression, it is actually very different from the way her transformation feels, while still remaining fundamentally similar. She slips the stone in her pocket, and gets to her feet again.

It's getting late... I shouldn't stay up here, they might come looking for me. I'll just have to be careful tonight.

As she takes her concentration from the task of keeping herself together to form these thoughts, her whole arm blurs. Crewe quickly gets it back under control and sighs as she goes to get Conquistador. It would be a long night at the campsite.
