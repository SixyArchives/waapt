171134 Luke92424th Jun 2012 09:39:28 AM Get RP Mod

Starcutter - Yesterday

Having made there way to the control room of the Starcutter, a certain Charizard, Kingdra and Sceptile approach the well-dressed Elgyem working at the control panel, the alien Pokémon briefly halting his work to engage in a conversation with them.

Magolor: <Hey, what's up?>

Phyco floats forward slightly, asserting himself as their side's representative in this conversation.

Phyco: <Well, since that Dunsparce and Purrloin seem to be making little, if any effort to help you repair your spacecraft, me and my acquaintances here have decided to take matters into our own tails, or claws, in their case, and get to work on finding the remaining parts of the Starcutter.>

In the background, Comet frowns to herself.

Comet: <What, so we're nothing more than acquaintances to him?>

Phyco: <So, if you would be so kind, could you point us in the direction of the next piece of your ship?>

Unfortunately, Phyco does not receive the expected answer, the Elgyem simply responding with a shrug.

Magolor: <I wish I could tell you where it was, but my scanners still haven't been able to locate the next part just yet.>

Phyco: <...What?>

Annoyed, the Kingdra snorts angrily.

<You mean to say that despite the advanced technology at your fingertips, you haven't been able to do something as simple as track down a part of your ship?!>

Magolor: <It would under normal circumstances, but the scanning systems were damaged in the crash and aren't running at top performance. Right now, I can only scan so much of the planet's landscape at a time, which means I have a lot of ground to cover.>

Phyco: <Well...that's just typical.>

The Kingdra looks turns away with an annoyed huff, leaving Treeo to take over.

Treeo: <Is there anything else we could do for you, though?>

Comet: <Yeah! No offense, but there isn't much to do on your ship, and sitting around doing nothing all day is beginning to drive me crazy!>

Magolor stops for a second, rubbing his hands together as he thinks for a few moments.

Magolor: <...There is something you can do.>

Treeo: <Oh?>

Comet: <What is it?>

Phyco: <...?>

Magolor turns back to his control panel, manipulating the buttons on his control panel with his telekinetic powers to bring up the image of a golden gear like object on the Starcutter's display.

Magolor: <What you see here is an energy sphere.>

Phyco: <...That's a gear, not a sphere. Unless you aliens got them the wrong way around?>

Annoyed, Comet punches Phyco's bubble, shaking the Kingdra inside around a bit.

<HEY!>

Comet: <Shut up and let him talk!>

Treeo: <...You were saying?>

Magolor: <Yes...Are all Pokémon on this planet like this?>

he shrugs to himself, then continues with his explanation.

<Now, while these are not essential for the Starcutter to fly, they are needed for the ship to operate at it's full power. Unfortunately, however, most of them came loose as the ship crashed down to this world. Like the essential parts, they've been scattered across the local area.>

He taps a few more buttons, bringing up a map of the continent, three areas highlighted in particular.

<My scans have pinpointed that most of the energy spheres have ended up in the places know as Cookie Country, The Rasin Ruins, and the Onion Ocean.>

Comet blinks, tilting her head curiously.

<"Cookie Country?" "Raisin Ruins?" "Onion Ocean?" Did Stratus name these places while he was hungry?>

Magolor: <...Stratus?>

Treeo: <Friend of ours. Bit of a glutton.>

Magolor: <Oh, I see. Anyway, while Mezzo, Every and their Pokémon have visited these areas to recover parts to the Starcutter, they seem to have neglected to search for any of these Spheres while they were there.>

Phyco: <There's a reason for that, it's because they a bunch of useless->

Again, Phyco is interrupted, this time by Comet picking his bubble up and shaking it furiously.

Comet: <STOP BEING SO RUDE!>

Phyco: <ARGH! PUT ME DOWN, YOU INSANE FIRE LIZARD!>

Comet does so. By dropping him the now dazed Kingdra to the floor, the dizzy seahorse now floating upside down in his back, a dazed look on his face.

Magolor: <Oh my. Is that how you punish Pokémon from misconduct here?>

Comet: <Well, it works for him...>

Phyco: <Urk.> @_@

Magolor: <...Alright. Now, if you wouldn't mind, could you go back to these places and see if you could find any energy spheres for me? I appreciate it immensely if you were to be kind enough to do that for me.>

Comet smiles, nodding eagerly.

Comet: <Sure, why not? Sounds like fun.>

Treeo: <I'd be happy to help. Even if this does seem awfully similar to our hunt for the shards.>

Comet: <Shards, Energy Spheres, who cares? It just means that we have two things to keep an eye out for now!>

Treeo: <I guess that wouldn't be too hard, then.>

Magolor gives the three the Elgyem equivalent of a smile. In this case, he flashes his hand-lights happily

Magolor: <Thank you so much! I'm sure with your help, we'll have the Starcutter back in perfect working order in no time!>

Comet: <Yeah! You can count on us! Now, how about we go and give Cookie Country a visit?>

Treeo: <Sounds like the best place to start, and the least dangerous.>

Recovering from being shaken around, Phyco flips back upright, skaing his head to clear the last of the dizziness.

Phyco: <Oof. Can't we go to the Onion Ocean first? I barely got to spend any time in the sea on our last visit?>

Treeo: <...Er...>

Comet: <You're more than welcome to head there and search by yourself, if you can handle it, that is.>

She turns to Treeo, nodding.

<Come on, let's get this show on the road.>

The two head for the exit, leaving Phyco alone with Magolor.

Magolor: <...Are you going with them? Or-?>

Phyco: <Of course not. I'm perfectly capable of handling the Onion Ocean by myself.>

There's a pause as Phyco thinks this over, before letting out a bubbly sigh.

<Who am I kidding...>

He then floats off after Comet and Treeo. Although he didn't want to admit it, he had a better chance of staying alive with them then he did by himself.

<Wait for me! Some of us don't have legs, you know!>
