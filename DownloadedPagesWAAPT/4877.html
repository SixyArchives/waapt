Fallarbor Town

After collecting his mother, Rex (or his childish aspect anyway) has decided to wander about town, taking his Pokemon along with him in order to get them some exercise. Spathi seemed a little grumpy from being woken up, but he capitulated after Rex mentioned a nearby Pokeblock machine in the contest hall. Ichtacya and Blossom also decided to tag along, chatting about how adorable the new Eevee was and what it would evolve into.

Blossom: <Well, I suppose the ultimate decision would go to my son...but an Espeon would be a nice choice, don't you think?>

Ichtacya: <Oh, Espeon are nice. Maybe li'l Eevee could meet Sol when Trainer comes back!>

Blossom: <Perhaps. Kim has always said good things about Silent and his Pokemon.>

Ichtacya: <Really? Even me? :3>

Blossom: <Even you.> *smiles* <She says that you're an adorable tag-a-long.>

Ichtacya: <Eeee~>

At the forefront of the group, Aqua lead the way, a spring in her step as she paraded her little brother around town. As usual, the little furball was clinging to her back, burbling happily at his sister's good mood. Rascal was to their left, smiling as he watched his two children.

Aqua: <So, papa, what will baby brother be when he grows up?>

Rascal: <I'm not sure. He may choose to be one of the Eeveelutions that we are. Or, he might decide on another form.>

Aqua: <Oh, yay! I hope he becomes something new!> *frowns* <Not Jolteon, though. Ewww, electric-types.> *realizes what she said and sweatdrops* <...No offense, Ohm.>

Ohm: <N-none taken...> *bleat*

Rascal: *shakes his head in some amusement* <That's his decision to make, Aqua.>

Aqua: <Okay, papa...> *lowers her voice to a whisper so that only the baby Eevee can hear* <Please don't be a yucky Jolteon. :3>

Baby Eevee: *giggles happily, pawing at Aqua's back*

In the center of the Pokemon, Rex and Faith chatted amicably, seeming more like good friends rather than a mother and her son. In Faith's hands was Sulfur, the woman deciding to keep him where she could see him in order to prevent him from getting into mischief. Rex, meanwhile, was being used as a perch for Amia and Tanith. The former had cuddled into his bushy hair, while the latter had wrapped around his shoulders again. Faith was slightly worried at the sight of the Seviper around her son's neck, though it was more out of fear of accidental poisoning than the idea of the snake being openly malicious. She briefly toyed with the idea of getting something to put on the Pokemon's tail when she noticed Rex was staring at her. It seems he had been asking her a question.

Rex: Mom?

Faith: Sorry sorry; I kind of zoned out there. What were you asking?

Rex: I was asking which Pokemon you think I should deposit with Professor Elm? See, I can't deposit Rascal because it'd be rude to take him away from his son. And Amia and Tanith are still kind of unfamiliar with most of my Pokemon, so it wouldn't be fair to deposit them immediately. And I wanted to train Sulfur, but I ended up getting more Pokemon than I planned. *sigh* Deciding which Pokemon to use can be so bothersome sometimes...

Faith smiled, patting her son's back gently. It was nice to see that all of the recent events hadn't dulled his spirit. Perhaps things would turn out well after all.
