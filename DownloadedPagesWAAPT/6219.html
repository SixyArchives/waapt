-The blue one said that she was nicer than I made her out to be... That's really not comforting to hear about a mon that took your flight... And those jaws- *shudder* I can't believe that humans find her kind endearing-

<Vesta: "Actually... Let me try something...">

<(Ex-)Goon!Conkeldurr: "... Try what?">

-Really, the people and mons here baffled me for the longest time... You caught a mon that had blood on his claws... And OWES his present existence to that... Because he was too cowardly to stand firm and be honorable... To not try and hide butchery behind excuses of it being under the "Hunter's Code" to try and sleep better at night... To not uproot and destroy lives because of fear for his own... To not have to know what it's like to have to turn on the closest thing that you have to a friend and someone who can relate to your guilt, your feelings of utter worthlessness, and to see that look of betrayal in their eyes...

Why would you not kill such a creature on the spot? And if not, why wouldn't you make his life utter hell?-

-A chiming noise can be heard-

<Kammy: "Oh, so that's what you were up to, Vesta.">

<Vesta: "Indeed... Does it feel any better?">

<(Ex-)Goon!Conkeldurr: "A bit, yeah...">

-For the first month or two here... I remember being scared... Of everything. I was afraid of the nurses... The mons who actually took care of me while my carapace was still in pieces and my left wing was still being together by some staples and a cast... I was afraid of the others that were brought here, everyone where I came from had heard stories of infiltrators... Mons that served as judge, jury, and executioner for anyone who dared to let themselves be captured alive... I was afraid of those refugees that were here. That we would be turned over them to be lynched. I was afraid of the humans and the mons here... Especially the ones who were from Kanto... I mean, you have your enemy more or less in your hands... Why not squish him and call it a day? And what good is a Flygon that's grounded? For the first couple of months, I more or less saw myself as a slave in relation to the others. If you shut up and did what they told you, they might leave you alone... And if you didn't... Well... There's a big world out there that's more than ready to snuff you out...-
