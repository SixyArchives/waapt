"Use Weather Ball, quick!"

"Grotle too? Okay then...here comes the last one!"

A Roserade appeared following another flash of light, the sunlight fading as the Gym returned to its normal coloration. The two Pokemon eyed each other curiously, Claudia's body returning to normal before the two trainers shouted out their next commands.
