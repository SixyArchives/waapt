Recovery Room

-Thyme realizes that she and Tempest are alone, for the most part, and having spent most of last night thinking about it, decides to get something off her chest.-

Thyme: <...Tempest?>

Tempest: <Oh, what is it, Thyme?>

Thyme: <There's something I want to tell you.>

Tempest: <Oh...Hang on.>

-Tempest climbs onto Thyme's bed, sitting next to her.-

Tempest: <Okay, go ahead.>

Thyme: -Looks awkward.- <Well, yesterday, when Ferno was about to...finish me off, I couldn't help but think about, well...how sad my death would probably make you feel.>

Tempest: <Oh. Well, you're right. I would be devastated if I lost you, Thyme...You're the best friend that I've ever had...And I can't imagine doing anything without you there with me.>

-Tempest reaches down and strokes a healthy part of Thyme's skin gently, the Serperior relaxing slightly as he does this.-

Tempest: <You've been so nice to me, Thyme. Even when I just wanted to be alone, you never gave up on me.>

Thyme: <Why would I have done that, though? You're so sweet Tempest. I don't see how anymon wouldn't like you. Which makes it all the more unfair that you had to be a Delta.>

Tempest: <You're the same, Thyme.> -Sighs.- <I wish that we weren't Delta Pokémon sometimes. Then we would never have gotten involved in this whole mess in the first place.>

Thyme: <...But then we never would have met, would we?>

Tempest: <...Yeah, we wouldn't of...>

-The two gaze at each other, both glad that things had conspired in such a way to make their meeting possible. Finally gathering the courage to say what she wished to say, Thyme raises her neck and gently nuzzles Tempest's head.-

Thyme: <Tempest...I love you.>

-Upon saying those words, Thyme's stomach does a cart-wheel. Why did she have to say it? How would Tempest react? Would he reject her? Would he only want to be friends? Would things ever be the same between them again? Should she had just kept quiet? All those questions and more raced through the Serperior's mind.

...But all of them became meaningless when Tempest gently kissed her on the head, then gazed at her, a warm smile on his face.-

Tempest: <I love you too.>

-Elated, Thyme nuzzles Tempest lovingly, both of the Pokémon glad that, no matter how bad things got, they would have each other to help them through it.-
