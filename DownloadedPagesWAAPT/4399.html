 109965 Crewe, Mon, 7th Nov '11 6:58:35 PM from Johto Get RP Mod
He's yours now Wolf, do what you will with him.

Crewe sits down on the log outside the tent, holding her head in her hands and breathing deeply.

No more... No more... No more releasings, no more unhappiness. I'll be a Trainer now. I have to be a Trainer.

After a few minutes, she slowly lifts her head to see the remnants of her team gathered in a semicircle around her, not too close. All have various levels of concern on their faces (yes, even Hecate, though I bet none of you saw that coming, did you?), and Crewe smiles weakly at them, hoping she didn't start crying. Which she probably did, but she doesn't want to admit it and so ignores it.

"Hi, guys..."

Dana runs over and props herself up on her knee.

<Trainer?> the little Rattata tilts her head.

Crewe scratches her behind the ear, then speaks in the hopes of cutting her off in case she was intending to ask about Robin.

"Hi, Dana. Do you want to do something fun? Like we used to?"

Dana immediately perks up. <Yay! Fun! Let's do something fun!> She drops down and chases her tail a little.

Crewe scoops her up and sets her on her shoulder, then stands up and faces her Pokemon. She clears her throat and starts talking.

"I'm sorry, all of you. I haven't been fair to you, I know. And I know I've made promises I haven't been so great at keeping..." she locks eyes with Hecate for a moment, then the Zoroark looks away.

"But that ends today. I swear to you all, I will be the Trainer you've deserved from the start, the Trainer I never was. And none of you will ever go unloved, as long as you remain on this team, and forever afterwards."

She takes a deep breath and pets Dana.

"From now on, I promise I won't be Battler. I will do the best I can for each and every one of you, but I will never cross the line again."

She locks eyes with Skywalker. "I'm sorry, Skywalker. I never should've done that to you. And I'm sorry that you found it okay for me to act that way."

He opens his mouth to speak, then closes it and looks away. Crewe turns back to the group.

"Now, if it's okay with all of you... I think it's time we found a new place to explore. As a team."

Electivire steps forward and thrusts her fist into the air.

<As a team!>

<AS A TEAM!> the rest of her Pokemon join in, Dana waving her paw in the air, and Hecate even smiling a little, if almost imperceptibly.

Crewe smiles and hugs Electivire. The rest of her Pokemon rush forward and join the group hug, and Crewe buries her grin in Electivire's shoulder, glad to feel as if her team is finally the family it was really meant to be, all along.
