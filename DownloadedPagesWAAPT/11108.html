Ignis, in the dead of night.

A figure stares from the palace walls onto the Ignis courtyard and palace. She is constantly moving, taking a good look at the location of the palace guards as wells a find an opening inside. Miu is looking around to see if there are any of Izumi's personal gaurd are going to sneak up on them.

Tsubasa: There's gotta be an opening somewhere...

Miu gently pecks Tsubasa and points to a vacant opening on the 3rd floor.

Tsubasa: Thanks boy! You're the best!

Miu flies Tsubasa over to the opening. She unpacks a few nessesities and gestures to Miu.

Tsubasa: Stay here boy. I'll be back soon

Miu titters in response and lies low as Tsubasa ducks into the castle. She slips, slides and weaves her way through till she comes to Izumi's personal chambers.

She stares at the sleeping figure in the room for a while, before snapping out of it and gently ghosting over the floorboards, not even so much as a creak, until she is looming over the sleeping warlord. She removes her gloves and silently and softly gets to work removing the necklace.

Tsubasa: C'mon... C'mon...

Tsubasa's hands glide quickly to the back of Izumi's neck. The intensity of the moment causing sweat to bead on Tsubasa's forehead. It feels like she's been at this for ages until...

  • Click*

She undoes the clasp and pulls the necklace gently away from Izumi's neck. Tsubasa grins madly as she inspects the necklace. Though, the amount of oxidisation and grime on the necklace leave an imprint on Tsubasa's hand.

Tsubasa: Urgh... Has this tacky old thing ever been washed? No matter, Hikari 1 - Mad Lady 0

Tsubasa sneaks out of the room, she turns back to give one smug gring to Izumi before.

Tsubasa: Oh, Crap...
