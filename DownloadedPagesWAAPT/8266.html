Stratus: <Look at it this way: Lake has had eleven years of not knowing who on Earth his father is to build up a mental image of some sort of super awesome Pokémon to be his father. Kinda' like...>

<Imagine a friend of yours tells you about, say...a really juicy piece of Stantler flank they had the other day.>

Deluge nodded, her lower jaw hanging open somewhat at the thought of this.

Deluge: <Go on...>

Stratus: <Right. Now, imagine that you spend a long time trying to hunt down a Stantler so that you can try their flank for yourself. And when you finally do manage to take one out and take a bite...>

<Unless it's a really darn good piece of Stantler, more often than not, it won't live up to the mental image you built up in your head as you were hunting for it, you get what I'm saying?>

Deluge: <...Oh yeah...>

Stratus turned to look back at Deluge, a cascade of saliva now dripping down from her open jaws, a ravenous look on her face.

Stratus: <Deluge...you're drooling.>

The Feraligatr blinked, only to notice that she was, indeed, dribbling all over the place. She quickly wiped the saliva away with the back of her arm before giving Stratus a Mareepish look.

Deluge: <A-heh...sorry. But I get the point you're making here.>

She frowned, shrugging.

<But are you sure that this is how Lake feels about you?>

The Dragonite frowned at this.

Stratus: <Haven't you noticed that since he found out I was his Father, Lake hasn't called me "Dad" or "Pop" or anything like that even once?>

The Feraligatr said nothing, pausing awkwardly.

Deluge: <...I thought that was because you two were on a first name basis?>

Stratus shook his head.

Stratus: <Nope. He even said so himself yesterday that he still didn't see me as his Dad...>

Deluge: <...Oh, wow.>

It was then that the Feraligatr in question showed up, possibly accompanied by Ursula and anyone else who had decided to come along. Thankfully though, he hadn't heard any of the prior conversation.

Lake: <Hey...how are things going? Keep your distance, guys. If she gets hungry, that bubble won't hold Mom for long.>
