184130 Luke92417th Aug 2012 05:45:43 AM Get RP Mod

Winner's Path

Of the three of them, Lake is the first to wake up, squinting at the morning light as it shines on him through his Bubble Shield. He let out a loud yawn, then turned to check on Luke and Stratus, who were still asleep.

Luke: "Zzz...Skye..."

Stratus: <Zzz...Valiant! Watch out for the truck!>

What a coincidence. They were both dreaming about Skarmory.

Smirking at this, Lake sat up, leaning against the inside of his bubble as he took in his surroundings.

It was hard to believe, but...they were finally home. In their world. After months of hopping between two other universes - one completely unlike their own, one more similar, but still vastly different - they were back where they belonged. And it felt so good.

But the question remained: What were they going to do now?

Of course, they were likely going to head back home to the PEFE to let the rest of the team and let them know they were okay, as well as meet those new arrivals Luke had mentioned catching before he had come universe hopping with them.

There was also the matter of his family. He had a good idea where they could be right now, but...he wasn't going to do that for a while yet. Mainly because Nadia was busy in the other universe right now, but partly because of nerves. Reuniting with your family so long seemed like a harrowing prospect.

So the road ahead seemed uncertain, and at times, somewhat rough...but at least it was across more familiar ground.
