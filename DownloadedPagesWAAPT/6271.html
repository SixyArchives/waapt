Route 32 - PMD-B

In spite of several hundred years passing, along with the destruction of humanity, to the casual observer, very little seemed to have changed about Route 32. It was still miles of seemingly endless grassland that eventually gave way to coastline, with very little to break the greenery apart from the odd tree, hill, or a ruined structure in the south that Lake assumed was what was left of the Goldenrod Magnet train line.

There was very little in the way of noise, too, apart from the odd Pidgey tweeting or Spearow cawing every now again, or the wind blowing through the grass.

It was actually quite peaceful. But Lake couldn't really relax, he was still in shock that he and Nadia, of all Pokémon, had founded Bluescale several hundred years ago after the Draconic Uprising, and he couldn't think how.

Sure, he had experience in leading a Pokémon team, but that was only six Pokémon compared to a whole community looking to him for guidance, and he had no idea how he would go about keeping things running smoothly, and while he apparently had Nadia's help...well, she didn't really seem like much of a leader either.

He was glad she hadn't heard that last thought, as she probably wouldn't be happy with him.

Trying to put aside his worries for the moment, Lake shades his eyes and scans the horizon for any sign of life.
