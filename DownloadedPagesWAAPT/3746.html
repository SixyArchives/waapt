93644 Luke92424th Sep 2011 09:24:57 AM Get RP Mod

Tarroco Island - Beach

-If Harriet could chirp, she would.

However, Honchkrow could only caw loudly instead. So she had to make do with cawing with joy instead.

She was far too happy to care at her lack of vocal grace, though. After what had seemed like an eternity of being in stuck in that coral reef, almost constantly submerged in cold, smothering water, rendering unable to stretch her wings and simply fly, they had finally returned to land...And fresh air at last.

Fresh air that she could spread her wings and soar effortlessly through.

The feeling of simply flapping her wings and flying was liberating. Almost overwhelming, even. Never had the simple act of flying made Harriet feel so...happy.

She had taken it for granted, really. Flying was an amazing ability, and it was only once she had it taken away that she had realized this.

It was true what they say: You don't miss something until it's gone.-

Pleo: <Hey, Harriet!>

-The Honchkrow was shaken out of her thoughts by the voice of Pleo calling up to her. Guessing that he probably wanted to talk to her about something, Harriet swoops down and lands in front of him.-

Harriet: <Hey, Pleo! What's up?>

Pleo: <Oh, I've just been watching you fly! You're really good!>

Harriet: -Grins.- <Aw, thanks!>

-A thought occurred to Harriet. Pleo was a bird, so surely he could fly as well? In fact...She hadn't seen him fly at all in the short time they were on Tarroco island before taking him down to the Reef. Why was that?-

Harriet: -Tilts her head.- <Pleo, can you fly?>

Pleo: <Oh. Um...> -Looks awkward.- <...Not really.>

Harriet: <Huh? Not at all?>

Pleo: <Well, my Dad showed me a few things, but he left before he could finish teaching me.>

Harriet: <Aw, that's awful, Pleo.>

-Jeez, he couldn't even teach him to fly properly before he left? Lugia was beginning to sound like an unfit father.-

Harriet: <What did he manage to teach you?>

Pleo: <Only how to glide, so far.>

Harriet: <Right...>

-Hmm. That was better than nothing, but still nowhere near as good as true flight.

An idea suddenly stuck the Honchkrow. Pleo was a bird. She was a bird. Birds teach younger birds to fly, so...Why couldn't she do the same with Pleo? He was going to be traveling with them for the time being anyway, and it didn't seem like his Dad would be coming back any time soon.

The only problem was that Lugia might not be so keen on the thought of a stranger like her teaching his son to fly. After all, teaching your own hatchlings to fly was an important experience for a parent bird Pokémon.

But then...It didn't seem fair on Pleo for him to wait until his father came back from goodness knows where in order for him to learn how to fly. So there was really no reason why she shouldn't teach him instead.

And if Lugia didn't return until after she had finished teaching him, he would just have to deal with it.

...Or he could get angry and vaporise her with Areoblast.

Maybe that wouldn't happen. Harriet hoped so, anyway.-

Harriet: <Hey, Pleo? Since you're going to be with us for the time being, would you like it if I taught you how to fly?>

Pleo: <Huh? You...You would really show me how?>

Harriet: -Nods.- <Sure. There's really no reason why I couldn't show you a thing or-WHOA!>

-Harriet suddenly finds herself on the receiving end of a great big hug from Pleo.-

Pleo: <Thank you so much, Harriet! You're such a nice Honchkrow! I'll be the best behaved student I can be for you! Promise!>

Harriet: -Blushes.- <Aww, you're a good kid, so I bet you will. Now...>

-Pleo lets go of Harriet, allowing her to spread her wings.-

Harriet: <...Let's get started, shall we?>
