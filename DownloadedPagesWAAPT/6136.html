PMD

-far out of Treasure Town, a second dungeon lies. This one is a large salt flat, extending as far as the eye can see. The sun beating down makes it hard to see, but black dots marching (or flying, in the case of a few) along in orderly groups are easy enough to make out. In the center, if you can get that far, a massive marble monolith rises, sunlight reflecting off it and adding to the haze that surrounds the entire area. Inside, there is a large network of pathways and rooms, lit by the sun filtering through narrow slits by the top, as well as the glowing crystals that light many homes in the PMD. Pokemon—Bisharp, Houndoom, and the occasional Honchkrow or Mandibuzz—hurry through these halls, all appearing to somewhere to be and something to do-

-at the very top of the tower is the largest room. At the north end, farthest from the entrance to the hall, is a window, wrapping around most of the far wall at eye level, but very narrow. On the east wall is a large, black, polished area where the marble has been smoothed to a shine. On the west wall there are maps pinned with utmost precision, and a few newspapers and scrawled reports-

-a shiny Zoroark stands at the north end, looking out the window calmly, with her paws held behind her back. Her mane falls just a little over the side of her face, obscuring one eye-

-after a few moments, an Alakazam enters the room-

Zoroark: -without turning around- Ah, Cesar... Would you bring up the image, please?

Cesar: Yes, Black Knight.

-the Alakazam turns to the polished section of the wall and closes his eyes, raising his spoons. They begin glowing, and on the wall an image of a large orb, at the top of a tower, appears. The Zoroark turns from the window to look at it. She smiles slightly-

Black Knight: The Time Space Orb... When will we be prepared to retrieve it?

Cesar: We've been training the troops on methods of getting through the tower with minimum casualties. We should be ready... by about next week.

Black Knight: So soon... Excellent work, Cesar. And your team is certain these methods will work?

Cesar: -nods- We have some of the brightest minds from around. Many of them have lived or attempted to pass through Destiny Tower themselves.

Black Knight: Yes, I know.

-she reaches out and nearly touches the image, but doesn't to avoid disrupting it-

Black Knight: When everything is ready, we will move out immediately. Soon, the Time Space Orb will be ours, and then our organization will be completely unopposed.

Cesar: Uh, you know that Van Kleiss is—

Black Knight: Do not speak to me about that ridiculous character. Van Kleiss will not retrieve the orb before we do, no matter how hard he tries. If he tries... then we will deal with him.

-she turns from the wall back to the window-

Black Knight: Dismissed. Continue with your work.

Cesar: Yes, ma'am.

-he retreats from the room, then image fading. Black Knight continues staring at the narrow window, eyes narrowed against the sun, a smile on her face-
Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
