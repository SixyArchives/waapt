At the PEFE HQ Chapel

-Vinollo and Rize are comforting each other-

Rize — <...and Fibben... heh, weird name for an Ursaring... his Trainer was a high school kid, I think...>

Vinollo (smiles somewhat) — <What ever happened to him?>

Rize — <Who knows. Released all of his Pokémon then went to live to another region with his family. Or something...>

Vinollo (turns head) — <He was the kind of Ursaring that liked Wiki Berries. Like the Aipoms do... did...>

-both stare idly-

Rize — <Those Aipom twins made a good team. Covered my back for quite a while...>

Vinollo — <All of them were taken down despite their training... ugh... good thing I didn't have the time to call Roa for this... otherwise... >

Rize (tilts head) — <Roa?>

-Vinollo looks away awkwardly, Rize is about to pursue the matter when he hears something and looks around; he notices Maekrite, who walks in before Vinollo and bows; Rize takes the moment to clean his face a bit-

Vinollo — <Oh! If isn't it the human's caretaker.> — stands up — <Greetings, Maekrite. Any problem?>

Maekrite (nods) — <My greetings, Maestro. General, too...> — bows to Rize, returns sight to Vinollo — <I, uh, am released from the medical care...>

Rize (smiles) — <Good for you. We were thinking you were taking too long already.>

-Maekrite shies-

Maekrite — <Uh... I was spending some time with my parents...>

-both Nidorino blink, then let out a simultaneous "oh" and ask Maekrite to continue-

Maekrite — <Well... I was wondering... sir, > — lowers head — <how soon must I be ready to return with you... I'd like to be ready for->

Vinollo (tilts head, gets serious) — <What you mean "I"? You are not giving yourself assignments, Milite.>

Maekrite (head to the ground) — <Yes... no, Maestro... I just thought... with this battle, we would return...>

-Vinollo separates from his brother and walks around Maekrite, brushing his flank in a commandeering manner-

Vinollo — <Didn't we talk about this? Or didn't the beating last time horn-drilled this in your brain well enough?> — snorts, turns around Maekrite — <You are assigned to protect the human. I thought that was what made you happy, besides.>

Maekrite — <I just->

Vinollo — <No hesitation, kit! You are to protect and accompany him, it is the edict of our culture, of the human one, and very explicitly of u Frithrah.> — walks in front — <We will decide when we have had enough... which granted, might very well be now, but you are to go where Silent goes.>

Rize (leans on the ground casually) — <He is with these humans, trying to solve the situation that afflicts this world. We have heard about what happened to the Time Pony from the other mons around, you know. Sounds pretty messed up.>

Vinollo — <We have decided to support Frithvaorah where we can. We might have reached the point where we must leave him and return. But if Silent goes ahead, it is your duty to protect him.>

Rize — <Isn't that what you would do, anyway?>

Maekrite — <And if he doesn't?>

-Maekrite leans his head to where I am, sitting there distracted, then lowers his head to Vinollo and nods in agreement-

Rize — <You're lying to yourself, young kit.>

Maekrite (blinks) — <Lying?>

Vinollo — <You follow him back if he goes back, obviously. But... If Silent went to where there was danger and I ordered you to stay put with us, and ignore him, would you?>

-Maekrite raises his head and looks at his superior somewhat dumbfounded, as if trying to discern a challenge in the words of his superior, then tries to mumble something, shakes his head-

Maekrite — <You wouldn't... he is important to the Colony... not only here... You would instead order me to fet->

Vinollo (leers) — <That is not the question you were given.> — sits in front — <So?>

-Maekrite flinches, sighs and shakes his head-

Rize (chuckles) — <Hah... see? Such simple soul.>

-Vinollo walks to Maekrite and stares him down, with a peaceful tone-

Vinollo — <We are... probably... going back to the Colony, and there we will give the families of the fallen the news. And we will return to our lives, > — swings a paw around somewhat distractedly — <somewhat. We will do our job.>

Rize — <Yeah... heh, with our loses we'll have to, uh, repopulate.>

Vinollo — <And you will go do yours.>

Rize — <And spend the time you have with your Trainer.>

Vinollo — <Is that clear? You go and protect him from danger.> — smiles — <And when the battle ends you bring us back news from Frithvaorah.>

Rize (growls) — <Ah, yes, remember to do that! Surely our elders will be eager to add the tales of those battles beyond imagination to our lore.>

-Maekrite considers for a moment, then bows to Vinollo-

Maekrite — <I will.>

-He bows again, to be carefreely smacked in the head by both brothers, who smile at him. Vinollo then dismissed Maekrite and sends him away, to sit by my side-

-Vinollo and Rize return to their musings, and Vinollo breathes somewhat alleviated when he notices Rize has forgot to ask him about Roa-
