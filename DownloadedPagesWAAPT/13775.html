Chiptune DJ

Hermit Jarod's House

Valorie's mind was slightly adrift as Jarod told her stories about the evils of the man and mainstream training. Great—He was literally a dirty hippie. How did she get herself into this, again? She sighed and Jarod stopped in his tracks. "...Ah—You don't look like you're the type for any of that rough nonsense anyway." he admitted, waving a hand dismissively.

"Yeah... I should go." Valorie replied, in an entirely lackluster mood. She needed a doctor for crying out loud; ...that and a bath.

Jarod bit his bottom lip. "Are you sure?" he asked in a disappointed tone.

"Yeah..." Valorie echoed half-heartedly.

"...Well, as much as it was nice having company I suppose I should let you go. I'll ask Rex to walk you back, just to make sure you're safe. Which city do you want him to take you to?" Jarod asked, moving to head out the door with Valorie close behind.

He moved out behind the house, to which Rex greeted him with a powerful grunt. Observing the two's body language Valorie quickly concluded that Rex didn't have a lot of respect for Jarod.

"Hey... listen; I want you to keep Rex. Maybe you can train him better than I did." Jarod remarked, turning to Valorie.

"Wait, really..?" Valorie responded, taken off guard.

<I am NOT servant to some prissy little not-to-be-taken-seriously girl!> Rex protested angrily in the form of a roar loud enough to make Jarod sway as it was directed at him.

Jarod turned, getting the general gist of what was being said. "...C'mon, we've been together this long and that's how you repay me?'' he asked, folding his arms. Rex lowered his head in an almost threatening manner, staring Jarod down.

"Uh..." Valorie started, slightly uncomfortable.

The tense moment passed and Rex stood back up, grunting dismissively.

<As you wish... I will make it my duty to protect this girl, but this will be the last favor I do for you.> Tyrantrum grimly remarked, but it was lost on the two humans.

"Wow, this monster is a piece of work." Shu remarked to Valorie's mind only. Val furrowed her brows as Jarod nodded in approval and moved to hand Rex's pokeball to her. She took the ball; it needed a bath, or maybe an acid bath. She was becoming increasingly intolerant of feeling filthy.

"...Thanks~" Valorie offered somewhat coyly.

"Yeah... I figure I owe you; I could get in a lot of trouble for yesterday." Jarod replied, subtly nervous. He didn't want /anyone/ coming to his house to bother him about his negligence... unless they were cute and liked conversation!

Valorie waved with her good arm as Rex began leading her away, traveling off back towards Cyllage City. She had a feeling Diane and Skye were wondering why she hadn't been around last night.
