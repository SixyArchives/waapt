"The enemies are likely heading to Azalea. That town won't hold for long!"

"When can Rize and his group make it back here?"

"We're not sure, marlirah. He fears that letting those troops advance will eventually mean the Colony will be surrounded by the enemy."

"However, if there's even a slight hint of truthfulness in that Serperior commander not wanting to damage the city, we can at least help Rize cross through the coast, since sea forces won't be firing against the city."

"We gave their commander a first reprimand for their behaviour and corresponding warning shot some hours ago, during the skirmish that resulted in Frithvaorah leaving the city under chase. We managed to protect him and his Pokémon, then a display of 36% of our immediate poisonstorming power at the basic level left the entirely of the Commander's troop badly poisoned, according to our reports."

Breeder #2 (with glee) — <I hear from our Noctowl partners that the Serperior also got a nice surprise gift from one of us, too...>

"Yes, marlirah. The Owsla have confirmed the hit and the branding, which took place right inside the enemy column less that forty-five seconds after the Poison Deluge call was activated. The agent who executed the kill will report fully on the operation at your discretion, anytime tomorrow."

-The breeders reassume their positions, also accompanied by Taisho and two elders-

Mayonaka — <Ahem... let's see... what positions do we have left in the city?>

"Pretty much none, marlirah. The humans there fell defenseless with the very first attacks. The Nildrow were well prepared and willful, but all of us were taken by surprise. The enemy managed to destroy the spire that the humans use for communication, too..."

"From there our presence decayed sharply starting by the west side, when the last Nidoran and Rattata scouts had to evacuate the tunnels. That was the last time we got news from Silent before the recent events..."

Breeder #3 — <I see... what about the east side?>

"At first our presence there was unaffected, thanks to the secret access at Silent's house. However, the operations that resulted in the burning of the humans's item store building as well as the recovery of Frithvaorah triggered a sharp change in the enemy's policy, who began expelling us on sight."

"Between that and the fact that we ourselves have walled this route, we have pretty much no Army agents in the city. There's only some small groups by the east, helping gather the last remaining kits as well as coordinate with our assists."

Breeder #3 (sighs) — <This gets complicated. What the heck happened with the burned building anyway?>

"An emergency Owsla operation, marlirah. Silent got trapped there while fetching resources, when the enemies first attacked. He apparently understood our strategies and did something that lit up the tower..."

Breeder #1 — <This reminds me of the lore of u Frithrah's tower we had before Mr. Luke came visit...>

"It fulfilled three purposes — carried the news across the land, disabled the store and its resources temporarily while we can't access them, and drew enough attention that several kits were able to reach our route from across the city right before the curfew."

"They are being sent to Ecruteak as we speak. We hope that they'll be safe there..."

"...Honestly, we don't even know if the other humans are alive."

Mayonaka — <...Great. And I think my sarcasm there is obvious.>

"However, without Silent to explain the logistics, we will be forced to temporarily rely on Frithvaorah to have those humans work for us."

Breeder #2 — <What will happen to him?>

Breeder #3 — <I want him, or one of his Pokémon, assigned to the team that is checking our lore.> — points to Ecruteak — <With things as bad as they are getting, we need to know what else did we get wrong.>

"Yes, marlirah."
