Well, aside from being useful for purifying Pokémon, all they really do otherwise is make Pokémon happier, kind of like how it usually works with Pokémon and massages.

- Points to the first one -

- Points to the second one -

The Excite Scent is 800 Pokédollars, and is more effective than the Joy Scent.

- Points to the third -

And the Vivid Scent costs 1200 Pokédollars, and is the most effective of the three.

- Puts the scents back into the case -

Although, I also think I heard somewhere that what purification method works best depends on the Shadow Pokémon itself, as in what nature it has. So, try out different things, and see which method seems to work the best, I guess.
