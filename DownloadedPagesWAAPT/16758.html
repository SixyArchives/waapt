Illusion HQ

A familiar honey-blond is whisking through the halls of Illusion. Seemingly with no rhyme or reason, and with no place in mind to arrive at.

In fact his behaviour for the past week have been rather odd, for him at least. His hair and appearance have been wild rather unkempt - as opposed to his usual stringency with attire - with baggy, bloodshot eyes to boot. He is much more boisterous and energetic, belting out loud passages with of nothing a bravado uncharacteristic of the usually stoic and reserved Kalosian. He also seems to be much more talkative, brimming to the rim with various insane and nonsensical plans and general restlessness as well as being increasingly quick to anger. His actions towards some of the female members of Illusion seems to be different too, often winking at or randomly twirling them when he passes through the hallways when before he would pay them no mind.
