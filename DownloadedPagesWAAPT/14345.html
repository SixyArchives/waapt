"I don't think that's the case, but... Tell me, honest, you aren't angry about any of it?" asked Lyuri, looking a bit puzzled

"Well, I overreacted a bit, I knew the topic would make Herbert uncomfortable so I didn't want to bring it up with him around and I'm well-aware that he says things without thinking. I don't really hold it against him."

"I guess... Aren't you treating him as if he was special a bit? I mean, I'm sure you would be upset if it was anyone else."

"Not really. I can understand why anyone would think like him, after all we're hardly an usual couple. "Not really. I need you to stay like that if I plan on keeping this relationship going." said Lyuri, smirking.

"Hikari!" said both of them, looking deeply embarrassed.

Hikari giggles and starts recording something else.

"... Anyway, talk to him tomorrow. I feel guilty about the whole thing and you're better at helping people that I am." said Lyuri, looking at the ground, still blushing hard

"... I probably would even without you telling me to. I'm too worried about him now to think of doing something else." said Duke, sighing in worry.
