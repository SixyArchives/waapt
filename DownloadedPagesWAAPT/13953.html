Her eyes squinting shut she jolted to her feet, emitting a high pitched shriek of terror as she totally tried to pull her ears off in an ill-advised attempt to cure herself of this state as if she believed her real ears were hidden underneath. Webbing formed between her fingers followed by them too being drawn together, no doubt something similar going on underneath her boots.

"N-No! No!! Help!! Heee—!!" Valorie cried out increasingly desperately, but her voice was suddenly squelched as her vocal cords failed her. She could feel the transformation growing stronger. W-What sort of monster would do this to her? What sort of... monster was she?!

As Valorie's legs fused together she collapsed onto her knees and then her side, attempting to curl into a ball as the transformation only grew hastier and hastier.
