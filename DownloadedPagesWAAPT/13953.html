Valorie's eyes dilated in horror as she tugged on her new nose only to find that it was thoroughly attached to her face. Her blood ran cold as she felt her ears twist and stretch into the form of white fins. A shiver ran down her spine and she quickly grabbed at her ears to confirm what she'd already felt.

Her eyes squinting shut she jolted to her feet, emitting a high pitched shriek of terror as she totally tried to pull her ears off in an ill-advised attempt to cure herself of this state as if she believed her real ears were hidden underneath. She stumbled forward, feeling her legs being drawn together by an invisible force that made it increasingly difficult to walk. Webbing formed between her fingers followed by them too being drawn together, no doubt something similar going on underneath her boots.

"N-No! No!! Help!! Heee—!!" Valorie cried out increasingly desperately, but her voice was suddenly squelched as her vocal cords failed her. She could feel the transformation growing stronger. W-What sort of monster would do this to her? What sort of... monster was she?!

As Valorie's legs fused together she collapsed onto her knees and then her side, attempting to curl into a ball as the transformation only grew hastier and hastier. Soon enough... it was a dratini that lay on the ground instead of the human that had once been there, trembling in fear and refusing to open her eyes.
