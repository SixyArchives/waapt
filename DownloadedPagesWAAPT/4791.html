Rex's Mindscape

A gentle, warm breeze drifted through the area, no doubt from a heater. Wood creaked gently as several people touched down on the floor, its shine somewhat diminished by the trainers dirty shoes. From all appearances, they seemed to be in a house.

To the group’s left was a small kitchen, the floor tiled with linoleum. Several name brand cereals sat atop the fridge, some seeming as though they hadn’t been touched in a while. Other more health alternative brands seemed to be more recently used, no doubt belonging to Rex’s mother. A few containers of what appeared to be Pokémon food sat in the pantry, surrounded by other snacks such as pretzels, crackers, and fruit chews.

To the right, was a door leading to a small bedroom. In it were two beds, one slightly scrunched up as though it had been used recently. It was adorned with several plush covers, the top one with a pleasant flower design. The other was bare of anything, seeming as though it hadn’t been touched in years. A punching bag sat in the corner, worn from much use over the years. At its base were a few boxing gloves. Atop a shelf next to the bag was a set of pictures, just barely visible from that distance.

The first was a picture of Faith and a slightly younger Rex, both smiling widely at the camera lens. The second was a picture of a younger Faith, making a dramatic pose with a man some might identify as the Karate King. The third picture was slightly harder to make out, but it depicted a baby Rex being held by Faith, sometime in her late twenties perhaps? To her left was a man, though his features were near indiscernible from the light of the nearby window.

In front of the group was a small living area. A large mat lay in the center of the room, perfect for any Pokémon to lie on. In front of it was a large sofa, for any human who sought comfort. A few more exercise devices laid off to the side, barbells and such, and in the center was a television. It shone a blue light, bathing in the couch in its glow. And, from over the couch’s edge, a pair of Spinda ears could be seen, twitching happily.
