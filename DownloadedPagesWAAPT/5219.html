Kanto, Route 9. Pt 1

On top of a rocky hill, a Nidorino walks out of the warren hole for the Afternoon silflay*. After scouting around for any possible elil* or other dangers, he goes back into the burrow to tell the other Nidos that it is safe out and that there wasn't a embleer* around.

One by one, various Nidorans and Nidorino's pop out of the warren. Only 4 does emerge from the warren. For, you see, this warren was founded not that long ago by a group of rogue Nido bucks, only picking up a few hlessil* along the way.

As they graze on the grass, a large Nidorino approaches a smaller one, who seems to be pondering something.

Laurel: <Hazel-rah*, what is on your mind? You seem bothered by something.>

Hazel: <The future of the warren Laurel. That is what has been bothering me.>

Laurel takes a huge mouthful of grass and starts chewing on it

Laurel: <The future of the warren sir? Look around!>

The two glance at the various bucks eating around them.

Laurel: <Our warren is made of the healthiest, toughest bucks any warren could ask for! Considering what we have been through, its not suprising!>

Laurel gwaffs.

Hazel: <But that's the problem Laurel. We have a lot of bucks... Too many bucks>

Hazel glances to the sun.

Hazel: <The amount of bucks in our colony greatly outnumber the does, and most will not produce litters yet as they are still trying to get used to the new warren. So far, only Cloves is a marli* and even then, most of her offspring are young bucks.>

Laurel stops eating for a moment before staring Hazel.

Laurel: <What are you getting at Hazel-rah?>

Hazel: <I am saying that after a few years, there will be no more does. Without any does there will be no more litters and a few more years after that. No Warren>

Laurel gulps his food.

Laurel: <Are you suggesting that wae go out and find does?>

Hazel: <Indeed, and considering the Spearow gossip I have overheard. I may know of a place where we can find does that would willingly leave their colony.>

Laurel: <And what is this place sir?>

Hazel pauses, eats a small tuft of grass and answers Laurel.

Hazel: <It is simply known as Efrafa.>

  • Silflay: Above ground food.
  • Elil: Predator.
  • Embleer: "Stinking, Awful smell", often used as a term meaning "the smell of a predator".
  • Hlessil: Nidos that don't live in a warren/rogues.
  • Rah: "Prince" a title indicating power. Often used for the Chief Nido of a warren.
  • Marli: Mother doe.
