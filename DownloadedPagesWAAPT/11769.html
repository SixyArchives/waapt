   Diane's Shadow Chamber   

The pain was unbearable.

First was the obvious: the physical pain. Although no cuts, bruises, burns or other sorts of injuries actully happened, Diane felt like... well, she couldn't decide what it felt like, actually. Her mind cycled through the list of what it could be: knives, electrocuion, fire, freezing, drowning... It was impossible to describe exactly. All she was absolutely sure of was, it felt like she was dying. And she wanted to. Just to end this. But no, all the pain was her imagination; her body was too healthy for her to really die. She was helpless, forced to take it all in.

~She's a monster... A monster, and she doesn't even care that she is...~

The half of her mind not occupied with the pain wanders to other things.

~How did this even happen!? One moment, I'm sleeping, next I'm in a cage...~

She looks at the armband attached to her leg.

~How couldn't I have noticed it!? How did...~

She shouts something she doesn't even care about not making sense with, the emotional distress clashing with her physical pain.

~...I was useless... Hopeless...~

No matter how hard she tries, she can't push the thought away, the pain slowly making it a reality.

~...Skye didn't do anything to stop it, either... She was there... She left me... Alone... Trapped... Doomed to endure this...~

She starts to stare off into the "distance", her mind starting to shut down as darker ideas form

~...Oh, what does it even matter... I'm useless anyway...~

The dark idea sticks in her mind.

~...No. I can't be useless. All I need is whatever power comes with this. I'm stuck with Cipher now. If I can live through this torture, I don't need anyone else. Heck, maybe I can even get rid of Paula eventually.~

The remaining pain fades as a small part of her shuts down. Her emotions, her morality, her response to pain, even her memories start fading; they all no longer mattered to her. If anything, they would impede her.
The type chart has finally changed. Steel no longer resists Dark and Ghost, and Eskay is no longer immune to Insanity.
