285012 Herbert40k20th Aug 2013 03:47:36 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
Chords of Steel

Spectra - Around 600 years ago

-Lucanus is again walking through the streets of one of Spectra's poorer villages, though this time around he has abandoned his armour in favour of plainer clothes. Of course, Lucanus' definition of "plainer" would probably be laughable to the Spectran villagers dressed in rags, but it's a step down from his usual attire, at least.-

Lucanus: Hmm... I wonder whether I should attempt to find myself another partner Pokemon. After all, I often find myself wanting for company, a problem that is only magnified by my residence in the kingdom of unlife. But considering the fates that my last two partners met, it seems likely that I lack the responsibility to take on another charge... perhaps I should resign myself to-

-He pauses mid-thought as he notices a familiar face pass by.-

Lucanus: ...Ah, hello again! We met yesterday, did we not?

-The young woman freezes up in shock, and her frightened expression does not change upon recognising Lucanus.-

Lucanus: Oh, my apologies if I startled you... Kohaku, wasn't it?

-She nods.-

Lucanus: A pleasure to meet you again, then. So... apologies in advance if I am being too intrusive, but I find myself in the lamentable state of having very little human contact as of late... then again, this is mostly self-inflicted. With that in mind, would you mind walking with me for a while?

-Kohaku shakes her head.-

Lucanus: Capital! Although I must say, you are remarkably quiet today. Am I intimidating you?

Kohaku: N-no, but some p-people are saying that... well, there's a r-rumour going around...

Lucanus: Oh?

Kohaku: I shouldn't be t-talking about this outside... come with m-me...

-She leads Lucanus through the village, until she comes to a street where most of the houses appear to have been leveled. Lucanus surveys the area with a mixture of curiosity and morbid fascination.-

Lucanus: ...This is the area where you live, I presume?

-Kohaku nods again.-

Lucanus: What happened here, exactly?

Kohaku: One of the G-Ghost Lord's enforcers came d-down here a few days ago... he said that s-since some of us had tried to revolt, he was going to p-punish us all for that...

-Lucanus' expression sours.-

Lucanus: ...Ah yes. Revy, one of the Ghost Lord's favourite attack hounds. Trust me, I have already heard tales of his... antics. My condolences. I suppose I could make some effort to repair the damage he has inflicted...

Kohaku: ...You c-could?

Lucanus: Of course. I feel that it would be a betrayal of basic human decency not to do so. I am in a rather priviledged position despite my servitude, after all. Requisitioning the materials needed may be difficult, but if all else fails I can always assist with the labour myself. These are plans for another day, though. For now, I believe you were taking me somewhere.

Kohaku: R-right...

-She continues leading him along until she comes to a small, still intact hut - though going by its appearance it is something of a miracle that it has managed to remain this way.-

Lucanus: Your home?

-Kohaku nods again, and opens the door to allow Lucanus to step inside. The house appears to only consist of the one room, with very little furniture save for a few chairs, a couple of cupboards, a bed and what appear to be a pair of makeshift sleeping bags. There are two occupants inside - a middle-aged man clearly worn down by premature aging, who stands up upon Lucanus and Kohaku entering, and an old woman who does not appear to acknowledge the two.-

Kohaku: That's my f-father, Haru, and my grandmother... she c-came to live with us after that Revy guy... well...

Haru: And just who have you brought with you, Kohaku?

Lucanus: Oh, me? My name is Lucanus Cain. It's a pleasure to meet you.

-He extends a hand, which Haru shakes vigourously.-

Haru: Lucanus, eh? My daughter told me about how generous you were yesterday. Thanks to you, we won't be going hungry for another week at the least! So, is there anything I can do for you?

Lucanus: Oh no, I would rather not intrude on your hospitiality. I am only here because I believe your daughter wanted to tell me something.

Haru: Hmm? And what would that be, Kohaku?

Kohaku: Well... I was g-going to tell him about the r-rumours...

-Haru's genial expression immediately hardens.-

Haru: ...Ah.

Lucanus: What are these rumours about, exactly?

Haru: ...You've been good to us. I suppose I can let you know what's going on. But if anyone asks, I never told you anything, okay?

Lucanus: Erm... understood, I suppose.

Haru: You see, there's been some talk going around about the possibility of us commoners getting together and... well, starting a revolution.

Lucanus: A revolution, you say?

-Lucanus raises an eyebrow inquisitively.-

Lucanus: ...Tell me more.
