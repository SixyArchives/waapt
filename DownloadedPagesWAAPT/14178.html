Illusion HQ

Um... okay, I guess.

-she pauses to gather her thoughts, frowning-

Well... you know how Obscura feeds off your emotions and stuff, right? I could use Night Slash before by wanting to... well, hurt someone. Not seriously hurt them, but... beat them. Best them. So I could use it during sparring matches just fine, y'know? And my shields have always worked off wanting to protect someone, including myself. Don't let anyone touch them, hurt them, that kinda stuff.

And then my friends and I went to Orre. They probably had a good reason for it, but I just kinda went along because everyone else was going there. And while we were there, I wandered off on my own, and Cipher... kidnapped me.

-she looks down, fidgeting uncomfortably-

They... did something to me. Messed with my head. I couldn't remember who I was or who my family or friends were, and anything that would remind me of them gave me this terrible headache... and I couldn't... couldn't feel any emotion, or at least any good ones. Nothing but an animal's fear of death. I didn't care about anyone but myself, I didn't feel sympathy or remorse when I hurt someone. And I hurt people a lot. If someone hurt me or made me angry, I hurt them. If someone bothered me, I hurt them. If I was bored, I hurt someone. If someone pointed me in the direction of someone, I hurt them. I... I put a bunch of my friends in the hospital before they finally got me back from Cipher. I... well, I was a monster.

-she pauses-

My friends finally got me back and brought me back to myself—clearly, or I wouldn't be standing here telling you this right now. And my Obscura got stronger afterwards. Or at least, I could produce more of it. It was harder to control than before, and I'd never been all that great about controlling it.

-she closes her eyes briefly-

Soon after I came back to the group... I almost killed someone. Not one of my friends, one of the people working for Cipher. We were attacking their base, and I ran into her. She... she was a real jerk to me while I was with Cipher, and even before Orre when I ran into her in Holon. She threatened my Pokemon, and I freaked out, and... well, she almost died.

... Luckily, my friend came along and calmed me down enough that I could get rid of the Obscura, but it was close.

-she clears her throat-

So, um, pretty much... I can't bring myself to use Night Slash anymore. I can't make myself want to hurt someone, because I'm petrified that I might, well, actually really hurt somebody. I'm... good with the shields. I can shape them, and change their size, and control a whole bunch of them at once. But I... can't use Night Slash.
