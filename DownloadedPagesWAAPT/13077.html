Goldenrod City: Rex's House

Settled on his couch with Jemma resting in his lap, Rex found himself fiddling with his Pokédex, a contemplative look in his eyes. And, across from the two of them sat another Pokémon, the ball of red and yellow fluff leering at the feline with no small amount of irritation.

Rascal: <...Why are you sitting in his lap?>

Jemma: <Mya, it's my right as your master~>

Rascal: <...lousy cat...>

Rex looked over at the Flareon with an apologetic smile, hoping that he'd be able to tolerate the rather abrasive Skitty's presence for just a while longer. Meeting his partner's eyes, Rascal relented, though he still looked a bit wary of Jemma's presence.

Jemma: <Myahaha~>

Rascal: <...rrr...>

Looking away from the two of them for a moment, Rex turned his attention back to the device in hand.

Rex: Hmm...

The Pokédex. What a wondrous tool he'd been given! To think that he was able to learn so much about the world and travel to so many places to meet Pokémon all because of a little trip he'd made to New Bark Town in the past.

And the knowledge he'd been able to gain as a result of that trip was something that boggled his mind every time he thought on it. The world of Pokémon was steadily evolving, new research topics springing into existence with each passing day. Just recently, a new type, Fairy, had been discovered. And imagine his surprise at learning that he'd been in possession of one such Pokémon ever since his trip to Unova.

Gin: <Zzz...>

The Pokémon in question, a male Whimsicott, was nestled atop a couch pillow behind Rascal, having chosen a spot close to his teammate for warmth. It was rather odd that a Fairy-type, let alone one that was part Grass, would get so close to a Fire-type for warmth. But perhaps that spoke of the bond they'd forged as they traveled through Unova.

Ohm: <Baaah~>

Seated in front of the couch, the female Ampharos bleated happily as a television program featuring a flock of Mareep began to play on the television. Glancing in her direction momentarily, Rex mused on other information that he'd heard around, namely about Dragon-types. There was research coming in from a region called Kalos that Ampharos had genetic similarities to Dragons, something that intrigued him no small amount. Despite that, Ohm didn't seem to share any of the weaknesses to Fairy-type attacks that Dragons did.

Caspian: <Aww, can't we watch something else? This show is boring...>

Ohm: <But Mareep!>

As the Ampharos and the Dewott bickered over what to watch, Rex found himself sighing softly, pocketing his Pokédex. The National Datachip with information from Kalos had yet to be sent to him.

It seemed his pursuits of knowledge would have to wait a while longer.
