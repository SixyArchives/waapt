-Herbert gives his mons an affectionate look, before kneeling down in front of Ripley.-

Herbert: Ripley, I know you and I have never gotten along well. And that's understandable, considering how I've treated you. I know it's too late for an apology to solve everything. But at least I can give you the one thing I know you want.

-He pulls Ripley's Quick Ball off of his belt, and stares at it for a moment before tossing it aside.-

Herbert: You're free to go, Ripley.
