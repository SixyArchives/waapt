Goldenrod City, West District, Yesterday

(Shortly after this post)

Just like every day in the last week, a Jolteon is trotting across the streets of the city, part of his morning jogging session under the command of the Owsla; wearing a couple of PEFE-approved weights on his legs, breathing in and out like his godfather taught him, the Pokémon calmly examines the city around him, calculating the time he needs to arrive to the exit.

As the Jolteon crosses one of the streets near the Underground Tunnel towards the flower shop district, various ears and eyes turn towards him to follow his progress. Now faster, firmer and more confident than in previous sessions, the Jolteon uses his trotting time to practice some of his moves too, barking, swinging his paws and bristling his fur at various imaginary opponents.

After that, the Jolteon arrives to an agricultural fair and a swap meet; the various humans and Pokémon move boxes and carts making room for Pierce to trot across the site, the Pokémon yay'ing and greeting at the humans and mons around him. An old couple from Almia takes a picture of him and they offhandedly comment that such a working Pokémon is a good example for the children.

Then, right after the Jolteon makes a turn and head to the main avenue in Goldenrod City, the eyes that were following him before decide to join in on the adventure: from various alleys and stands, young Pokémon start popping out to chase after the Jolteon and accompany him.

Pierce gives a friendly blink at an Oddish.

<Yippie!>

Pierce waves his paw to greet a Rattata.

<Come on! Let's go fastaaaa!>

Pierce barks at a cooing Hoothoot.

<What the heck, flying is too boring. I'm gonna hop-run after the Jolteon~>

From a backalley, a drug dealer Hypno in a longcoat is trying to seduce some kits of various species when he notices the Jolteon rushing. In a moment of distraction, he turns back, letting the kits watch the Jolteon.

<It's the fats Jolteon!!!!>

All the kits leave the drug dealer behind as they also chase after the Eeveelution; a moment after, the Hypno shrugs and tosses the drugs and the longcoat to a trashcan.

Suddenly!Reformed!Hypno: <What the heck, it didn't pay enough and the trenchcoat made me sweat like a Swinub...> — also joins the jogging group.

~ Link: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NubH5BDOaD8] ~

Some minutes later, Pierce reaches the main avenue of Goldenrod City and turns south, a small group of about 10 Pokémon following him for the next segment of his trek. He continues his routine passing through various stands and stores; eventually another assortment of various Pokémon such as Zigzagoon, Kirlia and Sandile have joined.

As he reaches the police office stand before the Magnet Train overpass, the Jolteon looks to the side to see the police officer posted there together with his trusty companion, a bulky Typhlosion wearing a cop cap.

Stoatine Officer Stokes♂ -flicker ears, puffs chest: <Jogging session, young Jolteon?>

Pierce: <It's much funnier than the other days!> — barks as he goes past.

The human officer rubs the back of his head not understanding much of what is going on, but decides that if Stokes says the Jolteon is friendly, he can be allowed to pass. Of course, he looks quizically as the group of Pokémon shortly runs past them as well, following the steps of the Jolteon.

Stoke♂: <All is well~>

Shortly after, Pierce goes past the overpass, where some Pidgeys of the non-silly variety chirp at him; he later reaches a wide street that goes to the east and turns to take it, effortessly but still cautiously leaping over a couple of cars in the process.

At that very moment a certain Eevee family is orderly marching in the other direction under the watchful eye of their father Espeon.

Eevee kit #2: <Eeee! It's Pierce!>

Eevee kit #4: <Wheeeee~ Lokk at him he is fast!>

Eevee kit #5: <Vaporeons are just better~>

Rex rushes past and before the Espeon can do anything to prevent it, the kits all turn tail and happily go chasing after Pierce as well, yipping and growling loudly.

Espeon: <O_o My mate is going to kill me... kits wait~> — chases after as well, joining the large group following Pierce.

Some blocks beyond, and after having rushed past a school, Pierce's group has become pretty large, with various Rattata, Hoothoot, Buizel, Nidoran, Zigzagoon, Tangela, Snubull, Spinarak, Eevee and a Wooper. Pierce notices he is already running past the Dept. Store to the south, so he turns next corner and crosses to a plaza, leaping over some benches for extra exercise, with the various Pokémon behind him following orderly.

Next street, the Jolteon identifies a particular building - the local PEFE office - and a group of Pokémon waiting for him. Pierce yips as he sees some familiar faces and does notice when a Mawile, a Noctowl, an Ariados and other PEFE mons join the group running behind him.

Pierce -barks up: <Oh hai! It's Harriet!>

From atop the next building a couple of birds wave their wings at him both as a greeting and as an encouragement for the Jolteon to keep running.

From the next alleys various Owsla members also join in, finding some pleasure in this new variant of their usual mission of protecting the Nidoran kits that roam around the city, most of them already collected into this jogging group.

As he reaches the eastern districts of the city, Pierce notices that the terrain is now steeper, the streets narrower and also more lonely. That won't disturb him however because he also feels the scent of the forest and distinguishes the treetops beyond the end of the hill. Pierce collects his strength and speeds up fiercely, quickly leaving the rest of the group behind as he is faster and nimbler as a Jolteon, now only he and the breeze enjoying the moment.

Suddenly the Pokémon distinguishes a figure waiting for him at the end of the street, right before some staircases that lead to a terrace that marks the end of the city. As the Jolteon rushes past him, Maekrite turns around and chases after Pierce as well, quickly catching up.

Both Pokémon rush up the long staircase, though Pierce swiftly and skillfully gains the ahead position, building a growing advantage over his big bro. Leap after leap the Jolteon reduces the distance separating him from the summit to essentially nothing, while Maekrite struggles to reach him a distance behind and the joggers group has just arrived to the staircase.

Pierce -looks around him: <...I made it!>

The Jolteon brakes to a halt and looks around, finding himself atop the terrace, with the ample space for himself the only thing that separates him from returning to the Colony. He yips, hops around and bares his teeth at Destiny, then proudly turns around to see a somewhat tired Maekrite catching up.

Pierce: <Big brother! I am not tired!> — hops around and goes rub against the Nidorino.

Maekrite: <Wonderful!> — playfully pecks Pierce — <You have improved a lot! You look much more fit now...>

The two Pokémon fistbump and enjoy a short talk before the jogger group that has pretty much become a caravan of about 150 Pokémon joins them in celebration, which include howling to the moon (or sun), sharing some random berries and picking Pierce around lifting him to the air.
