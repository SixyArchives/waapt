Castelia City, Rens Apartment?

-Ren is sitting at his in his apartment drawing up diagrams for an appeal. Ren sets the pen hes using down then picks up the cup of tea to drink from it. After taking a few sips of tea and sighing, Ren starts to work on his diagrams again, but his stereo turns on by itself and starts to play a song he once heard in a play. Disturbed, Ren stands and walks over to the stereo and flips the power switch  which has absolutely no effect on the stereo. Ren sudden realizes that while the tune is the same and the voices pitch perfect, the lyrics he knows so well are different and sound very wrong in his mind-

And the child who then went out from the garden
And when he turned to look, the pathway back was gone
So he turned and set his eyes
To the land that lies beyond...

-Ren is no longer in his apartment, but in the middle of a desert with the bleached skeleton of a Camerupt lying nearby and rocky cliffs rise in the distance. Ren finds he has no need for his cane or a leg brace as the song continues playing-

Red rock and outcrop stone
And the sun glares off a bleaching bone
There's no comfort or softness here
There's only the wasteland

The land of the hunter, the stalker, and the skinner
Where you're either the diner or the dinner
And the line between man and beast keeps getting thinner
In the wasteland

In the wasteland, the land laid waste
Fruit of knowledge has a bitter taste
But the bliss of ignorance can never be replaced
It's lost to the wilderness

-The scene changes again. Now Ren is watching his leg being mauled as a spectator and not a participant. Ren sinks to his knees, remembering that blood soaked time that changed his life forever while the songs lyrics cut deep into his mind-

And there will come a day, in a chilly rain
A child whose borne both sweat and pain
Will feel it again and deal back the pain
To the wasteland

In the wasteland, the land forlorn
The child who fears the pain of scorning
Must seek whats past and heal whats torn
Or be lost in the wilderness

-Ren is back in the desert again, however there is nothing but sand as far as the eye can see and with no rocky cliffs on the horizon. Ren walks though the desert the song blazing in his ears-

Heat-baked and dust storm-driven
And one false step stays unforgiven
And all that you know is you weren't made to live in
The wasteland

In the wasteland, the land bereft
The heart can shatter with a final cleft
And what hope for the future you have left
Is lost in the wilderness

The wild beast howl and the wild winds blow
And the seeds of the tree of knowledge grow
And you know things now you wish you didn't know
In the wasteland

-As the final verse starts Ren hears a crunch and looks down to see him stepping on the right leg of a human skeleton clutching what appears to be a green sword in its right hand and his stun cane its left. The bones of the left leg are in an odd shape, like they had been broken many times before. Ren finally notices his glasses on the bleached white skull and screams as the song finishes-

Holon Center, Rens room

-Ren snaps awake into a bolt upright position panting like he just ran a mile in three minutes. The sweat covering his body having absolutely nothing to do with his girlfriends warm tails covering his legs and feet or the sleeping outfit hes wearing.-
