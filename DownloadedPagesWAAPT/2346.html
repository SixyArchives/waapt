???:' That's right. This isn't Earth. It's another world entirely. Lake, turn around.

Ho-oh: -Smiles- Hello Lake. Good to see you again.

-Ho-oh hops off the roof of the cottage and lands in front of Lake.-

Lake: <Oh. Right.>

Lake: <Yes. Where is this? And how did I get here?>

Ho-oh: Well Lake. This...is the Dream World.

Lake: <The Dream World?>

Ho-oh: Yes. The Dream World is a separate world that all Pokémon can visit when they sleep. Like you are right now. Your physical body is still in Jubilife City, but your mind, and my mind, are in the Dream World.

Lake: <Huh? How does that work?>

Ho-oh: Hmm...Think of it as like the human internet. The internet is a global network formed of millions of individual computers all connected to each other. The Dream World works in pretty much the same way, except with Pokémon instead of computers.

A Pokémon "Logs on" to the Dream World simply by falling asleep, allowing their mind to form a connection with the other Pokémon in the world that are currently asleep. Look...

-Ho-oh gestures with his wing at the other islands floating in the sky.-

Ho-oh: All those islands you see belong to another sleeping Pokémon. In fact, all of the ones in the vicinity belong to the Pokémon you're closest to. For example, there's Woolly's.

-The bird points to a house that looks like one you would find on a farm.-

Ho-oh: And there's Stratus' home.

-The next home the bird points too is a cave opening on the surface of a small lake.-

Lake: <They're all different.>

Ho-oh Indeed. Your home can be freely changed to best suit your needs. Your home here is currently on the "Default setting" right now, but there's no reason why you can't change it later.

Lake: <Wow. This all sounds really cool, but I don't quite understand. Did only come to me to show me around? I'm sure I wouldn't need a legendary like you for that.>

Ho-oh: -Chuckles- No Lake, I didn't come here just to show you the Dream World. I just thought it would be nice to let you know about it before we moved on to the true reason I visited. You see Lake, I'm here to talk to you about you and Nadia...
