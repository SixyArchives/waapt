Not so Above It All

AU, a secluded valley

-Austerium stands before a mass of Pokemon, an Altaria at his side. Most of the crowd before him is comprised of Aerodactyl, Flygon, and Altaria, though there are a few scattered Salamence. He lowers his head and murmurs something to the Altaria beside him, who nods. Austerium shakes out his wings and steps forward-

Austerium: <You stand before me, the best fliers of the corps. I have a duty to assign the best of the best. General Valterra has informed me of a new tactic she has devised to prevent the humans from escaping their cities when the Council makes their move. This new tactic requires air support. That is where you come in.>

-his eyes scan the crowd-

Austerium: <Some time ago, a flying contest was held, and the winners escorted me to a conference with the Council. Airmon Jezzabelle, Major Lacy... you will hold co-command of this squad.>

-he silences the Altaria behind him with a flick of his tail as the smaller mon begins to protest the command of an Aerodactyl-

Austerium: <Colonel Featherstone assures me there were many of you that came very close to beating these fliers. The colonel will seek you out individually to assign you to the squad. Are there any questions?>

-a Flygon near the back buzzes upward and hovers above the crowd-

Flygon: <If we go with this troop, are we gonna miss droppin' bombs on the apes, your generalship?>

Austerium: <First of all, 'you generalship' is not my title. Address me as 'General' or 'General Austerium'. As for your question, this squad will not, in fact, be participating in the strafing runs; 'droppin' bombs on the apes', as you so elegantly put it. They will be working closely with General Valterra and her select team to ensure that the efforts of the bombers are not in vain, and the humans do not escape their fate. Any other questions?>

-a shiny Altaria near the middle of the pack raises a wing and hops onto the back of a Salamence beside her-

Altaria: <With all due respect, General Austerium, it is said of General Valterra that she doesn't take her work seriously, she sleeps all the time, and also that she eats mountains. Is this true?>

Austerium: -hesitates before answering- <General Valterra would not have her position if she could not fulfill it.>

Altaria: -gasps- <Arceus, it is true! I don't want to work with the Sleeping Mountain, even though I did almost win!>

That Salamence: <Hey, I tied with you... >_>>

Austerium: -snaps his wings open as similar protestations spread through the crowd, lashing his tail until they fall silent- <I did not say anything of the sort!>

Random!Flygon: <You totally implied it!>

Austerium: -glares in the general direction of the voice, but can't find the source; gives up and turns back to address the whole crowd, folding his wings against his side again- <General Valterra is a great general, and it is an honor to be selected. Of course, only those willing to accept this honor will be offered it. It is true that General Valterra occasionally acts somewhat informally, but I can assure you that she takes her work very seriously when it suits her.>

Altaria: <What was that?>

Austerium: <Nothing! Now, you are all dismissed!>

-he turns around and walks off, the Altaria beside him hopping after-

Austerium: <That could have gone better, Colonel.>

Colonel Featherstone: <I'm so sorry, General! Kyle is a tad uncouth, but usually Sweetie is much better behaved!>

Austerium: <Her name is Sweetie? Well, no wonder she misbehaved.>

Featherstone: <What was that, General?>

Austerium: <Nothing, nothing... Do they really call Valterra the 'Sleeping Mountain'?>

Featherstone: <Yes, sir. I'm sure they don't mean offense—>

Austerium: <What do they call me?>

Featherstone: <Well—er—that is, sir—>

Austerium: -swishes his tail- <Yes, Colonel?>

Featherstone: <... The Blue Stickler, sir.>

Austerium: <... Not quite as flattering as 'the Sleeping Mountain'.>

Featherstone: <N-no, sir. Should I tell them all to stop?>

Austerium: <Why, that sounds like quite a stickler thing to do.>

Featherstone: <So, that's a—>

Austerium: <Let them have their jokes, colonel. As long as they can follow me when the time comes, they could call me the Fat Fellow if they liked.>

Featherstone: <... Would you like me to suggest that na—>

Austerium: <No, Featherstone.>

Featherstone: <... Y-yes, sir.>

Austerium: -opens his wings and prepares to fly off- <Go get me my squadron, colonel. And... Featherstone...>

Featherstone: -preparing to return to his mons; looks back- <Yes, sir?>

Austerium: -looking thoughtful- <Maybe suggest something a little more... awestriking. Say... Red-Winged Thunder. Or... Blue Death.>

Featherstone: -trying not to snicker- <Yes, sir. I'll do that, sir.>

Austerium: <Yes... see that you do. Good day, Colonel.>

Featherstone: <Good day, General.>

-the two fly off in opposite directions-
