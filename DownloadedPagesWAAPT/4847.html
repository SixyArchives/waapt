Hoenn Desert

-The group is currently asleep, with two exceptions-

<Francis: "Ugh, I can't believe that I can't see the stars. Friggin' sand.">

-Still sulking in his bubble-

<Chord: "...">

<Francis: "What's with you?">

<Chord: "... You don't think that I'm a jerk too, do you?">

<Francis: "Are you going to drop dead if I say 'yes?'">

-With a faint hint of panic in his voice-

<Chord: "B-But I'm not! H-Honest! I- I- I just don't take some other mons so well...">

<Francis: "If that's what you want to call it, sure.">

<Chord: "But... Oh...">

-And looks like he's about to cry-

<Francis: "What's wrong with you now? Someone pushed your buttons and you got a little snappy, so what?">

<Chord: (*sniffle*) "Huh?">

<Francis: "I mean, I've had to put up with my share of headaches in my life. I can understand.">

<Chord: "You can? I mean, of course! They're all just overreacting!">

<Francis: "Hmm. So, is there something else on your mind?">

<Chord: "Huh?">

<Francis: "Well, ever since your brain-dead friend went back, you've seemed a little off... Do you miss him?">

<Chord: "I do not! I'm glad that I'm away from him!">

-Francis just stares at Chord-

<Chord: "Well, okay... Maybe I do miss him... But only a little!">

-Thinking-

<Francis: "Hmm... Well he acknowledged it... After I called him out on it. That's marginally better than I was expecting... I guess...">

<Francis: "Why do you care what the others think about you anyways?">

<Chord: "Because... They're my teammates... And... They're my friends...">

<Francis: "... And?">

<Chord: "Aren't your teammates the same way for you?">

<Francis: "...">

<Chord: "Wait. What? But why not? I mean, it's not as if your team is full of idiots! Or if it is, they seem to do a good job at hiding it...">

<Francis: "... Hmm... Maybe they are... Pokemon and humans do seem to be pretty good at hiding things...">

-Thinking-

<Francis: "When it suits them...">

<Chord: "Uh... Okay... Well, I mean... Er... What are you going to do now?">

<Francis: "Probably try, and in all likelihood fail at getting some sleep.">

<Chord: "Oh. Uh... Well... If you didn't want to, there's- I mean... We could always talk together...">

<Francis: "... Maybe another night, Chord.">

-Chord starts to look despondent again-

<Francis: "I'll admit, I might not know you much. And I'm not sure what exactly you're up to. But you seem like a nice mon. Or at least someone who wants to be one.">

<Chord: "Well... Uh... Thanks?">

<Francis: "Goodnight, Chord. Get some rest. Humans live on a different schedule than your kind and mine do.">

-And flies off a short distance-

<Chord: "... Goodnight, Francis.">
