CC: ...if it helps, i don't think you have to worry about whether or not he trusts you.

CC: ...the question is whether or not trusting you will actually make him listen to you.

CC: Hm… True.

CC: I’m not sure how if I’m doing that right, though. Aside from convincing him when I say something, it’s worth noting.

CC: Which, considering how people react to a lot of the things I say, is going to be difficult.

CC: ...yeah...
