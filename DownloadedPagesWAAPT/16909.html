"Ray, please listen to me, I'm sorry, I didn't want to blow you off like that, I..I just had some really important stuff that I needed to go and do, people that I needed to talk to, and once that was done I decided to tail Locke, Lane and Tori. I wanted to make sure that everything was gonna go well, I was trying to be a good sister..please, believe me.."

She slumped against the door, which was then opened by Ray, clad in a purple bathrobe. Ray sighed, and crouched down next to Marie, giving her a tight hug and a smile. Her hair was still wet and smelled like strawberries, and Marie breathed in the sent, hugging Ra tightly."It's okay, Marie, I was just a bit upset, it's fine..everything's fine..."

Marie and Ray's room, last night

Ray sat on the bed, hugging the fuzz pink sweater, clad in a bathrobe, her expression a bit far away. Marie walks into the room, drying her hair. She frowns at Ray."Something wrong?"

Ray shook her head, and smiled at her."Nah, just soaking in how good it feels to be clean right now. I kinda lost my swimwear at the beach, so I had to change while being buried to the neck in sand."

Marie winced, and Ray nodded."Yeah..it feels good to be clean. Thankfully, Jason got me some clothes, " she waved the sweater, and Marie gave a tiny frown at that, but Ray continued, a bit of a thoughtful frown of her own, "Hey, wait, wasn't Jason with you at the Cinema? What was he doing there?"

Marie coughed a bit."Oh, yeah, I had to talk to him about some things, mainly some advice for..friends, and then he was with me when we saw the group, and followed them together." Ray nodded, "Did you get any good advice?" Marie shrugged."We'll see."

Ray smiled, and got under the covers of the bed, while Marie laid down beside her. As she turned her body to the side, Ray thought, before dozing off, "...Why don't I believe them..?"
