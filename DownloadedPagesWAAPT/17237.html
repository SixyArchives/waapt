430913 Luke992427th Nov 2014 07:01:02 PM from England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Is that a kind of food?
Izzy: <Argh...>

Izzy rubbed her throbbing head. This was a headache that wasn't going to leave her anytime soon. She blinked wearily as her senses came back to her, staring down through the window at the ocean below.

...Wait.

She blinked, staring up at the crack she'd left in the glass, if they could just hit it hard enough to smash it, then maybe...But how would they managed to get the robot to slip and-

<Bingo.>

She had a plan, pulling herself to her feet, she watched as Stratus was easily knocked away from the robot from a well-placed Iron Tail. She couldn't...no, wouldn't do anything until her friend was out of that awful thing's mouth.

<TEAM! I have a plan, but first we need to->

Suddenly, the robot's jaws locked as it tried to chew, as if it was suddenly gnawing on something chewy. From inside the cabin, the sound of an alarm buzzed.

Torque: <Jaws jammed? By what?!>

The robot tried to bite down again, only for it's mouth to be forced open by something transparent a blue. It almost looked as if Lake had been chewing gum and he was trying to blow a bubble.

Actually, that was mostly accurate as Lake forced himself mostly out of the mouth, the Feraligatr enveloped in a Bubble Shield, panting heavily.

Lake: <Phew...saved my life again!>

Troque: <What the heck-?!>

Gelatine: <LAKE!>

Stratus: <SON!>

Izzy: <PARTNER! YOU OKAY?!>

Lake nodded down to them as he hopped out of the Tyrantrum's mouth as it snapped shut behind him, bouncing back up as he hit the floor before stopping in front of Izzy and giving her a weak smile.

Lake: <I've...been better...>

Torque: <NO WAY! CHEATER!>

Stratus: <Look who's talking!>

While the Tyrantrum launched another laser barrage at Stratus as they continued to fight in the background - Gelatine assisting with another Dragon Pulse - Izzy was able to get a better look at Lake's injuries.

To put it likely, it looked like he had been put through a blender. Blood oozed from open wounds all over his body, his bright red crests were covered in notches from where he'd been chewed on. A few of the claws on his hands and legs had snapped, and there was barely anything left of the yellow scarf Izzy had never seen him take off.

She couldn't help but wince at his condition. But she was glad he was okay.

Izzy: <Aw, buddy. I'd hug ya' if you weren't in that bubble. How did you even have the Hit Points to make that thing?>

Lake: <I barely did. In fact...I'm sure I've only got one left. Once this thing's gone...I'm out.>

Izzy nodded understandingly.

Izzy: <It's okay. I know what to do, but I'm gonna' need you're help. Feel up to it?>

He nodded, a-bit weakly.

Lake: <I'm always up for it!>

Stratus: <WAAAAAAH!!!!>

The two turned to see Stratus slam into the window, adding another large crack to it.

Stratus: <Check please!>

Izzy: <Okay, boys! I need you to break this window! Gelatine->

She whipped around to face the Goodra, who was so nervous that she was leaking goo everywhere again.

Gelatine: <W-what do you need me t-to do? AAAAHHHH!>

She quickly rushed out of the way as Torque tried to stomp on her with his robot's large foot.

Izzy: <Scamper around in front of that robot!>

The Goodra blinked incredulously at her as Lake and Stratus began to strike the glass behind Izzy.

Gelatine: <Huh?!>

Izzy: <Just do it! I can't explain why!>

Gelatine: <U-uh...okay!>

The Goo-dragon did so, unknowingly leaving a slippery trail of slime that Torque was too preoccupied laughing at Lake and Stratus trying their hardest to shatter the window.

Torque: <Don't waste your time! That glass is thick enough to withstand almost any attack! The website said that only a legendary would be powerful enough to bre->

BOOOOOOOOOOOM!!!

The whole airship rocked, knocking the Pokémon off their feet and almost causing the Mecha-Tyrantrum to lose it's balance.

<WHAT IN THE WORLD-?!>

There was a buzz as the P.A system switched on, a voice familiar to Torque on the other side.

Sinclair: <SIR! There's a huge thermal signature just off the west side of the ship near your location! Scans show it's power readings' are off the scale!>

Torque: <WHAT?!>

Lake: <HEADS UP!>

Stratus: <WHAAA!>

The Feraligatr and Dragonite quickly dived out of the way as a blinding beam of solar energy shattered the window, shards of glass falling everywhere. From outside, the beating of huge wings as a Pokémon bearing a familiar trainer came into view.

Luke: GUYS! Sorry I'm late!

Realising who it was, Lake flashed him a toothy grin.

Lake: <That's okay! Heroes always arrive late!>

Izzy was less startled by her trainer's sudden appearance, and more of his choice of mount.

Izzy: <NO WAY!>

The Pokémon in question was an impossibly large bird. Bigger than any bird Izzy had ever seen. A head with large red eyes, a crest of golden feathers and a large gold beak sat on the end lengthy neck. It's body was covered in red feathers with a white underbelly, two large black talons hanging from underneath.

A large plume of more golden feathers made up it's tail. Most magnificent of all though, were it's wings. It's feathers all seemed to glow as this Pokémon was a rainbow incarnate. Feeling triumphant at that moment, the bird cried happily, it's voice almost like the ringing of bells.

Ho-Oh: <Woo-hoo! Just call me Dyna-blade!>

Still in his robot, Torque snarled angrily, either not knowing or not caring that the newcomer was a legendary.

Torque: <Who are you to crash my party?!>

The bird glared back at the robot.

Ho-Oh: <And who are you to attempt to subjugate my region?>

Torque: <It's not subjugation! It's kindly asking the government to move aside so that I can be a benevolent emperor instead!>

The phoenix rolled his eyes dismissively, as if he had heard it all before.

Ho-Oh: <Listen. I've known more than my fair share of conquerors and warlords in my long life.>

Luke: Oh, muk! Warlords of Draenor is out!

Ho-Oh: <You wouldn't believe how many of them have told me some variation of what you just said. Do you know what happened to them?>

The robot shook it's head.

Torque: <No?>

Ho-Oh: <I drove. Them. OUT. You can have the Johto region...>

The Pheonix raised his wings, now burning with technicolour flames.

<OVER MY DEAD BODY!>

The robotic Tyrantrum roared loudly as Torque squeezed the control levers in his cabin.

Torque: <THAT CAN BE ARRANGED! RAAAAAARGH!!!>

He charged forward, but then suddenly found himself sliding across the ground towards the window, having slipped on the puddle of goo Gelatine had left there as per Izzy's instructions.

<WAAAAAAAHHHHHHH!!!>

Izzy: <LUKE! Move it!>

Luke: Ho-Oh!

Ho-Oh: <Whoa, nelly!>

The bird quickly flapped up and out of the way, leaving the robot clear to slide out of the window.

Torque: <NO! NO! NO! NOOOOO!>

Torque tried in vain to regain control as he reached the edged and tipped over, flailing the robots arms to try and maintain balance.

<AAAAAHHHHHHH!!!>

He tipped forward, back, then forward again...before coming to a precarious halt leaning over the edge. Panting heavily in his cabin, the dinosaur released the death grip on the control levers, sitting back in his seat as he sighed in relief.

<...Phew!>

Just behind him, however, Stratus smirked to himself as he raised a claw.

Stratus: <Hey, Torque!>

From inside the cabin, Torque responded.

Torque: <Yes?>

Stratus: <Have a nice fall!>

He then pushed the robot ever so gently with his claw. But this was more than enough to tip it forward...

Torque: <NO! NO!>

And finally over the edge, plummeting towards the water below.

<NOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO!!!>

Splash!

The four Pokémon peered over the edge, looking for any sign of the Tyrantrum, but the ocean was too far below to make anything out.

Lake: <...He's okay, right?>

A few seconds later, however, a small red speck popped out of the ocean, seemingly keeping himself afloat without a little bit of trouble.

Torque: <Cold! COLD! SINCLAIR, QUICK! I NEED MY FLOATIES!>

Satisfied, Stratus flashed his son a grin.

Stratus: <He's good!>
