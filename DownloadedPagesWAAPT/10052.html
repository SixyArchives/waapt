"... "All right, that's good enough for me... So... Any remaining doubts about my feelings towards you?" asked Duke, with a slightly playful smile.

"W-Well... It's a lot of info to process and-"

"And now?" asked Duke, with a smile and a few chuckles.

"I'm not even going to say anything anymore." said Lyuri, with a very happy and wide smile as she hugged Duke as tightly as she could.

Duke then hugged her back, gently "Should we go watch the stars now?" he asked, enjoying the moment.

"All right, but just wait a bit." said Lyuri, trying to enjoy the moment as much as she could.
