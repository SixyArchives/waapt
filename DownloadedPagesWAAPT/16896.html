Marie and Ray's room

Ray sat hunched up in bed, alone, her diary in her left hand, while she wrote away with her right.

"Dear diary, " she started out, "I have a big secret to tell you." She looked around the room, tittering, her eyes frantic and wide, and upon seeing that no one was around, she sighed, taking a deep breath, relaxing as she went back to writing, licking her lips as she did so.

"I think I might be cheating on Marie with Jason." Ray shook a little as she said so, exhaling. After that, she tapped her chin with her pencil, before writing again."I say "might" because I don't know if I really am, I don't know if our talks, our huggling, and sleeping next to each other...I don't know if that's cheating. Is it?"

"I'm scared of my feeling, diary. I mean, I love Marie, I really do....but does she love me? I mean, Jason seems to see me for me, he cares about me..Marie just seems like she's scared of me.''"

Ray looks around again, her eyes wide, but still, she was alone, so she kept at it." But..after all this time, shouldn't Marie have learned to open up more? Or does she still need me to love her in secrets..love her in the shadows? Or should I love in the sun, in the open..with Marie...or Jason? Shouldn't everyone get the chance to have their try at love?" She sighed, and buried her face in the diary, groaning. She then shrugged, gave a smile, and continued. "You know, even if this does end up biting me in the butt later, I'm still glad I wrote all of this. Really, it's scary, writing this down, pulling your feelings out of you and putting them down somewhere, but it feels so good. I mean, it feels good that this'll claw at me a little less from the inside, and I'll feel...free, but after a bit, hehehe."

Then, suddenly, Marie comes in through the door, looking absolutely exhausted."Hey Ray, what's going on?" Ray hurriedly hides the diary underneath the covers, and then slowly starts moving it to the side."''Nothing, just reading a bit. Wanna sleep?"

Marie nodded slowly, and then simply collapsed on to the bed. Ray smiled, ruffling her hair, and put the diary away.
