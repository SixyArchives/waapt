PEFE-HQ - Luke's Module

Upon getting up that morning, Luke had decided to check his Pokégear's messages for the first time in a long while, only to find...

100+ Unread messages.

Luke: o_O

Several Minutes later...

Luke: "...So to sum it all up, Cipher have been doing Arceus knows what to the team, so we're gonna' go over there and deal with them. Now, we're well aware that Cipher would be capable of snagging any one of you..."

He paused to look over at the Pokémon he had decided to bring along with him.

"So I want you all to remember The Golden Rule while we're in Orre: Never go anywhere alone! It'll be much easier for those Cipher jerks to jump you and snag ya' if you wander off by yourself! Is everyone clear?"

Lake the Feraligatr: <No going alone, got it!>

Comet the Charizard: <Loud and clear.>

Treeo: <Righto!>

Woolly: <Alright!>

Stratus: <Wait, so I can't even go off and get a bite to eat...?>

An awkward silence followed as Luke gave the Dragonite a disapproving look.

Luke: "You're on a diet, remember? That means no going off for snacks, either!"

Stratus: <Grrrr!>

Stratus kicked an imaginary pebble in frustration as Luke's last team member spoke up.

Crowley: <Why ya' bringing me along? I've got better things to do 'den mess up some Cipher idiots in 'da middle of 'da desert!>

Luke: "I'm bringing you - and the others - along because you're the strongest Pokémon I've got. If anyone stands a chance against the might of Cipher, it's you. You get me?"

Seemingly unimpressed, the Honchkrow stifled a yawn.

Crowley: <Whatever. Let's just get this over with.>

Luke: "Alright. Guardé will take us there and back. Guardé?"

The Gallade, who had been standing off to the side quietly, stood to attention.

Guardé: <Whenever you're ready, Sir Luke.>

Luke: "Uh...could you take us to Phenac City?"

Guardé: <Understood.>

Comet: <Oh Muk. Maybe we should hold onto each other so that we don't get- >

~Blip!~

Phenac City - Orre

Comet: <Seperat- Ack!>

Guardé: <Good luck with vanquishing Cipher, friends! GUARDÉ AWAY!>

The Gallade blip'd away, leaving the Pokémon and their trainer in the middle of Phenac City near the fountain. Apart from them, their was nobody there apart from a very beamused Jogger and his Castform, who were naturally shocked at the sight of a whole team of Pokémon appearing from thin-air.

Castform: <Where did they come from?>

Jogger: "Let's just get out of here..."

On first glance, there didn't appear to be any sign of any Cipher activity. Or anything bad at all, for that matter. In fact, Phenac City appeared to be quite the pleasant place indeed. Hardly the sort of place they had imagined.

Treeo: <...I think we might be a bit late for the party.>

Crowley: <Great! Less work for me to do!>

Luke: "I knew I should have checked the dates on those messages..."
