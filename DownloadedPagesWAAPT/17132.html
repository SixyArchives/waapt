"HEY!"

<Izzy, darling. I know just what you're going through right now.>

Izzy: <Don't call me that.>

Izzy: <Rufflet, huh? Were you cute?>

Luke: "Give me a second, and..."

Luke had pulled out his Pokédex, and with a few taps of some buttons...

"Here we go!"

He turned it around to show Izzy, having brought up a picture of a Rufflet. The thought that Unite had once been a small, cute little bird like the one in the picture made the lizard nearly break out laughing.

Izzy: <Wow! You totally were cute! Pfft!>

Even Lake was laughing a bit.

Lake: <He ain't kidding! I remember when he was about the same size as you, yet he was still as big and boisterous as he is now! It was so adorable!>

By this point, the Braviary was fuming.

Unite: <CUT IT OUT WITH THE BABY PICTURES ALREADY!>

He sighed, regaining his composure.

<What I'm saying here is that I was just like you in the fact that I really wanted to evolve too. For obvious reasons.>

Izzy: <Did it take a while for you to evolve, then?>

Unite: <Well, to put it in leveling terms: Rufflet don't evolve into Unite until they reach Level fifty-four.>

This was such a shock to Izzy that her frills flared up.

Izzy: <Level fifty-four?! Why the florf does it take that long?!>

Unite: <Because Game-Freak didn't bother to balance us for anywhere but the end-game.>

Izzy blinked, confused at such a breach of the fourth wall.

Izzy: <...You've lost me.>
