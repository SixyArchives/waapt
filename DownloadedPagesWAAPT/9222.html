Dragon's Den

Sakura's current mons are reunited next to the Dragon's Den, just having a friendly chat with each other.

"<So, you said you heard that Sakura just called Rawst and that from what you heard there were no hard feelings between them?>" asked Seth, glancing at Daisuke, who didn't seem bothered by that at all.

"<Pretty much, at least that's what I THINK>" Meteo answered, rather confused with the sudden glance at Daisuke.

"<But didn't Dai predict that they would have problems?>" asked Dylan, looking confused.

"<I never said when, just that it would happen.>" Daisuke pointed out, while looking at his crytal ball.

"<Just admit that you can't predict anything, a couple having a fight every once in a while is a common occurance, you're just predicting the obvious.>" commented Seth.

"<Fine, do you want an elaborate prediction that will happen? I can give you that.>" said Daisuke, with a slightly annoyed tone.

"<Feel free to try, not like I believe in that anyway.>" said Seth, giving a shrug.

Daisuke then starts looking at the crystal ball and looking at Seth's future, surprisingly enough, he seemed quite shocked with what he saw, he was open-mouthed and just staring at the ball without doing any moviment.

"<I get the feeling this one is really, REALLY BAD.>" said Meteo, noticing Daisuke's sudden change.

"<Why do all members of your line that I've met have a weird quirk?>" commented Seth, with a sigh at Meteo's usual change of tone, then he looks at Daisuke "<What did you see?>" he asked, a bit curious as well.

"<... A cave, a wounded child, a fainted Sakura, and you having to save them from certain death, however, you only save one.>" said Daisuke, with a slightly worried tone.

The Pokémon stood in silence, Meteo, Seth and Dylan just glanced at each other, not being sure of what to think of that, even Seth seemed a bit shaken up by that prediction.

"<... I doubt we'll even go inside a cave ever again, there's no way that'll happen.>" said Seth, folding his claws and trying to keep his cool.

"<I hope so...>" said Dylan, clearly worried.

Daisuke and Meteo just remained in the silence, the mood certainly having darkened quite a bit as they just stood there staring at each other.
