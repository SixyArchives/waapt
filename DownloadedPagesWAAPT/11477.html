Unnamed Island

-the Dunsparce have found their way onto shore. Inspection has revealed the land to be part of a small island chain, and that there is a cave nearby-

Another hour or two later

-Revy's corpse, now largely unrecognizable, is placed reverently into a small hole dug in the wet soil inside the cave-

-599

Unnamed Island

-the grave is no longer visible. Instead, a lichen patch has spread outwards from it, now covering most of the cave-

-dozens of Dunsparce nests have been placed throughout the cave, some visible and some not. Harrison and Guinevere are curled up in one nest around several eggs, while Giuseppe and Jean are tucked away in a tunnel dug into the cave wall-

Harrison: <It's today, Guinevere. I can feel it.>

Guinevere: <You say that every day, Harrison. Just accept that—>

-the two are cut off by a crack coming from the eggs, and they turn to watch in excitement-

-500

-the island is now flush with Dunsparce, most of the other wildlife having receded. Lichen grows in patches everywhere you look-

-though Harrison, Guinevere, Giuseppe, Esmeralda, and Jean are long dead, traces of their scales, their eyes, their wings can be seen in flashes as they slither and tunnel around, over, and through the island-

-300

-Humans come to the island. The Dunsparce, so far distinct from their ancestors that the resemblance is barely noticeable and the memories have washed away, are nervous at first, but gradually realize that these new creatures mean no harm. From time to time, one Dunsparce or another runs afoul of the Vow, or a human treads on a nest and is driven off, but by and large relations are sound-

-50

Tour Guide: —And this is Kin Island, home to the largest known Dunsparce population in the world.

-those very Dunsparce watch from shadows, tunnels, and even trees. This island is populated now, and their living space has been reduced. Still, they are content-

-17

-a new Dunsparce is born. She is the image of Guinevere, and of Harrison too were he female. While their recollections are hazy, tradition is still strong in the colony, and she is given a name belonging to one of the Swarm-

-Cornelia-

-4

-a boy comes to the island, looking for them. Though none know it, his resemblance to Revy matches that of Cornelia's to Guinevere. This draws them to him, and he collects many of them, Cornelia included. Though none know where they are going, all trust him-

-2

-most of those that left with the boy have not returned, though the colony is not unduly worried. They have many new mouths to feed, and plenty to feed them-

-one has returned, however-

-she looks at her old colony through eyes tinted red by an omnipresent visor, but she remembers them regardless-

-Cornelia knows perfectly well she can't go back. She has the scent of humans on her, making her a stranger to her kind. But she can still protect them-
