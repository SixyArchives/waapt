Unnamed Island

-the grave is no longer visible. Instead, a lichen patch has spread outwards from it, now covering most of the cave-

-dozens of Dunsparce nests have been placed throughout the cave, some visible and some not. Harrison and Guinevere are curled up in one nest around several eggs, while Giuseppe and Jean are tucked away in a tunnel dug into the cave wall-

-500

-300

-Humans come to the island. -Cornelia-

-4
