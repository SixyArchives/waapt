"Your Oliver appears to be abnormal."

Mortimer: "He always was quicker than the rest."

"Oh lovely. Puns."

Mortimer: "Are you sure you have the time to banter?"

"Hmmm?"

Pi is struck from behind. Hard.

He goes down on one knee, then blasts an Electro-Ball directly backwards.

It glances off the Sableye, but does less than trace amounts of damage, do to his crazy speed.

"Oh my. This is bad, isn't it? Hmmm."

He leaps for his sword, but is intercepted with a Foul-Play, grabbed by Oliver and thrown into the wall.

Mortimer: "I don't think so, rat."

"..."

"Kist. This is going on too long."

Mortimer: "Oh dear. Oliver, don't let him touch you with that. Nasty stuff."

Oliver hangs back, wary of the deadly poison.

"Heh heh."

Taking advantage of his enemy's hesitance, Pi dashes forward to grab...

The armband.

"Can't let him have this."

Mortimer: "Ah. I was right to keep that away from you. I shall have to discover what it's significance is later, though. Oliver, kill."

"OH NO YOU DON'T"

<"Bring it, chucklehead.">

Mortimer: "..."

Mortimer: "Oh my."

Mortimer: "Rat, you just got much more dangerous in my eyes. I think I'll need collateral. What was the name of that girl? Zeta Bismarck? Ah yes. Her."

<"..."> |:

<"..."> |:<

<"..."> D:<

<"   DON'T TOUCH HER.   ">

The room rapidly cools.

Soon, it begins to get unbearable, as Pi uses a rage-fueled Sheer-Cold.

Calmly, slowly, in a tranquil state, Pi steps over and retrieves his sword.

He considers killing Mortimer, but then dismisses it as unnecessary bloodshed.

<"...">

<"Goodbye, scum.">

Route 111 - Desert - AU

-A certain party with a severe lack of screentime is currently moving along a ridge, the view of a sprawling desert valley fanning out below-

<Zoe: "Heh, it's really been a while...">

-Staring wistfully-

<Speck: "Yeah... It has...">

<Gligar!Vlad: "Hmm? Why did we come all the way out here again?">

<Vesta: "Oh. It's part of Speck's job, he's supposed to tell a contact around here about how things are going here.">

<Speck: "Eh? I... Am? But I thought that I was-!"> ._.

<Vesta: "Telling them wrong things.">

<Speck: "... Oh. Right...

But... How would I even know wh-">

-It's at this point that a female Flygon makes her presence known, by landing behind Speck's group-

<Staccata: Is everyone ready for assignments?>

<Speck: "ACK!">

<Zoe: "Whoa!">

<Gligar!Vlad: "Wait, is this th- Oh! Hi! You're that Flygon from the valley!"> ^v-v^

-And swoops in with a glomp-

<Vesta: "... Assignments?"> ._.

-Staccata blinks at being glomped, and rather awkwardly pats Gligar!Vlad on the head-

<Staccata: Yes, assignments. Did you think I was just going to let you stay here and do nothing?>

-Buzzing (or whatever Flygon equivalent thereof) somewhat nervously at the abrupt appearance of the more able-bodied Flygon and her stern demeanor-

<Speck: "U-Um... Fair enough... Wh- What was it that you w-wanted?">

<Staccata: "It's time for you all to start earning your keep. Let's see... Sandslash!">

<Zoe: "Oh? What's up? Need some holes dug or something?">

<Staccata: "Maybe later... For now though, you'll be training some of the Sandshrew and Sandslash in battle. Obviously I'm not always so up to the task, but they need to be able to fight.">

<Zoe: "Oh...? Where would I find them?">

<Staccata: "They'll be in the caves at the northwest end of the desert. If they're asleep, wake them up.">

<Zoe: "Got it. Though not sure why anyone would be asleep this early... Hup!">

-And Rollouts down a path-

<Staccata: "Hmm...Ampharos. Do you know how to signal?">

<Vesta: "Very much so, yes. Been doing it ever since I was a little lamb.">

<Staccata: "Excellent. Proceed to the wall around the southern end of the desert. You'll be working with a group of Magneton to flash signals to New Mauville.">

<Vesta: "Understood.">

<Gligar!Vlad: "Oh! Oh! Do I get to do something?"> ^v-v^

<Staccata: "...Um. Go around hugging anyone who looks down, I suppose. We do need all the morale boosts we can get.">

<Gligar!Vlad: "Yay! I'm good at hugging!">

-And zips off, leaving a stammering flight-challenged Flygon behind-

<Speck: "U-Um... Y- You wanted s-something from me?">

-Staccata stares at Speck for a long time before replying-

<Staccata: "Yes... You are the messenger to the dragons, are you not?">

-Backpedaling a bit-

<Speck: "Um... Y-Yeah..."> O_O;

<Staccata: "Excellent...you'll be reporting back to them, then. Tell them whatever they want to hear to make sure they don't bring a war party when they come to pick up the dragons here.">

-Hearing the words of the Flygon, and responding in an uncertain tone-

<Speck: "I... I... Unh...">

-Staccata pauses for a moment...and extends a grasper, rather awkwardly patting Speck on the back-

<Staccata: "Don't worry... you're what they call a dovah, right? You can tell them whatever Torchic-Tauros story you like, they'll lap it up just fine if it seems like good news.">

<Speck: "Huh?! I... I mean... If you're sure it'll work...

But... Why are you doing this for me?">

<Staccata: "...">

-As gently as likely possible-

<Staccata: "...You seem nice enough...and I can't exactly throw you back to the rest of the dragons, can I?">

-Pauses, flicks tail once, and continues in a slightly sharper tone-

<Staccata: "If you're going to be here, you may as well make yourself useful.">

-Taken aback by the Flygon's tone... Thinking-

<Speck: "... She'd never say that if she knew what I've done...

... But... I guess I can at least be useful...

I'm good at at least that much...">

-Shaking head to return back to senses-

<Speck: "U-Um... Thanks... I guess... And... I guess that's fair...">

-And continues on to his designated meeting point further down a pass-

-Staccata watches the other Flygon leave, thinking. After a while, she sighs quietly, and wings her way back into the desert-
