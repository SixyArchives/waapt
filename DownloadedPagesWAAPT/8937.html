-Scott is earning money just as before, playing songs on a street corner. Specifically, he's playing a score from a movie about dragons.*

-

-He hangs up hurriedly.-

Trent: ...This can't be good... -He starts dashing toward the gate, deciding to trust Scott.-

-After the call with Trent, Scott calls his sister.-

Scott: ~Argh, why does it always take forever to make a call when you're in a hurry!?~

Alli: Oh, hey, K-

Scott: Alli, listen. I need to know where you are, ASAP.

Alli: Oh, that's easy. I'm by the Pokemon Musical building.

Scott: ~Shit, that's on the opposite end of town, and not even near the exit to Driftveil...~ Well, could you start heading toward the south gate? I'll head toward you, and we can meet up sort-of halfway.

Alli: Um... alright?

Scott: Yeah. I'll explain later. See you.

-He hangs up, then looks around quickly-

Scott: ~Come on, there's gotta be something...~

-He sees a motorcycle.-

Scott: ~...Never used one before, but learning now is quicker than walking...~

-He mentally thanks the idiot who left the keys in the ignition, starts it up, and gets the hang of it through trial and error. He then heads in the direction of his sister.-

Scott eventually meets up with Alli

Alli: There you a-

Scott: Quick, hop on. I'll explain once we're out of here.

Alli blinks, and climbs onto the motorcycle wordlessly.

Scott: Before you ask, no, I've never done this before.

He sets off again.

-Scott and Alli finally meet up with Trent near the south gate-

Trent: Okay, now will you tell us what's up?

Scott: Yes... but we need to start heading toward Castelia quickly. Unfortunately, there's not enough room on this for all of us, and we need to stick together, so...

-He gets off, followed by Alli. Noticing a group of dragons near the gate, he sends out Skye.-

Scott: We're gonna have to fly past the gate, since it doesn't look like they're gonna let us out on foot...

Trent: Seriously, wh-

Scott: Just send out a flying mon!

-Trent blinks, then sends out Cline-

Scott: Let's go!

-He climbs onto Skye's back. Trent climbs onto Cline's.-

Skye: <Any idea what's happening?>

Cline: <Nope. Sorry.>

Alli: What about me?

Scott: Hope you don't fall!

-Skye and Cline take off, the former grabbing Alli-

Route 4

-The group lands, Skye being fairly worn out from carrying two people.-

Trent: Okay, seriously. What the hell is going on?

Scott: Remember how said I was able to understand mons?

Trent: Yeah...?

Scott: I overheard some talking about a bomb.

Trent and Alli: What!?

Alli: ...You've been through a lot of crazy stuff... I guess we'll have to trust you...

Scott: Now... if they aren't bluffing... then it's likely they'll keep it up elsewhere. And we don't know what that "elsewhere" consists of... so I need to warn my brother. He's in Johto.

Alli: Right...

Scott: But to get ahold of him, I need to talk to another friend of mine... So give me a sec...

He calls Diane. Meanwhile, the wind is starting to pick up...

Phone log: Scott - AU!Diane

Scott: Diane? You there?

Diane: Yeah, what's wrong?

Scott: Listen, is Keith around?

Diane: Um... yeah, why?

Scott: I need to talk to him as soon as possible.

Diane: All right... Keith? Scott needs to talk to you for a second.

Keith: Oh? Wonder what this is about... Um, hello?

Scott: Keith, listen. You and Diane need to get out of there as soon as possible. A bomb is about to be dropped on Nimbasa, and we're not sure if whatever's happening is a worldwide thing.

Keith: If it's worldwide, what makes you think leaving Ecruteak would be safer?

Scott: I don't know! Just go! Get a boat to Sinnoh or something!

Keith: ...Right. Least populated, most likely to be attacked last. All right. I guess I'll have to trust you.

Scott: Yes! I gotta go now, there's a sandstorm starting to kick up! We need to get to Castelia as quick as possible to do the same! See you!

End Call

Trent: This is not looking good... We need to rest somewhere.

Diane: Right...

The sandstorm rages...

Scott: ...

Alli: Guys...

Route 4

...mmmmmmmmmmmmMMMmmmmmmmmmmmm...

Trent: ...You've gotta be kidding me.

Olivine

Keith: ...Here we are... The last time we'll likely see this place intact...

Route 4

The sandstorm rages...

...mmmmmmmmmmmmMMMmmmmmmmmmmmm...

Route 4

The sandstorm rages...

...mmmmmmmmmmmmMMMmmmmmmmmmmmm...
