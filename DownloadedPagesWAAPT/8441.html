Goldenrod Department Store

While most of the people who had taken a trip to the department store that day to do some not-quite-but-very-nearly last minute Christmas Shopping had expected the store to be busy, few would have anticipated spotting a Feraligatr accompanied by a Porygon-Z seemingly doing his own shopping.

It did make Lake feel rather conspicuous, the way everyone kept staring at him as he and Ad browsed the wares, a few patrons even going out of the way to keep their distance, perhaps afraid that he would suddenly attack them.

If only there were more Pokémon like him, so that he wouldn't feel so out of place whenever he needed to do something a human would do...but it wasn't a huge problem. He had more important things on his mind.

Like what to buy everyone close to him for Christmas.

Lake: <I think I understand why Luke's mother always got so stressed around this time of year.>

Ad Min turns away from browsing an electronics display with interest to look at the Feraligatr.

Ad Min: Why is that?

Lake: <Because you have to figure out the best gift for a whole bunch of peoplemons, buy said gifts - before Christmas day, I might add - and hope that they like 'em. Nothing easy about that.>

Ad Min: Funny. I would expect you to be good at this sort of thing considering your heightened empathy.

Lake: <Well, I have some idea what to get for a few peoplemons...>

He pauses in front of a display of plushies, gazing at it in interest.

<But others I'm not so certain.>

He pauses as he picks up a Feraligatr plushie from the display, gazing at it curiously.

Ad Min: Ooh, are you planning on buying that for Nadia? I understand she likes plushies.

Lake: <Uh...more like I was thinking of getting it for Toothless.>

Ad Min: ...Oh.

Feraligatr Plushie: <You know, it's polite to ask a girl first before setting her up on a date.>
