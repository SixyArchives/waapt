Eridanus: Ugh. Next in order... Fox! Use Rock Slide!

Viola: Use Bulldoze again!

Fox was sent out, and got slowed down by the glueish web getting everywhere. He then parroted Shuckle and send out bunch of rocks via ripple at Crustle, while getting hit by the shockwave thing, getting slowed down even further.

Viola: Use your claws with X-Scissor!

Eridanus: oh... you... Dig!

Fox dug tunnel, digging around the arena...

Viola: Do not lose focus... Focus is important both in battle and when taking a photo, you know!

As Fox leapt out of the tunnel, dealing damage... he got sent flying by a limegreen X Crustle formed with its claws.

Fox: <Well... that hurt.>

Eridanus: Bite it!

Viola: Use Smack Down and bring it down to earth!

Fox tried charging at Crustle to bite it with its massive pincers... but that didn't work, and he got mauled by a fast-velocity rock.

Eridanus: Not good... that leaves only Vivi...

Vivi: <I shall avenge my brooothers! To the starship!>

Forge: <...welp.>

Eridanus: Flame Charge to get speed back!

Viola: Use X-Scissor again!

As Vivi started to run, the web it got everywhere incinerated, and its speed was brought back to normal. And then it hit Crustle. And then got thrown back by the X-Scissor.

Eridanus: Heat Wave!

Viola: Send Rock Slide!

Vivi got hit by the ripple of rocks, while sending a blow of hot wind. It didn't burn Crustle, but did some damage...

Vivi survived the attack, somehow.

Vivi: <Waah...?>

Viola: Shell Smash into Bulldoze!

Eridanus: ...what was that?! Two moves?! That works?! How.... Flame Charge!

As Vivi ran through the Gym's arena, Crustle broke its armor, making it stronger and faster, but also more fragile, and then sent a shockwave at Vivi, who crashed into Crustle, being even more faster... the shockwave did damage... and then the Shell Smash wore off.

But the wizard was undeterred.

Vivi: <Stupid... bug... insect... someting thingie! Whadda ya think yer doing to me?>

Viola: Your Pokemon... it has something... a glint in its eyes... It... refuses to go down. This situation is... Very interesting! But, it won't last too much longer. Use X-Scissor!

Eridanus: ...there's something weird here... I don't know what. Use Flame Charge, collide even faster! The faster you are, the greater force of impact! And that means more damage! That's the laws of physics for you!

Vivi was concealed in the flames once again, becoming even faster than before, starting to slightly blur. He went through the X-Scissor as if it was nothing... and then started to glow white.

Eridanus: Oh. Good. Lord.

Viola: It is! Your Pokemon... is...

Vivi's white glow started to intensify, until he was all covered in clear, strong white.

Eridanus: ...yes. Yes. YES!

As they collided, an explosion happened, sending both flying back, covered in smoke. Crustle was still standing. But when the smoke went off... Where Vivi was standing was no more a Fennekin... But a Braixen!

Eridanus: Now the battle is more... even.

Fox: <HOLY GROUDON. DID HE JUST.>

Forge: <YES, YES HE DID, MAN.>

Vivi: <I don't feel like myself anymore. I feel... I feel... I FEEL ZE POWER.>

Eridanus: Vivi... let me show what you have learned.

Viola: This can't end now! Smack Down!

Vivi started to spin its clockwise, releasing a beam of pink circles towards Crustle, which attacked with a fast velocity rock. The rock didn't do as much to Vivi as previous attacks. Meanwhile, Crustle got visibly confused by the attack, and pushed back.

Viola: It can't be... Shell Smash into X-Scissor!

Eridanus: Vivi is now visibly more powerful... That's it, Vivi! One final Flame Charge!

Vivi started running once again; this time on two legs, while Crustle buffed itself and charged at opponent. Yet another explosion occurs... leaving Crustle down, and Vivi somewhat hanging on.

Viola: Oh well... I guess evolution made it stronger than I thought. Well, you know what happens. Take this Badge a a proof of victory, and this TM.

Eridanus received the Bug Badge!
Eridanus received TM83 Infestation!

Viola: It's Infestation. A trapping move of Bug-type. Can be useful, if you know how to use it correctly.

Eridanus: Thank you. Honestly, this surprised me.

Viola: Yes, evolution of your Fennekin was... something unexpected. It's what makes battles exciting. Something that happens can turn the tide of battle. Makes for a great memory. By the way of memories... Some people suggest that your mind is like a camera - it can capture and save memories... everywhere... Maybe there's a grain of truth in this. I am fairly sure the situation would make up for a great shot, though. Sure there was determination.

Eridanus: Well then, I'll be off. I have somewhere to go. Until the next time!
