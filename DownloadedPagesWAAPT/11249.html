Sophia: <Try lifthing this. Remember, you must know the object with all your senses. Focus on the ball only. Isolate it in your mind.>

Ralts!Nathan: <I'll try.>

Nathan: <This really strains me. Was it like this to you as a Ralts, too?>

Sophia: <Better believe it. Usually psychokinetic abilities manifest after several battles or trials.>

Harvey: <Surely you didn't expect it to be that easy, did you? Birds don't naturally know how to fly, we had to train to do that!>

Nathan: <You're right. But I'm not gonna give up. I'll just try again. Later.> Nathan removed his armband and became human again.

"For now, let's eat something."
