Solaceon, that night

- Axel is sitting alone when Terry and Gino walk up to him. -

Terry: Axel?

Axel: Yes, newbie? Well, I guess you aren’t the newbie anymore with Ruto here now…

Terry: I was asking Gino here about your general hostility towards new members of the team… and he said Lina found you severely injured.

Axel: Gino! Why’d you spill the beans on that?!

Gino: Because he needed to find out eventually, nyan!

Terry: What he couldn’t tell me- and what I’m curious about- is how you ended up that way.

Axel: Alright. You two really wanna know?

- they both nod-

Axel: Alright.

- He sighs-

The reason I don’t like new members at first is because I’m afraid I’ll get too close to them…

Past, somewhere in Johto

- A trainer walks into a house filled with Empire Soul merchandise. He lets a Houndour out of a Pokeball. –

Trainer: Alright, guys, I found one with the right personality! Meet our version of Axel!

- The Houndour looks around and sees a Gengar, an Octillery, a Skarmory, a Cloyster, a Garchomp, a Zoroark, and a Nidoking. –

Axel: Didn’t know the freak show was in town.

Gengar: He’s perfect!

About a year later

- Axel, now a Houndoom , walks up to the Gengar –

Axel: You wanted me for something, Xemnas?

Xemnas: You’ve met our newest member, right, Axel?

Axel: The Riolu? Roxas? Yeah.

Xemnas: I want you to be his... mentor.

Axel: Oh, come on! I'm still mad that you made me care for that lazy good-for nothing Corphish! I can't believe that slob managed to evolve...

Xemnas: I feel this one will be... different.

Axel: Fine, then...

A few hours later

Axel: And that's how you open chests with your paws. Any questions?

Roxas the Riolu: ...

Axel: ...

Roxas: ...I could do that blindfolded.

Axel: -Grins- Woah! Kid's got a snarky streak. I like that.

A few days later

- Axel and Roxas are slurping Sea Salt vanilla ice cream out of bowls. Suddenly, Roxas senses something. He looks up and notices a Porygon approaching. -

Roxas: Hello!

Porygon: - in a feminine voice- Oh! Um... hi?

Axel: Are you one of the new guys?

Porygon: Yes...

Roxas: Would you like to sit and watch the sunset with us?

Porygon: Um, sure...

Axel: What's your name, anyways?

Porygon: Xion...

- Xemmnas watches from a distance. -

Xemnas: Yes... all according to the script...

A few weeks later

- Xemnas has called his fellow Pokemon to a meeting -

Xemnas: I have good news, everyone!

Demyx the Crawdaunt: I hope it's good! You woke me up from my nap!

Xemnas: We're leaving our pathetic excuse for a trainer!

- There is much murmuring among the Pokemon; only Xion, now a Porygon-2, looks concerned. -

A few days later

- Axel and Xion are arguing-

Axel: Wait, you're leaving?

Xion: Look... I've found out things about Xemnas... He's planning horrible ideas... Manipulating us all...

Axel: I don't like him either, but you can't just leave! What about Roxas? What about me?

Xion: But that's exactly what he wants! and I'm not going to let him suceed!

- She floats off-

Axel: Go on! You just keep running! But I'll always be there to bring you back!

A few weeks later

- Axel is talking with a Roserade and a Luxray -

Larxene the Luxray: ...So me and Marluxia were going to overthrow Xemnas. You in on it?

Axel: Um... sure... Something weird - and familiar - is going on...

A few days later

- Axel is confronting Xemnas -

Axel: OK, first Xion leaves... Then I get sucked into conspiracies... and now Roxas leaves? Something is going on here, and it all seems strangely familiar. I want to find out why.

Xemnas: Don't you see, Axel? It's all part of the game.

Axel: You're starting to sound like that Dunspar-

- He realizes something-

...You're emulating that game our old trainer loved so much, aren't you?!

Xemnas: Indeed.

- Axel snarls-

Axel: You deserted our Trainer for a life of petty crime... you scared off some of my friends... you're turning our group against each other... all for the sake of some stupid GAME?!

Xemnas: Ah, Axel. Always so rebellious...

Axel: How's this for a rebellion?!

- He Dark Pulses Xemnas, who gets knocked back by the force -

Xemnas: Traitor! Traitor!

- The others begin rushing in and attacking Axel, who runs-

Xemnas: Good riddance, fool.

A few days later

- An exhausted Axel is making his way through the undergrowth of a forest, and collapses in the middle of a path. -

Axel: I'm going to die here...

- Suddenly, he notices a Shiny Salamence-

Axel: Oh Arceus... that Salamence is going to finish me off...

Gino: I found something, nyan!

- Lina rushes in and picks up Axel -

Lina: Oh you poor thing! I'll get you to a Pokemon Center...

A few hours later

- Axel is healed and is gettting some love from Lina-

Lina: Can you see if he has a name, Chrome?

Chrome: He said earlier his name was Axel...

Lina: Aww, Axel, you're so cute!

- Axel wags his tail-

Present

Gino: So that's what happened to you!

Terry: Interesting... Are you interested in tellign the others?

Axel: No. I could barely bring myself to tell you two.

Terry: But what if they try and find you?

- Axel looks up at the moon -

Axel: I'll just have to hope that doesn't happen.
