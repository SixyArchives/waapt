Off to a mountain to the west, he finally distinguishes something...

A figure flying around the mountain, describing very precise circles and apparently practising some maneuvers.

Salamence — <Heh, now that might be something...>

Salamence flies ahead a short distance, being careful to keep an eye on the ground below him. As he gets closer and closer to the mountain, the figure flying in circles there becomes more and more clear to his vision — not that he would have needed it what with the very specific flight pattern.

Local!Salamence — <Uhm, this Dragon Claw is turning cool! Tiruriruri...~> — flies, observes.
