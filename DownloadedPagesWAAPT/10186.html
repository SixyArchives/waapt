<"Amphy"!Shaun: "Good.">

Celadon - AU - Clinic

<Chansey: "A mon that is professional can often win the respect of even those that oppose her. Since you're in a chatty mood... Do you mind telling me a bit about yourself? Do you have a name?">

<Vektor: "Uh... 'Vektor', I guess...">

<Chansey: "Hmm? Interesting... It's less of a mouthful than most that I've heard from the patients here... So where are you from, and how'd you wind up here?">

-Visibly freezing up at the question-

<Vektor: "I- I... Uh...">

<Chansey: "... If you don't want to talk about it, suit yourself.">

-And takes the potion bottle and begins to leave-

<Chansey: "Do eat the food, though. Starving yourself when hurt doesn't do yourself any favors.">

-And leaves-

<Vektor: "Hmm...">

-Leaving a Haxorus to pick at his food slowly-

That Night - Clinic

-A certain Haxorus is up, unable to sleep-

<???: "... I guess we should be happy that we didn't get off worse...">

<Vektor: !!! >

-And turns and sees a familiar defeated-looking Fraxure-

<Vektor: "My Arceus, I'm seeing things...">

-And turns away, attempting to ignore its presence-

<???: "... You were going to kill me earlier, weren't you?">

-Not responding, trying to pretend that the presence isn't there-

<Vektor: "...">

<???: "But why didn't you? I mean... I'm scared of dying... But... How could this possibly end any other way-?">

-Roaring-

<Vektor: "SHUT UP! Is that all you can think about?!">

-Flinching, though regaining composure-

<???: "... Yes.">

-Looking down at himself, and seeing a Fraxure's body in place of his own, with the other occupant in the room gone-

<Vektor: "Ack! Wh-What?">

-Staring a moment, dumbfounded-

<Vektor: "It's just who you really are... In any other circumstance... You'd be me... It's why your armor is thinner than normal. Even with that body... Nothing's changed... I'm still living on order to order... I'm still so lonely... I'm-">

-Pausing-

<Vektor: "I'm still afraid...">

-Shaking head-

<Vektor: "No! Something did change! It can be made to happen again! I'm not this! Not anymore!">

-And sees that the room has gone back to normal... Looking down and seeing familiar bandages-

<Vektor: "... Not... Anymore...">

-And slowly drifts off to an uneasy sleep-

Sometime The Next Day

-A certain Haxorus is getting bandages removed from his hostile encounter with the local murders-

-Inspecting the regions underneath-

<Heal Bell!Dragonite: *whistle* "That spray those Chansey were going on about really must be something... I certainly wasn't expecting this little scarring.">

<Vektor: "Huh?">

-And inspects his formerly bandaged arm, and true enough, the only signs that it was once injured are a few faint, easy to miss chinks in his hide-

<Heal Bell!Dragonite: "I mean, it's less of a story during mating season, but hey. You've got plenty of others to make up for it!"> ^_^

<Vektor: "... I guess...">

-Noticing the Haxorus' seeming apathy to the whole matter-

<Heal Bell!Dragonite: "Eh? That's weird... I could have sworn that someone said that Opal Roar claws put a lot of emphasis onto their sc-">

-It is at this moment that a Druddigon enters-

<Supervisor!Druddigon: "Is that Haxorus that was supposed to be reassigned here?">

<Heal Bell!Dragonite: "Uh... Yeah. He's right here, why?">

<Supervisor!Druddigon: "Could you give us a bit of a moment?">

<Heal Bell!Dragonite: "Hmm? I don't suppose it could hurt...">

-And departs, quietly closing the door behind itself-

<Supervisor!Druddigon: "So I hear that you've been a little high strung out there.">

<Vektor: "Mmph. It's just a bit of a sudden transition, that's all.">

<Supervisor!Druddigon: "Well... I was just going to say... That I might have an opening for you... Something that will a bit easier on your nerves.">

<Vektor: "... Huh? What would that be?">
