Dear Aiura,

By the time you are reading this letter, it is possible that I may have met my end. The very fact that you are reading this letter in the first place means that I have taken the risk of finding some suitable method of delivery. As it stands, these may well be the last days of my life. I doubt you would believe me were I to tell you the full story, but suffice it to say that I have fallen out of favour with the Ghost Lord. I have no wish to regain my former standing. Rather, I plan on doing everything I can to ruin it further. I shouldn't need to outline the potential consequences of such an action to you.

If this truly is to be my last letter, then I will not waste it by pleading for forgiveness or redemption. I am beyond either. Nor will I bother with overwrought declarations of love. After everything I've put you through and the degrading manner in which I've treated you, I would not be surprised if you wanted nothing more to do with me, even if I were to see this war through to its end. As much as the thought pains my heart, I have come to terms with this fact.

No, instead I wish to thank you. Thank you for being a part of my life, for making my brief tenure as a Warlord my fondest memory. Thank you for giving me a reason to keep living, keep fighting, even as everything else in my life collapsed around me. Thank you for your continued faith in me even as I became everything I had once fought against. And above all, thank you for being the most beautiful, intelligent and caring human being I have ever had the privilege of meeting.

Thank you, Aiura.

Yours, Lucanus
