She wore a black, short sleeved shirt. A hood pulled over her head. Around her neck she wore a necklace, some sort of pendant hanging from a thing, metal chain. Standing beside her was Turntbl, the wild Scrafty bit down on a twig he held between his teeth. This place was plenty hotter than Sinnoh. He rolled the twig in his mouth and looked over to his master.

She grinned and pushed up her rose-colored glasses. Raising her hand, which was covered over in a black glove, toward the tall, fancy hotel erected in the distance. Turntbl gave a thumbs up and smirked. This would be fun. The two soon raced toward the Ambrette Hotel. With Turntbl's help they were soon scaling the side of the building. The pair soon arrived on the floor belonging to their prey. She held her hand out to Turntbl and he handed her his twig. The trainer used the twig to pick the window open. How did she do it? Oh well it was no ordi— no it was a normal twig. The girl simply jammed open the window. After jimmying the window open she crawled into the room. Turntbl quietly closed the window and looked over to Rex who was snaking along the ground.

Turntabl seated himself out on the windowsill and crossed his leg over his knee. He waited for it.

"BANZAI!" The cosplaying trainer leapt up and moved to pounce the sleeping Mezzy before crawling under her covers, long, pants tail wagging.
