Lucius: This... er, this is Lucanus. One of my ancestors.

Lucius: ...I guess you haven't heard, then...

Sarah: Heard what?

-Lucius doesn't say anything for a few moments. When he does, his voice is distinctly weak.-

Lucius: ...Sarah... I'm... dead.

-The area drops several degrees in temperature.-

Sarah: ...You what...?

-Lucius hesitates, before pulling his scarf down and showing a nasty cut on his throat.-

Lucius: Dead. Killed. Passed on. Alice, too... I'm here because this was the only way I could have—

Sarah: Who did it?

-Lucius cringes at the emotional burst.-

Lucius: Sarah—

Sarah: Who the fuck did this to you?! You promised you wouldn't get yourself killed, so you better either be joking, or tell me who the fuck killed you!

-Lucius takes a few steps back in terror.-

Lucius: ...T-two people... One was this creepy white-haired guy named Abel — Kieran Abel — and the other was someone from Cipher. She called herself S—

Lucanus: Pardon the interruption, but perhaps you should take a moment to calm down before you decide you want to try hunting down these two.

-Sarah turns away.-

Sarah: I've already decided.

-She walks away, fading out of sight.-

Lucanus: ...So my fear was justified. This needless revenge cycle takes another round.

Lucius: Ugh... Dammit, there I go again... Even in death, I can't keep my damn mouth shut...

Lucanus: All we can do now is wait... And hope that she stops herself before she does something she regrets.

-He leads Lucius back toward the fog, fading out of sight.-
