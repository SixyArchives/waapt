Sarah: Who did it?

-Lucius cringes at the emotional burst.-

Lucius: Sarah—

Sarah: Who the fuck did this to you?! You promised you wouldn't get yourself killed, so you better either be joking, or tell me who the fuck killed you!

-Lucius takes a few steps back in terror.-

Lucius: ...T-two people... One was this creepy white-haired guy named Abel — Kieran Abel — and the other was someone from Cipher. She called herself S—

Lucanus: Pardon the interruption, but perhaps you should take a moment to calm down before you decide you want to try hunting down these two.

-Sarah turns away.-

Sarah: I've already decided.

-She walks away, fading out of sight.-

Lucanus: ...So my fear was justified. This needless revenge cycle takes another round.
