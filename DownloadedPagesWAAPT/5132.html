Future (Lilycove City: Contest Hall)

Rex: But but...!

Rex looked pleadingly at the woman behind the counter in front of him, Dahlia giving a similar look from his shoulder. It wasn't fair...they'd come this far and they were told that they couldn't even compete!?

Receptionist: I'm sorry, sir; it's Hoenn policy. You can't enter the Master Rank competition here until you complete the three other ranked contests with your Bellossom.

He sighed, running a hand through his hair in irritation. He honestly didn't feel like doing a lot of back-tracking just for a contest...but it did seem really important to Dahlia. Besides, he had captured her from her home and taken her around the world to help him with things; the least he could do was reciprocate.

Rex: Alright. So, if I do these contests, I can come back here and compete, right?

The woman frowned, a feeling of dread starting to well up in the boy's stomach.

Receptionist: Yes, but...

She sighed, not wanting to ruin his mood any further, but it couldn't be helped, given the situation.

Receptionist: You see, the Battle Frontier will be opening soon. As such, Battle Halls will be given a lot more precedence in the near future, requiring the presence of our judges. I'm afraid that your application for Contest Registration has come too late at this point in time.

Rex: ... ... ...

Receptionist: I-I'm sorry... At the very least, I can sign you up now in the event you wish to participate in future Hoenn contests.

Dahlia: <... ... ...>

Without a word to Rex or even a glance at the receptionist, Dahlia leapt from her trainer's shoulder, shuffling away off towards the reception area. Rex feel a pang of guilt, stepping away from the counter and following after her. He hoped that she wasn't too angry...
