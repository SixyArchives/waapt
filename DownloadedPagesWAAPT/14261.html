"Oh wow, you broke the high score." said Duke, looking both amused and somewhat scared "Remind me to not ever let you get hold of a gun."

Lyuri gives a proud smile "Your turn, let's see how fast you are on the trigger."

Duke seemed a bit nervous, but still accepted the challenge. He didn't last nearly as long as Lyuri in the game

"You take way too long aiming! By the time you fired you'd be dead twice over!" said Lyuri in a incredulous tone.

"Never to take me to a war where firearms are involved I guess." said Duke, sweatdropping "What now?"

"Hmmm..."

Lyuri then scans the arcade for something fun and she quickly finds the typical DDR stage and smirks

"How about some dancing?"

"E-Eh...?" asked Duke, already dreading the idea "But I-"

"You just need some good rhythm and you'll be fine, you don't even really need to dance that much! What, are you too manly to do it?" asked Lyuri, with a daring smile

Duke sighs and just gives Lyuri a half-hearted nod of agreement. The girl then drags him to the stage and teaches him how to play.

"Now what song..." said Lyuri, looking at all the songs in the game "... Oooh, I didn't know they had this!"

They start playing the game and, to Duke's surprise, Lyuri started singing along as she played and she put quite a lot of effort into her singing. Duke almost felt like stopping and just listening to her sing, but he kept on just to not disappoint Lyuri. He ended up having quite a lot of fun much to his own surprise. Once they were done they got off the stage, Lyuri being the clear winner again

"Okay, I'm going to teach you the art of playing videogames when we get back to the hotel." said Lyuri, folding her arms in mock frustration

Duke just chuckles "By the way, you're a great singer. Your voice distracted me while I was playing, was that your cunning plan?" he asked, smiling

Lyuri blushes, clearly embarrassed "O-Oh... I guess I got a little carried away."

"Can I ask a favor?"

"Yeah?"

"Can you record a... What's the term again... Cover, of the song? I would love to be able to hear you singing whenever I want."

Lyuri blushes even harder "I guess I could ask Hikari to help me do that."

"Great! So, anywhere else you want to go?" asked Duke, looking quite happy

"Uh... Yeah... I wanna see the city... At night. On the top of the Prism Tower." said Lyuri, her face getting less red

Duke raises an eyebrow "But you-"

"It's fine."

Duke looks worried for a moment, but then gives a hint of a smile "All right then, to the Prism Tower we go."
