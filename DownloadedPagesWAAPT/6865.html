Advancing on Team Iris was a full squad of mons, all slowly marching forward, the earth moving with each simultaneous stomp toward the team. At least a dozen or more Bastiodon, their shields gleaming, stood side by side one another, a full force pushing ever closer. A low, harmonious war chant could be heard emanating from the Bastiodon, marching with a steadfast confidence that all in their path would be crushed. Behind them, another humming noise could be heard echoing off the walls... Wingbeats. Brief flashes could be seen as they sped along behind the Bastiodon, peeking through the tiny cracks in the wall; Yanmega. Their compound eyes were taking note of each mon before them, who were strongest, and where the easiest to snipe would be. They moved out of sync with one another, darting around safely behind the the Bastiodon, each eager to make the shot first. Behind those sounds, however... Was a larger one. Four sets of feet crashing into the earth at once, shaking the ground beneath them with every slow step. Covered in gleaming crimson armor, they stood behind the Yanmega. Their ruler's enforcers, the elite marched, ready for whatever stood in their way. Members of The Rhyperior Guard. This united squad, and the sound of their unstoppable force, became louder with each passing second in the ears of Team Iris, as they came into view.
