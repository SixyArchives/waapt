Nihilism strokes Psyche's head. "Indeed... Trust is such a strange thing, anyway. Nothing to truly hang on to. And why should you care anyway? You are the only one who can truly give meaning to your life, and you are doing such a MARVELOUS job with that."

Psyche rocks on the ground. "No... You're wrong..."

Despair touches a finger to her forehead. "Dearie, you know we're right. You know it in your heart of hearts. Just accept it, and you won't have to live this pitiful life anymore."
