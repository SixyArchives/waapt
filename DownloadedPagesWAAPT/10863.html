Pugilis - Around 600 years ago

-Lucanus is preparing to make his public address, striding up and down the war room nervously.-

Revan: ~Arceus, if you're this nervous about making a speech, then you're going to need a change of breeches whenever you lead a battle. You rely on me to write most of the policies, you should at least have the stomach to read them out.~

Lucanus: I... I suppose so. This is my duty, after all.

-Hesitating slightly, he clears his throat and steps out onto the balcony with Revan behind him, where a relatively large crowd is waiting for him.-

Lucanus: People of Pugilis!

-His appearance is met with rather patchy applause.-

Citizen #13: Who's the posh toff again?

Citizen #7: His name's Lucanus, remember? The new kid Takuma appointed.

Citizen #13: Oh yeah, him.

Lucanus: Now, I am unaccustomed to public speaking, so I will make this speech brief. But I assure you that I have been hard at work strengthening Pugilis in preparation for the dark days ahead. I am delighted to announce that we have many new allies, all of whom are ready to fight by our side should the need arise. The first and greatest of these alliances is - as I am sure most of you are already aware - our defense pact with the noble nation of Illusio!

-This announcement is not met with jubilation, but rather a somewhat derisive mumbling throughout the crowd.-

Citizen #18: We're allying with Illusio? What have they ever done for us?

Citizen #21: Yeah! This kind of thing would never have happened if Takuma was still in charge!

Lucanus: I am aware that our two kingdoms have not been on the friendliest of terms in the past. Our rivalry has even boiled over into open war at several points, but now I hope that we can put those differences between us and embrace the days ahead as allies. We cannot squander ourselves on petty animosity when there are greater and darker threats that we must face together.

-The muttering continues, but soon dies down when Lucanus clears his throat.-

Lucanus: Illusio is not the only nation that stands by our side, however. I am pleased to announce that we can also count the nations of Terrera and Yaksha amongst our allies!

-This time, there is a much fuller cheer, but it is soon interrupted by yells within the crowd.-

Citizen #108: What about our own problems, though?

Citizen #117: Like the food shortages!

Citizen #42: Yeah! I had to eat my own Machoke yesterday! And he tasted awful! >:(

Lucanus: Erm...

-Lucanus turns to Revan.-

Lucanus: What should I do about the food shortages?

Revan: ~Tell them that you've made an arrangement with Illusio and that they're willing to supply us. It'll help improve public opinion of our alliance.~

Lucanus: But there is no such arrangement.

Revan: ~Not yet, anyway.~

Lucanus: What are you - ah, I see!

-He turns back to the crowd.-

Lucanus: Our allies in Illusio have graciously agreed to provide us with whatever supplies we may need to help us through our difficult times!

-This announcement is met with a cheer.-

Lucanus: Soon, Pugilis will be at the centre of a mighty alliance! We will not bend the knee to tyrants or conquerors! And eventually, we will have the strength to overthrow the Ghost Lord himself!

-With that, Lucanus turns and walks off the balcony, Revan following behind.-

Revan: ~Must you always be so melodramatic? You made it sound as though we were planning on embarking on some sort of crusade.~

Lucanus: Who knows? Someday we may do just that.

-Revan sighs.-

Revan: ~I swear, Lucanus, one of these days you're going to be the death of me.~

Lucanus: Not now, though. For now, we have our own matters of state to attend to.

Revan: ~Namely that neither Terrera nor Yaksha have been informed of our decision to formalise our alliance.~

Lucanus: ...How did I manage to forget that? Servants, bring me some parchment at once! I have messages to send!

-A nondescript serving girl brings Lucanus two sheets of parchment, and he quickly scribbles down a pair of messages before offering them to Brandon and Takuma.-

Lucanus: Take these to Terrera and Yaksha as fast as possible.

Brandon: Of course, my lord.

Takuma: What am I, your servant? Have the spymaster do it or something.

Lucanus: The spymaster? But I don't even know who-

Serving Girl: Here.

-Lucanus blinks in disbelief at the serving girl, whilst Takuma sighs.-

Takuma: Ayame, why do you always insist on these pointless disguises?

Serving Girl Ayame: I'm just keeping in practice. After all, there's not much point in being a spy if everyone knows who you are, is there?

Lucanus: ...Look, could you just deliver this message?

Ayame: Sure thing, boss.

-Ayame takes the one message and sets out for Yaksha with her Machoke behind her, whilst Brandon takes the other and heads off to Terrera, accompanied by Sumac.-
