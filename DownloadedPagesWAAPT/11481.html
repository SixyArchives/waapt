Ignis

A palace guard walks over to Agitato.

Guard: Civillian, state the reason for your presence at the Ignisaen Residential Palace.

Spectran Catacombs

Tsubasa looks around at the crowded catacomb halls. It seems that a number of various Spectran people - from all walks of life - have gathered together in the rebel spirit. Tsubasa glances at the crowd - nervous at its massive size – before Gaston gives her a knowing glance and nod. Tsubasa nods back and swallows her nervousness as she makes her speech

Tsubasa: My fellow, brothers, sisters, Spectrans of all ages! I am Tsubasa of Avia. You have gathered ’ere today because of a rumour that has been goin’ around the dusty, neglected streets of th’ city! A rumour that has hit home for all of you in th’ catacombs at this moment!

A rumour about a planned revolution to overthrow this country of the Tyrant who rules it!

There is a large cheer from the crowd. The words seem to resonate with the Spectran populace.

Tsubasa: Now, my fellow Spectran’s, I have somethin’ to confess with you. I am a honest person, so I won’t be hiding any secrets from you. My confession, to you, is that I was once employed by the bossman ‘imself. The tyrannical Ghost Lord.

There is a murmur among the crowd. Tsubasa seems nervous for a moment, but regains her courage and continues.

Tsubasa: Now, I know what yer thinkin’, my dear Spectrans, but have no fear. I did not take up the job because I wanted to help the bastard. I took it up because ‘e promised to make life better for his people. Well you know what? I did ‘is little fetchquest, and you, my friends, are still living in the dust and squalor that has plagued this country since ‘is inauguration. However, while in the castle under ‘is short service, if found out things about ‘im.

The crowd has gone quiet. Intently listening into what Tsubasa is saying.

Tsubasa: The most important thing is, ‘e has an utter contempt for his compatriots, and for ‘is people. That he would rather spend countless amounts of time, gold and lives for petty wars paying theives and assasins to fetch a small piece of jewellery owned by a warlord halfway across the country. Yes, my fellow Spectrans, the Ghost Lord cares more about the necklace of the also tyrannical Izumi of Ignis than he cares about the wellbeing of his people. Not to mention the utterly inhumane way he treats his POW, giving them A Fate Worse Than Death.

There is a grumbling among the crowd.

Tsubasa: We don’t deserve to be treated like this. We don’t deserve to live in squalor while ‘e sits fat n’ happy on his throne. We deserve a country that everyone, from the rich man in his palace to the poor man in the broken down shack, can live happy and content. We deserve better than this! WE DESERVE A FREE SPECTRA!

There is a chant of agreement with Tsubasa.

Tsubasa: DOWN WITH THE TYRANT! DOWN WITH THE GHOST LORD!

Crowd: DOWN WITH THE TYRANT! DOWN WITH THE GHOST LORD!

Tsubasa: FREE SPECTRA!

Crowd: FREE SPECTRA!

The crowd cheers, crying chants of “Free Spectra!” and “Down with the Ghost Lord!”. Gaston looks over to Tsubasa from the platform and smiles.
