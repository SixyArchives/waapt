123677 Crewe26th Dec 2011 10:41:38 AM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
Li'l Punk
EFFIE sent out ARGO and DISCOVERY

SHANE sent out HARIYAMA and SHIFTRY

Argo and Discovery square up across from the pair of very intimidating Magma Pokemon. The two glance at each other, then take a deep breath and prepare to attack.

"You know what to do," Effie says calmly, stepping back. She's nervous about this battle—her team doesn't battle often, and never against such tough-looking opponents, but she has faith in her Pokemon.

"Arm Thrust. Razor Leaf," the Magma man snaps, folding his arms over his chest and smirking.

Discovery shoots her Aurora Beam at the ground, freezing it over, then starts sliding along towards the Shiftry, readying a Powder Snow to blind her. Argo charges right at the Hariyama, preparing a Take Down.

However, Discovery would find her path cut off by a large volume of pale yellow stomach, and is launched back as Shane's Hariyama Arm Thrusts her head back against her shoulders. Coughing a few times, she tries to fire another Aurora Beam at the ground, but is unable too. The Hariyama lumbers over and picks her up, then spits out a Brine and uses Seismic Toss to launch her away. The Sealeo lands on her side and skids a few yards. She tries to get up, but drops down, and Effie recalls her.

Argo, meanwhile, is interrupted from coming to Discovery's aid by a flurry of Razor Leafs from the Shiftry, herding him away from the Hariyama. He gets cut up and take a lot of damage, retreating slightly and eyeing the two.

EFFIE sent out BLUEBIRD

Bluebird bristles, and immediately starts Quick Attacking in a circle around the two, letting out Sonic Booms at the Shiftry and Water Guns at the Hariyama. Argo watches her carefully, then launches a Mud Bomb at the Hariyama, timing it so as not to hit his teammate. For a moment, it seems as if the two are beginning to wear down the bewildered Magma Pokemon.

"Dig. Brick Break," Shane snaps, locking eyes with Effie.

The Shiftry immediately dives underground, disappearing from view. The Hariyama lashes out at Bluebird, who dodges with Quick Attack and opens her mouth to taunt him when the Shiftry flies up out of the ground in front of her. Crying out in surprise, she tries to backpedal, but runs into the torn up ground and twists her ankle. Crying out again, now in pain, she starts limping away, only for the Shiftry to land on top of her and fire a point blank Razor Leaf at her. One errant leaf punctures her floatation sack, and despite a weak Water Gun directed at her, the Shiftry doesn't move until Bluebird drops, unconscious.

Effie recalls her, and though she lays a hand on her last Pokeball, she doesn't send out Bounty.

"Argo," she says softly. The Marshtomp lets out a growl and nods, then eyes the two advancing Magma Pokemon.

Argo settles himself and glares at the Hariyama and Shiftry, then settles down to endure their attacks.

"Brick Break," Shane says coolly, looking at his Pokemon. Then his eyes slide back to Effie, and he adds, "His arm."

Effie's eyes widen in realization. "ARGO!" she yells, taking a step forward, one arm outstretched.

The Marshtomp starts to move, but too late. Both Magma Pokemon bring their hands down hard on his arm.

There's a distinct snap and Argo lets out a roar, releasing his Bide at the Hariyama. The large Fighting type staggers back, panting, but the Shiftry unleashes one last Razor Leaf.

Argo takes her attack and drops to his knees. He raises his head and tries to fire one last Water Gun, but slumps to the ground before he's able to. Effie recalls him just as the Shiftry lunges for him. Her fingers close around her last Pokeball, then releases it.

"You win," she murmurs, then turns to go. Shane smirks as she leaves, arms folded.

"And don't come back!" he calls arrogantly after her.

Effie balls up her fists, then opens them again. She lifts her chin, unclenches her teeth, and strides off towards the Pokemon Center.
Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
