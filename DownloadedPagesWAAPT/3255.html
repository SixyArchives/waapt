Snubbull Messenger — <From what I heard from a Trainer, they are from a place called Unova where there is a war.>

Mayonaka (turns to a random messenger) — <And you say the southern troops reported humans commenting on this?>

Wooper Messenger — <Yes, miss. Something about it happening in many places around the world. But it seems the humans don't believe this...>

-Mayonaka sighs-

Mayonaka — <There is no time. Better be safe than sorry...> — turns to the messenger and the three Nidorino — <Spread the word about this situation. I want two extra squads to strengthen our presence in the city... Rize?>

Rize — <Yes, marli-rah?>

Mayonaka — <I want an assembly of soldiers and allies ready at the city borders. Send a messenger to the Nildrow and tell them they either come to talk peacefully, or have their species executed as traitors to u Frithrah.>

-Rize and the others show surprise, but after an initial shock he merely nods-

Mayonaka — <Now disperse, all of you. I'll go consult this with the elders.>

-Each Nido and ally departs in their own direction. Rize stops by some trees and exposes the branding in his leg's backside to the light of the moon; in short notice a patrol of four Nidorans and a Hoothoot teleport themselves on-screen...-

Nidoran — <What is required of us, Maestro?>

Rize — <There's a shift in the tide. Tell Vinollo I need to fetch Hyzenthlay, and also to send an ally, one who understands human language, to the southern gates near the Ilex Forest. On the Double.>

Hoothoot — <I'll take that part.>

Nidoran (nods) — <Yes, Maestro.>

-The Nidorans and the Hoothoot disappear into the shadows, and Rize continues his way towards Rt.36.-
