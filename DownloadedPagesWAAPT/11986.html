Agate Pokémon Center

Sakura is with Grant in her room, both of them watching TV and are sitting on the bed.

"... Hey, Grant..." said Sakura, looking rather thoughtful

"Yeah?"

"How long have we been together again?"

"You're sixteen now, so I guess this means I'm eleven."

"It doesn't even look like it's been that long does it?"

Grant then playfully pats Sakura on the head and grins "Well, I suppose that for most of those years I was just your pet right?"

Sakura giggles a bit "True... You ended up becoming a lot more since that accident though..."

"Yeah, now I'm your bodyguard, mount and pet." joked Grant, chuckling a bit.

Sakura then gives a sad smile "Lately you've been something else as well though."

"Hm?"

"You've grown a lot since you hatched from that egg, you're really mature while still being very playful; you care a lot for me and the others and you're probably the best partner I could ever hope to have..."

Grant then envelops Sakura with one of his arms and brings her close to him "It's really good to hear that, you know. I owe a lot to you, Sakura, since you're the one that took care of me when I was still a little puppy, it's only fair that I repay you for that now that you need someone else to..." he said, until he was cut off

"Replace my parents?"

Grant then gives Sakura a puzzled look at first, but slowly started to get it "... I don't think I can give you the love of a good parent, I was going to say that you need someone else to protect you." he said, with a small smile.

"You're wrong, really..." said Sakura, leaning her head on Grant's shoulder "Ever since you became an adult human and I saw how you looked, I started to realize just how much of a father you became for me, you look so cool and tough, but also very gentle and playful, even if my actual father wasn't like this, I started to think of you as my second father."

Grant smiles "I can't say I didn't see that coming, you were all too eager to call me your father when covering my identity up, instead of going for the friend route."

"I was, wasn't I? Maybe it's because I want you to be my father so much that I try to deceive myself and others to think like such." Sakura then giggles a bit "... While you are human, can you be my father? Please?"

Grant then looks up for a bit as he thinks "... I will be what you want me to be, I can be your pet, your friend, your brother, your father, your bodyguard or even a complete stranger, it all depends on what you tell me to do. As your loyal companion, I'm going to follow your every command." he said, looking at Sakura with a smirk and giving some chuckles.

Sakura then smiles and pets Grant on the back "For now, you can just be my father."

"Very well then, from now on I'm going to be your father while in human form. Does that make you happy?"

Sakura then raises her head, looking directly at the television "You have no idea."

"Good. That's all that matters for me." said Grant, nuzzling against Sakura

Sakura giggles a bit "Growlithe instincts acting up?" she joked.

"It's been a while since I've been an Arcanine again, I'm starting to miss it." said Grant, playfully.

Sakura giggles more "All right, tomorrow you can take the armband off, at least that way you won't start licking me in public while human."

"Can I do so as an Arcanine?" he joked

"I don't mind, you might be tall, strong and cool, but you're still my fluffy puppy." said Sakura, hugging Grant tightly.

"Right... I can live with that, no problem." said Grant, hugging Sakura back and stroking her hair "... You really miss them, don't you?"

Sakura then starts crying, but smiling "More than anything in this world, but I think I can endure the longing as long as I still have you with me."

"Rest easy then, I wouldn't be able to leave you no matter what happened. I care about you way too much to let that happen."

"That's all I wanted to hear today." said Sakura "... There's also something that I've wanted to tell for a long time now."

"And that is?"

"I love you, dad!" said Sakura, hugging Grant even tighter now and still crying a lot while having a big happy smile on her face.

Grant then strokes Sakura's hair some more "Heh... In that case, I love you too, my daughter." he said.

They remained there for a while, eventually Sakura fell asleep on Grant and he keps stroking her for a while, before gently putting her on her bed properly, he then started to head outside, but stops at the door

"I forget, is it my or Rawst's turn to be security?" he then glances at Sakura "... Well, I can fill in for him, I don't think she needs help to sleep anymore, but I guess I can bring a mattress here or something, kinda like old times." he smiles

He then goes to take a mattress from his actual room and brings it all the way to Sakura's room, putting it on the ground and finally lying on it and falling asleep next to Sakura.
