Mandarin South, Pokécenter, At the Same Time

A completely not random Nidorino is sleeping on a chair, at a table filled with random documents, pictures and folders. While his sleep was initially easy, comforted by his Trainer's company, now it is solitary, uneasy and erratic, accompanied by some minor grumbles.

Suddenly, Maekrite's ears flare up and he awakens, sweating. Noticing he is again alone, he decides to leap down from the chair and take a look at the Pokécenter. His ears drop as he notices Silent isn't there, at this point most likely already gone past the lobby and into the Recovery Area.

Maekrite pushes a paw gently against the door for a moment, but then he notices it is coated in poison. Like the rest of his body, as he notices with a frown.

<Why now? Why? ...I am already feeling really hrakaant...>

Feeling momentarily ashamed, he decides that he should go out of the Pokécenter to look for a random sink or a creek where he can clean himself. In the recess of his mind, however, a warning flares up that he'd likely end up having to fight for his life. From a dark corner of his mind a rationalization comes, that it won't be a teammate... that thus he won't have to hold back and this time finish the kill of whatever idiot who doesn't take the message he's on a bad mood.

Shaking himself (and spraying some stings of solidified venom around), the Nidorino straightens his head and then goes to look for somewhere to wash. The faster he does, the less the chance to end up in another useless skirmish.

"...Wait! Remember the Li-"

Pushed a couple of steps back but still safe from the attack, the Machamp grumbles something inintelligible. As one of the hands tries to reach up to the head and rub it, a sudden pain continues the Pokémon to continue attacking.

<Come on, brute! Finish it now! I need to get off this island!>

Silent moves out from behind a tree and watches at the Spoink latched on to the Machamp's head, projecting a strong Light Screen field as well as other protections.

<Silent, my Sun energy is running out!> squeals the Nidorino, ears up. <Do I use "it" or not?>

<I'm not returning back there! I want OUT of here!> screeched the Spoink projecting her voice psychically. Suddenly, she charged up an Extrasensory. Instantly a yellow swirling beam was aimed and landed at the Nidorino, who started yelling in pain. <Get OUT of my way, pincushion!>

"Maekrite!"

After a short ride back to the city, Officer Jenny turns to Silent, who is packing his stuff to head to port. She holds a recorder turned off, and says nothing.

"Well?" Silent asks. "Are you going to ask me to follow you to the police station for a report?"

"...Uck!" the Jenny grimaces after remembering. "I guess I can ask for a remote report tomorrow then." She crosses her arms. "You understand that we are not always pestering you because we want to, right?"

"That's... to the other side of the Archipelago. You might want to talk to the captain, perhaps he can reward your assistance in this issue. Why there, if I may ask?"

"Off the record?"

"Off the record."
