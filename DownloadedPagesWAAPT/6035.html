Nelki: <Still, this looks pretty serious...> — checks some of the wounds, getting a Zigzagoon out of the way.

Teala: <...Kits wait!>

Random Mons: <...ouch~>

Teala: <P-please? I... I don't want to harm Maekrite. I did my job...>

Nelki: <...Fine, I'll let Hamada decide what to do once you give your report. Now let's go, you need [healing].>

Teala: <T-thanks... I... I think I am fine now...>
