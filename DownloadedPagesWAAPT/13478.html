"Well, Coyote was always the smartest and bravest of the spirits- Always prepared with a trick or excuse to get away with anything, especially when it involved getting his favorite food, the Cornn Berries that the humans would work so hard to grow.

One day was the humans' great festival day, and all the humans and their pokemon left their fields alone, trusting them to be safe on such a holy day. However, Coyote was not bound by any such rules, now was he? The only one he was beholden to was himself, and his hungry belly.

And so, at the highest point of the day, when the humans were at their festival, Coyote snuck into the fields of the humans and ate all the Cornn Berries they had worked so hard to grow, filling his belly up. However, those Cornn Berries were very dry- so he went and drank up the whole river that watered the plants! How terrible!

When the humans came down from the Festival, they saw the bloated Coyote sitting in the middle of the now-dry river, next to their now-empty fields. They were so furious! They said to Coyote, "Why did you do this? We would have happily left an offering to you at the Festival!"

But Coyote didn't say anything. He COULDN'T say anything- he had eaten so much that he couldn't speak, and he was too bloated and fat to move! Unable to defend himself, the angry villagers went and cut Coyote open to get their precious berries and water back. Humbled and wounded, Coyote left, licking his wounds. If he had only taken a few berries, if he had only had moderation, this terrible situation would have never come to pass.

And so ends another tale of Coyote, " Cebu says, closing her fan.
