Alice: Ah, here he is now. Good afternoon, uncle.

O'Sullivan: Long time no see, Alice. Good to see you kids again. Anyway, I know you two didn't call me away from my desk just to chat. You need something? It's gonna have to be quick, mind, since I have a meeting I need to get to.

Alice: Of course. Lucius, would you care to explain our plan?

Herbert: Erm... yeah, sure. You see, we were going to head to Orre soon in order to fight Cipher. They've been raising hell lately - I mean, there was that news report about the gunfight in Coulombia just yesterday - and they snagged Kirk and a bunch of my friends' Pokemon to boot. So we're going after them. And I guess the reason Alice called you over here was to ask for some help? You know, since you're Interpol and all.

-He glances over at Alice, who nods, before turning back to O'Sullivan, who has gone pale.-

O'Sullivan: ...Arceus above, you two. You know this is vigilantism, right? I'm supposed to stop you from doing this kind of thing.

Alice: And you would rather let Cipher continue their operations unopposed?

O'Sullivan: Only reason I'm not detaining you right now. Look, I'm hardly in Interpol's good books myself at the moment. That trial back in Nimbasa was a bloody farce. I can try to make a couple of charges against you and your friends disappear if need be, maybe offer some equipment and intel, but try to keep things above board, okay? I'm a copper, not a miracle worker.

Alice: Rest assured that we have no intention of stooping to Cipher's level in order to stop them.

O'Sullivan: ...I should certainly hope so.

-O'Sullivan turns to leave, but after a moment turns to face Alice and Herbert again.-

O'Sullivan: Look, just... stay safe, you two, alright? You're good kids. I'd hate for you to end up the way your dad did...
