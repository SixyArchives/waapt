"Check on those two, " she says briskly, turning to go back to Crewe's room, where a sudden crash and a squeal indicates Trainer dropped a shelf. "And then keep cleaning up. Leave them!" she snaps as Battler goes to pick up the lights, then strides back towards the room. "Trainer!"
