Kadabra♂ — <So spoiled.> — sighs and shakes his head — <You'll need my help for your human to understand.> — to me — <You raised him stubborn.>

????!Maekrite — <I'm gonna be fine.>

-I frown at Maekrite, obviously there's something that he doesn't want to tell me in front of the Kadabra, not to mention the little detail that I can understand him-

— Just... tell the Kadabra exactly what's bothering you so that we can go away.

-Maekrite's ears flicker, then he thinks for a moment, leering at the Kadabra the entire time, and lowers his head-

— Did the thing with the Houndoom go OK?

????!Maekrite — <Yeah, it did...> — scowls.

-I refrain myself from answering with a "So?" until the Kadabra motions towards me-

Kadabra♂ — <He says things went OK. Not that he seems to be too happy about that.>

— Thanks... - to Maekrite - So, then?

????!Maekrite — <There's a very important Nidoran... he said I have to go an important place North, soon.>
