PEFE HQ

Some of the mons are watching the news reports in the TV sets across the interhabitat corridors.

Random Bronzong: <This is ridiculous. I was just there last night and everything was peachy.>

Random Bronzong: <Point...>

Meanwhile, the Pokémon talking to it gets closer to the TV to watch more attentively, as do some others, leaving room for more Pokémon to walk in and watch the news. Among them, a single Marowak with her bone... and an Aerodactyl with a concerned expression.

Zoltan: <Oh? Why are so many Pokémon gathered here?>

Kat: <...What?> — watches the news, tries to read the headlines from the distance — <Sinnoh? This is really going on? Zoltan, wait here for a moment and don't move.>

Zoltan -puts up big eyes: <B-but... Kat!> — watches her make her way into the crows.

Some of the Pokémon there are chattering, while the anchorman explains that most if not all contact with the region is lost as a sat picture shows the region covered in a mist.

A Luxray and a Glameow walk out and start crying, talking about the welfare of their families and mates, giving Kat the room she needs to walk closer to check the newsflash. One random Kadabra wonders if Lucien's mons would be allright. Kat can simply watch for the moment, shocked.

Somewhere else in HQ

Meanwhile, Teala walks into a large room where some cranes are moving and organizing several containers. She sees some Pokémon arguing more or less violently at the other end of the room and steps in carefully.

Teala: <...What is this place? What is all this stuff?> — looks at the cranes nervously.

She looks at where Maekrite is pacing around, being observed by a Shiftry and a Manectric, the Electric Pokémon sat on a crate.

Maekrite: <...And why wouldn't I be able to?>

Shiftry: <For one, because it is my assignment. For two, because not even us work at this anylonger.>

Manectric: <Yeah, it's all done with machines...> — yawns, moves to another crate as the crane picks the first one — <A shame, because I got quite good food out of it.>

Teala ponders for a moment if to walk in, but the crates and cranes make her nervous. She starts grumbling and eyeing around for a moment.

Somewhere else in HQ

Helios is flying across some of the corridors, trying to get to one of the transfer rooms.
