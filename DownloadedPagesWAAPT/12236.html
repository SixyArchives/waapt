Spear Pillar, Unknown time

As both Pokemon squared off a swarm of Magneton and Magnezone started floating up to Spear Pillar carrying what appeared to be sections of chain link. They formed a cage around the combatants. Arcs of electricity surged through it.

"An electrified cagematch again?"

Sed shrugged. "What can I say. I have an obsession."

You do realize this puts you at a disadvantage. What with usin' a Water-Type and all."

"True but I fight best with my back to a wall."

"Or in this case electrified chain link."

"Smart. Hydro Pump Tanker!"

"Wild Charge!"

Electricity coursed around Sparkers body as he charged towards the Blastoise. High Pressure jets of water were being fired out of it's cannons but Sparkers managed to dodge them expertly. He struck the large turtle Pokemon sending shocks through it's body. Sed just smirked.

"Now."

Tanker opened up it's maw and fired a Hydro Pump point blank at Sparkers.

<Gaaaah!>

The Arcanine was tossed by the strong current and impacted into the far side of the cage, getting a not so healthy does of electro shock therapy.

"Sparkers!"

Sparkers quickly picked himself up and shook the water out of his fur.

<I'm fine. Just a little bath and electrolysis.>

"Wild Charge again!"

Once again Sparkers charged at it's target head on. The Blastoise responded with another Hydro Pump.

"Now Dig!"

Sparkers tunneled under the earth avoiding the blast of water. The ground underneath their feet rumbled a little.

Sed shook his head. "Hmmph. So predictable. Tanker, Iron Defense!"

Tanker withdrew into it's shell, turning metallic in the process. The ground underneath it erupted as Sparkers ascended from the earth and struck the Blastoise. Tanker was sent up into the ceiling of the cage where it was likely shocked, though the damage appeared to be minimal. It bounced back to the ground and sat there. Still within it's shell. Completely still. Sparkers began to pace in front of it.

<C'mon out and fight me like a real 'mon!> He readied himself to Wild Charge it again.

"Sparkers wait! It's a tra-"

The shell began spinning rapidly as high volumes of water started gushing out. Des quickly drew a sword and stuck it into the ground just as the massive wave washed over him, keeping him anchored in place. The area was continuously being swamped with water so Des was forced to keep his eyes closed. However he could still feel. And what he felt was heat. In a blast of air all the water was removed and the area became very humid. Des opened his eyes to find the electric cage in broken and thrown about the place accompanied by huge columns of steam.

"What..."

On the other side of the field was Sed, who apparently had the same idea Des did. Sword stuck in the ground, clutching to it for dear life. Tanker has taken a knee. It was panting as that last attack had taken a lot out of it. Sparkers looked...pissed to say the least. He had an animalistic scowl on his face and his enter body was covered in flames that shifted from blue to green to purple.

"That's my puppy..."
