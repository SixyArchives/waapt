 153998 Luke 924, Mon, 23rd Apr '12 9:26:38 AM Get RP Mod
Lake: <Huh?>

It's then that Lake pauses to take note of his surroundings, shocked to find that they are no longer on a beach, but in the middle of what appears to be a busy town square filled with Pokémon of all shapes and sizes, some browsing the various good on display at the tent like stools, others running said shops, while a few simply chat with each other about various things, although Lake was too far out of earshot to pick up anything.

Wondering just where in the world they had ended up, he once again pulls out his Pokégear to check it's map, only to be met with a "No Signal" message.

Okay, that wasn't right. Pokégears were well known for working anywhere. Assuming that there was something wrong with it's GPS capabilities, he switched to the Radio function, tuning it into one of the Orange Island's local radio frequencies, only to hear nothing but static.

Now worried, he switches to the phone function, then tries to place a call to Luke, hoping that he would still be able to contact him somehow.

Lake: <Come on, work...>

Pokégear: BEEP! No service is available in this area. Please try again later.

Lake: <...>

Lake was beginning to suspect that they weren't on Earth anymore. Let alone the Orange Islands.

Maybe the universe really didn't want him to find Tempest...
