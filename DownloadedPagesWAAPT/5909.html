Somewhere in the Johto Nidoran Colony, four days ago

(Continued after this post)

Teala pants and looks around her; she has finished pulverizing some rocks that the various Pokémon from the mountains are now collecting stones and minerals from. Several of them wave their paws/wings/etc to her though still won't come close, Rough Skin and all. But still, Teala is happy.

Or would be, if the social aspect of her mind didn't remind her, once and again, that a DST Druddigon is a guard and hence she should be able to guard which means fighting and beating opponents, not...

Teala -glances at the nearest large rock: <....Rocks.>

«What a failure», speaks again that little voice in her head.

The Druddigon shakes herself, walks up to the rock and takes a good look at it; then she sighs and examined her own little wounds from training. All of them but one (the foot one) are fully healed, and she has collected less wounds in the last few days she has been training with Maekrite offscreen, the Nidorino teaching her how to deal with opponents that attack one's flank.

But when it comes to opponents her size and shape she is still losing, and she can not help but think that for as long as she is with Tracer, that's the kind of opponent she is going to face.

"<Why are they beating me? T-they are wild mons...>"

"«Because you are not a good guard»

"<...Granted, they fight two on one...>"

"«You suck so much that can not even get support»

"<I-I know I can do it, but not if they manage to knock me to the ground...>"

"«Which is where you should be, until you can do something allright»

Teala stares at the rock and thinks to her last battles, not noticing a hidden form watching her from between some other nearby rocks. Even though she has been faring better against the Royal Couple practising with her, she still can not beat them both. Even the times she uses Surf, at most she has managed to knock one of them down. She looks around, realizes that the environment is a key factor — if this was a cave, those Nidos would be much more restrained than she would.

And she'd have [assistance], Golett and Mienfoo coming in ready to assist, as willing to give their all, even their lives, to protect what they all cared for together. Assuming they respected her enough that they'd deem assisting her valuable, that is. And when they'd be done they'd bump fists, go tell battle tales and probably nuzzle each other even if just for a short moment...

Teala: <...They have families living here...> — looks down the hill.

The Druddigon can not help but feel envy. When the training sessions finish, the Nidorinos return to their burrows to [celebrate] with their mates and kits and they can enjoy some nuzzles. Maekrite, while he might not have his own family, has his siblings to visit him... and Teala can not help but ponder how Maekrite has been looking both more happy and wistful in the last few days. Heck even Pierce, he gets a lot of love from the little Eevees and the Zigzagoons when he walks away after his training sessions.

But Teala has nomon to nuzzle here. Rough Skin does not exactly help... but that's not all of it. She's not close enough to anymon her so as to nuzzle or hug after a training session. Maekrite, at most, but every time she tries, she feels [weird] and can not bring herself up to it.

If only she was good enough to merit a hug...

If only she was good enough to beat up her opponents, to knock them down, to do her job well...

If only she was as good as Maekrite has told her she [can] be...

Then Tracer would be proud of her.

Then she would have a place in her team, instead of always be looking at what she can not do.

Then perhaps Maekrite would be proud of her too.
