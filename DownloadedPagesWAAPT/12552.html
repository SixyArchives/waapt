You Wouldn't Like Me When I'm Bored

Phenac City, Silph Labs

-Dune is eating some toast as he drops his team's Pokeballs into the lab's resident healing machine.-

Dune: That Medicham wasn't natural. *Nom* Would she have gone after me if Crewe hadn't shown up? *Nom* Would she... would she have gone after Galvani?

-That last question was harder to ask than Dune would have anticipated. But why? Galvani was his starter. In fact, until he caught Garuda, she was his only Pokemon for years. They'd fought Cipher Peons together before.-

Dune: *Sigh*

-Was it because the Medicham, this "Spider", had taken things further? Would she have hurt Galvani like the others? Would she have hurt Galvani if she were human? Dune shakes his head. That's a ridiculous thought. But its one that refuses to leave.-

Dune: *Nom*

-It was his duty to protect his team as it was their duty to protect him. But... Darn it why was he feeling so weird around Galvani? Ever since she first put on that armband, what, two weeks ago, now? Ever since then, it feels like things have been flipped on their head.-

Dune: *Nom*

-Had it been because of his exposure to Ren and Kitsune, Arika and Chrome? Or would he have these feeling even if he hadn't? Dammit, what sort of precedent was there for something like this?! Is it okay to feel feelings for someone that, while you had known them for most of their life, had no real gender or sex until after eleven years of personal interaction?-

Dune: *Nom*

-She's trying so hard to be human... was it because of him? What sort of issues were going through her head? Is this just a temporary rush of hormones, or is this something... more? Dammit this is all so complicated...-

Dune: *N-GACK!*

-Dune is ambushed from behind by a sudden hug.-

Galvani: I'm so sorry I'm so sorry I'm so sorry!

Dune: *Cough, hack* Sorry... for what?

Galvani: I know you told me to go with Ren and your reasoning was sound but when I heard that you were attacked by some Super Medicham I freaked out when I thought you were hurt and I should have been there!

-Dune attempts to turn to look at her, but she doesn't let go and instead spins with him.-

Dune: Galvani... can I at least see your face?

-She lets go, letting Dune turn around to look at her. Her face is red, and tears are forming in her eyes. Dune places a hand an her cheek, wiping away a tear with his thumb.-

Dune: Listen... Its my job to protect you just as much as its your, and everyone else on the team's, job to protect me and each other. I... don't know what I would have done if you had been there.

Galvani: But... we're partners. I'm always supposed to be there for you... like you've been there for me... like Sam and Tesse.

Dune: ... Huh?

Galvani: Sam and Tesse promised each other that they would protect each other. That they'd be partners until the end. I want... I want to make the same promise.

-Dune gives a very heartfelt smile.-

Dune: You don't have to.

-He brings her in for a heartfelt hug.-

Dune: I'll always be there for you. Remember that.

-Galvani returns the hug until a certain rumbling comes from Galvani's abdomen.-

Dune: ... Would you like some toast?

-Galvani nods. She's hungry enough to where she almost doesn't notice the buzzing sensation coming from her left wrist. Almost.-
