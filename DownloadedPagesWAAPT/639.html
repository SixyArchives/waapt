Lampdude 1: Since when do you ever talk to anyone?

Lampent: Hey!

Lampdude 2: Awww, wazzamatter? Are you mad ad us fow insultin yo fwend?

Lampent: Stop talking to me like that!

Lampdude 1: Hey dude! Maybe she only talks to that Elgyem because she's got the same mental disorder as he does!

Lampdude 2: Haha, yeah! Maybe they just sit there and talk about fwowers and gumdwops and how mean it is that all the twainers come and twy to sepawate them!

Elgyem: Hey! Lampent!

Lampent: Huh? Elgyem? Elgyem! You're shouting! You never shout!

Elgyem: You! You meanies! -points at the two male Lampent- Stop yelling at my friend!

Lampdude 1: Oh yeah? And what are you going to do about it?

Elgyem: -suddenly loses his newfound confidence- Uh...

Lampdude 2: We're ghost types! We're strong against you, you freak a nature!

Lampdude 1: I don't know man, he's such a retarded dumbo of a evolutionary failure I'm not sure if he can ever be classified as a Pokemon!

Elgyem: But...

Lampdude 2: I bet he doesn't even have a real brain inside that giant head of his!

Lampdude 1: Heh! Yeah! It's probably just a huge dome filled with oatmeal!

Elgyem: I am not...!

-The two Lampent continue to degrade Elgyem, his friend floating there, unsure of what to do. Eventually, the Lampdude's jeering becomes too much for him, and the Elgyem shoots out of the Celestial Tower from a window, into the open air, and runs into the tall grass in Route 8.

The Elgyem sits in the rain for quite some time, crying. Eventually, a Beheeyem ventures out of the Celestial Tower and finds the little Elgyem.-

Beheeyem: Elgyem.

-He looks up with his peripheral vision, finding the Eldest Beheeyem standing above him. He looks back down-

Beheeyem: Come. I will help you.

-The Eldest Beheyem holds out her hand. Elgyem, with a little bit of hesitation, bumps his hand with hers and follows her back into the Celestial Tower. Along the way, Eldest Beheeyem quietly rewrites Elgyem's memories of the incident, making him forget he left the Tower, but still remembering what happened to him and his Lampent friend.

After a few minutes of floating back up the stairs, Elgyem meets up with his Lampent Friend, and they begin to talk, in the same corner they first met, about whatever crosses their minds.

Elgyem's Lampent friend never tried to speak with other Lampent again.-
