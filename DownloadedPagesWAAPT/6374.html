159330 Luke92416th May 2012 06:16:09 PM Get RP Mod

Moor of Icirrus

Three vaguely deer like Pokemon, along with an Equine, observe the frosted over landscape from the top of a small hillock, the leader of the group seeming somewhat concerned about the frosty weather.

Cobalion: <This cold snap...there's something not right about it.>

One of the other members of the group huffs, this one a lot more stocky and short than his counterparts, but built with much more bulk.

Terrakion: <Well gee, boss. Why don't ya' tell us what colour the sky is too, while you're at it.>

The third member, who is slimmer and much more graceful than the other three, gives her compatriot a stern look.

Virizion: <This isn't the time for jokes! This could be a very serious matter!>

Terrakion rolls his eyes.

Terrakion: <Well, how about we stop wasting our time stating the obvious and try to figure out why it's snowing in May.>

The forth member, a creme and rather lanky equine Pokémon, waves a hoof at the two.

Keldeo I: <Come now, arguing isn't going to help. Besides, maybe we're getting worried over nothing? Cold snaps happen all the time, you know.>

The leader of the group, the most regal looking of the four who was plated in brilliant cobalt armor, turns to face the others, giving Keldeo I a knowing nod.

Cobalion: <A valid point, Kelp.>

Terrakion: <Heheh. Kelp...>

Keldeo I, kind of embarrassed at the use of his real name, blushes somewhat awkwardly.

Keldeo I: <I thought we agreed that I was to go by the name "Keldeo I?">

The faintest hint of a smirk crosses Cobalion's face.

Cobalion: <Oh, but we know each other far too well to be so formal.>

He chuckles quietly, then shakes his head, his expression becoming serious once again.

<That said, though, a small cold-snap is hardly a problem. However...>

He gazes upward at the steely-Grey sky.

<...This cold-snap has lasted far longer than any in the past, and there is only so long the population of our land of Unova will be able to weather it.>

Virizion nods gravely.

Virizion: <If this continues for too long, Pokémon not adapted to the cold will begin to die off, the plants will wither, and soon after, the very land itself will die. Unova would be nothing more than a wasteland.>

Terrakion: <Bet the Ice types, would love it, though.>

Terrakion tilts his head.

<What's with all the doom and gloom, though? It's gonna' be summer soon. I'm sure this cold snap will have passed by then.>

The grass type Pokémon nods in agreement.

Virizion: <I know, we all do. But we have to consider all the possibilities.>

Keldeo I: <Hmm...maybe we shouldn't worry about this right now. As Terrakion said, the summer will be coming soon. Perhaps we should wait and see what happens. If it's still this cold in the middle of June, then we'll know we have a problem on our hooves.>

Terrakion: <Sounds okay with me!>

Virizion pauses, pondering this.

Virizion: <Seems fair enough. But...would any of you object if I looked into this anomaly? If the worst does come to the worse, it would pay to be prepared.>

Cobalion pauses for a second, then gives Virizion a nod.

Cobalion: <If that is what you would like, Virizion, then by all means, go ahead.>

Terrakion: <Just don't get too worried, ya' hear?>

Keldeo I: <Agreed.>

Cobalion nods, a small smile crossing his face.

Cobalion: <We would be worried ourselves, if that were to happen.>

Virizion blushes ever so slightly, glad for their concern.

Virizion: <I'll try my best to keep things under control.>

Cobalion: <Excellent. Now, is there anything else we need to->

<"WHEEEEEEEEEEEEE!">

The four turn to see a younger equine, similar to Kelp Keldeo I, attempting to skate across a frozen pond. Noticing the group watching, he waves a hoof up to them.

Keldeo: <Hey, guys! I can Ice ska-whoa!>

Losing his footing, the young colt Pokémon slips, falling onto his front belly as he skids across the ice.

<WHOOOOAAAAAAAAAAA!!!>

A few seconds later, he slides onto the grass, unharmed, but slightly dazed.

<...I'm okay! Phew.>

Terrakion can't help but chuckle at the young horse Pokémon's attempt to skate.

Terrakion: <Well, at least somemon is enjoying the weather.>

Keldeo I: <Hey, that looks pretty fun actually. I think I'll give it a go myself.>

Terrakion: <Not until I've been first!>

Terrakion then proceeds to charge down the hill at top speed.

Keldeo I: <HEY! Get back here! You're gonna' break through the ice with that thick behind of yours!>

The large horse quickly follows after him, leaving Cobalion and Virizion alone at the top of the hill.

Cobalion: <So...are you going to go off and investigate these matters, Virizion?>

The grass Pokémon hesitates as she gazes down at the three Pokémon playing below. Sure, she had a serious issue to look into, but...that didn't mean she couldn't still have some fun in the meantime, no?>

Virizion: <I suppose it could wait, if just for a little bit.>

Cobalion smiles, chuckling quietly.

Cobalion: <I would say that's a wise choice.>

Virizion smiles back at him, before the two both bound down towards the frozen waters to have some fun, if only for a little while, at least.
