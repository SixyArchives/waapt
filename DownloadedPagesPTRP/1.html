Castelia City Park

As the sun shone brightly down upon the busy city of Castelia, a young man in his early twenties, along with his companion Pokémon - A frankly huge blue crocodilian with a large set of jaws, a distinctive red crest on his head, back, and tail, and a strong set of arms and legs - had stopped in the city park to take a break from their tour of the city.

Their idea of taking a break, however, was having a battle.

For you see, the human was a Pokémon trainer, and the Feraligatr was his faithful starter Pokémon that he had picked way way back at the beginning of his journey from Professor Elm's Laboratory.

They had been through many trials and tribulations since then, but had decided to take a break from that for a while to take a vacation to the faraway region of their Unova so that they - as well as the other Pokémon on their team, who were at that point safely sealed in their Pokéballs - could get some well deserved rest.

Of course, that didn't mean that they couldn't still have the odd battle now and then.

Lake the Feraligatr: <I'm not sure about this, Luke...>

The human - apparently named Luke - gave his friend a perturbed look.

Luke the Human:' "What? Why? You love battling!"

Lake: <Yes, of course I do!>

He turned, giving Luke a worried glance.

<But this will have been the first time I've battled since...>

He trailed off, recalling the partially painful memory of his last major battle.

<Well, you know.>

Luke blinked, gazing at him with a concerned frown.

Luke: "Not feeling confident, huh?"

Closing his eyes briefly, Lake confirmed his suspicions with a glum nod.

Lake: <I know it's been a while, but I still haven't regained any faith in myself. I messed up really really badly during a crucial moment. Who's to say that won't happen again?>

Luke answered simply by raising his arm and patting the side of his friend's head gently in a small act of reassurance. He would have pet his head gently...but he couldn't reach without standing on something.

Luke: "This won't be the same. That battle was super important. But this? This is just for fun. You don't need to worry about winning, so just relax and enjoy yourself. Okay?"

He still felt rather apprehensive about battling, but Lake had to admit: battling was fun. Even if he had been too afraid to step on the battlefield recently, he was beginning to develop an itch that only a good fight would scratch.

Lake: <...Okay. I'll give it a shot. But only under one condition.>

Luke gave him a curious look, happy that he had agreed to battle.

Luke: "That being?"

Lake: <I want to go out first.>

His trainer gave him a grin.

Luke: "Well, that's what I was planning, so sure, why not?"

Returning the grin, Lake proceeded to join Luke in scanning the area for anyone that looked like a potential trainer, his fear of battling temporarily replaced with excitement, If only momentarily.
