1830 Fletcha9th Apr 2015 03:34:56 PM from wherever you least expect... Get RP Mod
I have no idea what to put here
Unless otherwise stated, all humans speak normally and all Pokemon speak monese. Humans can only understand each other while Pokemon can understand monese and "human" (idk what else to call it :P). That's how it works for normal people.

Now, people can gain the ability to understand monese through a few methods, the most common one being that if a human transforms into a Pokemon, he/she retains the knowledge of monese he/she learned while being a Pokemon when he/she's turns back into a human. There are other methods, such as using a translator (which are pretty darn rare) or learning it as one would learn a human language (which would take a long time).

Worry not though, as every RP character eventually learns monese, and even if your character hasn't yet, if he/she has been around Pokemon for a while they generally get a gist of what the Mon is trying to say, just no specifics.

Edit: So in Jack's case his Pokemon would speak but he wouldn't be able to exactly understand them until he learns monese himself some time in the future
