The first is, since you're writing in prose, you should probably have a few more line breaks in your paragraphs; it makes it a lot easier to read. Try making each instance of dialogue its own paragraph. The other is just a capitalization thing; Pokemon names, move names, and the word "Pokemon" itself are treated as proper nouns, so the first letter of each word should be capitalized.
