Aaaaand that wraps up the test! There are two things you should note, but neither is anything that we're going to keep you out of the RP for. The first is, since you're writing in prose, you should probably have a few more line breaks in your paragraphs; it makes it a lot easier to read. Try making each instance of dialogue its own paragraph. The other is just a capitalization thing; Pokemon names, move names, and the word "Pokemon" itself are treated as proper nouns, so the first letter of each word should be capitalized.

Nintendium Knight
Well, ah... it's been a bit since I've been in any of the RP forums. I'm somewhat interested in joining this thing.

I understand that new players are like a snowball rolling down a hill- it takes some time, but once you've got a lot of snow you're going to be leaving a bigger mark. (Pardon the strange simile.) I'm not sure how often I'll be able to keep posting once Christmas Break is over on this Thursday, but I'll try to appear as often as homework and video games permit. So, I may need help fleshing my trainer out and making him more quirky, but may as well share what I have so far!
  • Name: Christopher "Chip" Wayne
  • Age: Around 15-16
  • Gender: Male
  • Description: Brown eyes, black hair. His hair is about medium-length, wavy. It's also messy, but you wouldn't be able to tell underneath the knit hat he's usually wearing. A similar thing applies to his eyes, since he's usually wearing tinted (sun-blocking) goggles (that also double as his glasses. He's nearsighted.) He's usually wearing a white, slightly stained t-shirt, blue jeans, and boots. He also has a digital watch, that's red- no wait, it's actually a metallic silver.
  • Ties: None.
  • From: Outskirts of Castelia City, Unova
  • Backstory: Being raised around the Castelia area, Chip is time and time again impressed by the technology that places around the world come up with. This has given him a vision... he doesn't know how, but he wants to revolutionize the world. As a result, he's often tinkering with contraptions in his room, trying to figure out how they work and what he can make from them. He's had some success- so far, he's built a functional R/C car, a lamp, and (after meeting his Rotom) some kind of toy gun. He's recently embarked on a journey to see some of the other wondrous contraptions of the world, of course with permission from his father.
  • Personality: Although he's heard of the nasty things that go on, he's very much a naive black-and-white person. Very friendly to everyone, although his kindness leads to blindness when it comes to being lied to and such. It doesn't help that his impressions of other reigons from what he hears are, beyond mishaps with Teams and legendaries, entirely positive.

Pokmon:
  • Tesla the Rotom (Starter)
    • Genderless
    • Ability: Levitate
    • Nature: Hasty
    • About: Tesla is a Rotom that Chip met when he was 12. Owned by another Unovian Trainer at first, it was released when his former owner felt he was too old to take care of his Pokmon. The Rotom eventually hopped into a power outlet and decided it would remain in the power stream, feeding upon other people's electricity bills. It would usually travel between Nimbasa and Castelia... how convenient that those cities are close together.

They met a little after a city-wide power outage (it's a coastal city, alright?), and it just so happened to be passing through Chip's home when the power went out. (Chip was 12 at the time.) He decided to take care of it for the next few hours, but when the power came back on , the unnamed Rotom was glad to actually have someone who cared for it again. It decided to stay with Chip- and this is what eventually led to Chip becoming a licensed Pokmon Trainer.
  • Personality: While usually a silent smartass to everyone, Tesla still cares for his Trainer very much and tries to protect him, as you'd expect from a starter. Although he tries to be a voice of reason to Chip's naivety, I'm thinking that points in WAAPT will be the first time that it's really important. Chip has never had to deal with bad guys first-hand, after all.

How's this? Feedback and possible tweaks would be appreciated.
