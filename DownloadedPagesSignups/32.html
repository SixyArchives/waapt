My character:

Name: Tecusa Tec (She goes mainly by Tec since it's simpler to remember and say) Hybrid

Age: 15-16

Gender: Female

Appearance: She is a medium height female trainer with plain if messy blonde hair that's usually hidden by a backwards turned blue cap. She has light green eyes and wears a green T-Shirt with blue shorts that go just over the knees. She wears white tennis shoes to finish the ensemble.

Organisation: A rookie pokmon breeder, former trainee for a normal focus gym.

Region of origin: Kanto (The Orange Islands)

Backstory: Tecusa grew up rather lonely with only her older sister Janice as a role-model to go by as her parents were mysteriously absent. She was rather isolated from others and while she trained in raising normal type pokmon to eventually take the helm of the gym she never quite felt like it fit. Her journey began when her sister was killed in a Rhyperior stampede in an area that the pokmon weren't normally found.

Extra: Due to her rather limited human interaction Tecusa who is a low leveled empath often finds her emotions being confused by others. If she has a close enough bond to a pokmon she is able to understand it and on occasions in battle synch with it able to give telepathic commands, but it's not perfect and so far the only team member she can do this consistently with is Mint (her rattata)

(Is that last part allowed? I just wanted to get that down in concrete since it's kind of fundamental to her character...)

Her Pokmon:

Species: Rattata (Starter)

Nickname: Mint

Gender: Female

Ability: Run Away

Personality/Bio: Mint was Tecusa's first pokmon, as in her very first pokmon. Hatched from an egg they formed an instantaneous bond and are usually inseparable. While Mint is usually rather rash and brash when things get really tough she can usually be found cowering with her trainer behind something, she is also highly observant. Mint loathes pokballs with a passion.

Other Notes: Being a pokmon that was bred from an egg she has some different marks from a usual rattata the most notable being spots on her back like the ones commonly found on a cyndaquil. Mint can also be seen wearing an everstone collar around her neck.

Moves of Special Note: Mint knows Flame-Wheel as an egg move and after a run in with a TM knows Ice-Beam.

Species: Pichu

Nickname: Stutter

Gender: Male

Ability: Lightningrod

Personality/Bio: Since he was poached as an egg and then accidentally ended up in Tecusa's hands he is rather timid and shy and is only really close with Tecusa. He always speaks with a stutter and often wrings his paws together as a nervous habit, he is also easy to bring to tears.

Other Notes: Stutter's fur is rather mattered and his ears are off kilter. He also wears a scarf around his neck to identify him from other pichu.

Moves of Special Note: Stutter knows the move Thunder-Punch and it was confirmed rather painfully by Tecusa that he hatched with it.

Species: Litwick

Nickname: Ace

Gender: Male

Ability: Flame Body

Personality/Bio: Caught completely by accident Ace is rarely if ever used and holds a deep resentment against Tecusa and the rest of the team for this. He is also rather self motivated and often tends to ignore direct commands preferring instead to focus on other things.

Other Notes: Due to his deep resentment against the rest of his team his flame for some reason burns green (even though that's not the usual shiny altercation) and it freaks the others out.

Moves Of Special Note: He knows psychic and isn't afraid of using it on his team mates
