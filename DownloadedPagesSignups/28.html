Hello! ^_^

You may recognize me as the guy who wrote the OP to this thread. This is relevant because I once mentioned at the end that "Signups are never truly closed."

Well... I changed it to "never permanently closed," because we're closing signups for the next 3 and a half months or so.

Why? We have two simultaneous arcs coming up fairly soon, the Holon arc and the Conquest arc. Neither arc is particularly newbie-friendly. So we all thought it would be best if we didn't accept any new players while these arcs are going on, for their sake and ours.

So, no new players will be accepted until at least the Holon arc is over. (Conquest may extend past it.) Anyone attempting to sign up will be politely told to wait until around October.

So... Sorry for any inconvenience, and see you all around October!
