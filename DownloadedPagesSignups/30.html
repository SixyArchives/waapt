739 Crewe7th Oct 2013 05:53:42 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod

Be careful of powerplaying. Even if a move is a guaranteed hit, it's a good idea not to just say it hit. We generally have some common sense around here, and if you think the other player isn't being fair about your attacks you can talk to them about it, but in general, if you find yourself saying that a character that isn't yours definitely did something (it's okay to say something like "presumably so-and-so" and such), that's a good sign that you need to go back and rewrite that sentence, or at least ask permission.

He has no ties to any organizations.

He's from Olivine City in Johto.

[Background] Martin's dad, Walt, works at the local Pok Mart and his mother, Henriette, is an assistant nurse at the Pokmon Center. Growing up in a waterside town, Martin has always found the water very calming. He trains a team of mostly psychic and water types and prefers a laid-back lifestyle. His first and most treasured Pokmon is his Espeon given to him as an Eevee from his older brother, Gregory. has recently decided to trade the familiar life of Johto to embark on a cross-region journey.

Pokmon Team:

Espeon

Female

Ability: Synchronize

Bio: When Gregory caught an Eevee in his journey in Kanto, his brother's tenth birthday was only a little bit away. After much deliberation, Martin decided to evolve Eevee into Espeon; and Espeon became the first Pokemon on his time; and would always remain that way. Espeon is very compassionate, and very relaxed and calm like her trainer. They're a dynamic duo; and while Martin loves the rest of his Pokemon, Espeon could never be replaced.

Jumpluff

Male

Ability: *Leaf Guard
