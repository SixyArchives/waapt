Be careful of powerplaying. Even if a move is a guaranteed hit, it's a good idea not to just say it hit. We generally have some common sense around here, and if you think the other player isn't being fair about your attacks you can talk to them about it, but in general, if you find yourself saying that a character that isn't yours definitely did something (it's okay to say something like "presumably so-and-so" and such), that's a good sign that you need to go back and rewrite that sentence, or at least ask permission.
