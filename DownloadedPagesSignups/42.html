Name: Channah
  • Female, early twenties
  • Appearance Short and Asian with short black hair. Typical weird cooltrainer getup consisting of black pants, red-black-and-white shoes, red t-shirt, and white hoodie with black accents. Overall, a streamlined, aggressive, eye-hurting outfit. Very Hoenn. Much trumpets.
  • Ties None. Cant even figure out a scarf.
  • Home region Raised in Violet City, Johto; now lives in Hoenn.
  • Backstory She was adopted by pleasant middle-class Kanto people who moved to Johto after Team Rocket started a comeback tour. Her brother groomed her for training early, giving her a Rattata when she turned 6 (but she started training at 10). A precocious if dependent ace trainer, she was a rising Indigo League star until abruptly freaking out and quitting before the Champion battle. What happened was that her brother retired and left her mentor-less, then some Rockets stole several of her Pokmon, which left her a bit of a nervous wreck. She then gave up competitive training and played tourist, collecting junk and Pokmon, move on nothing to see here. Until one day when she woke up in a seaside cave (kidnapped and left for dead by Team Rocket, who wont have anything to do with her beyond this). She escaped to find herself in a weird new region, and actually kind of pissed off at everything. As a side note, she has zero taste in music and a blog.

  • Bio Channahs right-hand man. Woman. Bird. Lo is stern, a touch arrogant, and doesnt look twice at anything unevolved. She and Channah know Lo could fullfill her potential with a more focused trainer, but neither would ever consider it seriously.

Female Tentacool

The spawn of a killer Tentacruel that had been plaguing the beaches of southern Hoenn for years, Big Savings is what happens when Finding Nemo and Jaws meet. She doesnt appear to have a personality but is definitely one of the worlds very few Cnidarian foodies. Kale smoothies, bitches.
