Name: Channah
  • Female, early twenties
  • Appearance – Short and Asian with short black hair. Typical weird cooltrainer getup consisting of black pants, red-black-and-white shoes, red t-shirt, and white hoodie with black accents. Overall, a streamlined, aggressive, eye-hurting outfit. Very Hoenn. Much trumpets.
  • Ties – None. Can’t even figure out a scarf.
  • Home region – Raised in Violet City, Johto; now lives in Hoenn.
  • Backstory – She was adopted by pleasant middle-class Kanto people who moved to Johto after Team Rocket started a comeback tour. Her brother groomed her for training early, giving her a Rattata when she turned 6 (but she started training at 10). A precocious if dependent “ace trainer”, she was a rising Indigo League star until abruptly freaking out and quitting before the Champion battle. What happened was that her brother retired and left her mentor-less, then some Rockets stole several of her Pokémon, which left her a bit of a nervous wreck. She then gave up competitive training and played tourist, collecting junk and Pokémon, move on nothing to see here. Until one day when she woke up in a seaside cave (kidnapped and left for dead by Team Rocket, who won’t have anything to do with her beyond this). She escaped to find herself in a weird new region, and actually kind of pissed off at everything. As a side note, she has zero taste in music and a blog.

Female Swellow
  • Nickname – Lo
  • Ability – Guts
  • Bio – Channah’s right-hand man. Woman. Bird. The game would describe her as “proud of its power” – She lives for battling, has a high pain tolerance, and unlike Channah, can get in the zone without freaking out. Lo is stern, a touch arrogant, and doesn’t look twice at anything unevolved. She and Channah know Lo could fullfill her potential with a more focused trainer, but neither would ever consider it seriously.

Female Tentacool
  • Nickname – Big Savings
  • Ability – Clear Body
  • Bio – Channah met her when she woke up in the cave, floating just outside. The spawn of a killer Tentacruel that had been plaguing the beaches of southern Hoenn for years, Big Savings is what happens when Finding Nemo and Jaws meet. She doesn’t appear to have a personality but is definitely one of the world’s very few Cnidarian foodies. Kale smoothies, bitches.

Male Cinccino
  • Nickname – Dolabella
  • Ability – Cute Charm
  • Bio – Caught lobbing walnuts at university students, Dolabella’s a handful. He likes to unsettle and mislead, using his fluffiness as a lure. His misanthropy might’ve been picked up from his previous trainer, who released him for unknown reasons. He’s a jerk, but Channah thinks he’ll come around, and he enjoys humoring her. Gives him something to do.

Male Wynaut
  • Nickname – Gage
  • Ability – Shadow Tag
  • Bio – The son of two members of Channah’s brother’s Wobbuffett herd that he gave her upon retirement. Super intelligent, but quiet most of the time because he grew up imitating Channah, and thinks it’s more polite to listen than talk. He likes to people-watch. Dolabella leaves him alone for some reason.

Male Krookodile
  • Nickname – Titus
  • Ability – Moxie
  • Bio – Forced Channah to capture him when he clung to her leg in the Sand Resort and wouldn’t let go. He’s an overly relaxed fellow and is kind of like a big lazy dog that eats table scraps. Unless he gets worked up and remembers that he likes the taste of blood. But it’s pretty hard to get him out of his natural state of boom-de-yada.
