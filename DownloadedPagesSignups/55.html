Koro "Rolling" Geinoji

Age: 20 - 23

Gender: Male

Place of birth: Vermilion City, Kanto

Appearance: Koro typically wears a White hoodie with one of the very few things his Smeargle, Bach ever painted on, a picture of Mew playing along side of Darkrai, on it's back (what inspired this will be cover in the back story). He also wears relitivly loose fitting pants because thats just whats comfortable for him, they don't sag they are just loose fitting. He has light blonde almost grey hair and dark blue eyes. On speical Ocations and for performances by his circus troupe of pokemon, The Rolling Circus, Koro dons a light red long coat and a black fedora in the fashion of a Ring master.

Backstory: Koro recieved he first pokemon on his fifth birthday as a gift and a pet, a Smeargle puppy who had just hatched from it's egg, whom he named Bach. Along side the Smeargle puppy, he recieved an ocarina. He had such a love for music and performance; and Bach, For some reason, had a much greater love for music than the normal Smeargle love of art, They bonded almost instandly. They were happy for three years, learning music and acrobatics, until a Team Rocket attack ended in his parent's death. In his grief, Koro ran away rather than going to an orphanage. After about two days of performing with Bach for meals and money they ended up in a forest outside of Cerulean City, in which they ran into Darkrai while it was still wandering. Koro was terrified by Darkrai's menacing form, noticing that Bach got between koro and the nightmare Pokemon, in retaliation Darkrai only said, "Fear not young one, I mean you no harm. I simply sensed your grief, and thought might want need another friend, other than just your loyal companion." Now that Koro could see that Darkrai wasn't going to hurt him, he wasn't afraid anymore. They played together until nightfall when Darkrai fled and left Koro with only memories of a black knight Pokemon and a phrase in his heart that left nothing but compassion, "I thought you might need a friend." After that he continued travling and recruiting new companions, all the while never capturing one by force, and while each of his companions does have a ball he doesn't require them to go in unless they absolutly want. Now after twelve years of travle he is the founder and ring master of the Rolling Circus of Pokemon a travling performance troupe of Pokemon and he has learned the language of Pokemon after much tutoring from his togepi Omelet.
