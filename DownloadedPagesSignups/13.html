  • Background:
    • Pi Warden - Pi's mother passed away when he was two. In the absence of any close family, guardianship of Pi fell to Kal. From a young age, Pi was raised as an 'elite warrior' by his insane uncle, Kal Warden. Kept in isolation, abused, worked to an inch of his life, Pi developed a hard exterior. He is highly skilled in all forms of combat, from infiltration to directly fighting. Recently, he has gone AWOL, escaping Kal's clutches in order to locate his father, who had left his mother before his birth.
    • Kal Warden - Pi's insane uncle, and his guardian. He was driven insane by a member of the Family of Blood. There are no plans for him to EVER appear in person.

  • Species: Chandelure
  • Nickname: Jack
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Flash Fire

In the present, they are fast companions, able to easily read each other's intentions and words, do to the intense training they had undergone together. Jack is much more friendly than Pi.

  • Species: Golett
  • Nickname: Dorfl
  • Gender: None

  • Bio: A complete and utter sociopath, Dorfl's tendency towards evil is only restrained by the sharp eye Pi keeps on him. Luckily, he is not able to create to much chaos, due to his smaller stature and relative youth.

    Slot 3 

  • Species: Jellicent
  • Nickname: Zapata
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Water Absorb
  • Bio: Zapata was given to Pi in secret by his aunt, who felt sorry for him and the isolation Kal forced upon him. Zapata is somewhat vain and pompous, demanding respect from those around him. This arrogant air belies a deeply kind and caring soul. He does his best to support and care for Pi, whenever he can.
