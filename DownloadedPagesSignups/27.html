Ok, I think I've ironed out all the wrinkles in my application. Let me know if I still need to flesh out or tone down anything. And yes, every one of my Pokemon is immune to sandstorms.

  • Name: "Dune" Morton
  • Age: 21
  • Gender: Male
  • Appearance: Brown haired and brown eyed, sun-baked tan skin and about 5'10''. Dresses in loose, light clothes made for long romps in the desert and a survivalists backpack filled with supplies. Always wears a pair of custom-fit Go-Goggles made for improved peripheral vision, with a long tan scarf wrapped around his lower face to help breathing during sandstorms. Also sometimes wears a broad synthetic leather hat when the sun's especially bright.
  • Ties: Worked with Silph Co. acting as security and support for desert research stations in Orre, he received his custom Go-Goggles from a grateful research head. Otherwise acts as a free agent, picking up odd jobs when short on cash.
  • Home Region: Phenac City, Orre
  • Basic Backstory: Second youngest of four siblings, Dusty was left with a lot of spare time. Feeling more at home in the desert wastes than he ever did in the bustling city, Dune began going farther out into the desert, but couldn't make it too far, as he lacked a vehicle. He eventually signed up with Silph Co. on commission (they also provided transport, conveniently), helping set up research stations near locations of seismic activity around the region. Dune thrilled in exploring the forgotten crags and caves of the region, even if he did get lost a few times. He eventually stocked enough funds to make his way out of Orre, though he's kept ties with Sylph for convenience. He's currently wandering the various continents, trying to "fill in the blank spaces on the map".
  • Time zone: Mountain
  • Poison Darts

  • Species: Magnezone
  • Nickname: Galvani
  • Gender: None, though responds to male pronouns.
  • Ability: Sturdy
  • Bio: Dune's first Pokmon, Galvani was found nearly burned out after he had attempted to hook himself up to Phenac City's primary power station. Dune rushed the young Magnemite to the Pokmon Center, where it made an almost-full recovery; the overload had damaged Galvani's ability to vocalize, reducing him to beeps, whistles and chirps that even other Pokmon have trouble understanding. Afterwards, the little Magnemite never left Dune's side.

  • Species: Sigilyph
  • Nickname: Garuda
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Magic Guard
  • Bio: A Sigilyph that attacked one Silph scientist too many, Dune caught Garuda on one of his early jobs. He considers Dune his territory now, and becomes highly agitated when someone comes close without permission. Specializes in air superiority.

  • Species: Swampert
  • Nickname: Rill
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Torrent
  • Bio: A gift from Dune's supportive father, Rill was Dusty's last Pokmon received before leaving Orre. Despite his immense strength, Rill is very shy, only feeling comfortable around Dune and the rest of the team. He can be found most often hiding in mud to avoid attention.

  • Species: Golurk (Shiny)
  • Nickname: Big Dom
  • Gender: None, answers to male pronouns
  • Ability: Iron Fist
  • Bio: Hatched from an egg given to Dune as payment for a long-distance delivery, Big Dom takes his duty as his team's protector very seriously. Tutored in many types of punching moves to take advantage of his Iron Fist Ability, he is especially fond of phasing underground and erupting from beneath whenever Dune is threatened.

  • Species: Clefable
  • Nickname: Selene
  • Gender: Female
  • Ability: Magic Guard
  • Bio: Captured as a Clefairy while Dune was hiking through the mountains, Selene (or just Sel) was Dune's second actual catch. Dune decided to take advantage of her enormous potential and had her tutored by any move trainers he came across, making Sel incredibly versatile. She has a strong beta complex, and will usually relegate herself to support and logistics.

  • Species: Tyranitar
  • Nickname: Ajax
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Sand Stream
  • Bio: Dune's latest and most challenging catch, Ajax was rumored to guard an ancient underground castle. While these rumors turned out to be false, the sheer power of a new Tyranitar more than makes up for the disappointment of another false lead. Ajax is an unusually outgoing Pokmon who loves to strike up a conversation, and can turn aside threats with words just as easily as with size or strength. Just don't make him angry.
