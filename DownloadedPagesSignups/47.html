1161 Eskay6422nd Mar 2014 03:49:47 PM from Hoenn Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: [TOP SECRET]
I want to tell you that I am L
1. Although we have no particularly strict leveling or experience system, Pokemon do still generally follow their in-game growth patterns from all generations; for example, a Vibrava would learn how to use Earthquake "not long before" evolving into Flygon. Similarly, a newly-hatched Magikarp isn't going to know Flail, but a "decently-trained" one might, depending on your own personal interpretation of how long that would take. As for TM and Egg moves from the start, it follows the same guidelines as in the games; passed down as long as the child mon is compatible. No major differences there.

2. I know exactly how you feel about Acid not affecting Steel-types, but unfortunately it still doesn't. I've been wanting a Poison-type move that nullifies Steel's immunity for a while, but unfortunately that's Gamefreak's decision, not ours. Take note, Gamefreak Spy.

3. TMs of previous generations... Personally, I'd say their availability depends on the generation/s they were TMs in. Gen 4 TMs are likely still circulating, while Gen 2 TMs are less likely to be easily gotten. And I agree with Crow concerning Gen 3 TMs.

Overall, your signup looks good.

And it makes me much happier than it should that one of your character's mons is a Trapinch. :3
