Name: Janice (Jan to Tec) Hybrid

Age: 26 (upon her death)

Gender: Female

Appearance: She has tan skin and her eyes glow silver while her hair is white. She wears a green jacket with blue stripes down the sleeves and black pants along with black sneakers.

Organisation: Spirit Realm (she doubles as Tec's spirit guardian)

Region of origin: Kanto (Orange Islands)

Backstory: Janice was born to Mr and Miss Hybrid and had a rather plain childhood being raised specifically to take the healm of their normal type gym a position she cherished. At the age of fifteen her parents gifted her with a little sister, only to disappear less than a year later. She taught and trained her sister how to raise Pokmon and watched when she hatched Mint her first official Pokmon. She was killed by a Rhyperior stampede in an area where the Pokmon weren't usually found. She ended up becoming her sister's spirit guide after a while of seeing how terribly her journey started.

Extra: Effectively being a ghost she tends to forget that not everyone is able to see her and often expects them to anser her when they actually can't. She can communicate with the living through dreams when the matter is extremely pressing though. She was also tone-death during life and thus mostly immune to the effects of Sing.

Her Pokmon from the past (and one from the present):

Species: Raticate (Starter)

Nickname: Storm

Gender: Male

Ability: Guts

Personality/Bio: Storm was initially raised by a rather abusive trainer but when Janice found him and tended to him he slowly became rather close to her and to trust again. He can be quite gruff but is honestly a big softy beneath it all.

Other Notes: Now being a spirit Pokmon upon the request to remain as Janice's partner he's eyes glow silver.

Moves Of Special Interest: He somehow gained the ability to use the move Curse upon becoming a spirit Pokmon, he has no idea how.

Species: Fearow

Nickname: Reaper

Gender: Male

Ability: Keen Eye

Personality/Bio: Rescued as a Spearow from drowning in a river Reaper became rather loyal to Janice and evolved as an attempt to protect her from being killed.

Other Notes: His left wing was once broken and never quite healed properly so he couldn't fly straight and was always off balance.

Moves Of Special Interest: None

Species: Jigglypuff

Nickname: Moonshine

Gender: Female

Ability: Cute Charm

Personality/Bio: Janice hatched this little goofball as an Igglybuff shortly before her trip into the wilderness to gather some unique non-Kanto Pokmon to really challenge the trainers who challenged the Gym. She is sweet and silly and always bouncing around and happy to sing for everyone.

Other Notes: Like the Jigglypuff from the Anime when people fall asleep when she sings her song she pulls out a marker and draws on their face.

Moves Of Special Interest: None

Species: Eevee

Nickname: Silver

Gender: Male

Ability: Run Away

Personality/Bio: A mix up with eggs ended with little Silver hatching and seeing Janice first and becoming the baby of the group. Silver was always innocent and asking questions and never had a Pokball of his own because he found them scary.

Other Notes: He had bright silver eyes and whispy silver collar fur. He is also the only Pokmon of Janice's that survived the Ryperior encounter (he's current whereabouts are unknown).

Moves Of Special Interest: Toxic after an encounter with a TM and he hatched with Endure.

Species: Smeargle
