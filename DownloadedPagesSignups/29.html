  • Name: Miles Rian

  • Age: 13

  • Gender: Male

  • Appearance: Miles has short brown hair. He tends to wear blue - specifically, a blue jacket over a white t-shirt and blue jeans. He keeps his pokeballs hidden from view in his jacket pockets.

  • Ties: A simple townsperson from Fuchsia. The people he knows best are the workers at the Safari Zone.

  • Region: Kanto, Fuchsia City.

  • Backstory: Miles had a normal childhood. Or he would have if not for one thing - the Rians did not want their son to become a trainer. They hoped that he would go straight into a "normal" job like being a cashier at the local zoo. In hindsight, the fact that Fuchsia was even more pokemon-centered than many other cities in Kanto should have told them how futile these hopes were. Inevitably, Miles managed to enter the Safari Game and capture his starting team. His next move was to challenge the Gym Leader, but this did not turn out well for him. Realizing that his pokemon would need more training before they could win such a battle, he left town to see what he could do to help his team become stronger... He did not start off wanting to disobey his parents, but he was smart enough to realize that Not disobeying them would lead him nowhere as surely as Flaming Fennekin.

His team:
