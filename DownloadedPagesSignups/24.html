Well, here goes. Hope I formatted this right. Apologies in advance for needless details and parabolic curves that wouldn't stay out. Oh, and US Eastern Time.

Arlyle
Appearance: Adolescent male. Longish blood-colored hair. Baggy pants, boots, gloves, and an oversize puffy jacket anywhere that isn't Orre is cold.
Occupation: None
From: Phenac City, Orre. He trained under Justy, which is unfortunate because Double Team makes him dizzy.
Personality: Theres more than a touch of the Creepy Child in this kid, with his patient stare and tranquil monotone. One gets the feeling hes not fully awake. However, he generally makes sense, and is partial to blues-rock. Hes serious in a plodding, gullible way, and makes prime troll fodder. Hes drawn to Ghost-types, ice cream, and empty places. And plays the harmonica.
Backstory: His mother, a desperately ambitious trainer, made it to the E4, where Phoebe wiped the floor with her. Broke (pre-E4 binge), she sold her Max Potions to move to Orre and mope in anonymity. There she had Arlyle, and now he's come of age she told him to go abroad (nothing worth doing in Orre) and come back a Champion or not at all. Hell try, because hes a good son.

Bronwin the female Hoppip
Bio: Arlyles first Pokmon, met at a PokSpot on vacation. Protective of Arlyle, hates his mom, and wishes hed look after himself better. Shes frenetic and stubborn, and if she were human shed be a revolutionary in a first-world country.

Sertorius the male Gligar
Bio: Sertorius saw Arlyle at a PokSpot, silently clung to his hair for five minutes, and then spit in his ear. Ever since hes been a fixture on the back of his trainers head. His childishness grates on everyone but Arlyle, who serenely lets him nap in his coat. Surprisingly profane when he feels like talking.

Henry the Dusknoir
Bio: Arlyles moms most trusted mon, who she sent as backup, with instructions not to use unless in danger. He knows a mountain of moves, understands Arlyle well from playing with him as a child, and can drive stick. A little imperious and controlling. Sertorius calls him the Genie From Hell, which is wrong.

Trainer's name: Renard "Ren" Kaleb Clavis

Age: Young Adult, between 25-30

Gender: Male

Appearance: Ren is a big, but not overweight, man with a height of around 6 feet. Ren has short, dark brown-almost black hair usually combed back. Ren has dark green eyes and wears glasses do to astigmatism. Ren prefers dark colored clothes and to dress smartly, wearing vests over his shirts with a pair of matching pants and sensible shoes and occasionally, a matching hat. Ren's left leg is heavily scarred do to a riding accident in his teens and carries a collapsible cane with him for extra balance when needed.

Any ties to an organization: Ren is often commissioned by various people, groups, and organizations of varying degrees of importance to design and produce everything from clip art to magazine ads to the outfits to be worn by families at their holiday parties.

Region of origin: Born in Goldenrod City, Raised in Celadon City, Attend college in La Rousse City. Currently lives in Castelia City.

Backstory: Quirky, hammy, a bit snarky, and a fan of pop culture, Renard Valenum Clavis is the son of Erik Tobias Clavis and Clara Sophia Desmond Clavis and younger brother to Martin Reese Clavis.

Ren grew up in comfort due to him coming from a long line of highly successful lawyers and business men. From a young age Ren showed an aptitude for the arts. Ren's parents ecouraged Ren and his talent (Martin was already well on his way on on becoming an excellent business man). In his teens, Ren had a bad accident while riding a Rapidash, the aftermath of which left Ren with his left leg heavily scared and a trick ankle. Ren now walks with a cane in cold and/or stormy weather or whenever he needs extra support because of this. Ren parents, wanting to make sure Ren would be able to provide for himself, allowed Ren to attend the La Rousse College of Art and Technology.

During his third year of college, Ren developed a love of pokemon coordinating. And opted to be come a proffesional coordinator. Ren finished is B As in graphic design and fashion design - the latter of which would serve him well in his coordinator carreer. After a few years of running The Johoto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh contest circuts, Ren started to gain reconition as a coordinator; known for his pokemon highly visual apppeals and designing and making accessories for his pokemon. Ren has since branched off from contests and is currently making a name for himself in pokemon musicals. Ren currently resides in Castelia City.

Nickname: Rio
Gender: Male
Ability: Inner Focus
Personality/Bio: As Ren's first pokemon, he's fiercely loyal and highly protective to him. Rio shows respect to anyone who he perceives a "friendly". While Rio may have a strong sense of right and wrong, don't expect him to actively go and stop a mugging unless Ren wants to stop it as well unless the mugger is tottering on the edge of a Moral Event Horizon. Nickname: Kitsune
Gender: Female
Ability: Drought

Nickname: Sorcar
Gender: Male
Ability: Magic Guard
