Arlyle
Appearance: Adolescent male. Longish blood-colored hair. Baggy pants, boots, gloves, and an oversize puffy jacket – anywhere that isn't Orre is cold.
Occupation: None
From: Phenac City, Orre. He trained under Justy, which is unfortunate because Double Team makes him dizzy.
Personality: There’s more than a touch of the Creepy Child in this kid, with his patient stare and tranquil monotone. One gets the feeling he’s not fully awake. However, he generally makes sense, and is partial to blues-rock. He’s serious in a plodding, gullible way, and makes prime troll fodder. He’s drawn to Ghost-types, ice cream, and empty places. And plays the harmonica.
Backstory: His mother, a desperately ambitious trainer, made it to the E4, where Phoebe wiped the floor with her. Broke (pre-E4 binge), she sold her Max Potions to move to Orre and mope in anonymity. There she had Arlyle, and now he's come of age she told him to go abroad (nothing worth doing in Orre) and come back a Champion or not at all. He’ll try, because he’s a good son.

Bronwin the female Hoppip
Bio: Arlyle’s first Pokémon, met at a PokéSpot on vacation. Protective of Arlyle, hates his mom, and wishes he’d look after himself better. She’s frenetic and stubborn, and if she were human she’d be a revolutionary in a first-world country.

Sertorius the male Gligar
Bio: Sertorius saw Arlyle at a PokéSpot, silently clung to his hair for five minutes, and then spit in his ear. Ever since he’s been a fixture on the back of his trainer’s head. His childishness grates on everyone but Arlyle, who serenely lets him nap in his coat. Surprisingly profane when he feels like talking.

Henry the Dusknoir
Bio: Arlyle’s mom’s most trusted mon, who she sent as backup, with instructions not to use unless in danger. He knows a mountain of moves, understands Arlyle well from playing with him as a child, and can drive stick. A little imperious and controlling. Sertorius calls him the Genie From Hell, which is wrong.
