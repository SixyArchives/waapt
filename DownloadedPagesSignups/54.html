Any ties to an organization: He's not the best in a group.

Region of origin: Originally born in Unova region, he lived in an apartment building in Nimbassa City.

Backstory: Teddie, or Super Nerd as he prefers to be called, was born with a need to connect. To the internet that is. From an early age this boy was hooked to computers, and what made them tick, going as far as to take apart his father's PC to see how it worked. It didn't turn out that well.

One month long grounding, and a new computer later, S.N. began studying anything there was to know about computers.

At the age of 14, he created his most prized possession, his hoodie, which was laced with a special thread like wire, and projects a screen on the top half of the hood, showing him not only the computer, but the real world through a special camera. In all honesty, this was pretty darn lucky of him, and it took him the good portion of a year.

Well, it was an average day when he found none other than a Porygon in his email...but something was off about it. The coloring wasn't pink and blue, neither it's shiny blue and white, but a neon pink and black color, similar to a missing texture in a game...then it hit him.

The Porygon was hacked. Who could of done this to a poor defenseless program? Or was this a faked porygon, something that wasn't supposed to be? Whatever it was, something inside of Super Nerd called out to it, and was soon downloaded onto his computer, and into one of his father's spare pokeballs.

Now, Super Nerd is off on his journey, wanting to find some help for his Porygon, and see if he can find where she came from.

Nickname: Byte
