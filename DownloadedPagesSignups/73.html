Name: Jonathan, but always goes by "Jack"

Age: Early twenties

Gender: Male

Ethnicity: Mixed (his father is Asian, and his mother is [mostly] white)

Brief description of his appearance: Somewhat tall (between 6'1 and 6'2) and has somewhat short, black hair. He usually is clean-shaven and is in pretty good shape, mostly from swimming a lot. He likes wearing shorts (they are comfy and easy to wear) and often has a t-shirt on. His current one says "I went to the Kanto Safari Zone, and all I caught was this stupid shirt!" He also wears a backpack with plenty of stuff inside and a belt on which he keeps his pokeballs.

Region of Origin: Unova, has family in Kanto.

Ties to any organizations: Not really any.

Bio: Jack was born and raised in Virbank City, Unova. His mother native to Unova, and his father moved there from Kanto. His mother always tried to discourage his interest in Pokemon, but he always was fascinated by them. His father was a daycare man in Kanto, so whenever they took a trip to visit him, he got to see all these new and exotic Pokemon, and he loved it. His grandfather ended up giving him two eggs (on separate occasions) which were left to him by people who didn't want them. However, he was unable to fully pursue his love of Pokemon until his later teen years. Other than the Pokemon his mother tentatively let him keep, he never really had many friends. He spent most of his childhood with his Pokemon or his schoolwork.

Personality: Jack tries to be friendly and amiable. He is fairly introverted and can be rather awkward around people he doesn't know, but he makes an effort to be friends. However, he apologizes for minor things too often. He has a good sense of humor; however, his jokes can be a little difficult to understand sometimes. He also sometimes has difficulty putting his thoughts into words. He likes things done right and isn't against taking charge when he needs to. He doesn't like to start arguments or fights, but he is not afraid to defend himself and others, even strangers. During battles he tries to think strategically, but he can get a bit too competitive, which can lead to rash decisions.

Pokemon

Species: Gyarados

Nickname: Overkill (he was Jack's first Pokemon)

Gender: Male

Ability: Intimidate

Bio/Personality: Overkill was Jack's very first Pokemon. He was a Magikarp that Jack stole from a restaurant tank when he was just seven years old. He tried releasing Overkill into the ocean, but he refused to leave and when he became a Gyarados, they became inseparable from each other, even though he had to stay secret from Jack's mother for a long time. Overkill has a fun, jolly personality, especially for a Gyarados. He likes to play around and swim with others, even letting people ride on his back. While normally fun-loving, he can become very serious if the situation calls for it.

Species: Torterra

Nickname: Herbie

Gender: Male

Ability: Overgrow

Egg Moves: Seed Bomb, Body Slam, and Amnesia

Bio/Personality: Herbie is the second Pokemon Jack received. Jack's grandfather gave him Herb as an egg and they quickly grew to like each other. He initially didn't like Overkill too much, but they eventually grew to enjoy each others' company. Herb even learned to swim in order to be able to spend time with them. Herb has a cautious, careful personality. He doesn't like to take risks or rush headlong into a fight. He is also rather reserved and keeps to himself a lot. However, he does enjoy giving others rides or letting them rest and nap on his back. Another thing notable about him is the tree on his back produces berries.

Species: Heliolisk

Nickname: Sasha

Gender: Female

Ability: Dry Skin

Egg Moves: Agility

Bio/Personality: Sasha was the third Pokemon Jack got. She also came from an egg which he received from his gramps. She grew to like Herb very quickly and they formed a good friendship with each other, forming a sort of Red Oni, Blue Oni duo. She also was the first Pokemon that Jack's mother warmed up to, and helped Jack convince her to accept his love for Pokemon more. Sasha is quite fearless and bold. She can be hotheaded sometimes and quick-tempered, but she can also be very cuddly and friendly. She enjoys swimming with Overkill, basking in the sun on top of Herbie, or eating some of his berries.

Species: Arcanine

Nickname: Danny

Gender: Male

Ability: Justified

Bio/Personality: Danny is the last Pokemon Jack has. Jack found him as a Growlithe while on a trip to the Virbank Complex. Danny was slow to accept Jack at first, but eventually warmed up to him and all the others. He gets along with the others now and is quite friendly with most people. Danny has a relaxed, laid-back personality. He likes to sit back and let things happen as they may. He is quite cuddly, even more so than Sasha, and he enjoys sharing berries with Sasha and tolerates the water enough to swim with everyone.
