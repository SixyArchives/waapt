Here we gooooo! BTW I asked on the creative chat about multiple personalities, and got an overall positive response. Who knows, I might set a good precedent. Especially format wise.

Time-Zone: Pacific Time

TRAINER:

    Personality 1: 

  • Name: Mortimer
  • Age:
    • Physical - Mid-Thirties
    • Mental - Same
  • Gender:
    • Physical - Male
    • Mental - Same
  • Appearance:
    • Body - Tallish, pale-skinned man. He has a sharp face, with cold, glittering eyes, of a striking ice-blue hue. He is no limp noodle, but not exactly a body builder, roids man.
    • Clothes: Always dresses very sharp. A white suit, white dress-pants, and a white, broad rimmed hat. Those who know both him and Riley have often remarked on the similarity in their taste in hats.
  • Organization: He is a mysterious man. Among the criminal Teams, he is known as one of the most excellent free-lance KnowledgeBrokers out there. He has been photographed by the International Police while making deals with such shady elements as Team Rocket, Team Galactic, and even the three eco-terrorist organizations - Team Aqua, Magma, and Plasma. He also has a reputation as someone you don't want to cross. Those that have tried in the past have often... disappeared, sometimes years after the fact.
  • Region of Origin: He has never said. In fact, his origins in general are a complete mystery. The man is known to enjoy his privacy. However, the few times he has been particularly flustered, he had slipped into a south Hoenn accent.
  • Backstory: Little is known about Mortimer. He is a cold, cunning man. His goals, if he even has any, are shrouded in shadow. What is known is that he is the best Knowledge Broker of... questionable morality currently offering services. He'll do almost anything for the right price. He is also extremely hard to notice, to the point where it's suspected that he has a supernatural ability in a special form of stealth: People often notice his presence, but then almost immediately dismiss him as no one to worry about. Another notable trait is that he absolutely loathes direct Pokemon battles. He will avoid them at all costs, preferring to quietly leave the scene and strike at obstacles in a more indirect, and as he puts it, "elegant" manner. NOTE: He is on the International Police's Most Wanted List, Number [CLASSIFIED]. If located, please notify nearest officer. APPROACH WITH CAUTION!

    Personality 2: 

  • Name: "Lord" Cheskers Chesterton, "Gentleman Adventurer"
  • Age:
    • Physical - Mid-Thirties
    • Mental - Mid/Late-Forties
  • Gender:
    • Physical - Male
    • Mental - Same
  • Appearance:
    • Body - Same as above, but with a softer, kinder face with forest green eyes that often twinkle with merriment.
    • Clothes - Remarkably similar to Mortimer, but with a gray color scheme instead of white.
  • Organization: Had been known to gladly assist the International Police whenever he encounters agents in the field. However, he is an independent entity.
  • Region of Origin: He is often vague on this point, usually saying "somewhere in the southern reaches of Hoenn, my friend".
  • Backstory: A quite genial and brave soul, Cheskers is the very model of a Gentleman Adventurer. He loves to explore new regions, and cut new Routes out of the wild. However, he rarely emerges from the mind of Mortimer, and only does so when said "criminal" decides to take a little "vacation." As a split personality, he has little control on when and how long he appears, being subservient to that of 'Mortimer'. One interesting and slightly sad thing, beyond his lack of knowledge of the other personality and his lack of control, is that he has devoted his life to hunting down 'Mortimer' for unknown reasons.

POKEMON:

     Slot 1: 

  • Species: Sableye
  • Name: Oliver
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Stall
  • Bio: A very quiet creature, Oliver is only ever used by Mortimer. In fact, he is the only Pokemon Mortimer has been known to use. Cheskers denies his existence. Oliver is one of the most useful 'tools' Mortimer has in extracting information; Used as a scout, a drone, and a torture assistant, Oliver is amazingly versatile in uses for Mortimer's business. Oliver is also the only living being Mortimer appears to care for. They both have an amazingly strong bond, considering the nature of both beings. He is, as far as can be ascertained, Mortimer's starter.

     Slot 2: 

  • Species: Castform
  • Name:
    • Nickname- T
    • Normal - Tucker
    • Rainy - Trent
    • Sunny - Thompson
    • Icy - Theodore
  • Gender:
    • Physical - Male
    • Mental - Male
  • Ability: Forecast - with complications.
  • Bio: Chesker's Pokemon, T is a Castform. They have "been together since the start," according to his Trainer. And, just like his Trainer, T has multiple personalities, one for each form. This actually leads to difficulty with his Ability, Forecast. Instead of changing with the weather, like a normal Castform, T changes form depending on his mood.
    • Tucker - Shy and plain, Tucker has very little confidence in himself.
    • Trent - Trent is often depressed, and mopes about often.
    • Thompson - A fiery hot-head, Thompson loves to charge into battle.
    • Theodore - The personality most often at the front, Theo is a classic Ice King.

I hope this is correct, and I can't wait to join you guys! PLEASE, give me any constructive criticism necessary. I will in no way, shape, or form take offence! And now, for the obligatory Non Sequitur: Amicable Halberds!
