The Penitent
I would like to apply to join this RP!

Name: Edgar Dickinson A.K.A. "The Penitent"

Age: Mid twenties

Gender: Male

Physical Appearance: Lanky, pale skin with long dark hair. The Penitent has high cheekbones, with a very thin, almost gaunt build. This build is sometimes concealed by a long, dark cloak, complete with a cowl that hides his face.

Ties to Organizations: Ex-member of Team Rocket, leaving for reasons that made clear in the backstory section.

Region of Origin: Kanto, originally from Celadon City, now based in Lavender Town.

Backstory: Edgar Dickinson joined Team Rocket in his teens, and a couple of years after joining were part of an ill-fated raid in Lavender Town, attempting to steal a Marowak and a Cubone. The raid went awry, and while the Cubone was still poached successfully, Dickinson killed the Marowak in the confusion. In the weeks and months following the incident, Dickinson noticed that he was being followed by a mysterious, unidentifiable Ghost. He complained of this haunting to his co-workers and superiors, but they merely ignored him (asking if he had been taking one too many acid pops). The constant, silent presence of the spectre drove him nearly to the point of madness, until he managed to secure a prototype Silph Scope during Team Rocket’s brief occupation of Silph Co. With the aid of the Silph Scope, Dickinson discovered that the Ghost that had been haunting him was, in fact, the Marowak that he had killed. She charged him to take care of her son, the Cubone, raising him and training him in her place. Frantically, he agreed, stealing the Cubone (nicknaming it Q) from Team Rocket and deserting the criminal organization, taking on the moniker of the Penitent. Soon after adopting Q, the Ghost disappeared, but the Penitent kept his word, training him as they travel throughout Kanto, on the run from both the law and Team Rocket.

Pokémon Profile: The Penitent’s only Pokémon is Q, the Cubone that he has sworn to protect and train. All his others belonged to Team Rocket and were left behind when he deserted the gang.

Species: Cubone (Starter)

Nickname: Q

Gender: Male

Ability: Battle Armor

Personality/Bio: Q is a melancholy Pokémon, silent the vast majority of the time, unless he is weeping. He is traumatized by the death of his mother, the Marowak, and he regards the Penitent with a strange mix of hatred and love. Q is wise beyond his years, and while his will never forgive the Penitent for killing his mother, he recognizes the potential for redemption and is grateful that the Penitent has saved him from Team Rocket and is training him. Q often acts as a sort of Morality Pet for the Penitent, quietly making him a better person. Although he is quite a high level, Q refuses to evolve, out of respect for his mother and an inability to move on. Q will only evolve once he has finally come to terms with his mother’s death. Also, candy apples.
