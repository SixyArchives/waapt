First Trainer Name- Landon Everrande.

Age- 16

Appearance- A 5 feet 10 boy with short white hair, a green headband and green eyes. He's a loner, although he sometimes travels with his twin brother, and it's hard to form a connection with him if you're not a Pokémon. However, it's not impossible, and he's incredibly loyal to anyone who manages to befriend him. He wears a green vest, gray pants and climbing shoes he uses to get in trees easily, where he prefers to be if he's not just walking around. He doesn't like to stay in cities for too long.

Occupation/ Organization- Independant, traveling the land on his own or with his brother because he prefers it that way.

Region of Origin- Unova, Lacunosa Town.

Ethnicity- Ambigious

History- After an accident in Lacunosa regarding a certain ice dragon in his childhood, his family moved to Hoenn. However, a year later, Landon was seperated from them due to their house burning down. He met Haunter, and they formed a team, surviving on their own. He began to travel, catching some more Pokémon on the way and eventually meeting up with his family again. He rarely challenges others, but if someone challenges him, he'll likely accept.

Starter Pokémon:

Species- Haunter

Nickname- None

Gender- Male

Ability- Levitate

Personality- Serious. Haunter is the brains of the group, and Landon's first Pokémon after meeting him when he was alone. Their relationship is strange. Haunter considers himself a wild Pokémon helping Landon out on his quest, which the latter doesn't like to talk about, and in return, Haunter gets to spend time with a trainer. He does have a Poké Ball though, but he rarely enters it. He acts like a parental figure to Sneasel and Aerodactyl, both very young Pokémon. With his Psychic, he can speak to humans. See below for that

Other Pokémon:

Species- Sneasel

Nickname- Celcius

Gender- Male

Ability- Pickpocket

Personality- Hardy. The youngest of the team after being hatched from an egg, He's just like Landon. When in battle, he is determined, even if he's the most fragile on the team due to his extreme Fighting weakness. But that doesn't stop him from trying to prove himself to his 'daddy'. He gets into trouble sometimes due to having a habit of challenging stronger opponents.

Species- Aerodactyl (Shiny)

Nickname- Aeryl

Gender- Male

Ability- Rock Head

Personality- Docile. The second youngest, revived from an Old Amber. He's unlike the stereotype Aerodactyl, loving his human trainer and being generally nice to everyone... except those trying to harm his trainer. Then, he can get as worse as any other Aerodactyl. He's the default flyer, as he's strong enough to carry both Landon and his brother, and goes at amazing speed when high up in the air. Capable of Mega Evolution. (If that's allowed)

Species- Pikachu

Nickname- Bolt

Gender- Female

Ability- Static

Personality- Naughty. A Pokémon who likes to tease opponents, and was the first real 'catch' Landon made. Turning out to be a breeding reject, Landon kept her as he felt sorry for her. Egg Move: Volt Tackle

Occupation/ Organization- Independant, traveling the land with his brother.

Region of Origin- Unova, Lacunosa Town.

Yay, first response!

First, any Pokemon of a species that can Mega Evolve can do so. However, you have to earn the Key Stone and proper Mega Stone in-RP first, and that usually only happens when you get more acclimated.

Second RE: Monese rules: Pokemon can speak monese in brackets like so:

Grif the Scrafty: <Sup, newbie?>

They can understand each other and humans just fine, and most of the RP's major human characters can understand them too, so it's highly reccomended you let them get a word in edgewise when appropriate. However, your human characters probably can't understand anything but Haunter's telepathy at first- there's things they need to do in-RP to truly understand Monese.

Third, you're missing some things indicating you've read Every's Essay and the Tone Essay, so go back and read those.

Fourth is noting it's rare for people to start out with more than one PC, but it's also not a problem at all.
