Signing up. Pyrrhic Pyroar!

  • Timezone: EST [GMT -4:00]
  • Name: Divinebow
  • Age: 20
  • Gender: Male (Wholesome Crossdresser)
  • Appearance: Pale-skinned, slender, boy with blue eyes, an effeminate build, pigeon-toed feet, and long, wavy, purple hair. His usual attire is an icy-blue, midriff-baring top with a matching-color pleated skirt, and high heel, open-toe sandals with no socks. He carries a white longbow with icy-blue trim with him, but he does not seem to have a quiver of arrows.
  • Personality: Divinebow is a sweetheart. He's very kind — if not excessively so — to others, and he's very understanding and passionate toward Pokmon. While he can be rather meek and easily folds under pressure, he isn't one to give up easily, although this results in him being very prone to making errors or bad decisions while under the pressure. Angering him (usually by harming either one of his Pokmon or a very close friend) results in him showing a volatile, spiteful, unrelenting, and surprisingly sadistic side; however, this is somewhat alleviated by the fact that he usually drops grudges rather quickly, even if he failed to carry out any revenge.
  • Organizations: None
  • Region of Origin: Kalos, Anistar City
  • Backstory: Divinebow has generally lived a peaceful life in Anistar City. While he did not mind his parents, he never felt all too connected to them. He preferred the the company of Pokmon — particularly Vivillon — and his adventurous side often led him to Snowbelle City and its forest. He started his journey at age 10 with the first Vivillon he befriended, a Tundra pattern morph named Break. He grew quite attached to Lumiose City and Camphrier, where he decided to take up archery and fashion as secondary hobbies. His thin build led him to crossdressing and overall being mistaken as a female, which along with eventually catching another Vivillon by the name of Inina earned him the title of the Vivillon Mistress. While he stopped practicing archery over the months to focus more on being a Pokmon Trainer, he still carries his bow with him, and he remains interested in fashion (of the crossdressing sort).

    • - Pokmon Team-

  • Name: Break
  • Species: Vivillon (Tundra Pattern)
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Friend Guard
  • Personality/Bio: Affable and friendly, although to the point of unintentionally white-knighting. He's very passionate for his friends, and is willing to place himself in danger to protect them. On the flipside, however, he tends to be blind to any flaws they may have, which leaves him susceptible to states of denial and Heroic BSOD moments if he were to learn unsettling truths about those he cares about. Break was the first Pokmon that Divinebow befriended, and was technically his starter when he managed to get his hands on a Dive Ball. However, Break usually prefers to stay outside of his Ball, travelling side-by-side with Divinebow in the open.

  • Name: Infinity
  • Species: Aegislash
  • Gender: Male
  • Ability: Stance Change
  • Personality/Bio: Very hot-headed and passionate for battle, and usually doesn't back from a challenge. Outside the battlefield, he's kindly and upkeeps a chivalrous moral conduct. Infinity's relentless and fiery fighting style often enables him to Critical Hit more often, or otherwise hit very hard with his attacks. On the flipside, however, his relentlessness also comes with recklessness. While he can hit hard, he's also susceptible to Critical Failure. There are times where his attacks may leave only a simple scratch due to missing his mark, and sometimes he can even end up harming himself with a miss by plummeting into an obstacle. There are even occasions where he can end up jamming himself into the ground, rendering him a sitting duck until he can pull himself out. Despite his very hit-or-miss offense, he tends to have overall reliable defense, skillfully able to maneuver himself and use his shield to take the brunt of incoming attacks he's aware of, if not using King's Shield to outright protect himself. While Infinity generally prefers to fight fairly and "with valor", he is not afraid of using underhand tactics against villians and opponents that he harbors hatred toward. Unsurprisingly fitting of his personality, Infinity's Hidden Power type is Fire. Infinity first met Divinebow when he was training his own archery skills in Camphrier. He challenged Break to a battle, which proved to be heated but in Infinity's favor. While Infinity was going in for the finishing blow on Break, Divinebow used the opportunity to successfully catch the Aegislash in a Timer Ball and make him his second team member. Infinity has no qualms with travelling with Divinebow, and continues to view Break as a worthy opponent throughout their adventure.

  • Name: Inina
  • Species: Vivillon (Polar Pattern)
  • Gender: Female
  • Ability: Compound Eyes
  • Personality/Bio: Silent, yet wise. Inina is usually the tactician of the group and go-to girl for Divinebow when he needs advice. Inina does not speak that often outside of in battle and with Divinebow, so not all that much is really known about her. However, what is known is that she has surprisingly low self-esteem, and an inferiority complex where she feels like she''s vastly overshadowed by Break when it comes to battling. While she's generally weak on the offensive and defensive side (especially compared to Break), Inina shines with her utility and tacticional prowess, making Doubles, Triples, and Tag Teams the field where she shines. She generally spreads status-inflicting powders, or outright explosive Powder which she usually uses in conjunction with Infinity's Hidden Power Fire. She's also proficient at whipping up gusts of wind to disrupt her opponents with. Inina was the third Pokmon befriended by Divinebow, and she was met in Santalune Forest. Break's flattering words were what managed to win Inina over, and she was caught in a Premier Ball without any struggle. Like Break, though, Inina prefers to stay outside of her Ball.

  • Name: Valkyrie
  • Species: Zorua
  • Gender: Female
  • Ability: Illusion
  • Personality/Bio: Surprisingly serious and down-to-earth for her species, albeit rather arrogant and cynical. She generally tends to see everything in a negative light initially, and she completely protests at the thought of evolving into a "hideous" Zoroark. Valkyrie's pack was almost entirely wiped out by an opposing pack in Snowbelle's forest, and she narrowly avoided her own death thanks to the intervention of Divinebow and Inina's Sleep Powder the day he had decided to revisit the forest. She was the fourth Pokmon befriended by Divinebow, and decided to join him solely out of gratitude and the fact that she had nothing to return to. Valkyrie was caught in a Moon Ball, and she did not make a struggle like Inina.
