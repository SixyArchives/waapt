Your Trainer's name: Jessica Stark
Age: 17-19 (range)
Gender: Female
Appearance: A picture is worth a thousand words, or in this case about twenty five bucks.◊ Wild, unkempt hair, built muscles and steel toed boots leave little to the imagination of what this woman stands for—Or does it? A revealing light pink and white top with matching hat and rather tight jeans paint an entirely different picture. One thing is however obvious; This is a girl that doesn't mind doing some seriously hard work.

Region of origin: Small ranch in the more rural part of Unova.

Backstory: Jessica is a very relaxed person that just lays back and takes life as it comes, keeping things simple as she might put it. She was raised on her father's ranch in Unova, a ranch that specialized in beef. Her father had wanted a boy and Jessica was very aware of this fact. This coupled with an intense admiration of her father and she managed to develop a femininity complex, going out of her way to be tough, collected, confident and hard working based on the qualities she observed from her father for lack of a better definition of what made a man. While her parents noticed this debatably unhealthy obsession they were unable to convince Jessica to stop as she'd already convinced herself that she knew what she wanted. Attempts to teach her that being herself was okay produced no results and they ultimately moved on.

Her father adopted her as a right-hand 'man' on the ranch at her own request, allowing her to follow in his footsteps. Despite early concerns the two got along great, Jessica demonstrating that she was willing to work until her body quit on her and that she wasn't afraid to get a little dirt on her or take a hit as the occasion had it. She grew up with her parents, never quite leaving home to become a trainer out of a loyalty to the ranch. Time passed however and money became an issue as competing larger beef supplies threatened the ranch's upkeep. It was at this point that Jessica finally decided to leave home, promising her father that she would make him proud and find a way to keep the ranch up and running.

Now Jessica travels the world. She loves her travels, finding an intense desire to explore and socialize with others she'd been neglecting in her earlier years. Yet, she still worries about home, occasionally sending back money she makes in battles or through selling excess Moomoo Milk to travelers. She never truly left the ranch behind though, for the pokemon she took with her were none other than her favorites from back home.

He also plays Last Legend, Anima, Sonic the Sandslash and Grasswhistle by Eons. Later, he sort of closeted these hobbies, preferring to keep a serious, on -the-job demeanor. Locke's father had been working on a case with Looker to bring down some members of Team Galactic, and had put Jupiter's sector, which had begun to act independently from Galactic, behind bars. They swore revenge, and got it, when they set some of the members that were still loose on the Elroy house, and burnt it down with the family still inside. Locke's mother managed to push Locke out of the house before it exploded in an inferno of flames and propane. Left without parents, Locke was sent to stay with his grandmother. Locke had, from the incident, sustained bad burns on his left upper-arm, an inability to trust or open up to people, near crippling doubt in himself, and a desire to wipe out the world's evil. He worked hard and within a few years got into the International Police, and now helps them combat crime, any way he can.
