Name: Fletcha

Age: 18-19

Gender: Male

Ethnicity: Caucasian

Brief description of his/her appearance: Tall (6'3"), lean, and lanky. Average length blonde hair, definitely not muscular and/or strong. Likes wearing t-shirts, shorts, and sneakers, but will change into other casual clothes depending on the temperature. Has a backpack in which he keeps all of his items and pokeballs. He has a smartphone (I guess in-universe version of an iPhone) with which he occasionally uses to surf the web, play games, or use as a psudeo-pokedex (maybe there's a pokedex app or something). Here's a depiction with of what Fletcha looks like◊

Ties to any organizations: None

Personality: Fletcha is friendly, has a good sense of humor, likes to crack jokes, and tries to see the best in people/pokemon. He is also fairly introverted, can sometimes be a bit lacking in self confidence,and be a bit socially awkward around strangers. In part because of this he is mostly un-confrontational and has a habit of apologizing for trivial mistakes. However, he is much more outgoing and confident among close friends He is more of a follower than a leader, except with things he is passionate about. He generally tries to talk things out before battling in dangerous, but isn't afraid to defend himself and/or his friends once a battle starts. Can be a bit naive at times. In arguments he tries to state his viewpoint, but he respects other people/pokemon's points of view. Also has somewhat of a hard time explaining things quickly. He can be very competitive, and during battles he tries to think and act strategically most of the time, but he occasionally tries to be spontaneous and unpredictable. A little bit afraid of heights. (I know this is way too long but whatever)

Region of origin: Hoenn, lived with his parents in Oldale Town before setting off on his journey.

Backstory: Both of Fletcha's parents are pokemon trainers who already went on their own journeys when they were Fletcha's age, but have since settled down and have average jobs. They have taught Fletcha what they know about being a pokemon trainer and battes while he was growing up, so that he would be well prepared when he starts his own journey Also, They each acquired a fair amount of pokemon on their respective journeys, which in turn means that Fletcha has had a lot of exposure to pokemon while growing up. Fletcha has a few close friends as he has grown up, but otherwise isn't that social. Fletcha himself has recently begun his journey to collect badges, and looks forward to the upcoming adventures that his journey will bring. His parents as trainers both were able to get as far as facing the elite 4.

Pokemon

Species: Torkoal

Nickname: George

Gender: Male

Ability: White Smoke

Egg Moves: Yawn and Clear Smog

Personality/Bio: Torkoal was originally caught by Fletcha's father when he was on his journey. As such, Torkoal and Fletcha have known each other for a long time. Torkoal has a longing for adventure and decided that coming along with Fletcha would be much more fun and fulfilling than just hanging out at home (To clarify, Torkoal is officially Fletcha's pokemon now). Torkoal also tries to look out for Fletcha whenever he can. While Torkoal is an older mon and past his prime, he has a lot of experience under his shell knows quite a few moves. He is also a physical tank. As for personality, Torkoal isn't afraid of much and has Nerves of Steel (Partly due to the white smoke ability, since stuff like intimidate doesn't work on him). Act's as the Team Dad, and rarely acts like a Grumpy Old Man when upset. Occasionally imparts advice and anecdotes from when he was with Fletcha's dad, which may or may not be interesting and/or helpful. Likes to taunt (not the move, with words) the opposition during a battle.

Species: Goomy (Starter)
