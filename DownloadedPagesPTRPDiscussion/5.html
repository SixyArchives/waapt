119 Eskay6417th Dec 2012 08:21:50 AM from Hoenn Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: [TOP SECRET]
I want to tell you that I am L
You know... I actually did have a plot idea that could have worked, but it specifically omitted Unova. It was originally for a fangame I dropped the idea for:

Kanto

It's just a year after the events of R/S/E. Team Rocket's supposedly down, Teams Magma and Aqua have changed their ways... Things are looking peaceful. At least, as peaceful as you can get in a world where people fight each other with superpowered animals.

But there's yet another group milling about: The Illusion Project. Led by a girl named Mirage, this group is searching for rare Pokémon, trying to find a link to the Mother of Monkind: Mew.

After traveling the region most of the way, the group reaches Cinnabar... where after a few Gym Battles and other stuff, it starts to get really hot... and suddenly, Groudon appears, still mildly irritated from Team Magma's efforts just a year ago.stirring the volcano beneath the island. As hard as it may be, Groudon must be calmed quickly!

After doing just that, the group continues on its normal path... and they hear about a scuffle by Cerulean Cave. Rushing over, it turns out Mirage had awakened Mewtwo... and man, if Mewtwo wasn't pissed. Looks like we have another Legendary to calm down. 

After the Mewtwo incident, Mirage promises to change her ways. As the groups part, however, one could almost hear a whisper... one saying, "Damn gullible kids..."

Luckily, we haven't actually specified where in the Pokemon timeline the RP takes place yet. We haven't talked about Pokestar, PWT, or anything else that was in one game that wasn't in a previous game. The only problem I can see is, we have no reason to end up in Kanto at the same time, or even to go there relatively soon.

It's unfortunately looking like the whole "Slice of Life RP" thing won't really work as intended, so straying from that may well be necessary.
I assure you, L Is Real 2401.
