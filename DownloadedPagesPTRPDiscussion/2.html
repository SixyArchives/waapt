Name: Kyla

Species: Kirlia

Gender: Female

Ability: Synchronize

Nature & Characteristic: Jolly, Hates to lose

Moves:
  • Confusion

  • Teleport
  • Magical Leaf

Name: Lester

Species: Swellow

Gender: Male

Ability: Guts

  • Wing Attack
  • Double Team

Ability: Insomnia
