Name: Alex

Species: Combusken

  • Focus Energy

Name: Kyla

Species: Kirlia

Gender: Female

Ability: Synchronize

Nature & Characteristic: Jolly, Hates to lose

Moves:
  • Confusion
  • Double Team
  • Teleport
  • Magical Leaf

Personality/Bio: Michael's first captured Pokemon. Kyla is almost always cheerful, and sure to brighten up someone's day. She hates losing, be it a fight or something else, and puts maximum effort into whatever she does.

Name: Lester

Species: Swellow

Gender: Male

Ability: Guts

Nature & Characteristic: Impish, Somewhat of a clown

Moves:
  • Focus Energy
  • Quick Attack
  • Wing Attack
  • Double Team

Name: Kumoko

Species: Spinarak

Gender: Female

Ability: Insomnia

Nature & Characteristic: Modest, Quick tempered

Moves:
  • Spider Web
  • Agility
  • Pin Missile
  • Sludge Bomb

Personality/Bio: Kumoko was first found as an egg. Being very young, she's not as strong as the other members of Michael's team, though she has learned some good moves. She's modest and tries not to stand out much, though it's easy to get her angry, which kind of negates her efforts.
