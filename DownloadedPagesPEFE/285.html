I am not sure how much we want to get into history but this idea builds off the idea that the four move limit is an artificial limit. They could afford to use every valid TM on each of their Pokemon and have a move for anything their opponent could throw at them. In order to make things fair, move sets are limited to 4 so that those who can afford all the T Ms don't have an unfair advantage.
