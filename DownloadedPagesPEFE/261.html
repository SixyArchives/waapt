You could justify it by saying that all pokemon do possess some latent psychic abilities that form this semi-physical plane through the combined power of all sleeping 'mons. Just say that most pokemon aren't skilled enough to use them for more than enhancing what they already had adaptations to use with actual psychic moves being the exception.
