When the fight broke out, Fingers' first priority was protecting himself. He immediately started Fury Swiping his way towards the nearest streetlight. He made it with only light wounds, scurrying out of reach of the pack. Perched on the post he was safe to hurl Swift stars down on the cats. Hopefully giving Sparks and Flint a chance to get out of there. Jet and Kori seemed to be taking on the Claw leaders.

The Aipom heard Jet's plea/order and continued to Swift while coming up with a plan. The two were surrounded. He couldn't hope to knock out all the Claws, what could he do? Then it hit him, Sparks was pretty small. Fingers had lifted bigger things before. He couldn't get Croc, but he would be able to dig away.

Fingers took a deep breath and leaped. Jumping on the heads of the gang, he ran towards Sparks and didn't stop. Merely Thieving him as he went past. And shouting "Dig!" to Croc. In a matter of seconds he was up another streetlight, Pichu held tightly in his tail-hand. From their high point they saw the gang leaders knock out Jet and Kori, kidnapping the Sneasel. Calling back their gang.

The streets now clear, Fingers made his way cautiously over to Jet and the Sableye. But didn't get there before the Buizel collapsed again. A Sableye that was now planning on carrying Jet down into the sewers.

Archie let out an exasperated sigh. He picked up Jet's unconcious form, with no small amount of effort, and headed to the nearest manhole. He turned to the others. "You coming?"

Fingers looked around, the alley was a disaster and the shouting indicated the humans had noticed. Any minute the trainers would be here, and it wouldn't be safe. Normally he'd be long gone by now, but he felt he owed Jet something for sheltering him for the night. And this was a prime example of why Fingers preferred to settle debts immediately.

"I guess so, who knows what you're going to do to Jet?"

At this point Fingers realised he was still holding Sparks off the ground in his tail, he grimaced and placed the Pichu down. "Uh, sorry."
