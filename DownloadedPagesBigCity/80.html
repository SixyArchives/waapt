"Agh!" Jet grunted as he was rammed against a cargo container by a Psybeam. The fight hadn't been going well. Janus had managed to avoid most of his attacks, while every one of his seemed to land. Jet sank to his knees as the Psybeam cut off, breathing heavily as he tried to stand.

Janus walked up to Jet, grinning. "Well, well. It seems that you've met your better, haven't you?"

Jet glared at the Kecleon, though he was still breathing heavily. Janus sighed. "Well, I'm bored now. I suppose I should finish you off."

His eyes began glowing in preparation for another Psybeam. He fired it, causing a cloud of smoke to fly up from where Jet was, the glow of a Protect appearing a split second before the attack hit. Jet then shot out of the cloud, screaming as he launched an Ice Punch into Janus's face. The blow knocked Janus through the air, Jet keeping after him by ramming an Aqua Jet into the Kecleon's body. Janus went flying even farther, meeting another fist before Jet could get to him.

Jet came out of the Aqua Jet and saw that Hamlet had appeared out of nowhere. The Lopunny was bruised, cut, and bleeding, but he had a feral look on his face as he launched the Thunder Punch into Janus's jaw. The Kecleon went flying into the wall, smashing a hole through it. After the dust cleared, it revealed the Kecleon knocked out.

Hamlet looked back at Jet. "Are you alright?"

Jet nodded. "Yeah. I'm fine. Who were you up against?"

"Another Kecleon. This one's brother, apparently. I couldn't get to you because he kept attacking me, but after I beat him I got here as soon as I could."

Jet nodded. "Okay. Thanks for the help; I don't know if I would have won if you hadn't come in." He looked around, spotting a door on the wall closest to them that had randomly opened. "Come on. Let's go find the others."

The two ran out of the room, leaving the confusing labyrinth behind.
