Jet managed to stand, then used Aqua Jet to blast up to one of the cranes above the arena. Jet was breathing very heavily now. Breloom shook his head. "I'm disappointed, Jet. I thought you'd improved."

Breloom dodged it by jumping backwards, leaving the ice to freeze the crane below him. He then swung an Iron Tail at Jet's head, smashing it into the Buizel's cheek and rolling it up before it threw him off the crane, sending him falling to the arena below.

Jet slammed into the arena in a cloud of dust, lying there motionless as Breloom jumped down. Jet was still twitching, grunting in pain as he tried to rise. Breloom watched his former pupil try to rise, a small smile on his face. He's persistent, like always, he thought. His tail began to glow. But it's time to finish this.

He spun abruptly and swung the tail downwards towards Jet's back, only for it to meet a glowing, blue paw. Jet had managed to flip over and block the Iron Tail with an Ice Punch. Breloom smiled again. "I must say, you have improved some. But not nearly enough."

Jet clenched his paws. "Shut up..." he growled. The fins on his elbows began to glow and he raised his arms, swinging them downwards to throw a pair of white slashes towards Breloom. "JUST SHUT UP!"

Breloom took it in stride, flipping through the air and smashing the Razor Wind aside with an Iron Tail. He then ran at Jet, throwing a Mach Punch that Jet barely managed to dodge, feeling the shockwave from it ruffling the fur on his cheek.

Jet managed to backflip away from his teacher, taking advantage of the respite to look around.

Okay...surrounded by shipping crates...Staraptor was able to use the environment to his advantage...so why can't I?

He spun up an Aqua Jet, but instead of aiming it at Breloom, aimed it at a shipping crate. He shot towards it, angling himself to bounce off, repeating the process on a number of other shipping crates, bouncing around through the air, speeding up with each deflection. Breloom looked around with no trace of confusion or fear. Good, he thought with a small smile. Using the environment to his advantage.

Jet could feel himself getting faster with each rebound, eventually aiming himself at another overhanging crane. He bounced off of that, breaking out of the Aqua Jet and charging up an Ice Punch while boosting its power with a Razor Wind. He swung the punch at Breloom with all his remaining strength, screaming at the top of his lungs as Breloom turned and met his attack with a Mach Punch, causing a large explosion and a cloud of smoke.

It eventually cleared, revealing Jet still standing. Breloom lay on his side in the middle of a crater, unconscious. He had a small smile on his face, as if pleased with being knocked out. Jet was breathing very heavily, and began walking out of the arena, not paying attention to the cheers of the crowd. He stumbled as he came down the stairs, landing on his knees and supporting himself with his hands on the ground, bending over and coughing. His throat felt sore, and he ached everywhere.
