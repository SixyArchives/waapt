Jet and Breloom charged at each other immediately, meeting in the center of the arena and immediately beginning to strike and punch and kick at one another, fists and feet flashing at one another only to be blocked or dodged. Jet dodged a sweeping kick by backflipping onto one hand and pivoting, his foot smashing Breloom in the face as he did so.

Breloom stumbled backwards and shook his head to clear it before looking at Jet and charging at him again. After another short exchange of blows, Breloom caught one of Jet's punches then launched a powerful kick to his chest, sending Jet sprawling onto his back and throwing a cloud of dust into the air. Jet sat up, breathing a bit heavily before popping the bones in his neck and standing back up. The Buizel jumped into the air and launched a kick at Breloom's head. Breloom crossed his arms and blocked it, then threw Jet off of him. Jet began throwing blows at him again and eventually threw a kick. Breloom caught it, holding Jet in place as he smashed his fist twice into Jet's belly, then hit him with a spinning kick to the head, drawing a loud, sympathetic "Oooh!" from the crowd.

Jet flew away from Breloom, sliding backwards on his stomach. He managed to get up on his arms with effort, glaring and baring his teeth at Breloom. Breloom merely got into another ready stance, standing on one foot while extending one arm and keeping the other by his head.

Jet got up, though this time Breloom began the attack. He charged at Jet, throwing blows at him in a ceaseless rain that Jet barely managed to block and dodge. Eventually, he began scoring hits, striking Jet on the nose, kneeing him in the stomach, and hitting him in the side of the head before throwing his foot upward, smashing Jet in the chin and sending the Buizel flying backwards with a cry. Jet managed to raise himself up on his arms again, breathing heavily.

Breloom shook his head and smiled. "Come now, Jet. I know you're better than this."
