Kori woke up tired. Even after Liepard's Hypnosis had worn off, her sleep had been fitful. She had hoped the last night would be a haze, but no such luck; she could remember every single moment of it.

But the desperation had mostly left her, and with a clearer head Kori took stock of her situation. As far as she could tell, she was alone; Razor and Liepard had evidently left sometime during the night. She was still tied, and she was sure to have lacerations where the rope was tight and cutting into her. Her shoulder was stiff and covered with dried blood, but it had stopped bleeding. So had her paws.

Anger was beginning to surface in her mind, but she pushed it away; she figured now was the time she would have a clearest head, and she didn't want to cloud her mind with anger.

Shifting her arms, she extended her arm and tried to cut through the ropes.

The only thought running through her head: Get out. Now.

Not so Above It All
Kori woke up from a slight doze she had drifted into during the early morning; she had tried not to sleep last night, afraid Razor and Liepard would show up while she couldn't see them.

Shaking her head to clear her mind, she finished cutting the ropes on her hands and bent down to slice off the rest of them. Finally free, she glanced around to see if any Claws were watching. Not seeing any, she darted for the nearest building, leaping up onto one o the window ledges. She jumped again, aiming higher, and when she didn't quite make it, shot her arm up to grab the ledge. Unfortunately, she used the arm connected to her wounded shoulder. As the injury tore open, she fell back with a cry, landing on the ground with a distinct thud.

Her shoulder throbbing, Kori looked around frantically for a way out that didn't involve climbing, praying that Razor and Liepard would sleep in and leave her alone long enough for her to get out.
