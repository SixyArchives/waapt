Combusken looked at the three. "Don't worry, he does that sometimes. Don't take it personally, he's nervous around groups of Pokemon he doesn't know."

He gestured to the room in general. "Anyways, make yourselves at home. Go around, mingle with people, get in fights if you want. There are a lot of good fighters here."

Meanwhile, Absol was standing in the center of a darkened room. The only light came from a group of Chandelure scattered around, and a Haunter hung in front of the Dark-type, the permanent grin on his face even wider than it normally was.

"So..." Haunter said. "You expect me to welcome these kids with open arms?"

"They'll require your services eventually, " Absol replied. "You have the largest and most experienced spy network in the city and beyond. Nothing goes on here without your knowledge."

"Flattery will get you nowhere, " Haunter replied. "So, what do you want us to do?"

"Help them, but also don't make it easy for them to get to you, " Absol replied. "Put them through tests; disgust them, frighten them, show them fear. They need to prove to me that they're ready for what's coming."

"And if they're not?"

"Then we're all doomed, " Absol replied.

Haunter grinned and nodded. "Okay, we'll do that."

Absol nodded. "Thank you, Haunter. Just to reassure you that you're not pulling all my weight, I'm calling in more favors than just from you."

Haunter laughed. "I'll bet!" After he was done laughing, a massive pink tongue swept out of his mouth and slapped onto Absol's face, dragging up the Dark-type from chin to forehead and throwing his head backwards in a spray of saliva as the tongue flew off. Absol frowned, unaffected other than being covered in drool.

"Oh come on!" Haunter said, nudging Absol good-naturedly. "Lighten up! At least I didn't make it paralytic."

"Don't do that on the young'uns, either, " Absol said. "I said gross them out, not paralyze them."

Haunter grinned again. "You got it! But now you know our SOP. We did it to you when you got here, remember?"

"Yeah, yeah." Absol replied. "No one can see where your base is, so you have to knock me out again. Can't you just do it in a less disgusting way?"

"Nah, " Haunter said. "This is more fun!"

The last thing Absol saw while he was conscious was another massive tongue headed right for his face.
