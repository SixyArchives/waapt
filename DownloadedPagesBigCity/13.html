Jet was running through the factory district towards where Glitch lived. The place was quiet; the factories had shut down for the day. Finally, Jet came to the condemned building. It wasn't scheduled for demolition because it wasn't structurally unsound yet, but due to numerous computer accidents had been declared unsafe.

Finally, Jet walked into a room with a single computer sitting on the floor. The Buizel walked over and sat down on the floor in front of it.

"Glitch. It's Jet, I have to talk to you."

The Porygon-Z slowly came out of the computer screen. "Ah, Jet!" he said cheerfully. "What can I do you for today? It's been so long since we've talked. I've gotten lonely. You look terrible. I heard the police saying something about a fight between Pokemon near the waterfront. Were you there?"

"I want to know where the Claw Gang's hideout is. They've taken a friend of mine captive. and I need to rescue her."

"Her? Glitch asked, now getting right in Jet's face. "A girlfriend? Oh Jet, you're such a lady's man!"

"Can it!" Jet said, his cheeks flushing. "Just tell me where they are!"

"Ah ah ah!" Glitch said, twitching one fin. "You know my rules, Jet. There's a tournament going on in the dockyards two days from now. A lot of backlot Pokemon will be there, some of the strongest in town. Go there and participate in it. Who knows? You may meet a few strong Pokemon to help you."

"A tournament?" Jet asked. "Really?"

"Unless you'd rather not rescue her?" Glitch asked, his eyes widening suggestively.

"I promised to protect her, and I will!" Jet shouted. He breathed deeply, calming himself. "Fine. I'll enter the damn tournament. But you'd better cough up when it's done."

"Don't I always?" Glitch asked, winking at the Buizel before disappearing back into the computer.

Jet narrowed his eyes and considered punching the computer, but knew that wouldn't solve anything. Glitch would just go somewhere else. He got up, bowed to Glitch, and left. "Thanks, Glitch. I knew I could count on you."

He made it back to the club without incident, going back to the room. Looking around at the others, he sighed. "You guys are going to need to start training."
