Jet quickly backflipped in order to get back on his feet as Staraptor came in again, this time pulling up short a short distance away from Jet. In the blink of an eye Staraptor's feet, wings, and beak were flashing towards Jet, making the Buizel duck, dodge, and block, unable to get a blow in edgewise.

Damn it, damn it, damn it! Jet thought as Staraptor drove him backwards even more. He finally managed to get off a Water Gun, which Staraptor dodged then slammed Jet across the face with his wing, followed by two feet to the Buizel's gut and a spinning backsweep with a wing that knocked Jet across the arena then, each blow drawing a "Agh!" out of the Buizel.

Staraptor landed, a smirk on his face as Jet got up. The Buizel was already breathing heavily, and the fight hadn't been going on long.

This isn't going well... Jet thought. He's a lot faster and stronger than I am. He breathed deeply before getting into a fighting stance again. Staraptor charged at him again with another Wing Attack, though this time Jet used Aqua Jet to avoid the attack and slam into Staraptor's back, sending the bird sprawling. Staraptor quickly rose and charged at Jet with Close Combat again. This time Jet was better prepared and able to fight back, though due to the differing length of his arms he had to get in closer, something Staraptor had no problem with still fighting with its feet.

This time Jet did better. Both scored glancing hits but no real blows, and eventually Jet jumped away from Staraptor, breathing heavily. Staraptor took to the air again, this time flying straight at Jet, an Aerial Ace building up around its body. Jet met it head-on with an Aqua Jet before grabbing Staraptor's neck and slamming an Ice Punch into his belly. Staraptor coughed up saliva and fell earthward, landing flat on its face as Jet bounced off his back and hit the ground rolling.

Chatot pointed at Jet. "And our final winner for this round! Let's give all our winners a big hand!"

Jet gave a relieved grin and ran back to the others.
