Lucario stood on the roof of the Fight Gang headquarters, looking out over the lights of the city. In the blink of an eye Absol was beside him, the Dark-type looking in the same direction. "They met my expectations." he said.

"And he gave them the information that you wanted him to give, " Lucario replied. "Though why you couldn't have told them that..."

"It was better for them to find out on their own. They've been the movers and shakers in this city. Destroying the Claws, all but invading the Spook Brigade's headquarters..."

"But what to do about the group that has taken over the Thorn Gang? The Blades, they call themselves."

"Simple. We remove them."

"How? With them? They've been through enough."

"Not just with them. Their group is growing."

"But what of all the gang members? They can't fight through them alone."

"We contact the Flame Gang. A friend of theirs is the leader. We give them a few days to recover, then we move."

"And if it fails? If something unexpected develops?" Lucario said, looking at Absol out of the corner of his eye.

"Then there will be nothing left of this city, even with every gang working together, " Absol replied. He looked at Lucario. "I'll go look for the Pignite in charge of the Flame Gang. Cinder should have arrived by now. Meet him and tell him that he'll be working with them soon."

"Of course, " Lucario said. "Good luck."

Absol nodded and leapt off the building, the Dark-type outlined in the moon for a moment before landing on another building and running off to look for Flint.

Hamlet walked past Striker, ruffling the Mienshao's head before grabbing a plate and starting to pile it with food. "Just take one of everything. That's what I do."
