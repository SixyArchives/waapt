Kori arrived in Gallade's alley and looked around for anyone from the clearing.

"Kori? That you?" A voice came from somewhere; Kori couldn't quite tell.

"Yes, it's me. And I have some info." She looked around, resisting the urge to work up an Ice Punch. Suddenly, something hit her from behind, knocking her on her face. Flipping around, she drew her fist back, then stopped when she saw a familiar, smiling face.

"Leo! I thought Tuft was in charge of telling Gallade what his spies have to offer?" Kori asked as she got to her feet, dusting herself off.

"I figured you might be coming back, so I got out of getting sent off to investigate this gang headed by some Poison types and told Tuft I'd take over for a little while. Zangoose's pretty annoyed, though. He got sent to the gang instead."

"All right. Well, my friends are waiting for me back at the Fight Gang headquarters, but I found some stuff out from a different spy network—y'know, the one with the ghosts and whatnot?" Leo nodded, and Kori told him what the Haunter had told them.

"Wow. Well, I'll pass on the message. Anything else?" Leo prepared to Dig, looking over his shoulder at Kori.

"Y'know the thing with Dragon types? There's one I know is clean. Tatsu. He's a Hydreigon, he helped me out once. Also, mind telling Gallade I still have some stuff to do undercover in the Fight Gang? I know Noctowl offered to get me out of the system, but I'd like to be able to help out." Kori also prepared to leave.

"No problem. You know... I think you have a positive effect on Gallade. You remember how he was when you first met him? He's changed. He drops his hat less. His coat billows the way he wants it to. And he doesn't mess up. We're almost being taken seriously by other city 'mons." Leo started Digging.

"Huh. Nice to know. I'll see you around, Leo. Bye!" Kori Quick Attacked to the end of the alley, waving at the Sandslash.

"Bye, Kori!" Leo dove underground and started Digging away.

As soon as he was out of sight, Kori began the long trek back to the Fight Gang HQ.
