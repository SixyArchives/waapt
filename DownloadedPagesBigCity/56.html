1378 SpicySomething3rd Apr 2011 01:34:12 PM Get RP Mod
Gaske waited with bated breath after the first noise indicated someone was nearby. He wasn't disappointed, barely a heartbeat later, he heard a rustling, several short thumps and a muted gasp that could have been anything from someone getting jumped, or just stumbling on a bit of debris the alley was littered with.

Then silence.

What he shouldn't do, he knew, was leap out and sink his claws into whoever was out there to placate his nerves.

With the bare minimum of noise and movement he tilted the cardboard box he was under so he could see the ground in both directions. Odd; no feet. Levitation or flight was possible, but both methods created sound of some kind. But it was silent, the noise-makers had vanished.

Strange.

Slowly, he emerged from his hiding place, and glanced around, sudden movement at the edge of his vision made him whirl to face it, arms outstretched, then paused, confused.

This wasn't what he'd been expecting...
