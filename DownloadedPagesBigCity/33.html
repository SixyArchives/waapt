Jet raised an acknowledging paw at Fingers, taking up a combat stance and facing Meganium. His opponent smirked, confident in his type advantages and greater size.

Chatot raised his wings. "Go!"

Meganium began immediately, throwing a Vine Whip at Jet. Jet crossed his arms, blocking the vines with Protect, the attack slamming into the shield that resulted from his arms. As the vines retracted Jet fired a Water Gun that Meganium sidestepped, countering it with a Razor Leaf that Jet blocked with Razor Wind. He then ran forward towards Meganium, an Ice Punch building up on his paw. He jumped and threw it at Meganium, only to be blocked by a Reflect.

Jet's eyes widened for a moment before a Vine Whip slammed into his cheek, rolling the skin up and throwing him across the arena. He managed to land on his feet, firing off another Razor Wind that was smashed aside by Meganium's Vine Whip. Jet was about to launch another Water Gun when Meganium let loose a blue powder that spread all over the arena and onto Jet.

The Buizel was about to shoot the Water Gun off when he began to feel drowsy.

Sleeping Powder...crap... He stumbled drunkenly around the arena, causing Meganium to smirk and, with a loud yell, fire a Petal Dance straight at Jet. Jet managed to become lucid enough to try and resist the high wind and petals, but his head was slowly turned upwards until he was tossed into the air, slamming into the ground face-first and struggling to get up.
