He then ran forward towards Meganium, an Ice Punch building up on his paw. He jumped and threw it at Meganium, only to be blocked by a Reflect.

Jet's eyes widened for a moment before a Vine Whip slammed into his cheek, rolling the skin up and throwing him across the arena. He managed to land on his feet, firing off another Razor Wind that was smashed aside by Meganium's Vine Whip. Jet was about to launch another Water Gun when Meganium let loose a blue powder that spread all over the arena and onto Jet.

The Buizel was about to shoot the Water Gun off when he began to feel drowsy.
