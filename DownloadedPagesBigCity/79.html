"Strange, why do I feel fine? I should still be on the verge of death." He looked around and found he was resting on a rather beat up couch and in a room with no real windows. Suddenly, the only door to the room opened.

"Hey Guys...er, wait. Hey Guy!" A tiny spider crawled up to the enormous dragon. "I'm Plug! Welcome to my newest home! Well, technically it's not really a home, but I'll save all the details for later. Looks like you're feeling well!"

"Yes...I would like to meet the one who rescued me, so as to give my thanks." Tatsu was trying very hard to focus on the puny Pokemon.

"Well, that would be me!"

An awkward silence followed the statement.

Then, one of Tatsu's arms looked closely at Plug. "No, seriously. There's no way you could have. What can you carry, huh? 2 pounds?"

"Well! I do admit I'm small, but I can safely say I can carry 5 pounds!" Plug shot back at the arm.

The opposite head spoke up. "I'm having a hard time believing you, but you seem sincere, and I don't see any gain for you to rescue us, so thank you."

Tatsu looked hard at the Joltik, not that he wasn't already staring to actually focus on him. "Fine. I also give my gratitude. I wish to discover how in the world you managed to carry us, but if you do not wish to-"

"Oh, that's easy, come on! Follow me!"

Plug led Tatsu out the room.
