"Dragonite has a mission for you, " Salamence replied. "Ingratiate yourselves with the Thorn Gang. They control the territory around this building, do they not?"

"Yes, sir." Katan replied. "And our mission?"

"Continue Razor's work. Destabilize the balance of power among the city gangs as much as possible. But stay out of sight. If you see defeat, merely return to the canyons. You are one of our most trusted groups. It would be a shame to waste you here."

Katan bowed. As he spoke, fourteen other Scythers and Scizors appeared in an arc behind him. "You have nothing to worry about, my lord. The Blades are ever at your service."

Salamence nodded. "Very good. We look forward to hearing of your successes."

With that, he took off again, circling the building once before heading away from the city. Katan turned to his compatriots. "The Thorn Gang makes their base on the tenth floor, don't they?"

"Yes, sir." a Scyther replied. Katan nodded.

"Good. Send them an emissary. Ask for a partnership. We help them gain power in the city, and they help us in our goals."

The group saluted and disappeared into the shadows once again. Katan allowed himself a smirk.
