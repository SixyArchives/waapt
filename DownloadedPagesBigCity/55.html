1367 SpicySomething2nd Apr 2011 04:50:01 PM Get RP Mod
The city had a chill about it, Gaske thought. It wasn't the air he was sure of that; in fact it was positively balmy. But the eyes of the Pokemon he passed, the way they avoided his eyes and kept their heads down made him uneasy.

Gaske noticed these things, it was his business to notice things after all, especially other peoples. He'd be the first to admit he didn't have the instant-absorb abilities some detectives had, but he was thorough, and patient. Few Pokemon understand the term 'patient' as much as a Banette.

At the moment, he had been employed to track down a missing diamond. Normally, he would have dismissed the job out of hand; it would have changed four times and be halfway across the country by now, but his client had assured him it had... unique properties and would likely only be known, and coveted, by a few individuals. Gaske had his doubts, but times were tough and he'd been paid a small retainer already.

To his surprise, he'd got a solid lead, and had learned where the thieves were meeting to hand over another ill-gotten gain. He'd got to the alleyway early and had hidden under a few cardboard boxes left to rot. Suddenly, he heard a scraping, as if someone had brushed against a wall...
