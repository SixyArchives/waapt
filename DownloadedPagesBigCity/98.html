Katan sat in the "throne room" of the Thorn Gang's building. The Scizor was cradling his chin on one claw, a bored expression on his face. He'd received word that one of his superiors would be coming to the building. And he was late. Katan despised tardiness.

Finally a flapping of wings heralded the expected arrival. Flygon landed on a windowsill, stepping quickly into the room and turning his head to face Katan. "So...you're here waiting."

"Yes, " Katan replied, the expression not leaving his face.

"And where is the rest of your gang?"

"Dispensing our product, if you catch my meaning." the Scizor replied, getting up and walking towards Flygon with his claws clasped together behind his back. "Fang, Feather, and Volt are already dependent on us for shipments. If we cut them off or make it look like more are getting to one gang, the others will turn on them."

"So it aids our cause, " Flygon muttered, smirking. "Very good. And what else have you found out?"

"A group of Pokemon from the Fight Gang went to the Spook Brigade last night. They know of us."

"And?"

"A Spinarak we sent to their headquarters confirmed that they'll try to attack us. So your arrival is fortuitous."

"Indeed, " Flygon replied, smirking. "Very well. Continue as if nothing is wrong. We'll let them come to us."

"Ow..." Cinder groaned as he lay on the mat, various spots on his body covered in frost. Jet didn't look much better off, the Buizel also groaning in pain at the numerous scorch marks on his own form. Both had given as much as they had, and taken as much as they could.

"I'll admit..." Jet said weakly. "You've...got quite the arm..."

"So...d'you, " Cinder replied. Both managed to sit up and Jet smiled. "You'll be a big help, I think."
