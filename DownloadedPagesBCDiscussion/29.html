There are the docks with the houses and warehouses around. The place was big enough to host a battle between at least four distinct gangs if I'm counting correctly. Beyond the place there's the beach. How nearby it is to the docks I have no idea. But we know the city is a coastside or lakeside one.

If I am counting correctly (from memory, not from the thread), there are at least three different squares somewhere across the city. One of them seemed to be close to Jet's place. Which also reminds me the sewers and stuff. Also Jet's place is hidden bug big enough that a lot of mons including an Absol fit in there. A second square seemed to be somewhere in the suburbs. One of the three squares has a statue. I don't see any indication that the squares seem to be close to each other, but none that they are in different gang territories either.

Don't know exactly where Thorn Gang's HQ are relative to the other landmarks around the city, but I think it was the tallest building around. Seems to suggest it was located in the suburbs. There were abandoned, or at least pretty inactive, constructions around it. Also bubble wrap
